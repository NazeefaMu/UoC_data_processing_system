## Title: Mobitel-ANCL partnership grows into 11th consecutive year
## Supertitle: Prestigious Schoolboy Cricketer of the Year contest completes 40 years:
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, December 15, 2017 - 01:00
Prestigious Schoolboy Cricketer of the Year contest completes 40 years:
Mobitel-ANCL partnership grows into 11th consecutive year
Mobitel, one of the leading telecommunication giants in Sri Lanka announced their continuation as main sponsors of the prestigious Schoolboy Cricketer the Year Awards for the eleventh consecutive. The awards will be held next year.
Mobitel has been working in collaboration with The Associated Newspapers of Ceylon Limited to spread the excitement and pomp that the Schoolboy Cricketer of the Year Awards brings to the country on a grand scale.
Chief Executive Officer of Mobitel Nalin Perera met with high ranking officials of ANCL at the main boardroom of Lake House yesterday to confirm Mobitels continuation as the main sponsor for next years Schoolboy Cricketer of the Year Awards which has now become a much looked forward to annual event in the school cricket calendar.
Commenting on the occasion Mobitel CEO Nalin Perera said: I am delighted to see Sri Lankas first ever school cricket awards show completing 40 years of resounding success. It has remained the most looked forward to event in Sri Lankas school cricket calendar. Mobitel is honored to be associated with the Observer-Mobitel Schoolboy Cricketer of the Year contest, which has produced many legendary Sri Lanka cricketers since 1978/79.
Winning a title at this event is a dream of all schoolboy cricketers and the Observer-Mobitel Schoolboy Cricketer of the Year contest has remained the stepping stone for the big league. This will be the 11th consecutive year that Mobitel will be sponsoring this exemplary event. We have shared a great partnership with the Sunday Observer for over a decade - in hosting this great show to foster school cricket and appreciate milestone achievements of schoolboy cricketers. Undoubtedly, this event has been a great source of encouragement and inspiration to schoolboy cricketers who are aspiring to play for the national team in the future. We wish them every success!
