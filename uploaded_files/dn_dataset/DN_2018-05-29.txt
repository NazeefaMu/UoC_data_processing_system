## Title: 24 killed, 170,000 affected due to bad weather
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, May 28, 2018 - 17:38
24 killed, 170,000 affected due to bad weather

At least 24 individuals killed and more than 170,000 individuals affected in 20 districts due to the ongoing adverse weather condition, the Disaster Management Center (DMC)said.
Water levels of main rivers are currently showing normal levels but water levels at Kalu River and Nilwala River are dropping slowly.
Ratnapura, Kalutara, Kegalle, Nuwara-Eliya districts are still under land slide threats the DMC added.
The DMC urged all relief donating agencies to work with Ministry of Irrigation, Water Resources and Disaster Management and may liaise with NDRSC for relief assistance related matters.

## Title: Jaffna, Kilinochchi, Mullaitivu to be incorporated in development programme - PM
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, May 29, 2018 - 01:06
Jaffna, Kilinochchi, Mullaitivu to be incorporated in development programme - PM
The Jaffna, Kilinochchi and Mullaitivu districts will be incorporated to the countrys overall development process, Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe said.
He issued this pledge when he addressed public officials at a discussion held at the Kilinochchi Kachcheri during his visit to the Kilinochchi and Jaffna districts to review the progress of development work yesterday. The Prime Minister said the development strip encompassing Hambantota, Kandy and Moneragala would be extended to Jaffna to incorporate those districts to the countrys development programme.
The Prime Minister who said that development projects were already underway in Jaffna, added that Kilinochchi and Mullaitivu too required more and more development projects.
The Prime Minister who noted that the Northern Province was in abject poverty in comparison to the other Provinces, said that Kilinochchi and Mannar were the least developed areas in the province.
He said the objective of the government was to develop the Northern Province and make it economically strong in line with other areas in the country.

## Title: Rohitha Abeygunawardenas case fixed for trial on July 6
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, May 28, 2018 - 13:00
Rohitha Abeygunawardenas case fixed for trial on July 6

The Colombo High Court fixed the corruption case filed against former Minister and Kalutara District Parliamentarian RohithaAbeygunawardena, for July 6.
He is alleged to have acquired assets estimated at Rs.41.1 million unlawfully.
When the case came up before High Court Judge A.A.R. Heiyanthuduwa, the court was informed through the defense counsel that Rohitha Abeygunawardena was not appearing in court since he is suffering from an illness.

## Title: T.B. Ekanayake arrives at FCID
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, May 28, 2018 - 12:27
T.B. Ekanayake arrives at FCID

Former State Minister of Lands T. B. Ekanayake was summoned by the Financial Crimes Investigation Division (FCID) to record a statement.
The FCID alleged him to have been involved in misusing state vehicles during his tenure as the State Minister of Cultural Affairs.
He was notified to appear before the FCID last Friday but the date was re-fixed for today after the State Minister informed that he was unable to appear before the FCID since it was a Parliamentary seating day.
