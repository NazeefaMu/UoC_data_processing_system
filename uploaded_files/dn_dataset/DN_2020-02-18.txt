## Title: Boat factory in Karainagar to get facelift
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, February 18, 2020 - 17:26
Boat factory in Karainagar to get facelift
The Boat production company in Karainagar, Jaffna will be updated with new technology for the benefit of the entire country, Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Minister Douglas Devananda's said.He made an inspection tour in the factory recently and discussed what has to be done to develop the factory with the officials of the Cey-Nor Foundation at his ministry.The Minister said the boat production in the past was not done properly, and it should be carried out scientifically.He said that help is sought from Norwegian boat manufacturers who use the most modern technology in this regard.Norwegian investors also participated in the discussion.

## Title: CoPA report of 844 Govt. Institutions presented to Parliament
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, February 18, 2020 - 15:34
CoPA report of 844 Govt. Institutions presented to Parliament
The Committee on Public Accounts (CoPA) report on 844 institutions of State, Provincial Councils and Local Government for the Fiscal Year of 2018, was tabled in Parliament today by its Chairman, State Minister Lasantha Alagiyawanna. That report consists of over 800 pages.Public Accounting Systems, Financial Control and performance of Government Institutions are annually evaluated by a computer networking system and the report that was prepared accordingly was tabled by the Chairman.According to the computer networking system in evaluating the State, Provincial Councils and Local Government institutions functioning under the direction of the Committee on Public Accounts, nearly 110 organizations which have achieved a high performance will be presented with awards at the Parliamentary Complex under the patronage of President Gotabaya Rajapaksa on the 28th of February 2020.

## Title: Institutions to be established to develop Tourism
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, February 18, 2020 - 16:05
Institutions to be established to develop Tourism
New institution will be established to develop, promote and market Sri Lanka as a best tourist and travel destination both in Sri Lanka and abroad.Industrial Export, Investment Promotion, Tourism and Aviation Services Minister Prasanna Ranatunga said the government has paid its attention to amalgamate Sri Lanka Tourism Development Authority, Sri Lanka Tourism Promotion Bureau and Sri Lanka Convention Bureau and set up this new Institution to create Sri Lanka as one of the most popular international tourist destinations.He further said there is a suggestion to amend the Sri Lanka Tourism Act No. 38 of 2005 for the betterment of the tourism sector. The amendment is expected to be submitted to the Cabinet for the approval in the future, he added.He further said that priority has been given to elevate the current status of Sri Lanka Institute of Tourism and Hotel Management ( SLITM ) as a degree awarding institution. Plans are also underway to offer a bachelor degree in Hospitality Management in the near future.
