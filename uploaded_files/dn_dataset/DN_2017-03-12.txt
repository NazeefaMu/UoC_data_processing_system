## Title: Fire on Daham Mawatha, Maharagama
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Sunday, March 12, 2017 - 16:15
Fire on Daham Mawatha, Maharagama
A fire has ravaged several houses on Daham Mawatha in Maharagama.
Reports say that as many as seven houses are burned.
The Fire Brigade is on the scene and attempting to put out the fire.
The cause of the fire is yet unknown.
Maharagama Police is investigating.

## Title: Science come back strongly with 69/3 win
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Sunday, March 12, 2017 - 01:00
Science come back strongly with 69/3 win
Science College with two defeats behind them came back strongly to pull off a massive win by 69 points (11 tries, 7 conversions) to 3 (1 penalty) win over Prince of Wales in their Singer Schools rugby league match played at Longden Place yesterday.
At half time Science led 29-0.

## Title: Sri Lanka must fulfil obligations of UNHRC Resolution: TNA
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Sunday, March 12, 2017 - 15:30
Sri Lanka must fulfil obligations of UNHRC Resolution: TNA
The Tamil National Alliance (TNA), in a statement released over the weekend, said Sri Lanka must fulfill its obligation of the United Nations Human Right Council Resolution of October 2015, under the strictest conditions.
It further proposed that an office of the UN High Commissioner for Human Rights is established in Sri Lanka to monitor the implementation of the Resolution.
