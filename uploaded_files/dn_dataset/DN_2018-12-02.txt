## Title: O/L examination commences tomorrow
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Sunday, December 2, 2018 - 18:50
O/L examination commences tomorrow
The G.C.E. Ordinary Level examination 2018 will commence tomorrow and will last for 09 days, concluding on the 12th December.
Commissioner General of Examinations, Sanath B Pujitha, said that 656,641 candidates will sit for the exam at 4661 examination centres this year.
The commissioner general further notified that mobile phones and smartwatches will be strictly prohibited within the premises of the examination centres.
Meanwhile, Police Media Spokesperson SP Ruwan Gunasekara said that special security arrangements will be in place for the G.C.E. Ordinary Level Examination.
