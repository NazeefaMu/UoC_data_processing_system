## Title: Committee to look into private bus owners' issues
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, December 3, 2016 - 10:00
Committee to look into private bus owners' issues

The President has assured bus owners he will appoint a committee to look into their issues.
The announcement comes in the wake of an islandwide protest by private bus owners yesterday.
The private bus owners called off their strike this morning after the President yesterday agreed to meet with them.
The private bus owners are protesting the proposed government decision to impose a minimum fine of Rs.25, 000 on 7 traffic offences.

## Title: Hashan Tillakaratne, Upul Chandana to coach national team
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, December 3, 2016 - 14:45
Hashan Tillakaratne, Upul Chandana to coach national team

Former cricket captain Hashan Tillakaratne and player Upul Chandana were appointed as coaches to the national team.
Hashan Tillakaratne will be batting coach and Upul Tharanga fielding coach.

## Title: Dengue fine increased to Rs. 25, 000
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Sunday, December 4, 2016 - 21:00
Dengue fine increased to Rs. 25, 000

The fine imposed on failing to keep personal or business premises clean and thereby encouraging mosquito breeding has been increased to Rs. 25, 000 Health Minister Rajitha Senaratne said.
Over 46, 000 cases of dengue were reported this year
