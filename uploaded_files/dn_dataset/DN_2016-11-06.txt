## Title: Michael Buble suspends career after son diagnosed with cancer
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Sunday, November 6, 2016 - 13:15
Michael Buble suspends career after son diagnosed with cancer

Michael Buble's three-year-old son has been diagnosed with cancer.
The Canadian singer said in a social media statement that he and his partner Lusiana are devastated about the recent cancer diagnosis of our oldest son Noah.
Buble said they would both put their careers on hold to devote all our time and attention to helping Noah get well.
The singer has said he expected the battle to be tough: We have a long journey in front of us and hope that with the support of family, friends and fans around the world, we will win this battle, God willing, he said.

## Title: Tarantino will retire after two more movies
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Sunday, November 6, 2016 - 17:15
Tarantino will retire after two more movies

Quentin Tarantinohas confirmed plans to retire after directing two more films.
"Drop the mic. Boom. Tell everybody,'" the filmmaker said on Thursday while being interviewed at the Adobe Max conference in San Diego.
While Tarantino hasn't officially announced his next film following 2015'sThe Hateful Eight, the 53-year-old mentioned during the event that he is working on a historical, 1970's-based nonfiction project that "could be a book, a documentary, a five-part podcast."
Tarantino is a cult filmaker, known for his aestheticization of violenceand gore.
His films include Reservoir Dogs (1992), Pulp Fiction, (1994), Kill Bill Volume 1 and 2 (2003, 2004) and Inglourious Basterds (2009).
