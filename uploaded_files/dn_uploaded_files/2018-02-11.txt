## Title: EC Chairman explains reasons for delay in results
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Sunday, February 11, 2018 - 03:35
EC Chairman explains reasons for delay in results

Chairman of the Elections Commission Mahinda Deshapriya said that a delay had occurred in issuing official results of the Local Government elections due to several reasons.
Issuing a statement Deshapriya said some hand delivered results of polling stations had to be recounted to assure the accuracy of the process.
He said another delay occurred when adding the results of postal votes to the final results of some local government bodies.
