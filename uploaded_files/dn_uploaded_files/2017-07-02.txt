## Title: Sixteen KDU patients in H'tota
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Sunday, July 2, 2017 - 01:00
Sixteen KDU patients in H'tota
Sixteen patients with Chronic Kidney Disease of unidentified etiology (KDU) have been identified in Hambantota, District Health Services Office Director Dr. Miyasi Gajanayaka said. She said 300 kidney patients with diabetes were also identified.
