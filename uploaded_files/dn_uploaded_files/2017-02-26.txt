## Title: A Pakistani and a Sri Lankan nabbed with 1.5 kg heroin
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, February 25, 2017 - 12:00
A Pakistani and a Sri Lankan nabbed with 1.5 kg heroin

A Pakistani and a Sri Lankan were arrested by Police Narcotics Bureau short while ago for possession of 1.5 kg of heroin.
The arrests were made in Bambalapitiya and Wellawatta.
