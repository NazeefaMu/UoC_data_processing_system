## Title: Australia beat Sri Lanka by 2 wickets in 3rd ODI
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 28 August 2016 10:49 pm

Australia beat Sri Lanka by 2 wickets in 3rd ODI


	

Australia beat Sri Lanka to take a 2-1 lead in the five match One Day International series, at the Rangiri Dambulla Stadium today.


Batting first Sri Lanka were bowled out for 226 in 49.1 overs, and Australia reached the target in 46 overs, for the loss if eight.


George Bailey top scored for Australia with 70 which Matthew Wade scored 42.
