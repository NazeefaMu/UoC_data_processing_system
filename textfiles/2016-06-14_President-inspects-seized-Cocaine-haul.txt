## Title: President inspects seized Cocaine haul
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 14 June 2016 05:35 pm

President inspects seized Cocaine haul


	



President Maithripala Sirisena today visited the customs Orugodawatte Rank Container Terminal (RCT) and inspected the Cocaine haul seized by Custom officials. Finance Minister Ravi Karunanayake also participated in the visit. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
