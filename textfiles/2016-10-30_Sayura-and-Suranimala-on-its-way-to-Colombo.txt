## Title: Sayura and Suranimala on its way to Colombo
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 30 October 2016 10:46 am

Sayura and Suranimala on its way to Colombo


	



The two ships of the Sri Lanka Navy; SLNS Sayura and SLNS Suranimala which were on an a training and goodwill visit to India, arrived at the Port of Colombo yesterday (29) after a successful visit. On their way home, the two ships also conducted a few training exercises, such as the Man Overboard Exercise, Station Keeping by Distance Line Exercise and Towing Exercise.(Pix by Navy Media)
