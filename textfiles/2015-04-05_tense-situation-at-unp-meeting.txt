## Title: Tense situation at UNP meeting
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 April 2015 11:58 am

Tense situation at UNP meeting


	

A tense situation prevailed at an UNP meeting in Ampara, chaired by Prime Ministers Ranil Wickremesinghe, when some of the UNP supporters protested while MP P. Dayaratne was speaking at the event.
Some supporters, led by local government UNP members claimed that Mr. Dayaratne joined former President Mahinda Rajapaksa’s government some seven years ago betraying the UNP.
