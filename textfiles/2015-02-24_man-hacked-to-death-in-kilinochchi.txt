## Title: Man hacked to death in Kilinochchi
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 February 2015 05:33 am

Man hacked to death in Kilinochchi


	

A 36-year-old man was hacked to death by five persons near Punnaniravi in Kilinochchi last night, the Police said.
Police identified the victim as a resident of Vishwamadu and he was succumbed to his injuries while being rushed to the Kilinochchi Hospital.
Police said the reason for the assault was not ascertained yet. Police are conducting investigations to arrest the suspects who had fled the area.
