## Title: UNP nominates Fonseka to Parliament
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 8 February 2016 06:19 pm

UNP nominates Fonseka to Parliament


	

The UNP Working Committee has today endorsed the appointment of Field Marshal Sarath Fonseka to Parliament through the United National Front (UNF) National List to fill the post of MP which fell vacant with the death of former minister M.K.A.D.S. Gunawardane, UNP Assistant General Secretary and Minister Akila Viraj Kariyawasam said. (Yohan Perera)
