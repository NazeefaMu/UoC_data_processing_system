## Title: ‘Signal tower’ protest by SLT manpower workers
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 October 2016 01:01 pm

‘Signal tower’ protest by SLT manpower workers


	

Two manpower workers of Sri Lanka Telecom (SLT) launched a protest on the top of a signal tower at the SLT Head Office, Fort this morning, demanding the authorities to add them to the permanent cadre, Police said.
