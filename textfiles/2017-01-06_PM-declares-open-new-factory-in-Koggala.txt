## Title: PM declares open new factory in Koggala
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 6 January 2017 09:35 pm

PM declares open new factory in Koggala


	




Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe today declared open the new factory belonging to ATG Hand Care Ltd in Koggala. ATG Hand Care is a joint venture of ATG Ltd and British investor John Ward Ceylon. The new factory will create 8,000 job opportunities here. Cabinet ministers representing both allies in the government and ATG Hand Care Ltd Managing Director Fasal Ahabdeen were present at the opening.
