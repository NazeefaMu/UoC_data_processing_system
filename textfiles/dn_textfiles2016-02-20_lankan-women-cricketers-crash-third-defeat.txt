## Title: Lankan Women cricketers crash to third defeat
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, February 20, 2016 - 01:00

Lankan Women cricketers crash to third defeat

Sri Lanka Women cricketers suffered their third straight defeat at the hands of India Women when they were beaten by seven wickets in the third ODI of the ICC Women's Championship played at Ranchi yesterday. Scores: Sri Lanka Women 112 in 38.2 overs (Dilani Manodara 23, Shikha Pandey 2/24, Deepti Sharma 6/20, Preeti Bose 2/8) Indian Women 114/3 in 29.3 overs (Veda Krishnamurthy 61 n.o., Deepti Sharma 28)
