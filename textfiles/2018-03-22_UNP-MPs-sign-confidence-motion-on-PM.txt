## Title: UNP MPs sign confidence motion on PM
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 March 2018 03:05 pm

UNP MPs sign confidence motion on PM


	

All UNP MPs have signed a motion expressing confidence in Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe, party sources said today.

They said they had signed the document when they met at the parliamentary complex this morning under the chairmanship of the Prime Minister.

Sources said the UNP MPs had vowed to defeat the motion. (Yohan Perera and Ajith Siriwadana)
