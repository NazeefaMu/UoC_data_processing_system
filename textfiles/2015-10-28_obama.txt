## Title: Video: Obama on fire
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 28 October 2015 11:30 am

Video: Obama on fire


	


An effigy of US President Barrack Obama was set ablaze by the Frontline Socialist Party (FSP) protestors outside the US Embassy, in Colombo today. The protesters accused the US of genocide in Syria. Pix by Nisal Baduge.Video by Indika Sri Aravinda
