## Title: 1st Senior Scout Summit in Colombo
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 21 May 2017 11:50 pm

1st Senior Scout Summit in Colombo


	



Inaugural Senior Scout Summit organised by the Sri Lanka Scout Association was held today at the Hilton Colombo. The summit was attended over 400 delegates representing all the districts, senior scouts and Rover scouts. World renowned trainer Mrs. Rita Goh from Institute of Training and Development of Malaysia was seen receiving a token from Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Dr. Harsha De Silva. Sri Lanka Scout Association Chief Commissioner Janaprith Fernando and President of Sri Lanka Scout Association Srinath Goonarathne at the picture.
