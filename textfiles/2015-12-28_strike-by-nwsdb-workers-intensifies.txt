## Title: Strike by NWSDB workers intensifies
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 28 December 2015 06:07 am

Strike by NWSDB workers intensifies


	

The National Water Supply and Drainage Board (NWSDB) Joint Trade Union Alliance has intensified its countrywide trade union action with even those providing emergency and breakdown services joining the strike.
The strike action was launched last Saturday over the non-payment of the workers’ annual bonus. (Indika Manoj)
