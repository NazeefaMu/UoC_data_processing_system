## Title: Two hacked to death in Poddala
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 20 January 2018 12:51 pm

Two hacked to death in Poddala


	

A 75-year-old man and his 65-year-old sister were found hacked to death inside a house at Wakwella, Poddala in Galle today.

Police said they have yet to determine the cause of the double homicide. No suspect had been arrested so far.
