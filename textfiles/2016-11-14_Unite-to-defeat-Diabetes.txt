## Title: Unite to defeat Diabetes
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 14 November 2016 03:51 pm

Unite to defeat Diabetes


	



The Centre for Diabetes Endocrinology and Cardio Metabolism was today launched in Colombo with the patronage of President Maithripala Sirisena commemorating the World's Diabetes Day 2016. A donation of Rs. 500,000/- was handed over to the research fund of the College of Endocrinologists, Sri Lanka by Dr. Uditha Bulagahapitiya to Dr. Gaya Katulanda.
