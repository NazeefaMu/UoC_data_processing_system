## Title: Convicted!
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 8 September 2016 05:44 pm

Convicted!


	



Former MP Duminda Silva and four others convicted on charges of murdering former presidential advisor Bharatha Lakshman Premachandra is seen leaving the High Court premises after he was sentenced to death. Mr. Premachandra and three bodyguards were gunned down on October 8, 2011 at Angoda on the day of the Local Government elections. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
