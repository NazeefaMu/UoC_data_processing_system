## Title: Man stabbed at musical show
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 November 2015 04:03 am

Man stabbed at musical show


	

An individual was stabbed to death at a musical show in Seethawaka, Awissawella early this morning, police said.
The victim has been identified as 33-year-old Sisira Kumara, a resident of Awissawella.
He Police said the victim was stabbed at 12.45 a.m at the musical show which was held at the Seethawaka President's College Grounds and succumbed to his injuries after being admitted to the Awissawella Hospital.
Police are investigating the incident.
