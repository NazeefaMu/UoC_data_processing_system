## Title: Mobile Service in Biyawila
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 26 February 2017 09:32 pm

Mobile Service in Biyawila


	



State Minister of Defence Ruwan Wijewardene held a mobile service of his ministry in Ihala Biyawila yesterday. Three rural roads were opened by the State Minister concurrently. The picture shows the State Minister helps an elderly person to wear a pair of spectacles. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
