## Title: Daisy Forest released on personal bond
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 August 2016 12:09 pm

Daisy Forest released on personal bond


	

Yoshitha Rajapaksa’s grand aunt Daisy Forest was today released by Colombo Additional Magistrate Augusta Atapattu on a personal bond in connection with the inquiry into the purchasing of a property in Mount Lavinia.

She was present in court on noticed to do so, on a request made by the FCID.

Jayantha Weerasinghe, who appeared for Ms. Forest informed court that his client would report to the FCID on August 31 to record her statement.(Shehan Chamika Silva)
