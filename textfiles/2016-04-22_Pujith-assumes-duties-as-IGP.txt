## Title: Pujith assumes duties as IGP
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 22 April 2016 05:31 pm

Pujith assumes duties as IGP


	



The newly-appointed Inspector General of Police (IGP) Pujith Jayasundara assumed duties today in a ceremony at the Police Head Quarters. Religious rituals took place seeking blessings for the new IGP under the patronage of Hunupitiya Gangarama Temple's Chief Incumbent the Ven. Galboda Gnanissara Thera.
