## Title: Prices of 47 essential drugs reduced
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 September 2016 06:00 pm

Prices of 47 essential drugs reduced


	

Prices of 47 essential drugs, including those prescribed for diabetes and heart ailments were reduced with the immediate effect under the first phase of the National Medicinal Drugs Policy, Health, Nutrition and Indigenous Medicine Minister Rajitha Senaratne announced a short while ago.
