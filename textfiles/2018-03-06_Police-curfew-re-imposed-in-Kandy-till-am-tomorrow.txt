## Title: Police curfew re-imposed in Kandy till 6 am tomorrow
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 March 2018 07:27 pm

Police curfew re-imposed in Kandy till 6 am tomorrow


	

The Police curfew which was lifted at 6 pm today in Kandy District would be reimposed from 8 pm until 6 am tomorrow, Police said.  

The curfew was imposed following the tense situation in the Teldeniya and Pallekela police divisions.
