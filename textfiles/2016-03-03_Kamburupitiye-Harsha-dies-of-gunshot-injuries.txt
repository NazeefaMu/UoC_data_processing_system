## Title: Kamburupitiye Harsha dies of gunshot injuries
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 March 2016 03:43 pm

Kamburupitiye Harsha dies of gunshot injuries


	

Kamburupitiye Harsha, the underworld gangster who was critically injured when shot at by two gunmen who arrived on a motorcycle, succumbed to his injuries at the National Hospital a short while ago, Hospital sources said.

The victim was inside his home at Obeysekarapura in Welikada when the shooting took place, Police said and added that the motorcyclists were wearing face-covered helmets.
