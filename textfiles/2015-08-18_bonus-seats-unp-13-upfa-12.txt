## Title: Bonus seats: UNP 13, UPFA 12
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 August 2015 02:11 pm

Bonus seats: UNP 13, UPFA 12


	

The Elections Department allocated the bonus seats as follows: UNP-13 seats; UPFA-12; JVP-2 and ITAK-2.
With the bonus seats, the United National Party (UNP) has 106 seats in parliament while the United People’s Freedom Alliance (UPFA) has 95 seats, ITAK 16 and JVP 6.
