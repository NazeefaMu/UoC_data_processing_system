## Title: SL to end violence against children by 2030
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 2 June 2017 05:13 pm

SL to end violence against children by 2030


	



The National Partnership to End Violence Against Children (NPEVAC) in Sri Lanka, was launched on Friday as part of a new collaborative partnership between the government, UN agencies and other international organizations.Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
