## Title: Court orders to exhume Lasantha's body
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 8 September 2016 11:14 am

Court orders to exhume Lasantha's body


	

Mt. Lavinia Magistrate today ordered to exhume the body of former Sunday Leader Editor Lasantha Wickrematunge who was assassinated in January 2009.

The Magistrate ordered to exhume the body on September 27 and to conduct a second post-mortem examination.
