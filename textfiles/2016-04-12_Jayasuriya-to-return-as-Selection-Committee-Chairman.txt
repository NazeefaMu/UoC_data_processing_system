## Title: Jayasuriya to return as Selection Committee Chairman
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 12 April 2016 12:42 pm

Jayasuriya to return as Selection Committee Chairman


	

Former SLC Selection Committee Chairman Sanath Jayasuriya would return to the post next month, SLC President Thilanga Sumathipala said today.
