## Title: CID arrests Sajin de Vass Gunawardena
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 June 2016 01:48 pm

CID arrests Sajin de Vass Gunawardena


	

Former UPFA MP Sajin de Vass Gunawardena was arrested by the CID today on charges of forcibly obtaining Rs. 30 million from a businessman.
