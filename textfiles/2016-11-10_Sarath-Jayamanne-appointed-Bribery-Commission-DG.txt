## Title: Sarath Jayamanne appointed Bribery Commission DG
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 10 November 2016 09:33 pm

Sarath Jayamanne appointed Bribery Commission DG


	

President’s Counsel Sarath Jayamanne has been appointed as Director General of the Commission to Investigate allegations of Bribery or Corruption by President Maithripala Sirisena today.

Mr. Jayamanne received his appointment letter from the president at the President Secretariat office.
