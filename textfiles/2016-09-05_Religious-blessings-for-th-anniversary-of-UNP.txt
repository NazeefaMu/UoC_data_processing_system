## Title: Religious blessings for 70th anniversary of UNP
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 5 September 2016 11:37 am

Religious blessings for 70th anniversary of UNP


	

Religious observances organised by the United National Party (UNP) to invoke blessings for the 70th anniversary of the party was held at Sirikotha yesterday. An all-night Pirith Chanting Ceremony was conducted last night. Party Leader Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe, flanked by other party stalwarts, was seen at the event.
