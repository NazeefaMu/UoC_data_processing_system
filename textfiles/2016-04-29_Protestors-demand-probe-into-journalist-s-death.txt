## Title: Protestors demand probe into journalist’s death
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 29 April 2016 07:17 pm

Protestors demand probe into journalist’s death


	

Several media groups held a protest outside the Fort Railway Station today demanding a proper investigation into the killing of journalist Tharamalingam Sivaram and action against those who are responsible for his death. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
