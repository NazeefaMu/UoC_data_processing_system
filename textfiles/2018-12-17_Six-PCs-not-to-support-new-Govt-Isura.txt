## Title: Six PCs not to support new Govt.: Isura
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 December 2018 10:48 am

Six PCs not to support new Govt.: Isura


	

Chief Ministers of six provincial Councils had decided not to support the new government headed by Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe, Western Province Chief Minister Isura Devapriya said yesterday.

He told a news conference that the decision was taken at a meeting held in his residence in Colombo attended by Chief Ministers of Western, North Central, Central, Southern, North Western and Uva Provincial Councils. (Indika Sri Aravinda)
