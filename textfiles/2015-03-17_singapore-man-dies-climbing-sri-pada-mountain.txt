## Title: Singapore man dies climbing Sri Pada mountain
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 March 2015 04:10 am

Singapore man dies climbing Sri Pada mountain


	

A Singaporean man aged 55 died while climbing Sri Pada mountain with a group of Singaporean nationals early this morning near Indikatupaana area, the Police said.
They said the Singapore Embassy had been informed about the incident.  Police suspects the death caused by a heart attack.
