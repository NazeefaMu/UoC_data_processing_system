## Title: WOMEN'S COOP anniversary celebrations
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 28 June 2016 11:29 pm

WOMEN'S COOP anniversary celebrations


	



Cultural dancing and musical events were held at the Hewavitharana Maha Vidyalaya, Rajagiriya to celebrate the anniversary of the Bandaranayake Pura branch of the Sri Lanka Women's Development Services Cooperative Society Ltd. (WOMEN'S COOP), last Sunday. Pix by Kithsiri de Mel
