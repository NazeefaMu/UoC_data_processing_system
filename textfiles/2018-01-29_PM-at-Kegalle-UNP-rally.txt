## Title: PM at Kegalle UNP rally
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 29 January 2018 02:09 pm

PM at Kegalle UNP rally


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe participated in the UNP election rally held in Kegalle on Sunday. He said the present government will go on till 2020. UNP General Secretary Minister Kabir Hashim, Minister of Health Dr. Rajitha Senaratne and a large number of party supporters  were a present at the rally. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
