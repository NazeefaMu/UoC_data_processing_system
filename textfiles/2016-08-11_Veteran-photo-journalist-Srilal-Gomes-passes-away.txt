## Title: Veteran photo journalist Srilal Gomes passes away
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 August 2016 10:55 am

Veteran photo journalist Srilal Gomes passes away


	

Veteran photo journalist Srilal Gomes passed away at the age of 61 this morning.

Funeral will be held on Saturday at the Roman Catholic Church Cemetery, Sapugaskanda.
