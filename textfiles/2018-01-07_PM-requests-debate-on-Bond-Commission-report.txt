## Title: PM requests debate on Bond Commission report
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 7 January 2018 12:48 pm

PM requests debate on Bond Commission report


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe said today that he requested Speaker Karu Jayasuriya to convene Parliament to hold debate on Bond Commission report.

The Prime Minister made this remark at a special UNP convention held at Campbell Park Borella today.(Yohan Perera)
