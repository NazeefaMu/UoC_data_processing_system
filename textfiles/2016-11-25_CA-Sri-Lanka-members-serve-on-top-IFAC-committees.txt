## Title: CA Sri Lanka members serve on top IFAC committees
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 25 November 2016 12:00 am

CA Sri Lanka members serve on top IFAC committees


	



 

 

Six members of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Sri Lanka (CA Sri Lanka), including the institute’s incumbent President Lasantha Wickremasinghe and several past presidents, have been appointed by the International Federation of Accountants (IFAC) to serve in some of its top committees. 
Wickremasinghe serves as Technical Advisor to IFAC Small and Medium Practices (SMP) Committee Chair Monica Foerster, while the institute’s Immediate Past President Arjuna Herath will serve as Chair of the Professional Accountancy Organisation (PAO) Development Committee. 
CA Sri Lanka Past President Reyaz Mihular has once again been nominated as a board member to the International Ethics Standards Board for Accountants (IESBA) for the third consecutive term from January 1, 2017 to December 31, 2019, while CA Sri Lanka Council Member Manil Jayesinghe will serve as a board member for the International Accounting Education Standards Board (IAESB) for the term commencing from January 1, 2016 to December 31, 2018. 
CA Sri Lanka Past Presidents including Sujeewa Mudalige served as a member of the Nominating Committee of the International Federation of Accountants for the year 2016, while Nishan Fernando serves as a member of the International Accounting Education Standards Board’s (IAESB) Consultative Advisory Group (CAG) representing the South Asian Federation of Accountants (SAFA).
