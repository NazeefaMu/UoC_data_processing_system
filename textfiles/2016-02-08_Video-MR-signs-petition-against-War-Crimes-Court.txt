## Title: Video: MR signs petition against War Crimes Court
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 8 February 2016 01:12 pm

Video: MR signs petition against War Crimes Court


	



Former President Mahinda Rajapaksa is seen signing a petition prepared by the National Movement to Protect War Heroes against what it describes as an International War Crime Court to ‘hunt down’ soldiers. The event was held at the Sri Sambudhaloka Viharaya in Colombo 1. Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
