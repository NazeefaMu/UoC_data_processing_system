## Title: Ordination ceremony
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 27 March 2016 10:17 am

Ordination ceremony


	



Novice Buddhist monks attended a mass ordination ceremony at Sri Niketha Piriwen Maha Viharaya in Horampella, Minuwangoda yesterday where  eleven children aged between 8-12 years old were ordained as monks. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
