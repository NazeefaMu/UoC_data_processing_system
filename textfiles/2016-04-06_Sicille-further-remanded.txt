## Title: Sicille further remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 April 2016 11:32 am

Sicille further remanded


	

Former Ceylinco Chairman Lalith Kotelawala's wife Sicille was further remanded till April 20 by Colombo Chief Magistrate Gihan Pilapitiya today over the F&G case.

Chief Magistrate noticed the Colombo Judicial Medical officer (JMO) over the delay in submitting a report on the health condition of the suspect.

Crime Investigation Division (CID) submitting another report to the court said they needed more time to record a statement from the suspect.

Mrs. Kotelawala was the eighth suspect in the case filed against her husband Lalith and eight others on charges of misappropriating Rs. 4.3 billion at the F&G Property Developers. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
