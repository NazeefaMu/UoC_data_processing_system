## Title: PM visits affected areas in Kandy
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 11 March 2018 10:10 am

PM visits affected areas in Kandy


	



Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe and a group of Ministers including Ranjith Madduma Bandara, Lakshman Kiriella, Ruwan Wijewardene and Rauf Hakeem visited Kandy yesterday to inspect the areas which were affected by the recent violent incidents.Pics by Pradeep Pathirana
