## Title: Namal arrives at FCID
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 July 2016 11:10 am

Namal arrives at FCID


	

UPFA MP Namal Rajapaksa arrived at the Financial Crime Investigation Division (FCID) today as he was summoned to record a statement over an incident of financial misappropriation amounting to Rs. 70 million.
