## Title: Navam Maha Perahara commences
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 21 February 2016 10:21 pm

Navam Maha Perahara commences


	



The annual Navam Maha Perahara of the Gangarama Temple, Colombo began this evening. President Maithripala Sirisena is seen placing the Karanduwa on the Elephant prior to the commencement of the Perahara.
