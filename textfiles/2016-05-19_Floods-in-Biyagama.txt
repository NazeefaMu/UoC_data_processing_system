## Title: Floods in Biyagama
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 19 May 2016 08:46 pm

Floods in Biyagama


	



State Minister of Defence Ruwan Wijewardene yesterday visited the Biyagama electorate in a boat and inspected the damage caused by the heavy floods. The Minister visited the camps where affected people have been relocated and distributed dry foods and other essential goods among them.
