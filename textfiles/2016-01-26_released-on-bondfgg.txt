## Title: Hirunika’s Defender released on bond
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 January 2016 07:27 am

Hirunika’s Defender released on bond


	

UNP MP Hirunika Premachandra’s Defender vehicle, which was used by several staff members of the MP to allegedly abduct a man from Dematagoda was released on Rs. 4.5 million bond by Additional Magistrate Mohamed Mihar today.
The Police informed the Magistrate that the investigations relating to the vehicle had been concluded and that they had sent the extracts of the investigations to the Attorney General’s Department for further consultation.
The case was fixed for April 18. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
