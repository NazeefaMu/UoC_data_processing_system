## Title: MR visits Moragahakanda
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 24 October 2016 10:21 am

MR visits Moragahakanda


	



Former President Mahinda Rajapaksa yesterday visited the construction site of the Moragahakanda Dam. He had visited the site on his way back after taking part in a religious ceremony held in Somawathiya in Polonnaruwa.
