## Title: Sri Lanka square T20 series with three-wicket win
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 January 2017 09:37 pm

Sri Lanka square T20 series with three-wicket win


	

Sri Lanka beat South Africa by three wickets in the T20 played in Johannesburg today. Chasing 114 to win Sri Lanka reached the target with two balls to spare. Angelo Mathews top scored with an unbeaten 54.
