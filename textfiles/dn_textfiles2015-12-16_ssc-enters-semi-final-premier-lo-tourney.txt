## Title: SSC enters semi-final of Premier L/O tourney
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, December 16, 2015 - 01:00

SSC enters semi-final of Premier L/O tourney

SSC entered the semi-finals of the AIA Premier inter-club limited-over cricket tournament yesterday with their win over Badureliya SC yesterday.
Colts and NCC also won their matches against Ports Authority and Tamil Union respectively.
Dhanushka Gunathilake skipper of SSC scored 106 off 74 balls with 10 fours and six sixes to lead SSC to their win.
Three matches were abandoned without a ball bowled due to wet ground conditions at Maitland Crescent, Braybrooke Place and Panagoda.
The last six matches of the first round will be played today. The semi-finals are scheduled for December 23 and 24 and final on December 30.
+At Maitland Place: SSC beat Badureliya SC by seven wickets.
Badureliya SC: 144 in 40 overs (Lassana Perera 47 n.o., Hasantha Fernando 31, Tharindu Ratnayake 3/25, Lakshan Jayasinghe 2/13, Dilhara Fernando 2/38)
SSC: 145/3 in 17.2 overs (Dhanushka Gunathilake 106, Chamara Kapugedara 29 n.o.)
+At Maitland Place: NCC beat Tamil Union by 39 runs (D/L rule)
NCC: 244/7 in 34 overs (Sandun Weerakkody 81, Upul Tharanga 72, Jehan Mubarak 26, Nisala Tharaka 3/48, Chamika Karunarathne 2/29, Ramith Rambukwella 2/44)
