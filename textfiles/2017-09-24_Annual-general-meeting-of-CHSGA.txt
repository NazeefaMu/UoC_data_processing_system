## Title: Annual general meeting of CHSGA
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 24 September 2017 11:05 am

Annual general meeting of CHSGA


	



Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe participated as the chief guest of the annual general meeting of the Ceylon Hotel School Graduates Association (CHSGA)that was held in Tangalle yesterday. Minister of Tourism and Christian religious affairs John Amaratunga and Sri Lanka Tourism Authority Director General Paddy Withana and Tourist Board Chairman Kavan Ratnayake also participated.
