## Title: Avishka played a key role in Cambrian Juniors success
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, February 16, 2016 - 01:00

Avishka played a key role in Cambrian Juniors success

 
Prince of Wales College emerged all island under 13 division one champions conducted by Sri Lanka Schools Cricket Association for the Singer Trophy.
Cambrians as a team with sheer hard work dedication and devotion brought honour and glory to their alma-mater.
One of their leading batsman Avishka Fernando was the only player to score a century notching up a magnificent 114 runs and it was against Dharmapala Vidyalaya, Kottawa and he scored 69 versus St Joseph's College against Kalutara Vidyalaya he made 67 totalling 399 runs in their teen matches and in the final match against Mahinda College his contribution was 29 runs.
In a friendly cricket match against a visiting under 13 team from India he scored a hurricane 124 runs and won the Best Batsman award.
