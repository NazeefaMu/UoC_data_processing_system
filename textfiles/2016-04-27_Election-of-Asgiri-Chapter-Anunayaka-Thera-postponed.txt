## Title: Election of Asgiri Chapter Anunayaka Thera postponed
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 April 2016 07:39 pm

Election of Asgiri Chapter Anunayaka Thera postponed


	

The election of the new Anunayake Thera of the Asgiri Chapter of the Siam Nikaya was postponed indefinitely this evening.

The Anunayake Thera of the Asgiri Chapter and Chief Incumbent of the Mahiyanganaya Rajamaha Viharaya were to be elected this evening.(Nadeeka Daya Bandara)
