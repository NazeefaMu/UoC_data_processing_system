## Title: Unauthorized houses demolished
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 17 February 2016 04:26 pm

Unauthorized houses demolished


	



The residents including children of Henmulla Nawa Niwasa Watte and the Kajima Watte on Ferguson Road are seen outside their demolished houses.


The unauthorized houses in the area were yesterday demolished by the Urban Development Authority (UDA) after it had issued several quit notices. The demolishing was carried amidst objections being raised by the residents. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
