## Title: Joint press at DMC
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 28 May 2017 04:10 pm

Joint press at DMC


	

A news briefing was held at the Disaster Management Centre (DMC) on Sunday to educate media about the adverse weather condition prevailing in the country and the casualties of the calamity.
