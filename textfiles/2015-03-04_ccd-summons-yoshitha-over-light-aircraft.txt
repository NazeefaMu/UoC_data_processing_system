## Title: <U+200B>CCD summons Yoshitha over light aircraft
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 March 2015 03:52 am

<U+200B>CCD summons Yoshitha over light aircraft


	


	The Colombo Crime Division (CCD) has summoned former President Mahinda Rajapaksa’s son Lt. Yoshitha Rajapaksa to record a statement in conncetion with the light aircraft, that was found from the Economic Centre at Narahenpita on January 13.
	Yoshitha had been informed to come to the CCD through the Navy Commander. 
	The veteran film producer Chandran Ratnam had given a statement to the police saying this aircraft had belonged to him and been given to Yoshitha at the latter’s request.
