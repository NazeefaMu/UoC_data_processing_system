## Title: JO Women’s Front stage a protest
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 20 September 2018 08:48 pm

JO Women’s Front stage a protest


	


Joint Opposition Women’s Front staged a protest outside the BMICH premises today urging the Government to take actions over the incident where milk packets containing poisonous substance were distributed among the protesters of the ‘Janabalaya Kolombata’ held recently.
