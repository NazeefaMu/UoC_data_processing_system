## Title: Standing tall !
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 22 March 2015 01:47 pm

Standing tall !


	


General Sarath Fonseka, a former Chief of Defence Staff and Army Commander was promoted to the rank of Field Marshal by President Maithripala Sirisena today evening at a special function held at the Defence Ministry grounds. This is the first time ever that an Army Officer had been promoted to this rank. Pic by Kushan Pathiaraja
