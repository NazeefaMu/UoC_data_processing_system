## Title: Hotel owner found strangled to death
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 31 October 2016 09:18 am

Hotel owner found strangled to death


	

A 54-year-old hotel owner was found strangled to death at his hotel in Urapola, Nittambuwa early this morning.

Police said the victim, a resident of Urapola, was found with pesticide sprinkled on him. (Tony Karunanayaka)
