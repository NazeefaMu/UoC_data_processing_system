## Title: CC recommends SC judge Priyasath Dep for CJ post
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 28 February 2017 08:20 am

CC recommends SC judge Priyasath Dep for CJ post


	

The Constitutional Council which met last evening has recommended Supreme Court judge Priyasath Dep for the Chief Justice post, the government information department said today.
