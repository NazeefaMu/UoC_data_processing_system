## Title: March 13 declared Day of Mourning
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 10 March 2016 12:43 pm

March 13 declared Day of Mourning


	

March 13 has been declared a Day of Mourning in view of the passing away of the Asgiriya Chapter Mahanayake most Ven. Galagama Attadassi Thera, Home Affairs Ministry said.

The last rights will be conducted at the Kandy Police Ground in Asgiriya at 3.00 p.m. on Sunday, March13.
