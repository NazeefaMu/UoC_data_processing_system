## Title: Maldivian nabbed at BIA while smuggling Rs. 6.7 Mn
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 September 2016 10:58 am

Maldivian nabbed at BIA while smuggling Rs. 6.7 Mn


	

A 53 year old Maldivian was arrested by the Airport Police at Bandaranaike International Airport when he attempted to smuggle out USD 45,000 (Rs. 6.7 Mn) to Maldives last evening.

The suspect had hidden the currency in his travelling bag. (T.K.G.Kapila)
