## Title: She has found silence is golden
## Section: Political Gossip
## Date: 6 September 2016 12:00 am

She has found silence is golden


	

A certain dainty fair Diyawanna member who made sensational news in the recent past has suddenly gone mute. 




Some say that the fair one has gone into a shell following certain events that left her highly disillusioned.


However, even the Diyawanna colleagues quite close to her do not know for sure the real cause for her continued silence, they say.
