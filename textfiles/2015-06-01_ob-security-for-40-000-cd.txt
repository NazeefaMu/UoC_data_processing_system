## Title: Job security for 40,000 CDF cadres
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 1 June 2015 07:16 am

Job security for 40,000 CDF cadres


	


		Some 40,000 members of the Civil Defence Force (CDF) were yesterday given appointment letters confirming their employment as permanent and pensionable. The letters were handed over at a ceremony held at the Medirigiriya National School under the patronage of President Maithripala Sirisena. Pix Courtesy - Presidential Media
