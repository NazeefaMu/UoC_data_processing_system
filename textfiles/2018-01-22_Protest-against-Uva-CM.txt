## Title: Protest against Uva CM
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 22 January 2018 12:49 pm

Protest against Uva CM


	



Parents of the Tamil Girls’ Maha Vidyalaya in Badulla today staged a protest outside the school urging the authorities to arrest Uva Chief Minister Chamara Sampath Dassanayaka, Provincial Director of Education R.M.P. Rathnayake and Provincial Secretary of Education G.A.M.S.P. Ambanwela in connection with the incident where the Chief Minister forced a lady Principal to kneel in front of him.
