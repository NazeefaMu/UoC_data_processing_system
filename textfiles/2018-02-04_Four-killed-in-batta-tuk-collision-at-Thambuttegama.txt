## Title: Four killed in batta-tuk collision at Thambuttegama
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 February 2018 01:45 pm

Four killed in batta-tuk collision at Thambuttegama


	

Three adults and a 10-year-old-child had been killed at 8 a. m. this morning when a light truck and a three-wheeler collided head on at Thispanepura, Thambuttegama, the Police said. 

All victims were residents of Hurigaswewa, Galnewa.

In addition, four passengers in the lorry were injured and admitted to the Thambuttegama Hospital, according to the Police. (Dayarathna Abogama)
