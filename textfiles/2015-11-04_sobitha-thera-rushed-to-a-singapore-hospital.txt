## Title: Sobitha Thera rushed to a Singapore hospital
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 November 2015 04:20 am

Sobitha Thera rushed to a Singapore hospital


	

The chief incumbent of Kotte Nagavihara and the leader of People's Movement for a Just society Ven. Maduluwawe Sobitha Thera was rushed to a Singapore hospital last night due to a sudden illness, sources said.
They said the Thera was rushed to a Singapore hospital by a special flight at midnight yesterday. (Deepa Adikari)
