## Title: PRECIFAC summons Shiranthi
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 January 2016 02:37 am

PRECIFAC summons Shiranthi


	

Former first lady Shiranthi Rajapaksa is to appear before PRECIFAC on January 25, sources said.
They said the former first lady had been summoned for allegedly providing a house belonging to the National Housing Development Authority at less than the stipulated price to her media coordinating officer, under the previous regime. (Manopriya Gunasekera)
