## Title: Three sentenced to death in N’Eliya
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 September 2016 12:04 pm

Three sentenced to death in N’Eliya


	

Three people including a father and son were today sentenced to death by Nuwara-Eliya High Court judge Lalith Ekanayake on charges of having murdered a man on August 06, 2003 in Dayagama.

A 39-year-old father of two had been hacked to death by the accused (Ranjith Rajapaksa)
