## Title: Concrete panel collapses injuring constable
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 29 October 2016 03:30 pm

Concrete panel collapses injuring constable


	

A concrete panel of an old building which houses the Panadura Police had collapsed today injuring a constable who was on duty.

Police said the injured constable was rushed to the hospital. (Bimal Shyaman Jayasinghe)
