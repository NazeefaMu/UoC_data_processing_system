## Title: Several activists leave Transparency International
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 January 2017 05:20 am

Several activists leave Transparency International


	

Several activists have left Transparency International of Sri Lanka following controversies over financial transactions between the organisation and other entities, sources said.

Among the activists who left or were sacked by the anti-corruption watchdog are a former senior manager, it is learnt.
