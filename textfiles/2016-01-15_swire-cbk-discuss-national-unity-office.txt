## Title: Swire, CBK discuss national unity office
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 January 2016 06:47 am

Swire, CBK discuss national unity office


	


		 		 
		 
			
Former President Chandrika Bandaranaike Kumaratunga and Visiting State Minister for Foreign and Commonwealth Office Hugo Swire discussed the setting up of the new office for National Unity and Reconciliation.
In a tweet, the minister said “Discussed transitional justice & new Office for National Unity & Reconciliation w/former #SriLanka pres Kumaratunga”
