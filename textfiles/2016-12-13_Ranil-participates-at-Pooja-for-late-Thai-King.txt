## Title: Ranil participates at Pooja for late Thai King
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 13 December 2016 10:24 pm

Ranil participates at Pooja for late Thai King


	



Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe participated in a Mathakavasthra Pooja for the late King Bhumibol Adulyadej of Thailand which was held at the Deepaduthtramaya Thai temple in Kotahena yesterday. Chief Prelate of Amarapura Chapter Dauldena Gnanissara Thera conducted the Pooja with the participation of the Ven. Kotugoda Dhammawasa Anunayaka Thera and Chief Incumbent of the Deepaduthtramaya Thai temple Ven Waskaduwe Mahindawansa. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
