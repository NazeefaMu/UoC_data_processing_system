## Title: Four old-boys arrested over assault on student
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 November 2016 06:13 pm

Four old-boys arrested over assault on student


	

Four suspected old boys of Dharmaraja College in Kandy, have been arrested by the police today in connection with the recent assault on a student of the same school at the school gate.

The suspects who are aged between 19 and 21 were to be produced before the Kandy Magistrate, police said.

Police and Education officials earlier launched separate investigations on the incident in which a student of a leading boys’ college in Kandy was beaten up.
