## Title: Dilshan scores 42 in his final ODI
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 28 August 2016 06:46 pm

Dilshan scores 42 in his final ODI


	

TM Dilshan today ended his ODI career after he was dismissed for 42 in the 3rd ODI against Australia at the Rangiri Dambulla International Stadium,in a total score of 226.

Sri Lanka won the toss and opted to bat first and were bowled out for 226 runs.
