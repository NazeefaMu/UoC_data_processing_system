## Title: Lanka Patuna bridge declared open
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 21 May 2017 12:11 pm

Lanka Patuna bridge declared open


	



President Maithripala Sirisena declared open the new bridge built across the Lanka Patuna lagoon yesterday.The bridge which is 150 meters long, built under the supervision of Road Development Authority will facilitate the devotees who visit the Lanka Patuna temple.
