## Title: President to visit Sanchi
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 8 May 2016 06:07 pm

President to visit Sanchi


	

President Maithripala Sirisena would visit Bhopal on May 14 to visit Sanchi, the world-famous Buddhist heritage site in Raisen district of Madhya Pradesh, Indian media reported.

“Madhya Pradesh Chief Secretary Antony DeSa and Director General of Police Surendra Singh visited Sanchi and reviewed preparations for the visit," media said quoting an official release.
