## Title: President to visit Maldives
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 July 2015 02:16 pm

President to visit Maldives


	


		 		 
		 
			
President Maithripala Sirisena will undertake a state visit to participate at the commemoration of the 50th Anniversary of Independence of the Republic of Maldives on July 26 in Male, the Foreign Ministry said.
The President will be accompanied by the Minister of Finance Ravi Karunanayake and senior officials of the government.
President Sirisena will be welcomed on his arrival in Maldives on Sunday afternoon with a ceremonial Guard of Honour.
He will participate at the official ceremony in Male to mark the Golden Jubilee of Independence of Maldives and the official reception being held later that evening. The President will pay a courtesy call on President Abdulla Yameen Abdul Gayoom of Maldives.
President Sirisena and the delegation will return to Sri Lanka in the morning of 27th July, 2015.
Sri Lanka and Maldives established formal diplomatic relations on 26th July 1965. Thus, this year also marks the 50th Anniversary of the establishment of formal diplomatic relations between the two countries, the statement added.
