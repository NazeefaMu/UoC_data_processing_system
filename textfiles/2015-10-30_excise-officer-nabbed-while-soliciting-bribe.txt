## Title: Excise officer nabbed while soliciting bribe
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 October 2015 11:36 am

Excise officer nabbed while soliciting bribe


	

The Northern Province Assistant Excise Commissioner was arrested by the Bribery Commission officials today while soliciting a bribe of Rs.120,000 to renew the license of a foreign liquor shop.
The Commission said the arrested would be produced before the Jaffna Magistrate’s Court tomorrow.
