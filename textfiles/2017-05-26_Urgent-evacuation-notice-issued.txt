## Title: Urgent evacuation notice issued
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 May 2017 04:58 pm

Urgent evacuation notice issued


	

Residents living by the Kelani River and belonging to the Divisional Secretariats of Kolonnawa, Kaduwela, Wellampitiya, Kelaniya, Biyagama, Sedawatte, Dompe, Hanwella, Padukka and Avissawella were instructed to immediately evacuate to safer places, the Disaster Management Centre (DMC) said.
