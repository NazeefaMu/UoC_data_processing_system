## Title: No danger to Ambatale water plant: Water Board
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 May 2016 09:35 pm

No danger to Ambatale water plant: Water Board


	

Ambatale water plant operations manager R.P.P. R. Rajapakse said today there was no danger to the Ambatale Water Treatment Plant due to the adverse weather experienced in the country as rumoured.

Mr. Rajapaksa said that water supply to Colombo could be supplied without any interruption.
