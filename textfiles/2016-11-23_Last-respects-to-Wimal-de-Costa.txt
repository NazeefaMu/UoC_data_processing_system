## Title: Last respects to Wimal de Costa
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 23 November 2016 03:22 pm

Last respects to Wimal de Costa


	


Many artistes paid last respects to veteran actor Wimal Kumara de Costa at the Sudarshi, the Sinhala Cultural Institute, Colombo. He passed away on November 20 and his funeral will take place today at the Borella Cemetery. (Pix by Kushan Pathiraja)
