## Title: Four killed train-car collision at Omanthai
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 September 2018 11:46 am

Four killed train-car collision at Omanthai


	

At least four women were killed and another two hospitalised when the KKS –Matara express train crashed with a car at an unprotected railway crossing at Pandrikeida Kulam in Omanthai this morning.

The Police said the injured were admitted to the Vavuniya Hospital.
