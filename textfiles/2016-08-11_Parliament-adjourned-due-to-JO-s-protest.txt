## Title: Parliament adjourned due to JO's protest
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 August 2016 01:06 pm

Parliament adjourned due to JO's protest


	

Parliament proceedings were adjourned a short while ago after joint opposition MPs wearing black bands protested against the enactment of the Office of Missing Persons Bill.

A party leaders’ meeting has been summoned by the Speaker to discuss this matter.
