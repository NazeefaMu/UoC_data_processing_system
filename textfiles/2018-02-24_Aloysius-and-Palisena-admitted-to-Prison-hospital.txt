## Title: Aloysius and Palisena admitted to Prison hospital
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 February 2018 09:37 am

Aloysius and Palisena admitted to Prison hospital


	

The owner of Perpetual Treasuries Limited Arjun Aloysius and CEO Kasun Palisena who were in the remand custody in connection with the alleged Central Bank bond scam, have been admitted to Prison Hospital, sources said.


A spokesman for the Prisons department said that both of them were admitted to the prison hospital after they complained of breathing problem.
