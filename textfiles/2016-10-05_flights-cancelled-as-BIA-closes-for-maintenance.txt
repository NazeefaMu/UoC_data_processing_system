## Title: 200 flights cancelled as BIA closes for maintenance
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 October 2016 11:36 am

200 flights cancelled as BIA closes for maintenance


	

Over 200 flights are to be cancelled from January to April 2017 due to maintenance work at the Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA), Sri Lankan Airlines Chairman, Ajith Dias said today.

The airport will remain closed from 8.30 am to 4.30 pm each day from January 6, 2017 to April 6, 2017.
