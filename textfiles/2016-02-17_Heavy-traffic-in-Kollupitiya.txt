## Title: Heavy traffic in Kollupitiya
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 February 2016 01:49 pm

Heavy traffic in Kollupitiya


	

Heavy traffic was reported in Kollupitiya and along the Ananda Coomaraswamy Mawatha because of a protest carrying out by a group of Ceylon Electricity Board (CEB) employees at Kollupitiya Junction.
