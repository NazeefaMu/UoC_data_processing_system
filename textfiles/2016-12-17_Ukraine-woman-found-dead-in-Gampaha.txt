## Title: Ukraine woman found dead in Gampaha
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 December 2016 11:54 am

Ukraine woman found dead in Gampaha


	

A 66-year-old Ukraine woman was found dead in a house at Medagama in Gampaha yesterday, Police said.

They said initial investigations revealed that she had attempted to commit suicide on several occasions earlier.
