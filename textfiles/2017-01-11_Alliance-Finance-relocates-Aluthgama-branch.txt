## Title: Alliance Finance relocates Aluthgama branch
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 11 January 2017 11:11 am

Alliance Finance relocates Aluthgama branch


	



 

Alliance Finance Company PLC, Sri Lanka’s third oldest finance company, recently moved its Aluthgama branch to a brand-new location, at No: 123, Galle Road, Aluthgama.
The fully-fledged branch was ceremoniously declared open by AGM Regional Financial Services Sujan Cooray, AGM Recovery Hilary Nanayakkara, Head of Marketing Dillon Thajudeen, along with the presence of other senior management, well wishes and customers. Customers in Aluthgama and the surrounding area can now experience the wide range of benefits offered by Alliance Finance, which has 60 years of proud history. 
Alliance Finance ensures that its customers get the best service in town with its innovative financial solutions and speedy service with the financial facilities such as leasing, gold loans, mortgage loans and investment opportunities such as fixed deposits and savings accounts with enormous 
financial returns. 
Customers can now enjoy hassle-free leasing service to facilitate their financial needs.
