## Title: German tourist drowns in Bentota Sea
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 November 2015 06:37 am

German tourist drowns in Bentota Sea


	

A 75-year-old German tourist drowned whilst bathing in Bentota Sea yesterday evening.
The male was reportedly residing in a private holiday resort in Bentota area with his spouse and the offspring.
The body is transferred to Nagoda Hospital Morgue for the post mortem. Bentota Police is conducting inquiries.
