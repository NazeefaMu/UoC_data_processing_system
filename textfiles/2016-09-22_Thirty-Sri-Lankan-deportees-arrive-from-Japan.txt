## Title: Thirty Sri Lankan deportees arrive from Japan
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 September 2016 02:54 pm

Thirty Sri Lankan deportees arrive from Japan


	

Thirty Sri Lankans, who were deported from Japan on charges of overstaying their visas, arrived in Sri Lanka a short while ago escorted by sixty seven Japanese security officers, sources said. (T.K.G. Kapila)
