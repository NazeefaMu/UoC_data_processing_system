## Title: She is next in line for purohita status!
## Section: Political Gossip
## Date: 4 January 2016 06:30 pm

She is next in line for purohita status!


	


A fair Diyawanna member is in for a least expected godsend, they say.
It has been decided at a high level conference to offer the next chance of being a purohita to this fair one. 
“This is something I never expected,” the highly elated fair one has told her intimates.
