## Title: 34 Police officers transferred
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 March 2015 03:55 am

34 Police officers transferred


	

Thirty- four police officers- including three SSPs, three SPs, 21 ASPs and seven OICs were transferred with immediate effect, Police Headquarters said today.
The officers have been transferred by the police chief based on service requirements with the approval of Secretary to the Public Security, Christian Religious Affairs and Disaster Management Ministry, Police said.
