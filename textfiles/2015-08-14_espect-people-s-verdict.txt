## Title: MR asks Maithri to respect people’s verdict
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 14 August 2015 06:13 am

MR asks Maithri to respect people’s verdict


	

In a response to President Maithripala Sirisena’s letter, former President Mahinda Rajapaksa said today that he hopes President Sirisena would respect the verdict of the people at the parliamentary elections in the same manner he respected the verdict on January 09 and left the Temple Trees by early morning.
