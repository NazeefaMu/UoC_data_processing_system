## Title: Outgoing Pakistan HC calls on CDS
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 6 May 2017 10:12 am

Outgoing Pakistan HC calls on CDS


	



The outgoing High Commissioner of Pakistan in Sri Lanka, Major General (Retired) Syed Shakeel Hussain paid a farewell call on the Chief of Defence Staff Air Chief Marshal Kolitha Gunatilleke at the Office of the Chief of Defence Staff  in Colombo on Friday.
