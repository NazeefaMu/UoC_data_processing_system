## Title: President at UNP convention today
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 10 September 2016 09:38 am

President at UNP convention today


	

President Maithripala Sirisena will attend the 70th UNP convention which will begin at 9.30 am today (10) at Campbell Park Colombo, under the leadership of party leader Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe.

A resolution on the future economic plan of the government will be presented by the Prime Minister at the convention. (Diana Udayangani)
