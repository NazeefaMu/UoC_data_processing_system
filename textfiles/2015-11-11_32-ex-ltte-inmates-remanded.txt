## Title: 31 ex-LTTE inmates remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 November 2015 05:13 am

31 ex-LTTE inmates remanded


	

The Colombo Magistrate's Court today re-remanded 31 ex-LTTE inmates till November 24 as the Attorney General's Department had not yet instructed the prison authorities to release them.
When the case was taken up today, prisons officials informed the court that they had held discussions with the AG’s Department but they had not been instructed as yet to release the inmates. (Shehan Chamikara)
