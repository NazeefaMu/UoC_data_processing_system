## Title: President holds a special meeting in Kandy
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 8 March 2018 09:31 am

President holds a special meeting in Kandy


	



President Maithripala Sirisena held a special meeting yesterday at President’s House in Kandy to discuss the current situation in the Kandy district.

Mahasangha led by Anunayaka Theros of Malwattha and Asgiriya chapters, and Islamic Moulavies and other religious leaders, ministers, government officers, and chiefs of security sections gathered in this discussion. Pix by President’s Media
