## Title: Kalawana SLFP organiser steps down
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 August 2016 12:06 pm

Kalawana SLFP organiser steps down


	

SLFP Parliamentarian Janaka Wakkumbura said he would resign from his post as the SLFP organiser for Kalawana, Ratnapura in accordance with a decision taken by the SLFP Kalawana Central Committee.


“They asked me to step down and I will respect the decision,” he said.
