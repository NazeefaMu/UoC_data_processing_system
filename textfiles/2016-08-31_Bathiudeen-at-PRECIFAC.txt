## Title: Bathiudeen at PRECIFAC
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 31 August 2016 10:01 am

Bathiudeen at PRECIFAC


	

Minister of Industries and Commerce Rishad Bathiudeen arrived at the Presidential Commission of Inquiry to Investigate and Inquire into Serious Acts of Fraud, Corruption and Abuse of Power, State Resources and Privileges (PRECIFAC) a short while ago.

The minister was summoned by the commission to record a statement regarding alleged financial irregularities which had taken place in Sathosa.
