## Title: SLC President assumes duties
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 January 2016 06:45 am

SLC President assumes duties


	


Deputy Speaker Thilanga Sumathipala today assumed duties as the Sri Lanka Cricket (SLC) President. He was elected to the presidentship with 88 votes defeating the former SLC secretary Nishantha Ranathunga by 56 votes. Pix by Waruna Wanniarachchi
