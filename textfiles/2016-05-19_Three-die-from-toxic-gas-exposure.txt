## Title: Three die from toxic gas exposure
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 May 2016 04:31 pm

Three die from toxic gas exposure


	

Three people exposed to toxic gas while cleaning an old well in Lunugala Maha Vidyalaya died on admission to the Lunugala Hospital


A fourth individual, who is in critical condition, was transferred to the Bibile General Hospital. (Palitha Ariyawansa)
