## Title: Video: Scholarship for deserving students
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 15 May 2015 04:17 pm

Video: Scholarship for deserving students


	


State Minister of Defence Ruwan Wijewardene handing over a scholarship to a child of a solider at the Gampaha District Secretariat today. Nearly 100 children received scholarships in a programme organized by the Ranaviru Seva Authority. Chairperson of the Authority Anoma Fonseka, wife of former Army Commander Sarath Fonseka also participated in the ceremony. Pics By Pradeep Pathirana
