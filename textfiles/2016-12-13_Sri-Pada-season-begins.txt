## Title: Sri Pada season begins
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 13 December 2016 08:19 am

Sri Pada season begins


	



The annual Sri Pada Pilgrim season had begun today. The pilgrim season will runs from today to Wesak Poya day (May), reaching its peak mid-season at Medin poya. Pix by Ranjith Rajapaksa
