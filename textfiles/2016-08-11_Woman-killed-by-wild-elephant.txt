## Title: Woman killed by wild elephant
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 August 2016 11:03 am

Woman killed by wild elephant


	

A 42-year-old mother of one child was killed by a wild elephant at Suriyawewa this morning. The victim was identified as H.E. Chandrani of Meegahathenna.
