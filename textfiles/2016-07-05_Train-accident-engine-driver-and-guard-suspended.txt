## Title: Train accident: engine driver and guard suspended
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 July 2016 10:26 am

Train accident: engine driver and guard suspended


	

The driver and guard of the locomotive engine which crashed in to a train at the Wilwatte Railway Station have been suspended, Railway Superintendent Wijeya Samarasinghe said.


Four passengers were injured yesterday when the locomotive engine collided with a train travelling from Rambukkana to Colombo at Wilwatte Railway Station. The injured were admitted to the Mirigama Hospital.     


A three member committee has been appointed to investigate the incident, even though initial investigations found that the signal lights and the breaks of the locomotive engine were in working condition.
