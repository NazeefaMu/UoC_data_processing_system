## Title: Arjun Aloysius, Kasun Palisena arrested
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 February 2018 08:05 am

Arjun Aloysius, Kasun Palisena arrested


	

Perpetual Treasuries Limited (PTL) owner Arjun Aloysius and PTL CEO Kasun Palisena were arrested by the Criminal Investigation Department (CID) this morning.

Palisena was arrested at his residence in Wellawatta while Aloysius was arrested at his Kollupitiya residence.
