## Title: UNP on way to form Govt. - Amaratunga
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 August 2015 05:44 am

UNP on way to form Govt. - Amaratunga


	


	Based on incoming election results, UNP Gampaha District candidate John Amaratunga today predicted Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe would be able to form a strong UNP government.
	He said the UNP had won the Gampaha electorate for first time at a general election since 2001. “We intend to keep our promises and develop the country as well as the district,” he said.
