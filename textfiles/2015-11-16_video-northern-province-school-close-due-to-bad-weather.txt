## Title: Video: Northern province school close due to bad weather
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 16 November 2015 06:40 am

Video: Northern province school close due to bad weather


	


All schools in the Northern Province will stay closed today (16) owing to the inclement weather prevalent in the North. Seen here are two young students who had braved the rainy weather to attend school, unaware of the temporary closure of their school.  Wanni Romesh Madusanka
