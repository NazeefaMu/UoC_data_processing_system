## Title: J.R. Jayawardena Commemoration
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 1 November 2015 10:15 am

J.R. Jayawardena Commemoration


	


Prime Minister Ranil Wickremeisnghe and Ravi Jayawardene the son of late President J. R. Jayewardene participating in religious observances at a ceremony organized to mark the 19th death anniversary of late President at J. R Jayewardene Center today. Family Members of late President, Speaker Karu Jayasuriya and other distinguished guests were present at the ceremony. Pix By Kushan Pathiraja
