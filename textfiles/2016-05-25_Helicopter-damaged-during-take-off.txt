## Title: Helicopter damaged during take off
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 May 2016 11:46 am

Helicopter damaged during take off


	

A Bell 206 helicopter was damaged during takeoff in Hingurakgoda SLAF Camp, Air Force Spokesman Group Captain Chandima Alwis said.

The spokesman said the chopper flown by a trainee pilot during a training session had tilted to a side during takeoff. The pilot was not injured but the chopper was damaged in the incident. (Darshana Sanjeewa)
