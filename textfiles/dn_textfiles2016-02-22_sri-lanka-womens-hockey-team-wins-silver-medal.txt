## Title: Sri Lanka women's hockey team wins silver medal
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, February 22, 2016 - 01:00

Sri Lanka women's hockey team wins silver medal

Sri Lanka women's hockey team won a silver medal at the inaugural South Asian Games Ladies Hockey Championship held in Guwahatl, India this month. In the finals Sri Lanka lost to India 0-10 an improvement from the 0-12 loss in the league match played earlier.
