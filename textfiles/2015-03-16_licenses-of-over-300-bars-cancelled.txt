## Title: Licenses of over 300 bars cancelled
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 March 2015 01:36 pm

Licenses of over 300 bars cancelled


	

The President has issued a directive cancelling the licenses of some 349 liquor bars due to operating in violation of the Excise Act regulations, the President’s Media Division said today.
It said the bars were found to be operating within the vicinity of religious institutions and schools.
