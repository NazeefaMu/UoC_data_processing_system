## Title: Yoshitha arrives at courts
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 February 2016 10:49 am

Yoshitha arrives at courts


	

Former President Mahinda Rajapaksa’s son Yoshitha and four others including Nishantha Ranatunga and Rohan Weliwita, who were in remand custody, arrived at the Kaduwela Magistrate’s Court a short while ago.

 They were remanded over the alleged financial irregularities at the CSN.
