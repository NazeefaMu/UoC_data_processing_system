## Title: Ajantha Ranasinghe passes away
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 February 2016 02:43 pm

Ajantha Ranasinghe passes away


	

Veteran journalist and lyricist Dr. Ajantha Ranasinghe passed away this morning at the age of 75 while undergoing treatment at the Colombo National Hospital after a brief illness.
