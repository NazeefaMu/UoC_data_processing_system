## Title: Daily Mirror bags ‘Most Popular Newspaper on Social Media’ award
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 July 2017 08:37 pm

Daily Mirror bags ‘Most Popular Newspaper on Social Media’ award


	

Daily Mirror won the ‘Most Popular Newspaper on Social Media’ award at the Mashable Social Media Day Awards - 2017, held in Colombo today.

Etisalat Lanka hosted the Mashable Social Media Day - 2017 held at the Excel World.
