## Title: President visits Kebithigollewa
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 31 July 2017 10:18 am

President visits Kebithigollewa


	



President Maithripala Sirisena made an inspection tour in the Kebithigollewa area yesterday to look into the issues faced by the people who have been affected as a result of the dry weather conditions prevailing in the country.Pics by President's Media
