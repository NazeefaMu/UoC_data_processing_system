## Title: 3,665 new teachers appointed
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 20 October 2017 12:03 pm

3,665 new teachers appointed


	



Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe distributed appointment letters to 3,665 new teachers of National Diploma Holders in Teaching at a ceremony held at the Temple Trees, today. Education Minister Akila Viraj Kariyawasam and State Minister for Education V.S. Radhakrishnan were also present. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
