## Title: Avant-Garde vessel ordered to be released
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 November 2016 04:13 pm

Avant-Garde vessel ordered to be released


	

Galle Chief Magistrate Nilupuli Lankapura today ordered that vessel Avant-Garde, be released to Sri Lanka Shipping Company on a bond of Rs. 35 million.

The Avant Garde was taken into custody and anchored in the Galle port on charges of transporting illegal weapons and ammunition. (D.G. Sugathapala)
