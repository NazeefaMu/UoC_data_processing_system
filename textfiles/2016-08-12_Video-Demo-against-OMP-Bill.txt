## Title: Video: Demo against OMP Bill
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 12 August 2016 05:58 pm

Video: Demo against OMP Bill


	



The National Collective of War heroes (Jathika Ranawiru Ekamuthuwa), several other civil society organizations and the MPs of the Joint Opposition held a demonstration at the Battaramulla War Heroes' Monument today. The demonstrators charged the Bill to set up the Office of the Missing Persons would betray the soldiers. The Picture shows MP Pavithra Waniarachchi and others engaged in religious observances during the demonstration. Pix by Nisal Baduge
