## Title: President leaves for South Korea
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 November 2017 03:38 pm

President leaves for South Korea


	

President Maithripala Sirisena left for South Korea from the Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA) today on a three-day official visit.

He left for South Korea this afternoon with a delegation of 17. (T.K.G.Kapila)
