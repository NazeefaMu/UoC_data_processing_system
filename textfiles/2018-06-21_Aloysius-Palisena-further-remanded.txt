## Title: Aloysius, Palisena further remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 21 June 2018 02:40 pm

Aloysius, Palisena further remanded


	

Perpetual Treasuries Limited (PTL) Owner Arjun Aloysius and its CEO Kasun Palisena, who were arrested by the CID over the Central Bank bond scam, were further remanded till July 5 by the Fort Magistrate’s Court today. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
