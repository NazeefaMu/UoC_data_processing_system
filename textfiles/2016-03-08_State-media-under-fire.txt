## Title: State media under fire
## Section: Political Gossip
## Date: 8 March 2016 12:00 am

State media under fire


	



 

Heads of several state media institutions recently came in for a lot of flak from a leading politician.

 


He had pointed out that although one could observe several changes for the better at state level, there was no marked change in the behavior of the state media.


It has also been revealed, the posts of General Manager, Deputy General Manager (Programmes) and Deputy General Manager (Marketing) at a state media institution have remained vacant for the past six months.
