## Title: Abducted LTTE cadre ‘Ram’ at TID
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 April 2016 10:30 am

Abducted LTTE cadre ‘Ram’ at TID


	

Rehabilitated former LTTE member known ‘Ram’ was arrested by the Terrorist Investigation Division (TID) yesterday, Police said.

Ram’s wife had complained to the Thirukkovil Police on Sunday stating that her husband was abducted by a group of unknown individuals.

Police said the former LTTE member, who was rehabilitated and reintegrated into society, is been questioned by the TID.
