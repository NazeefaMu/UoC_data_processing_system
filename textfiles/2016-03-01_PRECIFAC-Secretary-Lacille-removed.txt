## Title: PRECIFAC Secretary Lacille removed
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 March 2016 04:28 pm

PRECIFAC Secretary Lacille removed


	

Presidential Commission of Inquiry to Investigate and Inquire into Serious Acts of Fraud, Corruption and Abuse of Power, State Resources and Privileges (PRECIFAC) Secretary Lacille De Silva has been removed from the post with immediate effect, sources said.

A meeting is in progress at the Presidential Secretariat to appoint a new secretary to the commission.
