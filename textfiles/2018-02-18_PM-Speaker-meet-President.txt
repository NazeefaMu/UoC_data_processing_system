## Title: PM, Speaker meet President
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 February 2018 07:03 pm

PM, Speaker meet President


	

A special discussion is underway between President Maithripala Sirisena, Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe and Speaker Karu Jayasuriya at the President’s official residence, reportedly to discuss the Prime Minister’s position, informed sources said.
