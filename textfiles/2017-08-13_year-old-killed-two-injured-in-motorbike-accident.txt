## Title: 16-year-old killed, two injured in motorbike accident
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 13 August 2017 07:54 pm

16-year-old killed, two injured in motorbike accident


	

A 16-year-old was killed and two other youths were injured when their motorbike collided with a Cab at Kotagedara in Piliyandala this afternoon.

Police said the victims had not been wearing helmets at the time of the accident.

The injured were admitted to the Colombo South Teaching Hospital.
