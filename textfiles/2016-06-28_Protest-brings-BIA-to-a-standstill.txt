## Title: Protest brings BIA to a standstill
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 28 June 2016 01:22 pm

Protest brings BIA to a standstill


	

Flights in and out of the Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA) came to standstill because of an ongoing protest by Immigration officers.

The protest was against the setting up of CCTV cameras at the terminal buildings.

Passengers are also said to be inconvenienced because of the protest. (T.K.G.Kapila)
