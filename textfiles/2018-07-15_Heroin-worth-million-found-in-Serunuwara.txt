## Title: Heroin worth 17 million found in Serunuwara
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 July 2018 08:22 pm

Heroin worth 17 million found in Serunuwara


	

Heroin worth 17 million had been recovered at the Seenanveli Beach in Serunuwara today, Police said.

Following a tip-off the Serunuwara Police had recovered five packets of heroine weighing 1.416 kgs buried in Seenanveli Beach.

Serunuwara Police is conducting further investigation. (TK)
