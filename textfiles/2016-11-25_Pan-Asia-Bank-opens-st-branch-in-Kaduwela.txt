## Title: Pan Asia Bank opens 81st branch in Kaduwela
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 25 November 2016 12:00 am

Pan Asia Bank opens 81st branch in Kaduwela


	

Pan Asia Bank Director/Chief Executive Officer Dimantha Senevirathna opening the branch in the presence of Chief Operating Officer Kamoor Sourjah, Deputy General Manager Retail Banking and SME Naleen Edirisinghe, Kaduwela Branch Manager Dhanushka Wijesinghe and Area Manager Colombo North Hiran Perera

Pan Asia Bank Director/Chief Executive Officer Dimantha Senevirathna accepting the first deposit at the Kaduwela branch opening 

 

 

Pan Asia Bank ceremonially opened its 81st branch in Kaduwela yesterday, at No: 508/4, Avissawella Road, Kaduwela.
Pan Asia Bank Director/Chief Executive Officer Dimantha Senevirathna was the chief guest at the opening. The senior management of the bank, members of the business community and customers in the area were among the other distinguished guests.  
Speaking at the opening ceremony, Seneviratne stated that Pan Asia Bank is extremely happy that they were able to open the doors to fast innovative banking solutions that are based on understanding true customer requirements, to Kaduwela. He further stated that as the fastest growing commercial bank in Sri Lanka, it was fitting that Pan Asia Bank makes its presence in Kaduwela, it being one of the fastest growing cities in Sri Lanka. Pan Asia Bank Kaduwela branch is headed by Danushka Wijesinghe. The branch will be open from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. on weekdays and from 9:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m. on Saturdays.
