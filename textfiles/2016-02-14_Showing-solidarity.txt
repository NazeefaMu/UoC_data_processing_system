## Title: Showing solidarity?
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 14 February 2016 09:26 pm

Showing solidarity?


	




Navy SC beat Army SC 32-30 in a thrilling Dialog Rugby league encounter today at Diyagama. Several Navy SC players seemingly showed solidarity to former captain Yoshitha Rajapaksa, who was remanded over allegations of fraud, by wearing a band that said "YOO7" on their wrists.
