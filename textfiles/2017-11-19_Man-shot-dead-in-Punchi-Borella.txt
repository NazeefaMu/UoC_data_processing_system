## Title: Man shot dead in Punchi Borella
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 November 2017 11:01 am

Man shot dead in Punchi Borella


	

A 45-year-old man was found shot dead at Punchi Borella this morning, the Police said.

They said the victim was shot, while travelling in a three wheeler at D.W. Fernando Mawatha near the Nalanda College in Punchi Borella.

The victim was identified as resident of Maligawatte.
