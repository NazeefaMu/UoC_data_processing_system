## Title: Former MP Wasantha Perera remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 August 2015 05:57 am

Former MP Wasantha Perera remanded


	

Former Matale District UPFA MP Lakshman Wasantha Perera who was arrested today on charges of allegedly abducting a man and assaulting, was remanded till August 6 by Matale Magistrate.
He was arrested after he surrendered to the Matale Police this morning.(Mahesh Keerthirathna)
