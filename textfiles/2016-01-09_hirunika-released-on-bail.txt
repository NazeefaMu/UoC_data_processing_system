## Title: Hirunika released on bail
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 January 2016 07:58 am

Hirunika released on bail


	

Colombo District United National Front MP Hirunika Premachandra who was arrested by the Colombo Crime Division (CCD) this morning over an abduction of a person in Dematagoda was released on bail by the Colombo Magistrate’s Court today.
She was released on two sureties of Rs. 100, 000 each by the Additional Magistrate Mohammed Mihar.
She was ordered to appear before the Magistrate’s Court on January 12.
