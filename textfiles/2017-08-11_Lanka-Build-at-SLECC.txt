## Title: Lanka Build at SLECC
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 11 August 2017 07:14 pm

Lanka Build at SLECC


	




The International Building & Construction Show, Lanka Build-2017, was launched at the Sri Lanka Exhibition & Convention Centre (SLECC) on Friday. The exhibition will bring together suppliers of building and construction equipment, materials and technology and key decision makers. Pics by Kithsiri De Mel
