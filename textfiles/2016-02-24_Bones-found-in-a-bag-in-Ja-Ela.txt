## Title: Bones found in a bag in Ja-Ela
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 February 2016 12:21 pm

Bones found in a bag in Ja-Ela


	

Several human bones were found inside a fertilizer bag near the Ekala Pola Junction in Ja-Ela this morning, Police said.


Investigations are being conducted to determine if the bones belong to one skeleton or several.
