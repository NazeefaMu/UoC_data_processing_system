## Title: Negombo’s Newstead Girls’ College celebrates bicentenary
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 2 June 2016 05:27 pm

Negombo’s Newstead Girls’ College celebrates bicentenary


	



Newstead Girls’ College, which is one of the leading girl’s schools in Negombo celebrated its 200th anniversary today. Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe was the Chief Guest at the anniversary celebration held at the school premises. (Pix by Pradeep Pathirana)
