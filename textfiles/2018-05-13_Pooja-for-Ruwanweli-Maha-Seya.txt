## Title: Pooja for Ruwanweli Maha Seya
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 13 May 2018 09:46 am

Pooja for Ruwanweli Maha Seya


	



Minister of Megapolis and Western development Patali Champika Ranawaka organized an illumination Pooja, Kanchuka Pooja and offering of flowers to Ruwanweli Maha Seya yesterday with the participation of parents of the war heroes and disabled soldiers.
