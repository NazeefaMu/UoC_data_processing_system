## Title: Postal voting: Ballot papers handed over for delivery
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 13 January 2018 05:50 pm

Postal voting: Ballot papers handed over for delivery


	

The Elections Department will hand over the postal ballot papers to the postal authorities for delivery to voters in seven provinces, an official said yesterday.

Postal voting will take place on January 24 and 25 for public servants other than those serving in the police service and the district election offices. Postal voting for employees attached to the district election offices will be held on January 22. (Kelum Bandara)
