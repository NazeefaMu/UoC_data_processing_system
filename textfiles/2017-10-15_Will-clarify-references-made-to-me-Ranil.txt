## Title: Will clarify references made to me: Ranil
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 October 2017 08:39 pm

Will clarify references made to me: Ranil


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe is reported to have written to the Presidential Commission of Inquiry on the Central Bank bond issue that he was ready to clarify any matters with regard to the references made to him at the sittings. (Yohan Perera)
