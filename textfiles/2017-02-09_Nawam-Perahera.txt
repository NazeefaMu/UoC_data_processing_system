## Title: Nawam Perahera
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 9 February 2017 10:07 pm

Nawam Perahera


	



State Minister of Defence Ruwan Wijewardene and Minister of Primary Industries Development Daya Gamage place the golden casket containing the sacred relics on the back of the leading elephant at the commencement of the Nawam Perahera of the Gangarama Temple in Hunupitiya this evening.
