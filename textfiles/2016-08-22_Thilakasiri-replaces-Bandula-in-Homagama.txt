## Title: Thilakasiri replaces Bandula in Homagama
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 August 2016 02:01 pm

Thilakasiri replaces Bandula in Homagama


	

Western Province Minister Gamini Thilakasiri has been appointed as the new SLFP organiser to the Homagama electorate by President Maithripala Sirisena this morning.

Minister Thilakasiri was appointed to the post following the resignation of former organiser MP Bandula Gunawardena on Sunday.
