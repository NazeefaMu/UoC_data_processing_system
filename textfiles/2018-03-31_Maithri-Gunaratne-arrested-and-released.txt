## Title: Maithri Gunaratne arrested and released
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 31 March 2018 06:50 pm

Maithri Gunaratne arrested and released


	

Secretary of the United National Freedom Front and former UNP provincial councillor Maithri Gunaratne, was arrested by the Kadugannawa police with two shotguns, at Pilimathalawa, Police said.

However, Mr. Gunaratne has been released on bail by the Kandy Magistrate after he produced copies of the licences for the two shotguns.
