## Title: Gampola child abduction: Suspects remanded till May 18
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 7 May 2017 03:55 pm

Gampola child abduction: Suspects remanded till May 18


	

The three suspects who were arrested by the police in connection with the abduction of  two-and-half-year-old child in Gampola remanded until May 18, Police said.

Police said the 26-year-old youth who is reported to be the child’s uncle and the other two were remanded after they were produced before the Gampola Magistrate’s Courts.
