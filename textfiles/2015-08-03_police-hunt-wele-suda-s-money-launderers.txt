## Title: Police hunt Wele Suda's money launderers
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 August 2015 03:08 pm

Police hunt Wele Suda's money launderers


	


	Four people suspected to have aided and abetted notorious drug kingpin Gampola Vidanage Samantha Kumara alias ‘Wele Suda’ to launder large sums of money earned through the narcotics trade are being wanted by the police.
	Police Spokesman Ruwan Gunasekara said police have received detailed reports of the four suspects of whom two are women, who are at large since last year.
	All four suspects were identified as those from the Wellawatte, Dehiwela and Kirulapone areas where Wele Suda was chiefly operating from.
	The public may provide any information pertaining to the suspects to Police hotlines: 0112320141, 0112329145 and 0112422176.  (Kurulu Koojana Kariyakarawana)
