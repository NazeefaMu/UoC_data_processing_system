## Title: Fourteen Indian fishermen arrested in SL waters
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 November 2015 08:49 am

Fourteen Indian fishermen arrested in SL waters


	

Navy and Coast Guard yesterday arrested fourteen Indian fishermen along with three fishing trawlers while they were poaching in Sri Lankan waters in close proximity to Delft Island.
The arrested fishermen and the trawlers were brought to Karainagar and handed over to the officials of the Department of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources. (Navy.lk)
