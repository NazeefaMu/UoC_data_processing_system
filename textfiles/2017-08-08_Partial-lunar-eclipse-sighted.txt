## Title: Partial lunar eclipse sighted
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 8 August 2017 12:32 am

Partial lunar eclipse sighted


	



The partial lunar eclipse was sighted last night (8) over the Colombo skies between 9.20p.m and its peak at 11.51 pm. According to Professor Chandana Jayaratne the lunar eclipse was visible all over Asia, Europe, and Africa. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
