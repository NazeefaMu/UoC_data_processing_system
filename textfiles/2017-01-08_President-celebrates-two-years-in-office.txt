## Title: President celebrates two years in office
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 8 January 2017 09:09 pm

President celebrates two years in office


	



The 2nd anniversary ceremony of President Maithripala Sirisena in office was held at the Bandaranaike Memorial International Conference Hall (BMICH) today. Andhra Pradesh Chief Minister Chandrababu Naidu was the Guest of Honour at the event. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
