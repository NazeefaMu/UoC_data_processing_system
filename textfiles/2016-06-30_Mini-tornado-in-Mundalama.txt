## Title: Mini-tornado in Mundalama
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 30 June 2016 01:24 pm

Mini-tornado in Mundalama


	



A gale force wind that hit Handathoduwawa fishery village in Mundalama caused much havoc yesterday evening. More than 30 houses were damaged and many other property including fisheries boats and household effects destroyed. Pix Hiran Priyankara
