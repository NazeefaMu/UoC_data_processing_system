## Title: Last rites of Venerable Galagama Sri Attadassi Thera
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 13 March 2016 08:14 pm

Last rites of Venerable Galagama Sri Attadassi Thera


	



The last rites of the deceased Prelate of the Asgiri Chapter, the Most Venerable Galagama Sri Attadassi Thera, was held today at the Asgiriya Police Grounds in Kandy. Pix by Jamal Chandrasiri
