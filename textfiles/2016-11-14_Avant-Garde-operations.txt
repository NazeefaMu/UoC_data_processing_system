## Title: Avant Garde operations
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 14 November 2016 03:24 pm

Avant Garde operations


	



Having taken over the Onboard Security Team Operations (OBST) from Avant Garde Maritime Services, the Navy had carried out a total of 6,150 shipments to the Galle harbour and 496 shipments to the Colombo harbour, with an average of approximately 554 shipments month.(Navy Media)
