## Title: Man hit by train in Ahungalla
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 October 2016 08:03 pm

Man hit by train in Ahungalla


	

A 40-year-old man was killed after being hit by an express train travelling from Colombo to Matara at Ahungalla a short while ago.  Police said the victim had been sitting on the railway track waiting for the train to come and run him over.
