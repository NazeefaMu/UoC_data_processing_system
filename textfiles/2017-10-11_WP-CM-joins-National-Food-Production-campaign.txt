## Title: WP CM joins National Food Production campaign
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 11 October 2017 04:36 pm

WP CM joins National Food Production campaign


	



Western Province Chief Minister Isura Devapriya planted a sapling at the Chief Minister’s Office Premises in Colombo on Wednesday to mark the launch of National Food Production campaign by President Maithripala Sirisena. Western Provincial Council Secretary M.A.B. Daya Senarath and Wattala Pradeshiya Sabha Member Dalton Janaka were also seen in the picture. Pics by Nisal Baduge
