## Title: Susanthika hospitalized
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 June 2016 09:21 am

Susanthika hospitalized


	

Olympic silver medalist Susanthika Jayasinghe was admitted to the Gampha hospital in the early hours today following an assault at her Gampha residence, police said.

A police complaint was also lodged with the Weliweriya police and the husband of Susanthika was arrested in connection with the assault.
