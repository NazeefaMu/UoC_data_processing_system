## Title: Chinese Ambassador calls on RW
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 October 2018 10:02 pm

Chinese Ambassador calls on RW


	

Chinese Ambassador Cheng Xueyuan paid a courtesy call on UNP Leader Ranil Wickremesinghe this evening.

Earlier, the envoy called on Prime Minister Mahinda Rajapaksa at his residence and conveyed wishes from Chinese President Xi Jinping.
