## Title: 306 arrested for drunk driving
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 December 2015 10:47 am

306 arrested for drunk driving


	

A total of 306 drivers had been arrested for Driving Under Influence (DUI) in a special operation launched island wide from Wednesday, the Police said.
They said 118 drivers were arrested on Wednesday, 83 drivers on Thursday and 105 drivers on Friday.
