## Title: Protests against VAT in several areas
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 7 July 2016 10:32 am

Protests against VAT in several areas


	

The Collective of Trade Associations from several areas including Hambantota, Ambalantota, Ratnapura and Balapitiya staged protests against the increase in the Value Added Tax (VAT) this morning.


Most of the shops in these areas were closed for hours.
