## Title: Twenty Police officers including six DIGs, three SPs transferred
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 August 2018 11:02 am

Twenty Police officers including six DIGs, three SPs transferred


	

Six DIGs inducing Crimes DIG Kamal Silva and DIG in-charge of the North Western, Ajith Rohana, had been transferred with immediate effect based on service requirements, Police said.

They said three Superintendent of Police (SPs), two Assistant Superintendent of Police (ASPs), three Chief Inspectors (CI) and six Inspectors (IP) were also transferred.

The National Police Commission (NPC) approved the transfers recommended by IGP Pujith Jayasndara. (Darshana Sanjeewa)
