## Title: Fire at textile warehouse in Pepiliyana
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 November 2016 10:30 pm

Fire at textile warehouse in Pepiliyana


	

A fire broke out at a well-known textile warehouse in Pepiliyana, Borelasgamuwa this evening, Fire Brigade said. It also said that six fire brigade vehicles have been sent to the scene.
