## Title: Court order on protesting doctors at Education Ministry
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 September 2016 11:02 am

Court order on protesting doctors at Education Ministry


	

Kaduwela Magistrate today issued an order calling on the doctors occupying the Ministry of Education to vacate the premises.

About 200 doctors were waiting in the auditorium of Education Ministry since yesterday requesting a meeting with Minister Akila Viraj Kariyawasam over an anomaly in admitting Government doctors’ children to State National Schools.
