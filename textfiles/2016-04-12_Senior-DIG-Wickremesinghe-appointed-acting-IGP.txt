## Title: Senior DIG Wickremesinghe appointed acting IGP
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 12 April 2016 08:40 am

Senior DIG Wickremesinghe appointed acting IGP


	

President Maithripala Sirisena has appointed Senior DIG S.M. Wickremesinghe as the acting IGP, police said.

N. K Illangakoon who served for five years as the 33rd IGP, retired yesterday.
