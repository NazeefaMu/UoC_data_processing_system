## Title: Out of politics, he takes to religion!
## Section: Political Gossip
## Date: 3 May 2016 12:00 am

Out of politics, he takes to religion!


	

This actor-turned politician now languishing in political wilderness has deposited his family gold jewelry inside some statuettes made to order. 

 


When in power, the politico used to throw his weight about like the typical baddy at the bazaar and now highly disillusioned, he is said to be devoting most of his time to religious observances.
