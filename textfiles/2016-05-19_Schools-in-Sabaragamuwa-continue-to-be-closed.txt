## Title: Schools in Sabaragamuwa continue to be closed
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 May 2016 12:34 pm

Schools in Sabaragamuwa continue to be closed


	

The closure of schools in Sabaragamuwa province due to the prevailing flood situation has been extended.

The Education Department had decided on Tuesday night (17) to close all national schools and provincial schools in the province.

A senior official of the Provincial Education Ministry said he was hopeful that the situation would be back to normal by next week to reopen the schools. (Prince Rathnayaka)
