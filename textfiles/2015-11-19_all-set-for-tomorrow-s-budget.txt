## Title: All set for budget
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 19 November 2015 10:26 am

All set for budget


	


	The final perusal of the budget which is to be presented for 2016 was held at the Presidential Secretariat with the participation of President Maithripala Sirisena.
	Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe, Finance Minister Ravi Karunanayake, Minister Malik Samarawickrame, Central Bank Governor Arjuna Mahendran and several officials of the Finance Ministry also took part at the discussion. Pic by President’s Media
