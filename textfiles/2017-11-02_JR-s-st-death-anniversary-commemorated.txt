## Title: JR’s 21st death anniversary commemorated
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 2 November 2017 01:36 am

JR’s 21st death anniversary commemorated


	



The family of former President J R Jayawardene commemorated his 21th death anniversary at a ceremony held at the Jayawardene Centre in Colombo today. The event also commemorated the demise of the former President’s wife Elina who died in 2007 and son Ravi who passed away earlier this year. Pix by Kithsiri de Mel
