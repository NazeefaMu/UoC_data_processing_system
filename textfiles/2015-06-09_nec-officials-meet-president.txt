## Title: NEC officials meet President
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 9 June 2015 11:35 am

NEC officials meet President


	


A group of officials from the National Education Commission (NEC) met with President Maithripala Sirisena today at the Presidential Secretariat. The officials apprised the President regarding their current progress of educational plan implementation and future plans. Pix by President’s Media
