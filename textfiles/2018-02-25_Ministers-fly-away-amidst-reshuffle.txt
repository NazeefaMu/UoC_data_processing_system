## Title: Ministers fly away amidst reshuffle
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 February 2018 11:30 am

Ministers fly away amidst reshuffle


	

Several ministers had made plans to go abroad amidst the Cabinet reshuffle which took place today.
Petroleum Resources Development Minister Arjuna Ranatunga has already gone to Doha.

Science, Technology and Research Minister Susil Premajayantha is scheduled to go to New Delhi today.

Special Assignment Minister Sarath Amunugama is scheduled to visit Bangkok, Thailand.

Meanwhile, Labour and Trade Unions Relations State Minister Ravindra Samaraweera is scheduled to visit London. (T.K.G. Kapila)
