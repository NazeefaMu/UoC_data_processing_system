## Title: PM discuss EP CM issue with military
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 May 2016 05:00 pm

PM discuss EP CM issue with military


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe today met the military hierarchies and discussed the about recent incident where the Eastern Chief Minister Nazer Ahamed rebuked a Navy officer at an event in Sampur, Trincomalee.

Accordingly, the Premier is to discuss the matter with President Maithripala Sirisena soon after he returns to the island.
