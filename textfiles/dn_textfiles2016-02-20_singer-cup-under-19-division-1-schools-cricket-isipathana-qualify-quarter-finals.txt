## Supertitle:

## Date: Saturday, February 20, 2016 - 01:00

Singer Cup Under 19 Division 1 Schools Cricket: Isipathana qualify for quarter-finals

Isipathana College advanced to the quarterfinal of the Singer Trophy Under 19 division I inter-schools cricket tournament yesterday when they took first innings points off Nalanda College at the BRC grounds.
Ashel Kulatunga of Nalanda had a match bag of ten wickets.
Isipathana College: 280 and 142/4 in 33 overs (Vishad Randika 54, Pramod Madhuwantha 34 n.o., Asel Kulatunga 2/32)
Nalanda College: 201 (7/1 o/n) in 72.4 overs (Dasun Seneviratne 71, Malinga Amarasinghe 39, Pramod Madhuwantha 6/60, Lahiru Dilshan 3/41)
Avishka, Praveen in double century stand
Avishka Fernando and Praveen Perera shared an unfinished 211 second wicket partnership for St. Sebastian's College in their school match against Ananda College at Wijerama Mawatha yesterday.
Fernando was unbeaten on 121 scored off 113 balls and Perera was 84 not out at the close.
