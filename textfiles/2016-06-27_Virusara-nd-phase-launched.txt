## Title: ‘Virusara’ 2nd phase launched
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 27 June 2016 06:18 pm

‘Virusara’ 2nd phase launched


	



The second phase of Virusara Privilege card was launched at Rajarata Vidyalaya in Hingurakgoda today by President Maithripala Sirisena. Rural Development Minister Sarath Fonseka, State Minister for Defence Ruwan Wijewardene, Ministry Secretary Karunasena Hettiarachchi, Ranaviru Seva Authority Chairperson Anoma Fonseka and the Commanders of tri forces were also present at the event.
