## Title: Minor tremor felt in Balangoda
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 March 2016 04:09 pm

Minor tremor felt in Balangoda


	

Geological Survey and Mines Bureau (GMSB) said a minor tremor has been recorded in Balangoda, a short while ago.

The GMSB said the tremor was lesser than 2 magnitudes recorded in Imbulpe, Mahawathenna and Weligepola areas according to the Pallekele Seismic Station.

However, the Disaster Management Centre said no damages have been reported.
