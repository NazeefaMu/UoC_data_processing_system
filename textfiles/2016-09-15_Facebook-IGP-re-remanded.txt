## Title: ‘Facebook IGP’ re-remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 September 2016 10:22 am

‘Facebook IGP’ re-remanded


	

A man, who was arrested by the CID over impersonating the IGP on Facebook deceiving women to obtain various unlawful advantages, was re-remanded till September 19 by Colombo Additional Magistrate Nishantha Peiris.

On a complaint received from a woman, the CID arrested him on arrival at the Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA)

According to the complainant the alleged suspect, Madushan Weerasekara, had deceived her impersonating as IGP on Facebook. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
