## Title: 28 injured in bus accident at Bogasthenna
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 28 June 2016 12:13 pm

28 injured in bus accident at Bogasthenna


	

Twenty six passengers and the driver and the conductor of the SLTB bus they were travelling in were injured when the bus crashed into a wayside tree at Jagulla, Bogasthenna on the Galauda – Badulla Main Road today, Police said.  

The injured were admitted to the Kandegedera Hospital while 15 of those in a critical condition were transferred to the Badulla Hospital.(Prasad Rukmal and Nishantha Kumara)
