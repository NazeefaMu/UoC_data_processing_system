## Title: One killed, six injured in trishaw accident
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 10 February 2017 12:20 pm

One killed, six injured in trishaw accident


	

A 55-year-old man was killed and six others including two children were injured when the three-wheeler they were travelling in skidded off the road and toppled over at Dickoya in Hatton this morning, Police said.

They said the deceased was identified as KinamsamiRajaratnam from Ketabulawe in Nawalapitiya.

Investigations had revealed that the driver was under the influence of liquor. 

The Hatton Police are investigating. (Ranjith Rajapaksa)
