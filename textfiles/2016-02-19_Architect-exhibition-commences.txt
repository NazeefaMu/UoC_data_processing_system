## Title: Architect 2016 exhibition commences
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 19 February 2016 09:14 pm

Architect 2016 exhibition commences


	



The Architect 2016 exhibition organised by the Sri Lanka Institute of Architects was launched at the BMICH today.

The exhibition which has more than 300 trade stalls and 132 display panels would conclude on Sunday. Pix by Kithsiri de Mel
