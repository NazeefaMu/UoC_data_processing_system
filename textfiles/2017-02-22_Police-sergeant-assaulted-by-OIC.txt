## Title: Police sergeant assaulted by OIC
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 February 2017 10:25 am

Police sergeant assaulted by OIC


	

A Police sergeant attached to the Yatiyanthota Police Station was hospitalized after he was allegedly assaulted by its OIC last night.

Police said he was admitted to the Awissawella Hospital.

They said Seethawakapura Assistant Superintendent of Police (ASP) is conducting investigations into the incident. (DS)
