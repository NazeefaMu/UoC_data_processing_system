## Title: NDB opens latest branch  in Tissamaharama
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 21 December 2015 03:14 am

NDB opens latest branch  in Tissamaharama


	

(From L to R.) Assistant Vice President Administration - Kumar Gunawardena, Regional Manager – Chandima Alwis, Assistant Vice President Branch Network Management – Indika Ranaweera, Vice President Administration and Services - Mancius Paiva, Vice President Branch Network Management - Sanjaya Perera, Branch Manager - Suranga Perera
NDB opened its latest branch in Tissamaharama with the intention of further strengthening its financial services to the residents in the Southern Province. With this opening, NDB branch network has now grown to ninety three (93), 
The branch was declared open by NDB’s Vice President Administration and Services - Mancius Paiva, along with the patronage of the Senior Management of the bank as well as valued customers from the area.
NDB will offer a gamut of Retail Banking products including Current/Savings accounts, Fixed Deposits, Housing Loans, Pawning services, Vehicle Leasing as well as Insurance Services to the residents of Tissamaharama to help them grow and secure their wealth while enjoying easy access and convenience of Banking with automated facilities such as Visa Debit Cards, Internet Banking and Western Union Money Transfer Services.  
NDB’s Tissamaharama branch is located at New Town, Tissamaharama.
