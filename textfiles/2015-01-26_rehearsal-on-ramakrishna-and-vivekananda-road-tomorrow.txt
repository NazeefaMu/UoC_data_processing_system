## Title: Rehearsal on Ramakrishna and Vivekananda Road tomorrow
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 January 2015 11:01 am

Rehearsal on Ramakrishna and Vivekananda Road tomorrow


	

The Ramakrishna Road and Vivekananda Road in Wellawatta connecting the Galle Road and the Marine Drive would be made one-way tomorrow between 6.00 a.m. to 11.00 a.m. and between 3.00 p.m. to 7.00 p.m., the Police said.
The flow system is only a rehearsal, the Police said in a media communiqué.
Vehicles would be allowed to enter the Marine Drive from Galle Road through Ramakrishna Road. No traffic would be allowed in the opposite direction.
The Police said vehicles should use the Vivekananda Road to enter the Galle Road. No traffic flow would be allowed in the opposite direction on the Vivekananda Road as well.
Vehicles should use Somagiri Place to enter Vivekananda Road from Ramakrishna Road, while vehicles would be allowed only Ramakrishna Terrace to drive towards Ramakrishna Road from Vivekananda Road.
