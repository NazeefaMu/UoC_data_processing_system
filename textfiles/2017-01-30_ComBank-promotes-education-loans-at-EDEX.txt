## Title: ComBank promotes education loans at EDEX 2017
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 30 January 2017 10:21 am

ComBank promotes education loans at EDEX 2017


	



 

The education loans offered by Commercial Bank of Ceylon for the pursuit of higher education or professional education in Sri Lanka or overseas generated much interest at the EDEX 2017 exhibition held in Colombo recently. The bank’s stall drew large numbers of interested visitors seeking information and the bank also offered visitors the facility of applying for loans to pay course fees or examination fees at the stall.
Commercial Bank also participated in the EDEX 2017 exhibition at the Kandy City Centre on January 27 and 28.
