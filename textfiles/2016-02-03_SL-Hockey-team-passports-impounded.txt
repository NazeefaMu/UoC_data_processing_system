## Title: SL Hockey team passports impounded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 February 2016 12:22 pm

SL Hockey team passports impounded


	

The Colombo District Court issued an enjoining order preventing the National Hockey team from participating in the SA Games in India.  


A leading hockey palyer Diluka Weerasuriya had filed legal action citing irregularities in the selection process for the National team.
