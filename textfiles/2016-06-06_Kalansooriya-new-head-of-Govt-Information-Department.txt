## Title: Kalansooriya new head of Govt. Information Department
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 June 2016 05:42 pm

Kalansooriya new head of Govt. Information Department


	

Ranga Kalansooriya, senior journalist and former Director General of Sri Lanka Press Complaint Commission, today assumed duties as the Director General of Government Information Department.

Mr. Kalansooriya is also the Regional Advisor - Asia Pacific at International Media Support - Denmark.
