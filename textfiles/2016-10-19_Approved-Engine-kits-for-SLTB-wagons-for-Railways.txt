## Title: Approved: Engine kits for SLTB, wagons for Railways
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 October 2016 07:25 pm

Approved: Engine kits for SLTB, wagons for Railways


	

Proposals have been made to procure 163 new Ashok Leyland engine kits for Sri Lanka Transport Board (SLTB) under Indian assistance, Cabinet Co-Spokesman Gayantha Karunathilake said.

Meanwhile, approval has been granted for 20 container carrier wagons and 30 Fuel Tank Wagons for Sri Lanka Railways Department.

The proposal was made by the Transport and Civil Aviation Minister Nimal Siripala de Silva. (Chaturanga Pradeep)
