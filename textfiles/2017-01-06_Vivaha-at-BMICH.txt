## Title: ‘Vivaha 400’ at BMICH
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 6 January 2017 12:16 pm

‘Vivaha 400’ at BMICH


	



The ‘Vivaha 400’ wedding fair is on at the Sirimavo Bandaranaike Memorial Hall, BMICH from 6 to 8 January. The fair features a bridal fashion show along with exhibits for weddings, brides, grooms and other wedding services. Pix by Kithsiri de Mel
