## Title: Kusal celebrating his first ODI century
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 28 March 2017 08:37 pm

Kusal celebrating his first ODI century


	



Kusal Mendis scored his first ODI century as Sri Lanka were bowled out for 311 in the second ODI against Bangladesh at the Dambulla International Stadium today. Rain during the innings break delayed the resumption of the match. Pix by Pradeep Dilruckshana
