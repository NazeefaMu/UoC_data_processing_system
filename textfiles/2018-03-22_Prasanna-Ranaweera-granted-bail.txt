## Title: Prasanna Ranaweera granted bail
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 March 2018 11:40 am

Prasanna Ranaweera granted bail


	

The Mahara Magistrate’s Court today granted bail to UPFA Gampaha District MP Prasanna Ranaweera who was remanded over an assault incident.

He was charged with assaulting a candidate who contested at the Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sabha at the last Local Government elections from the SLFP.
