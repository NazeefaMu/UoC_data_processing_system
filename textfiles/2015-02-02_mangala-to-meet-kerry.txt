## Title: Mangala to meet Kerry
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 2 February 2015 06:46 am

Mangala to meet Kerry


	

External Affairs Minister Mangala Samaraweera is due to meet US Secretary of State John Kerry on February 12 in Washington during which he will extend an official invitation for Kerry to visit Sri Lanka.
Minister Samaraweera addressing the media a short while ago after his meeting with Assistant Secretary of State for South and Central Asian Affairs Nisha Biswal, said the government is looking forward to further strengthening relations with the US. (Lakna Paranamanna)
