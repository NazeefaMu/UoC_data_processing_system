## Title: Galle CC hammer Army for 302/3
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, December 19, 2015 - 01:00

Galle CC hammer Army for 302/3

 
Galle CC scored 302-3 against Army SC on the opening day of their AIA Trophy Premier League inter-club match at Galle International Cricket Stadium yesterday.
Nisal Randika (92 n.o.) and Chamara Silva (59) added 120 in an unbroken fourth wicket partnership. T.N. Sampath (70) and Damitha Hunukumbura (36) were also involved in a century stand - 111 runs for the first wicket.
Galle CC: 302/3 at close in 90 overs (Nisal Randika 92 n.o., T.N. Sampath 70, Chamara Silva 59 n.o., Damitha Hunukumbura 36, Romesh Buddhika 26, Seekkuge Prasanna 2/68) vs Army SC
