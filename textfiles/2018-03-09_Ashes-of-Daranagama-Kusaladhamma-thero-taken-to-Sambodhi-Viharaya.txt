## Title: Ashes of Daranagama Kusaladhamma thero taken to Sambodhi Viharaya
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 9 March 2018 06:02 pm

Ashes of Daranagama Kusaladhamma thero taken to Sambodhi Viharaya


	



Ashes of late most Venerable Daranagama Kusaladhamma Thera was collected and taken to the Sri Sambodhi Viharaya, Colombo for interment by Ven. Thero’s mother and other relatives. Pix by Nisal Baduge
