## Title: Sicille Kotelawala re-remanded over F&G case
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 March 2016 12:44 pm

Sicille Kotelawala re-remanded over F&G case


	

Former Ceylinco Chairman Lalith Kotelawala's wife Sicille was further remanded till April 6 by Colombo Chief Magistrate Gihan Pilapitiya today over the F&G case.

Mrs. Kotelawala was the eighth suspect in the case filed against her husband Lalith and eight others on charges of misappropriating Rs. 4.3 billion at the F&G Property Developers. (SCS)
