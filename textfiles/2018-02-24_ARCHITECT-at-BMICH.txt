## Title: ARCHITECT 2018 at BMICH
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 24 February 2018 04:05 am

ARCHITECT 2018 at BMICH


	



ARCHITECT 2018 exhibition was launched at the Bandaranaike Memorial International Conference Hall (BMICH) on Thursday (22) under the patronage of President Maithripala Sirisena and Megapolis and Western Development Minister Patali Champika Ranawaka. The exhibition will be held till Sunday (25). Pix by Kithsiri de Mel
