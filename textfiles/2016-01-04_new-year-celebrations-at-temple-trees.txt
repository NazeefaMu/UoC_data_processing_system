## Title: New Year celebrations at Temple Trees
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 January 2016 08:01 am

New Year celebrations at Temple Trees


	


Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe met the ministers at the Temple Trees today to wish them for the New Year. At the event, he stressed the need for obtaining the support of those who oppose the government, to its camp. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
