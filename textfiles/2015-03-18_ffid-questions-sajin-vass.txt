## Title: FFID questions Sajin Vass
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 March 2015 05:46 am

FFID questions Sajin Vass


	

MP Sajin Vass Gunawardena is being questioned by the Financial Fraud Investigation Division (FFID) this morning over allegations of non-payment of leasing installments pertaining to 33 vehicles, the Police said.
