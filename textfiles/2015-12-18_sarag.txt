## Title: Two teenagers drown in Saragama Wewa
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 December 2015 04:06 am

Two teenagers drown in Saragama Wewa


	

Two teenagers aged 13 and 14 have drowned in the Saragama Wewa at Thiththawella in Kurunegala this morning, Police said.
The two victims had been exercising near the Saragama Wewa with two other friends and had drowned after falling into the tank while attempting to wash their faces.
