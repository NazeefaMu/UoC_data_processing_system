## Title: Ten Bangladesh nationals arrested in Panadura
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 June 2016 09:44 am

Ten Bangladesh nationals arrested in Panadura


	

Ten Bangladesh nationals who were staying without valid visas were arrested in Panadura yesterday by the Panadura Division Crime Investigation Unit, Police said.

They said three people, who aided and abetted them, were also arrested on information received by the police.
They are to be produced in Panadura Magistrate’s Court today.
