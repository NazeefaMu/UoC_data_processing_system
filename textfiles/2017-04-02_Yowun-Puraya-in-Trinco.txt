## Title: Yowun Puraya in Trinco
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 2 April 2017 01:36 pm

Yowun Puraya in Trinco


	



The Yowun Puraya 2017 closing ceremony was held in Trincomalee yesterday. President Maithripala Sirisena was the chief guest of the event while Prime Minister Ranil Wickremasinghe, Opposition Leader R Sambandan,State Minister Niroshan Perera,Director General of the National Youth Council Eranda Weliange and several government ministers and MPs also Participated .Pix by Pradeep Pathirana.
