## Title: <U+200B>Sultan Ibrahim of Johor in SL
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 7 January 2016 06:26 pm

<U+200B>Sultan Ibrahim of Johor in SL


	

His Majesty Sultan Ibrahim of the Malaysian State of Johor and his entourage of ten persons arrived today on a private visit to Sri Lanka. During his visit he made a courtesy call on President Maithripala Sirisena. State Defence Minister Ruwan Wijewardene welcomed the visiting Sultan Ibrahim of Johor at the Bandaranaike International Air Port.
