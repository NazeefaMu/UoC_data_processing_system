## Title: FCID questiones RDA DG & ADG
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 29 April 2015 05:49 am

FCID questiones RDA DG & ADG


	

The Financial Crimes Investigations Department (FCID) has commenced its investigations reagarding alleged road construction contracts during the previous government.
Police spokesman ASP Ruwan Gunasekara said the Director General W.A.S Weerasinghe and Additional Director General R.M Amerasekera of the Road Development Authority (RDA) are being questioned by the FCID regarding alleged misappropriation of funds of the Katunayake Expressway contract and another 26 Road constructions contracts.
