## Title: Drivers warned of heavy mist on 18 Bend Road
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 December 2015 01:15 pm

Drivers warned of heavy mist on 18 Bend Road


	

Police today advised commuters and drivers using the 18 Bend Road on the Mahanuwara - Mahiyanganaya Road to be cautious due to the heavy mist prevailing in the area because of the torrential rains.
