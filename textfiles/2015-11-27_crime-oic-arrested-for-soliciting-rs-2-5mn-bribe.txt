## Title: Crime OIC arrested for soliciting Rs. 2.5mn bribe
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 November 2015 12:12 pm

Crime OIC arrested for soliciting Rs. 2.5mn bribe


	

Officer in Charge of the Crime Branch of the Narahenpita Police was nabbed by the Bribery Commission sleuths for soliciting a bribe of Rs. 2.5 million from a businessman a short while ago, in Kohuwala.
The businessman, a suspect of a forged document case was demanded the sum not to take legal action against him by police.
