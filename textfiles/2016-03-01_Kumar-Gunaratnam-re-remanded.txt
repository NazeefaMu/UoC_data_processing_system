## Title: Kumar Gunaratnam re-remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 March 2016 01:12 pm

Kumar Gunaratnam re-remanded


	

Frontline Socialist Party (FSP) Politburo member Kumar Gunaratnam was further remanded till March 14 by the Kegalle Magistrate’s Court today.

He was arrested by the Police in the Anguruwella area in Kegalle on November 04, last year on charges of violating immigration laws. (RK)
