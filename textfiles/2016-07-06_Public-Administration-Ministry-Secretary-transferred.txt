## Title: Public Administration Ministry Secretary  transferred
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 July 2016 04:58 pm

Public Administration Ministry Secretary  transferred


	

Public Administration Ministry Secretary J. Dadallage is reported to have been transferred from the ministry from Tuesday.

Mr. Dadallage told Daily Mirror he received a letter on Tuesday saying transferred with immediate effect but had appealed against the decision. "No reasons have been cited for my transfer," he said.(Kelum Bandara)
