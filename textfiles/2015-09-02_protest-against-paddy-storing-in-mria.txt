## Title: Protest against paddy storing in MRIA
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 2 September 2015 06:47 am

Protest against paddy storing in MRIA


	

A group of people staged a protest at Mahinda Rajapaksa International Airport (MRIA) this morning against the Paddy Marketing Board’s decision to use airport warehouses as a paddy storage space.
Police said the protesting residents and airport employees obstructed a lorry carrying paddy, from entering the airport premises.
