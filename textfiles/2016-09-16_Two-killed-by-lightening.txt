## Title: Two killed by lightening
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 September 2016 10:54 am

Two killed by lightening


	

Two men were killed after they were struck by lightning in Mangalagama, Ampara while they were kept vigil at a chena cultivation last evening, Police said.

The victims were identified as 46-year-old Polwattelage Wijeyratne and 20-year-old Shanaka Madushanka. Police are conducting further investigations.
