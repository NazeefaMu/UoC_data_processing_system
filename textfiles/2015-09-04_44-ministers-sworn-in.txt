## Title: 44 Ministers sworn in
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 September 2015 12:38 pm

44 Ministers sworn in


	


Forty four Cabinet Ministers of the newly set up National Unity Government (NUG) were sworn in by President Maithripala Sirisena at a ceremony held at the Presidential Secretariat today. Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
