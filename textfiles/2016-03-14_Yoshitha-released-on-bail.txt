## Title: Yoshitha released on bail
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 14 March 2016 06:47 pm

Yoshitha released on bail


	



The Colombo High Court today released Yoshitha Rajapaksa and three other directors of the Carlton Sports Network (CSN) on a bail of Rs.100,000 with two sureties of Rs.1 million.They were also ordered to cooperate with the ongoing FCID investigations into the money laundering case against them.
