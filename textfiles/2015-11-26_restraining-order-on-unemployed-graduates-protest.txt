## Title: Restraining order on Unemployed Graduates’ protest
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 November 2015 05:34 am

Restraining order on Unemployed Graduates’ protest


	


	Kaduwela Magistrate today issued a restraining order on the Convener of the Unemployed Graduates’ Union Dhammika Munasinghe and five others preventing the entry of a demonstration by the Unemployed Graduates’ Union into the parliament road this morning.
	The order was made on the request of Thalangama police. (Irangi Edirisinghe)
