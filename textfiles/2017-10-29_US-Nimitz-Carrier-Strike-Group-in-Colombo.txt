## Title: US Nimitz Carrier Strike Group in Colombo
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 29 October 2017 10:55 am

US Nimitz Carrier Strike Group in Colombo


	



The United States’ Nimitz Carrier Strike Group, which comprises the oldest American aircraft carrier in active service, USS Nimitz, the cruiser USS Princeton, and destroyers USS Howard, USS Shoup, USS Pinckney, and USS Kidd, arrived at the port of Colombo yesterday.
