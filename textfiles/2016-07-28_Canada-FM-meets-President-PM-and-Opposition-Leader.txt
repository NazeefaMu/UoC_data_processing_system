## Title: Canada FM meets President, PM, and Opposition Leader
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 28 July 2016 11:45 am

Canada FM meets President, PM, and Opposition Leader


	



Visiting Canadian Minister of Foreign Affairs Stéphane Dion called on Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe at Temple Trees today. During the visit Stéphane met President Maithripala Sirisena, Foreign Minister Mangala Samaraweera and Opposition Leader R. Sampanthan. This visit to Sri Lanka by a Canadian Foreign Minister comes after 13 years and in less than a year after the formation of Prime Minister Justin Trudeau’s Government.
