## Title: PM in Deniyaya
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 5 November 2017 06:20 pm

PM in Deniyaya


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe participated at a ceremony held in Deniyaya to distribute lands to the landless in the area yesterday. Over 2000 families were given land deeds at the ceremony. Minister of Lands Gayantha Karunatilake and Minister of Law and Order Sagala Ratnayake also participated at the event. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
