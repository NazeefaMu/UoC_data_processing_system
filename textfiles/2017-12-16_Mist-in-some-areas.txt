## Title: Mist in some areas
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 December 2017 09:15 am

Mist in some areas


	

Mist is likely at some places in the Western, North-Western, Sabaragamuwa and Central Provinces during the morning, the Department of Meteorology said yesterday.

Duty Meteorologist Janaka Kumara said could be brought about by rainfall at night making the atmosphere cooler.

He said rains were likely in the Northern, North-Central, Eastern and Uva Provinces with fairly heavy rains of about 75mm accompanied by strong winds of up to 50kmph at some places in the Western, Northern, North-Central, Central and the Sabaragamuwa Provinces and in the Galle and Matara districts. (Sheain Fernandopulle)
