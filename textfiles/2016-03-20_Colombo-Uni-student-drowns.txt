## Title: Colombo Uni. student drowns
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 20 March 2016 09:24 am

Colombo Uni. student drowns


	

A final year student of the Management Faculty of the Colombo University had been reported missing since last night, while bathing at the Kalu Ganga river in Anguruwathota, the Police said.

They said a group of students of the University had gone for a bath in the river, when the 24-year-old Damith Perera, a resident of Ambalangoda, had gone missing.

The divers from the Navy are searching with the assistance of the Police and residents.
