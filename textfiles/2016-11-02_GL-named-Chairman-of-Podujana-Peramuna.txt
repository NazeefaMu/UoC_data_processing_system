## Title: GL named Chairman of Podujana Peramuna
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 2 November 2016 12:36 pm

GL named Chairman of Podujana Peramuna


	

The political party  Ape Sri Lanka Nidahas Peramuna  had been renamed as Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna and Prof. G.L. Peiris was named as its new Chairman, sources said.

They said that the Elections Department had accepted the new name and the party would be represented by a symbol of a Flower Bud.
