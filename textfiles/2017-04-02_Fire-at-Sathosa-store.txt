## Title: Fire at Sathosa store
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 2 April 2017 06:05 pm

Fire at Sathosa store


	



A fire had broken out at the Sathosa store at the Narahenpita Economic Centre this evening. Colombo Fire brigade was deployed to the scene to douse the fire. Pix by Nisal Badhuge
