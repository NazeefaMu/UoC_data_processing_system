## Title: PM Chief Guest at golden jubilee celebrations
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 27 May 2016 07:31 pm

PM Chief Guest at golden jubilee celebrations


	

 



Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe was the Chief Guest at the golden jubilee celebrations of Sri Lanka Tourism. UNWTO’s Operational Programmes and Institutional Relations Executive Director Marcio Favilla was the guest of honourat Friday’s event. (Pix by Kushan Pathiraja)
