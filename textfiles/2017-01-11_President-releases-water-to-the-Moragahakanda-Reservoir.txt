## Title: President releases water to the Moragahakanda Reservoir
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 January 2017 11:34 am

President releases water to the Moragahakanda Reservoir


	

President Maithripala Sirisena inaugurated the filling of water to the Moragahakanda Reservoir this morning.

The Moragahakanda project is the biggest multi-functional irrigation project.
