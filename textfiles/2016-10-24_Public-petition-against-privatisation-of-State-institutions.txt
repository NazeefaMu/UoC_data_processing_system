## Title: Public petition against privatisation of State institutions
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 24 October 2016 03:44 pm

Public petition against privatisation of State institutions


	



The inauguration of signing a public petition demanding to stop the privatisation of State Owned Enterprises (SOEs) was held today in front of the Pettah Bodhi Tree in Colombo.

The signing campaign will be held in several districts and the petition will later be presented to President Maithripala Sirisena, according to organisers. Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
