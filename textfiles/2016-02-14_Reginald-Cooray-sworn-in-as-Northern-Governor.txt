## Title: Reginald Cooray sworn in as Northern Governor
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 14 February 2016 12:48 pm

Reginald Cooray sworn in as Northern Governor


	

Former Minister Reginald Cooray was sworn in as the Governor of the Northern Province before President Maithripala Sirisena, a short while ago, the presidential office said.
