## Title: Police shoot at vehicle in Pannipitiya
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 July 2016 11:36 am

Police shoot at vehicle in Pannipitiya


	

Police opened fire at a speeding vehicle, which was believed to be smuggling drugs, at Pannipitiya this morning.

The driver of the vehicle had ignored directions by police officers to pull over, which had prompted the officers to take action.

Police said the car had fled the scene amidst gunshots. No injuries have been reported and no arrests have been made as yet.
