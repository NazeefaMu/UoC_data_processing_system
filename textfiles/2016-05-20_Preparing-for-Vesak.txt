## Title: Preparing for Vesak
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 20 May 2016 11:56 am

Preparing for Vesak


	


A kid is seen hanging Vesak lanterns at a temporarily built hut in Colombo.  Even with heavy floods, people were seen getting ready for Vesak. Vendors have put up lanterns, Buddhist flags and other decorations at display at their shops. Pix by Nisal Baduge
