## Title: Ven. Brahmanawatte Seevali Thera passes away
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 April 2017 11:24 am

Ven. Brahmanawatte Seevali Thera passes away


	

Lekakadhikari Thera of the Sri Lanka Amarapura Maha Sanga Sabha, Venerable Brahmanawatte Seevali Thera has passed away at the age of 82 today.
