## Title: STF brought in to control tense situ. in Batticaloa
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 December 2016 01:00 pm

STF brought in to control tense situ. in Batticaloa


	

The Special Task Force (STF) has been deployed to areas of Batticaloa to maintain law and order in the areas following a clash between two parties yesterday, Batticaloa Police said.

Police said the clash had triggered following a heated argument among the two parties.

It also said the situation had been brought back to normal.
