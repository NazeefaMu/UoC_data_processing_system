## Title: France wins 2018 FIFA world cup
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 July 2018 10:32 pm

France wins 2018 FIFA world cup


	

France won the 2018 FIFA World Cup by beating Croatia in the final played today at the Luzhniki Stadium, Moscow, Russia, the Fédération Internationale de Football Association (FIFA) tweeted a short while ago.

The French football team scored four goals against the two scored by the Croatian opponents.
