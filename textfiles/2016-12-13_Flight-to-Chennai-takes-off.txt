## Title: Flight to Chennai takes off
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 13 December 2016 11:30 am

Flight to Chennai takes off


	

The Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA) announced the takeoff its  first flight to Chennai a short while ago after some of the flights were suspended because of the Cyclone ‘Vardah’.

The BIA Duty Manager the SriLankan airlines flight UL129 was scheduled to leave for Chennai at 7.20 a.m. but finally left at 11.26 a.m. 

“The flight took off after we received clearance from the Chennai International Airport,” he said adding that five flights were scheduled to leave for Chennai today.

He requested passengers to contact SriLankan on No: 1979 for flight details. (Chaturanga Pradeep and T.K.G. Kapila)
