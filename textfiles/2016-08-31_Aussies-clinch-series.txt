## Title: Aussies clinch series
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 31 August 2016 09:24 pm

Aussies clinch series


	



Australia beat Sri Lanka by six wickets to win the fourth One Day International and win the series with a game to spare. Seen here are the Australian batsmen walking off the field after winning the match. Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
