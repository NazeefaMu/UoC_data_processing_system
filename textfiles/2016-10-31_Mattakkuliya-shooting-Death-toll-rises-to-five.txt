## Title: Mattakkuliya shooting: Death toll rises to five
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 31 October 2016 08:57 am

Mattakkuliya shooting: Death toll rises to five


	

The death toll for the Mattakkuliya shooting has risen to five with an individual who was undergoing treatment at the Colombo National Hospital succumbing to his injuries today.

Four persons were killed in the shooting and three others were admitted to the hospital on October 23.

The Police arrested eleven suspects including the main suspect 'Kudu Roshan' in Ratnapura for the incident.
