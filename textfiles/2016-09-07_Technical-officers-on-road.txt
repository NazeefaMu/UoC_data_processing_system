## Title: Technical officers on road
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 7 September 2016 01:48 pm

Technical officers on road


	



The Government Technical Officers Union today launched a protest march in Pettah and the march was heading towards the Presidential Secretariat. The Union is demanding a solution to six main issues related to their profession. Pix by Damith Wickramasinghe
