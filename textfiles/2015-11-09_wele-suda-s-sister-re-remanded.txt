## Title: Video: Wele suda’s sister re-remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 November 2015 08:00 am

Video: Wele suda’s sister re-remanded


	

Drug kingpin Samantha Kumara alias Wele Suda’s sister Dilini Nilmini and her two accomplices were ordered to be further remanded till November 23 by Maligakanda Magistrate Dulani Jayasekara today for possessing 111 grams of heroin worth more than Rs.1.7 million.
The Police told the Magistrate that the report on the seized heroin had yet not received from the Government Analyst Department and investigations were underway upon the information revealed from the suspects. (T. Farook Thajudeen and Shehan Chamika Silva)
