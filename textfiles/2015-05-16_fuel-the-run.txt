## Title: ‘Fuel the Run’
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 16 May 2015 04:35 am

‘Fuel the Run’


	


‘Fuel the Run’ a marathon organized by Ministry of Power and Energy took off at Independence Square this morning with President Maithripala Sirisena as the chief guest of event. Power and Energy Minister Patali Champika Ranawaka completed the full marathon along with participants. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
