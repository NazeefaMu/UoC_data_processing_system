## Title: UNESCO DG Irina Bokova in SL
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 14 August 2016 11:08 am

UNESCO DG Irina Bokova in SL


	



United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) Director General, Irina Bokova was welcomed by Education Minister Akila Viraj Kariyawasam at the Bandaranaike International Airport yesterday. Bokova is on a three-day official visit.
