## Title: 31 injured in private bus accident
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 January 2015 04:03 pm

31 injured in private bus accident


	

At least 31 persons were injured following a head-on collision involving two buses in Pahala Naragala Wedage Watta junction on the Horana - Bulathsinhala Road this evening, Police said.
One of the private buses was heading from Horana to Bulathsinhala while the other was heading to Bulathsinhala from Horana at the time the accident took place.
The injured were admitted to the Horana Base Hospital and the drivers of the two buses were among the injured. (Chaturanga Pradeep)Pix by Buddhika Kumarasiri
