## Title: Vigneswaran welcomes Maithri to Jaffna
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 23 March 2015 11:34 am

Vigneswaran welcomes Maithri to Jaffna


	


President Maithripala Sirisena is seen warmly welcomed by Northern Province Chief Minister C.V. Vigneshwaran today when he arrived in Jaffna to release 425 acres which were under the army, to their original owners.
