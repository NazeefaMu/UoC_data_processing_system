## Title: UN Vesak Day celebrations concluded
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 14 May 2017 10:25 pm

UN Vesak Day celebrations concluded


	

The week long international UN Vesak Day celebrations concluded today in Kandy. Nepal President Bidhya Devi Bhandari was seen receiving a souvenir in honour of her participation for the event from President Maithripala Sirisena.  Representatives from 72 countries attended the event that lasted from May 7 to May 13.
