## Title: Wimal bailed out
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 7 April 2017 04:45 pm

Wimal bailed out


	


National Freedom Front Leader Wimal Weerawansa is seen outside the Colombo Fort Magistrate’s Court after he was granted bail. He was arrested and remanded for allegedly misusing 40 state vehicles belonging to the State Engineering Corporation causing a loss of Rs. 90 million.
