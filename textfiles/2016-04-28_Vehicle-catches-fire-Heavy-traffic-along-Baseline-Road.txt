## Title: Vehicle catches fire: Heavy traffic along Baseline Road
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 28 April 2016 07:06 pm

Vehicle catches fire: Heavy traffic along Baseline Road


	

Heavy traffic was reported along the Baseline Road as a vehicle caught fire on the Dematagoda flyover today, Police said.

They said a fire brigade team from Colombo had been sent to the location.
