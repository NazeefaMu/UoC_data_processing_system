## Title: Jackson Anthony at PRECIFAC
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 12 August 2016 10:37 am

Jackson Anthony at PRECIFAC


	

Veteran artiste Jackson Anthony and eight others arrived at the Presidential Commission of Inquiry to Investigate and Inquire into Serious Acts of Fraud and Corruption (PRECIFAC) a short while ago over financial irregularities connected to the inauguration ceremony of the Hambantota Port in 2010.
