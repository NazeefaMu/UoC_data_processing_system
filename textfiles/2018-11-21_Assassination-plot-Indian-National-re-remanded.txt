## Title: Assassination plot: Indian National re-remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 21 November 2018 03:53 pm

Assassination plot: Indian National re-remanded


	

The Indian National who was arrested over the alleged plot to assassinate President Maithripala Sirisena was re-remanded till December 5 by Colombo Fort Magistrate Ranga Dissanayake today.

Based on his medical reports, the CID informed the court that he was suffering from a mental illness.
