## Title: Man arrested while transporting 5kg of heroin
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 March 2016 12:11 pm

Man arrested while transporting 5kg of heroin


	

A man was arrested by the Police Narcotic Bureau on charges of transporting 5kgs of heroin in a three-wheeler in Welisara last night, Police said.


They said the arrested suspect, a resident of Colombo 14, would be produced in the Mahara Magistrate’s Court.
