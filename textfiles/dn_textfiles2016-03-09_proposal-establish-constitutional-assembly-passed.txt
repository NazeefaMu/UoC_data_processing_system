## Title: Proposal to establish a Constitutional assembly passed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Wednesday, March 9, 2016 - 18:45

Proposal to establish a Constitutional assembly passed

The proposal to establish a Constitutional assembly was passed unanimously in Parliament a short while ago. Earlier, Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe presented a resolution in Parliament appointing the constitutional assembly to draft a new constitution for Sri Lanka.
