## Title: Fmr Embilipitiya ASP remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 February 2016 11:01 am

Fmr Embilipitiya ASP remanded


	

Former Embilipitiya Police ASP D.W.C Dharmaratna who was arrested yesterday over the death of a youth was remanded until February 17.

The ASP was arrested by the CID yesterday and produced before the Embilipitiya Magistrate Court today.
