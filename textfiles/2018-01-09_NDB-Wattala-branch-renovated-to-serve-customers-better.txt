## Title: NDB Wattala branch renovated to serve customers better
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 9 January 2018 12:00 am

NDB Wattala branch renovated to serve customers better


	

From left: Regional Manager (North Western) Romesh Thisera, VP SME, Middle Market and Business Banking Indika Ranaweera, CEO/Director NDB Dimantha Seneviratne, VP Personal Banking and Branch Network Management Sanjaya Perera and Branch Manager (Wattala) Chaminda Perera

 

The National Development Bank PLC renovated and reopened its Wattala branch to a more spacious and a conveniently accessible location in a bid to provide a better service experience to people in the area. 


With the relocation of its branch, NDB renewed its profound banking relationships with the individuals and businessmen in the Wattala area, opening its doors to the aspiring entrepreneurs to benefit from the Bank’s many customized SME financing facilities and advisory services while inspiring individuals to reach their financial aspirations with ease. NDB pioneers in providing exceptional Micro Finance solutions for small scale entrepreneurs and gives constant empowerment to women and propel financial guidance to grow in their life. With its expertise and knowledge in SME financing, NDB will continue to offer a range of SME banking solutions including short term working capital loans, supplier and distributor finance and long term funding which will be beneficial to the traders, distributors as well as professionals and proprietors in the area. 


Supplemented by enhanced technology and the expertise of its staff members,  the re-opened  Wattala branch will continue to enrich the lives of its residents by providing a host of Retail Banking services; such as current and savings accounts, children’s savings accounts, NRFC/RFC accounts, fixed deposits, housing loans, education loans, leasing facilities, credit and debit cards, bancassurance, mobile banking as well as Western Union Money transfer services coupled with the convenience of Internet Banking and a Call Centre that operates 24 hours.
