## Title: Eight suspects re-remanded over Dematagoda abduction
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 January 2016 07:08 am

Eight suspects re-remanded over Dematagoda abduction


	

The eight suspects who were in remand custody on charges of allegedly abducting a person of a Dematagoda drapery store on December 21, were further remanded till January 6 by Colombo Magistrate’s Court today.
