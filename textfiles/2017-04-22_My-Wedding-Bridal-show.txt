## Title: ‘My Wedding’ Bridal show
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 22 April 2017 06:10 am

‘My Wedding’ Bridal show


	


“My Wedding” Bridal show got underway at the BMICH yesterday. The exhibition showcased bridal dresses, accessories, shoes and decorations. Hair dressing and bridal specialists exhibited their skills at the show which will continue till Sunday (23). Pix by Kithsiri De Mel
