## Title: Sampath Bank locates newest off-site ATM in Maharagama
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 27 June 2016 12:00 am

Sampath Bank locates newest off-site ATM in Maharagama


	



Sampath Bank opened their newest off-site ATM in Dehiwela Road, Maharagama. This new ATM is equipped with ATM features such as cash withdrawals, fund transfers, utility bill payments, Cardless Cash facility, Mobile Cash facility etc. Sampath Bank believes that this would add more convenience to their valued customers.
