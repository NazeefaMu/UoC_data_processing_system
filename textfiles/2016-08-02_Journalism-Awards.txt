## Title: Journalism Awards 2015
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 2 August 2016 10:38 pm

Journalism Awards 2015


	



Daily Mirror’s Piyumi Fonseka and Chandeepa Wettasinghe won awards at the Journalism Excellence Awards 2015 held at the Mount Lavinia Hotel this evening. Piyumi won Denzil Peiris Young Reporter of the Year and Chandeepa won Business Journalist of the Year. Pix by Waruna Wanniarachchi
