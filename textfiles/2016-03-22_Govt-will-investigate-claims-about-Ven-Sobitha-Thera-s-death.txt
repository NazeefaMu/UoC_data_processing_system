## Title: Govt. will investigate claims about Ven. Sobitha Thera’s death
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 March 2016 11:54 pm

Govt. will investigate claims about Ven. Sobitha Thera’s death


	

Fisheries Minister Mahinda Amaraweera said today the government would discuss about the rumours behind the death of Ven. Maduluwawe Sobitha Thera and do everything needed to reveal the facts.

“We will look into the claims by various people about the death of Ven. Sobitha Thera. As a government we have nothing to conceal. We will work openly and do what is needed to reveal everything,” he said.

Minister Amaraweera expressed these views while taking part in a health camp in Ambalantota today. (Ajith Siriwardana)
