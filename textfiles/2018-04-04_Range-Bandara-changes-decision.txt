## Title: Range Bandara changes decision
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 April 2018 05:21 pm

Range Bandara changes decision


	

State Ministers Palitha Range Bandara and Wasantha Senanayake today said they will vote against the No-Confidence Motion on Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe.

Earlier, State Minister Range Bandara said he would vote in favour of the No-Confidence Motion.

Addressing a news briefing, Minister Range Bandara said they decided to vote against the motion to safeguard the National Government.
