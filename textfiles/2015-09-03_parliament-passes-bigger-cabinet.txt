## Title: Parliament passes bigger Cabinet
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 September 2015 01:48 pm

Parliament passes bigger Cabinet


	


	A proposal to increase number of ministers in the Government has been passed in Parliament with 143 voting for and 16 against the motion.
	63 were absent.
	Parliament approved the increase of Cabinet ministers to 48 and non-cabinet to 45.
