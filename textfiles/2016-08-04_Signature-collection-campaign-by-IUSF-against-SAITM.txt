## Title: Signature collection campaign by IUSF against SAITM
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 August 2016 09:30 pm

Signature collection campaign by IUSF against SAITM


	



The Inter-University Students Federation (IUSF) today conducted a signature collection campaign in front of the Fort Railway Station urging the government to abolish the South Asian Institute of Technology and Medicine (SAITM) campus in Malabe. Two foreign tourists were among those seen signing the document. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
