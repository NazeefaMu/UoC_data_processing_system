## Title: President, PM at Premadasa commemoration
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 1 May 2016 12:18 pm

President, PM at Premadasa commemoration


	

The 23rd death anniversary of President Ranasinghe Premadasa falls today. President Maithripala Sirisena, Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe and the family members of late President Premadasa including his son Minister Sajith Premadasa are seen during the commemoration held at Hultsdorf in Colombo.
