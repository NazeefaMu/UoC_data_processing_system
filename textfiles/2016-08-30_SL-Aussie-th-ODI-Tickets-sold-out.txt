## Title: SL-Aussie 4th ODI: Tickets sold out
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 August 2016 08:06 pm

SL-Aussie 4th ODI: Tickets sold out


	

The Sri Lanka Cricket (SLC) today announced that all tickets for tomorrow’s 4th ODI between Sri Lanka and Australia in Rangiri Dambulla International Stadium had been sold out.

It requested the fans not to gather at the stadium gates as no ticket would be sold at the gates.  

“No tickets will be sold at the venue on the day of the match. Please purchase your tickets from the relevant counters or online prior to the match to avoid inconvenience,” the SLC said in a statement.
