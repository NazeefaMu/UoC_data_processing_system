## Title: Sarath Kumara Gunaratne released on bail
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 20 November 2015 07:54 am

Sarath Kumara Gunaratne released on bail


	

Former Deputy Minister Sarath Kumara Gunaratne who appeared before Negombo Magistrate’s Court today over allegedly threatening a police officer, was released on two sureties of Rs. 500,000 by Chief Magistrate Poornima Paranagamage.
Mr. Gunaratne was noticed by the court over allegedly threatening and obstructing a police officer attached to the Negombo Police during the previous parliamentary election.  (Tony Karunanayake)
