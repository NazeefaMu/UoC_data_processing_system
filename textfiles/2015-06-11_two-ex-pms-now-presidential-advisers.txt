## Title: Two ex-PMs now presidential advisers
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 June 2015 07:07 am

Two ex-PMs now presidential advisers


	

Former Prime Ministers D.M. Jayaratne and Ratnasiri Wickremanayake have been appointed senior advisors to President Maithripala Sirisena on political matters, the Government Information Department said today.
