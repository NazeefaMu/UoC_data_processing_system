## Title: Keheliya discharged from ITN controversy
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 December 2015 10:40 am

Keheliya discharged from ITN controversy


	

The PRECIFAC said today that former minister and MP Keheliya Rambukwella was discharged from alleged charges over ITN advertisement issue as there was no evidence against him.
The PRECIFAC is currently investigating into the allegations on the alleged non-payment of money to the Independent Television Network (ITN) during the last Presidential campaign.
