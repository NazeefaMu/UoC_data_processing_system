## Title: ‘Pibidemu Polonnaruwa’
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 17 January 2016 02:12 pm

‘Pibidemu Polonnaruwa’


	


Road development projects under ‘Pibidemu Polonnaruwa’ five-year district development programme was launched today by President Maithripala Sirisena.
In parallel to the programme, title deeds were offered to land owners in Thamankaduwa Provincial Divison.
Higher Education Minister Lakshman Kiriella, State Minister Dilan Perera, North-Central Province Chief Minister Peshala Jayarathne and some other parliamentarians participated in the launch of the development programme. (Photos - Presidential Media Unit)
