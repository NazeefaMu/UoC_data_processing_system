## Title: PB quizzed by FCID
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 31 March 2015 05:02 am

PB quizzed by FCID


	

P.B. Jayasundera, the former Secretary of the Finance Ministry is being questioned by the police Financial Crime Investigation Division (FCID) over the Hedging deal in which the government lost 200 million US dollars, Police spokesman ASP Ruwan Gunasekara said. (DS)
