## Title: Video: Health is wealth
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 6 September 2016 12:24 pm

Video: Health is wealth


	



President Maithripala Sirisena, Health Minister Rajitha Senaratne, WHO General Director Margaret Chan and WHO South East Asia Director General Poonam Singh participated in a fitness programme held at the premises of Galleface Hotel this morning.

The programme was held parallel to the 69th session of the World Health Organization’s (WHO) South East Asian Regional Committee.
