## Title: SL President meets Pakistan President
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 24 March 2018 09:37 pm

SL President meets Pakistan President


	

President Maithripala Sirisena who is on an official visit to Pakistan met with Pakistani President Mamnoon Hussain today. Here President Sirisena posed for a photograph with the children and grandchildren of Pakistani President Mamnoon Hussain. (Photo courtesy President’s Media)
