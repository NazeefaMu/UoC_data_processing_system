## Title: Dasa Sil Matha on hunger strike against new Constitution
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 January 2017 10:14 pm

Dasa Sil Matha on hunger strike against new Constitution


	

A Dasa Sil Matha is seen sitting in front of the historic Dalada Maligawa in Kandy where she has embarked on a non-stop hunger strike saying that the present government is trying to divide the country by amending the Constitution.

She has opted to remain anonymous and will continue to protest against the high-handed actions of the government. (J.A.L. Jayasinghe)
