## Title: Navy to release 100 acres in Mullikulam
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 April 2017 02:16 pm

Navy to release 100 acres in Mullikulam


	

Navy Commander Ravindra Wijegunaratne has assured that the Navy would immediately release about 100 acres of land to the people in Mullikulam.

He gave this assurance during a cordial discussion with those residing in Mullikulam whilst on an inspection visit to the North-Western Naval Area on Saturday.
