## Title: Thilakaratne new CAA Chairman
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 20 November 2015 05:22 am

Thilakaratne new CAA Chairman


	

Former CEO of Laksala, Hasitha Thilakaratne has been appointed as the new Chairman of the Consumer Affairs Authority (CAA), government sources said.
Mr. Thilakaratne had also served as the Chairman of the National Gem and Jewellery Authority.
Previous Chairman of the CAA Dr. R.M. K. Ratnayake resigned last week.
