## Title: SLFP appoints two new electoral organisers
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 August 2016 12:59 pm

SLFP appoints two new electoral organisers


	

Deputy Minister Anuradha Jayaratne was appointed as Sri Lanka Freedom Party (SLFP) Ududumbara Organiser while Central Province Chief Minister Sarath Ekanayake was appointed as SLFP Yatinuwara Organiser by President Maithipala Sirisena a short while ago.
