## Title: Lasantha killing: MIU officer re-remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 29 September 2016 01:55 pm

Lasantha killing: MIU officer re-remanded


	

The Military Intelligence Unit (MIU) officer, remanded in connection with the killing of journalist Lasantha Wickramatunga, was today re-remanded till October 13 by the Mount Lavinia Magistrate.
