## Title: Will appoint new CB Governor: President
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 29 June 2016 12:05 pm

Will appoint new CB Governor: President


	

President Maithripala Sirisena said today that he had decided to appoint a new Governor to the Central Bank within a few hours.

He said this at the Kidney disease prevention programme held in Giradurukotte, Badulla this morning.
