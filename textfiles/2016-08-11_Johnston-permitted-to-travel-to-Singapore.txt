## Title: Johnston permitted to travel to Singapore
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 August 2016 12:18 pm

Johnston permitted to travel to Singapore


	

Colombo Chief Magistrate Gihan Pilapitiya today permitted former minister Johnston Fernando to travel to Singapore for medical treatment for his wife.

His passport had been impounded in a case filed by the Bribery Commission alleging that he had not declared his assets and liabilities.

The Court directed him to return to Sri Lanka by September 6. (Farook Thajudeen)
