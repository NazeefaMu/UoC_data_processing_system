## Title: Update: Kelani river water level drops by 4ft
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 May 2016 09:57 am

Update: Kelani river water level drops by 4ft


	

The Water level in the Kelani River in Nagalagam Street had decreased by three feet, the Disaster Management Centre said today.

The DMC said the water level keeps falling but warned the residents alongside the river to be alert.  

The water level in the Kelani River, Nagalagama Street had dropped to 3.6 feet as of today. It rose to seven feet resulting in heavy floods in Colombo last week.
