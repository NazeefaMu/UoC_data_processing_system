## Title: Two men stabbed to death in Pannala
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 14 April 2017 10:20 am

Two men stabbed to death in Pannala


	

Two men aged 30 and 32 were found stabbed to death at Elibichchiya in Pannala last night, Police said. A neighbour of the victims was arrested on charges of the killings. 

Police investigations revealed a personal dispute had let to the crime. The arrested suspect is to be produced in Kuliyapitiya Magistrate's court on Friday.
