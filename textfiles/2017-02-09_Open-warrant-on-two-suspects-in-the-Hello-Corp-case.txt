## Title: Open warrant on two suspects in the Hello Corp case
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 February 2017 12:05 pm

Open warrant on two suspects in the Hello Corp case


	

Colombo Additional Magistrate Jeyaram Trotsky today issued an open warrant through Interpol for the arrest of two suspects absconding Court in the inquiry against Namal Rajapaksa and five others under the Money Laundering Act in connection with the purchase of shares in Hello Corp.

The two suspects absconding court have been identified as Indika Prabath Karunajeewa and Iresha Silva said to be living abroad.

Meanwhile, the FCID told today court that the Attorney General had advisedit to indict the suspects in the High Court. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
