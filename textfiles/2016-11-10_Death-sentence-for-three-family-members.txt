## Title: Death sentence for three family members
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 10 November 2016 04:04 pm

Death sentence for three family members


	

Three members of a family, found guilty of murdering a resident of Kegalle in 2005, were today sentenced to death by Kegalle Chief Magistrate Dhammika Ganepola.
