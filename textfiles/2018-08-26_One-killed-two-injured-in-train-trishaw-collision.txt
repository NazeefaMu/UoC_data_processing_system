## Title: One killed, two injured in train-trishaw collision
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 August 2018 08:50 am

One killed, two injured in train-trishaw collision


	

A 34-year-old was killed and another two injured when a three-wheeler collided with a Colombo bound train at a railway crossing at Hamson Town near the Wanawasala Railway Station in Peliyagoda this morning.

Police said the injured were admitted to the Colombo National Hospital.
