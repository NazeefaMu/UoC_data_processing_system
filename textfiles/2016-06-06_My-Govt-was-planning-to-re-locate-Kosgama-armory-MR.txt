## Title: My Govt.was planning to re-locate Kosgama armory: MR
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 June 2016 03:27 pm

My Govt.was planning to re-locate Kosgama armory: MR


	

Former president and Kurunegala District MP Mahinda Rajapaksa said today his government was in the process of re-locating the armory of the Kosgama Army Camp.

He told the reporters that he could not make a statement as the Former President regarding the incident because all the facts should be concerned before making a statement as it is a matter of national security.
