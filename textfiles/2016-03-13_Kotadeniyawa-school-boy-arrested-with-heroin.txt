## Title: Kotadeniyawa school boy arrested with heroin
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 13 March 2016 11:58 am

Kotadeniyawa school boy arrested with heroin


	

Police on Friday arrested the school boy acquitted of Seya Sadewmi’s murder and two others for the possession of heroin.

The suspects were arrested in a house in Divulapitiya with 62 mg of heroin in their possession. Police said the suspects were residents of Divulapitiya, Badalgama and Hunumulla.
