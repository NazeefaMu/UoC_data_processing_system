## Title: India celebrates 68th Republic Day
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 26 January 2017 11:55 am

India celebrates 68th Republic Day


	



Indian nationals in Sri Lanka celebrated the 68th Republic Day of India at the residence of the Indian High Commissioner in Colombo today. The event was held under the patronage of the High Commissioner, Taranjit Singh Sandhu. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
