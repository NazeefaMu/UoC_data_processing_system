## Title: Senadipathi comes before PRECIFAC
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 January 2016 05:20 am

Senadipathi comes before PRECIFAC


	


	The chairman of the Avant Garde Company, Nissanka Senadipathi has arrived at the PRECIFAC this morning to give a statement in connection with the  alleged purchase of weapons from the Rakna Lanka Security Company.
