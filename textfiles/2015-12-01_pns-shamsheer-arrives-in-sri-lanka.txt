## Title: PNS Shamsheer arrives in Sri Lanka
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 1 December 2015 06:10 pm

PNS Shamsheer arrives in Sri Lanka


	


High Commissioner of Pakistan in Sri Lanka Maj Gen (R) Syed Shakeel Hussain and Sri Lanka Navy Chief Ravi Wijeyagunawardena welcomed Ship Captain Rizwan Kahlid today. This was after the Pakistan Naval Ship PNS Shamsheer arrived at the Colombo Port for a 4-day goodwill visit. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
