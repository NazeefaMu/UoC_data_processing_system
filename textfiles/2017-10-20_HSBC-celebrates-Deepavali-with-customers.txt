## Title: HSBC celebrates Deepavali with customers
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 20 October 2017 11:17 am

HSBC celebrates Deepavali with customers


	



HSBC Sri Lanka celebrated Deepavali, the festival of lights with customers and staff at HSBC branches in Wellawatte, Galle, Jaffna and Kandy recently.


 In keeping with tradition, Mark Prothero CEO of HSBC Sri Lanka and the Maldives together with senior management from Retail Banking and Wealth Management joined customers and staff at the HSBC branch in Wellawatte, where the bank’s annual Deepavali celebrations kicked off, to decorate colourful kolams.  Customers who were the special invitees at these branches, were also treated to a traditional festive feast.
