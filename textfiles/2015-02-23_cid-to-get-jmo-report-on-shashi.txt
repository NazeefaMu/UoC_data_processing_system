## Title: CID to get JMO report on Shashi
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 February 2015 07:08 am

CID to get JMO report on Shashi


	

Colombo Chief Magistrate Gihan Pilapitiya today permitted the CID to obtain a report from the Judicial Medical officer on the health condition of Shashi Weerawansa, who was earlier arrested on charges of obtaining a diplomatic passport by producing fraudulent documents.
The CID, in a motion filed in Court sought permission to obtain a medical report on former minister Wimal Weerawansa’s wife Sashi who had admitted herself to a private hospital in Malabe.  (TFT)
