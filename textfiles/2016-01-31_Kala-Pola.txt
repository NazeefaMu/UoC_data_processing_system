## Title: ‘Kala Pola’
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 31 January 2016 09:34 pm

‘Kala Pola’


	




Foreigners and locals were seen  in Sri Lanka’s biggest annual open air art fair for paintings and sculpture, the ‘Kala Pola’ which held along the sidewalks of Ananda Coomaraswamy Mawatha, Colombo 7 today.


The ‘Kala Pola’ was organized by the George Keyt Foundation and John Keels Holdings PLC and the event graced by German Ambassador to Sri Lanka and the Maldives Jurgen Morhard. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
