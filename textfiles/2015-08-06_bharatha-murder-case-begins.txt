## Title: Trial-at-bar on Bharatha murder case begins
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 6 August 2015 10:33 am

Trial-at-bar on Bharatha murder case begins


	


The trial-at-bar against Duminda Silva and 12 other accused came up in the Colombo High Court today. Hirunika Premachandra, the daughter of slain politician Bharatha Lakshman Premachandra was also present in the court premises. (Pix by Kushan Pathiraja)
