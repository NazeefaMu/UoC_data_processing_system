## Title: CBSL announces 25 basis points to lending policy
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 April 2018 09:17 pm

CBSL announces 25 basis points to lending policy


	


The Central Bank today announced 25 basis points to its lending policy rate in a press conference held in Colombo under the patronage of CBSL Governor Dr. Indrajit Coomaraswamy. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
