## Title: To oil the wheels?
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 8 July 2016 04:11 pm

To oil the wheels?


	



Ayurveda Expo 2016, the international indigenous healthcare exhibition and symposium organized by the National Chamber of Commerce in association with the Health and Indigenous Medicine Ministry was launched by President Maithripala Sirisena at the BMICH today. President and Health Minister Rajitha Senaratne are seen inspecting some of the bottled virgin coconut oil put on sale at the exhibition.
