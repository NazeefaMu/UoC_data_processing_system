## Title: Closing ceremony of the ‘Jathika Yowun Puraya’
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 1 April 2018 06:46 am

Closing ceremony of the ‘Jathika Yowun Puraya’


	



President Maithripala Sirisena and Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe attended the closing  ceremony of the ‘Jathika Yowun Puraya 2018’ was held at the Livestock Development Centre in Nikaweratiya, yesterday.  Nearly 7000 youth from all over the country joined for this camp and the youths from foreign countries too attended this event.
