## Title: Sangha takes up housemaid's case
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 2 December 2015 12:32 pm

Sangha takes up housemaid's case


	


The National Sangha Council today handed over a letter to the Ambassador of Saudi Arabia in Colombo urging the country to review the death sentence by stoning of a Sri Lankan woman in Saudi Arabia. The monks urged the ambassador to soften the capital punishment. Pix by Nisal Baduge
