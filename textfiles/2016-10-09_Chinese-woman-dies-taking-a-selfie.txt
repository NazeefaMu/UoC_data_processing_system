## Title: Chinese woman dies taking a selfie
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 October 2016 11:22 am

Chinese woman dies taking a selfie


	

A 25-year-old Chinese woman was killed when she was thrown out of the Colombo bound train from Ambalangoda, Galle this morning.

Police said the woman was taking a selfie on the foot board of the train as it reached the Madampagama station in Ambalangoda when the accident occurred.  (Anuradha Priyadarshana and D.G.Sugathapala)
