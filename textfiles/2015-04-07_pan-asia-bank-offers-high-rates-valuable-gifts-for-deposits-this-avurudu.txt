## Title: Pan Asia Bank offers high rates, valuable gifts for deposits this Avurudu
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 7 April 2015 03:53 am

Pan Asia Bank offers high rates, valuable gifts for deposits this Avurudu


	

Sinhala & Tamil New Year is an event that brings Sri Lankans from all walks of life together where they look forward to the auspicious times to spruce up their homes and give gifts to their loved ones. 
To share the spirit of this festive season Pan Asia Bank, has come up with a promotion where a range of valuable gifts are to be given away to its customers this Avurudu season when they make a deposit with the bank. 
This promotion which is valid from 1st of April to 30th April will offer free rice cookers , steam irons and traveling bags to customers who open a Fixed Deposit or make a deposit to a Savings account or a children’s savings account (conditions apply). Uniqueness about this promotion is that, in addition to these valuable gifts, customers will also receive an attractive interest rate on their deposits.
Going an extra mile this Avurudu season, the bank has added another reason for its customers to enjoy the season by offering gifts for every Western Union transaction made at any of its branches island wide. This is a special promotion for all western Union money transfers performed at all Pan Asia Bank branches island wide throughout the month of April or until stocks last.  
There is no better time than Avurudu for families to start invest for their future. So drop into Pan Asia Bank for Avurudu Ganu Denu this year where the branches will be opened even on 14th April from 3.00 p.m. To 5.00 p.m. for Avurudu Ganu Denu.
Pan Asia Bank has been in operation for 19 years and possesses a network of 78 branches and offers a full range of Retail, SME and Corporate Banking products and services. 
Pan Asia Bank’s strong performance and stability is underscored by the BBB rating by Fitch Ratings Lanka Limited and BBB+ by Lanka Rating Agency. Pan Asia Bank was also recently adjudged as the fastest growing commercial bank in Sri Lanka in 2014 by United Kingdom’s Global Banking and Finance Review.
