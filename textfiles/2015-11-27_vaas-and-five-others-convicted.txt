## Title: Vaas and five others convicted
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 27 November 2015 03:57 pm

Vaas and five others convicted


	


Former DIG Vaas Gunawardena, son Ravindu and four police officers were sentenced to death by Trial–at–Bar Judges Lalith Jayasuriya, Amendra Senaviratne and Kusala Sarojini Weerawardena at Colombo High Court today. Pictures show the convicted are being escorted to the prison from the court after the judgment was given. Pix by Waruna Wanniarachchi
