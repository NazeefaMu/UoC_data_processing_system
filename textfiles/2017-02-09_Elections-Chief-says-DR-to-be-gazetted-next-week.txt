## Title: Elections Chief says DR to be gazetted next week
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 February 2017 07:26 am

Elections Chief says DR to be gazetted next week


	

Elections Commission Chairman Mahinda Deshapriya said today they needed 90 days to conduct the elections from the date of the gazette notification after any technical defects were rectified.


He said the delimitation report (DR) would be gazetted by next Wednesday.


He said this after meeting Local Government and Provincial Councils Minister Faizer Mustapha in connection with the appeals submitted on the delimitation report.
