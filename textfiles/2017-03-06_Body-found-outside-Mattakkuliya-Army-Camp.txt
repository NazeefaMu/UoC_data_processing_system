## Title: Body found outside Mattakkuliya Army Camp
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 March 2017 02:13 pm

Body found outside Mattakkuliya Army Camp


	

The body of an unidentified man was found outside the Mattakkuliya Army Camp down Ferguson Road yesterday, Police said.

It is believed the victim was between 55-60-years-old. The body has been sent to the Colombo National Hospital, Police said.
