## Title: Saudi fire: No Lankan casualties
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 December 2015 09:58 am

Saudi fire: No Lankan casualties


	

The Sri Lankan Mission in Saudi Arabia has not informed the Foreign Ministry about any Sri Lankans being injured in the fire which erupted at a hospital in Southern Saudi Arabia in the early hours of today, Ministry Spokesperson Mahishini Collonne said.
She said the Sri Lankan mission is in touch with the ministry but had not reported of any Sri Lankan casualties in the fire.
