## Title: Handicraft by senior citizens
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 July 2016 10:38 am

Handicraft by senior citizens


	



In a bid to achieve self empowerment members of the St. Anthony’s Senior Citizens Association are producing handicraft using environment friendly oil paper. The programme was established for senior citizens to gain some independence and employment. Pix by Hiran Priyankara Jayasinghe
