## Title: Speaker awarded “Vishwa Kirthi Sri Lanka Janaranjana”
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 28 March 2017 06:03 pm

Speaker awarded “Vishwa Kirthi Sri Lanka Janaranjana”


	



Speaker Karu Jayasuriya was conferred the Honorary Title “Vishwa Kirthi Sri Lanka Janaranjana” by the Sri Lanka Ramanna Maha Nikaya.

It was in recognition of his distinguished services rendered as an illustrious citizen and an internationally respected statesman and the meritorious work he has done to the Sambuddha Sasana especially his untiring dedication and devotion to the Ramanna Maha Nikaya.

The Speaker received the 'Aktha Patra' from Most Venerable Agga Maha Panditha Napana Pemasiri Thera today. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
