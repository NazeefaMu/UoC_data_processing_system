## Title: PRECIFAC announces deadline to submit complaints
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 March 2016 04:40 pm

PRECIFAC announces deadline to submit complaints


	

The time given by the Presidential Commission to the public to submit their complaints and petitions regarding inquiries into Serious Acts of Fraud, Corruption and Abuse of Power, State Resources and Privileges (PRECIFAC) will end on March 24, 2016.

Commission Secretary H.W. Gunadasa requested the public to submit their complaints, petitions and information to the Commission before the deadline.
