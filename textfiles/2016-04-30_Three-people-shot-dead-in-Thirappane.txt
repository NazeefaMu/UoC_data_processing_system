## Title: Three people shot dead in Thirappane
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 April 2016 04:20 pm

Three people shot dead in Thirappane


	

Three people were shot dead inside a car at Thirappane in Anuradhapura this afternoon, police said.

They said the bodies were inside a car by the roadside.

Thirappane police are investigating.
