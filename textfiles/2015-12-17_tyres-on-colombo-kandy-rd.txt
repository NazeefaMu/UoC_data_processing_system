## Title: Protesters set fire to tyres on Colombo-Kandy Rd
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 17 December 2015 09:03 am

Protesters set fire to tyres on Colombo-Kandy Rd


	


A group of SLTB bus conductors and drivers today staged a demonstration blocking the Colombo-Kandy Road against an incident where a bus driver had been allegedly assaulted by former Provincial Council Opposition Leader Anura Manchanayake. Pix by Rohana Kumara
