## Title: Sabaragamuwa CM escort squad driver remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 July 2015 07:16 am

Sabaragamuwa CM escort squad driver remanded


	


	The driver of the escort squad of the Sabaragamuwa Chief Minister, who was arrested yesterday allegedly with 3 Kgs of ganja in the vehicle, had been remanded till July 22, the Police said.
	Excise Department officials recovered the ganja from the vehicle of the escort squad during a raid at Atthanagoda.
	The suspect was remanded after being produced in the Kegalle Magistrate’s Court.
