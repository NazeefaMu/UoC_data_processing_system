## Title: Businessman shot dead in Tangalle
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 September 2018 01:32 pm

Businessman shot dead in Tangalle


	

A 42-year-old businessman was killed when two men arrived on a  motorcycle opened fire at him  at Wadigala in Tangalle this morning.

Police said the victim who was an owner of a vehicle service centre, was died after admission to the Tangalle Hospital.
