## Title: Killing of businessman’s daughter --Suspect surrendered
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 June 2017 09:11 pm

Killing of businessman’s daughter --Suspect surrendered


	

The man who is alleged to have killed the eldest daughter of a billionaire businessman in Kottawa last Thursday was remanded till July 4 after he surrendered to the Police and was produced in court..

Police said the suspect is a resident in Badabadda in Hakmana.

The body of 26-year-old Tharindi Aloka, the eldest daughter of the businessman, was found hacked to death and lying in her bedroom in her father’s three-storey house.

The police are continuing investigations. (Thilanka Kanakarathna)
