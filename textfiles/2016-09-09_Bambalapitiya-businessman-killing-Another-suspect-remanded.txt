## Title: Bambalapitiya businessman killing: Another suspect remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 September 2016 11:05 am

Bambalapitiya businessman killing: Another suspect remanded


	

Another suspect, who was arrested by the CCD in connection with the alleged abduction and killing of a Bambalapitiya businessman, was today ordered to be remanded till September 15 by Colombo Additional Magistrate Nishantha Peiris.

Nine suspects are in remand custody over the incident. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
