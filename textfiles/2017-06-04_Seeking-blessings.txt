## Title: Seeking blessings
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 June 2017 11:57 pm

Seeking blessings


	

President Maithripala Sirisena today observed a religious ritual during a Bodhi Pooja conducted by the Chief Incumbent of Gangarama Temple Ven. Galaboda Gnanissara Thera to bestow blessings on Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe, who went abroad for medical reasons. Pix by Nisal Baduge
