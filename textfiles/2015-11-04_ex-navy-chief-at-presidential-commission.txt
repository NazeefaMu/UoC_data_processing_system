## Title: Ex-Navy Chief at Presidential Commission
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 November 2015 06:31 am

Ex-Navy Chief at Presidential Commission


	


	Presidential Commission of Inquiry to Investigate and Inquire into Serious Acts of Fraud, Corruption and Abuse of Power, State Resources and Privileges (PRECIFAC) currently recording a statement from navy commander Somathilake Dissanayake on the controversy with regard to the floating armoury belonging to Rakna Arakshaka Lanka Ltd.
	A senior official of the commission confirmed that the former navy commander was summoned to clarify certain matters that had arisen with regard to the evidence recorded earlier in this connection.
