## Title: Exams Timetable for 2016
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 January 2016 03:33 pm

Exams Timetable for 2016


	

The timetable for key examinations to be held during 2016 was announced by the Examinations Department today.
Accordingly the GCE Advanced Level Examination will be held from August 2 to  27, the Grade 5 Scholarship examination on August 21 and the GCE Ordinary Level Examination from  December 6 to 27.
