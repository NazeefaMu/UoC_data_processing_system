## Title: Welgama reports to FCID
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 June 2016 03:38 pm

Welgama reports to FCID


	

Former transport minister Kumara Welgama has reported to the FCID today to record a statement in connection with import of spare parts for the CTB without following tender procedures under the previous government.
