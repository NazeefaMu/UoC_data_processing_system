## Title: Man hit by train at Wellawatta
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 May 2016 11:05 am

Man hit by train at Wellawatta


	

A man was hit by a train travelling from Kalutara to Maradana near the Wellawatta Station this morning, Police said.


The identity of the man is yet to be ascertained.
