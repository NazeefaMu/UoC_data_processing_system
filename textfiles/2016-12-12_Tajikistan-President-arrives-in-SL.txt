## Title: Tajikistan President arrives in SL
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 12 December 2016 05:27 pm

Tajikistan President arrives in SL


	

Tajikistan President Emomali Rahmon arrived in Sri Lanka this evening with a delegation of 12 officials including Tajikistani Foreign Minister for a three-day official visit.

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe arrived at the Bandaranaike International Airport to welcome the Tajikistan President. (T.K.G.Kapila)
