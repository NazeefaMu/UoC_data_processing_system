## Title: Two more Dy. Ministers sworn in
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 10 September 2015 08:43 am

Two more Dy. Ministers sworn in


	


	Kandy District MP Anuradha Jayaratne and Kegalle District MP Dushmantha Mithrapala were today sworn in as Deputy Ministers by President Maithripala Sirisena.
	Mr.Jayaratne was appointed Deputy Minister of Mahaweli and Environment and Dushmantha Mithrapala as Deputy Minister of Buddhasashana and Justice.
