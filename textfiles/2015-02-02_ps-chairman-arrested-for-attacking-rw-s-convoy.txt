## Title: PS Chairman arrested for attacking RW’s convoy
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 2 February 2015 10:19 am

PS Chairman arrested for attacking RW’s convoy


	

Balapitiya Pradeshiya Sabha Chairman Dayaratne De Silva was today arrested for allegedly attacking the then Opposition Leader Ranil Wickremesinghe's vehicle convoy during a visit to the area to assess the damage caused by adverse weather conditions at the time, the Police said.
