## Title: First Int'l Yoga Day in Colombo
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 21 June 2015 12:12 pm

First Int'l Yoga Day in Colombo


	


Sri Lanka joined the first “International Day of Yoga’’ (IDY) celebrations with an official event at Galle Face, Colombo today. The event has been planned by the Indian High Commission in Sri Lanka. Pic by Kushan Pathiraja
