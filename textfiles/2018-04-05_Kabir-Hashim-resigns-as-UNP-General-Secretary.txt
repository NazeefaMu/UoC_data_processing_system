## Title: Kabir Hashim resigns as UNP General Secretary
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 April 2018 09:47 am

Kabir Hashim resigns as UNP General Secretary


	

United National Party (UNP) General Secretary Kabir Hashim today said that he had decided to resign as the General Secretary of the United National Party.

He also said he expects to hand over the letter of resignation to the party leader Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe during the course of the day.
