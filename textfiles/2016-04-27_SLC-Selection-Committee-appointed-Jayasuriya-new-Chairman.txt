## Title: SLC Selection Committee appointed: Jayasuriya new Chairman
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 April 2016 07:10 pm

SLC Selection Committee appointed: Jayasuriya new Chairman


	

Selection Committee of the Sri Lanka Cricket (SLC) (2016/2017) headed by Sanath Jayasuriya has been appointed today by Sports Minister Dayasiri Jayasekara.

Sanath Jayasuriya has been appointed as the Chairman of the Selection Committee, while Romesh Kaluwitharana, Ranjith Madurusinghe and Eric Upashantha have been appointed as members of the committee.

The new committee would take office from May 1, the SLC announced.
