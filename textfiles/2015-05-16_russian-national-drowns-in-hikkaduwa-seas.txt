## Title: Russian national drowns in Hikkaduwa seas
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 May 2015 05:04 am

Russian national drowns in Hikkaduwa seas


	

A 40-year-old Russian national drowned in the seas off Thiranagama in Hikkaduwa while having a sea bath last evening, the Police said.
They said his body was recovered by the police and navy lifeguard personnel.
His body is lying at the Mortuary of the Karapitiya Hospital.
