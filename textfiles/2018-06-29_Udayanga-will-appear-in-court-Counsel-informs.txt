## Title: Udayanga will appear in court: Counsel informs
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 29 June 2018 11:45 am

Udayanga will appear in court: Counsel informs


	

Former Sri Lankan Ambassador to Russia Udayanga Weeratunga would arrive in Sri Lanka and appear in Court over the MiG aircraft deal, his counsel informed the Fort Magistrate’s Court today.
