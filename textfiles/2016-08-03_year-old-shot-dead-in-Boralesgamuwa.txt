## Title: 30-year-old shot dead in Boralesgamuwa
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 August 2016 08:46 am

30-year-old shot dead in Boralesgamuwa


	

A 30-year-old man was shot dead by an unidentified gang outside the Katuwawala Temple in Boralesgamuwa last night, Police said.
