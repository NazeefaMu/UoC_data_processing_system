## Title: Javelin thrower Priyantha Herath rewarded
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 22 September 2016 07:29 pm

Javelin thrower Priyantha Herath rewarded


	


Javelin thrower Priyantha Herath who secured bronze at the just concluded Paralympic Games in Rio was given a heroes welcome on his return to the island. The picture shows, Herath is presented with a cash award of Rs. 1.5 million by Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe at a function held at the Kingsbury Hotel in Colombo.
