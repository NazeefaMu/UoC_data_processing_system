## Title: Pakistan PM visits Maligawa
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 6 January 2016 09:28 am

Pakistan PM visits Maligawa


	


Pakistan Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif today visited the Sri Dalada Maligawa in Kandy and the Botanical Garden at Peradeniya. The Premier planted a ‘Karanda’ tree at the Botanical Garden to commemorate his visit to Sri Lanka. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
