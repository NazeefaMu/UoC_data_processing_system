## Title: Release them…
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 15 September 2016 12:35 pm

Release them…


	



Family members of the Military Intelligence Corps (MIC) officers, who were remanded in connection with the abduction of journalist Pradeep Ekneligoda, today staged a protest outside the Fort Railway Station demanding that the officers be released. It’s a year since they were remanded. Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
