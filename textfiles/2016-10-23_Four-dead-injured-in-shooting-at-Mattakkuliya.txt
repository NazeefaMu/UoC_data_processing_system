## Title: Four dead, 3 injured in shooting at Mattakkuliya
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 October 2016 08:18 pm

Four dead, 3 injured in shooting at Mattakkuliya


	

Four people were killed and three were critically injured after a group of unidentified men shot at them in Mattakkuliya area a short while ago.

The injured were admitted to Colombo National Hospital.
