## Title: Mahindananda remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 September 2016 02:28 pm

Mahindananda remanded


	

Parliamentarian Mahindananda Aluthgamage, who was arrested by the CID over money laundering charges on purchasing a property worth Rs. 27 Million in Colombo 7, was ordered to be remanded till September 22 by Colombo Additional Magistrate Nishantha Peiris today. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
