## Title: PM completes 40 years in Parliament
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 25 July 2017 06:10 pm

PM completes 40 years in Parliament


	



President Maithripala Sirisena and the members of the Cabinet today celebrated Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe's completion of 40 years as a parliamentarian. The President and the Premier are seen cutting a cake amid cheers from the Cabinet ministers. Pic by Prime Minister’s Media
