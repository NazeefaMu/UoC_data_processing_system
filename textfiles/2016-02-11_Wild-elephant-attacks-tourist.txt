## Title: Wild elephant attacks tourist
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 February 2016 06:30 pm

Wild elephant attacks tourist


	

A foreign tourist, who attempted to photograph a wild elephant which frequented the Habarana Devala Kade on the Polonnaruwa- Habarana main road this morning, was attacked by the animal.

The injured tourist was admitted to the Polonnaruwa General Hospital.

A hospital spokesman said the tourist’s left lower limb was injured but that he was out of danger. (K.G. Karunaratne)
