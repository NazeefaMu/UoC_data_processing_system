## Title: Ruwan presents house to Jaffna family
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 3 April 2016 11:29 pm

Ruwan presents house to Jaffna family


	



State Minister of Defense Ruwan Wijewardene presents the key for a new house to the family of Vidya who was gang raped and killed in Jaffna. The new house is located in Vavuniya. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
