## Title: Lankan woman refugee detained
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 November 2015 02:51 am

Lankan woman refugee detained


	


	A 57-year-old woman refugee from Sri Lanka was on Monday detained near Dhanushkodi Arichalmunai in Tamil Nadu on Monday by the Coastal security police, hours after she clandestinely reached the shores Rameswaram without valid travel documents.
	Mary Margaret, wife of one Kingsley of Trincomalee was found walking aimlessly close to the seashore after agents in a clandestine boat from Northern Sri Lanka purportedly dropped her in the wee hours, sources said. (Deccan Chronicle)
