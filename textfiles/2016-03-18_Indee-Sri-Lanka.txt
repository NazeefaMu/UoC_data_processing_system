## Title: ‘Indee Sri Lanka 2016’
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 18 March 2016 02:07 pm

‘Indee Sri Lanka 2016’


	

‘Indee Sri Lanka 2016,’ the Indian machinery suppliers and tech show was launched today at the BMICH with the participation of Transport and Civil Aviation Minister Nimal Siripala De Silva and Indian High Commissioner Y.K. Sinha. It was organized by the Indian High Commission and the Indian Ministry of Commerce and Industry. Pix by Kithsiri de Mel
