## Title: New Cabinet likely to take oath tomorrow
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 October 2018 04:25 pm

New Cabinet likely to take oath tomorrow


	

It is reported that the Cabinet of the new government was scheduled to take oath at the Presidential Secretariat tomorrow morning.

The list of the MPs to be sworn in as ministers is being prepared by President Maithripala Sirisena and Prime Minister Mahinda Rajapaksa, sources said. (Ajantha Kumara Agalakada)
