## Title: Don’t dump Colombo’s garbage in Kotikawatte
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 18 April 2017 05:38 pm

Don’t dump Colombo’s garbage in Kotikawatte


	



Kotikawatta residents this evening staged a protest against moves to dump garbage collected from the Colombo Municipality limits in Kotikawatte. They burnt tyres and blocked the Old Avissawella Road. Pix by Waruna Wanniarachchi
