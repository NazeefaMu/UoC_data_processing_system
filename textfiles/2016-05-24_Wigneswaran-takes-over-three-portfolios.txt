## Title: Wigneswaran takes over three portfolios
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 May 2016 01:30 pm

Wigneswaran takes over three portfolios


	

Northern Province Chief Minister C.V. Wigneswaran has taken over three portfolios earlier held by Northern Provincial Council Minister P. Sathyalingam.

The provincial ministries of Social Welfare, Rehabilitation and Women Affairs will now come under the purview of the Chief Minister who was sworn in as the subject minister by Governor Reginald Cooray at his office. (Romesh Madhusanka)
