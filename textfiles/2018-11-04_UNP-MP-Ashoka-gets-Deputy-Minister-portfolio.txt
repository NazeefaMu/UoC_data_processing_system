## Title: UNP MP Ashoka gets Deputy Minister portfolio
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 November 2018 05:15 pm

UNP MP Ashoka gets Deputy Minister portfolio


	

Batticaloa District UNP MP R.D. Ashoka Priyantha has been appointed as Deputy Minister of Cultural and Internal Affairs, and Regional Development (Wayamba).
