## Title: Vass, his son sentenced to death
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 November 2015 10:33 am

Vass, his son sentenced to death


	

Former DIG Vass Gunawardane, his son and four others were sentenced to death by the High Court Trial-at-Bar on charges of abducting and murdering of businessman Mohommed Shyam.
