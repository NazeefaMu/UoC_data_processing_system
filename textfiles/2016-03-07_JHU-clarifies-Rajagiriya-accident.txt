## Title: JHU clarifies Rajagiriya accident
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 7 March 2016 02:31 pm

JHU clarifies Rajagiriya accident


	



The Jathika Hela Urumaya (JHU) today held a news conference regarding the Rajagiriya accident in which Minister Patali Champika Ranawaka’s vehicle was allegedly involved. JHU National Organizer Nishantha Sri Warnasinghe and Legal Consultant Tudor Perera are seen addressing the media at the news conference. Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
