## Title: Navy open Dialog Rugby League 2016/17 with easy win
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 November 2016 10:20 pm

Navy open Dialog Rugby League 2016/17 with easy win


	




The first match of the Dialog Rugby League 2016/17 took at Police Park Grounds today. Navy SC emerged winners as they comprehensively beat Police SC, with the final scores reading 29-8. Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
