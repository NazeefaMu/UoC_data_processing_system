## Title: NTB upgrades Pettah  branch
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 8 August 2017 12:00 am

NTB upgrades Pettah  branch


	



 

Nations Trust Bank’s branch at Pettah (Bankshall Street) has been refurbished and is now equipped with a more spacious interior with even more facilities such as a 24/7 cash deposit machine (CDM), business banking, leasing and hire purchase, investment products, trade financing, factoring, remittance services and all other banking services. 


The newly refurbished branch remains at its usual, familiar location but now delivers the full range of Nations Trust Bank’s products and services to customers seamlessly, using the latest technology and amidst a well-designed space. 


An opening ceremony was held on the 3rd of August in the presence of valued customers, members of staff and guests. 


The chief guest at the ceremony was Bandara Jayathileke, Deputy General Manager, SME Banking, Nations Trust Bank.
