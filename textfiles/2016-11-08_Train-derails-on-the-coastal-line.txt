## Title: Train derails on the coastal line
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 8 November 2016 10:33 am

Train derails on the coastal line


	



Train services on the coastal line, between Ambalangoda and Balapitiya –had come to a standstill due to a derailment, the Control Room said.

The engine of fuel transporting train (No. 778) derailed between Ambalangoda and Balapitiya this morning, it said.
