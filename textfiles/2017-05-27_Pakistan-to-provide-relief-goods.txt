## Title: Pakistan to provide relief goods
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 May 2017 02:26 pm

Pakistan to provide relief goods


	

Pakistan High Commission today said that it is in constant touch with Sri Lankan authorities to finalize the required relief goods as requested and would soon provide necessary relief goods to the government and people of Sri Lanka

Issuing a statement, Acting High Commissioner of Pakistan, Dr. Sarfraz Ahmad Khan Sipra said the people and Government of Pakistan are aggrieved over the floods and mudslides triggered by Moonsoon rains in Sri Lanka which caused death and destruction in many parts of the country.

Dr. Sipra said that people of Pakistan are standing shoulder to shoulder with the Sri Lankan people in these times of grief and suffering. 

He further added that as a true and trusted longstanding friend, Pakistan has always been providing unconditional assistance to Sri Lankan brethren in their need of hour.
