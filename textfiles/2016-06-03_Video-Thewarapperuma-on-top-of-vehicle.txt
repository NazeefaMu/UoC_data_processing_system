## Title: Video: Thewarapperuma on top of vehicle
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 3 June 2016 01:24 pm

Video: Thewarapperuma on top of vehicle


	



Internal Affairs, Wayamba Development and Cultural Affairs Deputy Minister Palitha Thewarapperuma climbed the roof of his vehicle when Sabaragamuwa University students staged a protest at its main gate opposing his participation at the opening of a cultural centre in the University. The Deputy Minister remained on the roof for about two hours. Pix by M. Geethakara
