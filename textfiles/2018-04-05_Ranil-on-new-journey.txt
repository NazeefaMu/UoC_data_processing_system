## Title: Ranil on new journey
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 April 2018 12:35 pm

Ranil on new journey


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe today said he would go on a new journey with all who supported the Government.

Prime Minister Wickremesinghe said this at a function held at UNP headquarters Sirikotha this morning.

“The Government will begin a new programme shortly,” he said.

Also he said UNP would have new faces soon. This he said was targeted at 2020 Parliamentary Elections. (Yohan Perera)
