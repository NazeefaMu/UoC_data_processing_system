## Title: Video: A jail term, sometime!
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 July 2016 04:28 pm

Video: A jail term, sometime!


	


President Maithripala Sirisena, who was in a convivial mood at the inauguration ceremony of a new Excise office in Matara, jailed a few of his Ministers at the detention centre of the complex.

Ministers LakshmanYapaAbeywardena and Chief Minister Shan Wijayalal were among those present at the ceremony
