## Title: Champions Trophy final: India bowl first
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 June 2017 02:53 pm

Champions Trophy final: India bowl first


	

India won the toss and elected to field against Pakistan in the final of the ICC Champions Trophy 2017 at The Oval, London today.

India have a lead of 13-2 in head-to-head clashes with Pakistan in ICC tournaments (World Cups, World T20s, Champions Trophies)

 

Playing XIs:

India: Rohit Sharma, Shikhar Dhawan, Virat Kohli (captain), Yuvraj Singh, MS Dhoni (wk), Kedar Jadhav, Hardik Pandya, Ravindra Jadeja, Ravichandran Ashwin, Bhuvneshwar Kumar, Jasprit Bumrah

Pakistan: Azhar Ali, Fakhar Zaman, Babar Azam, Mohammad Hafeez, Shoaib Malik, Sarfraz Ahmed (captain and wk), Imad Wasim, Mohammad Amir, Shadab Khan, Hasan Ali, Junaid Khan
