## Title: Heavy traffic as protest continues at Thotalanga
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 February 2016 08:26 am

Heavy traffic as protest continues at Thotalanga


	

The Colombo-Negombo road is currently blocked at Thotalanga as the protest demonstration by area people continued for the second day.

Due to the heavy traffic in the area motorists are advised to use alternate routes.
