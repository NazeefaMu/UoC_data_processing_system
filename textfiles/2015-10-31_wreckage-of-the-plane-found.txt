## Title: Wreckage of the plane found
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 31 October 2015 09:43 am

Wreckage of the plane found


	

Wreckage of the Russian plane that crashed early this morning has been found in the Hassana area in Egypt.
 It disappeared from radar screens when travelling at 9,500m (31,000ft), Egyptian officials said.
Egyptian officials said most on board are likely to have died. (BBC)
