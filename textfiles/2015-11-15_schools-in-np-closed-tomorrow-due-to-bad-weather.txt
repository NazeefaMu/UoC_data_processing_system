## Title: Schools in NP closed tomorrow due to bad weather
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 November 2015 05:09 pm

Schools in NP closed tomorrow due to bad weather


	

All schools in the Northern Province will stay closed today (16) owing to the inclement weather that is being experienced in the North, the Northern Provincial Education Secretary’s Office announced. (Wanni Romesh Madushanka)
