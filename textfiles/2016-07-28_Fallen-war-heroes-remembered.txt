## Title: Fallen war heroes remembered
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 28 July 2016 04:14 pm

Fallen war heroes remembered


	



A ceremony was held today to commemorate the lives of members of the security forces who were old boys of Nalanda College Colombo, who gave their lives for the sake of the country during the 30-year war with the LTTE. State Minister of Defense Ruwan Wijewardene was the chief guest at the event. Principal of Nalanda College Ranjith Jayasundara, Air Force Commander Air Marshal Gagana Bulathsinhala were joined at the commemoration ceremony by the relatives of the departed security personnel.
