## Title: Police Carol service
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 23 December 2017 04:00 am

Police Carol service


	



The annual carol service organized by the Police Buddhist and Religious Affairs Association was held at the Police Field Force Headquarters Ground in Colombo yesterday.

Archbishop Malcolm Cardinal Ranjith and IGP Pujitha Jayasundara were the guests of honor at the event.
