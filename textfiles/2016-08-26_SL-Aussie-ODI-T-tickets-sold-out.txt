## Title: SL-Aussie ODI, T20 tickets sold out
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 August 2016 08:59 pm

SL-Aussie ODI, T20 tickets sold out


	

All tickets for Sunday’s 3rd ODI in Dambulla and 2nd T20 in Colombo between Sri Lanka and Australia sold out there will be no tickets at the gates, Sri Lanka Cricket said.
