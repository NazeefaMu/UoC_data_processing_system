## Title: Diesel production tax increased
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 August 2016 05:14 pm

Diesel production tax increased


	

The Government has increased the production tax on diesel by Rs. 10 per liter with effect from August 20, the Finance Ministry announced today.

Accordingly, the production tax on diesel has been increased to Rs. 13 from Rs. 3.

Ministry said the market price of diesel would not be increased due to the new tax revision.

The ministry said the crude oil prices in the world market have declined and the Government has revised the production tax on diesel in order to claim the advantage from the world oil price decline.
