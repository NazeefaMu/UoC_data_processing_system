## Title: Dead body found inside a Bambalapitiya school
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 April 2015 01:59 pm

Dead body found inside a Bambalapitiya school


	

A body of a 44 year-old woman, attached to the minor staff of a leading school in Bambalapitiya has been found inside the school's store room, Police said.
