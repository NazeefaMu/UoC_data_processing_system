## Title: Wild elephant attacks foreign couple, man killed
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 May 2016 06:36 pm

Wild elephant attacks foreign couple, man killed


	

A foreigner was killed and his female companion injured when a wild elephant attacked them while they were riding on a motorbike on Habarana-Sigiriya Road this afternoon, police said.

They said the man was identified as a 26-year-old Ukraine national.

The injured was admitted to the Habarana Hospital.
