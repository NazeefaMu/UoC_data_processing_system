## Title: Final Randoli Perahera
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 8 August 2017 06:02 am

Final Randoli Perahera


	



The final Randoli Perahera of the historic Kandy Sri Dalada Maligawa paraded the streets last night. Picture shows the Tusker Indiraja carrying the sacred relics casket. Pix by J.A.L Jayasinghe, L.B. Senarathne and Chamil Prasad.
