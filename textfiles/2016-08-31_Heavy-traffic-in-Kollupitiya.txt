## Title: Heavy traffic in Kollupitiya
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 31 August 2016 04:14 pm

Heavy traffic in Kollupitiya


	

Heavy traffic is reported in Kollupitiya due to the protest march carried out by the members of the Inter University Students' Federation (IUSF) against the South Asian Institute of Technology and Medicine (SAITM).

The protesters were heading towards the Temple Trees violating a court’s order issued not to proceed towards High Security Zones.
