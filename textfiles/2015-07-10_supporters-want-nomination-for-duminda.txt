## Title: Video: Supporters want nomination for Duminda
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 10 July 2015 12:55 pm

Video: Supporters want nomination for Duminda


	

Former MP Duminda Silva’s supporters staged a protest outside the SLFP Headquarters in Colombo urging the SLFP to give him nomination to contest at the general election.
The protesters later marched towards the Mahaweli Centre in Colombo 07.
According to media reports, SLFP seniors are not in favour of giving nomination to Mr. Silva and two others including Mervyn Silva and Sajin da Vaas Gunawardane.
