## Title: Kolitha Gunatilleke new CDS
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 June 2015 11:54 am

Kolitha Gunatilleke new CDS


	

Air Marshal Kolitha Gunatilleke has been appointed as the Chief of Defence Staff with effect from June 16, Air Force Spokesman Wing Commander Gihan Seneviratne said today.
