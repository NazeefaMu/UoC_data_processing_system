## Title: Power supply back to normal: CEB
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 March 2016 02:06 pm

Power supply back to normal: CEB


	

The Ceylon Electricity Board (CEB) today assured that there would be no disruption to the power supply in the country in the wake of three generators at the Norochcholai Coal Power Plant resuming operations.

CEB chairman Anura Wijepala said 600 MW would be added to the national grid with the resumed operations. (Chaturanga Pradeep)
