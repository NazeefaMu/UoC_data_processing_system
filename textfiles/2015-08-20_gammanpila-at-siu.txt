## Title: Udaya Gammanpila at SIU
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 20 August 2015 07:28 am

Udaya Gammanpila at SIU


	

Pivithuru Hela Urumaya (PHU) General Secretary Udaya Gammanpila has arrived at the Police Special Investigation Unit (SIU) this morning to give a statement, police said.
They said a statement was being recorded from Mr. Gammanpila over a complaint with regard to defrauding shares of a company.
