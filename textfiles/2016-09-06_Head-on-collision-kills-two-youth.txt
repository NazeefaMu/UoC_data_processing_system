## Title: Head-on collision kills two youth
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 September 2016 08:32 pm

Head-on collision kills two youth


	

Two youth were killed when the car they were travelling in collided head on with a lorry at Sirambiady on the Puttalam-Anuradhapura Road this evening.

The deceased aged 23 and 25 were residents of Puttalam. (Hiran Priyankara)
