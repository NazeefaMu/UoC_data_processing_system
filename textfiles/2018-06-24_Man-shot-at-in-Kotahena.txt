## Title: Man shot at in Kotahena
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 June 2018 10:20 pm

Man shot at in Kotahena


	

Two men arrived on a motorbike opened fire at a man at Jampettah Street in Kotahena this evening, Police Spokesman Ruwan Gunasekara said.

He said the victim was admitted to the Intensive Care Unit (ICU) of the National Hospital, Colombo.
