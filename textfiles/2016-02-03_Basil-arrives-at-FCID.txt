## Title: Basil arrives at FCID
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 February 2016 10:29 am

Basil arrives at FCID


	

Former Economic Development Minister Basil Rajapaksa arrived at the Financial Crimes Investigation Division (FCID) a short while ago to give a statement over allegations of financial fraud.


Earlier, the FCID also arrested Mr. Rajapaksa, in connection with the alleged financial misappropriations at the Divi Naguma Department.
