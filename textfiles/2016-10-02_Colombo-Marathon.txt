## Title: Colombo Marathon
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 2 October 2016 04:46 pm

Colombo Marathon


	



Over 7,500 runners from 41 countries participated in the 16th LSR Colombo Marathon which was held today. 

The marathon organized by Lanka Sportreizen was begun at the Independent Square and ended at Negombo Beach. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
