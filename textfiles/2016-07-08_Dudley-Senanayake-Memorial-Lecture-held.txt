## Title: Dudley Senanayake Memorial Lecture held
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 8 July 2016 02:43 am

Dudley Senanayake Memorial Lecture held


	



The 16th Dudley Senanayake Memorial Lecture was delivered yesterday by Hon. Chito Gascon, Commissioner of the Human Rights Commission in Philippines. The event was organised by the Dudley Senanayake Foundation in cooperation with the Friedrich Naumann Foundation which was attended by DSF Chairman D.M. Swaminathan, State Minister of Defence Ruwan Wijewardene and other dignitaries. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
