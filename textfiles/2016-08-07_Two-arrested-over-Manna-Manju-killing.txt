## Title: Two arrested over 'Manna Manju' killing
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 7 August 2016 08:54 am

Two arrested over 'Manna Manju' killing


	

Two suspects, wanted in connection with the killing of Dinesh Sanjaya alias 'Manna Manju’ had been arrested in Dehiwala by the Kelaniya Police on Friday.

Police said two gunmen on a motorcycle had opened fire at 39-year -old Manju opposite Sylvester’s Road in Mount Lavinia on July 21.
