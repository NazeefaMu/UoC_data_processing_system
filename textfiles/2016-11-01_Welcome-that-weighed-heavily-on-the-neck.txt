## Title: Welcome that weighed heavily on the neck
## Section: Political Gossip
## Date: 1 November 2016 12:00 am

Welcome that weighed heavily on the neck


	



A Minister who was the chief guest at a ceremony where houses were presented to a group of displaced families was welcomed with a hefty garland that had to be carried by four people.

 


The politician had later remarked that this was the biggest ever garland to have been put on him at a reception during his entire political career.
