## Title: Anusha suspended over fixing probe
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 January 2016 10:32 am

Anusha suspended over fixing probe


	

Anusha Samaranayake, a fast bowling coach attached to Sri Lanka Cricket (SLC) had been suspended today pending an inquiry into the alleged match-fixing probe, SLC Secretary Mohan de Silva said.
However, he said a net bowler named Gayan Vishwajith had been discontinued from entering SLC or its grounds over the same charges.
