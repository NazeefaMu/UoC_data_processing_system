## Title: CID records statement from IGP
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 November 2018 05:13 pm

CID records statement from IGP


	

Criminal Investigations Department (CID) has recorded a statement from Police Chief Pujith Jayasundara this morning over the Anti-Corruption Force convener Namal Kumara’s allegations, police said.

They said that the latest statement was recorded as a part of the investigations into the alleged assassination plot on President Maithripala Sirisena and former Defence Secretary Gotabhaya Rajapaksa.
