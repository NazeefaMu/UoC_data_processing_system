## Title: Video: Bank robbery attempt kills two
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 November 2015 08:43 am

Video: Bank robbery attempt kills two


	

A man who had attempted to rob a private bank in Kekirawa, Anuradhapura was killed along with the security officer of the bank when a hand grenade exploded, Police said.
The security officer had attempted to prevent the robber from entering the bank, which had resulted in a scuffle between the robber and the security officer. A hand grenade in the possession of the robber had then exploded, killing the two. (Rohana Chandradasa)
