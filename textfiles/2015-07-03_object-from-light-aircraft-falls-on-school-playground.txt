## Title: Object from light aircraft falls on school playground
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 July 2015 07:38 am

Object from light aircraft falls on school playground


	

An object believed to have been from a light aircraft has fallen on the M.D.H. Jayawardena Junior School play grounds at Thalangamathis morning, police said.
They said Civil Aviation Authority was informed of the object which appeared to be about two feet long and 1.5 feet wide and that there had been no reports of anyone being injured.
The police are investigating.
