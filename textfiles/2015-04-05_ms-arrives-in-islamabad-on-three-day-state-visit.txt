## Title: MS arrives in Islamabad on three-day state visit
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 April 2015 08:46 am

MS arrives in Islamabad on three-day state visit


	


	President Maithripala Sirisena arrived in Islamabad today, kicking off a three-day state visit, Radio Pakistan reported.
	This is President Sirisena’s first visit to Pakistan which was agreed upon the request of President Mamnoon Hussain and Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif, a statement released by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs stated.
	During the visit, President Sirisena will meet President Hussain, who is scheduled to host a state banquet in his honour while Prime Minister Nawaz is scheduled to hold official talks with the president
	Following the talks, a number Memorandums of Understanding (MOUs) will be signed along with agreements of cooperation between the two countries.
