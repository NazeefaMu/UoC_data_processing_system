## Title: Seylan Bank opens 166th branch in Kandana
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 19 September 2017 12:00 am

Seylan Bank opens 166th branch in Kandana


	

Strengthening its vision to offer ultimate banking solutions to the people of Sri Lanka, Seylan Bank opened a fully-fledged branch in Kandana, expanding the bank’s footprint to 166 branches island-wide.


The branch was ceremoniously opened on the 15thof September by Kapila Ariyaratne, Director/CEO of Seylan Bank PLC. 


Located in Negombo Road, Kandana, the new branch is equipped with latest facilities to offer convenient banking solutions for customers in and around the area of Kandana. 


The new branch offers customers tailor-made services that cater to varied products including personal loans, housing loans, savings, and business banking solutions. Furthermore, the branch will be open to customers on weekdays from 9 a.m. to 4p.m.
