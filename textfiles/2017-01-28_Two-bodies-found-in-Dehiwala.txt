## Title: Two bodies found in Dehiwala
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 28 January 2017 10:49 am

Two bodies found in Dehiwala


	

The bodies of two unidentified men were found at two separate places at Badovita in Dehiwala this morning, Police said.

They said the body of one person was found while floating in the lake behind the Lakeside Hotel at Badovita and the body of the other person was found in the canal near the Badovita Bridge.
