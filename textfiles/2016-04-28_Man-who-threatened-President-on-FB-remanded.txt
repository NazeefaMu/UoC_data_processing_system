## Title: Man who threatened President on FB remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 28 April 2016 04:39 pm

Man who threatened President on FB remanded


	

A man, who was arrested today on charges of threatening President Maithripala Sirisena in the social media, was remanded till May 6, by the Colombo Chief Magistrate’s Court today.

The suspect, a resident of Yakkala, was arrested by the CID at the Bandaranaike International Airport today.  He had been deported from Malaysia as he had been staying there without a valid visa. (FT)
