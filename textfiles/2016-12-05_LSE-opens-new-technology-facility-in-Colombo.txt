## Title: LSE opens new technology facility in Colombo
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 5 December 2016 11:09 pm

LSE opens new technology facility in Colombo


	



The London Stock Exchange opened its state-of-the-art facility in Colombo at the Trace Expert City under the patronage of Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe. The facility will create 400 jobs and they would provide support services for the group’s global network.  Chairman of LSE Group Donald Brydon was also present at the event.
