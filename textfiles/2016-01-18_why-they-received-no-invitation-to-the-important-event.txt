## Title: Why they received no invitation to the important event
## Section: Political Gossip
## Date: 18 January 2016 06:30 pm

Why they received no invitation to the important event


	

A number of purohitas had not received invitations to a certain state celebration held recently with much fanfare. This glaring omission has been attributed to the inefficiency and incompetence of the personnel of the government establishment responsible for organizing the event. 
“The personnel appointed by the politico who once presided over this establishment continue to work there. They have failed even to maintain an updated list of the Ministers of this government,” an informed source explained.
