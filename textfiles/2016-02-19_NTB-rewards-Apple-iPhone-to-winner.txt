## Title: NTB rewards Apple iPhone 6 to winner
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 19 February 2016 12:00 am

NTB rewards Apple iPhone 6 to winner


	

The Apple iphone 6 was presented by Renuka Fernandoto the winner H. D. A. Harendra 

 

Nations Trust Bank opened their flagship digitalized branch at the World Trade Centre, emphasizing further the bank’s ambitious approach towards digital banking. 


In light of the opening event, a promotional raffle draw was held during the proceedings,  commemorating the opening of the new branch. The winner, H. D. A. Harendra, won the draw which gave him the opportunity to walk away with a brand new Apple iPhone 6. The winning prize was presented by Renuka Fernando, Director/CEO, Nations Trust Bank.
