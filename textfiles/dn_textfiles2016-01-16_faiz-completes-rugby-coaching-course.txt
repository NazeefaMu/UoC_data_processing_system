## Title: Faiz completes Rugby Coaching Course
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, January 16, 2016 - 01:00

Faiz completes Rugby Coaching Course

Akkaraipattu Educational Zone Rugby Co -ordinator and Physical Education Teacher I.L.M.Faiz successfully completed his rugby coaching course conducted by National Institute of Sports Science under the purview of the Sports Ministry. He was awarded the certificate by Sports Minister Dayasiri Jayasekara held at Sports Ministry recently.
Faiz is a top sportsman in Addalaichenai. He has excelled as a sportsman, coach and administrator. He possess experience in coaching rugby, volleyball, cricket, football and athletics and has shown a very keen interest in taking sports to the lesser known schools in the Akkaraipattu Educational Zone. Faiz won the championship of the All Island Teachers Sports Meet 2015 conducted by the Education Ministry which wad held at Nuwara Eliya Municipal Play Grounds recently. He secured three Gold Medals in 4x400 M, Tripled Jump, Long Jump and won a Silver medal in 4x100M in the 36 - 40 age event.
Coach Faiz, is a past student of the Palamunai Minhaj Maha Vidyalaya and a Physical Education Diploma holder of the Addalaichenai National College of Education.
