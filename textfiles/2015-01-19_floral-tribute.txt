## Title: Floral tribute
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 19 January 2015 04:40 pm

Floral tribute


	


A special reception organized by Ven. Athuraliye Rathana Thera was held at Sadaham Sevana in Rajagiriya today to symbolize the Maha Sangha’s acknowledgment to President Maithripala Sirisena. Picture shows a young girl presenting a tray of flowers to President Sirisena to be offered to the statue of Lord Buddha. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
