## Title: Power failure in Colombo
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 April 2016 04:06 pm

Power failure in Colombo


	

Power interruptions were reported in several areas in Colombo including the Port, Slave Island and Kollupitiya areas due to a technical failure, the Ceylon Electricity Board (CEB) said today.

Two sub stations in Colombo had caused the power failure, it said.
