## Title: Wattala-Mabole UC goes to UNP
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 February 2018 12:18 pm

Wattala-Mabole UC goes to UNP


	

The UNP won the Wattala-Mabole Urban Council securing 10 seats with 8767 votes. UNP – 8767 (10)
SLPP  – 4389 (5)
SLFP  – 1302 (1)
JVP     – 683 (1)
