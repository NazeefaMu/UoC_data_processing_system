## Title: Video: Sajin Vass out on bail
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 8 July 2016 12:54 pm

Video: Sajin Vass out on bail


	



Former UPFA MP Sajin Vass Gunawardena was released on bail by Colombo Chief Magistrate Gihan Pilapitiya today. The former MP was arrested by the CID on charges of having failed to declare the Rs.600 million he is said to have earned from a private company. He was released on a cash bail Rs.500,000 with four sureties of Rs.5 million each. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
