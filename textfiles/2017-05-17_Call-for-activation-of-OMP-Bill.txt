## Title: Call for activation of OMP Bill
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 17 May 2017 05:15 pm

Call for activation of OMP Bill


	

Family members of the disappeared today staged a protest in front of the Opposition Leader’s office urging the authorities to activate the Office on Missing Persons (OMP) Act.  They handed over a petition to MP M.A. Sumanthiran. Pix by Nisal Baduge
