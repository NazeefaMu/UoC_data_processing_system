## Title: NTB American Express Card offers 40% savings for cardmembers at  Cinnamon Hotels & Resorts
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 25 April 2016 12:00 am

NTB American Express Card offers 40% savings for cardmembers at  Cinnamon Hotels & Resorts


	

As one of the most prestigious and well accepted  credit cards in Sri Lanka, Nations Trust Bank American Express Card offers its loyal cardmembers a wealth of privileges complementing their lifestyle. American Express Cards are renowned the world over for extending unparalleled lifestyle perks to its members. The prestige and status of being a member of American Express Card is second to none.


Nations Trust Bank American Express Cardmembers regularly avail of a wide retinue of offers locally and internationally that cover the entire gamut of lifestyles. In keeping with its reputation of pampering its cardmembers and sweeping them off their feet with amazing offers, Nations Trust Bank American Express Card once again announces an irresistible offer. 


Nations Trust Bank American Express Cardmembers can now avail of 40% savings at leading Cinnamon Hotels & Resorts (excluding city hotels) valid during the promotional period of 1st May to 31st July 2016. Reservations for this promotion can only be made from 20th April 2016 to 30th June 2016. The Cinnamon properties participating in this promotion are Cinnamon Lodge Habarana, Cinnamon BeyBeruwela, Cinnamon Citadel Kandy, Cinnamon Wild Yala, Habarana Village by Cinnamon, Trinco Blu by Cinnamon, HikkaTranz by Cinnamon and Bentota Beach by Cinnamon.


Nimesh Fernando, Head of Cards, Nations Trust Bank PLC commented: “At Nations Trust Bank American Express Cards, we understand that every member has different needs to complement their lifestyle. And this is why such a wide range of Cinnamon Hotels and Resorts have been included in this offer. These properties span the entire experiential range from beaches, wildlife safaris to cultural and bio diverse regions. Whether it is soaking up the sun, reveling in water sports or taking in a rich cultural vista, this offers Cardmembers a refreshingly different holiday experience.We are delighted to have the Cinnamon Hotels & Resorts onboard this promotion”.


Each of the hotels offers a distinct experience and Cardmembers can take their pick: Cinnamon Lodge Habarana - eco parks, elephant rides, night watch village trails; Cinnamon BeyBeruwela –water sports and boat rides to Little Adam’s Peak; Cinnamon Citadel Kandy - Meet the Brass Crafter, boat rides and hikes to the Knuckles range; Cinnamon Wild Yala - bird-watching and safaris (guided tours/night safari); Habarana Village by Cinnamon - elephant rides and spa; Trinco Blu by Cinnamon - water sports, scuba diving, whales, snorkeling and fishing; HikkaTranz by Cinnamon - dolphins, scuba diving and rainforests; and Bentota Beach by Cinnamon - water sports.


The Nations Trust Bank American Express Card is focused on infusing excitement and prestige in member lifestyles. This is a wonderful opportunity for Cardmembers to make full use of the scintillating choice of holidays at any of the Cinnamon properties listed above. For hotel reservations, Cardmembers simply need to call 0112 161 161 or visit www.cinnamonhotels.com 


The Nations Trust Bank American Express Card is issued by Nations Trust Bank PLC, one of the fastest growing banks in Sri Lanka today, operating 93 branches and an ATM network covering 134 locations and the issuer and sole acquirer for American Express Cards in Sri Lanka. Reputed for being the benchmark of customer convenience in the industry, it offers a host of innovative financial products and services to a wide customer base. 


The Cinnamon brand represents a vibrant new Sri Lanka, successfully competing with the world’s biggest brands. It offers a combination of a contemporary Sri Lankan outlook with world-class service, and modern conveniences with old-world charm. Launched in 2005, the chain currently houses 14 properties, including 3 City Properties in Colombo, 8 Resorts around Sri Lanka and 3 Island Resorts in the Maldives. Each property comes with its own personality, presence and experience, but they all drive the same point home – Inspired Living.
