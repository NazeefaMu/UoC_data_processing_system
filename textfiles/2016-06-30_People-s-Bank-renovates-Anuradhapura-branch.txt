## Title: People’s Bank renovates Anuradhapura branch
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 30 June 2016 12:00 am

People’s Bank renovates Anuradhapura branch


	



 

People’s Bank recently relaunched its Anuradhapura branch offering loyal customers the access to state-of-the-art banking services that include various loans and pawning facilities coupled with an enhanced customer service experience. 
The branch currently located in the ancient city of Anuradhapura, famous for its well-preserved ruins of ancient Sri Lanka is also a hub for local pilgrims who will be able to access the 24-hour PET machine located at the branch.
The renovated branch was opened by N. Vasantha Kumar - CEO/General Manager, in the presence of senior officials including Boniface Silva - Deputy General Manager (Channel Management), Mahinda Premanath - Deputy General Manager (Banking Support Services), A. K. Bandara - Assistant General Manager, Chandrika Nissanka – Regional Manager, Anuradhapura, IduminiRathnayaka, I. A. D. N. Premasiri, D. M. D. Dissanayaka -  Assistant Regional Manager, K. M. P. P. Kulathunga - Manager. Staff, well-wishers and other dignitaries were also present at the event.
