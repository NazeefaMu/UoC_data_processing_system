## Title: 'Cormorant Strike IX - 2018’
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 26 September 2018 10:19 pm

'Cormorant Strike IX - 2018’


	



The final phase of joint military training exercise of the Army, 'Cormorant Strike IX - 2018’ was held today at Kuchchaveli, Trincomalee with State Minister of Defence Ruwan Wijewardena participating as the chief guest, together with Chief of Defence Staff Admiral Ravindra Wijegunaratne, Navy Commander and, Army Commander Lieutenant General Mahesh Senanayake. A large number of Foreign and local participants and observers joined this year’s exercise. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana.
