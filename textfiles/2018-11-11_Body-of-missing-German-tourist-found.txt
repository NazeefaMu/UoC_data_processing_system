## Title: Body of missing German tourist found
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 November 2018 01:20 pm

Body of missing German tourist found


	

The body of the 35-year-old German female tourist, who fell off the World’s End in Horton Plains, this morning, has been found from the Nonpareil estate, Nuwara Eliya, police said.
