## Title: India restrict Sri Lanka to 217/9 in 50 overs
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 August 2017 06:49 pm

India restrict Sri Lanka to 217/9 in 50 overs


	

Visiting India restricted Sri Lanka to 217 for nine in 50 overs in the third One Day International (ODI) held in Pallekele International Stadium a short while ago.

It's a do-or-die match for Sri Lanka, while India would like to seal the series. Jasprit Bumrah took 5 for27 for the visitors. For the hosts, Lahiru Thirmanne top-scored with 80.
