## Title: Media introduced to new SLC Manager Gurusinghe
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 5 March 2017 08:08 pm

Media introduced to new SLC Manager Gurusinghe


	



Sri Lanka Cricket (SLC) today held a media briefing at the JAIC Hilton to introduce newly-appointed Manager Asanka Gurusinghe. He was flanked by SLC President Thilanga Sumathipala, Cricket Committee Chairman Aravinda De Silva, Selection Committee Chairman Sanath Jayasuriya, SLC Secretary Mohan De Silva and Cricket Captain Angelo Matthews.
