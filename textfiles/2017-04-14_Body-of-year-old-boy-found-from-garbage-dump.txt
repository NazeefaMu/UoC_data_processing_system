## Title: Body of 12 year-old boy found from garbage dump
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 14 April 2017 10:59 pm

Body of 12 year-old boy found from garbage dump


	

The body of a 12-year-old boy who was buried under the pile of garbage at Meetotamulla was found by the rescue workers this evening, hospital sources said.
Currently, some 12 persons, who were injured due to the Meetotamulla incident were receiving treatment at the National hospital, Colombo.
Meanwhile, Disaster Management Center said residents around the Meetotamulla garbage dump were being evacuated due to the risk of continue to collapse.
