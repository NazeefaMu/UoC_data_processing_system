## Title: Water cut in Colombo
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 November 2016 02:44 pm

Water cut in Colombo


	

The water supply will be interrupted in several areas in Colombo for eight hours from 9.00 pm today to 5 am tomorrow due to an urgent repair, the National Water Supply and Drainage Board (NWSDB) said.

Accordingly Grandpass, Sea Street, Kotahena, Mattakkuliya and areas from New Kelani Bridge on the Baseline Road to Dematagoda Junction will be affected.
