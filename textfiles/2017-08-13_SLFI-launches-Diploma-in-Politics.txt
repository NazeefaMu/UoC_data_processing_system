## Title: SLFI launches Diploma in Politics
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 13 August 2017 09:36 pm

SLFI launches Diploma in Politics


	




The 'Politics and Political Leadership' National Diploma programme organised by the Sri Lanka Foundation Institute (SLFI) was officially inaugurated at the institute on Sunday. A number of ministers, other political leaders and the Election Commission Chairman attended the event. Pics by Nisal Baduge
