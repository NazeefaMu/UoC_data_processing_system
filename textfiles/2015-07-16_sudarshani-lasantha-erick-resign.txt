## Title: Video: Sudarshani, Lasantha, Erick resign
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 July 2015 09:04 am

Video: Sudarshani, Lasantha, Erick resign


	

SLFP Deputy Ministers Sudarshani Fernandopulle, Lasantha Alagiyawanna and Erick Weerawardane today resigned from their Ministerial portfolios.
They said that their resignations were “to contribute for the future of the SLFP.”
Addressing a news conference, they said they have tendered their resignations to the President today.   Video by Sanath Desmond
