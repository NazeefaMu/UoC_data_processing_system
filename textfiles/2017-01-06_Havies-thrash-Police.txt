## Title: Havies thrash Police
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 6 January 2017 10:57 pm

Havies thrash Police


	



Havelock SC came from behind to thrash Police SC by 29 to 13 to earn an all important bonus points in their Inter-Club Rugby encounter played under lights at the Havelocks ground today.

At the short whistle Police led the winner by one point (6-5) but put on an outstanding performance in the final half to record a crushing win.

Havelocks scored their points through four goals, three tries and one penalty while Police had collected their points through a goal and two penalty. Pic by Pradeep Dilrukshana
