## Title: They become soul mates for mutual benefit
## Section: Political Gossip
## Date: 21 March 2017 12:00 am

They become soul mates for mutual benefit


	



 

A family member of a very high political authority has gone into a business partnership with a prominent purohita.

 


The purohita is said to be enjoying various concessions thanks to his well- connected partner. 
The family member also readily comes to the rescue of the purohita whenever he gets into hot water due to his dubious activity, they say.
