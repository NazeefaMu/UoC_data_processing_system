## Title: Navy Commander welcomes IGP to Naval HQ
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 22 July 2016 02:17 pm

Navy Commander welcomes IGP to Naval HQ


	

Police Chief Pujith Jayasundara called on Navy Commander Ravindra Wijegunaratne at the Naval Headquarters this morning. The visiting IGP was accorded with a Guard of Honour in accordance with naval traditions. This visit of IGP Jayasundara marked his first official meeting with the Navy Commander, having assumed duties as the Inspector General of Police.
