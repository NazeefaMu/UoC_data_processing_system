## Title: Nishantha Wickramasinghe summoned by PRECIFAC
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 January 2016 08:25 am

Nishantha Wickramasinghe summoned by PRECIFAC


	

SriLankan airlines’ former chairman Nishantha Wickramasinghe and its Chief Executive Officer Kapila Chandrasena have been asked to report to the PRECIFAC on Tuesday.
A senior official of the commission said they were summoned in connection with a complaint on several irregularities in the SriLankan airlines catering service. (Ajith Siriwardana)
