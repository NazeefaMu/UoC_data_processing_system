## Title: 15 year old crashes into mother and child
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 January 2016 03:44 am

15 year old crashes into mother and child


	

A 10-year-old girl and her 47-year-old mother were killed on the pavement in Dematagoda along Baseline Road, when a car driven by a 15 year old boy crashed into them last night.
The victims had succumbed to their injuries when they were admitted to the National Hospital. Police said the driver has been arrested.
