## Title: Meethotamulla tragedy; death toll reaches 11
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 April 2017 06:24 am

Meethotamulla tragedy; death toll reaches 11


	

Ten more persons died while receiving treatment at the National hospital, following the Meetotamulla garbage dump collapse, bringing the death toll to ten, hospital sources said.

Earlier the rescue workers found the body of a 12-year-old boy.
