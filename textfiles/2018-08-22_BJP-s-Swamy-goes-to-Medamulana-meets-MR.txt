## Title: BJP's Swamy goes to Medamulana, meets MR
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 August 2018 12:44 pm

BJP's Swamy goes to Medamulana, meets MR


	

Bharatiya Janata Party's (BJP) Dr. Subramanian Swamy arrived in Sri Lanka today and called on former President Mahinda Rajapaksa at Medamulana.

In a tweet, Mr. Rajapaksa said, “I welcomed my good friend Dr. Subramanian Swamy and I’m honoured to accept his invitation to speak in New Delhi,” 

He said Dr. Swamy is a long-time friend of Sri Lanka who has always vehemently spoken out against LTTE terrorism.

“He has always had Sri Lanka’s best interest at heart,” Mr. Rajapaksa said.
