## Title: Istanbul attack: No reports of Lankans affected
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 29 June 2016 10:55 am

Istanbul attack: No reports of Lankans affected


	

While condemning the three suicide bombs attack in the main international airport in Istanbul which killed about 36 people and wounded close to 150, Ministry of External Affairs stated that there were no reports of Sri Lankans being affected as of now.

According to the reports, the vast majority of those killed were Turkish nationals.
