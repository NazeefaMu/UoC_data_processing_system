## Title: ‘Battle of Brothers’
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 24 February 2017 12:04 pm

‘Battle of Brothers’


	



A vehicle parade ahead of the Battle of the Brothers encounter, between Isipathana College and Thurstan College in Colombo was held yesterday with the participation of students from both schools.  The annual encounter will take place on Friday (24) and Saturday (25) at the Sinhalese Sports Club (SSC).
