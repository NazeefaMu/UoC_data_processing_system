## Title: Athurugiriya shooting: Weapon found
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 August 2016 01:30 pm

Athurugiriya shooting: Weapon found


	

The weapon which was used to shoot the Lieutenant Colonel Pradeep Kumara Nettasinghe’s wife was found in Diyawannawa Lake in Battaramulla today, police said.

The Lt. Colonel was arrested earlier today in connection with the shooting incident in which his wife was injured after she was shot at by an unidentified gang in Athurugiriya. (Irangi Edirisinghe)
