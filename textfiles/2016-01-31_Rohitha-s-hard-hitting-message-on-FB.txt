## Title: Rohitha’s hard-hitting message on FB
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 31 January 2016 05:52 pm

Rohitha’s hard-hitting message on FB


	

Hours after the arrest of his brother Yoshitha Rajapaksa, former President Mahinda Rajapaksa's third son Rohitha in a Facebook post, warned the ‘Yahapalanaya’ government with the message: “Dear Yahapalanaya..... You just stood on the tail of THE lion, now don't expect the lion, not to rip u in to parts.”

He also shared a picture of Yoshitha in handcuffs with the Facebook post.
