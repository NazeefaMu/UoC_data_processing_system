## Title: Two deputy ministers and a state minister appointed
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 April 2016 12:48 pm

Two deputy ministers and a state minister appointed


	

Two deputy ministers and a state minister were appointed by President Maithripala Sirisena today at the Presidential Secretariat.

UNP MP Palitha Thewarapperuma and UPFA MP Manusha Nanayakkara were appointed as Deputy Ministers while UPFA MP Lakshman Seneviratne was appointed as a State Minister.

MP Nanayakkara was sworn in as Foreign Employment Deputy Minister while MP Thewarapperuma was sworn in as Internal Affairs, Wayamba Development and Cultural Affairs Deputy Minister.

Meanwhile, MP Seneviratne was sworn in as Science, Technology and Research State Minister.
