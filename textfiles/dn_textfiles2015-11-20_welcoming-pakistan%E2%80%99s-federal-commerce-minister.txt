## Title: Welcoming Pakistan’s Federal Commerce Minister
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Friday, November 20, 2015 - 01:00

Welcoming Pakistan’s Federal Commerce Minister

 
A dinner reception was held in the honour of Pakistan’s Federal Commerce Minister Engr. Khurram Dastgir Khan last week at the Hilton Residences Union Ballroom. The minister was here in Sri Lanka for a three day official visit to boost bilateral investment and trade between the two countries.
