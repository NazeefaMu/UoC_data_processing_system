## Title: Lake House employees demand pay hike
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 September 2016 11:22 am

Lake House employees demand pay hike


	

A group of employees of the Lake House Publishers launched a protest campaign outside the Lake House building in Fort, Colombo today demanding a salary increment.  Several employees were seen protesting on the roof of the building.
