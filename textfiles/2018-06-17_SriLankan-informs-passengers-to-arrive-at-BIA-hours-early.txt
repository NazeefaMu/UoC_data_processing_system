## Title: SriLankan informs passengers to arrive at BIA 4hours early
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 June 2018 09:00 pm

SriLankan informs passengers to arrive at BIA 4hours early


	

All passengers requested to arrive at the Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA) 4 hrs prior to departure time from tomorrow (18) due renovation work at BIA, issuing a travel alert SriLankan Airlines said today.
