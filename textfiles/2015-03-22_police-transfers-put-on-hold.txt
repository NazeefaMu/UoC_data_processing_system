## Title: Police transfers put on hold
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 March 2015 09:19 am

Police transfers put on hold


	

President Maithripala Sirisena has stopped all Police transfers with effect from today, the presidential media said.
He had directed that these transfers would be decided by a special three-member committee comprising the Presidential Secretary, Law and Order Ministry Secretary and the Police chief.
