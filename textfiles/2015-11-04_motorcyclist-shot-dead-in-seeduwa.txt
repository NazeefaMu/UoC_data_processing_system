## Title: Motorcyclist shot dead in Seeduwa
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 November 2015 02:06 pm

Motorcyclist shot dead in Seeduwa


	

A man riding a motorcycle was shot dead by unidentified men arrived on another motorcycle near the Muthuwadiya Bridge in Seeduwa a short while ago, Police said.
Seeduwa Police are investigating.
