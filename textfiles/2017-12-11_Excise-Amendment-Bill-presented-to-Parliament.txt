## Title: Excise Amendment Bill presented to Parliament
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 December 2017 12:00 pm

Excise Amendment Bill presented to Parliament


	

The Excise Amendment Bill, under which rules would be made to obtain a license when tapping coconut and palmyrah trees, was presented to Parliament today.

Under the section 15 of the Bill, a license has to be obtained to tap coconut and palmyrah trees. No license is needed to tap Kithul trees according to the legislation. (Yohan Perera and Ajith Siriwardana)
