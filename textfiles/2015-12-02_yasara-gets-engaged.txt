## Title: Yasara gets engaged to Fazil
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 2 December 2015 06:19 pm

Yasara gets engaged to Fazil


	


	Former Head of Channel at the Carlton Sports Network, Yasara Abeynayake, daughter of veteran film director Ananda Abeynayake, got engaged to former Sri Lanka Rugby 7s captain and Kandy Sports Club skipper Fazil Marija in a simple ceremony.
	The ceremony was held at a star-class hotel in Colombo, today, with several politicians and celebrities attending. Here are some of the pictures of the event circulating in the social media.
