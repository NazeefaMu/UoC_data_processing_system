## Title: Aluthgamage fails to fulfill bail conditions, remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 April 2018 04:57 pm

Aluthgamage fails to fulfill bail conditions, remanded


	

MP Mahindananda Aluthgamage, who was arrested and released on bail by the Fort Magistrate’s Court, was remanded a short while ago as he failed to fulfil bail conditions.

MP Aluthgamage was arrested by the Financial Crimes Investigation Division (FCID) today on charges of misappropriating government funds amounting to Rs.39 million in 2014 to buy Carrom and Checker-boards.

He has failed to provide evidence that his passport is with the Colombo High Court.Earlier, the Fort Magistrate’s Court imposed a travel ban on the MP. (Yoshitha Perera)
