## Title: UK’s Prince William, Kate have third child
## Section: International
## Date: 24 April 2018 10:11 am

UK’s Prince William, Kate have third child


	



REUTERS, 23rd APRIL, 2018- Kate, the wife of Prince William, gave birth to a boy at a hospital in London on Monday, the third child for the British royal couple and now fifth in line to the throne.


Kensington Palace said the baby was born at 1001 GMT and weighs 8 lbs 7oz (3.8 kg). William was present for the birth at St Mary’s Hospital in west London where their other two children, George and Charlotte, were also born, it added.
