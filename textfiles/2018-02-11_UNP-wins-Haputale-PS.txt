## Title: UNP wins Haputale PS
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 February 2018 09:28 am

UNP wins Haputale PS


	

The UNP won the Haputale Pradeshiya Sabha garnering 9,746 votes and 7 seats. The SLPP has secured 8,375 votes along with 6 seats. The UPFA which secured 8,164 votes has also won 6 seats.
