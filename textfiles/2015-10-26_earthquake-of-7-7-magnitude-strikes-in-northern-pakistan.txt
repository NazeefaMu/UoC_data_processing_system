## Title: Earthquake of 7.7 magnitude strikes in northern Pakistan
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 October 2015 09:46 am

Earthquake of 7.7 magnitude strikes in northern Pakistan


	

A powerful earthquake of 7.7 magnitude has struck in northern Pakistan, causing tremors in that country, Afghanistan and India.
Buildings in the Pakistani capital Islamabad rattled for at least two minutes, while tremors were also felt in the Afghan capital Kabul.
