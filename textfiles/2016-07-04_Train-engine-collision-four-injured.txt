## Title: Train engine collision; four injured
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 July 2016 06:40 pm

Train engine collision; four injured


	

Four passengers had been injured when a locomotive engine collided with the train plying from Rambukkana to Colombo at Wilwatte Railway Station this evening, the Railway Control Unit said.


The injured had been admitted to the Mirigama Hospital.
