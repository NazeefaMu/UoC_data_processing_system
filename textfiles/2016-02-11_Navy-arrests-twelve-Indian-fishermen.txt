## Title: Navy arrests twelve Indian fishermen
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 February 2016 12:45 pm

Navy arrests twelve Indian fishermen


	

Twelve Indian fishermen were arrested by Sri Lanka Navy along with two fishing trawlers while poaching in Sri Lankan waters North-West of Thalaimannar last night.


The arrested fishermen were handed over to the officials of the Department of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources for further action. (Wanni Romesh Madushanka)
