## Title: President meets Iranian President Rouhani
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 13 May 2018 01:14 pm

President meets Iranian President Rouhani


	

President Maithripala Sirisena is on a two-day official visit to Iran met Iranian President Hassan Rouhani today.

The President left for Iran yesterday at the invitation of the Iranian President.

During his visit, the President is expected to obtain many economic and developmental benefits to Sri Lanka.
