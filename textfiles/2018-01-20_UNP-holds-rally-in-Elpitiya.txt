## Title: UNP holds rally in Elpitiya
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 20 January 2018 06:15 am

UNP holds rally in Elpitiya


	



The United National Party (UNP) held a campaign in line with the local government election in Elpitiya yesterday. Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe and other allies of the party including Ministers Rajitha Senaratne, Gayantha Karunatilake, and Ajith P Perera were present at the rally.
