## Title: Two men shot dead in Elpitiya
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 November 2015 05:13 am

Two men shot dead in Elpitiya


	

Two men aged between 20 and 25 who were travelling in a motorcycle had been shot dead in Ura Gaha, Elpitiya this morning by two unidentified gunmen.
After the shooting, the two gunmen had escaped from the scene in a motorcycle, police said.
Police said the victims; residents of Elpitiya area had died on the spot. (Piyumi Fonseka)
