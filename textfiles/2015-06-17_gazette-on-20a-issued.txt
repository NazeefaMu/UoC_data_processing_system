## Title: Gazette on 20A issued
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 June 2015 04:17 am

Gazette on 20A issued


	

The gazette notification on the 20th Amendment to the Constitution had been published, the Government Printer said today.
The gazette notification in English language had been printed and the Sinhala and Tamil versions are to be printed and published later today, the Printing Department said.
