## Title: Indian Army Chief arrives in SL
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 29 November 2015 11:10 am

Indian Army Chief arrives in SL


	

Indian Army chief General Dalbir Singh arrived in Sri Lanka a short while ago.
The 6-member delegation was welcomed by SL Army Officers and the Indian High Commissioner to Sri Lanka at the Bandaranaike International Airport.
General Dalbir Singh will be in the country till December 04. During his visit he will discuss upgrading country’s ageing warplanes, tanks and air defense guns. (T.K.G.Kapila)
