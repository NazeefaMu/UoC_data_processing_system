## Title: Mohammed Faroz wins 20th edition of PB Foreign Remittance Gold Fortune
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 23 November 2016 12:00 am

Mohammed Faroz wins 20th edition of PB Foreign Remittance Gold Fortune


	



 

A.G Mohammed Faroz, an account holder at the Nintavur People’s Bank branch was named the 20th lucky winner of the weekly People’s Bank Foreign Remittance Gold Fortune 2016.
Present to conduct the draw was Chandrasiri Fernando - Assistant General Manager(Banking Supporting Services), People’s Bank and Anoma Mudduwa- Assistant General Manager, People’s Bank.
The draw was launched with the aim of promoting the use of People’s InstantRemit, People’s e-Remittance, Swift and Telex amongst those living and working abroad when sending money to their loved ones in Sri Lanka.
The promotion encourages people to make remittances of any amount to be eligible to receive a gold sovereign coin on a weekly basis for a period of 28 weeks up to December 2016.
