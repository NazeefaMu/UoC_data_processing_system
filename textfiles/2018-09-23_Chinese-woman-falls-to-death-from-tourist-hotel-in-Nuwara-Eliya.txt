## Title: Chinese woman falls to death from tourist hotel in Nuwara Eliya
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 September 2018 11:06 am

Chinese woman falls to death from tourist hotel in Nuwara Eliya


	

A female Chinese national was killed when she is reported to have fallen from a tourist hotel at Magastota in Nuwara Eliya this morning.

Police said the woman died after falling from the balcony of the hotel’s third floor.

The Nuwara Eliya Police are investigating the incident. (Text and pics by Shelton Hettiarachchi)
