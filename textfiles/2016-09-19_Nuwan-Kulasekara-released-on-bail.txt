## Title: Nuwan Kulasekara released on bail
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 September 2016 10:24 pm

Nuwan Kulasekara released on bail


	

Fast bowler Nuwan Kulasekara who was arrested following a fatal accident involving his vehicle at Ranmuthugala, Kadawatha was released on bail this evening, Police said.

He was later released after being produced before Mahara Additional Magistrate Ms. Thanuja Jayathunga on a surety bail of Rs.100,000. (Chaturanga Chandrasekara)
