## Title: Discussion between President and PM on new CB Governor
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 29 June 2016 04:33 pm

Discussion between President and PM on new CB Governor


	

President Maithripala Sirisena and Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe are reported to be discussing the appointment of the Central Bank’s new Governor.

The new Governor is expected to be named shortly.
