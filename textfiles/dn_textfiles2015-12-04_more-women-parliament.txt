## Title: More women in Parliament
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Friday, December 4, 2015 - 01:00

More women in Parliament

The Cabinet approved to amend the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance to increase women's representation and introduce the ward system, Minister Rajitha Senaratne said. This will make the election more efficient and productive, Local Government and Provincial Councils Deputy Minister Karunarathna Paranavithana said.
