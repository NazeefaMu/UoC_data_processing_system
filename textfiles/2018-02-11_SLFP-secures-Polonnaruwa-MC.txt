## Title: SLFP secures Polonnaruwa MC
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 February 2018 03:02 pm

SLFP secures Polonnaruwa MC


	

The Sri Lanka Freedom Party (SLFP) led by President Maithripala Sirisena was able to secure the Polonnaruwa Municipal Council with 9,020 votes and five seats.

The Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna (SLPP) garnered 5,530 votes and three council seats. The United National Party (UNP) won two seats.
