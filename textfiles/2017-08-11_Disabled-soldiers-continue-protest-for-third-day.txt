## Title: Disabled soldiers continue protest for third day
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 11 August 2017 01:01 pm

Disabled soldiers continue protest for third day


	



 The protest launched by a group of disabled soldiers continued for the third day outside the Fort Railway Station in Colombo today. The protesters also organized a march towards Colombo Fort from the Technical Junction causing traffic along the Olcott Mawatha. Pics by Nisal Baduge
