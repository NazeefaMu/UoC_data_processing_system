## Title: Thewarapperuma fiasco: Parents of 9 students arrested
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 July 2016 01:24 pm

Thewarapperuma fiasco: Parents of 9 students arrested


	

The parents of the nine students who were admitted to the Meegahathenna Primary school by Deputy Minister Palitha Thewarapperuma, were arrested by the police on charges of unlawful assembly and illegal entry to the school.
