## Title: Stirring the pot
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 13 July 2016 04:15 pm

Stirring the pot


	



US Assistant Secretary of State for South and Central Asian Affairs Nisha Biswal today participated in ‘Nuga Sewana’ TV programme telecast on Sri Lanka Rupavahini where she was taught to make delicious local food by Sri Lanka’s top chef Dr. Publis Silva.
