## Title: Homagama mayhem: Two more monks surrender
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 February 2016 03:30 pm

Homagama mayhem: Two more monks surrender


	

Sihala Ravaya convener the Ven. Magalkande Sudaththa Thera and the Ven Badalkumbure Ariyashantha Thera today surrendered to the Homagama Police in connection with last week’s pandemonium outside the Homgama Magistrate’s court.  


Police said the two monks would be produced in the Court later today. Meanwhile, Sudaththa Thera said six more monks would surrender to the police tomorrow. (Buddhika Kumarasiri)
