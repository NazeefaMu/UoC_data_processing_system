## Title: Will not be with the SL team: Mahela
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 20 March 2016 11:55 am

Will not be with the SL team: Mahela


	

Former Sri Lankan cricketer Mahela Jayawardena rejected rumors published on social media stating that he will join the Sri Lankan team.

In a tweet Mahela said, “I don’t know where all this is coming from. I am doing studio commentary during the game, will not be with the SL team.”
