## Title: Perahera Sandeshaya handed over
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 8 August 2017 07:33 pm

Perahera Sandeshaya handed over


	



Diyawadana Nilame Nilanga Dela handed over the Perahera Sandeshaya (Sannasa) to President Maithripala Sirisena to inform the successful completion of the Kandy Esala Perahera, at the President’s House in Kandy, today. Pics by President’s Media
