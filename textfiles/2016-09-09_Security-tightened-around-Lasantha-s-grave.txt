## Title: Security tightened around Lasantha’s grave
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 9 September 2016 03:50 pm

Security tightened around Lasantha’s grave


	



Police have provided security to the cemetery in which the former Sunday Leader Editor Lasantha Wickrematunge is buried. Wickrematunge’s body is to be exhumed on September 27, following a Court Order to conduct a second post-mortem examination. He was assassinated in January 2009.
