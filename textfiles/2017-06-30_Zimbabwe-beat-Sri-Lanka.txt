## Title: Zimbabwe beat Sri Lanka
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 30 June 2017 07:30 pm

Zimbabwe beat Sri Lanka


	

Zimbabwe beat Sri Lanka by six wickets in a record run-chase in the first ODI in Galle today.  Zimbabwe made 322 for four wickets with 14 balls to spare in reply to Sri Lanka’s 316 for five wickets. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
