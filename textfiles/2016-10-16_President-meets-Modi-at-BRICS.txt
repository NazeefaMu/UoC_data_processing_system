## Title: President meets Modi at BRICS
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 October 2016 09:46 am

President meets Modi at BRICS


	

President Maithripala Sirisena met Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi at the BRICS Outreach Summit in Goa, India this morning.

The President tweeted that it was a pleasure to meet the Indian neighbor and that he hoped to discuss the development of the region.
