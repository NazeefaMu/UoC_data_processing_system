## Title: Five year-old girl missing in Kotadeniyawa
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 12 September 2015 05:32 am

Five year-old girl missing in Kotadeniyawa


	

A five year-old girl in Akarangaha in Kotadeniyawa has disappeared last night, while she was sleeping at home, the Police said.
The child had been at home with her parents, brother, grandfather and grandmother when she went missing.
The Police said they suspected that the girl might have been abducted by someone, who crept into the house through a window of the partly built house.
Police said they have launched a search operation to locate the child.(Tony Karunarathne)
