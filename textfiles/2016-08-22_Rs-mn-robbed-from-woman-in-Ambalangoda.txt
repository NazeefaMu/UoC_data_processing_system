## Title: Rs.1.8 mn robbed from woman in Ambalangoda
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 August 2016 01:12 pm

Rs.1.8 mn robbed from woman in Ambalangoda


	

Rs. 1.8 million in cash belonging to a woman had been robbed by two motorcyclists in Ambalangoda today, the Police said.

The Police said the woman had withdrawn the money from a private bank to be deposited in another bank.

The suspects had used a knife to threaten the woman before making away.
