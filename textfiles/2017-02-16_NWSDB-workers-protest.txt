## Title: NWSDB workers protest
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 16 February 2017 10:31 am

NWSDB workers protest


	



The trade union collective of the National Water Supply and Drainage Board yesterday staged a protest calling the authorities to resolve their salary anomalies and issues pertaining to promotions. The protest was held near NWSDB Office in Pelawatte, Battaramulla. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
