## Title: Sri Lankan Heroes: do we have any now?
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 November 2015 07:12 am

Sri Lankan Heroes: do we have any now?


	

Every country produces great leaders and heroes for an era. Sri Lanka is a country which has produced many heroes in every sphere in the past, including social, political, economic and cultural arenas.
However, whether we have produced any real heroes in the modern times is a question that many have raised these days. In the Sri Lankan political drudgery, has anyone managed to rise up as a true hero that the country can be proud of? In an attempt to get the opinions and views of the citizens, Daily Mirror gives the readers the chance to voice their thoughts who they believe are the real heroes in the country right now.
