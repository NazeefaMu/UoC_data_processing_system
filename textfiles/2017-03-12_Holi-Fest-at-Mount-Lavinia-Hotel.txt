## Title: Holi Fest at Mount Lavinia Hotel
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 12 March 2017 04:45 pm

Holi Fest at Mount Lavinia Hotel


	



The colourful Indian festival of Holi was celebrated at the Paradise Beach at the Mount Lavinia Hotel this morning. The event has been organised by the Colombo Indians and many local and foreign families were seen participating at the event. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
