## Title: Yoshitha’s Mt. Lavinia land to be surveyed
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 September 2016 03:39 pm

Yoshitha’s Mt. Lavinia land to be surveyed


	

Mount Lavinia Additional Magistrate Sulochana Weerasinghe today ordered the land belonging to Yoshitha Rajapaksa and his grandaunt Daisy Forestat in Mihindu Mawatha, Mt. Lavinia be surveyed on September 20.

The court was informed that the document relating to purchase of the land, valued at Rs. 50 million, including the land deed were questionable.(Dayarathna Pathirana)
