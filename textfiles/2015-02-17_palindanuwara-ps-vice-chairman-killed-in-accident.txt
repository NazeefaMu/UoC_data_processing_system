## Title: Palindanuwara PS Vice Chairman killed in accident
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 February 2015 03:49 pm

Palindanuwara PS Vice Chairman killed in accident


	

Vice Chairman of the Palindanuwara Pradeshiya Sabha was killed when his car veered off the Road at Makeli Ella in Baduraliya this evening, police said.
They said the 62- year-old R. Banduprema who was at the wheel at the time of the accident was killed on the spot.
