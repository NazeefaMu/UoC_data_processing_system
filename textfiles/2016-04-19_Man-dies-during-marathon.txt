## Title: Man dies during marathon
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 April 2016 11:53 am

Man dies during marathon


	

A 38-year-old man died while running a marathon at an Avurudu festival in Sooriyawewa this morning.

Police said the victim, a resident of Sooriyawewa had collapsed during the marathon organised by the Sooriyawewa district secretariat office and the Pradeshiya sabha.

He had died due to cardiac arrest, Police said.
