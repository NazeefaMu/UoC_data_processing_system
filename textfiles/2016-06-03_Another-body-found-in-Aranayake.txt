## Title: Another body found in Aranayake
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 June 2016 12:10 pm

Another body found in Aranayake


	

Another body was found today at the Aranayake landslide site sixteen days after the tragedy in Samaasara Kanda.

The body was found near a stream about a kilometer away from the landslide site. (Tharindu Jayawardane)
