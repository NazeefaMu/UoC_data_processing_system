## Title: Video: Basil further remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 May 2015 04:49 am

Video: Basil further remanded


	

Former Economic Development Minister Basil Rajapaksa, who was remanded till today, has been brought to Kaduwela Magistrate's Court this morning.
Mr. Rajapaksa, ministry’s former Secretary Nihal Jayatilake and former Divineguma fund Director General R.R.K. Ranawaka were further remand till May 7 by the Magistrate.
They were arrested by the Police Financial Crimes Investigation Division (FCID) on April 22 and remanded till May 5 by the Kaduwela Magistrate on April 23.
