## Title: Ranil at Viharamahadevi Balika Vidyalaya
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 24 June 2016 08:27 pm

Ranil at Viharamahadevi Balika Vidyalaya


	



Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe today declared open the Indoor stadium of Viharamahadevi Balika Vidyalaya in Kiribathgoda. State Minister of defence Ruwan Wijewardene and Principal of the school were present at the event. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
