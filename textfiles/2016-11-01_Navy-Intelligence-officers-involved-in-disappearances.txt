## Title: Navy Intelligence officers involved in disappearances
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 November 2016 11:54 am

Navy Intelligence officers involved in disappearances


	

Navy intelligence officers in Welisara were directly involved in the disappearance of two individuals in 2009, the Criminal Investigations Department (CID) informed the Colombo Magistrates Court today.

Filing a comprehensive report before Colombo Additional Magistrate Nishantha peiris, the CID stated that Wadiwel Pakkilisami and Rathnasami Paramanandan were allegedly abducted by unknown individuals in a white van.
According to the CID both Pakkilisami and Paramanadan were not suspected of being engaged in terrorist activities nor involved in such activities nor aided such activities. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
