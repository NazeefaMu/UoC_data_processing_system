## Title: Let's build on 2015
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 7 February 2018 10:18 pm

Let's build on 2015


	



UNP’s final rally was held in Maradana this evening with the participation of Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe where he told the people not to lose what they achieved in 2015 and to use their votes wisely. UNP Mayoral candidate for Colombo Municipal Council Rosy Senanayake and UNP MP Mujibur Rahaman were also present. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
