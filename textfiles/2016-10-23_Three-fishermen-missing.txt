## Title: Three fishermen missing
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 October 2016 08:08 am

Three fishermen missing


	

Three fishermen from Thaleimannar who went out to sea on Friday are reportedly missing, police said.

A complaint had been lodged at the police station yesterday. The fishermen aged 22, 23 and 25 were identified as residents of Cable House in Thaleimannar.
