## Title: Thai Pongal Festival at Temple Trees
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 15 January 2018 10:36 pm

Thai Pongal Festival at Temple Trees


	



The National Thai Pongal Festival which was organized by the Prime Minister's office together with the Ministry of Resettlement Rehabilitation Prison Reforms and Hindu Affairs, was held at Temple Trees yesterday. Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe, Leader of Opposition R Sampanthan, Ministers D M Swamynathan, Mano Ganesan  and MP Ravi Karunanayake participated in the festival.
