## Title: Excise officer arrested with Kerala Ganja
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 12 November 2017 09:39 am

Excise officer arrested with Kerala Ganja


	

An officer attached to the Excise Department office in Vavuniya was arrested on charges of possessing Kerala Ganja during a raid in Kaththankudi yesterday.

Police said the 25-year-old suspect, a resident of Kalmunai, was arrested by the Baticaloa Division Crime Prevention Unit along with 775 grams of Kerala Ganja.

He was to be produced in the Baticloa Magistrate’s Court.
