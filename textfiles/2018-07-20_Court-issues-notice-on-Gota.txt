## Title: Court issues notice on Gota
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 20 July 2018 11:39 am

Court issues notice on Gota


	

The Colombo Additional Magistrate today issued notice on seven suspects including former Defence Secretary Gotabaya Rajapaksa to appear in the Colombo Additional Magistrate’s Court over the alleged misappropriation of public funds for the construction of the D. A. Rajapaksa memorial museum at Weeraketiya. (Farook Thajudeen)
