## Title: Multitasking army troops at Meetotamulla
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 18 April 2017 08:16 am

Multitasking army troops at Meetotamulla


	



A multiplicity of different roles and tasks, were being carried out by Army troops in affected Meetotamulla areas, together with members of sister services, Police and the STF, took a different turn yesterday morning with more focus on the survivors while Army rescue operations were continuing uninterrupted using heavy machinery. (Army.lk)
