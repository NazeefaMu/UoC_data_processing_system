## Title: Swinging to rhythm of jazz
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 27 February 2018 03:11 am

Swinging to rhythm of jazz


	



Jazz sensation Wycliffe Gordon and sextet, staged a performance for the residents of Slave Island yesterday as part of the 70th anniversary celebration of US - Sri Lanka Partnership. The group, hosted by the US Embassy in Sri Lanka, performed in and around Colombo on Saturday and will be staging more performances in Galle and Matara. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
