## Title: President at SLFP youth convention
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 2 April 2017 08:21 pm

President at SLFP youth convention


	

President Maithripala Sirisena today attended the SLFP Youth Front Convention at the Sugathadasa indoor stadium Colombo.  He was seen launching the SLFP Youth Front’s official website at the event. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
