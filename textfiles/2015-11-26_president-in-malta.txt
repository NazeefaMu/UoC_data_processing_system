## Title: President in Malta
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 26 November 2015 05:27 pm

President in Malta


	


President Maithripala Sirisena, left for Malta today to attend the Commonwealth Heads of Government Meeting (CHOGM) – 2015, to be held at Fort St. Angelo in Birgu from November 27-29. Picture shows President Sirisena with the first lady at the Malta International Air Port.
