## Title: Tissa Central cricket champions
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, February 19, 2016 - 01:00

Tissa Central cricket champions

 
Tissa Central College emerged champions of Kalutara cricket inter schools boys cricket tournament 2016 conducted by Kalutara Education Office Sports Unit held at Morontuduwa Dhammananda Navoya school ground recently. Sixteen Cricket teams participation in the tournament and Dhammanda Navoya Vidyalaya Morontuduwa was the runners up of the tournament.
