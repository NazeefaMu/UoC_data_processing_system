## Title: VAT increase: Galle traders stage protest
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 June 2016 10:53 am

VAT increase: Galle traders stage protest


	

Many of the Shops in Galle have been closed today as a protest against the government’s move to increase the Value Added Tax (VAT) to 15%.

The protest has been organized by the Galle District Chamber of Commerce and Industry Board.

Home Affairs Minister Wajira Abeywardena visited that area and assured the protesters that he would try to resolve this matter after discussion with the Finance Minister. (D.G. Sugathapala)
