## Title: Man shot dead at Mt. Lavinia
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 July 2016 10:21 am

Man shot dead at Mt. Lavinia


	

A 39-year-old man was shot dead by two unidentified men in Mount Lavinia last night, Police said.

They said the victim was shot at in front of his house by two persons arrived on a motorcycle.

The victim was identified as Dinesh Sanjaya, a resident from Kawdana in Mount Lavinia.

The suspects have not been arrested yet.
