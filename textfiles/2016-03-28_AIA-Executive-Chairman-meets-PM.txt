## Title: AIA Executive Chairman meets PM
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 28 March 2016 12:00 am

AIA Executive Chairman meets PM


	

Representing the largest independent publicly listed Pan-Asian life insurance group with a presence in 18 markets across the Asia-Pacific region, AIA Executive Chairman Mark Tucker on Wednesday called on Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe in the latter’s office at the parliamentary complex. Tucker discussed Sri Lanka’s future potential and opportunities for investments. AIA has so far invested close to US $ 300 million in Sri Lanka. Minister Sagala Rathnayake, AIA Sri Lanka CEO Shah Rouf and Dinesh Weerakkody participated in the discussion.
