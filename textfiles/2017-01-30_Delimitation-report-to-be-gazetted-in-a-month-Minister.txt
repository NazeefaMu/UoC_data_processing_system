## Title: Delimitation report to be gazetted in a month: Minister
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 January 2017 01:35 pm

Delimitation report to be gazetted in a month: Minister


	

The Delimitation Committee Report will be gazetted within a month, Provincial and Local Government Minister Faizer Mustapha informed the Supreme Court today through his lawyer.

He said this when a Fundamental Rights petition filed by People’s Action for Free and Fair Elections (PAFFREL) was taken up for hearing this morning.

The committee report was handed over to the minister on January 17. (KB)
