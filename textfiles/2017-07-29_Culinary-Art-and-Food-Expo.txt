## Title: ‘Culinary Art and Food Expo’ 2017
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 29 July 2017 04:43 pm

‘Culinary Art and Food Expo’ 2017


	



The 17th edition of the ‘Culinary Art and Food Expo’ organized by the chefs’ Guild of Lanka and supported by professional exhibition organization, CDC event and travel Pvt.Ltd was launched yesterday at the Bandaranaike Memorial International Conference Hall (BMICH). The event was inaugurated by the President Maithripala Sirisena. Pix by Kithsiri De Mel
