## Title: Boat capsised: Five fishermen missing
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 February 2016 09:51 am

Boat capsised: Five fishermen missing


	

Five local fishermen have gone missing last night when their boat capsised after it collided with another ship while fishing in deep sea.


Police said six fishermen from Hikkaduwa and Dodanduwa had left Galle harbor last Thursday but only one of them returned.


Police said he returned in another vessel.
