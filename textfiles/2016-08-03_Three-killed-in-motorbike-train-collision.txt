## Title: Three killed in motorbike-train collision
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 August 2016 01:08 pm

Three killed in motorbike-train collision


	

A mother and her two children were killed when the motorcycle they were riding on collided with a Colombo-bound train from Kankasanthurai at a railway crossing in Weragala, Wellawa at Kurunegala today.
