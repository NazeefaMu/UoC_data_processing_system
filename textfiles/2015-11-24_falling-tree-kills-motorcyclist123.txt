## Title: Falling tree kills motorcyclist
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 November 2015 02:22 pm

Falling tree kills motorcyclist


	


	A 37-year-old motorcyclist was killed and at least two three wheelers were badly damaged when a roadside tree fell on them on Baseline road in Borella this evening due to heavy rains, Police said.
	They said the motorcyclist, a resident of Kelaniya, died on admission to the Colombo National Hospital.
	His wife, who was on the pillion, escaped unhurt. (Chaturanga Pradeep)
