## Title: Shyam murder: Vass, his son found guilty
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 November 2015 09:59 am

Shyam murder: Vass, his son found guilty


	

The High Court Trial-at-Bar has found former DIG Vass Gunawardane, his son and four others guilty of the charges of abduction and murder of businessman Mohommed Shyam.
