## Title: Gunaratnam re-remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 December 2015 08:11 am

Gunaratnam re-remanded


	

Frontline Socialist Party (FSP) polit-bureau member Kumar Gunaratnam was further remanded till December 30 by Kegalle Additional Magistrate Malani Jayawardane today.
He was arrested by the Police in Kegalle in November on charges of violating immigration laws. (RK)
