## Title: Sailor found dead in Kollupitiya
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 September 2016 09:50 am

Sailor found dead in Kollupitiya


	

The body of a sailor was found hanging in his room at a lodge in Kollupitiya last night.

Police said the deceased, a resident of Nugegoda, had checked in at the lodge last Friday. The body was recovered when the management informed the police.
