## Title: Future Minds-2015
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 20 June 2015 06:06 am

Future Minds-2015


	


Future Minds-2015, the Education and Career Guidance Exhibition organised by the Nalanda College for the 10th consecutive year was commenced at the BMICH in Colombo yesterday.  The exhibition was declared open by Higher Education Minister Sarath Amunugama and will conclude on Sunday.
