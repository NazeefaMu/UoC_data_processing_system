## Title: Torrential rains wreak havoc
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 26 May 2017 01:18 pm

Torrential rains wreak havoc


	

Torrential rains experienced across most parts of the country has left many people displaced. A number of cities including Ratnapura and Morawaka are severely affected by floods while at least 12 people were reported dead due to landslides and floods.
