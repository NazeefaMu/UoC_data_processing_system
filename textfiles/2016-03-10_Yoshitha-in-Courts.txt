## Title: Yoshitha in Courts
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 10 March 2016 03:00 pm

Yoshitha in Courts


	



Yoshitha Rajapaksa, son of former President Mahinda Rajapaksa, was brought to the Kaduwela Magistrate’s Court today where he was further remanded till March 24. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
