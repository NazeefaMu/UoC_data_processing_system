## Title: Isura Devapriya hands over baler machines
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 18 September 2017 03:08 pm

Isura Devapriya hands over baler machines


	



Western Province Chief Minister Isura Devapriya today handed over baler machines to be used in the project called “Batahira Kasala Nisi Magata”.
The event was held at the office of the Chief Minister. (Pics by Nisal Baduge)
