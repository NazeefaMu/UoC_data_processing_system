## Title: Book by late Sri Lal Gomes launched
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 25 August 2016 04:10 pm

Book by late Sri Lal Gomes launched


	



The book titled ‘Thiththa Rasa’ by the veteran photo-journalist Sri Lal Gomes, who passed away recently, was launched today at the Mahaweli Centre, Colombo. Picture shows his two grand daughters presenting the first copy of the book to the wife of late politician Gamini Dissanayake, Srimani. Pix By Pradeep Dilrukshana
