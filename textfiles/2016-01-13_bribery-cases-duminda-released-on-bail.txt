## Title: Bribery cases: Duminda released on bail
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 13 January 2016 05:13 am

Bribery cases: Duminda released on bail


	

Former MP Duminda Silva was released on bail in the three cases that had been filed by the Bribery Commission against him on charges of non-declaration of assets in 2011, 2013 and 2014.
He was released on a cash bail of Rs. 10, 000 and two sureties of Rs. one million each.(Shehan Chamika)
