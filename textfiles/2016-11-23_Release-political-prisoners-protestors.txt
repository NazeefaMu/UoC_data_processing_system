## Title: Release political prisoners- protestors
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 23 November 2016 10:06 pm

Release political prisoners- protestors


	



National Movement for the Release of Political Prisoners yesterday engaged in a protest march from Kollupitiya to the Presidential Secretariat demanding the release of political prisoners. They handed over a petition to the Presidential Secretariat. Pix by Damith Wickramasinghe
