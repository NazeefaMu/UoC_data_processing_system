## Title: <U+200B>Ven. Watinapaha Somananda Thera questioned
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 April 2015 02:13 pm

<U+200B>Ven. Watinapaha Somananda Thera questioned


	


		 		 
		 
			
A statement has been recorded from the former Coordinating Secretary to the President on Religious Affairs, Ven Watinapaha Somananda Thera, by the Financial Crimes Investigation Division(FCID)over distributing Rs. 600M worth clothing during the election period.
Police said that the FCID questioned him for six hours.
