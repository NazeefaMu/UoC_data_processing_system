## Title: FCID questions Prasanna Ranatunga’s wife
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 2 July 2015 09:08 am

FCID questions Prasanna Ranatunga’s wife


	

The wife of Western Provincial Council Chief Minister Prasanna Ranatunga is being questioned by the Financial Crime Investigation Division (FCID), the Police said.
It was reported she was being questioned over a land deal.
