## Title: Seylan Bank relocates Bogawantalawa branch
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 23 November 2016 12:00 am

Seylan Bank relocates Bogawantalawa branch


	



 

The Seylan Bank Bogawantalawa branch was relocated at Main Street, Bogawantalawa recently. 
The branch was declared open under the patronage of Chithral De Silva, Deputy General Manager, Branches of Seylan Bank PLC. The branch was moved to a new location to provide improved facilities to the bank’s clientele.
