## Title: Kumar Gunaratnam further remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 January 2016 05:45 am

Kumar Gunaratnam further remanded


	

Senior member of Frontline Socialist Party (FSP) Kumar Gunaratnam was further remanded till February 05, by the Kegalle Magistrate’s Court today.
He was arrested by the Police in the Anguruwella area in Kegalle on November 04, last year on charges of violating immigration laws. (RK)
