## Title: Sajin Vass re-remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 July 2016 10:45 am

Sajin Vass re-remanded


	

Former UPFA MP Sajin Vass Gunawardena was further remanded till July 8 by Colombo Chief Magistrate Gihan Pilapitiya today.

The former MP failed to declare how he earned Rs. 600 million from a private company. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
