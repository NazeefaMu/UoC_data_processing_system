## Title: India complete 9-0 sweep
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 7 September 2017 02:33 am

India complete 9-0 sweep


	



India completed a 9-0 sweep across all three formats, winning the one-off Twenty20 international at the R. Premadasa Stadium yesterday. India, chasing 171 to win reached the target with four balls to spare and seven wickets in hand. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
