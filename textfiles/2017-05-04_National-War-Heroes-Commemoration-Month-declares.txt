## Title: National War Heroes Commemoration Month declares
## Section: Caption Story

National War Heroes Commemoration Month declares


	



The first flag of War Heroes Commemoration Month was pinned on President Maithripala Sirisena by Chairman of Ranawiru Seva Authority Anoma Fonseka today declaring the National War Heroes Commemoration Month.Pix by President's Media
