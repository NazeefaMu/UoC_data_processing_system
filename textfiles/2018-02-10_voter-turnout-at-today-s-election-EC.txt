## Title: 65% voter turnout at today’s election: EC
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 10 February 2018 06:21 pm

65% voter turnout at today’s election: EC


	

The overall voter turnout at today’s Local Government election was reported as 65 per cent, Commissioner General of elections R.M.A.L. Ratnayake said.

Nearly 15.8 million voters were eligible to cast their votes today to elect 8,356 members to 341 local government bodies.
