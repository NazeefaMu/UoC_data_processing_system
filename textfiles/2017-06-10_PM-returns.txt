## Title: PM returns
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 10 June 2017 10:47 pm

PM returns


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe returned to the country today after a private visit to the USA. 
A group of UNP ministers and MPs were at the Bandaranaike International Airport in Katunayake to welcome the Premier.
Earlier, several government ministers confirmed that Premier Wickremesinghe was in the United States for medical treatment.
