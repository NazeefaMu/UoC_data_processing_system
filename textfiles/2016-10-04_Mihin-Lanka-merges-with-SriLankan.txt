## Title: Mihin Lanka merges with SriLankan
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 October 2016 09:38 am

Mihin Lanka merges with SriLankan


	

All routes of Mihin Lanka would be absorbed to SriLankan Airlines from October 30.

The decision had been made by the government of Sri Lanka, as part of its long-term strategy to restructure the local aviation industry and strengthen the national carrier’s offering and efficiency.
