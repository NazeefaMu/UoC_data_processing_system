## Title: Devaduta Perahera in Kelaniya
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 28 January 2018 07:50 pm

Devaduta Perahera in Kelaniya


	



The Devaduta Perahera in the Duruthu Perahera festival of the Kelaniya Rajamaha Vihara took place on Saturday night. It included performance of Upcountry, Low country, and Sabaragamuwa dancing groups and several other features that made it a colourful cultural pageant. Pix by Lal S.Kumara
