## Title: SLFP rebels invited for CC meeting tonight
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 August 2016 06:09 pm

SLFP rebels invited for CC meeting tonight


	

The members of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party (SLFP) who are in its Central Committee but aligned with the Joint Opposition were invited to Central Committee meeting, which would be held tonight, United People's Freedom Alliance (UPFA) General Secretary Mahinda Amaraweera said.
