## Title: Bath for Perahera elephant
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 9 February 2017 11:59 am

Bath for Perahera elephant


	

Elephants are being bathed ahead of the annual Nawam Maha Perahera of the Hunupitiya Gangaramaya Temple. The Perahera will be held with traditional performances today and tomorrow. Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
