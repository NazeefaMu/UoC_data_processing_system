## Title: Body of 34 year-old man found in Borella
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 8 January 2016 11:23 am

Body of 34 year-old man found in Borella


	

The body of a 34-year-old man was found in the upper floor of a two-storey house in Borella this afternoon following a phone call to the police emergency line 119.
Police said the body was identified as that of Liyanage Dinesh Kumara, who was a resident of Kaduwela.
