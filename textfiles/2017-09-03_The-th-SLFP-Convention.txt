## Title: The 66th SLFP Convention
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 3 September 2017 06:42 pm

The 66th SLFP Convention


	



The 66th Sri Lanka Freedom Party (SLFP) Convention held at the Campbell Park under the patronage of President Maithripala Sirisena this evening. Former president Chandrika Bandaranaike Kumaratunga, SLFP parliamentarians and party supporters were presented at the event. Pix by Nisal Baduge
