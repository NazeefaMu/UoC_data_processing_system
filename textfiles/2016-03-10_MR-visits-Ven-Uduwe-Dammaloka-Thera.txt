## Title: MR visits Ven. Uduwe Dammaloka Thera
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 10 March 2016 05:44 pm

MR visits Ven. Uduwe Dammaloka Thera


	

Former President Mahinda Rajapaksa today went to the Mahara prison to visit Ven. Uduwe Dammaloka Thera, who was remanded on charges of illegally keeping a baby elephant in his possession.


The Thera was remanded till March17 by the Colombo Additional Magistrate Nishantha Peiris yesterday.
