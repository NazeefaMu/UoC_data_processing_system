## Title: PM receives guard of honour
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 17 September 2017 10:34 pm

PM receives guard of honour


	


Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe was given a guard of honour at Mawathagama police station where he declared open a new building. IGP Pujith Jayasundara was also present at the event. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
