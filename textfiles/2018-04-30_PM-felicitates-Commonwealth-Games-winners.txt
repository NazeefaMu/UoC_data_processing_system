## Title: PM felicitates Commonwealth Games winners
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 30 April 2018 04:23 pm

PM felicitates Commonwealth Games winners


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe felicitated the winners of Commonwealth Games at Temple Trees in Colombo today. National Olympic Committee Chairman Suresh Subramanium, Secretary Maxwell De Silva and the entire team which represented Sri Lanka at the Commonwealth Games 2018 were present at the event. Sri Lanka won six medals including one silver medal at this year’s Commonwealth Games. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
