## Title: Lab controller of Horana rubber factory arrested
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 21 April 2018 05:38 pm

Lab controller of Horana rubber factory arrested


	

The Senior Laboratory Controller of the Horana Rubber Factory where five deaths were reported in the ammonia tank tragedy was arrested by the police today in connection with the incident.

The Police said the 42-year-old female laboratory controller would be produced in the Horana Magistrate's Court tomorrow.
