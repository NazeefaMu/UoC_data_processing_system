## Title: Subjects under State Defence Ministry released
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 February 2015 01:48 pm

Subjects under State Defence Ministry released


	

The gazette notification detailing the institutions that come under the State Defence Ministry has been issued.  Accordingly, the four institutions that have been allocated to the State Defence Ministry are-:
1. Defence Services Command and Staff College
2. Ranaviru Seva Authority
3. Defence Services School
4. National Cadet Corps
