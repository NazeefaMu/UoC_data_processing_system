## Title: His tie up with an underworld character raises eyebrows
## Section: Political Gossip
## Date: 26 July 2016 12:00 am

His tie up with an underworld character raises eyebrows


	



 

A vociferous advocate of yahapalanaya, this leading purohita with a connection to saline water has always been at the vanguard of the campaign against corruption.

 


The fact that a known underworld character has become a frequent visitor to this purohita has set gossipy tongues wagging. According to Ministry employees this privileged visitor would boldly walk into the purohita’s office on a regular basis to have a friendly chat for about half an hour.
