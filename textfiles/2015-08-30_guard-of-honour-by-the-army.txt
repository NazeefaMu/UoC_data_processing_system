## Title: Guard of Honour by the Army
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 30 August 2015 01:16 pm

Guard of Honour by the Army


	


The Sri Lanka Army carried out a rehearsal at the parliament grounds this afternoon to prepare for the guard of honour to be accorded at the ceremonial opening of the 15th parliament on Tuesday when the 225 members elected to parliament at the August 17 general election will meet for the first parliamentary sessions.
