## Title: No Sri Lankan casualties in Medina
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 July 2016 12:15 pm

No Sri Lankan casualties in Medina


	

No Sri Lankan casualties have been reported from the suicide bomb attack in Medina, Saudi Arabia, the Foreign Affairs Ministry said.

However, the ministry has published the contact details of the Sri Lankan Mission for queries or if any Sri Lankan is in need of assistance.

The Head of Chancery in Riyadh: +9661566634856 and the Acting Consul General in Jeddah: +966534969198.
