## Title: Ranjith, Mano resign from SriLankan Airlines
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 13 November 2018 01:01 pm

Ranjith, Mano resign from SriLankan Airlines


	

SriLankan Airlines Chairman Ranjith Fernando resigned today while Director Board Member Mano Tittawella had resigned on October 30, officials said.
