## Title: Joint opposition visits Yoshitha at Welikada
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 1 February 2016 01:49 pm

Joint opposition visits Yoshitha at Welikada


	



Several members of the joint opposition including Dinesh Gunawardena, Wimal Weerawansa, Gamini Lokuge and Bandula Gunawardana today visited Yoshitha Rajapaksa and the four others remanded at the Welikada prison. Pix by Nisal Baduge
