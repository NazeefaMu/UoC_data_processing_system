## Title: Protest for film 'Usawiya Nihandai'
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 19 October 2016 01:14 pm

Protest for film 'Usawiya Nihandai'


	

A collective of media and civil organisations launched a protest campaign outside the Colombo District Court where a case regarding the film 'Usawiya Nihandai' was taken up for hearing today. The protesters were demanding the restraining order issued on the film being screened be removed.
