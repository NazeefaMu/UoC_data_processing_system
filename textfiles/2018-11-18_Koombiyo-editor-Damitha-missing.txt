## Title: ‘Koombiyo’ editor Damitha missing
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 November 2018 11:15 am

‘Koombiyo’ editor Damitha missing


	

A complaint has been lodged with the Welikada Police stating that 40-year-old Damitha Chandrasiri, the editor and script writer of the ‘Koombiyo’ tele drama has been missing for few days.

Police said a friend of his has lodged the complaint that Mr. Chandrasiri, who is a resident of Anuradhapura, has been missing since several days from his boarding in Nawala.

Police stations have been notified about the disappearance.(Darshana Sanjeewa)
