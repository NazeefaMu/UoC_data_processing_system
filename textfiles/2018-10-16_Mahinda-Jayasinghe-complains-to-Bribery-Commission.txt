## Title: Mahinda Jayasinghe complains to Bribery Commission
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 16 October 2018 10:41 pm

Mahinda Jayasinghe complains to Bribery Commission


	



Ceylon's Teachers' Services Unions General Secretary Mahinda Jayasinghe today lodged a complaint to the Commission to Investigate Allegations of Bribery or Corruption against Education Minister Akila Viraj Kariyawasam. Pix by Nimalsiri Edirisinghe
