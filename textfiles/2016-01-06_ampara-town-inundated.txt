## Title: Ampara town inundated
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 6 January 2016 01:03 pm

Ampara town inundated


	


The Clock Tower area in Ampara town is flooded due to heavy rains in the Ampara District this morning. The Ampara-Inginiyagala Road was also flooded due to spilling over of the Konduwatuwana tank.  Pix by Wasantha Chandrapala
