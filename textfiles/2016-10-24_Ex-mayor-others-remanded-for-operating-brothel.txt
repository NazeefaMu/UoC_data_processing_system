## Title: Ex-mayor, 3 others remanded for operating brothel
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 October 2016 06:12 pm

Ex-mayor, 3 others remanded for operating brothel


	

Batticaloa’s former mayor Sivageetha Prabhakaran, her husband E.  Prabhakaran and two others were remanded till November 7 today on charges of operating a brothel at her residence in Batticaloa.

They were produced before Batticaloa Magistrate M. Ganesharaja today. (Jaufer Khan)
