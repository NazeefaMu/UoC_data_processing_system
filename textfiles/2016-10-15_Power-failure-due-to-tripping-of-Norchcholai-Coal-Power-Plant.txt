## Title: Power failure due to tripping of Norchcholai Coal Power Plant
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 October 2016 12:28 pm

Power failure due to tripping of Norchcholai Coal Power Plant


	

Power supply in several areas mainly in the Northern part of the country was disrupted due to tripping of the Norchcholai Coal Power Plant, Ceylon Electricity Board (CEB) said.

CEB Chairman Anura Wijepala said the tripping of the Anuradhapura-Puttalam transmission line led to the tripping of the Coal Power Plant.

He said power supply would be restored within an hour.
