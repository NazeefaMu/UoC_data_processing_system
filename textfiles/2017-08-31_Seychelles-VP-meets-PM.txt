## Title: Seychelles VP meets PM
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 31 August 2017 09:33 pm

Seychelles VP meets PM


	

Visiting Seychelles Vice President Vincent Meriton met Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe at Temple Trees today. Mr. Meriton  is in Sri Lanka to participate in the Indian Ocean Conference which is being held in Colombo. Pics by Pradeep Pathirana
