## Title: Civil Defence Force clean up P’naruwa Mahiya Wewa
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 28 June 2016 02:56 pm

Civil Defence Force clean up P’naruwa Mahiya Wewa


	

The Department of Civil Defence Force launched a sharamadana campaign today to clean up Mahiya Wewa in Polonnaruwa which is polluted by Salveniya and Hydrilla weeds.

Nearly 500 personnel participated in the shramadana campaign.

Civil Defense Force Director General Chandraratne Pallegama also participated in the campaign. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
