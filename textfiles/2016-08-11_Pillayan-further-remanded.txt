## Title: Pillayan further remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 August 2016 02:34 pm

Pillayan further remanded


	

The former Eastern Province chief minister Sivanesathurai Chandrakanthan alias Pillayan was today further remanded till August 24 by Batticaloa Magistrate M. Ganesharaja.


The CID arrested Pillayan on October 11 last year in connection with the assassination of former MP Joseph Pararajasingham.
