## Title: The world's tallest artificial Christmas tree
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 25 December 2016 07:33 pm

The world's tallest artificial Christmas tree


	



A towering Christmas tree claiming the world record in height for an artificial Christmas tree was unveiled on Christmas Eve. The structure is expected to remain up until January 6. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
