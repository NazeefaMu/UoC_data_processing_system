## Title: Arrested fishermen are being released immediately-BJP
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 13 April 2016 09:57 pm

Arrested fishermen are being released immediately-BJP


	



Claiming that the atrocity against the Tamil Nadu fishermen came down by the effort of the Modi government, India’s BJP national president Amit Shah on Wednesday said that the arrested Indian fishermen are being released immediately by the Sri Lankan authorities, The Times of India reported.


"The talks for the permanent solution got underway with the Sri Lanka. What the change we made is that there is no shooting and attack on the fishermen now. The arrested fishermen are being released immediately," Shah told media in Trichy.
