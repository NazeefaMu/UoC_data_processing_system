## Title: BOC excels at Excellence in Integrated Reporting Awards 2016
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 26 July 2016 12:00 am

BOC excels at Excellence in Integrated Reporting Awards 2016


	

BOC Assistant General Manager Budget and Strategic Planning and MIS Vipula Jayabahu receiving the award from Finance Minister Ravi Karunanayake in the presence of Industry and Commerce Minister Rishad Bathiudeen 

 

 

Bank of Ceylon (BOC) won the Merit award for Annual Report 2015 under state-owned enterprises category at the Excellence in Integrated Reporting Awards – 2016 conducted recently by the Institute of Certified Management Accountants of Sri Lanka to coincide with the CMA National Management Accounting Conference. 
The award ceremony was conducted with the aim of promoting the adoption of Integrated Reporting and enhancing the quality of corporate reporting in Sri Lanka. As a leading bank in the country, BOC always strives to step forward with the novel global initiatives. In 2015, it issued its third integrated Annual Report in line with guide lines of International Integrated Reporting Council (IIRC), which was recognized at CMA Awards 2016.
This is the second time CMA has conducted this award competition and the 10 best integrated reports and the overall winners were selected by a panel of five local and foreign judges. 
BOC Assistant General Manager, Budget, Strategic Planning and MIS Vipuala Jayabahu received the award at the CMA National Management Accounting Conference held recently.
