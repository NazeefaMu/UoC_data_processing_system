## Title: President inspects Muthurajawela sanctuary
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 22 March 2018 08:37 pm

President inspects Muthurajawela sanctuary


	

President Maithripala Sirisena today inspected Muthrajawala Sactuary and instructed to cease all the activities of construction and fillings of land paving way for environmental pollution, which prevails around the Sanctuary. Pics by President's Media
