## Title: Protesting Uni. students tear-gassed
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 December 2018 03:31 pm

Protesting Uni. students tear-gassed


	

Police fired tear gas and used water cannons to disperse protesting students of the Inter University Students’ Federation (IUSF) at the Ceramic Junction a short while ago, the Police said.
