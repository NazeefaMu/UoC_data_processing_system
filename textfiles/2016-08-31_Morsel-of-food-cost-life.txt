## Title: Morsel of food cost life
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 31 August 2016 11:47 am

Morsel of food cost life


	



An elephant which was searching for food in a paddy field was shot dead near the Kayanwala Lake in the Sigiriya sanctuary last night.

Wildlife Officers in Sigiriya found the body of the elephant with several gunshot wounds this morning. (Pix Kanchana Kumara Ariyadasa)
