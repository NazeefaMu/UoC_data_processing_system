## Title: Three policemen intredicted
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 April 2016 09:24 am

Three policemen intredicted


	

Three policemen attached to the Laggala police station have been interdicted in connection with the stolen of firearms from the police station, police said.


They said that one T-56 weapon and five revolvers were stolen from the Police station at around 1 am yesterday, by an unidentified man who entered the police station.
