## Title: Six students injured following clash in Moratuwa
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 February 2017 11:26 am

Six students injured following clash in Moratuwa


	



Six students of the Kothalawalapura College, Ratmalana were injured after a clash with a group of students of St. John's College, Nugegoda during a cricket match played at Janajaya Vidyalaya Ground, Moratuwa yesterday afternoon, Police said.

The injured were admitted to the Lunawa Hospital.

They said ten students of St. John's College were arrested in connection with the incident.
