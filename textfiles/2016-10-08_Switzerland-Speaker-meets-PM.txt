## Title: Switzerland Speaker meets PM
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 8 October 2016 11:26 am

Switzerland Speaker meets PM


	



Visiting Speaker of the National Council of the Federal Assembly of Switzerland Christa Markwalder met Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe at Temple Tress yesterday. Markwalder arrived in Sri Lanka on October 06 for a three day official visit. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
