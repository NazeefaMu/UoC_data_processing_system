## Title: PM distributes Samurdhi in Matale
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 16 September 2018 11:21 am

PM distributes Samurdhi in Matale


	



A ceremony to distribute Samurdhi to 5,000 people in the Matale District was held in Matale with Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe as the Chief Guest on Saturday. Minister of Social Empowerment P Harrision, Deputy Minister Ranjan Ramanayake, State Ministers Ranjith Aluvihare and Wasantha Aluvihare were also present.
