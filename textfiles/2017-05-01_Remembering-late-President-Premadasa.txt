## Title: Remembering late President Premadasa
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 1 May 2017 12:33 pm

Remembering late President Premadasa


	

President Maithripala Sirisena and Prime Minister Ranil Wickremeisnghe are seen paying floral tribute to the statue of late President Ranasinghe Premadasa at Hultsdorf in Colombo today during the ceremony held to mark the late President’s 24th Death anniversary.
