## Title: Video: Sluice gates of Deduru Oya opened
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 3 November 2015 07:29 am

Video: Sluice gates of Deduru Oya opened


	


Seven of the eight sluice gates of Deduru Oya were open today as the tank reached spill level due to the heavy rain experienced in the area. Four gates had been opened by two feet and the three others had been opened by three feet each. Pix by Sanjeewani Ariyaratne
