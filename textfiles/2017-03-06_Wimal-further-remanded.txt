## Title: Wimal further remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 March 2017 01:23 pm

Wimal further remanded


	

Former Minister Wimal Weerawansa, who was arrested by the FCID on charges of misusing 40 state vehicles belonging to the National Engineering Corporation (NEC), was ordered to be further remanded till March 20 by Colombo Fort Magistrate Lanka Jayaratne today. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
