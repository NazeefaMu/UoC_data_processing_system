## Title: Video: Gnanasara Thera files bail application
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 28 January 2016 06:06 am

Video: Gnanasara Thera files bail application


	

A bail application of General Secretary of the Bodu Bala Sena Ven. Galagodatte Gnanasara Thera, who is on remand on the charge of contempt of court, was filed this morning in the Homagama magistrate’s court.  Attorney at Law Sumudu Kantha Hewage handed over the motion to the Court Registrar. (Muditha Dayananda)
