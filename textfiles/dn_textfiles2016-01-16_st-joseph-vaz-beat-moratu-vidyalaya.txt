## Title: St. Joseph Vaz beat Moratu Vidyalaya
## Supertitle: Singer Cup Under 19 Inter School Cricket Tournament
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, January 16, 2016 - 01:00

Singer Cup Under 19 Inter School Cricket Tournament
St. Joseph Vaz beat Moratu Vidyalaya

St. Joseph Vaz College Wennappuwa beat Moratu Vidyalaya by innings and 55 runs in the first round match of Singer Trophy Under 19 inter schools cricket tournament played at Albert F. Peiris ground Wennappuwa yesterday.
Devapathiraja College Rathgama beat Sri Jayawardhanapura MV Kotte by 9 wickets at Rathgama.
Thulina Dilshan scored 151 runs. Dhananjaya Perera of St. Joseph Vaz scored unbeaten 102 runs.
