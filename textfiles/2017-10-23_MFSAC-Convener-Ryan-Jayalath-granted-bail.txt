## Title: MFSAC Convener Ryan Jayalath granted bail
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 October 2017 12:22 pm

MFSAC Convener Ryan Jayalath granted bail


	

Medical Faculty Students’ Action Committee (MFSAC) Convener Ryan Jayalath was granted bail by the Colombo High Court today.

He was arrested and remanded on charges of forcibly entering the Health Ministry premises and damaging Health Ministry property during a protest held against the SAITM. (Farook Thajudeen)
