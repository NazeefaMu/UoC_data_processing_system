## Title: Court allows foreign experts to examine CCTV footage
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 7 January 2016 06:31 am

Court allows foreign experts to examine CCTV footage


	

Colombo Additional Magistrate Nishantha Pieris today (07) granted  permission to  the CID  to refer the CCTV  footage  in connection with the  rugby player  Wasim Thajudeen’s  death to foreign experts .
The Magistrate also ordered the CID to inform Court about the institutions the CID intended to seek advice within a week.  
He also said further orders would follow after the necessary documents were submitted
