## Title: PM's China visit helps boost economy: Sajith
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 April 2016 04:06 pm

PM's China visit helps boost economy: Sajith


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe’s three day visit to China had helped to revive the unsuccessful projects such as the Magampura Mahinda Rajapaksa Port and the Mattala Rajapaksa International Airport, Housing and Constructions Minister Sajith Premadasa recently said.

Addressing a media briefing, Minister Premadasa said these unsuccessful projects had been constructed using loans at very high interests.

“Those projects were like white elephants, as there was no way to make them profitable. The Prime Ministers’ China visit has helped make these projects successful in order to develop the country’s economy," he said.

“During his visit to China he had has also opened up new employment opportunities for Sri Lankan the youth and in the tourism industry,” he added.
