## Title: Basil arrested for third time this year
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 July 2016 11:44 am

Basil arrested for third time this year


	

Former Economic Development minister Basil Rajapaksa, was arrested by the Financial Crime Investigation Division (FCID) a short while ago.

The former minister was at the FCID this morning to give a statement over misappropriating funds of the Divi Neguma Development Department.
