## Title: FCID quizzes Johnston, Cabraal
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 December 2015 06:38 am

FCID quizzes Johnston, Cabraal


	

Former Minister Johnston Fernando and former Central Bank Governor Ajith Nivard Cabraal were summoned to the Financial Crimes Investigation Division (FCID) this morning to record statements on two different cases.
The two arrived at the FCID around 9.45am accompanied by their private attorneys, are being questioned at the moment, Police said.
Former Minister had been summoned to record a statement over granting a Mahapola Lottery to a Malaysian. (Kurulu Koojana Kariyakarawana)
