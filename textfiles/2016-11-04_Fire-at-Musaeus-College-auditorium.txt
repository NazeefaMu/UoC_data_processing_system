## Title: Fire at Musaeus College auditorium
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 November 2016 03:42 pm

Fire at Musaeus College auditorium


	

A major fire has broken out in the Musaeus College auditorium in Colombo 7, the Fire Brigade said and added that it had deployed 10 fire-fighting  trucks to help douse the fire.  Three ambulances had also arrived at the scene.
