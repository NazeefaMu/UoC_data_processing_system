## Title: SLAF continues relief operations
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 22 May 2016 01:58 pm

SLAF continues relief operations


	



Sri Lanka Air Force is continuing rescue operations and relief aid missions to flood affected areas. SLAF Bell 212 helicopter carried out a reconnaissance mission this morning.

SLAF has completed four Beech 200 aircraft missions, twenty five Bell 212 missions, nine Bell 412 missions and eleven Mi 17 missions, since May 16, to dispatch goods immediately to relief centres in inaccessible areas.
