## Title: An infatuation of an elderly playboy
## Section: Political Gossip
## Date: 4 July 2017 12:00 am

An infatuation of an elderly playboy


	

A romantic drama about an elderly playboy of a state institution handling accounts and a young cute employee has become the latest hot topic for the gossipy ones at this place. The aging Romeo is the head of the institution. 




The comely fair one is said to be spending the major part of her office time inside the cubicle of the elderly bigwig. 


Some employees have taken advantage of this ‘affair’ by making it a practice to secure certain exclusive benefits from the big man through the influence of his ladylove, they say.
