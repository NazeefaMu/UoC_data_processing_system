## Title: Colombo Defence Seminar
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 2 September 2015 11:02 am

Colombo Defence Seminar


	


	Army Commander Lt. General Krishantha De Silva, and Air Force Commander Air Marshall Gagana Bulathsinhala chat with Former High Commissioner of Sri Lanka for the UK Chris Nonis at the 5th Defence Seminar held at the Galadari Hotel Colombo. The seminar first started in 2009 after the end of the war and had become an annual event in the country since then. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
