## Title: Worker’s death: Bogala mine closed temporarily
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 29 January 2017 11:33 am

Worker’s death: Bogala mine closed temporarily


	

The Bogala graphite mine had been closed temporarily since the fatal accident yesterday, the Police said.

The graphite mine in Bogala, Kegalle came to standstill yesterday after a miner was killed and two others injured when a rock fell into the mine last morning.

A 42-year-old resident of Bogala had died in the accident. The other two were hospitalised last morning.

The Pindeniya Police are investigating.
