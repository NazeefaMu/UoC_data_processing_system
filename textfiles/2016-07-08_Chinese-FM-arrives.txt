## Title: Chinese FM arrives
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 8 July 2016 04:29 pm

Chinese FM arrives


	

Chinese Foreign Minister Wang Yi accompanied by a delegation of nine members arrived in Sri Lanka a short while ago at the invitation of Foreign Minister Mangala Samaraweera.

He is scheduled to meet President Maithripala Sirisena, Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe and the Foreign Affairs Minister.

He is scheduled to leave on Sunday.(T.K.G. Kapila)
