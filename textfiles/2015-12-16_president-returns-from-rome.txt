## Title: President returns from Rome
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 December 2015 03:25 am

President returns from Rome


	

President Maithripala Sirisena who was in Rome for a two-day official visit arrived in the country a short while ago.
During the visit, the President met Pope Francis and a host of other leaders.
SLFP ministers including Transport Minister Nimal Siripala de Silva and MPs was seen at the Katunayake International Airport to welcome them.
