## Title: 29 injured in bus accident
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 July 2016 10:44 am

29 injured in bus accident


	

Twenty nine passengers were injured when a SLTB bus plying from Kabaragala to Kandy rammed into a wayside mound of earth at Madolkele, Wattegama today, police said. The injured were admitted to the Madolkele Hospital.
