## Title: Case filed after phone rings in Court
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 November 2015 12:44 pm

Case filed after phone rings in Court


	

A court case was filed today against former solicitor general and President’s Counsel (PC) Rienzie Arsecularatne whose mobile phone had reportedly rung insidethe Galle High Court while a case was in progress.
However, Mr. Arsakularatne told Court that he was not guilty and the court case was postponed to next January by Galle High Court Judge K.B.K.Hirimburegama.
