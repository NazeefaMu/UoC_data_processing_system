## Title: Six suspected members of 'Aava Gang' arrested
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 November 2016 09:39 pm

Six suspected members of 'Aava Gang' arrested


	

Six persons suspected to be members of the north -based criminal gang named 'Aava Gangsters' have been arrested and kept under interrogation, the Jaffna Police said.


Four of them were arrested on Saturday (05), and the rest today in Chunnakam, Jaffna according. The last two were staying at the same house.


A police team from the Colombo Crime Investigation Division made the arrest. (Romesh Madushanka)
