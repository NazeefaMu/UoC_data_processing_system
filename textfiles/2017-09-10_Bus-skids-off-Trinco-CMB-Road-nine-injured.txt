## Title: Bus skids off Trinco-CMB Road; nine injured
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 10 September 2017 12:40 pm

Bus skids off Trinco-CMB Road; nine injured


	

At least nine persons, including three foreigners, were hospitalised after a luxury bus skidded off the road and crashed into an electric post at Agbopura on the Colombo-Trincomalee Main Road early this morning, to Police said.

The Police said the driver and the conductor of the bus were among the injured and they were admitted to the Kantale and Trincomalee Hospitals.
