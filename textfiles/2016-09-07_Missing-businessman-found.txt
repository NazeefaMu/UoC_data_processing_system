## Title: Missing businessman found
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 7 September 2016 11:18 am

Missing businessman found


	

Mohammed Nasli, the 35-year-old businessman from Bandaragama, who was reported missing in Trincomalee has been arrested in Balangoda today, Police said.

The father of the businessman had complained to the Bandaragama Police that his son was missing since Sunday.

In his complaint, Mr. Nasli’s father said his son and three others had left for Trincomalee on Saturday to attend a gold auction to be held the next day and had taken up lodgings at Kantale.
