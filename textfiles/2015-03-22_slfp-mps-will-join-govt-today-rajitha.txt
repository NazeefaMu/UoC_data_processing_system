## Title: SLFP MPs will join govt. today: Rajitha
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 March 2015 06:41 am

SLFP MPs will join govt. today: Rajitha


	


	Minister Rajitha Senaratne said today that the Sri Lanka Freedom Party (SLFP) MPs would join hands with the government today signifying a historical turning point in Sri Lankan politics.
	Several SLFP parliamentarians will join the Government today to form a unity government, Minister Senaratne said at a meeting held in Kalutara.
	However when the Daily Mirror contacted SLFP General Secretary Anura Priyadarshana Yapa, he said that the party had not taken a decision on it yet and they were to meet the President later today.(Rathnasiri Premalal)
