## Title: PM back in SL
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 January 2017 06:40 pm

PM back in SL


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe returned to the island a short while ago after attending the World Economic Forum (WEF) in Davos, Switzerland. The PM arrived at the Katunayake Bandaranaike International Airport on flight No EK348 of the Emirates Airlines at 18.05 p.m. today.  (T.K.G.Kapila)
