## Title: Ban Ki-moon meets President
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 1 September 2016 09:56 pm

Ban Ki-moon meets President


	



Visiting UN Secretary-General Ban Ki-moon called on President Maithripala Sirisena at President’s House. He will also meet several ministers including Foreign Affairs Minister Mangala Samaraweera, Speaker Karu Jayasuriya, Political Party Leaders and representatives from civil society.
