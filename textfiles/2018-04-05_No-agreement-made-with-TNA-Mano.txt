## Title: No agreement made with TNA: Mano
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 April 2018 10:10 pm

No agreement made with TNA: Mano


	

In response to a question by a journalist, Minister Mano Ganesan today said that no agreement has been made with the Tamil National Alliance (TNA) in writing when seeking their support against the No-Confidence Motion (NCM).

He expressed this to media after attending a function held at the Vijaya Vidyalaya in Dehiwala.

Minister said the TNA had come up with its stance and the Prime Minister had simply responded to it. (Indika Sri Aravinda)
