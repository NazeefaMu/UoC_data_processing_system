## Title: Were they asleep when country plunged into darkness?
## Section: Political Gossip
## Date: 22 March 2016 12:00 am

Were they asleep when country plunged into darkness?


	



 

All service centers at the periphery of this leading state establishment are provided with a night-out facility including a comfortable bed room for the use of the respective officers in charge.

 


These local stations are out of bounds for outsiders and this has given a carte blanche to the officials running them to enjoy a shut-eye undisturbed even when they are supposed to be on duty.


A calamity of national proportions that country had to face recently was a result of the bed rooms provided to these high ups, they say.
