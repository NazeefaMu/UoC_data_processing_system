## Title: Two arrested over leopard killing remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 June 2018 04:53 pm

Two arrested over leopard killing remanded


	

The two suspects who were arrested by the Kilinochchi police in connection with the incident where a leopard was brutally killed at Ambalakulam in Kilinochchi on Thursday (21) were today remanded until June 29 by Kilinochchi Magistrate.

The suspects were identified as Nadarasa Mohan Raza (42), a resident of Ambalakulam and Nadaraja Sukumal (39), a resident of Udaya Nagar. (Romesh Madushanka)
