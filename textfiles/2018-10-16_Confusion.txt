## Title: Confusion!
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 16 October 2018 02:48 pm

Confusion!


	



People in Jaffna have complained that name boards on roads and several state departments in the district had been wrongly translated into Sinhala and urged the authorities to rectify them immediately. The people said pilgrims were often misled by the wrongly written Sinhala name boards. Pix by Romesh Madushanka
