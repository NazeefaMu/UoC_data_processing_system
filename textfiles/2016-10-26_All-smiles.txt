## Title: All smiles...
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 26 October 2016 04:24 pm

All smiles...


	



The first and the only green university in South Asia, NSBM (National School of Business Management) University was declared open by President Maithripala Sirisena at Pitipana in Homagama today. Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe, Ministers Mahinda Samarasinghe, Duminda Dissanayake, Mohammed Hilmi and MPs Dinesh Gunawardane and Bandula Gunawardena also attended the event.
