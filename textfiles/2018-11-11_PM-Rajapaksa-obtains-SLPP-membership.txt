## Title: PM Rajapaksa obtains SLPP membership
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 November 2018 12:13 pm

PM Rajapaksa obtains SLPP membership


	

Prime Minister Mahinda Rajapaksa has obtained the membership of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna (SLPP), at his official residence, Wijerama road, Colombo, a short while ago.
