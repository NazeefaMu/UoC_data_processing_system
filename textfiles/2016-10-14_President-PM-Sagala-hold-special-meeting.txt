## Title: President, PM, Sagala hold special meeting
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 14 October 2016 07:39 am

President, PM, Sagala hold special meeting


	

President Maithripala Sirisena and Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe met last night for a special discussion at the official residence of the former, informed sources disclosed.

According to the sources, Law and Order Minister Sagala Ratnayake had accompanied the Prime Minister.

The discussion comes in the wake of the President's criticism of the CID, FCID and Bribery Commission -- and speculation about differences of opinion between the two leaders. (Yohan Perera)
