## Title: BIA main runway closure: passengers asked to be present five hours early
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 January 2017 10:29 am

BIA main runway closure: passengers asked to be present five hours early


	

Passengers using the Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA) were requested to be present at the airport five hours prior to the departure time, Deputy Transport Minister Ashoka Abeysinghe said.

The main runway at the BIA will be closed for renovation daily for three months from today from 8.30 am to 4.30 pm.
