## Title: Miss Intercontinental 2016
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 17 October 2016 11:58 am

Miss Intercontinental 2016


	



Miss Puerto Rico Heilymar Rosario, was crowned Miss Intercontinental 2016 at the pageant at Stein Studios in Ratmalana last evening.

Miss Sri Lanka Tracy Zylva, emerged 1st runner up, while the 2nd runner up was Miss Ghana Silvia Commodore.

Title holders from over 60 countries were in Sri Lanka for this event.

This was the first time that a reputed global pageant was held in Sri Lanka and the first time in 20 years that a major pageant was held in South Asia. Pix by Nisal Baduge
