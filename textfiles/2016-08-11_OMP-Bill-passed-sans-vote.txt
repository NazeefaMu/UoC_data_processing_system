## Title: OMP Bill passed sans vote
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 August 2016 03:15 pm

OMP Bill passed sans vote


	

The amended draft Bill to set up an Office of Missing Persons (OMP) was passed in Parliament a short while ago without a vote being taken.

The debate on the Bill was scheduled to end tomorrow but with the Bill being passed the debate too was concluded today.
