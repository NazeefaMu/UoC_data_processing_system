## Title: Earth embankment collapses in Laggala
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 26 February 2017 11:34 pm

Earth embankment collapses in Laggala


	



An embankment of earth had collapsed at a site of the Moragahakanda -Kalu Ganga project in Laggala today. Four rescue teams belonging to the Army Commando Regiment had been deployed to handle the rescue effort. Pix by Indika Aruna kumara
