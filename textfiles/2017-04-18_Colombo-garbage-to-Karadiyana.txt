## Title: Colombo garbage to Karadiyana
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 18 April 2017 04:02 pm

Colombo garbage to Karadiyana


	



The Kesbewa Magistrate granted permission to the Colombo Municipal Council (CMC) to dump garbage collected from Colombo at Karadiyana in Piliyandala on a temporary basis.Pics by Muditha Dayananda
