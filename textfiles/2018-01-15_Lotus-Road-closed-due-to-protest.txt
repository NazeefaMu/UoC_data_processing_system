## Title: Lotus Road closed due to protest
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 January 2018 12:24 pm

Lotus Road closed due to protest


	

The Lotus Road from Ceramic Junction towards Galle Road, outside the Finance Ministry, in Colombo has been closed due to a protest carried out by a group of war-disabled soldiers' spouses, police said.
