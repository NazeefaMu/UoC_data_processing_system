## Title: FSP front-liners Jagoda, Kuruwita get bail
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 May 2016 03:18 pm

FSP front-liners Jagoda, Kuruwita get bail


	

Frontline Socialist Party (FSP) Propaganda Secretary Pubudu Jagoda and Central Committee Member Sujith Kuruwita were released by the Maligakanda Magistrate Court today with two sureties of Rs. 100,000 each.
