## Title: PM in Jakarta
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 1 August 2016 11:03 pm

PM in Jakarta


	



Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe arrived in Jakarta, Indonesia this evening for a two-day official visit, the Prime Minister's office said.

The Prime Minister led delegation, comprising Prof. Maithri Wickremesinghe, Ministers Kabir Hashim, A.H.M.Fowzie and State Minister Sujeewa Senasinghe was welcomed by Indonesian Minister Enggartiasto Lukita. (Prime Minister media)
