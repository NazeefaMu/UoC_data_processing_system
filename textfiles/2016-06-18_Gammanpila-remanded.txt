## Title: Gammanpila remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 June 2016 03:48 pm

Gammanpila remanded


	

UPFA MP Udaya Gammanpila was remanded till July 01 by the Fort magistrate a short while ago.

He was arrested by the Police Special Investigation Unit (SIU) this morning over a complaint that he had used an allegedly fraudulent Power of Attorney to sell shares of an Australian company.
