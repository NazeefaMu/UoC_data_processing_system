## Title: UPFA tops Ratnapura postal votes
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 August 2015 05:24 pm

UPFA tops Ratnapura postal votes


	


	United People’s Freedom Alliance (UPFA) topped the postal votes in the Ratnapura district with 11,368 votes (49.46%) while the United National Party (UNP) obtained 9,673 votes (42.09%). The Janatha Vimukthi Peramuna (JVP) obtained 1808 votes (7.87%).
