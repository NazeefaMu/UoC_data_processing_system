## Title: Sri Lanka Insurance begins business for 2018
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 4 January 2018 12:37 am

Sri Lanka Insurance begins business for 2018


	





The inauguration of business activities for 2018 at Sri Lanka Insurance took place on January 2 amidst a gathering comprising Chairman SLIC Hemaka Amarasuriya and Executive Director Nilu Wijedasa. 


The event comprised religious observances performed by leaders of all four religions, cutting a cake to commemorate the 56th anniversary of the organisation which coincided with the acceptance of new businesses.
