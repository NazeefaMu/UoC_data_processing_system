## Title: B'day wishes from the President
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 24 March 2016 05:05 pm

B'day wishes from the President


	



President Maithripala Sirisena is seen here sharing a piece of cake with Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe at Temple Trees during an event held to celebrate the Premier's 67th birthday today. Mrs. Wickremesinghe, former President Chandrika Kumaratunga, several ministers and MPs were gathered at the function to greet him. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
