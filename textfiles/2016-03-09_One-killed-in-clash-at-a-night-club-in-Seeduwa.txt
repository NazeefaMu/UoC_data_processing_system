## Title: One killed in clash at a night club in Seeduwa
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 March 2016 11:52 am

One killed in clash at a night club in Seeduwa


	

A 22-year-old youth was killed during a clash between two groups at a night club in Liyanagemulla, Seeduwa early this morning.

Police said the victim was beaten with clubs during the clash and had been admitted to the Ragama Hospital where he succumbed to his injuries.

Police identified the victim, a resident of Seeduwa, as Gayan Darshana. No arrests have been made as yet.
