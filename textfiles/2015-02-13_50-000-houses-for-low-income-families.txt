## Title: 50,000 houses for low-income families
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 13 February 2015 11:33 am

50,000 houses for low-income families


	


Housing Development and Samurdhi Minister Sajith Premadasa revealed the Ministry’s programme to construct 50,000 houses for low-income families. He told a news conference that the lands and the building materials would be provided free of charge to the people. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
