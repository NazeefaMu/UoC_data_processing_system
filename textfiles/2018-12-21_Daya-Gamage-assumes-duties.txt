## Title: Daya Gamage assumes duties
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 21 December 2018 10:39 pm

Daya Gamage assumes duties


	

Newly appointed Labour, Trade Union Relations, and Social Empowerment Minister Daya Gamage today assumed duties at the ministry. Mr. Gamage’s wife Anoma Gamage also participated in the event. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
