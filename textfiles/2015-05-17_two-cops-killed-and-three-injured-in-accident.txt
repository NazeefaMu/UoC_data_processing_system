## Title: Two cops killed and three injured in accident
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 May 2015 03:22 am

Two cops killed and three injured in accident


	


	Two police officers were killed and another three were injured when a van collided head on with a tipper truck at Ududumbara on the Kandy-Mahiyanganaya main road early this morning.
	Police said the officers, attached to the Galaha police station, were returning in the van from a funeral at Madugalle, Ududumbara.
	The injured Police officers were admitted to the Kandy hospital and the deceased have been identified as a Sub-Inspector and a Police Constable, the Police said.
	The driver of the tipper was arrested.(DS)
