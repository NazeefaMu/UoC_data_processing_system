## Title: Basil’s petition fixed for support
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 February 2016 11:04 am

Basil’s petition fixed for support


	

The Supreme Court today fixed for support for March 28, the fundamental rights petition filed by former minister of economic development, Basil Rajapaksa against the FCID arresting and remanding him on money laundering charges.

The petition was taken up before a bench comprising justice K.Sripavan, Anil Goonarathne and K.T. Chithrasri. (S.S. Selvanayagam)
