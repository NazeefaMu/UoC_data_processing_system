## Title: New SLFP branch office in Kotte
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 6 September 2016 09:57 pm

New SLFP branch office in Kotte


	



The Sri Jayewadenepura Kotte branch office of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party (SLFP) was opened by President Maithripala Sirisena today. The President is pictured here unveiling the plaque to mark the opening. SLFP Kotte Organiser Janaka Ranawaka is also present. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
