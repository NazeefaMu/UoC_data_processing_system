## Title: Kahawatte murder: 35-year-old man arrested
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 December 2015 01:38 pm

Kahawatte murder: 35-year-old man arrested


	

A 35-year-old man was arrested by the Criminal Investigation Department (CID) today on charges of killing a 48-year-old woman of Opathawatta in Kahawatta, Police said.
The woman, an estate worker, was found hacked to death in a tea estate in Opathawatta on September 28. The arrested is being detained by the CID for further investigations.
