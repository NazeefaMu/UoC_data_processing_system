## Title: Full-face helmets: Stay order on ban extended
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 June 2015 04:51 am

Full-face helmets: Stay order on ban extended


	

The Court of Appeal today extended the interim order issued staying the operation of the police advisory banning the use of face-covered helmets, until July 14.
Justice Vijith K. Malalgoda (President) gave this ruling sequent to two writ petitions filed by a Chartered Accountant and a Retired Army Major who sought an order prohibiting the authorities from taking any action on banning the use of face-covered helmets. (Lakmal Sooriyagoda)
