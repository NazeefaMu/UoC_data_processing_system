## Title: US conducts PASSEX with SL Navy
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 26 July 2016 03:27 pm

US conducts PASSEX with SL Navy


	



Naval personnel of the US and Sri Lanka conducted a passage exercise (PASSEX) today onboard the US Navy’s vessel ‘New Orleans’ which is harboured at the Colombo Port. Pix by Samantha Perera
