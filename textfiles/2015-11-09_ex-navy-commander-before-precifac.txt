## Title: Video: Ex-Navy Commander before PRECIFAC
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 November 2015 05:32 am

Video: Ex-Navy Commander before PRECIFAC


	

Former Navy Commander Jayanath Colombage and former Minister Jagath Pushpakumara were summoned to the PRECIFAC this morning to record statements over two separate cases.
The Navy Commander had been summoned in connection with the investigations into Rakna Lanka Ltd.
