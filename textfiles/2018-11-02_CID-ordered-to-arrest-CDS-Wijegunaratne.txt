## Title: CID ordered to arrest CDS Wijegunaratne
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 2 November 2018 01:21 pm

CID ordered to arrest CDS Wijegunaratne


	

The Colombo Fort Magistrate’s Court today ordered the CID to arrest Chief of the Defence Staff (CDS) Admiral Ravindra Wijegunaratne for harbouring Lieutenant Commander Prasad Hettiarachchi and produce him in Court before November 9. (Yoshitha Perera)
