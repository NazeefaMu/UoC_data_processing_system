## Title: Slight decline of Kelani river water level
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 May 2016 09:40 am

Slight decline of Kelani river water level


	

The water level of the Kelani River in the Colombo area has showing a slight fall by now, the Disaster Management Centre (DMC) said.

DMC spokesman Pradeep Kodippili said the water level in the area now shows a decline but it would again rise if the rain continues.
