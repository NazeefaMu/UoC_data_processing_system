## Title: Video: Marapana resigns
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 November 2015 04:45 am

Video: Marapana resigns


	


	Law and Order Minister Thilak Marapana announced his resignation from the ministerial post a short while ago.  
	Holding a special news conference, the Minister announced this decision.
	Meanwhile, President Maithripala Sirisena has informed the special Cabinet meeting that he has received the resignation letter of Minister Marapana.
