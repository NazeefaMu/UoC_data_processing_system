## Title: Blood donation camp at Musaeus College
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 31 December 2017 12:13 pm

Blood donation camp at Musaeus College


	



Musaeus College today held a blood donation camp during which nearly 600 donors, including college staff and parents, donated blood on voluntary basis. It was organized under the guidance of Chairman of Musaeus College, Mr. Ajita de Zoysa. The Prefects Guild of the school recently donated Rs. 1. 2 million worth equipment to Cancer Hospital, Maharagama. Pics by Kushan Pathiraja
