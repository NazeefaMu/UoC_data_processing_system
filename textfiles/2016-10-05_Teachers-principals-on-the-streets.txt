## Title: Teachers, principals on the streets
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 5 October 2016 09:07 pm

Teachers, principals on the streets


	



The collective of teachers and principals today marched towards the Finance Ministry, Colombo Fort in a protest, on several demands including wage hikes. The protest with thousands of teachers and principals started from Hyde Park Grounds, but were refrained from entering the Finance Ministry by police.
