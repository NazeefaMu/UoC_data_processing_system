## Title: It's not his day
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 January 2018 02:54 pm

It's not his day


	



A hungry jackal has missed out on a dove dinner after ambushing a group of birds and leaving with nothing but a mouth full of feathers at Nossob Waterhole, Kgalagadi, South Africa.

The recycling company owner came across the black backed jackal trying to catch doves at the waterhole. (Daily Mail)
