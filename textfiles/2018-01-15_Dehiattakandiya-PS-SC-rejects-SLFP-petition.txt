## Title: Dehiattakandiya PS: SC rejects SLFP petition
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 January 2018 12:04 pm

Dehiattakandiya PS: SC rejects SLFP petition


	

The petition filed by the Sri Lanka Freedom Party (SLFP) in connection with the rejection of its nomination list for the Dehiattakandiya Pradeshiya sabha was rejected by the Supreme Court today. (S.S. Selvanayagam)
