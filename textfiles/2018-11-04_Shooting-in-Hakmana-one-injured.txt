## Title: Shooting in Hakmana, one injured
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 November 2018 05:49 pm

Shooting in Hakmana, one injured


	

One person was injured when a group of gunmen arrived in a van and a motorcycle, opened fire in Kabiliyapola, Hakmana this evening, police said.

The injured was admitted to the Gangodagama Hospital in Hakmana.

Hakmana Police is conducting investigation. (TK)
