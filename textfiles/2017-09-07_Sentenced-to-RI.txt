## Title: Sentenced to RI
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 7 September 2017 02:30 pm

Sentenced to RI


	



Former Presidential Secretary Lalith Weeratunga and former Telecommunications Regulatory Commission (TRC) Director General Anusha Palpita were seen handcuffed together outside the Colombo High Court premises after the duo was sentenced to three-year Rigorous Imprisonment (RI) over the ‘Sil Redi’ distribution case.
