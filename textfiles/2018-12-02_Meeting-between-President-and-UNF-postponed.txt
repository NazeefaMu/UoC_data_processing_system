## Title: Meeting between President and UNF postponed
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 2 December 2018 07:02 pm

Meeting between President and UNF postponed


	

The meeting between President Maithripala Sirisena and the United National Front (UNF) party representatives which was scheduled to be held this evening (2), has been postponed 8 pm, tomorrow (03), MP Mano Ganesan said.
