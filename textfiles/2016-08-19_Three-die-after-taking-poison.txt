## Title: Three die after taking poison
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 August 2016 02:50 pm

Three die after taking poison


	

A 47-year-old man, his 12-year-old daughter and 9-year-old son died after allegedly taking poison in Wasala Road, Kotahena this morning, the Police said.

They said the victims died on admission to the National Hospital. The Police are investigating.
