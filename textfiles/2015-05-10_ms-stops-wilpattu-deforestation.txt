## Title: MS stops Wilpattu deforestation
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 10 May 2015 03:31 am

MS stops Wilpattu deforestation


	

President Maithripala Sirisena had ordered for the immediate halt to deforestation and land clearing at the Wilpattu National Park, the President’s media said yesterday.
The statement said: “President had instructed all State officials in the area and all the respective authorities such as Secretary to the Ministry of Environment to take immediate legal action in this regard.”
