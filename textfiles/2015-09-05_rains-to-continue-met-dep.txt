## Title: Rains to continue: Met Dep.
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 September 2015 09:23 am

Rains to continue: Met Dep.


	

The Meteorology Department said today showers could be expected in the Western and Sabaragamuwa provinces and in the Galle and Matara districts.
It said thundershowers would develop at several places in the Eastern, North Western, North Central, Central and Uva provinces after 04.00 p.m.
The department warned of strong winds during thundershowers.
