## Title: Fine art of living
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 25 January 2015 04:21 pm

Fine art of living


	


Kala Pola 2015 - Sri Lanka’s annual open-air art fair got off the ground today along Nelum Pokuna Mawatha, Colombo 07. Rohitha Rajapaksa the son of former President Mahinda Rajapaksa was one of the visitors at the fair last afternoon. Pic by Kushan Pathiraja and Nisal Baduge
