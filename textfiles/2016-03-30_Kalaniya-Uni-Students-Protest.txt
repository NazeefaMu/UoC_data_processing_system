## Title: Kalaniya Uni. Students’ Protest
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 30 March 2016 08:59 pm

Kalaniya Uni. Students’ Protest


	

A protest march organised by the student union of the Kalaniya University held a protest march from Kalaniya University to University Grant Commission (UGC) urging the authorities resolve their issues including privatization of the state universities.  Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
