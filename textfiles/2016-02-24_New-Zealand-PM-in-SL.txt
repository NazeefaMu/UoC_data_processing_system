## Title: New Zealand PM in SL
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 February 2016 08:23 am

New Zealand PM in SL


	

New Zealand Prime Minister John Key arrived in Sri Lanka last night on a three-day official visit.

Prime Minister Key and his delegation were welcomed by Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe at the airport.

During his stay here the visiting Prime Minister Key will hold bilateral discussions with President Maithripala Sirisena and his Sri Lankan counterpart. (T.K.G. Kapila-Katunayaka)
