## Title: PM returns
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 20 July 2016 10:07 am

PM returns


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe today returned to the country concluding his three-day visit to Singapore.

He left for Singapore on Sunday with five other delegates.

During the visit, he met Singapore Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong and signed several bilateral agreements. (T.K.G.Kapila)
