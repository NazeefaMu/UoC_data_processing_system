## Title: President accepts Dilrukshi’s resignation
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 October 2016 06:06 pm

President accepts Dilrukshi’s resignation


	

President Maithripala Sirisena today accepted the letter of resignation of Bribery Commission Director General Dilrukshi Dias Wickramasinghe, sources said.

Dilrukshi Wickramasinghe handed her letter of resignation to the President on Monday.
