## Title: Nihal Fernando PC withdraws representing PTL
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 7 September 2017 11:16 am

Nihal Fernando PC withdraws representing PTL


	

President’s Counsel Nihal Fernando today making a special statement before the Bond Commission announced that he would withdraw from representing Perpetual Treasuries Limited after CEO Kasun Palisena’s evidence was concluded.
