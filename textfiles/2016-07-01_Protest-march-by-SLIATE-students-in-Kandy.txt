## Title: Protest march by SLIATE students in Kandy
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 1 July 2016 09:59 pm

Protest march by SLIATE students in Kandy


	



Students of the Sri Lanka Institute of Advanced Technological Education (SLIATE) in Kandy staged a protest march in the city today demanding that their monthly Mahapola allowance be increased up to Rs. 5,000.  Pix by Nadeeka Daya Bandara
