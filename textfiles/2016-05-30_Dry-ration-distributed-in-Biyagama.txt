## Title: Dry ration distributed in Biyagama
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 30 May 2016 03:39 pm

Dry ration distributed in Biyagama


	



Dry rations were distributed at several places in Biyagama yesterday by the Ruwan Wijewardene Foundation for the people who were displaced from recent floods in the area.

The Dry rations were distributed by Western provincial councilor Prasanna Sampath under the patronage of State Defence Minister Ruwan Wijewardene. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
