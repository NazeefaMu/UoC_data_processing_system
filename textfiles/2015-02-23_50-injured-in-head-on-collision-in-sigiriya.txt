## Title: 50 injured in head-on collision in Sigiriya
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 February 2015 06:01 am

50 injured in head-on collision in Sigiriya


	

At least 50 passengers were injured when the bus they were travelling in and a lorry travelling in the opposite direction collided head-on near the Navy Camp at Digampathana in Sigiriya, a short while ago, the Police said.
The injured were rushed to the Dambulla Hospital. (Lahiru Pothmulla)
