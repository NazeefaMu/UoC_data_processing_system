## Title: 58-year-old woman arrested with heroin
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 November 2015 05:23 am

58-year-old woman arrested with heroin


	

A 58 - year old woman was arrested today near Sirisara Housing Complex in Borella for allegedly possessing 127 grams of heroin, Police said.
The arrested is to be produced in Maligawatta Magistrate's Court today.
