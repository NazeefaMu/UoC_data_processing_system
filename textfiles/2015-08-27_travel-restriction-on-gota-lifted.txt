## Title: Travel ban on Gota lifted
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 August 2015 05:53 am

Travel ban on Gota lifted


	

The Galle Magistrate’s Court today lifted the foreign travel ban imposed on former Defense secretary Gotabhaya Rajapaksa and four others following the investigations into the floating armoury of Avant-Garde at Galle Harbour.
The Galle Magistrate had earlier issued an order on a request by police preventing former Defence Secretary Gotabhaya Rajapaksa, former Navy Commander Admiral Somathilaka Dissanayake and two others from leaving the country.(D.G. Sugathapala)
