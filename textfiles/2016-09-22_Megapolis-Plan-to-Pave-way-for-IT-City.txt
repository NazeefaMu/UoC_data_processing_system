## Title: Megapolis Plan to Pave way for IT City
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 22 September 2016 08:30 pm

Megapolis Plan to Pave way for IT City


	



An IT City will be set up under the Western Province Megapolis Development Plan with the first phase of the construction work launched under the patronage of Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe in Mahahena, Homagama today in the presence of Megapolis and Western Development Minister Champika Ranawaka. A Science Centre, a Satellite Centre and a four-lane road is to be developed under the first phase of the project. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
