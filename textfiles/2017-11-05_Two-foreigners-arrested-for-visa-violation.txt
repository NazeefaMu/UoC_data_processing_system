## Title: Two foreigners arrested for visa violation
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 November 2017 10:57 am

Two foreigners arrested for visa violation


	

An Indian and a Pakistani were arrested last night by the Dehiwala police for overstaying their visas and violating immigration laws, Police said.

They said the suspects aged 27 and 48 had been working at a restaurant in Dehiwala at the time of the arrest.

 Dehiwala Police are investigating.
