## Title: Heavy traffic in Town Hall
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 February 2016 03:31 pm

Heavy traffic in Town Hall


	

Heavy traffic congestion was reported in Town Hall, Colombo due to a protest launched by the United Professionals Movement (UPM) and the Organization of Engineering Associations (OEA).

The protest march was organized by the professionals to oppose the implementation of the proposed India – Sri Lanka ETCA.
