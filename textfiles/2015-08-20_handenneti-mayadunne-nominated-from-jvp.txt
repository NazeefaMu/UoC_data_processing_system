## Title: Handunnetti, Mayadunne nominated from JVP
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 20 August 2015 04:55 am

Handunnetti, Mayadunne nominated from JVP


	

The Janatha Vimukthi Peramuna (JVP) has nominated former Auditor General Sarath Chandra Mayadunne and its defeated Matara District candidate Sunil Handunnetti as its national list MPs, party sources said.
The names have already been sent to Commissioner of Elections Mahinda Deshapriya. (Yohan Perera)
