## Title: Fire at Sathosa in Narahenpita
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 2 April 2017 12:59 pm

Fire at Sathosa in Narahenpita


	

A fire had broken out at the Sathosa store at the Narahenpita Economic Centre a short while ago, the Fire brigade said.

Official at the Colombo fire brigade department said three vehicles from the CMC had been sent to the scene to douse the fire.
