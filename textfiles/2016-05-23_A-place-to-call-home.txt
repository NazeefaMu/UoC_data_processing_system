## Title: A place to call home
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 23 May 2016 05:15 pm

A place to call home


	




Many people who were affected by the floods in the Kelaniya area are finding shelter in temporarily built huts alongside the New Kelani Bridge. A brother and his younger sister, are seen embracing each other in such a hut. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
