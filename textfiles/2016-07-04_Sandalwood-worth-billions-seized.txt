## Title: Sandalwood worth billions seized
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 July 2016 07:00 am

Sandalwood worth billions seized


	


A large stock of red sandalwood, worth more than a billion rupees, was seized by the Police and Naval officials at Erambugodella in Kalpitiya yesterday. The sandalwood was handed over to the Customs. Pix by Hiran Priyankara Jayasinghe
