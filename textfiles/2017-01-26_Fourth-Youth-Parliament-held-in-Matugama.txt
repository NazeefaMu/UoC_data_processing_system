## Title: Fourth Youth Parliament held in Matugama
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 26 January 2017 03:28 am

Fourth Youth Parliament held in Matugama


	



Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe was the Chief Guest at the opening Ceremony of the 4th Youth Parliament held in Matugama Youth Centre yesterday. Minister of Justice and Buddha Sasana Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe, State Minister of National Policies and Economic Affairs Niroshan Perera and Chairman of the National Youth Services Council Eranda Wellage were also present.
