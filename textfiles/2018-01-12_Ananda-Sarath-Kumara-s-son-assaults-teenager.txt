## Title: Ananda Sarath Kumara’s son assaults teenager
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 12 January 2018 12:50 pm

Ananda Sarath Kumara’s son assaults teenager


	

SLFP Anamaduwa organizer Ananda Sarath Kumara’s son Ayesh Shanaka Kumara and another youth were arrested on charges of assaulting a 19-year-old youth in Nawagaththegama yesterday and later released on bail with a surety of Rs.200, 000 each.

The teenager got himself admitted to the Anamaduwa Hospital and later to the Kurunegala Teaching Hospital.

Ananda Sarath Kumarawas earlier found guilty on charges of having forced a female school teacher to kneel at Nawagaththegama. (Jude Samantha)
