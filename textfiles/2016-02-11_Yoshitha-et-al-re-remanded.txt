## Title: Yoshitha et al re-remanded
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 11 February 2016 01:22 pm

Yoshitha et al re-remanded


	



Lieutenant Yoshitha Rajapaksa and four others CSN officials including Nishantha Ranatunga, Rohan Weliwita were remanded till February 25 by the Kaduwela Magistrate over an alleged financial irregularity at the CSN. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
