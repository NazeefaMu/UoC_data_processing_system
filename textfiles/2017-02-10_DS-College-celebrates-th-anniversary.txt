## Title: DS College celebrates 50th anniversary
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 10 February 2017 01:16 pm

DS College celebrates 50th anniversary


	



DS Senanayake College in Colombo celebrated its 50th anniversary at the college premises this morning with President Maithripala Sirisena gracing the occasion as the Chief Guest. Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
