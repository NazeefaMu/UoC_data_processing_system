## Title: Two youths drown in Maussakele
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 August 2015 03:20 am

Two youths drown in Maussakele


	


	Two youths aged 17 and 19 had drowned while they were bathing at the Maussakele Reservoir in Maskeliya yesterday, the Police said.
	Their bodies had been recovered by the villagers and the Police.
	The victims were residents of Maskeliya. The Police said.
