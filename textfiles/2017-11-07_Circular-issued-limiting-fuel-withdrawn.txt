## Title: Circular issued limiting fuel withdrawn
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 7 November 2017 07:22 pm

Circular issued limiting fuel withdrawn


	

The circular, which was issued today declaring that fuel should be pumped only to vehicles, had been withdrawn with immediate effect, the Petroleum Resources Development Ministry said.

It said Minister Arjuna Ranatunga had ordered to withdraw the circular because there are adequate fuel supply to the market.
