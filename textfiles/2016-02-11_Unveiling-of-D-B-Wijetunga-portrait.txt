## Title: Unveiling of D.B. Wijetunga portrait
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 11 February 2016 06:02 pm

Unveiling of D.B. Wijetunga portrait


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe and Justice Minister Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe are seen sharing alight moment in Parliament where a portrait of Sri Lanka’s fourth President D.B. Wijetunga was unveiled. Opposition Leader R. Sampanthan was also seen at the event. The portrait was unveiled by President Maithripala Sirisena.  Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
