## Title: Case against former SG for contempt of court
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, November 30, 2015 - 19:30

Case against former SG for contempt of court

A case was file against former Solicitor General and President’s Counsel (PC) Rienzi Arsakularatne for contempt of Court after his mobile allegedly rung at the Galle High Court while a case was being heard today.
The case against him was postponed till January, while President’s Counsel Rienzi Arsakularatne maintained he was not guilty.
