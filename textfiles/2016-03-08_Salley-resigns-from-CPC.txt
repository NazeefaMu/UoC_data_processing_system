## Title: Salley resigns from CPC
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 8 March 2016 01:03 pm

Salley resigns from CPC


	

Central Provincial Council member Azath Salley has resigned from the CPC today.

Mr. Salley said he handed over the resignation letter dated January 30 to the UNP General Secretary.

“Because my letter was not accepted, I resigned from the provincial council after handing over the letter to the Provincial Council Secretary,” he said.
