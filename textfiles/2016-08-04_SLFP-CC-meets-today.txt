## Title: SLFP CC meets today
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 August 2016 02:06 pm

SLFP CC meets today


	

The Sri Lanka Freedom Party Central Committee (SLFP CC)headed by President Maithripala Sirisena is expected to meet this evening.

SLFP General Secretary and Minister Duminda Dissanayakehad earlier said that disciplinary action would be taken against the members who participated in the ‘paadayathra’ organized by the joint opposition
