## Title: Hotel Show-Colombo at BMICH
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 21 July 2018 01:02 pm

Hotel Show-Colombo at BMICH


	


The ‘Hotel Show-Colombo’exhibition was launched by Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe and Tourism Development Minister John Amaratunga at the BMICH yesterday. Southern Development Minister Sagala Ratnayake was also present at the event. The exhibition is being held under the theme "Future of Hospitality Today" and will showcase all needs of the future of the Hospitality industry. It will be held on Saturday 21st and Sunday 22nd as well. Pix by Kithsiri de Mel
