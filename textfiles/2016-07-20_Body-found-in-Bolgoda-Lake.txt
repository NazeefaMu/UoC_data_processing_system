## Title: Body found in Bolgoda Lake
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 20 July 2016 04:12 pm

Body found in Bolgoda Lake


	

The body of a 40-year-old man was found in Bolgoda Lake in Piliyandala this morning, Police said. They said the deceased was yet to be identified.
