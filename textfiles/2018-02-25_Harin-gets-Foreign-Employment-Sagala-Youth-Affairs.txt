## Title: Harin gets Foreign Employment, Sagala Youth Affairs
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 February 2018 11:58 am

Harin gets Foreign Employment, Sagala Youth Affairs


	

Minister Harin Fernando was appointed as the new Cabinet Minister of Digital Infrastructure and Foreign Employment.

Former Law and Order Minister Sagala Ratnayake was appointed as the new Cabinet Minister of Youth Affairs and Southern Development.
