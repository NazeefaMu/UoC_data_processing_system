## Title: Won't allow taxes to be a burden to people: MS
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 April 2016 11:37 am

Won't allow taxes to be a burden to people: MS


	

President Maithripala Sirisena said he would not allow economists to enforce any tax that would burden the public.

Speaking at a meeting in Polonnaruwa this morning, the President made the comment responding to a number of media reports on taxes.
