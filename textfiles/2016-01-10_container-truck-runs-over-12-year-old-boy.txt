## Title: Container truck runs over 12-year old boy
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 10 January 2016 03:53 am

Container truck runs over 12-year old boy


	


	A 12-year-old child had been run over by a container truck at Koskumbura in Kelaniya last night, the Police said.
	The Police said the boy, who was a resident of Kelaniya, has succumbed to his injuries being admitted to the Colombo National Hospital.
	The driver of the container has been arrested.
