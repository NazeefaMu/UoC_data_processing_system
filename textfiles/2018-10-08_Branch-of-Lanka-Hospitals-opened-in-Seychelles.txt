## Title: Branch of Lanka Hospitals opened in Seychelles
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 8 October 2018 09:15 pm

Branch of Lanka Hospitals opened in Seychelles


	



President Maithripala Sirisena, who was on a three-day official visit in Seychelles, today participated in the opening of the branch of Lanka Hospitals Corporation in Seychelles.

He also paid a visit to the Seychelles National Hospital and donated a stock of medicine and equipment.
