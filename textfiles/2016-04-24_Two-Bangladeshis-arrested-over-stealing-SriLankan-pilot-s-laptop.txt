## Title: Two Bangladeshis arrested over stealing SriLankan pilot's laptop
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 April 2016 08:06 pm

Two Bangladeshis arrested over stealing SriLankan pilot's laptop


	

Two Bangladeshi nationals were arrested by the Airport Police officers today on charges of stealing a laptop belonged to a pilot attached to the SriLankan Airlines at Paris Airport.

They were taken into the custody while travelling from France to Bangladesh via Sri Lanka.

Police officers recovered the stolen laptop in possession of the suspects who were aged between 25 – 33. They will be produced before the Negombo Magistrate tomorrow (25). (Sanath Desmond)
