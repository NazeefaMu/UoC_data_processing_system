## Title: Boat capsize kills two
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 20 May 2016 10:26 pm

Boat capsize kills two


	

A forty seven year-old man and a 15 –year-old boy were killed after a boat capsized at Ambulgama in Hanwella this evening, police said.


They said a group of people had been riding boats in flood waters when the tragedy occurred.
