## Title: Southern Stars in Sri Lanka
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 14 September 2016 11:22 pm

Southern Stars in Sri Lanka


	




The Australian Women cricketers, also known as the Southern Stars, led by Meg Lanning arrived in the island on their maiden tour to Sri Lanka where they are scheduled to play four ODIs and a T20. Seen here is Sri Lanka captain Chamari Attapattu along with Lanning at a press briefing held today. Pix by Pradeep Dilruckshana
