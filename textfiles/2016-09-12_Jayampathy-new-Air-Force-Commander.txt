## Title: Jayampathy new Air Force Commander
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 12 September 2016 11:18 am

Jayampathy new Air Force Commander


	

President Maithripala Sirisena today appointed Air Vice Marshal Kapila Jayampathy as Commander of the Sri Lanka Air Force.

He was promoted to the rank of Air Marshal.

Former Air Force Commander Air Marshal Gagan Bulathsinghala retired from his post today.
