## Title: Jabir promoted as Richard Pieris Finance Executive Director
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 19 November 2015 06:30 pm

Jabir promoted as Richard Pieris Finance Executive Director


	


		 		 
		 
			
Richard Pieris Finance Ltd Chief Executive Officer K.M.M. Jabir has been elevated to the post of Executive Director of the company.
Jabir is a highly ranked professional in the finance and banking industry for over 30 years. He is a fellow member of the Institute of Bankers of Sri Lanka (IBSL), passed finalist of Chartered Institute of Management Accountants (CIMA) and a member of Sri Lanka Institute of Marketing (SLIM). 
He was holding very senior positions in various financial institutions. He was the Deputy General Manager Operations at People’s Leasing and Finance PLC and left to set up Richard Pieris Finance Ltd in 2013.
