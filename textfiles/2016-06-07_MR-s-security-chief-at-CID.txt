## Title: MR’s security chief at CID
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 7 June 2016 10:15 am

MR’s security chief at CID


	

Former President Mahinda Rajapaksa’s chief of security, Major Neville Wanniarachchi, arrived at the Criminal Investigation Department today to give a statement.

Previously, the Colombo Chief Magistrate ordered the CID and the FCID to investigate Major Wanniarchchi for alleged fraud and corruption.
