## Title: Speaker orders IGP to provide security to Ketagoda
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 20 February 2015 01:41 pm

Speaker orders IGP to provide security to Ketagoda


	

Speaker Chamal Rajapaksa has ordered IGP M K Ilangakoon to provide security to MP Jayantha Ketagoda after his house was surrounded by a group of individuals demanding his resignation, the Parliament has been told a short while ago.
