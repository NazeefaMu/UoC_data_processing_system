## Title: PM receives most admired leader award
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 9 September 2018 10:21 pm

PM receives most admired leader award


	



Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe receives the most admired leader for national contribution from Chairman Indian Chamber of Commerce Jawahar Vadivelu at Sri Lanka’s most admired companies awards 2018 organized by CIMA Sri Lanka and the International Chamber of Commerce Sri Lanka held at the Shangri-La Hotel this evening.
