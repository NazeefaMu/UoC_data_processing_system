## Title: Late PM Dudley Senanayake remembered
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 13 April 2015 07:26 pm

Late PM Dudley Senanayake remembered


	

The 42nd commemoration ceremony of former Prime Minister Dudley Senanayake was held today at the foot of the statue, Borella. State Minister of Defense Ruwan Wijewardene, Deputy Minister of Tourism and Sports Wasantha Senanayake and former Minister Rukman Senanayake offered floral tribute at the ex-PM’s statue. Pic by Pradeep Pathirana
