## Title: CID to take over Jaffna murder investigation
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 20 May 2015 05:04 pm

CID to take over Jaffna murder investigation


	

The Police Crime Investigation Division (CID) had deployed a special police team to investigate the rape and killing of an 18-year-old girl at Punguditivu in Jaffna, Police Spokesman ASP Ruwan Gunasekara said.
Meanwhile Police Chief N.K. Illangakoon also visited Jaffna to personally supervise the investigations into the incident, ASP Gunasekara said.
