## Title: ComBank receives FinanceAsia award for Best Bank in Sri Lanka
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 7 September 2016 12:00 am

ComBank receives FinanceAsia award for Best Bank in Sri Lanka


	



Commercial Bank of Ceylon was formally presented the award for the ‘Best Bank’ in Sri Lanka in 2016 by FinanceAsia at the magazine’s annual Country Awards in Singapore recently. 


The award was the sixth ‘FinanceAsia Country Banking Achievement Award’ the bank has received from FinanceAsia.
