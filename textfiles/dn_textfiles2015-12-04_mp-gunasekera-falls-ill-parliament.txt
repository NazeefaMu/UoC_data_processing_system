## Title: MP Gunasekera falls ill in Parliament
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 4, 2015 - 01:00

MP Gunasekera falls ill in Parliament

UPFA Moneragala district Parliamentarian Padma Udayashantha Gunasekera was admitted to the Colombo National Hospital after he fell ill while in Parliament.
The doctor in the Parliament Medical Room had said that Gunasekera be admitted to the National Hospital. He was rushed to the National Hospital around 3.45 p.m. in an ambulance belonging to Parliament.
Gunasekera suffered a heart ailment several weeks ago while attending a rally in Moneragala and was airlifted to the Colombo National Hospital.
