## Title: Thewarapperuma arrested
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 July 2016 07:16 pm

Thewarapperuma arrested


	

Deputy Minister Palitha Thewarapperuma was arrested when he surrendered to the Meegahathenna Police a short while ago.

He was arrested on charges of unlawful assembly and illegal entry to the Meegahathenna Primary school.
