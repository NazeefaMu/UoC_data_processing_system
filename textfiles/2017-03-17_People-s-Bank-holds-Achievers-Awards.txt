## Title: People’s Bank  holds Achievers Awards
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 17 March 2017 10:55 am

People’s Bank  holds Achievers Awards


	



 

People’s Bank Achievers Awards 2016 was held at the Eagle’s Lakeside Hotel, Attidiya recently. The picture shows People’s Bank Chairman Hemasiri Fernando presenting the Delgoda branch the Gold award for achieving the Highest Per Employee-Based Business Performance, in the presence of Chief Executive Officer/General Manager N. Vasantha Kumar and Deputy General Manager Channel Management Boniface Silva.
