## Title: SLCP holds Annual Scientific Congress
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 12 August 2016 08:28 am

SLCP holds Annual Scientific Congress


	


The inauguration ceremony of the 19th Annual Scientific Congress of the Sri Lanka College of Paediatricians (SLCP) was held yesterday at the Galle Face Hotel in Colombo. It was held under the auspicious of President Maithripala Sirisena and the President of the Royal College of Paediatrics and Child Health, UK, Professor Neena Modi.
