## Title: Three NFF leaders join SLFP
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 December 2017 03:08 pm

Three NFF leaders join SLFP


	

National Freedom Front (NFF) Deputy Leader Weerakumara Dissanayake, National Organiser Piyasiri Wijenayake and North Central Provincial Councillor P.B. Kumara today joined the Sri Lanka Freedom Party (SLFP) led by President Maithripala Sirisena.

They obtained their new SLFP memberships from the President at the Presidential Secretariat.

In a statement, the President’s Media Division said the three members pledged their support to President Sirisena at the upcoming local government election.
