## Title: President visits Niagara Falls
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 September 2017 05:16 pm

President visits Niagara Falls


	

President Maithripala Sirisena, who is in New York to attend the UN General Assembly, toured Niagara Falls on Friday, Niagara Gazette reported.

“Before returning home, he made a stop in Niagara Falls to take in the natural wonder, according to Destination Niagara USA,” it said.

The DoubleTree by Hilton downtown hosted President Sirisena’s stay and he was also given a VIP tour of the Niagara Falls State Park including a ride on the Maid of the Mist, the news agency added.
