## Title: Chavakachcheri  accident victims’ bodies airlifted to Ratmalana
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 18 December 2016 08:58 pm

Chavakachcheri  accident victims’ bodies airlifted to Ratmalana


	




Bodies of 11 victims of the accident  that occurred in Chavakachcheri Jaffna were airlifted to the Ratmalana Airport today. Pix by Nisal Baduge
