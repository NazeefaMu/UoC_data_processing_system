## Title: Search ops to locate German tourist fell from World’s end
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 November 2018 11:40 am

Search ops to locate German tourist fell from World’s end


	

A search operation was underway to locate a 35-year-old German female tourist, who fell from the World’s End, in Horton Plains, Nuwara Eliya, police said.

“The Army is conducting search operations to locate the tourist,” a senior official said.
