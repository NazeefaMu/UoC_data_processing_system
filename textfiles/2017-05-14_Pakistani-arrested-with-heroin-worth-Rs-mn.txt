## Title: Pakistani arrested with heroin worth Rs.10mn
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 14 May 2017 12:37 pm

Pakistani arrested with heroin worth Rs.10mn


	

A 32-year-old Pakistani national was arrested for possessing 1Kg of heroin at the Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA) by the Airport customs this morning.

The suspect arrived at the BIA from Dubai via Emirates EK650 flight with heroin worth around Rs 10 million hidden in his shoes.

The BIA customs conducting further investigations. (T.K.G.Kapila)
