## Title: Devans draw with Rajans
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, November 7, 2015 - 01:00

Devans draw with Rajans

The Under-19 tournament cricket match between Dharmaraja College, Kandy and Maliyadeva College, Kurunegala ended in a 'no decision" at Lakeview in Kandy yesterday.
Rajans were all out for 171 runs and in reply, Maliyadeva, Kurunegala scored 115, losing five wickets in their first innings before the match was called off at 3 p.m. due to bad weather.
For Rajans, Lahiru Samarakoon scored an unbeaten 48 and scored 57 for Rajans. Sanjeewan Priyadarshana claimed 4 wickets for 28 runs for Maliyadewa.
Thamidu Somaratne of Maliyadewa scored 57 including six boundaries and a six.
Dharmaraja: 171 in 53.1 overs (Lahiru Samarakoon 57, Sanjeewan Priyadarshana 4 for 28,Vishwa Dissanayake 3 for 27, Damitha Silva 2 for 49)
Maliyadeva: 115 for 5 in 39.3 overs (Damith Perera 35, Thamidu Somaratne not out 47, Lahiru Samarakoon 2 for 37, Gihan Vitharana 2 for 20)
