## Title: Kataragama protestors tear gassed
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 21 January 2018 05:10 pm

Kataragama protestors tear gassed


	

The Police a short while ago fired tear gas to disperse protesting people following yesterday's shooting incident in Kataragama.

It was reported that more than 60 individuals had been arrested over violence. (M.K.Nandasena)
