## Title: Won’t support impeachment move against President: Mano
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 November 2018 12:28 pm

Won’t support impeachment move against President: Mano


	

The Tamil Progressive Alliance (TPA) would not support any impeachment move against President Maithripala Sirisena, MP Mano Ganeshan said today.

He said they are not in favour of adding more fire to the current crisis.

The United National Party (UNP) yesterday said it would explore the possibility of removing President Sirisena in conformity with Article 38 (2) of the Constitution.
