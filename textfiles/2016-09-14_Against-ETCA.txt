## Title: Against ETCA…
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 14 September 2016 11:31 pm

Against ETCA…


	



Center for National Trade Unions today staged a protest outside the Fort Railway Station against the signing of ETCA agreement, Private Medical Colleges and privatizing of state institutions. They also urged the government to fulfill the demands of the working people. Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
