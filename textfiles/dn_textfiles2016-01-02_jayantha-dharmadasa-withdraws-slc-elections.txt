## Title: Jayantha Dharmadasa withdraws from SLC elections ?
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, January 2, 2016 - 17:00

Jayantha Dharmadasa withdraws from SLC elections ?

Business personality Jayantha Dharmadasa, who was scheduled to run for Sri Lanka Cricket (SLC) Presidency at the SLC elections in 2016, has withdrawn from the race, sources stated.
