## Title: President in New Delhi
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 10 March 2018 03:57 pm

President in New Delhi


	

President Maithripala Sirisena arrived in New Delhi to participate in the International Conference on solar energy to be held tomorrow (11). When arrived at Indira Gandhi Airport in Delhi,today (10) he was received by Ruchigan Shyam,Secretary for the western part of Ministry of Foreign Affairs, High Commissioner for India to Sri Lanka Tharanjit Singh and High Commissioner for Sri Lanka Chitrannganee Wagiswara. (Pics courtesy President’s Media)
