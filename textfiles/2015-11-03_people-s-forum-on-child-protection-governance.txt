## Title: ‘People’s Forum on Child Protection Governance’
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 3 November 2015 05:15 pm

‘People’s Forum on Child Protection Governance’


	


‘The People’s Forum on Child Protection Governance’ was held at the Lakshman Kadirgamar Institute for International Relations and Strategic Studies with participation of former MP Rosy Senanayake and other dignitaries. The forum was organised to discuss about child rights and what policies need to be implemented in order to protect them in the future. Pix by Kithsiri De Mel
