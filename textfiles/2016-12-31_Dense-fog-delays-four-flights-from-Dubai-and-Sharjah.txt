## Title: Dense fog delays four flights from Dubai and Sharjah
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 31 December 2016 10:43 am

Dense fog delays four flights from Dubai and Sharjah


	

Four flights scheduled to arrive from Sharjah and Dubai have been delayed because of the dense fog in the Mediterranean zone, Airport sources said.

They said two Air Arabia flights scheduled to arrive at the Bandaranaike International airport at 3.05 a.m. and 3.55 a.m. from Sharjah and two flydubai flights scheduled to arrive from Dubai at 4.25 a.m. and 5.20 a.m. have been delayed. (T.K.G. Kapila)
