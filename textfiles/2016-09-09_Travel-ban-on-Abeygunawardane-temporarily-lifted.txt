## Title: Travel ban on Abeygunawardane temporarily lifted
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 September 2016 02:35 pm

Travel ban on Abeygunawardane temporarily lifted


	

The travel ban imposed on MP Rohitha Abeygunawardane was temporarily lifted today by Colombo High Court Judge Nissanka Bandula Karunaratne.

He was permitted to travel abroad between September 25 and October 25.

Mr. Abeygunawardane was indicted by the Bribery Commission on charges of illegally acquiring assets.

The case was postponed for October 31. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
