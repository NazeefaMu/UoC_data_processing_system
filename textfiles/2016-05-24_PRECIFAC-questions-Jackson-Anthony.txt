## Title: PRECIFAC questions Jackson Anthony
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 May 2016 12:46 pm

PRECIFAC questions Jackson Anthony


	

Veteran artiste Jackson Anthony is being questioned by the PRECIFAC over the funds allocated for the opening and the water filling ceremony of the Hambantota Port in 2010, PRECIFAC officials said. (AS)
