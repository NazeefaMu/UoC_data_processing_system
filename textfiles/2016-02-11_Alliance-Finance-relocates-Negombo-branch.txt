## Title: Alliance Finance relocates Negombo branch
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 11 February 2016 12:00 am

Alliance Finance relocates Negombo branch


	



Alliance Finance Negombo branch, previously located at No: 6/A, Station Road, Negombo, is now relocated at No: 210, St. Joseph Street, Negombo, to provide more convenience and superior service to its valued customers. 
Providing dynamic and innovative financial solutions since 1956, Alliance Finance Company PLC is Sri Lanka’s leading financial house. With a range services providing easy and affordable leasing facilities for new and used vehicles, its product portfolio includes hire purchase, specialized loan products, fixed deposits, saving accounts, equipment leasing and gold loans.
