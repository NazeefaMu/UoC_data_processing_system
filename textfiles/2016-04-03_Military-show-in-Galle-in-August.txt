## Title: Military show in Galle in August
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 April 2016 04:15 pm

Military show in Galle in August


	

A military show which is going to be a tourist attraction will be held in Galle in August this year. Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinge said over the weekend.

The premier said this to the youth whom he met when visiting them at the Yovun Pura youth camp in Sigiriya.

He gave a variety of pledges to the youth when visiting each stalls erected by them representing all districts.

Accordingly, he told the Hambantota youth that the government will make the airport in Mattala commercially viable.

He said the youth would be able to secure employment through these projects. (Yohan Perera)
