## Title: Body recovered from Badovita Canal
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 13 January 2016 06:25 am

Body recovered from Badovita Canal


	

The body of a 57-year-old man was recovered from the Badovita Canal in Attidiya, police said. They said the body with the hands tied was identified as that of a tailor.
