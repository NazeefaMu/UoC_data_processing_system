## Title: Monsoon gradually establishing over the island: Met. Dept.
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 May 2016 12:20 pm

Monsoon gradually establishing over the island: Met. Dept.


	

The South-West Monsoon condition was gradually establishing over the country resulting in rains in the Western, North-Western, Southern, Central and the Sabaragamuwa Provinces, the Metrology Department said today.

It said thunder showers might occur at a few places in the Eastern Province in the afternoon.

“Strong windy conditions over the country and in the sea areas around the island will continue, particularly over the Southern, Eastern and Northern sea areas,” the Department said.
