## Title: Girl Guides Association centenary
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 12 February 2017 10:23 pm

Girl Guides Association centenary


	



Sri Lanka Girl Guides Association held a function to mark its centenary at the BMICH Colombo today. Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe and his wife Prof.Maithri Wickremesinghe were seen greeting and awarding the veteran girl guides who participated at the event.
