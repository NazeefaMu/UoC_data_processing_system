## Title: Wesley qualifies for semi-finals
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, February 26, 2016 - 01:00

Wesley qualifies for semi-finals

 
Wesley College advanced to the semi-finals of the Singer Trophy Under 19 division I inter schools cricket tournament when they won their quarter-final on first innings against St Anthony's College Katugastota at Campbell Park yesterday.
Scores:
St. Anthony's: 166 in 48.2 overs (Kanishka Uggalpaya 73, Thamashana Abeykoon 20, Yomesh Ranasinghe 6/53, Andrew Parais 2/52) and 82 in 22.1 overs (Andrew Parais 4/54, Yomesh Ranasinghe 3/23)
Wesley: 189 (145/6 o/n) in 60.3 overs (Shamod Athulathmuldai 59, Movin Subasinghe 25, Thilina Perera 24, Sandaruwan Dharmaratne 5/73, Deon Stouter 2/19) and 12/2 in 2.1 overs.
