## Title: Perahera Sandeshaya handed over
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 26 August 2018 09:44 pm

Perahera Sandeshaya handed over


	

Diyawadana Nilame Nilanga Dela handed over the Perahera Sandeshaya (Sannasa) to President Maithripala Sirisena to inform the successful completion of the Kandy Esala Perahera, at the President’s House in Kandy today. Pics courtesy President’s Media
