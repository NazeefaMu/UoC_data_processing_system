## Title: Woman hotelier abducted
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 March 2016 12:11 pm

Woman hotelier abducted


	

A 49-year-old woman hotelier had been abducted at Kapparathota in Weligama by three unidentified men today, the Police said.

They said that the men had also taken away a vehicle belonged to her from the hotel. The Police said they suspected that one of the hotel employees might have planned the abduction.
