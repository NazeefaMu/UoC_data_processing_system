## Title: Damaged houses in Kithulgala
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 16 June 2016 04:16 pm

Damaged houses in Kithulgala


	

Houses damaged due to blasts reverberating from the construction of the Broadlands Hydro Power Plant in Kithulgala. The power plant is being constructed on Maskeliya and Kehelgamu Oya, two main branches of the Kelani River. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
