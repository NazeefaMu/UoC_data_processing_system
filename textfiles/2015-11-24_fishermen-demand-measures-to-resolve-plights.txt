## Title: Fishermen demand measures to resolve plights
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 24 November 2015 09:33 am

Fishermen demand measures to resolve plights


	


All Ceylon Multi-Day Trawler Owners Association today launched a protest in Kollupitiya requesting the President to take necessary steps to lift EU ban on Sri Lankan fish exports and to solve their other issues. Pix by Nisal Baduge
