## Title: UA attends MDRT Experience & Global Conference in Hong Kong
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 5 February 2016 08:11 am

UA attends MDRT Experience & Global Conference in Hong Kong


	

Union Assurance (UA) is home to innovators that set the benchmarks for the life insurance industry in Sri Lanka. UA once again creates history at the Million Dollar Round Table (MDRT) Experience and Global Conference, where 83 personnel, who included a large number of UA’s personal financial advisors (PFA) and representatives of the sales management team, took part, making it the largest contingent to be present from the insurance industry of Sri Lanka. 
This is the third consecutive year UA has sent the largest contingent for the MDRT Experience and Global Conference, which was held in Hong Kong recently. 
Founded in 1927, the MDRT acts as the premier association of financial professionals and is an international, independent association of more than 42,000 of the world’s leading life insurance and financial services professionals from more than 470 companies in 71 countries. The MDRT members demonstrate exceptional professional knowledge, strict ethical conduct and outstanding client service. The MDRT membership is recognized internationally as the standard of excellence in the life insurance and financial services business. 
“UA PFAs are known to be professional as well as the best qualified to offer financial protection to individuals and enterprises in the country. We continue to invest in training and development for our employees to further strengthen professional competencies and to create world-class insurance professionals who are able to cater to an ever-evolving clientele. Employees are also rewarded duly to keep the level of motivation high and are supported in their journey for success,” said General Manager Marketing and Distribution Rukman Weeraratne.
“As a result of the continuous commitment for people development, UA was a proud recipient of a prestigious Gold award for the third consecutive year from the Sri Lanka Institute of Training and Development (SLITAD) People Development Award in 2015,” added Weeraratne.The PFAs, who have qualified for this international conference, are the ones that have demonstrated high standards of professionalism towards their customers and have been ethical in all their business dealings while being excellent contributors to the success of UA by strengthening the company’s strong position in the insurance industry.
