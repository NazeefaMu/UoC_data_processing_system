## Title: Katchatheevu Island’s feast held
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 24 February 2018 05:48 pm

Katchatheevu Island’s feast held


	


The annual feast of the St. Anthony's Church in Katchatheevu Island was held today. A large number of Catholics arrived from Sri Lanka and South India participated in the feast. The Sri Lankan Navy provided the facilities for the devotees to visit the Church. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
