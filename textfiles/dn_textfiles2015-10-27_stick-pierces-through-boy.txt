## Title: Stick pierces through boy
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 27, 2015 - 16:15

Stick pierces through boy

A 16-year-old student was admitted to the Colombo National Hospital today after a stick pierced through his body. Thushara Idunil Lakshan of Bulathsinghala, Galahena had fallen off a mango tree and the stick had pierced through his body. The boy’s father is abroad while his mother is dead.
He is living with his elder brother who is married.
Lakshan was admitted to the Horana Base Hospital and then transferred to the Colombo National Hospital.
