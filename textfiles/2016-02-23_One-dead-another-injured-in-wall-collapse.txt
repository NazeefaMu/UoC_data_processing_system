## Title: One dead, another injured in wall collapse
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 February 2016 04:34 pm

One dead, another injured in wall collapse


	

A 17-year-old youth was killed and another injured when a parapet wall collapsed on them while digging a drainage system at Mabola in Wattala this morning, police said.


They said both the labourers were admitted to the Ragama Hospital where one of them died from his injuries.
