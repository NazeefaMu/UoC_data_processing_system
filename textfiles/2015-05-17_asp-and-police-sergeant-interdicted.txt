## Title: ASP and Police Sergeant interdicted
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 May 2015 11:58 am

ASP and Police Sergeant interdicted


	

An ASP and a Police Sergeant were interdicted for neglecting their duties when an army soldier carried a gun to a meeting attended by President Maithripala Sirisena in Agunukalapalassa, police said.
A statement released by Former President Mahinda Rajapaksa said the soldier, who was arrested and remanded, was a party of his security team.
