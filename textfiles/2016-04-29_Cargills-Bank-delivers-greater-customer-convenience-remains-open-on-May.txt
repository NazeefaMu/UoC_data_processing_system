## Title: Cargills Bank delivers greater customer convenience; remains open on May 2
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 29 April 2016 12:00 am

Cargills Bank delivers greater customer convenience; remains open on May 2


	

All Cargills Bank branches will remain open for business on May 2, 2016, which is a statutory bank holiday in lieu of May Day. This special holiday banking service is to enhance the overall service delivery to its customers. Nine bank branches will be open from 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. while its Maharagama and Jaffna branches will be open from 9:00 a.m. to 8:00 p.m. on the day to enable customers to transact their business. 
The bank operates branches in Colpetty (head office), Old Moor Street, Maitland Crescent, Matara, Maharagama, Vavuniya, Hawa-Eliya, Thanamalwila, Kurunegala, Jaffna and Chunnakam. Delivering on its premise of greater customer convenience, Cargills Bank also provides customers access to their accounts through 14 bank-owned stand-alone ATMS and over 3,000 ATMs islandwide linked to the Lankapay Common ATM switch. Customers can also transact anytime of any day through Cargills Bank Internet banking.
