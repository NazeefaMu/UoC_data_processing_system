## Title: Series win for India
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 6 August 2017 05:52 pm

Series win for India


	



India recorded an emphatic innings and 53-run win over Sri Lanka tour to win their three-match series with a game to spare. Dimuth Karunaratne scored 141 for Sri Lanka, while Ravindra Jadeja bagged five wickets.Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
