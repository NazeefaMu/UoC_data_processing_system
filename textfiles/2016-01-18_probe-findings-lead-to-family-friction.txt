## Title: Probe findings lead to family friction!
## Section: Political Gossip
## Date: 18 January 2016 06:30 pm

Probe findings lead to family friction!


	


The past shady activities of a certain former prominent politico now being unveiled before a sleaze probe have caused disharmony in his family.
It has come out in evidence that the politico had used his political clout to transfer some state property to his ladylove.
His legal wife has also confronted the politico with the revelation that he had gifted a certain property worth Rs.10 million to the other woman.
