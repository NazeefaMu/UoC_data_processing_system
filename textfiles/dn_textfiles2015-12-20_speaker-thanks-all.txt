## Title: Speaker thanks all
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Sunday, December 20, 2015 - 09:30

Speaker thanks all

Speaker Karu Jayasuriya in Parliament thanked all the people's representatives who made contributions to make the budget 2016 a success with their suggestions and proposals
 
The Speaker making a special announcement at the end of the vote thanked President Maithripala Sirisena, Prime Minister Ranil Wickremasinghe, Deputy Speakers, Leader of the House Lakshman Kiriella, Chief Government Whip Gayantha Karunathilake, Opposition Leader R.Sampanthan and Chief Opposition Whip Anura Kumara dissanayake for their cooperation to conduct the debate successfully.
 
He also thanked both government and Opposition MPs for their active participation in the budget debate.
 
The Speaker also expressed his gratitude to the Parliament Secretary General, the Serjeant-at-Arms and Parliament staff, security and Police personnel, the officials of all ministries and state institutions, and media personnel for their dedicated and committed service and cooperation throughout the budget debate.
 
The Speaker also extended Christmas wishes to all
