## Title: Saudi Prince arrives in Sri Lanka
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 July 2017 03:30 pm

Saudi Prince arrives in Sri Lanka


	



His Royal Highness Alwaleed Bin Talal Abdulaziz AL Saud, Prince of the kingdom of Saudi Arabia arrived in Sri Lanka on an official visit and had discussions with Foreign Minster Ravi Karunanayake at the Foreign Ministry today. Pix by Damith Wickramasinghe
