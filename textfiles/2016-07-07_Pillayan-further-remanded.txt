## Title: Pillayan further remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 7 July 2016 01:00 pm

Pillayan further remanded


	

Former Eastern Province Chief Minister Sivanesathurai Chandrakanthan alias Pillayan was further remanded till July 21 by the Batticaloa Magistrate today.


He was arrested in connection with the murder of ex-MP Joseph Pararajasingham. (Jawferkhan)
