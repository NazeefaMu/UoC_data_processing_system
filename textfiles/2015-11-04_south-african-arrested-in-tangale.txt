## Title: South African arrested in Tangalle
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 November 2015 06:38 am

South African arrested in Tangalle


	

A 37-year old South African national has been arrested in Tangalle on charges of staying in Sri Lanka without a visa or passport.
Police said he was arrested yesterday evening at a guest house at Madilla, Tangalle.
The suspect is to be produced at Tangalle Magistrate's Court today.
