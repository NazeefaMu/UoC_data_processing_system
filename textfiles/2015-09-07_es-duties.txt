## Title: Dayasiri assumes duties
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 7 September 2015 12:03 pm

Dayasiri assumes duties


	


Sports Minister Dayasiri Jayasekara today assumed duties at the Sports Ministry. Sri Lanka Cricket Chairman Sidath Wettimuny and other officials congratulated the new Minister who assumed duties following religious observances.  Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
