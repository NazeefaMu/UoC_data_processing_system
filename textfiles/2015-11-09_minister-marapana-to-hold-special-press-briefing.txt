## Title: Minister Marapana to hold special press briefing
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 November 2015 04:35 am

Minister Marapana to hold special press briefing


	

Law and Order Minister Thilak Marapana has called a special press conference at his residence in Nugegoda this morning.
It was speculated that he would announced a curtail decision at this press conference.
