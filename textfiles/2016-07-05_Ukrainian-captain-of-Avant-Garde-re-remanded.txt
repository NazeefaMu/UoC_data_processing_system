## Title: Ukrainian captain of Avant-Garde re-remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 July 2016 01:03 pm

Ukrainian captain of Avant-Garde re-remanded


	

The 49-year-old Ukrainian captain of the Avant-Garde vessel was further remanded till July 18 by Galle Magistrate Nilupuli Lankapura today. He was arrested on charges of importing illegal fire arms and keeping ammunitions in his possession illegally.
