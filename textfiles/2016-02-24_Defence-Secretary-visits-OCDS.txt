## Title: Defence Secretary visits OCDS
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 24 February 2016 10:32 am

Defence Secretary visits OCDS


	



Karunasena Hettiarachchi, Secretary to the Ministry of Defence, visited the Office of the Chief of Defence Staff yesterday. Mr. Hettiarachchi was received by the Chief of Defence Staff, Air Chief Marshal Kolitha Gunatilleke, the Chief of Staff, Major General Lasantha Wickramasuriya and the Director Generals.
