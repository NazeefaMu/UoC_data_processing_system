## Title: Drowned student couple: boy’s body found
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 12 February 2015 05:36 am

Drowned student couple: boy’s body found


	

Body of the 17-year-old school boy who was alleged to have jumped into the Deduru Oya with a girl was found in Deduru Oya Reservoir today, the Police said.
They said search operations were in progress to recover the body of the missing girl.
