## Title: ‘CISM’ Run- 2017
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 18 February 2017 10:42 am

‘CISM’ Run- 2017


	



 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 


The International Military Sport Council (CISM) Day Run - 2017 was held at the Galle Face Green this morning which coincided with the World Military Day. Army Commander Lieutenant General Crishanthe De Silva, Navy Commander Vice Admiral Ravindra Wijegunaratne and Air Force Commander Air Marshal Kapila Jayampathi, Senior Army, Navy and Air Force officers took the lead in the CISM run.
