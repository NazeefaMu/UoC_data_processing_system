## Title: Durdans Hospital submits medical report on Sicille Kotelawala
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 8 February 2016 12:37 pm

Durdans Hospital submits medical report on Sicille Kotelawala


	

Colombo Chief Magistrate Gihan Pilapitiya today inspected the medical report submitted by the doctors of the Durdans Hospital on Mrs. Sicille Kotelawala in which they had said that they have no objections to transfer Mrs. Kotelawala to the Colombo National Hospital.

Defence Counsel said they also had no objection to transfer their client to the National Hospital but would submit a motion to the Colombo Fort Magistrate’s Court tomorrow, since Fort Magistrate had earlier ordered not to transfer her.  (Shehan Chamika Silva)
