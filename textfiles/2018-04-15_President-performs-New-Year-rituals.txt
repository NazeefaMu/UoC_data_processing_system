## Title: President performs New Year rituals
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 15 April 2018 12:45 pm

President performs New Year rituals


	



President Maithripala Sirisena yesterday performed New Year rituals at his official residence in Colombo with the participation of First Lady Jayanthi Sirisena, other members of the immediate family, several parliamentarians and artists.
