## Title: Remembering FR
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 1 January 2016 04:18 pm

Remembering FR


	


The 90th death anniversary of F.R. Senanayake, a leading member of the Sri Lanka's independence movement, was commemorated in Colombo today in the presence of a distinguished gathering of family members and friends. The late leader's great-grandson Kositha Senanayake is seen paying tribute after garlanding the statue. Pic by Samantha Perera
