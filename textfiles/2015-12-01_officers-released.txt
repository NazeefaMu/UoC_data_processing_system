## Title: Rs. 125mn bribe: Customs officers released on bail
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 December 2015 05:11 am

Rs. 125mn bribe: Customs officers released on bail


	

The three Customs officers who were in remand custody over soliciting a bribe of Rs. 125 million, were released by Colombo Chief Magistrate Gihan Pilapitiya today.
The three officers were released on Rs. 100,000 bail and two sureties of Rs. 5 million each. They were banned from travelling abroad and were asked to report to the Bribery Commission on the last Sunday of every month.
The three Customs officers including a superintendent were arrested by the Bribery Commission on October 15 for soliciting a bribe of Rs.125 million from a businessman in Panchikawatta. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
