## Title: Alms giving by rehabilitated drug addicts
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 19 July 2016 03:56 pm

Alms giving by rehabilitated drug addicts


	




Rehabilitated drug addicts undergoing vocational training at the Tertiary and Vocational Education Training Centre at Senapura, Welikanda distributed alms this morning to invoke blessings on the people who died from drug addiction and to rescue addicted to drugs.
