## Title: President meets hacker
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 November 2016 08:00 pm

President meets hacker


	

President Maithripala Sirisena today met the youth, who had hacked into the President' official website.

The CID arrested the 17-year-old boy from Kadugannawa on charges of hacking President Maithripala Sirisena’s official website in August. He was later placed under the supervision of the probation officers for three-year period.
