## Title: Majestic Navam Maha Perahara
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 23 February 2016 01:53 pm

Majestic Navam Maha Perahara


	



The annual Navam Maha Perahara of Gangaramaya Temple, Colombo was held last night. The festival featured elephants dressed in traditional attire, kandyan dancers and drummers. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
