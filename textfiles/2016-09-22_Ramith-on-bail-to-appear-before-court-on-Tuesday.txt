## Title: Ramith on bail; to appear before court on Tuesday
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 September 2016 11:38 am

Ramith on bail; to appear before court on Tuesday


	

Sri Lankan cricketer Ramith Rambukkwella, who was arrested early today over a motor accident, had been released on police-bail a short while ago, the Police said.


He was ordered to appear before Colombo Magistrate on Tuesday, September 27.


He was arrested over an accident that took place at the Independence Avenue in Colombo early this morning.
