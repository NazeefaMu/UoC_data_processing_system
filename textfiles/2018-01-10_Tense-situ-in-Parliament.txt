## Title: Tense situ in Parliament
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 10 January 2018 11:30 am

Tense situ in Parliament


	



A tense situation prevailed in Parliament today after joint opposition Parliamentarians staged a protest in the middle of the chamber during Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe’s speech on the Bond Commission’s report.
