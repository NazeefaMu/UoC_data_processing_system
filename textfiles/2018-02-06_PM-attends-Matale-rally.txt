## Title: PM attends Matale rally
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 6 February 2018 07:11 pm

PM attends Matale rally


	



Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe participated in an election rally held in Matale today. State Minister of Agriculture Wasantha Aluwihare, MPs Ranjith Aluwihare, Rohini Wijerathna and a large numbers of party supporters participated in the rally.
