## Title: Court order banning protests in H’tota
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 January 2017 07:45 pm

Court order banning protests in H’tota


	

The Hambantota Magistrate has issued a court order banning all protests in the Hambantota area within the course of the day.


Sources said this order was conveyed to 26 individuals personally.
