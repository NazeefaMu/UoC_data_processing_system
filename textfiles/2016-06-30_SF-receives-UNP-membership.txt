## Title: SF receives UNP membership
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 June 2016 12:35 pm

SF receives UNP membership


	

Regional Development Minister and Field Marshal, Sarath Fonseka today obtained membership of the United National Party (UNP).

His membership card was handed over by Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe, who also appointed Minister Fonseka as the UNP Organiser for Kelaniya.
