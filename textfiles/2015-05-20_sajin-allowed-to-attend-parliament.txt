## Title: Sajin allowed to attend parliament
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 20 May 2015 05:23 am

Sajin allowed to attend parliament


	

Former External Affairs Monitoring MP Sajin de Vass Gunawaradena was today granted permission by the Colombo Fort Magistrate to attend today’s parliamentary sessions.
MP Gunawaradene was earlier remanded for allegedly misusing the vehicles belonging to the Presidential Secretariat during the previous government. (Lakmal Sooriyagoda)
