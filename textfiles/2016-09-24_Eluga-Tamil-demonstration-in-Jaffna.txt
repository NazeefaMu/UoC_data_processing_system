## Title: ‘Eluga Tamil’ demonstration in Jaffna
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 24 September 2016 01:46 pm

‘Eluga Tamil’ demonstration in Jaffna


	



The demonstration ‘Eluga Tamil’ (Let Tamils Rise!) organised by the Tamil People’s Council (TPC) led by Chief Minister C.V. Wigneswaran commenced in Jaffna today. All the shops in Jaffna were seen closed while Private Bus Owners Association in Jaffna also participated in the demonstration which brought the bus service to a standstill.
