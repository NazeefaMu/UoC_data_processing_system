## Title: Nalaka Kaluwewa appointed Actg Director General of Information
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 October 2018 02:01 pm

Nalaka Kaluwewa appointed Actg Director General of Information


	

Acting Additional Secretary of Presidential Secretariat, Nalaka Kaluwewa has been appointed Acting Director General of Information, President's Media Division said.

It said the appointment had been made under the instructions of the President.
