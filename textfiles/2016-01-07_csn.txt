## Title: FCID visits CSN again
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 7 January 2016 09:16 am

FCID visits CSN again


	


	Police Financial Crimes Investigation Division (FCID) Officers today visited the Carlton Sports Network (CSN) television to collect data of the emails in connection with the ongoing investigation.
	The Police said three officers, two computer experts and two IT Police officers went to the CSN office this morning to investigate email accounts with a search warrant obtained from the Kaduwela Magistrate’s Court.
	The FCID officers had also visited the CSN television office on several occasions to collect evidence relevant to the probe. They had collected certain documents pertaining to the inquiry and relating to its assets.(DS)
