## Title: Late president D.B. Wijetunga’s daughter passes away
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 February 2017 08:08 am

Late president D.B. Wijetunga’s daughter passes away


	

The late President D.B. Wijetunga’s daughter Chithrangani Wijetunga passed away at the age of 60 this morning at a private hospital in Kandy, hospital sources said.

She was admitted to the hospital yesterday due to a sudden illness and she was to be transferred to a hospital in Colombo for further treatment. (Chamil Rupasinghe)
