## Title: 18 students injured as classroom roof collapses
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 March 2017 12:20 pm

18 students injured as classroom roof collapses


	

Eighteen grade five students were admitted to the Kandy Teaching Hospital this morning when a part of the roof of their classroom collapsed due to the heavy rains experienced in the area, police said.

The incident was reported from the Sririmalwatta Navodya School in Wattegama, Kandy.
