## Title: <U+200B>Democracy was upheld during CBK's term-President
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 July 2015 03:20 am

<U+200B>Democracy was upheld during CBK's term-President


	


	President Maithripala Sirisena said yesterday that the Bandaranaike family did everything for the country without corruption and fraud. 
	The president was speaking at a function to mark the 70th birthday of former president Chandrika Bandaranaike Kumaratunga. He said democracy was upheld during the term of Mrs. Kumaratunga, and everybody was consulted when taking decisions. “She was humble enough to correct her mistakes whenever they were pointed out,” he said.
