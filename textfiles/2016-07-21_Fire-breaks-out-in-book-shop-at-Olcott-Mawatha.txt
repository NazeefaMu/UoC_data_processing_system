## Title: Fire breaks out in book shop at Olcott Mawatha
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 21 July 2016 10:05 pm

Fire breaks out in book shop at Olcott Mawatha


	



A fire broke out at a bookshop in Olcott Mawatha in Pettah this evening. About eight fire trucks were rushed to the scene of the fire in an attempt to bring the fire under control. No one was injured as a result of the fire and the cause of the fire had not yet been determined. Pix by Nisal Baduge
