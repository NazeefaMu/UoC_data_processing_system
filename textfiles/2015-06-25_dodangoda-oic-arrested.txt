## Title: Dodangoda OIC arrested
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 June 2015 08:07 am

Dodangoda OIC arrested


	

Dodangoda Police OIC was arrested today in connection with an accident which occurred in Beruwala last night, Police said.
They said the car which was alleged to have driven by the OIC had collided with a vehicle injuring three people. (LSP)
