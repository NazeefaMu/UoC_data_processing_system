## Title: Striking port workers observe ‘sil’
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 13 December 2016 05:44 pm

Striking port workers observe ‘sil’


	



The Magampura Port workers, on their 7th day of sathyagraha, observed sil at the Port premises today. They are demanding that the 483 of them be absorbed into the permanent cadre of the Ports Authority. Pix by Dilip Jayasekara
