## Title: Police officer in EC convoy dies
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 12 January 2015 05:40 am

Police officer in EC convoy dies


	

One police officer of the Elections Commissioner’s convoy was killed and another injured when their motorbike collided with a school van on the Maharagama-Kottawa Road this morning, the Police said.  
The injured police officer was admitted to the Colombo South Teaching Hospital.
