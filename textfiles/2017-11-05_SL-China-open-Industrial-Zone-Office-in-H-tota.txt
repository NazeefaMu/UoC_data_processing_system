## Title: SL, China open Industrial Zone Office in H'tota
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 5 November 2017 09:36 am

SL, China open Industrial Zone Office in H'tota


	



Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe declared open the Sri Lanka-China Logistics and Industrial Zone Office in Hambantota. Ministers Mahinda Amarawwera, Mahinda Samarasinghe, Sagala Ratnayake Chinese Ambassador in Sri Lanka Yi Xianlang participated in the event. Pics by Pradeep Pathirana
