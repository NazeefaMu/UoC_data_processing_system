## Title: Veteran Journalist Ariyadasa Peiris passes away
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 10 February 2017 04:49 pm

Veteran Journalist Ariyadasa Peiris passes away


	

Veteran Journalist and Radio personality Dr. Ariyadasa Peiris passed away this morning at the age of 91, his family sources said.

He was a pioneer senior announcer at the then Radio Ceylon.
