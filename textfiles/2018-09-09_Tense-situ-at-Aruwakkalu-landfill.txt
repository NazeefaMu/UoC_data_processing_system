## Title: Tense situ at Aruwakkalu landfill
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 9 September 2018 05:11 pm

Tense situ at Aruwakkalu landfill


	


A tense situation prevailed allegedly on false information on the Aruwakkalu Hygienic Solid Waste Disposal Project (AHSWDP) in Puttalam early today. Megapolis and Western Development Minister Patali Champika Ranawaka held a discussion with members of Pradeshiya Sabha and Divisional Secretary of Wanatawilluwa, when the protest was held.
