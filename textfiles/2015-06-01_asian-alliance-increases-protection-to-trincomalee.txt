## Title: Asian Alliance increases protection to Trincomalee
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 1 June 2015 02:50 am

Asian Alliance increases protection to Trincomalee


	


Asian Alliance Insurance has opened its second Life insurance branch at Trincomalee, expanding its Life network in the country to 53 branches.  
The new Trincomalee Metro Branch located at N. C. Road, Trincomalee, will offer the full-range of services offered by Life branches of Asian Alliance Insurance. It will be equipped with the company’s state-of-the-art ICT system and will be staffed by experienced personnel, enabling a professional service of high standard to be offered to clients.
The branch was ceremonially opened by Asian Alliance Insurance’s Chief Operating Officer – Life, Chula Hettiarachchi in the presence of several other senior company officials including Sandamal Hettiarachchi (DGM – Colombo Central SBU), Kapila Sooriyaarachchi (DGM – Panadura SBU) and Mihilal Nishantha (AGM – Negombo SBU).  
Asian Alliance Insurance PLC is held by Softlogic Capital PLC and is part of the Softlogic Group, which is recognized as one of Sri Lanka’s most diversified and fastest-growing conglomerates with interests in Healthcare, Retail, ICT, Leisure, Automobiles and Financial Services. Significant stakeholders in the company also include FMO and DEG that are rated ‘AAA’ Development Financial Institutions from the Netherlands and Germany respectively.
