## Title: Video: Premier meets Indian Minister Sushma Swaraj
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 5 February 2016 05:38 pm

Video: Premier meets Indian Minister Sushma Swaraj


	




Indian External Affairs Minister Sushma Swaraj today met Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe at Temple Trees during her two-day official visit to Sri Lanka.


The discussions will focus on important areas such as economic cooperation, trade, power and energy, technical and maritime cooperation, social, cultural and educational matters. Pix by Waruna Wanniarachchi
