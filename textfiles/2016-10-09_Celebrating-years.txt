## Title: Celebrating 140 years
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 9 October 2016 09:52 am

Celebrating 140 years


	



Prince and Princess of Wales College, Moratuwa yesterday celebrated 140 years.  A walk was organized by the school and past pupils association along the Galle Road in Moratuwa. Pix by Kithsiri de Mel
