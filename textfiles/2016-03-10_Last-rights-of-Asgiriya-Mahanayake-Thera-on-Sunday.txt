## Title: Last rights of Asgiriya Mahanayake Thera on Sunday
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 10 March 2016 09:50 am

Last rights of Asgiriya Mahanayake Thera on Sunday


	

The last rights of most venerable Mahanayake of the Asgiriya Chapter Ven. Galagama Attadassi Thera will be held at the Kandy police ground in Asgiriya at 3.00 p.m. on Sunday (13). (Nadeeka Dayabandara)
