## Title: Namal Rajapaksa arrested
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 July 2016 02:08 pm

Namal Rajapaksa arrested


	

UPFA MP Namal Rajapaksa was arrested a short while ago by the Financial Crime Investigation Division (FCID) in connection with financial misappropriation during the previous Government.   

He was summoned by the FCID this morning to record a statement over an incident of financial misappropriation amounting to Rs. 70 million.

The Police said he would be produced in court today.
