## Title: 30 women injured in wasp attack
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 October 2016 02:05 pm

30 women injured in wasp attack


	

At least thirty tea pluckers who were working at an estate in Bagawanthalawa have been hospitalised following a wasp attack this morning, Police said.

They were admitted to the Bagawanthalawa Hospital. (Ranjith Rajapaksa)
