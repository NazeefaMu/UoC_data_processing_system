## Title: Fallen branch damages vehicles near Eye Hospital
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 29 December 2015 06:28 am

Fallen branch damages vehicles near Eye Hospital


	


		 		 
		 
			
Several vehicles have been damaged due to fallen branch of a giant tree at De Saram Place near the Colombo Eye Hospital. No causalities have been reported. A branch of the giant Bo tree has fallen early morning today on vehicles including a van and a car that had been parked under it.
The Fire Service Department is taking steps to remove the fallen branch.(H.M.Dharmapala)
