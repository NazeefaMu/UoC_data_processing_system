## Title: Top Chinese advisor calls on PM
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 7 April 2017 06:31 pm

Top Chinese advisor calls on PM


	

Visiting Chairman of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Yu Zhengsheng met Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe at the Temple Trees today. Zhengsheng visited Sri Lanka at the invitation of Speaker Karu Jayasuriya. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
