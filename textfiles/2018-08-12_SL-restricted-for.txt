## Title: SL restricted for 299/8
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 12 August 2018 06:53 pm

SL restricted for 299/8


	


Sri Lanka scored 299 for eight from their 50 overs in the 5th ODI against South Africa at the R. Premadasa Stadium a short while ago. Captain Angelo Mathews scored 97 not out. South Africa lead the series 3-1. Pics by Kushan Pathiraja
