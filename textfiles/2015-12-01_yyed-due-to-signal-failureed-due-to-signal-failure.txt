## Title: Trains delayed due to signal failure
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 December 2015 11:51 am

Trains delayed due to signal failure


	

Trains travelling via the Maradana Railway Station, on the mainline and along the coastal belt were delayed as a result of a signal failure at the Maradana Railway Station, the Railway Control Room said.
It said the technicians were attending to the repairs.
