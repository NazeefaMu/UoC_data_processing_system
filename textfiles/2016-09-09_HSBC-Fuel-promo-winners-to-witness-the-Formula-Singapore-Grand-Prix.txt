## Title: HSBC Fuel promo winners to witness the 2016 Formula 1 Singapore Grand Prix
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 9 September 2016 12:00 am

HSBC Fuel promo winners to witness the 2016 Formula 1 Singapore Grand Prix


	

The winners with Nilantha Bastian, Head of Retail Banking and Wealth Management, Aasiri Iddamalgoda, Head of Cards, Products and Services, HSBC together with Siddharth Agarwal, Senior Vice President, Lanka IOC PLC and Surein Gomez, Asst. Sales Manager, Lanka IOC PLC

Six lucky HSBC credit card customers will travel to Singapore this September to witness world famous racers as they take to the track for a pulsating competition at the 2016 Formula 1 Singapore Grand Prix.


The winners Punya Arambewela, S. Godigamuwa, P. M. Balasuriya, M. Muzanif, R Weerakkody and Mudiyanselage Daminda were each presented with a fully sponsored travel package to catch the live action from the 16-18 September at the Marina Bay Street Circuit in Singapore. The entertainment off track is expected to be as exciting as on- track, as HSBC customers get to witness live performances by international artists. 


Winners were selected during monthly draws, through HSBC’s fuel promotion in partnership with LIOC, which required customers to use their HSBC credit card to pump fuel at any LIOC fuel station island-wide for a minimum value of Rs. 1000.
