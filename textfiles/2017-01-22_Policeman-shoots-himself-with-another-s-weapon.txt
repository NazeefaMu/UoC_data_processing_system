## Title: Policeman shoots himself with another’s weapon
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 January 2017 09:07 am

Policeman shoots himself with another’s weapon


	

A police officer of the Mangalagama Police received serious injuries when he shot himself with the fellow officer’s duty weapon last night.

Police said the injured was rushed to Gonagolla Hospital and transferred to the Ampara general hospital.
