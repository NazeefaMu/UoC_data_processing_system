## Title: CP Governor Ellawala no more
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 14 March 2016 08:03 am

CP Governor Ellawala no more


	

The Governor of the Central Province Surangani Ellawala passed away this morning at the Kandy Hospital at the aged of 76.

She was the spouse of former MP Nanda Ellawella and mother of former UPFA MP Nalanda Ellawela.
