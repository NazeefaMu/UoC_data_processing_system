## Title: Ranil attends ‘Dathu Mandira’ opening ceremony
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 31 October 2016 06:27 pm

Ranil attends ‘Dathu Mandira’ opening ceremony


	



Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghes attended the opening ceremony of ‘Dathu Mandira’ at the Gothatuwa Kanda Viharaya, Gothatuwa today. Deputy Speaker Thilanga Sumathiapala was also present. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
