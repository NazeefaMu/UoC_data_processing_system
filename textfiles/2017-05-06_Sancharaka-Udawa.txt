## Title: Sancharaka Udawa 2017
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 6 May 2017 02:44 pm

Sancharaka Udawa 2017


	


‘Sancharaka Udawa’ 2017, Sri Lanka’s biggest tourism fair was held at the BMICH today. The ceremony was declared open by the Tourism Development Minister John Amaratunga. Stalls with tourism promotion and souvenirs were exhibited during the event. Pixs by Kithsiri De Mel
