## Title: FCID summons Muzammil
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 August 2016 02:54 pm

FCID summons Muzammil


	

National Freedom Front (NFF) spokesman Mohammed Muzammil was summoned by the FCID to record a statement in connection with the investigations into several questionable appointments made when he was the Working Director of the State Engineering Corporation.
