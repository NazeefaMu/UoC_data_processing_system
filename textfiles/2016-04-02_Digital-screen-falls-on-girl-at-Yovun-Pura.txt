## Title: Digital screen falls on girl at Yovun Pura
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 2 April 2016 07:13 am

Digital screen falls on girl at Yovun Pura


	

An injured girl was rushed to hospital when a digital screen erected at the Yovun Pura Exhibition at Sigiriya fell on her last afternoon.

The organizers of Yovun pura said the girl had sustained serious injuries to her limbs and spine and she was seen in a state of shock.

The girl was rushed to the medical centre within the Yovun Pura site initially and later she was transferred to the Dambulla Hospital, according to the sources. (Yohan Perera and Kanchana Kumara Ariyadasa)
