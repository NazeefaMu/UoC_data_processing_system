## Title: Man kills wife in Tangalle, commits suicide
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 April 2018 03:39 pm

Man kills wife in Tangalle, commits suicide


	

A 22-year-old woman was killed by her husband near the Weligediya Beach at Kudawella in Tangalle this afternoon, police said.

They said the husband had committed suicide afterwards in the Suriyawella area.
