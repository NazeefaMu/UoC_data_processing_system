## Title: Last day of the Exhibition and Book Fair
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 28 July 2016 10:50 am

Last day of the Exhibition and Book Fair


	



The Colombo National Education Book Fair now being held at the Public Library Premises will end today. The Exhibition and Book Fair is open to the public from 9.00 am to 7.00 pm. Pix by Nisal Baduge
