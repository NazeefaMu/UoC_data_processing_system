## Title: Protest by trade unions at Parliament Road
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 25 February 2016 05:54 pm

Protest by trade unions at Parliament Road


	



The United Trade Union Federation yesterday staged a protest at the Parliament roundabout condemning the attack on Provincial Council Member and Secretary of the All Ceylon Samurdhi Development and Agricultural Research Assistants’ Union Jagath Kumara’s residence. The demonstration created a heavy traffic congestion on Borella-Battaramulla Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
