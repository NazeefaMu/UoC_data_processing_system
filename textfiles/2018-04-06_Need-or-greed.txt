## Title: Need or greed
## Section: Political Gossip
## Date: 6 April 2018 10:09 am

Need or greed


	



This lovely lady now occupying the chair of a recently elected local body has reportedly informed the white-collar high-ups concerned of her wish to have a brand new cab for her official transport. 


This latest item on the lady’s wish list has raised many an eyebrow as the vehicle used by her predecessor is readily available and is in a sound condition. 


The lady’s apparent insatiable desire for new things ever since taking the coveted office is the main talking point among the employees of this important mini-governing body, they say.
