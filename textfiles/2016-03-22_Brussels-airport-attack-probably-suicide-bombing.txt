## Title: Brussels airport attack probably suicide bombing
## Section: World
## Date: 22 March 2016 06:54 pm

Brussels airport attack probably suicide bombing


	

The twin blasts at Brussels airport were probably caused by at least one suicide bomber, a Belgian prosecutor said on Tuesday.

“There were two explosions in the departure area, one probably caused by a suicide bomber,” Frederic Van Leeuw told a news conference, alongside the Belgian premier Charles Michel. (REUTERS)
