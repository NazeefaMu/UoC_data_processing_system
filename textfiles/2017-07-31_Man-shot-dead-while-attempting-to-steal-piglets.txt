## Title: Man shot dead while attempting to steal piglets
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 31 July 2017 09:50 am

Man shot dead while attempting to steal piglets


	

A man who allegedly attempted to steal piglets from a piggery at Pussallawa in Kosgama, was shot dead by a security guard early this morning.

Police said the victim was yet to be identified and the security guard has been arrested.
