## Title: Ven. Gnansara Thera released on bail
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 February 2016 03:55 pm

Ven. Gnansara Thera released on bail


	

BBS General Secretary Ven. Galagodaatte Gnansara Thera, arrested and remanded on January 26, on charges of contempt of court, was released on bail by the Homagama Magistrate today.


The Thera was granted bail with two sureties of Rs.100,000 each.
