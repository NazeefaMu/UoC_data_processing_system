## Title: Bathiudeen questioned by PRECIFAC
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 August 2016 11:29 am

Bathiudeen questioned by PRECIFAC


	

Industry and Commerce Minister Rishad Bathiudeen today arrived at the Presidential Commission of Inquiry to Investigate and Inquire into Serious Acts of Fraud, Corruption and Abuse of Power, State Resources and Privileges (PRECIFAC).

A spokesman for the commission said a statement is being recorded from the Minister by the Police unit of the commission.
