## Title: Ex STC chief grilled by FCID
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 May 2015 07:47 am

Ex STC chief grilled by FCID


	

Former State Trading Corporation (STC) Chairman Pradeep Gunawardane is being questioned by the Financial Crimes Investigation Division (FCID) over an alleged financial irregularity at the STC during his tenure.
Police Spokesman Ruwan Gunasekara said the irregularity, running into Rs. 2 billion had occurred when importing rice and milk powder.
