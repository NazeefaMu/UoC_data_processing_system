## Title: No decision at Kurunegala
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, December 17, 2015 - 01:00

No decision at Kurunegala

The Singer All Island School Under 13 Cricket Tournament Division III, 2nd Round cricket match between Maliyadeva College, Kurunegala and Prince College, Kurunegala ended in a no decision due to rain played at Maliyadeva College Ground recently.
Homesters batting first scored 177 for 9 in 54.1 overs with Amesh Thennakoon making 53 runs and Akash Rajapaksha contributing 30 runs. In reply visitors were 70 for 5 at the close. Chamidu Wickramasinghe scored 36 runs and Vihanga Gunaratna made 21 runs.
Maliyadeva College - 177 for 9 wickets in 54.1 overs (Amesh Thennakoon 53, Nandika Balalla 19, Akash Rajapaksha 30, Gayana Weerasinghe 20, Chamindu Wickramasinghe 3 for 59, Isuru Sapuwida 3 for 18, Deshan Piyasinghe 2 for 11).
Prince College - 70 for 5 wickets in 22.1 overs (Chamindu Wickramasinghe 36, Vihanga Gunarathna 21, Gayana Weerasinghe 2 for 13, Sakindu Wijerathna 2 for 14).
