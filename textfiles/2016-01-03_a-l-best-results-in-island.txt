## Title: A/L best results in Island
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 January 2016 04:02 am

A/L best results in Island


	


	Dasun Jayasingha of Colombo Royal College and Umesha Karunawellada of Ratnawali Balika Vidyalaya, Gampaha have topped the island rankings at the GCE Advanced Level Examination in 2015, Maths and Bio-Science streams respectively.
	Meanwhile, Jeeva Nayanamali of Maliyadeva Girls’ College, Kurunegala and Akil Mohommed of Maliyadeva Boys’ College, Kurunegala have topped the Island rankings in Arts and Commerce streams respectively.
