## Title: Randoli Perahera parades the streets
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 14 August 2016 08:16 pm

Randoli Perahera parades the streets


	



The first Randoli Perahera of the historic Kandy Esala Perahera Festival paraded the streets last night. A large number of devotees and tourists were present in Kandy to witness the procession.
