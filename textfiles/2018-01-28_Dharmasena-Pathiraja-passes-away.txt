## Title: Dharmasena Pathiraja passes away
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 28 January 2018 08:59 am

Dharmasena Pathiraja passes away


	

Award winning film director and screen writer Dharmasena Pathiraja passed away at the age of 74 at a private hospital in Kandy this morning.

The funeral will take place today (28) at 4 pm at the Kandy Mahaiyyawa Cemetery. The remains now lie at the Nuwara Mal Shalawa (Funeral Parlour), Keppetipola Road, Kandy.
