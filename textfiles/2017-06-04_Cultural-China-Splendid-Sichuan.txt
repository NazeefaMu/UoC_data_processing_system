## Title: 'Cultural China, Splendid Sichuan’
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 June 2017 08:36 pm

'Cultural China, Splendid Sichuan’


	


‘Cultural China, Splendid Sichuan’ Sri Lanka tour 2017 organized by the Embassy of People’s Republic of China, Department of Foreign and Overseas Chinese Affairs and Sichuan Provincial People’s Government was held at the Nelum Pokuna Mahinda Rajapaksa Theatre today. The event was colourful with many Chinese traditional events marking the 60 years of Chinese and Sri Lankan relationship. -Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
