## Title: Gammanpila to make a statement to SIU
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 June 2016 05:27 pm

Gammanpila to make a statement to SIU


	

MP Udaya Gammanpila has been asked to report to the Police Special Investigation Unit (SIU) on Monday to give a statement over a complaint with regard to defrauding shares of a company.

He was summoned by the SIU last Monday but he had requested for another date saying that he was unable to make it that day.
