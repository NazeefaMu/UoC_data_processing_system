## Title: Victor Ratnayaka’s wife remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 January 2018 05:06 pm

Victor Ratnayaka’s wife remanded


	

Veteran singer Victor Ratnayake’s wife Hashini Amendra was remanded till January 17 by the Tangalle Magistrate today.

She was arrested by the Tangalle Police this morning for the alleged misappropriation of Rs. 1.28 million worth of jewelry belonging to a state bank. (Chandrasena Gamage)
