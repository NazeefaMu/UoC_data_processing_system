## Title: Heavy traffic due to protest in Meethotamulla
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 March 2017 10:35 am

Heavy traffic due to protest in Meethotamulla


	

The Road from Orugodawattha to Wellampitiya on the Colombo-Awissawella road was blocked due to a protest carried out by the resident in Meethotamulla against the garbage dump.

Police advised motorist to use alternative roads to avoid the traffic congestion.
