## Title: Flood affected areas to be marked HSZs : President
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 May 2016 07:40 pm

Flood affected areas to be marked HSZs : President


	

President Maithripala Sirisena today instructed the officials of the newly establish special Environmental Relief Task Force to mark the flood affected areas as High Security Zones.

The President had ordered them to be on the lookout for encroachment in the Colombo area and not to reclaim lands in the Colombo District any more.
