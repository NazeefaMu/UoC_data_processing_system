## Title: Basil still in ambulance
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 20 May 2015 09:21 am

Basil still in ambulance


	


The Police take precaution as supporters of former Minister, Basil Rajapaksa attempt to converge on the ambulance carrying him. Mr. Rajapaksa, a once powerful minister in the country is accused of embezzling public funds.He was further remanded till May 27 by the Kaduwela Magistrate today. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
