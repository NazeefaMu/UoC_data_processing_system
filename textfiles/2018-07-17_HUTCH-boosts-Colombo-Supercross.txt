## Title: HUTCH boosts Colombo Supercross
## Section: Press Releases
## Date: 17 July 2018 01:01 pm

HUTCH boosts Colombo Supercross


	



HUTCH, Sri Lanka’s fastest growing mobile telecommunications company, continued its support to motor racing in the country by stepping forward to power the recently-concluded Colombo Supercross 2018, the motor sports event organized by the Sri Lanka Navy in partnership with the Sri Lanka Association of Racing Drivers and Riders (SLARDAR).The Colombo Supercross 2018 races were held at the exciting gravel racing track in Welisara, Ragama. A large number of drivers and riders including the tri-forces took part in the event making it one of the most successful events in this year’s motor racing calendar
