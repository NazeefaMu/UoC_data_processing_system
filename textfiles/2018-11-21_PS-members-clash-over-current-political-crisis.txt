## Title: PS members clash over current political crisis
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 21 November 2018 12:12 pm

PS members clash over current political crisis


	

UPFA and UNP members of the Balapitiya Pradehsiya Sabha clashed over the current political crisis today. UPFA members criticized Speaker Karu Jayasuriya’s behaviour in Parliament recently, while UNP members criticized President Maithripala Sirisena’s actions.
