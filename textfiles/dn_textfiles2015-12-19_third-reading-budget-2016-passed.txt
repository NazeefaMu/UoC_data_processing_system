## Title: Third reading of the Budget 2016 passed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Saturday, December 19, 2015 - 19:15

Third reading of the Budget 2016 passed

 
The third reading of the Budget 2016 was passed in Parliament this evening with 160 votes in favour, 51 against, while 13 parliamentarian were absent while the vote was taken. The JVP and the 'joint opposition' voted against the budget during the third reading.
