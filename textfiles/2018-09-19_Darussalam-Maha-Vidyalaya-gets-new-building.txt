## Title: Darussalam Maha Vidyalaya gets new building
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 19 September 2018 02:03 pm

Darussalam Maha Vidyalaya gets new building


	



Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe declared open the new four-storey building of the Darussalam Maha Vidyalaya in Maligawattha on Wednesday. Member of Parliament Mujiber Rahman also attended the event. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
