## Title: Sicille remanded over Ceylinco Shriram case
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 7 April 2016 11:10 am

Sicille remanded over Ceylinco Shriram case


	

Former Ceylinco Chairman Lalith Kotelawala's wife Sicille Kotelawala was ordered to be further reprimanded till April 19th over the alleged misappropriation of funds in Ceylinco Shriram Company, by the Colombo Forth Magistrate today.
