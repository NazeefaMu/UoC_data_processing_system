## Title: MJF Training Centre declared open
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 1 September 2018 07:00 pm

MJF Training Centre declared open


	


Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe declared opened the MJF Training centre, which is a vocational training centre built especially for the differently-abled children in Kalkudah on Saturday. Dilmah Chairman Merrill J Fernando was also present. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
