## Title: Cabana fire kills a man in hotel
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 September 2016 07:24 am

Cabana fire kills a man in hotel


	

A 47-year-old man was reported to have burnt to death after a cabana caught fire at a tourist hotel in Pottuvil last night, police said.

They said the victim was identified as Sellaiya Kaalidasan, a resident of Kiriwandolawatta area in Deniyaya.

Pottuvil police are conducting investigations.
