## Title: SriLankan cancels flights to Chennai
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 12 December 2016 11:48 am

SriLankan cancels flights to Chennai


	



All SriLankan flights leaving for Chennai from the Bandaranaike International Airport have been canceled until tomorrow morning due to the cyclone Vardah.

Therefore, SriLankan flights UL - 121, UL - 123, UL - 127 and UL – 129 scheduled to leave for Chennai would be canceled.

All Chennai bound passengers are advised to contact SriLankan Airlines for more details
