## Title: Two killed, two injured in head-on collision
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 October 2016 11:31 am

Two killed, two injured in head-on collision


	

Two women were killed and two others including a three year-old child injured when a three-wheeler collided head on with a cab at Madola on the Ratnapura-Avissawella Road early this morning, Police said.

They said the accident occurred when the three-wheeler tried to overtake another vehicle.

The deceased aged 70 and 40 were residents of Thuruvila, Erannagoda.

The injured were admitted to the National Hospital, Colombo.
