## Title: Ruwan meets Google Loon pioneer
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 6 April 2016 10:52 am

Ruwan meets Google Loon pioneer


	

 



A special discussion was held between President, State Minister of Defence Ruwan Wijewardene and Sri Lankan scholar of Social Capital Institute in America Chamath Palihapitiya who had been a pioneer of Google Loon project in order to strengthen the Sri Lankan Wi-Fi coverage.
