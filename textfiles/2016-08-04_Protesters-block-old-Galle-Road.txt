## Title: Protesters block old Galle Road
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 August 2016 12:55 pm

Protesters block old Galle Road


	


 
A group of people obstructed vehicular traffic along a part of the old Galle Road this morning urging the authorities to repair the Panadura, Pallimulla and Gorakapola stretch of the old Galle Road. Pix Bimal Shaman Jayasinghe
