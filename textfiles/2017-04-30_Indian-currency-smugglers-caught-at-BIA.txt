## Title: Indian currency smugglers caught at BIA
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 April 2017 06:31 am

Indian currency smugglers caught at BIA


	

Two Indians were arrested by the customs at the Bandaranayake International Airport yesterday when they attempted to smuggle out Rs. 120 million foreign currency to Singapore.

According to the airport customs officials, the suspects had bills from 14 different countries in their possession.
