## Title: Warnasinghe is President’s Special Project Director
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 February 2016 04:41 am

Warnasinghe is President’s Special Project Director


	

Former Western Province Health Minister Nishantha Sri Warnasinghe has been appointed by President Maithripala Sirisena as the President’s Special Project Director in the Western Province.


The appointment was given to him after President Sirisena invited Mr. Warnasinghe to the President’s Office yesterday.


Earlier, Mr. Warnasinghe was removed from the ministerial portfolio after the budget submitted by him was defeated in the council. (Ajantha Kumara Agalakada)
