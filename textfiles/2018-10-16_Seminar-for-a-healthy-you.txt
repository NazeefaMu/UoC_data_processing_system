## Title: Seminar for a healthy you
## Section: News Features
## Date: 16 October 2018 12:00 am

Seminar for a healthy you


	

A seminar on surviving Cancer and Lifestyle, Obesity and Diabetes was held on September 22 (Saturday) at the GFS Hall, Ananda Coomaraswamy Mawatha, Colombo 3 at 10 am.  


The speakers were Dr. Manilka Sumanatilleke (MBBS, MD (Col), MRCP (Lon), MRCP - Diabetes & Endocrinology (UK), FRCP (Edin), FACE (USA), Consultant Endocrinologist - National Hospital of Sri Lanka, President - Sri Lanka College of Endocrinologists) and Dr. Cynthia Jayasuriya (ENT Specialist, Diploma in Laryngo Otology, London).
