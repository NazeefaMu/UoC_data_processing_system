## Title: PM submits six subcommittee reports to Constitutional Assembly
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 November 2016 10:14 am

PM submits six subcommittee reports to Constitutional Assembly


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe today presented the Constitutional Assembly the reports of the six subcommittees appointed for the purpose of drafting a new Constitution.

Six sub committees on Fundamental Rights, Judiciary, Finance, Law and Order, Public Service and Centre Periphery Relations were appointed by the Constitutional Assembly to assist it in evolving a new Constitution.
