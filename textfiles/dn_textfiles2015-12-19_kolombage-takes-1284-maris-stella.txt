Kolombage takes 12/84 for Maris Stella

 
Maris Stella College Negombo took first innings points in their drawn Singer Trophy Under 19 inter-school cricket match against De Mazenod College Kandana at Kadirana yesterday.
Sachindu Kolombage took a match bag of 12 wickets for 84 runs.
