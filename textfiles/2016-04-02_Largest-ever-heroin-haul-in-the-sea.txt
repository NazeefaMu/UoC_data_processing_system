## Title: Largest ever heroin haul in the sea
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 2 April 2016 04:49 pm

Largest ever heroin haul in the sea


	

The Police today issued photos of the largest ever heroin haul in the sea, 101 kg of heroin which was nabbed by the Sri Lanka Navy and the Police Narcotic Bureau(PNB) in a joint operation carried out in the sea.
