## Title: Stranded pilgrims return to the country
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 December 2015 04:12 pm

Stranded pilgrims return to the country


	

About 222 pilgrims who were reported to have been stranded in Dambadiva pilgrimage returned to the country this evening.
The pilgrims returned in two groups that departed from Bodh Gaya and Trichy on Mihin Lanka Airlines this afternoon.
