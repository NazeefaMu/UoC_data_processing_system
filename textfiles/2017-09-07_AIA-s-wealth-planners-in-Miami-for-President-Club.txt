## Title: AIA’s wealth planners in Miami for President Club
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 7 September 2017 12:00 am

AIA’s wealth planners in Miami for President Club


	



Seven of AIA’s top performing Wealth Planners and Wealth Planners’ Managers travelled to Miami, USA together with the Best Distribution Head for the President Club (2016).  The President Club is the most prestigious event organized by AIA Group for AIA’s top achievers, worldwide and this year’s event was held at the Ritz Carlton.  Sri Lanka’s winners were selected based on the highest levels of performance and here we see them along with CEO Pankaj Banerjee and Deputy CEO Upul Wijesinghe.
