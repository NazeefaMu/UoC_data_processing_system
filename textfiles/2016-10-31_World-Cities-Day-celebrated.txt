## Title: World Cities Day celebrated
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 31 October 2016 03:14 pm

World Cities Day celebrated


	



The national celebration of the World Cities Day-2016 was held under the theme of ‘Inclusive Cities Shared Development’ at the Suhurupaya in Battaramulla today under the patronage of Prime Minister RanilWickremesinghe, Megapolis and Western Development Minister PataliChampikaRanawaka and Deputy Minister LasanthaAlagiyawanna.
