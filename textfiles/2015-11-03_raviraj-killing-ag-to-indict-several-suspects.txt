## Title: Video: Raviraj killing: AG to indict several suspects
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 November 2015 02:15 pm

Video: Raviraj killing: AG to indict several suspects


	


	The CID today informed Colombo Additional Magistrate Nirosha Fernando that within the next few weeks the Attorney General would indict several suspects in the High Court on charges of killing former Jaffna District Parliamentarian Nadarajah Raviraj.
	The detectives said the suspect earlier arrested in this connection would be released if found innocent.
	Based on this submission, Magistrate Fernando directed the CID to notice all the suspects in the case to be present in Court on the next call date. (T. Farook Thajudeen)
