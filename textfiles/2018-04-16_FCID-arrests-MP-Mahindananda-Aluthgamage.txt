## Title: FCID arrests MP Mahindananda Aluthgamage
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 April 2018 12:22 pm

FCID arrests MP Mahindananda Aluthgamage


	

United People's Freedom Alliance (UPFA) MP Mahindananda Aluthgamage was arrested by the Financial Crimes Investigation Division (FCID) today on charges of misappropriating government funds amounting to Rs.39 million in 2014 to buy Carrom and Checker-boards.

Police Spokesman SP Ruwan Gunasekara said Mr. Aluthgamage was arrested when he arrived at the FICD this morning to give a statement in connection with the incident.

He would be produced in the Fort Magistrate’s Court.
