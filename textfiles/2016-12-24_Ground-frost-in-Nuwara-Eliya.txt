## Title: Ground frost in Nuwara Eliya
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 24 December 2016 11:26 am

Ground frost in Nuwara Eliya


	



Ground frost appeared in several areas in the Nuwara Eliya District following temperature fell to 6ºC this morning. The frost could be a threat to vegetable cultivation in the area. Pix by Shelton Hettiarachchi
