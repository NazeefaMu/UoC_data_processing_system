## Title: Bartleet changes investment banking arm’s name
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 31 August 2016 12:00 am

Bartleet changes investment banking arm’s name


	

Religare Bartleet Capital Markets (Pvt.) Ltd, the investment banking arm of the Bartleet Religare group, has changed its name to Bartleet Wealth Management 
(Pvt.) Ltd.


The Bartleet Religare group operates in a wide area of the financial sphere, including stockbroking, market research and asset management.
Bartleet Religare is a joint venture between the internationally active financial giant Religare Group of India and Barleet Mallory Stock Brokers of Sri Lanka.
