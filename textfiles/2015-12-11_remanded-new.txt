## Title: Kumar Gunaratnam further remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 December 2015 07:32 am

Kumar Gunaratnam further remanded


	

Frontline Socialist Party's polit-bureau member Kumar Gunaratnam was further remanded till December 18 by the Kegalle Magistrate’s Court today.
He was arrested by the Police in the Anguruwella area in Kegalle on November 04 and had been remanded till today.
