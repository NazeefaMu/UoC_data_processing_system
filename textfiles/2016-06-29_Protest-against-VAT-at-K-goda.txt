## Title: Protest against VAT at K’goda
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 29 June 2016 03:29 pm

Protest against VAT at K’goda


	



Kiribathgoda Traders Association Collective held a protest today at the Kiribathgoda town against the VAT increase. They blocked the Kandy-Colombo Road for several hours. Protesters burnt a coffin in the middle of the town to symbolize the death of VAT. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
