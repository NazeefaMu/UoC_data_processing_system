## Title: Five men, who killed a man, sentenced to death
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 31 October 2016 06:33 pm

Five men, who killed a man, sentenced to death


	

Five men, who killed a man in Maligakanda in 2001, were sentenced to death by Colombo High Court today.

High Court Judge Pathmini N Ranawaka convicted the suspects, Thiruchelvam Nicholas, Wadivel Ravindra, Dinesh Kumar, Chinna Thambi and Ravi on two counts over the murder of a man in Sangaraja Mawatha, Maligakanda on March 14, 2001. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
