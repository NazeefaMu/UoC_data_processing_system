## Title: Woman’s body found in Bolgoda River
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 8 December 2016 07:23 pm

Woman’s body found in Bolgoda River


	

The body of a 35-year-old woman was found in the Bolgoda River near the Panadura Railway Station today, Police said.

The cause of the victim’s death and her identity had not been ascertained yet. (Bimal Shyaman Jayasinghe)
