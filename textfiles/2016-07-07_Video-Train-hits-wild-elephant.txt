## Title: Video: Train hits wild elephant
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 7 July 2016 12:33 pm

Video: Train hits wild elephant


	




A wild elephant of about 30 years was severely injured when it was knocked down by a train on Wednesday (06) night. The veterinary surgeon of the Wildlife Department had attended to the injured animal. Pic by Nimal Jayaratne
