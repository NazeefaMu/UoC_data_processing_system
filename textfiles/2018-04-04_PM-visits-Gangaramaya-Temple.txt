## Title: PM visits Gangaramaya Temple
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 April 2018 11:41 pm

PM visits Gangaramaya Temple


	



Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe, who defeated the no-confidence motion brought up against him by the joint opposition, visited the Gangaramaya Temple this night. Ven. Kirinde Assaji Thera is seen invoking blessings on Mr. Wickremesinghe and his wife. Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
