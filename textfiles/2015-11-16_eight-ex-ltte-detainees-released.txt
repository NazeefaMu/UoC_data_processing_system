## Title: Video: Eight ex-LTTE detainees released
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 November 2015 08:36 am

Video: Eight ex-LTTE detainees released


	

Another eight ex-LTTE detainees were released under strict bail conditions by Colombo Additional Magistrate Aruni Artigala today.
They were released on two surety bails of Rs.1 million each and were ordered to report to the TID every other Sunday. (Shehan Chamika)
