## Title: Video: Body recovered with stab injuries
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 November 2015 07:11 am

Video: Body recovered with stab injuries


	

The body of a 28-year-old man with stab injuries on the neck, was recovered from an abandoned paddy field near the Tumbovila Bridge in Piliyandala this morning, Police said.
They said two gold chains and a bunch of keys were also found with the body. The body was handed over to Kalubowila Hospital. (Dayaratne Pathirana)  Video by Dayaratne Pathirana
