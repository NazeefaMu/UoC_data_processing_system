## Title: Sirisena Amarasekara appointed Prime Minister’s Secretary
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 October 2018 04:03 pm

Sirisena Amarasekara appointed Prime Minister’s Secretary


	

President Maithripala Sirisena has appointed Sirisena Amarasekara as the Secretary to new Prime Minister Mahinda Rajapaksa.
