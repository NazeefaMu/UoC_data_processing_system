## Title: PRECIFAC records statement from MR
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 20 November 2015 04:34 am

PRECIFAC records statement from MR


	

Former President and Kurunegala District MP Mahinda Rajapaksa arrived at the PRECIFAC this morning to give a statement in connection with the non-payment of money owed to the ITN. Former Ministers G.L. Peiris and Keheliya Rambukwella had accompanied Mr. Rajapaksa.WATCH
