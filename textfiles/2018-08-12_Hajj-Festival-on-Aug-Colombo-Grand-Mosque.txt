## Title: Hajj Festival on Aug. 22: Colombo Grand Mosque
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 12 August 2018 11:50 pm

Hajj Festival on Aug. 22: Colombo Grand Mosque


	

Muslims in Sri Lanka will celebrate the Hajj festival on August 22 following the sighting of the new moon, the Colombo Grand Mosque announced today.
