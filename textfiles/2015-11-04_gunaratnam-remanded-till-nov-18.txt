## Title: Gunaratnam remanded till Nov. 18
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 November 2015 03:17 pm

Gunaratnam remanded till Nov. 18


	

Frontline Socialist Party (FSP) Central Committee member Kumar Gunaratnam, who was arrested this afternoon, was remanded till November 18 by Kegalle Magistrate Prasanna Alwis.
Mr. Gunaratnam was arrested by the Police at Anguruwella in Kegalle for allegedly violating immigration and emigration laws and regulations. (Rohan Kumara)Pix by Rohan Kumara Gunarathne
