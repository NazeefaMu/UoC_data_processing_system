## Title: PM addresses SL Human Capital Summit
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 11 August 2016 09:15 pm

PM addresses SL Human Capital Summit


	



Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe attended the Sri Lanka Human Capital Summit-2016 which was held at the Taj Samudra Hotel in Colombo today. The Premier delivered the keynote speech under the theme ‘Sri Lanka as a Talent Capital.’ Pix by Nisal Baduge
