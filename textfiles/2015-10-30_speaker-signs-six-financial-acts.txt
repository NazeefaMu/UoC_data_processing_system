## Title: Speaker signs six financial Bills
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 October 2015 12:24 pm

Speaker signs six financial Bills


	

Speaker Karu Jayasuriya has today signed six financial Bills including the Bill to implement the Mansion Tax, enacting them with effect from today.
The six Bills include Inland Revenue (Amendment) Bill, Nation Building Tax (Amendment) Bill, Gaming and Betting and Gaming Levy (Amendment) Bill, Economic Service Charge (Amendment) Bill, Value Added Tax Amendment Bill and a Financial Bill which was reported to be the Mansions Tax Amendment Bill. (Yohan Perera)
