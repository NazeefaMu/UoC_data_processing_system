## Title: Video: Bus topples
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 17 July 2016 10:06 am

Video: Bus topples


	



A bus travelling to Gonapola on the Piliyandala-Moragahahena Road toppled into a paddy field this morning.  Only the driver was injured, as there were no passengers. Police suspect the driver might have lost control of the bus. The accident damaged a pipeline beside the road.(Darshana Sanjeewa)
