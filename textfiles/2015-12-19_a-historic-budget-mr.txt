## Title: <U+200B>A historic budget- MR
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 December 2015 11:12 am

<U+200B>A historic budget- MR


	

While thanking Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe for making some amendments to  the Budget 2016, former President Mahinda Rajapaksa today termed the Budget 2016 as a ‘historic budget’ as he never seen such a budget before.
“I have not seen such a budget earlier, this showed the inefficiency of the government, so I would like to term this budget as a historical one,” former President and Kurunegala district MP Mahinda Rajapaksa told in Parliament.
He also said that he never fought against Tamil people, but fought against terrorist leader Prabhakaran.
