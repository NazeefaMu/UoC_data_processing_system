## Title: Ranil back from UK
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 January 2016 08:02 am

Ranil back from UK


	


	Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe, who left on a ten-day visit to the UK on December 22, had arrived in the country this afternoon.
	Earlier, the Prime Minister’s office said that it was not an official visit and he was expected to spend some time in Ireland as well.(T.K.G. Kapila)
