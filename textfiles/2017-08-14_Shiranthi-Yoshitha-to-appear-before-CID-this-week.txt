## Title: Shiranthi, Yoshitha to appear before CID this week
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 14 August 2017 02:11 pm

Shiranthi, Yoshitha to appear before CID this week


	

Former president Mahinda Rajapaksa’s wife Shiranthi and son Yoshitha had been asked to report to the Criminal Investigation Department (CID) this week to record statements in connection with a defender jeep which had been allegedly used to provide protection to a VIP after it was given to the Siriliya Saviya Foundation by the Red Cross Organisation in 2011.

Police Spokesman SP Ruwan Gunasekara said Ms. Rajapaksa was asked to report to the CID tomorrow (15) while Yoshitha had been asked to report on Wednesday (16). (Darshana Sanjeewa)
