## Title: Six JO members to crossover:Range Bandara
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 June 2016 02:48 pm

Six JO members to crossover:Range Bandara


	

Six members of the Joint Opposition would crossover to the Government shortly, State Minister Palitha Range Bandara told a media briefing today.


However, the he fell short of mentioning who the MPs would be.


“You will find out who they are, when they make the move," Bandara told journalists. (Yohan Perera)
