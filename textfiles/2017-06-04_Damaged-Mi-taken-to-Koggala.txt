## Title: Damaged Mi-17 taken to Koggala
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 June 2017 12:31 pm

Damaged Mi-17 taken to Koggala


	



The Mi17 helicopter of Sri Lanka Air Force which was damaged while making a force-landing in Baddegama due to a technical fault, was transported to the Koggala Air Force camp for renovation yesterday after being divided into pieces. Pix by D.G. Sugathapala
