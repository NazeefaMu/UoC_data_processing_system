## Title: 12 Indians arrested in Kattankudy
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 March 2016 01:05 pm

12 Indians arrested in Kattankudy


	

Twelve Indian nationals including seven women, who were staying in the country violating immigration and emigration laws, were arrested yesterday in Kattankudy, Police said.

They said the suspects had come to the country on tourist visa and were engaged in business activities illegally.

They are to be produced in Batticaloa Magistrate’s Court today.
