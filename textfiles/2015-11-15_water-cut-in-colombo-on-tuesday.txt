## Title: Water cut in Colombo on Tuesday
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 November 2015 03:13 pm

Water cut in Colombo on Tuesday


	

Water supply to several areas in Colombo suburbs would be suspended on Tuesday because of an emergency repair to water supply lines, the National Water Supply and Drainage Board said today.
Accordingly, water supply to the Moratuwa MC limits, Ratmalana, Soysapura, Panadura, Keselwatte, Nallaruwa, Kiriberiya, Raigama, Bandaragama, Welmilla, Kumbuka, Horana, Morontuduwa, Piliyandala, Kesbawa MC area, Siyambalagoda, Polgasowita, Vetara, Heraliyawala, Pahalagama, and Ambalangoa would be suspended.
