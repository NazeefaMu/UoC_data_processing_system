## Title: Deserted Railway Stations
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 11 December 2017 04:22 pm

Deserted Railway Stations


	



Main Railway Stations around Colombo including Fort, Maradana and Dematagoda appeared to be deserted due to the trade union action carried out by the Railway Trade Union Alliance from December 7.  Pix by Kithsiri De Mel
