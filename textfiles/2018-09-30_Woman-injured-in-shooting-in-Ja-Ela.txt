## Title: Woman injured in shooting in Ja-Ela
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 September 2018 01:19 pm

Woman injured in shooting in Ja-Ela


	

A woman received gunshot injuries when a gunman opened fire at Weligampitiya in Ja-Ela this morning.

Police said the gunman in a car fired shots at the woman who was travelling in a car.

The injured woman was admitted to the Colombo North (Ragama) teaching Hospital. (TK)
