## Title: Body of child washed ashore
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 December 2015 01:08 pm

Body of child washed ashore


	

The body of a three-year-old boy was washed ashore at the Dehiwela beach this evening, Police said.
They said the mother of the boy had lodged a complaint with the Wellawatte Police this afternoon that the boy had gone missing from their house in Wellawatte.
