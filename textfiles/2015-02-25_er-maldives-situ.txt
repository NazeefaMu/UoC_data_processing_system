## Title: SL shows concern over Maldives situ
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 February 2015 05:16 pm

SL shows concern over Maldives situ


	


	The government today expressed its concern over the development in the neighbouring Maldives.
	Issuing a statement it said, Sri Lanka, as a friendly neighbouring country is concerned about the recent developments in the Maldives.  It is hoped that all stakeholders in the Maldives will resolve their differences in a peaceful manner, within the legal framework in that country.
