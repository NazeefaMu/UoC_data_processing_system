## Title: HNB Assurance top achievers attend Annual MDRT Conference in Canada
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 20 July 2016 12:00 am

HNB Assurance top achievers attend Annual MDRT Conference in Canada


	



Fourteen top achievers of HNB Assurance (HNBA) took wing recently to Vancouver to participate in the Million Dollar Round Table (MDRT) conference to be felicitated along with the global best of the best in relation to the insurance and financial services.


Formed in 1927, MDRT is an independent association with a global count of over 43,000 professionals affiliated to insurance and financial services in over 500 companies, where membership is exclusively to those that surpass the threshold.


HNBA representatives, Kumudumalee Silva, Soniya Premani, Chamara Mendis, Malindika Priyadarshani, Aruna Chandra Arawinda, Vidusha Prasad, Asoka de Silva, Milinda Liyanage, Thirukkumar Nagalingam, Chamara Fonseka, Thusitha Bandara, Dayagaran Ratnarasa, Manjula Krishanthi, Ishara Fernando had the opportunity to represent the company at this global event. Kumudumalee Silva is also the winner of Court of the Table (COT) membership for her exceptional performance during 2015.
