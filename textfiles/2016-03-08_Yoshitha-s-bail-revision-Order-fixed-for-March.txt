## Title: Yoshitha's bail revision:  Order fixed for March 14
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 8 March 2016 02:39 pm

Yoshitha's bail revision:  Order fixed for March 14


	

The order on the bail revision applications filed on behalf of Yoshitha Rajapaksa and four others was fixed for March 14 by Colombo High Court Judge A.A.R. Heiyanthuduwa today.

The date was fixed after considering the submissions made by the petitioners and the respondents.

Counsel Gamini S. Marapana submitted counter arguments on behalf of the petitioners while Senior State Counsel Thusith Mudalige appeared for the respondents. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
