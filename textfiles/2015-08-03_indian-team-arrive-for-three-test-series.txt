## Title: Indian team arrive for three-Test series
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 3 August 2015 03:23 pm

Indian team arrive for three-Test series


	


The Indian national cricket team arrived in Sri Lanka this evening to play a three Test series, starting later this month. The first Test starts in Galle on the 12th, before moving to Colombo. The second Test will be played at the P. Sara Oval starting from the 24th and the final Test will commence on the 28th. Pix by Deepa Adikari
