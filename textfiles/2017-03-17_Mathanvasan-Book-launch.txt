## Title: Mathanvasan Book launch
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 17 March 2017 09:20 pm

Mathanvasan Book launch


	



Daily Mirror’s sister paper Tamil Mirror Editor P Mathanvasan presents the first copy of his book titled ‘Hands that defeat suppression-Tamil mirror Editorials’ to Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe at its launch in Colombo today. The Minister of National Dialogue National Integration and Languages Mano Ganesan felicitate Daily Mirror and Tamil Mirror cartoonist Awantha Artigala in the traditional Hindu way at the launch.
