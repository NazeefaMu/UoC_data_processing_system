## Title: Explosives and ammunition found in Kosgoda
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 October 2016 12:14 pm

Explosives and ammunition found in Kosgoda


	

Police have recovered 1.75 kilograms of C4 explosives and 17 bullets of T56 at a deserted land in Kosgoda this morning, Police said.

They said the explosives were recovered on information received by the police.

Several detonators were also found in the location.
