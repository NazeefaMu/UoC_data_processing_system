## Title: Many areas affected by floods
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 May 2017 08:38 am

Many areas affected by floods


	

Many areas in the country have been affected by floods due to heavy rainfall. The prevalent rains have had a heavy impact in the Southern and Sabaragamuwa provinces.

The Disaster Management Center (DMC) said people of Kelani-Ganga related Divisional Secretariat areas in the Kolonnawa, Biyagama, Kelaniya, Kaduwela, Dompe, Hanwella, Padukka and Avissawella are advised to take adequate measures to face a major flood situation.
