## Title: PM opens new police station
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 30 September 2018 02:10 pm

PM opens new police station


	



The newly constructed police station in Wanathawilluwa was opened by Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe on Saturday. Minister of Law and Order and Public Administration Ranjith Madduma Bandara,Deputy Minister Nalin Bandara Jayamaha,State Minister Ranga Bandra MP Hector Appuhamy and IGP Pujith Jayasundara were also present.
