## Title: Tense situation at North Central PC
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 8 August 2017 12:14 pm

Tense situation at North Central PC


	

A tense situation was reported at the North Central Provincial Council sessions today after Joint Opposition members of the Council set fire to a copy of the agenda. Then the meeting was adjourned for 45 minutes.

The Opposition members disrupted the session by shouting and wearing black bands and left the council at 10.20 a.m.  By Dayarathna Embogama
