## Title: Roads around Parliament to be closed for rehearsals
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 January 2015 01:46 pm

Roads around Parliament to be closed for rehearsals


	


	Traffic would be restricted on roads around the Parliament Grounds from 8. 30 a.m. to noon from tomorrow till February 4 in view of the rehearsals of the Independence Day celebrations, the Police said.
	Traffic would be restricted on Sri Lanka Japan Friendship Road, the Road in front of the Parliament, the road from Polduwa Junction to the Parliament and the road towards the Parliament from Palam Thuna Junction.
