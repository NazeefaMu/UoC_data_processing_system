## Title: PM at Rajagala
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 16 October 2018 08:24 pm

PM at Rajagala


	



Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe participated in the traditional laying of relics at the ancient archaeological site Rajagala in Ampara today. Minister of Social Welfare and Primary Industries Daya Gamage, Minister of Higher Education and Cultural Affairs Wijedasa Rajapaksha and Deputy Minister Anoma Gamage participated in the ceremony, Pics by Pradeep Pathirana
