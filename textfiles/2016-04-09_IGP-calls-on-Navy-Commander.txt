## Title: IGP calls on Navy Commander
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 9 April 2016 09:19 am

IGP calls on Navy Commander


	

 



The outgoing Inspector General of Police (IGP) N.K. Illangakoon called on the Commander of the Navy, Vice Admiral Ravindra Wijegunaratne at the Naval Headquarters yesterday, He was awarded with a Guard of Honour on his arrival. The Commander of the Navy praised the exemplary character shown by the IGP during his five year tenure in the highest seat in the Department of Police.
