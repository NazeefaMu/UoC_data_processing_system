## Title: President visits Somawathi Rajamaha Viharaya
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 19 February 2018 09:56 am

President visits Somawathi Rajamaha Viharaya


	



President Maithripala Sirisena engaged in religious observances at the Somawathi Rajamaha Viharaya in Polonnaruwa yesterday. Ven. Thalakolawewe Medhananda Thera and Ven. Pubudupura Sumanarathana Thera chanted Seth Pirith invoking blessings on the President. Pix by President’s Media Division
