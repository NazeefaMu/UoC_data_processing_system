## Title: UN urged to resolve Kashmir issue immediately
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 21 July 2016 03:31 pm

UN urged to resolve Kashmir issue immediately


	



The Kashmir Study Forum Sri Lanka along with Pakistani and Sri Lankan community organized a peaceful demonstration outside the United Nation Office at Bauddaloka Mawatha, Colombo today against Kashmir violence. The demonstrators handed over a memorandum with thousands of signatures urging the UN to resolve the Kashmir issue immediately. Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
