## Title: Niluka Ekanayake sworn in as CP Governor
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 March 2016 11:13 am

Niluka Ekanayake sworn in as CP Governor


	

Niluka Ekanayake was sworn in as the new Governor of the Central Province today, filling the vacancy created by the demise of Surangani Ellawala who held the post earlier, the President’s Media announced.
