## Title: Debate on Bond, PRECIFAC reports postponed indefinitely
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 20 February 2018 02:09 pm

Debate on Bond, PRECIFAC reports postponed indefinitely


	

The debate on the Bond Commission report and the PRECIFAC Report, which was scheduled to be taken up in Parliament today, was postponed indefinitely due to the non-availability of the English and Tamil translation of the PRECIFAC report. (Ajith Siriwardana and Yohan Perera)
