## Title: Thousands in ‘Two Mile Sea Swim’ competition
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 5 March 2017 06:03 pm

Thousands in ‘Two Mile Sea Swim’ competition


	



The ‘Two Mile Sea Swim’ competition organized by the Mount Lavinia Hotel in collaboration with the Sri Lanka Aquatic Sports Union was held today in the sea off the Mount Lavinia Beach. Around 1,000 swimmers were seen competing under several categories. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
