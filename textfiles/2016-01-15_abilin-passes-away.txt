## Title: ‘Abilin’ passes away
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 January 2016 05:55 am

‘Abilin’ passes away


	

Veteran actor Chandrasiri Kodithuwakku popular for playing the character ‘Abilin’ in ‘Kopi Kade’ television series has passed away today at the age of 67.
Mr. Kodithuwakku was undergoing treatment in the ICU after he was admitted to the Gampaha Hospital yesterday.
