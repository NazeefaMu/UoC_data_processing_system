## Title: Nalinakapuram project: President hands over 100 houses
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 31 October 2016 10:40 pm

Nalinakapuram project: President hands over 100 houses


	



The Nalinakapuram project under which hundred houses constructed in the Jaffna for the war displaced, was officially completed today when President Maithripala Sirisena handed over 100 houses built to the owners. The handing over ceremony was held in Keramale KKS-(Kankesanthurai) amidst the participation of TNA MPs, the Northern Province Governor, State Minister of Defence Ruwan Wijewardene participated.
