## Title: Sampath Bank’s off-site  ATM network expands further
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 23 July 2015 02:47 am

Sampath Bank’s off-site  ATM network expands further


	


The newest ATM of Sampath Bank was ceremonially opened at Arangala Junction by Pradeep Perera, Head of Internal Audit.
This new ATM is the latest addition to Sampath Bank’s wide spread ATM Network. People in Arangala area can now do their day-to-day banking with ease and comfort through this newest ATM. General ATM features can be accessed at this location including cash withdrawals, fund transfers, utility bill payments, Cardless Cash facility, Mobile Cash facility etc.
