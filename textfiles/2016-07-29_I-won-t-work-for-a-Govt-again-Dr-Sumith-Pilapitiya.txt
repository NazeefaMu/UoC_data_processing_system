## Title: I won’t work for a Govt. again: Dr. Sumith Pilapitiya
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 29 July 2016 11:10 am

I won’t work for a Govt. again: Dr. Sumith Pilapitiya


	

Dr. Sumith Pilapitiya, who resigned as the Wildlife Department Director General recently, said in an exclusive interview with Daily Mirror that he did not want to work for the government or in a public sector background again.

“I am not talking about this government or that government. I do not want to work for the government or in a public sector background again. If the department needs my help, I am more than willing to help them only on a voluntary basis.
