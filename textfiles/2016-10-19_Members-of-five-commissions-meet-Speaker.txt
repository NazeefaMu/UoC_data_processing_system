## Title: Members of five commissions meet Speaker
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 October 2016 08:09 am

Members of five commissions meet Speaker


	

The members of five independent commissions met Speaker Karu Jayasuriya yesterday.

The members of the Bribery Commission, the National Police Commission, the Finance Commission, the Elections Commission and the National Procurement Commission met the Speaker on the latter's invitation. At the meeting, the activities of these commissions and the issues connected with their day to day operations were discussed. (Sujith Hewa Julige)
