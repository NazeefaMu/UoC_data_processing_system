## Title: Fmr. IGP Balasuriya at CID
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 29 July 2016 11:25 am

Fmr. IGP Balasuriya at CID


	

Former Inspector General of Police (IGP) Mahinda Balasuriya has been summoned by the CID to record a statement with regard to the investigation on the murder of journalist Lasantha Wickrematunge.
