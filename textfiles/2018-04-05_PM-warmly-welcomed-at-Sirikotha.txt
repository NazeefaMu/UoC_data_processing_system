## Title: PM warmly welcomed at Sirikotha
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 5 April 2018 01:08 pm

PM warmly welcomed at Sirikotha


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe, who survived the No-Confidence Motion yesterday, was warmly welcomed at United National Party (UNP) headquarters Sirikotha today by UNP members. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana.
