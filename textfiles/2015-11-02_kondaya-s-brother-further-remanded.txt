## Title: Kondaya’s brother further remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 2 November 2015 05:00 am

Kondaya’s brother further remanded


	

The main suspect of Seya Sandewmi’s murder elder brother of Dunesh Priyashantha alias ‘Kondaya’, Saman Jayalath has been further remanded November 16 by Minuwangoda Magistrate D.A. Ruwan Pathirana today. (Tony Karunanayeka)
