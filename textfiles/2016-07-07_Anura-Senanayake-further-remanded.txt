## Title: Anura Senanayake further remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 7 July 2016 02:06 pm

Anura Senanayake further remanded


	

Former Senior DIG Anura Senanayake and former Crime OIC of the Narahenpita police Sumith Perera were further remanded till July 20 by Colombo Additional Magistrate Nishantha Peiris today.

They were arrested for allegedly withholding evidence into the Wasim Thajudeen murder case. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
