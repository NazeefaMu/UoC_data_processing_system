## Title: Bank in Dambulla robbed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 13, 2015 - 05:15

Bank in Dambulla robbed

An armed person had robbed a  private bank in Dambulla town this morning, the police stated. It is reported that the alleged robber had threatened the employee at the bank counter with a hand grenade. Afterwards the robber had kidnapped an employee of the bank and fled in a three-wheeler. However the bank employee had arrived unharmed after a short while,  the police added.
The Dambulla police are conducting further investigations.
