## Title: Wimal at FCID
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 26 September 2016 04:03 pm

Wimal at FCID


	



National Freedom Front Leader MP Wimal Weerawansa is seen at the Financial Crime Investigation Division (FCID) today. He was summoned to record a statement over his alleged involvement in misusing a fleet of vehicles belonging to Presidential Secretariat during the election campaigns.
