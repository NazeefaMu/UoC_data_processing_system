## Title: <U+200B>More Ministers appointed
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 March 2015 10:27 am

<U+200B>More Ministers appointed


	

Eleven more Cabinet Ministers, five State Ministers and ten Deputy Ministers were sworn in before President Maithripala Sirisena at the Presidential Secretariat a short while ago, the President’s Media said.Cabinet Ministers
1.    A.H.M.Fousie                               Minister of Disaster Management
