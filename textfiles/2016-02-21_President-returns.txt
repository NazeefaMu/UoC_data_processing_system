## Title: President returns
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 21 February 2016 08:45 am

President returns


	

President Maithripala Sirisena arrived in the country this morning after concluding his official visits to Germany and Austria.


President left for Germany on February 15 at the invitation of the German Chancellor Angela Merkel and after concluding a three-day visit to Germany, he left for Austria on February 19.
