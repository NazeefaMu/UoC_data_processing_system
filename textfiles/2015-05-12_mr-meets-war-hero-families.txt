## Title: MR meets war hero families
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 12 May 2015 09:31 am

MR meets war hero families


	

Former President Mahinda Rajapaksa today met the family members of deceased army soldiers from the Kalutara District. The meeting was held at the Abhayaramaya in Narahenpita. Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
