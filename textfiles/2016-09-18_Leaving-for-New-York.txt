## Title: Leaving for New York
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 18 September 2016 12:45 pm

Leaving for New York


	



President Maithripala Sirisena left for the United States today to attend the 71st United Nations General Assembly (UNGA) Sessions. Ministers Mangala Samaraweera, Gayantha Karunathilaka, Nimal Siripala de Silva, Arjuna Ranathunga and Faizer Mustafa and the Member of Parliament Nalaka Kolonne were among the delegation of the President.
