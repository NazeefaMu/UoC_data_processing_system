## Title: 'Thirasara Yugayata Maga' book launch
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 28 February 2017 10:34 pm

'Thirasara Yugayata Maga' book launch


	

The book Thirasara Yugayata Maga was launched at the BMICH on Tuesday (28). Author Asoka Abeygunawardana and Dr.Ranil Senanayeke are seen presenting a copy to President Maithripala Sirisena. Head of the Sri Lanka National Council Ven. Athuraliye Rathana Thera, several ministers and other distinguish members were present on the occasion. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
