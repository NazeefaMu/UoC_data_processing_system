## Title: Ranil to visit Australia
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 12 February 2017 11:06 am

Ranil to visit Australia


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe is scheduled to leave for Australia for four-day official visit tomorrow.

Prime Minister's Office said today that Mr. Wickremesinghe will undertake the visit of the invitation of Australian Government.

During his visit, the Prime Minister will hold bilateral discussions with the Australian Prime Minister Malcolm Turnbull and his cabinet ministers in Melbourne and Canberra.
