## Title: She is his medium!
## Section: Political Gossip
## Date: 29 December 2015 04:16 am

She is his medium!


	


He is a local politico raised to purohita status of a provincial council quite recently. But he has already incurred the wrath of the council members by issuing an instruction which they had found both strange and puzzling: that they should send to him whatever requests and proposals they have got only through a certain fair member. 
He has asked even the ruling party members not to approach him direct regarding any official matter. All members from both sides of the council have now decided to teach this purohita an unforgettable lesson, they say.
