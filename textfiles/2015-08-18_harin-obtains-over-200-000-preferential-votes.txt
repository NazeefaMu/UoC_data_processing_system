## Title: Harin obtains over 200,000 preferential votes
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 August 2015 09:00 am

Harin obtains over 200,000 preferential votes


	

Uva Province Chief Minister Harin Fernando made it to the first place in the Badulla district UNP list with 200,806 preferential votes while Ravindra Samaraweera came in the second place with 58,507 votes.
Chaminda Wijesiri and Aravinda Kumara made it to the third and fourth places in the UNP list respectively.
Former Opposition Leader Nimal Siripala de Silva topped the UPFA preferential list with 134,406 votes while Chamara Sampath and Thenuka Vidanagamage made it to the second and third places respectively.
