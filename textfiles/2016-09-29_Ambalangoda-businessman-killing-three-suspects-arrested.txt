## Title: Ambalangoda businessman killing: three suspects arrested
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 29 September 2016 11:09 am

Ambalangoda businessman killing: three suspects arrested


	

Three suspects including the main suspect were arrested yesterday in connection with the murder of Galle District Cricket Association President and millionaire businessman Premasiri Halambage, Police said.

They said two weapons and a car were also taken into custody.

Millionaire businessman Halambage was murdered on August 12 at his house in Ambalangoda.(Hemantha De Silva)
