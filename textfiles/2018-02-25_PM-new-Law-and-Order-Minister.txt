## Title: PM new Law and Order Minister
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 February 2018 11:44 am

PM new Law and Order Minister


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe was appointed as the new Cabinet Minister of Law and Order during the Cabinet reshuffle commenced at the Presidential Secretariat today, President’s Media Division said.

President Maithripala Sirisena said the government needs to improve its policies, programmes and actions to serve the people better following the people's message expressed at the recent local government election.  

“The first priority of my government is the people and the country,” he said.
