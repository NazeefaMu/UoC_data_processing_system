## Title: Three killed in head-on collision
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 31 March 2015 04:25 am

Three killed in head-on collision


	


	Three people were killed and another person was injured when a three-wheeler they were traveling in, collided head-on with a private bus at Akarawatta in Hettipola on the Hettipola- Kuliyapitiya main Road.
	The Police said the injured person was admitted to the Kuliyapitiya Hospital and the driver of the bus has been arrested.
