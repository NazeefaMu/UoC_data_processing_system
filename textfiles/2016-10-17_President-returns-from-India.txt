## Title: President returns from India
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 October 2016 04:50 pm

President returns from India


	

President Maithripala Sirisena returned to the country a short while ago after successfully concluding his two-day official visit to India.

President Sirisena left for India on Saturday with a delegation of 17 to attend the BRICS-BIMSTEC Outreach Summit which was held in Goa. (T.K.G. Kapila)
