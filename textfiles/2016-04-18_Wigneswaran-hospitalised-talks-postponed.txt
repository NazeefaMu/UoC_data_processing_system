## Title: Wigneswaran hospitalised; talks postponed
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 April 2016 02:34 pm

Wigneswaran hospitalised; talks postponed


	

Northern Province Chief Minister C.V. Wigneswaran had been admitted to the Jaffna General Hospital due to a sudden illness yesterday, hospital sources said.

The meeting scheduled for today with President Maithripala Sirisena and Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe in Colombo over the release of lands in the North has also been postponed due to this, officials said. (Siraaj Hazim)
