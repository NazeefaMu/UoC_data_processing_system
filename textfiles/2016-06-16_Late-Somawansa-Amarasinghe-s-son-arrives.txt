## Title: Late Somawansa Amarasinghe’s son arrives
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 16 June 2016 05:45 pm

Late Somawansa Amarasinghe’s son arrives


	



Isuru Amarasinghe, son of former JVP Leader late Somawansa Amarasinghe arrived in Sri Lanka from London this evening accompanied by Wasantha Samarasinghe of the JVP to pay last respect to his father.
