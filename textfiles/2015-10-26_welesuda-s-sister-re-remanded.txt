## Title: Video: Wele suda’s sister re-remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 October 2015 10:21 am

Video: Wele suda’s sister re-remanded


	

Maligakanda Magistrate today further remanded drug kingpin Wele Suda’s sister Dilini Nilmini and her two accomplices till November 09 on charges of possessing 111 grams of heroin worth more than Rs.1.7 million
Borella Police Anti-Crimes Unit arrested the suspects and seizeda super luxury vehicle used for the transport of heroin.Video by Courts Prasanna
