## Title: Body found in Beira canal
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 June 2016 05:00 pm

Body found in Beira canal


	

A human body had been recovered from a canal leading towards the Beria Lake behind a Warehouse of Sathosa at D. R. Wijewardene Mawatha in Colombo 10, the Police said.

They said the deceased had not been identified yet.
