## Title: ‘Nippon Maru’ in SL
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 14 February 2016 04:39 pm

‘Nippon Maru’ in SL


	



The ‘Nippon Maru’ Japanese ship carrying a group of 243 youths representing 11 countries that were part of the ‘Ship for World Youth’ (SWY) programme arrived at the Colombo Port today.

A welcome ceremony was held at the Port with the participation of the National Policies and Economic Affairs State Minister Niroshan Perera. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
