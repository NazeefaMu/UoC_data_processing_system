## Title: Ranil at ‘Othello’
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 23 November 2016 11:14 am

Ranil at ‘Othello’


	


William Shakespeare’s play ‘Othello’ was staged at Tower Hall, Borella yesterday where Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe was invited as the Chief Guest. The drama was organised by Shakespeare Centre, Colombo. (Pix by Pradeep Pathirana)
