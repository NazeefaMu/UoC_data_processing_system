## Title: President holds bilateral talks with German Chancellor
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 February 2016 05:48 pm

President holds bilateral talks with German Chancellor


	

President Maithripala Sirisena who is on a state visit to Germany met the Federal Chancellor of Germany Angela Merkel today.


President Sirisena who arrived in the Chancellor’s Office was received by the German leader.


A Guard of Honour was also held to welcome the President, who is on a three-day state visit to Germany.


Subsequently, the Chancellor Merkel and President Sirisena started bilateral discussions.
