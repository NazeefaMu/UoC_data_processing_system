## Title: Probe Senadhipathi’s claims: Rajitha, Patali and Arjuna
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 November 2015 05:35 am

Probe Senadhipathi’s claims: Rajitha, Patali and Arjuna


	

Health Minister Rajitha Senaratne, Megapolis and Western Development Minister Patali Champika Ranawaka and Ports and Shipping Minister Arjuna Ranatunga had requested IGP N.K. Illangakoon to probe the allegations made by Avant Garde Chairman Major (Retd) Nissanka Senadhipathi that the two ministers had solicited bribes.
The ministers said the allegations were baseless.
