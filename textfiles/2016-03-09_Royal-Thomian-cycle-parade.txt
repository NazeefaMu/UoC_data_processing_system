## Title: Royal-Thomian cycle parade
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 9 March 2016 08:46 pm

Royal-Thomian cycle parade


	



Students of St. Thomas’ College Mount Lavinia and Royal College Colombo participated in cycle and vehicle parades ahead of the 137th Battle of the Blues between the two schools, which begins tomorrow. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja and Nisal Baduge
