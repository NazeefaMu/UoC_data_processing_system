## Title: Musaeus College auditorium on fire
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 November 2016 04:25 pm

Musaeus College auditorium on fire


	


The Picture shows the auditorium of the Musaeus College in which a fire broke out today. No casualties were reported in the fire and Fire Brigade had deployed at least ten fire fighting trucks to douse the fire. Pix by Damith Wickramasinghe, Nisal Baduge
