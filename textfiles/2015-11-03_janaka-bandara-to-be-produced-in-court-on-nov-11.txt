## Title: Janaka Bandara to be produced in Court on Nov.11
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 November 2015 07:18 am

Janaka Bandara to be produced in Court on Nov.11


	


	Matale Magistrate Sampath Gamage today directed prison officials to produce UPFA MP Janaka Bandara Tennakoon in court on November 11. He was remanded in connection with a killing that had taken place in 1999.
	Mr. Tennakoon was taken into police custody on October 6 while receiving treatment at a private hospital in Colombo. (Mahesh Ruwan Keerthirathna)
