## Title: Body found in a swimming pool
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 August 2016 10:02 am

Body found in a swimming pool


	

The body of a 26-year-old was found in a swimming pool in Asgiriya, Gampaha last night.

Police said the victim identified as a resident of Kotugoda. The magisterial inquiry into the death is to be held today.
