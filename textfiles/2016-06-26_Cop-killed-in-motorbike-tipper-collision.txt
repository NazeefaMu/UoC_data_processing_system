## Title: Cop killed in motorbike-tipper collision
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 June 2016 08:02 pm

Cop killed in motorbike-tipper collision


	

A 48-year-old police officer attached to the Chunnakam Police died in an accident occurred this evening on Sangupiti Bridge on the Jaffna-Mannar Main Road in Kilinochchi, Police said.

They said the police officer's motorbike had collided with a tipper truck traveling from Jaffna.

Police said the driver of the tipper truck was arrested. (Romesh Madushanka)
