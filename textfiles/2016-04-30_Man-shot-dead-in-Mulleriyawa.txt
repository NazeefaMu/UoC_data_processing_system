## Title: Man shot dead in Mulleriyawa
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 April 2016 02:36 pm

Man shot dead in Mulleriyawa


	

A 41-year-old man was shot dead by three armed men who arrived on two motorbikes at Pethiyagoda in Mulleriyawa last evening, Police said.

They said the victim was identified as Ranil Ramyajith of Makola, Kiribathgoda.

The suspects were not identified yet and the motive of the killing has also not been ascertained so far.
