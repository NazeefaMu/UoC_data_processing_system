## Title: Colombo drenched in showers
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 11 October 2017 05:36 pm

Colombo drenched in showers


	



Heavy showers were experienced in Colombo over the day inconveniencing people’s day to day life. Mini flood situations were seen especially in by-roads and street workers and pedestrians were seen facing difficulties. Pix by Damith Wickramasinghe
