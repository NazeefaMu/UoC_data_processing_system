## Title: Heavy traffic on Galle Road due to UNF rally
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 December 2018 02:31 pm

Heavy traffic on Galle Road due to UNF rally


	

Heavy traffic was reported along the Galle Road from Kollupitiya Junction towards Colombo Fort due to a protest rally organized by the United National Front (UNF) at the Galle Face Green, Police said.
