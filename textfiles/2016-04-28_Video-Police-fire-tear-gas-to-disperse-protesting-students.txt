## Title: Video: Police fire tear gas to disperse protesting students
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 28 April 2016 04:29 pm

Video: Police fire tear gas to disperse protesting students


	



The Police had used water cannons and fired tear gas to disperse the medical students who were protesting near the Lotus Road in Colombo against the Private Medical University Malabe. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
