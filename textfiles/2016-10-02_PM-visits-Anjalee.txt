## Title: PM visits ‘Anjalee’
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 2 October 2016 11:32 am

PM visits ‘Anjalee’


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe visited the Auckland Zoo to see ‘Anjalee’ a Sri Lankan elephant that was gifted to New Zealand in June 2015.







Prime Minister Wickremesinghe arrived in Auckland yesterday to meet New Zealand Prime Minister John Key during a three-day visit.

The Sri Lankan delegation headed by the Premier attended the State Welcome Ceremony organized in their honour.
