## Title: Love is in the air
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 27 August 2017 06:17 pm

Love is in the air


	

The Independence Square in Colombo is becoming a popular monument. Newly weds use the location as a grand background to capture the special moments of their life. A newly wedded Chinese couple was caught on camera, while collecting the unforgettable moments for their wedding album at the venue today. Pix by Nisal Baduge
