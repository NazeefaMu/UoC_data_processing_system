## Title: UNP to decide on new posts on Thursday
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 April 2018 05:47 am

UNP to decide on new posts on Thursday


	

The United National Party (UNP) Politburo will on Thursday decide on the new party posts that are to be set up, sources said yesterday.

On April 10 the Politburo discussed the setting up of new positions such as that of Propaganda Secretary and Trade Union Secretary.

Sources said the party would also look at the possibility of reinstating the post of national organizer and the appointment of the General Secretary, Treasurer and Chairman. (Yohan Perera)
