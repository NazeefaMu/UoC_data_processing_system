## Title: Kahatagaha Mine workers protest
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 30 March 2016 05:26 pm

Kahatagaha Mine workers protest


	



A group of 87 workers at the Kahatagaha Graphite Mine in Dodangaslanda is reportedly protesting in the mine demanding a bonus for the New Year. Pix by Sampath Senavirathne and Pushpakumara Jayarathne
