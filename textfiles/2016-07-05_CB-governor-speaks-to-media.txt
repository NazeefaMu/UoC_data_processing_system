## Title: CB governor speaks to media
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 5 July 2016 09:18 pm

CB governor speaks to media


	



Prominent Lankan economist Indrajit Coomaraswamy today held his first news conference as Sri Lanka's Central Bank Governor. He said he would give "his absolute best" in deploying his responsibilities as the Governor. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
