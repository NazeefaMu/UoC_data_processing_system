## Title: More than 13 SLFP MPs to vote in favour
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 April 2018 09:16 pm

More than 13 SLFP MPs to vote in favour


	

More than 13 SLFP MPs and Ministers today said they will vote in favour of the No-Confidence Motion against Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe.

The Ministers said they have decided to vote in favor of the motion as they have no faith in the Prime Minister. Also they said they have made this decision in accordance with conscience. (Yohan Perera and Ajith Siriwardana)
