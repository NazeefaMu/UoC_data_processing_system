## Title: Elderly woman’s body found in Rajagiriya canal
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 21 June 2016 02:32 pm

Elderly woman’s body found in Rajagiriya canal


	

The body of an 83-year-old woman had been recovered from a canal at Buthgamuwa in Rajagiriya today, the Police said.

They said the victim was a resident of Sahaspura, Borella and the body had been placed at the Borella Police Morgue, following the Magisterial Inquiry.  

The cause for the death is yet to be ascertained.
