## Title: “Buddha Rashmi Vesak festival 2018”
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 24 April 2018 06:04 pm

“Buddha Rashmi Vesak festival 2018”


	



A well-wisher is seen donating financial contribution for the Buddha Rashmi Vesak festival organized by the Hunupitiya Gangaramaya Temple in collaboration with Prime Minister’s Office “Temple Trees” to be held on 29,30,1 and 2 along the Nawam Mawatha and surrounding areas of the Temple and in the Temple Trees premises featuring pandols, vesak lanterns and display of sacred relics of the Buddha.
