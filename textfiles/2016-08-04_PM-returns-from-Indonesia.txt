## Title: PM returns from Indonesia
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 August 2016 08:09 am

PM returns from Indonesia


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremeisnghe today returned to the country concluding his three-day official visit to Indonesia.

He left for Indonesia on Monday to attend the 12th Annual World Islamic Economic Forum.

The Premier left for Indonesia with Professor Maithri Wickremeisnghe, Ministers Kabir Hashim, A. H. M. Fowzie, Deputy Minister Sujeewa Senasinghe, Prime Minister’s Secretary Saman Ekanayake, Head of the five year development programme of the government, Arjuna Mahendran and Additional Secretary Saman Attaudahetti.
