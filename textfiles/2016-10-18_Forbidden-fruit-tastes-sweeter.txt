## Title: Forbidden fruit tastes sweeter
## Section: Political Gossip
## Date: 18 October 2016 12:00 am

Forbidden fruit tastes sweeter


	



 

This politico is a top team member in yahapalanaya. He is said to have become a regular visitor to the residence of a comely lady who is no longer in active politics due to her defeat at the polls.

 


 Both this lady and her husband continue to be provided with Police security thanks to the patronage of the powerful purohita.


The affair between the political bigwig and the lady has become the grist to the political rumour-mill for some time.
