## Title: Brothel raided in Pelawatta, 8 arrested
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 12 December 2016 10:56 am

Brothel raided in Pelawatta, 8 arrested


	



Seven women and a male doctor were arrested by the Nugegoda Police last evening on charges of operating a brothel under the guise of a spa at a luxury house in Pelawatta.

Police said the arrested women are aged between 18 and 28 and were identified as residents of Matara, Galle, Balangoda, Gampaha and Mathugama. (Muditha Dayananda)
