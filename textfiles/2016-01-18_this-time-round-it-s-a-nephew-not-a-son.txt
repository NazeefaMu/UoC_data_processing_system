## Title: This time round it’s a nephew, not a son
## Section: Political Gossip
## Date: 18 January 2016 06:30 pm

This time round it’s a nephew, not a son


	


A nephew of a leading politician has placed the order for the purchase of a super luxury Maybach limousine, they say.
This high speed luxury car used by the super-rich in the world has not been imported to this country so far. 
The vehicle is said to be meant for use only on super highways.
