## Title: Kandy-Mahiyangana road blocked due to landslide
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 20 February 2015 01:46 am

Kandy-Mahiyangana road blocked due to landslide


	

Kandy-Mahiyangana main road has been blocked due to a landslide at the first bend on the 18 hairpin bends, police said.
