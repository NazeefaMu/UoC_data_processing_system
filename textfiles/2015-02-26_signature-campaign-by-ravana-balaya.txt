## Title: Signature campaign by PHU
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 26 February 2015 11:49 am

Signature campaign by PHU


	


The Pivithuru Hela Urumaya (PHU) today organised a signature campaign outside the Fort Railway Station urging former President Mahinda Rajapaksa to contest as the prime ministerial candidate of the UPFA at the General Elections. Pix by Nisal Baduge
