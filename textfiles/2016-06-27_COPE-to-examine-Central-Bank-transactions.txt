## Title: COPE to examine Central Bank transactions
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 June 2016 08:41 am

COPE to examine Central Bank transactions


	

The Committee on Public Enterprises (COPE) will summon the authorities of the Central Bank on June 29 for an examination of its transactions, it is learnt.

COPE, a parliamentary watchdog committee, met last week and examined the financial situations in various other government institutions.

The State Engineering Cooperation was one of them. COPE had found that as many as 1000 employees on its payroll idled without any specific tasks being assigned to them.

COPE is at present headed by JVP MP Sunil Handunnetti.(Kelum Bandara)
