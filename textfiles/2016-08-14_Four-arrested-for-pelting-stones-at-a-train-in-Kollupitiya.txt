## Title: Four arrested for pelting stones at a train in Kollupitiya
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 14 August 2016 01:14 pm

Four arrested for pelting stones at a train in Kollupitiya


	

Four people were arrested by the railway security officers today on charges of throwing stones at Mount Lavinia bound Colombo passenger train in Kollupitiya.

Police said the suspects; residents of Maligawattha were handed over to the Kollupitiya Police.
