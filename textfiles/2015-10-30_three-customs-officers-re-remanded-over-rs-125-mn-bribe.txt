## Title: Video: Three Customs officers re-remanded over Rs. 125 mn bribe
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 October 2015 10:01 am

Video: Three Customs officers re-remanded over Rs. 125 mn bribe


	

The three Customs officials who were arrested and remanded on charges of soliciting a bribe of Rs. 125 million were further remanded till November 13 by the Colombo Chief Magistrate today.   
The three officials including a superintendent were arrested by the Bribery Commission on October 15 while soliciting a bribe of Rs.125 million in Panchikawatta.
A deputy Customs superintendent and an assistant superintendent were among the arrested. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
