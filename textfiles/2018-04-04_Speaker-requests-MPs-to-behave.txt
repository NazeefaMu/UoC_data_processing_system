## Title: Speaker requests MPs to behave
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 April 2018 11:13 am

Speaker requests MPs to behave


	

Speaker Karu Jayasuriya today requested the MPs to safeguard the dignity of Parliament when debating the no-confidence motion against Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe.

“Please behave and maintain the dignity of the House when you are debating today,” he told the MPs.(Yohan Perera and Ajith Siriwadana)
