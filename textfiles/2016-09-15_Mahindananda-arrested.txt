## Title: Mahindananda arrested
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 September 2016 10:38 am

Mahindananda arrested


	

Parliamentarian Mahindananda Aluthgamage was arrested by the CID a short while ago, the Police said.

He arrived at the CID to give a statement in connection with the construction of a house belongs to him at Kinsey Road in Colombo this morning.
