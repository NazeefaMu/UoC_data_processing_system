## Title: Anuki Chamathka tops O/L rankings
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 28 March 2017 08:27 am

Anuki Chamathka tops O/L rankings


	



Anuki Chamathka of Visakha Vidyalaya, Colombo topped the island rankings at the GCE O/L examinations held last year, the Examinations Department announced today.

S.M. Munasinghe of Mahamaya Girls' College, Kandy clinched the second place while R.M. Sugath Ravindu Sanwara of Ananda College, Colombo and Dimith Oshadhi Mirissage of Rahula College, Matata were placed third in the rankings.

Meanwhile, H.B.Pabasara Malithi Kumari Herath of Rathnavali Balika Vidyalaya, Gampaha ranked the fourth place.

D.M. Ranumi Dhisarani Nanayakkara of Devi Balika Vidyalaya, Colombo and A. Damsara Medhavi of Sanghamitta Balika Vidyalaya of Galle and A. Abhinandan of Hindu College, Jaffna ranked the fifth.

K.G.G.E Randini de Silva of Southlands College, Galle and E.A. Yasara Umashi of Sujatha Balika Vidyalaya, Matara ranked the sixth place at the GCE O/L rankings. (Chaturanga Pradeep)
