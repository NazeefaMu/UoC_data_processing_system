## Title: Postal services at a standstill
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 27 June 2017 07:49 pm

Postal services at a standstill


	

Services at the Central Mail Exchange (CME) came to a standstill today due to the indefinite strike launched by the United Postal Trade Union’s Front. Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
