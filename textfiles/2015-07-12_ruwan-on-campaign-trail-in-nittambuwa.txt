## Title: Ruwan on campaign trail in Nittambuwa
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 12 July 2015 04:50 pm

Ruwan on campaign trail in Nittambuwa


	


State Minister of Defence, Ruwan Wijewardene, today met UNP members in Nittambuwa, Attanagalla while campaigning for the upcoming General Elections. Around 20 SLFPers were said to have joined the UNP during today's event. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
