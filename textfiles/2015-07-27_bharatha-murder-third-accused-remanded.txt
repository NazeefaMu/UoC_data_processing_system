## Title: Bharatha murder: Third accused remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 July 2015 10:47 am

Bharatha murder: Third accused remanded


	


	Chaminda Ravi Jayanath, the third accused in the Bharatha Lakshman Premachandra murder case was today remanded till August 6 by Colombo High Court for violating bail conditions.
	The CID had lodged complaint to Court that the third accused had violated a bail condition by not presenting before the CID in terms of bail order issued by High Court.
	When the accused surrendered before Court through a lawyer, he was remanded by High Court judge Devika de Livera Tennakoon.(Lakmal Sooriyagoda)
