## Title: CB Governor: PM to reveal behind-the-scene secrets
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 June 2016 06:58 pm

CB Governor: PM to reveal behind-the-scene secrets


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe is to make several disclosures on matters related to Central Bank Governor Arjuna Mahendran, sources close to him said yesterday.

Sources said he would make a disclosure on a certain person who had been behind publishing several reports on the Central Bank Governor.

They also said the Prime Minister was to call for explanation form a certain United National Party (UNP) MP who is alleged to have been behind the publishing of reports on the Governor. (Yohan Perera)
