## Title: 29-year-old stabbed to death by brother-in-law
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 2 October 2016 12:22 pm

29-year-old stabbed to death by brother-in-law


	

A 29-year-old man was stabbed to death last night in Neluwa allegedly by his brother-in-law, police said.

The victim, Nagaraja, a resident of Deniyaya had argued with his brother-in-law who had later stabbed him with a knife.

The suspect was arrested by the Neluwa police. (Sumathi Deeyagahage)
