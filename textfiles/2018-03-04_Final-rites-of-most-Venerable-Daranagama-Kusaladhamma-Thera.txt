## Title: Final rites of most Venerable Daranagama Kusaladhamma Thera
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 March 2018 06:53 pm

Final rites of most Venerable Daranagama Kusaladhamma Thera


	



The remains of the Chief incumbent of Sri Sambodhi Viharaya, Colombo and founder of ‘The Buddhist’ TV channel most Venerable Daranagama Kusaladhamma Thera is kept for public viewing at the Sri Sambodhi Viharaya, Colombo 7. The final rites of the Thera is scheduled to be held on Thursday at the Independence Square with State patronage. Pics by Nisal Baduge
