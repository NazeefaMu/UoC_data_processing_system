## Title: India celebrates 71st Independence Day
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 15 August 2017 10:54 am

India celebrates 71st Independence Day


	



Indian nationals in Sri Lanka celebrated the 71st Independence Day of India at the residence of the Indian High Commissioner in Colombo today. The event was held with the patronage of Indian High Commissioner, Taranjit Singh Sandhu. Pix By Damith Wickramasinghe
