## Title: Microsoft Imagine Cup; Moratuwa Uni team wins 2nd place
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 28 July 2016 10:46 pm

Microsoft Imagine Cup; Moratuwa Uni team wins 2nd place


	


Moratuwa University team ‘BitMasters’ won the 2nd place under Innovation category at world finals of Microsoft Imagine Cup held in Redmond, Seattle, the USA.
