## Title: PM visits Kosgama
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 June 2016 02:30 pm

PM visits Kosgama


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe today visited Kosgama where a major fire broke out at the Army Camp’s ammunition depot yesterday.

The Prime Minister held a special meeting with government officials of the area and discussed several issues including providing relief to the residents and evacuees.
