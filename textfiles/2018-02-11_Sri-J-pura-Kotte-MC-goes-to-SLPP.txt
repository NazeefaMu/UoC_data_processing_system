## Title: Sri J’pura – Kotte MC goes to SLPP
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 February 2018 12:16 pm

Sri J’pura – Kotte MC goes to SLPP


	

The SLPP secured a majority of votes for the Sri Jayawardanepura – Kotte Municipal Council securing 18 seats with 22068 votes. SLPP  – 22068 (18)
UNP    – 17990 (12)
UPFA  – 7676 (5)
JVP     – 4885 (3)
