## Title: SL condemns London attack
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 June 2017 11:06 am

SL condemns London attack


	

President Maithripala Sirisena today condemned the terror attack in London in which six people were killed and dozens injured.

In a twitter message, the President said “London bridge terror rampage is a dastardly and cowardly act. My thoughts are with the people of London in this hour of grief,”.

Meanwhile, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs said that there were no reports so far of Sri Lankans affected by the terrorist attack.
