## Title: Up country railway service at a standstill
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 2 November 2016 08:40 am

Up country railway service at a standstill


	

Up country railway services had been disrupted due to caving in of the railway line between Kadugannawa and Balana, the Railway Control room said.

They said the passengers of the Kandy-Colombo train had been transferred using buses to Rambukkana this morning.

The train services on the upcountry line had been restricted up to Rambukkana, they said.
