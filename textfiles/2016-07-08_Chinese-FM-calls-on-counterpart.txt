## Title: Chinese FM calls on counterpart
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 8 July 2016 10:50 pm

Chinese FM calls on counterpart


	



Visiting Chinese Foreign Minister Wang Yi called on Foreign Affairs Minister Mangala Samaweera at the Foreign Ministry today. During the visit, Wang will also meet President Maithripala Sirisena and Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
