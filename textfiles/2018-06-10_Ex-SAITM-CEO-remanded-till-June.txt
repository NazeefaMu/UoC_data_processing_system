## Title: Ex-SAITM CEO remanded till June 14
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 10 June 2018 05:24 am

Ex-SAITM CEO remanded till June 14


	

Former SAITM CEO Dr. Sameera Senaratne was remanded till June 14 by the Kaduwela Magistrate’s Court yesterday over the shooting which took place in Malabe last year.

The Criminal Investigation Department (CID) yesterday arrested Dr. Senaratne for allegedly staged the shooting together with a Pradeshiya Saba member.

The CEO escaped unhurt on February 6, 2017 when two men on a motor bike wearing full-face helmets opened fire at the car, driven by him near the SAITM campus in Malabe. (DS)
