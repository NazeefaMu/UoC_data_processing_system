## Title: Muzammil further remanded till July 13
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 7 July 2016 12:24 pm

Muzammil further remanded till July 13


	

National Freedom Front Spokesman Mohammed Muzammil, was today ordered to be further remanded till July 13 by Fort Magistrate Lanka Jayaratne over the inquiry conducted against him for alleged misappropriation of Rs.6.1million public funds by misusing a vehicle attached to the Presidential Secretariat between 2009-2014. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
