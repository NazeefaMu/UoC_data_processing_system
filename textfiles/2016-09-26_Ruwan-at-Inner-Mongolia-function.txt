## Title: Ruwan at Inner Mongolia function
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 26 September 2016 09:44 pm

Ruwan at Inner Mongolia function


	



State Minister of Defence Ruwan Wijewardene today visited the 'China Inner Mongolia Cultural Week', which was held at the Defence Services College Colombo.

The 'China Inner Mongolian Cultural Week' was organised by the China Cultural Centre in Sri Lanka and Cultural Department of Inner Mongolian Autonomous Region Inner Mongolia Photographers Association to reflect cultural heritage of Inner Mongolia, which is an autonomous region of China.

Performance of cultural heritage and displays of arts and craft were exhibited during the event. Organisers, well-wishers and school staff were present at the occasion. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
