## Title: Heavy traffic at Town Hall
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 March 2018 12:44 pm

Heavy traffic at Town Hall


	

Heavy traffic was reported in Ward Place and Town Hall in Colombo 7 due to a protest March being carried out by a group of non-academic staff members of state universities.

The protest was organized by the University Trade Union Joint Committee over several demands.

The protest march is moving towards the University Grants Commission (UGC) in Ward Place. (Thilanka Kanakarathna)
