## Title: Protest against Welikada shooting
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 17 February 2015 09:40 am

Protest against Welikada shooting


	


An organisation calling themselves Go Rights Collective, staged a protest outside the Welikada Prison today urging the authorities to implement law against those who were behind the shooting of prison inmates of the Welikada prison in 2012. Pix Kushan Pathiraja
