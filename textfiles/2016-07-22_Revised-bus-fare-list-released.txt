## Title: Revised bus fare list released
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 July 2016 02:16 pm

Revised bus fare list released


	

Transport Ministry along with the National Transport Commission (NTC) has announced the revised bus fare which would be effective from August 1 for Normal, semi Luxury, luxury and Super Luxury services.

Stern action would be taken against any operator who charges fare in excess of the approved fare, the NTC said.

The revised bus fare between Maharagama- Galle would be Rs. 390, Maharagama- Matara- Rs. 470 and Kaduwela- Matara- Rs.490 on Southern Expressway. (Chaturanga Pradeep)
