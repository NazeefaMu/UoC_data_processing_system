## Title: Brothel raided in Maharagama
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 October 2015 04:50 am

Brothel raided in Maharagama


	

Four women including a manager were arrested during a raid by the police Investigation Unit Maharagama on an alleged brothel being run under the guise of an Ayurvedic Medical Centre in Piliyandala, Maharagama yesterday, police said.
The arrested women suspects were aged between 26 and 32 were residents of Badulla, Piliyandala, Galewela and Veyangoda areas, police said.
The suspects are to be produced in the Nugegoda Magistrate’s Court today. (Chaturanga Pradeep)
