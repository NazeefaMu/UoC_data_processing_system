## Title: Two men with swords arrested in Jaffna
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 October 2016 09:55 pm

Two men with swords arrested in Jaffna


	

Two men , suspected to be members of a group that calls itself AAVA , in Jaffna, were arrested by the police today. The two men, who  were travelling  by bike , had two swords in their possession at the time of their arrest. They had told police that the swords were for a drama  that they were practicing  for. (Pradeep Kumara)
