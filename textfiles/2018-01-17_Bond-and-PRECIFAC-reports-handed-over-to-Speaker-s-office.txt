## Title: Bond and PRECIFAC reports handed over to Speaker’s office
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 January 2018 12:04 pm

Bond and PRECIFAC reports handed over to Speaker’s office


	

The Bond Commission report and PRECIFAC reports were handed over to the Speaker’s Office this morning.

The Speaker’s office stated a legal officer of the Presidential Secretariat handed over 26 copies of the Bond Commission report and 36 copies of the report of the Presidential Commission of Inquiry to Investigate and Inquire into Serious Acts of Fraud, Corruption and Abuse of Power, State Resources and Privileges (PRECIFAC) .
