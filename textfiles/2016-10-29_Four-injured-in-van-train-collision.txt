## Title: Four injured in van-train collision
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 29 October 2016 01:41 pm

Four injured in van-train collision


	

Four people were injured when the van they were travelling in collided with a service train at a railway crossing in Nagawilluwa, Puttalam today.

The injured were admitted to the Puttalam Hospital. (Hiran Priyankara)
