## Title: Video: Wimal at court over passport issue
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 28 October 2015 04:25 am

Video: Wimal at court over passport issue


	

National Freedom Front (NFF) Leader MP Wimal Weerawansa arrived at the Negombo Magistrate’s Court this morning over the issue concerning his passport.
He was arrested by the CID on Friday at the Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA)over an issue regarding his passport and produced in the Negombo Magistrate’s Court.
The Magistrate released MP Weerawansa on Rs. 10,000 cash bail and two sureties of one million rupees each.
