## Title: Motorbikes for policemen
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 12 May 2015 01:08 pm

Motorbikes for policemen


	

Minister of Public Order and Christian Affairs John Amaratunga today handed over 1147 motorbikes to policemen in a ceremony at the Field Force Headquarters. IGP N. K. Ilangakoon also participated at the event. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
