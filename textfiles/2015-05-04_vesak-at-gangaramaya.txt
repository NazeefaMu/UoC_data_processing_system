## Title: Vesak at Gangaramaya
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 May 2015 06:12 pm

Vesak at Gangaramaya


	

The second day of the Buddha Rashmi Vesak Zone near the Gangaramaya Temple, Colombo was declared open by Navy Commander Jayantha Perera, Disaster Management Minister A. H. M. Fowzie and Ven. Kirinde Assaji Thera of the Gangarama Temple this evening. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
