## Title: One killed, another injured in Kadawatha shooting
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 10 January 2016 05:35 am

One killed, another injured in Kadawatha shooting


	


	One person was killed and another injured when unidentified men hacked and shot at them at Gonahena, Kadawath this morning.
	Police said the two were critically injured when they were admitted to the Ragama Hospital where one was succumbed to his injuries.
	Police are yet to determine the cause of the incident and no one has been arrested as yet.
