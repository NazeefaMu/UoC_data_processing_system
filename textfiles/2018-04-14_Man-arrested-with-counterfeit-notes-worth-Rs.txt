## Title: Man arrested with counterfeit notes worth Rs. 12,000
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 14 April 2018 06:30 am

Man arrested with counterfeit notes worth Rs. 12,000


	


A 26-year-old man was arrested by the police on charges of possessing 12 counterfeit notes of Rs.1,000 in Horana yesterday.

Police said the suspect who is a resident of Batagowa would be produced in the Horana Magistrate's Court today.
