## Title: Harsha appointed National Policies State Minister
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 February 2018 12:14 pm

Harsha appointed National Policies State Minister


	

Deputy Minister Dr. Harsha De Silva was appointed as the State Minister of National Policies and Economic Affairs. 

Minister Ajith P. Perara was appointed as the State Minister of Prisons and Rehabilitation while MP J. C. Alawathuwala was appointed as the Deputy Minister of Home Affairs.

Meanwhile, State Minister Piyasena Gamage was appointed as the State Minister of Youth Affairs and Southern Development.

MP Ravindra Samaraweera was appointed as the Cabinet Minister of Wildlife and Sustainable Development.
