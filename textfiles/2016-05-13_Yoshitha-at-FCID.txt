## Title: Yoshitha at FCID
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 13 May 2016 10:16 am

Yoshitha at FCID


	

Yoshitha Rajapaksa arrived at the Financial Crimes Investigation Division (FCID) this morning to record a statement on the alleged financial irregularities at the Carlton Sports Network (CSN).

He was summoned to the FCID on the same matter last Wednesday as well.
