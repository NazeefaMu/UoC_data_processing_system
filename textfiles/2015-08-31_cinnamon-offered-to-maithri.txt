## Title: Cinnamon offered to Maithri
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 31 August 2015 12:15 pm

Cinnamon offered to Maithri


	


Cinnamon products were offered to President Maithripala Sirisena last evening by Shashi Prasanna Wadige after offering cinnamon to Sri Dalada Maligawa in Kandy as a decades-long tradition related to Esala Festival. Pix by President’s Media
