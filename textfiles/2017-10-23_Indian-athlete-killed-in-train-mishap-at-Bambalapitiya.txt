## Title: Indian athlete killed in train mishap at Bambalapitiya
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 October 2017 04:04 pm

Indian athlete killed in train mishap at Bambalapitiya


	

An Indian athlete (44) was killed when he was hit by a train near the Bambalapitiya Railway Station today.

The Police said the victim was among a group of ten Indian athletes who were in Sri Lanka to attend the Inter Mercantile Athletics Championships.

Some of the touring athletes had gone to buy tea packs from a shop at Bambalapitiya when the victim had got off their bus to take photographs.

The Police said they suspected that a train had hit the victim when he attempted to take a photograph. (Darshana Sanjeewa)
