## Title: Knee injury forces Malinga to return home
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 March 2016 05:01 pm

Knee injury forces Malinga to return home


	

As feared, Lasith Malinga will return home from the World Twenty20 after failing to recover sufficiently from his knee injury, Sri Lanka Cricket announced on Friday (March 19).
