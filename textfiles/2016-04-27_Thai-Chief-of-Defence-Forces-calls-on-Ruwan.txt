## Title: Thai Chief of Defence Forces calls on Ruwan
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 27 April 2016 10:03 pm

Thai Chief of Defence Forces calls on Ruwan


	



Chief of Defence Forces of Royal Thai Armed Forces General Sommai Kaotira, who is in Sri Lanka on a three-day official visit, called on State Minister of Defence Ruwan Wijewardene at the Defence Ministry today. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
