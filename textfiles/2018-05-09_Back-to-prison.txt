## Title: Back to prison...
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 9 May 2018 01:15 pm

Back to prison...


	



President’s former Chief of Staff I. H. K. Mahanama and former State Timber Corporation (STC) Chairman P. Dissanayaka, who were in remanded custody over accepting a bribe of Rs. 20 million, were further remanded till May 22 by Colombo Chief Magistrate Buddhika Sri Ragala today. (Pics by Pradeep Pathirana)
