## Title: Kamanthi received Int'l Award
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 25 June 2017 08:05 pm

Kamanthi received Int'l Award


	



Daily Mirror Journalist Kamanthi Wickramasinghe, recipient of the 2017 Michael Konig Young Journalist Bursary received the certificate from Jose Papa, Managing Director, Cannes Lions at the Cannes Lions International Festival of Creativity in Cannes, France, on 22nd June 2017.
