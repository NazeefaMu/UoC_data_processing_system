## Title: 13 hospitalised following wasp attack
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 November 2016 06:12 pm

13 hospitalised following wasp attack


	

At least thirteen people including eight foreigners were hospitalised following a wasp attack at Rangiri Dambulla Temple this morning.

The injured were admitted to the Dambulla Base Hospital. (Kanchana Kumara Ariyadasa)
