## Title: Annual Volkswagen parade
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 9 July 2017 12:32 pm

Annual Volkswagen parade


	



Annual World Volkswagen Day Celebration organized by the Volkswagen Beetle Owners’ Club along with the Mount Lavinia Hotel, was held today. The parade was started at the Galle Face Hotel and ended at the Mount Lavinia Hotel. Pics by Kushan Pathiraja
