## Title: Canine Carnival
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 25 September 2016 07:32 pm

Canine Carnival


	



Canine Carnival ‘Unleash’ organized by the Ceylon Kennel Club was held at Hyde Park Grounds today.

Participants had come with their dogs for the event which comprised of fashion show and several entertaining games for dogs and their owners. Pix by Kithsiri de Mel
