## Title: ‘Project Eden’ to protect Mother Nature
## Section: News Features
## Date: 14 October 2017 12:00 am

‘Project Eden’ to protect Mother Nature


	

‘Project Eden,’ an exhibition organised collectively by the Interact Clubs of D.S. Senanayake College, Colombo North and the British School in Colombo, will be held tomorrow (15) at D.S. Senanayake College from 8.00 a.m. to 6.00 p.m.  

 



 

The main objective of this initiative is to educate the youth on pressing environmental issues including wildfires, deforestation, coastal erosion, wildlife endangerment and global warming. 


The project discusses solutions to these problems in order for the youth of today to become the saviours of tomorrow. 

 


Project Eden will be an interactive session and prove to be a unique platform to enhance knowledge



Project Eden will be an interactive session and prove to be a unique platform to enhance knowledge. 
The organisers hope that all participants will make use of this opportunity in enhancing new environmental bonds for the future.
