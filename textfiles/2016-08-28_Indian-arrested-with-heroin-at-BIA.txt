## Title: Indian arrested with heroin at BIA
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 28 August 2016 01:16 pm

Indian arrested with heroin at BIA


	

An Indian national was arrested with 838 grams of heroin at the Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA) this morning.

The police narcotic bureau arrested the 38-year-old suspect who had just arrived in the country from India.
