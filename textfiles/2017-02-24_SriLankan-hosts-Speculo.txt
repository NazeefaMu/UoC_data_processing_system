## Title: SriLankan hosts Speculo-2017
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 24 February 2017 03:43 pm

SriLankan hosts Speculo-2017


	



Speculo-2017, a national painting and visual arts competition sponsored by the national carrier SriLankan Airlines was declared open at the J. D. A. Perera Gallery today. SriLankan Airlines Chairman Ajith Dias, General Manager Marketing Saminda Perera and Media Development Manager Deepal Perera were present at the occasion. The exhibition features 80 professional artistes. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
