## Title: Dematagoda Chaminda in Bogambara prison
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 9 September 2016 10:39 am

Dematagoda Chaminda in Bogambara prison


	



Dematagoda Chaminda, who was one of the convicts in the murder of former presidential adviser Bharatha Lakshman Premachandra, was brought to the Bogambara prison in Pallekele this morning. Pix by Nadeeka Daya Bandara
