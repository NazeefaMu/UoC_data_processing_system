## Title: Kerry hails SL’s commitment for reconciliation
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 13 April 2016 03:20 pm

Kerry hails SL’s commitment for reconciliation


	



In a message to greet Sri Lankan people on the occasion of the Sinhala and Tamil New Year, US Secretary of State John Kerry said today that Sri Lanka has made historic accomplishments in the past one year, signaling the people’s commitment for reconciliation, tolerance and peace.

“On behalf of President Obama and the American people, I join you in celebrating the Sri Lankan New Year,” Kerry said adding that “I offer my best wishes for a happy New Year and continued success as you move ahead with the new constitution and further efforts to strengthen Sri Lanka’s democracy and prosperity”.
