## Title: DM bags Social Media award
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 9 July 2017 08:12 pm

DM bags Social Media award


	



Daily Mirror won the ‘Most Popular Newspaper on Social Media’ award at the Mashable World Social Media Day Awards – 2017. Wijeya Newspapers Ltd, Senior Marketing Manager Umair Wolid and Social Media Executive Kolin Peter were seen received the award.
