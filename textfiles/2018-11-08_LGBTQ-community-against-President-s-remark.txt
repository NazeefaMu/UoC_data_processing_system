## Title: LGBTQ+ community against President’s remark
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 8 November 2018 09:13 am

LGBTQ+ community against President’s remark


	


The People's Movement for Justice and Democracy staged protest at the Liberty Roundabout at Kollupitiya yesterday against the statement made by President Maithripala Sirisena at a public rally on Monday regarding the LGBTQ+ community. A number of LGBTQI+ rights supporters and activists took part in the protest. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
