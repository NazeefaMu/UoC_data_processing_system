## Title: French tourist and guide arrested on abuse charges
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 May 2016 12:06 pm

French tourist and guide arrested on abuse charges


	

A 66-old-French tourist and his Sri Lankan guide were arrested by the Ambalangoda Police yesterday on charges of abusing a 14-year-old school boy.


Police said the boy was admitted to the Balapitiya Hospital. The suspects are to be produced in Court today. (Ahangama Sumathipala Diyagahage)
