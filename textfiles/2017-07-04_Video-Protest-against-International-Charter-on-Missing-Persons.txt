## Title: Video: Protest against International Charter on Missing Persons
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 July 2017 12:52 pm

Video: Protest against International Charter on Missing Persons


	

A protest campaign was conducted by the Federation of National Organization in front of the Fort Railway Station this morning against the International Charter on Missing Persons that is to be submitted to the parliament tomorrow. The agitation was led by Ven. Bengamuwe Nalaka Thera, Ven. Induragare Dhammarathana Thera and Ven. Medagoda Abayathissa Thera. (Pix by Nisal Baduge)
