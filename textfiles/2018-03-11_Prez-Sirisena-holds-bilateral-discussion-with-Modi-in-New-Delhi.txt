## Title: Prez Sirisena holds bilateral discussion with Modi in New Delhi
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 March 2018 01:30 pm

Prez Sirisena holds bilateral discussion with Modi in New Delhi


	

President Maithripala Sirisena who is on an official visit to the India had held bilateral discussions with Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi in Delhi a short while ago.

Indian media reported that the two leaders had discussed ways to boost cooperation between India and Sri Lanka.
