## Title: G.S.Vithanage appointed SriLankan Chairman
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 14 November 2018 11:32 am

G.S.Vithanage appointed SriLankan Chairman


	

G.S. Vithanage , the Secretary of Transport and Civil Aviation Ministry has been appointed as the Chairman of the Srilankan Airlines, the Finance Ministry said today.

The appointment has been made by Ministry Secretary S.R. Attygalle.
