## Title: Police fire tear gas to disperse protesting students
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 28 April 2016 03:21 pm

Police fire tear gas to disperse protesting students


	

The Police had used water cannons and fired tear gas to disperse the medical students who were protesting near the Lotus Road in Colombo against the Private Medical University Malambe.

Police said the students tried to walk towards the Presidential Secretariat.
