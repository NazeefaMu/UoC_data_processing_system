## Title: Ports Authority stun group leaders CCC
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, January 4, 2016 - 01:00

Ports Authority stun group leaders CCC

 
Ports Authority CC beat Group A leaders CCC by nine wickets and Colts CC beat Badureliya SC by an innings and 46 runs on day two of the AIA Premier league first round matches played at Maitland Crescent and Maggona yesterday.
Lahiru Milantha opener of Ragama CC scored an unbeaten 165 runs in 215 balls with 15 fours and three sixes against Tamil Union - his second century of the season. Milantha and Udara Jayasundara (100) put on 229 runs partnership for the first wicket.
Asanka Silva, Saracens’ number eight batsman scored an unbeaten 102 off 176 balls with eight fours and two sixes. Asanka and Rajith Priyan (64) shared in an eighth wicket partnership of 143.
Chamara Silva (Galle CC), Dasun Shanaka (SSC) and Udara Jayasundara (Ragama CC) also scored centuries yesterday.
Chamikara Edirisnghe, left-arm spinner of NCC took 12 wickets for 95 runs against Army SC while Prabath Jayasooriya of Colts took 10 wickets for 83 runs.
