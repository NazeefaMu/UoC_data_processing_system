## Title: Malinga to join Aussie T20 series
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 February 2017 10:58 am

Malinga to join Aussie T20 series


	

Sri Lankan paceman Lasith Malinga would join Sri Lanka cricket team for the upcoming Australian T20 series, Sri Lanka Cricket Board President Thilanga Sumathipala said today.
