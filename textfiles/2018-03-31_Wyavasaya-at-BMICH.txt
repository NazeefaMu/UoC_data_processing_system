## Title: Wyavasaya-2018 at BMICH
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 31 March 2018 11:50 am

Wyavasaya-2018 at BMICH


	



Wyavasaya 2018, the trade and educational exhibition organised by the National Policies and Economic Affairs Ministry was launched by Project Management,Youth Affairs and Southern Development Minister Sagala Ratnayake at the BMICH on Friday. The exhibition will be held from 10 am to 10 pm on Saturday and Sunday. Pix by Kithsiri de Mel
