## Title: A heartbreaking send-off
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 24 June 2018 07:57 pm

A heartbreaking send-off


	

Hundreds of people were seen gathered along the roads to pay last respect to Police Sergeant Suranga Pradeep Weerasinghe who was killed during the exchange of fire between the police and an underworld gang in Matara on Friday. His final rites were held at the Matara public cemetery this evening. Pix by Krishan Jeewaka Jayaruk
