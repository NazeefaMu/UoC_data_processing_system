## Title: PM to visit China tomorrow
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 12 August 2016 12:27 pm

PM to visit China tomorrow


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremeshinghe is to undertake an observation visit in China from tomorrow to August 17, Prime Minister’s office said.

The delegation including Minister Anura Priyadarshana Yapa and Patali Champika Ranawaka are expected visit industrial zones, container terminals, financial centres and other industrial hubs during the visit.
