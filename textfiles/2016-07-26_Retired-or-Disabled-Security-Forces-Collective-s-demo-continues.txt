## Title: Retired or Disabled Security Forces Collective's demo continues
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 July 2016 09:41 am

Retired or Disabled Security Forces Collective's demo continues


	



The protest launched by the Collective of Retired or Disabled Military and Police Personnel urging the President to intervene in the proposed pension scheme, continued for the second day today outside the Fort Railway Station.

The protesters pledged not to leave until their demands were granted.

The road in front of the Railway station remains closed.
