## Title: Traffic restricted on Low Level Road
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 June 2016 10:13 pm

Traffic restricted on Low Level Road


	

Traffic on the Low Level Road from Orugodawatte to Kaduwela has been restricted between 9.00 P.M. to 5.00 a.m. for three days from today due to clearing of garbage and debris of houses destroyed by the floods in the Wellampitiya and Kotikawatte areas, police said.

They said movement of buses and lorries would be restricted, while other vehicles that exit from Colombo alone the low Level Road were requested to use alternative routes.

They said the residents of the areas from Orugodawatte to Kaduwela were allowed to use the road.
