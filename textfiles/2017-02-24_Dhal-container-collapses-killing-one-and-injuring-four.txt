## Title: Dhal container collapses killing one and injuring four
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 February 2017 06:09 pm

Dhal container collapses killing one and injuring four


	

One person was killed and four others were injured when a dhal storage container collapsed on them at a warehouse in Pattiwela, Kelaniya today, Police said.
