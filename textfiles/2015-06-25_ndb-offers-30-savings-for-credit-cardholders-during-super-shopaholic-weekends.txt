## Title: NDB offers 30% savings for credit cardholders during ‘Super Shopaholic’ weekends
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 25 June 2015 02:33 am

NDB offers 30% savings for credit cardholders during ‘Super Shopaholic’ weekends


	


This June and July, NDB Good Life Credit Cardholders will have the opportunity to enjoy sizeable savings while shopping at their favourite clothing stores.  
 
NDB Good Life Credit Cards will offer 30  percent savings for purchases made at popular shopping destinations, including Hameedia, Signature, The Raymond Shop, Addidas, Fashion Bug, Cool Planet and CIB shopping Centre. The offers are extended during the ‘Good life weekends‘ in the months of June and July.  
NDB Good Life Credit Cards hopes to offer cardholders a series of ‘good life weekends’ with savings on a variety of lifestyle features including dining and hotel stays.  
The suite of NDB ‘Good Life’ Credit Cards is available in five categories of Silver, Gold, Platinum, Signature and Infinite; each category designed with unique benefits and rewards to fulfil diverse lifestyle requirements of a wide-spread clientele. 
NDB Credit Cards are loaded with superior deals and first-time offers from much sought-after retail merchants, hotels and restaurants; coupled with easy instalment plans for high value purchases, periodical bill wipe out and cash back promotions. NDB ‘Good Life’ Credit Cards will provide a balance transfer option that would allow quick and easy transferring of the outstanding balance on an existing credit card to a new NDB ‘Good Life’ Credit Card and pay back at a nominal interest rate. Cash advances could also be made from the Credit Card, at any of the NDB ATMs or any Visa ATM in Sri Lanka and across the globe ensuring further convenience for Card Members. 
The globally recognized NDB ‘Good Life’ Credit Cards carry the chip enabled security that would assure highest security.  Additionally, NDB ‘Good Life’ Credit Cards encompass the ‘NDBSafe’ travel insurance policy which offers Card Members a personal accident and medical cover during their travel overseas when they purchase the airline ticket through the credit card; assuring their safety even while they are away from the country. Cardholders will also receive SMS alerts for each transaction carried out when overseas, enabling them to keep track of their expenses with ease. 
NDB Good Life Credit Cards could be used at over 2 million  VISA ATMs across 200 countries and are accepted over 25 million merchant acceptance points around the globe. Further for credit cards in the premium category – namely Platinum, Signature and Infinite category cardholders will have access to many global offers and deals that are made available through the VISA international franchise.  
Applications for NDB ‘Good Life’ Credit Cards could be made at any one of the 87 NDB Bank branches island wide.
