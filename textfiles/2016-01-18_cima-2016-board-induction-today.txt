## Title: CIMA 2016 board induction today
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 18 January 2016 06:30 pm

CIMA 2016 board induction today


	


		 		 
		 
			
The Chartered Institute of Management Accountants (CIMA) will hold the induction of its Sri Lanka board today, in which Manjula de Silva is to be inducted as the Chairman of the board for 2016. CIMA Regional Director Asia Pacific Irene Teng will also attend the event. The board induction will take place from 6:00 p.m. onwards at Cinnamon Grand Colombo.
