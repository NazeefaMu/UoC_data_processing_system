## Title: Power supply interruption
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 July 2017 09:50 pm

Power supply interruption


	

Power interruption has been reported in Panadura,Kalutara, Mathugama, Horana, Ambalangoda and Galle areas this evening due to the tripping of Biyagama- Pannipitiya line.

Power and Renewable Energy Ministry Media Secretary Pathum Pasqual said the Ceylon Electricity Board (CEB) engineers are investigating the matter and will restore the electricity as soon as possible. (Thilanka Kanakarathna)
