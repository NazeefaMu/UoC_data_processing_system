## Title: Prez inaugurates programme to renovate 2400 irrigation tanks
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 3 March 2018 04:33 am

Prez inaugurates programme to renovate 2400 irrigation tanks


	



The programme to renovate 2400 irrigation tanks commenced yesterday (2) under the patronage of President Maithripala Sirisena with the intention of resurrecting the ancient irrigation development in the country to emerge as a self-sufficient nation.
