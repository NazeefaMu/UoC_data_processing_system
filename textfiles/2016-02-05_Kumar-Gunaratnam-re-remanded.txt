## Title: Kumar Gunaratnam re-remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 February 2016 11:58 am

Kumar Gunaratnam re-remanded


	

Senior member of Frontline Socialist Party (FSP) Kumar Gunaratnam was further remanded till February 15 by Kegalle Magistrate Prasanna Alwis today.

He was arrested by the Police in the Anguruwella area in Kegalle on November 04, last year on charges of violating immigration laws.
