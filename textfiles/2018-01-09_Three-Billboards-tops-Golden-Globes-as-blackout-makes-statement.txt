## Title: ‘Three Billboards’ tops Golden Globes as ‘blackout’ makes statement
## Section: International
## Date: 9 January 2018 10:13 am

‘Three Billboards’ tops Golden Globes as ‘blackout’ makes statement


	



 

DPA, 08th JANUARY, 2018 - The movie “Three Billboards Outside Ebbing, Missouri” was the big winner Sunday at the Golden Globe awards ceremony as Hollywood women dressed in black and raised their voices against sexual harassment and abuse.


The movie won the top prize for best drama and two of its stars, Frances McDormand and Sam Rockwell, were honoured for their roles - McDormand as best actress in a drama and Rockwell as best supporting actor in a drama.
