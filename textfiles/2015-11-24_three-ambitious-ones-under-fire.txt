## Title: Three ambitious ones under fire
## Section: Political Gossip
## Date: 24 November 2015 06:30 pm

Three ambitious ones under fire


	


A Green party purohita more vocal on topical issues than most others recently took to task several colleagues for turning traitorous.
At a powwow held at a five-star hotel in the city this purohita had charged that at least there were three bigwigs fired with the ambition of becoming the second in command in yahapalanaya. The consensus of all purohitas who attended the discussion was that the threesome were living in a dream that would never come true, they say.
