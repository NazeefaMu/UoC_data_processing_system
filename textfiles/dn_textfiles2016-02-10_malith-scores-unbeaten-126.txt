## Title: Malith scores unbeaten 126
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, February 10, 2016 - 01:00

Malith scores unbeaten 126

Royal College Colombo in a strong position against Prince of Wales' College in their inter school second eleven cricket match played at Moratuwa.
Batting first Royalists declared at 370 for 5 with Malith Kariyawasam unbeaten on 126 and Kavindu Madara and Gayan Dissanayake contributed with 57 and unbeaten 51 runs respectively. At the end of day the Cambrians were 68 for no loss.
Royal College - 375/5 dec - Malith Kariyawasam 126 n/o, Kavindu Madara 57, Gayan Dissanayake 51 n/o, Madara Thalduwa 36, Uvin Herath 32, Supun Abeyrathne 32, Chamara Perera 2/55.
Prince of Wales - 68/0 - Sachin Pushpika 36, Hasindu Chamika 29.
