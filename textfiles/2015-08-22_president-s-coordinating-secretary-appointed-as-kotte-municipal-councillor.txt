## Title: President’s Coordinating Secretary appointed as Municipal councillor
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 August 2015 08:09 am

President’s Coordinating Secretary appointed as Municipal councillor


	

President’s Coordinating Secretary Rajika kodithuwakku has been appointed as a member of the Sri Jayawardenapura Kotte Municipal Council by President Maithripala Sirisena today.
He was sworn in by President Sirisena at President’s official residence.  
Some members of the Sri Jayawardenapura Kotte Municipal Council and Minister Champika Ranawaka participated in the event.
