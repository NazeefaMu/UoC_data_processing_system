## Title: Galle road closed from Kollupitiya Junction
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 September 2017 01:02 pm

Galle road closed from Kollupitiya Junction


	

Galle Road from Kollupitiya Junction towards Galle Face has been closed due to a protest march, Police said.

The motorists travelling towards Galle Face and Fort are advised to travel via Piththala Junction.
