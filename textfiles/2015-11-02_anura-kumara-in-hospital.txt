## Title: Anura Kumara in hospital
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 2 November 2015 04:49 am

Anura Kumara in hospital


	

JVP Leader and Chief Opposition Whip Anura Kumara Dissanayake was admitted to Sri Jayawardenepura Hospital late Sunday night following a sudden illness.
According to party sources, Mr. Dissanayake’s condition was not serious. He is said to be suffering of an attack of gastritis. He was expected to undergo some medical tests according to sources.
