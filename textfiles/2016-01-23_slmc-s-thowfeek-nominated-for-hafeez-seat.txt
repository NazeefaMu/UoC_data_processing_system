## Title: SLMC’s Thowfeek nominated to Hafeez seat
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 January 2016 07:05 am

SLMC’s Thowfeek nominated to Hafeez seat


	

Sri Lanka Muslim Congress (SLMC)’s Sharif Thowfeek was nominated to fill the seat that fell vacant after the resignation of MP Abdul Hafeez from his parliamentary seat last week, the Election Commission said today.
