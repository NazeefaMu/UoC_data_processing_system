## Title: Test batsman Kaushal airlifted to Colombo
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 April 2016 06:54 pm

Test batsman Kaushal airlifted to Colombo


	

Test batsman Kaushal Silva has been airlifted to Colombo this evening after being hit by a ball on the head during a training session in Pallekele, Sri Lanka Cricket said.


However, it said that the cricketer, who was admitted to a private hospital in Colombo as a precautionary measure, is out of danger.
