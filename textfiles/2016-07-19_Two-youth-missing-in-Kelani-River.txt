## Title: Two youth missing in Kelani River
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 July 2016 10:36 am

Two youth missing in Kelani River


	

A 16-year-old boy and a 19-year-old girl were reported missing after have gone to bath in the Kelani river at Hanwella yesterday.

Police said the Navy divers launched a search operation for the duo who is residents of Hanwella.
