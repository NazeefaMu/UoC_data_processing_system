## Title: Children’s Day celebration
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 30 September 2018 04:41 pm

Children’s Day celebration


	


Children’s Day celebration in line with the World Children's Day tomorrow was held yesterday at the Viharamahadevi Park in Colombo. The event was organised by the Colombo Municipal Council and Rupavahini Corporation. Pix By Nimalsiri Edirisinghe
