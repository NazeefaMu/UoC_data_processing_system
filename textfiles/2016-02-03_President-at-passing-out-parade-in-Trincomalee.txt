## Title: President at passing out parade in Trincomalee
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 3 February 2016 10:04 am

President at passing out parade in Trincomalee


	



President Maithripala Sirisena was the Chief Guest at the 53rd Cadet Entry Intake yesterday. Eighty-one Midshipmen completed their training at the Naval and Maritime Academy (NMA) in Trincomalee yesterday. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
