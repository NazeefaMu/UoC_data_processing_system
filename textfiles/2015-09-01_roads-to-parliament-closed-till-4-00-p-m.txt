## Title: Roads to parliament closed till 4.00 p.m.
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 September 2015 08:33 am

Roads to parliament closed till 4.00 p.m.


	


	Vehicular traffic on the roads leading to parliament would be restricted for two hours this afternoon because of the special ceremony to be held in parliament, police said.
	The road from Polduwa Junction to Jayanthipura; Jayanthipura to Chianham Bridge and the road in front of the parliament would be restricted for vehicular traffic up to 4.00 pm today.
