## Title: Video: Police fire tear gas at IUBF protesters
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 29 April 2015 08:52 am

Video: Police fire tear gas at IUBF protesters


	

Police fired tear gas and used water cannons to disperse the Inter-University Bhikku Federation (IUBF) students who were protesting outside the University Grants Commission (UGC) in Colombo 7. (LSP)
