## Title: TTSC Cricket Sixes tomorrow
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, November 28, 2015 - 01:00

TTSC Cricket Sixes tomorrow

 
The 33rd Travel Trade Sports Club Cricket Sixes 2015, sponsored by Cinnamon Grand Colombo for the fourth successive year, will be held tomorrow at the BRC Grounds, commencing 8.30 am.
In the 2014 tournament held at Thurstan College grounds, Cinnamon Grand Colombo and John Keells Hotels "B" were declared joint winners, as the Final was abandoned due to bad light. The sponsorship handing over ceremony was held at the Cinnamon Grand Colombo last Friday where Chris Quyn, Resident Manager , Cinnamon Grand Colombo presented the Sponsorship package to the President, TTSC, Denesh Silva; also present at the event was Asanka Fernando, Director of Sales, Cinnamon Hotels and Resorts.
