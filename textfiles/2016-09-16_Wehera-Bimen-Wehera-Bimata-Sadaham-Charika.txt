## Title: Wehera Bimen Wehera Bimata Sadaham Charika
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 16 September 2016 08:46 pm

Wehera Bimen Wehera Bimata Sadaham Charika


	




State Defense Minister and the UNP Secretary Buddhist Affairs Ruwan Wijewardene today donated funds to the Chief Incumbent of the Bollagala Sri Sunandharamaya Malwattha Biyagama Ven. Aludeniya Sumanarathana Thera under the programme “Wehera Bimen Wehera Bimata Sadaham Charika”. The donations also comprised of books and stationary for students of the daham pasal.  Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
