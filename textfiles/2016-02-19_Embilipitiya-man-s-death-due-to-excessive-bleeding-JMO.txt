## Title: Embilipitiya man’s death due to excessive bleeding: JMO
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 February 2016 11:53 am

Embilipitiya man’s death due to excessive bleeding: JMO


	

The JMO report, on the young man’s death at Embilipitiya during a clash with the Police, revealed that his death was due to the severe shock caused by excessive bleeding from cut injuries.

Embilipitiya Additional Magistrate Prasanna Fernando today released the JMO report prepared by Ratnapura Hospital’s Medical Officer T.B. Gunatilake.
