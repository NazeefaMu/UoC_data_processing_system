## Title: A case of a wise foe being better than stupid ally
## Section: Political Gossip
## Date: 18 January 2016 06:30 pm

A case of a wise foe being better than stupid ally


	


“Your doings have caused me much trouble. Hereafter, you had better consult others before you act.”
A onetime political strongman had given the above admonition to a leader of a political party
The political party leader had got this tongue-lashing for announcing a certain high-handed decision he had taken without consulting his political allies close to the one-time strongman.
