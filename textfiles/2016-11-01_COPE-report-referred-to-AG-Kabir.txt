## Title: COPE report referred to AG: Kabir
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 November 2016 01:12 pm

COPE report referred to AG: Kabir


	

The COPE report which held former Central Bank (CB) Governor Arjuna Mahendran responsible for the CB Bond Scam has been referred to the Attorney General, UNP General Secretary Minister Kabir Hashim said today.

He said this during a news conference held at Temple Trees.
