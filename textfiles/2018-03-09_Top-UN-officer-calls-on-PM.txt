## Title: Top UN officer calls on PM
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 9 March 2018 02:22 pm

Top UN officer calls on PM


	



Visiting UN Under-Secretary-General for Political Affairs Jeffrey Feltman met with Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe at Temple Trees in Colombo today. Feltman Arrived in Sri Lanka on Thursday for a three-day visit. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
