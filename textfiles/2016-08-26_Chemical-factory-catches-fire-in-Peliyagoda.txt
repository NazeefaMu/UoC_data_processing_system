## Title: Chemical factory catches fire in Peliyagoda
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 August 2016 04:01 pm

Chemical factory catches fire in Peliyagoda


	

A major fire has reportedly broken out in a chemical factory at Naranminiya in Peliyagoda a short while ago, Police said.

The Fire Brigade has arrived at the scene to douse the fire.
