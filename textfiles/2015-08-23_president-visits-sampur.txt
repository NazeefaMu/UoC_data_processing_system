## Title: President visits Sampur
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 23 August 2015 04:40 am

President visits Sampur


	


 

	President Maithripala Sirisena yesterday released lands and deeds in the High Security Zones to 234 war displaced families in Sampur. Former President Chandrika Bandaranaike Kumaratunga, Governor of Eastern Province Ostin Fernando, Chief Minister Nasir Ahamed , RaRauff Hakeem, Lakshman Kiriella, Daya Gamage, D.M Swaminathan and R. Sampanthan were also attend the event.Pix by President's Media
