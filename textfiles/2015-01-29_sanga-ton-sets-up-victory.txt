## Title: Sanga ton sets up victory
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 29 January 2015 09:44 am

Sanga ton sets up victory


	

Kumar Sangakkara hit his 21st one-day international century to set up a 34-run victory for Sri Lanka over New Zealand in Thursday's seventh and last game of the one-day series in Wellington.
Tillakaratne Dilshan made 81 and shared a 104-run second wicket partnership with Sangakkara, who finished 113 not out.
Allrounder Corey Anderson took 3-59 and senior fast bowler Tim Southee 2-50.
New Zealand won the seven-match series 4-2.Full Scorecard
