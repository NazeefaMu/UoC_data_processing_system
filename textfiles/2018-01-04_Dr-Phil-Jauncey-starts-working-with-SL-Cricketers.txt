## Title: Dr. Phil Jauncey starts working with SL Cricketers
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 January 2018 04:22 pm

Dr. Phil Jauncey starts working with SL Cricketers


	



Dr. Phil Jauncey, the Australian based performance psychologist, who was invited by Sri Lanka Cricket to help raise the performance levels of the national cricket team, started his stint with the Lankan team on Wednesday at the SLC headquarters. (SLC)
