## Title: President inaugurates Dhathu exposition in Thailand
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 2 November 2015 12:49 pm

President inaugurates Dhathu exposition in Thailand


	


President Maithripala Sirisena inaugurated the exposition of Sarvagna Dhathu from Mahiyangana Rajamaha Viharaya, in Buddhamanthan Temple in Thailand today. The Prime Minister of Thailand General Prayut Chan–o-cha also participated at the ceremony. The special public exposition of the Sarvagna Dhathu from Mahiyangana Rajamaha Viharaya was inaugurated amidst the chanting of Seth Pirith by the Maha Sangha including Ven. Ittepane Dhammalankara Nayaka Thera, the chief incumbent of the Mahiyangana Raja Maha Viharaya, Ven. Urulawatte Dhamma Rakkitha Thera. Pix by President Media
