## Title: Lalith Weeratunga at FCID
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 May 2016 11:49 am

Lalith Weeratunga at FCID


	

Former presidential secretary Lalith Weeratunga arrived at the Financial Crime Investigation Division (FCID) this morning to give a statement over the allocation of Rs. 200 million to former media minister Keheliya Rambukwella for a surgery.
