## Title: British man dies after falling off cliff in Weligama
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 June 2016 11:42 am

British man dies after falling off cliff in Weligama


	

A 23-year-old British national died after he fell off a cliff at,Bandalwatte Place in Weligama last night, police said.


They said the victim had been taking part in a party organised by a hotel situated near the cliff when the tragedy occured.
