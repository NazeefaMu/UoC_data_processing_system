## Title: Sicille Kothelawala remanded over F&G case
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 February 2016 10:31 am

Sicille Kothelawala remanded over F&G case


	

Former Ceylinco Chairman Lalith Kothelawala's wife Sicille Kotelawala was remanded till February 25 by the Colombo Fort Magistrate over the F&G case.

Sicille Kotelawala was the eighth suspect in the case filed against her husband Lalith Kothalawala and eight others on charges of misappropriating Rs. 4.3 billion at the F&G property developers.(Farook Thajudeen)
