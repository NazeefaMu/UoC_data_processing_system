## Title: Induction ceremony of New Anunayake
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 30 January 2016 09:51 pm

Induction ceremony of New Anunayake


	



The ceremonial induction of Venerable Professor Kotapitiye Rahula Thera as Anunayake of the Supreme Sangha Council of the Kotte Chapter of Siam Maha Nikaya took place at Kotte Raja Maha Viharaya on Friday.

The event which was chaired by the Most Venerable Dr. Ittepane Dhammalankara Thera was attended by the President Maithripala Sirisena and the ex-President Mahinda Rajapaksa. Pic by Pradeep Dilrukshana
