## Title: 700 bottles of illicit arrack in custody
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 27 May 2016 12:51 pm

700 bottles of illicit arrack in custody


	



A vehicle transporting 700 bottles of illicit arrack was nabbed by the Excise Department officials today in Ja-Ela. Two people were arrested. An official said the arrack had been brewed using waste water. Pix by Nisal Baduge
