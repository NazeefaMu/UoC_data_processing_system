## Title: Appeal submitted on behalf of Gnanasara Thera
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 June 2018 01:17 pm

Appeal submitted on behalf of Gnanasara Thera


	

Counsels of Ven. Galagodaatte Gnansara Thera had submitted an appeal to the Homagama Magistrate’s Court to be forwarded to the High Court requesting the Judges to acquit and release the Thera. (CS)
