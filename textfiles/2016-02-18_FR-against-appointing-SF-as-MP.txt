## Title: FR against appointing SF as MP
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 February 2016 04:21 pm

FR against appointing SF as MP


	

The Centre for Policy Alternatives (CPA) today filed a Fundamental Rights (FR) petition against the appointment of Field Marshal Sarath Fonseka as a Parliamentarian.

Executive Director of the organisation P. Saravanamuttu filed the petition seeking an interim order to prevent the Field Marshal from acting as an MP.

Field Marshall Fonseka, UNP General Secretary Kabir Hashim and the Speaker were among the respondents named by the petitioner.
