## Title: 12-hour water cut in Colombo
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 November 2016 09:30 am

12-hour water cut in Colombo


	

A 12-hour water cur will be imposed in several areas in Colombo from 9.00 am to 9.00 pm today due to a maintenance work of the power supply at the Ambatale water treatment plant, the National Water Supply and Drainage Board (NWSDB) said.

The water supply will be interrupted in Colombo, Dehiwala-Mount Lavinia, Kotte and Kaduwela Miniciple Council area, Maharagama, Boralesgamuwa, Kolonnawa, urban council areas, Kotikawatte, Mulleriyawa Pradeshiya Sabha areas and Ratmalana.
