## Title: One killed in beggar’s clash
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 January 2016 04:31 am

One killed in beggar’s clash


	


	A 46-year-old man was stabbed to death during a clash among several beggars in Nawagamuwa last night, Police said.
	The victim, a resident of Hanwella succumbed to his injuries after he was admitted to the Nawagamuwa Hospital.
	Police arrested two suspects in connection with the murder.
