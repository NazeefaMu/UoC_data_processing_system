## Title: 10 killed, several injured in bus-van collision
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 December 2016 02:18 pm

10 killed, several injured in bus-van collision


	

At least ten people including three women were killed and several others injured when a van collided head on with a bus at Chavakachcheri in Jaffna this afternoon.

The injured were admitted to the Jaffna and Chavakachcheri Hospitals.

The victims were passengers of the van which was reportedly heading to Nagadeepa carrying some pilgrims. (Romesh Madushanka)
