## Title: TNA commiserates with Chennai flood affected
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, December 5, 2015 - 01:00

TNA commiserates with Chennai flood affected

 
The Tamil National Alliance yesterday expressed its deepest condolences to those who lost their family members due to floods in Chennai.
The alliance in a statement said that the TNA is deeply concerned about the flood situation in Chennai the 4th largest city in India. "Our deepest condolences goes to those who have lost their family members and loved ones due to this natural disaster. We sympathise with thousands of people who are displaced and who have lost their livelihoods as a result of this devastation
"We also extend our appreciation to the Tamil Nadu Government and the other state and private sector institutions for their tireless efforts to provide relief for the affected people.
"We hope that this situation will end soon and the people will be back to normalcy."
