## Title: Carabiniere at Colombo port
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 11 January 2017 07:39 pm

Carabiniere at Colombo port


	

The Italian Naval Ship ITS Carabiniere arrived at the Colombo Port today on a four-day goodwill visit.  The Commander of the ship, Francesco Pagnotta held a news briefing on-board the ship for local media. Pix by Damith Wickramasinghe
