## Title: Thajudeen’s body parts: Ex-JMO’S bail application rejected
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 September 2016 02:46 pm

Thajudeen’s body parts: Ex-JMO’S bail application rejected


	

The anticipatory bail application filed by Colombo's former chief judicial medical officer Ananda Samarasekera, seeking court to prevent his arrest in connection with Wasim Thajudeen’s missing body parts, was today rejected by Colombo Additional Magistrate Dulani Amarasinghe. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
