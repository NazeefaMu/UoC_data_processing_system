## Title: 25 injured in Hatton bus accident
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 November 2017 07:34 pm

25 injured in Hatton bus accident


	

Around 25 people have been injured when a SLTB bus plying between Dayagama and Hatton veered off the road at Ensiwatte area in Hatton this evening.

Injured passengers were admitted to the Dickoya base hospital for treatment.

Hatton Police are conducting investigations. Text and pix by Ranjith Rajapakse
