## Title: H.W. Gunasena SPC’s new Fisheries Minister
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 20 July 2016 02:49 pm

H.W. Gunasena SPC’s new Fisheries Minister


	

President Maitripala Sirisena has today appointed Southern Provincial Councillor H.W. Gunasena as the SPC’s Minister of Fisheries, Livestock, Environment, Electricity and Rural Industries.

He replaces Southern Provincial Councillor D.V. Upul who held this portfolio previously.
