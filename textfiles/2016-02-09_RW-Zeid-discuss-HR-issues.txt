## Title: RW, Zeid discuss HR issues
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 February 2016 11:11 am

RW, Zeid discuss HR issues


	

UN High Commissioner for Human Rights Zeid Ra’ad Al Hussein met Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe this morning and discussed a range of current challenges and opportunities for strengthening the rule of law and protection of human rights in Sri Lanka.
