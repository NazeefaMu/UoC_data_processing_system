## Title: American Center closed indefinitely
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 January 2018 03:05 pm

American Center closed indefinitely


	

The American Center in Colombo which is being operated by the US government will remain closed until further notice, the US Embassy said today.

However, it said that scheduled passport and visa services at the Embassy will continue during the lapse in appropriations as the situation permits.
