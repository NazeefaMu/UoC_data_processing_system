## Title: President arrives in New York
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 19 September 2016 10:56 am

President arrives in New York


	



President Maithripala Sirisena arrived at the John F Kennedy Airport in New York last evening.  He was received at the Loews Regency Hotel by Minister Mangala Samaraweera, Deputy Minister Ajith P Perera, Sri Lanka’s Permanent Representative to UN, Dr Rohan Perera, Ambassador in Washington, Prasad Kariyawasam and the officials.
