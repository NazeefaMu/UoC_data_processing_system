## Title: Prisoner found hanging in prison cell
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 September 2016 10:45 am

Prisoner found hanging in prison cell


	

A prisoner was found dead hanging in a cell at the Matara Prisons this morning, the Prisons Department said.

They said the inmate, a resident of Matara, had been sentenced to 12 years imprisonment over a child abuse incident recently. The body had been sent to the Matara Hospital for post mortem examination.
