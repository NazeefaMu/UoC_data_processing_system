## Title: Nightclub killing: SF Loku’s brother arrested
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 31 October 2015 10:33 am

Nightclub killing: SF Loku’s brother arrested


	

SF Loku’s brother Asitha Ranasinghe has been arrested in Tangalle this afternoon, police said.
SF Loku was the mastermind behind the killing of the nightclub owner Wasantha Soyza in Anuradhapura recently.
Police Spokesman Ruwan Gunasekara said he is to be handed over to the Anuradhapura police for investigations.
