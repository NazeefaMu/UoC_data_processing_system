## Title: President meets John Key
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 24 February 2016 01:25 pm

President meets John Key


	



New Zealand Prime Minister John Phillip Key met with President Maitripala Sirisena at the Presidential Secretariat today. John Key arrived in Sri Lanka last night on a three-day official visit. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana.
