## Title: MS to visit Pakistan this month
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 April 2015 02:10 pm

MS to visit Pakistan this month


	

President Maithripala Sirisena will visit Pakistan this month, the Government said today.
Cabinet Spokesman Rajitha Senaratne said a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) would be signed between the Ceylon Shipping Corporation and the Pakistan Shipping Corporation on mutual cooperation with regard to shipping affairs.
An agreement on Academic Cooperation will also to be signed during the visit.
