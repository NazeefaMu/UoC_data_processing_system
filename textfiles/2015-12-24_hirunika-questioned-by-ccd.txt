## Title: Hirunika questioned by CCD
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 December 2015 12:27 am

Hirunika questioned by CCD


	

MP Hirunika Premachandra was questioned by the Colombo Crime Division (CCD) on Tuesday following a complaint by a 34-year-old man that he had been abducted by the MP's personal staff, Police spokesman ASP Ruwan Gunasekera said.
He said that the CCD officers had questioned the MP at the CCD office in Colombo.
