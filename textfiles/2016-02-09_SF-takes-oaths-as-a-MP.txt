## Title: SF takes oaths as a MP
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 February 2016 01:40 pm

SF takes oaths as a MP


	

Field Marshal Sarath Fonseka took oath as a Member of the Parliament before the speaker Karu Jayasuriya a short while ago.


The United National Party's working committee on Monday decided to appoint Field Marshal Fonseka as a UNP national list MP, a seat which was left vacant by the recent demise of Minister M. K. A. D. S Gunawardene.
