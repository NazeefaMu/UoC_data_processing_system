## Title: SLBFE official of President’s Mobile Service found dead
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 August 2018 11:52 am

SLBFE official of President’s Mobile Service found dead


	

An officer of Sri Lanka Bureau of Foreign Employment (SLBFE) attached to Presidential Mobile Service, was found dead at a hotel in Jaffna last night, the Police said.

They said four officials of the SLBFE had been lodging at the hotel and the 60-year-old, a resident of Gampaha -was found dead at his room this morning.

They said the post-mortem examination into the death had revealed the victim had died due to a cardiac arrest.(Romesh Madushanka)
