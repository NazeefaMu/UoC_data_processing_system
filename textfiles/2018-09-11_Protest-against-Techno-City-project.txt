## Title: Protest against ‘Techno City' project
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 11 September 2018 06:31 pm

Protest against ‘Techno City' project


	



The Movement for protecting Homagama People's Rights today carried out a protest march from the Fort Railway Station against the Technological City Development Project that is to be launched in the areas like Malabe, Athurugiriya, Kaduwela, and Homagama.
