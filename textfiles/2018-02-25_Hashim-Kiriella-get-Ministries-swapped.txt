## Title: Hashim, Kiriella get Ministries swapped
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 February 2018 11:50 am

Hashim, Kiriella get Ministries swapped


	

Former Public Enterprise Development Minister Kabir Hashim was appointed as the new Cabinet Minister of Higher Education and Highways.

Former Higher Education Minister Lakshman Kirielle was appointed as the new Cabinet Minister of State Enterprise and Kandy Development.
