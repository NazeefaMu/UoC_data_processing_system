## Title: Presidential Commission to probe SriLankan and Mihin Air
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 December 2017 06:22 pm

Presidential Commission to probe SriLankan and Mihin Air


	

A Special Presidential Commission would be appointed to look into the Sri Lanka Airlines and the Mihin Air, soon after the receiving of the Bond Commission report, President Maithripala Sirisena said.
The President made this comment at a meeting with the Sri Lanka Freedom Party organizers at the President's House.
