## Title: Karuna to join TULF
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 October 2015 06:29 am

Karuna to join TULF


	

Former MP and Vice President of the SLFP Vinayagamoorthy Muralitharan alias Karuna Amman said he will resign from the SLFP and join the Tamil United Liberation Front (TULF) led by Anandasangaree.
