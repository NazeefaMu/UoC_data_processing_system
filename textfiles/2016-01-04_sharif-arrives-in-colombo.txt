## Title: Sharif arrives in Colombo
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 January 2016 12:02 pm

Sharif arrives in Colombo


	


Pakistan Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif arrived in Colombo, a short while ago. This will be Sharif's first visit to Sri Lanka since President Maithripala Sirisena took office a year ago. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
