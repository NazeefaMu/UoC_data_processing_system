## Title: Miss Tourism contestants meet PM
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 19 February 2016 11:12 am

Miss Tourism contestants meet PM


	

Contestants of Miss Tourism 2016 World Pageant yesterday met Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe at Temple Tress. The Finals of Miss Tourism 2016 World Pageant will be held on February 21 at Taj Samudra, Colombo. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
