## Title: Four Lankans attempting to flee to Canada arrested at BIA
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 8 July 2016 11:09 am

Four Lankans attempting to flee to Canada arrested at BIA


	

Four Sri Lankans,aged between 24 and 33, were arrested by the CID at the Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA) today when attempting to migrate to Canada through Turkey using fake visas.

The suspects, who included a brother and a sister, are residents of Mollipuram and Ilavalei.(Deepa Adikari)
