## Title: Nissanka Senadhipathi arrested
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 September 2016 06:11 pm

Nissanka Senadhipathi arrested


	

Avant-Garde Maritime Security Limited Chairman Nissanka Senadhipathi and former Major general Palitha Fernando were arrested by the Bribery Commission a short while ago.

They were arrested after they came to the commission to record a statement over the Avant-Garde issue.
