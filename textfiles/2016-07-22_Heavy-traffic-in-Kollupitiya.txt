## Title: Heavy traffic in Kollupitiya
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 July 2016 01:05 pm

Heavy traffic in Kollupitiya


	

Heavy traffic was reported in Kollupitiya due to a protest launched by a group of teachers and principals at Flower Road in Kollupitiya over several of their demands.

Police said more than 350 teachers have participated in the protest.
