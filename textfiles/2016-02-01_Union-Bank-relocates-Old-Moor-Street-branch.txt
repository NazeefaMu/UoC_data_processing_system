## Title: Union Bank relocates Old Moor Street branch
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 1 February 2016 11:40 am

Union Bank relocates Old Moor Street branch


	

Union Bank CEO Indrajit Wickramasinghe lighting the traditional oil lamp at the ceremony

Union Bank relocated its Old Moor Street branch to a more spacious location at No: 343, Old Moor Street, Colombo 12, in a bid to provide an enhanced banking experience to people in the area. The branch operates from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

during weekdays. Old Moor Street, a renowned commercial area in the heart of Colombo, is a famous construction material trade zone and home to numerous small and large businesses. Union Bank’s Old Moor street branch has been in operation for over 10 years since 2005, offering financial solutions focusing on individuals and business financial needs in trade,

commodities, manufacturing and large industries such as steel, with the aim of empowering such aspiring tradesmen through the bank’s unique financial products and services. With the relocation of its branch,

Union Bank renewed its banking relationships with the individuals and businessmen in the area, opening its doors to aspiring entrepreneurs to benefit from the bank’s many customized small and medium enterprise (SME) financing facilities and advisory services while inspiring individuals to reach their financial aspirations with ease. The reopened branch is intended to offer a broader spectrum of services, with the addition of an onsite trade desk for speedy processing of import documentation and a dedicated service point for Elite Banking customers.
