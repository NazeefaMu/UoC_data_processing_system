## Title: Ruwan visits Yovun Pura
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 2 April 2016 08:13 pm

Ruwan visits Yovun Pura


	

State Minister of Defence Ruwan Wijewardene yesterday visited Yovun Pura exhibition in Seegiriya. The Yovun Pura Camp has been revived after a lapse of 27 years. It will be held untill April 3. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
