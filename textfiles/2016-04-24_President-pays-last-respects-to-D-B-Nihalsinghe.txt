## Title: President pays last respects to D.B. Nihalsinghe
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 24 April 2016 02:02 pm

President pays last respects to D.B. Nihalsinghe


	



President Maithripala Sirisena yesterday paid his last respects to the late Veteran film director, cinematographer and producer Dr. D.B. Nihalsinghe at the Borella Cemetery premises. He passed away at a private hospital after a brief illness at the age of 77. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
