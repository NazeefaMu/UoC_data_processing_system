## Title: Prez meets Iranian President
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 13 May 2018 06:55 pm

Prez meets Iranian President


	

President Maithripala Sirisena who is on a two day state visit to Iran met with Iranian President Hassan Rouhani today. President Sirisena was warmly welcomed by the Iranian President with a Guard of Honour when he arrived at the Saadabad Palace in Tehran. Pix courtesy President Media
