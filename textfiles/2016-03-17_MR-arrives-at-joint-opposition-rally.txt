## Title: MR arrives at joint opposition rally
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 March 2016 05:28 pm

MR arrives at joint opposition rally


	

Former President Mahinda  Rajapaksa arrived at the rally organized by the joint opposition at Hyde Park Grounds in Colombo.

Former Leader of the Janatha Vimukthi Peramuna, Somawansa Amarasinghe, Parliamentarians Chamal Rajapaksa, Wimal Weerawansa, Dinesh Gunawardane, Rohitha Abeygunawardane and Kumara Welgama also arrived at the rally.
