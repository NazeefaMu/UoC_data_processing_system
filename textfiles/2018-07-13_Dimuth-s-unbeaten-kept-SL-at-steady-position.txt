## Title: Dimuth’s unbeaten 158 kept SL at steady position
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 13 July 2018 08:32 am

Dimuth’s unbeaten 158 kept SL at steady position


	



Dimuth Karunaratne’s unbeaten score of 158  steered Sri Lanka to have a total of 287-10 (Overs 78.4) in the day one of the first test match of the South Africa tour of Sri Lanka, 2018 held in Galle. Pix by Samantha Perera
