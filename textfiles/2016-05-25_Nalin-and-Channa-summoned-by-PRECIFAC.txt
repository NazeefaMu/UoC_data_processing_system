## Title: Nalin and Channa summoned by PRECIFAC
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 May 2016 02:02 pm

Nalin and Channa summoned by PRECIFAC


	

Well-known dancer Channa Wijewardane and popular singer Nalin Perera have been asked to report to the Presidential Commission of Inquiry to Investigate and Inquire into Serious Acts of Fraud, Corruption and Abuse of Power, State Resources and Privileges (PRECIFAC) on May 27 to record their statements on the monies paid to them for their performances at the opening ceremony of the Hambantota Harbour.
