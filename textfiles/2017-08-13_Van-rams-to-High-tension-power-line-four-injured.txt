## Title: Van rams to High tension power line - four injured
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 13 August 2017 06:26 pm

Van rams to High tension power line - four injured


	

Two passengers of a van and two other girls who were walking beside the road were injured when the van knocked against a high tension electric supply post at Kiribathkumbura on the Colombo-Kandy main road today.

Police said some of the victims were hospitalised because of burn injuries.

The four victims were undergoing treatment at the Peradeniya Hospital. (Senerath Bandara)
