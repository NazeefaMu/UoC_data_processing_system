## Title: SLPP wins Kaduwela MC
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 February 2018 12:23 pm

SLPP wins Kaduwela MC


	

The SLPP won the Kaduwela Municipal Council securing 26 seats with 71016 votes. SLPP  – 71016 (26)
UNP    – 31429 (11)
UPFA  – 15549 (5)
JVP     – 15071 (5)
