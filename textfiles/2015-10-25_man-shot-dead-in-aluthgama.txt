## Title: Man shot dead in Aluthgama
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 October 2015 07:13 am

Man shot dead in Aluthgama


	

A 38-year-old man was shot dead by unidentified gunmen near the Kande Viharaya in Aluthgama this morning.
Police said the assailants arrived on a motor cycle. The victim succumbed to his injuries after admission to the Nagoda Hospital.
