## Title: E01: Use Kadawatha and Athurugiriya exits tomorrow
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 December 2015 11:37 am

E01: Use Kadawatha and Athurugiriya exits tomorrow


	

Motorists travelling to Colombo on the Southern Expressway (E01) are advised to use the exit points at Kadawatha and Athurugiriya to avoid possible vehicle congestion, mainly at the Kottawa exit point, tomorrow, the RDA officials said.
A large number of vehicles are expected to use the E01 following the four-day long holiday, which started on Thursday, December 24.
