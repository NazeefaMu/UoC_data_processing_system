## Title: U.S. Ambassador holds welcome reception for Indian HC to SL
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 12 February 2017 02:33 pm

U.S. Ambassador holds welcome reception for Indian HC to SL


	



U.S. Ambassador to Sri Lanka Atul Keshap hosted a dinner for newly-arrived Indian High Commissioner to Sri Lanka, Taranjit Singh Sandhu. The High Commissioner was previously Deputy Chief of Mission at the Indian Embassy in Washington, DC.
