## Title: Fishermen block Tissa-Kataragama Road
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 13 May 2018 11:09 am

Fishermen block Tissa-Kataragama Road


	

The Tissamaharama-Kataragama Main Road has been blocked at Kalamatiya, Hungama due to a protest carried out by the area fishermen.

The fishermen are protesting over a fuel subsidy.
