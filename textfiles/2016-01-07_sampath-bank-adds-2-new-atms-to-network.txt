## Title: Sampath Bank adds 2 new ATMs to network
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 7 January 2016 06:30 pm

Sampath Bank adds 2 new ATMs to network


	

Chief Manager Trade Services Chathura Rudesh ceremonially opening the new ATM in AlubomullaChief Manager Branch Banking Deepal De Silva ceremonially opening the new ATM in Bambalapitiya
Sampath Bank opened two off-site ATMs at No: 334/C, Kurusa Junction, Pamunugama, Alubomulla and Bambalapitiya Housing Scheme, Galle Road, Colombo 4.
These two new ATMs are equipped with general ATM features such as cash withdrawals, fund transfers, utility bill payments, Cardless Cash and Mobile Cash facilities, etc., and the bank believes this would add more convenience to its valued customers.
