## Title: Thailand cave rescue: Update
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 8 July 2018 10:43 pm

Thailand cave rescue: Update


	

Four of the boys have been rescued from the Tham Luang caves in Chiang Rai province, with nine people still trapped underground, the Guardian reported.

All four boys safely reached a hospital in Chiang Rai, the nearest major city.

Rescue mission underway.
