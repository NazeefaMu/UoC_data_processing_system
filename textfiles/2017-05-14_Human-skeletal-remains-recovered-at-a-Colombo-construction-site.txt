## Title: Human skeletal remains recovered at a Colombo construction site
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 14 May 2017 08:27 am

Human skeletal remains recovered at a Colombo construction site


	

Skeletal remains suspected to be human were recovered from the construction site of the Shangri-La Hotel in Colombo yesterday.

Police said the bones were recovered during the excavation carried out at the site.
