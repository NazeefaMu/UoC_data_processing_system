## Title: Two killed as motor car falls into Kothmale Oya
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 February 2018 06:20 pm

Two killed as motor car falls into Kothmale Oya


	

Two people were killed when a car travelling from Nuwara-Eliya to Gampaha toppled into the Agra Oya, a tributary of Kothmale Oya this evening, Thalawakele Police said.
 
Police said the vehicle which came in at an excessive speed had run off the road at the Hatton - Nuwara- Eliya main road and toppled some 200m down the Agra Oya, around 3pm.

Police had identified the deceased woman as 24-year-old Halawathage Piyumishan Prasadi Perera, a resident from Gampaha. The body of the man is yet to be identified.
 
Police said the two bodies were sent to the Lindula Hospital. (Ranjith Rajapaksa, Shantha Chandrasiri)
