## Title: Pan Asia Bank and LOLC Insurance to offer bancassurance
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 7 December 2015 02:50 am

Pan Asia Bank and LOLC Insurance to offer bancassurance


	

From: L-R: Dinesh Rosa – Senior Bancassurance Manager, Nadika Opatha – Head of Coporate Sales Life Insurance, Nilanga Wickremasinghe-COO LOLC Life Assurance Ltd, Kalsha Amarasinghe  - Director LOLC Life Assurance Ltd/ Executive Director LOLC group, Kapila Jayawardena-Chairman LOLC GIL/ Group MD LOLC, Dimantha Seneviratne-Director/CEO, Kamoor Sourjah - COO, Naleen Edirisinghe-DGM Retail & SME Banking, Takura Abhayasinghe- Head of Business Banking and Branch Sales and Dilshan Seneviratne – Manager Prime Banking and Branch Sales
Pan Asia Bank recently signed an MOU with LOLC Insurance Co. to offer its customers the option to fulfill their insurance requirements at Pan Asia Bank Branches. As part of this strategic tie-up, insuranceofficers from LOLC Life and General Insurance companies will work closely with selected set of Pan Asia Bank Branches to cater to insurance needs of Pan Asia Bank customers. 
Director/ Chief Executive Officer of Pan Asia Bank Dimantha Seneviratne stated that, “Pan Asia Bank always strives to give customers, the best of convenience and value for money.  As we celebrate 20 years of banking this year, we are happy to join LOLC Life and enhance the portfolio of services available to customers through our branch network.  They will have the convenience of attending to their banking and insurance needs, under one roof, thereby making their visit to Pan Asia Bank more convenient and rewarding,” he commented.  The signing of MOU with regard to this operation took place recently at Pan Asia Bank head office, with the participation of top officials from both companies.
