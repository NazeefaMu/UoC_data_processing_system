## Title: Two youth killed in motorcycle accident
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 December 2016 10:54 am

Two youth killed in motorcycle accident


	

Two youth were killed when the motorcycle they were riding on veered off the road and crashed onto a parapet at Horawala in Welipenna last night, Police said.

They were pronounced dead on admission to the Nagoda Hospital.

They said the victims aged 21 and 25 were identified as residents of Horawala, Ellamulla. (Bandu Thambawita)
