## Title: Doctor stabbed at private hospital in Colombo
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 December 2016 05:55 pm

Doctor stabbed at private hospital in Colombo


	

A specialist doctor attached to a private hospital in Colombo is reported to have been stabbed  this evening and had undergone a surgery, Police said.


 They said the suspect was arrested by the police at the hospital premises.
