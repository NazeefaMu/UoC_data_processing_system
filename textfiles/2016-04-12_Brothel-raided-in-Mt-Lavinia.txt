## Title: Brothel raided in Mt. Lavinia
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 12 April 2016 07:20 am

Brothel raided in Mt. Lavinia


	

Four women, including the manager of a brothel which had been operating as an Ayurvedic medical centre at Mount Lavinia in Ratmalana were arrested by the Mount Lavania Police last evening, Police said.
The women, aged between 18 to 36 were from Mount Lavinia, Godampola, Aluthgama, Bandarawela, and Uragasmanhandiya and they would be produced before the Mount Lavinia Magistrate today, Police said.
The Mount Lavinia Police are conducting further investigations. (Chaturanga Pradeep)
