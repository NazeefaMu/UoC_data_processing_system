## Title: SWRD Bandaranaike remembered
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 8 January 2017 12:04 pm

SWRD Bandaranaike remembered


	



The 118th birth anniversary of late Prime Minister S.W.R.D. Bandaranaike was commemorated at his statue near the Galle Face Green this morning. President Maithripala Sirisena, Former President Chandrika Bandaranaike, Ministers Duminda Dissanayake, Mahinda Amaraweera, S.B Dissanayake and Nimal Siripala de Silva were also participated.
