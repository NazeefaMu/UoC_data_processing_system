## Title: Accident at D. R. Wijewardene Mawatha
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 30 January 2015 01:12 pm

Accident at D. R. Wijewardene Mawatha


	


A jeep collided with a private bus at D. R. Wijewardene Mawatha in Colombo 10 today. The vehicles were severely damaged but no casualties were reported. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
