## Title: MS congratulates Macron
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 8 May 2017 01:55 pm

MS congratulates Macron


	

Congratulating French president-elect Emmanuel Macron in a twitter message, President Maithripala Sirisena said the victory would provide for a great platform for relations between the two nations to prosper.

“Congratulations to @EmmanuelMacron on your remarkable victory. It provides a great platform for relations between our nations to prosper”, President twitted.
