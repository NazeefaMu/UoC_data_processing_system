## Title: Dappula De Livera appointed acting AG
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 November 2017 02:52 pm

Dappula De Livera appointed acting AG


	

Additional Solicitor General Dappula De Livera has been appointed as the Acting Attorney General, sources said.

The appointment was made as Attorney General Jayantha Jayasuriya is on a foreign tour.
