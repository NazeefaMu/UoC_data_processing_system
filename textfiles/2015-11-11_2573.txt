## Title: Video: Two brothers killed in accident
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 November 2015 05:01 am

Video: Two brothers killed in accident


	

Two youth in the same family died when their motorcycle collided with a tipper truck on the Mattala Road in Hambantota this morning, Police said.
Police said the 24-year-old, a resident of Andarawewa, Hambantota was driving his 16-year-old brother to a private class when the accident occurred.
Driver of the tipper truck has been arrested. (Sanath Gamage)
