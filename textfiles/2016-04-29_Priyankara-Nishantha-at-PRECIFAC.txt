## Title: Priyankara, Nishantha at PRECIFAC
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 29 April 2016 11:48 am

Priyankara, Nishantha at PRECIFAC


	

Former Civil Aviation Minister Priyankara Jayaratne and SriLankan airlines' former chairman Nishantha Wickramasinghe arrived at the PRECIFAC today for a public hearing over irregularities in connection with the SriLankan airlines catering service.
