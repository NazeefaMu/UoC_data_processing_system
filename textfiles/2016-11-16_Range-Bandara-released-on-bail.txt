## Title: Range Bandara released on bail
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 November 2016 12:42 pm

Range Bandara released on bail


	

State Minister Palitha Range Bandara was released on bail by the Colombo High Court today over allegedly obtaining Rs.5.4 million by dishonestly inducing a person to deliver a vehicle registration permit.

After the indictments were served over the allegations, the accused was released on a cash bail of Rs. 25,000 with two sureties of Rs. 500,000 each by the Judge Sarojini Kusala Weerawardena. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
