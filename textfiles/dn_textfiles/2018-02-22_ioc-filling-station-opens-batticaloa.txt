## Title: IOC filling station opens in Batticaloa
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, February 22, 2018 - 01:00

IOC filling station opens in Batticaloa

The Thilaka Filling Station in Kalmunai was opened at Batticaloa Road, Kalmunai recently.
The opening was presided over by Thilaka Filling Station Proprietor V. Alagurajah and was graced by Lanka IOC Managing Director Shyam Bohra along with Lanka IOC officers and prominent people of the area.
The chief guest was Lanka IOC Managing Director Shyam Bohra, who inaugurated the sale at the IOC station.
