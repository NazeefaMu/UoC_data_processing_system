## Title: AG gearing for Presidential Commission bond probe
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, January 27, 2017 - 01:00

AG gearing for Presidential Commission bond probe

Attorney General Jayantha Jayasuriya, PC said he is in the process of preparing the legal framework for the Presidential Commission to be appointed by President Maithripala Sirisena to investigate the Central Bank’s controversial Treasury Bond issue.
He said the COPE report on the bond issue has also been referred to his Department by Speaker Karu Jayasuriya. The COPE report had also been forwarded to the Attorney General’s Department by the Prime Minister’s office.
A six member panel of Attorneys were appointed to study the report and the observations and advice made by it have been sent to the Prime Minister's Office.
