## Title: Ishan Bandara qualifies to semi finals at Commonwealth games
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, April 11, 2018 - 11:09

Ishan Bandara qualifies to semi finals at Commonwealth games

 
Sri Lakan Boxer Ishan Bandara qualified to the men's 52 kg wight class boxing semi finals at the 21st Commonwealth games held in Australia a short while ago.
He was selected to semifinals after defeating his Lesotho opponent.
