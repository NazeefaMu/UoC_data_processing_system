## Title: Hard ball cricket into School Games next year - Education Minister
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, October 4, 2017 - 01:00

Hard ball cricket into School Games next year - Education Minister

The opening ceremony of the 33rd All Island School Games 2017 organised by Health, Physical Training and Sports Branch of the Education Ministry was held on Monday at Royal Sports Complex, Colombo with the participation of Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe, Education Minister Akila Viraj Kariyawasam and Education State Minister V. Radhakrishnan.
Western Province will host the School Games this year and the events will be worked off at 48 venues in Colombo. The closing ceremony will be held at the Mahinda Rajapaksa Stadium, Diyagama under the patronage of President Maithripala Sirisena on October 15, at 2.45 pm.
The opening ceremony saw around 9,000 students participating at colorful drill display events. Peterite Akalanka Peiris, Asian gold medalist swimmer and B.M. Hasini Prabodha of Holy Family Convent who won the gold medal in Triple Jump event at Athletic Sports Games, Turkistan lit the Olympic lamp.
Eduction Minister Akila Viraj Kariyawasam said, ‘We have taken many decisions to uplift school sports and decided to attach 3,850 sports teachers to schools. We have planned to include the hard ball cricket to School Games next year and hope it will strengthen national level cricket in years to come'.
