## Title: CBSL grants Peoples Merchant Finance  extension for merger
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Wednesday, May 9, 2018 - 01:00

CBSL grants Peoples Merchant Finance  extension for merger

The Monetary Board of Central Bank has granted Peoples Merchant Finance a 6 month extension until September 30, to effect the merger with Peoples Leasing & Finance.
