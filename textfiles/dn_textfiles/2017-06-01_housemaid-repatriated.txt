## Title: Housemaid repatriated
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 1, 2017 - 01:00

Housemaid repatriated

A woman who was allegedly kept by force in a house in Saudi Arabia to harvest her kidney, returned to Sri Lanka yesterday morning.
Following the reports that the 31-year-old victim who was a housemaid in Saudi Arabia, was held by force in order to harvest her kidney.Foreign Employment Minister Thalatha Athukolara had taken immediate measures to bring the woman back to Sri Lanka.
