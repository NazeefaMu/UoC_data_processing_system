## Title: China women win Asia under 20 sevens
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, August 9, 2017 - 01:00

China women win Asia under 20 sevens

China women s team won the Asia Rugby under 20 7s competition, which took place in Hong Kong along with the men’s 7’s. The Chinese women swept aside the opponents. in this round robin tournament.
Thailand finished second after beating hosts, Hong Kong 36-12 in their last match .
