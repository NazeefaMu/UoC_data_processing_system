## Title: Premier League Tier ‘A’ Cricket: Badureliya lead NCC  on first innings
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, January 12, 2017 - 01:00

Premier League Tier ‘A’ Cricket: Badureliya lead NCC  on first innings

Badureliya SC took a first innings lead against NCC on the opening day of their Premier League Tier ‘A’ Inter- Club cricket match played at Maggona yesterday.
NCC was bowled out 185 by Duvindu Tillakaratne who took 6 wickets for 78 runs. Badureliya SC were 187 for 3 wickets at the close.
Scores:
NCC: 185 in 44.4 overs (Pawan Wickramasinghe 42, Thimira Jayasinghe 41, Angelo Perera 32, Tharindu Kaushal 21, Duvindu Tillakaratne 6/78, Asanka Silva 3/41)
Badureliya CC: 187/3 at close in 43 overs (Shehan Fernando 85, Nadeera Nawela 48 n.o., Sahan Wijeratne 24 n.o., Imal Liyanage 27, Chaturanga de Silva 2/69) - YK
