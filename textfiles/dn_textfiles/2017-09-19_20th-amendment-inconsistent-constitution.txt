## Title: 20th Amendment inconsistent with the Constitution
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, September 19, 2017 - 13:45

20th Amendment inconsistent with the Constitution

 
Speaker Karu Jayasuriya said that some clauses of the 20th Amendment are inconsistent with the Constitution and would require the  2/3 majority in Parliament and a referendum.
Speaker Karu Jayasuriya made the above statement in accordance to a Supreme Court ruling.
The 20th Amendment to the Constitution, aimed at holding elections for all Provincial Councils on the same date, was scheduled to be taken up for debate in Parliament tomorrow.
