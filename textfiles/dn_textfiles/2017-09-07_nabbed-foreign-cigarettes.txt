## Title: Nabbed with foreign cigarettes
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 01:00

Nabbed with foreign cigarettes

Police have taken into custody a suspect with foreign cigarettes.
According to the Police, the arrest was made at 25th Mile Post on the Mulatiyana -Kotapola main road. Twenty one foreign-made cigarettes were seized at the time of the detection from the possession of the suspect. He is a resident of Wilpita in Akuressa.
Action will be taken to prosecute him in the Deiyandara Magistrate’s Court, Police said.
A team of police officers led by sub inspector Anura Mulapita attached to the Mawarala Police Station conducted the raid on a tip-off.
