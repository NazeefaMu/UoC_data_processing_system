## Date: Friday, July 28, 2017 - 01:00

Voice for the voiceless

A silent protest was held in front of Colombo Hyde Park on July 24 against animal cruelty towards Sri Lanka’s street dogs and cats. The event was organised by the Alliance For The Protection of Community Dogs, Sri Lanka. Pictures by Shan Rambukwella
