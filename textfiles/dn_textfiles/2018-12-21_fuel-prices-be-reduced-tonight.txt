## Title: Fuel prices to be reduced from tonight
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 21, 2018 - 11:22

Fuel prices to be reduced from tonight

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe stated that a decision has been taken to cut down the fuel prices with effect from midnight today. He announced this addressing today’s parliament session.Accordingly, the price of Lanka Auto Diesel will be reduced to Rs 101 and Super Diesel price will be cut down to Rs 121 and the prices of Octane 92 Petrol will be slashed to Rs 125, while Petrol 95 Octane is to be cut down to Rs 149.
