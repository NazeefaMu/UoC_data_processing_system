## Title: Decapitated body found
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 1, 2018 - 01:00

Decapitated body found

A decapitated body of a middle-aged man was discovered on Tuesday, around 7.30 pm at Pinwatte, Panadura. The body was handed over to the Panadura Base Hospital.
Police believe the deceased was a drug addict.
