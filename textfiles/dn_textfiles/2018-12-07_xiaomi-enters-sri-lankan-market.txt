## Title: Xiaomi enters  Sri Lankan market
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Friday, December 7, 2018 - 01:00

Xiaomi enters  Sri Lankan market

Global technology leader Xiaomi announced its official entry into the Sri Lankan market to strengthen its presence and extend more local support to the market.
The company also announced the launch of Redmi Note 6 Pro, the company’s first quad-camera smartphone in Sri Lanka. It also announced portfolio of its bestsellers in Sri Lanka - POCOPHONE F1, Mi A2, Mi A2 Lite, Redmi S2, Redmi 6 and Redmi 6A along with it.
Redmi Note 6 Pro features a two-day battery that Redmi smartphones are known for, and for the first time brings an AI-powered quad-camera experience to the segment. POCOPHONE F1 features the flagship Qualcomm Snapdragon 845 platform along with the industry-leading LiquidCool Technology cooling system and a long-lasting 4000mAh battery.
Sanket Agarwal, Head - Overseas Expansion, Indian subcontinent, XiaomiIndia said, “At Xiaomi, we always strive to push our boundaries and make higher level innovation accessible to all our users at honest pricing. We are proud to be bringing a host of new smartphones to the market which establishes our true philosophy. We are excited to bring Xiaomi officially into Sri Lanka for millions of our Mi Fans here. Over the last few years, the Sri Lankan smartphone market has witnessed tremendous growth and we look forward to contribute to the local industry.”
Redmi Note 6 Pro features a contemporary arc design and sports the capable Qualcomm® Snapdragon™ 636 octa-core processor. It brings to the table an AI-powered quad-camera setup and a 4000mAh two-day battery with support for Qualcomm Quick Charge 3.0. With high-speed LPDDR4X RAM in tandem, Redmi Note 6 Pro makes for quite a capable setup.
Redmi Note 6 Pro comes with a 6.26” FHD+ IPS display with a 19:9 aspect ratio which is reinforced with Corning® Gorilla® Glass to protect the display against wear and tear. Together with the 500-nit maximum brightness and 1500:1 contrast ratio, it delivers stunning visuals, whether you’re watching videos or playing games.
Redmi Note 6 Pro comes with MIUI 10 out of the box based on Android Oreo. MIUI 10’s multitude of optimizations mean that Redmi Note 6 Pro offers a bump in battery life, as well as features including Wi-Fi passthrough (essentially making your phone a Wi-Fi repeater) and a completely revamped recents menu that makes best use of available screen space.
POCOPHONE F1 is a dream come true for power users, who can enjoy gaming at high frame rates and use intensive applications without worrying about slowdowns resulting from overheating.
POCOPHONE F1 churns out gorgeous images with its AI dual camera consisting of a 12MP On the front, POCOPHONE F1 deploys a 20MP sensor featuring Super Pixel technology that combines the information of four pixels into one large 1.8µm pixel for clearer images with less noise. Both the rear and front cameras are supported by AI features for photos, such as AI Portrait, AI Beautify, and AI Scene Detection.
Redmi S2 comes in Black, Gold and Blue in the 3GB + 32GB variant, and in Black in the 4GB + 64GB variant.
