## Title: Orchid House – overall champions of Zahra Vidyalaya, Addalaichenai
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, April 1, 2017 - 01:00

Orchid House – overall champions of Zahra Vidyalaya, Addalaichenai

Orchid House emerged overall champions in the annual Inter-House Sports Festival - 2017 of Addalaichenai Zahra Vidyalaya under the organization and directions of its Principal M.S.M.Fairoos which took place at school play grounds recently.
Eastern Province Health, Indigenous Medicine, Social Services, Probation Child Care and Rural Electrification Minister A.L.M.Nazeer was the chief guest at the sports festival.
Eastern Provincial Councilor and Opposition Leader M.S.Udumalebbe, Eastern Provincial Councilor A.T.Thavam, Akkaraipattu Zonal Education Director A.L.M.Cassim and Addalaichenai Divisional Education Officer M.A.C.Gassaly were present as guests at prize giving ceremony and distribution prizes and certificates to the winners.
The Principal M.S.M.Fairoos received the Guests and delivered the welcome address. Principals, Teachers, Parents, Past Pupils and well – wishers all participated on this occasion.
The Judges selected each House, in accordance with the performances and how best the March pasts were done. At the end of the sports competitions, which attracted hundreds of viewers.
All of them were anxiously awaiting the final result of the inter house sports meet 2017. It was first (1st) place by Orchid House, 2nd place was won by Lotus House, 3rd place was won by Jesmin House.
