## Title: Indian Air Force Commander calls on President
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, December 13, 2017 - 01:00

Indian Air Force Commander calls on President

Indian Air Force Commander Air Chief Marshal Birender Singh Dhanoa called on President Maithripala Sirisena at the President’s Official residence yesterday. Air Force Commander Air Marshal Kapila Jayampathy and Indian High Commissioner in Colombo Shri Taranjit Singh Sandhu were present. Picture by Sudath Silva
