## Title: Best students in A/L meet President
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 10, 2018 - 01:00

Best students in A/L meet President


The students of Matara Sujatha Vidyalaya and Mahinda Rajapaksa Vidyalaya who ranked best in the island at the General Certificate of Education (A/L)-2017 met President Maithripala Sirisena yesterday at the President’s Official Residence.
The President appreciated the students and extended his warm congratulations with blessing to the students for the success of their higher studies.
The students of Mathara Sujatha Vidyalaya, first in the Bioscience stream, Dilini Sandunika Palihakkara, first in Commerce stream, S.A Dulani Rasanthika, forth in Arts stream, Uchini Shihara Suriyarachchi and the students of Mahinda Rajapaksa Vidyalaya, Paarami Prasadini Ransirini Hettiaarachchi, first in Engineering Technology stream and B.A. Hasith Punsara, first in Biosystem Technology, were present. President Sirisena presented them with gifts.
The President appreciated the service of principals and teachers.
Minister Lakshman Yapa Abeywardena, the students’ parents and others were present.
