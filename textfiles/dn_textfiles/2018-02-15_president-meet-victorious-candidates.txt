## Title: President to meet victorious candidates
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Thursday, February 15, 2018 - 01:00

President to meet victorious candidates

President Maithripala Sirisena has summoned all candidates who emerged victorious under his leadership at the recently concluded Local Government Elections for a special meeting at Fort Presidential Residence today.The candidates who won the elections representing SLFP, UPFA, CWC, National Congress and EPDP will take part in the meeting.
