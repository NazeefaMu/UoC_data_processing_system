## Title: Wariyapola Sri Sumangala have it easy
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, May 3, 2017 - 01:00

Wariyapola Sri Sumangala have it easy

Wariyapola Sri Sumangala (St. Paul’s) College Kandy scored a fine win over St.Thomas’ College Matale by 35 points ( five goals) to 7(one goal) in their inte school shiled game at the Bogambara Stadium. On Tuesday evening , at half time winners led 14-00.
Referee - A Muthumala.
