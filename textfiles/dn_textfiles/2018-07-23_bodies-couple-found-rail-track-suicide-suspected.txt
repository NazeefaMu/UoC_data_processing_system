## Title: Bodies of couple found on rail track, suicide suspected
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, July 23, 2018 - 01:00

Bodies of couple found on rail track, suicide suspected

Unidentified bodies of a young man and woman were found on the Panadura Pinwatte rail track on Saturday by the public who informed the Pinwatte Police Post.
Policemen were not available at the post since they had gone on special duty. Therefore, they informed the incident to the Panadura South Police.
The bodies were lying at the Panadura Base Hospital Mortuary. Police believe that the victims were residents of the Pinwatte, Narampitiya area. Investigations are continuing.
