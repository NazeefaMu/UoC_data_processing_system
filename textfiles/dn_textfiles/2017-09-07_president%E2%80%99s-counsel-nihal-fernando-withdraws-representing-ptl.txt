## Title: President’s Counsel Nihal Fernando withdraws from representing PTL
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 11:30

President’s Counsel Nihal Fernando withdraws from representing PTL

 
President’s Counsel Nihal Fernando informed the Bond Commission that he will withdraw from appearing on behalf of Perpetual Treasuries Limited (PTL).
He said the would withdraw from representing PTL after PTL CEO Kasun Palisena has concluded giving evidence before the Commission.
