## Title: Fined for selling beer sans permit
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Friday, July 21, 2017 - 01:00

Fined for selling beer sans permit

A suspect who pleaded guilty to selling beer without a permit was imposed a fine of Rs. 20,000 by Matara Chief Magistrate and Additional District Magistrate Uresha de Silva. Excise officials made this detection during a raid conducted in the Weligama area.
Beer sold without a permit was taken into custody from the possession of the accused.
It was produced in Court as case productions by excise officials.
Officials led by Senior Excise Inspector Janaka Silva, OIC Excise Station, Matara carried out the raid and prosecuted in the Matara Magistrate’s Court.
