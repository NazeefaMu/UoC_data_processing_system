## Title: Dhamma sermon at Vajiraramaya
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, December 4, 2017 - 01:02

Dhamma sermon at Vajiraramaya

The Daham Sabha Mandapa of the series of Sadaham Yathra Dhamma sermons was held yesterday (Full Moon Poya Day) at Sri Vajiraramaya temple in Bambalapitiya, with the participation of President Maithripala Sirisena. The Dhamma sermon was delivered by Ven. Meegoda Sukitha Thera. Several Ministers and Parliamentarians were among the large gathering of devotees who attended the event. Picture by Kapila Ariyawansha - President’s Media Unit).
