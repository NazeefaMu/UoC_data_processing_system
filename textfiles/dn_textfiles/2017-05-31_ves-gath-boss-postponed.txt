## Title: Ves Gath Boss postponed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Wednesday, May 31, 2017 - 01:00

Ves Gath Boss postponed

The Sinhala adaptation of Carlo Goldoni’s Servant of Two Masters titled as Ves Gath Boss which was scheduled to be staged on June 3 and 9 has been postponed due to the inclement weather condition prevailing in the country. The director of the Sinhala production, Sisil Gunasekara said that it is not ethical to be occupied in the entertainment industry, when the nation is facing a crisis. 
Ves Gath Boss was to be staged to mark the 40th anniversary of Gunasekara’s artistic career.
