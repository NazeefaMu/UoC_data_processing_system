## Title: Death toll rises to sixteen at Meethotamulla garbage dump
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, April 15, 2017 - 13:45

Death toll rises to sixteen at Meethotamulla garbage dump

The death toll at Meethotamulla has risen to 16, Army Spokesman Brigadier Roshan Seneviratne said. 
A section of the Meethotamulla garbage dump collapsed yesterday destroying over 100 houses. 
The injured were rushed to the Colombo National Hospital. 
The tri-forces are involved in rescue operations which are continuing.
