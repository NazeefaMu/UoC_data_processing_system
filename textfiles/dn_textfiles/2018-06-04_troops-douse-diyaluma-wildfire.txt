## Title: Troops douse Diyaluma wildfire
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, June 4, 2018 - 01:00

Troops douse Diyaluma wildfire

A fire that broke out near the Diyaluma area in the Haldummulla Divisional Secretariat in the Baddulla District was brought to the notice of the troops of the 5 Gemunu Watch (GW) of the 11 Division under the Security Force Headquarters – Central (SFHQ-Cen) on Friday (1). Troops managed to douse it within two hours. Over 25 troops on the instructions of Major General Rukmal Dias, Commander, Security Forces – Central (SF-Cen) took part in the operation.
