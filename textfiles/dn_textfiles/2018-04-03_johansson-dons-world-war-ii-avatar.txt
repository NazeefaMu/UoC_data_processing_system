## Title: Johansson dons World War II avatar
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Tuesday, April 3, 2018 - 01:00

Johansson dons World War II avatar

Scarlett Johansson will be seen in a period film set in the backdrop of World War II. The film titled ‘Jojo Rabbit’ will be produced by Taika Waititi of ‘Thor: Ragnarok’ fame. According to reports, Johansson is in final negotiations for the project set in the Nazi-era Germany. The film is going to be a satirical, based on the struggles faced by a 10-year-old boy in an increasingly fascist Germany. Johansson will essay the role of the kid’s mother.
According to reports, the flick is written by Waititi who is producing it as well alongside Carthew Neal and Chelsea Winstanley. The filming is expected to begin in the spring. ANI
