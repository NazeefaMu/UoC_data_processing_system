## Title: PM unveils Technology  Operation Centre for LSE
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, December 6, 2016 - 10:00

PM unveils Technology  Operation Centre for LSE

 
Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe unveiled the technology operation center for the London stock exchange group in Sri Lanka, yesterday (5).
The Premier said this center would provide opportunities to young talent pool of innovators and entrepreneurs of the country. He said it would also help the proposed reforms in the 2017 Budget.
‘It is my firm belief that its opening in Sri Lanka will provide the countries in the region as well as our own unimaginable opportunities in the future’ the Prime Minister said in a statement.
This London Stock Exchange is a world classed facility and financial brand.
