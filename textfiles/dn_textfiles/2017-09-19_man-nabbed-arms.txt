## Title: Man nabbed with arms
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, September 19, 2017 - 01:00

Man nabbed with arms

A 57-year-old man was arrested with a two-bore gun, a muzzle loader and ammunitions at Godmuna, Pitigala by Pitigala police, galle.
Police carried out the raid at the suspect's house on a tip-off.
Investigations are continuing.
