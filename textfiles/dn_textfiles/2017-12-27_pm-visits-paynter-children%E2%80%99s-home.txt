## Title: PM visits Paynter Children’s Home
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, December 27, 2017 - 01:00

PM visits Paynter Children’s Home

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe visited Paynter Children’s Home in Nuwara Eliya on Christmas Day (25) and offered numerous gifts to its children. The Prime Minister was presented with a portrait of him drawn by a child at the home. Dr. Maithree Wickrmesinghe, wife of the Prime Minister, was also present.
