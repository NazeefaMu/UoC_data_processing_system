## Title: President inaugurated the Sri Sambhodi Vesak Zone and unveiled a Buddha statue
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, May 12, 2017 - 01:00

President inaugurated the Sri Sambhodi Vesak Zone and unveiled a Buddha statue
