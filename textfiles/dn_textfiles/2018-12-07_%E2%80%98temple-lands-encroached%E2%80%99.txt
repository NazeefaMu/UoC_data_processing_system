## Title: ‘Temple lands encroached’
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 7, 2018 - 01:00

‘Temple lands encroached’

Kirinda Raja Maha Vihara Chief Incumbent Ven. Yatagala Saddamangala Thera said that a large extent of land belonging to the temple had been encroached recently.
Tissamaharama Divisional Secretary Kalinga Priyawansa said that the relevant departments had been informed to identify the extent of land belonging to the Kirinda Raja Maha Vihara soon.
