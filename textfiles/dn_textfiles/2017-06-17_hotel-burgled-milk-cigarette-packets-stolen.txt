## Title: Hotel burgled: milk, cigarette packets stolen
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, June 17, 2017 - 01:00

Hotel burgled: milk, cigarette packets stolen

A stock of powdered milk and cigarettes has been stolen at a hotel at the Sri Lanka Transport Board (SLTB) central bus terminal of Mahiyanganaya.
Burglars broke into the hotel at the bus terminal on Wednesday night and stole milk and cigarette packets. Mahiyanganaya Police are investigating.
