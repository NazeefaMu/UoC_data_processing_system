## Title: Residents rescue abducted teenaged girl from captors
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, September 29, 2017 - 01:00

Residents rescue abducted teenaged girl from captors

A teenage girl abducted in Maduruwita, Gampaha by three persons including her former lover and held inside a room in Kotugodawatta, Seeduwa was freed from captivity within a matter of half an hour by some neighbours who had chased after the abductors, Police said.
The three persons involved were later arrested following a complaint made to Gampaha Police.
The former lover who was injured during the scuffle to free the girl is warded at the Gampaha hospital under police guard.
The three suspects were due to be produced before the Gampaha Magistrate yesterday.
