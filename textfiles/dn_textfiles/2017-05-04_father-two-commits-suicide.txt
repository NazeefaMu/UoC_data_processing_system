## Title: Father of two commits suicide
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 4, 2017 - 01:00

Father of two commits suicide

A 74-year-old father of two committed suicide by drinking poison at the burial ground of the Kapuwatta area.
His body was taken to the Dambulla Base Hospital for the postmortem examination, on Tuesday. According to the police, the cause of death was not revealed yet.
The deceased was identified as A.M. Kiribanda Abeykoon, a resident in the same area.
Dambulla Police are investigating.
