## Title: China’s top political advisor on tour in Pakistan, Sri Lanka
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 6, 2017 - 01:00

China’s top political advisor on tour in Pakistan, Sri Lanka

China’s top political advisor Yu Zhengsheng will pay an official visit to Pakistan and Sri Lanka, Xinhua news agency reported yesterday.
Yu is the Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People’s Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC). He is set to visit the two countries at the invitation of Pakistani Senate Chairman Mian Raza Rabbani and Speaker Karu Jayasuriya,
