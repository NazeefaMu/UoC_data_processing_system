## Title: Book launch
## Supertitle: 
## Section: T&C
## Date: Wednesday, October 3, 2018 - 01:00

Book launch

Navodi Anupama’s latest collection of short stories and poetry will be launched at Arakavila Dance and Theatre Foundation on October 7 at 3 pm under the patronage of Anoja Weerasinghe and Thushari Welagedara. The collection contains the short stories and poetry written by Anupama following the exposure to world literature. The launch coincides with the 10th anniversary of Arakavila Dance and Theatre Foundation and World Children’s Day.
