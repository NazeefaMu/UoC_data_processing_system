## Title: Tamil Journalists’ Union AGM held
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 10, 2018 - 01:00

Tamil Journalists’ Union AGM held

Sri Lanka Tamil Journalists' Union Annual General Meeting was held at Colombo Tamil Shang-am auditorium under last year President Barathi on January 7. Senior journalist Ananth Palakidnar was elected as the new President, Jeeva SathaSivam as new Secretary and Nimal Raj as Treasurer.
The newly elected committee approved the AGM reports and resolutions. Picture shows the newly elected office bearers and committee members.
