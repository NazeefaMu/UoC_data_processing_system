## Title: President to address SLFP rally in Anuradhapura today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Wednesday, January 10, 2018 - 01:09

President to address SLFP rally in Anuradhapura today

The maiden political Local government election rally will be held at 2 pm at the Anuradhapura Salgado Ground with President Maithripala Sirisena in the chair. Cabinet Ministers and SLFP Parliamentarians will participate.
The President will pay a visit to Atamasthanadhipathi Ven.Pallegama Siri Nivasa Nayaka Thera and the Maha Sanga at the Udamaluwa Temple to blessings and participate in religious rituals including special Bodhi Pooja Pinkamas. The President will open the newly constructed water purification plant at Pandulagama at 12. The rally will follow.
SLFP General Secretary Duminda Dissanayake said around 100,000 persons are expected to be present at the rally. A number of UNP and JVP supporters in Anuradhapura will receive SLFP membership at the meeting.
