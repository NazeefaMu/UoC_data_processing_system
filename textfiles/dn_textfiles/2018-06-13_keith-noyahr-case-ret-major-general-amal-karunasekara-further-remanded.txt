## Title: Keith Noyahr case: Ret. Major General Amal Karunasekara further remanded
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Wednesday, June 13, 2018 - 17:12

Keith Noyahr case: Ret. Major General Amal Karunasekara further remanded

 
The Former Military Intelligence Director and Chief of Staff of the Army, Major General (Retired) Amal Karunasekara who is alleged to have been involved in abducting and assaulting Journalist Keith Noyahr was ordered to be further remanded until June 26.
The Mount Lavinia Magistrate issued the order today.
Keith Noyahr was abducted and assaulted in 2008.
