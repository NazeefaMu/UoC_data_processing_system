## Title: One Day ID Service for OL students
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, November 20, 2017 - 01:00

One Day ID Service for OL students

An expedite programme (one day service) will be launched by the Department of Registration of Persons for candidates who are registered to sit for the GCE Ordinary Level examination this December. Commissioner General, Viyani Gunathilaka said the programme will commence on November 25 at 8.30 am and continue until 4.15pm.
He said It is compulsory for an applicant to be present at the Department personally to submit their applications signed by Principals in their respective schools.
