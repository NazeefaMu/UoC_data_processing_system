## Title: Premier League Inter-Club Cricket: Panadura SC 353; Kalutara TC 28/1
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, December 8, 2016 - 01:00

Premier League Inter-Club Cricket: Panadura SC 353; Kalutara TC 28/1

Kalutara TC scored 28 for one wicket in reply to Panadura SC’s first innings of 353 on the first day of the Premier League Inter-Club Tier B Cricket match played at the Panadura esplanade yesterday. The match continues today.
Panadura SC: 353 in 80.1 overs (Chamara Silva 58, Charitha Buddhika 55 n.o., Sanjaya Chathuranga 50, Mohammed Shilmi 43, Mangala Kumara 4/100, Tharindu Sriwardene 3/64)
Kalutara TC: 28/1 at close in 10 overs. - YK
