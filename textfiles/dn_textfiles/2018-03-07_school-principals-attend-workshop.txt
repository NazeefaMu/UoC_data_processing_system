## Title: School principals attend workshop
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, March 7, 2018 - 01:00

School principals attend workshop

A two-day workshop on capacity building for school principals in the Akkaraipattu educational zone was conducted by the Education Ministry recently at Arugam Bay Tri Star Hotel auditorium, Pottuvil.
This was organized by the Akkaraipattu zonal education office under the supervision of the Education Ministry. About 72 principals in the Akkaraipattu, Addalaichenai and Pottuvil educational divisions attended the workshop. The inaugural session was held with the participation of all principals, Akkaraipattu Zonal Education Director A.M. Rahmathullah, Pottuvil Sub-Zonal Education Director N. Abdul Wahab, Deputy Education Directors A.H. Fous, A.G. Fazmil, M.L.M. Mohideen, Assistant Education Directors M.M. Sithy Fathima, A.M. Nowferdeen, S. Amjathkhan, M. Mathuri and the Akkaraipattu educational zone academic staff.
National Institute of Education Senior Lecturer A. Arumugam, Eastern Province Audit Department Chief Auditor H.M.M. Rasheed, attorney-at-law M. Mujeeb and Deputy Education Director A.G. Fazmil were the resource persons.
Topics included planning and implementation, school administration, finance management, auditing, children’s rights and security.
Akkaraipattu Zonal Education Director A.M. Rahmathullah said that past activities and future plans were discussed in detail.
Divisional education officers, assistant education directors, advisors and subject coordinators participated.
