## Title: Addalaichenai Black Night Sports Club cricket champs
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, April 27, 2018 - 01:00

Addalaichenai Black Night Sports Club cricket champs

Addalaichenai Black Night Sports Club became champions of the limited over’s cricket tournament conducted by Wisdom Youth Club, Addalaichenai at the Beach Grounds, Addalaichenai recently.
Addalaichenai Wisdom Youth Club President W.Abdul Wajith organized the tournament to mark the completion of 20 years of the youth club. Altogether 14 prominent teams from Addalaichenai DS division took part. Addalaichenai Black Night Sports Club beat Oluvil Knight Rider Sports Club in the final.
Addalaichenai Black Night Sports Club won the toss and batted first and scored 53 runs for loss of two wickets in their allocated 5 over’s. In reply Oluvil Knight Rider Sports Club, scored only 25 runs for loss of three wickets in the 5 over’s.
The Addalaichenai Pradeshiya Sabha Vice-Chairman M.S.Jawfer, Addalaichenai Divisional Secretariat Sports Officer A.M.M.Razeen and
Grama Niladhari Aslam Sajah participated as the guests and awarded the trophy to winning team and players.
