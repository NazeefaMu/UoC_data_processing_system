## Title: Doll Exhibhition
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 7, 2018 - 01:00

Doll Exhibhition

A doll exhibition by Pushpa Padmaperuma will be held at the Lionel Wendt Art Centre in Colombo 07 from December 14 to 16.
The profits from the exhibition is in aid of children with special needs. Former First Lady Hema Premadasa will inaugurate the exhibition.
Pushpa Padmaperuma, who has been making handmade quality toys for nearly four decades, is a prominent figure in the entrepreneurial world. She initiated her collection of handmade dolls as a small business which grew to international fame, while her first exhibition was held in 1984.
