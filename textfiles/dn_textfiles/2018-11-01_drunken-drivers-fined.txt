## Title: Drunken drivers fined
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 1, 2018 - 01:00

Drunken drivers fined

Kurunegala Chief Magistrate Sampath Hewawasam fined 10 suspects Rs.15,000 each for drunken driving yesterday. They had pleaded guilty to the charges.
Three-wheeler, bus and car drivers were among the accused, who were arrested on Tuesday night on the Kurunegala – Dambulla main road.
