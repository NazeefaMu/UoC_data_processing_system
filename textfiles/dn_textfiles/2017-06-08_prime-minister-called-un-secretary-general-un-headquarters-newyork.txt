## Title: Prime Minister called on UN Secretary General at the UN headquarters in NewYork
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 8, 2017 - 01:00

Prime Minister called on UN Secretary General at the UN headquarters in NewYork

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe called on UN Secretary General António Guterres at the UN headquarters in NewYork yesterday.The UN Secretary General commended Wickremesinghe for participating in the UN Ocean Conference.Guterrres said both Sri Lanka and Portugal have very close association with the ocean.Prime Minister Ranil wickremesinghe briefed the UN Secretary General on measures being adopted by the government to promote reconciliation among communities.
