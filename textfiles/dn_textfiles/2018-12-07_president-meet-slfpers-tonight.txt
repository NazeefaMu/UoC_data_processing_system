## Title: President to meet SLFPers tonight
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 7, 2018 - 13:37

President to meet SLFPers tonight

The Central Committee of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party(SLFP) is scheduled to meet this evening to discuss the party reforms and to resolve current political crisis.
The meeting  will be chaired by President Maithripala Sirisena and it  is expected to be held at the President’s House from 7.00 p.m onwards.
