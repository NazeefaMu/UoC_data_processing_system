## Title: Drug dealer arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 6, 2018 - 01:00

Drug dealer arrested

A drug dealer was arrested by the Galewela Police with 240 miligrams of heroin last Tuesday.
According to Police Media Unit sources, the 26-year-old suspect is a resident of St.Sebastian Road, Colombo -12.
It was further reported that the suspect has been a heroin dealer for a long time and is also a drug addict. He is reported to be a henchman of a major drug dealer in the Keselwatte area. The arrest was made on information received by the Galewela Police. The suspect was to be produced to be produced before the Galewela Magistrate. Galewela Police are investigating.
