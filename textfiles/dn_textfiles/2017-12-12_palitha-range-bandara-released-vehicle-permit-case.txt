## Title: Palitha Range Bandara released from vehicle permit case
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Tuesday, December 12, 2017 - 15:18

Palitha Range Bandara released from vehicle permit case

 
Skills Development and Vocational Training State Minister Palitha Range Bandara was released from an indictments filed by Attorney General for allegedly obtaining Rs.5.4 million by dishonestly inducing a person to deliver a vehicle registration permit, today.
The Colombo High Court ordered the release of the Minister, following the virtual complainant's decision not to proceed with the case. 
When the case came up before Colombo High Court A.A.R. Heiyanthuduwa, Deputy Solicitor General Dilan Ratnayake appearing for the Attorney General informed Court that virtual complainant Dinesh Ratnayake has taken a decision not to proceed with the case since both parties entered into a settlement.
The virtual complainant has tendered an affidavit citing that he has no intention to proceed with the case.
