## Title: Ragama Devalaya annual 'deva pooja' begins today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, August 19, 2017 - 01:00

Ragama Devalaya annual 'deva pooja' begins today

The 14th annual ‘Deva Pooja’ of the historical Ragama Devalaya will be commenced today at 6 pm.
The pooja is to invoke blessings on President Maithripala Sirisena, Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe, Government and Opposition MPs, Tri-forces, Police and people of the country.
A dansala will be conducted at 7.00pm. Underprivileged children will be donated schoolbooks on the same day.
