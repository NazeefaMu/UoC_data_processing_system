## Title: Olympian Manjula Kumara receives house
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, October 4, 2017 - 01:00

Olympian Manjula Kumara receives house

In recognition of his achievements, Olympian Manjula Kumara was gifted the deed and keys of a house worth Rs 4.2 million by the Urban Settlement Development Authority (USDA) at the World Habitat Day celebration held in Matara recently.
The title deed to the house situated in Moratuwa was handed over by the State Minister of Enterprise Development Lakshman Yapa Abeywardena while the keys were handed over by Minister of Finance and Media Mangala Samaraweera and Minister of Housing Sajith Premadasa.
Manjula Kumara a past student of Vijitha Central College, Dikwella hails from Morawaka and represented Sri Lanka at the 2004 Olympic Games held in Athens.
