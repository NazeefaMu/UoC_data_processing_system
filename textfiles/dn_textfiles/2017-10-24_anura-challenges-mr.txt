## Title: Anura challenges MR
## Supertitle: Debate on Constitution
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 24, 2017 - 01:00

Debate on Constitution
Anura challenges MR

JVP Leader and Chief Opposition Whip Anura Kumara Dissanayake yesterday said that he is prepared to have a debate with former President Mahinda Rajapaksa on the new Constitution.
Dissanayake said that Parliamentarian Wimal Weerawansa had challenged him for a debate on the new Constitution and he is prepared to have a debate with no one else but former President Mahinda Rajapaksa who is the leader of Parliamentarian Wimal Weerawansa. “Our party will accept the challenge made by Parliamentarian Wimal Weerawansa.
The party will field a representative who is suitable to have a debate with Weerawansa,” he said. Speaking at a meeting held in Tangalle on Sunday, Dissanayake added that his party is ready to bring to light the corrupt who are disguised as patriots.
