## Title: President visits ancient temple in Weeraketiya
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, August 27, 2018 - 01:00

President visits ancient temple in Weeraketiya

President Maithripala Sirisena, who visited Weeraketiya Ancient Raja Maha Vihara, engaged in religious rituals on Saturday. The President, on arrival at the temple, met the Chief Incumbent of Ancient Naigala Raja Maha Vihara Ven.Muruthawela Sumana Nayaka Thera and discussed his well-being. A huge crowd gathered around the Vihara on the Nikini full moon poya day and the crowd welcomed the President warmly. The President, who engaged in a friendly conversation with the people, did not forget to discuss about their well-being, too. Picture by President’s Media Division
