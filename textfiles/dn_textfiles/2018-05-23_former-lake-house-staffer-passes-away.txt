## Title: Former Lake House staffer passes away
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 23, 2018 - 01:00

Former Lake House staffer passes away

Former Lake House staffer Joe Fernandopulle passed away yesterday. He was the husband of Monica and beloved father of four, including Aelian (Lake House), Shantha, Lester and Jessica. Cortege leaves residence 55/10, Akurupitiya Road, Kelaniya on Thursday, May 24 at 2.00 pm for the burial at St. Mary’s Church, Peliyagoda.
