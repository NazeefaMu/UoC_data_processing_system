## Title: Colombo Additional Magistrate releases Gnanasara Thera on bail
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 21, 2017 - 15:30

Colombo Additional Magistrate releases Gnanasara Thera on bail

(File Photo)
Bodu Bala Sena (BBS) General Secretary Galagodaatte Gnanasara Thera was released by the Colombo Additional Magistrate a short while ago.
The decision was made after he was arrested and taken before courts by the Police Organised Crimes Division today.
