## Title: Chinese Coronavirus patient at the IDH discharged
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, February 19, 2020 - 11:07

Chinese Coronavirus patient at the IDH discharged

The only COVID-19 positive Chinese tourist, who was diagnosed from Sri Lanka as positive, was discharged from the Infectious Disease Hospital a short while ago.This patient positive for COVID-19 is clinically cured and in good health.She was discharged from the hospital soon after the final medical report was received confirming her ‘COVID-19 free status’.
