## Title: Katuneriya Sebs win by 10 wickets
## Supertitle: Singer U-19 Inter Schools Cricket
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, October 18, 2018 - 01:00

Singer U-19 Inter Schools Cricket
Katuneriya Sebs win by 10 wickets

St. Sebastian’s College Katuneriya won by 10 wickets against Revatha College Balapitiya in their ‘Singer Trophy’ Under 19 inter Schools Cricket tournament match played at Katunerya on yesterday. Other matches were drawn.
