## Title: One-year RI for sex offender
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Monday, October 1, 2018 - 01:00

One-year RI for sex offender

Galgamuwa Magistrate P. Jayasooriya sentenced a 43-year-old suspect who had molested a 13-year-old schoolgirl while travelling in a bus, to one year’s rigorous imprisonment, recently. He was also fined Rs.1,500.
A senior police chief in Kurunegala told the Daily News that 456 cases of sexual abuse were reported from Kurunegala and Puttalam during the past nine months.
