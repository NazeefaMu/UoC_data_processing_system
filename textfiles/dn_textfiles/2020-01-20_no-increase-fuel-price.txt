## Title: No increase in fuel price
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, January 20, 2020 - 16:06

No increase in fuel price

State Energy Minister Rohitha Abeygunawardena today affirmed that there will be no rise in fuel prices whatsoever under any circumstance.Addressing a press briefing at SLPP head office in Battaramulla the Minister further said that since people are undergoing a hard time experiencing a high cost of living, fuel prices will not be increased.
