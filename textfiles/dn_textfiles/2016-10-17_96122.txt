## Title: Nilan guides Panadura Royal to victory
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, October 17, 2016 - 01:00

Nilan guides Panadura Royal to victory

Fourth year coloursman and captain of the side and wicket keeper Nilan Fernando guided Royal College, Panadura to a first innings win over Tissa Central College, Kalutara in their inter-school under 19 cricket match played at Panadura.
Nilan who bats right hand scored an unbeaten chanceless 102 runs in their first outing for the 2016/2017 cricket season. His brilliant century consisted of sixteen hits to the ropes and one over and facing 166 balls and was associated a third wicket partnership of 105 runs with Dilshan Kavinda who contributed 33 runs.
Taking the strike first Panadura Royalists scored 242 runs and in reply visitors were all out fr 217 runs with Dulan Avishka scoring at unbeaten 101 runs and Dilshan Kavinda claimed 5 for 57 and in their second essay Panadura Royalists declared at 217 for 7 and at stumps visitors were 54 for 3. Panadura Royalists are coached by Thilina Fernando.
Chief Scores
