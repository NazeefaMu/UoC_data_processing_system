## Title: Match drawn at Campbel Place
## Supertitle: Singer U-19 Cricket
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, December 11, 2017 - 01:00

Singer U-19 Cricket
Match drawn at Campbel Place

St. Sebastian’s College Moratuwa drew with Nalanda College, Colombo in their ‘Singer Cup’ Under 19 inter Schools Cricket tournament match at Campbel Place yesterday.
* At Campbel place.
St. Sebastian’s: 182 in 64.4 overs (Dulaj Silva 58 n.o., Tashik Perera 43, Gihan Senanayake 22, Chamindu Wijesinghe 5/42, Raveen de Silva 3/51) and 192/9 in 48 overs (Tharusha Fernando 73, Ravindu Madushanka 33 n.o., Sukitha Prsaanna 23, Kaveesh Madhurapperuma 4/51, Nipun Devinda 3/18)
Nalanda: 191 (58/4 o/n) in 68.2 overs (Kaveesh Madhurappaeruam 47 n.o., Mahima Weerakoon 40, Nipun Devinda 20, Gihan Senanayaka 3/62,Praveen Cooray 2/16, Tashik Perera 2/31, Praveen Cooray 2/35). YK
