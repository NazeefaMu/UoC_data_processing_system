## Title: Buttala police raid liquor den
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 28, 2018 - 01:00

Buttala police raid liquor den

Two suspects directly involved in producing illegal liquor was was apprehended by divisional crime eradication unit at Katugahagalge area in the Buttala Police Division yesterday morning.
Police said one million liters of ‘Goda’ and equipment which had been used by the suspects to produce illegal liquor was taken into police custody. It was also found that the suspects had produced the illicit brew on a large scale, to sell the previous day as liquor outlets were closed for Poya.
The suspects said to be residents in Buttala and Okkampitiya were to be produced at the Wellawaya Magistrate’s Courts by Police.
