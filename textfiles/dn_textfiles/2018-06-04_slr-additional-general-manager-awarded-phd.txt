## Title: SLR Additional General Manager awarded PhD
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, June 4, 2018 - 01:00

SLR Additional General Manager awarded PhD

Sri Lanka Railways Department Additional General Manager Wijaya Samarasinghe was awarded a Doctorate (PhD) at a convocation held by the Maha Chulalongkorn Raja Vidyalaya University in Thailand in May, 2018. This is the first time a Sri Lanka Railways officer received a doctorate.
Samarasinghe received his primary education at Ananda College, Colombo. During his school career, he had functioned as captain of the Wijaya House, the Buddhist Association Chief Secretary as well as a Prefect.
He had obtained a Bachelor's Degree in Commerce from the Colombo University as well as a Master's in Arts. He was the Colombo University Buddhist Brotherhood President and also functioned as the Inter-University Buddhist Federation President. He was a Sri Lankan delegate of the University Leadership Camp which was held in Tokyo, Japan.
Samarasinghe received the Vice-Chancellor's Award for the Best Student at the Colombo University.
He was an Assistant Transportation Superintendent, Transportation Superintendent, Commercial Superintendent, Operating Superintendent, Director (Planning) as well as the National Railways Museum Director throughout his service period of 36 years.
In addition, he is the current Railways Literary Sports and Social Institute Deputy President and the Railway Operating and Transport Management Training Institute Director/Chairman.
