## Title: SLEME overall champs
## Supertitle: SLAVF Inter Regiment Athletic Meet
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, February 27, 2018 - 01:00

SLAVF Inter Regiment Athletic Meet
SLEME overall champs

Sri Lanka Electrical and Mechanical Engineers (SLEME) shone brilliantly securing first place with 284 points while Sri Lanka Light Infantry (SLLI) athletes won second place with 211 points on the third and final day of the Sri Lanka Army Volunteer Force (SLAVF) Inter Regiment Athletic Meet 2018 concluded at Diyagama Mahinda Rajapaksa Stadium yesterday.
Recruit L.G Dananjaya of SLEME and Lance Corporal E.M.M Ratnakumari of 5 Sri Lanka Army Women’s Corps (SLAWC) were adjudged the Best Male and the Female Athletes at the meet.
More than 750 athletes, representing all volunteer Regiments in the Army vied against their fellow-athletes showcasing a high standard of professional sportsmanship in 48 track and field events.
Organized with pomp and pageantry to match with international standards, the closing ceremony was graced by Lieutenant General Mahesh Senanayake, Commander of the Army as the Chief Guest at the invitation of the Major General Piyal Wickramaratne, Commandant, SLAVF, the heavyweight behind this meet a huge success.
Instructors at the Panagoda Army Physical Training School (APTS) contributed to the closing ceremony by staging an impressive combined spectacle of physical training of high standard.
