## Title: Dell appoints Roshan Nugawela as Sri Lanka, Maldives Country Head
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, May 4, 2017 - 01:00

Dell appoints Roshan Nugawela as Sri Lanka, Maldives Country Head

Roshan Nugawela has been appointed Country Manager for the recently established Dell EMC organization in Sri Lanka and Maldives.He will be responsible for the overall business of Dell EMC in the two markets.
The appointment comes on the back of the completion of Dell's acquisition of EMC Corporation in September last year, creating Dell Technologies: the world's largest privately-controlled technology company with the industry's most comprehensive and innovative portfolio from the edge to the core to the cloud.
Dell Technologies is a unique family of businesses that provides the essential infrastructure for organizations to build their digital future, transform IT and protect their most important asset, information.Dell EMC, a part of Dell Technologies family of businesses, enables enterprise customers to modernize, automate and transform their data center using industry-leading converged infrastructure, servers, storage and data protection technologies.
It is a top priority for Dell EMC to ensure the continuity of excellent service to customers and partners. By bringing both companies together, Dell EMC has become an even more strategic IT partner that will help customers in the region build their digital future, transform IT and protect their most important asset - information.
