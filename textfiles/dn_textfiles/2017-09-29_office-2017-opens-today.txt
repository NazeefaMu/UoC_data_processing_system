## Title: Office 2017 opens today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Friday, September 29, 2017 - 01:00

Office 2017 opens today

An exclusive exhibition on complete workplace solutions, kicks off from today (29) till October 1, at the Sirimavo Bandaranaike Memorial Exhibition Center, Colombo.
Office 2017 emerges as a hub for innovation and offers dedicated sectors for the best technology to give efficient and 'smart' workplaces.
It showcases the best in IT sector, commercial real estate, office management, furniture, printing solutions, office lighting, telecommunication, storage solutions, IT vendors, training solutions, office stationery, signage boardas, security systems, office automation and many more.
It brings together the most important manufacturers and suppliers from all over Sri Lanka and overseas for a three-day exhibition on complete workplace solutions, all under one roof. The event offers a unique opportunity to interact with the administrative professionals who influence and make purchasing decisions in their workplace.
It has become the most awaited show to witness new product launches and new technologies, where more than 100 exhibitors will participate.
Office 2017 is organized by Lanka Exhibition and Conference Service (Pvt) Ltd, the professional exhibition organizer and the Presidential Award Winner for four consecutive years - 2008, 2009, 2010 and 2011. The organizers have planned several demonstration areas within the exhibition for innovative products and services that will hit the market soon. The event invites all stakeholders to come together under one roof for a mega office-related exhibition. "Innovation and creativity all find home at this show, contributing to the overall improvement and business growth of the company," CEO, Lanka Exhibition and Conference Services (LECS), Aasim Mukthar said.
Alpha Industries (Pvt) Ltd and Metropolitan Office (Pvt.) Ltd join hands with Office 2017 as Gold Sponsors. Capital Media (Pvt) Ltd - Echelon Magazine, Research Intelligence Unit (RIU), IIMS Creations and The Associated Newspapers of Ceylon Ltd. (ANCL) also join hands as Official Sponsors of this premier event.
