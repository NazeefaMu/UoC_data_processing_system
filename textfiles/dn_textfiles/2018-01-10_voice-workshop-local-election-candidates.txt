## Title: Voice workshop for Local Election candidates
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Wednesday, January 10, 2018 - 01:00

Voice workshop for Local Election candidates

A voice workshop with the aim of improving speech ability and communication skills of the local election candidates will be held at the Katahanda Media Centre premises (55, Buwanekaba Mawatha, Mirihana, Nugegoda) on January 13 and 14 from 10 am to 3 pm.
The workshop will provide the candidates with sharp insights on addressing public meetings, personality development, voice control, attractive speech, techniques of language and various other methods of catching the public attention.
The workshop will be moderated by Srimath Indrajith Liyanage who claims experience in electronic media for over three decades.More information could be obtained from contacting Liyanage on 0773749767.
