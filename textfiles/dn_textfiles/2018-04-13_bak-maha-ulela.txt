## Title: Bak Maha Ulela
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, April 13, 2018 - 01:00

Bak Maha Ulela

A Bak Maha Ulela, organised by the Pahala Yagoda Hiru Sports Club for the third consecutive year, will be held on April 15 from 7 am at Sri Swarnapali Vidyalaya grounds.
The main events will include cross country running, Kotta Pora and Avurudu Kumari contest followed by a musical show.
