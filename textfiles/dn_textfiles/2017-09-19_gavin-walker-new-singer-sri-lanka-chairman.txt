## Title: Gavin Walker new Singer Sri Lanka Chairman
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Tuesday, September 19, 2017 - 01:00

Gavin Walker new Singer Sri Lanka Chairman

Singer Sri Lanka announced that Gavin J Walker, Non Executive Director has been appointed as Chairman of the company with effect from September 15, 2017.
Hayleys PLC and its subsidiaries last week announced the purchase of 61.73% of shares of Singer (Sri Lanka) PLC, for a consideration of Rs 10.9 billion, making it the single largest  acquisition for a listed company in Sri Lanka in recent times.
Hayleys PLC and its subsidiaries is anticipated to yield substantial opportunities for new business synergies within the Hayleys Group.
