## Title: Errant hotelier released on bail
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Friday, June 2, 2017 - 01:00

Errant hotelier released on bail

A man who was found guilty of running a hotel without permission and provided food unfit for consumption to visitors, was released on a personal bail of Rs. 50,000 with two sureties by Batticaloa Magistrate M. Ganesharaja on Wednesday.
Batticaloa Municipal Council PHIs raided the hotel during a search operation.
