## Title: Seven Governors sworn in
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Friday, April 13, 2018 - 01:00

Seven Governors sworn in

The Governors appointed to seven provinces were sworn in before President Maithripala Sirisena at the Presidential Secretariat, yesterday.
Hemakumara Nanayakkara (Western Province), K. C. Logeswaran (North Western Province), Niluka Ekanayake (Sabaragamuwa Province), Reginald Cooray (Central Province), Marshal Perera (Southern Province), M. P. Jayasinghe (North Central Province) and P. B. Dissanayake (Uva Province), were thus appointed. President’s Secretary Austin Fernando was also present.
