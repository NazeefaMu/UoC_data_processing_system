## Title: Anusha Kodithuwakku wins bronze at Commonwealth semi finals
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, April 11, 2018 - 08:41

Anusha Kodithuwakku wins bronze at Commonwealth semi finals

 
Sri Lankan Boxer Anusha Kodithuwakku won the bronze medal at the 21st Commonwealth games semi finals held in Asutralia this morning.
She was defeated by her Indian opponent, Mary Kom at the women's 45-48 kg weight class boxing.
