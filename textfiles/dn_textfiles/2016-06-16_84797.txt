## Title: Dilshan guides OTSC to victory
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, June 16, 2016 - 01:00

Dilshan guides OTSC to victory

A fine bowling spell of 6 for 15 by Dilshan Mendis enabled old Thomians Sports Club to beat Nugegoda Sports Club by 113 runs in their inter club division three second round cricket match played at Mt Lavinia and conducted by Colombo District Cricket association.
Batting first Old Thomians scored 153 runs and visitors skittled out for a paltry 40 runs.
Old Thomians SC – 153 in 41.2 overs (Ashen Peiris 23, Fahim Zalim 19, Dilshan Mendis 18, Serian Silva 3/20, Surain Silva 3/38)
Nugegoda SC – 40 in 12.1 overs (Anura Nishantha 19, Dilshan Mendis 6/15, Nilshan Fernando 4/25)
