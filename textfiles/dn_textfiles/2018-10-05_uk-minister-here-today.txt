## Title: UK Minister here today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, October 5, 2018 - 01:00

UK Minister here today

UK Minister for Asia Mark Field will visit Sri Lanka on October 5 and 6.
Field will meet Foreign Affairs Minister Tilak Marapana, Speaker Karu Jayasuriya, Public Administration and Management and Law and Order Minister Ranjith Maddumabandara, Sports and Provincial Councils and Local Government Minister Faiszer Musthapha.
He will meet Leader of the Opposition R. Sampanthan, members of civil society and Commissioners of the Office on Missing Persons.
Minister Field will discuss a range of issues, including the UK’s continued support for Sri Lanka achieving meaningful and lasting reconciliation in the interests of all Sri Lankans.
