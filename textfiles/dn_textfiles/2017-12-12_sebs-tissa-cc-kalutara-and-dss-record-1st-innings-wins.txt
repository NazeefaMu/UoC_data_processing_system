## Title: Sebs, Tissa CC-Kalutara and D.S.S record 1st innings wins
## Supertitle: ‘Singer U-13 Div. I Inter Schools Cricket
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, December 12, 2017 - 01:00

‘Singer U-13 Div. I Inter Schools Cricket
Sebs, Tissa CC-Kalutara and D.S.S record 1st innings wins

St. Sebastian’s College Moratuwa, defending champion Tissa Central College Kalutara and D.S. Senanayake College Colombo registered first inning wins against Dharmasoka College Ambalangoda, Ananda Shashthralaya Kotte and Kalutara Vidyalaya respectively in their second round matches of the ‘Singer Cup’ Under 13 division I inter schools cricket tournament.
Chithru Fernando (St. Sebastian’s) took 6 wickets for 11 runs against Dharmasoka and Asela de Silva (Tissa) took 6 wikets for 59 runs against Ananda Shashthralaya. Gaviru Senahas of D.S. Senanayake scored 102 runs against Kalutara Vidyalaya.
* At Moratuwa. St. Sebastian’s won first inning.
Dharmasoka: 49 in 21.2 overs (Chithru Fernando 6/11, Yenul Hemachandra 3/11) and 144/6 in 38 overs (Senitha Helambage 47, Chanaka Jananga 31, Akash Mendis 26, Kaveesha Mandil 20, Sharan Fernando 2/25, Chithru Fernando 2/25)
St. Sebastian’s: 96 in 34 overs (Dilakshan Fernando 38, Chirana de Silva 4/14, Tharin Sithupa 3/26)
* At Kalutara. Tissa Central won first inning.
Ananda Shashthralaya Kotte: 54 in 21.5 overs (Nirodha Tharushan 19, Sanjeewa Silva 4/02, Dhananjaya de Silva 2/14, Nethmal Fonseka 2/17) and 153/8 decl. in 43.5 overs (Nadeesh Yasiru 38, Anupa Nimasra 32 n.o., Nirodh Tharushan 31, Asela de Silva 6/59)
Tissa Central: 184 in 35.3 overs (Sanjeewa Silva 77 n.o., Asela de Silva 29, Lakindu Kalhara 2/27, Punsara Harisath 2/28)
* At Bandaragama. D.S. Senanayake Colombo won first inning.
D.S. Senanayake: 188 in 43.5 overs (Gaviru Senahas 102, Pasindu Kavishka 4/31, Dinuka Thisara 4/38) and 11/1 in 1 overs
Kalutara Vidyalaya: 108 in 47.4 overs (Dinuka Thisara 36, Bathiya Fernanado 3/21). YK
