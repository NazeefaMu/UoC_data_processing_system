## Title: Gnanasara Thero withdraws FR petition
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 22, 2017 - 13:15

Gnanasara Thero withdraws FR petition

 
The Chief Secretary of the Bodu Bala Sena (BBS) organization Galagodaththe Gnanasara Thero withdrew the Fundamental Rights petition, submitted to the Supreme Court.
The petition was filed through a lawyer on June 13 requesting to prevent Gnanasara Theros’ arrest.
