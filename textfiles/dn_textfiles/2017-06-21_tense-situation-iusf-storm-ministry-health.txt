## Title: Tense situation as IUSF storm Ministry of Health
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 21, 2017 - 17:15

Tense situation as IUSF storm Ministry of Health

 
A group of students, led by the Inter University Students Federation (IUSF) have created a tense situation at the Ministry of Health by storming the ministry premises, removing name boards and burning them.
The Special Task Force of the Police was called in to ease the tension caused by the protesters.
The Inter University Students Federation is protesting the South Asian Institute of Technology and Medicine and is insisting the campus is shut down.
