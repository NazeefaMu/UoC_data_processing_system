## Title: Police officer forces schoolboy to strip
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 16, 2017 - 01:00

Police officer forces schoolboy to strip

A Police officer of Chilaw Police Station had stripped a schoolboy naked removing his sarong in public to search him, during a raid conducted in Maligaawatta area in Chilaw.
The schoolboy has stopped attending school due to the embarrassment he had to face. The stripping-off had been witnessed by friends and neighbouring community of the boy. The victim is supposed to sit for G.C.E. O/L Exam by the end of 2017. His parents had levelled allegations against the policeman for his inappropriate behavior. The Chilaw Police had not recorded the complaint on the incident made by the parents of the student.
