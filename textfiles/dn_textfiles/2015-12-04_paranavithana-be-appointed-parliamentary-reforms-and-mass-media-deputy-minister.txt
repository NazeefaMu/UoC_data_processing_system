## Title: Paranavithana to be appointed Parliamentary Reforms and Mass Media Deputy Minister
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Friday, December 4, 2015 - 01:00

Paranavithana to be appointed Parliamentary Reforms and Mass Media Deputy Minister

Karunarathna Paranavithana is to be appointed as Parliamentary Reforms and Mass Media Deputy Minister. This will be in addition to his current portfolio of Local Government and Provincial Councils Deputy Minister. He is expected to be sworn in today, sources said.
