## Title: Army gun down Police 32-18
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, December 12, 2017 - 01:00

Army gun down Police 32-18

Army Sports Club team earn a hard fought 32 points (4 tries, 3 conversions, 2 penalties) to 18 (3 tries, 1 penalty) win over Police outfit in their Dialog ‘A’ Division inter club league rugby first round match played at Ratmalana yesterday.
The score was dead locked 8 all at half time.
Winners Army scored their tries through Ashan Bandara, Sugath Nanayakkara, Sameera Bulathsinhalage and Sampath Kumara one apiece with the three Conversions and the two penalties were slotted in by full back Gayan Salinda.
While the losing Police scored their tres through Wajid Fawmy- two and Sujan Kodithuwakku-one with the solitary Penalty has been put over by ex peterite captain cum fly half Sandesh Jayawickrama.
