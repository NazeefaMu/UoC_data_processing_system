## Title: Distribution of Coaching Licenses today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, October 10, 2016 - 01:00

Distribution of Coaching Licenses today

The distribution of Coaching Licenses for school cricket coaches in the Southern Province (Galle, Matara and Hambantota) will take place at the Galle International Cricket Stadium today commencing 10 am.
This event will be conducted by Sri Lanka Cricket and the Sri Lanka Schools Cricket Association. More than 75 school cricket coaches will receive their coaching licenses. - YK
