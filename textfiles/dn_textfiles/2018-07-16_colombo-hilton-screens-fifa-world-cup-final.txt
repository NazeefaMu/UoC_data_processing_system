## Title: Colombo Hilton screens FIFA World Cup final
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, July 16, 2018 - 01:00

Colombo Hilton screens FIFA World Cup final

Colombo Hilton has been the hot spot for Lankan soccer fans who gathered in their numbers to watch yesterday’s FIFA World Cup grand finale between France and Croatia on giant screens at the pool side restaurant IL Ponte and the Grand Ballroom. The Daily News captured the FIFA World Cup excitement at the Colombo Hilton through the lens of our staff photographers, Hirantha Gunathilaka and Nirosh Batepola.
