## Title: Jewellery thieves held
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 6, 2018 - 01:00

Jewellery thieves held

Two suspects involved in a robbery of jewellery valued at Rs. 200,000 from a house in the Yabaraluwa area, were arrested by Biyagama Police, who also recovered all the stolen jewellery, on Sunday (3) morning. The suspects were from the Yabaraluwa and Malwana, Walgama areas. They were due to be produced before the Mahara Chief Magistrate. The police team which made this arrest was led by Bigama Police OIC Chief Inspector Asoka Karunaratne and SI Prabath Kumara Yasawardhane.
