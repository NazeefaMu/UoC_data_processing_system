## Title: Woman’s body found in home
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 14, 2018 - 01:00

Woman’s body found in home

Police recently discovered the body of a 68-year-old woman in her home recently.
The deceased, a resident of Kolamedinya in Bandaragama, was identified as Manel Kuruppu.
Horana Acting Magistrate Pushpa Kannangara had ordered police to inspect the house after neighbours had complained that a foul smell was coming from the house. Police had discovered the corpse on a bed.
A postmortem was to be conducted. Investigations are continuing.
