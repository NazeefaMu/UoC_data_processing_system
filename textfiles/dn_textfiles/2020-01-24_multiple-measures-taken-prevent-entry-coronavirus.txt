## Title: Multiple measures taken to prevent entry of Coronavirus
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, January 24, 2020 - 14:18

Multiple measures taken to prevent entry of Coronavirus

Multiple safety measures have been implemented including a Health Desk at the Bandaranaike International Airport against the deadly Coronavirus, Health Minister Pavithra Wanniaarachchi today informed the House.According to the Health Minister all passenger who are arriving at the BIA will be informed about the Health Desk prior to their descent along with instructions to seek the assistance of the medical team positioned at the desk if any passenger is feeling symptoms of the infection.
