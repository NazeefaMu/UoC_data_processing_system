## Title: SDB ‘Lakdaru’ scholarship seminar series at Kaduwela
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, June 28, 2018 - 01:00

SDB ‘Lakdaru’ scholarship seminar series at Kaduwela

SDB bank has organized an island wide Lakdaru Scholarship seminar series for Grade 5 students.
This will be carried out as a CSR project and few of the seminars will be from Tamil medium too. 11th seminar of the series for 2018 was held on May 27, at Bomiriya Central College, Kaduwela. In parallel to the Scholarship Seminar Series, there was separate seminar for parents regarding development of skills and attitudes of children and child philosophy.
Further, parents were instructed regarding their financial administration and banking activities at the same venue.
During the seminars, there was an opportunity to open Lakdaru accounts. Special features of the Lakdaru account include attractive gift schemes, a free hospitalization insurance cover up to Rs.50,000/-, rewards for educational achievements and an additional interest than normal savings.
Entrance for these seminars is free of charge and SDB bank invites the children for these seminars who will b sitting for the Grade 5 scholarship examination.
