## Title: Forensic Audit Reports Submitted to Parliament
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, January 30, 2020 - 17:12

Forensic Audit Reports Submitted to Parliament

The Central Bank has submitted the five complete Forensic Audit report on the CBSL Bond Issue along with annexures and exhibits to Parliament, a release issued by the Parliamentary Communication Department said“It has been submitted on the request in writing by the Secretary General of Parliament to the Governor of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka Prof. W.D Lakshman as per the instruction given by the Speaker of Parliament Karu  Jayasuriya”Accordingly, several Government and Opposition Members of Parliament have expressed in Parliament that the audit reports would not be complete without the relevant annexures and exhibits whereas a special parliamentary debate with regards to the Forensic Audit report is scheduled for the 18 th and 19 th of February 2020.
