## Title: Seven youths arrested with 30 pkts of heroin
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, January 28, 2017 - 01:00

Seven youths arrested with 30 pkts of heroin

 Seven youths with 30 packets of heroin were taken into custody at Nikaweratiya in Kurunegala by a police team led by SP Sanjeewa Bandara. The suspects, between 20-25 age group, were students of technical colleges in the area.
They were later found to be responsible for several house breaking as well, police sources said.
The suspects were to be produced before court.
