## Title: New State Ministers and Deputy Ministers appointed by President
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 31, 2017 - 13:00

New State Ministers and Deputy Ministers appointed by President

 
Four new State Ministers and four new Deputy Ministers were appointed by President Maithripala Sirisena, a short while ago.
The new State Ministers are as follows:
Wasantha Senanayake -  Foreign Affairs State Minister
Eran Wickramaratne -  Finance State Minister
Lakshman Yapa Abeywardena - Public Enterprise Development State Minister
Palitha Range Bandara -Irrigation State Minister
The new Deputy Ministers are as follows:
Harsha de Silva -  National Policies and Economic Affairs Deputy Minister
Ranjan Ramanayake - Social Empowerment Welfare and Kandyan Heritage Deputy Minister
Karunarathna Paranawithana - Skills Development and Vocational Training Deputy Minister
Lasantha Alagiyawanna - Finance and Mass Media Deputy Minister
