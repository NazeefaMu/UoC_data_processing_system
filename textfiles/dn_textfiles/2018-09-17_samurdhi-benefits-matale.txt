## Title: Samurdhi benefits for Matale
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, September 17, 2018 - 01:00

Samurdhi benefits for Matale

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe presides over a ceremony held at Matale Edward Grounds to mark the distribution of Samurdhi relief entitlements to people in the Matale district, on September 15. Miniser P. Harison, Deputy Minister Ranjan Ramanayake, State Minister Ranjith Aluvihare and Deputy Minister J.C. Alawatuwala were also present on the occasion.
