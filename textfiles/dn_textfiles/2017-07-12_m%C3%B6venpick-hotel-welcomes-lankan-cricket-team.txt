## Title: Mövenpick Hotel welcomes Lankan cricket team
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, July 12, 2017 - 01:00

Mövenpick Hotel welcomes Lankan cricket team

The Sri Lankan cricket team arrived on Monday at the Mövenpick Hotel Colombo, the host hotel for the Zimbabwe tour of Sri Lanka for both teams. Pictures show the Sri Lankan team arriving and being greeted by the General Manager Markus Marti along with the rest of the Mövenpick family.
