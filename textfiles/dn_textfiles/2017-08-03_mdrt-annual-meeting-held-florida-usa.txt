## Title: MDRT Annual Meeting held in Florida, USA
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, August 3, 2017 - 01:00

MDRT Annual Meeting held in Florida, USA

Thirty five MDRT members from AIA Sri Lanka participated in the recently held MDRT Annual Meeting held in Orlando, Florida, USA. The meeting brings together financial professionals from around the world to share and learn best practices. MDRT is internationally recognised as the standard of excellence in the life insurance and financial services business. Its members are required to generate a higher level of production and demonstrate exceptional professional knowledge, strict ethical conduct and outstanding client service. AIA Sri Lanka has the highest number of MDRT members from Sri Lanka.
