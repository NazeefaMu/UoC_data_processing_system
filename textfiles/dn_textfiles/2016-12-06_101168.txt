## Title: President opens Thriposha factory in Kandana
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, December 6, 2016 - 01:00

President opens Thriposha factory in Kandana

 
President Maithripala Sirisena opened a modernised Thriposha factory in Kandana yesterday.
The government has allocated 500 million rupees from Budget 2016 to renovate the factory - now a full government owned company under the Health Ministry. The Budget 2015 annually allocated only 200 million rupees in this regard.
The factory production capacity will increase with the installation of modern equipment.
The President also engaged in an observation tour of the factory.
Ven. Athuraliya Ratana Thera, Minister Rajitha Senaratne, Deputy Minister Faisal Cassim, MP Kavinda Jayawardana, Health Ministry Secretary Anura Jayawickrama, Health Services Director General Dr. Palitha Mahipala, Sri Lanka Thriposha Limited Chairman M.D. Bandusena and many others participated.
