## Title: Cabinet nod to implement concessionary loans
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, February 15, 2018 - 14:51

Cabinet nod to implement concessionary loans

 
The Cabinet agreed to implement concessionary loans under Enterprise Sri Lanka, as proposed by Finance and Mass Media Minister Mangala Samaraweera.
The loan schemes will be implemented through public and private banks for youth, small and medium scale entrepreneurs, self-employed and female entrepreneurs.
The above implementation was previously proposed in the 2018 budget by the Minister.
