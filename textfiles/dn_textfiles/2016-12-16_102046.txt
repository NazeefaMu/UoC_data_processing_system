## Title: 36th National Squash Championship starts on Saturday
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, December 16, 2016 - 01:00

36th National Squash Championship starts on Saturday

“The 36th National Squash Championship conducted by Sri Lanka Squash and powered by ‘Yardley’ will be worked off from the 17th to the 20th of December at the Tamil Union Squash courts’ said Lt Cdr Priyadarshana Jayawardena (rtd) secretary of Sri Lanka Squash at a media briefing held at the Colombo Swimming Club last Wednesday (14th).
“There has been a 50% increase in the number of entries for this years championship compared to last year. We have over a hundred entries this year and the top ranked male and female players in Sri Lanka will be seen in action” he said.
Sri Lanka’s number one male player Ravindu Laksiri will be looking forward to retain his title for the fourth consecutive year while top ranked womens player Mihiliya Metharani would go all out to regain her title which she held from 2012 to 2014.
This year’s women’s championship will see stiff competition with 2011 champion Sharya Guruge’s return to the game.
The 36th National Squash Championship is sponsored by UTN Trading Company Pvt Ltd sole distributors of Yardley London products in Sri Lanka.
The sponsorship cheque was handed over to Suranjith Premadasa president of SLS by Ravi Katoch Country Manager for Yardley London and Udaya Nissanka Chairman of UTN Trading Pvt Ltd last Wednesday.
