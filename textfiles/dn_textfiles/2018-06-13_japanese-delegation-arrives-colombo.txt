## Title: Japanese delegation arrives in Colombo
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 13, 2018 - 01:00

Japanese delegation arrives in Colombo

A Japanese delegation led by former Prime Minister Yukio Hatoyama arrived in the country yesterday. They were given a rousing welcome at the Katunayake International Airport.
Ministers Vajira Abeywardena and Harin Fernando and Deputy Minister Manusha Nanayakkara were at the Airport to receive the delegation.
The Japanese delegation is due to call on President Mathripala Sirisena and Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe for discussions.
