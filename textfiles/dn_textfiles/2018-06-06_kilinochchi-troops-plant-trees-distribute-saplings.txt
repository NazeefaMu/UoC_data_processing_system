## Title: Kilinochchi troops plant trees, distribute saplings
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 6, 2018 - 01:00

Kilinochchi troops plant trees, distribute saplings

All ranks of the 571 and 574 Brigades under the 57 Division of the Security Force Headquarters - Kilinochchi (SFHQ-KLN) launched a tree planting programme and a project for distribution of saplings among civilians in the Puddumurippu, Malayalapuram, Ampakamam and Marthanagar areas in Killinochchi on Sunday (3), to mark World Environment Day.
Saplings of jak, palu, weera, orange, arecanut, breadfruit and many other varieties were planted in compounds and on roadsides after distributing those plants under the programme initiated on the instructions of, 57 Division General Officer Commanding Major General Vijitha Ravipriya, with the blessings of Security Forces - Kilinochchi (SF-KLN) Commander Major General Niyshshanka Ranawana.
The inaugural event took place in the Paraipanchchan area in Kilinochchi on Sunday in the presence of the 57 Division GOC as the chief guest, Commanding Officers of 7 SLLI, 4 SLSR, 9 VIR and 16 (V) SLLI troops representing all formations. Several civil authorities were also associated with the inaugural occasion.
The 57 Division expects to extend the project to other villages in close coordination with respective divisional secretariats and grama niladharis, as and when more saplings are received.
During the brief inauguration, the importance of the project of this nature was highlighted and everyone was urged to cooperate with the tree planting drive for the greater benefit of the nation.
In the meantime, troops serving the 571 and 574 Brigades of the Security Force Headquarters - Kilinochchi (SFHQ-KLN), together with members of the civil community in the area, launched a shramadana campaign and cleaned the Kilinochchi township on Saturday (2) to mark World Environment Day and National Environment Week.
More than 350 Army personnel from 7 SLLI, 4 SLSR, 9 VIR and 16 (V) SLLI troops of the 57 Division assisted the cleaning of Kilinochchi town and the area between the 238th mile post and the 254th mile post along the A-9 road.
