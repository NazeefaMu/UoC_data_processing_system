## Title: Thundershowers expected in several provinces today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, April 16, 2018 - 11:23

Thundershowers expected in several provinces today

Thundershowers are expected particularly in the Western, Sabaragamuwa, Southern, Central, North-western and Uva provinces in the afternoon, today the Meteorology Department said.
Thundershowers are expected in the coastal areas of Southern province and Ampara district in the morning too.
There may be temporary localized strong winds during thundershowers.
The Met. Department urged the public to take adequate precautions to minimize damages caused by lightning activity.
