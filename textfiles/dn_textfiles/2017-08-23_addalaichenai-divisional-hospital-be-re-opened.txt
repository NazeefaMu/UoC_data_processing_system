## Title: Addalaichenai Divisional Hospital to be re-opened
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 23, 2017 - 01:00

Addalaichenai Divisional Hospital to be re-opened

The renovated Addalaichenai Divisional Hospital and several newly constructed buildings in the Addalaichenai DS Division are to be opened by City Planning, Water Supply and Drainage Minister and SLMC Leader Rauff Hakeem and Health, Nutrition and Indigenous Medicine Minister Dr. Rajitha Senarathna on August 27, said Eastern Province Health Indigenous Medicine, Probation and Childcare, Social Welfare and Rural Electrification Minister A. L. M. Nazeer.
A special discussion regarding the development of areas in the Addalaichenai DS Division was held at the Addalaichenai Divisional Hospital auditorium recently.
Developing the Addalaichenai Divisional Hospital, setting up a Accident Care Unit; developing the MOH, Addalaichenai District Ayurvedic Hospital, Government Veterinary Surgeon’s offices and the Alamkulam Primary Medical Care Unit; placing the foundation stone for the Sheriff Hajiyar carpet road development work, developing Addalaichenai's public playgrounds, are some of the projects that are to be implemented.
Provincial Health Minister A. L. M. Nazeer, along with other officers, visited the hospital and other places to oversee the progress of the development works. Eastern Provincial Health Director Dr. M. Muruganantham, Kalmunai RDHS Dr. A. L. Alawdeen, Private Secretary to the Minister U. M. Wahid and Health Department officials attended the meeting.
