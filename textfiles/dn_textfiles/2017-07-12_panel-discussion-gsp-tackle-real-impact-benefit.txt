## Title: Panel discussion on GSP+ to tackle real impact of benefit
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Wednesday, July 12, 2017 - 01:00

Panel discussion on GSP+ to tackle real impact of benefit

A panel discussion open for the public featuring Minister Dr. Harsha De Silva, Economist Deshal De Mel and industry representatives of apparel and seafood exporters association will be held today, at 5.30 pm, under the theme 'GSP+, How Real is It?'.
Organized by the Young Professionals Organisation of the United National Party, the event will be held at The Lighthouse, Lakshman Kadirgamar Institute, also featuring the Secretary General of the Joint Apparel Association Forum (JAAF) Tuli Cooray and Spokesperson of Sri Lanka Seafood Exporters Association Col. Channa Weeratunga.
The panel discussion aims to tackle difficult questions from the public and identify the real impact of the GSP+ concession on the Sri Lankan economy.
Free admission to the event will be on first come first served basis.
