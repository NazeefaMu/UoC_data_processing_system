## Title: Independence anniversary to be celebrated in Saudi Arabia
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, February 27, 2018 - 01:00

Independence anniversary to be celebrated in Saudi Arabia

The 70th anniversary of independence will be celebrated today in Saudi Arabia, under the patronage of Sri Lankan Ambassador Azmi Thassim.
Saudi Embassy-Approved Recruitment Agents Association President Mohamed Mahroof Mohamed, together with Ambassador Thassim, conveyed their best wishes to all Sri Lankan expatriates working in Saudi Arabia.
They also wished all Sri Lankans around the world continued peace and prosperity in the coming years. On the occasion, Mahroof said they looked forward to stronger ties between Sri Lanka and Saudi Arabia.
