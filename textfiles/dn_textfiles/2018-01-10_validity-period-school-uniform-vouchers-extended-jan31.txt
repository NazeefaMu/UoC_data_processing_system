## Title: Validity period of school uniform vouchers extended to Jan.31
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 10, 2018 - 01:00

Validity period of school uniform vouchers extended to Jan.31

The Ministry of Education yesterday, announced that the validity period of school uniform vouchers would be extended up to January 31.
The extension had been granted in lieu of students who had not been able to purchase their school uniforms during the stipulated time period.
The vouchers officially expired on December 31, 2017.
