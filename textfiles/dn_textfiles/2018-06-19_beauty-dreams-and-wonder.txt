## Title: Of beauty, dreams and wonder
## Supertitle: 
## Section: T&C
## Date: Tuesday, June 19, 2018 - 01:00

Of beauty, dreams and wonder

Pageants Sri Lanka hosted the Grand Finale of Miss and Mrs Sri Lanka 2018 for USA on June 2 at Amora Lagoon, Katunayake. This event was open to contestants who were single between the ages of 18 to 35 and for those who were married between the ages of 21 to 50 across the country. The franchise holder of Miss and Mrs. Sri Lanka 2018 for USA was Boston Lanka Media Networks and Shamil Malik was the National Pageant Director. Three dazzling crowns were given to Miss. International, Mrs. International and International Ms. The winners will represent Sri Lanka at the Miss, Mrs and Miss Teen International Pageant in West Virginia, USA. 16 contestants competed in the Grand Finale of Miss and Mrs. Sri Lanka 2018 for USA.
