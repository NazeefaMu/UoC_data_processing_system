## Title: People’s Bank opens ATM centre at Kalmunai
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, July 5, 2018 - 01:00

People’s Bank opens ATM centre at Kalmunai

People’s Bank opened an ATM centre at Kalmunai Ashraff Memorial Base Hospital premises in the Ampara district for the convenience of the customers recently. The Chief Guest on this occasion Kalmunai Ashraff Memorial Base Hospitals’ Medical Superintendent Dr.A.L.F.Rahman who was the chief guest on this occasion opened the ATM.
