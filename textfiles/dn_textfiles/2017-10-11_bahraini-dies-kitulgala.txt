## Title: Bahraini dies in Kitulgala
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, October 11, 2017 - 16:00

Bahraini dies in Kitulgala

 
A Bahraini national died while climbing the Sandun Ella in Mahabage, Police said today.
The victim was identified as 55-year-old Abdul Zahara Abdul Huzen Jesim Mohammed.
The body is lying at the morgue of Theligama hospital.
Kitulgala Police is conducting further investigations.
