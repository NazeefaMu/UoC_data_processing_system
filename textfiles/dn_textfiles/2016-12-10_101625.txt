## Title: Sarasaviya Film Festival
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, December 10, 2016 - 01:00

Sarasaviya Film Festival

Hogana Pokuna directed by Indika Ferdinando was screened for the fourth day of Sarasaviya Film Festival yesterday. The film was produced by Kusumsiri Liyanaarachchi. Mass Media and Parliamentary Reforms Minister Gayantha Karunathilake and veteran actress Neita Fernando were the chief guests. Picture shows artistes with Minister Karunathilake. ANCL Legal Director Srimevan Dias was also present. The Sarasaviya Film Festival 2016 ias sponsored by Sri Lanka Telecom. Picture by Shan Rambukwella
