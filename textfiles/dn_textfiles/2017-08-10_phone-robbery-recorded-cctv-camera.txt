## Title: Phone robbery recorded on CCTV camera
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, August 10, 2017 - 15:15

Phone robbery recorded on CCTV camera

 
An individual stealing a vendor’s phone worth Rs. 15,000 was recorded on a CCTV camera last evening.
The suspect stole the phone while purchasing eggs from the vendor.
Hatton Police is investigating.
