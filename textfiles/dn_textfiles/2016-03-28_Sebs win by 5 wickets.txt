## Title: Sebs win by 5 wickets
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, March 28, 2016 - 01:00

Sebs win by 5 wickets

St. Sebastian's College beat Prince of Wales College by five wickets in their annual inter school 31st Limited Over Cricket match played at Tyronne Fernando Stadium, Moratuwa yesterday. Now the tally stands at Sebs 15, Cambrians 13, two no decisions and one tie.
Cambrians batting first were all out for 171 runs with Kasun Ekanayake making the top score of 62 runs while Malintha Peiris and Mishen Silva claimed 3 for 12 and 3 for 35 respectively. In reply Sebs were 172 for 5 with Navanidu Fernando unbeaten on 72 runs with eight fours and Tharusha Fernando 45.
Prince of Wales - 171 in 47 overs (Dilanka Maduranga 24, Kasun Ekanayake 62, Avindu Fernando 23, Mishen Silva 3/35, Malintha Peiris 3/12, Praveen Jayawickrama 2/35)
St. Sebastian's - 172 for 5 in 46.1 overs (Avishka Fernando 22, Navanidu Fernando 72 n/o, Tharusha Fernando 45)
