## Title: Nipuna Deshan scores century
## Supertitle: Singer U-19 Inter Schools Cricket
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, November 9, 2017 - 01:00

Singer U-19 Inter Schools Cricket
Nipuna Deshan scores century

Nipuna Deshan of Sri Sumangala College, Panadura scored 101 runs against Royal College, Colombo in their Singer Cup Under 19 inter Schools Cricket tournament match at Panadura yesterday.
All-rounder Vihan Gunasekara steered D. S. Senanayake College, Colombo to 92-run win over Bandaranayake College Gampaha.
Tharusha Rukshan of Royal, Colombo took 6 wickets for 60 runs against Sri Sumanagala Panadura.
At Bandaragama. D.S. Senanayake won by 92 runs.
D.S. Senanayake: 172 in 50.1 overs and 184/5 decl. in 51 overs (Vihan Gunasekara 61, Chamath Yatawara 45, Janindu Jayawardhane 5/61)
