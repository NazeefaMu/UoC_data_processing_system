## Title: BIA will welcome Emirates Airbus A380 on August 14
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, August 10, 2017 - 01:00

BIA will welcome Emirates Airbus A380 on August 14

The recently upgraded Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA) will welcome the iconic 21st century Airbus A380 when Emirates flight EK 654 from Dubai will touch down at the airport at 16:10 hours on Monday, August 14. Among other upgrades, the runway at BIA was expanded by 15 meters, (7.5 meters on either side) to accommodate this aircraft type. The A380 will return as Emirates EK655 departing at 22:10 hours on the same day.
