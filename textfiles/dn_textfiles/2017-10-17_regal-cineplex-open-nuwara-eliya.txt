## Title: Regal Cineplex to open in Nuwara Eliya
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Tuesday, October 17, 2017 - 01:00

Regal Cineplex to open in Nuwara Eliya

The newly built Regal Cineplex in Nuwara Eliya will be opened on October 18 along with the Deepavali Tamil release film Vijay’s ‘Mersal’.
This 2k Digital Cinema with A/C and heaters is equipped with Barco Projector system and 7.1 Dolby sound system and has a seating capacity for 105 and with four daily shows at 10.30 am, 2 pm, 5.30 pm, 8.30 pm. The second Cinema with 160 seats will be opened in November 2017.
The film hall is located near the main road next to Windsor Hotel. It is owned and operated by Ceylon Theaters (Pvt) Ltd. CEL release films in Tamil, Sinhala, English and Hindi will be screened.
