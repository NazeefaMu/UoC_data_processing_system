## Title: Car-motorcycle collision kills one, injures another in Ampara
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, September 29, 2017 - 10:15

Car-motorcycle collision kills one, injures another in Ampara

 
A car collided with a motorcycle on the Maha Oya road,in Karangawain , Ampara, killing the driver and injuring the passenger on the motorcycle, earlier today.
The motorcyclist identified as Sunil Shantha from Tissapura, Uhana succumbed to injuries after being rushed to the Ampara Hospital.
Post-mortem examinations will be conducted today.
The Police arrested the car driver.
Ampara Police is investigating.
