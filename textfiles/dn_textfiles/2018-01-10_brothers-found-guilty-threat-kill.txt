## Title: Brothers found guilty of threat to kill
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 10, 2018 - 01:00

Brothers found guilty of threat to kill

Two brothers, Gunaratna Banda and Ajith Saliya Jayasinghe were sentenced to two years' rigorous imprisonment, suspended for ten years, with a fine of Rs 1 lakh (each Rs 50,000), by Kurunegala High Court Judge Menaka Wijesundara after being found guilty of threat to kill on April 30, 2013.
In this case, the accused were found guilty of threatening to kill Rajapaksha Mudiyanselage Premaratna Banda of Pothuhera, by assaulting him with a mammoty and poles.
The Prosecution with the assistance of State Counsel Charaka Dharmasiri had proved the charges against the two accused beyond doubt.
