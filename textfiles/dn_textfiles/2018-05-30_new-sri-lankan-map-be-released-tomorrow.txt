## Title: New Sri Lankan map to be released tomorrow
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 30, 2018 - 14:31

New Sri Lankan map to be released tomorrow

 
A new Sri Lankan map including the additional two kilometers of the Port City and several development projects such as Moragahakanda reservoir, will be released tomorrow, Sri Lanka Survey General P.M.P. Udayakantha said.
Printed copies of the new map drawn by the 1:500 ratio will be produced in print copies by mid June, the Survey General said.
