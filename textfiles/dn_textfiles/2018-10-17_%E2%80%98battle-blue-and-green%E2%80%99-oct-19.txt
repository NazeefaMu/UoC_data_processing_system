The ‘Battle of the Blue and Green’, a cricket big match between Kegalu Balika Vidyalaya and St. Joseph’s Balika Vidyalaya will be played on October 19 at the Rambukkana Maheepala Herath grounds from 8.30 a.m. The match has been organized as a part of Kegalu Balika Vidyalaya’s 75th anniversay celebrations.
The organisers expect to popularise hardball cricket among girls schools and the match will also expected to bring talented girls cricketers to the forefront. The two schools are known to be the first to start a girls cricket big match.
