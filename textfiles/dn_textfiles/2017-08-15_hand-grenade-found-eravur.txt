## Title: Hand grenade found in Eravur
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, August 15, 2017 - 12:30

Hand grenade found in Eravur

 
A hand grenade was found in a land at Odaviyar Road,Eravur by the owner of the land today.
Eravur Police said that the grenade was wrapped in a polythene bag.
The land owner was identified as M. Musthafa a former member of the Eravur Town Council.
The police defused the grenade.
Eravur Police is conducting further investigations.
