## Title: Construction delayed, no change in BIA routing
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, June 19, 2018 - 01:00

Construction delayed, no change in BIA routing

SriLankan Airlines yesterday said passengers could reach the airport at usual time - three hours prior to flights’ departure time” as the constructions planned at the Katunayake Airport was slightly delayed.
“SriLankan wishes to inform all passengers that due to a slight delay in the construction planned, we are not restricted in space. Therefore, passengers can report at usual times - three hours prior to departure time of flights,”an statement issued by the airline said.
