## Title: Court issues orders on anti-SAITM protesters
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 10, 2017 - 12:45

Court issues orders on anti-SAITM protesters

 
Court ordered Inter-University Students' Federation (IUSF) Convener Lahiru Weerasekara, Convener of the Inter-University Bhikkhus’ Federation (IUBF) Ven. Tampitiye Sugathananda Thero and Acting Convener of the Medical Students’ Action Committee Susitha Priyankara Perera to refrain from entering any public institutions or causing inconvenience to the public during their scheduled protest against SAITM today.
The protest is demanding the protection of state education and the abolishing of SAITM.
