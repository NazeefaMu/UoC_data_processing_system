## Title: British HC on holding elections
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, October 26, 2018 - 01:00

British HC on holding elections

British High Commissioner in Colombo James Dauris yesterday tweeted that holding elections on time will ensure that the values of democracy flourish.
“Many people will be disappointed that the term in office of a sixth of nine Provincial Councils has expired without voting for new representatives taking place or planned ,
“In a tweet, he also said timely elections are key to ensuring that the values of democracy flourish in our countries”. the tweet said.
