## Title: Schools in several provinces closed due to bad weather
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 30, 2017 - 11:25

Schools in several provinces closed due to bad weather

 
Schools in Western, Central, Southern and Uva provinces were ordered to be closed today by Education Minister Akila Viraj Kariyawasam, due to bad weather.
Term examinations which were scheduled to be held today were also ordered to be postponed by the Minister.
