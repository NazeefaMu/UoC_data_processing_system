## Title: Sebs win by five wickets
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, January 30, 2018 - 01:00

Sebs win by five wickets

In another game in the under 13 division three St. Sebastians College beat Marapona Vidyalaya by five wickets at Moratuwa. Batting first visitors were all out for 151 runs with Nimesh Hansajith scoring 57 runs and in reply at stamps Sebs were 161 for 5 with Tharindu Nayanajith scoring 59 runs.
Marapona Vidyalaya – 151 – (Lakshitha Udesh 31, Nimesh Hansajith 57, Akash Dissanayake 2/21, Malinda Silva 2/28, Ryan Dissanayake 3/25.
St. Sebastians – 161/5 – (Malinda Silva 47, Tharindu Nayanajith 59, Ushen Silva 20, Nadun Lakshitha 3/35).
