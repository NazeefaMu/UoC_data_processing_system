## Title: Dry rations for Tangalle flood affected
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, July 15, 2017 - 01:00

Dry rations for Tangalle flood affected

Housing and Construction Minister Sajith Premadasa distributed dry rations worth over Rs.3,200,000 among flood affected people in the Tangalle Divisional Secretariat area. Ministry employees and philanthropists funded the programme.
