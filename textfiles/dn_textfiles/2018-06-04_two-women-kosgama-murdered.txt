## Title: Two women of Kosgama murdered
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, June 4, 2018 - 01:00

Two women of Kosgama murdered

Two women had been killed at Kosgama on Saturday following a monetary dispute.
The two women, aged 34 and 78, residents of Waralla Mandiya, Kosgoma, were relatives and they were murdered at their house. The suspect, a relative of the murdered, had fled the area and Kosgama police are looking for them.
The Avissawella Magistrate conducted the inquiry into deaths. (FJ)
