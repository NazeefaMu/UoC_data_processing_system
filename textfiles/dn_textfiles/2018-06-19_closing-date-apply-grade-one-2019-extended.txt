## Title: Closing date to apply for Grade One 2019 extended
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, June 19, 2018 - 01:00

Closing date to apply for Grade One 2019 extended

The deadline to accept applications for the 2019 Grade One admissions in State schools has been extended to July 10, 2018.
The Education Ministry has announced in has been done due to the ongoing postal strike.
The Ministry said applications prepared as per the specimen form and instructions should be sent to Heads of Schools by the registered post to reach them before July 10, 2018.
The specimen application form was published on the website http://www.moe.gov.lk.
