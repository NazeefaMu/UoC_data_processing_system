## Title: Journalist Mel Gunasekara murder case: Further trial fixed for July 5
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Thursday, June 1, 2017 - 01:00

Journalist Mel Gunasekara murder case: Further trial fixed for July 5

The Colombo High Court yesterday fixed for July 5, further trial into the murder of journalist Mel Gunasekera.
The Attorney General had filed indictments against accused Anthony Russan George, a home painter by profession for committing the murder of senior business journalist Mel Gunasekara.
The case will be taken up defence submissions on next trial date.
Senior business journalist, Mel Gunasekera was found dead with cut injuries caused by a sharp weapon at her residence in Battaramulla on February 2, 2014.
Senior State
Counsel Shanil Kularatne appeared for the Attorney General.
