## Title: No child’s play
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Friday, January 27, 2017 - 01:00

No child’s play

A dance drama titled as Paramitha directes by senior thespian Kodikara Pathirana was staged recently at the D A Rajapaksa Centre, Embilipitiya under the supervision of Bodhiraja Dharmayathana Chief Incumbent Venerable Dr Omalpe Sobhitha Thera. Probation and Children’s Affairs Commmissioner Dilini Dharmadasa graced the occasion as chief guest. The drama is a collective effort of the residents of Bodhiraja Children’s Home. Viduruvan Gamage and Ranjith Abeywardene composed music to the play.
