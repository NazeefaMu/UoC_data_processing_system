## Title: Three accused sentenced to life imprisonment
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 01:00

Three accused sentenced to life imprisonment

The three accused in the Kurunegala Malkaduwawa Jewellery case were sentenced to life imprisonment by Kurunegala High Court Judge Menaka Wijesundara recently.
The accused, Manawa Utum Mudiyanselage Thissa alias Podi Mahaththaya, Prasanna Jayawardana and Konaralage Wijitha entered a house at Kurunegala, Malkaduwawa area as policemen and robbed jewellery worth over Rs. 1,000,000 on November 1, 2001.
State counsel Lahiru Jayamanna told court the gang had posed as police personnel and entered the house. They frightened the inmates by brandishing weapons and had stolen jewellery, electrical appliances and other valuables worth over Rs. 1,000,000, the State Counsel told court.
