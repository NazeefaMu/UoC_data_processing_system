## Title: Polythene use banned from September
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, July 12, 2017 - 13:30

Polythene use banned from September

 
Cabinet approved the proposal to prohibit the use of polythene lunch sheets, Styrofoam and shopping bags with effect from September.
The measure was taken as an initiative towards providing a solution to the problem of accumulation of non-degradable waste.
