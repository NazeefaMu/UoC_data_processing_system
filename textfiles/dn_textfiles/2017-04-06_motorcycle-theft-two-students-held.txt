## Title: Motorcycle theft: Two students held
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 6, 2017 - 01:00

Motorcycle theft: Two students held

Two Advanced Level students of a leading school in Kurunegala, who had allegedly stolen a motorcycle worth Rs. 450,000 were taken into custody by Kurunegala Police.
They had stolen the motorcycle to participate at the Big Match parade of their school. The students are residents of Dodangaslanda and Katupotha.
