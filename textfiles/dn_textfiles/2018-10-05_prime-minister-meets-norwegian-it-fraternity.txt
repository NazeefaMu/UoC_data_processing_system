## Title: Prime Minister meets Norwegian IT fraternity
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Friday, October 5, 2018 - 01:00

Prime Minister meets Norwegian IT fraternity

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe who was on an official visit to Norway this week attended a business reception hosted by IKT - Norge and Sri Lanka Association for Software and Services Companies (SLASSCOM) on Wednesday. Ambassador of Sri Lanka for Norway, Prof Arusha Cooray and Chairman, SLASSCOM, Jeevan Gnanam were also present on the occasion.
