## Title: No decision at Galahitiyawa
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, January 6, 2018 - 01:00

No decision at Galahitiyawa

St. Sebastian's College, Moratuwa drew with Galahitiyawa Cenral College in their inter school under 13 division two tournament cricket match played at Galahitiyawa.
Batting first the homesters had 210 for 3 with G. S. Mahindarathne and Pumath Gunawardene scoring unbeaten 51 and 50 respectively, in reply Sebs were 190 for 2 at stumps with Nadeesh Fernando unbeaten on 70 runs.
