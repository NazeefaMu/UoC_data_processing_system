## Title: COMMISSION GRILLS NISHANTHA, CHANDRASENA FOR OVER 8 HOURS
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, August 24, 2018 - 01:08

COMMISSION GRILLS NISHANTHA, CHANDRASENA FOR OVER 8 HOURS

The Presidential Commission investigating into SriLankan Airlines yesterday recorded statements of former Chairman Nishantha Wickramasinghe and former CEO Kapila Chandrasena.
This is over alleged frauds that have taken place at the airline.Following a study of documents and the evidence that transpired before the Commission, officials began recording statements from them.
The statements were recorded from them for over eight hours.
The Commission is investigating into the alleged frauds that have taken place at SriLankan Airlines, SriLankan Catering and Mihin Lanka.
