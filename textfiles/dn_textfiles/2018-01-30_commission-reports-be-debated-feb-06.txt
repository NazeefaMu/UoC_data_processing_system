## Title: Commission reports to be debated on Feb. 06
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, January 30, 2018 - 15:31

Commission reports to be debated on Feb. 06

 
Reports pertaining Presidential Commission of Inquiry (PCoI) into Treasury Bond issue and Presidential Commission in to Serious Acts of Fraud, Corruption and Abuse of Power, State Resources and Privileges (PRECIFAC) will be taken up for debate in Parliament at 10 a.m. on February 6.
The date was fixed after a discussion with Party Leaders at a meeting convened by Speaker Karu Jayasuriya, at 12 noon, today.
