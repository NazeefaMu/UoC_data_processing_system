## Title: Tangalle Excise officials rake in over Rs. 1,822,000
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, July 15, 2017 - 01:00

Tangalle Excise officials rake in over Rs. 1,822,000

Excise officials attached to the Tangalle Exci1se Station have collected over Rs. 1,822,000 through court fines from excise raids in the Tangalle division during the last six months.
Over 300 raids have been conducted.
Manufacture of illicit liquor, possession and sale of arrack without permits, running illicit liquor distilleries, production, possession and sale of illegal spirits, possession and sale of foreign manufactured cigarettes, possession and sale of ganja and other dangerous drugs were charges made against the suspects.
