## Title: Annual water cutting ceremony of the historic Thanthamalai Murugan Temple
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, August 10, 2017 - 01:00

Annual water cutting ceremony of the historic Thanthamalai Murugan Temple

The annual water cutting ceremony of the historic Thanthamalai Murugan Temple, Batticaloa, was held at its pond recently. Thousands of devotees from all over the country performed their vows. Chief priest of the temple Sivasiri M.K. Sachchithananthak Kurukkai conducted the rituals. Picture by Sivam Packiyanathan, Batticaloa Special Correspondent
