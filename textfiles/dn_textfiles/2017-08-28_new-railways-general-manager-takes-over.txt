## Title: New Railways General Manager takes over
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, August 28, 2017 - 01:00

New Railways General Manager takes over

The new General Manager of Railways, S.M.Abeywickrema assumed duties in office yesterday at the GMR’s office at the Railways Department in Colombo 10. He is the 28th General Manager, in the Sri Lanka Railway Service. Transport and Aviation Minister Nimal Siripala de Silva greeting the new GMR. Picture by Vipula Amarasinghe
