## Title: Army sing carols in Puttalam today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 28, 2018 - 01:00

Army sing carols in Puttalam today

The Army will sing carols for the first time to entertain the community of African origin in Puttalam today at 4.00 pm at the St.Mary's Church ground in Puttalam with Army Commander Mahesh Senanayake as the Chief Guest. Brigedier Dhammika Dissanayake of the 143 Unit said the children of the African origin will be given school books and bags and the families will receive dry rations worth over Rs. 5 lakhs.
