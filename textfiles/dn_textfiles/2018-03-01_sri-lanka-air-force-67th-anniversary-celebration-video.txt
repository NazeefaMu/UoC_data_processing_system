Sri Lanka Air Force 67th Anniversary celebration: VIDEO


The Sri Lanka Air force is celebrating the 67th Anniversary on March 2.
The Air Force which played a vital role to defeat the three decade long civil war which claimed hundreds of lives came into existence on March 2, under the name, Royal Ceylon Air Force.
