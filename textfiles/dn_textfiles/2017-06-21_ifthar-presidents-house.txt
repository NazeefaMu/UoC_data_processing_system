## Title: Ifthar at President's house
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 21, 2017 - 01:00

Ifthar at President's house

President Maithripala Sirisena hosted the Iftar Dinner for the Muslims observing the daily Ramadan fast at the President’s House last night. Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe, Ministers Faizer Mustapha, Ravi Karunanayake, Rishard Bathuideen, State Minister A.H.M.Fowzie, President’s Secretary P.B.Abeykoon, Ambassadors, High Commissioners, and large group of Muslim religious leaders and devotees were present.The Muslim religious leaders and devotees present on the occasion also praised the action taken by the President to usher national integration and reconciliation in the country.
