## Title: Piliyandala Town SC win
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, May 19, 2018 - 01:00

Piliyandala Town SC win

A splendid 105 runs by Dinuka Darshana Godakanda helped Piliyandala Town Sports Club to defeat Lanka Lions Cricket Club Homagama by 154 runs in their inter club Colombo District Division three tournament cricket match conducted by Sri Lanka cricket and played at Piliyandala.
Dinuka had twelve hits to the ropes and three over He batted for 75 minutes and faced 69 balls. Batting first homesters scored 251 for 7 with Gavindu Devan claiming 4 for 4 and in reply visitors were bowled out for 97 runs.
Piliyandala TSC – 251/7 in 34 overs – Dinuka Darshana Godakanda 105, Sasindu Gomes 42, Lasantha Kumara 22, R. A. Duminda 22, Gavindu Devan 4/44, D. S. Dias 2/42.
Lanka Lions CC – 97 in 20.1 overs – A. S. Maduranga 48, S. B. C. Vinod 3/19, Lasantha Kumara 3/30, Kusalindu Hansajith 2/19.
