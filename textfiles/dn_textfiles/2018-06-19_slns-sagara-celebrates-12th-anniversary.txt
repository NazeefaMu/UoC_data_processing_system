## Title: SLNS Sagara celebrates 12th anniversary
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, June 19, 2018 - 01:00

SLNS Sagara celebrates 12th anniversary

SLNS Sagara, one of the Offshore Patrol Vessels (OPVs) of the Sri Lanka Navy, celebrated its 12th anniversary last Saturday (16). To coincide with the anniversary celebrations, the Navy held a family get-together featuring a lot of fun-filled activities for children and family members, organized under the directive of the ship’s Commanding Officer Captain Janaka Nissanka.
