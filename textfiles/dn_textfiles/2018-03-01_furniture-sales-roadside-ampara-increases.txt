## Title: Furniture sales by roadside in Ampara increases
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, March 1, 2018 - 01:00

Furniture sales by roadside in Ampara increases

There is an increase in the sale of furniture in the Ampara district, brought from other districts. The manufactured furniture is brought to the road side and the sales are carried out daily.
There is no showroom or electricity wages. Therefore, they are selling these furniture at a reduced rate.
In certain areas, these vendors are taking these furniture on the shoulder and selling them at cheaper rates.
