## Title: Injunction order prevents SPCA holding AGM
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, December 28, 2018 - 01:00

Injunction order prevents SPCA holding AGM

The Southern Province Cricket Association (SPCA) has been prevented from holding their Annual General Meeting by the Colombo District Courts after considering a case filed by Harsha Munasinghe of Dangedara, Galle.
The court issued an injunction order yesterday.
