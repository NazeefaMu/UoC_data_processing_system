## Title: Peterite Umesh scores double century
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, September 24, 2016 - 01:00

Peterite Umesh scores double century

Umesh Kurera of St. Peter’s College Negombo scored 235 runs against Tissa Central College Kalutara in the Singer Trophy Under 19 inter-schools cricket tournament match played at Dalupotha yesterday.
Thusith de Zoysa of St Sylvester’s took a match bag of 11 for 38 to rout Anuradhapura Central for 69 and 22 and give his team an resounding innings win at Peradeniya.
