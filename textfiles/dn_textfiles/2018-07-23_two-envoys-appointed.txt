## Title: Two envoys appointed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, July 23, 2018 - 01:00

Two envoys appointed

Prof. Arusha Cooray has been appointed the next Ambassador of Sri Lanka to the Kingdom of Norway. She is expected to take up the assignment shortly. Prof. Cooray is an academic specializing in Economics..
Former Minister Nandimithra Ekanayake has been appointed the Ambassador of Sri Lanka to the Republic of the Union of Myanmar. He assumed duties on July 20. Previously Ekanayake has served as a Cabinet Minister, Provincial Chief Minister and a Member of Parliament.
