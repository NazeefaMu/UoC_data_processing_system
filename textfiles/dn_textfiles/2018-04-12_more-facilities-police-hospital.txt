## Title: More facilities for police hospital
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 12, 2018 - 01:00

More facilities for police hospital

IGP Pujitha Jayasundara said police hospital would be developed and modernised on par with private hospitals. He said that even retired police personnel could receive treatment from the police hospital.
The modernised police hospital emergency treatment unit and the out patient unit in Narahenpita, was opened by the IGP yesterday.Five modernised OPD rooms and a dressing room and emergency unit was built at a cost of over Rs.16 million.
