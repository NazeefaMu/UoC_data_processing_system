## Title: Parliament adjourned due to tense situation
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 10, 2018 - 11:33

Parliament adjourned due to tense situation

 
Parliament was adjourned for 10 minutes following a tense situation which erupted during the reading of the special statement on the Bond Commission by Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe, a short while ago.
A Party Leaders meeting was held immediately during the adjournment.
