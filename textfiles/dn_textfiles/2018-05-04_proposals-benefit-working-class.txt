## Title: Proposals to benefit working class
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, May 4, 2018 - 01:00

Proposals to benefit working class

Four trade unions affiliated to the UNP will forward five proposals for the benefit of the working masses on May 6 at the May Day rally.
The rally will take place with the participation of the Prime Minister at the Sugathadasa Indoor Stadium, said National Trade Union Deputy Chairman Sunil de Silva. The proposals will be presented by the chairmen of the National Trade Union, ‘Rajya Sewa Ekabadda Wurthiya Samithi Sammelanaya’, ‘Jathika Wathu Kamkaru Sangamaya’ and ‘Jathika Guru Sangamaya’.
