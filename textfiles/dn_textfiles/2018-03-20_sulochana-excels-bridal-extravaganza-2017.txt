## Date: Tuesday, March 20, 2018 - 01:00

Sulochana  excels at  Bridal  Extravaganza 2017

Sulochana Jayalath of Salon Suhasna in Veyangoda managed to clinch first place in the western bridal segment at the Bridal Extravaganza 2017 which was held recently at the Youth Council in Maharagama. Around 100 beauticians took part in the event. Sulochana received her prizes from renowned beautician Premasiri Hewawasam. The event was organized by Hewawasam’s Kulagedara hair and beauty academy.
