## Title: Man shot in Raddolugama
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 8, 2018 - 01:00

Man shot in Raddolugama

A person was shot at near the Raddolugama housing scheme last night. A gunman on a motorcycle had opened fire at the victim and fled. The injured man was admitted to the Ragama hospital with gun shot injuries.
The victim identified as Nilanga Maheshan 36, of Gallawatte Raddolugama, a suspect remanded and bailed out in a murder case that happened in Seeduwa six months ago.
