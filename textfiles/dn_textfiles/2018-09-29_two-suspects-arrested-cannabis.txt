## Title: Two suspects arrested with cannabis
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, September 29, 2018 - 01:00

Two suspects arrested with cannabis

Two youth were arrested with 4.26 kg of Kerala cannabis in Vavunia by Vavunia Police on Monday evening on a tip off.
They were transporting the stuff from Jaffna to Mahiyanganaya in a private bus when police arrested them. Police stopped the bus near the Nochchimote bridge in Vavunia. The two youth had brought the cannabis to sell them to the spectators of the Mahiyanganaya procession. They will be produced before the Vavunia Magistrate, police said.
