## Title: Justice of Peace appointed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, November 20, 2017 - 01:00

Justice of Peace appointed

A. Jayantha Luxman Peiris of 187, New Road, Hunupitiya, Wattala, has been appointed as an All-Island Justice of Peace, recently.
He is an old boy of Ananda College, Colombo, where he was a senior prefect as well as a senior Cadet. He holds a BSc in Physical Science, with a First Class Honours from the University of Kelaniya and a MSc in Agricultural Economics from the University of Reading, UK, under the scholarship awarded by the President’s and Mahapola Scholarship Fund and an MSc in Quantitative Development Economics from the University of Warwick, UK.
Currently, he serves as a Non-Executive Director of BRAC Lanka Finance PLC, which is a subsidiary of the LOLC Group, following the retirement from the Central Bank of Sri Lanka as an Additional Director after the completion of 25 years of service. Prior to that, he served in the Co-operative Wholesale Establishment (CWE) as a senior manager and also in the Sri Lanka Army Volunteer Force as a Commissioned Officer.
He captained the cricket team of the University of Kelaniya and got colours for hockey and cricket.
He was a member of the post-graduate cricket team of the University of Reading, UK, as well. He is a well-known social worker, presently engaged in social, religious, cultural, educational and welfare activities of the area.
