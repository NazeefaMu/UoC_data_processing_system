## Title: Two arrested with 2kg Kerala Ganja in Thambalagamuwa
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 24, 2017 - 15:00

Two arrested with 2kg Kerala Ganja in Thambalagamuwa

 
Thambalagamua Police arrested two suspects with more than 2 kilogrammes of Kerala Ganja in Thambalagamuwa today.
The suspects were arrested while they were waiting for a buyer.
The raid was made according to a tip-off received by the Police.
The suspects have been identified as residents of Kinniya and Ayiladi.
The suspects and the confiscated Kerala Ganja will be produced before Court.
Thambalagamuwa Police is conducting further investigations.
