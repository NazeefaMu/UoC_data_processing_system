## Title: D.A. Rajapaksa’s commemoration
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, November 6, 2018 - 01:00

D.A. Rajapaksa’s commemoration

Late D.A. Rajapaksa will be commemorated during a ceremony that will be held tomorrow, near the D.A. Rajapaksa statue in Tangalle.
Prime Minister Mahinda Rajapaksa, as well as parliamentarians Chamal Rajapaksa, Basil Rajapaksa, Gotabhaya Rajapaksa, and Namal Rajapaksa will take part in the event.
