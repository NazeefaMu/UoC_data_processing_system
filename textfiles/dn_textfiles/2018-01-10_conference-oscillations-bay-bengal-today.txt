## Title: Conference on oscillations in Bay of Bengal today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 10, 2018 - 01:00

Conference on oscillations in Bay of Bengal today

The International Conference on Monsoon Intra-Seasonal Oscillations in Bay of Bengal will be held today at 9.00 am at the Cinnamon Grand Hotel, Colombo under the patronage of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Development Minister Mahinda Amaraweera.
Intra seasonal oscillations changed the weather in the Bay of Bengal during the recent past.
The National Aquatic Resources Researches and Development Agency along with the University of Notre Dame has implemented a special project to observe the oscillations and the conference was organised as a part of the project, Ministry sources said.
The conference will make important decisions on finding ways to predict future disaster situations. It will make policy decisions on the disaster risk management and also make decision on fisheries industry and naval activities that help improve the fields, the ministry sources said.
