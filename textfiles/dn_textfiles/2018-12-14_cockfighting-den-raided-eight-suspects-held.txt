## Title: Cockfighting den raided: eight suspects held
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 14, 2018 - 01:00

Cockfighting den raided: eight suspects held

Police raided a cockfighting den in Kurunegala and arrested eight suspects recently. The suspects are residents of Pannala.
Three roosters and three motorcycles were also taken into custody.
Police said that the suspects were to be produced at the Kuliyapitiya Magistrate’s Court yesterday.
Meanwhile, four suspects who had pleaded guilty for distilling and selling illegal liquor were fined Rs.77,000 by Wariyapola Magistrate Darshana Silva, yesterday.
