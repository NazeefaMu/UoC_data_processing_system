## Title: People‘s leasing and Finance donated a multi media project
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, November 9, 2017 - 01:00

People‘s leasing and Finance donated a multi media project

People‘s leasing and Finance donated a multi media project and other related equipment worth Rs. five lakhs to the Kiribathgoda Base Hospital Auditorium recently. Here People‘s Leasing Company Deputy General Manager (Marketing) Laksanda Gunawardana hands over anen item to the hospital Medical Superintendent Predeep Dharshana. Head of the People‘s Leasing community project section Roshni Motha, Chief Manager Head Office Chaminda Herath, Kelaniya branch Manager Chandana Kumara and Dr. Mahesh Perera were also present at the ceremony.
(Malwana Group Mahanama Vithanage)
