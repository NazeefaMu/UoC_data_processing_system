## Title: Kap planting ceremony at Kataragama
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, June 4, 2018 - 01:00

Kap planting ceremony at Kataragama

The kap planting ceremony of Ruhunu Kataragama Maha Devalaya was held recently with the presence of Acting Basnayaka Nilame Dilruwan Rajapaksa. The chief kapumahathmaya Somipala T. Ratnayaka said that the Kataragama Esala Mangalyaya is held annually from time immemorial to mark the wedding of Kada Kumaru and Goddess Walli.
He said 45 days after the kap planting, the Esala festival will commence. The Esala Mangalyaya will commence on July 13 and will finish on July 27. The Randoli perahera will be held on July 28. The water-cutting ceremony at the Menik Ganga will be held tomorrow (5).
