## Title: Mangala appointed UNP Vice Chairman
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Wednesday, June 6, 2018 - 01:06

Mangala appointed UNP Vice Chairman

Finance and Media Minister, Mangala Samaraweera has been appointed as the Vice Chairman of the United National Party.
UNP leader, Ranil Wickremesinghe has also included him in the Working Committee of the party.
The Minister tweeting his letter of appointment yesterday, said: “Received letter of appointment as a Vice Chairman of the @officialunp by Hon. @RW_UNP. 30 years ago when I was first appointed as the SLFP organiser for Matara district, I didn’t even, in my wildest dreams thought that I would get such a position in the grand old party”. The appointment stands effective as of April 26, 2018.
