## Title: AG advice sought to regulate massage clinics
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, June 5, 2018 - 01:00

AG advice sought to regulate massage clinics

Health Minister Dr. Rajitha Senaratne will consult the Attorney General regarding the massage clinics mushrooming island-wide, Ministry sources said.
A large number of massage clinics operate island-wide with forged documents. The Maharagama city area alone has over 300 but only four clinics were approved, the sources said. Therefore, Minister Senaratne decided to consult the Attorney General regarding the issue. The Minister has decided to set up a regulatory body in this regard,<U+200B> the sources said.
