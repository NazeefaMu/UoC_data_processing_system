## Title: Court open for basketball and badminton
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Tuesday, September 19, 2017 - 01:00

Court open for basketball and badminton

A new indoor sports complex with state-of- the-art Basketball and Badminton courts was ceremonially declared open by Mohan Lal Grero, the Founder of the Lyceum International Schools and Kumari Grero, the Coordinating Principal recently at Shanthi Mawatha, Nugegoda for the benefit of the students of Lyceum International School, Nugegoda.
This sports complex which has been named “Kalonice” meaning “Beauty of Victory”, is endowed with a special wooden flooring, done at a cost of Rs.3.5 million. This special occasion was graced by the members of the management, officials, parents and students of the school.
