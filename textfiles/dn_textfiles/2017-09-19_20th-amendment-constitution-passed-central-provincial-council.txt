## Title: 20th Amendment to the Constitution passed at Central Provincial Council
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, September 19, 2017 - 11:15

20th Amendment to the Constitution passed at Central Provincial Council

 
The Central Provincial Council agreed to the 20th Amendment to the Constitution which suggests to hold all Provincial Council Elections on the same day, a short while ago.
Forty voted in favour and nine voted against the decision.
Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe proposed the 20th Amendment assuring that it will make a positive impact on financial resources and ensure democracy.
