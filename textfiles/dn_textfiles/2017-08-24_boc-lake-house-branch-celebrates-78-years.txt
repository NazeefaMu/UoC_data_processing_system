## Title: BOC Lake House branch celebrates 78 years
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, August 24, 2017 - 01:00

BOC Lake House branch celebrates 78 years

Lake House Branch of the Bank of Ceylon celebrated the BOC 78th Anniversary Celebration at their branch with a series of activities.
With the confidence and trust that has been build over the years in the Sri Lankan Banking and Financial Services sector, Country’s NO.1 Brand steps into its successful 78th year of service as “Banker’s to the Nation” on August 1.
The Bank has been committed to socio-economic development through financial inclusion, providing genuinely value-added services to those who seek reliable and convenient financial solutions and the overall upliftment of  the country’s banking and financial landscape to align it with international banking standards.
