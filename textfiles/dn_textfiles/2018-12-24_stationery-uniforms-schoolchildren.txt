## Title: Stationery, uniforms for schoolchildren
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, December 24, 2018 - 01:00

Stationery, uniforms for schoolchildren

The Alfred Sampath Foundation will distribute stationery and uniforms to schoolchildren today at 2.00 pm at the Gothamipura Community Hall, Borella under the patronage of Colombo Municipal Councilor Alfred Sampath. Colombo Mayor Rosy Senanayake and CMC members will participate.
