## Title: President Maithripala Sirisena participated in religious observances at St Anne’s Shrine in Thalawila
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 14, 2018 - 01:00

President Maithripala Sirisena participated in religious observances at St Anne’s Shrine in Thalawila

President Maithripala Sirisena participated in religious observances at St Anne’s Shrine in Thalawila yesterday. A souvenir to mark the 250th anniversary of the Shrine was also presented to the President on the occasion. Chilaw Bishop Rt. Rev. Dr. Valence Mendis blessing the President. Priests representing the Chilaw Diocese and politicians in the area participated. (Picture by President’s Media Division).
