## Title: Motorcycles for staff in several health services
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, September 29, 2018 - 01:00

Motorcycles for staff in several health services

Motorcycles will be provided for 2,886 health staffers of the Health Promotion and Diseases Preventive Services at a cost of Rs. 865 million, Health Ministry Spokesman said.The Cabinet has approved the offer for 140 public health nurses, 762 Public Health Inspectors (PHIs), 210 Public Health Nursing Officials, 175 Entomologists, 1,560 midwives and 39 Public Health Field Officers.
