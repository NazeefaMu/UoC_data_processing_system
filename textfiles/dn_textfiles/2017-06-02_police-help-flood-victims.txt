## Title: Police help flood victims
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, June 2, 2017 - 01:00

Police help flood victims

Kirulapone Police Chief Inspector Upul Samarasinghe along with other officers attached organized a community service project to distribute sanitary items, 250 mega water bottles and dinner for flood victims who had taken refuge at several locations.
Relief items were distributed in Provincial Secretariat Divisions such as Kolonnawa South and East, and Meethotamulla area. Kirulapone Police also organized a medical camp for the displaced sheltered at Vidyawardena Junior College of Wellampitiya. Dr. Rasika Bandara of Oxfred Lanka Medical Institute provided voluntary service in this regard.
