## Title: Singer Cup U19 Division 1 Schools K/O: St. Peter’s meets S. Thomas’ in quarters today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, March 31, 2017 - 01:00

Singer Cup U19 Division 1 Schools K/O: St. Peter’s meets S. Thomas’ in quarters today

Defending joint champions St. Peter’s College Bambalapitiya will meet S. Thomas’ College Mt. Lavinia in the quarterfinal of the Singer Trophy Under 19 division 1 inter-schools two-day cricket tournament at Thurstan College ground today and tomorrow.
S. Thomas’ advanced to the quarterfinals as champions of group ‘C’ and St. Peter’s came through as runner-up of group ‘A’.
Romesh Nallaperuma will lead S. Thomas’ and Lakshina Rodrigo will captain St. Peter’s.
Richmond College Galle, Thurstan College and Ananda College have advanced to the semi-finals so far.
Winner of this match will meet Ananda in one semi-final. -YK
