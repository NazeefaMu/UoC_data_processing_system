## Title: Singer Cup U19 Div 2 Schools (2-day) K/O : St. Anne’s Kurunegala emerge champs
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, April 1, 2017 - 01:00

Singer Cup U19 Div 2 Schools (2-day) K/O : St. Anne’s Kurunegala emerge champs

 St. Anne’s College Kurunegala emerged champion of the Singer Cup under 19 inter-schools Division Two Limited Overs cricket tournament when they beat Sri Devananda College Ambalangoda by 30 runs according to D/L system in the final played at Colts ground yesterday. The match was stopped due to rain and it was rescheduled for 33 overs with a winning target of 194. Sri Devananda were bowled out 163 in 31.1 overs.
Scores:
St. Anne’s: 248 in 49.5 overs. Randeera Ranasinghe 54, Wanitha Wanninayake 52, Kavindu Ranasinghe 34, Pubudu Ganegama 30, Charith Rukshan 3/45, Amindu Udara 2/16)
Sri Devananda: 163 in 31.1 overs (Charith Chinthaka 47 n.o., Chathura Milan 22, Pubudu Ganegama 3/23) - (YK)
