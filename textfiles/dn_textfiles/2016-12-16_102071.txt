## Title: Disabled man nabbed with kasippu
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 16, 2016 - 01:00

Disabled man nabbed with kasippu

Mihintale Police raided an illicit hooch den in a residence at Samagipura and arrested a suspect with 125 bottles of kasippu stored in cans kept for sale on Unduwap Poya Day on December 13. The suspect is a disabled person who had suffered the disability after trampling a trap gun placed in a jungle to trap game. Here, officers attached to the Mihintale Police examine the kasippu haul.
