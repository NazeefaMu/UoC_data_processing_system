## Title: RDB Bank relocates Ratnapura branch
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, August 10, 2017 - 01:00

RDB Bank relocates Ratnapura branch

Regional Development Bank (RDB), the premier and the fastest growing state owned development bank in the island relocated its Ratnapura bBranch and district office to a more spacious and a conveniently accessible location at Main Street, Ratnapura in a bid to provide a better and modern service experience to customers in the area.
With the relocation of its branch, RDB renewed its genuine banking relationship with the individuals and the businessmen in the area, opening its doors to the aspiring entrepreneurs to benefit from the banks many customized financial facilities and advisory services. Distributing of credit facilities and gift items of the highest achievers of children’s savings accounts were delivered with parallel to the event.
RDB General Manager and CEO T.A. Ariyapala, Regional General Manger- Sabaragamuwa Gamini Abeyratne, Assistant General Manager Gnanaratne Mapalagama, District Manager Vijayananda Bodhipakshe, Branch Manageress D.R.D.P. Nishanthi and staff of the RDB together with customers participated at the opening ceremony.
