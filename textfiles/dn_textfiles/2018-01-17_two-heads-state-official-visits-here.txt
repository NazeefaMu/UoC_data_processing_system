## Title: Two Heads of State on official visits here
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 17, 2018 - 01:00

Two Heads of State on official visits here

Two State leaders are to pay official visits to Sri Lanka next week, a Foreign Ministry source said.
The two Heads of State are Singapore Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong and Indonesian President Joko Widodo who are visiting Sri Lanka on the invitation of President Maithripala Sirisena.
Singapore Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong will arrive in Sri Lanka on January 22 while Indonesian President Joko Widodo will visit Sri Lanka on January 25.
A Free Trade Agreement (FTA) is to be signed between Sri Lanka and Singapore during the official visit of Singapore Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong.
Both Heads of State are to hold discussions with President Maithripala Sirisena and Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe during their official visits on strengthening bilateral relations.
