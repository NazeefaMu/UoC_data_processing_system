## Title: Saman Ekanayake appointed Secretary to PM
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, December 17, 2018 - 01:00

Saman Ekanayake appointed Secretary to PM

Saman Ekanayake has been appointed as the Secretary to the Prime Minister by President Maithripala Sirisena in accordance with the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, a media release issued by the Prime Minister’s Office said.
