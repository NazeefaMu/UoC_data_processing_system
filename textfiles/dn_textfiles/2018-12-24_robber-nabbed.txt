## Title: Robber nabbed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, December 24, 2018 - 01:00

Robber nabbed

Wellawatte police arrested a robber who had broken into six houses last week. Police said the suspect has been a Boossa Prison inmate who had been released for two weeks.
He was identified as an IRC called Velaudhan Sureshalias alias Bomba Suresh or Kabaraya. He has been sentenced to a long prison term.
Police said he will be handed over to the Bussa Prison.
