## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features

Srimath offers calendars and diaries free

Katahanda Media Centre has taken steps to distribute calendars and diaries free of charge. Anyone interested in obtaining the calendars and diaries free may contact Katahanda Media Centre Director Srimath Indrajith Liyanage on 0773749767.
This is Liyanage’s corporate social responsibility performance carried out for the 10th consecutive time. For more information visit www.srimathliyanage.com.
