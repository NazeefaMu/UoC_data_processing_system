## Title: PINDAPATHA BY 500 MONKS ON SECOND DAY OF DIYAWANNA VESAK ZONE
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local

PINDAPATHA BY 500 MONKS ON SECOND DAY OF DIYAWANNA VESAK ZONE

Over 500 Buddhist monks were on Pindapatha from the Parliamentary Complex to the Speaker’s residence on the second day of the Diyawanna Vesak Zone.  Picture shows members of the Maha Sangha on Pindapatha. (Inset): A member of the Muslim community serving alms to a Buddhist monk.
