## Title: Prize-giving ceremony of Makthab Unit held
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, December 19, 2017 - 01:00

Prize-giving ceremony of Makthab Unit held

The prize-giving ceremony of Makthab Unit of the All Ceylon Jamiyathul Ulema Organization was recently held, sponsored by the Board of Trustees of Al-Jamiyathul Fakhira Jumma Masjid at the Mosque premises, to celebrate the Holy Prophet Muhammad’s (Sal) birthday.
