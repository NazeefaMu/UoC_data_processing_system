## Title: President Maithripala Sirisena in Kathmandu
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, August 30, 2018 - 01:08

President Maithripala Sirisena in Kathmandu

President Maithripala Sirisena arrived in Kathmandu, Nepal yesterday afternoon to attend the fourth Summit of the Bay of Bengal Initiative for Multisectoral Technical and Economic Cooperation (BIMSTEC), which will be held in Kathmandu today and tomorrow. Nepal’s Deputy Prime Minister and Defence Minister Ishwar Pokhrel welcoming the President at the airport. Picture by Sudath Silva
