## Title: LB Finance expands to provide better, convenient Financial Services to customers
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Monday, August 27, 2018 - 01:00

LB Finance expands to provide better, convenient Financial Services to customers

LB Finance, Sri Lanka’s trail-blazer in innovative financial services recently opened and relocated new branches and centers around the island to provide better and convenient service for the customers.
Malabe branch opening highlights 
Henceforth the Kandana and the Malabe branches too were relocated at new premises which are more convenient for the customers in these areas. The Kandana Branch was relocated at No 39, Colombo Road, Kandana, while the Malabe Branch was relocated at No 835/2A, Thalangama North, Malabe.
The Kandana branch was declared open by Deputy General Managers, Marlon Perera and Ainsley Motha. While the Malabe branch was opened by Executive Director, Ravi Yatawara and Deputy General Manager, Marlon Perera.
The Senior Management along with the staff of LB Finance and the community of the area both business and retail were also present at the ceremonies.
Public in these areas and the surrounding areas can now experience the wide range of benefits offered by LB Finance, which is a leading and stable Finance Organization with a
proud history of around 47 years. LB Finance ensures that customers get the best service in town with innovative financial solutions and speedy service.
The financial facilities range from leasing, gold loans, mortgage loans, factoring, housing loans, educational loans, margin trading and investment opportunities such as Fixed deposits and Savings accounts and many other financial solutions with competitive benefits and returns.
Customers can enjoy hazel free services to facilitate their financial needs from the new and upgraded units.
Established in 1971, LB Finance has become a trusted and a much respected financial company in Sri Lanka. As a finance company close to the hearts of Sri Lankans, LB Finance will continue to offer attractive financial solutions to the citizens. LB Finance PLC’s long-term rating of ‘A-(lka)’ Outlook Stable was affirmed by Fitch Ratings Lanka Ltd during the annual ratings review of NBFI.
