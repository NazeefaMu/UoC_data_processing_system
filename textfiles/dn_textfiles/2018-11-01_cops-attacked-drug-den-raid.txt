## Title: Cops attacked in drug den raid
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 1, 2018 - 01:00

Cops attacked in drug den raid

Two suspects who attacked two constables wounding them during a raid on a drug den were taken into custody by the Galewela Police yesterday.
The incident occurred at a house in Kiralagolla area yesterday dawn when officers attempted to arrest the drug smugglers.The house was an illegal drug den which belonged to the suspects and used by the smugglers as a gambling center. The two constables who were injured were admitted to the Galewela and Dambulla hospitals.
