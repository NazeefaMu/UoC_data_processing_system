## Title: COMBANK receives 18th Best Bank award  from Global Finance
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Friday, October 27, 2017 - 01:00

COMBANK receives 18th Best Bank award  from Global Finance

The Commercial Bank of Ceylon recentlyreceived the Global Finance award for Sri Lanka’s Best Bank in 2017 at an awards ceremony at the National Press Club in Washington DC during the IMF/World Bank Annual Meeting. This is the 18th year that Commercial Bank has won this prestigious award.The Bank’s Chairman Dharma Dheerasinghe and Managing Director Jegan Durairatnam accepted the award on behalf of the Bank.
