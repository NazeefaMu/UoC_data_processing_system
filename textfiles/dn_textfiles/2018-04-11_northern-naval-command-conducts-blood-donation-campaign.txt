## Title: Northern Naval Command conducts blood donation campaign
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, April 11, 2018 - 01:00

Northern Naval Command conducts blood donation campaign

Naval personnel of the Northern Naval Command organised a blood donation at the Northern Province Central Blood Bank, Teaching Hospital, Jaffna, on April 7 as a part of Navy’s social responsibility project, .
It was organised under the patronage of Commander Northern Naval Area Rear Admiral Meril Wickramasinghe on a request made by Dr. K.D.C. Wimalarathna of the Jaffna Teaching Hospital. A large number of donors took part in the campaign.
Jaffna Teaching Hospital Director Dr. T Sathyamoorthi and the hospital staff participated.
