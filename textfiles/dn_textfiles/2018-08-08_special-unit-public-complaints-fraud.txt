## Title: Special unit for public complaints on fraud
## Supertitle: Moragahakanda, Kalu Ganga Development project
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 8, 2018 - 01:00

Moragahakanda, Kalu Ganga Development project
Special unit for public complaints on fraud

A special unit has been established to entertain public complaints regarding any acts of fraud or corruption involved in the construction work of the Moragahakanda, Kalu Ganga Development project, with sufficient evidence.
This is in keeping with an assurance made by President Maithripala Sirisena during the water retention ceremony of the Kalu Ganga reservoir held on July 23, a press release by the President’s Media Unit said.
All such complaints should be sent to Director Investigations, Mahaweli Development and Environment Ministry, Sobadam Piyasa, No. 416/C/1, Robert Gunawardene Mawatha, Battaramulla or call 011-2034159 or fax 011-2879976.
The Moragahakanda - Kalu Ganga Reservoir Project, costing Rs. 230,000 million is the largest multi-purpose development project to be undertaken in the country and the President’s desire is to complete it systematically and efficiently to bestow all its benefits on the people, the release said.
