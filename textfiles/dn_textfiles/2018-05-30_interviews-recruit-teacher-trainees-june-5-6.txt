## Title: Interviews to recruit teacher-trainees on June 5, 6
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 30, 2018 - 01:00

Interviews to recruit teacher-trainees on June 5, 6

Education Minister Akila Viraj Kariyawasam said today (28) in Kuliyapitiya, Kurunegala that his Ministry has taken steps to recruit 4,746 teacher-trainees to 19 National Colleges of Education countrywide this year and interviews in this regard will be held on June 5 and 6.
Those who have passed the GCE (A/L ) Exam-2015 will be recruited for the position. The Minister said the letters to enroll the applicants will be posted next week.
