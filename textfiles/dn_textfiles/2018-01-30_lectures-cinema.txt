## Title: Lectures on cinema
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Tuesday, January 30, 2018 - 01:00

Lectures on cinema

Dil Films International (Pvt) Ltd who had provided the latest technology to film movies for 35 consecutive years have made way for those who are interested in filmmaking to learn the ropes of the subject. Praveen Jayaratne will be putting together a series of lectures by renowned filmmakers in the scene. Those who take part in these events will also get the opportunity to learn how to work with some of the latest technological equipment in the field of filmmaking. Further details of the event can be obtained from 517/18, Kaduwela Road, Thalangama North, Battaramulla or by calling 0112789827.
