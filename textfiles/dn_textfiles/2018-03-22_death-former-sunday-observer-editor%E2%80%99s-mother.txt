## Title: Death of former Sunday Observer Editor’s mother
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, March 22, 2018 - 01:00

Death of former Sunday Observer Editor’s mother

The death occurred of retired English trained teacher Mrs U. Mercy Edna (83), mother of former Sunday Observer Editor-in-Chief Dinesh Weerawansa. She was the wife of former Teachers’ Training College lecturer, late W.Cosmas Damian.
The body is lying at the Western Funeral Parlour, Coppara Junction, Negombo (near Maris Stella College).Cortege leaves for the burial at Sellakanda Roman Catholic Cemetery, Dalupotha, Negombo at 4 pm today.
