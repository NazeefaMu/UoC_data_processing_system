## Title: Brilliant century by Tharusha Nethsara
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, October 14, 2016 - 01:00

Brilliant century by Tharusha Nethsara

 
 A brilliant 116 runs by Tharusha Nethsara which included eighteen fours and two sixes and facing 120 balls and occupying the crease for 88 minutes helped Royal College Panadura to defeat Basilica College Ragama by ten wickets in their inter school under 13 division one tournament cricket match conduced by Sri Lanka Schools Cricket Association and worked off at Panadura.
Batting first Panadura Royalists declared at 202 for 7 and Sachin Chamuditha too contributed 47 runs and in reply visitors made 115 runs with Rositha Silva scoring 50 runs and Sachith Chamuditha claimed 4 for 16 and following on visitors Collapsed for 98 runs with Thivanka Silva capturing 8 for 27 Homesters notched up the required score for no loss. Thivanka Silva had a match bag of 11 for 58 (3/31 and 8/27).
Royal College Panadura – 207/7 dec Tharusha Nethsara 116, Sachin Chamuditha 47, Rohitha Silva 2/71, Yasith Ishina 2/38, Dilan Noel 2/48 and 10/0.
