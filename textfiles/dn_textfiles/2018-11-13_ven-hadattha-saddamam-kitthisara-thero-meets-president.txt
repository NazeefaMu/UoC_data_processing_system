## Title: Ven. Hadattha Saddamam Kitthisara thero meets President
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, November 13, 2018 - 01:00

Ven. Hadattha Saddamam Kitthisara thero meets President

Ven. Hadattha Saddamam Kitthisara of the Ayungsan Thawaya Thako Meditation Centre, Yangoon, Myanmar, met President Maithripala Sirisena at the President’s official residence in Colombo and presented him with his collection of essays on meditation. Amarapura Nikaya Central and Wayamba Chief Vice Sanga Leader and Chief Prelate of the Pologolla Sri Salawana Bodhi Maha Viharaya, Ven. Seelananda Nahimi Thera was also present on the occasion.
