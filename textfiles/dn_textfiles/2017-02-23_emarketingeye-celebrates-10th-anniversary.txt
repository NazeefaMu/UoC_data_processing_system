## Title: eMarketingEye celebrates 10th anniversary
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, February 23, 2017 - 01:00

eMarketingEye celebrates 10th anniversary

eMarketingEye (www.emarketingeye.com), the award-winning Sri Lankan Digital Marketing Agency and a pioneer in the industry, is celebrating its 10th anniversary.
Established in 2007, eMarketingEye has grown to be one of the best in the Asian region over the past ten years, having gained international recognition for its expertise in providing end-to-end online marketing solutions to the travel and hospitality industry.
The company is a Google AdWords and Analytics Certified Partner Company that offers the total range of online marketing services including Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Web Development, Pay Per Click Marketing (PPC), Social Media Optimization, Online Marketing Consultancy, Content Development, Internet Booking Engine, E-Mail Marketing, Web Analytics and Hotel Fact Sheet Development.
eMarketingEye is the only digital marketing agency in Sri Lanka specialising in the travel and hospitality industry. It provides integrated marketing solutions for a diverse range of clients and has garnered an impressive portfolio in this 10-year period. A majority of eMarketingEye’s client list includes leading international travel and hospitality brands.
The CEO of eMarketingEye, Rajitha Dahanayake, was also the recipient of the coveted ‘National Young Entrepreneur Award in the Extra Large Category’ at the Sri Lanka Entrepreneur of the Year awards in 2013. Commenting on the 10th anniversary, Dahanayake said “It has been anextraordinary10-year journey where we have achieved many great things. I would like to thank our valued clientele, some of them who have been with us since the beginning, for placing their trust in us at all times. Our ethos is to provide complete transparency on all online marketing activities while offering clients ROI-centric solutions that are tailor-made to their requirements. I must also thank the wonderful eMarketingEye team for constantly pushing the boundaries and keeping alive their desire to learn and innovate.”
Expressing his views on the current trend in the market, Dahanayake stated, “Content Marketing has seen a major growth over the years and has become the latest trend in the Online Marketing industry, with a focus on engaging users on platforms that provide attractive, timely and quality content.
In fact, this concept is not something new to eMarketingEye, as we have been incorporating Content Marketing into our SEO strategy over the past few years. At present, we have largest clientele in the market and produce close to 20,000 content related blogs, articles and social posts annually. We are also renowned for our travel content marketing platform which is the largest of its kind owned by an agency in the Asian region.”
