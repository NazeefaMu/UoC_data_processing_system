## Title: Stamp to mark the 170th anniversary of St. Mathew's College
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, August 3, 2017 - 01:00

Stamp to mark the 170th anniversary of St. Mathew's College

 Issuance of a new postage stamp to mark the 170th anniversary of St. Mathew's College took place under the patronage of Postal Services and Muslim Religious Affairs Minister Abdul Haleem and Deputy Speaker Thilanga Sumathipala, yesterday. Here, Minister Haleem handing over the new postage stamp to the Deputy Speaker. Principal U. D. S. Wickremarathna was also present. Picture by Wasitha Patabendige
