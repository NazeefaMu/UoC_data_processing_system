## Title: UPFA hands over Badulla LGE nominations
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Tuesday, December 12, 2017 - 01:00

UPFA hands over Badulla LGE nominations

The United Peoples Freedom Alliance (UPFA) yesterday handed over nominations for several local authorities in the Badulla district.
The UPFA handed over nominations for the Badulla Municipal Council and Haputale Urban Council and six Pradeshiya Sabhas namely Lunugala, Ella, Badulla, Meegahakivula, Kandaketiya, and Haldumuula.
The nominations were handed over to the District Returning Officer by an UPFA team led by Minister Nimal Siripala de Silva.
