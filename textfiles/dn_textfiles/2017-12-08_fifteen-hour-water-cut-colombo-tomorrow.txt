## Title: Fifteen-hour water cut in Colombo tomorrow
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 8, 2017 - 14:50

Fifteen-hour water cut in Colombo tomorrow

 
Water supply to several areas in Colombo will be interrupted for fifteen- hours commencing from 2 p.m., tomorrow.
Water supply to Moragasmulla, Rajagiriya, Obeysekerapura, Bandaranaikepura and areas surrounding the Rajagiriya and Nawala will be interrupted due to an urgent repair in a main pipeline carrying water to these areas.
