## Title: Nawaloka Ground Welisara donates flood relief to schoolchildren
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Wednesday, July 12, 2017 - 01:00

Nawaloka Ground Welisara donates flood relief to schoolchildren

The Nawaloka Ground Welisara Cricket Club, as a Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) donated school books and stationary to 368 schoolchildren affected by the recent floods to a remote school in Ratnapura, under the patronage of the Mudduwa Grama Niladhari.
The President and members of the club made this donation a succes by collecting funds from club members and other donors in the area. The fund allocated for this CSR initiative was almost two lakhs. Here members of the club are seen making the donation to children in the district.
