## Title: St. Joseph’s beat D.S. Senanayake
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, October 4, 2017 - 01:00

St. Joseph’s beat D.S. Senanayake

 St. Joseph’s College Colombo beat D.S. Senanayake College Colombo by seven wickets in their ‘Singer Trophy’ Under 19 inter Schools Cricket match played at Bloomfield ground yesterday.
Revan Kelly, Ashen Daniel and Salinda Seneviratne played key roles in St. Joseph’s victory Revan Kelly made a century and Ashen Daniel and Salinda Seneviratne had match bags of nine wickets each
Kalhara Senarathne (St. Anthony’s Katugastota) gave a fine all-round performanes against Kingswood Kandy. He scored a century and took 3 wickets in the match.
Both from Sri Rahula Katugatota Chamika Samarasekara and Lahiru Wijerathne scored centuries against Nugawela Central College. Kavindu Nimesh of Siri Piyarathana Padukka made a century against Ananda Shastralaya Kotte yesterday.
Ravindu Suharshana (Maris Stella Negombo), Rukmal Disanayake (Dharmaraja Kandy), Deegau Bandara (Kingswood Kandy), Pethum Madhushanka (Devapathiraja Rathgama) and Dinidiya Polkotuwa (Nugawela Central) gave fine bowling performances yesterday.
