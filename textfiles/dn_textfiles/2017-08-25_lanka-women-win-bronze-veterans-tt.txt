## Title: Lanka women win bronze at Veterans TT
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, August 25, 2017 - 01:00

Lanka women win bronze at Veterans TT

 The Sri Lanka Women’s table tennis team comprising (from left) Nilmini Wijetunge, Tamara Hewage and Sudharma won a bronze in the 50 age group category at the recently concluded SAF Veterans Games held at the S. Thomas’ College indoor sports complex, Mt Lavinia. Tamara and Nilmini also won a bronze in the 50 age category women’s doubles.
