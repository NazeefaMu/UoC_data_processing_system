## Title: Choral bliss
## Supertitle: 
## Section: T&C
## Date: Tuesday, October 30, 2018 - 01:00

Choral bliss

Sri Lanka’s largest school based choir concert, Festival of Choirs, organised by the Western Music Society of Royal College, Colombo, is set to unravel on October 30 at the Bishops College Auditorium, from 6.30 pm.
The event will witness the participation of choirs from Visakha Vidyalaya, Methodist College, S. Thomas’ College, Lyceum International School, Wesley College, and Royal College, among other schools.
