## Title: World T20  - England beat New Zeland
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, March 30, 2016 - 22:30

World T20  - England beat New Zeland

Chasing a modest 154-run target, England batsmen fired in unison to beat New Zealand by seven wickets to enter the final of 2016 World Twenty20. England won with 17 balls remaining.
New Zealand 153/8 (20/20 ov)
England 159/3 (17.1/20 ov)
