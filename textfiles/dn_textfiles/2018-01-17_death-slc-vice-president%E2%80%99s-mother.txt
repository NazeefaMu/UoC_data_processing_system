## Title: Death of SLC vice- president’s mother
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, January 17, 2018 - 01:00

Death of SLC vice- president’s mother

Sri Lanka Cricket announced the demise of the beloved mother of K. Mathivanan, vice president of Sri Lanka Cricket.
Her remains lie at the Jayaratne funeral parlour. Cortege will leave the parlour at 11 am today for cremation at the General Cemetery, Borella.
