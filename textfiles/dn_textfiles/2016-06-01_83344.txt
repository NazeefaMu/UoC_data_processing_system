## Title: Drawn match at Panadura
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, June 1, 2016 - 01:00

Drawn match at Panadura

Royal College, Panadura drew with St. Anthony's College, Wattala in their inter-school under 15 division one tournament cricket match played at Panadura.
Taking the strike early homesters declared at 141 for 8 with Prasanna Pushpakumara scoring 47 and W. A. Tharusha claimed 4 for 10. At stumps visitors were 83 for 4.
Royal College – 141/8 dec. - Prasanna Pushpakuamra 47, Ranesh Danidu 21, Sandeepa Kalhara 20, W. A. Tharusha 4/10, Rashmika Mevan 2/20, U. C. Dilshan 2/40.
St. Anthony's College – 83/4 – Oshanda Jayatilleke 36, Prashan Kalhara 2/22.
