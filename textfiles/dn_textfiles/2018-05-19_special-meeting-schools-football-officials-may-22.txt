## Title: Special meeting for schools football officials on May 22
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, May 19, 2018 - 01:00

Special meeting for schools football officials on May 22

The Sri Lanka Schools Football Association will hold a special meeting for all masters in charge and coaches of schools football on May 22 (Tuesday) at Royal College Colombo at 1 pm.
Nalanda Dissanayake, general secretary of Sri Lanka Schools Football Association requests principals of all 194 member schools to send their football masters in charge and coaches for this important meeting as matters related to football tournaments for 2018 will be discussed.
