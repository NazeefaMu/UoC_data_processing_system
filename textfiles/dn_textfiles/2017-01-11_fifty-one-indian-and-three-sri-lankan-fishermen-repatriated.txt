## Title: Fifty one Indian and three Sri Lankan fishermen repatriated
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 11, 2017 - 01:00

Fifty one Indian and three Sri Lankan fishermen repatriated

Fifty one Indian and three Sri Lankan fishermen who were held in custody in respective countries for violating International Maritime Boundary Line and engaging in illegal fishing practices, were released from custody and repatriated to their counties yesterday. The Sri Lanka Navy assisted in the repatriation of fishermen in the International Maritime Boundary Line North of Kankesanthurai.
Navy’s Landing Craft L 820, Sri Lanka Coast Guard Craft ‘CG 48’ and Indian Coast Guard Ship ‘Sagar’ were deployed for the repatriation mission.
