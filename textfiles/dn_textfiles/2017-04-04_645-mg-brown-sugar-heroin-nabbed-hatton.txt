## Title: 645 mg brown sugar heroin nabbed in Hatton
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, April 4, 2017 - 11:45

645 mg brown sugar heroin nabbed in Hatton

 
The Hatton Excise Office officers have nabbed 645 mg of brown sugar heroin from Kudagama, Hatton, last night.
Excise Office Officer In Charge T.M.G. Thilekeratne said that nine people were arrested along with the narcotics.
The Excise Office OIC said that the suspects will be produced before the Hatton Magistrate Courts today.
Further investigations are underway.
