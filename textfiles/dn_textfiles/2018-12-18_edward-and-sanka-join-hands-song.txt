## Title: Edward and Sanka join hands for a song
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Tuesday, December 18, 2018 - 01:00

Edward and Sanka join hands for a song

Seasoned musician Edward Jayakody and popular vocalist Sanka Dineth have come together for a duet. The song titled ‘Asiri Surakimu Samonala’ was launched recently. The song focuses on the tragic situation that Adam’s Peak is facing today. It sends across the message that Adam’s Peak should not be littered with rubbish.
Tharaka Ravinath had penned the lyrics of the song. Sanka had set the tune of the song.
The music video had been captured in Adam’s Peak. Link Natural Products had produced the song as a social service initiative.
