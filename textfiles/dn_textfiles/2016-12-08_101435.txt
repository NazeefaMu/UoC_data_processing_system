## Title: Injured housemaid to be brought home
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, December 8, 2016 - 01:00

Injured housemaid to be brought home

Foreign Employment Minister Thalatha Athukorala yesterday said that arrangements will be taken to bring the Sri Lankan woman who is receiving treatment at a hospital in Kuwait following an accident to Sri Lanka soon.
She said the Sri Lankan mission in Kuwait will make necessary arrangements to bring her back to the country. The woman had fallen from the upstair house which she was working in December last year
However, she had made a statement that she was thrown out from the top floor by her employer.
The victim is a resident of Kokunewa, Ranorawa. Her family members have also sought help from the ministry to bring her back to the country.
