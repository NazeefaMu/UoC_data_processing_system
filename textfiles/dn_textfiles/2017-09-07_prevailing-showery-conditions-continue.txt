## Title: Prevailing showery conditions to continue
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 11:00

Prevailing showery conditions to continue

 
The Meteorology Department said that the prevailing showery condition is expected to continue particularly in the South-western part of the country.
Heavy falls about 100-150 mm are expected at some places, particularly in the Uva province and in Ampara and Batticaloa districts.
The Meteorology department requested the public to take adequate precautions to minimize damages caused by lightning activity.
