## Title: Cabinet reshuffle at 10 a.m. tomorrow
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, April 30, 2018 - 13:59

Cabinet reshuffle at 10 a.m. tomorrow

 
The proposed Cabinet reshuffle will take place at 10 a.m. tomorrow.
All Ministers and Parliamentarians were notified to be present at the Presidential Secretariat at 9.30 a.m., tomorrow.
The decision was made to further stabilize and reform the unity government after being defeated at the recently held Local Government Elections.
The UNP only secured 33 percent of the votes and the SLFP together with the UPFA secured 13 percent of the overall votes at the Local Government Elections.
