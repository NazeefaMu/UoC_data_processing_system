## Title: Police arrest 58 youth in FB party
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 29, 2018 - 01:00

Police arrest 58 youth in FB party

Haputhale Police have taken 58 youths including a girl along with illegal narcotics into custody organised through social media party last evening.
These persons had gathered at a party organised via facebook at the Viharagala area, Haputale while they were taken into custody with illegal narcotics by police.
The persons, taken into custody are around the aged 23, 24 and 25 while the girl is also 23. These suspects are residents of Badulla, Passara and suburbs. The party had been organised by a resident of Pahalagama in Badulla.
