## Title: Singer U19 Cricket: Thomians win Gilmour Jayasuriya challenge trophy
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, January 26, 2018 - 01:00

Singer U19 Cricket: Thomians win Gilmour Jayasuriya challenge trophy

S. Thomas’ College Mt. Lavinia won the Gilmour Jayasuriya Challenge Trophy with a first innings win over St. Joseph’s College Colombo in their ‘Singer Cup’ Under 19 inter-schools cricket match played at Mt Lavinia yesterday.
Dunith Wellalage (St. Joseph’s) was adjudged man of the match and Manthila Wijeratne (S. Thomas’) the best batsman. Ashen Daniel (St. Joseph’s) won the best bowler’s award.
Vinura Dulsara of Mahinda Galle scored his third century of the season with 123 against De Mazenod College Kandana at Galle.
