## Title: Grenade explosion at Trincomalee Air Force firing range
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 16:15

Grenade explosion at Trincomalee Air Force firing range

 
A grenade accidently exploded during a training session at the Air Force firing range in Morawewa, Trincomalee.
Four soldiers were injured and rushed to the Morawewa  hospital.
