## Title: Free medical camp at Sirikotha
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 2, 2017 - 01:00

Free medical camp at Sirikotha

A free medical camp organised by the United National Art Forum to mark Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe’s 40 years of uninterrupted political life, was held at the UNP Headquarters ‘Sirikotha’ in Pitakotte last Sunday. It was held under the patronage of UNP General Secretary Minister Kabir Hashim. Minister S.B.Navinna also participated.
