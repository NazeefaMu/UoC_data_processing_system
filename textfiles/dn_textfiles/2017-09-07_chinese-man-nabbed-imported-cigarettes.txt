## Title: Chinese man nabbed with imported cigarettes
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 10:45

Chinese man nabbed with imported cigarettes

 
A Chinese man was nabbed with 1,200 packets of imported cigarettes in Colpetty earlier today.
The suspect was released on police bail.
He is due to arrive before the Colombo Fort Magistrate Courts today.
Colpetty Police is investigating.
