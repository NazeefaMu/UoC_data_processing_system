## Title: Three more ‘Aava’ group members arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, August 10, 2017 - 10:30

Three more ‘Aava’ group members arrested

 
Three more ‘Aava’ group members were taken into custody by the Chunnakam Police last night.
The suspects, Thureisinghem Raj Kumar alias Ajith Kumar and Ravichandran Stralington alias SR are resides of Manippai and Krishnapillai Kirubbakaran alias Kisba resides in Chunnakam.
Chunnakam Police said that the suspects will be produced before the Mallakam Magistrate courts today.
Further investigations are underway.
