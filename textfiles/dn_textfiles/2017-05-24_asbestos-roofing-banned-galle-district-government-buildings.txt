## Title: Asbestos roofing banned in Galle district government buildings
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 24, 2017 - 01:00

Asbestos roofing banned in Galle district government buildings

The Chief Secretary of the Southern Provincial Council has prohibited the use of asbestos roofing sheets on all government buildings being built in the province. Issuing a circular in this regard he has ordered that thick iron sheets are used for roofing in buildings now coming up in the province as well as those planned for the future. At the Galle District Environmental Committee meeting held yesterday, Galle GA S. T. Kodikara advised all heads of institutions to stop using asbestos roofing sheets in all government buildings built in the district.
