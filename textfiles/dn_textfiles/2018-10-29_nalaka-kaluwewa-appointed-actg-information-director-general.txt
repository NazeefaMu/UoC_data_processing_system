## Title: Nalaka Kaluwewa appointed Actg. Information Director General
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 29, 2018 - 01:00

Nalaka Kaluwewa appointed Actg. Information Director General

Acting Additional Secretary of the Presidential Secretariat, Nalaka Kaluwewa has been appointed Acting Director General of Information. This appointment has been made under the instructions of President Maithripala Sirisena and subject to the approval of the Cabinet, the President’s Media Unit said.
