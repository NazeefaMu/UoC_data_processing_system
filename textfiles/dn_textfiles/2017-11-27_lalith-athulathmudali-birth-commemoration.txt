## Title: Lalith Athulathmudali Birth commemoration
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, November 27, 2017 - 01:00

Lalith Athulathmudali Birth commemoration

A commemorative ceremony to mark the 81st birth anniversary of the late Minister, Lalith Athulathmudali, was held yesterday (26) with the placing of floral tributes at the foot of his statue at the Royal College Roundabout. Parliamentarian Ravi Karunanayake, Lotteries Board Chairperson Shermila Perera, Kesaralal Gunasekera and Provincial Councillor Samaraweera Weeraman participated. Picture by Ranjith Asanka.
