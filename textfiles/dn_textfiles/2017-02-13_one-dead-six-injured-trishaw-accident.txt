## Title: One dead, six injured in trishaw accident
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, February 13, 2017 - 01:00

One dead, six injured in trishaw accident

One person died and six others injured and hospitalised when a three wheeler carrying a group of pilgrims after a pooja at a kovil in Dickoya, Hatton, ran off the road and plummeted down a 20 ft precipice along an estate road around 12.30 a.m. on February 10.
The deceased was identified as S.Rajaratnam, 47, of Ketambulawa, Nawalapitiya.
Two children and a woman were among the injured. Hatton Police investigating the incident said according to the driver's statement, a break failure had caused the accident.
