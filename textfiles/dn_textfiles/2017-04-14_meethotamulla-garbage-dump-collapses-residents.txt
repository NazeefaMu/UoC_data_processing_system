## Title: Meethotamulla garbage dump collapses on residents
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, April 14, 2017 - 17:15

Meethotamulla garbage dump collapses on residents

DEVELOPING STORY
Parts of the garbage dump in Meethotamulla have collapsed, damaging many homes. 
Two have been injured and rushed to the Colombo National Hospital. 
President Maithripala Sirisena has ordered the Disaster Management Minister and the tri-forces to step in. 
An Airforce helicopter has also been deployed.
