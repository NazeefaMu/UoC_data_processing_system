## Title: ComBank rights issue oversubscribed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, June 22, 2017 - 01:00

ComBank rights issue oversubscribed

Commercial Bank announced that the Rights issue of shares approved by shareholders at the Extraordinary General Meeting held on May 19 to issue 84,649,465 Voting shares and 5,811,601 Non-voting shares was oversubscribed.
The only Sri Lankan bank to be ranked among the Top 1000 banks of the world for six years consecutively, Commercial Bank operates a network of 256 branches and 668 ATMs in Sri Lanka.
