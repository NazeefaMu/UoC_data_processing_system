## Title: Customs nabs Indian with gold items
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, November 27, 2017 - 01:00

Customs nabs Indian with gold items

An Indian female (45) bound to Chennai by UL 127 at 12:30 hrs on November 11 carrying several gold pieces was intercepted by Airport Security personnel and handed over to the Customs. The gold pieces were hidden in her hand luggage.
Gold pieces of 114.28gms valued at LKR 628,540 were seized.
The suspect was released on a penalty of Rs. 10,000. The seized gold items were forfeited.
