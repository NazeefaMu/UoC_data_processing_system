## Title: Two hundred kilos of Kerela ganja seized
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 4, 2017 - 01:00

Two hundred kilos of Kerela ganja seized

A Navy team from the Wayamba Naval Command and Kalpitiya Police seized 200 kg of Kerela ganja hidden in Kaluwarakuda in Wilpattu, following interrogation of 4 suspects arrested during a joint operation conducted recently.
The suspects and the drug haul was handed over to the Kalpitiya Police.
