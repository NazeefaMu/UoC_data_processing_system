## Title: Tharindu Athnayake captures 8/22
## Supertitle: SLC Major Emerging League
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, November 12, 2018 - 01:00

SLC Major Emerging League
Tharindu Athnayake captures 8/22

Tharindu Athnayake, spinner of SSC took 8 wickets for 22 runs against Sri Lanka Under 19’s ‘Red’ in the second inning of the SLC ‘Major Emerging League 2018/19’ tournament at SSC yesterday.
SSC beat Sri Lanka Under 19’s ‘Red’ by innings and 5 runs and CCC beat Sri Lanka Under 19’s ‘Blue’ by 2 wickets.
Madhawa Warnapura of steered CCC to win over Sri Lanka Under 19’s ‘Blue’ at CCC.
