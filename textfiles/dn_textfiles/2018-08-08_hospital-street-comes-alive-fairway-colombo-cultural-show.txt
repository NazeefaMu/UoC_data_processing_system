## Title: Hospital Street comes  alive with Fairway  Colombo Cultural Show
## Supertitle: 
## Section: T&C
## Date: Wednesday, August 8, 2018 - 01:00

Hospital Street comes  alive with Fairway  Colombo Cultural Show

The Fairway Colombo Cultural Show turned out to be yet another whopping success as it attracted many locals and foreigners alike who were entertained by a series of vibrant and colourful cultural dances and traditional performances. The event was graced by Tourism and Christian Religious Affairs Minister John Amaratunga and Public Administration & Management and Law & Order Minister Ranjit Madduma Bandara.
The Cultural Show is one of many events covering most of the days in any given month hosted by Fairway Colombo, organized with the intention of enriching people’s experience by adding fun and colour to evening activities while showcasing local art and cultural talent.
Fairway Colombo Event Calendar at the Hospital Street
The Fairway Colombo Cultural Show – every Monday and Thursday
The Fairway ColomboVinyl Night – every first and third Tuesday
The Fairway ColomboJam on the Street – every first and third Wednesday
The Fairway ColomboArt & Jazz – every second Weekend
The Fairway ColomboLatino Night – every second and fourth Tuesday
The Fairway Colombo Street Food Festival – every last Weekend (Saturday & Sunday)
