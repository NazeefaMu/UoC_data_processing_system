## Title: People’s Insurance re-launches its logo
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Monday, October 30, 2017 - 01:00

People’s Insurance re-launches its logo

People’s Insurance, a leading insurer with seven years of sound insurance portfolio, recently announced the re-launch of its insurance brand. Backed by its stalwart parent companies – the People’s Bank and the People’s Leasing Finance – the People’s Insurance has shown tremendous growth over the years providing innovative solutions to the nation, built on a solid foundation of utmost trust and care.
