## Title: Pension for farmers
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, February 12, 2018 - 01:00

Pension for farmers

About 96,100 farmers of the Kurunegala District have obtained membership in the farmers’ pension scheme and 17,923 members are now drawing a pension, Kurunegala District Agricultural and Agrarian Insurance Fund Assistant Director U.R.A.K.C. Kemadasa told the Daily News yesterday.
