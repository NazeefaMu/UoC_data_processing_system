## Title: Ven. Assaji Mahanayake Thera, new Amarapura Maha Sangha Sabha President
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 31, 2018 - 01:00

Ven. Assaji Mahanayake Thera, new Amarapura Maha Sangha Sabha President

Ven. Shastrapathi Ganthune Assaji Mahanayake Thera of the Amarapura Chulaganthi Nikaya was elected President of the Sri Lanka Amarapura Maha Sangha Sabha at a delegates meeting held at the BMICH, Colombo recently.
Delegates from 22 Chapters of the Nikaya attended the meeting.
Supreme Mahanayake Thera of the Sri Lanka Amarapura Maha Nikaya presided.
The other office bearers elected were, Vice Presidents – Ven. Nuwara Eliye Chandrajothi Mahanayake Thera of the Udarata Amarapura Chapter and Ven. Ahungalle Seelavisuddhi Mahanayake Thera of the Amarapura Saddhammavansa Chapter, Maha Lekakhadhikari Ven. Dr. Pallekande Ratanasara Anunayake Thera of the Amarapura Vajirawansa Chapter, Deputy Maha Lekakhadhikari of the Amarapura Ambagahapitiya Chapter, Assistant Lekakhadikari's Ven. Shastrapathi Balapitiye Sri Seevali Thera, Ven. Shastrapathi Dodampahala Siri Suguna Nayake Thera and Ven. Shastrapathi Beragama Wimalabuddhi Nayaka Thera.
