## Title: Burnt body of man found in house
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 22, 2017 - 01:00

Burnt body of man found in house

A body of a person who had died of burns was found lying on a bed in a house in the Deron Falls division of Petrico Group Bogawanthalawa.
It was identified as that of 70-year-old Subramanium Maradai.The Magisterial Inquiry was due to be held yesterday.
