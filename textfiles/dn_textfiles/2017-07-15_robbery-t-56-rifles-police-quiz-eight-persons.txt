## Title: Robbery of T-56 rifles: Police quiz eight persons
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, July 15, 2017 - 01:00

Robbery of T-56 rifles: Police quiz eight persons

Anuradhapura Police investigating the break in to the armoury of the Sri Lanka Army Armoured Corps Camp at Kelettewa, Anuradhapura, and robbery of two T-56 rifles, are questioning and recording statements from eight persons, including the captain in charge of the armoury. This follows a complaint made to police by a Lance Corporal.
Among others questioned are an army lieutenant, a sergeant, a corporal and three soldiers who were on duty near the armoury, Police said. Investigations are conducted under the direction of SSP Pieris.
