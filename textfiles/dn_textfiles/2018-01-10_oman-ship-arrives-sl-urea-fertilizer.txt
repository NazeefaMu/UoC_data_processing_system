## Title: Oman ship arrives in SL with urea fertilizer
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 10, 2018 - 01:00

Oman ship arrives in SL with urea fertilizer

An Oman ship carrying 48,000 tons of urea fertilizer arrived at Colombo Port on January 8. Director of the Secretariat Ravi Krishan Jayewardena said that 250 Lorries are distributing fertilizer to Anuradhapura, Polonnaruwa, Mahiyanganaya and Hambantota from today.
He further said that, 16,000 tons of urea fertilizers will be released within 3 days. And also, Another ship bringing 32,000 tons of urea fertilizer from Pakistan will reach Colombo Port next month.
