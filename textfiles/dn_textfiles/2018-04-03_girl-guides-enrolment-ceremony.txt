## Title: Girl Guides enrolment ceremony
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Tuesday, April 3, 2018 - 01:00

Girl Guides enrolment ceremony

Eight girls of York International School Wattala were induced as Girl Guides at a ceremony held recently. The enrolment ceremony marking the inauguration of the 93rd Kelaniya Girl Guides Association was held under the patronage of the York International Group Chairman Rohan Perera. Girl Guides Zonal Commissioner of Kelaniya Pushpa Nagasena, York International School Sapusgaskanda principal Hema Bambaranda, York International school Wattala principal Samson Roy and deputy principal Roshelle Caldera were present. The guests were welcomed by the Girl Guides captain Natasha Liyanage.
