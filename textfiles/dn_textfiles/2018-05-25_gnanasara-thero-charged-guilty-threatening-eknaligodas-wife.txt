## Title: Gnanasara Thero charged guilty for threatening  Eknaligoda's wife
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 24, 2018 - 17:27

Gnanasara Thero charged guilty for threatening  Eknaligoda's wife

 
General Secretary of the BBS Ven. Galagoda Aththe Gnanasara  was  convicted of charges pressed against him for allegedly threatening Sandhya Eknaligoda, the wife of missing journalist Prageeth Eknaligoda, a short while ago. 
The verdict will be delivered on June 14.
