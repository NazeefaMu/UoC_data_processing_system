## Title: Two on motorcycle with swords arrested in Chavakachcheri
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, November 27, 2017 - 10:31

Two on motorcycle with swords arrested in Chavakachcheri

 
Two individuals carrying swords on a motorcycle were arrested by the Chavakachcheri Police earlier today.
The Police say that the two, who were riding on the motorcycle without a valid license, may have intended to commit some sort of crime.
The two arrested, aged twenty-one and twenty-two are residents of Chavakachcheri.
They will be produced before the Chavakachcheri Magistrate today.
Chavakachcheri Police is investigating.
