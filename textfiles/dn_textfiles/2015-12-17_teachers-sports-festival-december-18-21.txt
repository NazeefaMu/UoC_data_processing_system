## Title: Teachers Sports Festival from December 18 to 21
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, December 17, 2015 - 01:00

Teachers Sports Festival from December 18 to 21

The All Island Teachers Sports Festival - 2015 conducted by Education Ministry will be held at Municipal Ground, Nuwara Eliya and Gamini Central College Ground, Nuwara Eliya on December 18, 19, 20 and 21.
More than 118 track and field events and four team events will be worked off on these four days.
Teachers Training Colleges', Pirivenas' and Government Approved Schools' teachers, principals and education administration officers can participate in this festival, the organisers said.
