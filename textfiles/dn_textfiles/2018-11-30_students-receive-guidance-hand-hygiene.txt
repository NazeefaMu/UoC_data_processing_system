## Title: Students receive guidance on hand hygiene
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, November 30, 2018 - 01:00

Students receive guidance on hand hygiene

The Addalaichenai Office of the Medical Officer of Health (MOH) conducted an awareness programme under the theme ‘Hand Washing’ at the Addalaichenai Iqrah Vidyalaya, recently.
This event was organised by MOH Dr. A. M. Ismail under the guidance of Kalmunai Regional Health Services Director Dr. A. L. Alawdeen.
The aim of this programme was to create awareness on importance of hygiene habits among the students. The organisers introduced proper hand washing methods with soaps to schoolchildren from grade 1 to 7.
Addalaichenai Medical Officer of Health Dr. A. M. Ismail, PHI A. M. Jawfer, Principal A. L. Yazeen, Development Officer A. M. Azwer and public health inspectors were present on the occasion.
The Addalaichenai office of the Medical Officer of Health (MOH) has conducted many programmes such as medical clinics, dental clinics, eye clinics and seminars for the benefit of schoolchildren.
