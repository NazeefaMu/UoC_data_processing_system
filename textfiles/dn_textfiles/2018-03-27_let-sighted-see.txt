## Title: Let the sighted see
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Tuesday, March 27, 2018 - 01:00

Let the sighted see

The women’s sector of the Sri Lanka Federation of the Visually Handicapped celebrated International Women’s Day recently. Farms Lanka Head Clarence Mendis, Dr Pradeep Chandana, and Dr Boodhini Samaranakaye graced the event. A magazine titled Sithuvam too was launched. Pictures by Saman Sri Wedage
