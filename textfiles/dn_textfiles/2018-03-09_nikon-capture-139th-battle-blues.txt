## Title: Nikon to capture 139th Battle of the Blues
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, March 9, 2018 - 01:00

Nikon to capture 139th Battle of the Blues

Photo Technica (Pvt) Ltd, exclusive distributor for world leading Nikon cameras in Sri Lanka will capture this year’s 139th Battle of the Blues Royal- Thomian annual cricket encounter as the official camera and photography partner for the match that will be played from today till March 11 at the SSC grounds.
The CEO of Photo Technica (Pvt) Ltd Harsha Malvenna said that his company was proud to be sponsoring the match for the third consecutive year and was happy to be a partner in the future too.
