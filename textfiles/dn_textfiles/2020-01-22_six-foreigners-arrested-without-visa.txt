## Title: Six foreigners arrested without visa
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 22, 2020 - 13:09

Six foreigners arrested without visa

Five Uzbekistan females were arrested who were staying in the country without valid visa and violating immigration and emigration laws.  They have been arrested in Wellawatte and Bambalapitiya. They will be produced before the Magistrate Court today.Meanwhile, a Saudi Arabian has been arrested at Kollupitiya.The suspect will be presented before the Fort Magistrate’s Court today.
