## Title: Seylan Bank join hands to take Sri Lankan Flag to Greater Heights
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, April 1, 2016 - 01:00

Seylan Bank join hands to take Sri Lankan Flag to Greater Heights

 
An experienced mountaineering duo JayanthiKuru-Utumpala and Johann Peiris, have both individually and as a team successfully attempted many of the world's most challenging treks with sheer determination and confidence. As a Bank that has grown from strength to strength now, looking forward for the next phase of growth , the Bank with a heart has joined hands to support the duo in order to reach the pinnacle of the Everest. As the first Sri Lankan duo to attempt the summit of Mt. Everest which is 8,848m above sea level, Seylan bank extended their wishes to JayanthiKuru-Utumpala and Johann Peiris. Seylan Bank's Chief Manager- Marketing, Mr. Gamika de Silva presenting the cheque to JayanthiKuru-Utumpala and Johann Peiris, the mountaineering duo. Mr. Thusitha Vitharana, Manager - Media and Advertising was also present at this occasion.
