## Title: Benedictines’ Annual Requiem Mass
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, November 6, 2018 - 01:00

Benedictines’ Annual Requiem Mass

The Council of Management of St. Benedict’s College Old Boys’ Union has organised its annual Requiem Mass in memory of its departed members, Rev. Fathers, Rev. Brothers, teachers, members of the non-academic staff and students.
The Annual Requiem Mass will be celebrated at St. Lucia’s Cathedral, Kotahena on November 8 at 6.45 pm by Old Benedictine Priest Rev. Fr. Lasantha de Abrew.
All Benedictines, family and friends are invited.
