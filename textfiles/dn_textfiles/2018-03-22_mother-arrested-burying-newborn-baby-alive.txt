## Title: Mother arrested for burying newborn baby alive
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, March 22, 2018 - 01:00

Mother arrested for burying newborn baby alive

A mother, suspected of burying her newborn baby alive behind the house, was arrested by Dambulla Police on Monday.
According to the Police, the suspect, a mother of three, is a resident of Nikatawana in Dambulla and her husband had been abroad for four years.
The body of the baby was unearthed before Dambulla Magistrate Kosala Bandara Ilangasinghe and Matale Judicial Medical Officer Vaidyaratne and sent for the post-mortem. The suspect was remanded until March 27 after being produced before the Dambulla Magistrate’s Court on Tuesday.
Further investigations will be conducted by a team of police officers attached to Dambulla Police.
