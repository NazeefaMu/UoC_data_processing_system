## Title: Murder suspect nabbed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 4, 2017 - 01:00

Murder suspect nabbed

A suspect wanted in connection with the murder of a 45-year-old father of two, H. M. Lalith of Nallathanniya, was arrested by Nallathanniya police on May 1. The suspect was produced before Hatton District Judge and Magistrate Prasad Liyanage and remanded till May 17.
