## Title: Police dog Cora dies
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 30, 2018 - 01:00

Police dog Cora dies

Cora, one of Sri Lanka’s most successful drug sniffer dogs, died in Kandy yesterday.
In the last Sri Pada season alone, Cora helped the Police to nab over 500 people who tried to climb the sacred mountain carrying illegal drugs.
Cora, in her successful three-year career, has helped Police in over 1,000 drug busts.
Cora worked in Peliyagoda, Ampara, Batticaloa and Bandarawela Police divisions before arriving in Kandy.
According to the police, Cora was one of the country’s most successful drug detection dogs.
Cora was eight years old at the time of death.
