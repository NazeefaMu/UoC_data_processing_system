## Title: Four escape from Negombo jail
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 31, 2017 - 01:00

Four escape from Negombo jail

Four remand prisoners escaped from the Negombo Prison in the wee hours of yesterday (May 30).
Prisons department media spokesman Thushara Upuldeniya said the four prisoners were held in connection with a murder case and several robberies. A joint operation has been launched.
