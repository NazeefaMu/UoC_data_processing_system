## Title: Ceylinco Insurance to pay over Rs 2.1 bn claims
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Friday, June 2, 2017 - 01:00

Ceylinco Insurance to pay over Rs 2.1 bn claims

With the death toll from mudslides and floods in Sri Lanka rising and also many business institutions reported damaged, Ceylinco Insurance once again has come forward to settle some of the claims.
So far total claims intimated by eEylinco Insurance was over 1,950 and the total value of claims estimated to be over Rs 2.1 billion. “We have inspected over 90 % of them, balance 10 % are not reachable as yet. We have had over 375 claims up to Thursday morning. As we always do, all claims will be settled within 14 days, ” an official from the company said.
