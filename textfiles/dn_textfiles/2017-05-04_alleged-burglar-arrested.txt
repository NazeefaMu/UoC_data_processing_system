## Title: Alleged burglar arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 4, 2017 - 01:00

Alleged burglar arrested

A man who is alleged to have involved in crimes, including house breakings and robberies in Colombo, was arrested by the special crime investigation unit attached to Mirihana Police yesterday.
The suspect is a resident of Waskaduwa, Kaluthara. Police recovered 6.5 gold pounds worth Rs. 500,000, eight laptops worth over Rs. 700,000, three LED flat TV sets, seven wrist watches, a DVD set and currency notes worth Rs. 29,000. Police captured the suspect while hiding in a house in Waga, Welikanna in Hanwalla.
