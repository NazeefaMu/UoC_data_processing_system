## Title: Meethotamulla Disaster: Death toll climbs to 19
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Sunday, April 16, 2017 - 11:00

Meethotamulla Disaster: Death toll climbs to 19

 
Nineteen bodies have been recovered from Meethotamulla, where a rubbish dump collapsed on Friday, burying as many 150 residences.
Four children are among those dead. Rescue workers, made up of the tri-forces are continuing operations in the affected area.
