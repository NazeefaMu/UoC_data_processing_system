## Title: President visits Lakshauyana area in Polonnaruwa
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, July 23, 2018 - 01:00

President visits Lakshauyana area in Polonnaruwa

President Maithripala Sirisena who was in the Polonnaruwa Lakshauyana area shares a light moment with some of the villagers. The President inspected some of the development projects initiated under the 'Pibidemu Polonnaruwa' programme yesterday.
