## Title: Welikada Prison riots: IP Neomal Rangajeewa, Emil Ranjan re-remanded till April 24
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Tuesday, April 10, 2018 - 13:53

Welikada Prison riots: IP Neomal Rangajeewa, Emil Ranjan re-remanded till April 24

 
IP Neomal Rangajeewa and Prison Commissioner Emil Ranjan who are alleged to have been involved in killing seven prisoners at Welikada Prison were further remanded until April 24.
 
The seven prisoners were killed during a riot which took place at the Welikada prison.
 
IP Neomal Rangajeewa and Emil Ranjan were arrested on March 28.
