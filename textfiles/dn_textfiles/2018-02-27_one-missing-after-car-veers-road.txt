## Title: One missing after car veers off road
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, February 27, 2018 - 01:00

One missing after car veers off road

The driver and three others including a woman were rescued after the car in which they were travelling, veered off the road and toppled into the Viyana canal on the Badulla-Mahiyangana road near the 7th mile post, around 7.30 am yesterday.
However, according to the police, one person is reported missing. The car had been travelling from Bandarawela to Mahiyanganaya.
Search operations are being carried out with assistance of Mahiyanganaya Police, for the missing person. The woman who was rescued had sustained minor injuries, but was admitted to the Mahiyanganaya Hospital for treatment.
