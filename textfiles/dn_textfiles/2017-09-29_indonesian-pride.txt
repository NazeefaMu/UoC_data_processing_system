## Date: Friday, September 29, 2017 - 01:00

The Indonesian pride!

The Embassy of the Republic of Indonesia held a diplomatic reception to celebrate the 72nd anniversary of Indonesia’s National Day and the longstanding friendship between Indonesia and Sri Lanka at Hotel Taj Samudra recently. The event was attended by Chief Guest, Provincial Councils and Local Government Minister Faiszer Musthapha, the Diplomatic Community in Sri Lanka, Government Officials, businessmen and key dignitaries of Indonesia.
The occasion showed a glimpse of Indonesia’s rich culture as well as the country’s charm and contribution to the world’s peace and security. The guests enjoyed the dance performances and videos of Indonesia. Here are some highlights of the event.
