## Title: Revision petition against Rajitha’s release on bail hearing today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Tuesday, January 21, 2020 - 11:20

Revision petition against Rajitha’s release on bail hearing today

The Colombo High Court on Friday fixed today to decide whether it issues formal notices on former Health Minister Rajitha Senaratne regarding a revision application filed by the Attorney General in connection with the controversial ‘White Van’ press conference.
