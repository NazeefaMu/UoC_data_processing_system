## Title: Trials to set up Beach Volleyball pools
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, December 7, 2018 - 01:00

Trials to set up Beach Volleyball pools

Men and women Beach Volleyball players are to be selected to form national pools as part of preparations for the South Asian Games to be held next year. All players interested in attending the trials are requested to report to the Sugathadsa Beach Volleyball Courts on December 8 by at 8.00 a.m. The trials are scheduled to commence at 8.30 a.m. BP
Applications called for Beach Volleyball Coaches.
Beach Volleyball coaches are to be selected to undertake the training of national pools that will be set up soon. The Volleyball Federation wants all applicants to forward their applications on or before 10th December. Applications should be addressed to General Secretary, Sri Lanka Volleyball Federation.No. 33. Torrington Place, Colombo 07.
