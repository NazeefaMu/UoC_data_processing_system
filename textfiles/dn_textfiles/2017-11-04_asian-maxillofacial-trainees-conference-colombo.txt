## Title: Asian Maxillofacial Trainees Conference in Colombo
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, November 4, 2017 - 01:00

Asian Maxillofacial Trainees Conference in Colombo

The 3rd Asian Maxillofacial Trainees Conference will be held at Taj Samudra Hotel in Colombo from November 10th to 12th, Health Ministry sources said..
The Asian Association of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery with the collaboration of the Sri Lanka Association of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeons have organized this event. This event of the Maxillofacial trainees of the Asian region, will be addressed by both national and international speakers of repute, sharing their expertise, knowledge and experience with the participants, the sources said.
Professor N.A. De S. Amaratunga, Professor Emeritus in Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery, University of Peradeniya, W.M. Tilakaratne, Dean and Professor of Oral Pathology, University of Peradeniya and the President of the International Association of Oral Pathologists and several other local scholars are among many local and foreign invited speakers at this event.
