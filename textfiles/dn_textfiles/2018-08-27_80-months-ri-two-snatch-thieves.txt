## Title: 80 months' RI for two snatch-thieves
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, August 27, 2018 - 01:00

80 months' RI for two snatch-thieves

Two snatch-thieves were sentenced to 80 months' rigorous imprisonment (RI) and fined Rs.16,000 by Dambulla Magistrate I.M.S.B. Illangasingha, recently.
The suspects were said to have committed the crimes while travelling in luxury vehicles, and each suspect had two cases filed against them. The victims are residents of Kandalama, Kiralagolla, Kiralessa and Athabediwewa, while the accused are residents of Medamahanuwara and Mundalama.
