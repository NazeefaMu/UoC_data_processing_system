## Title: Sir D. B. Jayatilaka remembered
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, February 15, 2018 - 01:00

Sir D. B. Jayatilaka remembered

A ceremony to commemorate the 150th Birth Anniversary of Sir D. B. Jayatilaka was held yesterday at the Public Trustee Department under the patronage of Speaker Karu Jayasuriya.
Addressing the occasion, Speaker Karu Jayasuriya said Sir D.B. Jayatilaka donated most of his assets to the State and the Public Trustee Department at Bauddhaloka Mawatha, Colombo 7. It was named Thurban House, a building which was Sir D. B. Jayatilaka’s own house gifted to the State.
Chief Sanghanayake for Colombo and Chilaw provinces and Chancellor of the University of Kelaniya Dr. Welamitiyawe Kusaladhamma Nayaka Thera delivered an anusasana on the occasion.
Indian High Commissioner to Sri Lanka Shri Taranjit Singh Sandhu and Public Trustee Sanath Weeraratne also addressed the occasion.
