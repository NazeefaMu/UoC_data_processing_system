## Title: Peterite Santhush takes 6 wickets for 5 runs
## Supertitle: ‘Singer Cup’ U19 Schools Cricket:
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, October 26, 2018 - 01:00

‘Singer Cup’ U19 Schools Cricket:
Peterite Santhush takes 6 wickets for 5 runs

St Peter’s College right-arm fast bowler Santhush Gunathilake took 6 wickets for 5 runs against De Mazenod College Kandana on the first day of their ‘Singer Trophy’ Under 19 inter schools cricket match played at Bambalapitiya yesterday. St Peter’s were 24-5 at close.
Sasanka Nirmal skipper and off-spinner of Holy Cross College Kalutara took 7 wickets for 36 runs against Dudley Senanayake Central College Tholangamuwa.
The match between Dharmapala College Pannipitiya and St. Thomas College Matara ended in a draw.
