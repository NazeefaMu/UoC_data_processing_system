## Title: Cricket tourney at Panadura
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, August 24, 2018 - 01:00

Cricket tourney at Panadura

The 14th annual cricket tournament of the Panadura Cricket Coaching school will be held on August 24 25 and 26 at the Panadura esplanade.
The tournament is being conducted in five age categories, namely Under 11, 12, 13, 15 and open. The players will enjoy playing with the white cricket ball and coloured clothing for the first time. Best bowler , Best batsman, Best fielder and man of the match in each category will receive awards.
The chief guest will be former Panadura SC cricketer Charitha Buddhika.
