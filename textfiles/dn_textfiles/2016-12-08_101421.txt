‘Divrumgala’ at the Ruhunu Kataragama Maha Devalaya

 
 
The famous ‘Divrumgala’ at the Ruhunu Kataragama Maha Devalaya entrance. In the past, devotees had sworn on it to prove their innocence in the presence of estranged parties. But this custom has almost died down today. – Picture by Kataragama Group Corr.
