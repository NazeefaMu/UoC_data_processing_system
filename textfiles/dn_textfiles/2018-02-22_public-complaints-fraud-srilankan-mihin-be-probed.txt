## Title: Public complaints of fraud in SriLankan, Mihin to be probed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, February 22, 2018 - 01:00

Public complaints of fraud in SriLankan, Mihin to be probed

The Presidential Commission probing SriLankan Airlines, SriLankan Catering and Mihin Lanka Ltd. is making preparations to entertain public complaints regarding alleged acts of fraud and corruption which had occurred in these institutions. The Commission, which met on February 20, decided to set a month’s deadline to entertain such public complaints.
The Commission will take steps to publish newspaper advertisements in this connection in all three languages within the next few days.
Accordingly, the public would get an opportunity to submit written complaints to the Commission regarding alleged acts of fraud and corruption committed in the three institutions during the period from January 6, 2006 to January 31, 2018.
The proposed press advertisement had been sent to the Attorney General's Department for approval, it is reliably learnt.
