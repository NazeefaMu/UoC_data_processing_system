## Title: Richmond register first innings win
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, November 7, 2015 - 01:00

Richmond register first innings win

 
Richmond College Galle registered a first innings win over Gurukula College Kelaniya in the first round match of ‘Singer Trophy’ Under 19 inter schools cricket tournament at Kadirana grounds Negombo yesterday.
Gurukula: 85 in 50 overs (Yohan Nimesh 19, Teshan Malinda 18, Charith Asalanka 3/11, Wanindu Hasaranga 3/18, Lakshan Dhananjaya 2/15)
Richmond: 187/6 declared (37/1 overnight) in 31 overs (Wanindu Hasaranga 76, Charith Asalanka 57, Jayamal Wanniarachchi 2/65)
 
Cambrians win on first innings
An unbeaten knock of 41 runs by Thilan Nimesh and a four wicket haul by Dhanushka Fernando (4 for 31) helped Prince of Wales College Moratuwa to gain a first innings win over Thurstan College in their inter-schools under 19 encounter played at Moratuwa yesterday.
Prince of Wales: 129 in 42 overs (Vihanga Weerakkody 34, Avindu Anesh 28, Isitha Wijesundara 3/18, Charana Nanayakkara 2/23) and 246/9 in 80 overs (Thilan Nimesh 41 n.o., Shanoj Dharshika 38, Thilanka Madhuranga 35, Vihanga Weerakkody 34, Vishwa Chathuranga 30, Sandu Soysa 19, Avindu Kanesh 17, Sanjaya Fernando 16, Dilan Madushanka 3/44, Thushal Madushanka 3/70, Chanuka Prabath 2/19)
Thurstan: 88 (53/8 o/n) in 29.3 overs (Charana Nanayakkara 19, Pansilu Deshan 14, Ayesh Weerathne 12, Dhanushka Fernando 4/31, Thilan Nimesh 3/23, Savindu Pieris 2/15)
 
Royal Colombo lead on first innings
Fine all round performance by Ganith Sandeepa (5 for 29 and 46 not out) guided Royal College Colombo to a commanding position over Royal College Panadura in their under 19 encounter commenced at St.John’s College Ground Panadura yesterday.
Batting first, homesters were all out for 126 with Pawan Madhurajith making a useful contribution of 48 runs. Ganith Sandeepa captured five wicket while his fellow teammate Lasith Embuldeniya took three wickets for 38.
Royal Panadura: 126 in 55 overs (Pawan Madhurajith 48, Sachin Aminda 19, Amila Dilan 19, Ganith Sandeepa 5/29, Lasith Embuldeniya 3/38, Charuka Hathurusinghe 2/26)
Royal Colombo: 135/7 in 29.4 overs (Ganith Sandeepa 46 n.o., Helitha Vithanage 28, Tiran Danapala 23, Pawan Madhurajith 3/26)
 
Joes have the edge
St. Josephs College Colombo were leading first inning against De Mazenod College Kandana in day one in the first round match of ‘Singer Trophy’ Under 19 inter schools cricket tournament at at Darley road Colombo yesterday.
De Mazenod Kandana bowled out 106 runs and St. Joseph’s Colombo scored 136 runs for 6 wickets. Match will be continued today for day two.
De Mazenod: 106 in 29 overs (Lasith Croospulle 43, Akash Senarathne 27, Malshan Rodrigo 5/32, Sachitha De Silva 3/15)
St. Joseph’s: 136/6 in 35 overs (Dineth Madurawala 48, Dario Dissanayake 20, Akash Senarathne 3/44, Avishka Indrajith 2/27)
Zahira shot out for 133
Mahinda College Galle scored 56 runs for 3 wickets against Zahira College Colombo in day one in the first round match of ‘Singer Trophy’ Under 19 inter schools cricket tournament at Maradana.
Zahira Colombo bowled out 133 runs. Only 72.2 overs played in day one due to bad light. Match will be continued today for day two.
Zahira: 133 in 40.2 overs (Sajith Sameera 54, Iroshan Lakruwan 35, Krishan Rangajith 6/20, Pasindu Dilshan 3/25)
Mahinda: 56/3 in 32 overs (Gihan Dhanushka 20 n.o.)
