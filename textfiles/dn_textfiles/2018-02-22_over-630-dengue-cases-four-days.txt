## Title: Over 630 dengue cases in four days
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, February 22, 2018 - 01:00

Over 630 dengue cases in four days

A total of 9,650 dengue cases including eight deaths have been reported up to February 19, Epidemiology Unit sources said.
The Daily News reported last week that there were 9,016 dengue cases between January 1 and February 15, according to Epidemiology Unit sources. This reveals an occurrence of 634 cases in four days.
The highest number of dengue cases (1,598) was reported from the Colombo District, while the second highest number (908) was reported from the Gampaha District. The third highest number of dengue cases (840) was reported from the Jaffna District. The Colombo Municipal Council (CMC) reported a total of 472 dengue cases during the same period. The other parts of the Colombo District recorded a total of 1,126 dengue cases. The total number of dengue cases reported from the Western Province is 3,101.
