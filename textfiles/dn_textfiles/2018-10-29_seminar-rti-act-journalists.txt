## Title: Seminar on RTI Act for journalists
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 29, 2018 - 01:00

Seminar on RTI Act for journalists

The Government Information Department held a seminar on the Right to Information (RTI) Act for journalists last Saturday, at the Batticaloa District Secretariat auditorium.
Department Director Nirmalee Priyankane Kumarage headed the event, while Attorney-at-Law Mohamed Azad was the resource person.
Batticaloa Information Officer S.V. Jeevananthan and Desathiya magazine Editor L.P. Thilagaretne were also present.
