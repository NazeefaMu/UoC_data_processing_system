## Title: RI, fine for disturbing Court in drunken state during case on illicit liquor
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, June 2, 2017 - 01:00

RI, fine for disturbing Court in drunken state during case on illicit liquor

 A suspect of an illicit liquor case was taken into custody for disrupting proceedings while his case was being heard at Kuliyapitiya Courts. The suspect was found to have consumed liquor when Kuliyapitiya Magistrate Ruwan Dhammika Disanayake ordered Police to obtain a medical report. Subsequently, he was sentenced to two and half years Rigorous Imprisonment and also fined Rs. 50,000. The suspect is from Kuliyapitiya.
