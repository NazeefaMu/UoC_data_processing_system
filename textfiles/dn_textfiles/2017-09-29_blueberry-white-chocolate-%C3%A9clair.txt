## Title: Blueberry White Chocolate Éclair
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Friday, September 29, 2017 - 01:00

Blueberry White Chocolate Éclair

Ingredients:
Choux Pastry:
*125 ml Milk
*125 ml Water
*100g Salted butter
*Pinch of salt
*150g Flour
*04 Eggs, at room temperature
Make the pastry:
Heat the oven to 200°C. Bring milk, butter and salt to a boil in a 4-qt. saucepan over high heat. Add flour,stir until a dough forms. Reduce the heat to medium and cook, stirring, the dough constantly with a wooden spoon, until slightly dried, about two minutes. Transfer the dough to a bowl and using a hand mixer beat in eggs one at a time until smooth.
Using a piping bag, pipe 4-inch lines of dough onto parchment paper-lined baking sheets. Place the tray in the oven and reduce the temperature to 180°C. Bake until golden brown, about 30 minutes and cool.
Ingredients:
White Chocolate Blueberry Mousse:
*50 g White Chocolate
*170 g Blueberries, plus more
for garnish
*170 g Sugar
*01 Teaspoon kosher salt
*200g Cream cheese, softened
*150g Heavy cream, chilled
Make the mousse:
In a small saucepan, mash the blueberries with the sugar and salt and bring to a simmer over moderate heat. Cook, stirring until jammy about 10 minutes. Remove from the heat and scrape the jam into a food processor. Let cool completely.
Add the cream cheese to the food processor and puree with the jam until smooth. In a large bowl, beat the heavy cream to soft peaks. Fold in the berry cream until blended.
Melt the white chocolate and fold with the blueberry mousse.
To assemble:
Make few slits of the baked éclair and fill with the white chocolate blueberry mousse. Keep in the refrigerator.
Glace the top with coloured Deco gel or coloured apricot glaze. Decorate creatively with your own ideas or follow as per the picture with chocolate spiral, cookie crunchies and colour sponge.
