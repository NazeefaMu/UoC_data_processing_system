## Title: Jayaratna Galagedara joins Scope Cinemas
## Supertitle: 
## Section: T&C
## Date: Tuesday, July 24, 2018 - 01:00

Jayaratna Galagedara joins Scope Cinemas

Jayaratna Galagedara who has been a part of EAP Films and Theaters Limited for around 22 years has now joined Scope Cinemas Private Limited as its Marketing and Productions Manager. He was handed the appointment letter for the post by the company’s director Rangana Meemanage. Galagedara has not only managed EAP films and distributions. He has also acted and dubbed for several films.
