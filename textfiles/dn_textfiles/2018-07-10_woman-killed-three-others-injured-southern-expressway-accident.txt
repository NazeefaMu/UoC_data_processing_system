## Title: Woman killed, three others injured in Southern Expressway accident
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, July 10, 2018 - 01:00

Woman killed, three others injured in Southern Expressway accident

A woman died and three men were injured when a jeep crashed into the protective side bar of the Southern Expressway yesterday.
They were returning home after attending a funeral at Matara. The woman was thrown out of the car and died on the spot. Three men including the driver of the vehicle were admitted to the Elpitiya Hospital.
The deceased was identified as 45-year-old Nadeera, an employee of the National Youth Center, Maharagama. She was a dancing instructor. Investigations are continuing.
