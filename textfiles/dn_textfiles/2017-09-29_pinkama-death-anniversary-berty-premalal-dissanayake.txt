## Title: Pinkama on death anniversary of Berty Premalal Dissanayake
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, September 29, 2017 - 01:00

Pinkama on death anniversary of Berty Premalal Dissanayake

A commemorative Pinkama to mark the 4th death anniversary of the former North Western Province Chief Minister Berty Premalal Dissanayake was held at his 'Sanasuma' residence at Airport Road, Anuradhapura.
It included a 'Dharmadeshana' on September 26 night followed by a Sanghika Dana to 300 Bhikkhus on September 27 afternoon.
Former Prime Minister D.M. Jayaratne and members of the Dissanayake family along with a large gathering of friends, relatives and well wishers attended the pinkama.
