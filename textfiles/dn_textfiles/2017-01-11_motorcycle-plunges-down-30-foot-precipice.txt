## Title: Motorcycle plunges down 30 foot precipice
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 11, 2017 - 01:00

Motorcycle plunges down 30 foot precipice

A motorcyclist travelling from Balangoda towards Bandarawela skidded off the road near the Balangoda, Olugantota hospital bend and plunged down a 30 foot precipice on Monday evening.
The motorcycle wedged between trees above the Walawe river was spotted by residents.
However, the motorcyclist was missing and Balangoda hospital police said no motorcyclist was admitted to the hospital.
Neither the motorcyclist nor any other party had retrieved the motorcycle until nightfall.
