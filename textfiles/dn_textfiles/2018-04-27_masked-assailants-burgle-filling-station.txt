## Title: Masked assailants burgle filling  station
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, April 27, 2018 - 01:00

Masked assailants burgle filling  station

Two masked assailants who had allegedly arrived at the Bangadeniya Petrol Filling Station in a grey car with concealed number plates around 5 am on April 23, had stolen cash from the station locker upon threatening the supervisor at knife-point, a complaint made by the victim, Geekiyanage Wickremasinghe Fernando to Chilaw Police, said. He said the cash robbed was roughly around Rs. 98,140. Investigations are continuing.
