## Title: CID visits Arjun Aloysius’s house
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, September 12, 2017 - 15:30

CID visits Arjun Aloysius’s house

 
The Criminal Investigation Division attached to the Presidential Commission of Inquiry has visited former Perpetual Treasuries Director Arjun Aloysius’s house today.
The CID visited his home citing that Arjun Aloysius has not submitted his bank details to the Treasury Bond Commission today.
