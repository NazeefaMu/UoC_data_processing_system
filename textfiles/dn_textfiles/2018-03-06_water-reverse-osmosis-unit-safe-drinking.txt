## Title: Water from reverse osmosis unit safe for drinking
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, March 6, 2018 - 01:00

Water from reverse osmosis unit safe for drinking

Drinking water supplied by reverse osmosis units set up in schools in the North and East and kidney disease–prone areas, at the behest of President Maithripala Sirisena, is safe for drinking, tests made by the Government Analyst’s Department has proved, Government Analysts Department Director General A Welianga said. Welianga said his department has confirmed that this water is free of arsenic, cadmium and lead and is safe for drinking.
He said this water was tested by his department following a directive of the President and the water samples were supplied for testing by health officers attached to the Presidential Secretariat.
