## Title: President Sirisena instructs to deploy tri-forces for relief
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 30, 2017 - 16:40

President Sirisena instructs to deploy tri-forces for relief

 
President Maithripala Sirisena has given instructions to deploy tri-forces to provide relief for people affected by bad weather, today.
The President said in a twitter message, ‘This morning, I instructed to deploy the tri-forces to provide quick relief to all affected by the harsh weather condition’.
President Sirisena is currently on a three day state visit to South Korea to commemorate the 40th Anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between Sri Lanka and Korea.
