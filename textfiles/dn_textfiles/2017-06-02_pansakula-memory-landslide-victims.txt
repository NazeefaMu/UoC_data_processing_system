## Title: Pansakula in memory of landslide victims
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, June 2, 2017 - 01:00

Pansakula in memory of landslide victims

The Mahasanga attached to the Keppetiwalana Bada Gaspe Sri Shailathalarama Viharaya held a mass Pansakula ceremony in memory of the 23 people in the village who died in the recent floods caused by the Pahiyangala hill landslide.
This was followed by a distribution of 100 dry food parcels among the distressed people in the village.
