## Title: Indian, Singaporean biz group visits EP
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 12, 2018 - 01:00

Indian, Singaporean biz group visits EP

A business delegation from India and Singapore held discussions with the Eastern Province Governor Rohitha Bogollagama for investment in the fields of tourism, agriculture and fisheries in the province.
The delegation was headed by former minister from the state of Punjab in India, Raghbir Singh Jaura. The delegation proposed the development of Sampoor in Trincomalee as an integrated tourism resort.
Jaura said they were impressed by the investment potential in the Eastern Province, while the Governor expressed his gratitude for their interest in investing in the province and pledged his fullest support.
