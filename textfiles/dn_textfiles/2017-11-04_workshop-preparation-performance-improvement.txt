## Title: Workshop on Preparation of performance improvement
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, November 4, 2017 - 01:00

Workshop on Preparation of performance improvement

The Social Empowerment, Welfare and Kandyan Heritage Ministry is conducting a three day residential workshop on the preparation of performance improvement strategic planing from November 02 to 04 at the National Inland Fisheries and Aquaculture Training Institute in Kalawewa, Anuradhapura.
The main resource persons are Prof. M. Thilakasiri and Director General Samurdhi, Dr. Sunil Jayantha Nawarathne. Minister of Social Empowerment, Welfare and Kandyan Heritage S.B. Dissanayake participated in the workshop together with all heads and staff of the Ministry. The workshop commenced with an exercise session. Here the Minister and staff members seen warming up.
