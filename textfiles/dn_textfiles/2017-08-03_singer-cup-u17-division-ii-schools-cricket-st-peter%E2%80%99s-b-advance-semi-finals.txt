## Title: Singer Cup U17 Division II Schools Cricket:  St. Peter’s (B) advance to semi-finals
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, August 3, 2017 - 01:00

Singer Cup U17 Division II Schools Cricket:  St. Peter’s (B) advance to semi-finals

St. Peter's College Bambalapitiya (B) advanced to the semi-finals of the ‘Singer Trophy’ Under 17 division II inter schools cricket tournament beating Ananda College by seven wickets in the quarterfinal played at St Sebastian’s ground Moratuwa yesterday.
Scores:
Ananda (B): 145 in 46.3overs (Kavindu Perera 39, Darshana Fernando 39, Devan Anthony 2/15, Yohan Aruna 2/20, Lymson Jelard 2/38, Nirosh Arshana 2/38)
St. Peter's (B): 146/3 in 30.3 overs (Himsara Randeniya 53, Dinuka Rodrigo 50, Sukith Pabasara 2/30) -YK
