## Title: Body found from parking lot
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 01:00

Body found from parking lot

Dambulla Police found a body on Tuesday morning from a vehicle parking lot at Dambulla town opposite Dambulla Base Hospital.
Dambulla Police rushed to the location after receiving a tip-off and took the body to Dambulla Base Hospital.
The deceased was identified as Sarath Kumara Wijethilaka, 34, a resident of the Kalundewa - Walgamwewa area.
Police investigations revealed that the victim was frequenting Dambulla town. People said that the deceased had been behaving as a woman in public places.
However, no one was arrested so far. Dambulla Police are investigating.
