## Title: Former President’s Chief of Staff, former Timber Corporation Chairman further remanded till July 13
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, July 10, 2018 - 11:02

Former President’s Chief of Staff, former Timber Corporation Chairman further remanded till July 13

 
Former President's Chief of Staff I.H.K. Mahanama and the former State Timber Corporation Chairman P. Dissanayake, who were arrested by the Bribery Commission while accepting a bribe worth Rs. 20 million were further remanded until July 13.
The duo were arrested at a parking lot of a high-end hotel in Colombo from an Indian Businessman in May.
They had taken the bribe from the businessman pledging to aid them to obtain proper approval required to attain certain machinery and building space he needed for projects.
