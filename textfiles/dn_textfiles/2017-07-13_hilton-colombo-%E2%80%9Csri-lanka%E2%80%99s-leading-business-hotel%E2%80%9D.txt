## Date: Thursday, July 13, 2017 - 01:00

Hilton Colombo, “Sri Lanka’s Leading Business Hotel”

Hilton Colombo won “Sri Lanka’s Leading Business Hotel” award the most prestigious award in the Travel & Tourism industry at the World Travel Awards. Described as the “Oscars” of the Travel & Tourism industry by the Wall Street Journal, it is one of the most sought after events in the Tourism calendar. The Indian Ocean Gala Ceremony was held in the Maldives.
World Travel Awards was established in 1993 to acknowledge, reward and celebrate excellence across all sectors of the tourism industry. Today, the World Travel Awards brand is recognised globally as the ultimate hallmark of quality, with winners setting the benchmark to which all others aspire.
Commenting on the hotel’s great achievement General Manager, Manesh Fernando said in a press release, “We are honored to have been chosen as Sri Lanka’s Leading Business Hotel at one of the most prestigious events in the world. We sincerely take this opportunity to thank all our guests and everyone who has been supporting the Hilton Colombo over the years.”
