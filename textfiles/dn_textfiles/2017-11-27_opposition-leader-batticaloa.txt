## Title: Opposition Leader in Batticaloa
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Monday, November 27, 2017 - 01:00

Opposition Leader in Batticaloa

Opposition Leader R. Sampanthan participated in two meetings held at the Nalver Koddam Hall, Kalmunai and the Fedynance Hall, Thandavenveli, Batticaloa and explained the new constitution to the public on Sunday (25). Jaffna District TNA Parliamentarians M.A. Sumanthiran, Mavai Senathiraja, S. Yogesweran, ITAK General Secretary and Former Eastern Province Agriculture Minister K. Thurarajasingam spoke.
