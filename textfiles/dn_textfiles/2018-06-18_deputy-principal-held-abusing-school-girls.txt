## Title: Deputy principal held for abusing school girls
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, June 18, 2018 - 01:00

Deputy principal held for abusing school girls

A deputy principal who tried to sexually abuse school girls in the Galenbindunuwewa area was arrested by Galenbindunuwewa Police.
According to police, the 29-year-old deputy principal had attempted to molest three girls who were studying in Grade 5, and another girl in Grade 13. He was arrested after parents of the girls lodged a complaint at the Galenbindunuwewa Police Station. The suspect is from Mihintale. He was remanded till June 18, after he was produced in the Magistrate’s Court by the Galenbindunuwewa Police.
