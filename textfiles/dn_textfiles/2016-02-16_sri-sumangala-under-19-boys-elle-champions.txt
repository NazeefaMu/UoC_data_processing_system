## Title: Sri Sumangala under 19 boys Elle Champions
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, February 16, 2016 - 01:00

Sri Sumangala under 19 boys Elle Champions

 
Sri Sumangala Boys College, Panadura, emerged champions and Royal College, Panadura emerged Runner-up in the Boys under 19 Elle Tournaments organized by the Sports Unit of Panadura Divisional Education Office, under the instructions of Sports Education Unit of Western Province Education Department, and held at the Wadduwa Central College Playground recently.
