## Title: AIA Insurance takes customer rewards re-launched
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Wednesday, January 17, 2018 - 01:00

AIA Insurance takes customer rewards re-launched

AIA Insurance has taken customer rewards to a new level with the re-launch of AIA Real Reward.
With over 7,000 winners for the year, the rewards programme offers more benefits to the customer, while placing AIA even higher on the scale of superlative customer experience.
The new and improved Real Rewards programme sees an increase in the number of winners, with more than 600 customers standing a chance to win, every month. There will also be an additional 15 lucky winners selected from an annual grand draw.The programme is unparalleled in the industry and the grand prizes include holiday packages for two, as well as super market vouchers. The monthly prizes include holiday packages for two, tabs, smart-phones and mobile re-loads.
AIA’s Director Operations, Kelum Senanayake reiterated AIA’s commitment to its customers; “AIA Real Rewards which is in its 3rd year, is now bigger and better than it was before. Providing excellent customer experience is our top priority at AIA and we will constantly looking for ways to reward our customers for their patronage and loyalty.”
He said, “Winning with Real Rewards is simple. All you have to do is be an AIA customer and pay your premiums on time! If you pay your premiums annually or through a standing order- you have a very high chance of winning."
