## Title: Grenade explosion kills man in Mahiyangana
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, May 12, 2017 - 10:00

Grenade explosion kills man in Mahiyangana

 
A man died and a woman sustained injuries earlier today after a hand grenade thrown into their residence in Dodamwatta, Mahiyanganaya, exploded.
The two were rushed to the Mahiyangana Hospital but the sixty-four-year-old man died on admission.
The postmortem examinations of the deceased will be held today.
Mahiyangana Police is investigating.
