## Title: Kurunegala residents provided with drinking water
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, July 21, 2017 - 01:00

Kurunegala residents provided with drinking water

The Disaster Management Centre of the Kurunegala District has taken steps to distribute drinking water to families affected by the drought in the Kurunegala District. Accordingly, 36,665 families are provided with water by bowsers.
Assistant Director of the Kurunegala District Disaster Management Centre Viraj Dissanayake told the Daily News that 30 bowsers have been deployed for the purpose. In addition, 2,175 Samurdhi families of the Kurunegala District are offered a monthly allowances of Rs.10,000 each for four months , he added.
