## Title: WHO'S WALKING WHEN?
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Thursday, May 3, 2018 - 01:00

WHO'S WALKING WHEN?

Like every year, a bevy of Bollywood beauties will make India proud at the Cannes Film Festival this time, too. These include Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, Sonam Kapoor, Deepika Padukone and Kangana Ranaut.
The Manikarnika: Queen Of Jhansi star will make her big debut at the prestigious film festival, which is being held from May 8 to 19. As per reports, she has been chosen by a premium brand to represent and celebrate excellence in Indian cinema. The date for her appearance has not been officially announced yet.
DP, Ash and Sonam will represent the beauty brand that all of them endorse. This will be Deepika’s second year at the French Riviera as she walks down the red carpet on May 10 and 11. Ash, who completes her 17th year at the event, will be present on May 12 and May 13. Lastly, Sonam, who will tie the knot on May 7 and 8, will attend the festival on May 14 and May 15. This will be her eighth Cannes appearance.
