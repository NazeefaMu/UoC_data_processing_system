## Title: ‘Awakening (Voice) Women’ to mark International Women’s Day - 2017
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, March 3, 2017 - 01:00

‘Awakening (Voice) Women’ to mark International Women’s Day - 2017

A walk themed ‘Awakening (Voice) Women’ to mark International Women’s Day - 2017 was held at Ampara under the patronage of Petroleum and Petroleum Gas Deputy Minister Anoma Gamage on Wednesday organized by the Ampara District Secretariat. Ampara Additional District Secretary K. Wimalanathan, District Secretariat officials and a large number of women participated. Pictures by: I.L.M.RIZAN, Addalaichenai Central Corr.
