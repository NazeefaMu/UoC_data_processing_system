## Title: Addalachenai compost plant nears completion
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 3, 2018 - 01:00

Addalachenai compost plant nears completion

Construction work related to a compost plant under a solid waste management programme at a landfill site in the Addalaichenai Ashraff Nagar village, is nearing completion.
The estimated cost for the project, which is Rs. 80 million, was granted by the Provincial Councils and Local Government Ministry.
Addalaichenai Pradeshiya Sabha Chairman A. L. Amanullah visited the site to assess its progress along with officials, recently.
The programme was initiated last year under the Clean and Green City programme on the instructions of the Provincial Councils and Local Government Ministry, Addalaichenai Pradeshiya Sabha Secretary M. I. M. Fais said.
