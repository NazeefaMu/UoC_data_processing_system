## Title: Sports gear distributed in Addalaichenai
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, December 12, 2017 - 01:00

Sports gear distributed in Addalaichenai

Sports gear was distributed among sports clubs in Addalaichenai recently.
The function was held at the public secretariat of the former Eastern Province Health Minister A.L.M.Nazeer, Addalaichenai. Sports Deputy Minister H.M.M.Harees and former Eastern Province Health Minister A.L.M.Nazeer distributed the sports gear to club officials.
Former Eastern Province Health Minister A.L.M.Nazeer launched a programme to goodwill among players and sports activities in the region.
Under this programme, prominent sports clubs of the area, the Sobers SC, Al-Aqsha SC, Marksmen SC, Lucky SC, Ever Top SC, Super Orchid SC, Al-Jennah SC, Fina SC, New King SC, Heros SC, Ever Bright SC, Ashraff SC, Eleven Star SC and Young Lions SC received the donation.
Former Addalaichenai Pradeshiya Sabha member M.L.Kaleel and Sports Clubs officials were all present on this occasion.
