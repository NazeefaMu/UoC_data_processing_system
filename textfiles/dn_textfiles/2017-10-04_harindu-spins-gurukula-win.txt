## Title: Harindu spins Gurukula to win
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, October 4, 2017 - 01:00

Harindu spins Gurukula to win

A very fine bowling performances of 10 for 45 (5/2 and 5/43) by right arm leg spinner and captain of Gurukula Vidyalaya Kelaniya Harindu Indusara piloted his school to defeat Parakrama Vidyalaya, Gampaha by innings and 13 runs in their Inter School Under 13 Division One tournament cricket Match worked off at Mahara. This is their first game of the 2017 under 13 cricket season.
Batting initially Gurukula declared at 137 for 6 and in reply Parakrama Vidyalaya were skittled out for a paltry 46 runs and following on they were all out for 78 runs.
 
Gurukula Vidyalaya - 137/6 in 46 overs - Seneth Sulochana 43, Mohamed Murshad 36, H. D. batuwangala 2/16
Parakrama Vidyalaya - 46 in 29 overs - Harindu Indusara 5/2 and 78, H. D. Batuwangala 35, Harindu Indusara 5/43, Kavish Krishan 2/10, Janidu Hasith 2/5
First innings win
Gurukula Vidyalaya Kelaniya beat formidable Zahira College, Colombo on first innings in their Inter School Under 13 Division One tournament cricket match played at Colombo.
Batting first Zahirians declared at 130 for 9 and Prasad Kumara claimed 4 for 29. At stumps Gurukula were 132 for 4 with Senesh Sulochana scoring 67 runs.
Zahira College - 130/9 in 55 overs - Mohamed Milhaj 21, Prasad Kumara 4/29, Kaveesh Crishan 2/30, Viraj Madushanka 2/11
 
Gurukula Vidyalaya - 132/4 in 25 overs - Senesh Sulochana 67, Mohamed Murshad 35
