## Title: Police use tear gas and water cannons on Anti-SAITM protesters [Video]
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 24, 2017 - 14:30

Police use tear gas and water cannons on Anti-SAITM protesters [Video]

 
Police used tear gas and water cannons on the Anti- SAITM protest staged by medical faculty students’ parents a short while ago.
Lotus road was closed owing to the protest.
The protest is against legalising the South Asian Institute of Technology and Medicine in Malabe.
