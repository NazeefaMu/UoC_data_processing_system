## Title: ‘Gelawumkaruwaano’ Passion play
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, April 1, 2017 - 01:00

‘Gelawumkaruwaano’ Passion play

The Passion play ‘Gelawumkaruwaano’ will take the stage for the fifth time on April 7 and 8 at 7 p.m at St. Michael’s Church, Koralawella.
Rev. Dr. Valance Mendis, the Bishop of Chilaw, Rev. Dr. Maxwell Silva and Most Rev. Dr. Emmanuel Fernando, Auxiliary Bishops of Colombo are scheduled to grace the occasion as Chief Guests.
This Passion play has been staged under different themes and scripts annually since 2012, with a break only in 2014 and has received accolades and recognition at its highest level.
