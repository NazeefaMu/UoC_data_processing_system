## Title: Fairly heavy rains to be expected in Ratnapura, Kalutara, Kegalle today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 1, 2017 - 11:45

Fairly heavy rains to be expected in Ratnapura, Kalutara, Kegalle today

 
The Meteorology Department said that fairly heavy rains of about 75mm will be expected in Ratnapura, Kalutara and Kegalle districts in particular today.
Fairly strong winds of about 50 kmph will also be expected especially over the Southern province and Matale, Pollonnaruwa districts, the Meteorology Department said.
It said that while showers may occur at times in Sabaragamuwa, Southern, Western and Central provinces, showers or thundershowers will occur at several places in other provinces particularly in the afternoon.
