## Title: Fire erupts at Bandarawala Magistrate archives
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, April 30, 2018 - 11:22

Fire erupts at Bandarawala Magistrate archives

 
A fire erupted at the Bandarawela Magistrate archives this morning.
The Bandarawela Municipal Council and the Police were able to douse the fire completely.
The cause of the fire is yet to be revealed.
Bandarawela Police is investigating.
The State Analyst Department will also be called to conduct further investigations.
