## Title: Ceylinco Life propaganda unit upgrades  with Mercedes-Benz vehicles
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Friday, October 5, 2018 - 01:00

Ceylinco Life propaganda unit upgrades  with Mercedes-Benz vehicles

The vehicle fleet of the propaganda unit of Ceylinco Life has been upgraded with the acquisition of two new Mercedes-Benz Vito vehicles to enable the unit reach and serve its customers in greater style and comfort.
The unit plays an important role in advancing the company’s objective of creating awareness about the importance of life insurance by visiting all areas of the country, taking the message of life insurance to the public. It handles town storming programs, recruitment programs and customer education programs. The unit also conducts medical programs in rural towns of Sri Lanka, supporting one of Ceylinco Life’s on-going CSR initiatives.The new vehicles are installed with dynamic sound systems to aid the unit in its mission.
