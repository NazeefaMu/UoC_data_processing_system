## Title: Dr. Nimal Lucas wins 2 Silver and 2 Bronze Medals at NZ Masters
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, February 24, 2017 - 01:00

Dr. Nimal Lucas wins 2 Silver and 2 Bronze Medals at NZ Masters

Dr. Nimal Lucas won two silver medals and two bronze medals in the table tennis event at the New Zealand Masters Games held in Whanganui, New Zealand from February 6 to 12. He won silver medals in the over 65 men’s singles and over 65 men’s doubles. He won bronze medals in the over 70 men’s singles and over 70 men’s doubles. As Dr. Lucas was the sole participant from Sri Lanka, he was partnered by New Zealand players in the doubles events.
