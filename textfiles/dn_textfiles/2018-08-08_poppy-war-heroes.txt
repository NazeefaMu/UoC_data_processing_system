## Title: Poppy for war heroes
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 8, 2018 - 01:00

Poppy for war heroes

Sri Lanka Ex-Servicemen’s Association President Brigadier K.A. Gnanaweera pins the first poppy to commemorate fallen war heroes, on President Maithripala Sirisena, at the Presidential Secretariat, recently. The commemoration will also raise funds for the welfare of war heroes’ families. The Association’s General Secretary Lt. Col. Ajith Siyambalapitiya, Secretary Lt. Col. Melroy Amarakoon and Vice Presidents Maj. Gen. Mahinda Ambanpola, Rear Admiral Shemal Fernando and Wing Commander Malinda Wijenayaka were also present. Picture courtesy President’s Media Unit
