## Title: Premier to Finland on official tour on Oct. 9
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, October 4, 2017 - 12:30

Premier to Finland on official tour on Oct. 9

 
Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe is scheduled to visit Finland on an official tour in October 9, following a personal visit to Germany in October 5.
In a statement the Premier said that he will meet Finland’s Prime Minister Juha Sipila and several other high level leaders to hold bilateral discussions.
He intends to strengthen ties between the two countries and to form new ties in the economic and Industrial sectors.
Professor Maithri Wickremesinghe, Ports and Shipping Minister Mahinda Samarasinghe, Education Minister Akila Kariyawasm and several others are also to participate.
