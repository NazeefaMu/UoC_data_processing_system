## Title: Consumer Fair attracts avurudu shoppers
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Friday, April 7, 2017 - 01:00

Consumer Fair attracts avurudu shoppers

The Consumer fair 2017 commenced once again on April 2, at the Sri Lanka Exhibition and Convention Centre (SLECC). The pre-Awrudu fair will conclude on April 11.
The fair commences at 10am stretching on to 8 pm during its 10 days of business. The colourful event hosts many small and medium scale vendors selling an assortment of items that are available for affordable prices.
This year too the Consumer fair was able to attract a bulk of customers trying to complete their Awrudu shopping.
The fair hosted a number of stalls featuring apparel furniture, home appliances, toys and food.
