PM ON INSPECTION TOUR TO Pitabeddara

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe went on an inspection tour of Dudley Senanayake College, Pitabeddara is seen talking to students and villagers. Law and Order and Southern Development Minister Sagala Ratnayake was also present.
