## Title: Lorry & express train  crashe
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, October 5, 2018 - 01:00

Lorry & express train  crashe

Two persons on board a lorry were seriously injured when it crashed into a Kandy bound express train from Matara at the Wevulagoda railway crossing near the Hikkaduwa railway station.Train services on the Southern line were disrupted as a result of this accident.
