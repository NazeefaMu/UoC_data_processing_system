## Title: Kiddies treats from Rupavahini
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Tuesday, October 17, 2017 - 01:00

Kiddies treats from Rupavahini

The Sri Lanka Rupavahini Corporation celebrated World Children’s Day with many events which were held at the Rupavahini premises. A large number of children from various parts of the country took part. Games were organized by various institutions and they presented gifts to the children. Children showed their talents by singing, dancing, taking part in the art competition, playing basketball, throwing balls and etc. Pictures by Ruzaik Farook
