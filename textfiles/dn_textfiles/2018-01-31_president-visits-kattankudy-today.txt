## Title: President visits Kattankudy today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 31, 2018 - 01:00

President visits Kattankudy today

President Maithripala Sirisena will visit Kattankudy in Batticaloa today, to extend support to candidates of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party, on the invitation of M.L.A.M. Hisbullah, State Minister of Rehabilitation and Resettlement.
