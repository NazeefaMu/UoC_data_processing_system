## Title: Pubudu, Dhananjaya and Dineth prop up Annites
## Supertitle: ‘Singer Cup’ Under 19 Division II Inter Schools Cricket Tournament
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, April 11, 2018 - 01:00

‘Singer Cup’ Under 19 Division II Inter Schools Cricket Tournament
Pubudu, Dhananjaya and Dineth prop up Annites

Devapathiraja College Rathgama were 19 for 1 wicket against St. Anne’s College Kurunegala on day one of the final of the ‘Singer Trophy’ Under 19 division 11 inter schools (Two day K/O) Cricket tournament played at St. Anthony’s College ground Katugastota yesterday.
Devapathiraja won the toss and elected to field.
St. Anne’s Kurunagala was bowled out for 275 runs in 79.2 overs. Deputy skipper Pubudu Ganegama scored an unbeaten 68 runs and Dhananjaya Bandara scored 57 runs. Dineth Chandimal scored 51 runs and Randeera Ranasinghe made 46. Irushka Thimira took 5 wickets for 87 runs and Pawan Sandesh took 3 wickets for 45 runs.
Match will be continued today on day two.
St. Anne’s Kurunagala: 275 in 79.2 overs (Pubudu Ganegama 68 n.o., Dhananjaya Bandara 57, Dineth Chandimal 51, Dineth Chandimal 51, Randeera Ranasinghe 46, Wanitha Wanninayake 26, Irushka Thimira 5/87, Pawan Sandesh 3/45)
Devapathiraja Rathgama: 19/1 in 5.3 overs (Jeewaka Shasheen 14 n.o.) (YK)
