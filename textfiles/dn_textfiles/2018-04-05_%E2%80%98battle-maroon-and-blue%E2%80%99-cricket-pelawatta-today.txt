## Title: ‘Battle of the Maroon and Blue’ cricket at Pelawatta today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, April 5, 2018 - 01:00

‘Battle of the Maroon and Blue’ cricket at Pelawatta today

M.D.H. Jayawardana VidyalayaThe 3rd Battle of the Maroon and Blue cricket encounter between Mahamathya Vidyalaya, Athurugiriya and M.D.H. Jayawardana Vidyalaya, Koswatta will be played at the G.H. Buddhadasa Grounds, Pelawatta today (April 5). M.D.H. Jayawardana Vidyalaya will be led by Lahiru Dilshan and Kavindu Piyumal will play a key role as the vice captain. Mahamathya team led by Hasthika Udayanath while Tharindu Adithya will be his deputy.
Mahanama will have a slight edged over their opponents as the defending champions, but will receive a tough challenge from the Jayawardana Vidyalaya this year. As a prelude to the big match Mahamathya Walk was successfully conducted on April 3.
