## Title: Showers expected in Sabaragamuwa
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 28, 2018 - 01:00

Showers expected in Sabaragamuwa

Fairly strong gusty winds (up to 40-50 kmph) are likely over the North Central and North Western Provinces and Matale, Badulla, Ampara and Hambantota districts, the Meteorology Department said yesterday.
Showers or thundershowers will occur in the Sabaragamuwa Province and Kalutara, Galle and Mathara districts. Fairly heavy rainfall, about 50 mm, may occur at some places.
Several spells of showers will occur in the Central and North Western Provinces and in Colombo, Gampaha and Hambantota districts.
