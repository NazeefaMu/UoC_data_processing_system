## Title: Conducting tuition classes, seminars for grade five scholarship candidates banned from tomorrow
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, August 15, 2017 - 18:00

Conducting tuition classes, seminars for grade five scholarship candidates banned from tomorrow

 
Conducting tuition classes or special seminars for grade five scholarship examination candidates is banned from tomorrow, midnight, the General Commissionaire of Examinations said.
Accordingly, organising tuition classes, conducting tuition classes, seminars, workshops, lectures, conducting mock exams or publishing mock exam papers in print or digital media and possessing any publication is banned.
The 2017 Grade five scholarship examination will take place on August 20.
