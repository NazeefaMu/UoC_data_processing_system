## Title: Housing Minister inaugurates Sri Amaradeva Gama
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 3, 2018 - 01:00

Housing Minister inaugurates Sri Amaradeva Gama

The 60th model village named Sri Amaradeva Gama planned under the Hambantota Housing Development Project, was commissioned by Housing and Construction Minister Sajith Premadasa, recently.
The said model village is located at Siribopura, Hambantota and consists of 30 new houses. Each beneficiary family had been provided with free, 20-perch lands and low-interest loan facilities.
Addressing the public, Minister Premadasa said that the village was erected in memory of W. D. Amaradeva who was an icon in the Sri Lankan music industry.
In addition to the Sri Amaradeva Gama, construction work for another two villages in the Tissamaharama and Lunugamwehera areas had also been initiated recently. The said two villages would be named Sada Poojitha Amaradeva Gama and Swarapathi Amaradeva Gama, he added.
The minister said that plans were underway in order to build 25 model villages in 25 districts within the nest seven days. Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Development State Minister Dilip Wedaarachchi, late W. D. Amaradeva’s family, National Housing Development Authority officials and others participated.
