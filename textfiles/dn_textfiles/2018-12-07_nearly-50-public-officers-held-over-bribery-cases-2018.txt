## Title: Nearly 50 public officers held over bribery cases in 2018
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 7, 2018 - 01:00

Nearly 50 public officers held over bribery cases in 2018

About 48 public officers were taken into custody by the Bribery Commission this year over cases of bribery, said Investigations Director attached to the Bribery Commission Priyantha Chandrasiri.
The suspects include a former Ministry Secretary, a Judge, a Police IP and a Grama Niladhari.
About 32 cases are heard in High Courts and 16 cases in Magistrate’s Courts. About 2,956 complaints have been received by the Bribery Commission in 2018 till now. Of them, 1,848 complaints are investigated by the Bribery Commission, said Kurunegala District Senior Divisional Secretary W. E. Jayathilake, speaking at a meeting of Grama Niladharies, recently.
He added that all public officers should keep in mind to perform their official duties honestly in the coming year.
