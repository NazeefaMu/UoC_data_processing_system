## Title: Former prime minister Ranil Wickremesinghe calls on the chief prelates
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, November 26, 2018 - 01:00

Former prime minister Ranil Wickremesinghe calls on the chief prelates

Former Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe called on the Malwatte and Asgiriya Prelates at their respective temples in Kandy and received their blessings on November 23. After a brief discussion with the Prelates about the current political situation, Wickremesinghe attended a UNP rally in Kandy town. The media was not able to cover Wickremesinghe’s discussion with the two Prelates as they were closed-door meetings.
