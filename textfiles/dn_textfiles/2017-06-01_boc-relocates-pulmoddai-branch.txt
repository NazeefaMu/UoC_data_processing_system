## Title: BOC relocates Pulmoddai branch
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, June 1, 2017 - 01:00

BOC relocates Pulmoddai branch

Pulmoddai branch of the Bank of Ceylon relocated to its new premises, the relocated branch was opened on May 15. The new branch is located at Kokkillai Road, Ward No: 03, Pulmoddai with the objective of providing more efficient and friendly service to its customers with more sophisticated facilities. The relocated Pulmoddai branch was opened with the participation invitees and was presided over by Branch Manager A. Tharmenthiran. The Eastern Province Assistant General Manager D.M.K.S. Dissanayake was the chief guest at the event. Eastern Province Operations Manager B. Cynthia Martyn, Area Managers, Senior Managers. Text and Pictures by I.L.M. RIZAN, Addalaichenai Central Correspondent
