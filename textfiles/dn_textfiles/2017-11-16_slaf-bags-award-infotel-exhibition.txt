## Title: SLAF bags award at INFOTEL Exhibition
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 16, 2017 - 01:00

SLAF bags award at INFOTEL Exhibition

The Sri Lanka Air Force (SLAF) stall done by the Air Force's Information Technology Unit (ITU) at the INFOTEL National ICT Exhibition - 2017 organized by the Federation of Information Technology Industry Sri Lanka (FITIS), won the award for the exhibit and demonstration depicting social impact.
The SLAF stall, conceptualized with the guidance of Command Information Technology Officer Air Commodore Arosh Vithana, focused mainly on cyber security and was manned by officers and airmen cyber security specialists who were drawn from the Cyber Security Unit of the ITU.
INFOTEL is the premier ICT conference in the country. The exhibition was held at the BMICH from November 2 to 5 and was widely patronized by the ICT community of Sri Lanka. The award ceremony was held on November 4 at Hilton Residencies.
