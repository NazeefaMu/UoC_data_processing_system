## Title: Gazettes remove Ranil, appoint Mahinda Rajapaksa as PM
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 29, 2018 - 01:00

Gazettes remove Ranil, appoint Mahinda Rajapaksa as PM

Two Extraordinary Gazettes were published removing Ranil Wickremesinghe from the Prime Minister’s post and appointing Mahinda Rajapaksa to the post, last Friday.
Both Extraordinary Gazettes were published by the Secretary to the President, Udaya R. Seneviratne.
“It is is hereby notified that the President in the exercise of powers conferred upon him under the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, has removed Ranil Wickremesinghe, who was appointed by the President under Article 42(4) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka from the office of the Prime Minister of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, with immediate effect,” one of the gazettes said.
Extraordinary Gazette no. 2094/44 issued on Friday 26 October notified the appointment of former President Mahinda Rajapaksa as the Prime Minister.
“It is hereby notified that the President under Article 42(4) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, appointed Mahinda Rajapaksa, Member of Parliament, to the Office of Prime Minister of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, with effect from October 26, 2018.”
