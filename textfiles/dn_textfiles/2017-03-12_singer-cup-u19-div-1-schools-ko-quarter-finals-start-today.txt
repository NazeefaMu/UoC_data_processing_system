## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Sunday, March 12, 2017 - 01:00

Singer Cup U19 Div 1 Schools K/O: Quarter finals  start today

The quarterfinals of the Singer Trophy Under 19 division 1 inter schools two day cricket tournament will commence from today.
Quarterfinal fixtures:
11 & 12 March - Richmond College Galle vs Mahanama College Colombo at Aarons Country club ground Horana
16 & 17 March - Thurstan College vs Dharmasoka College Ambalangoda at Kadirana
16 & 17 March - Ananda College vs Maris Stella College Negombo at Maggona
S. Thomas’ College Mt. Lavinia vs St. Peter’s College at Bambalapitiya - (YK)
