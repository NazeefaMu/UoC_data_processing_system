## Supertitle: 
## Section: T&C
## Date: Tuesday, July 10, 2018 - 01:00

Fun and activities

To mark the International St John Day on June 23, The St. John Ambulance Association and Brigade in Sri Lanka organized a two days Leisure Activity Camp and District Commissioners Meeting on June 23 and 24 at Rangiri Aqua Adventure Park, Ibbankattuwa, Dambulla.
Members of the Board of Directors, Assistant Commissioners, District Commissioner of all parts of the Island, and other Senior Officials attended the sessions.
Several Events were conducted by G R M G M B Godamunne Assistant Commissioner as camp outdoor activities, including the learn to swim event and lifesaving training methods with the assistance from then Life Guard Division of the Sri Lankan Police.
