## Title: Gunfire in Mitiyagoda: No injuries reported yet
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 10, 2017 - 16:15

Gunfire in Mitiyagoda: No injuries reported yet

 
Gunfire was reported in Mitiyagoda today.
