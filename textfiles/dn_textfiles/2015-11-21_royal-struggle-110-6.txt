## Title: Royal struggle to 110 for 6
## Supertitle: Singer Cup' Under 19 Inter Schools Cricket Tournament
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, November 21, 2015 - 01:00

Singer Cup' Under 19 Inter Schools Cricket Tournament
Royal struggle to 110 for 6

Royal College Colombo scored 110 runs for 6 wickets in day one in the first round match of 'Singer Trophy' Under 19 inter schools cricket tournament match against Zahira College, Colombo at Reid Avenue yesterday.
Match will be continued today.
Royal: 110/6 in 26 overs (Tiran Dhanapala 27, Mohommed Najath 3/41, Sajith Sameera 2/1)Vs Zahira
Isipathana pile up 262
Isipathana College Colombo bowled out 262 runs in day one in the first round match of Under 19 traditional fixture against St. Peter's College at Bambalapitiya yesterday.
The match will be continued today for day two.
Isipathana: 262 in 92.4 overs (Vishad Randika 54, Sanjula Abeywickrama 54, Salinda Ushan 50, Ayana Sirwardhane 26, Vinu Mohotty 4/68, Taneesha Weerakoon 2/56). YK
