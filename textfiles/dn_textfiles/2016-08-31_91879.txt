## Title: Vidura to boycott SLFP convention
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Wednesday, August 31, 2016 - 01:00

Vidura to boycott SLFP convention

Horana SLFP organiser Vidura Wickramanayake yesterday said he will not participate in the SLFP’s 65th convention to be held on September 5 in Kurunegala.He said the SLFP Central Organisation in Horana consisting of 122 committees at grassroots level decided not to participate in the party’s 65th convention.
Wickramanayake added that he will not resign from the post of electoral organiser.
