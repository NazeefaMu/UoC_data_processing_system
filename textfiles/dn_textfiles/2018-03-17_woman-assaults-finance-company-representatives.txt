## Title: Woman assaults finance company representatives
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, March 17, 2018 - 01:00

Woman assaults finance company representatives

Two representatives (male and a female) of a leading finance company in the Galewela area, who visited a house to collect installments for a television bought by its residents on loan basis, were assaulted by the mistress of the house.
In this connection, the said representatives had complained to Galewela Police on Thursday (15).
The complainants further said that the woman had attacked them with a broom. Police are investigating.
