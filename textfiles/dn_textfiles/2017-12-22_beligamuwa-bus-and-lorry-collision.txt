## Title: Beligamuwa bus and lorry collision
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 22, 2017 - 01:00

Beligamuwa bus and lorry collision

A bus plying from Colombo to Anuradhapura on A6 road, crashed into a lorry at Beligamuwa near Galewela town injuring six persons including a woman on Wednesday.
Later, they were rushed to Galewela divisional hospital and were transferred to Dambulla Weera Denzil kobbekaduwa base hospital under the trying circumstances.
The hospitalized were R. M. Dilani Champika Nilanthi Rathnayaka-25, K. G. Ranjith Amarapala-54, R.M.K.M. Shantha Jayawardhana-42, Nuwan Rangana Balasuriya-29, E.M. Suriyabandara-30 and Jayantha Mendis-53. All victims are residents in Anuradhapura area.
Police said that the accident occurred due to the negligence of the drivers. Alleged vehicles along with the drivers were taken into custody by Galewela police and are conducting further investigations.
