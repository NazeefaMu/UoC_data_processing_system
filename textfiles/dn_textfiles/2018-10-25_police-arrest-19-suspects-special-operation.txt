## Title: Police arrest 19 suspects in special operation
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, October 25, 2018 - 01:00

Police arrest 19 suspects in special operation

Police recently arrested 19 suspects during a special operation conducted at Hakmana.
Among those arrested were three wanted suspects, two drug dealers, two suspects transporting sand without licences, four drunken drivers, seven traffic violators, as well two other individuals. The wanted suspects and drug dealers were to be produced at the Deiyandara Magistrate’s Court, police said.
