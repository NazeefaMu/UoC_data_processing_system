## Title: Yamamay now in store at ODEL
## Supertitle: 
## Section: T&C
## Date: Friday, December 7, 2018 - 01:00

Yamamay now in store at ODEL

Yamamay, a leading Italian lingerie and swimwear brand, which boasts of not one but two international supermodels being the face of the brand, was launched by ODEL, the premier Fashion and Lifestyle retailer in the country, at a packed gathering attended by Colombo’s elite.
Yamamay’s product range includes underwear, lingerie, corsetry, swimwear, nightwear, clothing and accessories. Its mission is to make every store experience an emotional, creative and engaging environment, where the consumer can benefit from the excellent quality/price ratio.
