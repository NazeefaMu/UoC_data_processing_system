## Title: Garbage dump in Badulla irks people
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 28, 2018 - 01:00

Garbage dump in Badulla irks people

A garbage dump in Badulla irks people in the town and visitors.
Authorities have turned a blind eye to the issue, residents complain. Badulusirigama, a suburb of Badulla, too, has become a garbage dump. Many people dump their litter at public places and garbage are scattered everywhere in the town. The public urge authorities to take steps to clear the town.
