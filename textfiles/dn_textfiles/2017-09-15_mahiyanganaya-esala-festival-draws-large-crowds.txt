## Title: Mahiyanganaya Esala festival draws large crowds
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, September 15, 2017 - 01:00

Mahiyanganaya Esala festival draws large crowds

The annual Esala festival of Mahiyanganaya ended with the water cutting ceremony at Mahaweli River and the Daval Perahera - the procession held in the morning after the ritual.
The procession moved to Raja Maha Viharaya of Mahiyanganaya.
The historic festival is organized by the temple and Sri Saman Devalaya located in the temple premises, assisted by the District Secretariat of Badulla, the Divisional Secretariat of Mahiyanganaya, the Police and local government agencies.It attracts thousands of visitors every year. Most of them are from far away areas such as Hanguranketa, Maturata, Valapane, etc.
