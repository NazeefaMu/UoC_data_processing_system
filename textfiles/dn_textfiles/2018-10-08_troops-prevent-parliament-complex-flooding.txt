## Title: Troops prevent Parliament complex from flooding
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 8, 2018 - 01:00

Troops prevent Parliament complex from flooding

Troops of the 12 Gajaba Regiment and the 4 Sri Lanka Light Infantry of the 14 Division under the Security Force Headquarters - West (SFHQ-West) prevented the parliament complex from flodding on Sunday (7).
Security Forces (West) Commander Major General Sathyapriya Liyanage directed 14 Division troops to the mission. Sixty three Army personnel put sandbags along the sideways of the road that leads to the entrance across the Diyawanna Oya to prevent the premises from flooding around 3.00 am on Sunday (7).
The troops prevented flooding the road.
The 8 Sri Lanka Sinha Regiment (SLSR) troops of the 58 Division under the SFHQ-West on Friday (5) removed a huge tree fallen on to the Colombo-Kandy road near 61st mile post in Kadugannawa.
Twenty-seven 8 SLSR troops cleared the road.
