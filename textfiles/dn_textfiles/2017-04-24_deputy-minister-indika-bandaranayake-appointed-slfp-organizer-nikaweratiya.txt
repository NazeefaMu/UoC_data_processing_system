## Title: Deputy Minister Indika Bandaranayake appointed SLFP organizer for Nikaweratiya
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, April 24, 2017 - 14:15

Deputy Minister Indika Bandaranayake appointed SLFP organizer for Nikaweratiya

 
President Maithripala Sirisena appointed Deputy Minister Indika Bandaranayake as the Sri Lanka Freedom Party’s (SLFP) Organizer for Nikaweratiya electorate.
He received his appointment letter from the President today at the President’s Official Residence.
Ministers Mahinda Amaraweera, Anura Priyadarshana Yapa and Secretary to the President, P. B Abeykoon were present at this event.
