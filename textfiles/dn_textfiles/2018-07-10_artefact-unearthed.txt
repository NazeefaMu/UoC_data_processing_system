## Title: Artefact unearthed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, July 10, 2018 - 01:00

Artefact unearthed

A rare and valuable artefact had been discovered from the home of a former STF soldier from Wellawa area, Kurunegla.
The find was made by Kurunegala Police on a tip-off. It is a carved ivory casket of relics belonging to the days of ancient kings, the Police told the Daily News.
Kurunegala Police are investigating. A police chief in Kurunegala said about 213 suspects were arrested countrywide during the past five months for treasure hunting and vandalism.
