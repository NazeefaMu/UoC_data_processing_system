## Title: Mahamaya Girls beat Dudley Senanayake Central
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, October 30, 2018 - 01:00

Mahamaya Girls beat Dudley Senanayake Central

Mahamaya Girls College, Kandy beat Dudley Senanayake Central College,Tholangamuwa by 28 runs in their Girls Singer Trophy Cricket tournament match played at Kurunegala Boyagama Army Grounds recently. Himashi Kodituwakku scored 38 runs while Udara Bandara claimed 3 wickets for 19 runs for the winning Mahamaya team. J.Ranaweera hit the highest score for Dudley Senanayake scoring 24 runs.
Chief Scores:
Mahamaya Girls College, Kandy: 96/7 in 19 overs - (Himashi Kodituwakku 38, K.Rukshali 3/10, J.Ranaweera 1/7, C.Siriwardena 2/18, P.Kumarasiri 1/10)
Dudley Senanayake Central Colege: 68 in 18.3 overs - (J.Ranaweera 24, J.Kumari 16, P.Kumarasiri 17, Udara Bandara 3/19, Himashi Kodituwakku 3/7, Sithumini Abeyratne 2/4, Thamara Bandara 1/19)
