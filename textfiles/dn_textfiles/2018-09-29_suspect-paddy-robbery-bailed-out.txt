## Title: Suspect in paddy robbery bailed out
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Saturday, September 29, 2018 - 01:00

Suspect in paddy robbery bailed out

Thambuththegama Magistrate Ramila Nadeeshani released on surety bail on Wednesday a suspect who stole eight gunny bags of paddy from a neighbour . The suspect, a resident of Kithsirigama, was released on Rs 100,000 surety bail.
Police seized only one gunny bag of paddy of the lot. The suspect came in a three wheeler had stolen eight bags of paddy laid to dry.
He had told police that he stole them since he has no money.
