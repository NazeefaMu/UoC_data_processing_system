## Title: Former Rambewa PS Chairman gets 5 years jail term for accepting bribe
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, August 3, 2017 - 16:00

Former Rambewa PS Chairman gets 5 years jail term for accepting bribe

 
Former Rambewa Pradeshiya Sabha Chairman was today sentenced to 5-year- rigorous imprisonment by Colombo High Court after he was found guilty to soliciting and accepting a bribe of Rs.100,000 from a youth engaging in agriculture, who sought a foreign employment opportunity in Korea in 2007.
High Court judge Gihan Kulatunga further ordered the accused Ranil Madawa Kamal Nilame (42) to pay a fine of Rs.40,000 for other counts. The accused was found guilty to three counts out of three.
The Director General of Commission to  Investigate Allegations Against Bribery and Corruption had filed indictments against former PS chairman for soliciting a bribe from a 24-year-old youth who seeking employment in Korea.
The former Chairman had solicited a sum of  Rs. 100,000 from the youth to issue an application form for foreign employment to Korea under the Government's foreign employment scheme.
The former Chairman was arrested on or around June 21, 2007 while accepting money from the youth at the Rambewa Pradeshiya Sabha premises.
Commission to Investigate Allegations of Bribery or Corruption's Deputy Director General Ganga Heiyanthuduwa appeared for the prosecution
