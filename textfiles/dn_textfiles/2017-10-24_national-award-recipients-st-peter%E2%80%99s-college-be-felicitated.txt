## Title: National Award recipients of St. Peter’s College to be felicitated
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 24, 2017 - 01:00

National Award recipients of St. Peter’s College to be felicitated

President Maithripala Sirisena felicitated five Peterites with the highest honour of the Nation on March 20 this year.
Abbasally Akbarally, Mineka Wickremasinghe and Dr. Lakshman Weerasena were honoured for their distinguished service to the nation and Prof. Wilfred Mendis and Prof. Rohan Jayasekara were honoured for their outstanding scientific and technological achievements.
The Rector, the Priest Community; the Tutorial staff and St. Peter’s College students will felicitate them at a ceremony on October 26.
