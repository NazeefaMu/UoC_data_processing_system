## Title: President meets former MPs and Councillors
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Friday, December 22, 2017 - 01:00

President meets former MPs and Councillors

Over 30 JVP members including former parliamentarians Dimuthu Abeykoon, R.P.A Ranaweera, S.K.Subasinghe and Anjaan Umma expressed support to the President’s programme developing the country while strengthening national reconciliation.They met President Maithripala Sirisena at the President’s official residence yesterday evening. This group of JVP members included seven former Provincial Councillors and two Pradeshiya Sabha members. Picture by Sadaruwan Amarasinghe
