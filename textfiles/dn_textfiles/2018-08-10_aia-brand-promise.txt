## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Friday, August 10, 2018 - 01:00

AIA brand promise

AIA Insurance launched a new brand promise titled ‘healthier, longer and better lives with a goal to help people make the most of life.” It’s not just about living life but about living life well- living life to the fullest,” Pankaj Banerjee, CEO of AIA said at the launch in Colombo.
