## Title: West Indies need 244 to win
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, October 24, 2015 - 08:45

West Indies need 244 to win

Sri Lanka was balled out for 206 runs (3rd day) in the second innings of the second test being played in Colombo a short while ago. West Indies now need to score 244 runs to win. In the 1st innings the West Indies were balled out for 163. 
Sri Lanka 200 and 206 (Mathews 46, Siriwardana 42, Brathwaite 6-29) lead West Indies 163 by 243 runs.
