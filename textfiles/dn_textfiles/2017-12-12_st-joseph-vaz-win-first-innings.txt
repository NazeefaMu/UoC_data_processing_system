## Title: St. Joseph Vaz win on first innings
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, December 12, 2017 - 01:00

St. Joseph Vaz win on first innings

A superb knock of an unbeaten 70 runs by Kavith Sehara helped St. Joseph Vaz College Wennappuwa to beat Dharmarajah College Kandy by 74 runs first innings in their inter school under 13 division one tournament cricket match played at Wennappuwa.
Batting first Rajans were 110 for 8 in their allotted overs and at Stumps Vazians were 184 for 3.
Dharmarajah College 110/8 in 55 overs (Yasith Dissanayake 33, Navindu Rashmika 2/16, Navod Sovith 2/19).
St. Joseph Vaz College 184/3 in 43 overs (Kavith Shehara 70 n/o, Menusha Perera 34, Nethmal de Soysa 33, J.M. Perera 1/22).
