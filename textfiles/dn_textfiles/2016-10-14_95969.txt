## Date: Friday, October 14, 2016 - 01:00

Singer Cup U19 Schools Cricket: Isipathana beat  Sri Sumangala

 
Isipathana College beat Sri Sumangala College Panadura by eight wickets in the Singer Trophy Under 19 inter-schools cricket tournament match played at the Colts grounds yesterday.
Pethum Nissanka of Isipathana made his second century of the season. Shanogeeth Shanmuganathan (Trinity) and Pasindu Perera (Holy Cross) also made centuries against St. Mary’s Kegalle and Devapathiraja Rathgama respectively.
