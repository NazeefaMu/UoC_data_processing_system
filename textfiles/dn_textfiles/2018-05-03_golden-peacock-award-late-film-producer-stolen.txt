## Title: Golden Peacock award of late Film Producer stolen
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 3, 2018 - 01:00

Golden Peacock award of late Film Producer stolen

It was reported that the Golden Peacock award which was conferred on the late film maker Dr. Lester James Peiris, had been stolen from his residence yesterday morning.
The Police Media Unit said that a complaint had been lodged at the Bambalapitiya Police Station in this regard.
According to Police sources, the complaint had been made via a telephone call.
The Bambalapitiya Police has immediately deployed a team to investigate. The funeral of the veteran Film Director and Producer was held at the Independence Square yesterday afternoon with State patronage.
