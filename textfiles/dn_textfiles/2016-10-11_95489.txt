## Title: Superb knock of 127 by Vinuja Ranpul
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, October 11, 2016 - 01:00

Superb knock of 127 by Vinuja Ranpul

A brilliant 127 runs by Vinuja Ranpul which included seventeen fours and two sixes and facing 77 balls and occupying the crease for 66 minutes and associating in a third wicket partnership of 170 runs with Induwara de Alwis 66 not out guided Prince of Wales College to defeat Piliyandala Central College by 205 runs in their inter-school under 13 division one tournament cricket match worked off at Moratuwa.
Centralians batting first declared at 80 for 9 with Induwara de Alwis claiming 4 for 14 and at stumps Cambrians were 285 for 3 and Tharindu Amerasinghe contributed 45 runs.
Piliyandala Central College 80/9 on 58.9 overs (Thisuka Thimindu 33, Induwara de Alwis 4/14, Kunchana Perera 2/11).
Prince of Wales 285/3 in 40 overs (Vinuja Ranpul 127, Induwara de Alwis 66 n/o, Thrindu Amersingeh 45, Sandun Ranatunge 30).
