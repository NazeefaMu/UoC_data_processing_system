## Title: SLFP condemns attack on Sri Lanka’s High Commissioner
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Wednesday, September 7, 2016 - 01:00

SLFP condemns attack on Sri Lanka’s High Commissioner

The Sri Lanka Freedom Party yesterday condemned the attack on Sri Lankan envoy in Malaysia Ibrahim Ansarat at the Kuala Lumpur Airport on Sunday.
SLFP General Secretary and Agriculture Minister Duminda Dissanayake at a press briefing in Colombo yesterday said that the SLFP condemned the attack on the Sri Lankan envoy and the protests that were held against former Sri Lankan President Mahinda Rajapaksa.
