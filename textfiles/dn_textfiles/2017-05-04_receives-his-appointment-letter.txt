## Title: Receives his appointment letter
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 4, 2017 - 01:00

Receives his appointment letter

Central Provincial Councillor Thilina Bandara Thennakoon has been appointed the Provincial Minister of Sports, Youth, Women, Rural Development and Industries. Here, Thennakoon receives his appointment letter from President Maithripala Sirisena. Ministers Nimal Siripala de Silva, Mahinda Amaraweera, Duminda Dissanayake and Central Province Chief Minister Sarath Ekanayake and Governor Niluka Ekanayake were also present.
