## Title: Hi Life Exhibition concludes
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Monday, October 29, 2018 - 01:00

Hi Life Exhibition concludes

Hi Life Exhibition Colombo, the biggest and most exclusive haute couture, personal style, home decor and luxury products were showcased in Sri Lanka recently and the exhibition was concluded recently in Colombo.
Hi Life Exhibition was organized by Hi Life and they showcased some of the finest work by almost 80 fashion designers from across India.
A world-class exhibition featuring top Indian designers were also participated at the event. Hi Life exhibition is an Indian ethnic fashion exhibition. They have very good fashion designers coming from major parts of India like Bombay, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, other states of India and they have showcased their fashion designs in Colombo.
