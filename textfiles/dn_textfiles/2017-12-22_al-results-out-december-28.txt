## Title: AL results out on December 28
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 22, 2017 - 01:00

AL results out on December 28

Results of the 2017 G.C.E Advanced Level Examinations will be released on December 28, the Examinations Department said yesterday.
Candidates can check their results online from the department’s website www.doenets.lk 2017 A/L Exam Results Release December 28, 2017.
