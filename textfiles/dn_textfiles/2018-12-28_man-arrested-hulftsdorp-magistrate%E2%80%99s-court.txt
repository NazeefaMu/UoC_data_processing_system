## Title: Man arrested at Hulftsdorp Magistrate’s Court
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Friday, December 28, 2018 - 15:48

Man arrested at Hulftsdorp Magistrate’s Court

A man arrested by the Keselwatte police near the Hulftsdorp Magistrate’s Court this afternoon.Who found in possession of a locally-made weapon and five cartridges ,Police said.They said the suspect was identified as Mahawattege Prasanna 48-year-old, resident of Mattakkuliya.Keselwatte police are conducting further investigations.
