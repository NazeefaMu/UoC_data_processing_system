## Title: President at CHOGM 2015
## Supertitle: 
## Section: World
## Date: Monday, November 30, 2015 - 01:00

President at CHOGM 2015

President Maithripala Sirisena’s visit to Malta to attend the Commonwealth Heads of Government Meeting (CHOGM) – 2015, ended on a highly successful note yesterday.
President Sirisena, who attended the CHOGM as the current Chair-in-Office, handed over the position to the Prime Minister of Malta Joseph Muscat. President Sirisena also met several key world leaders for discussions on the sidelines of the meeting. (Pictures courtesy President’s Media Division)
