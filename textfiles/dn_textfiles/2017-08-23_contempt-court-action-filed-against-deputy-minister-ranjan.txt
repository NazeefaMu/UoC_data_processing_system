## Title: Contempt of Court action filed against Deputy Minister Ranjan
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Wednesday, August 23, 2017 - 01:00

Contempt of Court action filed against Deputy Minister Ranjan

Ven. Magalkande Sudantha Thera of Bodu Bala Sena Organization yesterday made a complaint to Supreme Court seeking a contempt of court action against Deputy Minister Ranjan Ramanayake for allegedly making an insulting attack on the reputation of the judiciary and lawyers at a press conference held on August 21
