## Title: Furniture store in Yakkala catches fire
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, January 6, 2018 - 01:00

Furniture store in Yakkala catches fire

A furniture store located close to Yakkala along the Colombo-Kandy road caught fire at around 2:30 pm yesterday. While the immediate cause of the blaze is unknown, fire engulfed the entire building reducing it to cinders. The fire brigade attached to the Gampaha Municipal Council doused the blaze. Yakkala police are conducting further investigations. (Picture by Sisila Gunathilake,Gampaha District Special Correspondent).
