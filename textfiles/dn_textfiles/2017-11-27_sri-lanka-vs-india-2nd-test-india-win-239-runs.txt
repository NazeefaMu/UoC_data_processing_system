## Title: Sri Lanka vs. India 2nd Test: India win by 239 runs
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, November 27, 2017 - 13:32

Sri Lanka vs. India 2nd Test: India win by 239 runs

 
India won the 2nd Test against Sri Lanka by 239 runs, earning a lead of 1-0 in the three match series, at the VCA Stadium in Nagpur.
The Sri Lankan team was all out for 205 in the first innings and 166 runs in the second innings.
India declared a 610 score with a 405 lead in their first innings.
The final and 3rd Test will be held at the Feroz Shah Kotla, Delhi this Saturday.
