## Title: EDEX Expo-2017
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, January 21, 2017 - 01:00

EDEX Expo-2017

The three-day annual EDEX Expo-2017,Sri Lanka’s largest education exhibition and job fair, conducted by the Royal College Union (RCU) was inaugurated by Foreign Affairs Deputy Minister Dr. Harsha de Silva, Japanese Ambassador in Sri Lanka Kenichi Suganuma and Royal College Principal B.A. Abeyratne at the BMICH.
