## Title: Navy arrests six for illegal fishing
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, February 13, 2017 - 01:00

Navy arrests six for illegal fishing

Naval personnel attached to SLNS Vijaya located in Kalpitiya in the Northwestern Naval Command arrested six local fishermen engaged in illegal fishing practices without valid licences in the sea areas of Ilanthadiya and Nororochcholai on Saturday.
Two dinghies and an unauthorised net used for fishing activities were also taken into naval custody.
The fishermen along with the belongings were handed over to the Assistant Director of Fisheries, Puttalam and Department of Wildlife Conservation in Kandakkuliya for further investigations, the Navy Media Unit stated.
