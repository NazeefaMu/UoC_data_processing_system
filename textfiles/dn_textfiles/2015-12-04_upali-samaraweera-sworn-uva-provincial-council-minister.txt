## Title: Upali Samaraweera sworn in as Uva Provincial Council Minister
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Friday, December 4, 2015 - 01:00

Upali Samaraweera sworn in as Uva Provincial Council Minister

Upali Delton Samaraweera was sworn in before President Maithripala Sirisena as the Uva Provincial Council Minister of Agriculture, Irrigation, Lands, Animal Products and Clean Water Fishing.
He was sworn in at the President's Office in the Parliament complex yesterday.
Samaraweera who is a UNP Member of Parliament from the Badulla District, was the Opposition Leader of Uva Provincial Council for 15 years.
