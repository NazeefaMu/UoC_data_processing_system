## Title: RDB bank opens ATM at Kandy Hospital
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Wednesday, August 2, 2017 - 01:00

RDB bank opens ATM at Kandy Hospital

Regional Development Bank (RDB) , the premier and the fastest growing state owned development bank in the island located its ATM machine at Kandy Hospital recently, along with the participation of Deputy General Manager Jayasinghe Bandara Munasinghe,  Regional General Manager  Central Pprovince D.M.Senaratne Bandara, Assistant General Manager D.P. Dharmadasa  and Director of Kandy Hospital Dr. Saman Rathnayake.
