## Title: Air Force holds Oral Health and Medical Programme in Kandy
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, September 29, 2017 - 01:00

Air Force holds Oral Health and Medical Programme in Kandy

Under the guidance of the Commander of the Air Force, Air Marshal Kapila Jayampathy, the Medical and Oral Health Care Programme for the Maha Sanga and for the Staff of Sri Dalada Maligawa (Temple of the Tooth Relic) was conducted by the Sri Lanka Air Force on September 27 at the Sri Dalada Maligawa, Kandy.
This programme was organized jointly by the Directorate of Health Services and the Directorate of Dental Services of the SLAF.
Over five hundred patients were treated with Advanced Medical Equipment and Mobile Dental Equipment. This is the first occasion SLAF conducted a Medical Campaign at Dalada Maligawa in addition to the oral health care programme which was participated by leading consultants.
Director Health Services of SLAF Air Commodore Lalith Jayaweera, Director Dental Services of SLAF Group Captain W. K. M. Weerasekara, Diyawadana Nilame of the Sri Dalada Maligawa Pradeep Nilanga Dela, Air Force officers and other ranks participated in the programme.
