## Title: Elle tournament in Batticaloa
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 01:00

Elle tournament in Batticaloa

The thirty throw ball Elle tournament -2017 among the staff of the Magistrate, District and High Courts of Batticaloa organized by Batticaloa High Courts Welfare Association. ‘A’ group scored 3 rounds emerging champions held at the Batticaloa Municipal Council’s Paddalipuram Grounds, yesterday.
The tournament comprised two groups A & B. Batting first group B scored 2 rounds of the tournament.The champion trophy and runner up trophy were handed over by the Batticaloa Magistrate Courts Judge Maniccawasagar Ganesharaja.
