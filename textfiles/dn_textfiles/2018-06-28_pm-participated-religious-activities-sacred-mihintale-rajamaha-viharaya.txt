## Title: PM participated in religious activities at the sacred Mihintale Rajamaha Viharaya
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 28, 2018 - 01:00

PM participated in religious activities at the sacred Mihintale Rajamaha Viharaya

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe who participated in religious activities at the sacred Mihintale Rajamaha Viharaya, serving meals to devotees at a dansela organised by the Dana Vardhana Samithiya of Mudungoda, Gampaha and the illuminated Mihintale Raja Maha Viharaya. Pictures by Hirantha Gunathilake and Shan Rupassara.
