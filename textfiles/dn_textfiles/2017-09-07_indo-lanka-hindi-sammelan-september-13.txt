## Title: Indo-Lanka Hindi Sammelan on September 13
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 01:00

Indo-Lanka Hindi Sammelan on September 13

On the occasion of Hindi Diwas 2017, the Indian Cultural Centre, High Commission of India, Colombo is organizing a one-day conference ‘Indo-Lanka Hindi Sammelan’ in collaboration with the Hindi Department of the University of Kelaniya on September 13 from 11 am to 5 pm at the K-3 auditorium of the University of Kelaniya.
Hindi written in Devnagari script was approved as an official language by the Constituent Assembly on September 14 in 1949. To commemorate the occasion, Hindi Diwas is celebrated on September 14 every year.
Hindi is one of the most common foreign languages in Sri Lanka and almost 80 institutions in Sri Lanka are engaged in teaching Hindi. The Hindi Department at the University of Kelaniya is one of the leading institutions among them.
Indo-Sri Lanka Hindi Sammelan will feature renowned scholars from both countries who will present papers on the topic ‘Hindi-e learning - pride and possibilities’.
The first ever e-magazine in Hindi in Sri Lanka will be launched on the occasion edited and published by the Indian Cultural Centre and a Hindi translation of Sinhala book by Senior Prof. Upul Ranjith Hewawitanagamage, Head, Department of Hindi Studies, University of Kelaniya will be released on this occasion. All renowned Universities and institutions engaged in teaching Hindi in the island will participate in the programme. Apart from the conference, the Indian Cultural Centre will be organizing an all island Hindi essay competition, speech, dance and music competitions at the centre from September 6 to 14 to celebrate Hindi-Diwas 2017.
