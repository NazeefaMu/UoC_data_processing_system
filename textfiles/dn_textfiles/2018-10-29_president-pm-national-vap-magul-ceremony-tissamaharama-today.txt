## Title: President, PM at national Vap Magul ceremony in Tissamaharama today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 29, 2018 - 01:00

President, PM at national Vap Magul ceremony in Tissamaharama today

The National Vap Magul Ceremony will be held today under the patronage of President Maithripala Sirisena at the historic Sandagirigoda paddy field in Tissamaharamaya.
Prime Minister Mahinda Rajapaksa will also attend this occasion and this will be his first official event after assuming duties as Prime Minister.
In line with the Vap Magul ceremony, which is organised by the Agriculture Ministry, a bio-diversity fruit garden established at a temple premises will also be opened by the President. Over 70 local fruit verities are planted at this bio-diversity fruit garden.
A bumper harvest is expected this Maha season as a result of the rain experienced countrywide.
