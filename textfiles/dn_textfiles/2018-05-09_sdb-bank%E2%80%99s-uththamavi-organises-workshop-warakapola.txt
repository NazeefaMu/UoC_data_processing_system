## Title: SDB bank’s Uththamavi organises workshop  in Warakapola
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Wednesday, May 9, 2018 - 01:00

SDB bank’s Uththamavi organises workshop  in Warakapola

In line with the International Women’s Day, SDB bank organized branch level workshops for women islandwide.
The event organized by Warakapola branch was held on recently, at Thalgahadeniya Primary Thrift and Credit Co-operative Societies (PTCCS) Conference Hall with the participation of B.A. Kiriwandeniya – Chairperson of Uththamavee Guarantee Company and Government officials representing Warakapola Divisional Secretariat Office, Warakapola Police and Warakapola Base Hospital.
