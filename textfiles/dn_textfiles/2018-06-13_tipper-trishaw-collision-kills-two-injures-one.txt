## Title: Tipper-trishaw collision kills two, injures one
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 13, 2018 - 01:00

Tipper-trishaw collision kills two, injures one

Two were killed and another was injured in an accident when a three-wheeler collided head-on with a tipper on the A6 Road at Galewela, on Monday. Accordingly, two passengers in the three-wheeler had died, while its driver had sustained critical injuries and was admitted to the Galewela Base Hospital, prior to being transferred to the Kurunegala Teaching Hospital.
The deceased were of the ages 22 and 24 years. Galewela Police said that it was the 15th fatal accident that had occurred in the area this year, while the resulting death toll so far was 18.
