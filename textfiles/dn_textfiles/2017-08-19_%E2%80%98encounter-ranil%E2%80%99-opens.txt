## Title: ‘Encounter Ranil’ opens
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, August 19, 2017 - 01:00

‘Encounter Ranil’ opens

The exhibition depicting special events of Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe’s four-decade political journey, ‘Heta Dakina Ranil’ (Encounter Ranil), was opened by President Maithripala Sirisena at the BMICH on Thursday.
The exhibition appreciates the Premier’s service to the nation throughout his career. It will be open to the public from 10 am to 8 pm daily until Sunday, August 20.
