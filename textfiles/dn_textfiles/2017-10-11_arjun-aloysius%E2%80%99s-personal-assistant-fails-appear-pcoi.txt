## Title: Arjun Aloysius’s personal assistant fails to appear before PCoI
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, October 11, 2017 - 13:45

Arjun Aloysius’s personal assistant fails to appear before PCoI

 
Former Perpetual Treasuries (PTL) Director Arjun Aloysius’s personal assistant, who was due to submit PTL files before the Presidential Commission of Inquiry (PCoI) was hospitalised due to a cardiac arrest today.
