## Title: Damitha summoned before PRECIFAC
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, November 7, 2015 - 01:00

Damitha summoned before PRECIFAC

Actress Damitha Abeyrathne was summoned yesterday to the Presidential Commission of Inquiry to Investigate and Inquire into Serious Acts of Fraud, Corruption and Abuse of Power, State Resources and Privileges (PRECIFAC) to record a statement.
Commission sources said that she made a statement relating to the probe carried out into complaint about fraud and corruption at SriLankan Airlines.
A senior official of the commission said PRECIFAC had received a complaint that funds had been obtained from SriLankan Airlines for Mahinda Rajapaksa’s election campaign during the last Presidential election.
The Commission also recorded statements from actresses Ruwanthi Mangala, former MP Upeksha Swarnamalee (Paba) and veteran artiste Bandula Wijeweera regarding the same matter on October 29.
Meanwhile, a statement was recorded from former Navy Commander Admiral Jayantha Perera by the PRECIFAC on the Rakna Lanka Ltd. floating armoury.
A statement was also recorded from former Navy Commander Admiral Somathilake Dissanayake on the same issue last Wednesday.
Admiral Jayantha Perera was summoned to appear before the Presidential Commission of Inquiry to Investigate and Inquire into Serious Acts of Fraud, Corruption and Abuse of Power, State Resources and Privileges (PRECIFAC) yesterday, to record a statement with regard to the investigations into the Rakna Lanka Ltd. and its arms transactions.
A senior PRECIFAC official said that the former Navy Commander was summoned to clarify certain matters that had arisen with regard to the evidence recorded earlier in this connection.
The Commission recorded a statement from ex-Navy Commander Admiral Somathilake Dissanayake regarding the same issue on November 4.
Another ex-Navy Commander Admiral Jayanath Colombage is also to appear before the Presidential Commission on November 9 to record a statement regarding investigations into Rakna Lanka Ltd.
Former Defence Secretary Gotabhaya Rajapaksa, current Defence Secretary Karunasena Hettiarachchi and Rakna Arakshaka Lanka Ltd Chairman Victor Samaraweera were earlier summoned by the Commission to record their statements.
