## Title: Probe into drone flying near bathing woman
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, August 10, 2017 - 01:00

Probe into drone flying near bathing woman

Wennappuwa Police launched an investigation into an incident where a drone had flown near a location where a woman was bathing in an open area surrounded by sheets.The complaint was received from the woman against the owner of the drone.
According to the complaint, the owner of the drone had flown it several times to where the woman was bathing.
After arresting the suspect, the police found that the drone did not have a camera attached to it.
Police later released the suspect on police bail. Police said further action will be taken after receiving a report from technical officers.
