## Title: Seasonal discounts for  COMBANK Credit Card holders
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Friday, December 22, 2017 - 01:00

Seasonal discounts for  COMBANK Credit Card holders

Christmas is almost upon us and Commercial Bank has announced that Credit Cardholders can enjoy discounts of up to 25% on their purchases on specified days at all leading supermarkets, until the end of the year.
Arpico Supercentre outlets will offer a 20% discount on seafood till December 22 and the same discount on fruits and vegetables from December 25-29, on all Commercial Bank Credit Card purchases. This discount is applicable on purchases of over Rs. 1500 made between 4 pm and 9 pm at this supermarket on the specified dates.
Combank credit cardholders can also enjoy savings of 25% when shopping for meat items and ice-cream at Cargills Food City outlets until December 31. This offer is valid for purchases of over Rs. 1000, and the maximum discount per bill is Rs 1,500,the Bank said.
Shoppers at Keells Super outlets too can now enjoy a 25% discount on all vegetable purchases included on a bill of at least Rs.2500, when paying with their Commercial Bank Credit Cards.
This offer is valid during the weekends until the end of December.
Combank credit cardholders can also enjoy a 25% discount on fish and vegetables till 22nd of December, and the same discount on chicken parts and fish from December 27-29 at all LAUGFS Supermarkets. This discount is applicable on purchases of over Rs. 2000.
Additionally, Commercial Bank Credit and Debit cards offers up to 55% in discounts at over 2300 outlets retailing clothing and accessories, jewellery,watches,lifestyleproducts and many others this festive season.
