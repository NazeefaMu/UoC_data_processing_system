## Title: Twenty nabbed with cannabis
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 24, 2017 - 01:00

Twenty nabbed with cannabis

Twenty suspects with 81,500 milligrams of cannabis in their possession possession were taken into custody by the Tangalle Police Drug Eradication unit following a raid conducted at Middeniya.
The raid was conducted at Middeniya, Julampitiya and Galwadiya areas.The special Drug Eradication unit sources said the suspects had been handed over to the Middeniya Police for legal action.The Tangalle Drug Eradication unit took the suspects into custody.
