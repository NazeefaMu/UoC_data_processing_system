## Title: Elephant safaris booming in Sigiriya, Habarana
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 30, 2018 - 01:00

Elephant safaris booming in Sigiriya, Habarana

Elephant safaris had become popular in the Sigiriya and Habarana areas, where foreign tourists frequent. According to leading hoteliers, elephant safaris are at present, a lucrative business.
Meanwhile, the public said that elephant safaris were risky. Previously, certain tourists who had gone on elephant safaris had been hospitalised after falling off the animals.
