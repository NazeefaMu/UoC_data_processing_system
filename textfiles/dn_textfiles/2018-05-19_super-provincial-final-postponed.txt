## Title: Super Provincial final postponed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, May 19, 2018 - 01:00

Super Provincial final postponed

The final of the Super Provincial 50-over tournament Galle vs. Colombo which was scheduled to be played on Sunday (May 20) at the R Premadasa Stadium has been postponed indefinitely, owing to the prevailing bad weather conditions. A new date would be notified later, states an SLC media release.
