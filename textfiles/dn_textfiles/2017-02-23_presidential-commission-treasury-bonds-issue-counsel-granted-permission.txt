## Title: Presidential Commission on Treasury Bonds issue: Counsel granted permission to appear for Perpetual Treasuries
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, February 23, 2017 - 01:00

Presidential Commission on Treasury Bonds issue: Counsel granted permission to appear for Perpetual Treasuries

President Counsel Nihal Fernando appearing before the Presidential Commission of Inquiry to Investigate and Inquire into the Issuance of the Treasury Bonds issue yesterday, made an application seeking permission to appear on behalf of the
Perpetual Treasuries Limited at the proceedings currently taking place on the CBSL treasury bond issue.
PC Nihal Fernando sought permission to appear before the Presidential Commission together with lawyers Niranjan Arulpragasam and Romali Tudawe.
The inquiry session was adjourned for five minutes to discuss over the request made by PC Nihal Fernando.
Then the Presidential Commission, having considered the application, granted leave to the aforesaid personnel to appear before the Presidential Commission on behalf of the Perpetual Treasuries Limited. The Presidential Commission comprised of Supreme Court judges Justice K.T.Chitrasri Esquire, Justice Prasanna Jayawardena and Retired Deputy Auditor General Kandasamy Veluppillai.
Requesting permission from the Presidential Commission, PC Nihal Fernando said their appearance marks their interest in providing assistance and clarifications necessary to the commission on the matter in question .
PC Fernando also said they are not representing the former CBSL governor Arjun Mahendran but is appearing only on behalf of the Perpetual Treasuries Limited.
The Presidential Commission directing guidelines to PC Fernando advised him to mark his appearance every day since they are to carry out the inquiries within a very limited time frame.
“If you want to ask any questions at the inquiry, first you will have to put them through the Commission and then we will decide whether we can give you permission to question the individuals testifying.” Chairman of the Presidential Commission Justice K.T.Chitrasiri informed PC Nihal Fernando to which the latter readily agreed.
Meanwhile, Attorney Harshana Fernando made a similar request to appear before the Presidential Commission on behalf of the Tender Board Chairman W.A.Wijewardane.
Harshana Fernando sought permission to appear before the Presidential Commission together with lawyers Shanil Perera and Natasha Noordin.
Harshana Fernando said their appearance is on behalf of the Tender Board Chairman was with the view to provide the latter, with legal guidance lest he be summoned by the Presidential Commission to testify.
Both parties appearing on behalf of Perpetual Treasuries Limited and the Tender Board Chairman were given opportunities to question the CBSL Governor Indrajit Coomaraswamy at the inquiry session held at the Presidential Commission of Inquiry to Investigate and Inquire into the Issuance of Treasury Bonds yesterday.
