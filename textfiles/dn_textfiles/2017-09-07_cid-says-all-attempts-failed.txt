## Title: CID says all attempts failed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 01:00

CID says all attempts failed

All attempts to contact Perpetual Treasuries Limited owner Arjun Aloysius by the CID has failed, by late evening yesterday.
Accordingly, the CID has not being able to handover the notice issued by the Treasury Bond Commission summoning Aloysius to give a statement to the CID.
Aloysius’s out of radar status may change by today.
Meanwhile, the Attorney General’s Department says Aloysius has not left the country using either of the two passports owned by him. Aloysius has two passports, one Sri Lankan and the other Australian.
