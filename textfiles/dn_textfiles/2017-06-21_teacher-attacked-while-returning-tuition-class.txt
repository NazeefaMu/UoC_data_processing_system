## Title: Teacher attacked while returning from tuition class
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 21, 2017 - 01:00

Teacher attacked while returning from tuition class

A school teacher attached to the Kuliyapitiya Central College in Kurunegala was attacked with an iron bar by two unknown persons who had arrived on a motorcycle on June 15.
The severely injured teacher is now undergoing treatment at the Kuliyapitiya Government District Hospital. The victim D.G. Harsha Dhammika Dematawala, 40, is a resident of Padiwela area off Kurunegala.
He was attacked while returning home on his motorcycle after conducting a tuition class in Kuliyapitiya. The Police are investigating.
