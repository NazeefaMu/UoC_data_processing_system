## Title: Motorist dies in accident
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, October 26, 2018 - 01:00

Motorist dies in accident

A motorist succumbed to his injuries after his car collided head-on with a private bus which came from the opposite direction on the main road from Nattandiya to Dankotuwa.
According to the police, the accident took place at the 13th Mile Post, due to the reckless driving of both drivers.
The driver of the car succumbed to his injuries after being admitted to the Marawila Government Hospital with the help of the neighbouring community.
The victim was identified as Kurulugama Lekamlage Janaka Pradeep Kumarasinghe, 36. He was a resident of Millana in Getahatta.
The postmortem was to be carried out at the Marawila Hospital yesterday.
The driver of the private bus was taken into police custody by Marawila police.
Investigations are continuing.
