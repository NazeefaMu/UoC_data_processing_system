## Title: Illegal sand transporter nabbed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, July 21, 2017 - 01:00

Illegal sand transporter nabbed

A lorry driver who pleaded guilty of transporting sand illegally from Deduruoya was fined Rs.150,000 by Chilaw District Judge Heshan de Mel.
The suspect is a resident of Chilaw. The stock of sand was confiscated.Chilaw Police prosecuted.
A total of 23 police officers have been deployed to nab illegal transporters of sand and clay in Kurunegala and Puttalam Districts.
