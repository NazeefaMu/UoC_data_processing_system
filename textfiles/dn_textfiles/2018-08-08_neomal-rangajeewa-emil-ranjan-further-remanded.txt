## Title: Neomal Rangajeewa, Emil Ranjan further remanded
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Wednesday, August 8, 2018 - 01:00

Neomal Rangajeewa, Emil Ranjan further remanded

Police Narcotic Bureau Inspector Neomal Rangajeewa and former Prisons Commissioner Emil Ranjan Lamahewa, who were in remand custody over the Welikada Prison riots in 2012, were yesterday ordered to be further remanded till August 21 by Colombo Additional Magistrate Priyantha Liyanage.
Counsel Sanjaya Gamage appearing for the suspects informed the magistrate that the delay of the investigations prevented his client from pleading bail.
Criminal Investigation Department officials told court that they had to investigate many weapons and it deyaled the investigations.
The Counsel appearing for the suspects stated that in case the Magistrate’s Court refused granting bail, they would plead bail from the Higher Court.
The Magistrate fixed the inquiry for August 21.
