## Title: Chief Prelates visit Bodh Gaya
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, September 26, 2017 - 01:00

Chief Prelates visit Bodh Gaya

Most Venerable Mahanayaka Thera of Asigiriya, Ramanna and Sri Amarapura and Anunayaka Thera of Malwatte chapters sought the Buddha’s blessings at Bodh Gaya in India recently. They also participated in the Dhamma Sabha organised by the Maha Bodhi Society of India on the occasion of the 153rd Birth Anniversary Celebration of Srimath Anagarika Dharmapala at Bodh Gaya, India. The religious leaders were accorded a special reception in India.
