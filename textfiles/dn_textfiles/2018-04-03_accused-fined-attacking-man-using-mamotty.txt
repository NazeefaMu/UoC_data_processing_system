## Title: Accused fined for attacking man using mamotty
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, April 3, 2018 - 01:00

Accused fined for attacking man using mamotty

Two brothers wee fined Rs. 100,000 (each Rs. 50,000) and ordered to pay Rs. 50,000 as compensation to the injured person by the Kurunegala chief Magistrate Sampath Heweawasam after being found guilty of threatening to kill Rajapaksha Mudiyaselage Premarathne of Pothuhera on April 30, 2014.
In this case, the accused Gunarathna Banda and Ajith Saliya Jayathissa of Pothuhera Bavilgamuwa were found guilty of threatening to kill one Rajapaksha Mudiyanselage Premarathne of Pothuhera by assaulting using a mammoty.
The complainer's counsel told Court that his client was not in a good position to walk after the assault and moved that the two accused to be ordered to pay Rs. 200,000 as compensation to the victim.
However, the Magistrate ordered them to pay Rs.100,000 as compensation to the injured person.
The Judge also ordered that the accused should serve a six months jail term if they fail to pay the compensation.
