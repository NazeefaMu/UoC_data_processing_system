## Title: Two more priority bus lanes opened
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 30, 2017 - 01:00

Two more priority bus lanes opened

The construction of a transport centre will commence this year in the Government's bid to convert Battaramulla into the full-fledged administration city in the country, said Megapolis and Western Development Minister Patali Champika Ranawaka, yesterday.
Minister also said that the planning of the constructions and the whole project is reaching the last stage and would be able to commence this year.
He made these observations opening the priority bus lane from Diyatha Uyana to Borella from 6 am to 9 am yesterday, as an initial step of enabling related service in Battaramulla which is to be the administrative city.
He said that that another priority bus lane was opened from Nidahas Mawatha to Thummulla junction from 4 pm to 7 pm, yesterday. He also said that the Borella-Maradana road is under renovation and once it is complete in November, the priority bus lane from Borella to Maradana will be opened from 6 am to 9 am.
He also added that although inconveniences have to be faced by all on the first day, the project will be implemented as strengthening the public transport service is very much important as a solution for the traffic congestion.
Megapolis and Western Development Deputy Minister Lasantha Alagiyawanna also participated in the event.
