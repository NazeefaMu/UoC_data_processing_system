## Title: SriLankan  studying  10 point plan
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, April 27, 2018 - 01:04

SriLankan  studying  10 point plan

SriLankan Airlines is currently studying a 10 point plan for its future development and revamping submitted by the famed Nyras – Aviation Consultancy Institution, SriLankan Airlines Chairman Ranjith Fernando said.
“The objective of the study is to find out whether it would be beneficial for the future development and welfare of the airline,” he said.
