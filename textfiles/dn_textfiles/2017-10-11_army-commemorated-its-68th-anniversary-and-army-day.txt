## Title: Army commemorated its 68th Anniversary and the Army Day
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, October 11, 2017 - 01:00

Army commemorated its 68th Anniversary and the Army Day

Sri Lanka Army commemorated its 68th Anniversary and the Army Day yesterday morning with a colourful parade at Panagoda Army Cantonment grounds. The Chief Guest was, Lieutenant General Mahesh Senanayake, Commander of the Army. Here, the Commander inspecting the guard of honour. Picture by Rukmal Gamage
