## Title: Pathivatha launched
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Tuesday, February 20, 2018 - 01:00

Pathivatha launched

A biography of the late film director Dr Dharmasena Pathiraja was launched at a special felicitation ceremony held to honour the filmmaker. The event was held at the NFC film hall on February 8. Pathiraja’s films ‘Paradige’, ‘Mathuyam Davasa’, ‘In Search of Road’ and ‘Swaroopa’ too were screened at the event.
