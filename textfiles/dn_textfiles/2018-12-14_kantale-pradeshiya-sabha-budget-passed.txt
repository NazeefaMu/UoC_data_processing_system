## Title: Kantale Pradeshiya Sabha Budget passed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Friday, December 14, 2018 - 01:00

Kantale Pradeshiya Sabha Budget passed

The Budget proposals for 2019 presented at the Kantale Pradeshiya Sabha was passed with 13 votes in favour.
The Sri Lanka Freedom Party (SLFP), Sri Lanka Podujana Peamuna (SLPP) and one member from an independent group, along with the Lanka Mahajana Congress, voted in favour. The five members of the United National Party (UNP) and one independent member voted against the Budget.
UNP members Janaka Bandara and M. Samanjeeva were absent.
