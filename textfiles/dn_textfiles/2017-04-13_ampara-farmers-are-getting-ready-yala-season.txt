Ampara farmers are getting ready for the Yala season

 Ampara farmers are getting ready for the Yala season paddy cultivation. Preparatory tillage and canal cleaning are under way utilising excessive water flowing into the plantation area from the D. S. Senanayaka Samudraya. Picture by A.B. Abdul Gafoor, Ampara District Group Correspondent
