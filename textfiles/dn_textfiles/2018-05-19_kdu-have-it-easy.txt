## Title: KDU have it easy
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, May 19, 2018 - 01:00

KDU have it easy

Kotalawala Defence University beat formidable Old Thurstan Sports Club by seven wickets in their Inter Club division three Colombo District cricket tournament match played at Colombo. This tournament is conducted by Sri Lanka Cricket.
Batting first Old Thurstanites were skittled out for a paltry 89 runs with Tharanga Fernando claiming 3 for 11. In reply Kotalawala Defense University notched up the required score for the loss of three wickets with Pawantha Jayasinghe capturing 3 for 32. K. D. U is coached by former Vidyalian Sumith Prasanna.
Chief Scores:
Old Thurstanites - 89 in 24 overs - Prasanna Pushpakumara 21, Kavishan nandakumara 2/16, Tharanga Fernandop 2/11, harsha Salgado 2/15
Kotalawala DU - 90/3 in 11 overs - Harsha Salgado 31, Ishara Mendis 21, Pawantha Jayasinghe 3/32
