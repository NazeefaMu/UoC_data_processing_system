## Title: Story of Seetha with a difference
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Monday, January 15, 2018 - 01:00

Story of Seetha with a difference

“ChandanaWickremasinghe Dancers’ Guild” in collaboration with Indo Sri Lanka Foundation will present for a second consecutive year, a performance of the ballet to depict the enigma of the epic of Ramayana, “The Story of Seetha” with a difference by a group of professionals January 27 at the Bishop’s College, Auditorium, Colombo 2 at 7pm. The tickets could be purchased at the entrance of the Bishop’s College Auditorium on January 27 and advanced reservation of bookings could be made through www.mytickets.lk.
