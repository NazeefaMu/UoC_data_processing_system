## Title: Pinkama to mark death anniversary
## Supertitle: Premasiri Abeysinghe
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, November 6, 2018 - 01:00

Premasiri Abeysinghe
Pinkama to mark death anniversary

A pinkama to mark the first death anniversary of Premasiri Abeysinghe, former Editor of Lake House Budusarana publication, will be held at the Kahathuduwa Sri Nandanarama on November 10 under the patronage of Viharadhipathi Ven. Kahathuduwe Wimalatissa Thera.
On November 10, a Sath Budu pooja will be held at 6.30 pm followed by a dhammadesana delivered by Ven. Prof. Medagoda Abhayatissa Thera at 7 pm.
The pinkama will conclude with a sanghika dana to the Maha Sangha at the Kahathuduwa ‘Sinha Meduara’ residence on November 11.
The Nandanarama Viharaya Dayake Sabha and the Kulangana Samithi are organizing the event.
