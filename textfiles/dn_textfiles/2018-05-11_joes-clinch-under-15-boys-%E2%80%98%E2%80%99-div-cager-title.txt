## Title: Joes clinch under 15 Boys ‘A’ Div Cager title
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, May 11, 2018 - 01:00

Joes clinch under 15 Boys ‘A’ Div Cager title

St. Joseph’s College, Colombo 10 emerged boys champions at the recently concluded All Island Under 15 ‘A’ Division Basketball Tournament organized by the Sri Lanka Schools Basketball Association.
St. Joseph’s College beat Lyceum International School, Nugegoda by 64-34 in the final. They defeated Ananda College, Colombo 59-31 in semi finals and Lyceum International School, Wattala 62-39 in the Quarter-finals on their way to the final.
The captain of the Josephian team Shehan Fernando was adjudged as the Most Valuable Player of the tournament while the Vice Captain Shannon de Zilva was adjudged as the Best Offensive Player of the Tournament.
