## Title: Habaraduwa railway station opened
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, November 27, 2017 - 01:00

Habaraduwa railway station opened

The newly constructed Habaraduwa railway station was opened by Transport Minister Nimal Siripala de Silva yesterday.Tertiary Education Minister Chandima Weerakkody and Railways General Manager S.M. Abeywickrema was present.
(Pictures by Lankinda Nanayakkara,Imaduwa group correspondent)
