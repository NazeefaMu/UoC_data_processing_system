## Title: New Governors take oaths before President Srisena
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 12, 2018 - 13:20

New Governors take oaths before President Srisena

 
Newly appointed seven Governors took oaths before President Maithripala Sirisena at the Presidential Secretariat, in Colombo, today.
Hemakumara Nanayakkara – Western Province
K.C. Logeswaran – North Western Province
Niluka Ekanayake – Sabaragamuwa Province
Reginald Coorey - Central Province
Marshal Perera - Southern Province
M.P. Jayasinghe - North Central Province
P.B. Dissanayake – Uva Province
Secretary to the President Austin Fernando was also present at the occasion.
