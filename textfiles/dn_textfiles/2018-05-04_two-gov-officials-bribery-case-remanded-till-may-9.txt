## Title: Two gov. officials bribery case: Remanded till May 9
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, May 4, 2018 - 10:43

Two gov. officials bribery case: Remanded till May 9

 
President’s Chief of Staff, I.H.K. Mahanama and State Timber Corporation (STC) Chairman P. Dissanayake who were arrested for accepting a bribe of Rs.20 million from an Indian businessman were today remanded until May 9.
The two were arrested by the Bribery Commission while accepting the bribe at a five star hotel in Colombo, last night.
The deal was in regard to a deal in the shares of the Kantale sugar factory.
