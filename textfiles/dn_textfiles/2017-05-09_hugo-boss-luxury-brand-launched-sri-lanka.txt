## Title: HUGO BOSS, luxury brand launched in Sri Lanka
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 01:00

HUGO BOSS, luxury brand launched in Sri Lanka

HUGO BOSS celebrated the opening of a new BOSS Store on April 28, at the Arcade Independence Square, being the first premium luxury brand to be introduced in Sri Lanka.
Abans PLC, the flagship company of Abans Group of Companies and a leading retail brand, will be the authorised distributor for HUGO BOSS in Sri Lanka.
