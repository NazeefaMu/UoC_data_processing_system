## Title: Illicit liquor brewer arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Friday, December 28, 2018 - 01:00

Illicit liquor brewer arrested

Following a tip-off, an illicit brew manufacturer was taken into custody by Kandeketiya Police when his production den at Kahambaava, in Akurukaduva, off Kandaketiya, Viyaluva close to the Mahiyanganaya-Badulla road, was raided last Sunday(23) noon.
The Police seized 15 litres of brew mixture from the production den.
The suspect was released on Police bail after an interrogation.
