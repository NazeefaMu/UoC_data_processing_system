## Title: Magazine launched for Poson
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Wednesday, July 12, 2017 - 01:00

Magazine launched for Poson

Bathi Kusum an annual publication by the Buddhist Association of Kalubowila Teaching Hospital was launched at Hospital Auditorium recently.
Prof. Sanath Nandasiri, Visharada Malkanthi Nandasiri, Visharada Karunarathna Divylgane, Visharada Edward Jayakody, Visharada Charitha Priyadarshani, Visharada Deepika Priyadarshani, Visharada Luxmen Wijesekera, Visharada Amitha Wiedsinghe, Wimala Amaradewa, National Hospital Deputy Director General Dr. Anil Jasinghe, Director Dr. Asela Gunawardane and Buddhist Society President Consultant Surgeon Dr. Yasas Abewickrama were in attendance.
