## Title: Two children drown in agri well in Madhu
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, March 17, 2018 - 01:00

Two children drown in agri well in Madhu

Two children had drowned in an agriculture well in the Iragoiruppu Kulam area in Madhu around 6.30 pm on Thursday, the Police Media Spokesman’s office said.
The two children aged 7 and 5 have been identified as Mohommed Sathoor Mohommed Akvari and Mohommed Sathoor Mohommed Samraz, both residents of the Iragoiruppu Kulam area in Madhu.
The bodies of the two deceased children have been placed at the Mannar hospital mortuary pending the postmortem examination.
