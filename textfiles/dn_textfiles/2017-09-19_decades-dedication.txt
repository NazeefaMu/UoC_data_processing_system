## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Tuesday, September 19, 2017 - 01:00

Decades of dedication

Hadasara, a cultural performance to celebrate 45 years of Professor Ariyaratne Kaluarachchi’s artistic career, was staged at Nelum Pokuna Mahinda Rajapaksa Theatre recently. Coinciding with the performance of several short ballets on the Nelum Pokuna stage was the launch of the research study guide compiled by Professor Kaluarahchi.
Guru Deva Vannama, Puppety, Seetalen Piri, Agalum Vannama, Pannikkale, Barisil, Rajesthan, Sakala Bujan, Sinha Puthune, Tieka, Jasa Lenchina and Holi were among the short ballets and vocal enactments performed.
The event was held under the patronage of Social Empowerment, Welfare and Kandyan Heritage Minister S B Dissanayake and Cultural Affairs Minister S B Navinna. The University Grants Commission Vice Chaiman Professor P S M Gunarathna, Professor Sunil Ariyarathne, Nanda Malani, Dr. Rohana Weerasinghe, Dr. Ravindra Randeniya, Jackson Anthoni, Rodni Warnakulasuriya, Cultural Affairs Director Anusha Gokula Fernando, Hector Bethmage and Lalith Sarathchandra composed the distinguished spectacle.
