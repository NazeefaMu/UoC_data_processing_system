## Title: Pawan, Heshan contribute 198 for sixth wicket
## Supertitle: ‘Singer Cup’ U19 Schools Cricket
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, February 24, 2018 - 01:00

‘Singer Cup’ U19 Schools Cricket
Pawan, Heshan contribute 198 for sixth wicket

Pawan Ratnayake and skipper Heshan Hettiarachchi scored unbeaten hundreds and put together an unfinished sixth wicket partnership of 198 for Mahanama on the opening day of their ‘Singer Cup’ Under 19 inter-schools cricket match against St Sylvester’s at Lake View yesterday.
Ratnayake scored an unbeaten 107 and Hettiarachchi 104 not out.
Meanwhile 26 wickets fell on day one in the match between Anuradhapura Central College and S. de S. Jayasinghe Central College Dehiwala at Dehiwala.
In the matches concluded yesterday St. Peter’s College Negombo beat Asoka Vidyalaya Colombo by 2 wickets at Koswatte and Henegama Central College beat Nalanda Boys School Minuwangoda by 6 wickets at Henegama.
