## Title: Hindustani Vocal recital
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Tuesday, June 20, 2017 - 01:00

Hindustani Vocal recital

The Colombo Indian Cultural Centre has organized a Hindustani Vocal performance by Pt. Birendra Nath Mishra from Indiaat 6.00 pm on June 30 at ICC auditorium located at 16/2, Gregory’s Road, Colombo 7.
Dr. Birendra Nath Mishra will be accompanied by his son Abhishek Misra. All are cordially invited.
