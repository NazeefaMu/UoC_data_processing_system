## Title: Trade Ministry to be reformed to ensure effective investment flow
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 24, 2017 - 15:15

Trade Ministry to be reformed to ensure effective investment flow

 
The Cabinet Ministers agreed to reform the Development Strategies and International Trade Ministry to ensure effective investment flow.
Agreement was made to establish a separate Secretariat, to set up ten technical task forces to coordinate among relevant institutions and to instruct Legal Draftsman to make necessary legal reforms.
