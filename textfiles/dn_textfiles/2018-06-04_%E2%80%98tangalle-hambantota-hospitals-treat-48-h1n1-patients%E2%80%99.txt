## Title: ‘Tangalle, Hambantota Hospitals treat 48 H1N1 patients’
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, June 4, 2018 - 01:00

‘Tangalle, Hambantota Hospitals treat 48 H1N1 patients’

Forty eight suspected H1N1 patients are under treatment at the Tangalle and Hambantota Hospitals, sources at the hospitals said.
Of them, 38 are under treatment at the Hambantota General Hospital and of them, 26 are children, Hambantota General Hospital Director Dr. Danushka Perera said.
He said the hospital is fully equipped to treat the patients and two doctors were assigned to treat influenza patients.
