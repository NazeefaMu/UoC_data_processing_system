## Title: PM’s Office clarifies
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, September 29, 2017 - 01:00

PM’s Office clarifies

The Prime Minister’s Office has learnt that some errant persons and institutions collected money from people promising them employments in Japan, through a project initiated by the Prime Minister’s Office.
The Prime Minister’s Office assured that such a project had not been initiated by it and requested the public to inform the nearest Police Station of such errant people or institutions.
