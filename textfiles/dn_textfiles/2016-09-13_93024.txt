## Title: Ramukkana Gamini SC Volleyball champions
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, September 13, 2016 - 01:00

Ramukkana Gamini SC Volleyball champions

Ramukkana Gamini Sports Club Volleyball Team, Bandaragama emerged overall champions in the Kalutara District President’s Golden Trophy Dialog Volleyball Championship – 2016 at Pothupitiya Maha Vidyalaya Ground, Wadduwa.
