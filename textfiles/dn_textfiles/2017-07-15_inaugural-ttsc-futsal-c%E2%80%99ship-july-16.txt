## Title: Inaugural TTSC Futsal C’ship on July 16
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, July 15, 2017 - 01:00

Inaugural TTSC Futsal C’ship on July 16

The latest addition to the Travel Trade Sports Club multi sports events calendar, the Futsal 05-a-side Soccer Championships 2017, sponsored by Viluxur Holidays will be worked off on July 16 at the Futsal World Indoor Courts, T.B. Jayah Mawatha Colombo 10, commencing 09.00 am.
13 teams have attracted the inaugural tournament namely, Amja Travels, Avani Bentota, Cinnamon Grand Colombo (02 teams), Ditethelm Travels, Emirates, Galadari Hotel, Hilton Colombo Residence, Luxe Asia, The Kingsbury, Viluxur Holidays and Walkers Tours (02 teams).
Special awards are on offer for the Most Outstanding Player of the Tournament and the Most Promising Player of the Tournament.
