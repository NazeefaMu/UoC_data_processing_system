## Title: NFF sends letter of withdrawal to UPFA
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 24, 2017 - 09:30

NFF sends letter of withdrawal to UPFA

 
The National Freedom Front today sent a letter to UPFA General Secretary Mahinda Amaraweera to officially withdraw from the UPFA.
NFF Leader Wimal weerawansa said in Parliament this week that his party would function as an independent group in Parliament.
