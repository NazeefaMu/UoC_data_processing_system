## Title: E-Local Government system to increase efficiency
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, January 27, 2018 - 01:00

E-Local Government system to increase efficiency

The e-Local Government system, which will enable the public to make all payments for services to local authorities by means of electronic payments, cards and internet, will be launched on January 29 at 9.30 am by President Maithripala Sirisena with the participation of Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe at the Lotus Hall of the Bandaranaike Memorial Conference Hall (BMICH).
Based on a concept of Local Government and Provincial Councils Minister Faiszer Musthapha, the ministry together with the Digital Infrastructure Ministry, have initiated this programme which will serve to streamline the service delivery of all local government institutions, while ensuring transparency and reducing the opportunities for irregularities and corruption when payments are made for obtaining licences and approvals and other services.
This system will be implemented in all Local Government institutions island wide and as an initial step it will be implemented in 30 institutions on January 29.
Provincial Councils and Local Government Minister Faiszer Musthapha, Telecommunications and Digital Infrastructure Minister Harin Fernando, Secretary to the Provincial Councils and Local Government Ministry Kamal Pathmasiri and Secretary to the Telecommunications and Digital Infrastructure Ministry Wasantha Deshapriya will participate.
