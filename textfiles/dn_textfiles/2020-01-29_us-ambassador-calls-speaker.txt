## Title: US Ambassador calls on Speaker
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, January 30, 2020 - 14:31

US Ambassador calls on Speaker

The U.S. Ambassador Alaina Teplitz paid a courtesy visit on Speaker Karu Jayasuriya today at the Speaker’s official residence.The Ambassador was accompanied by Political Officer Joanna Pritchett to this meeting.
