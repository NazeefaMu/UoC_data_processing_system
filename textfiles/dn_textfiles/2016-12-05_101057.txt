## Title: Pakstani with heroin bound  for Sri Lanka nabbed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, December 5, 2016 - 01:00

Pakstani with heroin bound  for Sri Lanka nabbed

 
Officers of the Anti Narcotics Force (ANF) of Lahore arrested a passenger with three kgs of heroin, sources said yesterday.
They said the suspect was to fly to Colombo.
The heroin was found from his luggage. After registration of a case, the ANF shifted the accused to the headquarters for further action.
