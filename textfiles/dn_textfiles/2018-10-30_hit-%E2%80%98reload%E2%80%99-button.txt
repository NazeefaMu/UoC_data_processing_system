## Title: Hit the  ‘reload’  button
## Supertitle: 
## Section: T&C
## Date: Tuesday, October 30, 2018 - 01:00

Hit the  ‘reload’  button

Susantha Dharmapriya’s cinematic venture ‘Reload’ not only encompasses a timely storyline but it is also engages the latest technology such as graphics and animations. The production which embodies a meaningful theme will provide entertainment as well as leave something for the viewers to think about long before the credits roll on the screen.
Susantha s employed as a sound administrator at the National Film Corporation’s Sarasavi Film Studio. He has been witness to many national and international film productions. His debut film stars Dilhani Ashokamala, Kumara Thimadura, Giriraj Kaushalya, Roshan Pilapitiya, Tharuka Wanniarachchi, Sarath Chandrasiri, Nandana Hettiarachchi and others.
Kamal Dharmapriya Mahinda Uduwana and Dhammika Priyadharshani are the producers. The assistant director is Sampath Ratnayake. Haritha Wasala penned the script. Kapila Madagoda composed the melodies. Mahesh Karunaratne is the cinematographer. Seasoned film editor Elmo Halliday edited the project.
