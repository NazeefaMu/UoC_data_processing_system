## Title: Goat herd set on fire
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 30, 2017 - 01:00

Goat herd set on fire

A herd of goats had been set on fire in Hijra village area in Mullippothana Police division recently.
Five goats died due to getting burnt from the fire that affected the entire herd.
No suspects have been identified as yet and the Mullippothana Police are carrying out investigations on the incident.
