## Title: Blood donation
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 7, 2018 - 01:00

Blood donation

A blood donation campaign will be held at the Embillawatta Junction on the Maharagama-Boralesgamuwa road on December 9 at 9 am to mark the 15th commemoration of Ven Gangodawila Soma Thera with the support from the Blood Bank of the Maharagama Apeksha Hospital.
The Ven Gangodawila Soma Thera Commemoration Committee organises it, Committee Member P. Wijepala Perera said.
