## Title: Attack on policemen: Suspects remanded till August 10
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Thursday, August 3, 2017 - 01:00

Attack on policemen: Suspects remanded till August 10

Two suspects who were connected with the attack on the two Policemen on duty at Kopai in Jaffna, were remanded till August 10 on being produced before Jaffna Magistrate S Sadeeskaran yesterday.
The Magistrate ordered the suspects be produced before an identification parade on this day.
