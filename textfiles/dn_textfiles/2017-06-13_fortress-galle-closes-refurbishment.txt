## Title: Fortress  Galle  closes for  refurbishment
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Tuesday, June 13, 2017 - 01:00

Fortress  Galle  closes for  refurbishment

The Fortress Resort and Spa, Galle, will be closed from July 12 to 25 for refurbishment, as a part of the Company’s continued commitment to enhance its property and service standards.
