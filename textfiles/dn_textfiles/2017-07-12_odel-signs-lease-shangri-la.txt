## Title: Odel signs lease with Shangri-La
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Wednesday, July 12, 2017 - 01:00

Odel signs lease with Shangri-La

Odel has entered into an agreement with Shangri-La Hotels Lanka (Private) Limited yesterday to take on lease for a total area of 52,454 sq ft. in ‘The Mall at One GalleFace’, for a period of five years from the date of commencement, with an option to renew the agreement for another five-year period upon the terms and conditions the parties may then mutually agree.
