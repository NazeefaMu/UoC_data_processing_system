## Title: Girl, two boys nabbed for stealing picture from hotel
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 1, 2017 - 01:00

Girl, two boys nabbed for stealing picture from hotel

A girl and two boys who are alleged to have been involved in stealing a wall picture from a five star hotel in Colombo, was arrested by Kollupitiya Police.
According to Police, the three suspects had stolen the wall picture while having dinner at the hotel.
