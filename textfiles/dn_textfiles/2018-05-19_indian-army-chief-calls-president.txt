## Title: INDIAN ARMY CHIEF CALLS ON PRESIDENT
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, May 19, 2018 - 01:00

INDIAN ARMY CHIEF CALLS ON PRESIDENT

The Chief of Army Staff of the Indian Army General Bipin Rawat, who is on a goodwill visit, called on President Maithripala Sirisena at the President’s official residence in Colombo last morning. Army Commander Lt. General Mahesh Senanayake was also present.
