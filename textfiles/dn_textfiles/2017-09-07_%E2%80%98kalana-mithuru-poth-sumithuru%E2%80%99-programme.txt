## Title: ‘Kalana Mithuru Poth Sumithuru’ programme
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 01:00

‘Kalana Mithuru Poth Sumithuru’ programme

The inaugural ceremony of the 'Kalana Mithuru Poth Sumithuru' programme aimed to promote the reading habit among schoolchildren, was held under the patronage of President Maithripala Sirisena at President's House in Fort, Colombo, yesterday.
Education Minister Akila Viraj Kariyawasam, Education State Minister V. Radhakrishnan, Central Province Chief Minister Sarath Ekanayake, Secretary to the President Austin Fernando and several other chief Ministers and Provincial Education Ministers were also present.
