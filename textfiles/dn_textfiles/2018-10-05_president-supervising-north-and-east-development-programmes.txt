## Title: President supervising the  North and East development programmes
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, October 5, 2018 - 01:00

President supervising the  North and East development programmes

 The Presidential Task Force supervising development programmes in the North and East met for the third time under the patronage of President Maithripala Sirisena at the parliament premises yesterday afternoon. Opposition Leader R.Sampanthan, several ministers, North and Eastern province governors, the Presidents Secretary, government officials, Tri-Forces commanders and the IGP attended.
