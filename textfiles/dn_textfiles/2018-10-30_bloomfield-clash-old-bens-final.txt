## Title: Bloomfield clash with Old Bens in the final
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, October 30, 2018 - 01:00

Bloomfield clash with Old Bens in the final

The finals of the Colombo District Cricket Association Cricket Tournament organized by Sri Lanka Cricket was postponed due to torrential rain and it will be worked off in due course. Bloomfield Cricket Club who were unbeaten throughout the tournament will take on Old Bens Cricket Club in the finals and they will be captained by Kasun Ekanayake.
