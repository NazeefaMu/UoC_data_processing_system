## Title: LTTE diaspora seminar held in British Parliament
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 14, 2018 - 01:00

LTTE diaspora seminar held in British Parliament

The LTTE diaspora has conducted a series of seminars in different places in the United Kingdom including the British Parliament under the theme of “Let’s recognise Eelam Tamils in Sri Lanka”.
It has been reported that LTTE diaspora members representing more than 10 countries have participated in this seminar which was held at room No.10 of the British Parliament.
During the past few days, similar programmes have been conducted in Norway, France, Canada, United States and Australia where more diaspora members are living.
Thousands of people living in other countries have watched these seminars on social media.
The LTTE diaspora has made arrangements to invite the political authorities in those countries to participate in these programmes.
