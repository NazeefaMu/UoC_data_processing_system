## Title: Appointed Chief Prelate of Amarapura Chapter
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 1, 2017 - 01:00

Appointed Chief Prelate of Amarapura Chapter

Ven.Kotugoda Dhammavasa Nayake Thera has been appointed the Chief Prelate of Amarapura Chapter. He is a renowned Buddhist monk who has dedicated his life for the betterment of Amarapura Chapter. Ven.Dr.Pallekande Rathanasara Anunayake Thera has been appointed acting Maha Lekakadikari.
