## Title: President visits Sri Mangalarama Viharaya in Batticaloa
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, March 5, 2018 - 01:04

President visits Sri Mangalarama Viharaya in Batticaloa

President Maithripala Sirisena visited the Sri Mangalarama Viharaya in Batticaloa and called on Vihardhipathi Ven. Ampitiye Sumanaratana Thera to receive his blessings and inquire about his well being on Saturday. Picture by President’s Media Division.
