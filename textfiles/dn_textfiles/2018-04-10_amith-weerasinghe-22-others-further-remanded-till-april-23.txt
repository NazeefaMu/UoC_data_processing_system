## Title: Amith Weerasinghe , 22 others further remanded till April 23
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, April 10, 2018 - 15:17

Amith Weerasinghe , 22 others further remanded till April 23

 
Amith Weerasinghe from the Mahason Balakaya and 22 others who were arrested for being involved at the recent communal conflicts which erupted in Teldeniya were further remanded until April 23.
 
The Teldeniya Magistrate issued the order today.
