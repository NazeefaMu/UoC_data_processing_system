## Title: PEO Legend Rukmani Devi memorial musical concert
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Friday, September 29, 2017 - 01:00

PEO Legend Rukmani Devi memorial musical concert

Rupavahini’s latest music program, a concert which brings veteran lyricists, vocalists and music composers together will begin with the PEO Legend Rukmani Devi memorial musical concert. The program will be telecast on Rupavahini on September 29 at 10.30 pm. Uresha Ravihari, Samitha Mudunkotuwa, Nirosha Virajini, Kushani Sandareka, Nadeeka Guruge, Felix Anton, Lalith Ponnamperuma, Meena Prasadini and Upeksha Nirmani will be featured in the program. The music is by Suresh Maliyadda. The concept of the program is by Nilar N Cassim. Seasoned actor Ravindra Randeniya and A D Ranjith Kumara will present the program. The PEO Legend series is directed by Chinthaka Anuruddha. The PEO Legend series will be telecasting on the last Friday every month.
