## Title: RAVI CALLS ON INDIAN PM
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 7, 2017 - 01:00

RAVI CALLS ON INDIAN PM

Foreign Affairs Minister Ravi Karunanayake who arrived in New Delhi yesterday evening called on Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi at the latter’’s residence. This is Minister Karunanayake’s first foreign visit after being appointed as the Minister of Foreign Affairs Minister.
