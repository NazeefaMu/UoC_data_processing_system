## Title: Seelaratne Senarath marks 40 years
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 1, 2018 - 01:00

Seelaratne Senarath marks 40 years

A book titled ‘Mata Den Hathalihai’ (Now I’m forty), comprising veteran journalist and broadcaster Seelaratna Senarath’s experiences in the media, written by journalist Ashoka Piyaratne, will be launched today at 2 pm at the Western Province Aesthetic Resort, Colombo 7, under the patronage of Uruwarige Vannila Aththo, the leader of the indigenous community.
The book launch marks Senarath’s 40th year in the profession of journalism and the media field this year.
Senarath was the former Director/Editorial of the Associated Newspapers of Ceylon Ltd.
The book launch will be held parallel to the third anniversary of CRI Sri Lanka FM 97.9 channel.
