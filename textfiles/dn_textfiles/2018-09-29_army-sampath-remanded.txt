## Title: Army Sampath remanded
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, September 29, 2018 - 01:00

Army Sampath remanded

Army Sampath arrested with 4.5 g of heroin by Rambukkana Police was remanded until October 4 by Kegalle Additional Magistrate Senaka Illanganthilake. The suspect was arrested when he was at a rented house in Hiriwadunna,
Counsel Themiya Jayasena appeared on behalf of the suspect pleaded the Magistrate his client be given special security in the prison since he is under death threats.The Magistrate ordered the suspect be provided with special security.
