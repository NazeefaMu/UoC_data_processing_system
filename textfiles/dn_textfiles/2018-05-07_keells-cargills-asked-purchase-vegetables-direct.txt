## Title: Keells, Cargills asked to purchase vegetables direct
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, May 7, 2018 - 01:00

Keells, Cargills asked to purchase vegetables direct

The Keells and Cargills Food City have commenced purchasing vegetables direct from farmers.
It is an immediate solution to the serious problem that vegetable farmers in the hill country and other areas like Polonnaruwa, Anuradhapura and Dambulla were facing in recent weeks.
The process commenced with the intervention of Minister Dr. Sarath Amunugama.
A meeting was held recently at the Presidential Secretariat under the chairmanship of Minister Dr. Sarath Amunugama to discuss the problem faced by vegetable farmers. The Secretary to the President, Ministry Secretaries and Government Agents participated at the discussion where the Minister went into this problem in detail and directed the Government Agents to take measures to address the situation.
The Minister contacted the Heads of the Keells and Cargills Food City and requested them to purchase vegetables direct from the farmers.
They have now started purchasing vegetables from the farmers.
The Minister directed the Government Agents and the relevant Ministry Secretaries to be on constant alert regarding farmers’ problems.
