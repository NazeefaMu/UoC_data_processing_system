## Title: RTI seminar at Temple Trees
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, December 9, 2017 - 01:00

RTI seminar at Temple Trees

Finance and Mass Media Minister Mangala Samaraweera, Deputy Minister Lasantha Alagiyawanne and Ministry Secretary Dr. R. H. S. Samaratunga will host a seminar for Information officers from the Western Province on Monday, December 11, from 9 am to 4 pm at Temple Trees.
Close to 3,000 RTI officers have been trained and a commission established to pursue RTI requests. Over 150 junior civil servants have also been trained to respond to RTI requests.
