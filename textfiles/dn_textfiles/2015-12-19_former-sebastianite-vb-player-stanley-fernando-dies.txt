## Title: Former Sebastianite VB player Stanley Fernando dies
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, December 19, 2015 - 01:00

Former Sebastianite VB player Stanley Fernando dies

 
Former Sebastianite Noel Stanley Fernando died after a brief illness at the age of 87 years.
He played for the college Volleyball team and he was a very fine spiker during the late fifties. He joined the Ceylon Government Railway as under guard and retired as a head Guard.
He represented the Railway in volleyball in all divisions for more than one and a half decades and led the teams in time to time.
Also he captained the Moratuwmulla Central Volleyball team which won the championship for several successive years when volleyball was at peak at Moratuwa.
His remains lie at "Senani", Sri Premarathne Mawatha, Moratumulla, Moratuwa and cortege leaves residence at 3 pm on 20th December 2015 for Cremation at general Cemetery Rawatawatte Moratuwa.
