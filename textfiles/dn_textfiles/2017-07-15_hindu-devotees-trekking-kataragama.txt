## Title: Hindu devotees trekking to Kataragama
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, July 15, 2017 - 01:00

Hindu devotees trekking to Kataragama

A large number of Hindu devotees from all over the island are trekking to Kataragama. The Kataragama temple annual festival will begun on July 24.
“Gateway”, the jungle route to Kataragama, will be opened tomorrow for the ‘Pada Yathra’ pilgrims. The road will be closed on August 2.
About 150 pilgrims from Jaffna have reached Akkaraipattu on Saturday. They will trek to reach Kataragama to attend a festival at the Kataragama Murugan Devalaya on July 24.
