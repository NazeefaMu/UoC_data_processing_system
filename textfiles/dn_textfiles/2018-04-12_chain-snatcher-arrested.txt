## Title: Chain snatcher arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 12, 2018 - 01:00

Chain snatcher arrested

Two suspects, who snatched a gold chain worn by a woman who visited the Mahawewa Sathipola on April 4, were caught by a group of people and handed over to Marawila Police.
Following interrogation, police recovered the gold chain worth nearly Rs. 47,000 from their possession.
The suspects were due to be produced before the Marawila Magistrate.
