## Title: LSSP’s 83rd anniversary
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Friday, December 14, 2018 - 01:00

LSSP’s 83rd anniversary

The Lanka Sama Samaja Party (LSSP) will mark its 83rd anniversary on December 23 at 3.00pm, at the Eheliyagoda Cooperative Auditorium. LSSP Secretary-General Prof. Tissa Vitharana will deliver the keynote address.
