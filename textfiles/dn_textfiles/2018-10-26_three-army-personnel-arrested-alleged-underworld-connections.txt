## Title: Three Army personnel arrested for alleged underworld connections
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Friday, October 26, 2018 - 01:00

Three Army personnel arrested for alleged underworld connections

An Army Officer and two soldiers were arrested by a team of Police officers attached to the “ Siyabalaanduwa” Police station yesterday for allegedly supplying ammunition to criminals.
According to the Police Media Spokespersons office, the three Army personnel were arrested following information revealed by some arrested criminals, including a wanted underworld leader.
The Police had arrested several people with live grenades and ammunition in the Siyabalaanduwa area recently.
According to the Police Media Unit, live ammunition was found in a forest area behind the Army officer’s official residence during a search operation. Siyabalaanduwa Police are investigating.
