## Title: Astrologer Vijitha Rohana released on bail
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Wednesday, February 1, 2017 - 11:45

Astrologer Vijitha Rohana released on bail

 
Astrologer Vijitha Rohana Wijemuni has been released on bail a short while ago.
He was released on cash bail of Rs. 10,000, two surety bails of Rs. 1 million.
Wijemuni was arrested by the Crimes Investigation Division (CID) yesterday (31) for attempting to incite disaffection to the President.
The astrologer had refused to remove the video of him forecasting the President’s death stating that none of his forecasts have gone wrong so far.
