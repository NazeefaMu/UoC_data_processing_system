## Title: Udara in Ladies cricket team for Singapore
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, June 4, 2016 - 01:00

Udara in Ladies cricket team for Singapore

Udara Bandara of the Kandy Cricket and Athletic Academy is the first girl to be selected for the Ladies Cricket team of Colombo in their cricket tour of Singapore early this month.
Udara is one of the fastest bowlers in and around Kandy and also a useful right hand batswoman. Colombo Ladies Cricket team is scheduled to play five Twenty20 Matches and 16 year old Udara is the youngest to represent the team.
