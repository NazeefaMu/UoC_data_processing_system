## Title: St. Sylvester’s win at T20 cricket
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, March 28, 2017 - 01:00

St. Sylvester’s win at T20 cricket

St. Sylvester’s College retained the T20 Battle of the Babes trophy beating rivals Vidyarhta College by five wickets in the 7th T20 encounter of the series played at the Asgiriya Stadium yesterday.
Vidyartha batting first were dismissed for 128 in 20 overs and St Sylvester’s knocked the required runs off in 19.1 overs losing half the side.
Vidyartha 128 in 20 overs (Shashika Priyamal 33, Sahan Danwatha 23, Pulina Gunatilake 20, Nimsara Aththaragalla 3 for 17, Thusitha Soysa 2 for 19).
St. Sylvester’s 133-5 in 19.1 overs (Avindu 30, Nimsara Aththaragalla 23, Nipuna Kumarasiri 2 for 32, Kasun Karunathilake 2 for 14).
