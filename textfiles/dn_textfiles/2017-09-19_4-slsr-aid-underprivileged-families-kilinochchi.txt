## Title: 4 SLSR aid underprivileged families in Kilinochchi
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, September 19, 2017 - 01:00

4 SLSR aid underprivileged families in Kilinochchi

Troops of the 4 Sri Lanka Sinha Regiment (SLSR) under the 57 Division of the Security Force Headquarters - Kilinochchi (SFHQ-KLN) gave away dry ration packets to 10 underprivileged families in Murukkandi village in Kilinochchi using their own financial collection.After the troops observed that those families were undergoing hardships for their survival, 4 SLSR troops got together and came forward to organize this distribution of dry rations and essentials.
Each dry ration packet consisted of rice, dhal, sugar, tea, soap, flour, chillies, spices, milk powder packets, etc., each costing Rs. 2,500.
The initiative taken by 4 SLSR Commanding Officer Major Nalin Wickramaarchchi was attended by several Officers and other rankers who contributed to the project.
