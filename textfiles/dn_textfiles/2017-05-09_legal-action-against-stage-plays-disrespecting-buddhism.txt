## Title: Legal action against stage plays disrespecting Buddhism
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 17:30

Legal action against stage plays disrespecting Buddhism

 
Police said today that legal action would be taken against organizers who stage plays defaming Buddhist principles this Vesak.
Accordingly Police advised the public to refrain from staging puppet and other shows defaming or bringing disrespect to the principles of Buddhism.
