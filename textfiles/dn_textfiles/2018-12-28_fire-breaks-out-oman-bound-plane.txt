## Title: Fire breaks out in Oman bound plane
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 28, 2018 - 01:00

Fire breaks out in Oman bound plane

A sudden fire broke out in an Antonov 12 plane bound to Oman that was at the Mattala Air Port according to sources at the Mattala Air Port. There were seven members of the crew including the pilot.
The plane was on its way to Salam Air Port in Oman from Thailand carrying an engine and had landed at the Mattala Air Port.
It was to leave for Oman at 8.30 am.
As soon as the fire broke out, the Fire Brigade of the Air Port was deployed to douse the fire.
The sources at the Mattala Air Port said that the plane would be cleared to fly back after it was found that it was safe to fly.
