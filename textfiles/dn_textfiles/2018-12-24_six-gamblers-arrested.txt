## Title: Six gamblers arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, December 24, 2018 - 01:00

Six gamblers arrested

Six gamblers at a shelter in a paddy field in Lunuwewa were arrested on December 21 midnight by Thambuththegama police.
Several gamblers had fled when police raided the location. Those arrested are residents of Thambuththegama, Rajanganaya and Nochchiyagama.
Investigations are continuing to arrest the rest, police said.
The suspects were to be produced before the Thambuththegama Magistrate’s Court. Crime Investigation Unit OIC of the Thambuththegama police under the Thambuththegama Police OIC N.J. Atukorale led the raid.
