## Title: With love from Pakistan
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 10, 2017 - 01:00

With love from Pakistan

Pakistan on Wednesday sent a relief flight to Colombo containing 25 Metric Ton of rice to help Sri Lanka to mitigate the drought. Pakistan envoy in Colombo Maj. Gen. (R) Syed Shakeel Hussain handed over the relief consignment to government officials at the Bandaranaike International Airport. Picture by Samantha Weerasiri.
