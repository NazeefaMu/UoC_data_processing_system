## Title: Ten passengers injured in bus collision
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 24, 2017 - 18:30

Ten passengers injured in bus collision

 
Ten passengers were injured when two buses collide at Millabedda junction on the Passara  road today.
The injured were admitted to Hopton rural hospital for treatments.
Lunugala police is investigating.
