## Title: St. Joseph’s Wattala win on 1st innings
## Supertitle: Singer U-19 Inter Schools Cricket:
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, December 7, 2018 - 01:00

Singer U-19 Inter Schools Cricket:
St. Joseph’s Wattala win on 1st innings

St. Joseph’s College Wattala won first innings honours against Lyceum International School Panadura in their ‘Singer Trophy’ Under 19 inter Schools cricket tournament match played at Prision ground, Dalupotha yesterday.
l At Dalupotha.
St. Joseph’s Wattala: 117 in 35.3 overs (Malinga Silva 47, Aloka Madsushan 25, Chamod Dayan 22, Isuru Perera 5/37, Yenuka Rajapaksha 3/22) and 9/0 in 11 overs
Lyceum International School Panadura: 89 in 36 overs (Isuru Perera 53, Chamod Dayan 5/47, Malinga Silva 2/7)
l At Railway ground, Dematagoda.
Galahitiyawa Central: 238 in 63.5 overs (Manuditha Ravishan 93, Senesh Nadeeshan 74, Janitha lakshitha 4/75, Dinura Wanigasekara 3/04, Denham Miller 3/46)
Carey: 67/2 in 21.5 overs (Mohommed Mustaf 25, Abdul Rahman 23 n.o.). YK
