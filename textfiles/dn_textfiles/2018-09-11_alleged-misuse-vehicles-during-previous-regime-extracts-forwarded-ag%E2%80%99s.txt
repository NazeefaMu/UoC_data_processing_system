## Title: Alleged misuse of vehicles during previous regime: Extracts forwarded to AG’s Dept.
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Tuesday, September 11, 2018 - 01:00

Alleged misuse of vehicles during previous regime: Extracts forwarded to AG’s Dept.

The FCID yesterday informed Colombo Fort Magistrate Lanka Jayaratne that the extracts with regard to the case filed against five persons including NFF Media Spokesman Muhammad Muzammil in connection with the alleged misuse of vehicles provided by the Presidential Secretariat during the former regime have been forwarded to the Attorney General’s Department. This was intimated to Court by FCID officials when the case was taken up before the Colombo Fort Magistrate’s Court yesterday.
