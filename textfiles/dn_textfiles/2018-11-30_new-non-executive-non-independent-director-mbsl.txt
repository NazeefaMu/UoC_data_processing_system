## Title: New Non-Executive, Non-Independent Director for MBSL
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Friday, November 30, 2018 - 01:00

New Non-Executive, Non-Independent Director for MBSL

D.N.L. Fernando has been appointed as Non-Executive Non-Independent Director to the Board of MBSL with effect from November 29, 2018.
The appointment has been duly approved by the Central Bank of Sri Lanka. Fernando does not hold any shares in MBSL.
