## Title: Institute of Hospitality hosts  Chairman’s black-tie dinner
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Friday, December 22, 2017 - 01:00

Institute of Hospitality hosts  Chairman’s black-tie dinner

The Institute of Hospitality Sri Lanka held the annual Chairman’s black-tie dinner for the 19th consecutive year, recently.
The Institute of Hospitality is a professional body for managers working and studying in the hospitality, leisure and tourism industries, which has been playing a major role in tourism in Sri Lanka since ‘90s. Today the Institute has grown into a global authority in the hospitality business and continues to lead the role in the evolving tourism industry. The dinner was graced by Minister of Tourism Development John Amaratunga, British High Commissioner James Dauris and other distinguished guests.
