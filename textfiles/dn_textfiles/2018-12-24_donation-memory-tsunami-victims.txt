## Title: Donation in memory of tsunami victims
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, December 24, 2018 - 01:00

Donation in memory of tsunami victims

Pregnant women will receive goods at the Seenigama Devale on December 26 in memory of the tsunami victims. In charge of the devalaya Sarath Disevvahandi and his staff organized the event.
Several novice bhikkus and 50 children from the low-income families will receive school stationery and equipment. Fifty low-income families will be provided with food items, organisers said.
