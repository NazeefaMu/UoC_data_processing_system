## Title: Fine Century by Gangul Ravina
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, February 26, 2016 - 01:00

Fine Century by Gangul Ravina

 
Gangul Ravina of Kalutara Tissa Central College scored 120 not out against the Sri Jayawardhanapura Maha Vidyalaya the under 19 Singer Trophy School Cricket Tournament 2015/2016 conducted by Sri Lanka School Cricket Association held recently at Kotte Sri Jayawardenaprua Maha Vidyala ground.
The match was won by Tissa Central College by an innings and 140 runs.
Tissa Central College in title first innings 344 Gangual Ravina 120, Sahan Yasindu 87, Nipul Dharashana 55, and Dilan 38.
