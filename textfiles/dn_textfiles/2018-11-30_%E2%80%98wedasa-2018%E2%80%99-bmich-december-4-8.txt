## Title: ‘Wedasa - 2018’ at BMICH from December 4 to 8
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, November 30, 2018 - 01:00

‘Wedasa - 2018’ at BMICH from December 4 to 8

The ‘Wedasa - 2018’ medical science exhibition organized to mark the 25th anniversary of the Sri Jayawardhanapura University's Medical Faculty will be held from December 4 to 8 at the BMICH.
This exhibition will be held under three main divisions and will comprise 27 stalls to educate visitors. These stalls will be created under the themes a secure environment, comfortable life, mentality of youth, garbage recycling, eco-friendly surrounding, heart diseases, healthy foods, and accidents and first aid.
The exhibition will be open from 9 a.m. to 10 p.m. each day.
