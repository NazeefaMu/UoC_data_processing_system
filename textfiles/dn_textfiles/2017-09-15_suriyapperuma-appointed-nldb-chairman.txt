## Title: Suriyapperuma appointed NLDB Chairman
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, September 15, 2017 - 01:00

Suriyapperuma appointed NLDB Chairman

On the recommendations of President Maithripala Sirisena, Finance and Media Minister Mangala Samaraweera has appointed S. A. P. Suriyapperuma as the Chairman of the National Lotteries Development Board (NLDB).
