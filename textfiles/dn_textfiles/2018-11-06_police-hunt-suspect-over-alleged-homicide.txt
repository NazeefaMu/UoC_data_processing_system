## Title: Police hunt suspect over alleged homicide
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, November 6, 2018 - 01:00

Police hunt suspect over alleged homicide

Police have launched a manhunt for a suspect who had allegedly killed a clerk of the Nikaweratiya Magistrate’s Court in Kurunegala.
The victim was identified as 52-year-old D.M. Karunathilake, a resident of Heelogama, Rasnayakapura. The suspect is a 30-year-old youth of the same area.
Police believe that the death had occurred following an argument between the two.
A postmortem was to be held. Investigations are continuing.
