## Title: Removal of walking track: Dullas calls for action against Minister Amaratunga
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Wednesday, March 30, 2016 - 01:00

Removal of walking track: Dullas calls for action against Minister Amaratunga

Parliamentarian Dullas Alahpperuma yesterday said the country is waiting to see what sort of action would be taken against Minister John Amaratunga who has accepted the responsibility for removing a walking track at Wattala.
Speaking at a press conference at the Dr.N.M. Perera Centre in Colombo 8, Alahapperuma said Minister Amaratunga could have been arrested under the Public Property Act.
He said Minister Amaratunga admitted the damaged caused to the walking track which was constructed at a cost of Rs. 80 million.
"This walking track was destroyed using backhoes at a time when the Wattala Pradehsiya Sabha had been dissolved and the governemnt should take action against those who were responsible in destroying the walking track," he said.
