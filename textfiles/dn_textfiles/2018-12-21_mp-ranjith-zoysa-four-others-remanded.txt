## Title: MP Ranjith Zoysa, four others remanded
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Friday, December 21, 2018 - 01:06

MP Ranjith Zoysa, four others remanded

United People’s Freedom Alliance (UPFA) MP Ranjith de Zoysa and four others were arrested by the Godakawela Police on charges of assaulting a person in the Godakawela town on December 16. They were remanded by the Pelmadulla Magistrate till January 1.
Police Media Spokesperson SP Ruwan Gunasekara said the suspects had surrendered to the Godakawela Police last morning (20). They were subsequently produced before the Pelmadulla Magistrate’s Court and remanded.
