## Title: No fishery harbour will be leased  or sold: Amaraweera
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, September 15, 2017 - 01:00

No fishery harbour will be leased  or sold: Amaraweera

No fishery harbour will be leased or sold out under this government, Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Development Minister Mahinda Amaraweera assured.
He was addressing the media at the Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Development Ministry.
Refuting a statement made by JVP Parliamentarian Vijitha Herath who claimed that the fisheries harbours are to be leased out to foreign companies.
The Minister further said that somebody has misguided him by giving false information, adding that a cabinet paper was submitted seeking cabinet approval to allow local or foreign investors to start fisheries sector related industries within these premises.
