## Supertitle:

## Date: Thursday, October 20, 2016 - 01:00

Singer Cup U19 Schools Cricket  : Aloysian Ashen Bandara slams double century

 
St. Aloysius College Galle right-hander Ashen Bandara slammed an unbeaten double century against Anuradhapura Central College in their Singer Trophy Under 19 inter- school cricket tournament match at the Galle International Stadium yesterday.
Bandara scored an unbeaten 200 off 197 balls with 26 fours and shared a third wicket stand of 276 with Navindu Nirmal who made 105 off 179 balls with 8 fours. The match however ended in a draw.
