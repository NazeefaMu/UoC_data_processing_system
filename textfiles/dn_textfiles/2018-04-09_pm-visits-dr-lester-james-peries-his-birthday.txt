## Title: PM visits Dr. Lester James Peries for his birthday
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, April 9, 2018 - 01:00

PM visits Dr. Lester James Peries for his birthday

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe visited the Maestro of the Sinhala film industry Dr. Lester James Peries, who turned 99 at his Thimbirigasyaya residence, to offer his greetings yesterday morning. The Prime Minister who was accompanied by Prof. (Mrs) Maithree Wickramasinghe in conversation with Dr. Peries and Mrs. Sumithra Peries. (Picture courtesy Prime Minister’s Media Division)
