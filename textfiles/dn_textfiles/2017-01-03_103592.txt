## Title: President’s vision for  Sustainable Era initiative  for Sri Lanka 2030 launched
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, January 3, 2017 - 01:00

President’s vision for  Sustainable Era initiative  for Sri Lanka 2030 launched

The President’s vision for the Sustainable Era initiative for Sri Lanka 2030 was launched at the Presidential Secretariat yesterday. Prof. Mohan Munasinghe, founding chairman of the Munasinghe Institute for Development and senior advisor to the government appointed the first member of the expert committee receiving the appointment letter from President Maithripala Sirisena while Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe looks on.
