## Title: New Payagala ASP
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, August 27, 2018 - 01:00

New Payagala ASP

On the recommendation of the Police Commission, Police Inspector (IP) W.D.N.G. Welgama had been appointed Assistant Superintendent of Police (ASP) for the Payagala, Dodangoda and Agalawatte police divisions in Kalutara, by Inspector General of Police (IGP) Pujith Jayasundara, recently.
Welgama became a sub-inspector in 1986 and worked in the same post in Negombo, Colombo and Wellampitiya for 10 years. He was later promoted to IP, a post he held for 13 years.
