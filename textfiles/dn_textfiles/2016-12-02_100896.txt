## Title: Malaysian gets jail in fake travellers cheques case
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 2, 2016 - 01:00

Malaysian gets jail in fake travellers cheques case

A Malaysian who pleaded guilty to defrauding Rs. 10 million from a Casino in Colombo 10 was sentenced to one year imprisonment by Colombo Fort Magistrate Lanka Jayarathne.
The man had used fake travellers cheques to commit the offence. The magistrate also ordered him to pay a Rs. 3,000 fine and if he failed to do, will have to serve another four-month-imprisonment. He was arrested and produced in court by Criminal Investigation Department officials.
