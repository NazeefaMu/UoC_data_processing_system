## Title: STC grab honours in drawn match
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, January 11, 2017 - 01:00

STC grab honours in drawn match

A fine all round performance of 10 for 89 (6/76 and 4/13) and 84 runs by Lakshitha Rasanjana could not prevent St. Thomas College, Mt. Lavinia grabbing major honours in their inter school first eleven cricket match against Nalanda Vidyalaya, Colombo which was played at Colombo. Thomians taking the strike first scored 263 with Romesh Nallaperuma and Dinura Gunawardene scoring 73 and 63 runs respectively. In reply Nalandians made 166 with Pavith Rathnayake claiming 5 for 56.
In their second essay Thomians declared at 135 for 5 with Ravindu Kodithuwakku scoring 55 runs and set to make 229 runs for an outright victory at stumps Nalandians were 91 for 4.
St. Thomas – 263 – Romesh Nallaperuma 73, Dinura Gunawardene 63,Manthila Wijerathne 31, Sithara Hapuhinne 29, Kalana Perera 27, Lakshitha Rasanjana 6/76, K, Perera 3/50 and 131/5 dec - Ravindu Kodithuwakku 55, Lakshitha Rasanjana 4/13
Nalanda – 166- Lakshitha Rasanjana 84, K. Perera 29, Pavith Ratnayake 5/56, and 91/ 4 – Avishka Perera 33 n/o, Sachintha Subasinghe 22 n/o, Kasun Sandaruwan 21.
