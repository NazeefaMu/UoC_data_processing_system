## Title: Drug dealer possessing heroin arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 30, 2018 - 01:00

Drug dealer possessing heroin arrested

Marawila Police arrested a 25-year-old youth possessing 5 grams of heroin worth Rs.150,000, last Sunday.
The suspect, a resident of Marawila, had been dealing drugs to other youths in the locality in the guise of a vegetable seller.
Investigations are continuing. Police had arrested 4,268 drug dealers and drug addicts in Kurunegala and Puttalam during the past nine months, a senior police chief in Kurunegala told the Daily News yesterday.
