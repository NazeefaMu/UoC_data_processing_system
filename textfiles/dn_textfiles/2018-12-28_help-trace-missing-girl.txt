## Title: Help trace missing girl
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 28, 2018 - 01:00

Help trace missing girl

Police seek public help to trace girl gone missing since October 27, 2018.She is identified as Halendeniya Devage Ayesha Kalhaari (18) the wife of Krishan Madushanka Fernando residing in Prosperous Gardens, Tharuna Seva Mawatha, Mahalanda, Moronthuduwa. Police request the public to inform any information of this girl to the Officer in Charge of the Moronthuduwa Police Station on no 038-2295599 or the Police Public Relations Division on no 0112-2436161.
