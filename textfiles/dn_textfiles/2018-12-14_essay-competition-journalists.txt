## Title: Essay competition for journalists
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 14, 2018 - 01:00

Essay competition for journalists

The Turkish Embassy in Colombo, with the cooperation of the Sri Lanka Muslim Media Forum, (SLMMF), would organise an essay competition on International Relations for professional and prospective journalists.
The topic of the essay is ‘Turkey and Her Role in the Region and the World as a Regional Power’. The essays should be written only in English, and the word count should be between 750 and 1,000 words.
Please send your essays on or before December 31, along with your contact details and documents to prove your studentship or employment at a media institution, to the Sri Lanka Muslim Media Forum either by post to the ‘Sri Lanka Muslim Media Forum, 41/2, Vijitha Road, Nedimala, Dehiwala’, or by email to [email protected].
If you are sending your essays via email, please type ‘Essay Competition’ in the subject line, followed by your name.
