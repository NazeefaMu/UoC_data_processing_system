## Title: Pothupitiya MV - Elle champions
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, February 19, 2016 - 01:00

Pothupitiya MV - Elle champions

 
Pothupitiya Maha Vidyalaya emerged champions of the Kalutara Circuit Inter-School Girls Elle Tournament 2016 conducted by Kalutara Circuit Sports Unit of Kalutara Education Office.
Twelve girls Elle Teams participated in the tournament which was held recently at Morontuduwa Navodya Vidyalaya ground.
The runners up was the girls elle team of Morontuduwa Dhammananda Navodya Vidyalaya.
