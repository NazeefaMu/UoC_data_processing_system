## Title: Thikshila and bowlers steer Chilaw to an easy win
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, August 8, 2016 - 01:00

Thikshila and bowlers steer Chilaw to an easy win

As in group C, the two teams next to the leader SSC in Group D, Chilaw and Kalutara with same number of wins met each other at FTZ, Katunayaka in an extremely decisive tussle today before Chilaw won the battle by 62 runs to gain the ascendancy. When Chilaw took the first lease, Thikshila de Silva (62) and Raveen Yasas (32) helped them reach a competitive 160 before Chilaw bowlers led by Charith Rajapaksha bowled splendidly to restrict Kalutara reply to a total fewer than hundred to win the match by a wide margin.
Chilaw MCC U23: 160/8 in 20 overs (Thikshila de Silva 62, Raveen Yasas 32*, Gayan Chamara 2/32, Geeth Perera 2/18, Rusiru Ravishanka 2/28)
Kalutara TC U23: 98/7 in 20 overs (Geeth Perera 36, Charith Rajapaksha 3/23, Chalana de Silva 2/15, Raveen 2/13)
