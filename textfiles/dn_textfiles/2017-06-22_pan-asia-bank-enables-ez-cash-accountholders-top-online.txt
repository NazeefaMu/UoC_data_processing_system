## Title: Pan Asia Bank enables eZ Cash accountholders to top up online
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, June 22, 2017 - 01:00

Pan Asia Bank enables eZ Cash accountholders to top up online

Sri Lanka's pioneering and largest mobile money network, eZ Cash, is partnering with Sri Lanka's fastest growing commercial bank, Pan Asia Bank to top-up eZ Cash accounts via Pan Asia Bank internet banking anytime, anywhere from a smartphone, laptop or tab.
