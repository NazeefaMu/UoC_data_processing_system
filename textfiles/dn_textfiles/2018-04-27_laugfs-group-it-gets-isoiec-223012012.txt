## Title: LAUGFS Group IT gets ISO/IEC 22301:2012
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Friday, April 27, 2018 - 01:00

LAUGFS Group IT gets ISO/IEC 22301:2012

LAUGFS Group IT marks yet another landmark achievement by receiving the ISO/IEC 22301:2012 certification from Bureau Veritas for Business Continuity Management System (BCMS). Implemented with the expert assistance of PricewaterhouseCoopers (PwC), the management system is designed to protect, reduce and recover from internal and external threats encountered by organisations.
“This is a significant achievement for LAUGFS and a true testament of our continuous commitment towards ensuring a robust, secure and uninterrupted service to our customers,” commented LAUGFS Holdings Chief Information Officer, Indika Gunawardena.
“Being awarded the internationally recognized standard for business continuity further proves our strength and capability to withstand disruptive eventualities. Our ISO 22301 certification is applicable across our protected and disaster recovery sites. In our journey of process standardization, we were also certified with the ISO/IEC 27001:2013 last year for our Information Security Management System. This year, we will further strengthen our IT services management capabilities by pursuing ISO 20,000 for IT services Management.”
ISO 22301 is an international framework and benchmark that has been developed to guide businesses in identifying potential threats to a company’s critical business functions, and to build effective backup systems and processes to safeguard the interests of stakeholders to ensure continuity of critical business functions in the event of disruptions. As a leading conglomerate in Sri Lanka, LAUGFS’s ICT strategy provides continuous and timely technological solutions to create greater value in all areas of business. LAUGFS Group IT is a dedicated team of professionals constantly striving to infuse creativity and efficiency in ICT products, by offering world class technology solutions.
Reaffirming LAUGFS Group IT’s continuous drive for service enhancement and meeting high standards, the group has been bestowed with several awards including National Best Quality ICT Awards, Asia Pacific ICT Alliance Awards, Bestweb.lk and e-Swabhimani.
LAUGFS is one of the largest diversified business conglomerates and a trusted name in Sri Lanka. Founded in 1995, LAUGFS today has expanded across 20 industries in Sri Lanka and overseas.
