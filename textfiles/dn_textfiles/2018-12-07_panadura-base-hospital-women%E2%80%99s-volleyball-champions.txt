## Title: Panadura Base Hospital women’s volleyball champions
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, December 7, 2018 - 01:00

Panadura Base Hospital women’s volleyball champions

Panadura Base Hospital women’s and Palinda Nuwara MoH office men’s volleyball teams emerged champions of the Kalutara District Health Departmental Volleyball Tournament 2018 conducted by Kalutara District Health Superintendent office Sports unit.
The tournament was held recently at Pothupitiya Maha Vidyalaya ground under the guidance of Pothupitiya PHI Gamini Munasinghe.
Ten men’s teams and eight women’s teams participated in tournament. Ittapana Rural Hospital women’s team and Panadura Base Hospital men’s team were the runners-ups of the tournament.
