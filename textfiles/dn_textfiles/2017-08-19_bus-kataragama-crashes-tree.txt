## Title: Bus to Kataragama crashes into tree
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, August 19, 2017 - 01:00

Bus to Kataragama crashes into tree

A bus carrying pilgrims to Kataragama veered off the road and collided with a tree in the Hungamathe area when its driver attempted to avoid a motorcycle which came from the wrong side.
Hungama Police said the motorcyclist, who was in a critical condition, and a few bus passengers were admitted to hospital.
