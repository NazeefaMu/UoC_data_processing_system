## Title: Open warrant on ex-Navy officer
## Supertitle: Abduction of 11 youth in 2008:
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Saturday, May 19, 2018 - 01:00

Abduction of 11 youth in 2008:
Open warrant on ex-Navy officer

Colombo Fort Magistrate Lanka Jayaratne yesterday issued an open warrant on former Navy officer, Commander Chandana Prasad Hettiarachchi while re-remanding Kasthurige Gamini and Aruna Thushara Mendis until May 31.
This is in connection with the abduction and disappearance of 11 youth in 2008.
Former Navy Commodore D.K.P. Dassanayake has been accused of aiding and abetting in the crime.
Commodore Dassanayake was released on bail earlier and two suspects Kasthurige Gamini and Aruna Thushara Mendis were ordered to be remanded.They had been allegedly involved in abducting 11 youth, detained them illegally and caused them to disappear in 2008.
The Magistrate's Court earlier granted bail to the accused Commander Chandana Prasad Hettiarachchi, Commodore Sumith Ranasinghe of the Naval Intelligence Unit, Lakshman Udaya Kumara, Nalin Prasanna Wickremasuriya, Thammita Ihalagedara Dharmadasa and Rajapaksa Pathiranalage Kithsiri Sampath Munasinghe.
The Magistrate ordered the CID to conduct further investigations on the matter and report to court.
