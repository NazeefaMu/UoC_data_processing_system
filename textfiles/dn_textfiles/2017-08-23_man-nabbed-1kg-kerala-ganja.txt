## Title: Man nabbed with 1kg of Kerala Ganja
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 23, 2017 - 12:30

Man nabbed with 1kg of Kerala Ganja

 
A 21-year-old suspect with over 1kg of Kerala Ganja was taken into custody by Maligawatte Police last night. 
The suspect was found to be a resident of Maligawatte.
He was arrested during a raid carried out by the police based on a tip off.
The suspect will be produced before Maligakanda Magistrate Court today.
Further investigations are underway.
