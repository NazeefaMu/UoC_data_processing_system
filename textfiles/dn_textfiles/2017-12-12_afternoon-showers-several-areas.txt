## Title: Afternoon showers in several areas
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, December 12, 2017 - 11:31

Afternoon showers in several areas

 
Showers or thundershowers may occur in the Central, Sabaragamuwa, Uva, Eastern, Western and North-Western provinces and in the Galle and Matara districts after 2.00 p.m. the Meteorology Department said.
There may be temporary localized strong winds during thundershowers.
Misty conditions are expected at some places in the Western, North-Western, Sabaragamuwa and Central provinces in the morning.
The Met. Department urged the public to take adequate precautions to minimize damages caused by lightning activity.
