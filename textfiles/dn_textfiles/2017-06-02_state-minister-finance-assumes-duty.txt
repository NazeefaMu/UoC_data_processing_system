## Title: State Minister of Finance assumes duty
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Friday, June 2, 2017 - 01:00

State Minister of Finance assumes duty

The newly appointed State Minister of Finance Eran Wickramaratne assumed duties at the Ministry of Finance yesterday. Secretary to the Ministry of Finance Dr. R.H.S. Samarathunga, Deputy Secretary to Treasury, G.D.C. Ekanayaka and the Additional Secretary to the Ministry R. Semasinghe were also present.
