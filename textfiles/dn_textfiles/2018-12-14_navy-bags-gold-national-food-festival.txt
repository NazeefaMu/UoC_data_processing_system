## Title: Navy bags gold at National Food Festival
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 14, 2018 - 01:00

Navy bags gold at National Food Festival

The ‘National Food Festival - 2018’ organised by the Agriculture Ministry, in collaboration with National Food Promotion Board, was held at the Green Path off Viharamadevi Park between December 7 and 9.
Over 100 restaurants, including the Light House Galley of the Navy, took part in the festival conducted in view of promoting food preservation as well as local food varieties. The Light House Galley won the gold award for the ‘Best Restaurant’ at the event.
The stall of the Light House Galley was presented under the supervision of Commander Nuwan Arandara.
