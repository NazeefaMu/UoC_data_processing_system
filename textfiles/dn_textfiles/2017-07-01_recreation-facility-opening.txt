## Title: Recreation facility opening
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, July 1, 2017 - 01:00

Recreation facility opening

The Kumbichchankulama recreation facility in the Anuradhapura city will be inaugurated by Megapolis and Western Development Minister Patali Champika Ranawaka today.
The urban development authority has spent Rs. 48 million for the facility consisting of a gymnasium and information technology cyber cafe restaurant. The complex is located on the bank of the Kumbichchankulma tank.
