## Title: Keith Noyahr abduction: Suspects further remanded
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Friday, February 24, 2017 - 01:00

Keith Noyahr abduction: Suspects further remanded

Mount Lavinia Magistrate yesterday further remanded the suspects of the Keith Noyahr abduction and assault case until March 3.
The Criminal Investigation Department yesterday, filing another report before the Magistrate, described how the arrest was made and provided details of the investigations carried out so far.
The Magistrate after taking into consideration the submission made by the CID remanded the suspects until March 3. The Magistrate has ordered the CID not to release any pictures to the social media.
