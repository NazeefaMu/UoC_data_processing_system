## Title: Ferry service from Kankesanthurai to Rameshwaram
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, November 30, 2018 - 01:00

Ferry service from Kankesanthurai to Rameshwaram

A ferry service would be commenced from Kankesanthurai to Rameshwaram, India, for devotees visiting Bodh Gaya and other religious places in India.
Rehabilitation and Resettlement Minister Douglas Devananda said that he had discussed the matter with the Indian Government, to reduce Visa fees and sea fares.
