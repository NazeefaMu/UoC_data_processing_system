## Title: India Education Fair Colombo at the BMICH
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, February 15, 2018 - 01:00

India Education Fair Colombo at the BMICH

The two day India Education Fair Colombo began yesterday at the BMICH. This event showcases the scope of study in Indian universities. Leading universities from all over India is participating in the event. Here Minister of Vocational Training, Education State Minister V. Radhakrishnan at the fair.
