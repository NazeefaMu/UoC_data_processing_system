## Title: New headquarters for ACUSL
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, September 19, 2017 - 01:00

New headquarters for ACUSL

The Association of Cricket Umpires Sri Lanka will be celebrating their 74th anniversary this year with a series of ceremonies. They will be moving to a New Headquarters at P. Sarawanamuttu Cricket Stadium on September 22 at 8.30 am with the support of Sri Lanka Cricket and Ministry of Sports.
The Chief Guest on this memorable occasion will be the Deputy Speaker of Parliament and President of Sri Lanka Cricket Thilanga Sumathipala.
The Association is headed by Member of Parliament for Matara District Buddhika Pathirana.
All former umpires are invited to grace this occasion.
