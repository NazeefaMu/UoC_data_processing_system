## Title: NEW ARMY COMMANDER CALLS ON PRESIDENT
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, July 12, 2017 - 01:00

NEW ARMY COMMANDER CALLS ON PRESIDENT

Lieutenant General Mahesh Senanayake who assumed duties as the 22nd Commander of the Sri Lanka Army called on President Maithripala Sirisena at the Presidential Secretariat yesterday. Lieutenant General Senanayake met the President to formally inform the President of his assumption of duties as the Commander of the Sri Lanka Army.
The Commander is seen presenting a memento to President Maithripala Sirisena on this occasion. Picture by Sudath Silva
