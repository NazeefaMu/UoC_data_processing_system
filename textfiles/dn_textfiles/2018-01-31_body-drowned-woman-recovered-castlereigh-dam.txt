## Title: Body of drowned woman recovered in Castlereigh dam
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 31, 2018 - 16:34

Body of drowned woman recovered in Castlereigh dam

 
A body of a woman drowned in the Castlereigh dam was recovered by Norwood Police earlier today.
Search missions were carried out after receiving a tip off from residents near Castlereigh dam.
The Police said that the recovered body may be of a 28-year-old woman reported missing a week ago.
Norwood Police is conducting further investigations.
