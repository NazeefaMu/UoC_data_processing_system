## Title: Gold smugglers nabbed at BIA
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, January 23, 2020 - 15:09

Gold smugglers nabbed at BIA

Officers attached to Police Narcotics Division at the Bandaranaike International Airport have apprehended three suspects with gold weighing 5kg 657g valued nearly Rs. 50 million.The suspects were aged 15, 26 and 33 residents of Dematagoda and Grandpass areas while the minor is said to be from Kegalle.The suspects were handed over to Customs officers for further investigations by police narcotics officers
