## Title: Transformer explodes
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, June 4, 2018 - 01:00

Transformer explodes

A transformer exploded at Eruthayapuram, Batticaloa, injuring two, on Saturday.
The injured have been hospitalized.
The fluctuation in electricity has resulted in damage to several electric and electronic gadgets. CEB Engineer S. Ajithkumar and Electrical Superintendent R.Prasanthan are doing the needful to bring the situation to normalcy.
