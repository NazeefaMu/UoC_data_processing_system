## Title: Lake House Journalist’s book launch
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 8, 2018 - 01:00

Lake House Journalist’s book launch

The book Hiroshimawe Kandulu (Tears of Hiroshima) authored by Lake House Journalist Chandana Wijekoon, will be launched on October 11 at 5.30 pm, at the Colombo City Hotel’s Monara Banquet Hall.
Teshihiro Kitamura, Charge de Affairs of the Japanese Embassy in Sri Lanka will be the chief guest and Presidential Secretariat Cultural Affairs Director-General Kalakeerthi Dharmasiri Bandaranayake will deliver a lecture titled ‘Reconciliation lesson taught by Hiroshima’.
Hiroshimawe Kandulu is a Dayawansa Jayakody and Company publication.
