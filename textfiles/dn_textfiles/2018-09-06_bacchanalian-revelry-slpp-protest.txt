Bacchanalian revelry at SLPP protest

The Bacchanalian revelry which marked the road circus that was the Joint Opposition’s much heralded Janabalaya Colombata replete with all the yuppie thrills, inebriated scenes with papare bands resembling a big match atmosphere at the Lake House roundabout where they finally gathered last evening.
