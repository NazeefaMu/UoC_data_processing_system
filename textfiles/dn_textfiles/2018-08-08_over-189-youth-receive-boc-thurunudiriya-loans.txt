## Title: Over 189 youth receive BOC Thurunudiriya loans
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 8, 2018 - 01:00

Over 189 youth receive BOC Thurunudiriya loans

The Bank of Ceylon launched Thurunudiriya loan scheme assisted by the Policy Development Office of the Prime Minister’s Office was launched to empower the young generation.
One eighty nine young entrepreneurs were identified for the loans at a progress review meeting held on August 3 at the Kandy District Secretariat. Kandy District Secretary H.M.P. Hitisekara presided over the meeting.
Three youth from the Central Province received the loans and the Small Enterprises Development Division prepared plans for another 51 youth and submitted them to the Bank of Ceylon for loans.
Officials from the Kandy District Secretariat, Central Programme Management Unit of National Policies and Economic Affairs Ministry, Policy Development Office of the Prime Minister’s Office, Bank of Ceylon, Small Enterprises Development Divisin and the Divsional Secretariats of the Central Province participated.
The Thurunudiriya loan scheme grants loans without a guarantor and collateral or with lenient guarantor conditions for small-scale young entrepreneurs who hold a degree or a recognised vocational certificate. The applicants for the loan must have 3-year business experience.
