## Title: St Mary’s Dehiwela cricket tournament
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, April 27, 2018 - 01:00

St Mary’s Dehiwela cricket tournament

The Old Boys Union of St Mary’s College Dehiwela has organized a softball cricket tournament to raise funds for the college basketball court project.
The tournament will be held on April 28 (Saturday) at S de S Jayasinghe grounds, Dehiwela from 8 am onwards.
