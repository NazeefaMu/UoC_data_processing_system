## Title: People’s Movement meeting at SLFI today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, November 28, 2018 - 01:00

People’s Movement meeting at SLFI today

The executive, the legislature and the judiciary are the people's sovereignty and an attempt is going on to suppress people’s franchise via institutions set up as representative institutions of people’s sovereignty. Therefore, the People's Movement for a General Election was formed with the participation of wide range of stakeholders of society including Buddhist monks, Intellectuals, artistes, professionals and entrepreneurs. The first national conference of the People's Movement for a General Election will be held today at 3.30 pm at Sri Lanka Foundation Institution.
Anu Nayaka of the Malwatta Chapter Most Ven. Niyangoda Vijithasiri Thera, Secretary General of Asgiriya Chapter Ven. Dr. Medagama Dhammananda Thera, Anu Nayaka of the Rohana Chapter Ven. Omare Kassapa Thera, Ven.e Elle Gunawansa Thera, President's Counsel Manohara De Silva, Former Vice Chancellor of Uva Wellassa University Prof.. Premalal De Silva, Former President of the Institute of Engineers, Sri Lanka Dr. Ananda Ranasinghe, vfilmmaker Sumitra Peries, Musician Jagath Wickramasinghe, Information Technology Professional Lasantha Wickremesinghe, Consultant Pediatrician Prof.. Wasantha Devasiri, entrepreneur Samantha Kumarasinghe, former secretary to the Ministry of Mass Media Dr. CharithaHerath, Yuthukama Movement President Gavindu Cumaratunga will address the gathering.
