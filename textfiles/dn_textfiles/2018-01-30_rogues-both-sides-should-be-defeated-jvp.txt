## Title: Rogues in both sides should be defeated - JVP
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Tuesday, January 30, 2018 - 01:00

Rogues in both sides should be defeated - JVP

The Janatha Vimukthi Peramuna (JVP) yesterday said the rogues in both sides should be defeated at the upcoming Local Governemnt Elections.
Speaking at a press conference in Colombo yesterday, JVP Propaganda Secretary Vijitha Herath said the investigations into the Presidential Commission of Inquiry into the Central Bank Bond Issue and the Presidential Commission of Inquiry into Mass scale Corruption and Fraud should be expedited.
“We need a Parliamentary debate on these two reports to divulge the truth to the people. The people should know what has happened,” he said.
He said some leaders were talking about forming a government with the rouges who are allegedly involved in mass scale corruptions and financial irrugulalriteis.
The rogues in the government and the opposition should be divulged and defeated at this election.
“The people have received an opportunity to defeat the rogues at this election,” the JVP Propaganda Secretary said.
