## Title: Kaumadhi, Salani win swimming medals for High School
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, November 9, 2017 - 01:00

Kaumadhi, Salani win swimming medals for High School

Kaumadhi Kulatunge of Girls High School, Kandy won a swimming triple when she won the gold medals in all three events including under-20 age category Butterfly Stroke 50 metres, 100 metres and 200 metres in the recently concluded National Schools Games' Swimming Championship held at Sugathadasa swimming pool.
She is the first girl from Girls High School, Kandy to win 3 events at the National Schools' Swimming. Her School mate Salani Seneviratne who participated in the Under 16 age category at the same meet bagged two bronze medals in 50m Free style and 50m Back Stroke.
