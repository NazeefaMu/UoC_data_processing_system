## Title: Australia sends five dinghies and equipment for flood relief work
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 1, 2017 - 13:15

Australia sends five dinghies and equipment for flood relief work

 
Foreign Minister Ravi Karunanayake received five dinghies and equipment from Australian High Commissioner Bryce Hutchesson today to be used for flood relief work.
