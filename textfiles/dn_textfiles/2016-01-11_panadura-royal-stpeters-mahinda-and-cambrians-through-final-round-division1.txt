## Title: Panadura Royal, St.Peter's, Mahinda and Cambrians through to final round of Division1 Tourney
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, January 11, 2016 - 01:00

Panadura Royal, St.Peter's, Mahinda and Cambrians through to final round of Division1 Tourney

Royal-Panadura, St.Peters-Bambalapitiya, Mahinda-Galle and Prince of Wales-Moratuwa beat Mahanama-Colombo, Dharmapala-Pannipitiya, Tissa Central-Kalutara and St.Sylvesters-Kandy in their quarter-final matches respectively qualify to play in the final round of U13 D1 Inter-School Cricket Tournament 2015/16.
Quarter-final match details
Royal-Panadura beat Mahanama on first innings
Royal College, Panadura booked one of the places in the final round of U13 D1 inter-school cricket tournament after Rakith Vidusha and Prashan Kalhara in a dominant bowling performance sharing four wickets apiece restricted Mahanama who chose to bat after winning the toss to a paltry 89. Royal in reply made 188 to win the match on first innings that guaranteed their place in the final round. Tharindu Harshana (31) and Dananjaya Prasad (29) made bulk of scoring for the winners whilst Pawan Shenal and Viduna Devaraja sharing four wickets between them bowled well for the losers.
Mahanama : 89 in 42.2 overs (Samith Rasodan 21, Vinuka Rupasinghe 20, Rakith Vidusha 4/39, Prashan Kalhara 4/06)
Royal , Panadura: 188 in 56.2 overs (Tharindu Harshana 31, Dananjaya Prasad 29, Pawan Shenal 2/46, Viduna Devaraja 2/19)
