## Title: Visit by Iwao HORII, Parliamentary Vice-Minister for Foreign Affairs of Japan
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 30, 2017 - 01:00

Visit by Iwao HORII, Parliamentary Vice-Minister for Foreign Affairs of Japan

Japanese Parliamentary Vice-Minister for Foreign Affairs, HORII, will be visiting Sri Lanka from August 30 to September 2. During his stay, Vice-Minister HORII will participate in the “Indian Ocean Conference 2017” organized by the India Foundation in association with S. Rajaratnam School of International Studies, Singapore and will have bilateral meetings with Sri Lankan political leadership and others.
The Indian Ocean Conference 2017, an international conference co-organized by the India Foundation and a Singaporean academic institute, with support from Indian and Sri Lankan Governments.
This is the second round of conferences (the first conference was held in Singapore last year), which aims to discuss a variety of issues related to national security and economic development with major states and maritime partners in the Indian Ocean, focusing on “Peace, Progress and Prosperity,” as its overall theme.
