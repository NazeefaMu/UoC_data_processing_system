## Title: Bakery products sold with weevils
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 4, 2017 - 01:00

Bakery products sold with weevils

Three canteen owners who sold bakery products unsuitable for human consumption and some filled with weevils to consumers in the Wellawa area of Kurunegala were fined Rs. 15,000 by Kurunegala Additional Magistrate Chamara Wickramanayake.
The suspects were rounded up by PHI s of Kurunegala.
Kurunegala MOH prosecuted.
