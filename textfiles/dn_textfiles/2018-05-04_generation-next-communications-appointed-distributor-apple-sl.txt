## Title: Generation Next Communications  appointed distributor for Apple in SL
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Friday, May 4, 2018 - 01:00

Generation Next Communications  appointed distributor for Apple in SL

Generation Next Communications has been appointed as the authorized distributor for Apple in Sri Lanka. As the authorized distributor, Generation Next Communication Private Limited will distribute Apple products range, including iPhones, iMacs, MacBooks, iPads and Apple accessories.
Generation Next Communications is the authorized distributor for Apple in Afghanistan, Bangladesh, Brunei, Bhutan, Myanmar, Maldives, Mongolia, Laos, Nepal with Sri Lanka being the latest addition.
Rajeev Gooneratne, Chief Executive Officer, Generation Next Communications (Sri Lanka operations) said, “We are excited to be bringing a new offering to the Sri Lankan market supported by the highest levels of Apple accreditation and service.”
A haven for professionals and consumers alike, Generation Next Communications has brought in the latest gadgets and a wide variety of products to the Sri Lankan Market. Generation Next Communications is also the authorized distributor for HTC and Lava and are hoping to introduce many more elite brands to the Sri Lankan consumer from high-end to an affordable price range of mobile devices and products.
With Generation Next Communications global presence it is evident that the company has more offerings to the Sri Lankan consumer which would offer an authentic user experience.
This is a great accomplishment for a company that has been operating in the local market. Generation Next Communications Sri Lanka office will mark its first year of business in August 2018.
Generation Next Communications (GENXT) is the consumer electronic distribution arm of Bimsaria group with its head office in Singapore. Generation Next Communications has earned its reputation and trust as leaders in the mobile phone trade and retail industries, delivering a wide range of original mobile phone products and accessories which are genuine and are the latest range of products.
