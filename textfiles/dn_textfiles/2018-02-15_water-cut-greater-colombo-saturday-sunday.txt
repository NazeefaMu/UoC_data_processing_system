## Title: Water cut in Greater Colombo on Saturday, Sunday
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, February 15, 2018 - 01:00

Water cut in Greater Colombo on Saturday, Sunday

The water supply will be interrupted to several areas in the Colombo City for 24 hours from 9 am on Saturday February 17 to 9 am on Sunday February 18.
This is to facilitate urgent maintenance work on the main distribution line from the Ambatale water filtration plant to Maligakanda, the National Water Supply and Drainage Board announced yesterday.
The areas affected will be Colombo 1, 2, 3, 4, 7, 8, 9, 10 and Colombo 11 areas. Apart from this, Colombo 12 and 13 will experience a low pressure in the supply during this period, the NWSDB said in a press release.Customers and requested to store water in advance to tide over the situation.
