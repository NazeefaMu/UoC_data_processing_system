## Title: SLSRFA donate sports gear
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, January 16, 2016 - 01:00

SLSRFA donate sports gear

Sri Lanka Schools Rugby Football Association (SLSRFA) donated sports equipments for students in Akkaraipattu Education Zone recently. The event took place at the Public Grounds, Palamunai.
The sports equipments were funded by Ministry of Education under the guidance of Provincial Education Department. Tha main objective of this programme was to help the rural sportsmen to reach national level.
About 200 students from 10 schools in Akkaraipattu Educational Zone in Akkaraipattu, Addalaichenai and Pottuvil DS divisions were benefited by this programme.
Sri Lanka Schools Rugby Football Association Sports Development Manager Thanuja Kulatunga, Coaches M.I.M.Ashraff, Sujani Ratnayake, Subashini Karunaratne, R.Priyadharshani, Nilmini Weerasinghe, Akkaraipattu Educational Zone Physical Education In Service Advisor M. H. Hammath and Akkaraipattu Zonal Rugby Co-ordinator I. L. M. Faiz were present at the occasion.
