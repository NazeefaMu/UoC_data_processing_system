## Title: Heavy fine on power pirate
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, January 21, 2017 - 01:00

Heavy fine on power pirate

A man who was found guilty of tapping electricity illegally through an additional connection to the main wire, was fined Rs. 72,204 by Matara Acting Magistrate Lalitha Withanachchi.
Officials attached to the Colombo Special Detective Bureau of the CEB and Akuressa Ploice jointly made the detection.
