## Title: Suranga steers  Railways to  easy win
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, September 30, 2016 - 01:00

Suranga steers  Railways to  easy win

 
A superb unbeaten 92 from Suranga Perera saw Sri Lanka Railways coast to a comfortable nine-wicket win over Sri Jayewardenepura Hospital in a State Services Veterans division 30-over cricket match played at Railway ground, Mount Mary.
Sri Jayawardenepura Hospital 123 in 23.2 overs (R Peiris 34, P.T. Lanerolle 24, S.N. Thanayamge 23, W.K. de Silva 3/23, L Ranasinghe 4/16)
Sri Lanka Railways 124-1 in 14.2 overs (Suranga Perera 92 n.o.)
