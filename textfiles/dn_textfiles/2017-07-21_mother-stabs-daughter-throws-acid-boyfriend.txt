## Title: Mother stabs daughter, throws acid on boyfriend
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, July 21, 2017 - 01:00

Mother stabs daughter, throws acid on boyfriend

A woman who threw rubber acid on her daughters' boyfriend after stabbing her was arrested by Dedigama Police.
Police said the woman ran into a rage on seeing her daughter with her lover when she arrived home for lunch and stabbed her daughter. The daughter and her lover were admitted to the Kegalle base hospital.
The suspect woman is an employee of a rubber estate in the Dedigama area.
Police said the daughter had invited her lover to her mother’s bedroom in her absence.
The woman was produced before the Kegalle Magistrate's Courts.
