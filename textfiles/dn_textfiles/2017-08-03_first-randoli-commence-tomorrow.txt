## Title: First Randoli to commence tomorrow
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, August 3, 2017 - 01:00

First Randoli to commence tomorrow

The first Randoli of Kandy Esala Perahera will parade the streets tomorrow.
It will commence near the Dalada Maligawa at the auspicious time of 6.44pm. A large number of elephants and cultural performers will participate in the Randoli perahera.
