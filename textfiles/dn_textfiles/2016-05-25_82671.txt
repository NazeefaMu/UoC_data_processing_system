## Title: Ma’s Sports Day, a success
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, May 25, 2016 - 01:00

Ma’s Sports Day, a success

 
Ma’s Food Processing (Pvt) Ltd recently held Ma’s Sports Day in Dambulla with active participation of employees from main processing facility in Dambulla, coconut processing factory in Minuwangoda, Killinochchi and Colombo head office.
Ma’s recognizes the need of Sustainable Community Development and supports its community by granting a wide range of employee welfare support services to over 300 people living in Dambulla and Minuwangoda.
