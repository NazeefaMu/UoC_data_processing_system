## Title: Sri Chandrasekara MV enters final
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, December 12, 2017 - 01:00

Sri Chandrasekara MV enters final

Sri Chandrasekara Maha Vidyalaya, Moratuwa entered to the final of the ‘Singer Trophy’ Under 15 division III inter schools cricket tournament when they beat St. John’s College Jaffna by 4 wickets in the semi final played at Prision ground Pallekeleyesterday.
Sri Chandrasekara Maha Vidyalaya Moratuwa will meet Batapola MMV in the final in Colombo on December 28. St. John’s College Jaffna batted first and were bowled out for 166 runs in 50 overs.
T. Dinoshan scored 58 runs and S. Tiravan scored 22 runs. Nipun Lakshan took 3 wickets for 19 runs and Mihiran Viranga took 2 wickets for 27 runs.
Sri Chandrasekara MV, Moratuwa scored 168 runs for 6 wickets in 46.4 overs. Lakshila Sasindu scored 63 runs and Niran Viranga added unbeaten 41 runs. Jonothan Prasanga took 3 wickets for 11 runs.
St. John’s Jaffna: 166 in 50 overs (T. Dinoshan 58, S. Tiravan 22, Nipun Lakshan 3/19, Mihiran Viranga 2/27)
Sri Chandrasekara Moratuwa: 168/6 in 46.4 overs (Lakshila Sasindu 63, Niran Viranga 41 n.o., Jonothan Prasanga 3/11). YK
