## Title: Doctor Dolittle’s release date is out
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Thursday, April 6, 2017 - 01:00

Doctor Dolittle’s release date is out

Robert Downey Jr starrer Doctor Dolittle is set to release in the US on May 24 2019.
Universal studio announced the release date on Monday night, reported Variety.
Directed by Stephen Gaghan, the project is based on the 1920s tales by Hugh Lofting, following a peculiar doctor who can speak with animals, shunning human patients in favour of the critters.
The popular character previously appeared in the 1967 musical starring Rex Harrison and a 1998 reboot starring Eddie Murphy. PTI
