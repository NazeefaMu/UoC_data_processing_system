## Title: Strong winds, showers expected
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 31, 2017 - 01:00

Strong winds, showers expected

Very strong winds about 80 kmph is expected over the country and surrounding sea areas as the cyclonic storm ‘MORA’ is entering Bangladesh,Meteorology Department Director S. Premalal said.
These windy conditions are especially expected over the western slope of the central hills, he said.Heavy rain falls can be expected in parts of the country since the cyclonic storm is subduing, he said.
The rainy weather may subdue in the next two days, Premalal said.He said the the North East part of the country has received rain falls above 100 mm yesterday.Possibility for heavy rain and strong winds will reduce by tomorrow. But, under MORA’s influence, cloudy skies, windy and showery conditions are expected in the country today, he added. Showers or thundershowers will occur at times in the Western, Sabaragamuwa, Southern, North-western and Central provinces, it was further said.
