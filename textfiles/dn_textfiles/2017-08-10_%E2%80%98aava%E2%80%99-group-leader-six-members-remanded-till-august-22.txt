## Title: ‘Aava’ group leader, six members remanded till August 22
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, August 10, 2017 - 11:15

‘Aava’ group leader, six members remanded till August 22

 
The Jaffna Magistrate ordered to remand ‘Aava’ group leader, Sathyavail Nishanthan alias Nisha Victor and six other members till August 22, a short while ago.
The ‘Aava’ group leader and other suspects were arrested by the Terrorism Investigation Division (TID) on August 7 from Jaffna, Colombo Fort and Mattakkuliya.
The gang is alleged to have attacked two police constables with swords while they were engaged in field duty in Kopai, Jaffna on July 30.
