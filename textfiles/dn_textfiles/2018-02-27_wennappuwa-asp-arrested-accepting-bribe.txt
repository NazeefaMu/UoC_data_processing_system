## Title: Wennappuwa ASP arrested for accepting bribe
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, February 27, 2018 - 12:41

Wennappuwa ASP arrested for accepting bribe

 
The Wennappuwa Assistant Superintendent (ASP) of Police, who is alleged to have accepted a bribe of Rs. 30,000, was taken into custody by the Bribery Commission, today.
