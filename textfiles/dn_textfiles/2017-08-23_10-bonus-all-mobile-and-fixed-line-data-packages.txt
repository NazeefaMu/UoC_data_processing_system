## Title: 10% bonus on all mobile and fixed line data packages
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 23, 2017 - 16:00

10% bonus on all mobile and fixed line data packages

 
A 10% bonus will be granted on all mobile and fixed line data packages/cards from September 1 along with the removal of Telco levy the SL Telco Operators announced today.
The Finance Ministry on August 17 announced that it would remove 10% TRC levy on Internet Services with effect from September 1.
