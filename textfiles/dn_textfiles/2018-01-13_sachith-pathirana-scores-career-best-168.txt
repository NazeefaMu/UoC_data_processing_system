## Title: Sachith Pathirana scores career best 168
## Supertitle: Premier League Inter-Club Cricket:
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, January 13, 2018 - 01:00

Premier League Inter-Club Cricket:
Sachith Pathirana scores career best 168

Sachith Pathirana of CCC scored a career best 168 off 118 balls with 18 fours and 8 sixes against Bloomfield in their SLC Premier League Inter-Club cricket match played at Reid Avenue yesterday.
Pathirana and Madhawa Warnapura put on a double century stand of 231 for the sixth wicket to take CCC to a formidable first day total of 380-6 by the close.
Lahiru Milantha was another centurion scoring 112 off 139 deliveries with 6 fours and 4 sixes for Ragama CC against Moors SC.
Chathuranga De Silva of NCC took 6 wickets for 32 runs against Ports Authority CC at FTZ Katunayake on yesterday.
Matches will be held on the second day today.

Premier League Inter-Club Cricket – Tier ‘B’:
Matheesha, Kusal score tons
Matheesha Perera (Kalutara TC) and Kusal Edussooriya (Navy SC) made centuries on the opening day of the SLC Premier League Inter-Club Tier ‘B’ cricket matches that began yesterday.
Perera scored 122 against Galle CC and Edussooriya an unbeaten 100 against Negombo CC.
Matches will be continued on the second day today.
