## Title: Individual nabbed with 608 artificial drugs
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 17, 2018 - 11:08

Individual nabbed with 608 artificial drugs

 
The Police have arrested a man in Maradana for having in possession 608 artificial drugs.
The arrest was made after receiving a tip-off, last night.
The 30-year-old suspect is a resident of Maligawatta.
He will be produced before the Maligakanda Magistrate today.
Maradana Police is conducting further investigations.
