## Title: Katina Pinkama
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 30, 2018 - 01:00

Katina Pinkama

The annual Katina Pinkama of the Raddoluwa Sri Amara Maha Viharaya will be held on November 3 and 4 under the patronage of Viharadhipati Ven. Seeduwe Sumanawansa Nayake Thera.
On November 3, an all-night pirith ceremony will be held from 7.30 pm. On November 4, the Katina Perahera will leave premises no 2/B/79/L Raddolugama Housing Complex at 4 am and arrive at the viharaya for the Katina Cheevara Pooja to be held at 10.45 am. A sanghika dana to 60 members of the Sangha will follow. The pinkama will conclude with the katina nisansa dhammadesana at 7 pm.
