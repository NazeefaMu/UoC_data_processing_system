## Title: Parked bus in Uppuveli set ablaze
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, December 5, 2016 - 01:00

Parked bus in Uppuveli set ablaze

 
A bus parked opposite a house near the 5th mile post on the Kandy Road in Uppuveli was set ablaze on Saturday night.
Uppuweli Police conducting investigations said the bus had been handed over to its owner recently following a court case contesting ownership.
