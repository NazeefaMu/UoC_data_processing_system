## Title: Josephian Sports Awards on Nov. 10
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, November 9, 2017 - 01:00

Josephian Sports Awards on Nov. 10

St. Joseph's College, Colombo 10, a premier Catholic school in Sri Lanka will honour the sportsmen who have excelled in various sports on November 10 at 11.00 am in Rev. Fr. (Dr) Stanley Abeysekara Sports Complex.
The chief guest for this occasion will be Dushan Soza, an eminent old boy of the College who captained the College first eleven cricket team in the years 1975/1976.
Two hundred and thirty nine students will receive their certificates of merit. Seventy seven students will be awarded College Colours. Twenty eight students who represented Sri Lanka in the years 2015 and 2016 will be honoured and five students will receive the Josephian Blue.
Master Sachil Perera will receive the Sportsman of the Year award for the year 2015 and Master Jehan Daniel will receive the Sportsman of the Year award for the year 2016.
