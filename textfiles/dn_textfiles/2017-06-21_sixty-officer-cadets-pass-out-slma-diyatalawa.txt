## Title: Sixty Officer Cadets pass out from SLMA Diyatalawa
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 21, 2017 - 01:00

Sixty Officer Cadets pass out from SLMA Diyatalawa

A group of 60 Officer Cadets upon successful completion of their military training at Diyatalawa Sri Lanka Military Academy (SLMA) passed out amidst a colourful ceremony on Saturday (17).
State Minister for Defence, Ruwan Wijewardene was the Chief Guest at the Passing out Parade (PoP) at the invitation of the Army Commander Lieutenant General Crisanthe De Silva.
Cadet Officers who underwent the SLMA training in the Regular Intake 83 and the Volunteer Intake 57, upon receipt of their commissions in the formal PoP will take up duties around the country.
During the ceremony the Chief Guest laid a floral tribute to the SLMA war memorial in the premises and paid his respects to the fallen war heroes.
After the formal review of the parade, the Chief Guest awarded most prestigious Commissioning Swords to the Officer Cadets.
