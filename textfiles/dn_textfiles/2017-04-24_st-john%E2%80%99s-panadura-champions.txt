## Title: St. John’s Panadura champions
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, April 24, 2017 - 01:00

St. John’s Panadura champions

St. John’s College Panadura emerged champion of the Singer Trophy Under 19 division two 2- day inter schools cricket tournament when they won the final against St. Thomas’ College Matara on first innings at Surrey Village ground Maggona yesterday.
In reply to St John’s first innings of 207, St Thomas’ were bowled out for 103.
Scores: St John’s: 207
St Thomas’: 193 (33/0 o/n) in 89 overs (Tharusha Kavindya 33, Ishan Jayawardane 31, Hasheen Dilman 20, Gishanthika Jayaweera 20, Ashen Dilhara 3/48, Sithum Thelge 2/6, Thamindu Nawarathne 2/46) - YK
