## Title: Several former cricketers reject Sports Ministers’ call
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, June 15, 2018 - 17:07

Several former cricketers reject Sports Ministers’ call

 
Sports Minister Faizer Mustapha’s request made to former cricketers Mahela Jayawardena, Roshan Mahanama and Muttiah Muralitharan to be Sri Lanka Cricket (SLC) consultants was rejected today.
Mahela Jayawardena, stating reasons for his decision to decline the offer said that he doesn’t trust the system.
