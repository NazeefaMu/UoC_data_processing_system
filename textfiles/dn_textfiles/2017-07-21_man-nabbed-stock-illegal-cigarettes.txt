## Title: Man nabbed with stock of illegal cigarettes
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, July 21, 2017 - 01:00

Man nabbed with stock of illegal cigarettes

A group of naval personnel attached to the Western Naval Command in coordination with STF personnel in Colombo nabbed a suspect with 1,000 packets of illegal cigarettes, during a raid carried out at Aluth Mawatha area in Mattakkuliya yesterday. This was following an intelligence tip-off to the Navy. The arrest was made when the stock of illegal cigarettes had been made ready to trade.
