## Title: Atamie v Al Manar Big Match on October 20
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, October 17, 2016 - 01:00

Atamie v Al Manar Big Match on October 20

The third consecutive Big Match between Atamie International School Wattala and Al Manar International School Dematagoda will take place at the Moors SC grounds Braybrooke Place on October 20.
The two teams will play for two trophies in the under 14 and under 16 age groups.
