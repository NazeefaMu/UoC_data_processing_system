## Title: Nabbed with heroin
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, July 23, 2018 - 01:00

Nabbed with heroin

Six persons were taken into custody for possession of heroin in Viharagala, in the Haputale Police division.
They were transporting 332 heroin packets when police stopped their lorry. The arrested are between the ages of 35 and 39 and are residents of Angulana, Panadura. (AM)
