## Title: Sammanthurai New Sun SC cricket champions
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, August 3, 2017 - 01:00

Sammanthurai New Sun SC cricket champions

New Sun Sports Club of Sammanthurai became champion of the limited 8 overs softball cricket tournament conducted by Siva Sakthi Sports Club, Thurai Neelavanai at the Public Play grounds, Thurai Neelawanai recently.
Altogether 32 prominent teams from Ampara and Batticaloa districts took part.
New Sun Sports Club cricket team beat Thurai Neelawanai Central Sports Club cricket team by 34 runs in the final. New Sun SC Cricket team won the toss and bat first. New Sun SC scored 67 runs loss of 9 wickets in their allotted 8 overs. In reply Thurai Neelawanai SC, scored 33 runs in the 7.5 overs for all out. Winners were awarded Trophy and Rs.10,000.00 cash prize by Eastern Province Agriculture Minister K.Thurairasasingham and the Thurai Neelawanai Central Sports Club was awarded a cash prize of Rs.7,000.00 and a trophy.
