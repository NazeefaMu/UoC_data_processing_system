## Title: Wastage from prison to public area
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 29, 2018 - 01:00

Wastage from prison to public area

The refuse water of the Agunakolapelessa prison is released to a residential area, residents in the area said.
They said they cannot bear the foul smell from the wastage. Prison Superintendent W.P. Dhammika said that refuse water and wastage are released to the location temporarily.
