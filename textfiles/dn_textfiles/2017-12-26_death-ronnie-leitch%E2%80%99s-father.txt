## Title: Death of Ronnie Leitch’s father
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, December 26, 2017 - 01:00

Death of Ronnie Leitch’s father

Veteran singer and actor Ronnie Leitch’s father passed away. Funeral will take place on December 27 at 4.00 p.m at the Mount Lavinia Cemetery. Remains lie at Mahinda Florist at Mount Lavinia.
