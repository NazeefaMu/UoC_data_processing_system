## Title: Housemaid dies on flight to Colombo
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, April 7, 2017 - 11:15

Housemaid dies on flight to Colombo

 
A Sri Lankan housemaid has died onboard a flight to Colombo.
The housemaid, a fifty-five-year-old mother of three – was returning from Kuwait.
The reason for her death is yet unknown. He body was taken to the Negombo
General Hospital where a postmortem will be held.
