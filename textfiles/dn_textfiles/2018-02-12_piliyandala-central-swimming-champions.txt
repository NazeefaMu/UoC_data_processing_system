## Title: Piliyandala Central Swimming champions
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, February 12, 2018 - 01:00

Piliyandala Central Swimming champions

Piliyandala Central Swimming team Won the Boys overall championship and their Girls team Emerged runner up at the Vidudaya Inter school Swimming championship 2018 Organized by the department of Physical Education and the sports council of University of Sri Jayawardenepura.
The Boys team obtained 328 points.
