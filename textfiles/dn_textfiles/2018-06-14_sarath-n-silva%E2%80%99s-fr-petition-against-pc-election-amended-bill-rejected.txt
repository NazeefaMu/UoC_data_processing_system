## Title: Sarath N. Silva’s FR petition against PC election (Amended) bill rejected
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 14, 2018 - 11:11

Sarath N. Silva’s FR petition against PC election (Amended) bill rejected

 
Former Chief Justice Sarath N. Silva’s Fundamental Rights petition which was filed at the Supreme Court against the Provincial Council Election (Amended) bill was rejected today.
