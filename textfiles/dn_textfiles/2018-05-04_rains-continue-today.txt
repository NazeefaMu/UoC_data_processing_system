## Title: Rains to continue from today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, May 4, 2018 - 09:48

Rains to continue from today

 
Rainy conditions are expected to continue from today, the Meteorology Department said.
Accordingly rains above 100mm are expected particularly in Western, Central, Sabaragamuwa, North-western and Southern provinces and Anuradhapura district.
Temporary localized strong winds are also expected during thundershowers.
The Met. Department urged the public to take adequate precautions to minimize damages caused by lightning activity.
