## Title: Leopard killed in Kilinochchi: Two suspects arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Sunday, June 24, 2018 - 09:53

Leopard killed in Kilinochchi: Two suspects arrested

 
Two suspects who are alleged to have killed a leopard in Ambalakulam, Kilinochchi were arrested by the Kilinochchi Police this morning.  
Pictures of the leopard which was brutally killed took the social media by storm last Thursday.
However Leader of the House and Minister Laksham Kiriella assure d at Friday’s parliamentary gathering that the government had taken measures to conduct special investigations into killing the leopard.
