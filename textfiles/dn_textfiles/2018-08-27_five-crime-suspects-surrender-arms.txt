## Title: Five crime suspects surrender with arms
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, August 27, 2018 - 01:00

Five crime suspects surrender with arms

Five suspects linked to a number of crimes that took place within the Hikkaduwa police area, surrendered to the police last Friday.
A locally made revolver and a sword in their possession was also handed over to police.
Police had received a number of complaints against them including causing damages on three houses at Thiranagama, Hikkaduwa on August 10, attacking two and causing damage to a whree-wheeler and a motor vehicle and assaulting and causing injuries to a monk, a woman and a man by inflicting with firearms. The surrendered suspects are aged between 17 and 21.They will be produced before courts on completion of investigations.
