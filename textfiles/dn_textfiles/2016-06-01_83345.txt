## Title: Over Rs. 40m to develop Ampara grounds
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, June 1, 2016 - 01:00

Over Rs. 40m to develop Ampara grounds

The Digamadulla District Parliamentarian and Deputy Sports Minister H.M.M. Harees has allocated Rs 40.45 million to develop the following play grounds in the Ampara district.
Kalmunai Electorate
Maruthamunai Mashoor Moulana play ground, Natpiddymunai Leader Ashraff Vidyalaya play ground, Kalmunai Carmel Patrima College, Kalmunai Wesley Central College play ground, Kalmunai Zahira Central College play ground and Pandiruppu, Sainthamaruthu public play grounds.
Sammanthurai Electorate
Sammanthurai, Irakkamam, Varipathanchenai, 5th Colony Ameer Ali public play grounds.
Pottuvil Electorate
Addalaichenai, Nintvur, Thirukkovil, Palamunai public play grounds, Karativu Kanagaratnam play ground, Oluvil Al Hamra Maha Vidyalaya play ground and Pottuvil Pasarichenai play ground.
Ampara Electorate
Gemunu Pura, Jayawardenapura, Uhana, Padiyathalawa public play grounds and Naal Oya school play ground.
The allocations for the above mentioned play grounds have been already dispatched to the Ampara District Government Agent who has been directed by the Sports Deputy Minister H.M.M.Harees to commence work immediately.
