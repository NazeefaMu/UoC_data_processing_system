## Title: Nidahasugayak web site launch
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 10, 2018 - 01:00

Nidahasugayak web site launch

President Maithripala Sirisena yesterday, launched the website (Nidahasugayak.com) of the ‘Free Media Centre’ which would act as the propaganda arm of the United Peoples’ Freedom Alliance (UPFA) at the upcoming local government elections.The Centre has been established at No 65, Ananda Rajakaruna Mawatha, Colombo 10. Ministers Susil Premajayantha, S.B. Dissanayake, A.H.M. Fowzie, Mahinda Amaraweera, Chandima Weerakkody, Thilanga Sumathipala, Anurada Jayaratne and SLFP members were present at the launch.
(Picture by Chandana Perera- President’s Media Division)
