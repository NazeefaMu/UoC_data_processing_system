## Title: Lake House Strikes Gold
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 22, 2017 - 01:00

Lake House Strikes Gold

Sri Lanka Institute of Training & Development (SLITAD) at its annual People Development Awards 2016/2017 presented a Gold Award to the Associated Newspapers of Ceylon Ltd., for its commitment for Human Resource Strategy and Leadership, Performance Management, Training and Development, Reward and Recognition of Human Resources. The awards ceremony was held on December 20 at Cinnamon Lakeside with Policy Planning and Economic Development Deputy Minister Dr. Harsha De Silva as Chief Guest. The Gold Award is presented by Guest of Honour of SLITAD Peoples Award, Vice Chairman UGC Professor P. S. M. Gunarathna to ANCL Human Capital Deputy General Manager Narada Sumanarathna. The Human Capital Team look on.
