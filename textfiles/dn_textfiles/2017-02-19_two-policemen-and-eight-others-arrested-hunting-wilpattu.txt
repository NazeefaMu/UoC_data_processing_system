## Title: Two policemen and eight others arrested for hunting in Wilpattu
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Sunday, February 19, 2017 - 15:00

Two policemen and eight others arrested for hunting in Wilpattu

 
Wildlife officials arrested ten individuals including two police officers for allegedly hunting in Wilpattu National Wildlife Sanctuary, today (19).
Further investigations are underway.
