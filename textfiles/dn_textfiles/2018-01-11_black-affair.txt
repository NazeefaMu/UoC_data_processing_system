## Title: Black affair!
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Thursday, January 11, 2018 - 01:00

Black affair!

Hollywood actors and actresses stuck with a mostly black dress code to protest against sexual abuse in the entertainment industry at Golden Globes 2018 which was held at the Beverly Hilton Hotel in Beverly Hills, California, on Sunday.
Though many of the evening’s ensembles were exceptionally boring in silhouette, colour, and style, some A-listers stood out from the crowd in fashion fit for royalty, while others made a splash in the worst way. Here are some hits and misses which made it to the red carpet. Variety
