## Title: Anula Vidyalaya Girls win by 44 runs
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, June 27, 2016 - 01:00

Anula Vidyalaya Girls win by 44 runs

 
 An excellent batting performance by Amasha Perera (35 runs) helped Anula Vidyalaya, Nugegoda Girls Cricket Team to record a 44 run victory over the Sri Sumangala Girls School, Panadura in their Western Province Inter-School Cricket Tournament final to be crowned the Western Province Girls Cricket Champions.
Amasha Perera
 
This tournament was organized by the Department of Education under the guidance of the Zonal Sports Unit the match was played on June 15 at Sri Sumangala Boys School playground.
The Sri Sumangala Girls School, Panadura won the toss and elected to field first and Anula Vidyalaya, Nugegoda scored 92 runs for two wickets in six overs with Amasha making 35 and 44 runs from 11 wide balls.
Sri Sumangala Girls School, Panadura in reply scored only 48 runs for two wickets in six overs with Thanumi 31 runs were the best contribution for the Sri Sumangala Girls School, in bowling for Anula Vidyalaya, Nugegoda Harshitha captured one wicket for six runs.
