## Title: World Masters Games : Sri Lankans win at TT
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, April 29, 2017 - 01:00

World Masters Games : Sri Lankans win at TT

Former Sri Lanka and national table tennis player Dr. Nimal Lucas (third from left) won a gold in the 70-75 age category event and (below) another former Sri Lanka and national women’s player Tamara Hewage (second from right) won a bronze in the 50-55 age category women’s team event at the World Masters Games in Auckland.
