## Title: Youth kills girlfriend
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, March 26, 2018 - 01:00

Youth kills girlfriend

A 25-year-old man was arrested over the killing of a 24-year-old woman in Danvilana, Veyangoda yesterday.
Veyangoda Police found the girl’s body in a paddy field in Danvilana yesterday morning. She was later identified as Indika Sandamali, a resident of Deegalahinna, Elamulla. Both the suspect and the deceased had been working at a garment factory and were allegedly involved in a love affair.
