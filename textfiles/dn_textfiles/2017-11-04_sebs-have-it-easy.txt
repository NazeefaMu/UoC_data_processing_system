## Title: Sebs have it easy
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, November 4, 2017 - 01:00

Sebs have it easy

St. Sebastian’s College, Moratuwa beat Subarathi Vidyalaya, Godagama by 47 runs on first innings in their Inter School under 13 division three tournament cricket match played at Godagama.
Batting first homesters collapsed for 62 runs with Ryan Dissanayake and Malintha Silva claiming 5 for 13 and 5 for 28 respectively.
In reply when game came to a halt due to rain Sebs were 109 for 6.
[Chief Scores]
Subarathi Vidyalaya - 62 in 23.5 overs - Dinithu Hirushan 20, Ryan Dissnayake 5/13, Malintha Silva 5/28
St. Sebastian’s - 109/6 in 14.2 overs - Malintha Silva 37, Adesha Almeida 24
Sebs beat I Gate International
In another game in the same division and same age group St. Sebastian’s beat I Gate International School, Thalawathugoda by 107 runs on first innings in their inter school cricket match played at Mattegoda. Batting first homesters were skittle out for a paltry 55 runs and in reply Sebs declared at 162 for 8 with Naveesh Fernando 68 runs and at stumps homesters were 55 for 4.
[Chief Scores]
I Gate International School - 55 in 38 overs - Malintha Silva 3/8m Dinada Peiris 3/15, Yahiru Fernando 2/5 and 55/4 - Nohim Rusiru 20, Adesha Almeida 3/14
St. Sebastian’s College - 162/8 dec. in 40 overs - Naveesh Fernando 68, Dilusha Perera 24, Thisath de Silva 3/19, Lasindu Dulun 2/25, Saminda Mendis 2/30
