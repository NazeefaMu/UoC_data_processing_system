## Title: Grade Six admission appeals till Feb. 15
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, January 20, 2018 - 01:00

Grade Six admission appeals till Feb. 15

Appeals relating to the Grade Six admission based on the Grade Five Scholarship Exam result are accepted by the Education Ministry until February 15.
The application can be downloaded from the Ministerial website www.moe.gov.lk.
The duly filled appeal should be sent to the Education Director, School Activity Branch, Education Ministry, Isurupaya, Battaramulla by registered mail.
The appeals will be considered by a panel appointed and the decisions will be notified accordingly.
