## Title: Three-wheel-driver arrested for intoxicating passengers to steal
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 1, 2017 - 11:15

Three-wheel-driver arrested for intoxicating passengers to steal

 
Wellawatta Police arrested yesterday a three-wheel driver alleged to have robbed money and jewellery from passengers after intoxicating them.
The forty-seven-year-old suspect named Mohamed Nawas is a resident in Whalela Weediya, Colombo -10.
The Police said that the suspect has robbed money and jewelleries worth more than Rs. 600,000.
The suspect will be produced before the Mount Lavinia Magistrate Courts today.
Wellawatta Police is investigating.
