## Title: SAARC Ministers  discuss law and order
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, July 15, 2017 - 01:00

SAARC Ministers  discuss law and order

The eighth SAARC Ministers of Law and Order (Interior/Home Affairs) meeting was inaugurated at Galle Face Hotel, Colombo on Thursday. Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe inaugurated the session while Law and Order Minister Sagala Ratnayaka, Internal Affairs Minister S. B. Nawinna and Ministers from the SAARC region participated.
