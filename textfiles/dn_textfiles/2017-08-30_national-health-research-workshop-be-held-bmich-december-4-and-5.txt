## Title: National Health Research Workshop to be held at the BMICH on December 4 and 5
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 30, 2017 - 01:00

National Health Research Workshop to be held at the BMICH on December 4 and 5

The National Health Research Workshop will be held at at the BMICH in Colombo on December 04 and 5t under the patronage of Health Minister Dr. Rajitha Senaratne, according to Health Ministry spokesman.
Tthe spokesman said that Minister Senaratne launched the workshop web site at the Health Ministry recently. The minister pointed out that the Health Ministry had spent Rs. 141 million during this year (2017) for the researches conducted by doctors and other staff members.
Minister Senaratne instructed Health Ministry officials to obtain the Research Report of those doctors who had conducted various researches and used them during the workshop, he said.
Deputy Director General (Research and Training) Sunil De Alwis, was also present during the launching ceremony.
