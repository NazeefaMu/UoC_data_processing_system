## Title: Shalila Moonasinghe instructed to be removed from Litro Gas Chairman post
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, October 11, 2017 - 08:45

Shalila Moonasinghe instructed to be removed from Litro Gas Chairman post

The Public Enterprise Development Ministry has instructed to remove Shalila Moonasinghe from the Chairman post of the Litro Gas company due to the on going investigations.
Shalila Moonasinghe was arrested and remended till today in connection with the alleged transferring of USD 1.1 million from Far Eastern International Bank of Taiwan to his personal account in Sri Lanka by Colombo Fort Magistrate Lanka Jayaratne, yesterday. 
He was arrested by the Criminal Investigations Department (CID) on October 9.
