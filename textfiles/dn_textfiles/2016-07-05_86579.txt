## Title: Matthew Abeysinghe accorded warm welcome
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, July 5, 2016 - 01:00

Matthew Abeysinghe accorded warm welcome

Swimming sensation Matthew Abeysinghe who became the first ever Sri Lankan swimmer to achieve Olympic qualifying standards arrives at the Bandaranaike International Airport yesterday accompanied by his father Manoj Abeysinghe and (on right) is greeted by his mother Laura Abeysinghe. Matthew clocked 50.53 secs in the men’s 100m freestyle event at the Hong Kong swimming circuit championships in Hong Kong to earn an Olympic qualification.
