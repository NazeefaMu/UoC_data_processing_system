## Title: MSD officers arrested for sexually abusing Russian woman
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Sunday, January 29, 2017 - 13:45

MSD officers arrested for sexually abusing Russian woman

 
Two officers attached to the Ministerial Security Division (MSD), have been arrested for allegedly sexually abusing a Russian woman. 
The incident occured in Mount Lavinia a few nights ago, and the arrest was made on the complaint made by the Russian woman. 
Mount Lavinia Police is investigating.
