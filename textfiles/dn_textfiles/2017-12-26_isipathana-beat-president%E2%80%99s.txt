## Title: Isipathana beat President’s
## Supertitle: Singer Cup Schools Cricket
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, December 26, 2017 - 01:00

Singer Cup Schools Cricket
Isipathana beat President’s

Isipathana College beat President’s College Kotte by one wicket in their ‘Singer Cup’ Under 19 inter schools cricket match played at BRC grounds on Sunday.
* At BRC: Isipathana won by one wicket.
President’s Kotte: 108 and 221 (104/4 o/n) in 75.4 overs (Dilshan Sigera 60 n.o., Nirmal Dharmapriya 58, Ganindu Dewmina 41, Chamod Wickramasooriya 27, Sanjula Bandara 4/58, Lisula Vinath 2/24, Lahiru Dilshan 2/27)
Isipathana: 73 and 257/9 in 58 overs (Shamilka Wickramathilaka 63 n.o., Leshan Amarasinghe 51, Sanjula Bandara 47, Madushika Sandaruwan 22, Ashen Gunawardhane 21, Chamod Wickramasuriya 2/54, Pramuka Gayashan 2/63)
* At Horana: Match drawn.
Siri Piyaratana: 281/9 decl and 167/7 in 49 overs (Rajitha Ashan 46, Ravindu Nimesh 42, Dimuth Sampath 31, Pasindu Janith 6/77)
Taxila: 277 (90/2 o/n) in 80.2 overs (Shasindu Janeth 69, Malith Kaushalya 62 n.o., Thilina Tharinda 28, Dilshan Gimhan 28,Dimuth Sampath 4/91, Rajitha Ashan 3/21, Shanilka Deemantha 2/58) - YK
