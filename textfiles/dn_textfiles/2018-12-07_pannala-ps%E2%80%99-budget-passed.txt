## Title: Pannala PS’ Budget passed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Friday, December 7, 2018 - 01:00

Pannala PS’ Budget passed

The proposed Budget of the Pannala Pradeshiya Sabha for 2019, was passed with a majority of 27 votes, recently.
The pradeshiya sabha’s 13 UNP councillors were absent for the vote, Pannala Pradeshiya Chairman Ranjith Lansakkara said.
