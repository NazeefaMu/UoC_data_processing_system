## Title: Beruwala Capsizing: Body of 13-year old washes ashore
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, February 23, 2017 - 11:30

Beruwala Capsizing: Body of 13-year old washes ashore

 
The body of a thirteen year old washed ashore the Kalutara beach last evening, bringing the death toll from the capsizing to sixteen.
Forty people were aboard in the boat which travelled to Holy Cross Church in Kalutara on Sunday (19) when the incident occurred.
Eleven bodies were found in the immediate aftermath, eight reported missing.
With the discovery of the body of the thirteen year old, all missing bodies are accounted for, Police said.
