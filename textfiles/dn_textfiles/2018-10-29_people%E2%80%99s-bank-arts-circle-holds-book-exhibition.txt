## Title: People’s Bank Arts Circle holds book exhibition
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Monday, October 29, 2018 - 01:00

People’s Bank Arts Circle holds book exhibition

A book exhibition and fair to celebrate October, the month of reading was recently held at the third floor of People’s Bank Head Office.
The keynote speech was delivered by Veteran film Director Somarathne Dissanayake at the opening ceremony of the book exhibition.
The Corporate and Executive Management of the Bank including People’s Bank Acting Chief Executive Officer and General Manager, Rasitha Gunawardena and staff members were present at this event.
