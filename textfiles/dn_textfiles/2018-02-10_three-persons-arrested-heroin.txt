## Title: Three persons arrested with heroin
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, February 10, 2018 - 01:00

Three persons arrested with heroin

Tissamaharama police arrested three persons with heroin in Tissamaharama recently.
They were selling the drugs travelling by a luxury vehicle, police said.
The suspects were produced before the Tissamaharama magistrate last week, police said.
