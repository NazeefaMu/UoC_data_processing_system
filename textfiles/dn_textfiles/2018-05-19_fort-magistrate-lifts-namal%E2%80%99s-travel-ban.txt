## Title: Fort Magistrate lifts Namal’s travel ban
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Saturday, May 19, 2018 - 01:00

Fort Magistrate lifts Namal’s travel ban

Colombo Fort Magistrate Lanka Jayaratne yesterday ordered to temporarily suspend the travel ban imposed on MP Namal Rajapaksa.
This is regarding a magisterial inquiry for allegedly misappropriating Rs.70 million granted by the Indian Real Estate Company, Krrish Lanka, to develop rugby in Sri Lanka.
The FCID submitted to Court that they initiated investigations into several bank accounts following a court order and discovered that Rs.350,000 had been transferred to a bank account belonging to one Nihal Hemasiri Perera.
The FCID reported to the court that they have recorded a statement from Nihal Perera regarding this financial transaction. Perera had informed the FCID that he received the concerned money from an Italian.The FCID is conducting further investigations regarding the incident.
The FCID sought Singapore’s assistance pertaining to this financial transaction under the provisions of the Mutual Assistance in Criminal Matters Act.
Colombo Fort Magistrate Lanka Jayaratne sent a reminder to the Attorney General directing him to expedite his advice regarding the case. The case will be taken up again on July 13.
