## Title: New building complex for Trincomalee Campus
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 8, 2018 - 01:00

New building complex for Trincomalee Campus

A New building complex of the Faculty of Applied Science at the Trincomalee Campus of the Eastern University took place under the patronage of President Maithripala Sirisena at the campus premises yesterday. Here, President Sirisena and Opposition Leader R. Sampanthan unveiling the plaque. Minister Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe, Eastern Province Governor Rohitha Bogollagama, Parliamentarian Dayasiri Jayasekera, UGC Chairman Professor Mohan De Silva and IGP Pujith Jayasundera were present.
