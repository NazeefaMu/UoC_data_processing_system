## Title: 'Caltex Star Drive Car and Bike Chase' winners awarded
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, July 20, 2017 - 01:00

'Caltex Star Drive Car and Bike Chase' winners awarded

The third two winners of Caltex Star Drive – Car and Bike Chase” received their Bajaj Discover 125cc motorcycles plus a year’s worth of free engine oil at an event held at the Chevron House in Colombo 3, recently.
The winners of the third round were T.G. Jayanandana from Kundasale and W.K.D Wijesiri from Gampaha.
Caltex Star Drive – Car and Bike Chase” offers the consumers an opportunity to win a brand-new Honda BR-V 7-seater Compact SUV as the grand prize or one of ten brand new Bajaj Discover 125cc motorcycles along with a year’s worth of engine oil.
Four lucky winners have won Bajaj Discover motorcycles and a year’s supply of engine oil during the monthly draws held in April and May. Two more randomly selected winners will stand the chance of winning the motorcycles and the annual supply of engine oil at the draw to be held in July.
The final two motorcycles and the Honda BR-V Compact SUV will be awarded at a Grand draw which will be held in September 2017.
To stand a chance of winning these valuable prizes, the consumers simply need to reveal the secret code by scratching the panel on the reverse of the card attached to the promotional pack.
