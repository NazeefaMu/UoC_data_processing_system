## Title: Tamil Makkal Peravai holds rally in Batticaloa
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 10, 2017 - 13:45

Tamil Makkal Peravai holds rally in Batticaloa

 
A Tamil Makkal Peravai rally, initiated by North Province Chief Minister C.V. Wigneswaran was held near the Kalladi clock tower, today (10).
The rally was in demand of restoring lands occupied by the military, to find the missing, to leave the Tamil-Hindu culture in the North and East undisturbed and to unite the Northern and Southern parts of the country.
The rally titled ‘Eluga Tamil’ meaning ‘rise up Tamils’ began at Kalladi clock tower and halted at Kalladi bridge, Batticaloa.
Jaffna District MP Dharmalingam Siddharthan, Batticaloa District MP S. Viyalendran, Former District MP Suresh Premachandran,Eastern Province MP R.Thurairatnam andTamil Makkal Peravai Chairman S. Vasantharaja participated in the rally.
