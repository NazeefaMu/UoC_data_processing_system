## Title: Foreign Affairs Minister meets Japanese Ambassador to Sri Lanka
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 30, 2017 - 16:30

Foreign Affairs Minister meets Japanese Ambassador to Sri Lanka

 
Newly appointed Foreign Affairs Minister Tilak Marapane met with Japanese Ambassador to Sri Lanka H.E Kenichi Suganuma last evening at the Foreign Affairs Ministry in Colombo.
At the meeting the Foreign Affairs Minister and the Japanese Ambassador held discussions on strengthening international ties between the two countries.
Furthermore Kenichi Suganuma pledged to support Sri Lanka in development strategies in the future.
