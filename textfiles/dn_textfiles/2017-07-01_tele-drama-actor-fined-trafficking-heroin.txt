## Title: Tele-drama actor fined for trafficking heroin
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, July 1, 2017 - 01:00

Tele-drama actor fined for trafficking heroin

Panadura Provincial High Court Judge Padmini Ranawka Gunatilleke sentenced a tele-drama actor to one year’s imprisonment and fined Rs. 200,000 for possessing and trafficking heroin weighing over one gram.
The accused pleaded guilty to both charges when the case was called on June 28. State Counsel K. V. Dharseka stated in Court that at the time of his arrest by Piliyandala Police, he was in possession of 10 grams of heroin.
The packet was forwarded to the Government Analyst for examination and report.
