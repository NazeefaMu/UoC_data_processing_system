## Title: Two Indians arrested in Thuraiur for selling garments
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, October 4, 2017 - 10:30

Two Indians arrested in Thuraiur for selling garments

 
An Indian man and a woman who have arrived in Sri Lanka on tourist visa were arrested for selling garments, violating Immigration and Emigration laws in Thuraiur , last night.
The arrested man (28) and the woman (38) will be produced before the Kayts Magistrate Court today.
Kayts Police is investigating.
