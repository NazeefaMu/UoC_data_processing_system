## Title: US Congress on Armed Services meets President Sirisena
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 30, 2018 - 14:45

US Congress on Armed Services meets President Sirisena

 
Representatives from the US Congress on Armed Services met President Maithripala Sirisena at his official residence earlier today.
The representatives consisted of Chairman of the Committee McClellen Thornberry and other members, Enrique Cuellar, Vicky Hartzler, Carol Shea-Porter.
Secretary to the President Austin Fernando, Secretary of the Foreign Affairs Ministry Prasad Kariyawasm, The United States of America Ambassador to Sri Lanka also participated.
