## Title: Death of Lake House correspondent
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, February 12, 2018 - 01:00

Death of Lake House correspondent

D.W.D.C. Dasanayaka who served for over 15 years as Dambulla correspondent for the Lake House group of newspapers passed away on Friday (09) at Dambulla Weera Denzil Kobbekaduwa base hospital after a brief illness.
He was the registrar of deaths, marriages and births in Inamaluwa Korale, Matale District and president of Matale District deaths, marriage and birth certificates registrars’ society.
He was 63 at the time of his death and had three children. The funeral was held yesterday at Dambulla crematorium.
