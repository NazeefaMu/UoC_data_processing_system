## Title: Repair of power lines begins
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, March 1, 2018 - 01:00

Repair of power lines begins

Ceylon Electricity Board (CEB) regional officers have started repairing electricity transmission lines which were damaged by gusty winds experienced in the Western bank of the Central hills on Tuesday morning.
Due to stormy weather, several high-tension lines were damaged, causing a power failure in most parts of the Central hills and Hatton. The CEB succeeded in restoring power supply to several areas by yesterday afternoon. <U+200B>
As a result of the storm, many houses in the Hatton area <U+200B><U+200B>were<U+200B> damaged<U+200B>.
A significant damage to <U+200B>property, has also been reported.
