## Title: Former Maliyadeva skipper Daluwatte dies
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, November 21, 2015 - 01:00

Former Maliyadeva skipper Daluwatte dies

Former Maliyadeva College rugby skipper Indika Daluwatte died following a tragic motor accident friday afternoon.
Daluwatte who captained the Maliyadeva First XI team in 2003, also excelled in athletics and was a coloursman who excelled as number-eight for the school.
He also represented the Kurunegala Rugby Football Club. Funeral will take place on Monday at the General Cemetery, Kurunegala.
