## Title: Four deaths reported from several districts
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 30, 2017 - 11:34

Four deaths reported from several districts

 
Four deaths have been reported from Galle, Gampaha and Badulla districts due to bad weather, the Disaster Management Centre said.
Furthermore, twelve fishermen were reported missing.
Search missions are underway.
