## Title: Man stabbed to death in Haldummulla
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, February 22, 2018 - 11:23

Man stabbed to death in Haldummulla

 
A 56-year-old man was stabbed to death by a sharp object in Haldummulla, last night.
Initial investigations had revealed that the incident occurred following a quarrel.
The deceased man identified as Nimal Bandara, residing in Marangalawela succumbed to injuries after being rushed to the Haldummulla Hospital.
A suspect has been taken into custody.
Magisterial and postmortem examinations will be conducted today.
Haldummulla Police is conducting further investigations.
