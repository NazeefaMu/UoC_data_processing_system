## Title: Interim order extended till December 10th
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 7, 2018 - 15:05

Interim order extended till December 10th

The Supreme Court seven-judge-Bench headed by Chief Justice Nalin Perera  further extended till 10th of December, the Interim order staying the operation of the Gazette notification issued by the President to dissolve Parliament.
