## Title: Fuel price reduced
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, November 30, 2018 - 16:10

Fuel price reduced

Minister of Petroleum Resources Development Gamini Lokuge said, the Ceylon Petroleum Corporation will reduce the prices of petrol and diesel by Rs 5 per litre with effect from midnight today. 
He stated that this price reduction will affect all grades of petrol and diesel.
