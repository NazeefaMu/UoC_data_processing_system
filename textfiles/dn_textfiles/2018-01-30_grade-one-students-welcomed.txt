## Title: Grade one students welcomed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Tuesday, January 30, 2018 - 01:00

Grade one students welcomed

Good Shepherds Convent, Kandy welcomed its grade one students by offering flowers. The flowers were offered by the grade two students of the school. Principal Rev sister Sujani Fernando and Chief Guest Rev father Anton Grasian (O.M.I) made speeches at the event.
