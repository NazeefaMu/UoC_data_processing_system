## Title: Kalana Madhusanka hits third ton
## Supertitle: ‘Singer ’ U19 Cricket
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, December 28, 2018 - 01:00

‘Singer ’ U19 Cricket
Kalana Madhusanka hits third ton

Kalana Madhusanka skipper of Seethawaka National School Avissawella scored his third century of the season in their drawn match against President’s Maharagama in the ‘Singer Trophy’ Under 19 inter-schools cricket tournament at Ruwanwella yesterday.
Madhusanka passed 1150 runs in 10 innings for the season.
Sri Sumangala College Panadura beat Bandaranayake Gampaha by 202 runs and C.W.W. Kannangara MMV beat Vidyaloka College by 10 wickets.
