## Title: Asanke Bandara Dodanwela’s funeral today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 8, 2018 - 01:00

Asanke Bandara Dodanwela’s funeral today

The funeral of Asanke Bandara Dodanwela,former Deputy Mayor of Kandy and Coordinating Secretary to the President for Ministries of Defence, Mahaweli Development and Environment who passed away on Monday will take place at the Mahiyawa General Cemetery at 4 p.m today. Remains lie at his residence at No.140, Riverdale Road, Anniwatte, Kandy.
