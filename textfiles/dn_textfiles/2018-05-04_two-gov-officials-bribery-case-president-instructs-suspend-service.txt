## Title: Two gov. officials bribery case: President instructs to suspend service
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, May 4, 2018 - 10:24

Two gov. officials bribery case: President instructs to suspend service

 
President Maithripala Sirisena today instructed to suspend the service of President’s Chief of Staff, I.H.K. Mahanama and State Timber Corporation (STC) Chairman P. Dissanayake who were arrested for accepting a bribe of Rs.20 million from an Indian businessman.
The two suspects were arrested while accepting the bribe at the car park of a five star hotel, by the Bribery Commission last night.
Furthermore the President has instructed relevant officials to take strict legal action against the two alleged top government officials without showing any partiality.
