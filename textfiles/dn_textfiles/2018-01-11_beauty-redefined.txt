Beauty redefined!

They don’t call them beauty crazes for nothing. Trends may come and go and for some of us it seems that some of these beauty trends should never have even started in the first place. Fun, glamorous and downright strange these experimentations with make up not only test the creativity and ability of their makers but also the fearlessness of their users.
Here is a roundup of some of the strangest looks with make up from last year and some pointers on how to look your best in 2018.
