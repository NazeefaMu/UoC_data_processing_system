## Title: The 51st commemoration of late Minister D.A.Rajapaksa
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, November 30, 2018 - 01:00

The 51st commemoration of late Minister D.A.Rajapaksa

The 51st commemoration of late Minister D.A.Rajapaksa was held at the New Town Hall in Colombo yesterday.Prime Minister Mahinda Rajapaksa, Health Minister Chamal Rajapaksa and family members participated. Minister Arumugam Thondaman garlanded the statue of late D.A. Rajapaksa. Pictures by Sudam Gunasinghe.
