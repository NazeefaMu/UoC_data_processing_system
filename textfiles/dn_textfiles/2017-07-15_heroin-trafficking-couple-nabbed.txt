## Title: Heroin trafficking couple nabbed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, July 15, 2017 - 01:00

Heroin trafficking couple nabbed

A couple who allegedly involved in trafficking heroin, was arrested by the Divisional Crime Investigation Unit of Matara.
According to the Police, the couple was arrested while attempting to sell heroin to a person in Delkada, Matara.
The suspects were identified as husband and wife of two children.
Police had recovered one gram of heroin hidden in the woman's undergarment and also another 350 milligrams of heroin in the man's underwear.
The man has two pending cases related to keeping heroin
