## Title: Former Avant Garde Operations  Manager arrested, remanded
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, April 27, 2018 - 01:03

Former Avant Garde Operations  Manager arrested, remanded

Based on the investigations conducted by the Criminal Investigations Department (CID) with regard to the floating armoury MV Avant Garde which was taken into custody by the Navy in 2015, its Operations Manager at the time, a retired Lieutenant of the Sri Lanka Navy was arrested by the CID last morning, Police Media Spokesman SP Ruwan Gunasekara said.
The suspect, 43-year-old Millaniyage Samudra Nilupul de Costa of Wanawasala, Kelaniya was arrested by the CID yesterday.
The suspect was produced before the Galle Magistrate’s Court and remanded till May 3.
