## Title: Ashen scores century
## Supertitle: Singer U19 Cricket
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, February 12, 2018 - 01:00

Singer U19 Cricket
Ashen scores century

Sri Lanka under 19 world cup cricketer Ashen Bandara of St. Aloysius College Galle scored 101 off 107 balls with 11 fours and a six against D.S. Senanayake College on the opening day of the ‘Singer Cup’ Under 19 inter-schools cricket match played at the Galle International Cricket Stadium yesterday.
Hirusha Sandeepa of St. John’s Nugegoda scored an unbeaten 109 against Ananda Sastralaya Matugama.
Anuka Jayasinghe of Sri Sumangala Panadura took 5 wickets for 22 runs against Kegalu Vidyalaya.
Loyola College Negombo beat St. Patrick’s College Jaffna by 8 wickets at Godigamuwa.
