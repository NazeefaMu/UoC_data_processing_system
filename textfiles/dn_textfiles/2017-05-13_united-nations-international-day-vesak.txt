## Title: United Nations International Day of Vesak
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, May 13, 2017 - 01:00

United Nations International Day of Vesak

The 14th United Nations International Day of Vesak was inaugurated at the BMICH yesterday under the patronage of Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi. Picture shows the Indian Prime Minister being escorted to the venue by President Maithripala Sirisena, Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe and Justice and Buddhasasana Minister Wijayedasa Rajapakshe.
