## Title: Carpenter nabbed for robbing phone
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 12, 2018 - 01:00

Carpenter nabbed for robbing phone

A carpenter was arrested by Marawila Police for robbing a mobile phone worth Rs. 32,000 from a house of an army private in Panangoda, Nattandiya, where he was hired for a carpentry job.
Following a complaint made by the army private, police arrested the carpenter on suspicion. Following interrogation, he had confessed to having robbed the phone and sold it to a third party.
Police recovered the phone from the buyer. The suspect was due to be produced before the Marawila Magistrate.
