## Title: Miner killed in graphite mine collapse
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Sunday, January 29, 2017 - 13:00

Miner killed in graphite mine collapse

[File photo]
A miner attached to Bogola Graphite died after a mine collapsed on him last evening.
He was rushed to the Karawanella Hospital, but was pronounced dead upon admission. 
The mine has been closed pending inquiry, Bogola Graphite officials said.
