## Title: SOLDIER DIES IN CAB-TRAIN CRASH
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 10, 2017 - 01:00

SOLDIER DIES IN CAB-TRAIN CRASH

A soldier attached to the Aiyakachchi Army camp died and his colleague seriously injured when their cab crashed into a train bound for Colombo at Ariyalai yesterday morning. Picture by Bandula Seneviratne Vavuniya North Group correspondent
