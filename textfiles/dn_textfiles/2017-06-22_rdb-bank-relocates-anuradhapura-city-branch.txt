## Title: RDB Bank relocates Anuradhapura City Branch
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, June 22, 2017 - 01:00

RDB Bank relocates Anuradhapura City Branch

RDB Bank relocated its Anuradapura City Branch to a more spaciously and a conveniently accessible location at Main Street, Anuradhapura in a bid to provide a better and modern service experience to customers in the area.
With the relocation of its branch, RDB renewed its genuine banking relationship with the individuals and the businessman in the area, opening its doors to the aspiring entrepreneurs to benefit from the banks many customized SME financing facilities and adversary services.
RDB Bank Chairman Prasanna Premaratne, Executive Director Priyantha Abeysinge, General Manager and CEO T.A. Ariyapala and District secretariat R.M. Wanninayaka, North Central Province Regional Manger U.B. Semasinghe, and the staff of the bank and large number of customers were participated to the re-location ceremony.
