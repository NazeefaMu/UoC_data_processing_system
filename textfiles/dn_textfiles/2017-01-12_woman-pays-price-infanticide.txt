## Title: Woman pays price to infanticide
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, January 12, 2017 - 01:00

Woman pays price to infanticide

 Kurunegala Provincial High Court Judge Menaka Wijesundare sentenced a woman, accused of infanticide, to two year rigorous imprisonment suspended for 10 years. She was fined Rs. 20,000 in default of six months imprisonment.
A woman of Polpithigama was indicted by the Attorney General with committing the murder of her day old infant in December 2009.
Meanwhile, two persons who pleaded guilty to possessing over 700 milligram of heroin, was sentenced to six months RI suspended for five years and fined Rs.10,000 by the Kurunegala High Court Judge on Wednesday.
