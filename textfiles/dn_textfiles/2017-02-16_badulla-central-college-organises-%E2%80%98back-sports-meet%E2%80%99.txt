## Title: Badulla Central College organises ‘Back to Sports Meet’
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, February 16, 2017 - 17:30

Badulla Central College organises ‘Back to Sports Meet’

 
The Colombo branch of the Past Students Association of the Badulla Central College held an event titled 'Back to Sports Meet' at the Pelawatta Buddhadasa Stadium.
The event was held with the intention of raising funds for development projects proposed for the Badulla Central College, 2017.
Cricket, netball and other athletic events were held at the ‘'Back to Sports Meet' event, with the four school teams - Mahaweli, Kelani, Walawe and Nilawala participating.
Badulla Central College PSA said the Mahaweli team won first place while Nilawala and Kelani had placed second and third.
