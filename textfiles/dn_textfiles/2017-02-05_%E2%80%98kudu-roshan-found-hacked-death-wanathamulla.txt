## Title: ‘Kudu Roshan' found hacked to death in Wanathamulla
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Sunday, February 5, 2017 - 13:45

‘Kudu Roshan' found hacked to death in Wanathamulla

 
Chamara Sandaruwan also known as ‘Kudu Roshan’ was found hacked to death near a playground in Wanathamulla.
Police are on the lookout for assailants.
‘Kudu Roshan’, a suspect drug smuggler was also wanted for several murders, Police said.
