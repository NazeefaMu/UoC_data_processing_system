## Date: Thursday, May 4, 2017 - 01:00

A A P 12th ANNIVERSARY

The 12th Anniversary of Academy for Administrative Professionals and Graduation Ceremony followed by the annual seminar workshop titled “Exceeding the Vision,” were held recently at the Kingsbury Hotel. .
Sunethra Jayaratne Nugawela, welcoming the distinguished gathering said that AAP is dynamic and diverse in bridging gaps of knowledge, trade skills, technology breakthroughs, networking, and most importantly keeping everyone happy and steady despite the winds of change blow across.
Chief Guest Rajendra Theagarajah delivered the Keynote address.
