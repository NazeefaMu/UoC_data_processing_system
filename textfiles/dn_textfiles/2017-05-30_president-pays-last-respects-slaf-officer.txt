## Title: President pays last respects to SLAF officer....
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, May 30, 2017 - 01:00

President pays last respects to SLAF officer....

President Maithripala Sirisena paid his last respects to SLAF’s Warrant Officer Y.M.Yaparathna, who died during a rescue operation in the flood-hit Neluwa area in Galle last Friday. The President who visited the late officer’s house at Kotugoda, Gampaha offered his deep condolences to the family members.
