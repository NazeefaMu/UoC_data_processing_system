## Title: ‘Believe in the Magic at Lake House’
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 22, 2017 - 01:00

‘Believe in the Magic at Lake House’

Chiristmas carols and Christmas festival organized by the the Associated Newspapers of Ceylon Ltd (ANCL) will begin today at 6.30 p.m in front of the Lake House premises.This programme will continue until December 24 daily from 6.30 pm to 10.30 pm.
The Army band will be featured today while the Army and Navy bands will be in attendance on December 23 followed by the Sri Lanka Air Force band on December 24.This is the first time that ANCL is organising a festival of this nature to mark Christmas and theme of this programme is 'Believe in the Magic at Lake House'. All are invited. For more details
