## Title: Gurukula have it easy
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, August 24, 2018 - 01:00

Gurukula have it easy

Gurukula Vidyalaya Kelaniya emerged as the Group leaders of the Second round and booked a berth in the pre quarter finals of the All Island under 17 division one cricket tournament conducted by Sri Lanka Schools Cricket Association when they trounced Richmond Collage Galle by eight wickets in he match worked off at Mahara.
Batting first formidable Richmondites scored 168 for 7 with Dimith sandaruwan unbeaten on 72 runs in reply Kelaniya lads notched up the required score for the loss of two wickets with a gallant knock of 93 runs by Praveen Nimesh.
Richmond Collage 168/7 in 50 overs (Dimuth Sandarawuan 72 n/o, Sanul Sandeesha 34, Gavin Nimesh 1/5.
Gurukula Vidyalaya 171/2 in 3.2 oves (Praveen Nimesh 93, Rashmika Mevan 39.
