## Title: Anupa misses century by one run
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, July 21, 2017 - 01:00

Anupa misses century by one run

The skipper of the under 15 division two cricket team of Prince of Wales College Anupa Heshan missed a well deserved century by a solitary run in their inter-school second round tournament cricket match played against Prince College, Ratnapura and worked off at Moratuwa. By virtue of this victory Cambrians are leading their group and qualified for the pre-quarters.
Batting first visitors were all out for 121 runs and in reply at stumps Cambrians were 333 for 9 with Shyma Gunarathne and Mahinsa de Silva scoring 66 runs and unbeaten 72 runs respectively. Thanusha Navin bowled claiming best for the visitors 3/37.
Chief Scores
Prince College – 121 in 43.2 overs (Lahiru Kasun 25, Diresh Chetana 20, Dinuka Fernando 4/48, Aditha Imasha 3/22)
Prince of Wales – 333/9 in 60 overs (Shyama Gunarathne 66, Anupa Heshan 99, Mahinsa de Silva 72 n/o, Shehan Rodrigo 29, Omesh Mendis 24, Lahiru Kasun 3/74, Thanusha Navin 3/37)
