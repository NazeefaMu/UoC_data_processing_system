## Title: Pizza Hut introduces  all new Pizzadilla
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, March 22, 2018 - 01:00

Pizza Hut introduces  all new Pizzadilla

Pizza Hut has once again released a new experience in taste to their much valued customers.
The Pizzadilla is an innovative new product from Pizza Hut, a delectable treat for one, with popular pizza flavours, encased in a crispy tortilla, baked to mouth-watering perfection.
Yasa Nadaraja, CEO of Gamma Pizzakraft Lanka (Pvt) Ltd., stated “When we invent a new product for our beloved fan base, we at Pizza Hut always think about what we could do different, to bring about a unique type of flavour that will add onto the already amazing experiences our customers have had with us. I am proud to say that the Pizzadilla is absolutely delicious, and it will prove to be a favourite among our extensive customer base.”
The Pizzadilla is available in seven delicious flavours starting at Rs. 500. Choose from Hot and Spicy Chicken, Tandoori Chicken, Sausage, Beef Pepperoni, Cheese, Paneer, and a special flavour for sweet lovers, Chocolate and Cream Cheese!
“We believe that the Pizzadilla is the perfect new product to start this year. It is absolutely an experience in deliciousness!” said Sharmila Arasaratnam, Director Marketing of Gamma Pizzakraft Lanka (Pvt) Ltd.
