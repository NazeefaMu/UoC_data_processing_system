## Title: Floods and landslides claim 203 lives; 96 missing and 63 injured
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 31, 2017 - 14:15

Floods and landslides claim 203 lives; 96 missing and 63 injured

The Disaster Management Centre (DMC) said that 203 people have died, 96 are missing and 63 are injured by the floods in Sri Lanka.
Around 631,346 people belonging to 164,264 families were affected in 15 districts, the DMC said.
It said further, that 77,643 people belonging to 19,784 families were located at 368 safe locations.
Cleaning process has begun in many areas.
