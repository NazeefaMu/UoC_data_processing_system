## Title: Illegal sand mining suspects arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, November 24, 2017 - 01:00

Illegal sand mining suspects arrested

Two persons and two tractors engaged in illegal sand mining at Sinnawatta in the Vellaveli Police area were arrested by the Kaluwanchikudy STF and the Vellaveli Police on a tip-off, said Police sources yesterday (22).
The Police said that they have been mining sand illegally for a very long period and the public had informed them which resulted in the Police officers raiding the place and seizing the tractors loaded with mined sand.
The suspects were taken in to custody for investigation and handed over to the Court.
