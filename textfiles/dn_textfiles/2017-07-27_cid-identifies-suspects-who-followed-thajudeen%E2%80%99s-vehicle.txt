## Title: CID identifies suspects who followed Thajudeen’s vehicle
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, July 27, 2017 - 17:45

CID identifies suspects who followed Thajudeen’s vehicle

 
The Criminal Investigation Department (CID) said today before the Colombo Magistrate Courts that the suspects captured in photographs while chasing after rugby player Wasim Thajudeen’s vehicle have been identified.
The investigative officers told the Colombo Additional Magistrate that the identified suspects will be arrested soon.
The case will be taken up again on August 24.
