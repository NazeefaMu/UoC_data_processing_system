## Title: Sri Lanka’s first NANO satellite into space in 2020
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, November 27, 2017 - 01:00

Sri Lanka’s first NANO satellite into space in 2020

The Science, Technology and Research Ministry planned to send Sri Lanka’s first NANO technology satellite into space in 2020.
Producing it will begin next year assisted by Russian scientists, Minister Susil Premajayantha said.
The Minister was addressing the media after participating in an awards ceremony jointly organised by the Peradeniya University and the Nan Yang University of Singapore at the Amaya Hotel in Kandy as its Chief Guest recently.
Minister Premajayantha said he observed students of the Samara University in Russia carrying out various experiments using NANO technology during an official tour he made there recently. The satellite would help Sri Lanka to obtain vital data relating to meteorology and several other fields.
The engineers from the Peradeniya University would involved in the project, the Minister said.
