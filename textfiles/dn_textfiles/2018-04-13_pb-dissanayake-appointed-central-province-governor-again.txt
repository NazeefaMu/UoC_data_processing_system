## Title: P.B. Dissanayake appointed Central Province Governor again
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, April 13, 2018 - 12:17

P.B. Dissanayake appointed Central Province Governor again

 
Uva Province Governor P.B. Dissanayake took oaths before President Maithripala Sirisena again as the Central Province Governor this morning.
He will also function as the Acting Governor of the Uva Province.
