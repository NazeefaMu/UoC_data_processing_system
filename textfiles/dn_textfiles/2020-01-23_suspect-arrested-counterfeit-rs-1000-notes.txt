## Title: Suspect arrested with counterfeit Rs 1000 notes
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, January 23, 2020 - 10:11

Suspect arrested with counterfeit Rs 1000 notes

A person has been arrested with nine counterfeit notes of the Rs. 1000 in his possession, by the Police at Wellawa. The suspect was arrested in a raid carried by the Police on a tip-off received.The suspect will be handed over to the Criminal Investigations Department for further investigations.
