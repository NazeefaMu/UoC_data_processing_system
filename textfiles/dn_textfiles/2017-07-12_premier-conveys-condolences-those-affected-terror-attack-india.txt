## Title: Premier conveys condolences to those affected by terror attack in India
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, July 12, 2017 - 01:00

Premier conveys condolences to those affected by terror attack in India

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe conveyed his condolences yesterday to the families affected by the terrorist attack on the Amarnath pilgrims in India.
A terrorist attack on a yari bus carrying pilgrims returning after visiting the Amarnath shrine killed seven individuals and injured three Indian Police officers on Monday night. The incident took place at Botengo village near Anantnag district on the Srinagar-Jammu National Highway in South Kashmir.
