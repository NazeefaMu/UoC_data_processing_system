## Title: Navy assists Kochchikade St. Anthony’s Church feast mass
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, June 16, 2018 - 01:00

Navy assists Kochchikade St. Anthony’s Church feast mass

The annual feast mass of St. Anthony’s Church in Kochchikade was held on June 13 with the participation of a large number of Catholic devotees across the island.
The holy mass was presided over by Archbishop of Colombo Malcolm Cardinal Ranjith.
Naval personnel attached to the Western Naval Command provided amenities for the event.
