## Title: Hunt for suspects over woman’s death
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, November 5, 2018 - 01:00

Hunt for suspects over woman’s death

Kalutara North Police launched an investigation to arrest those responsible for the death of an 80-year-old woman in Mahawaskaduwa, Waskaduwa, on Saturday.
According to police, the woman’s husband had gone to Colombo on Saturday for medical treatment and the body of the victim who had sustained injuries, was found in her home.
The victim was identified Walimuni Edwin Amitha Mendis of Mahawaskaduwa.The postmortem was to be carried out at the Government Hospital in Nagoda, Kalutara. Kalutara North Police are investigating.
