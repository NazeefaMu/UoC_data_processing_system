## Title: Hikkaduwa new bus stand opened
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, December 24, 2018 - 01:00

Hikkaduwa new bus stand opened

Southern Provincial Council (SPC) Chief Minister and Provincial Transport Minister Shan Vijayalal de Silva vested a new bus stand with the public. It was constructed at a cost of Rs. 200 Lakhs in Hikkaduwa recently.
Councillor Amarasiri Kuruwage, Hikkaduwa UC Chairman Vinnie Kariyawasam and Rathgama Pradeshiya Sabha Chairman T. Nimal were present.
