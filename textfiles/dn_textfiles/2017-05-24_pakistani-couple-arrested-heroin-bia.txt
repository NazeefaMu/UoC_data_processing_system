## Title: Pakistani couple arrested with  heroin at BIA
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 24, 2017 - 17:30

Pakistani couple arrested with  heroin at BIA

 
The Airport Police Narcotic Bureau (PNB) arrested a Pakistani couple for possessing heroin at the Bandaranaike International Airport this evening.
Police said there were 4 kg of heroin, worth over Rs.40 million during the arrest.
