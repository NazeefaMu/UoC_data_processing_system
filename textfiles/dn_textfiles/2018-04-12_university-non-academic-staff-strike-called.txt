## Title: University non academic staff strike called off
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 12, 2018 - 16:20

University non academic staff strike called off

 
The strike launched by the non academic staff of universities was called off a short while ago.
The decision was made after holding talks with the Higher Education and Highways Minister Kabir Hashim.
 
University Trade University Joint Committee Co-President Edward Malwattage said that all members will be notified to report back to work on April 17, ending the over month long strike. 
 
He added that the strike was called off after the Minister agreed to meet several demands.
