## Title: Man arrested for forging identity documents to sit A/L paper
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 30, 2017 - 10:30

Man arrested for forging identity documents to sit A/L paper

 
A thirty-two-year-old man was arrested by the Ugahana Police yesterday for forging identity documents to sit the Advanced Level Mathematics paper II.
The Police made the arrest after receiving a complaint from the Chief Invigilator of the Ugahana Examination Centre.
The suspect is a resident of Thekkawatta, Maha Oya.
He will be produced before the Ampara Magistrate Courts today.
Ugahana Police is investigating.
