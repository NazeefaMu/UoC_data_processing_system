## Title: Gambling den raided
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, September 29, 2018 - 01:00

Gambling den raided

Ambalantota police raided a gambling den at Diyawara in Ambalantota and arrested two women and six youths. A woman has managed the gambling den and she was also arrested. Mostly women were used to gamble at the location, police said. Several card packs and Rs. 16,290 were seized. Ambalantota OIC Jagath Wijegunaratnaanda led the raid.
