## Title: Ayurvedic dispensary opens
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 01:00

Ayurvedic dispensary opens

 The newly constructed Central Ayurvedic Dispensary at Pottuvil in the Ampara district was ceremonially opened, recently.
It was built at a cost of Rs. 5.5 million funded by the Eastern Province Health Ministry.
Eastern Province Health, Indigenous Medicine, Probation and Childcare, Social Welfare and Rural Electrification Minister A. L. M. Nazeer was the chief guest at the opening ceremony.
The government spends colossal sums for health and indigenous medicine development projects aiming to uplift the living condition of the people.
“It is the duty of the people to extent their fullest support to development projects carried out by the government,” said Eastern Province Health, Indigenous Medicine, Probation and Childcare, Social Welfare and Rural Electrification Minister A. L. M. Nazeer. Former Pottuvil Pradeshiya Sabha Chairman M. S. A. Washeed, M. S. M. Marzook, former Pottuvil Pradeshiya Sabha members, Eastern Province Ayurvedic Department Director Dr. R. Srithar and Health Department officials were also present.
