## Title: Sangabodhi in first innings win
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, November 7, 2015 - 01:00

Sangabodhi in first innings win

 
Sangabodhi Vidyalaya, Nittambuwa recorded a first innings victory over Kirillawala Central College in their 'Singer Cup' Under 13 Division III Cricket Match played at Hiripitiya Ground, Wathurugama recently.
Kirillawala Central - 140 all out in 45.1 overs (M. Tevidu 61, Kemal 3 for 9).
Sangabodhi - 161 for 4 wickets in 43 overs (K. Umayanga 57, B. Mathisha 63, M. Faizal 2 for 13)
