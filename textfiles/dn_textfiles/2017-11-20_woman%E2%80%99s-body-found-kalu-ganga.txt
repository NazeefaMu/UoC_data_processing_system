## Title: Woman’s body found in Kalu Ganga
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, November 20, 2017 - 01:00

Woman’s body found in Kalu Ganga

An unidentified body of a woman aged around 30 was found floating in Kalu Ganga near the Narthupana Bridge by Anguruwatota police on Saturday (18).
The body is at the Horana Hospital mortuary for identification.
