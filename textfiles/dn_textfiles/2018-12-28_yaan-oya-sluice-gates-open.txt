## Title: Yaan Oya sluice gates to open
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 28, 2018 - 09:14

Yaan Oya sluice gates to open

The Disaster Management Centre stated,the sluice gates of the Yaan Oya are scheduled to be opened today morning.The DMC cautions the people who reside downstream to be vigilant in this regard.It was reported that the water level of Yaan Oya reservoir has reached 36m as Hurulu Wewa reservoir is currently spilling over.
