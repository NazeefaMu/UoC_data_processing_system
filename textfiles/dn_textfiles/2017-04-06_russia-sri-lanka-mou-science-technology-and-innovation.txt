## Title: Russia-Sri Lanka MoU on Science Technology and Innovation
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 6, 2017 - 01:00

Russia-Sri Lanka MoU on Science Technology and Innovation

A Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) on Russia-Sri Lanka Bi-lateral Cooperation in Science, Technology, and Innovation was signed by Minister of Science, Technology, and Research of Sri Lanka Susil Premajayantha and Russian Minister of Education and Science Olga Vasilyeva.
The MoU was signed between the two ministers during the recent state visit of the President to the Russian Federation.
The MoU provides for broader bi-lateral cooperation in Science, Technology, and Innovation, which includes development of joint scientific research centres, conduct of joint research activities, exchange of specialists and scientists, as well as development of direct inter-agency collaborative arrangements between science and technology organizations of the two countries on the basis of mutual agreements reached by them.
