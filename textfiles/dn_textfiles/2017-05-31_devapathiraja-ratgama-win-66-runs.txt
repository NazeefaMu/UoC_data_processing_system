## Title: Devapathiraja, Ratgama win by 66 runs
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, May 31, 2017 - 01:00

Devapathiraja, Ratgama win by 66 runs

Devpathiraja College Ratgama , beat P. De S. Kularathne MV Ambalangoda by 66 runs in the first round match of the ‘Singer Trophy’ Under 17 division 1 inter schools cricket tournament played at Ratgama esplanade on yesterday.
Devapathiraja: 149 in 33.2 overs (Thisara Dilshan 30, Kavindu Madhusanka 20, Tashen Dilakshana 19, Pasindu Udayangana 4/27, Shakith Yuraj 3/38, Kumindu Nilhara 2/33)
P. De S. Kularathne: 83 in 35 overs (Dhanuja Lakmal 20, Pethum Madhusanka 3/11, Chamika Sandaruwan 3/11, Tashen Dilakshana 2/28) (YK)
