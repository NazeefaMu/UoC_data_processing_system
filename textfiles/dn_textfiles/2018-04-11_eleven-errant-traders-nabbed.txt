## Title: Eleven errant traders nabbed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, April 11, 2018 - 01:00

Eleven errant traders nabbed

Public Health Inspectors (PHIs) arrested 11 errant traders who were selling food unfit for human consumption, through raids conducted in the Kalutara and Matugama areas.
Kalutara District Health official P. D. Lional, along with Public Health Inspectors in the Palindanuwara, Agalawatte and Bulathsinhala areas, conducted the raids. The suspects were to be produced before the Matugama Magistrate's Court.
