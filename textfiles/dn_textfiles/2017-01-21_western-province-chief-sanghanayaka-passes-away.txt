## Title: Western Province Chief Sanghanayaka passes away
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, January 21, 2017 - 01:00

Western Province Chief Sanghanayaka passes away

The Chief Incumbent of Shantharamaya and Chief Sanghanayake of the Western Province Ven. Karagampitiye Shanthananda Thera passed away.
The Thera was an executive member of the Sri Kalyani Wansika Nikaya. The cremation will take place at the Mount Lavinia General Cemetery tomorrow at 3 p.m.
