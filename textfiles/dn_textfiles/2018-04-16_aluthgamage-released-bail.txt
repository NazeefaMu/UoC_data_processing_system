## Title: Aluthgamage released on bail
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, April 16, 2018 - 15:48

Aluthgamage released on bail

Former Sports Minister Mahindananda Aluthgamage who was earlier arrested by the Financial Crimes Investigation Division  (FCID) was released on bail a short while ago.
The former Minister was orderes to be arrested by the Colombo Fort Magistrate, after recording a statement.
Aluthgamage is alleged to have missapropriated state funds during the last Presidential Elections while importing sports equipment.
