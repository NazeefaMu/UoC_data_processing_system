## Title: State Education Minister in accident
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 21, 2017 - 01:00

State Education Minister in accident

State Education Minister V. Radhakrishnan was in Batticaloa to participate at the third International Yoga Day observances at the Weber Stadium yesday. It was organized by the Batticaloa Yoga Health Youth Society yesterday. The minister later visited the Kamadchiyamman Kovil in Mylambaveli and on his return from the temple to Batticaloa, the vehicle he was travelling in collided with one of his security vehicles at Thannamunai along the Batticaloa – Trincomalee highway in the Eravur Police area.
Fortunately, neither the Minister nor the security personnel were injured. Eravur Police are investigating.
