## Title: Crawford to direct The Croods 2
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Tuesday, October 24, 2017 - 01:00

Crawford to direct The Croods 2

The animated per-historic family has finally found its director.Reportedly, Joel Crawford, who most recently served as head of story on ‘Trolls’, will make his feature directorial debut on DreamWorks Animation’s ‘The Croods 2’.
Mark Swift is lined up to produce the movie.
Nicolas Cage, Emma Stone and Ryan Reynolds lead the returning voice cast, as the Croods face their biggest threat since leaving the cave: another family.
Additional cast members include Catherine Keener, Cloris Leachman and Clark Duke, as well as Leslie Mann, who is joining the cast.
The sequel is a follow-up to the studio’s 2013 Academy Award-nominated caveman comedy, which garnered USD 587.2 million at the global box office. ANI
