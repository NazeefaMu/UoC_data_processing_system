## Title: PM at the maliyadewa Girls collage prize giving
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 27, 2018 - 01:00

PM at the maliyadewa Girls collage prize giving

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe opened the newly constructed three-storeyed building in Maliyadeva Girls’ College, Kurunegala when he participated in the annual Prize Giving ceremony of the school as the Chief Guest yesterday. Education Minister Akila Viraj Kariyawasam was also present. Picture by the Prime Minister’s Media Unit
