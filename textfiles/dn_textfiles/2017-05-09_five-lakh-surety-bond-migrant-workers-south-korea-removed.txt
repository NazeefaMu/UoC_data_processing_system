## Title: Five-lakh surety bond on migrant workers to South Korea removed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 13:45

Five-lakh surety bond on migrant workers to South Korea removed

 
Foreign Employment Minister Thalatha Athukorala suspended the surety bond which was due to be imposed on Sri Lankan migrant workers in South Korea, today.
