## Title: Construction sector discussion at the President’s House
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, April 11, 2018 - 01:11

Construction sector discussion at the President’s House

A discussion was held under the chairmanship of President Maithripala Sirisena to identify issues concerning the construction sector at the President’s House yesterday. Housing Minister Sajith Premadasa, President’s Secretary Austin Fernando and Finance Ministry Secretary Dr. R.H.S. Samarathunga and senior officials participated at the discussion.Picture by President’s Media Division.
