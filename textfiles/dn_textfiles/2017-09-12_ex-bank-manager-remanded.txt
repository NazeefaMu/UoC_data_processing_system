## Title: EX-Bank Manager remanded
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, September 11, 2017 - 23:00

EX-Bank Manager remanded

A former manager of a state bank in Batapola arrested by the CID in connection with the alleged shortage of a sum of Rs. 26.5 million from the bank’s accounts was remanded till September 20 when he was produced before Balapitiya Magistrate Rajindra Jayasuriya by the Ambalangoda Police on September 8.
The former manager who was in hiding, was arrested by the CID which investigated a shortage of Rs. 26.5 million from the bank branch and handed the suspect over to the Ambalangoda Police for legal action.
