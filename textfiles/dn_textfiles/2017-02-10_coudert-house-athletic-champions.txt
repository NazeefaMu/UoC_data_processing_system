## Title: Coudert House athletic champions
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, February 10, 2017 - 01:00

Coudert House athletic champions

The Inter-House sports meet 2017 of Ave Maria Convent, Negombo was held at the school grounds at which major D C N Bombuwala (Sri Lanka Army Women's Corps. was the chief guest. Coudert House with 851 points emerged champions. Picture shows Coudert House Cpt. Shevodi Perera receiving challenge shield from the chief guest. Also in the picture are Rev. Sr. Shermila Jayawardena, Principal, Mrs. Thushari Perera, House Mistress, Mrs Anusha Victoria, Deputy House Mistress.
