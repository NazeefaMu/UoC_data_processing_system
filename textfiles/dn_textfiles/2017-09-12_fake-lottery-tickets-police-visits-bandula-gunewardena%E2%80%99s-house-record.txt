## Title: Fake lottery tickets: Police visits Bandula Gunewardena’s house to record statement
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, September 12, 2017 - 17:15

Fake lottery tickets: Police visits Bandula Gunewardena’s house to record statement

 
The Police visited MP Bandula Gunewardena’s house to record a statement on remarks he made regarding printing fake lottery tickets, today.  
Gunawardena publicly announced during a recent press conference that fake lottery tickets are being sold by National Lotteries Board (NLB) agents.
