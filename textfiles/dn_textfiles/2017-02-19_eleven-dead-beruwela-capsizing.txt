## Title: Eleven dead in Beruwela capsizing
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Sunday, February 19, 2017 - 16:30

Eleven dead in Beruwela capsizing

(File Photo)
The death toll from the incident in which a boat capsized in the seas of Beruwala has increased to 11, Police said.
Earlier today, eight people were reported drowned in the incident.
Several boats from the Navy and an Air force helicopter was deployed to carry out search missions.
