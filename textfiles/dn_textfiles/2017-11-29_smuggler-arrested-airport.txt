## Title: Smuggler arrested at airport
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, November 29, 2017 - 01:00

Smuggler arrested at airport

A Sri Lankan passenger trying to smuggle Rs. 2,320,000, was apprehended by customs officers.
The man, 46, a resident of Colombo, was arrested before boarding flight FZ 558 to Dubai at 2.14 am, yesterday morning. The suspect was released on a penalty of Rs 100,000, by Customs Deputy Director Jagath Obesekara.
