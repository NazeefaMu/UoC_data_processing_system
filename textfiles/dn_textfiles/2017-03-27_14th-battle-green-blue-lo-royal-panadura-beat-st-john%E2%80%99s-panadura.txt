## Title: 14th Battle of Green & Blue L/O : Royal Panadura beat St. John’s Panadura
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, March 27, 2017 - 01:00

14th Battle of Green & Blue L/O : Royal Panadura beat St. John’s Panadura

Royal College Panadura beat St. John’s College Panadura by four wickets in their Battle of Green & Blue annual limited overs cricket encounter played at the Panadura esplanade yesterday.
Scores:
St. John’s Panadura: 200 in 43.3 overs (Sithum Pramod 60, Sakun Rukshan 32, Eran Hansamal 3/26)
Royal Panadura: 201/6 in 44.1 overs (Sachin Aminda 46, Nilan Fernando 45, Gihan Anjana 2/25) - YK
