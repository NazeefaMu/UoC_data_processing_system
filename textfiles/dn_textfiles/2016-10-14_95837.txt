## Title: Matara Super League T20 q’finals today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, October 14, 2016 - 01:00

Matara Super League T20 q’finals today

 
The quarterfinals of the inaugural Matara Super League T20 cricket tournament conducted by the Matara District Cricket Association and Sports Ministry of Southern Province will be played at the Uyanwatte Stadium Matara today.
Matara Stars, Team Maroons, Team Gold, Ruhunu Fighters, Team Green, Weligama Attackers, Sportec Blasters and Thomian Youngsters have advanced to the quarterfinals. The quarterfinals will be played on October 13 and 14, semi-finals on October 15 and the final October 16. - YK
