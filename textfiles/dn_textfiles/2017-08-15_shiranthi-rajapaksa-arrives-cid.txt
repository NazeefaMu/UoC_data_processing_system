## Title: Shiranthi Rajapaksa arrives at the CID
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, August 15, 2017 - 10:30

Shiranthi Rajapaksa arrives at the CID

 
Former President Mahinda Rajapaksa’s wife, Shiranthi Rajapaksa arrived before the Criminal Investigation Division (CID) a short while ago.
The CID summoned the former first lady to record a statement regarding the jeep used to abduct the murdered rugby player Wasim Thajudeen.
The jeep donated by the Red Cross was owned by the Siriliya Saviya Foundation which functioned under Shiranthi Rajapaksa.
