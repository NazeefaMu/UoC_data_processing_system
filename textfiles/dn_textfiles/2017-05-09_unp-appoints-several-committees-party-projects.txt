## Title: UNP appoints several committees for party projects
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 01:00

UNP appoints several committees for party projects

The United National Party(UNP) Working Committee appointed several committees yesterday to implement projects for the development of the Party.
Party sources said the UNP Message Committee and TV Debate Committee were among them.
The sources also revealed that the UNP Message Committee consists of Foreign Affairs Minister Mangala Samaraweera, former Minister Imitiasz Bakeer Markar and Krishantha Cooray while the TV Debate Committee consists of International Trade State Minister Sujeewa Senasinghe, Power and Renewable Energy Deputy Minister Ajith P.Perera and Foreign Affairs Deputy Minister Harsha De Silva.
