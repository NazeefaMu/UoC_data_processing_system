## Title: Lanka Bell bill payments via eZ Cash
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Friday, February 17, 2017 - 01:00

Lanka Bell bill payments via eZ Cash

Sri Lanka’s pioneering mobile money network, eZ Cash, is partnering with Lanka Bell for bill payments. eZ Cash customers can now make Lanka Bell bill payments using eZ Cash through their mobile phones at over 20,000 eZ Cash merchant outlets islandwide.
