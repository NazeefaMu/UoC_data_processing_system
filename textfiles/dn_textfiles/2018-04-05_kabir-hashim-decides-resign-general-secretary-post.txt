## Title: Kabir Hashim decides to resign from General Secretary post
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 5, 2018 - 09:13

Kabir Hashim decides to resign from General Secretary post

Higher Education and Highways Minister Kabir Hashim said that he has decided to resign from his post as the United National Party (UNP) General Secretary a short while ago.
He said that the decision was made in order to avail his position to a new office bearer who will be appointed when restructuring the Party.
