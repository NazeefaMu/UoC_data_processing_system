## Title: Chief incumbent of Kataragama Kiriwehera shot at Temple premises
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 13, 2018 - 07:35

Chief incumbent of Kataragama Kiriwehera shot at Temple premises

 
An unidentified gunman open firied and injured Chief incumbent of Kataragama Kiriwehera  Venerable Kobawaka Damminda Thera and another Thero at the Temple premises.
Further investigations are underway.
