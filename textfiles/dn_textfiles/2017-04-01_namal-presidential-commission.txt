## Title: Namal before Presidential Commission
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, April 1, 2017 - 01:00

Namal before Presidential Commission

The Presidential Commission investigating into mass scale corruption and financial malpractices yesterday, recorded a statement from Hambantota District Parliamentarian Namal Rajapaksa over spending Rs. 1.7 million for the provision of training to youths attached to the Nil Balakaya.
Rajapaksa arrived at the Commission yesterday at 10.30 am and the Commission officials recorded a statement on how he found the money to spend for the training of these youths.
The Commission has initiated investigations on complaint that public funds amounting to Rs. 1.7 million had been misappropriated by providing training to these youths.
