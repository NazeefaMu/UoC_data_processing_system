## Title: Rodrigo, Fernando hit tons for Lankan CC
## Supertitle: Premier League Inter-Club Cricket – Tier b:
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, January 20, 2018 - 01:00

Premier League Inter-Club Cricket – Tier b:
Rodrigo, Fernando hit tons for Lankan CC

Lakshan Rodrigo and Shasheen Fernando made centuries for Lankan CC in their SLC Premier League Tier ‘B’ Inter-Club cricket match against Kalutara TC at Maggona yesterday. Rodrigo scored 121 off 75 deliveries with 21 fours and a six and Fernando 122 off 181 balls with 17 fours and a six. The pair put on 187 runs stand for the first wicket.
Matches will continue on the second day today.
