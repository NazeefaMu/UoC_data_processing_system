## Title: Spices price up
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 1, 2017 - 01:00

Spices price up

Th prices of pepper, cinnamon and ginger have shot up considerably, sources at the Southern Provincial Agriculture Department said.
A kilo of cinnamon fetches Rs.2,760, cinnamon oil per kilo is Rs. 2,500, a kilo of pepper fetches Rs. 1,000 and a kilo of ginger fetches Rs. 900.
