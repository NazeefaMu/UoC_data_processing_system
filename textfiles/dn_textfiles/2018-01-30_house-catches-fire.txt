## Title: House catches fire
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, January 30, 2018 - 01:00

House catches fire

A fire erupted in a temporary house around 9 am yesterday spread to an adjoining house under construction. The temporary house was gutted. No one was injured. Eight persons of the two families have been displaced. Hatton police are investigating. (Picture by Chandana Jayaweera)
