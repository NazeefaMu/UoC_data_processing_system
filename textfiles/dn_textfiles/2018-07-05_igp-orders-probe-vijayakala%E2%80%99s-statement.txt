## Title: IGP orders probe into Vijayakala’s statement
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, July 5, 2018 - 01:00

IGP orders probe into Vijayakala’s statement

IGP Pujith Jayasundera has directed Senior DIG in Charge of Crimes, to launch an investigation into the complaints against State Minister Vijayakala Maheswaran in connection with the speech on the LTTE.
Police media sources said several complaints had been received on Tuesday at the Police Headquarters against Vijayakala Maheswaran’s speech.
