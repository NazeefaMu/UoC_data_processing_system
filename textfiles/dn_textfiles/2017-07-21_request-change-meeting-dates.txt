## Title: Request to change meeting dates
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, July 21, 2017 - 01:00

Request to change meeting dates

Eastern Provincial Council (EPC) Opposition Leader M. S. Udumalebbe has sent a letter to President’s Secretary Austin Fernando requesting him to stop holding Divisional Coordinative Committee meetings on the days of EPC sittings.
The letter stated that the EPC sittings would take place on July 18, and a special meeting has been arranged with the EPC officials on July 17. Meanwhile, arrangements have also been made to hold the Divisional Coordinating Committee meetings for Addalaichenai, Akkaraipattu, Nintavur and Pottuvil divisions on the same days.
The President has appointed Health Deputy Minister Faizal Cassim and Udumalebbe as Co-chairman of the Divisional Coordinating Committee meeting of Akkaraipattu, Addalaichenai and Nintavur.
