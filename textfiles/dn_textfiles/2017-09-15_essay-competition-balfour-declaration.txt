## Title: Essay competition  on Balfour Declaration
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, September 15, 2017 - 01:00

Essay competition  on Balfour Declaration

The Sri Lanka Palestine Solidarity Committee together with the Embassy of Palestine in Sri Lanka, would be holding an essay competition to mark 100 years of the infamous Balfour Declaration that changed the destiny of the Palestinian people, the region and the world.
While the topic of the essay is The Balfour Declaration 100 years on: The quest for Justice in Palestine, the essay could be written in Sinhala, Tamil or English.
The word count should be around 1,500 words. The essay should be an analysis on the Palestinian people’s right for justice and freedom and cover questions such as:
Britain’s right to offer the Palestinian people’s land to European Zionists. The reasons behind this illegal declaration issued in November 1917; (Before the start of UK mandate on Palestine). The winners in each language stream would receive cash prizes of Rs. 60,000, Rs. 40,000 and Rs. 20,000, while Certificates will be issued to all participants.
