## Title: Wanted suspect nabbed in Hanwella
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, December 24, 2018 - 01:00

Wanted suspect nabbed in Hanwella

Police have taken into custody a supporter of underworld king-ping Urujuwa, who is presently in prison yesterday under the charges of demanding ransom from businessmen in the Hanwella area and suburbs.
The suspect Sisira Kumara (41) alias Boola was taken into custody along with a sword and two knives in the Embuldeniya area by Organised Crime Control Division officers last morning.
Police said the suspect has been demanding ransom making death threats to businessmen in the area.Owing to his threats several businessmen, who did not want to pay ransom had closed down their business entities. He will be produced before the Homagama Magistrate’s court today.
