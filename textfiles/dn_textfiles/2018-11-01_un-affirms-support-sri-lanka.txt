## Title: UN affirms support for Sri Lanka
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 1, 2018 - 01:08

UN affirms support for Sri Lanka

United Nations Resident Representative in Sri Lanka Hanaa Singer said the United Nations will lend any support to Sri Lanka in future as before.
She gave this assurance when she called on President Maithripala Sirisena at the Presidential Secretariat yesterday morning. During discussions, the President briefed Singer about the political situation in Sri Lanka and emphasised that the Government had taken all actions in accordance with the constitution.
A press release by the President’s Media Division said the President explained that the government is acting within the democratic structure, while maintaining law and order in the country.
The President also appreciated the support the UN gave Sri Lanka at all times.Singer in reply said the United Nations is prepared to provide any support to Sri Lanka in the future.
