## Title: Veteran journalist Sunil Madhawa Premathilake
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, March 18, 2017 - 01:00

Veteran journalist Sunil Madhawa Premathilake

Media and Parliament Reforms Minister Gayantha Krunathilaka yesterday visited veteran journalist Sunil Madhawa Premathilake at his Koswatte residence to inquire his health and wished him speedy recovery. The Minister provided financial assistance sponsored by the Lake House to Premathilake.
