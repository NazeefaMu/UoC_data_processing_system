## Title: Arrest warrant on Ryan Jayalath and four others
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, August 10, 2017 - 11:45

Arrest warrant on Ryan Jayalath and four others

 
Arrest warrants have been issued on the Convener of the Medical Faculty Students Action Committee, Ryan Jayalath and 4 others for not being present in Court.
Ryan Jayalath was issued an arrest warrant for forcibly entering the Ministry of Health on June 21 and for damaging public property.
