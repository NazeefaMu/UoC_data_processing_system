## Title: HC to consider bail chances
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Wednesday, July 12, 2017 - 01:00

HC to consider bail chances

The Colombo High Court yesterday fixed for August 4 to inquire about the possibilities of releasing on bail six suspects including former DIG Vass Gunawardena and his son Ravindu Gunawardena who had been remanded for the alleged possession of firearms.
Six suspects - Former DIG Vaas Gunawardena and his son Ravindu Vaas Gunawardena, Sub Inspector Indika Bamunuge, Police Constable (PC) Gamini Sarath Chandra, PC Priyanka Sanjeewa and PC Kelum Ranga were sentenced to death by Colombo High Court for committing the murder of millionaire businessman Mohamed Shyam in 2013 at Dompe.
Counsel Vishwa de Livera Tennakoon appearing on behalf of the suspects had sought bail through a revision application.
Senior State Counsel Lakmini Girihagama appeared fort the Attorney General.
