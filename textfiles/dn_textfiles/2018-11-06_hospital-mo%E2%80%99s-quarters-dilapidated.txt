## Title: Hospital MO’s quarters dilapidated
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, November 6, 2018 - 01:00

Hospital MO’s quarters dilapidated

The Rasnayakapura Hospital medical officer’s (MO’s) quarters was dilapidated, the hospital’s development committee told the Daily News, last Sunday.
Around 33,000 residents in Rasnayakapura’s 28 Grama Niladhari divisions depend on the hospital, they said, urging authorities to resolve the issue immediately.
