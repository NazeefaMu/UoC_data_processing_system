## Title: Science College emerges Cup champs
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, July 11, 2017 - 01:00

Science College emerges Cup champs

Science College emerged Champs at the schools U-18 Cup final of Sri Lanka’s premier sevens rugby tourney, Sri Lanka Super 7’s 2017 powered by Dialog at the Nittawela Grounds, Kandy recently. 
Asanga Seneviratne-President, Sri Lanka Rugby presenting the Plate Championship award to the joint winners,captains of St. Peter’s College, Colombo & Kingswood College, Kandy.
