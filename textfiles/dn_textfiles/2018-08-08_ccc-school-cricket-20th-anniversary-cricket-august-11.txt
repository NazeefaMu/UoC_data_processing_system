## Title: CCC School of Cricket 20th anniversary cricket from August 11
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, August 8, 2018 - 01:00

CCC School of Cricket 20th anniversary cricket from August 11

The premier Cricket Coaching School in Sri Lanka, C C C School of Cricket, founded by veteran coach Nelson Mendis, will celebrate it’s twentieth anniversary with the annual Inter House Cricket Tournament on August 11 and 12 at C C C grounds, Maitland Crescent, Colombo.
The popular Annual Inter - House Cricket Tournament will feature seven (07) age groups :- U–9, U-10, U-11, U-12, U-13, U-14 and over 14 with over 250 students taking the field to test their skills and expertise learnt over the years.
The four competing houses are named after cricketing legend - Don Brdman, Gary Sobers, Sachin Tendulkar and Mahela Jayawardena. Last year Tendulkar House won the Championship beating Jayawardena House in final.
Former Sri Lankan Test Cricketer and stylish opening batsman Malintha Warnapura will be the chief guest on the final day, who presently guiding the first XI cricketers of St. Peters’ College, Colombo.
