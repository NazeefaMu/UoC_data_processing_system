## Title: JO to hold rally in Trinco
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Saturday, July 1, 2017 - 01:00

JO to hold rally in Trinco

The ' Joint Opposition' is to hold a rally in Trincomalee against the sale of the Oil Tanks and Port, with the participation of former President Mahinda Rajapaksa.
Addressing a media briefing, MP Dallas Alahapperuma said the event is scheduled to be held on July 3rd, with the participation of leaders, civil society groups and political parties. Speaking at the briefing held at the Abayaramaya, in Narahenpita on behalf of the National Asset Protecting Centre, Alahapperuma said they will mobilise forces against the sale of national assets and the rising cost of living.
