## Title: Lankan ruggerites leave for Malaysia on Thursday
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 01:00

Lankan ruggerites leave for Malaysia on Thursday

Sri Lankan ruggerites led by Roshan Weeraratne will leave on Thursday May 11 to take part in the 2017 Asia Rugby Championship division I tournament which will take place in Ipoh, Malaysia.
Sri Lanka will be playing along with Malaysia, Philippines, United Arab Emirates. This edition of the tournament is as part of qualification for the 2019 Rugby World Cup. The Division 2 teams are from Singapore, Chinese Taipei, India and Thailand. The Division 2 tournament will be held at Taipei Municipal Stadium in Taipei in November. The Division 3 tournament was held in March in Tashkent, Uzbekistan at which Lebanon were placed first with Uzbekistan and Iran taking 2nd and 3rd places.
Division I matches will kick off from 14 May with two games - Sri Lanka v Philippines and Malaysia v United Arab Emirates, 17 May - Sri Lanka v United Arab Emirates, Malaysia v Philippines, 20 May - Philippines v United Arab Emirates, Malaysia v Sri Lanka.
The Sri Lanka squad: Roshan Weeraratne (Captain), Fazil Marija, Danushka Ranjan, Lavanga Perera, Richard Dharmapala, Jason Dissanayake, Thilina Wijesinghe, Ganuka Dissanayake and Sayuru Anthony (all Kandy SC), Dushmantha Priyadarshana, Sharo Fernando, Prasath Madusanka, Umesh Madhushan, Ashan Darling, Nishon Perera, Niroshan Fernando (all Havelock SC), Sajith Saranga, Sathya Ranatunga, Chanaka Chandimal, Lee Keegel and Richie Dharmapala (all Navy SC), Omalka Gunaratne, Tariq Salih (both CR & FC), Asoka Jayalal (Army SC), Joel Perera (Police SC), Head Coach – Fereti Verebula, High Performance Director - Inthi Marikar, Trainer – Nick Golbe Manager – Rohan Chinthaka.
