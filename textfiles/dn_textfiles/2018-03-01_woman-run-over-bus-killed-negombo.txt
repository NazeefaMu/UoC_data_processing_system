## Title: Woman run over by bus killed in Negombo
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, March 1, 2018 - 15:55

Woman run over by bus killed in Negombo

 
A bus plying from Katugoda to Negombo ran over   a woman  while she was crossing the road in Negombo this morning. She succumbed to injuries after being rushed to the Negombo Hospital.
The deceased 38-year-old woman identified as Jayaweerage Renu Shyamalika was a resident of Pamunugama.
The bus driver is due to be produced before the Negombo Magistrate.
Negomobo Police is conducting further investigations.
