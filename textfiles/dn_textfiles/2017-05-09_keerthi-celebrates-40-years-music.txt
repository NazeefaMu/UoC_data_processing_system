## Title: Keerthi celebrates 40 years in music
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 01:00

Keerthi celebrates 40 years in music

Renowned musician Keerthi Pasquel will be celebrating 40 years in music in 2017. A concert titled ‘Keerthi Gee Evolution of Music’ will be held at the BMICH on May 20 at 7 pm to celebrate the event. The concert will also feature musicians like Annesley Malawana, Eranda Fernando, Dhammika Bandara, Samitha Mudunkotuwa, Nirosha Virajini, Nelu Adhikari, Mariazelle Gunatileke, Kasun Pasquel. Charuni Pasquel and Chethana Ranasinghe. The music is by Sarath de Alwis, Mahesh Denipitiya and Nalaka Saji Jayasinghe.
