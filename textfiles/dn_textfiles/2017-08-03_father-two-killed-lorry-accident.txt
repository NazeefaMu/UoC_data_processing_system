## Title: Father-of-two killed in lorry accident
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, August 3, 2017 - 10:45

Father-of-two killed in lorry accident

 
A father-of-two died on the spot after being run over by a lorry in Dikwella, Tangalle earlier today.
The deceased was a resident in Kondeniya Watta, Dikwella.
He was run over by the lorry while standing on the pavement.
The nineteen-year-old lorry driver told Police that he had fallen asleep while driving the vehicle. 
The suspect will be produced before the Matara Courts.
Dikwella Police is investigating.
