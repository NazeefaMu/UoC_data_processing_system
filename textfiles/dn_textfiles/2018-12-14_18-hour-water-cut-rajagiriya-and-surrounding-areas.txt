## Title: 18-hour water cut in Rajagiriya and surrounding areas
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 14, 2018 - 11:13

18-hour water cut in Rajagiriya and surrounding areas

Water supply to Rajagiriya and surrounding areas will be disrupted for 18-hours commencing from tomorrow 12 a.m. and until Sunday 6 p.m., the following day, the National Water Supply and Drainage Board said.Accordingly, water supply to Moragasmulla, Rajagiriya, Athulkotte, Obeysekarapura, Bandaranayakepura, Nawala, Koswatte, and Rajagiriya to Open University of Sri Lanka Nawala, and surrounding areas.
