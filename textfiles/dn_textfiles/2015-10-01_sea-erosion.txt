## Title: Sea erosion
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, October 1, 2015 - 19:30

Sea erosion


Heavy sea erosion at the Kattankudy coastal area had caused severe loss to the government and property owners. The filling station built at a cost of Rs. 20 million by the government had been heavily damaged.
