## Title: Heavy rains expected in several provinces and districts
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, January 30, 2018 - 10:00

Heavy rains expected in several provinces and districts

 
Heavy rains of above 100mm are expected particularly in Eastern and Uva provinces and in Matale and Polonnaruwa districts, the Meteorology Department said.
Fairly strong gusty winds of above 40 kmph are expected in the northern parts of the island and in Mathara and Hambanthota districts.
Rains and thundershowers are expected in most provinces of the island.
There may be temporary localized strong winds during thundershowers.
The Met. Department urged the public to take adequate precautions to minimize damages caused by lightning activity.
