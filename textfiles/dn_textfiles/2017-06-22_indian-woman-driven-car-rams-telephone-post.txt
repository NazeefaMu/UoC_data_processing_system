## Title: Indian woman driven car rams into telephone post
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 22, 2017 - 01:00

Indian woman driven car rams into telephone post

An Indian woman who drove a car under the influence of liquor and caused an accident in Thibirigasyaya, was arrested by Bambalapitiya Police. The car driven by the woman crashed against a telephone post. The woman is a resident of Pamankada. According to the statement to the police by the woman, it was revealed that she had met with the accident while on her way home after a party. The woman was released on a personal bail of Rs. 500,000 by the Colombo Magistrate.
