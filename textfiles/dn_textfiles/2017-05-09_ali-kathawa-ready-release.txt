## Title: Ali Kathawa ready for release
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 01:00

Ali Kathawa ready for release

Professor Sunil Ariyaratne’s newest movie Ali Kathawa will hit theaters from May 18. The movie which is produced by Gunapala Ratnasekara stars Dananjaya Siriwardana, Tharuka Wanniarachchi, and Ravindra Randeniya in the lead roles.
