## Title: PM AT YOWUN PURAYA 2017
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, April 1, 2017 - 01:00

PM AT YOWUN PURAYA 2017

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe was the Chief Guest on the third day of Yowun Puraya 2017 organised by the Youth Services Council and Sri Lanka Federation of Youths Clubs at the McHeyzer Stadium, Trincomalee. Picture shows the Premier being received by Tamil youths. State Minister Niroshan Perera and National Youth Services Council Chairman and Director General Erandika Weliange were also present.
