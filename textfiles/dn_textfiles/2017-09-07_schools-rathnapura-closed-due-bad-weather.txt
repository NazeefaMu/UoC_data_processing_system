## Title: Schools in Rathnapura closed due to bad weather
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 18:00

Schools in Rathnapura closed due to bad weather

 
A decision has been made to close schools in Rathnapura, Ayagama, Eheliyagoda, Kuruvita and Elapatha tomorrow due to the prevailing heavy rain and flood conditions.
The National Building Research Organization (NBRO) requested the residents of above mentioned areas to evacuate to safer places to avoid the risk of landslides.
