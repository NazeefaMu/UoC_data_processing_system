## Title: Wattegedera Wimalasuriya further remanded
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 17, 2017 - 01:00

Wattegedera Wimalasuriya further remanded

Moratuwa Acting Magistrate Joseph Fernando on Friday, remanded Wattegedera Wimalasuriya until October 27, pending investigations into an unauthorised firearm case.
Police identified him as a local organiser of the Jathika Nidahas Peramuna. On information received, the Colombo Crime Investigations Bureau Officials arrested him last week while he was travelling in a van along the Galle Road, in Moratuwa and interrogated him.
The police who searched his house at Thelawala, Moratuwa had found a repeater gun and cartridges hidden in a cupboard. Upon further questioning it was revealed that three months ago a firearm had been sold for Rs.300,000 to a member of the under world.
