## Title: Sixth SLFUE Research Conference at Rajarata University
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 16, 2017 - 01:00

Sixth SLFUE Research Conference at Rajarata University

The sixth International Conference of SLFUE: Sri Lanka Economics Research Conference will be held on November 23 and 24 at Rajarata University of Sri Lanka, Mihinthale. The theme of this year's conference is "Strengthening Economic Resilience for Inclusive Growth", said Prof. Sena Nanayakkara the University's Media Coordinator.
The Sri Lanka Forum of University Economists (SLFUE) is a network of all Departments and Units of Economics and related disciplines within the Sri Lankan university system which was initiated in 2012 with the objective of promoting closer co-operation, dialogue, collaborative research and mutual enhancement among the academics in the field of Economics. As a major annual event, SLFUE organizes the international conference enabling academics and researchers in the field of Economics to discuss and deliberate their findings, publish their research work and disseminate the knowledge that will emerge to the wider public and policy makers.
The inauguration ceremony will start at 2 pm on November 23.
The Central Bank of Sri Lanka Governor Dr. Indrajit Coomaraswamy will be the Chief Guest and the event will be presided over by the Mihinthale Rajarata University of Sri Lanka Vice Chancellor Prof. Ranjith Wijayawardana.
The key note address will be delivered by former Head of the Poverty and Governance Unit of the UNDP Dr. Fredrick Abeyrathna.
This conference further enlightens the participants with the panel discussion to be held on ‘Balanced Economic Growth in Sri Lanka: Challenges and Policy Reforms’ with the contribution of prominent scholars in Sri Lanka namely Dr. Indrajit Coomaraswamy, Prof. CM Madduma Bandara, Prof. Sumanasiri Liyanage, Dr. Fredrick Abeyrathna and Dr. Lalithasiri Gunaruwan.
Technical sessions of the conference will commence on November 24.
