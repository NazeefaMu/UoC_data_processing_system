## Title: Birth anniversary of Tamil erudite Swami Vipulananda
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 4, 2017 - 01:00

Birth anniversary of Tamil erudite Swami Vipulananda

The 125th birth anniversary of Tamil erudite Swami Vipulananda, was commemorated in Batticaloa yesterday. The celebrations were organized by the Suwami Vipulananda centenary committee and the Batticaloa Municipal Council. It was followed by the garlanding of his statue at the water Fountain Park. The students of the Kallady Vivekananda Madya Maha Vidyalaya sang Vipulananda’s favourite song “ Vellai Nira Malligaiyo”. Batticaloa District Secretary P.S.M. Charles, Municipal Commissioner V. Thavaraja and Manmunai North Divisional Secretary K. Kunanadan also participated. Picture by Sivam Packiyanathan, Batticaloa Special Correspondent
