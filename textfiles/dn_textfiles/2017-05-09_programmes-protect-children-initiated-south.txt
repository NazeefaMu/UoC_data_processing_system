## Title: Programmes to protect children initiated in south
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 01:00

Programmes to protect children initiated in south

Child protection programmes initiated in the Matara District by the Institute of Community Strength has proved a success.
Institute for Community Strengths Director Condred Ranawake said the programmes are underway in Weligama, Matara, Devinuwara, and Dikwella.
Midigama North and Bandaramulle in the Weligama DS Division, Polhena and Totamuna in the Matara DS Division, Welegoda in the Devinuwara DS Division and Bathigama North in the Dikwella Divisional Secretariat Division were identified for initiating the programmes.
Participants of the programmes will receive knowledge of preventing child abuse, sexual diseases and protecting children from anti-social acts. They will be provided with leadership training.
Divisional Secretaries, childcare officers and grama niladaries take part in the programmes as resource persons.
The Save the Children International coordinates them and the Institute for Development of Community Strengths Matara manage the programmes.
The United States Agency For International Development funds the projects, Ranawake said.
