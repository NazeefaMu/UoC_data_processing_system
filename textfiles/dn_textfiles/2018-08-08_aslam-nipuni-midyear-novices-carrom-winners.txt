## Title: Aslam, Nipuni Midyear Novices carrom winners
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, August 8, 2018 - 01:00

Aslam, Nipuni Midyear Novices carrom winners

ACM Aslam of SL Army won the Boys’ Singles championship beating Avon Wickramasinghe of SL Air Force by 2/0 (25/12, 25/04), while Nipuni Dilrukshi of Mahamaya Balika Vidyalaya, Nugegoda clinched the Gils’ Singles beating Vindy Dilanya of Samudradevi Balika Vidyalaya, Nugegoda by 2/1 (18/25,25/03, 25/20) in the finals of the Midyear Novices Carrom Championship 2018.
The tournament was conducted from July 07 to 29 at the Carrom Federation (CFSL) headquarters, Kohuwala and consisted of four events namely, Boy’s Singles, Girl’s Singles, Boy’s Doubles and Girl’s Doubles. Over 350 slicers from island-wide participated in events.
KVSL Liyanage of University of Moratuwa (25/14) and Hirushi Malshani of Mahamaya Baliika Vidyalaya, Nugegoda (25/23) secured third places of Boys’ and Girls’ Singles respectively.
Results –
Boys’ Singles –
1. ACM Aslam of SL Army, 2. Avon Wickramasinghe of SL Air Force, 3. KLSV Liayange of University of Moratuwa
Girls’ Singles –
1. Nipuni Dilrukshi of Mahamaya Balika Vidyalaya, Nugegoda, 2. Vindy Dilanya of Samudra Devi BAlika Vidyalaya, Nugegoda, 3. Hurushi Malshani of Mahamaya Balika Vidyalaya, Nugegoda
Boys Doubles –
1. Dineth Dulakshan and Avon Wickramasinghe of SL Air Force, 2. Kavindu Pethum and Bawantha Pansilu of St. John’s College, Nugegoda, 3. Nalin Jayashantha and Dushyantha Nadeeshan of SL Army
Girls Doubles –
1. Dilsha Prasadini and Vindy Dilanya of Samudradevi Balika Vidyalaya, Nugegoda, 2. Yashani Kaushalya and Deshani Kaushalya of Mahamaya Balika Vidyalaya, Nugegoda, 3. Masha Nurmani and Nethma Sewmini of Mahamaya BAlika Vidyalaya, Nugegoda
