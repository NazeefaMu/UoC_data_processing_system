## Title: 21-year old apprehended for possession of “ICE”
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Friday, December 28, 2018 - 09:30

21-year old apprehended for possession of “ICE”

A 21-year-old has been arrested in Madhagal area ,Jaffna for transporting the drug “ICE”.A spokesperson for the Police confirmed that 86 g of ICE was discovered in the suspect’s possession.The suspect has been identified as a resident of Jaffna. He is due to be presented before the Mallakam Magistrate Courts today.Vaddukodai Police are conducting further investigations.
