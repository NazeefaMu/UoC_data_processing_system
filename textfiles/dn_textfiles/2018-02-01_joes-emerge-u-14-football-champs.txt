## Title: Joes emerge U-14 Football Champs
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, February 1, 2018 - 01:00

Joes emerge U-14 Football Champs

St. Joseph’s College, Colombo 10 emerged champions in All Island under 14 Football Tournament, beating Gateway International College, Colombo by 3-0 in the final recently.
Josephian Pathum Gimhan scored two goals while Lakshitan Nagendran scored a goal. Gimhan was adjudged the Best player of the Tournament. Joes' beat St. Patrick’s College, Jaffna in the Quarter finals (1-0) and De Mazanod College, Kandana (3-1) in the Semifinals.
