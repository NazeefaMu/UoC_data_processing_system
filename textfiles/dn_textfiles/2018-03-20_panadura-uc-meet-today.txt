## Title: Panadura UC to meet today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Tuesday, March 20, 2018 - 01:00

Panadura UC to meet today

The inaugural meeting of the newly-elected councillors of the Panadura Urban Council will be held at the Panadura Youth Council Auditorium today to elect the Chairman and the Vice Chairman.
The UNP led by Nandana Gunatilake won nine out of ten seats. According to political sources, Gunatilake, the party’s chief organiser of the Panadura electorate and former Chairman, is tipped to be chairman. P.D. Wilman is tipped to be the vice chairman. The inaugural meeting will be presided over by the Assistant Commissioner of Local Government.
