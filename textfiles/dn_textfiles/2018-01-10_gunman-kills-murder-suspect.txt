## Title: Gunman kills murder suspect
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 10, 2018 - 01:00

Gunman kills murder suspect

A person driving a jeep was shot dead by an unidentified motorcyclist at Pallama in Pannala on Monday morning.
The Police said he was killed in front of a timber mill off the Kurunegala-Negombo main road.
The victim was identified as a suspect on bail in connection with a double murder in Pannala. The 38-year-old victim was a father of three, a resident of Elibichchiya, the Bogaha colony, Pannala. He was travelling from Makandura to Pannala.
He was pronounced dead on admission to the Sanda Lanka Hospital.Pannala Police are investigating.
