## Title: Rough seas expected in Galle – Potuvil coast line
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 3, 2018 - 11:54

Rough seas expected in Galle – Potuvil coast line

 
Issuing a red notification, the Meteorology Department said that a sudden roughness of the seas will occur due to heavy thundershowers and strong winds above 70-80 kmph in the Galle - Potuvil coastline today.
The Naval and fishing communities were requested to be vigilant.
