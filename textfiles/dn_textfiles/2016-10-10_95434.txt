## Title: Singer Schools Cricket U19 awards today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, October 10, 2016 - 01:00

Singer Schools Cricket U19 awards today

The Singer Schools Cricket Under 19 Awards 2015/16 will take place at the Musaeus College Auditorium today from 3.30 p.m.
Asoka Pieris Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Singer (Sri Lanka) PLC Ltd will be the chief guest.
Roshan Mahanama, Sri Lanka former cricket captain and ICC Match referee will be participating as the guest of honour. The event is conducted by Singer (Sri Lanka) PLC Ltd and Sri Lanka Schools Cricket Association and will be telecast live via www.batsman.co website. - YK
