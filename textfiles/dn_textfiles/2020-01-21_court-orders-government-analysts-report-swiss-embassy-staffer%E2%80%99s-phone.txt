## Title: Court orders Government Analyst's to report on Swiss Embassy staffer’s phone
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Tuesday, January 21, 2020 - 17:40

Court orders Government Analyst's to report on Swiss Embassy staffer’s phone

The Colombo Chief Magistrate Lanka Jayarathna today issued an order directing the Government Analyst that an analytical report pertaining to the mobile phone belonging Swizz embassy officer Garliar Banrister Francis be submitted in court as a confidential report. The Magistrate fixed further inquiries for February 11.
