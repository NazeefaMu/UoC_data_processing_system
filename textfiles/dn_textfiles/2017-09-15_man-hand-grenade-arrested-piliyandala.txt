## Title: Man with hand grenade arrested in Piliyandala
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, September 15, 2017 - 10:30

Man with hand grenade arrested in Piliyandala

 
A man who possessed a hand grenade was taken into custody in Piliyandala last night.
The three-wheeler the suspect rode in at the time of the arrest was also taken into custody.
The suspect is due to be presented before the Kesbewa Magistrate Courts today.
Piliyandala Police is investigating.
