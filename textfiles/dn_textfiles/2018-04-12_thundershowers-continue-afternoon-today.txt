## Title: Thundershowers to continue in the afternoon today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 12, 2018 - 11:50

Thundershowers to continue in the afternoon today

 
Thundershowers over 100mm are expected in most provinces and particularly in the  Sabaragamuwa, Central,  Eastern, Southern and Uva provinces in the afternoon today, the Meteorology Department said.
 
There may be temporary localized strong winds during thundershowers. 
 
The Met. Department urged the public to take adequate precautions to minimize damages caused by lightning activity.
