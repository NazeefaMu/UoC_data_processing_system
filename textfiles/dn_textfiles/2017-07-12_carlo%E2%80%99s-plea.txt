## Title: Carlo’s plea
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, July 12, 2017 - 01:00

Carlo’s plea

Prof. Carlo Fonseka, the former head of the Sri Lanka Medical Council pleads to Dr. Anurudhdha Padeniya, the President of the Government Medical Officers Union during a press conference held. The GMOA visited Prof. Fonseka, who strictly instructed the GMOA to desist from any strike action on his behalf.
