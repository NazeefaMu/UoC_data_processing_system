## Title: Two suspects arrested with 62 g of heroin
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Saturday, December 29, 2018 - 01:00

Two suspects arrested with 62 g of heroin

Two suspects were arrested with heroin on Thursday in Grandpass.
One suspect was arrested with 52 grams of heroin on a tip off by the Colombo Crimes Division on Thursday morning.
The suspect, a woman (51), is a resident at Colombo 14. She was to be produced before Maligakanda Magistrate’s Court yesterday. The other suspect was arrested with 10 grams of heroin and 5 grams of ICE drug on a tip off by the Organized Crimes Prevention Division. A car, Rs 21,500, and a mobile phone were seized. The suspect (34) is a resident at Mattakkuliya.
He was to be produced before Maligakanda Magistrate’s Court yesterday.
