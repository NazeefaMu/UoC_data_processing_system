## Title: JS ‘Akebono’ on goodwill visit
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, April 11, 2018 - 01:00

JS ‘Akebono’ on goodwill visit

Japan Maritime Self Defence Force (JMSDF) Destroyer JS ‘Akebono’ arrived at the port of Hambantota on a goodwill visit on Monday. Sri Lanka Navy welcomed the ship in accordance with naval traditions.
Southern Naval Area Commander Rear Admiral Kapila Samaraweera, Naval Operations Director Commodore Sanjeewa Dias along with a group of officers attached to the Southern Naval Command, Coast Guard and Air Force made an inspection visit on-board ‘Akebono’ and subsequently took part in a conference on search and rescue held aboard the ship.
In addition, the Commanding Officer of the vessel, Commander Yuichi Haeno called on Southern Naval Area Commander Rear Admiral Kapila Samaraweera and shared views on matters of professional interest as well. Mementoes were exchanged to mark the occasion.
JS ‘Akebono’ is due to set sail for her next port of call today, on completion of her two-day visit.
