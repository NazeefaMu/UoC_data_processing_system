## Title: Three dead as three wheeler goes into canal
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, April 11, 2018 - 01:00

Three dead as three wheeler goes into canal

Three persons were killed when their three wheeler skidded off the road and fell into a canal at Thundeniya junction in Gampola last evening.
The driver had lost control of the of the three wheeler and it veered off the road and fell into the canal.
Three men who were in the rear seat were were seriously injured and succumbed on admission to the Gampola hospital.The victims who were in the 72–80 age group are residents of Warakapola, Medirigiriya and Gampola. Gampola police are investigating.
