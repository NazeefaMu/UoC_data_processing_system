## Title: Prime Minister hosts Sri Lanka cricket team for tea
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, September 21, 2016 - 01:00

Prime Minister hosts Sri Lanka cricket team for tea

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe hosted the Sri Lanka national cricket  team for tea at Temple Trees yesterday in recognition of their 3-0 Test  series win over Australia recently. Here, the Prime Minister is seen with  the national team along with Minister of Law and Order and Southern  Development Sagala Ratnayaka and President of Sri Lanka Cricket Thilanga Sumathipala.
