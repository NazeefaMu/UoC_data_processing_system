## Title: Drug addict arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 1, 2018 - 01:00

Drug addict arrested

Police recently arrested a drug addict possessing 5.1 grams of cannabis during a raid conducted at Kadewatte.
The suspect, a resident of Weligama, would be produced at the Matara Magistrate’s Court soon, police said.
