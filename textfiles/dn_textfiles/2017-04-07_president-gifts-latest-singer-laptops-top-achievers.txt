## Title: President gifts latest Singer laptops to top-achievers
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Friday, April 7, 2017 - 01:00

President gifts latest Singer laptops to top-achievers

Singer Sri Lanka continuing its tradition of encouraging students to excel in studies by gifting 26 of the latest Singer laptops at a felicitation ceremony held recently.The top 12 Grade 5 Scholarship exam students and the top two students from the seven different streams of the Advanced Level (A/L) examination received laptops.The laptops were handed over to the students by President Maithripala Sirisena together with the Prime Minister Ranil Wickramasinghe at a special event organized at the Presidential Secretariat on March 28. Singer Sri Lanka Group CEO Asoka Pieris, Finance Director Lalith Yatiwella and Digital Media Senior Brand Manager Sahan Perera were present. The President gifts a Singer laptop to one of the top-achievers.
