## Title: President - Pence discuss strengthening of bilateral relations
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 2, 2016 - 01:00

President - Pence discuss strengthening of bilateral relations

US Vice President elect Mike Pence, in a telephone conversation with Sri Lankan President Maithripala Sirisena last night, discussed strengthening bilateral relations between the two countries.
The Sri Lankan President congratulated Pence and US President-elect Donald Trump on their victory.
President Sirisena, during the telephone conversation, had told Pence that the two countries could work with each other in a broad democratic space.
The President’s Media Unit said the two leaders also discussed several other matters pertaining to bilateral relations between Sri Lanka and the US.
