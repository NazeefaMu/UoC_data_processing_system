## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, November 5, 2015 - 19:30

The Sri Lanka women's rugby squad


The Sri Lanka women’s rugby squad comprising Thanuja Weerakkody (captain), Sandika Hemakumari, Randika Kumudumali, Anusha Attanayake, Nadeeka Munasinghe, Dulani Pallikkondage, Dasuni de Silva, Ayesha Kaluarachchi, Sandeepa Sandamali, Thilini Senanayake, Wasana Weerakkody, Sanjeewani Wijetilleke and coach Kate Merchant pose for a ceremonial picture before their departure for the Olympic Games Asian zone qualifying tournament in Hong Kong on Wednesday night.
