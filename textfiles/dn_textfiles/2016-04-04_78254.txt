## Title: Sastrananda wins mini battle
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, April 4, 2016 - 01:00

Sastrananda wins mini battle

 
Sastrananda Vidyalaya, Dehiwala completed a good double when they beat their arch rivals Methodist High School, Puwakaramba, Moratuwa by 54 runs in their fourth annual limited overs cricket match worked off at Tyrone Fernando Stadium, Moratuwa. Earlier Sastrananda beat Methodians by eight wickets in their Big Match.
Batting first Sastrananda Vidyalaya scored 143 runs with Navod Maduranga scoring 42 runs and Sachin Navod claimed 4 for 19. In reply Methodians were bowled out for 89 runs with Navod Madushanka capturing 5 for 24.
Sastrananda Vidyalaya - 143 in 35 overs (Navod Maduranga 42, Vinod Rukshan 21, Gayan Jayasuriya 20, Sachin Navod 4/19, Sachinta Lakshan 3/43).
Methodist High School - 89 in 33.5 (Lahiru Abeykoon 21, Thilina Niroshan 15, Navod Madushanka 5/24, Kavindu Madushanka 2/27).
