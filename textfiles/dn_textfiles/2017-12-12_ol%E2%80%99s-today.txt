## Title: O/L’s from today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, December 12, 2017 - 01:00

O/L’s from today

 
The Ordinary Level Examination 2017 will be held from today to December 21.
According to the Examinations Department statistics, the examination, which commences at 8.30 am will be held countrywide at 5,116 examination centres with the participation of 688,573 candidates, who sit under new and old syllabuses.
