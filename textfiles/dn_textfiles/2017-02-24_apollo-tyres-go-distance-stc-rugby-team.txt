## Title: Apollo tyres to go the distance with STC rugby team
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, February 24, 2017 - 01:00

Apollo tyres to go the distance with STC rugby team

Ideal Wheels & Tyres Pvt Ltd, a member of the Ideal Group representing Apollo Tyres of India, are continuing their rugby sponsorship of the STC rugby team for a second year. In addition Apollo tyres are sponsors of the famous Manchester United soccer team in London. Here Nalin Welgama, Chairman, Ideal group along with Jagath Samarasekara, CEO of Ideal Wheels & Tyres hands over the sponsorship cheque to the Warden of S. Thomas College, Rev. Marc Billimoria at the college premises.
