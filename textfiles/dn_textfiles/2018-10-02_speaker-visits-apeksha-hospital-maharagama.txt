## Title: Speaker visits Apeksha hospital, Maharagama
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 2, 2018 - 01:00

Speaker visits Apeksha hospital, Maharagama

Speaker Karu Jayasuriya speaks to a patient at Apeksha hospital, Maharagama, when the Indira Cancer Trust offered lunch to residential patients last Wednesday, in memory of late Indira Jayasuriya, the Speaker’s younger daughter. The event was organised on the occasion of her 42nd birthday.
