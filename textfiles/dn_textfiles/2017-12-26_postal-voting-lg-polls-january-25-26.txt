## Title: Postal voting for LG Polls on January 25, 26
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Tuesday, December 26, 2017 - 01:00

Postal voting for LG Polls on January 25, 26

Postal voting for the forthcoming Local Government elections will be held on January 25 and 26, Additional Elections Commissioner M.M.Mohammed said.
Mohammed said officials were preparing a list of eligible voters for postal voting after perusing the applications.
