## Title: Katukurunda boat tragedy :Body of 13- year- old washed ashore; death toll now 16
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 24, 2017 - 01:00

Katukurunda boat tragedy :Body of 13- year- old washed ashore; death toll now 16

The body of a thirteen year old washed ashore off the Kalutara beach on Wednesday evening, bringing the death toll from the Katukurunda boat tragedy to sixteen.
Forty people were aboard the boat which travelled to Holy Cross Church in Kalutara on Sunday (19) when the incident occurred. Eleven bodies were found in the immediate aftermath, eight were reported missing.
With the discovery of the body of the thirteen year old, all missing bodies are accounted for, Police said. Courtesy: Daily News online
