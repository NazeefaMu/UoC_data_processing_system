## Title: Suwami Vipulananda commemoration
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, August 3, 2017 - 01:00

Suwami Vipulananda commemoration

The 125th birth anniversary commemoration of Tamil Erudite Suwami Vipulananda, jointly organised by the Sri Lanka Hindu Congress and Batticaloa District Hindu Youth Peravai, was held at Sivananda Vidyalaya, Kallady recently. Opposition Leader and TNA President R. Sambandan paid his respect at Vipulananda's Samathi at Kallady. Batticaloa District TNA Parliamentarian S. Yogeswaran, Eastern University Vice Chancellor Dr. K. Jeyasingam, Prof. S. Selvaraja, Batticaloa Zonal Education Director K. Baskaran participated. Picture by Sivam Packiyanathan, Batticaloa Special Correspondent
