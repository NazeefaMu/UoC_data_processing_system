## Title: Finance and Media Minister Mangala Samaraweera met CEO and Executive Managing Director of the  JBIC
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, October 18, 2017 - 01:00

Finance and Media Minister Mangala Samaraweera met CEO and Executive Managing Director of the  JBIC

Finance and Media Minister Mangala Samaraweera met CEO and Executive Managing Director of the Japan Bank for International Cooperation (JBIC) Tadashi Maeda on the sidelines of the WB/IMF Annual Meeting in Washington, D.C. on October 14,2017. The JBIC reiterated its support for Sri Lanka’s economic programme during the meeting. The JBIC CEO and Executive Managing Director will lead a delegation to Colombo later this year for further discussions.
