## Title: Malani Fonseka felicitated
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, August 24, 2018 - 01:00

Malani Fonseka felicitated

Sri Lanka’s Queen of Silver Screen, Malani Fonseka Felicitation Ceremony was held under the patronage of President Maithripala Sirisena at the BMICH, on Wednesday.
The ‘Malani Fonseka Foundation’ organised the ceremony in commemoration of her 50th year in the field of film and theatre.
Malani Fonseka has rendered a yeoman service to the Sri Lankan film industry through her versatile talents. She had won several international and national awards, including Presidential Honours.
President Sirisena presented the Felicitation Award and monetary aid to Malani Fonseka.
Famous Indian film critique Soma Chatterji delivered the memorial speech of the Abhinandana Ceremony while veteran film director Asoka Handagama also spoke.
Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe, Speaker Karu Jayasuriya, Ministers Arjuna Ranatunga, Lasantha Alagiyawanna, Parliamentarian Bandula Gunawardena, Secretary to the President Udaya R. Seneviratne, Asela Iddalwela, experts, artistes and other guests were present.
