## Title: PM Mahinda and Shiranthi Rajapaksa visits Dalada Maligawa
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 29, 2018 - 01:00

PM Mahinda and Shiranthi Rajapaksa visits Dalada Maligawa

Prime Minister Mahinda Rajapaksa and Shiranthi Rajapaksa who was in Kandy greeted people who gathered outside the Dalada Maligawa after their meeting the Mahanayake's of the Asgiriya and Malwatte Chapters.
