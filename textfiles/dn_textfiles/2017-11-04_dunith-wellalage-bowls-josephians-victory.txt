## Title: Dunith Wellalage bowls Josephians to victory
## Supertitle: Singer Cup Under 19 Inter Schools Cricket Tournament:
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, November 4, 2017 - 01:00

Singer Cup Under 19 Inter Schools Cricket Tournament:
Dunith Wellalage bowls Josephians to victory

St. Joseph’s College Colombo beat De Mazenod College Kandana by four wickets at Darley road and Thurstan College Colombo beat St. Thomas College Matara by 10 wickets at Thurstan ground. Bandarawela Central College beat St. Michael’s College Batticaloa in their Singer Cup schools under 19 cricket tournament matches which concluded yesterday
Left arm leg spinner Dunith Wellalage took a match bag of 11 wickets for 124 runs against De Mazenod Co llege Kandana and Sandaru Dias took 8 wickets for 30 runs in the second innings against St. Thomas College Matara. Charithra Basnayake of Bandarawela Central College took 11 wickets for 43 runs against St. Michael’s College Batticaloa at Batticaloa.
Opener Santhush Gunathilake of St. Peter’s Bambalapitiya scored an unbeaten 129 runs in 214 balls with 11 boundaries and a six. Sayura Jayakody skipper of Loyola College Bopitiya scored unbeaten 178 runs against Dr. Kulasinghe MV, Udammita while Daminda Reshan of Ananda scored 100 runs against Maliyadeva Kurunegala.
23 wickets fell on day one between S. Thomas’ College Mt. Lavinia and Prince of Wales College Moratuwa at Mt. Lavinia. The Thomians were leading on first innings against the Cambrians and St. Sebastian’s College Katuneriya were leading on first innings against St. Anne’s College Kurunegala at Katuneriya. Loyola College Bopitiya were leading on first innings against Dr. Kulasinghe MV, Udammita at Udammita.
