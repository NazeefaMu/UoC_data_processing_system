## Title: Six treasure hunters nabbed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, December 24, 2018 - 01:00

Six treasure hunters nabbed

Urubokka Police arrested six suspects yesterday and seized two motorcycles and a van along with equipment they brought to unearth treasures from an archeological site in Narandola.
The suspects are residents of Morawaka, Weeraketiya, Kakundeniya, Kiripitiya and Hagoda.
A Police team led by Urubokka OIC IP Gihan Chamila Rajapaksa conducted the raid.
Investigations are continuing.
