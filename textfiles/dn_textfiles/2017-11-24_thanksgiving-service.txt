## Title: Thanksgiving Service
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, November 24, 2017 - 01:00

Thanksgiving Service

A Service of Thanksgiving for the ministry of Lakshani P. Fernando, General Secretary of the Ceylon Bible Society (2002 to 2017), will be held at the Cathedral of Christ the Living Saviour, Bauddhaloka Mawatha, Colombo 7 at 4.00 p.m. on Saturday, December 2, 2017.
Fernando is the first woman to be appointed to this post in the 190 year history of the Ceylon Bible Society.Friends and well wishers are invited to attend this Service.
