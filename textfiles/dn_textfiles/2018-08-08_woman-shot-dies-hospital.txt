## Title: Woman shot, dies in hospital
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 8, 2018 - 01:00

Woman shot, dies in hospital

A woman was shot at, at the Armstrong Junction in Gonahena, Kadawatha yesterday around 1 p.m., according to the Police Media Spokesman’s Office. Admitted to the Ragama Hospital, she died, he said.
Police said no one was arrested so far for the shooting.
