## Title: Books for Ampara libraries
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 30, 2018 - 01:00

Books for Ampara libraries

Library books were distributed to Nintavur Al-Ashraq M. M. Vidyalaya (National School) Library by Ampara District Secretariat Cultural Unit in view of the National Reading Month (October), recently.
The Cultural Affairs Department and members of the Ampara District Book Writers jointly organised the event under the guidance of Ampara District Secretary D. M. L. Bandaranayake.
Under this programme, about 20 selected school libraries of the Tamil medium benefited.
The ceremony was held at the Al-Ashraq National School, Nintavur under the patronage of Ampara Additional District Secretary K. Vimalanathan.
The Nintavur Divisional Secretary T. M. Mohamed Ansar, Ampara District Secretariat Cultural Officer T. M. Rinzan, Nintavur Cultural Development Officer J. Sutharsan, Deputy Principals A. Gaffoor, A. K. Niyas and M. C. Sareena Ummah were also present.
