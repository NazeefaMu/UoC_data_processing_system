## Title: Seven treasure hunters fined
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, July 12, 2017 - 01:00

Seven treasure hunters fined

Seven treasure hunters who pleaded guilty for excavating an ancient place of worship situated at Kahallapalle forest reserve to dig out a valuable treasure were fined Rs. 830,000.Galkiriyagama D. Ramasinghe Range Officer prosecuted and Kekirawa Magistrate D. Weerawardena imposed the fine on the suspects.
