## Title: Accident in Ginigathhena kills one
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 1, 2017 - 14:45

Accident in Ginigathhena kills one

 
A vehicle veered off the road and plunged in to a 50-meter precipice near Ginigathhena on the Hatton-Colombo main road this morning.
A five-year-old boy succumbed to his injuries and three other injured were hospitalized.
The deceased S.A.Sanith Devinda was a Grade 1 student.
Ginigathhena Police is investigating.
