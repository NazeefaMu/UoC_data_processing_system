## Title: Unemployment rate down to 2.9%
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Monday, April 2, 2018 - 01:00

Unemployment rate down to 2.9%

Sri Lanka unemployment rate is dropping according to this year’s figures released by the Central Bank. It says that 4.3% male unemployment rate in 2007 has come down to 2.9% in 10 years’ time (2016). Similarly the female unemployment rate which was at 9% has reduced to 7%.
