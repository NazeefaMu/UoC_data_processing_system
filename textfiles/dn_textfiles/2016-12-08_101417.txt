## Title: Two injured in car crash
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, December 8, 2016 - 01:00

Two injured in car crash

A private car plying from Batticaloa to Nintavur veered off the road at Maruthamunai in the Kalmunai Police area and crashed into a lamp post. Two persons who were injured in the accident were admitted to the Kalmunai Ashraff Memorial Hospital. Kalmunai Police are investigating. Pictures by: I.L.M.RIZAN, Addalaichenai Central Correspondent
