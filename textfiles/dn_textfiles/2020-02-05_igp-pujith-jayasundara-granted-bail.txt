## Title: IGP Pujith Jayasundara granted bail
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Wednesday, February 5, 2020 - 14:08

IGP Pujith Jayasundara granted bail

Inspector General of Police (IGP) Pujith Jayasundara, was released on a cash bail of Rs.250,000 with two sureties of Rs.2.5 million each after being produced before the Colombo High Court.
