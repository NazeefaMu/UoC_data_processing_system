## Title: 20,000 unemployed graduates to receive Govt. service training
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, April 11, 2018 - 01:00

20,000 unemployed graduates to receive Govt. service training

Kurunegala District UNP Parliamentarian and Education Minister Akila Viraj Kariyawasam told Daily News yesterday (10) in Kurunegala that the National Policies and Economic Development Ministry has decided to recruit 20,000 unemployed graduates countrywide in June this year as Development Officers for the government service. These new Development Officers would be appointed to respective Divisional Secretariats and they would be trained for two years practically.
However, they would be paid an allowance during the time period that they would undergo service training. Finance Ministry has agreed to allocate necessary funds for this purpose, Minister Akila Viraj Kariyawasam who is the Deputy Secretary General of the UNP said to Daily News.
