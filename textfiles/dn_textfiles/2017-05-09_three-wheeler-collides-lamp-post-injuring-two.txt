## Title: Three wheeler collides with lamp post injuring two
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 01:00

Three wheeler collides with lamp post injuring two

A three-wheeler collided with a lamp post along the Bar Road, Amirthakali, Batticaloa on May 7. The driver and a man travelling in it were severely injured.
They were admitted to the Batticaloa Teaching Hospital.
Batticaloa Police are investigating.
