## Title: Dharmasena to officiate World T20 semi-final
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, March 30, 2016 - 01:00

Dharmasena to officiate World T20 semi-final

Sri Lanka's Kumar Dharmasena of the ICC Elite Umpires Panel will officiate with Australia's Rod Tucker the first semi-final of the ICC World T20 between England and New Zealand at the Feroz Shah Kotla grounds Delhi on Wednesday.
The third umpire will be Bruce Oxenford of Australia and the match referee David Boon also of Australia.
Sri Lanka's Ranmore Martinesz has been named fourth umpire for the Women's World T20 first semi-final between Australia and England which precedes the men's match at the same venue.
The appointments of umpires for the 3 April finals (men and women) at Eden Gardens in Kolkata will be announced on 1 April.
