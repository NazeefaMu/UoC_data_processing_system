## Title: Batticaloa DS employees protest over LRC Director’s shooting
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 24, 2017 - 01:00

Batticaloa DS employees protest over LRC Director’s shooting

Employees of the District Secretariat in Batticaloa held a protest over the shooting of the Land Reforms Commission (LRC) Director, Wimalraj Nesan, at the Batticaloa DS yesterday.
Nesan was injured after two unidentified gunmen who arrived on a motorcycle, shot him at Kalawanchikudi on Wednesday evening. The injured officer is currently being treated at the Batticaloa Hospital and is reportedly out of danger. Police have deployed two teams to nab the shooters.
