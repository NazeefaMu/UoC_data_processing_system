## Title: A city on the rise
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, February 1, 2017 - 01:00

A city on the rise

Cranes crowd the Colombo skyline as construction booms. Towering construction machines crowd the Colombo skyline signaling a bustling construction sector and a transforming city. A wave of new construction is happening, fueled by the city’s robust economy and the government’s new development initiatives. The Daily News takes look at the some of the new construction carried out in Colombo and surrounding areas.
