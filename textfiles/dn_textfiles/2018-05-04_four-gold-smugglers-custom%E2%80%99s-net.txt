## Title: Four gold smugglers in Custom’s net
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, May 4, 2018 - 01:00

Four gold smugglers in Custom’s net

Customs officers at the Katunayake airport arrested four Indian nationals who tried to smuggle in gold biscuits worth over Rs.11 million yesterday.
Customs Media spokesman Sunil Jayaratne said officers had found 16 gold biscuits weighing 1.65 kg’s from the four suspects who arrived on a flight from Chennai. The gold had been hidden inside the clothes of the suspects.The suspects who used the green channel at the airport had been subjected to a search as they had behaved suspiciously, Jayaratne said.
