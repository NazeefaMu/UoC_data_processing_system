Florals, stripes  and polka dots

The Kingsbury hosted its illustrious High Tea fashion show in August. This month’s featured fashion label was Ennigma, who unveiled an assortment of vibrant trendy wear carefully designed for every occasion. The outfits had bright colours, various designs including florals, stripes and polka dots which have been crafted from various fabrics.
