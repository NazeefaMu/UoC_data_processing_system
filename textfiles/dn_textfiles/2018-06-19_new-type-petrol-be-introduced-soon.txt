## Title: New type of petrol to be introduced soon
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, June 19, 2018 - 15:23

New type of petrol to be introduced soon

 
A new type of Petrol will be introduced soon by the Petroleum Resources Development Ministry which is more beneficial to three-wheels and motor vehicles, Petroleum Resources Development Minister Arjuna Ranatunga said today.
Addressing a ceremony held to hand over 13 appointments to Engineers at Ceylon Petroleum Corporation (CPC) the Minister said that experiments are still underway to introduce the said new type of Petrol.
