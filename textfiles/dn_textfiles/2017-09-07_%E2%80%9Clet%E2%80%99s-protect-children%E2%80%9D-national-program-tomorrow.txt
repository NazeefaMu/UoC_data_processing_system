## Title: “Let’s Protect Children” National Program tomorrow
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 16:00

“Let’s Protect Children” National Program tomorrow

 

The inaugural ceremony of the National Program: “Let’s Protect Children” will be held under the patronage of President Maithripala Sirisena, at the Provincial Sports Complex Digana, Kandy tomorrow.
The program is launched by the Presidential Secretariat according to a concept of President Sirisena.
Ending Child Labour in Sri Lanka, preventing child abuse and using children for unlawful activities, are among the main objectives of this national program.
