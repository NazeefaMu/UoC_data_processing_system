## Title: UK All Party Parliamentary group calls on SP Governor
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 10, 2018 - 01:00

UK All Party Parliamentary group calls on SP Governor

The UK All Party parliamentary group on a visit to Sri Lanka from 3-8 January paid a courtesy call on Southern Province Governor Dr. Hema Kumara Nanayakkara at his official residence at Upper Dickson Road, Galle.
The four member delegation led by Chairman MP Ranil Jayawardana included parliamentarians Michelle Donelan, Chris Green, and John Lamont.
The group had a brief discussion over the government’s development initiatives in the Southern Province and they expressed their satisfaction over them.
