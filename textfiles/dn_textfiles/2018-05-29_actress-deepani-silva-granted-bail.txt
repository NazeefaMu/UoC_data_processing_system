## Title: Actress Deepani Silva granted bail
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, May 28, 2018 - 15:38

Actress Deepani Silva granted bail

 
Actress Deepani Silva, who was arrested this morning in connection to a vehicle accident, was granted bail by the Panadura Additional Magistrate a shot while ago.
The actresses’ vehicle had reportedly collided with a three-wheeler and had injured a child.
