## Title: Employer sexually abuses 16-year-old male employee
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, January 28, 2017 - 01:00

Employer sexually abuses 16-year-old male employee

A hotel owner who abused a 16-year-old boy was arrested by the Galgamuwa police, Kurunegala. The suspect was produced before Galgamuwa Magistrate Ranjeewa Lakshan Warusavithana, who ordered him in remand custody till January 27. The victim was an employee of the suspect. He had been abused frequently by the suspect. About 28 child abuse cases were reported to police stations in the Kurunegala districts this year, a senior police officer said.
