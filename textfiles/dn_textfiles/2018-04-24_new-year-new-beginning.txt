## Supertitle: 
## Section: T&C
## Date: Tuesday, April 24, 2018 - 01:00

A New Year,  a new beginning

Visakha Vidyalaya, Colombo held it’s Avurudu Ulela under the guidance of Principal Sandamali Aviruppola, recently. After the rituals of avurudu were conducted in the classrooms, including boiling the pot of rice, taking the first meal, ‘ganu denu’ etc. the children left the classrooms to the grounds to engage in traditional avurudu games. They took part in the balloon competition, lime and spoon race, tug o’war, eating buns and the sack race among other events.
