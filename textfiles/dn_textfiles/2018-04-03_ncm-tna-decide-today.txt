## Title: NCM: TNA to decide today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Tuesday, April 3, 2018 - 01:00

NCM: TNA to decide today

The Tamil National Alliance (TNA) would declare its stance on the No-Confidence Motion today, said a spokesman for the TNA.
"We will make the decision on the morning of April 3 and officially announce our stance on the No-Confidence Motion,” said a spokesman for Opposition Leader and TNA leader R. Sampanthan speaking to the Daily News. The Tamil National Alliance has 16 seats in Parliament.
