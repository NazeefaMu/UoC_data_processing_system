## Title: President opens the Moragahakanda sluice gates
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, November 30, 2018 - 11:39

President opens the Moragahakanda sluice gates

Sluice gates of Moragahakanda-Kaluganga reservoirs project was opened by President Maithripala Sirisena a short while ago. 
The President is due to conduct an inspection tour of the sluice gates of the Moragahakanda-Kaluganga Project.
