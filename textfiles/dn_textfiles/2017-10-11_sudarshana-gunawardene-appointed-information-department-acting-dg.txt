## Title: Sudarshana Gunawardene appointed Information Department Acting DG
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, October 11, 2017 - 14:15

Sudarshana Gunawardene appointed Information Department Acting DG

 
Sri Lanka Broadcasting Corporation (SLBC) Chairman Sudarshana Gunawardene has been appointed as the Acting Director General of Government Information Department.
The former Information Department DG Dr. Ranga Kalansooriya resigned from the post on September 29 citing that he was resigning for personal reasons.
