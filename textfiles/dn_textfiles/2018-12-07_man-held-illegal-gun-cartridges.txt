## Title: Man held with illegal gun, cartridges
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 7, 2018 - 01:00

Man held with illegal gun, cartridges

Wariyapola Police, arrested a person with a No. 16 illegal gun along with two cartridges, while hunting wild animals near a government school.
The suspect was hunting near the Nebadawewa Government school in Awulegama area, Wariyapola. A Police team led by IP Nilantha Bandara, OIC, Wariyapola Police, arrested the suspect and was to be produced before the Wariyapola Magistrate yesterday day (06).
A Police officer in Kurunegala told the Daily News that about 567 poachers were arrested this year from the Kurunegala and Puttalam Districts by Police officers acting on a tip off.
