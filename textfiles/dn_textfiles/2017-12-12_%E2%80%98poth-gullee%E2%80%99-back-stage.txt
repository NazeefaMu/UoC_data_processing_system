## Title: ‘Poth Gullee’ back on stage
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Tuesday, December 12, 2017 - 01:00

‘Poth Gullee’ back on stage

Whyteleafe Performing Arts Academy will bring Nalaka Swarnathilake’s newest stage play ‘Poth Gullee’ back on stage for the school vacation. Nalaka, who was behind many children’s creations such as ‘Dangamalla’, ‘Anne’, ‘Mama Wenama Malak’ and ‘Pini Poda Wessak’, is now bringing his newest stage play ‘Poth Gullee’, a creation based on an award-winning children’s novel ‘Matilda’ by British author Roald Dahl. Poth Gullee will be staged on December 17 at Bishop’s College Auditorium, Colombo.
This drama tells the story of a bright little girl named Matilda, who is a child of wondrous intelligence. Unfortunately, Matilda is misunderstood by her family because she is very different from their ways of life. Ignored at home, Matilda escapes into a world of reading, exercising her mind so much she develops telekinetic powers. Drama is based on happenings in Matilda’s life at home and school.
The drama will feature talented artists Kusum Renu, Priyankara Rathnayaka, Ama Wijesekara, Sankara Wijerathna and 30 child artists from Nalaka’s Whyteleafe Performing Arts Academy who are studying at several schools in Colombo.
Nalaka Swarnathilake is well known for his children’s productions both on stage and on television and has established himself as one who strives to highlight the talents of children. Primary focus in his all creative works has been around several key topics like children, family and education.
