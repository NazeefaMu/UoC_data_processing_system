## Title: Saudi Prince to arrive in Sri Lanka to look for business opportunities
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, July 21, 2017 - 01:00

Saudi Prince to arrive in Sri Lanka to look for business opportunities

Prince Fahad Bin Muqrin Bin Abdulaziz Al-Saud, a leading businessman and a member of the House of Saud will arrive in Sri Lanka today with a delegation to explore investment opportunities.
According to the head of the National Programme on Ethnic Harmony, Abdul Cader Masoor Moulana, Prince Al-Saud will meet leaders of the Government, the Foreign Minister and business leaders among others. His visit will be coordinated by State Minister M.L.A.M. Hisbullah. Prince Al-Saud is also slated to visit the Eastern Province during his four day stay.
In addition to being a prominent businessman, Prince Al-Saud also leads several government institutions in Saudi Arabia.
