## Title: Suspect nabbed for house breaking
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, March 10, 2017 - 01:00

Suspect nabbed for house breaking

 A suspect who had broken into the house of a Sub Police Inspector was arrested by Maho Police in Kurunegala.
The suspect is an army deserter, 29, and father of one child from the Mawanella area. The suspect had worked in an estate in the guise of a watcher at the Sub-Inspector’s house. He had left his legal wife and had come to the estate with a girlfriend. He had broken into the house while on duty.
He is to be produced before the Maho Magistrate.
Investigations continue.
