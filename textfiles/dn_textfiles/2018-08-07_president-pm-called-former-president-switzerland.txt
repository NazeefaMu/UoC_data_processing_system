## Title: President, PM called on former President of Switzerland
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, August 7, 2018 - 01:00

President, PM called on former President of Switzerland

The former President of Switzerland Simonetta Sommaruga called on President Maithripala Sirisena yesterday. Picture by Sandaruwan Amarasinghe- President’s Media Division. 
Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe met a delegation led by the former President of Switzerland Simonetta Sommarugaat Temple Trees yesterday. Sommaruga is a member of the Federal Council and Head of Legal and Police Department. Secretaryto the Prime Minister Saman Ekanayake was present. Picture courtesy Prime Minister’s Media
