## Title: Female body exhumed from Rockwood estate
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 24, 2017 - 13:45

Female body exhumed from Rockwood estate

 
Hanguranketha Police exhumed a female body buried in Hewaheta, Rockwood estate, yesterday (24).
The body identified to be a twenty-eight-year-old mother of one named Sumatra Devi was buried in a grave sealed by cement.
Hanguranketha Police made this discovery with the complaint lodged by relations of the deceased saying that she was missing for two weeks.
The Police suspects the husband of the deceased who has fled from the area.
The body was handed over to the Nuwara-Eliya Hospital to conduct post-mortem examinations.
Hanguranketha Police is investigating.
