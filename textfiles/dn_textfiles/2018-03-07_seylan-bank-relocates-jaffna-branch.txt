## Title: Seylan Bank relocates Jaffna branch
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Wednesday, March 7, 2018 - 01:00

Seylan Bank relocates Jaffna branch

With a strong drive to provide convenient banking services, Seylan Bank ‘The bank with a Heart’ relocated its Jaffna branch to a spacious location recently.
The new branch is located at Stanley Road, Jaffna. Senior management of Seylan Bank, new and existing customers were welcomed to convey the Bank’s vision for the coming years on improving the services to better serve the customers in the northern region. Bringing the bank more closer to the customer, Seylan has been expanding its branch network across the island setting its footprint in all 25 districts. “Continuous improvement” being a part of the company values, it keeps up with the latest technology to ensure the delivery of a better and smooth banking experience.
