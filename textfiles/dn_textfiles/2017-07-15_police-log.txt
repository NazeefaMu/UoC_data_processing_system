## Title: Police Log
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, July 15, 2017 - 01:00

Police Log

Man knocked down by train
A man was knocked down by a train at the Imaduwa railway crossing in Ahangama.
The train was plying from Maradana to Matara.
According to the Police, the victim succumbed to his injuries after being admitted to Karapitiya Hospital.
The man was a 62-year-old resident of Ahangama, Imaduwa.
Man nabbed with grenade
A man who had a live grenade was arrested at Mihiripenna, Habaraduwa, by Habaraduwa Police. According to the Police, the explosive has been manufactured abroad. The suspect, 22, is a resident of Thalpe.
