## Title: Prince Edward, Countess to arrive in Sri Lanka today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 31, 2018 - 11:22

Prince Edward, Countess to arrive in Sri Lanka today

 
Queen Elizabeth II’s and Prince Phillip’s youngest son Prince Edward, Earl of Wessex and the Countess will arrive in Sri Lanka today to celebrate the 70th Independence Day.
The Prince and the Countess will participate in the event on behalf of the Queen to mark the longstanding relationships between Sri Lanka and United Kingdom.
He will attend the celebration as the Chief Guest following an invitation extended by the Foreign Affairs Ministry.
The 70th Independence Day will be held at the Galle Face Green on February 4 on the theme ‘One Nation’
