## Title: Summer Collection Runway 2017 on October 29
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Monday, September 25, 2017 - 01:00

Summer Collection Runway 2017 on October 29

Summer Collection Runway 2017 will be held on October 29, 2017 at BMICH and it is organized by NICK JASON’S MODEL (Modelling and Dancing Academy). The Summer Collection Runway 2017 show gives models a vital opportunity to start their modelling careers, providing a platform for Sri Lankan models to showcase their talent both locally and in the international arena.
