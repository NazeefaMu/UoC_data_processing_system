## Title: More thunder showers till April 16
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, April 11, 2018 - 01:00

More thunder showers till April 16

The Meteorological Department said showers or thunder showers will occur over most provinces of the island until April 16 after 2.00 p.m.
Showers will occur in the Eastern, Uva and Northern provinces and in Hambantota district during the morning.Heavy falls about 100 mm can be expected particularly at hilly areas at some places particularly in the Sabaragamuwa, Central, Western, North-western and Uva provinces and in the Galle and Matara districts.
Misty conditions can be expected at some places in the Sabaragamuwa and Central provinces during the morning.
The Met Department informs the public to take adequate precautions as severe lightning and gusty winds up to 70 to 80 kmph could be possible during thunder showers. The Met Department cautioned the public to avoid open areas and seek shelter preferably indoors and beware of fallen trees and power lines, avoid using wired telephones and connected electric appliances and avoid open vehicles during thunder showers.
