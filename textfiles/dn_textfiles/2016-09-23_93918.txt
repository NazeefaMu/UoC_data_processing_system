## Supertitle:

SINGER-DAILY NEWS MOST POPULAR  SCHOOLBOY RUGGERITE 2016    : Tight contest for  runner-up spot

 
While Royal College captain and number eight Nikhila Gunadheera maintained his lead at the top of the Singer-Daily News Most Popular Schoolboy Ruggerite 2016 contest, a battle has ensured between Royal College vice-captain and scrum half Ashok Wijekumar and Isipathana College’s number eight Nikhila Gunadheera for the runner-up spot.
Gunadheera who was displaced by Wijekumar the previous week has once again regained his position at number two.
Gunadheera has a total of 9201 votes to Wijekumar’s 9115 with only 86 votes separating the two. The rest of the positions remain unchanged with votes being received only for the top six ruggerites.
The weekly prize winner on the lucky draw is:
Ms S.H. Fathima Inshirah of 11/26-A, Rathwatta Mawatha, Badulla.
The winner will be contacted by Singer (Sri Lanka) PLC to collect the fabulous gift.
