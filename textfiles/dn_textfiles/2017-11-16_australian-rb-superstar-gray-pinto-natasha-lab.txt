Australian R&B Superstar Gray Pinto with Natasha at LAB

The Hilton Colombo presents Australian R&B sensation Gary Pinto together with the soulful Natasha this November at LAB, Hilton Colombo’s lounge and bar. The duo will be performing from November 14 to 17, 20 to 24 and 27– 30 at 7pm.
