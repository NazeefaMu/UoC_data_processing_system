## Title: Buddhist flag to be hoisted at all state office premises
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, April 27, 2018 - 01:00

Buddhist flag to be hoisted at all state office premises

Public Administration Ministry Secretary S. T. Kodikara had instructed all ministry secretaries and government departmental heads, to take steps to hoist the Buddhist flag in all state office premises during the Vesak week from April 26 to May 2, countrywide.
He had also instructed in a special circular, that government institutes should also be decorated for the event. About 250,000 government servants in the Kurunegala and Puttalam districts would observe sil at the Bingiriya Devagiri Raja Maha Viharaya, where the state Vesak festival had been planned to be held, Kurunegala District UNP Parliamentarian and Buddhasasana Minister Gamini Jayawickrama Perara said when he was contacted by the Daily News yesterday, in Kurunegala.
