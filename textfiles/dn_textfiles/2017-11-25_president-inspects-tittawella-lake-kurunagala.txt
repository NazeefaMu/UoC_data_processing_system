## Title: President inspects the Tittawella Lake in Kurunagala
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, November 25, 2017 - 01:00

President inspects the Tittawella Lake in Kurunagala

 President Maithripala Sirisena inspects the Tittawella Lake renovation work in Kurunagala yesterday.The cleaning up project has been undertaken by the Irrigation Department together with Sri Lankan Navy. Minister S.B. Nawinna looks on. (Picture by President's Media Division)
