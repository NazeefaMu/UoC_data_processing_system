## Title: Indian national arrested at BIA customs
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, May 19, 2018 - 12:30

Indian national arrested at BIA customs

An Indian national was arrested this morning at the customs section of Bandaranaike International Airport. He was allegedly attempting to smuggle 40 kilograms of 24 karat gold biscuits that were valued in total at around Rs. 40 million.
