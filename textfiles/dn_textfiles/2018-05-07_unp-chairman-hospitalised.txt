## Title: UNP Chairman hospitalised
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, May 7, 2018 - 01:00

UNP Chairman hospitalised

United National Party (UNP) Chairman Minister Kabir Hashim was admitted to a private hospital last Saturday (5).
According to his media secretary, Minister Hashim was hospitalised to receive medical treatment concerning a pharyngeal (throat) disorder. He is to undergo a minor surgery.
