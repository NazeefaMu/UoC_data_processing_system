## Title: ‘Angoda Lokka’s close associate ‘Solta’ arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, June 19, 2018 - 01:02

‘Angoda Lokka’s close associate ‘Solta’ arrested

Asitha Somathilaka alias ‘Solta’, a close associate of underworld criminal ‘Angoda Lokka, was arrested by the Colombo Crimes Division (CCD) in Borella yesterday in connection with two recent murders at Kottawa and Malabe, Police Spokesman SP Ruwan Gunasekara said.
The suspect has been arrested in connection with the murder of beautician Sanduni Abeyratne who was shot dead in the presence of her seven-year-old son and five-year-old daughter in Rukmale, Kottawa on November 30, 2017 and the killing of a millionaire businessman Roshan Premalal who was shot while strolling along the jogging track at Chandrika Kumaratunge road, in Malabe on April 25 this year.
Gunasekera said ‘Solta’ was arrested with the 9 mm pistol which he allegedly used in the above two murders. The suspect also had in his possession a magazine for the firearm, live bullets and close to 7 grams of heroin which has a street value of over Rs.500,000. The 33-year-old suspect, a resident of Mulleriyawa had been arrested from a hideout in the Borella area.
