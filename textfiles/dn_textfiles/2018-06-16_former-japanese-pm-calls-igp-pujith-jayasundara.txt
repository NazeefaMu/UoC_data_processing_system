## Title: Former Japanese PM calls on  IGP Pujith Jayasundara
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, June 16, 2018 - 01:00

Former Japanese PM calls on  IGP Pujith Jayasundara

A visiting Japanese delegation led by former Prime Minister Yakio Hatoyama visited the Police Headquarters in Colombo on a familiarisation tour on June 13.They later attended a special discussion held a with IGP Pujith Jayasundara and top brass at the IGP’s conference room where the police community service figured in the discussion.
