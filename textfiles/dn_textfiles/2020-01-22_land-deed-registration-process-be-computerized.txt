## Title: Land deed registration process to be computerized
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 22, 2020 - 11:16

Land deed registration process to be computerized

The Registrar General's Department has decided to register land deeds through new software.Aim of this program is to reduce the amount of fraud that occurs with land deeds, said the Registrar General N.C. Withanage.He said that regarding fake land deeds they receive over 3000 complaints annually.The new system is scheduled to be introduced by next month.
