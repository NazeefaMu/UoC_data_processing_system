## Title: Dengue eradication programme in Ampara District schools
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, August 3, 2017 - 01:00

Dengue eradication programme in Ampara District schools

Schools in Ampara District have implemented a dengue eradication campaign in school premises, recently. Dengue is on the rise in the Ampara district and 30 percent of deaths due to dengue are of  schoolchildren, according to the statistics of the Ministry of Health. 
 
The Ministry of Education issued a circular to keep school environments free from dengue mosquitoes. Students, along with their parents, participated in the programme.
