## Title: JNP visit N-E  archaeological sites
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Friday, October 5, 2018 - 01:00

JNP visit N-E  archaeological sites

A group of Jathika Nidahas Peramuna representatives visited archaeological sites in Mullaitivu, Oddusudan and Kurudumalai on an observation tour recently.
Parliamentarian Padma Udayashantha said the objective of their visit was to ascertain the truth regarding the destruction caused to archaeological sites in the North and East.
After the tour they hoped to bring the present situation to the attention of the government and the officials of the Archaeology Department.
Udayashantha said they observed the destruction caused to archaeological sites in Vavuniya, Mullaitivu, Trincomalee, Ampara and Batticaloa during the tour.
He said the government and the department should pay serious attention to the destruction caused to these sites.
The Jathika Nidahas Peramuna group included Parliamentarians Jayantha Samaraweera and Padma Udayashantha, media spokesman Mohamed Muzammil and several others.
