## Title: Telco operators to grant 10% bonus data on Internet packages
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Wednesday, August 23, 2017 - 01:00

Telco operators to grant 10% bonus data on Internet packages

Licensed telecommunications operators announced that all mobile and fixed line subscribers will be granted a 10% bonus usage on Internet packages/cards as a special gesture to mark the removal of the telco levy by the Government. Telco operators in a joint statement said that the removal of the telco levy on Internet by the Government is a forward looking move towards improving affordability and creation of a digital Sri Lanka.
