## Title: Bus conductor arrested with heroin
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Friday, December 28, 2018 - 01:00

Bus conductor arrested with heroin

A private bus conductor of Kurunegala was arrested with 1.720 g of heroin on December 24.
Produced before the Kurunegala Chief Magistrate Sampath Hewawasam, the suspect was remanded
He is identified as M. Harshan Hajleem of Kurunegala.
Kurunegala Police OIC IP Nawaratne Abeykoon led the arrest.
Over1,236 suspects were arrested in Kurunegala in the past 10 months for drug-related incidents, police said.
