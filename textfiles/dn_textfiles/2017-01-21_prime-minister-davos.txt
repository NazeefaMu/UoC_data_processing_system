## Title: Prime Minister in Davos
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, January 21, 2017 - 01:00

Prime Minister in Davos

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe, who is in Davos to participate in the World Economic Forum, held a series of discussions with leaders of many countries, heads of UN bodies and leading businessmen. Their discussion focused on a gamut of issues including strengthening bilateral relations, human rights, peace and reconciliation in the post-war Sri Lanka.
Leaders of many countries including Switzerland President Doris Leuthard, Norwegian Prime Minister Erna Solberg, and Vietnam Prime Minister Nguyen Xuan Phuc expressed their happiness over the progress made by Sri Lanka in the field of human rights, development and reconciliation in the aftermath of protracted war when they met the Sri Lanka Premier in Davos
