## Title: Hiruna – match bag of 12 for 53
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, October 30, 2018 - 01:00

Hiruna – match bag of 12 for 53

A superb match bag of 12 for 53 (6/13 and 6/40) runs by left arm leg spinner Hiruna Nimsara enabled Gurukula Vidyalaya, Kelaniya to defeat Basilica College Ragama by 30 runs on first innings in their inter-school under 13 division one tournament cricket match played at Ragama.
Batting first Basilica were all out for 75 runs and Gurukulians declared at 105 for 5 with Denura Demansith scoring 54 runs and in their second essay homesters were all out for 85 runs and at the close visitors were 23 for 3.
Basilica College – 75 in 52 overs – (Hiruna Nimsara 6/13, Harshana Janith 2/19 and 85 – Shashek Himendra 21, Hiruna Nimsara 6/40, Harshana Janith 3/27).
Gurukula Vidyalaya – 105/5 in 14.1 overs – (Denura Demansith 54, Vagee Shamantha 28, Lakruwan Dias 3/13 and 23/3).
