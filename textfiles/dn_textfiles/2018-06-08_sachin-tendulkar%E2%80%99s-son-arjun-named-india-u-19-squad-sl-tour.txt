## Title: Sachin Tendulkar’s Son Arjun named in India U-19 squad for SL tour
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, June 8, 2018 - 10:35

Sachin Tendulkar’s Son Arjun named in India U-19 squad for SL tour

 
Indian Cricketer Sachin Tendulkar’s son Arjun Tendulkar was included in the Indian under 19 squad for the two, four day matches to be held in Sri Lanka next month.
The 18-year-old is a left arm fast bowler and a lower middle order batsman.
