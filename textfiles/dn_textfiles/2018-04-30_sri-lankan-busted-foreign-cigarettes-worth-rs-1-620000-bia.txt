## Title: Sri Lankan busted with foreign cigarettes worth Rs. 1, 620,000 at BIA
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, April 30, 2018 - 11:53

Sri Lankan busted with foreign cigarettes worth Rs. 1, 620,000 at BIA

 
A 36-year-old Sri Lankan was apprehended by the Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA) Customs while attempting to smuggle in 162 cartoons of foreign cigarettes worth Rs. 1, 620,000 from Dubai this morning.
The suspect is a resident of Uhumeeya, Narammala.
Further investigations are underway.
