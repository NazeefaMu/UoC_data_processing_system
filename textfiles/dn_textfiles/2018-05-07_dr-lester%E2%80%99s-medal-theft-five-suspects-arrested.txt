## Title: Dr. Lester’s medal theft: Five suspects arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, May 7, 2018 - 12:17

Dr. Lester’s medal theft: Five suspects arrested

 
Five suspects who are alleged to have been involved in stealing late Dr. Lester James Peries’s ‘Golden Peacock Medal’ were arrested by the Police this morning.
The medal which went missing during the funeral of Dr. Lester James Peries, at his residence was found from a 177 Kaduwela – Colpetty private bus last Saturday morning.
Dr. Lester James Peries received the medal along with the Golden Peacock Award in 1965 at the International Film Festival of India (IFFI) for directing Gamperaliya film.
