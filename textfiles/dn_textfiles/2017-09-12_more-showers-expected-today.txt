## Title: More showers expected today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, September 12, 2017 - 11:00

More showers expected today

 
The Meteorology Department said that few places in Western, Sabaragamuwa and Southern provinces will experience showers today.
Showers or thunder showers are to be expected in the North-central province and in Matale, Mathara and Hambantota districts after 2.00 p.m.
