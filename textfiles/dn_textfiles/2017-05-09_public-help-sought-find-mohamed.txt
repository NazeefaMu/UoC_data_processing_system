## Title: Public help sought to find Mohamed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 01:00

Public help sought to find Mohamed

Public information is sought to help trace Mohamed Ifthikar Amit (26), who is reported missing from his residence at No. 47, Kerr Lane, Colombo 2, according to a complaint made to the Slave Island Police Station by his aunt Fathima Rishka Ahamed.
He is fair and 6 ft tall and said to be suffering from a mental disorder.
He was last seen in a blue t-shirt and a blue checked pair of shorts.
Any information regarding him should be furnished to the nearest police station or to Fathima Rishka Ahamed on 011-2302050.
