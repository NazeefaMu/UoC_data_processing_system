## Title: Thilanga Sumathipala voted SLC President - UPDATED
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Sunday, January 3, 2016 - 11:00

Thilanga Sumathipala voted SLC President - UPDATED

Thilanga Sumathipala was been elected the President of Sri Lanka Cricket (SLC) receiving 88 votes a short while ago. Nishantha Ranathunga who ran for the same office received 56 votes.
Other newly elected members :
 
Secretary  : Mohan de Silva
 
Treasurer :  Shammi Silva
 
Vice Presidents : Jayantha Dharmadasa, K Mathiwanan
