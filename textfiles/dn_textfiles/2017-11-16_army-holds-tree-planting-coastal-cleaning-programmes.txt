## Title: Army holds tree planting, coastal cleaning programmes
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 16, 2017 - 01:00

Army holds tree planting, coastal cleaning programmes

The 233 Brigade Headquarters of the Security Force Headquarters - East (SFHQ-E), determined to join the ongoing environment-friendly
Green Revolution, took steps to launch a tree-planting campaign plus a coastal cleaning programme in the general area of Vakarai on Sunday (12).
The 233 Brigade Headquarters, in coordination with the 18 Sri Lanka National Guard (SLNG) troops, provided saplings of coconuts, pines and mangroves at the invitation of 233 Brigade Commander Colonel Chandra Jayaweera.
Similarly, the 18 Sri Lanka National Guard troops of the 233 Brigade in Vakarai joined hands and cleaned up the coastal beach in Vakarai during a shramadana campaign that consumed the entire day.
During the programme, 40 coconut saplings, 20 twisted pines and 150 mangrove saplings were planted, covering the entire beach front in Vakarai.
The programme was conducted on the instructions of 23 Division General Officer Commanding (GOC) Brigadier Chula Abenayake.
A large number of troops from the 18 SLNG and the 233 Brigade participated in the programme.
