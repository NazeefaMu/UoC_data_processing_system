## Title: Jupiter House – champions
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 01:00

Jupiter House – champions

Jupiter House emerged champions at the Inter-House Sports Meet of Colombo South International College, held at the Narahenpita Shalika grounds recently. Here the chief guest Colombo University Law Section Senior Lecturer Mahanama Punchihewa presenting a trophy to the skipper of the victorious house. College Managing Director Mangala Wijekularatne and Principal Richmond Suraweera were also present. (Mahanama Vithanage, Malwana Group Correspondent)
