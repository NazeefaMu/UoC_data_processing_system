## Title: President resolves land scarcity issue of AETI
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, September 15, 2017 - 01:00

President resolves land scarcity issue of AETI

Fulfilling a long felt need, President Maithripala Sirisena on Wednesday, donated a three acre land to the Automobile Engineering Training Institute (AETI) for its’ further expansion projects.
The President made this donation after commissioning a new building complex of the AETI of the National Apprentice and Industrial Training Authority at Orugodawatte on Wednesday.
Skills Development and Vocational Training Minister Chandima Weerakkody notified the President that the shortage of land for expansion of the AEIT is a major hindrance to its progress, the President immediately issued instructions to the relevant officers to solve the land issue. Meanwhile the President instructed the officers to offer a set of modern vehicles, consisting of modern automobile technology for practical tests of the apprentices. Currently the apprentices conduct their practical training using a set of old vehicles, given to them in 1990.
