## Title: Death of Nalini Indatissa
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, December 9, 2017 - 01:00

Death of Nalini Indatissa

Nalini Indatissa, wife of late Sarath Indatissa passed away. She was the former Director of Education, former Principal Thalangamuwa Central College, Pinnawala Central College, Royal International School,Kegalle. She was the mother of Kalinga Indatissa (President’s Counsel and Nalinda Indatissa (Attorney-at-law). Her remains lie at No. 20, 1st lane, Epitamulla Road, Pita Kotte. Funeral arrangements will be notified later.
