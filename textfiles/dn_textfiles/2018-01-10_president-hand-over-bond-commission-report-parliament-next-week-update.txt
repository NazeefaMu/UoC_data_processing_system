## Title: President to hand over Bond Commission report to Parliament next week - UPDATE
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 10, 2018 - 10:45

President to hand over Bond Commission report to Parliament next week - UPDATE

 
President Maithripala Sirisena informed Speaker of Parliament Karu Jayasuriya, that the Bond Commission report will be handed over to Parliament next week.
A special Bond Commission report is due to be presented in Parliament today as requested by Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe.
The debate which was scheduled to be held today was delayed until further notice.
