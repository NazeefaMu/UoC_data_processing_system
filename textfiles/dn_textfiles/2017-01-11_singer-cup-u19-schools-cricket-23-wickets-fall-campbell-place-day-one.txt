## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, January 11, 2017 - 01:00

Singer Cup U19 Schools Cricket : 23 wickets fall at Campbell Place on day one

Twenty-three wickets fell on the opening day of the Singer Cup under 19 inter-schools cricket match between D. S. Senanayake and Nalanda at Campbell Place yesterday.
Muditha Lakshan of D. S. Senanayake took 7 wickets for 30 runs as Nalanda were bowled out for 116 in reply to DSS first innings of 163.
Sri Lanka youth cap Lasith Croosepulle of Maris Stella College Negombo scored his second ton (151) in the Singer Cup under 19 inter-schools cricket tournament to guide his school to an innings and 79 runs win over Bandaranayake College Gampaha yesterday.
Maliyadeva College Kurunegala and Dharmasoka College Ambalangoda were also in a winning way beating President’s College Rajagiriya and Anuradhapura Central College respectively.
Other teams to record wins were Sri Sumangala College Panadura who beat Siri Piyaratana MMV Padukka, St. Sebastian’s College Katuneriya beat Kingswood College Kandy, Azhar College Akurana beat Jinaraja Boys School Gampola and Sir John Kotalawala MV Kurunegala beat Nugawela Central College.
Lahiru Thambavita of Holy Cross Kalutara recorded his third ton of the season when he made 108 against Taxila Central Horana. Kalhara Shaminda (Kirindiwela Central) and Damitha Silva (Maliyadeva Kurunegala) scored their second hundreds for the season. Dilan Chathuranga (St. Benedict’s Kotahena) and Nawseen Arkam (Azhar Akurana) also scored centuries.
Wazeer Ashkar of Azhar Akurana took a match bag of 12 wickets against Jinaraja Boys Gampola, while Dhananjaya Perera of St. Joseph Vaz took 11 wickets against St. Mary’s Kegalle and Janindu Induwara (Sri Sumangala) had match figures of 10 for 56 against Siri Piyaratana Padukka.
