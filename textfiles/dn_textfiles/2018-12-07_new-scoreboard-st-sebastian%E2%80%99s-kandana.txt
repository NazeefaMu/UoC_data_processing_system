## Title: New scoreboard for St Sebastian’s, Kandana
## Supertitle: New scoreboard for St Sebastian’s, Kandana
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, December 7, 2018 - 01:00

New scoreboard for St Sebastian’s, Kandana
New scoreboard for St Sebastian’s, Kandana

A long felt dream for a Scoreboard for St. Sebastians College Kandana became a reality when W. A. M. S. Abeywickrama the father of an under 13 cricketer of the College Nimshan Abeywickrama donated a score board worth Rupees 25,000 recently and it was declared open by the Principal of the School Rev. Fr. Sudath Hemantha Fernando. 
After the opening ceremony the donor and the school officials posed for a photograph.
