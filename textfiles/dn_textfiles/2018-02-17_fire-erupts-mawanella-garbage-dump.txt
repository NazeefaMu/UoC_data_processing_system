## Title: Fire erupts at Mawanella garbage dump
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, February 17, 2018 - 01:00

Fire erupts at Mawanella garbage dump

A fire has erupted within the garbage dump on the Randiwela mountain peak in Mawanella.
The dump site has been maintained by the Mawanella Pradeshiya Sabha, which dumps over 15 tractors of garbage from the area daily.
The fire started in the early hours of yesterday was still raging, residents said.
Four acres of the Kirigala rubber estate were damaged and the dry weather has intensified the fire .
The residents complained that officials were not taking adequate measure to quell the fire and there was a risk of the garbage dump toppling and causing damage to the environment and the residents.
The fire and smoke has impacted residents in Karapone, Yatimahana and Kirigalawatte areas.
