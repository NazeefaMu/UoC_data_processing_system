## Title: Adhishtana Pahan pooja
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, March 8, 2017 - 01:00

Adhishtana Pahan pooja

Ashirvada Adhishtana Abathel Pahan pooja to invoke blessings of the deities on the people of Sri Lanka, especially on the female population in lieu of the International Women’s Day, will be conducted by Ven. Deegala Piyadassi Thera at Nawagamuwa Paththini Devalaya, Kaduwela at 6.30pm today.
