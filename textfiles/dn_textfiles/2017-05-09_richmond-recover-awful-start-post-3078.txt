## Title: Richmond recover from awful start to post 307/8
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 01:00

Richmond recover from awful start to post 307/8

Richmond College, Galle scored 307/8 against Ananda College, Colombo on day one of the Singer Cup U-19 inter schools Division one two-day cricket tournament final at the Thurstan ground yesterday.
Richmond College won the toss and decided to bat. Avindu Theekshana scored 80 runs in 160 balls with 10 boundaries. No. 9 batsman Sandun Mendis scored unbeaten 64 runs in 99 balls with 9 boundaries and a six. Sandun and Duveen Kalansooriya made unbroken 105-run stand for the ninth wicket.
Richmond started with an awful batting display where Ananda bowlers skittled out their four top order batsmen for just 22 runs. Avindu Theekshana and Thaveesha Abhishek made 76 runs stand for the fifth wicket. Avindu also partnered a 68-run stand with Kasun Tharaka for the seventh wicket. Supun Waragoda took 4/91 and Ashel Sigera took 2 wickets. The match will conclude today.
Richmond: 307/8 in 95 overs (Avindu Theekshana 80, Sandun Mendis 63 n.o., Thaveesha Abhishek 46, Duveen Kalansooriya 36 n.o., Kasun Tharaka 25, Ravishka Wijesiri 21, Dhananjaya Lakshan 12, Supun Waragoda 4/91, Ashel Sigera 2/80) vs Ananda - YK
