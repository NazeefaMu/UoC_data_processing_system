## Title: Vehicle entry restricted in Colombo –Hanwella, Sedawatta, Ambatale Junction, Kelanimulla line
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, February 22, 2018 - 17:04

Vehicle entry restricted in Colombo –Hanwella, Sedawatta, Ambatale Junction, Kelanimulla line

 
Vehicle entry at the Colombo-Hanwella, Sedawatta, Ambatale Junction to Kelanimulla will be restricted commencing from today until August 15 due to pipeline installations.
The Police advised commuters to use alternate routes to ease traffic.
