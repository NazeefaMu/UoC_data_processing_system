## Title: NDB appoints Fairfirst as insurance provider for Araliya
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Wednesday, July 12, 2017 - 01:00

NDB appoints Fairfirst as insurance provider for Araliya

NDB recently signed an agreement with Fairfirst Insurance as an exclusive partner for NDB’s newly launched women’s savings account ‘Araliya’.
“We are happy to join hands with Fairfirst and look forward for their support in providing our customers a unique and timely benefit through NDB Araliya,” said NDB Director/CEO Dimantha Seneviratne, at the signing of the agreement.
NDB believes that this new partnership will in return strengthen its product proposition to its selected market segment. Commenting on this partnership, Fairfirst Insurance said, “We are continuously widening our strategic partnerships with corporates and we are thrilled to have NBD as a business partner.” NDB remains dedicated to bringing its customers one step closer to accomplishing their life-long ambitions through the range of services that are accessible through its growing branch network.
