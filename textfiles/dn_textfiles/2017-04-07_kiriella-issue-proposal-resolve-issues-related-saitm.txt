## Title: Kiriella to issue a proposal to resolve issues related to SAITM
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, April 7, 2017 - 14:15

Kiriella to issue a proposal to resolve issues related to SAITM

 
Higher Education Minister Lakshman Kiriella said that a set of proposals to resolve issues related to the South Asian Institute of Technology and Medicine will be issued today by his secretary.
He said this while addressing the Parliament today.
