## Title: Ground frost affects Nuwara Eliya crops
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, January 6, 2018 - 01:00

Ground frost affects Nuwara Eliya crops

The Nuwara Eliya district is experiencing the fall of frozen dew or ground frost due to the drop in temperature. Residents of Nuwara Eliya say that the ground frost is harmful to vegetable cultivation and tea plantation.
The farmers point out that vegetable and tea harvests will decrease due to the falling of ground frost.
