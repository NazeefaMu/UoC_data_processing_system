## Title: ‘Nidahase Piya D S’  unveiled to the media
## Supertitle: 
## Section: T&C
## Date: Tuesday, September 18, 2018 - 01:00

‘Nidahase Piya D S’  unveiled to the media

‘Nidahase Piya D S’, the film based on the life story of the late D S Senanayake who had pioneered the leadership towards the country’s freedom held its media preview recently. Higher Education and Cultural Affairs Minister Dr Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe graced the event. Many artistes and journalists attended.
