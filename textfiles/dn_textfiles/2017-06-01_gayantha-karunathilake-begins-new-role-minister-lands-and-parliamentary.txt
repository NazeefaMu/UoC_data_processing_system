## Title: Gayantha Karunathilake begins new role as Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 1, 2017 - 12:45

Gayantha Karunathilake begins new role as Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms

 
New Minister of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms Gayantha Karunathilake settled in his new role today.
After assuming duties, Minister Karunathilake convened a special meeting with all the heads of departments and agencies under his Ministry at the auditorium of the Ministry of Lands in Battaramulla.
The Minister discussed about the future plans of the government and the steps to be taken to implement the national program to distribute one million deeds.
Secretary of the Ministry of Lands Dr. I.H.K Mahanama and officials representing departments under the Ministry of Lands and Parliamentary Reforms participated for the occasion.
