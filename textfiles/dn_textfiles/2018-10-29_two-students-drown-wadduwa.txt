## Title: Two students drown in Wadduwa
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 29, 2018 - 01:00

Two students drown in Wadduwa

Two students of Wadduwa Central College drowned in the sea of Wadduwa and their bodies were found floating in Koralawella, Moratuwa seas by fishermen on Friday, Police said.
Six boys had gone for swimming in the Wadduwa sea after the school on October 26 at 4.30 p.m. and suddenly all of them were caught by a wave and two boys drowned, Police said. Four boys were rescused by those who were on the beach at that time and admitted to the Panadura Base Hospital.
One boy was discharged, hospital sources said.
The deceased was identified as Hewafonskeage Amantha Lakman (17).
