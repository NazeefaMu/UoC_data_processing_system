## Title: Thajudeen appointed District coach
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, December 5, 2015 - 01:00

Thajudeen appointed District coach

Addalaichenai Divisional Secretariat Sports Officer S.L.Thajudeen was appointed Ampara District Athletic Coach. Thajudeen is a top sportsman who represented Addalaichenai division and has excelled as a sportsman, coach and administrator. Coach Thajudeen is a past student of the Addalaichenai Al-Muneera Vidyalaya and Addalaichenai Central College (National School).
He has served as the Sports Officer at the Addalaichenai Divisional Secretariat and later served at Akkaraipattu, Sammanthurai and Nintavur DS Divisions. He was the serving at Divisional Secretariat in Addalaichenai.
