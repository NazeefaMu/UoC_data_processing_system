## Title: Moves to set up 900 dialysis units
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 13, 2017 - 01:00

Moves to set up 900 dialysis units

About 900 dialysis units will be established in the country with Indian assistance, Health Minister Dr. Rajitha Senaratne said.
The minister said he will provide six doctors and nurses for the new dialysis unit set up at Medawachchiya at a cost of Rs. 4.5 million. Medical equipment worth Rs. 5 million have been installed at the centre.
Dr. Senaratne said he will provide three doctors and six nurses for the dialysis unit at the Kebithigollewa Base Hospital and provide seven doctors and 15 nurses for the dialysis unit at the Padaviya Base Hospital.
A new kidney treatment hospital with 250 beds will be constructed in Polonnaruwa. A mobile dialysis service will also commence, the minister said.
