## Title: NIC applications for OL students
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, March 13, 2018 - 01:00

NIC applications for OL students

Registration of Persons Department Commissioner-General Viyani Gunathilaka said applications for NICs would shortly be issued to all students sitting for the G.C.E. Ordinary Level examinations.He informed that 350,000 students would be issued identity cards, this year. All school principals and students have been informed of the issuance of NIC applications.
The Registration of Persons Department has sent a government circular to all schools, communicating the decision to issue NICs to children above 15 years.
Applicants should attach their photographs and birth certificate certified by the respective District or Divisional Secretary, along with the application.
Applications should be sent through school principals. The duly-filled applications must be sent to the Registration of Persons Department Head Office at Suhurupaya, Battaramulla, on or before March 31.
