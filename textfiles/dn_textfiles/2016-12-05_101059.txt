## Title: Three nabbed with rare sea turtle
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, December 5, 2016 - 01:00

Three nabbed with rare sea turtle

 
 Naval personnel attached to SLNS Buwaneka in Nachchikuda belonging to the North Central Naval Command arrested three persons who trapped a rare species of sea turtle in Anthonipuram area and were attempting to transfer the turtle. The sea turtle weighing around 60 kg was released into the sea. The suspects were handed over to the Mannar Fisheries Inspector for further investigations.
