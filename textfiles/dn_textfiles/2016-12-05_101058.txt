## Title: Watery grave in Devon Falls for Rajagiriya youth
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, December 5, 2016 - 01:00

Watery grave in Devon Falls for Rajagiriya youth

 
A 21-year-old drowned when he ventured into the Devon Falls for a bath with a group of 14 friends on Saturday.
The victim was identified as Waseem Thuwel of Rajagiriya.
His body which had got stuck under a rock in the bed of the waterway was retrieved by Dimbula Police during a search operation carried out with the help of neigbours.
The inquirer into sudden deaths, Patana, Dimbula, Sumanasiri Alwis who conducted the inquest, recorded a verdict of death due to asphyxiation from drowning.
