## Title: Uva Wellassa University closed due to spread of Chickenpox
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, March 13, 2018 - 11:38

Uva Wellassa University closed due to spread of Chickenpox

 
The Uva Wellassa University was closed today until further notice due to the spread of Chicken pox, Vice Chancellor Dr. Jayantha Lal Ratnesekera said.
He added that twenty-seven hostellers who were infected with the decease were sent home immediately and the non academic activities were suspended.
