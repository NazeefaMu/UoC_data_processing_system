## Title: Falling jak branch kills nine-year-old girl
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, January 28, 2017 - 01:00

Falling jak branch kills nine-year-old girl

A nine-year-old girl playing on the road by her residence in Opanaike was injured when a jak branch fell on her on February 25. She later succumbed to her injuries while being transferred to the Colombo National Hospital from the Ratnapura hospital. She was identified as Tharushi Pramodya Sathsarani. Opanaike police is investigating.
