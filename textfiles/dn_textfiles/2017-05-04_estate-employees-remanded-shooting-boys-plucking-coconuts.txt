## Title: Estate employees remanded for shooting boys plucking coconuts
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 4, 2017 - 01:00

Estate employees remanded for shooting boys plucking coconuts

Kuliyapitiya Magistrate Dhammika Dissanayake remanded the Assistant Manager and the Watcher of a coconut plantation belonging to the National Livestock Development Board (NLDB) for shooting at two boys plucking coconuts illegally.
The suspects are employees of Galpokuna NLDB Estate in Udubaddawa, Kurunegala. The boys who were shot at, are 15 and 19 years. The Assistant Manager had shot at the boys with the Watcher’s gun, it was reported.
The suspects were remanded until May 5.
Kuliyapitiya Police are investigating.
