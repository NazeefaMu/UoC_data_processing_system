## Title: Lake House Amadahara Vesak Zone- second day
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, May 12, 2017 - 01:00

Lake House Amadahara Vesak Zone- second day

The second day of the Amadahara Vesak Zone organised by the Associated Newspapers of Ceylon Limited (ANCL) in view of the International Vesak Week, was inaugurated by Deputy Minister of Parliamentary Reforms and Media Minister Karunarathna Paranawithana yesterday. The highlight of the Vesak Zone this year is the instrumental rendition of devotional songs by the percussion bands of the Army, Navy and the Police. The Navy percussion band performed yesterday. ANCL Chairman Kavan Ratnayaka,Board of Directors, members of the ANCL senior management and staff were also present.
