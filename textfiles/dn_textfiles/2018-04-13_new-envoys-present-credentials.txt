## Title: New Envoys present credentials
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, April 13, 2018 - 01:00

New Envoys present credentials

Five envoys presented their credentials to President Maithripala Sirisena in Colombo, yesterday. They were Moroccan Ambassador to Sri Lanka Mohamed Maliki, Austrian Ambassador to Sri Lanka Brigitte Öppinger- Walchshofer, United Arab Emirates Ambassador to Sri Lanka Sheikh Ahmed Hamad Al Mualla, Polish Ambassador to Sri Lanka Adam Burakowski, Tanzanian High Commissioner to Sri Lanka Baraka Haran Luvanda.Pictures by Sudath Silva.
