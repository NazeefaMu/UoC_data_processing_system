## Title: Police look for red car
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, May 12, 2017 - 01:00

Police look for red car

Thalangama Police are looking for a red car fled away after colliding with a three-wheeler near the Diyatha Uyana in Battaramulla.
The incident took place yesterday at about 11.40 am.
The three wheeler driver had escaped with minor injuries.
Investigations are continuing.
