## Title: Wasantha Senanayake assumes duty as State Minister of Foreign Affairs
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, June 2, 2017 - 14:45

Wasantha Senanayake assumes duty as State Minister of Foreign Affairs

 
Newly appointed State Minister for Foreign Affairs, Wasantha Senanayake assumed duties today at the Foreign Ministry.
President Maithripala Sirisena made the appointment on May 31.
