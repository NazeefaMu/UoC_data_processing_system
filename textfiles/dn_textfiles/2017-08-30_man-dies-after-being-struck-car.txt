## Title: Man dies after being struck by a car
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 30, 2017 - 13:00

Man dies after being struck by a car

 
A man died after being struck by a car plying on  Kurunegala, Kandy road near Mawathagama last night.
He was rushed to Mawathagama Hospital where he succumbed to his injuries.
Police said that the driver fled from the scene but later surrendered to the Katugasthota Police station
The deceased has not yet been identified.
The body is lying at the Mawathagama Hospitals’ morgue
Post-mortem examinations  will be conducted today.
