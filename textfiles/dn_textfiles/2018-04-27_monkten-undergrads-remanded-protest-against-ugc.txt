## Title: Monk,ten undergrads remanded in protest against UGC
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, April 27, 2018 - 01:00

Monk,ten undergrads remanded in protest against UGC

Police yesterday arrested and remanded ten undergrads including a monk for forcibly entering the University Grants Commission premises during the protest organized by the Inter University Bhikkhu Federation.
They have been remanded till May 10 after being produced before the Colombo Fort Magistrate yesterday.The Federation launched the protest citing that the government has curtailed the entrolment of students for Buddhist Philosophy and Buddhist Studies for the 2016 and 2017 academic year
Police dispersed protesting students using tear gas and water cannons.Heavy traffic prevailed around Town Hall and the adjacent areas due to this protest march.
