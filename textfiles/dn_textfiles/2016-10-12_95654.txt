## Title: Colombo University women’s Elle champs
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, October 12, 2016 - 01:00

Colombo University women’s Elle champs

Colombo University clinched the inter-University Sports Festival’s women’s Elle title beating Wayamba University in the final at the Kuliyapitiya, Wayamba University grounds recently.
Fifteen teams teams from Universities across the country participated.
The champions Colombo University team was coached by a veteran Elle player cum a coach, Krishantha Ranasinghe and Sports Instructrous Wasantha Rathnayaka was the team manager.
