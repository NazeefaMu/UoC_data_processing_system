## Title: Sri Lanka Soviet Russia Relations
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, March 27, 2017 - 01:00

Sri Lanka Soviet Russia Relations

Concurrent to President Maithripala Sirisena’s state visit to Russia, a book on “Sri Lanka Soviet Russia Relations” compiled by Sirimanna Karunatilleke was presented to the President at the Presidential Secretariat, Colombo recently. Russian Embassy Director Anaspsia Khokhlova, National Library Services and Documentation Board Chairman Dr. W.A. Abeysinghe, Russian Embassy Chief Executive Buddhapriya Ramanayake, Publisher Deshabandu Suimansiri Godage and “Rusiyawa’ Magazine Editor W.A. Sarath Lal Kumara were present.
