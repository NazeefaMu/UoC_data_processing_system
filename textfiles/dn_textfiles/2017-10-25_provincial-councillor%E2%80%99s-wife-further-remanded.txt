## Title: Provincial Councillor’s wife further remanded
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, October 25, 2017 - 01:00

Provincial Councillor’s wife further remanded

A Provincial Councillor’s wife, Harjot Kaur, was remanded till Oct 31 after she was produced before the Wattala Magistrate’s Court by Wattala police yesterday. She was in custody in connection with the shooting incident in Divulapitiya on Monday.
