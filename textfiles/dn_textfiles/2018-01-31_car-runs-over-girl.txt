## Title: Car runs over girl
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 31, 2018 - 01:00

Car runs over girl

A car plying towards Na Ula knocked down a 13-year-old girl carrying a little girl of one and half years of age near the Nalanda Gedi Ge Junction on the Jaffna-Kandy Road on January 20. They were admitted to the Nalanda Hospital and later transferred to the Matale Hospital where the 13-year-old girl succumbed to her injuries.
The deceased was identified as Kaushalya Sewandi, a resident of Mollipothana, Kanthale. The body is kept at the Matale Hospital mortuary for the post mortem.
The car driver was arrested.
Investigations are continuing.
