## Title: Chief Government Whip issues order: All govt. parliamentarians, minsiters to oppose NCM
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, April 3, 2018 - 21:24

Chief Government Whip issues order: All govt. parliamentarians, minsiters to oppose NCM

Lands and Parliamentary Reforms Minister and Chief Government Whip Gayantha Karunathilake had issued an order directing all parliamentarians and the ministers in the government, to vote against the No-confidence Motion moved against the Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe in parliament. The relevant order has been sent to all parliamentarians in the government by registered post.
The chief government whip has the authority to issue an order when an important election is to be held in parliament, in keeping with British parliamentary traditions.
If any member of the government were to object the order of the chief government whip, legal action could be taken against him/her using the letter as evidence, sources said.
