## Title: New water facility for Navatkuli Maha Vidyalaya
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, July 21, 2017 - 01:00

New water facility for Navatkuli Maha Vidyalaya

Troops of the 522 Brigade of the Security Force Headquarters - Jaffna (SFHQ-J), upon a request, set up a new drinking water facility in Navatkuli Maha Vidyalaya premises, recently.
This was on the guidelines of Major General Darshana Hettiarachchi, Commander, Security Forces - Jaffna (SF-J).
Members of the 522 Brigade offered patronage for the project after the requirement of the facility was brought to the notice of the Commander, SF-J when the children visited the recent Vesak Zone, organized by the SFHQ-J.
Accordingly, 522 Brigade commanded by Colonel Harendra Peiris implemented the project using the water transported by the Army from the Aiyyakachchi well.
The water tank and the pipe system, constructed by troops in the 522 Brigade provide several outlets in the school premises.
The drinking water facility project was recently inaugurated during a ceremony with the participation of Colonel Harendra Peiris, Commander, 522 Brigade as the Chief Guest, S. Pavalakumaran, Principal, Navatkuli Maha Vidyalaya, Senior Officers, teachers and students.
