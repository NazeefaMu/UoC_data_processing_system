## Title: Former Minister K.Velayutham passes away
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 13, 2015 - 12:30

Former Minister K.Velayutham passes away

Former Minister K. Velayutham passed away this afternoon at the age of 65. Velayutham was receiving care at a private hospital in Chennai, India when he passed away. K. Velayutham also served as the General Secretary of the Lanka Jathika Estate Workers Union.
