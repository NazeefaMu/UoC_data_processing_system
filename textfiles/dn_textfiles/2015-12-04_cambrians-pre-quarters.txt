## Title: Cambrians in pre-quarters
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, December 4, 2015 - 01:00

Cambrians in pre-quarters

Prince of Wales College entered into the pre-quarters by emerging as unbeaten group leaders of the second round of the under 13 division one cricket tournament when they beat Kalutara Vidyalaya by a massive 260 runs and played at Moratuwa.
Vidyalians batting first collapsed for a paltry 66 runs with a superb spell of bowling by Dinura Fernando who claimed 5 for 19 and at stumps Cambrians were 296 for 9 with Pasindu Pathum 54, Avishka Fernando 66 and Vinuja Ranpal 63 runs.
Kalutara Vidyalaya - 66 in 33.3 overs (Dinura Fernando 5/19, Pasindu Pathum 2/29).
Prince of Wales - 269/9 in 62 overs (Pasindu Pathum 54, Avishka Fernando 66, Vinuja Ranpal 63, Induwara de Alwis 25, Vidath Vivendra 26, Hashan Shevon 3/56, Vishara Sasmi 3/89)
