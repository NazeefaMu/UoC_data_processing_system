## Title: Kingfisher CA in seven wicket win
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, April 25, 2017 - 01:00

Kingfisher CA in seven wicket win

Kingfisher Cricket Academy beat St John’s Cricket Foundation, Hyderabad, India by seven wickets in their inter club under 14 cricket tournament match for the Nelson Mendis Trophy and played at Moratuwa.
Batting first visitors made 131 for 8 and in reply homesters notched up the required score for the loss of three wickets.
Chief Scores
St John’s CA – 131/8 in 24 overs (C P Venkatesh 56, Avinash Bollaram 18, T C Navneesh 20 n/o, Niman Umesh 3/27)
Kingfisher SC – 132/3 in 16.1 overs (Vishvajith Jaya 54 n/o, Madushika Perera 31 n/o, Rakshith 2/11)
