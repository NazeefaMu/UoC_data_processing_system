## Title: Unidentified body at Dambulla Hospital
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 7, 2018 - 01:00

Unidentified body at Dambulla Hospital

The body of a youth is being kept in the Dambulla Weera Denzil Kobbekaduwa Base Hospital morgue for identification.
According to police, the victim had been killed in a wild elephant attack near Habarana, last Friday.
The youth had succumbed to his injuries later that day.
He had disclosed that his name was Suriya Bandara and that he was 26 years old.
However, police had been unable to carry out investigations without more information and hence, seek assistance from the victim’s kith or kin.
