## Title: Mahinda Rajapaksa to resign from Premiership - Namal
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 14, 2018 - 19:31

Mahinda Rajapaksa to resign from Premiership - Namal

In a twitter message MP Namal Rajapaksa stated that Former President Mahinda Rajapaksa has decided to resign from the Premiership in order to ensure stability of the country.
