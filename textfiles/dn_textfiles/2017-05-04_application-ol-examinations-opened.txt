## Title: Application for O/L Examinations opened
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 4, 2017 - 17:15

Application for O/L Examinations opened

 
Applications for the Ordinary Level Examination 2017 have opened and will remain open till May 31, the Examination Department said.
This year’s examination is scheduled to be held from December 12 to 21.
