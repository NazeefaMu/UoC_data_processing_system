## Title: Invoking blessings for bountiful harvest
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 30, 2018 - 01:00

Invoking blessings for bountiful harvest

President Maithripala Sirisena participated in theVap Magul ceremony at the Tissamaharama Sandagirigoda paddy field yesterday morning.
The Maha Sangha, led by Viharadhpati Ven. Dr. Devalegama Dhammasena Nayake Thera, chanted seth pirith and invoked blessings on the President and the Prime Minister. MPs Chamal Rajapaksa and Mahinda Amaraweera were also present. Pictures by Sudath Silva.
