## Title: Kites take flight
## Supertitle: 
## Section: T&C
## Date: Friday, August 24, 2018 - 01:00

Kites take flight

The much anticipated Derana International Kite Festival 2018 organized by ‘Lokaya Saha Lokayo’ in collaboration with TV Derana was held at the Galle Face Green recently.
The annual event that attracts kite enthusiasts from across the globe hosted a multinational group of contestants from Nepal, Malaysia, Thailand, China, India, Taiwan, Singapore, Norway and other countries. Nearly 3,000 both local and foreign contestants signed up to participate in this year’s contest. The festival was concluded with a grand musical show attended by superstars following the competition.
