## Title: Cop arrested for breaking firearm in two
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 4, 2017 - 15:15

Cop arrested for breaking firearm in two

 
A Police constable was arrested for breaking his T-56 firearm in two.
The Police constable had reportedly broken his firearm in two in fury at being assigned gate-duty for not polishing his shoes.
He was arrested for causing damage to state property.
The Balapitiya Magistrate remanded the police constable till May 17.
