## Title: Vidya murder: Former Senior DIG further remanded [Video]
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, July 25, 2017 - 15:45

Vidya murder: Former Senior DIG further remanded [Video]

 
Former Senior Deputy Inspector General (DIG) Lalith A. Jayasinghe, alleged to have harboured, concealed and assisted the main suspect, 'Swiss Kumar' of the Vidya Sivaloganathan rape and murder case, was further remanded till August 8 by the Kayts Magistrate.
Jayasinghe was the Senior DIG of the Kandy Division at the time of the arrest.
