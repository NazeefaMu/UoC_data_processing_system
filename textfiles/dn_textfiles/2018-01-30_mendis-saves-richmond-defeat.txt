## Title: Mendis saves Richmond from defeat
## Supertitle: ‘Singer Cup’ U19 Schools Cricket
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, January 30, 2018 - 01:00

‘Singer Cup’ U19 Schools Cricket
Mendis saves Richmond from defeat

Sandun Mendis’ all-round performances saved Richmond College Galle from defeat against Dharmaraja College Kandy in their ‘Singer Cup’ Under 19 inter-schools cricket match played at Lake View Kandy yesterday.
Mendis scored an unbeaten 80 and took 11 wickets in the match.
Malinda Jayod skipper of Holy Cross College Kalutara scored his second century of the season at Maggona.
Thirty (30) wickets fell on day one between Veluwana MV Colombo and Madampa MMV Kuleegoda in their fixture at Himbutana.
Malsha Tharupathi of Adampa MMV took 12 wickets for 111 runs in the match against Veluwana MV Colombo.
