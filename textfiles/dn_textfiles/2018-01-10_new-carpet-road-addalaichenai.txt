## Title: New carpet road for Addalaichenai
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 10, 2018 - 01:00

New carpet road for Addalaichenai

A carpet road development project was launched in the Addalaichenai area. Construction works are under way in the Addalaichenai Sherif Hajiyar Road.
The Road Development Department (RDD) has implemented the project. Construction work has been initiated and the work is expected to be completed soon.
The estimate cost the project, which is Rs.23 million, was granted by City Planning Water Supply and Drainage Minister and SLMC Leader Rauff Hakeem. Former Eastern Province Health Minister A. L. M. Nazeer recently visited the road construction site and had discussions with the officials.
The Sherif Hajiyar Road and the Kappalady road in the Addalaichenai DS division are to be renovated under the special road development programme. Work on the road was ceremonially commenced by chief guest on the occasion City Planning Water Supply and Drainage Minister and SLMC Leader Rauff Hakeem recently.
