## Title: National Insurance Day declared
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 01:00

National Insurance Day declared

The Government has declared September 1 as 'National Insurance Day,' marking an important milestone in the insurance industry of Sri Lanka, recognizing the contribution made by the insurance industry to the national economy and to the welfare of the society. 
This is a realization of a joint effort by the regulator o the insurance industry, the Insurance Board of Sri Lanka (IBSL), and the representative body of the insurance industry; the Insurance Association of Sri Lanka (IASL). A special commemorative stamp and a first day cover were presented to the President to commemorate in inaugural National insurance Day by the Posts, Postal Services and Muslim Religious Affairs Deputy Minister, in the presence of representatives of IBSL and IASL. A ceremony was held commemorating the inaugural National Insurance Day on September 1 at the Independence Arcade, Colombo 7.
