## Title: Car collides with train killing three, injuring one
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, November 20, 2017 - 11:52

Car collides with train killing three, injuring one

(File Photo)
A car collided with a train, killing three individuals and injuring another in Pethiyagoda, earlier today.
The train transporting fuel to Colombo Fort ran over the car which was travelling towards Veyangoda.
One individual died on the spot and two others succumbed to injuries after being rushed to the Wathupitiwala Hospital.
The individual who died on the spot was taken to the Gampaha Hospital.
Of the three deceased, two were women and the other was a man.
Veyangoda Police is investigating.
