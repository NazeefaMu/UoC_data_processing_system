## Title: Police curfew extended until further notice
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, March 7, 2018 - 12:09

Police curfew extended until further notice

 
The Police curfew was further extended to the Kandy administrative district, until further notice.
A tense situation erupted following a clash between two groups in Teldeniya on March 5.
Police used tear gas and water cannons to disperse the crowds and several arrests were made.
Schools in the Kandy district were also ordered to be closed today.
