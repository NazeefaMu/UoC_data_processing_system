## Title: Inmate dies at Pitigala Police station
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, September 19, 2017 - 14:15

Inmate dies at Pitigala Police station

 
A prison inmate of the Pitigala Police station was found dead inside his cell today.
The victim was identified as Magodapathirage Hemawipul, a 42-year-old resident of Marakgoda.
The police suspect that the deceased had committed suicide.
Inspector General of Police (IGP) Pujith Jayasundara promised stern action against the officers on duty at the conclusion of an investigation.
