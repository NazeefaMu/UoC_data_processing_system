Singer Cup U19 Schools Cricket : Gurukula, Wattala Joes, Kuliyapitiya Central victorious

Gurukula College Kelaniya, St. Joseph’s College Wattala and Kuliyapitiya Central College won their Singer Cup under 19 schools cricket matches concluded yesterday.
Gurukula College beat Anuradhapura Central College by an innings and 28 runs, St. Joseph’s College beat Galahitiywa Central College and Kuliyapitiya Central beat Nugawela Central College.
Arosha Madushan of Bandaranayake Gampaha scored an unbeaten century (108) runs against De Mazenod College Kandana while Dimuth Sampath skipper of Siri Piyaratana MMV Padukka took a match bag of 11 wickets against Devapathiraja College Ratgama.
