## Title: Right to Information to be gazetted Feb. 3
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 11, 2017 - 10:15

Right to Information to be gazetted Feb. 3

 
Minister of Parliament Reforms and Mass Media, Gayantha Kaunatilaka announced today (11) that the Right to Information Act will be gazetted on February 3.
