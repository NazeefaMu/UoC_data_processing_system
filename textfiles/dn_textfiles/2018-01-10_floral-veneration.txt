## Title: Floral veneration
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Wednesday, January 10, 2018 - 01:00

Floral veneration

Nelum Mal Poojawa was organized by SANASA Federation and SANASA Movement with the sponsorship of SDB bank for the 18th time. The event took place on January 6 near Anuradhapura Sri Maha Bodhi.
Kiri Ahara Poojawa, Kapruk Poojawa and Thesaththa Gnana Poojawa were conducted on January 7 near Ruwanwelisaya, following Nelum Mal Poojawa.
The main objective of this event is to bless Sri Lankan citizens, SANASA members and the customers of SANASA Development Bank PLC (SDB) with a healthy and prosperous new year.
SANASA Federation Chairman and SANASA Leader Dr P A Kiriwandeniya, SDB Chairperson Samadanie Kiriwandeniya, SDB CEO Nimal C Hapuarachchi and members representing SDB bank, Sanasa Federation and Societies participated in this event.
