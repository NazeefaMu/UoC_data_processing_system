## Title: Pakistani’s with Rs 10 mn heroin
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 29, 2018 - 01:00

Pakistani’s with Rs 10 mn heroin

Two Pakistan nationals were taken into custody along with 891 grams of heroin worth more than Rs. 10 million at Colpetty yesterday.
The arrest was made on a joint operation by the Police Narcotics Division and the Criminal Investigation Department. The arrested were in the ages of 28 and 36.
