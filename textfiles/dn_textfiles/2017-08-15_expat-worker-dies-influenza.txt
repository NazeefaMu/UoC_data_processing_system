## Title: Expat worker dies of influenza
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, August 15, 2017 - 01:00

Expat worker dies of influenza

A woman expatriate worker died of an acute influenza condition two days after she returned home from a stint in Dubai, a report from Chilaw said.
Several other members of her family have also been admitted to the Chilaw hospital with similar symptoms.
However, health authorities said there is no cause for worry as everything is under control.
