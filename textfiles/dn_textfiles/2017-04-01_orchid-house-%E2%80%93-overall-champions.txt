## Title: Orchid House – overall champions
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, April 1, 2017 - 01:00

Orchid House – overall champions

Orchid House emerged overall champions in the annual Inter-House Sports Festival - 2017 of Addalaichenai Zahra Vidyalaya under the organization and directions of its Principal M.S.M.Fairoos which took place at school play grounds recently.
Eastern Province Health, Indigenous Medicine, Social Services, Probation Child Care and Rural Electrification Minister A.L.M.Nazeer was the chief guest at the sports festival.

(Yellow)– 282 points.
