## Title: Sayuru 124
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, October 31, 2017 - 01:00

Sayuru 124

A brilliant captain’s knock of 124 runs in 116 balls by Sayuru Sasanka guided Weera Puran Appu Model School Moratuwa to beat Science College Mount Lavinia on first innings by 105 runs in their inter-school under 13 division three tournament cricket match worked off at Moratuwa.
Weera Puran Appu Model School 258 (Sayuru Sasanka 124, Sahan Edirisinghe 27, Senura Ishan 3/37.
Science College 153/5 Thimal Fernando 50, Sayuru Sasanka 4/31.
