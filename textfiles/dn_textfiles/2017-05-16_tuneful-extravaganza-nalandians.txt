## Title: Tuneful extravaganza from the Nalandians
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Tuesday, May 16, 2017 - 01:00

Tuneful extravaganza from the Nalandians

Geethma 2017, a musical evening which was organised by the orchestra of Nalanda College, Colombo, was held at BMICH on May 5. The chief guests for the event were Vimala Amaradeva (late Pandit Amaradeva’s wife) and Prof. Kolitha Bhanu Dissanayake. The Jayantha Dissanayake was the teacher who was in charge of music at the event. Pictures by Sudath Malaweera
