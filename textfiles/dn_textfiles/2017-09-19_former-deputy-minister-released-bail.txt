## Title: Former Deputy Minister released on bail
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, September 19, 2017 - 01:00

Former Deputy Minister released on bail

 Police yesterday arrested former Deputy Minister Victor Anthony for allegedly obtaining Rs, 300,000 from a person of Nakulagamuwa by promising him a job in New Zealand. The former Deputy Minister was arrested on a complaint by the person.
The Deputy Minister was later released on bail after being produced before the Chilaw Magistrate yesterday.
