## Title: Fifth grader injured by explosives
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 23, 2017 - 01:00

Fifth grader injured by explosives

A student of Yawana Kumarapura Vidyalaya at Wellawaya was wounded as result of an
explosion of a 'thunder flash' explosive used to chase away wild elephants.
The victim, Ruchira Keshan, was admitted to the Hambatota General Hospital and later, to the Karapitiya Hospital for treatment.
He was to appear for the fifth year scholarship exam that was held recently.
