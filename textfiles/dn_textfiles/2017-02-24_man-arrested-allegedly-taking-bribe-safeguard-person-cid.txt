## Title: Man arrested for allegedly taking bribe to safeguard a person from the CID
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 24, 2017 - 01:00

Man arrested for allegedly taking bribe to safeguard a person from the CID

A suspect who was produced before Colombo Fort Magistrate Lanka Jayarathna for allegedly taking a bribe to safeguard a person from the Crime Investigation Department, was remanded yesterday till March 8
The suspect Rajapaksa Gunasekera Mudiyanselage Kamalitha Sithara Rajapaksa, who was accused of cheating Rs 300,000 and trying to rob Rs.100,000 from the same person was arrested and produced before Colombo Fort Magistrate by the CID after the victim Illangakoon Mudiyanselage Duminda Bandara Illangakoone from Anuradhapura had complained to the Crime Investigation Department on February 17.
According to investigations, a Police Constable who helped the suspect to cheat the victim had credited Rs 300,000 in instalments to his account. He has revealed that he has given Rs 20,000 to one of his friends and bought ready made garments worth Rs 17,000 for his friend.
The CID had informed Court that there were few more suspects to be arrested and investigations are still in progress.
The Court has ordered the CID to submit a full report of the incident. The Police have found a few ATM cards and Rs 17,500 in the suspect’s possession.
