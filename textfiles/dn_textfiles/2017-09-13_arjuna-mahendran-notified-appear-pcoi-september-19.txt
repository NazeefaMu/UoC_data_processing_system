## Title: Arjuna Mahendran notified to appear before PCoI on September 19
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, September 13, 2017 - 12:45

Arjuna Mahendran notified to appear before PCoI on September 19

 
The Presidential Commission of Inquiry (PCoI) investigating the Treasury Bond issue has notified former Central Bank Governor Arjuna Mahendran to appear before the Commission on September 19.
