## Title: Step with glory
## Supertitle: 
## Section: T&C
## Date: Tuesday, June 19, 2018 - 01:00

Step with glory

St Thomas Catholic International College Seeduwa held its annual walk `Thomian Visitation’ recently. Famous cricketers Chamara Kapugedera, Dammika Prasad and Dasun Chanaka participated at as the Chief guests together with the Rector of the College, Rev. Fr. Kennedy Perera, Vice Rector Rev Fr Malintha Chathuranga and Primary Principal, Rev Sr Nalini Fernando. The walk which started from St. Peter and Paul Church Katunayake ended up at the College grounds where children enjoyed the fun fair and games.
