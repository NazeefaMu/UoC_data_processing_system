## Title: Government jobs for unemployed national sportspersons
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, January 20, 2018 - 01:00

Government jobs for unemployed national sportspersons

Kurunegala District Parliamentarian and Sports Minister Dayasiri Jayasekara has forwarded a Cabinet paper to secure jobs in the government sector for the unemployed, talented national sportspersons who are in National Sports Pools. About 111 sportspersons in National Sports Pools were yesterday awarded with a monthly allowance of Rs.60,000 each to improve their physical and mental strength.
About 450 sports training programmes to train rural school sportspersons for national and international sports competitions are to be organised this year at a cost of Rs. 23.5 million.
Five thousands sportspersons are to be trained under the programmes sponsored by international sports experts, Sports Minister Dayasiri Jayasekara said speaking at a meeting of representatives of sports clubs in the Kurunegala District held in Panduwasnuwara yesterday.
