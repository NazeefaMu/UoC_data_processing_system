## Title: Five arrested for heroin possession
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 10, 2017 - 01:00

Five arrested for heroin possession

Five suspects who had 20 packets of heroin in their possession were arrested by Anuradhapura Police on February 7 afternoon.
Police said one of the suspects who was of Arabian descent had been engaged in the sale of drugs through the Easy Cash scheme and the other four were prospective buyers.
The man behind the racket, a father of one, is a resident of Wehera, Kurunegala and married to a woman from Pubudupura, Anuradhapura. All suspects were due to be produced before the Anuradhapura Magistrate yesterday.
