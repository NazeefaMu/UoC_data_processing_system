## Title: Three youth with heroin nabbed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, February 12, 2018 - 01:00

Three youth with heroin nabbed

Three youth who sold heroin using a luxury vehicle have been taken into custody by Tissamaharama Police.
Two youth who had bought heroin from them and the vehicle was also taken into custody. The suspects were to be produced before the Tissamaharama magistrate, ASP Sanath Amarasinha said.
