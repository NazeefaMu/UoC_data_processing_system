## Title: Death of Christies Perera
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 22, 2018 - 01:00

Death of Christies Perera

The death occurred of Christies Perera (69) of Wattala. He was a chorister for over 45 years and an organist at St.Anne’s Church, Wattala and a social worker.
He leaves wife Morina and children Samantha, Marise and Roshani and Nilukshani and Dennis.
Funeral today 3.15 p.m. at St.Anne’s Church burial ground,Wattala. Huge Lodge, 209 Averiwatta Rd,Wattala
