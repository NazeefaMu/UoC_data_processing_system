## Title: Electricity disrupted to 20,000 houses in Baddegama
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 1, 2017 - 01:00

Electricity disrupted to 20,000 houses in Baddegama

Electricity supply was disrupted to 20,000 houses in the Baddegama Electrical Engineer's Division during recent heavy rains and floods, Electricity Engineer B M A T Priyadarshana said.
He said maintenance teams were unable to reach affected houses in Mapalagama, Nagoda, Thalgaswala and Unanwitiya areas since flood waters had not receded. High voltage transmission lines had also been submerged by flood waters.
Electricity posts had fallen damaging electricity supply lines in some areas.
