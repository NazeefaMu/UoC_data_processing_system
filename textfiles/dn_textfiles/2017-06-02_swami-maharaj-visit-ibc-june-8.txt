## Title: Swami Maharaj to visit IBC on June 8
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, June 2, 2017 - 01:00

Swami Maharaj to visit IBC on June 8

Acclaimed itinerant ordained monk Bhakti Vinoda Swami Maharaj will visit Sri Lanka this month.
An outstanding erudite scholar and interpreter of the Bhagavad Gita,he has given a new lease of life to hundreds of individuals who cannot cope with the ups and downs of life in general by imparting the ancient Vedic wisdom contained in the Bhagavad Gita as it is.
He will the moderator and facilitator for an interactive function on the prevention of cow slaughter to be held at the International Buddhist Centre, Thummulla, Colombo 5 on Poya day June 8, 2017 and will also conduct a five day session on Gita Life from June 11 to June 16 at Scholar Management Services, Jayasuriya Auditorium, No. 3 Daisy Villa Avenue, Colombo 4.
