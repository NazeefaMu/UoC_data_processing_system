## Title: Arrested with cannabis
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, November 27, 2018 - 01:00

Arrested with cannabis

Hakmana Police, on a tip off, took into custody a suspect with one gram of cannabis in his possession at Thambiliapola in Hakmana. The suspect has been identified as an army deserter. The suspect was to be produced before the Deiyandara Magistrate’s Court.
Investigations are continuing.
