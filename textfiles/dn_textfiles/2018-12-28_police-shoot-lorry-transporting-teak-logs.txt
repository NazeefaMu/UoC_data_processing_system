## Title: Police shoot at lorry transporting teak logs
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 28, 2018 - 01:00

Police shoot at lorry transporting teak logs

A lorry which was transporting a consignment of teak logs was shot at by the Walasmulla Police when it failed to stop. The consignment of teak logs were being transported to Hakmana from Julampitiya in Middeniya.
