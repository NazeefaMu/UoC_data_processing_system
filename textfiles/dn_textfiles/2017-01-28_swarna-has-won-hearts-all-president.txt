## Title: Swarna has won the hearts of all: President
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, January 28, 2017 - 01:00

Swarna has won the hearts of all: President

Swarna Mallawarachchi is an icon who won the love of Sri Lankans as well as international fans, President Maithripala Sirisena said.
The President also wished strength and courage to Mallawarachchi to render further service to the development of the Sri Lankan cinema.
President Sirisena made these comments at a felicitation ceremony to pay tribute to Mallawarachchi’s contribution to the country’s cinema during the last 50 years.
The event under the patronage of President Sirisena was held at the Tarangani Hall of the National Film Corporation on Thursday.
The President presented a felicitation award to Mallawarachchi in recognition of her service to the Sri Lankan film industry during the last 50 years. The actress presented a commemorative book titled Cinamawe Gehaniya Swarna Mallawarachchi Panas Wasaraka Cinamabhimanaya to the President.
Mass Media Minister Gayantha Karunathilake, Deputy Minister Karunarathna Paranavitana, dignitaries including film directors and artistes participated.
