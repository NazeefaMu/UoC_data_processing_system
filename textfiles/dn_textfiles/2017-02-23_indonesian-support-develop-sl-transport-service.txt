## Title: Indonesian support to develop SL transport service
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, February 23, 2017 - 17:00

Indonesian support to develop SL transport service

 
The Indonesian Government assured today (23) to render its fullest support to Sri Lanka’s future development activities, especially railway services.
The Deputy Foreign Minister of Indonesia A.M. Fachir said this at the meeting held with Transport and Civil Aviation Minister Nimal Siripala De Silva at the minister’s office.
At the meeting Minister de Silva expressed his gratitude to Deputy Minister Fachir for pledging to assist projects in the transport sector.
Minister de Silva also said that he will deal with the Indonesian investors in this regard.
Deputy Minister Fachir arrived in Sri Lanka to discuss on steps which could be made by the Indonesian Government to support Sri Lanka’s development activities in parallel to President Maithripala Sirisena’s visit to Indonesia scheduled for March this year.
