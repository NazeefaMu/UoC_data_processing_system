## Title: Kelani Valley railway line to close from tonight
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 16, 2018 - 12:53

Kelani Valley railway line to close from tonight

 
The Kelani Valley railway line will be closed from 8 p.m. today until 4 a.m. Monday due to emergency maintenance work in the railway tracks, the Railway Department said.
