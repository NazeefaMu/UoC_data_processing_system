## Title: SP to manufacture textiles with  bamboo,  plantain fibre
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, May 12, 2017 - 01:00

SP to manufacture textiles with  bamboo,  plantain fibre

 Southern Provincial Industries Ministry is to implement projects to manufacture textiles using bamboo and plantain fibre.
The assistance of the Ruhuna University Faculty of Agriculture has been sought by the ministry in this regard.
