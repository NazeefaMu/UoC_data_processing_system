## Title: Army aids Northern schools
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 01:00

Army aids Northern schools

Army troops donated a stock of books to the library of Mullaithivu Maha Vidyalaya in Kilinochchi recently.
According to Army media, the donation, made in response to a request by school authorities, was coordinated by the troops of the 591 Brigade of Security Force Headquarters - Mullaithivu (SFHQ-MLT).
Meanwhile, troops in Jaffna provided assistance to Drieberg College in Chavakachcheri, Jaffna, to acquire a sanitary facility building. Troops of the 12 Gemunu Watch (GW) and 4 Vijayabahu Infantry Regiments (VIR) of 523 Brigade under the Security Force Headquarters - Jaffna (SFHQ-J), provided assistance to the school to acquire facilities. Rs. 300,000.00 had been provided by Onili S. Ranasinghe towards the worthy cause.
A pack of toilet utilities and accessories was also donated to the school recently.
