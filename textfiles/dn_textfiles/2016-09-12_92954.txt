## Title: Isipathana into final against Kingswood
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, September 12, 2016 - 01:00

Isipathana into final against Kingswood

Isipathana College secured their place in the Milo Under-18 All-Island 15-a-side Rugby Championship final with Kingswood College Kandy when they beat St. Anthony’s College Kandy by 8 points (1 try, 1 penalty) to nil in the semi-final worked off at Ananda College grounds in Rajagiriya on Saturday.
In the second semi-final giant killers Kingswood College thrashed Zahira College Maradana by 17 points (3 tries, 1 conversion) to 5 (1 try) at the same venue.
Meanwhile Trinity College Kandy beat Ananda College Maradana 17-0 in the Bowl semi-final and will take on Vidyartha in the final. Vidyartha defeated C.W.W. Kannangara 32-5 in the second semi-final.
