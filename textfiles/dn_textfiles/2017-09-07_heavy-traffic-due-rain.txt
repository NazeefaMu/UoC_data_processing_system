## Title: Heavy traffic due to rain
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 11:15

Heavy traffic due to rain

 
Heavy traffic is reported from many of roads in Colombo and suburbs due to heavy rain experienced in the city.
Colombo-Awissawella road has been blocked due to floods at Puwakpitiya.
