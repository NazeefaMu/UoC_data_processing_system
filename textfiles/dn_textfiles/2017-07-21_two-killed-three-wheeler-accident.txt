## Title: Two killed in three wheeler accident
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, July 21, 2017 - 01:00

Two killed in three wheeler accident

Two persons were killed in an accident at Bathalayaya Junction on the Mahiyangana – Giradurukotte Road on Tuesday.
According to Police sources, a three wheeler crashed into another three wheeler parked by the roadside. Two persons including a woman, who were on the three wheeler, were thrown out.
They were run over by a lorry that came behind the three wheeler, killing them on the spot.
The three wheeler driver was among the deceased. He was a retired soldier.
