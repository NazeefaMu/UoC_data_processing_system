## Title: Metal quarry damages houses
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, August 10, 2017 - 01:00

Metal quarry damages houses

A metal quarry functioning close to the Kavantissa village in Tissamaharama, is damaging houses in the area. Cracks have appeared on the walls of the houses. Some houses are on the verge of collapse. The dust from the metal quarry has caused respiratory diseases among people in the area.
