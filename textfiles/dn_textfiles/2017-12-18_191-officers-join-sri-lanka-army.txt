## Title: 191 officers join Sri Lanka army
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, December 18, 2017 - 01:00

191 officers join Sri Lanka army

The 92nd passing out parade at the Sri Lanka Military Academy in Diyatalawa was held under the patronage of President Maithripala Sirisena yesterday. The event saw 191 cadets and officers joining the army service after successful conclusion of military training. The President offered floral tributes, while Commandant of the Academy Brigadier Priyantha Senarathna received a memorial badge. Secretary of Defense Kapila Waidyaratne, Army Commander, Mahesh Senanayake, Commandant of the Academy Priyantha Senaratne and several other officers of the Sri Lanka Army participated.
