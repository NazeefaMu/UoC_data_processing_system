## Title: Indonesia’s stock exchange collapses
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Tuesday, January 16, 2018 - 01:00

Indonesia’s stock exchange collapses

More than 70 people were injured after a mezzanine floor at the Indonesia Stock Exchange collapsed onto the building’s lobby on Monday. Police cordoned off the complex as people fled the building and the injured, including students visiting the building, were taken away by stretcher. Others were seen sitting on steps outside the lobby.
“I can definitely confirm there are no deaths,” director of the Indonesian stock exchange, Tito Sulistio said on Metro TV.
