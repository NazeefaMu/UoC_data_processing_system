## Title: National Students’ Quality Circle Forum in Ratnapura
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 13, 2017 - 01:00

National Students’ Quality Circle Forum in Ratnapura

The Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council conducted the first National Students’ Quality Circle Forum at the Education Research and Development Training Centre, Meneripitiya, Ratnapura recently, in line with the International Students’ Quality Circle Forum, to be held in the country in May with the participation of students representing 48 countries.
Around 1,000 students representing nine provinces participated in the National Students’ Quality Circle Forum. The forum held for two days included many events such as students’ parliament, quiz programme, aesthetic presentations, lectures on quality improvements and quality maintain. The Sabaragamuwa Provincial Council will sponsor the International Students’ Quality Circle Forum.
