## Title: Youth arrested with 12 million worth heroin
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, December 24, 2018 - 11:28

Youth arrested with 12 million worth heroin

Marawila Police arrested a suspect with one kilogram of heroin worth over Rs 12 million in Marawila.
The arrested suspect is a 28-year-old resident of Marawila.
Further investigations are being carried out by the Marawila Police.
