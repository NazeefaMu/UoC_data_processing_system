## Title: ‘Dream School’ opened
## Supertitle: 
## Section: T&C
## Date: Tuesday, October 9, 2018 - 01:00

‘Dream School’ opened

A new three storey building titled ‘Dream School’, costing nearly Rs 40 million was donated to Maggona St Mary’s Vidyalaya by Holy Trinity Foundation Chairman Ranjan Gomes was opened on September 27 and named after the late Rev Fr Marcelline Jayakody. Bishop Anthony Jayakody blessed the new building after which Gomes opened the building by cutting the ribbon in the presence of the members of the Catholic clergy, dignitaries of other religions, the school principal, staff and students.
The building is equipped with four class rooms, a modern laboratory, computer room, library and an auditorium. The building was constructed and gifted according to a request made by Principal Ajanthe Chanakan and Sister Mary Nimashini.
