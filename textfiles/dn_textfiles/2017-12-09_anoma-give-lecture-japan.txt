## Title: Anoma to give a lecture in Japan
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, December 9, 2017 - 01:00

Anoma to give a lecture in Japan

The Training Director of the Educational Section of the National Olympic Committee Anoma Ratnayake will give a special lecture at the SFT Sports Academy Formation Support Project Seminar conducted by the University of Tsukuba.
The aim of this seminar is to see the progress of the diploma and sports courses conducted from the money allocated by University of Tsukuba, Nippon sports science university and NIFS university Anoma is also lectures in physical and sports education.
Over one hundred people will attend this seminar. She is the first Sri Lankan Woman to address an international seminar. Anoma who is an old girl of Sangamitta Balika Galle is an undergraduate of the Kelaniya University.
