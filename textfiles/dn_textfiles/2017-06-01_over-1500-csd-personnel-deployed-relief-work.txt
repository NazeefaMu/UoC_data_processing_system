## Title: Over 1,500 CSD personnel deployed for relief work
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 1, 2017 - 01:00

Over 1,500 CSD personnel deployed for relief work

Over 1,500 Civil Security Department personnel attached to the CSD Force Headquarters across the country are deployed for flood relief activities in Galle, Matara, Ratnapura, Hambantota and Kalutara districts.
Personnel from Force Headquarters in Wilpattuwa, Anuradhapura, Vavuniya, Kurunegala, Kebithigollawa, Killinochchi, Medirigiriya, Mullaithivu, Puttalam, Seruvila, Trincomalee, Welioya, Wennawatta, Ampara, Polonnaruwa, Mahaoya, Uhana, Kataragama, Moneragala, Gomarankadawala and Katunayake are engaged in relief operations and cleaning activities.
The deployment was made on the requests of several Divisional Secretariats of the affected areas.
Meanwhile, 27 trucks carrying relief goods collected by CSD Force Headquarters will be sent to affected areas.
