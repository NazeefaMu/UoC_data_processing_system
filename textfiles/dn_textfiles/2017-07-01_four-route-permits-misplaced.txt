## Title: Four route permits misplaced
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, July 1, 2017 - 01:00

Four route permits misplaced

Four route permits issued by the North Western Province Passenger Transport Authority have been misplaced.
They had been kept in a store belonging to the the authority.
Provincial Transport Authority sources said a special investigation is being carried out by the provincial audit department into the incident.
The route permits bearing the numbers 9413, 9414, 9458 and 9459 have got misplaced.
