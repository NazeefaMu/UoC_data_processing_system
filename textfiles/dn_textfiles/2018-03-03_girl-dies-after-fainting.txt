## Title: Girl dies after fainting
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, March 3, 2018 - 01:00

Girl dies after fainting

A fifteen year school girl had died after fainting in her school in Atabage Udugama Pussellawa yesterday.She was later admitted to the Gampola hospital where she died.
The girl had complained to her teacher of a severe headache which she suffered while she was engaged in dancing practices in the school.
The teachers had immediately admitted her to the Gampola hospital when she fainted. The girl Madubashinee Buddhika Liyanage was a resident of Millagahathanna, Panwilathenna in Pussallawa.
