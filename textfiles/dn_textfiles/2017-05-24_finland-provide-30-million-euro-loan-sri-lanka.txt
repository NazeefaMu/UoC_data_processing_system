## Title: Finland to provide 30 million Euro loan to Sri Lanka
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 24, 2017 - 15:45

Finland to provide 30 million Euro loan to Sri Lanka

 
The Cabinet Ministers approved today to obtain a loan of 30 million Euros from Finland to finance development projects in Sri Lanka.
Accordingly Finland has approved to grant concessionary financial facilities for projects in Sri Lanka which were initiated in 1983 to 2006.
Finland also agreed to provide more facilities under the Public Sector Investment Facility Programme.
