## Title: Two MoUs signed between Sri Lanka and Vietnam
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, April 18, 2017 - 01:00

Two MoUs signed between Sri Lanka and Vietnam

Two MoUs were signed between Sri Lanka and Vietnam yesterday. The MoU on the action plan for Agricultural Development Corporation for 2017-2019 and the MoU on Educational Co-operation were signed by Vietnam’s Agriculture Minister Cao Duc Phat and Vietnam’s Education Minister Pham Vu Luan respectively on behalf of Vietnam and by Sri Lanka Ambassador in Vietnam Hasanthi Dissanayakeon behalf of Sri Lanka.
The signing was done in the presence of Vietnam’s Prime Minister Nguen Xuan Phuc and Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe.
