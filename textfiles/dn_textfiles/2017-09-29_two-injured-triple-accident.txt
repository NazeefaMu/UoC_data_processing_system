## Title: Two injured in triple accident
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, September 29, 2017 - 01:00

Two injured in triple accident

Two persons were seriously injured when a car driven by Kalmunai North MoH Dr. R Ganeswaran, collided with a Pajero driven at high speed from the direction of Batticaloa near the Pandirppu Thanrapadi Amman Kovil junction, Pandirppu yesterday.
The Pajero also rammed into a motorcycle following the collision.
The MoH and the motorcyclist were injured and admitted to the Kalmunai hospital. Kalmunai police are investigating.
