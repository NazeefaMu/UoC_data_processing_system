## Title: PRESIDENT, PM ILLUMINATE MIHINTALE ALOKA POOJA
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 28, 2018 - 01:01

PRESIDENT, PM ILLUMINATE MIHINTALE ALOKA POOJA

President Maithripala Sirisena and Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe switched on the Mihintale Aloka Pooja Poson illumunations at the Mihintale Medamaluwa last evening, on the invitation of Mihintale Raja Maha Viharadhipathi Ven.Walawahengunaveve Dhammarathana thera.The event is organised by the Associated Newspapers of Ceylon Limited (ANCL) and held for the 56th year since 1963.This year’s Aloka Pooja was jointly sponsored by the Ceylon Electricity Board, Dialog and the National Savings Bank. ANCL senior management and senior officials of the sponsoring agencies, Minsters Ranjith Siyambalapitiya, P. Harrison and Chandrani Bandara were also present. Picture by Shan Rupassara.
