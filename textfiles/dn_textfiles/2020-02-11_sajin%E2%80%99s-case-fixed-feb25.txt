## Title: Sajin’s case fixed for Feb.25
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Tuesday, February 11, 2020 - 16:21

Sajin’s case fixed for Feb.25

The case filed against former UPFA MP Sajin de Vass Gunawardena over a money laundering charge was today fixed for February 25 by the Colombo High Court.The case was taken up before Colombo High Court Judge Gihan Kulatunga.At a previous hearing, the indictment was handed over to the accused and accused was released on a cash bail of Rs. 500,000 and two sureties of Rs. 5 million.
