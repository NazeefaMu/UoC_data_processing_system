## Title: Dayawathie Gamage passes away
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 13, 2017 - 01:00

Dayawathie Gamage passes away

Dayawathie Gamage, wife P.K. Gamage, President of Ex-Government Press, English Proof Readers’ Association passed away. Remains lie at 1625/1 Hataramanhandiya Malabe Road Kottawa. Funeral will be held on April 13 at 2 p.m at the General Cemetery, Kottawa.
