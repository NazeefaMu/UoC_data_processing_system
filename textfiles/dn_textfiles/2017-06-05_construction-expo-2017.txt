## Title: Construction Expo 2017
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Monday, June 5, 2017 - 01:00

Construction Expo 2017

Sri Lanka’s largest and most focused construction-related exhibition, ‘Construction Expo 2017’, featuring aspects related to the construction and building industry was held recently at the BMICH. The theme of the event was ‘Towards a Green Sri Lanka’. This year’s exhibition saw more than 15,000 visitors.
