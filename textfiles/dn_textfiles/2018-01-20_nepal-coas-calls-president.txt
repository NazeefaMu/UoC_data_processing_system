## Title: Nepal COAS calls on President
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, January 20, 2018 - 01:00

Nepal COAS calls on President

The Chief of the Army Staff (COAS) of the Nepal Army, General Rajendra Chhetri, who is on a goodwill visit to Sri Lanka, called on President Maithripala Sirisena at the President’s official residence in Colombo, on Thursday.
During the meeting, views were exchanged on the reinforcement of cooperation between the two armies for the task of nation-building, methods of quick response during natural calamities and bilateral relations between the two countries in military training.
The COAS of the Nepal Army thanked the President for the support received from Sri Lanka when Nepal faced natural calamities, praised the commitment made by the Sri Lanka Army and extended the gratitude of the Government of Nepal to the Government of Sri Lanka for providing its support.
The President recalled the historic religious, social and cultural relationship between Nepal and Sri Lanka and stressed the commitment to further improve these ties.
