## Title: Bank of Ceylon celebrates New Year
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Friday, January 6, 2017 - 01:00

Bank of Ceylon celebrates New Year

The Bank of Ceylon commenced its New Year operations with a ceremony on January 2nd 2017, the first working day. The ceremony took place with the patronage of the Bank’s chairman, Ronald C. Perera, Board of Directors and the General Manager D.M. Gunasekera.
