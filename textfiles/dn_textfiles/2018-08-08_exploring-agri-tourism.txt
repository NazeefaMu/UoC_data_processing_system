## Title: Exploring agri-tourism
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 8, 2018 - 01:01

Exploring agri-tourism

With Sri Lanka rated as one of the fastest growing tourist destinations in the world, the country is now exploring another rapidly growing segment, agri-tourism, to achieve greater heights in the industry. Tourists participating in collecting the mature rice crop from the field at the farm maintained by Dambulla Rajamaha Viharaya where traditional rice varieties are cultivated using indigenous methods.
