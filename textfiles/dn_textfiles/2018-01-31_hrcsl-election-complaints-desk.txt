## Title: HRCSL Election Complaints Desk
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 31, 2018 - 01:00

HRCSL Election Complaints Desk

The Human Rights Commission of Sri Lanka (HRCSL) has established an Election Complaints Desk to receive complaints regarding rights violations relating to the upcoming 2018 Local Government Election. The Desk shall pay particular attention to the rights violations of women candidates. The hotlines are 0773088135 or 0773762112. The following fax number and e-mail address also can be used to submit complaints: Fax: 0112505574, Email: [email protected]
