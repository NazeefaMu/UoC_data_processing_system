## Title: President inspects Pelawatta Security Headquarters building
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 01:00

President inspects Pelawatta Security Headquarters building

President Maithripala Sirisena on Tuesday inspected the Security Headquarters building complex being constructed in Pelawatta, Akuregoda.
The President, who inquired into the progress of the project, instructed officials to expedite the construction work. He also held discussions with the engineers. Secretary to the President Austin Fernando and Army Commander Lieutenant General Mahesh Senanayake joined the President in this visit.
