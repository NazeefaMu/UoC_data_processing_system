## Date: Tuesday, February 27, 2018 - 01:00

A noble  collaboration

Nobel House emerged champions at the Inter-House Music Competition of the British School in Colombo (BSC) which was held at the School Auditorium recently.
The Inter-House Music Competition is one of the biggest house events of the academic year with Nobel House, Newton House, Scott House and Shakespeare House providing top entertainment.
The event was conducted in different categories including the Best Choir, Best Junior Soloist, Best Senior Soloist, Best Instrumentalist, Best Band, Best Ensemble and Best Group Dance.
Overall this year’s program was an outstanding success with individuals and groups demonstrating their skills and accomplishment as performers and musicians.
