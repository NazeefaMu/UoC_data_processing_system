## Title: SANASA holds Nelum Mal Poojawa
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Wednesday, January 10, 2018 - 01:00

SANASA holds Nelum Mal Poojawa

Nelum Mal Poojawa organized by SANASA Federation and SANASA Movement with the sponsorship of SDB bank was held for the 18th time. The event took place on January 6, near Anuradhapura Sri Maha Bodhi.
Kiriahara Poojawa, Kapruk Poojawa and Thesaththa Gnana Poojawa were conducted on January 7, close to Ruwanwelisaya, following Nelum Mal Poojawa.
The main objective of this event is to bless Sri Lankan citizens, SANASA members and SDB bank customers with a healthy and prosperous new year.
SANASA Federation Chairman and SANASA Leader Vishwa Prasadini Dr. P.A. Kiriwandeniya, SDB bank’s Chairperson Samadanie Kiriwandeniya, SDB bank’s CEO Nimal C. Hapuarachchi and members representing SDB bank, SANASA Federation and Societies participated in this event.
