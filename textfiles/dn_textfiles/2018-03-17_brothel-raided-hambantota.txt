## Title: Brothel raided in Hambantota
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, March 17, 2018 - 01:00

Brothel raided in Hambantota

A brothel house situated near to the Hambantota Magistrate's Court was raided by the Tangalle Division Flying Squad last Sunday.
The arrest was done on a tip-off received by the Flying Squad. A man said to be the manager of the brothel was also arrested by police.
The two women said to be residents of Negombo and Kaduwela had engaged in this business for a period of time under the guise of renting rooms for couples. The women and the brothel manager were produced at the Hambantota Magistrate's Court.
