## Title: Presidential Cinema Awards
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, April 1, 2017 - 01:00

Presidential Cinema Awards

Indian actress Anjali Patil and theatre veteran Shyam Fernando won the best actress and the actor awards respectively for their exceptional performance in Prasanna Vithanage’s film Oba Nethiwa Oba Ekka (With You, Without You) at the Presidential Cinema Awards held at the BMICH last evening.
