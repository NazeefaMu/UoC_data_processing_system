## Title: St Mary’s hold the edge
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Sunday, March 19, 2017 - 01:00

St Mary’s hold the edge

Ananda Sashthralaya Matugama scored 123 runs for 7 wickets in 42 overs after St. Mary’s College Matugama had batted first and made 176 on the first day of the 54th Battle of Pasdunkorale annual cricket match played at Matugama public ground yesterday. Match will continue today.
Scores:
St. Mary’s: 176 in 41 overs (Ujitha Madhuranga, 61, Sanjula Krishan 44, Thushan Sanjula 7/57)
Ananda Shashthralaya: 123/7 at close in 42 overs (Vidura Manthika 33, Ravindu Chanuka 27*, Dasun Tharuka 2/18, Vijitha Maduranga 2/21, Chamindu Imesh 2/21)
