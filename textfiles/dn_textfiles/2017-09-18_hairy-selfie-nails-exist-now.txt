Hairy selfie nails exist now

Are you looking for a fashion style that’s sure to turn a few heads? Then look no further, because these hairy selfie nails are guaranteed to make people take notice…for one reason or another.
They were created by Dain Yoon, a Korean “visual illusion artist” with almost 200k Instagram followers. She’s known for making mind-bending body art, but her latest creations might just be her most bizarre yet. Not only has she painted five little portraits of herself onto her fingernails; she’s also added hair to make each of the faces extra lifelike! Bored Panda
