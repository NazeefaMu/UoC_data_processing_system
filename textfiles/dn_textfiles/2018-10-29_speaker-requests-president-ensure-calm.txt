## Title: Speaker requests President to ensure calm
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 29, 2018 - 01:00

Speaker requests President to ensure calm

Speaker Karu Jayasuriya in a memorandum addressed to President Maithripala Sirisena has requested him to ensure that an unfavourable atmosphere does not arise in the country as a result of Parliament being inactivated for 20 days until November 16 due to its prorogation.
The speaker stated that it is his prime duty is to protect the rights of the people.
