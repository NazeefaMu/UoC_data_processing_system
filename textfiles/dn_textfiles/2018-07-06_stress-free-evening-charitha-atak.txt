## Title: Stress free evening with Charitha Atak
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Friday, July 6, 2018 - 01:00

Stress free evening with Charitha Atak

Eminent Artist Wilson Gunaratna’s “Charitha Atak” (Updated Version) would go on boards at the Bishops Auditorium on July 7 at 6.30pm. The above play depicts the true political scenario of our mother land. Wilson Gunaratne who is an internationally acclaimed actor will portrait eight different characters with eight national concepts giving the audience fits of laughter from the beginning to the end of the play. Gunaratne says I don’t make jokes. I report the facts. One has to see it to believe it.
