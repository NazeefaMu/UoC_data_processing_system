## Title: One of the largest passenger ships in the world, Queen Elizabeth called over at the Colombo port
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, April 6, 2017 - 01:00

One of the largest passenger ships in the world, Queen Elizabeth called over at the Colombo port

 One of the largest passenger ships in the world, Queen Elizabeth called over at the Colombo port yesterday on route of their world voyage.The passengers took time off to visit Colombo and enjoy shopping and sightseeing. The ship anchored in Colombo and some of the passengers visiting the city. Pictures by Wimal Karunatilleke.
