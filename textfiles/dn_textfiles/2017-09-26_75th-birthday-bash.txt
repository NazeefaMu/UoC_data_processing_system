## Title: 75th birthday bash
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Tuesday, September 26, 2017 - 01:00

75th birthday bash

Sheranee Sellamuttu celebrated her 75th birthday with friends and family at a glitzy dinner at the Jubilee Room of the Galle Face Hotel.
Over 150 close friends and family got together to wish the birthday girl. Prime Minister Ranil Wickremasinghe and Professor Maithree Wickremasinghe graced the event. Longtime friend Dr. Hiranthi Wijemanne proposed the toast with Simon Seneratne reading out a birthday message from Sheranee’s sons Rajive, Dinesh, daughter in law Sonali and grandson Akash. Music for dancing, good food and wines made it an unforgettable evening. Pictures by Sulochana Gamage
