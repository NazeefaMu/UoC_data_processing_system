## Title: GMOA anti-SAITM vehicle parade to enter Colombo today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, September 15, 2017 - 11:45

GMOA anti-SAITM vehicle parade to enter Colombo today

 
The Government Medical Officers Association (GMOA) anti-SAITM vehicle parade is due to enter Colombo with 5,000 vehicles today.
GMOA Secretary Dr. Haritha Aluthge said on September 7, that when the parade reaches Colombo the entire city will be blocked and disrupted of all activities.
The vehicle parade travelled around the country on September 12, 13 and 14.
The demonstration is held in protest of government’s failure to solve the SAITM issue.
