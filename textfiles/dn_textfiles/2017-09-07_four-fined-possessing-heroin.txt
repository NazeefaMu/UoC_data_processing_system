## Title: Four fined for possessing heroin
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 01:00

Four fined for possessing heroin

Four suspects who pleaded guilty to the charges of possessing heroin were fined Rs. 40,000 by Matara Chief Magistrate and Additional District Judge Uresha de Silva.
Twelve milligrams of heroin seized from the possessions of these suspects during the raids were produced in Court as case productions.
The Magistrate ordered them to be destroyed.
According to the Police, the arrests were made during sudden raids and mobile operations conducted in Thihagoda, Polathugoda, Yatiyana and Kadawedduwa in the Thihagoda Police area.
A team of officers led by Inspector of Police Thushara Priyantha of the Thihagoda Police Station made these detections and prosecuted in the Matara Magistrate’s Court.
