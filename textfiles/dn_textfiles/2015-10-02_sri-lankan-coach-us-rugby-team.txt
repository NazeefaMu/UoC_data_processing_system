## Title: Sri Lankan to coach US rugby team
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, October 2, 2015 - 09:15

Sri Lankan to coach US rugby team

Natasha Perera a Sri Lankan by origin and who previously served as the assistant coach of the Southern California Griffin’s has been appointed as the new coach of the University of California, UoC sources reported this week.
Natasha who has a degree in Communications, with a concentration in Entertainment Studies from the University of California had represented the CSU Rugby Team for over 10 years previously.
