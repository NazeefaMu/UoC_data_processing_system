## Title: Stamp exhibition begins today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 14, 2018 - 09:33

Stamp exhibition begins today

The Postal Department will hold an exhibition of stamps at the postal headquarters on D.R. Wijewardene Road, Colombo 10.The exhibition will held from today and go on till 16th of December  It would be open for the public from 9.00 am to 6.00 pm.Old stamps, coins, notes, both local and foreign will be exhibited.
