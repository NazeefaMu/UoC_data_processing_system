## Title: Inciting posters recovered
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, June 17, 2017 - 01:00

Inciting posters recovered

Police yesterday succeeded in recovering a stock of posters bearing slogans that trigger communal disharmony when conducting a search at a shop belonging to a suspect arrested in connection with the attack on a Mosque in Mallawapitiya.
Two suspects who were involved in the attack on the Mallawapitiya mosque in Kurunegala were arrested by a special Police team yesterday.
They were remanded till June 19 after being produced before courts.
“These posters were recovered by the Police when a shop at Maraluwawa in wellawa belonging to a suspect now in remand custody was searched,” Police Media Spokesman DIG Priyantha Jayakody said.
“This stock of posters were carrying the slogans of Bodu Balasena, Govi Jana Peramuna and many other organizations,”Police said.
