## Title: BOC Sammanthurai awarded best service provider in East
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Wednesday, June 21, 2017 - 01:00

BOC Sammanthurai awarded best service provider in East

Text and Picture by A.B. Abdul Gafoor Ampara District Group Correspondent
The National Awarding Ceremony of the Bank of Ceylon for the year 2016 was held in Colombo recently to award the branches of Bank of Ceylon located islandwide for their service during the year 2016. BOC Sammanthurai got the first place in the year 2016 and was awarded the best service provider in the Eastern Province while Sammanthurai Branch was placed sixth in the under the best service provider contest.
