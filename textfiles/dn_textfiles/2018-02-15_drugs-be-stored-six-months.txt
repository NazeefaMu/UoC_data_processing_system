## Title: Drugs to be stored for six months
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, February 15, 2018 - 01:00

Drugs to be stored for six months

Health Minister Dr. Rajitha Senaratne has instructed authorities to store drugs required for six (6) months, Health Ministry spokesman said.
Minister Senaratne has ordered the Medical Supplies Division (MSD) of the Health Ministry and the State Pharmaceuticals Corporation (SPC) to coordinate daily to maintain an uninterrupted supply of drugs.
The required funds to maintain an uninterrupted drug supply has been provided. The SPC will buy drugs locally.The Minister had instructed SPC officials to set up a separate division to import drugs from foreign countries and recruit required human resources for this new section, he said.
Producing drugs locally is another topic discussed during the meeting. Health Services Director General Dr. Anil Jasinghe and MSD and SPC officials were present.
