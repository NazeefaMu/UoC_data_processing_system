## Title: Pond’s BB+ adds a pop of colour
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Friday, March 24, 2017 - 01:00

Pond’s BB+ adds a pop of colour

Pond’s recently unveiled the Pond’s BB+ looks for 2017 themed under ‘Neons and Nudes’ in collaboration with renowned Indian makeup artist Pallavi Symons.
Bringing out the Neons and Nudes palettes and creating vibrant looks for spring and summer, Pond’s BB+ together with Pallavi, demonstrated looks for all types of skin tones.
The segment included looks in the categories of day wear, work wear and evening wear covering base of all regular makeup occasions.
