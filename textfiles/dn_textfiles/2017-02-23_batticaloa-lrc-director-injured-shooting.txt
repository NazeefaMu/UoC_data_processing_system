## Title: Batticaloa LRC Director injured in shooting
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, February 23, 2017 - 01:00

Batticaloa LRC Director injured in shooting

Wimalraj Nesan, Director at the Land Reforms Commission (LRC) at the Batticaloa District Secretariat was injured in a shooting last evening.
According to the Police he was shot at by two unidentified gunmen who had arrived on a motorcycle at Kalawanchikudi.
The injured officer has been admitted to the Batticaloa Hospita and the Police are conducting further investrigations.
