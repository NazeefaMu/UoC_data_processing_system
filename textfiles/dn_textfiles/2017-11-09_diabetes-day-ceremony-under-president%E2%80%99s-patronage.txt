## Title: Diabetes Day ceremony under President’s patronage
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 9, 2017 - 01:00

Diabetes Day ceremony under President’s patronage

The national ceremony to mark World Diabetes Day on November 14 will be held at Galle Face Green in Colombo on November 12 under the theme, ‘Healthy Women - Healthy Nation’ under the patronage of President Maithripala Sirisena, said Endocrinologist and Diabetologist Dr. Uditha Bulugahapitiya.
Addressing a press conference held at the Sri Lanka Foundation Institute in Colombo, he said Health Minister Dr. Rajitha Senaratne, acting Director General of Health Services Dr. J. M. W. Jayasundara Bandara and others will participate. The celebration will commence with Diabetic Walk from Galle Face Green at 6.30 am.
According to Dr. Bulugahapitiya, several clinics to screen diabetes, obtain medical advice, carry out blood tests and other medical examinations, nutritional advice, etc. will be provided. In addition, lectures will be delivered by expert medical consultants.
There will be a medical exhibition and an awards presentation for winners of the school art exhibition held to mark World Diabetes Day. Free t-shirts, caps, spectacles, refreshments and more will be provided to all participants.
The event is organized by the Sri Lanka Diabetes Federation and the Sri Lanka College of Endocrinologists in collaboration with the NCD Unit of the Health Ministry.
