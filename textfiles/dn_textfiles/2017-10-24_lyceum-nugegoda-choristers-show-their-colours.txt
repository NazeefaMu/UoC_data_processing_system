## Title: Lyceum Nugegoda choristers show their colours
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Tuesday, October 24, 2017 - 01:00

Lyceum Nugegoda choristers show their colours

The third Annual Inter International Schools Choir competition was held on October 1 at the Defence School Auditorium. Lyceum Nugegoda emerged champions. Lyceum Panadura and Lyceum Wattala came in second and third. Many other leading international schools took part in this competition.
