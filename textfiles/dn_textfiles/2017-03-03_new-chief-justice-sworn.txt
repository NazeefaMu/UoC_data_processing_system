## Title: NEW CHIEF JUSTICE SWORN-IN
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Friday, March 3, 2017 - 01:00

NEW CHIEF JUSTICE SWORN-IN

The senior-most Judge of the Supreme Court, Priyasath Dep was sworn in as the new Chief Justice before President Maithripala Sirisena at the President’s official residence yesterday. Justice Priyasath Dep held the office of the Solicitor General from 2007 to 2011 and became a judge of the Supreme Court in 2011. He had fulfilled his duties as the Acting Chief Justice on several occasions. Justice Minister Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe, Secretary to the President P. B. Abeykoon and family members of Justice Priyasath Dep were present at the swearing-in ceremony.
