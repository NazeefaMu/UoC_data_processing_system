## Title: Online eZ Cash drug dealer arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, July 23, 2018 - 01:00

Online eZ Cash drug dealer arrested

The Galle Police Anti-vice Branch arrested a person for alleged drug dealing via eZ Cash online money transaction system.
The suspect was arrested following information received by two others who were arrested with heroin close to Anuradhapura.
The two had confessed that they had bought the drugs through eZ cash system.
The person arrested with 65 packets of heroin weiging of 1g and 560 mg of heroin at the Ukkalakulam area is allegedly the business dealer.
All three suspects are to be produced in Court.
