## Title: Mangala Samaraweera assumes duty as Media Minister
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, June 2, 2017 - 13:00

Mangala Samaraweera assumes duty as Media Minister

 
Minister Mangala Samaraweera assumed duty as Mass Media and Information Minister at the Media Ministry today.
The former Foreign Affairs Minister was appointed as Media Minister by President Maithripala Sirisena on May 22.
