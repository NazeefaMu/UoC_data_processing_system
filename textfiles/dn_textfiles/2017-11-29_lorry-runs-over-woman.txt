## Title: Lorry runs over woman
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, November 29, 2017 - 01:00

Lorry runs over woman

A thirty-five-year-old woman died in a lorry accident at the Madamawidi railway crossing in the Batticaloa–Trincomalee main road on Monday afternoon.
The woman had sustained head injuries and died in the accident.
She had fallen off a motorcycle driven by another and the lorry came behind had ran over her.
The deceased was identified as Abdul Lithipi Siththi Railna of Valachchenai.
The body was handed over to the relatives after the postmortem, police said.
The lorry driver was arrested.
Investigations are continuing.
