## Title: The renovation of St Joseph’s Church in Kolonnawa
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, June 22, 2018 - 01:00

The renovation of St Joseph’s Church in Kolonnawa

The renovation of St Joseph’s Church in Kolonnawa commenced yesterday in view of its 75th jubilee with the participation of Colombo Archbishop Rt.Rev. Malcolm Cardinal Ranjith and Megalopolis and Western Development Minister Patali Champika Ranawaka and Colombo district MP S.M. Marikkar. Picture by Rukmal Gamage.
