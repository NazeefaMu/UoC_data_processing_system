## Title: Indian Air force chief calls on PM
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, December 12, 2017 - 01:00

Indian Air force chief calls on PM

Indian Air Force Commander Air Chief Marshal Birender Singh Dhanoa yesterday called on Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe at Temple Trees. Picture shows the visiting Indian Air Force Chief presenting a memento to the Prime Minister. Picture courtesy Prime Minister’s Media Division
