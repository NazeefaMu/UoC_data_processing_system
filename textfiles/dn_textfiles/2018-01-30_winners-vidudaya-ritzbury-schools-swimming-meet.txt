## Title: The winners of the Vidudaya-Ritzbury Schools Swimming Meet
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, January 30, 2018 - 01:00

The winners of the Vidudaya-Ritzbury Schools Swimming Meet

The Vidudaya-Ritzbury Inter-School Swimming Championship 2018 was held at Sri Jayewardenepura University Swimming Complex on Sunday at which around 2,500 swimmers from 130 schools competed in 96 individual events and 24 relay events in the under 9, 11, 13, 15, 17 and 19 boys and girls categories .
