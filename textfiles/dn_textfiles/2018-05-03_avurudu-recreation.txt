## Title: Avurudu recreation
## Supertitle: 
## Section: T&C
## Date: Thursday, May 3, 2018 - 01:00

Avurudu recreation

National Housing Development Authority Head Office in Colombo its Welfare Association organised an Avurudu Festival Fun And Sports at Borella Cample Park play ground recently. NHDA Chairman S Palansooriya participated as the Chief Guest. The employees and children played many games and were awarded.
