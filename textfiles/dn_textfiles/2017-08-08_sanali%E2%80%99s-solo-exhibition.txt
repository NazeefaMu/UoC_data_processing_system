Sanali’s solo exhibition

Artist Sanali Wijesuriya’s debut solo exhibition was showcased recently at the J D A Perera Art Gallery. Ven Onegama Upali thera of Ananda Vidyalaya, Ven Rawanagoda Gyanasara thera, Art adviser to the Horana educational zone B A Mangala Priyantha graced the event. Pictures by Wasitha Patabandige
