## Title: Cargills Bank opens In-Store branch in Rajagiriya
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Tuesday, April 3, 2018 - 01:00

Cargills Bank opens In-Store branch in Rajagiriya

Cargills Bank opened its newest “In-Store” branch in Rajagiriya, as a part of its expansion drive throughout the island. Located at Cargills Food City, Sri Jayawardenapura Mawatha, Welikada, Rajagiriya, the latest branch opening will be Cargills Bank’s 18th branch.
The new branch was opened by the Chief Guest, Chairman and Non-Executive Director of Cargills Bank Louis Page and Guests of Honour, Deputy Chairman and Non-Executive Director of Cargills Bank Ranjith Page and President and Chief Executive Officer of The Coca-Cola Company James Quincey, in the presence of Deputy CEO and Managing Director of Cargills (Ceylon) Imtiaz Wahid, CEO of Cargills Food Company, Sidath Kodikara, COO of Cargills Foods Company Pvt Ltd Niroshan De Silva, Managing Director and CEO of Cargills Bank Ltd Rajendra Theagarajah, Executive Director of Cargills Bank Ltd Prabhu Mathavan, COO of Cargills Bank Ltd Rohan Muttiah, DGM Retail Banking of Cargills Bank Ltd Darshana Ratnayake and Cargills Bank Rajagiriya Branch Manager Eranga Silva.
“We are pleased to open our new branch in Rajagiriya which is a vibrant and a fast developing township. Whilst the opening of our new branch gradually increases ournetwork, we are glad to provide the community at large an opportunity to experience our superior “In-Store” Banking services through longer banking hours and 365-day banking. While we continue our expansion, this reinstates our commitment on the concept of supermarket banking, bringing banking and shopping under one roof” said Rajendra Theagarajah, MD and CEO of Cargills Bank Ltd.
“Cargills” is a brand that has been faithfully serving Sri Lankans for 174 years, built on a foundation of values and ethics. The Cargills Bank Debit Card with special offers is now a ‘must have’ for discerning customers.
