## Title: Six-year-old drowned
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 01:00

Six-year-old drowned

A six-year-old boy named E.M. Arosh died after falling into an unprotected 5-feet deep waterhole near his house at Paraghamukalene, Hasalaka.
Although he was rescued by his 14-year-old cousin Asiri Nanda Kumara and rushed to the Mahiyangana Hospital with the help of an uncle, Arosh was pronounced dead on admission to hospital. Inquirer into Sudden Deaths Mahiyangana K. Rubasinghe, who held the inquest returned a verdict of death caused by asphyxia from drowning.
