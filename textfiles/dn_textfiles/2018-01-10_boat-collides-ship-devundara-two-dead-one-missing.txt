## Title: Boat collides with ship at Devundara: Two dead, one missing
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 10, 2018 - 10:11

Boat collides with ship at Devundara: Two dead, one missing

(File photo)
A multi-day boat carrying 6 fishermen collided with a ship at Devundara sea killing two individuals this morning, Sri Lanka Navy confirmed.
Of the 6, one fisherman is reportedly missing while three others were rescued by the Navy.
Search missions and further investigations are currently underway.
