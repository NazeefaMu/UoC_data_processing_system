## Title: Seylan Bank relocates Kandy branch
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Friday, December 15, 2017 - 01:00

Seylan Bank relocates Kandy branch

Seylan Bank, a leading private banks, relocated its Kandy branch to a more spacious and convenient location along with more facilities for customers. Located at Dalada Veediya, Kandy, the newly relocated branch now offers seamless access to the bank’s products and services for customers in and around Kandy area.
