## Title: PM condole with Rauff Hakeem
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, September 25, 2017 - 01:00

PM condole with Rauff Hakeem

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe visited SLMC Leader Minitser Rauff Hakeem’s residence and expressed his sympathies on the demise of his mother Hajara Rauff (89) on Sunday noon. Her other sons were also present.
