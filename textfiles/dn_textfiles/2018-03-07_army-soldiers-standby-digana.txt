## Title: Army soldiers on standby at Digana
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, March 7, 2018 - 01:00

Army soldiers on standby at Digana

Following the tense situation at Digana on Monday, a contingent of 200 Army personnel are on standby to assist the Police if the need arises, the Army media unit said yesterday.
The Army said, 200 soldiers are on standby following a request made by the Police.
At the request of the Police, a contingent of 200 Army personnel of the 11 Division under the Security Force Headquarters - Central (SFHQ-Cen) are now on standby in affected areas, a spokesman said.
