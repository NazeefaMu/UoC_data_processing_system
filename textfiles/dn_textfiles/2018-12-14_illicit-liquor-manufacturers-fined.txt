## Title: Illicit liquor manufacturers fined
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Friday, December 14, 2018 - 01:00

Illicit liquor manufacturers fined

Four suspects who were found guilty of producing and selling illegal liquor were fined Rs. 77,000 by Wariyapola Magistrate Dharshana Silva on Tuesday. There were twin brothers among the suspects, the Police said. Wariyapola Police prosecuted.
