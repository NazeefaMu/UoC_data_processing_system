## Title: Dinesh Chandimal, Upul Tharanga to captain Sri Lanka cricket team
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, July 12, 2017 - 10:15

Dinesh Chandimal, Upul Tharanga to captain Sri Lanka cricket team

 
Sri Lankan Cricket (SLC) said today that Dinesh Chandimal was appointed to captain test matches while Upul Tharanga was appointed to captain One Day Internationals and T20 matches.
The decision was made after former Sri Lankan Cricket captain Angelo Mathews decided to step down from the post as Sri Lanka Cricket Captain, today.
