## Title: Avurudu  fun for all
## Supertitle: 
## Section: T&C
## Date: Tuesday, April 24, 2018 - 01:00

Avurudu  fun for all

Musaeus College, Colombo, held its Aurudu Festival for grade one and two children at the school’s primary section ground recently. The Chief Guest of the event was Musaeus College principal N Senadeera. The event was organized by the primary head mistress S Tennakoon and the grade one and two teachers.
