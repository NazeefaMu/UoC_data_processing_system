## Title: Tuanku Ja’afar Trophy champion Lankan lads meet President
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, May 23, 2018 - 01:00

Tuanku Ja’afar Trophy champion Lankan lads meet President

The champion Sri Lankan under-15 cricket team who won the Tuanku Ja’afar Trophy quadrangular series in Malaysia met President Maithripala Sirisena at the President's office in Colombo yesterday. The President conveyed his wishes to the youngsters and posed for a photograph with the team and the Tuanku Ja’afar Trophy. Sri Lanka beat South Africa by a commanding 131 runs in the final played at the Bayuemas Oval, Malaysia.
