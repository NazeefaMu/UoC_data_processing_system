## Title: Police use water cannons and tear gas on anti-SAITM protesters
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 10, 2017 - 16:45

Police use water cannons and tear gas on anti-SAITM protesters

 
Police fired water cannons and tear gas to disperse anti-SAITM protesters who were blocking roads in Kollupitiya today.
The protesters were demanding the protection of state education and the abolishing of SAITM.
