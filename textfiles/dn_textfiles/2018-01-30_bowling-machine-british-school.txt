## Title: Bowling machine for British School
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, January 30, 2018 - 01:00

Bowling machine for British School

The British School in Colombo (BSC) received a cricket bowling machine through the courtesy of the BSC Parent Teachers’ Association (PTA) during a ceremony held at the school premises recently.
Former Sri Lanka Captain Mahela Jayawardene inaugurated the cricket bowling machine in the presence of BSC Principal Dr. John Scarth and BSC Cricket Coach Aruna Batapolaarachchi.
Jayawardena speaking at this occasion emphasized the importance of the cricket bowling machine but also appealed to the young cricketers to enhance their natural talent which is the key for success.
