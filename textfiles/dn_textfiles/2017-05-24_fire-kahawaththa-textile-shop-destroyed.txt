## Title: Fire in Kahawaththa: textile shop destroyed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 24, 2017 - 13:00

Fire in Kahawaththa: textile shop destroyed

 
A textile shop in Kahawaththa was totally destroyed in a fire this morning.
 Police said that the reason for the fire is not yet identified.
Kahawaththa Police is investigating.
