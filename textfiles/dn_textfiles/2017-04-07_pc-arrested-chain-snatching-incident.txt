## Title: PC arrested in chain snatching incident
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, April 7, 2017 - 01:00

PC arrested in chain snatching incident

A Police Constable attached to the Cinnamon Gardens Police station was arrested for allegedly snatching the gold chain from a woman at Viharamadevi Park yesterday.
The husband of the woman and the Civil Security Department employees working at the Park gave a chase and apprehended the suspect.Later, he was handed over to the Cinnammon Gardens Police.
Police said that the suspect was a PC attached to the Cinnamond Gardens Police station. He was to be produced before the Colombo Fort Magistrate last evening.
