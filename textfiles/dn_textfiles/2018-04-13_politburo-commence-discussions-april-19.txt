## Title: Politburo to commence  discussions on April 19
## Supertitle: UNP internal reforms and restructuring
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, April 13, 2018 - 01:00

UNP internal reforms and restructuring
Politburo to commence  discussions on April 19

The 12-member politburo appointed to introduce internal reforms and ensure the restructuring of the United National Party (UNP), would commence discussions on April 19, UNP Deputy Leader Ravi Karunanayake said. Speaking to the Daily News, he said following the discussions, appointments to key positions were set to be announced prior to May 1.
“While all reforms and restructuring may not be possible prior to that the key positions however, will be announced before May 1,” he said. The United National Party elected a 12-member politburo to finalise internal reforms and restructure and recommend new party office-bearers, following a marathon meeting on party restructure that was held on April 7.
