## Title: UN Under-Secretary-General for Political Affairs meets Sampanthan
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, July 21, 2017 - 15:30

UN Under-Secretary-General for Political Affairs meets Sampanthan

 
UN Under-Secretary-General for Political Affairs Jeffrey Feltman met with Tamil National Alliance (TNA) Leader Sampanthan and other party members today in Colombo.
Jeffrey Feltman arrived in Sri Lanka on July 19 for a three day visit.
He held discussions with party leaders on the progress of peace building in Sri Lanka.
