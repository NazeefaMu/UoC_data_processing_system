## Title: Fish exports to become biggest forex earner
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 10, 2017 - 01:00

Fish exports to become biggest forex earner

The objective of the Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Development Ministry is to transform fish exports into the country’s biggest foreign exchange earner by 2020, Minister Mahinda Amaraweera said.
Addressing Hambantota District Media Personnel at a get together at the Ambalantota Seva Lanka Institute, the minister said unlike rice produced in Sri Lanka, its fish harvest has a great foreign demand. The ministry will increase fish exports to meet the ever increasing foreign demand, he said.
He said his ministry has planned a housing assistance scheme for fishermen by ensuring a Rs. 100,000 allowance for each fishermen for putting up a house of his own.
