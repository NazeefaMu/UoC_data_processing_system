## Title: Polgampola leaves JO
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Saturday, January 6, 2018 - 01:00

Polgampola leaves JO

Former Kegalle District MP Anuruddha Polgampola has left the Joint Opposition to form another group of his own.He said the party wanted to bring the UNP into power. This was the main reason for him to leave the party. He further said that the party has had several rejections of nominations ahead of the upcoming local government polls.
