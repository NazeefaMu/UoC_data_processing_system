## Title: Police seize 50kg of Kerala Ganja
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 24, 2017 - 12:30

Police seize 50kg of Kerala Ganja

 
Weliwetithura Police recovered 50kg of Kerala Ganja from the Udayasooriyan beach this morning.
The raid was made according to a tip-off received by the Police.
It was revealed that the Kerala Ganja was exported from India by a boat.
Navy and Police are investigating to arrest the escaped suspects and take the boat into custody.
