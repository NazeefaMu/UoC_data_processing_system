## Title: Woman with  heroin arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Wednesday, October 3, 2018 - 01:00

Woman with  heroin arrested

Police arrested a woman possessing 3.1 grams of heroin on Monday, at Kadirana.
The suspect, 36, was identified as a resident of Samithpura, Mattakkuliya. She was to be produced at the Hulftsdorp Magistrate’s Courts
Investigations are continuing.
