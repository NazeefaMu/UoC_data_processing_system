## Title: Showers expected in North and East
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, December 24, 2018 - 08:12

Showers expected in North and East

Several spells of shower will occur in Northern, Eastern and North-central provinces said the Meteorology Department.
Showers or thundershowers will occur elsewhere particularly after 2.00p.m.
Fairly heavy falls about 75mm can be expected at some places in Central, Sabaragamuwa and Western provinces.
Misty conditions can be expected at some places in the island during the morning.
There may be temporary localized strong winds during thundershowers. General public is requested to take adequate precautions to minimize damages caused by lightning activity.
