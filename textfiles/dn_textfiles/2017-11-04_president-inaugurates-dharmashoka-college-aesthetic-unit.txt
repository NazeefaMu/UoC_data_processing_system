## Title: President inaugurates Dharmashoka College Aesthetic Unit
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, November 4, 2017 - 01:00

President inaugurates Dharmashoka College Aesthetic Unit

President Maithripala Sirisena on Thursday opened the Aesthetic Unit of Dharmashoka College, Ambalangoda. Picture shows the President being presented with a memento by wPrincipal Hasitha Kesara Wettamuni. Chairman of the Elections Commission Mahinda Deshapriya, Southern Province Governor Hemakumara Nanayakkara and Chief Minister Shan Wijayalal de Silva were also present. Picture by Kapila Ariyawansa
