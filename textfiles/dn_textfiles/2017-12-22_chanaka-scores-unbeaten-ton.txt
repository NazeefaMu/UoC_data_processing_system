## Title: Chanaka scores unbeaten ton
## Supertitle: Premier League Tier ‘B’ Cricket
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, December 22, 2017 - 01:00

Premier League Tier ‘B’ Cricket
Chanaka scores unbeaten ton

Chanaka Ruwansiri of Lankan CC scored an unbeaten ton (120 runs) against Air Force SC in the 1st week of the SLC Premier League Inter Club Tier ‘B’ Cricket tournament match at Maggona yesterday.
Matches were called off earlier due to rain.
n At Maggona.
