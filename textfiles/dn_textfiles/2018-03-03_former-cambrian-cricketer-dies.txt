## Title: Former Cambrian cricketer dies
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, March 3, 2018 - 01:00

Former Cambrian cricketer dies

Former Cambrian, Thurstanite, Nomads Sports Club and Moratuwa Sports Club Cricketer Samantha Aponso died under tragic circumstances at the age of 59 years.
His remains lie at Polkotuwa Junction Galle Road Rawatawatte Moratuwa and the cottage leaves residence at 4 pm on 3rd March 2018 for burial at General Cemetery Rawatawatte, Moratuwa.
