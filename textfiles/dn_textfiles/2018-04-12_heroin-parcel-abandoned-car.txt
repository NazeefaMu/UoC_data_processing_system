## Title: Heroin parcel in abandoned car
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 12, 2018 - 01:13

Heroin parcel in abandoned car

A parcel containing heroin was found inside a vehicle that was abandoned on the Southern Expressway around 3.05 on Tuesday evening.
The vehicle that had defied police orders and proceeded from the Kaduwela entrance towards Matara was later found abandoned on the Southern Expressway.
Police had later searched the vehicle and found a parcel inside the vehicle with 56 grams and 100 milligrams of heroin.
Investigations are being carried out based on the vehicle registration number to nab the suspects.
The car had been handed over to the Athurugiriya Police for further investigations.
