## Title: Ragama CC beat Saracens
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, January 16, 2016 - 01:00

Ragama CC beat Saracens

Ragama CC beat Saracens CC innings and 159 runs in the first round match of "AIA Trophy" Premier League Inter Clubs Cricket tournament played at Surrey ground Maggona yesterday.
Dhananjaya De Silva (Tamil Union) and Roshen Silva (Ragama CC) scored centuries against Chilaw Marians and Saracens CC. Malinda Pushpakumara (Moors) took 8 wickets for 41 runs. 23 wickets fell on day one at CCC.
Other matches will be continued today.
