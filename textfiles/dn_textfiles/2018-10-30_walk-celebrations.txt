## Title: Walk for celebrations
## Supertitle: 
## Section: T&C
## Date: Tuesday, October 30, 2018 - 01:00

Walk for celebrations

President’s College Kotte organized ‘President's Walk 2018’ to mark the 40th Anniversary of the school recently. It was held under the patronage of Principal U D D G Ariyarathna. Deputy Principals, Vice Principals, teachers, parents, students and old boys were present.
