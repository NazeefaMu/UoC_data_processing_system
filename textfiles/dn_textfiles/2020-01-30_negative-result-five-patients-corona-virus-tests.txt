## Title: Negative result on five patients Corona Virus tests
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, January 30, 2020 - 12:45

Negative result on five patients Corona Virus tests

17 suspected individuals infected with Coronavirus are at National Institute of Infectious Diseases (NIID) under surveillance. 6 patients were admitted to the IDH over the past 24 hours and  Five were discharged during last 24 hours after tests confirmed that they had not been infected by the Coronavirus, Health Ministry spokesman said.
