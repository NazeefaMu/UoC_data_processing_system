## Title: Three persons attempting to smuggle foreign liquor, cigarettes nabbed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, May 4, 2018 - 01:00

Three persons attempting to smuggle foreign liquor, cigarettes nabbed

The Customs nabbed three persons on May 2 who were attempting to smuggle foreign liquor and cigarettes into the country.
The stock comprised 282 bottles of whisky, 253 bottles of wine, 46 bottles of beer and 1,000 cigarettes at the value of Rs. 1,776,000. The goods were attempted to smuggle in a passenger bag.
The suspects, a Sri Lankan shipper and two others, were handed over to the Police Narcotics Bureau on May 2. Investigations are continuing.
