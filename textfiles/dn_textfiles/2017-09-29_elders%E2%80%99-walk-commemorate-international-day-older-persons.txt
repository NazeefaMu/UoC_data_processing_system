## Title: Elders’ walk to commemorate 'International Day of Older Persons'
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, September 29, 2017 - 01:00

Elders’ walk to commemorate 'International Day of Older Persons'

The `International Day of Older Persons’ will be celebrated by the Social Empowerment, Welfare and Kandyan Heritage Ministry jointly with HelpAge Sri Lanka (HASL) and the National Secretariat of Elders on Sunday, October 1 at the BMICH.
President Maithripala Sirisena and Social Empowerment, Welfare and Kandyan Heritage Minister S.B. Dissanayaka, Social Empowerment, Welfare and Kandyan Heritage Deputy Minister Ranjan Ramanayaka and HelpAge Chairman Tilak de Zoysa will attend the ceremony which will begin at 10 am.
The event will be conducted under the theme `Stepping into the future; tapping the talents, contributions and participation of older person in society’.
The `Elders’ Walk’ is the significant feature of the day’s event. Over 600 elders from many parts of the country will take part in this walk commencing from 8.30 am from All Ceylon Women’s Buddhist Congress (ACWBC) premises, Bauddhaloka Mawatha to BMICH. About 50 youth will walk along with elders to show their respect for the senior citizens in Sri Lanka.
In addition to the Walk, elders will also take part in fun-filled activities such as singing, dancing and performing in dramas at the BMICH. (AK)
