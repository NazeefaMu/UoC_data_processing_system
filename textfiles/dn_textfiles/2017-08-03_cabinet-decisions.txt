## Title: CABINET DECISIONS
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Thursday, August 3, 2017 - 01:00

CABINET DECISIONS

 Implementation of the Second Integrated Road Investment Programme
The first phase of the above programme was implemented in 2014 and is under progress. The second phase aims at rehabilitating and maintaining 340 km of national roads and 3100 km of rural access roads in Northern, Eastern, Uva and Western Provinces.
The proposal made by Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe as the Minister of National Policies and Economic Affairs, to hold negotiations with Asian Development Bank and enter into relevant long term agreements for obtaining funds, was approved by the Cabinet of Ministers.
Maintaining and expanding the rail road network
The proposal made by Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe as the Minister of National Policies and Economic Affairs to hold negotiations and enter into agreements on facilities provided by the Australian Government, to implement a project at a cost of 7.6 million Euros for following activities, was approved by the Cabinet of Ministers.
* Re-establishment of seven bridges which are more than 100 years old in coastal, Northern and Matale lines
* Renovation of Maho, Galle and Dematagoda
* Provision of tools and equipment for maintenance of rail road network
Regulations for the use of air weapons for affirming security of wild animals
Air weapons to protect cultivation from wild animals has been alleged to be used in hunting wild animals. Accordingly, the proposal made by Minister of Sustainable Development and Wildlife, Gamini Jayawickrema Perera, to implement the following recommendations of the committee appointed to look into this matter, was approved by the Cabinet of Ministers.
* Examine the possibility of producing an air rifle with low power instead of the present 0.22 mm rifle and encourage producing an air rifle that can drive away animals by sound.
* Regulation of issuing 0.22 mm air rifles and include them in Firearms Ordinance
* Researching on controlling animals through biological methods with expert assistance.
* Limitation of using pesticides
* Use of modern scientific methods to control the damage caused by wild animals
* Use of traditional methods used in driving away wild animals by developing with modern technology
* Minimizing waste disposal in open areas that causes the attraction of wild animals
* Limit the importation of materials used in producing “Hakkapatas”
Establishment of a regional secretariat in Sri Lanka for South Asian Conference on Sanitation
The proposal made by Minister of City Planning and Water Supply Rauf Hakeem to establish the above secretariat in Sri Lanka for the exchange of knowledge and experience among South Asian countries, was approved by the Cabinet of Ministers.
Establishment of a policy framework of resettlement of the Strategic Cities Development Project
The proposal made by the Minister of Megapolis and Western Development Patali Champika Ranawaka to base the improved policy framework of resettlement on payment of compensation for affected persons due to the said project, was approved by the Cabinet of Ministers.
Importation of cashew nuts
The annual demand of cashew nuts is 20,000MT but the domestic production is only 10,000MT-12,000MT. To overcome this cashew nut shortage, the following method proposed by Minister of Agriculture Duminda Dissanayake, Minister of Public Enterprise Development Kabir Hashim and Minister of Primary Industries Daya Gamage was approved by the Cabinet of Ministers.
*Importation of 6,000MT under a license issued by Director General of Agriculture for domestic markets and 15,000MT for re-export under Temporary Importation for Export Processing Programme (TIEP).
*Value addition for cashew nuts imported under TIEP and assure the sum of cashew imported are exported.
*Implementation of an accelerated programme to become self-sufficient in cashew in 2020.
Ownership of lands and housing facilities for plantation communities
The action plan presented by the Minister of Hill Country New Villages, Infrastructure and Community Development, which is prepared to grant ownership of houses which were given to plantation communities under various housing projects from 2010 to 2016 in several districts including Nuwara Eliya, Badulla and Kegalle, was considered by the Cabinet of Ministers.
Award of contracts for the construction of a building in University of Jaffna
The proposal made by the Minister of Higher Education and Highways Lakshman Kiriella to award following contracts was recommended by the Standing Cabinet Appointed Procurement Committee.
*New building for Department of Engineering Technology and Bio System Technology – Rs. 424.43 million
*New eight storeyed building for the faculty of medicine - Rs. 564.67 million.
Purchase of water bowsers for Local Government institutes in Eastern Province
The proposal made by the Minister of City Planning and Water Supply to purchase 23 water bowsers was recommended by the Cabinet Appointed Procurement Committee, as a short term solution to recurrent droughts in the area, was approved by the Cabinet of Minister.
Supply and distribution of accessories and DI pipes for the main pump house of Angunakolapelessa Water Supply Scheme
The above water supply project provides safe drinking water to the new prison complex of the area and about 2,000 families. The proposal made by the Minister of City Planning and Water Supply Rauff Hakeem to award the above contract was recommended by the Cabinet appointed Procurement Committee, at a cost of Rs. 166.74 million, was approved by the Cabinet of Ministers.
Proposals made by the Minister of Health
The proposals made by Minister of Health, Nutrition and Indigenous Medicine Dr. Rajitha Senarathna to award the following contracts as recommended by the Standing Cabinet Appointed Procurement Committee, was approved by the Cabinet of Ministers.
* Purchasing of essential theatrical items and equipment
* Purchasing of vaccines for child immunization programme (Diphtheria, Tetanus, Pertussis, Hepatitis B and Haemophilus Influenza Type B) at a cost of USD 568,800.
* Purchase of medicine for treating kidney patients at a cost of USD 2.19 million
Balance construction work for a storeyed building for Polonnaruwa District Secretariat
The proposal made by the Minister of Home Affairs, Vijira Abeywardana to award the contract for the construction of the second phase of the above project for locating all sections of the district secretariat in the same premises, was approved by the Cabinet of Ministers.
Printing of school textbooks to be distributed free of charge in 2018
The proposal made by the Minister of Education Akila Viraj Kariyawasam to print 18.53 million copies of 285 textbooks from 25 printing companies recommended by the Cabinet Appointed Procurement Committee, was approved by the Cabinet of Ministers.
Leasing of Race Course in Nuwara Eliya
The present lease period of the above racecourse expires on 31-12.2017 and the proposal made by the Minister of Sports Dayasiri Jayasekara to select a suitable buyer through calling for open bids for the next two years, was approved by the Cabinet of Ministers.
New Cities Development Project in Narahenpita – Kirimandala Mawatha
The proposal made by Minister of Megapolis and Western Development Patali Champika Ranawaka to utilize lands owned by various public institutions for the above project and to provide alternate lands for the said public institutions, was approved by the Cabinet of Ministers.
Payment of compensation to parties affected by the acquisition of lands to Light Rail Transit Project
Payment of a fair compensation to affected parties will help to acquire lands without delay. Accordingly, the proposal made by Megapolis and Western Development Minister Patali Champika Ranawaka to follow the methods of Land Acquisition and Resettlement Committees and Super Land Acquisition and Resettlement Committees in this regard, was approved by the Cabinet of Ministers.
Acceleration of rehabilitation and reconstruction activities in areas affected by floods and landslides
The proposal made by Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe as the Minister of National Policies and Economic Affairs to authorize District Secretaries to award contracts less than Rs. 50 million to contractors registered at Construction Industries Development Authority and to implement other midterm and long-term relief activities through relevant ministries and Provincial Councils, was approved by the Cabinet of Ministers.
Development of social ethics and morals based on reconciliation and equality through preschool education
The joint proposal made by the Minister of Provincial Councils and Local Government Faizer Mustapa and Minister of Education Akila Viraj Kariyawasam to introduce a series of lessons with half hour per week with practical activities on the above purpose, was approved by the Cabinet of Ministers.
Amendment of dates of Provincial Council Elections
According to the decision taken by the Cabinet on 25-07-2017, the 20th Amendment to the Constitution and Provincial Councils Elections (Amendment) Bill was prepared by the Legal Draftsman and the proposal made by Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe to publish them in the gazette and present in Parliament for approval, was approved by the Cabinet of Ministers.
Providing relief to people affected by prevailing dry weather
About 500,000 families in 17 districts have been affected and the following proposal made by President Maithripala Sirisena for providing relief was approved by the Cabinet of Ministers.
* Provide dry rations worth Rs. 5,000 monthly from August 2017 for families who have lost their livelihood and on the basis of obtaining their labour for common work.
* Provide compensation under relevant insurance covers for damage to cultivation.
* Continue drinking water distribution programmes.
