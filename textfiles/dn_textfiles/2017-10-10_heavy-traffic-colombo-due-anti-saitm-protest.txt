## Title: Heavy traffic in Colombo due to anti-SAITM protest
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 10, 2017 - 14:15

Heavy traffic in Colombo due to anti-SAITM protest

 
 
Severe traffic congestion has been reported in the Colombo Town Hall area due to a protest against SAITM.
Police has requested commuters to use alternate routes.
