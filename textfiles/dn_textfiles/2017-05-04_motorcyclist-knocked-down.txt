## Title: Motorcyclist knocked down
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 4, 2017 - 01:00

Motorcyclist knocked down

A motorcyclist who was riding on the Dambulla-Wewalawewa road collided with a tipper near Nikawatawana junction, killing him on the spot.
Police said the tipper driver had driven his vehicle recklessly.
The deceased is Nimal Karunathilaka, 44, a father of three. The driver, along with the tipper, were taken into custody.
Dambulla police are investigating.
