## Title: Town Hall hampered with traffic owing to protest
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 21, 2017 - 15:30

Town Hall hampered with traffic owing to protest

 
Town Hall is hampered with traffic due to a protest by the Inter University Student’s Federation (IUSF). 
The Special Task Force was called to ease the tension caused by the protesters at the Health Ministry. 
The protest is against the South Asian Institute of Technology and Medicine (SAITM) in Malabe.
