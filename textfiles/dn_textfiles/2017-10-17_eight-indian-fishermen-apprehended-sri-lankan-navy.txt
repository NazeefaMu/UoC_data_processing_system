## Title: Eight Indian fishermen apprehended by Sri Lankan Navy
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 17, 2017 - 10:00

Eight Indian fishermen apprehended by Sri Lankan Navy

 
Eight Indian fishermen were on
Tuesday detained by the Sri Lankan Navy, while fishing near the Delft Island.
The fishermen belong to Tamil Nadu's Rameswaram.
They have been taken to Sri Lanka's Mannar naval camp, and the investigation is underway.
