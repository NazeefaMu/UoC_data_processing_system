## Title: ‘Amaradeva Asapuwa’ in memory of Pandit Amaradeva
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 23, 2017 - 01:00

‘Amaradeva Asapuwa’ in memory of Pandit Amaradeva

President Maithripala Sirisena and Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe will lay the foundation stone for the proposed ‘Amaradeva Asapuwa,’ a music ashram in memory of late outstanding musician Pandit Dr. W. D. Amaradeva at ‘Ape Gama’ complex, Battaramulla on August 28 morning. It will consist of a research facility, archives and a museum.
Finance and Media Minister Mangala Samaraweera, Deputy Minister Lasantha Alagiyawanna, Ministry Secretary Dr. R. H. S. Samaratunge, Amaradeva Foundation President Wimala Amaradeva and Foundation Director Ranjan Amaradeva will also participate in the ceremony.
The Finance and Media Ministry is taking steps to build the ‘Amaradeva Asapuwa’ on the directions of President Maithripala Sirisena.
