## Title: Case against Daisy Forrest referred to AG
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Saturday, May 20, 2017 - 01:00

Case against Daisy Forrest referred to AG

The FCID conducting investigations into an alleged money laundering case against Daisy Forrest, 89, the grandmother of Yoshitha Rajapaksa yesterday informed the Colombo Chief Magistrate’s Court that they have sought the Attorney General’s advice regarding future legal action against the suspect.
The suspect is currently out on bail over a money laundering case into 31.5 perches of land with a house valued at Rs.36 million in Ratmalana, alleged to have been purchased by Yoshitha Rajapaksa.
President’s Counsel Jayantha Weerasinghe appeared for the suspect.
