## Title: Van crashes into an electric pole: 5 injured
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 23, 2017 - 13:30

Van crashes into an electric pole: 5 injured

 
Five passengers were hospitalised after a van veered off the road and crashed into a high tension electric pole at  Satturunkkondan on the Batticaloa-Trincomalee main road this morning.
The injured were admitted to the Batticaloa hospital 
Police said the accident occurred when the driver of the van had fallen asleep at the wheel.
Batticaloa Police is conducting further investigations.
