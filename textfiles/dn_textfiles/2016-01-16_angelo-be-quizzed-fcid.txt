## Title: Angelo to be quizzed by FCID
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, January 16, 2016 - 12:45

Angelo to be quizzed by FCID

Sri Lanka cricket captain Angelo Mathews is scheduled to make a statement to the police Financial Crimes Investigations Department (FCID) on Monday after being summoned, regarding an alleged match fixing attempt.
 
Earlier, cricketers Kusal Perera and Rangana Herath were questioned over the same incident. Opener Kusal Perera had earlier, informed cricket officials regarding a bookie approaching the player during the concluded Series against West Indies in October 2015.
