## Title: Puttalam Courts, production room broken into
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 01:00

Puttalam Courts, production room broken into

The production room of the Puttalam Courts Complex has been broken into by some unidentified persons who have entered it from the roof by removing tiles. All documents and productions relating to the Puttalam district Court and Magistrate Courts are stored in the production room.
Investigations are on to establish which items have been missing after the break in.
outside parties have been barred from entering the Courts Complex until investigations are completed.
