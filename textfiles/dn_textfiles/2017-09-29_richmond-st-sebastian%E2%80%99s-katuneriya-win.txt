## Title: Richmond, St. Sebastian’s Katuneriya win
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, September 29, 2017 - 01:00

Richmond, St. Sebastian’s Katuneriya win

Singer Cup U19 Schools Cricket:
Richmond College Galle and St. Sebastian’s College Katuneriya won their matches in their Singer Trophy Under 19 inter-schools cricket tournament yesterday.
Kamindu Mendis and Thaveesha Abhishek of Richmond College Galle scored centuries against St. Servatius College Matara at Galle International Cricket stadium
Richmond beat St. Servatius College Matara by an innings at Galle and 115 runs at Galle and St. Sebastian’s Katuneriya beat Sri Devananada College Ambalangoda by 70 runs at Katuneriya.
Left-hand opener Mendis scored 133 off 184 deliveries with 13 fours and a six. Thaveesha Abhishek scored unbeaten 100 off 106 balls with 8 fours and 2 sixes. Mendis and Abhishek shared a 163 runs stand for the fourth wicket.
