## Title: Tea factory thief caught
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, September 29, 2017 - 01:00

Tea factory thief caught

A person who is alleged to have robbed a number tea factories, was arrested by Morawaka Police at 4th Kilo Meter post on the Morawaka-Neluwa highway yesterday.
The suspect was arrested while riding on a motorcycle.
Police detected a locally made hand bomb in his possession. Police found that the suspect was connected to a number of thefts of cash and assets from several tea factories at Akuressa, Urbokka, Kosmodara, Hiniduama, Neluwa, Imaduwa, Yakkalamulla and Keeniya.
The suspect was to be produced before the Magistrate. Marawaka Police are investigating.
