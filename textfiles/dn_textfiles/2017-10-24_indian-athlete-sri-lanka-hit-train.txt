## Title: Indian athlete in Sri Lanka hit by train
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 24, 2017 - 01:00

Indian athlete in Sri Lanka hit by train

A 64-year old Indian athlete died yesterday hit by a train at Bambalapitiya while he was viewing the sea.
Police said the victim, Rajendran Panidai, was hit by the Maradana bound Kalutara South train at 2 pm at Bambalapitiya. Panidai had arrived in Sri Lanka with a group of Indian athletes to attend the Inter Mercantile Athletics Championship.
Bambalapitiya Police are investigating.
