## Title: New jersey for Al-Naja Sports Club players
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, November 4, 2017 - 01:00

New jersey for Al-Naja Sports Club players

The Al-Naja Sports Club, Addalaichenai introduced the New Jersey and New Flag of the club recently. The Jersey and flag launching ceremony was held at the Addalaichenai Beach guest house.
The ceremony was organized by the Al-Naja Sports Club President S.Ithrees. The Addalaichenai Divisional Secretary T.J.Athisayaraj was the chief guest on this occasion. Ampara District Athletic Coach S.L.Thajudeen, Youth Services Officer M. T. M.Haroon, Grama Niladhari M. I. Azwer and Konawatta Jummah Mosque President S. M. M. Ameen were participated as guests. Players who brought honour to Al-Naja Sports Club and their coaches were felicitated at the ceremony.
