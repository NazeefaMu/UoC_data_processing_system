## Title: TNA, UNF leaders meet President today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Friday, November 30, 2018 - 01:00

TNA, UNF leaders meet President today

The TNA will meet the President at 6.00 pm and the UNF at 7.00 pm today as agreed at the discussion held between President Maithripala Sirisena and Speaker Karu Jayasuriya yesterday, the Speaker’s office said. The Speaker met the President on a request made by Education and Higher Education Minister Wijeyedasa Rajapakshe yesterday.
