## Title: Lightning strikes woman in field
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 29, 2018 - 01:00

Lightning strikes woman in field

A 40-year-old mother of one child struck by lightning was admitted to the Dambulla Base Hospital on Friday.
The victim, Padma Dayani, is a resident of Kolabissa, Diulankadawela, Habarana. She was admitted to the Habarana Hospital before she was transferred to the Dambulla Hospital.
She was in her paddy field in the rain when the lightning struck her.
Habarana police are investigating.
