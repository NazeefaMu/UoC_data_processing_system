## Title: Liquor shops, casinos to be shut on April 29 and 30
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, April 27, 2018 - 01:00

Liquor shops, casinos to be shut on April 29 and 30

Government yesterday announced that all liquor shops, slaughter houses, casinos, betting centres and clubs should be closed on April 29 and 30, as these two days have been declared as Vesak days , a statement issued by the Government Information Department said yesterday.
The Information Department also said that the government has already declared the week beginning from April 26 as National Vesak Week, in view of Vesak festival.
