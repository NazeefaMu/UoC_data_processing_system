## Title: Strong winds to lash many regions today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, June 4, 2018 - 01:00

Strong winds to lash many regions today

Fairly strong winds up to 50 kmph can be expected over the Southern Province and the Moneragala, Matale, Nuwara-Eliya and Polonnaruwa districts today, the Meteorology Department said.
Windy condition is expected to strengthen over the island and in the sea areas around the island in next few days (from June 6).
Prevailing showery condition in the South-western part and cloudy condition will continue further, it added.
Showers or Thundershowers will occur at times in the Western, Southern, Sabaragamuwa, Central and North-Western Provinces.
Heavy rainfalls about 100 mm can be expected at some places, particularly in the Sabaragamuwa Province.Showers or thundershower may occur at a few places in the Ampara and Badulla districts after 2 pm.
Light showers may occur at few places in the Anuradhapura district.
