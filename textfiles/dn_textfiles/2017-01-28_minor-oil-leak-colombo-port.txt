## Title: Minor oil leak at Colombo Port
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, January 28, 2017 - 14:00

Minor oil leak at Colombo Port

[File Photo]
A minor oil leak was detected in the supply line between Colombo port and Kolonnawa, where the oil is stored.
Petroleum Industries Minister Chandima Weerakkody confirmed the leak and said Petroleum Corporation and Ports Authority officers were working to stem it.
