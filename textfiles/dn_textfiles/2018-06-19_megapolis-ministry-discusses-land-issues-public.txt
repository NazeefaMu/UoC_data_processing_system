## Title: Megapolis Ministry discusses land issues with public
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, June 19, 2018 - 01:00

Megapolis Ministry discusses land issues with public

A forum to answer questions relating to land and construction issues raised by the public in the Colombo district, was held at Arcade Independence Square, yesterday.
The event was organised by the Megapolis and Western Development Ministry, to mark the 40th anniversary of the Urban Development Authority (UDA).
It was held under the patronage of Megapolis and Western Development Minister Patali Champika Ranawaka and was participated by representatives of 13 local government bodies as well as representatives from the National Water Supply and Drainage Board (NWDSB), Ceylon Electricity Board (CEB), Central Environmental Authority (CEA), Govijana Seva Department, Sri Lanka Land Reclamation and Development Corporation (SLLRDC), Coast Conservation and Coastal Resource Management Department, Irrigation Department and Divisional Secretariats.
Thousands of people participated in the event, which commenced at 10 a.m. and concluded at 6 p.m.
