## Title: Bertie Wijesinha’s final journey
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, April 13, 2017 - 01:00

Bertie Wijesinha’s final journey

Prefects of S. Thomas’ College Mt Lavinia carrying the casket containing the remains of former Sri Lanka cricketer and coach Bertie Wijesinha to the College chapel yesterday to be received by the Warden S. Thomas’ College Rev. Mark Billimoria and Chaplain STC Rev. Amal Fernando. Wijesinha’s funeral took place at the General Cemetery Kanatte yesterday. Pix by Herbert Perera
