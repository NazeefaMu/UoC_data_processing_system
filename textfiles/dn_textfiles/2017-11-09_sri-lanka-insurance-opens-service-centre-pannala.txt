## Title: Sri Lanka Insurance opens service centre in Pannala
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, November 9, 2017 - 01:00

Sri Lanka Insurance opens service centre in Pannala

The Sri Lanka Insurance Consumer Service Centre has opened a service centre on the Pannala-Negombo road for the convenience of its customers in Pannala recently.
Sri Lanka Insurance Deputy General Manager Sales and Life Insurance Roshan Kolas, Provincial Sales Manager Kurunegala Upul Gamage, Sales Manager Giriulla Aruna Dissanayake and Senior Financial Advisor S.M. Nalaka at the opening ceremony. (AW)
