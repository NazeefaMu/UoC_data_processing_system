## Title: Piyasena Gamage sworn in as the State Minister of Law and Order and Southern Development
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Friday, December 29, 2017 - 01:00

Piyasena Gamage sworn in as the State Minister of Law and Order and Southern Development

Galle District Parliamentarian Piyasena Gamage has been appointed Law and Order and Southern Development State Minister. He was sworn in as the State Minister before President Maithripala Sirisena at the President’s Official Residence, yesterday. Picture by Sudath Silva.
