## Title: Vishva excels for St. Joseph’s Vaz
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, January 29, 2018 - 01:00

Vishva excels for St. Joseph’s Vaz

The all-round cricketer Vishva Rajapaksha of St. Joseph’s Vaz College, Wennappuwa representing his alma mater in the under 13 division one cricket tournament organized by Sri Lanka Schools Cricket Association and participating in eleven matches he scored 487 runs with five half centuries with the top score of an unbeaten 57 runs and became the highest run getter for his school and also claimed 28 wickets with a one ten wicket haul and the best being 16 for 11 runs and helped his college to reached the pre-quarters.
