## Title: President Sirisena expresses condolences on London attack
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Sunday, June 4, 2017 - 13:15

President Sirisena expresses condolences on London attack

 
President Maithripala Sirisena today tweeted his condolences to the terror attack in central London.
‘London bridge terror rampage is a dastardly and cowardly act. My thoughts are with the people of London in this hour of grief,’ the President tweeted.
Six people have been killed and three assailants were shot dead by London police.
