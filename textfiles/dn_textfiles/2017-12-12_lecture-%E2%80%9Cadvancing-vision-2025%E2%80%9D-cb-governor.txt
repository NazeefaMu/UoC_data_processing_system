## Title: Lecture on “Advancing Vision 2025” by CB Governor
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, December 12, 2017 - 01:00

Lecture on “Advancing Vision 2025” by CB Governor

“Advancing Vision 2025” will be the topic of a lecture by Central Bank Governor Dr. Indrajit Coomaraswamy to be held tomorrow (December 13) at 6 p.m. at the OPA Auditorium, 275/75, Prof. Stanley Wijesundera Mawatha, Colombo 7 (Off Bauddhaloka Mawatha). The lecture is sponsored by the British Scholars’ Association of Sri Lanka.
