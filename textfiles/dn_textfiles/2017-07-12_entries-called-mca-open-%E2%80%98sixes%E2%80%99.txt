## Title: Entries called for MCA Open ‘Sixes’
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, July 12, 2017 - 01:00

Entries called for MCA Open ‘Sixes’

The popular MCA Open Cricket ‘Sixes’ 2017 is scheduled to held on August 5 and 6 at the MCA grounds and 03 other selected venues (venues depending on the number of entries received)
Entries have been called from the 200 strong MCA member firms and will close on July 26. As done in the previous years, the tournament will be conducted in 02 segments. The Premier League, the ‘B’ and ‘C’ divisions teams playing in Tier ‘A’ (turf wickets) and the ‘D’, ‘E’, ‘F’ and ‘G’ in Tier ‘B’ (matting wickets).
In the 2016 tournament conducted on July 23 and 24, where 60 teams participated, LB Finance emerged the Tier ‘A’ winner beating Mobitel and Janashakthi Insurance won the Tier ‘B’ segment beating Stafford Motor Co.
