## Title: Police use water cannons on anti-SAITM protesters
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 21, 2017 - 17:30

Police use water cannons on anti-SAITM protesters

 
Police fired water cannons to disperse anti-SAITM protesters who were creating a tense situation by blocking roads in front of the Health Ministry today.
A large number of students have been hospitalised.
