## Title: Jaffna university students shooting: Five policemen re-remanded
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 24, 2017 - 01:00

Jaffna university students shooting: Five policemen re-remanded

The five policemen arrested for allegedly being involved in Jaffna university students’ shooting incident, were re-remanded yesterday until March 9.
The Jaffna Magistrate issued the order against the five policemen. Two Jaffna university students were shot dead while returning home from a function on October 20, 2016. Daily News online
