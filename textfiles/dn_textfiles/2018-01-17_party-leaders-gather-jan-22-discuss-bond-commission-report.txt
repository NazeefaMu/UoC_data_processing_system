## Title: Party Leaders to gather on Jan. 22 to discuss Bond Commission report
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 17, 2018 - 15:53

Party Leaders to gather on Jan. 22 to discuss Bond Commission report

 
Speaker Karu Jayasuriya said today that Party leaders will gather on January 22 to discuss further measures to be taken regarding the Bond Commission report.
He said this, after the Bond commission reports and the Serious Acts of Fraud, Corruption and Abuse of Power, State Resources and Privileges (PRECIFAC) reports were presented to him in Parliament.
Chief of Staff of Parliament Neil Iddawala further informed yesterday that the reports will be uploaded online by the Presidential Secretariat.
‘The reports will be tabled only after Parliament resumes on January 23’, Iddawala said.
