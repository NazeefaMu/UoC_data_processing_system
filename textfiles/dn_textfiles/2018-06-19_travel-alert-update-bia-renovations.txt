## Title: Travel alert update-BIA renovations
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Tuesday, June 19, 2018 - 17:43

Travel alert update-BIA renovations

SriLankan Airlines inform all passengers that in view of a slightdelay in the construction planned, they are not yet restricted in space at this early stage of the renovation. Hence passengers have the option of still reporting at normal times - that is three hours prior to departure time of flights.
SriLankan Airlines expresses their apologies for any inconvenience caused to passengers.
