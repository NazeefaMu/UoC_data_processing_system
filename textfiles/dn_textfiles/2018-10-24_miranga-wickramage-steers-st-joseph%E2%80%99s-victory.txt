## Title: Miranga Wickramage steers St. Joseph’s to victory
## Supertitle: Singer Cup U19 Schools Cricket:
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, October 24, 2018 - 01:00

Singer Cup U19 Schools Cricket:
Miranga Wickramage steers St. Joseph’s to victory

Miranga Wickramage steered St. Joseph’s College Colombo to an innings and 22 runs win over Gurukula College Kelaniya in their Singer Trophy Under 19 inter schools cricket match played at Darley Road yesterday.
Wickramage took a match bag of 11 wickets. Piliyandala Central College also won their match against Galahitiyawa Central College by 9 wickets. All the other matches were drawn.
