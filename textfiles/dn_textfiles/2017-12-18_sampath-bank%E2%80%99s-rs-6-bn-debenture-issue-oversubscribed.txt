## Title: Sampath Bank’s Rs 6 bn  debenture issue  oversubscribed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Monday, December 18, 2017 - 01:00

Sampath Bank’s Rs 6 bn  debenture issue  oversubscribed

Sampath Bank’s Rs 6 billion debenture issue on December 15 has been oversubscribed, the bank said in a stock exchange filing.
The basis of allotment will be notified to the CSE in due course, the bank said.
