## Title: Colombo High Courts releases former DIG Anura Senanayake on bail
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Friday, June 2, 2017 - 11:30

Colombo High Courts releases former DIG Anura Senanayake on bail

 
Former Senior Deputy Inspector General Anura Senanayake alleged to have covered up and conspired the murder of Wasim Thajudeen was granted bail a short while ago.
The decision followed after the revision bail application was taken up before the Colombo High Court today.
Colombo Additional Magistrate J. Trotsky yesterday ordered to further remand Anura Senanayaka who was arrested on May 23, 2016.
President’s Counsel Anil de Silva, appearing on behalf of the suspect requested yesterday to release his client from remand custody since he is held in remand custody over a period of one year contrary to the provisions of Bail Act.
