## Title: Army releases 120.89 more acres of land in Northern Province
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, June 19, 2018 - 01:00

Army releases 120.89 more acres of land in Northern Province

Deeds and documents of 120.89 acres of land in the Thelippalai, Karachchiand and Maritimepattu areas that troops occupied so far handed over on Monday 18 under the guidelines given by Army Commander Lieutenant General Mahesh Senanayke.
Kilinochchi Security Forces Commander Major General Niyshshanka Ranawana on behalf of the Army Commander presented the documents of Thelippalai (Jaffna), Maritimepattu (Mullaittivu) and Karachchi (Kilinochchi) to President Maithripala Sirisena when he arrived in Kilinochchi on July 18 for a formal meeting
The President in turn handed them back to the District Secretaries of Jaffna, Mullaittivu and Kilinochchi for onward release formalities.
President Sirisena attending the Kilinochch meeting of the Presidential project, Daruwan Surakimu at the Kilinochchi Central College took a brief respite in the Security Force Headquarters - Kilinochchi (SFHQ-KLN) before he left Kilinochchi.
Moreover, Security Force Headquarters - Mullaittivu (SFHQ-MLT) recently opened up the public road that runs through the SFHQ-MLT premises, connecting Vattappalai and Puthukudiyirippu for the New Year - 2018. It remained closed nearly eight years until April. Security Force Headquarters - Jaffna (SFHQ-J) reopened the main road that runs between Kankesanthurai (KKS) - and Point Pedro (PPD) along the AB - 21 road for public transport. It facilitates the easy connection to Thondamanaru and other villages and commercial activities.
