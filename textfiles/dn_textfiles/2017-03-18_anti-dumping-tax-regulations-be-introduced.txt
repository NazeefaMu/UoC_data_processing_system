## Title: Anti dumping tax regulations to be introduced
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, March 18, 2017 - 01:00

Anti dumping tax regulations to be introduced

The Government has decided to implement rules and regulations to stop the dumping of low standardized goods from South Asian countries to Sri Lanka.
Anti Dumping tax regulations would be introduced under the instructions of Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe. It would stop the dumping of low cost electronic equipment to vehicles produced in South Asian countries such as Taiwan, China and India.
Implementation of these rules and regulations are aimed at increasing the revenue of the Sri Lankans through production at industrial zones islandwide.
