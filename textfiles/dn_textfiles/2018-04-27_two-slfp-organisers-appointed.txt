## Title: Two SLFP organisers appointed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Friday, April 27, 2018 - 01:00

Two SLFP organisers appointed


Ajith Basnayake was appointed Gampaha Electorate Sri Lanka Freedom Party Organiser, while Chaminda Kumara Sudusinghe was appointed Colombo District Sri Lanka Freedom Party Organiser. They received their appointment letters from President Maithripala Sirisena at his official residence, yesterday.
