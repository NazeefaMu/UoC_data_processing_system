## Title: Muthu Sivalingam appointed Primary Industries Deputy Minister
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, February 15, 2018 - 16:40

Muthu Sivalingam appointed Primary Industries Deputy Minister

 
Muthu Sivalingam, Nuwaraeliya District MP and the President of the Ceylon Workers’ Congress was appointed as the Deputy Minister of Primary Industries, today.
He took oaths before President Maithripala Sirisena at the Presidential Secretariat, in Colombo.
