## Title: Former Lanka SATHOSA Chairman  granted bail
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 12, 2018 - 11:28

Former Lanka SATHOSA Chairman  granted bail

 
Former Lanka SATHOSA Chairman Nalin Ruwanjeewa Fernando was granted bail by the Colombo Fort Magistrate a shot while ago.
 
He is alleged to have  misappropriated state funds amounting to Rs. 39 million while importing sports equipment during the last Presidential Election.
