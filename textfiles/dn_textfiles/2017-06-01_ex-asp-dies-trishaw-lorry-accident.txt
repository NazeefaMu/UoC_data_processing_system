## Title: Ex-ASP dies in trishaw - lorry accident
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 1, 2017 - 01:00

Ex-ASP dies in trishaw - lorry accident

A former ASP died and his wife was seriously injured and admitted to Karapitiya Hospital when a three wheeler driven by him collided with a lorry at Kotagoda, Akmeemana yesterday.
M. K. Piyadasa, 68, of Joolgaha, Galle, died in the incident.
He was returning home with his wife after attending a wedding at Thalagaha, Akmeemana.
Akmeemana Police are investigating.
