## Title: Nalini Indatissa’s funeral today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, December 11, 2017 - 01:00

Nalini Indatissa’s funeral today

The funeral of Nalini Indatissa, wife of the late Sarath Indatissa, will take place today.
She was the former Director of Education, former Principal Tholangamuwa Central College, Pinnawala Central College, and Royal International School, Kegalle. She was the mother of Kalinga Indatissa (President’s Counsel and Nalinda Indatissa (Attorney-at-law). Her remains lie at No. 20, 1st lane, Epitamulla Road, Pita Kotte.
