## Title: Stride  with pride
## Supertitle: 
## Section: T&C
## Date: Tuesday, June 26, 2018 - 01:00

Stride  with pride

Sri Jayawardenepura Maha Vidyalaya, Kotte, held a walk to celebrate its 196th anniversary. Titled ‘Japurians’ Walk’ the walk commenced from the school premises and ended back in the school after roaming the streets of Pitakotte. The event was organized by the School Development Committee under the guidance of the school’s principal Major. D.A.D.Wanaguru. Past pupils, students, parents and well
wishers took part.
