## Title: New CDS assumed duties
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 23, 2017 - 01:00

New CDS assumed duties

Former Navy Commander Admiral Ravindra C.Wijegunaratne yesterday assumed duties as Chief of Defence Staff at CDS office at BMICH yesterday. The Chief of Defence Staff was accorded a Guard of Honour at the BMICH.
