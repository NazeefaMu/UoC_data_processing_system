## Title: PM meets Chinese, Japanese delegations
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, February 6, 2018 - 01:00

PM meets Chinese, Japanese delegations

Japanese Minister of Reconstruction and Chairman of the Japan-Sri Lanka Parliamentarians League WataruTakeshita, and Yoichi Anami of the Liberal Democratic Party, who visited the country to attend the 70thIndependence Anniversary celebrations called on Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe at Temple Trees yesterday. JapaneseAmbassador in Sri Lanka Kenichi Suganuma and Hiroshi Ishikawa  Director General of the JapaneseForeign Ministry’s Asian and Oceanic Affairs Bureau were present
