## Title: Arrested for growing cannabis
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 17, 2018 - 01:00

Arrested for growing cannabis

Hamegamuwa Police raided one acre of cannabis in Hamegamuwa Tank reservation on Monday. According to Police, there was 3,880 cannabis plants with the height of seven feet in this cultivation. Police arrested a man (30) who is said to be the owner of this cultivation. He is a resident of Fisheries Village, Hambegamuwa.The suspect, along with the sample of cannabis, was to be produced before Wellawaya magistrate Court on Tuesday.
