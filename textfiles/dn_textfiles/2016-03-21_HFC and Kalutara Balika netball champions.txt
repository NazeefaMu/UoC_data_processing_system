## Title: HFC and Kalutara Balika netball champions
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, March 21, 2016 - 01:00

HFC and Kalutara Balika netball champions

The Holy Family Convent Kalutara and Kalutara Balika National School emerged champions once again in their under 15,17 and 19 Kalutara Zonal Inter-school Netball Tournament held at Kalutara Vernon U. Fernando Sports Complex on March 9, 2016.
This tournament was organized by the Education Ministry under the guidance of the Zonal Sports Unit and 24 schools took part in this Netball Tournament and both Teams have been selected for the Western Provincial Tournament.
Result of Final:
Under 15: Holy Family Convent Kalutara scored a Thrilling victory over Kalutara Balika National School by 9 points to 7.
Under 17: Holy Family Convent, Kalutara easily won over the Kalutara Balika National School by 26 points to 18.
Under 19: Kalutara Balika National school beat Holy Family Convent, Kalutara Team comfortably by 17 points to 10.
