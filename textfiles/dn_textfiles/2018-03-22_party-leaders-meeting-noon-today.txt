## Title: Party Leaders meeting at noon, today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, March 22, 2018 - 10:34

Party Leaders meeting at noon, today

 
A special Party Leaders meeting will be held with the initiation of Speaker Karu Jayasuriya in Parliament at 12 noon today.
The meeting will be held to decide on a date to debate on the No Confidence motion signed against Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesighe, by the Joint Opposition members in Parliament, yesterday.
A date to debate on the Presidential Commission of Inquiry into the Central Bank issue and the Presidential Commission on serious acts of crime (PRECIFAC) reports will also be determined at the meeting.
Furthermore the United National Party (UNP) members will hold a meeting regarding the No Confidence motion filed against the Premier today, Lands and Parliament Affairs Minister Gayantha Karunatileka said.
