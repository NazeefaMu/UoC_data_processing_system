## Title: Indian nabbed with Rs 9.6 mn ‘party’ drug
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, July 23, 2018 - 01:00

Indian nabbed with Rs 9.6 mn ‘party’ drug

An Indian national was nabbed by the Police Narcotics Bureau (PNB) for the possession of Methamphetamine (Ice) worth Rs 9.6 million at the Bandaranaike International Airport on Saturday evening.
The suspect had arrived in the country from Bangalore concealing 806 grammes of Methamphetamine in his luggage.This drug has been found in low quantities by drug prevention agencies during detections in recent months.The PNB is conducting investigations.
