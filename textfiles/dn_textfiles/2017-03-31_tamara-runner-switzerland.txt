## Title: Tamara-runner-up in Switzerland
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, March 31, 2017 - 01:00

Tamara-runner-up in Switzerland

Former Sri Lanka women’s singles table tennis player and national champion Tamara Hewage (left) continued to show her prowess in the ping pong world when she finished runner-up at the St Gallen women’s open tournament held in the city of St Gallen, Switzerland.
She hopes to take part in the world masters table tennis tournament in Auckland from April 20-30.
