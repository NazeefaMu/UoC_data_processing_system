## Title: 154th Police Day ceremony
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Thursday, March 22, 2018 - 01:02

154th Police Day ceremony

Thousands of police personnel who made the supreme sacrifice for the sovereignty and territorial integrity of the country were remembered at the 154th Police Day ceremony held at Police Field Force Headquarters yesterday morning. Inspector General of Police Pujith Jayasundera in conversation with Police Constable K. P.V. G. De Silva who lost his eye sight and limbs in action in combat against LTTE terrorists in Kebethigollewa. Picture by Saman Sri Wedage.
