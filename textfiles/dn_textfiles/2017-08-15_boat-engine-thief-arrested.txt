## Title: Boat engine thief arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, August 15, 2017 - 01:00

Boat engine thief arrested

A 30-year-old man was arrested by Kalpitiya police for stealing a engine from a boat anchored at the Kalpitya Thurayadi fishing port. Kalpitiya police said the suspect was arrested following a complaint lodged by the boat owner.
The boat anchored at the port and the owner noted the following day that its engine worth Rs. 250,000 was robbed. Kalpitiya Police are investigating.
