## Title: Two flights from Seychelles and Chennai diverted to Mattala
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, October 11, 2017 - 15:15

Two flights from Seychelles and Chennai diverted to Mattala

 
Two flights from Seychellesand Chennai, scheduled to land at the Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA)  in Katunaike were diverted to the Mattala Airport today, due to bad weather.  
The two flights are due to return to the BIA later today.
