## Title: Election candidate charged with rape
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, January 30, 2018 - 01:00

Election candidate charged with rape

A candidate contesting from a main political party for the Mahiyangana Pradeshiya Sabha has been arrested by Girandurukotte Police on the charges of raping a woman.
The victim is a schoolgirl residing in the Mahaweli Village of the Girandurukotte Police division.
She is awaiting her A/L results having sat for the exam last year.
Her mother had lodged a complaint with Girandurukotte Police and had explained that when her daughter was alone at home on January 26 at around 11.30 am, the suspect had broken into her home, raped her daughter and fled.
The suspect is a teacher at a government school in the area and runs a tuition class outside of school hours.
The suspect is to be produced before the Mahiyangana Magistrate’s Court yesterday. The girl has been ordered to be subjected to a medical examination by the Judicial Medical Officer at the Mahiyangana Hospital.
