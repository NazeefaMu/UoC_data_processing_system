## Title: One day sale
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Tuesday, November 14, 2017 - 01:00

One day sale

The Lanka Alzheimer’s Foundation will hold their annual sale on November 18 at the Lanka Alzheimer’s Foundation, Maradana. Doors will open to shoppers at 10 am and the sale will go on till 5 pm. Many food stalls will also be available for you to snack on during the day. A huge range of export quality garments comprising ladies, men’s and kids wear will be up for grabs. The event is held to support a worthy cause – Lanka Alzheimer’s Foundation.
