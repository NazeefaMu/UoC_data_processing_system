## Title: Dharmayuddaya prevails
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Tuesday, September 19, 2017 - 01:00

Dharmayuddaya prevails

With 65-days of screening, the popular movie Dharmayuddaya has collected Rs 5 million at Lido Cinema, Borella. More than 20,000 patrons have seen the film at this cinema.
This cinema has the capacity of 600 seats with a large car park. The cinema is equipped with 2K Digital Sound System with A/C and the Largest Cinema screen for sinhala films, daily screening four shows at 10.30, 1.30, 4.15 and 7 pm.
