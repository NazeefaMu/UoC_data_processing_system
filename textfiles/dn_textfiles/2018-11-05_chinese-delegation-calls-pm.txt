## Title: Chinese delegation calls on PM
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, November 5, 2018 - 01:00

Chinese delegation calls on PM

A Chinese delegation including renowned eye surgeon Yao Xiaoming, film director from Hong Kong Sampsan Yuen Yun Hong and Chinese International Medical Science Institute General Secretary Lai Pingsum, called on Prime Minister Mahinda Rajapaksa recently. Dr. Yao, who had performed 1,000 free eye (corneal) transplants in Sri Lanka during 2014, had pledged to do more free eye surgeries in Sri Lanka soon.
