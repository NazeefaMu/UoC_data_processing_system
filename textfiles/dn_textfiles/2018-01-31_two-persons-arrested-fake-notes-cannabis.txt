## Title: Two persons arrested with fake notes, cannabis
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 31, 2018 - 01:00

Two persons arrested with fake notes, cannabis

Two persons aged 29 and 18 were arrested by Wariyapola police with fake Rs. 1,000 notes and 1120 mg of cannabis during a raid carried out on January 29 night at Awulegama, Wariayapola.
Police investigations are continuing to ascertain whether they are members of an organised gang involved in printing and circulating fake currency notes. The notes seized bore the serial number s/203/249875, police said.
