## Title: Prime Minister to Japan next week
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, April 7, 2017 - 11:30

Prime Minister to Japan next week

Prime Minister Rani Wickremesinghe will visit Japan from April 10 – 16.
He is expected to hold talks with Japanese Prime Minister Shinzo Abe, followed by several other engagements.
The Government of Japan has announced that they are looking forward to the visit which they believe will serve to further strengthen the friendly relations between Japan and Sri Lanka.
The Prime Minister will be accompanied by Minister of Special Assignments Dr. Sarath Amunugama and Minister of Development Strategies and International Trade Malik Samarawickreme.
