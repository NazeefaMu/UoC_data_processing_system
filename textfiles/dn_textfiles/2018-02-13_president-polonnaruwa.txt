## Title: President in Polonnaruwa
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, February 13, 2018 - 01:00

President in Polonnaruwa

President Maithripala Sirisena who cast his vote at the Sri Vidyaloka Viharaya polling centre in Polonnaruwa new town on Saturday took time to visit the town. The President who visited the Nishantha Sculpture Centre at Ethumalpitiya viewed the wide array of carvings and interacted with a group of tourists who were among the clientele at the centre.
