## Title: Mohammed Rukshan scores first ton
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, May 19, 2017 - 01:00

Mohammed Rukshan scores first ton

Singer Cup U17 Division I Schools Cricket:
St. Anthony’s College Kandy and St. Joseph Vaz College Wennappuwa won their matches in the first round of the Singer Trophy Under 17 division 1 inter-schools cricket tournament yesterday.
St. Anthony’s College Kandy beat St. Thomas College Matale by 159 runs at Kandy and St. Joseph Vaz College Wennappuwa beat St. Xavier’s Maravila by 12 runs according parabola system at Wennappuwa.
Mohammed Rukshan of St. Anthony’s Kandy scored the first century for the season with an unbeaten 112 off 170 balls with 12 fours against St. Thomas’ Matale.
