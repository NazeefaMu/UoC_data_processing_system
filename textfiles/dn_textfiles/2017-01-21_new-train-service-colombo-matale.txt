## Title: New train service from Colombo to Matale
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, January 21, 2017 - 01:00

New train service from Colombo to Matale

The Department of Railways yesterday commenced a new train service from Colombo to Matale considering requests by upcountry passengers. Picture shows Transport and Civil Aviation Minister Nimal Siripala de Silva on board a train bound to Matale. Picture by Sarath Peiris
