## Title: Sleepy driver steers lorry off road
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, February 27, 2018 - 01:00

Sleepy driver steers lorry off road

A lorry ran off the Dambulla-Habarana main road at Inamaluwa and crashed into four wayside shops, causing serious damage in the early hours of February 26. The driver had allegedly dozed off at the wheel. The driver and his assistant were also injured in the accident and admitted to the Dambulla Hospital, Sigiriya Police said. The lorry was carrying a consignment of hal ura from Polonnaruwa to Colombo when the accident occurred. Police investigations are continuing. Picture by Sigiriya Special Corr.
