## Title: Gnanasara Thera released on bail
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 21, 2017 - 12:00

Gnanasara Thera released on bail

 
Bodu Bala Sena (BBS) General Secretary Galagodaatte Gnanasara Thera was released on bail by the Colombo Fort Magistrate a short while ago.
The decision was made after he surrendered to courts earlier today.
Gnanasara Thera is alleged to have behaved in an unruly manner inside the Homagama Magistrate’s Court on January 25, last year.
