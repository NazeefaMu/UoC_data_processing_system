## Title: SPC employees' salaries to be increased by 70 percent within three years
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, August 3, 2017 - 01:00

SPC employees' salaries to be increased by 70 percent within three years

The State Pharmaceuticals Corporation (SPC) makes an annual profit of Rs. 2 billion and the SPC has recorded a profit of Rs. 1.9 billion in 2016, Health Minister Dr. Rajitha Senaratne had stated.
Minister Senaratne had stated that the salaries of the SPC employees will be increased by 70 percent in three phases within three years. He expressed these views after opening the 35th SPC osu sala outlet at Piliyandala town, recently.
Minister Senarathne had stated that it is planned to increase the salaries of SPC employees by 30 percent this year and by another 20 percent during next year.
A salary increase of another 20 percent will be granted in 2019. The approval for this plan will be obtained from the Secretary to the Ministry of Finance.
Further, it is expected to spend Rs. 200 million for this salary increase.
"Two drug companies had given money to some health trade unions to act against me.
"I received the information about the two drug companies. I do not fear of them," the Minister said.
