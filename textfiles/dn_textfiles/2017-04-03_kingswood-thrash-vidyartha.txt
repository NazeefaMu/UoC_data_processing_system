## Title: Kingswood thrash Vidyartha
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, April 3, 2017 - 01:00

Kingswood thrash Vidyartha

Kingswood College defeated Vidyarhta College 50-13 in a Division I segment 2B game played at Bogambara. The Kingswoodians scored five goals and three tries to Vidyartha’s one goal and two penalties. It was the KIngswood ruggerites who dominated possession and territory running in eight tries to Vidyartha’s one. The Kingswoodians dismantled the Vidyartha defence in the first half and finished well in the second. At half time Kingswood led 24-6.
Referee: Priyan Suranga
