## Title: Andy Solomons - a fine knock of 152
## Supertitle: Premier League Inter-Club Cricket Tournament
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, December 9, 2017 - 01:00

Premier League Inter-Club Cricket Tournament
Andy Solomons - a fine knock of 152

Andy Solomons of Saracens SC scored 152 runs against Moors SC in the SLC Premier League Inter Club Cricket tournament on day one yesterday.
Manoj Sarathchandra (Tamil Union C & AC) and Gamindu Kanishka (Saracens SC) made centuries against Baduraliya CC and Moors SC. Lasith Embuldeniya (NCC) took 5 wickets for 66 runs against Chilaw Marians CC.
No play was possible between Ragama CC and SSC at Welagedara stadium Kurunegala yesterday due to wet ground conditions.
*At Moors. Saracens SC: 369/7 in 89.5 overs (Andy Solomons 152, Gamindu Kanishka 108, Sachithra Perera 24 n.o., Saliya Saman 20, Shiran Fernando 4/86, Tharindu Ratnayake 2/128) vs Moors SC
