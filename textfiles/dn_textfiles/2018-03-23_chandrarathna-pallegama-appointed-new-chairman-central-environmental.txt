## Title: Chandrarathna Pallegama appointed as new Chairman of Central Environmental Authority
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, March 23, 2018 - 13:49

Chandrarathna Pallegama appointed as new Chairman of Central Environmental Authority

 
Chandrarathna Pallegama, Director General of Civil Security Department was appointed as the new Chairman of the Central Environmental Authority.
He received his letter of appointment from President Maithripala Sirisena at the President’s Official Residence, today.
Chandrarathna Pallegama is a senior public servant in the Sri Lanka Administrative Service.
