## Title: President opens Vihara Mandiraya
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 3, 2018 - 01:00

President opens Vihara Mandiraya

A new Vihara Mandiraya built at the Bodhignana Asapura at Hewagama, Kaduwela, was opened by President Maithripala Sirisena on May 1.
After the opening ceremony, the President visited the Dhatu Mandiraya and participated in religious observances conducted by the Maha Sangha led by Ven. Dodampahala Rahula Thera. Higher Education and Cultural Affairs Minister Dr. Wijeyadasa Rajapakse, Megapolis and Western Development Minister Patali Champika Ranawaka, Navy Commander Vice Admiral Sirimewan Ranasinghe, IGP Pujith Jayasundara and a large number of people in the area were present on the occasion.
The Kaduwela Mahamewna Bodhignana Bhavana Asapuwa opened on March 25, 2005, under the direction of Ven. Kiribathgoda Gnanananda Thera, renders yeoman service by providing spiritual guidance to the people in the area.
