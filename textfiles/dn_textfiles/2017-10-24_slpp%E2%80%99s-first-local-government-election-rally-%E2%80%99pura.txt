## Title: SLPP’s first local government election rally in A’pura
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 24, 2017 - 01:00

SLPP’s first local government election rally in A’pura

The Sri Lanka Podu Jana Peramuna has decided to hold its maiden rally targeting the forthcoming local government election at Anuradhapura, said SLPP Anuradhapura District Leader and Parliamentarian S. M. Chandrasena.
He made this announcement addressing an awareness meeting attended by SLPP zonal leadership, former local government members and Trade Union activists at his Kurundankulama residence recently.
Chandrasena said the SLPP will kick start its local government election campaign with the rally held at the Anuradhapura public playground on November 12. The venue has been already booked by S. M. Ranjith Samarakoon. Chandrasena said Kurunegala District Parliamentarian and Former President Mahinda Rajapaksa will participate in Religious observances held at the Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi, Ruwanweliseya, Anuradhapura Mosque and Hindu Kovil on November 12 morning and the rally will begin thereafter at 2 pm attended by a large crowd including leaders of other political parties affiliated to the SLPP, the general membership and supporters.
