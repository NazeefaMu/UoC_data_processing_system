## Title: Mahindananda Aluthgamage before FCID
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, April 16, 2018 - 11:11

Mahindananda Aluthgamage before FCID

Former Sports Minister Mahindananda Aluthgamage arrived before the Financial Crimes Investigation Division (FCID) to make a statement a short while ago.
He is alleged to have misappropriated state funds worth Rs. 53.1 million while importing sports equipment during the last Presidential Election.
