## Title: Indian nationals staying illegally in Sri Lanka apprehended
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, August 15, 2017 - 01:00

Indian nationals staying illegally in Sri Lanka apprehended

Naval troops attached to the South Eastern Naval Command with the assistance of the Police Special Task Force personnel stationed in Marudamunai apprehended three Indian nationals who were illegally staying in Sri Lanka during a special search operation conducted in Nindavur recently.
The Indian nationals were taken into custody while they were engaged in harvesting a paddy field in the area. The arrested persons, aged 27, 36 and 41 years, are residents of Tamil Nadu, India.
They were handed over to Samanthurai Police for legal action, the Navy media unit said.
