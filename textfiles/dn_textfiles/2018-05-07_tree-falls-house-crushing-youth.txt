## Title: Tree falls on house crushing youth
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, May 7, 2018 - 01:00

Tree falls on house crushing youth

A tree had uprooted and fallen on a house at Gonawalapatana, Nawalapitiya extensively damaging a house and killing a 20-year-old sleeping in its sitting room around 8.30 p.m. on May 5.
He was rushed to the Nawalapitya Hospital, but was pronounced dead on admission.
His bother who was sleeping by his side had escaped with minor injuries.
Other family members had escaped unhurt since they were in another room.
Navalapiitya police are investigating.
