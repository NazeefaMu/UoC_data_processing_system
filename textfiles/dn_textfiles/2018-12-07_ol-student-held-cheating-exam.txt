## Title: O/L student held for cheating at exam
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 7, 2018 - 01:00

O/L student held for cheating at exam

Balangoda police yesterday arrested a student who allegedly answered the GCE Ordinary Level Exam English with the answers he received on his mobile from another.
The student was questioned following a complaint from the educational authorities, police said.
A tuition master had assisted the student sending answers through text messages, police said.
Police said they will question the tuition master about the alleged cheating.
Exam invigilators detected the incident and reported to the regional educational officials who lodged a complaint with police.
