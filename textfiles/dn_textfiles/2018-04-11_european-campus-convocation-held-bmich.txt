## Title: European Campus Convocation held at BMICH
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, April 11, 2018 - 01:00

European Campus Convocation held at BMICH

The 2018 Convocation of the European Campus was held at Bandaranaike Memorial International Conference Hall recently to confer Graduation for the graduates who have successfully completed and passed out from the faculties of Science, Arts and Management.
Commerce and Industry Minister Rishad Bathiudeen was the Chief Guest along with the special Guest, former Minister Segu Issadeen, S.Subaideen, Secretary of ACMC and Mohamed Rasmy were present at the convocation.
