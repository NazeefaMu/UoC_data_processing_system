## Title: Spandana celebrates five years
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Tuesday, March 13, 2018 - 01:00

Spandana celebrates five years

Spandana music band which hails from Kurunegala will celebrate five years this year with a concert on March 16.
The event will be held at the Sathyavadi Stadium in Kurunegala on March 16. Sanka Dineth, Viraj Perera, Dushyantha Weeraman, Rooni, Gayani Madhusha, Manjula Dilrukshi and others will perform.
