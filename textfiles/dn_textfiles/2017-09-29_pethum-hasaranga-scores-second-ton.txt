## Title: Pethum Hasaranga scores second ton
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, September 29, 2017 - 01:00

Pethum Hasaranga scores second ton

Singer Cup Under 19 Inter Schools Cricket:
Pethum Hasaranga of Vidyaloka College, Galle made his second ton (129 runs) against Presdent’s College, Maharaga in their Singer Under 19 inter schools cricket tournament match at the Galle International Stadium yesterday.
Ravindu Rathnayake (St. Thomas Bandarawela) scored 152 runs against Bandarawela MMV at Bandarawela and Harsha Karunaratne (Dudley Senanayake Central) scored 131 runs against Nugawela Central at Galagedara. Dimuth Erantha (St. Joseph’s Wattala) scored 116 runs against Sri Prangnanagda MV Raddoluwa at Prision ground Dalupotha yesterday.
