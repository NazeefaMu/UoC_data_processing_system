## Title: Commemoration of war heroes
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, August 10, 2018 - 01:00

Commemoration of war heroes

Lali Kobbekaduwa places a wreath at the statue of her husband, Lieutenant General Denzil Kobbekaduwa, at the commemoration of his 24th death anniversary, in Anuradhapura, on August 8. Several other war heroes who laid their lives for the country were also remembered. Lieutenant General Kobbekaduwa was one of the country’s finest officers. The commemoration was attended by members of his family and a host of military officers.
