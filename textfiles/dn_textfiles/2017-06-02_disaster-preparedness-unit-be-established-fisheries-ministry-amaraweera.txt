## Title: Disaster Preparedness Unit to be established in Fisheries Ministry: Amaraweera
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, June 2, 2017 - 12:15

Disaster Preparedness Unit to be established in Fisheries Ministry: Amaraweera

 
Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Development Minister Mahinda Amaraweera said today that a decision has been made to establish a Disaster Preparedness Unit.
He said this in a progress evaluation meeting held at the Fisheries Ministry premises yesterday.
He also said the Fisheries Ministry has reserved 350 boats for flood relief services carried out by the government.
The boats were distributed to Kalutara, Galle, Matara, Tangalle, Colombo and Gamapaha districts he said.
Minister Amaraweera said that he has appointed an emergency operation committee for flood relief activities.
The operation committee has a supply of 100 life jackets as well he said.
Minister Amaraweera expressed his gratitude to fishing community for supporting these decisions.
