## Title: Kasippu distiller held
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, January 12, 2017 - 01:00

Kasippu distiller held

 The Tangalle Police Drug Eradication Unit has taken into custody a suspect who distilled Kasippu at a rented house at Suriyawewa.
The Police found three barrels of goda, two gas cylinders, a gas cooker, a kasippu distilling apparatus and 15 bottles of Kasippu.
The suspect and the items were handed over to Suriyawewa Police.
