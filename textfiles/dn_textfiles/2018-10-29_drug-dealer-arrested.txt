## Title: Drug dealer arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 29, 2018 - 01:00

Drug dealer arrested

Dambulla police arrested a drug dealer near the Dambulla Dedicated Economic Centre on Friday evening with 700 mg of heroin.
The suspect has been convicted for the same offence earlier, police said. He was a resident of Heen-Moragolla.He was produced before the Dambulla District Magistrate's Court on Saturday. Dambulla police are investigating.
