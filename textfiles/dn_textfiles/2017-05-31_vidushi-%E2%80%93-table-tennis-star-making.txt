## Title: Vidushi – Table Tennis star in the making
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, May 31, 2017 - 01:00

Vidushi – Table Tennis star in the making

Eleven–year-old Vidushi Nethmini of Rathnavali Balike Vidyalaya, Gampaha is an outstanding young table tennis player.
She won the under 13 girls singles title at the 2017 all island invitation novices table tennis tournament held at Mahara Sports hall, Mahara recently. This is the first time she has participated in a table tennis tournament.
Gampaha Ratnavali Balika Vidyalaya principal Mrs. H. A. H. Jayawardena praised her for the progress she has made so far in the sport. Vidushi Nethmini is an all round student who has excelled in English announcing and is also a talented artist.
