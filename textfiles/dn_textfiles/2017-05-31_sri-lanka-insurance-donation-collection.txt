## Title: Sri Lanka Insurance donation collection
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 31, 2017 - 01:00

Sri Lanka Insurance donation collection

Sri Lanka Insurance has set up a donation collection centre at its head office at No. 21, Vauxhall Street, Colombo 2 to collect relief material for people affected by the floods.
The public can hand over unused clothes, school books and other school equipment, medicine and dry rations or vouchers to purchase such items at the location.
Call 0702 919191 for more information.
