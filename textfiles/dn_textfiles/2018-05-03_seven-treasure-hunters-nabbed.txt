## Title: Seven treasure hunters nabbed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 3, 2018 - 01:00

Seven treasure hunters nabbed

Seven treasure hunters who were in the process of excavating a dilapidated and historic archeological site in the Poroththukadawala area of the Galgamuwa electorate in Kurunegala, were arrested by Meegalewa Police.
Under the guidance of Nikaweratiya SP Sanjeewa Bandara and Galgamuwa ASP D. Gallege, the suspects were produced before Galgamuwa Acting Magistrate Kalpana Rajapakse, who ordered the suspects to be kept in remand custody until May 7.
