## Title: Eight gamblers arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 14, 2018 - 01:00

Eight gamblers arrested

Eight suspects were taken into custody when their gambling den was raided by Pannala Police on Wednesday.
The arrests were made while they were carrying out cockfighting at the gambling den.
The Police seized three motorcycles and three cocks from their possession.
They were identified as residents of Pannala.
The suspects along with the seized items were to be produced before the Kuliyapitiya Magistrate yesterday, IP P. Kumaradasa said.
