## Title: President inaugurates Colombo International Book Fair 2017
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, September 15, 2017 - 18:30

President inaugurates Colombo International Book Fair 2017

 
President Maithripala Sirisena inaugurated the Colombo International Book Fair 2017 organized by the Sri Lanka Book Publishers Association for the 19th consecutive year, at the BMICH, today (15).
After declaring open the exhibition, President Sirisena toured the exhibition and engaged in cordial discussions with writers and book publishers.
Education Minister Akila Viraj Kariyawasam, Deputy Minister Dr. Harsha De Silva and the President of the Sri Lanka Book Publishers Association H.D.Premasiri were also present.
