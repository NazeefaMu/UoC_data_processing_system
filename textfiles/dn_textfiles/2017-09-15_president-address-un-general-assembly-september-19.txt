## Title: President to address UN General Assembly on September 19
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, September 15, 2017 - 01:00

President to address UN General Assembly on September 19

President Maithripala Sirisena is scheduled to visit New York, US to attend the 72nd General Assembly of the United Nations (UN).
The President is due to address the General Assembly in the evening of September 19.
This is the third time that President Maithripala Sirisena is addressing the UN General Assembly.
The General Debate will open with a focus on the theme, ‘Focusing on People: Striving for Peace and a Decent Life for All on a Sustainable Planet’. The Sri Lankan delegation will leave tomorrow (16) to attend the 72nd United Nations General Assembly due to be held at the UN Headquarters in New York from September 17 to 23.
‘The President will also meet several leaders including Prime Ministers of Pakistan and Nepal for discussions on bilateral relations and international issues’, Senior Media Director to the President Dharma Sri Bandara Ekanayaka said.
