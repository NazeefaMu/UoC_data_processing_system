## Title: Conducting private tuition classes during school hours to be banned
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 5, 2018 - 15:16

Conducting private tuition classes during school hours to be banned

 
Education Minister Akila Viraj Kariyawasam said that a Cabinet paper will be issued to ban conducting private tuition classes commencing from 7.30 a.m. to 1.30 p.m.
The Minister said this today in Parliament
Furthermore, the Minister said that the respective cabinet paper will be drafted and presented before the Cabinet of Ministers soon.
Expressing his disregard, the Minister said that conducting tuition classes during school hours has now become a trend.
