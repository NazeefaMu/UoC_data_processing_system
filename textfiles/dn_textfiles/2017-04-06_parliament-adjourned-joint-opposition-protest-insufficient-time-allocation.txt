## Title: Parliament adjourned as Joint Opposition protest insufficient time allocation
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 6, 2017 - 12:30

Parliament adjourned as Joint Opposition protest insufficient time allocation

Parliament was adjourned today after a tense situation arose when the ‘Joint Opposition’ protested insufficient time allocated to them to debate the United Nations Human Rights Council resolution on Sri Lanka.
