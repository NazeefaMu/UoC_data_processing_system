## Title: New Health Secretary
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, July 19, 2018 - 01:00

New Health Secretary

The newly appointed Health Secretary B.G.S. Gunatilleke assumed duties at the Health Ministry yesterday, a ministry spokesman said. Gunatilleke is a past pupil of Horana Thakshila Madya Maha Vidyalaya and obtained a special degree in Botany and a Master’s in Sociology from Kelaniya University. She first joined the teaching profession, and later, the Sri Lanka Administrative Service in 1987.
Since 1987, Gunatilleke served in several state institutions holding senior positions, including Deputy Director General of Labour.
The former Health Secretary Janaka Sugathadasa retired, the spokesman said.
