## Title: Showers expected
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 1, 2018 - 01:00

Showers expected

The Showery condition is expected to enhance due to the low level atmospheric disturbance in the vicinity of Sri Lanka.Cloudy skies are also possible over most parts of the island.
The Meteorology Department has forecasted that Showers or thundershowers can be expected in most parts of the island.
Fairly heavy showers of about 100 mm may occur in some places particularly in the Northern, North Central, North Western and the Central Provinces. Misty conditions are expected in some places in the Western, Sabaragamuwa and Central Provinces in the morning. Temporary localised strong winds can be expected during thundershowers. The public is requested to take adequate precautions to minimize damages caused by lightning.
Meanwhile, showers or thundershowers will occur in the sea areas around the island. Heavy showers or thundershowers can be expected in some places over the sea areas extending from Batticaloa to Colombo via Trincomalee, Kankasanturai and Puttalam.
Winds will be northeasterly to northwesterly and wind speed will be 20-30 kmph over the sea areas extending from Batticaloa to Galle via Trincomalee, Kankasanturai, Puttalam and Colombo. Wind speed can increase up to 45 kmph at times in these sea areas.
Winds will be northwesterly or variable in direction and wind speed will be 15-25 kmph in the other sea areas around the island.
Temporarily strong gusty winds and rough seas can be expected during thundershowers.
