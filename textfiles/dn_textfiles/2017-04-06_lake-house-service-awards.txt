## Title: Lake House service awards
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 6, 2017 - 01:00

Lake House service awards

Consultant Editor of the Sunday Observer and former Editor-in-Chief of the Daily News Pramod de Silva, Senior Associate Editor of the Daily News Rodney Martinesz and layout designer Kumudu Gunasekera who completed 25 years of service at the Associated Newspapers of Ceylon Limited received awards from Chairman Kavan Ratnayake and Editorial Director Saman Wagaarachchi at the ANCL service awards held at the Kingsbury Hotel in Colombo on Tuesday evening. Pictures by Samantha Weerasiri, Saman Sri Wedage and Thilak Perera
