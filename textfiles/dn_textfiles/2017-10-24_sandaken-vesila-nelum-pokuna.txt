## Title: Sandaken Vesila at Nelum Pokuna
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Tuesday, October 24, 2017 - 01:00

Sandaken Vesila at Nelum Pokuna

The Old Boy’s Association of Nalanda College, Colombo, has organized a musical show which comprises songs penned by the late lyricist Premakeerthi de Alwis. The concert titled ' Sandaken Vesila' is a tribute to the veteran lyricist. It will take place at Nelum Pokuna on November 12 at 6.30 pm.
Around 28 songs penned by de Alwis will be sung at the event by renowned vocalists. Victor Ratnayake, Sunil Edirisinghe, T M Jayaratne, Priya Sooriyasena, Saman de Silva, Sunil Fernando, Rodney Warnakula, Sewwandhi Ranatunga, Nadini Premadasa, Janith Iddamalgoda and Pavan Mallawarachchi will be featured at the event. The music is by Suresh Maliyadde. Saman Athaudahetti will compere the event. Channa Wijewardena will choreograph the dance items.
