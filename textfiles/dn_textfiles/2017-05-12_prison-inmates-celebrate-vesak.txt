## Title: Prison inmates celebrate Vesak
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, May 12, 2017 - 01:00

Prison inmates celebrate Vesak

Prison officials and the inmates of the Welikada Prison held a ‘Vesak Kalapaya’ in front of the prison.
It was opened by Prison Reforms, Rehabilitation, Resettlement and Hindu Religious Affairs Minister D. M. Swaminathan yesterday.
The zone includes a pandal, lanterns, bhakthi geetha and many other illustrations depicting Buddhist ways of life.
The minister said the inmates irrespective of their faiths participated in the event.
