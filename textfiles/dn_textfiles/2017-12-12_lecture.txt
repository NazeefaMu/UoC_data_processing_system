## Title: Lecture
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, December 12, 2017 - 01:00

Lecture

‘Advancing Vision 2025,’ will be the topic of a lecture by Dr. Indrajit Cumaraswamy, the Governor of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka, held at 6 pm on December 13 at the OPA Auditorium, 275/75, Prof. Stanley Wijesundera Mawatha, Colombo 7 (off Bauddhaloka Mawatha).
The lecture is sponsored by the British Scholars’ Association of Sri Lanka.
