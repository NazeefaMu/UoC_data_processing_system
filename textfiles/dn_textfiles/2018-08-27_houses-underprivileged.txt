## Title: Houses for the underprivileged
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, August 27, 2018 - 01:00

Houses for the underprivileged

The Abuthalib Sadaka organisation provided houses to underprivileged families in the Gamberigaswewa village in Anuradhapura, last Saturday.
The project was initiated by the Masjid Al Jannah in the United Kingdom and funded by the Sri Lanka Muslim Association.
