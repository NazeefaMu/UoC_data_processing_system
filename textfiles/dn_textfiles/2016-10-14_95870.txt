## Title: Ramesh wins LSR  schools marathon
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, October 14, 2016 - 01:00

Ramesh wins LSR  schools marathon

 
 
Sachendra Ramesh
 
The last year’s athletic captain of Prince of Wales College, Moratuwa Sachendra Ramesh won the under 20 LSR Colombo inter-school marathon race held recently.
There were more than one thousand competitors who participated. In the same event held last year Ramesh finished third and in the all island under 19 twenty one kilometre marathon he was placed third and in last year’s John Tarbet Meet competing in the under 20 marathon (21 kilometres) he became second and in the National Youth Meet held in Badulla for under 20 age category he was placed third in the same race
He was the under 19 athletic champion in 2015 and under 21 athletic champion at the school sports meets and won 800, 1500 and 5000 metres events.
