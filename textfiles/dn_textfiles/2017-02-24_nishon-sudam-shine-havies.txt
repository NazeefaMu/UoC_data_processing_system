## Title: Nishon, Sudam shine for Havies
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, February 24, 2017 - 01:00

Nishon, Sudam shine for Havies

Former Peterite vice captain cum centre Nishon Perera and Isipathana College scrum half Sudam Sooriarachchi starred as they scored two superb tries apiece to help Havelocks SC beat Navy SC by 39 points (6 tries, 3 conversions, 1 penalty) to 13 (1 try, 1 conversion, 2 penalties) and ensure a berth in the final of the Dialog ‘A’ Division inter-club Clifford Cup knock-out rugby tournament.
The second semi-final played at Race Course grounds Reid Avenue saw Havelocks leading 15-3 at the breather.
The Havies entertained the crowd by playing an excellent brand of running rugby opening out the game throughout the entire 80 minutes.
For the winning Havies team the tries were scored by centre Nishon Perera (two), scrum half Sudam Sooriarachchi (two), replacement scrum half Vimukthi Rahula (one) and fly half Niroshan Fernando (one). The three conversions and the penalty were fired across by centre Hirantha Perera.
Captain cum prop forward Roshan Ranasinghe scored the solitary try for the sailors with full back Thilina Weerasinghe putting over two penalties and the solitary conversion.
Referee: Julien Castaingnede (France)
