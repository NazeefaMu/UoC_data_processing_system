## Title: 2017 State Literary Awards
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, September 18, 2017 - 01:00

2017 State Literary Awards

Minister Dr. Amunugama received an award at the 2017 State Literary Awards for his highly-acclaimed recent book ‘Lion’s Roar,’ which is based on the life and times of Anagarika Dharmapala. He received this award under the award category of special work written in English. Here, Dr. Amunugama receiving the award.
