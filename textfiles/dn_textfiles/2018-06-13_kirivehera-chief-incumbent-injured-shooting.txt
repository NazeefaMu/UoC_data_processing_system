## Title: Kirivehera Chief Incumbent injured in shooting
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 13, 2018 - 01:00

Kirivehera Chief Incumbent injured in shooting

Chief Incumbent of Kataragama Kirivehera Ven. Kobawaka Dhamminda Thera and another monk were injured when unidentified gunmen fired at them at around 10.30 p.m. yesterday.
According to Police sources, the incident had taken place while the Chief Incumbent of the Kataragama Kirivehera and another monk was at the temple premises.
The gunmen fled the area following the incident and Ven.Kobawaka Dhamminda Thera and another monk were admitted to the Debarawewa district hospital. They were later transferred to the Hambantoa General Hospital.
