## Title: Man gets RI for raping girl
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Friday, June 2, 2017 - 01:00

Man gets RI for raping girl

A man who pleaded guilty to raping a 16-year-old schoolgirl, was sentenced to two years' RI and fined Rs. 10,000 by Kurunegala High Court Judge Menaka Wijesundara on Thursday.
The Judge also ordered the man to pay Rs. 100,000 as compensation to the girl.
The accused is a resident of Polpitigama.
