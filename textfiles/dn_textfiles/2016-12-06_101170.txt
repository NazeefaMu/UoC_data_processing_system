## Title: WP Councillor Jagath Kumara granted bail
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, December 6, 2016 - 01:00

WP Councillor Jagath Kumara granted bail

 
Western Provincial Councillor Jagath Kumara who surrendered himself before Court for allegedly assaulting a police officer at a protest near Parliament Road on November 29 was yesterday released on a surety bail of Rs. 50,000 by Colombo Additional Magistrate Chandana Kalansuriya.
The Additional Magistrate severely warned the suspect not to repeat such an offence. The Welikada police carried out the investigations.
Magisterial inquiry against the suspect fixed for July 11.
