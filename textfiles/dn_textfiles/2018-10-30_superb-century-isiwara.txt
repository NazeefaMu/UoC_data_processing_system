## Title: Superb century by Isiwara
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, October 30, 2018 - 01:00

Superb century by Isiwara

A brilliant 113 runs by fifteen year old fresher Isiwara Dissanayake helped Royal College Colombo to gain a first innings win over P de S Kularatne Vidyalaya Ambalangoda in their inter school first eleven cricket match played at Karandeniya.
Isiwara hit six fours and eight sixes and occupied the crease for 200 minutes and faced 168 balls. Also he added 234 runs for second wicket with Ahan Wickramasinghe he who was unbeaten with a splendid century.
Batting first Royalists declared at 267 for 4 and in reply homesters were all out for 138 runs and following on at the close P de S Kularatne Central were reeling at 39 for 9 and Manula Perera had a fine match bag of 8 for 49 runs.
Royal College – 267/4 in 58 overs – (Isiwara Dissanayake 113, Ahan Wickramasinghe 135 n/o, Kumudu Nilhara 2/57, Manuja Theekshana 2/52.
P de s Kularatne Vidyalaya – 138 in 46.4 overs. Lakila Thehan 41, Lahiru Madushanka 2/24, Sonal Amarasekera 3/33, Manula Perera 5/28 and 39/9 – (Kanika Dayarathne 3/9, Manula Perera 3/21, Lahiru Madushanka 2/2).
