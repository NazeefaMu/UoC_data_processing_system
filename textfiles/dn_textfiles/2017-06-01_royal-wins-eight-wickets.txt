## Title: Royal wins by eight wickets
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, June 1, 2017 - 01:00

Royal wins by eight wickets

Royal College, Colombo beat S. de S. Jayasinghe Vidyalaya, Dehiwala by eight wickets in their Inter School Under 17 Division One tournament cricket match conducted by Sri Lanka Schools Cricket Association and played at Royal College grounds.
Batting first visitors collapsed for 82 runs with Dilshan Dias scoring 50 runs and Manula Perera claiming 4 for 12 runs and in reply Royalist notched up the required score for the loss of 2 wickets.
S. de S. Jayasinghe Vidyalaya - 82 in 23.4 overs - Dilshan Dias 50, Manula Perera 4/12, Dimal Wijesekara 3/16, Tharusha Rukshan 3/17
Royal College - 88/2 in 13.3 overs - Mathisha Amarasinghe 32 n/o, Thevindu enarathne 31 n/o
Thomians rout Moratu Vidyalaya
S. Thomas’ College Mt. Lavinia defeated Moratu Vidyalaya by 38 runs in their Inter School under 17 Division One Tournament Cricket Match conducted by Sri Lanka Schools Cricket Association and worked off at Moratuwa.
Thomians taking the strike early were all out for 127 runs and in reply Vidyalians were shot out for 59 runs with Kalana perera claiming 5 for 22.
S. Thomas’ College - 127 in 30.1 overs - Kalana Perera 28, Sithara Hapuhinna 24, Niraj Kavishka 3 for 24, Vinura Peiris 2/20
Moratu Vidyalaya - 59 in 11.1 overs - Nadith Mishendra 29, Kalana Perera 5/22, Thevin Eriyagama 3/8
