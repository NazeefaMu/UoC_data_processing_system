## Title: Parliament to commence today: galleries reopened
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, December 18, 2018 - 10:45

Parliament to commence today: galleries reopened

The Parliament to commence, for the first time following the swearing-in of Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe, at 1.00 p.m today..An adjournment motion will be taken up for debate during the Parliament session.The Parliament was last convened on the 12th of December.Meanwhile, the meeting of party leaders has been scheduled to commence at 12.00 p.m today. The Office of the Speaker stated that a decision concerning the positions of the Leader of Opposition and the Chief Organizer of the Opposition would be drawn at the party leaders’ meeting.The public gallery and the Speaker’s special guest gallery will be reopened under certain conditions, the Office of the Speaker further said.
