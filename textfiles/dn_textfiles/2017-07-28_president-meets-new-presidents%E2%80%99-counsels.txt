## Title: President meets new Presidents’ Counsels
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, July 28, 2017 - 01:00

President meets new Presidents’ Counsels

President Maithripala Sirisena met senior lawyers who were recently appointed as Presidents’ Counsels at the President’s House on Wednesday. Minister Wijayadasa Rajapakshe was also present.
