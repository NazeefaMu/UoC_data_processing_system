## Title: Range Bandara’s son granted bail
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 14, 2018 - 14:12

Range Bandara’s son granted bail

 
State Minister Palitha Range Bandara’s son Yashoda Range Bandara who was under remand custody for drinking and driving and misusing state vehicles was granted bail, a short while ago.
He was arrested after he crashed into a house in the Kottapitiya junction on the Chilaw–Puttalam Road.
