## Title: Panadura councillors sworn in
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Thursday, March 22, 2018 - 01:00

Panadura councillors sworn in

UNP Councillors elected to the Panadura Urban Council and the Panadura Pradeshiya Sabha were sworn in before Prison Reforms Deputy Minister Ajith Perera on Tuesday at an official ceremony held at the Panadura Youth Council Auditorium.
Religious leaders in the Panadura area with Wadduwa Samuddaramaya Temple Chief Incumbent and Adhikarana Sanghanayake Ven. Bopitiye Dhammissara, were present.
Chairman Nandana Gunathilleke signed his official document in the presence of Deputy Minister Ajith Perera.
