## Title: Hotel staffer charged for sexual assault
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 9, 2018 - 01:00

Hotel staffer charged for sexual assault

A hotel employee who tried to sexually assault two American women was arrested by Uppuveli Police, yesterday. The two women are employees of the Naval Ship USNS Mercy which berthed at the Trincomalee harbour.
“The two women had ordered a soft drink from the hotel in which they were staying. An employee had brought the drinks to the room and had attempted to sexually molest the two women aged 23 and 25,” stated an official from the Police Media Unit.
The employee was identified as a resident of Pudukudirippu and was scheduled to be produced before the Trincomalee Magistrate’s Court yesterday.
US Navy Hospital Ship, USNS Mercy arrived in Sri Lanka on April 25 to facilitate the Pacific Partnership-2018. It is currently berthed at the Trincomalee Port and will leave Sri Lanka on May 9.
