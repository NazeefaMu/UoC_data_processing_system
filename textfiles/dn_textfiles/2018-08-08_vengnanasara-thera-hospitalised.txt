## Title: Ven.Gnanasara Thera hospitalised
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 8, 2018 - 01:00

Ven.Gnanasara Thera hospitalised

Bodu Bala Sena General Secretary Ven.Galagodaaththe Gnanasara Thera has been admitted to hospital.
According to a Bodu Bala Sena organization Spokesman, Ven. Gnanasara Thera was expected to undergo surgery yesterday.
