## Title: St. Mary’s win by an innings
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, March 27, 2017 - 01:00

St. Mary’s win by an innings

St. Mary’s College Kegalle beat Kegalu Vidyalaya by an innings and 108 runs in their 37th Battle of the Gold in Kegalle played at Katuneriya yesterday.
Kegalu Vidyalaya: 68 in 29.1 overs (Peshal Nelumdeniya 21, Mass Rahim 3/12, Thimira Kumara 2/11, Lasith Udage 2/20) and 70 in 39.2 overs (Madhushan Gunasinghe 13, Thimira Kumara 3/12)
St. Mary’s: 246 in 62.4 overs (Sajeewa Ranjith 50, Gajitha Kotuwegoda 41, Thimira Kumara 34 n.o., Isuru Senanayaka 4/80) - YK
