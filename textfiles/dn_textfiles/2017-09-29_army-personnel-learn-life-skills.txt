## Title: Army personnel learn life skills
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, September 29, 2017 - 01:00

Army personnel learn life skills

The Psychological Operations Directorate of the Army in coordination with the Alcohol and Drug Information Centre (ADIC) conducted a training programme for instructors and other rankers participating in the ‘Anti-Drug’ campaign at the Sri Lanka Army General Service Corps Regimental Headquarters at Panagoda, recently.
A total of five Officers and 191 Other Rankers took part in the educational programme in which Major U. P. Mallawarachchi, Senior Psychiatrist at the Mental Health Unit at the Army Hospital delivered a lecture on ‘Life Skills and Motivation’. ADIC Senior Programme Officer Ajith Nawagamuwa spoke on ‘Alcohol and its Reality.’
The training programme was jointly organized by the Directorate of Psychological Operations.
