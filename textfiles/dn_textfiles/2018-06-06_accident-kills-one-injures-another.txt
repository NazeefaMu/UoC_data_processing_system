## Title: Accident kills one, injures another
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 6, 2018 - 01:00

Accident kills one, injures another

A motorcyclist was killed and a pillion rider was injured upon colliding with a tipper loaded with sand on the Badulla – Mahiyanganaya main road, on Monday (4).
According to police media sources, the motorcyclist had been speeding at the time of the incident. Subsequently, the vehicle had veered off the road and collided with the rear end of the tipper which had been parked on the side of the road.
Preliminary investigations further revealed that the said two individuals were under the influence of liquor when the accident had occurred.
The pillion rider, who had sustained critical injuries, was admitted to the Mahiyangana Base Hospital. Investigations are continuing.
