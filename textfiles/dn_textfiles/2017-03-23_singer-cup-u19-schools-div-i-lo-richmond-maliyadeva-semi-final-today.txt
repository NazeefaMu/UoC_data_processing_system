## Title: Singer Cup U19 Schools Div I L/O: Richmond-Maliyadeva semi-final today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, March 23, 2017 - 01:00

Singer Cup U19 Schools Div I L/O: Richmond-Maliyadeva semi-final today

Richmond College Galle will meet Maliyadeva College Kurunegala in the semi-final of the Singer Cup Under 19 inter-schools Division One Limited Overs cricket tournament at St. Peter’s College ground Bambalapitiya today. Richmond beat Maris Stella College Negombo and Maliyadeva beat last year’s runner-up Trinity College in the quarter finals. - YK
