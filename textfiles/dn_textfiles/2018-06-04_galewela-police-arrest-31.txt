## Title: Galewela police arrest 31
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, June 4, 2018 - 01:00

Galewela police arrest 31

On the instructions of the DIG Central Province, Galewela Police conducted a search operation in their purview on Friday (01) night and arrested 31 individuals for various offences, within two hours.
The majority of them were traffic offenders (20), including one person driving under the influence of alcohol. Nine persons were arrested on suspicion, police said.
Those arrested are to be produced in the Dambulla district or magistrate’s court.
The raid was conducted by a team of police officers under the direction of Galewela IOC Janaka Vitanatchci.
