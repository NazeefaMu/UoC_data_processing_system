## Title: Kahadamodara coast contaminated
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, November 6, 2018 - 01:00

Kahadamodara coast contaminated

Residents of Kahadamodara said that the coastal belt in the area was being contaminated by waste water flowing into the sea.
The pollution had caused the death of aquatic life, and tourist numbers had decreased in the area as well, they added.
