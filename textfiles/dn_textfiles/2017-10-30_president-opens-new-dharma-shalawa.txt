## Title: President opens new Dharma Shalawa
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 30, 2017 - 01:00

President opens new Dharma Shalawa

A new Wahalkada and a new Dharma Shalawa at the Pepiliyana Sunethra Devi Piriven Raja Maha Viharaya was opened by President Maithripala Sirisena yesterday. The Katina Poojava was also held yesterday with the participation of the President.The President viewing some of the paintings with Ven Dr. Medagama Abhayatissa Nayake thera. Picture by President’s Media Division.
