## Title: Dr.Lester James Peries' funeral on May 2 - UPDATE
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, April 30, 2018 - 11:00

Dr.Lester James Peries' funeral on May 2 - UPDATE

 
Funeral proceedings of veteran film Director Dr.Lester James Peries will be held at the Independence Square in Colombo at 4 p.m. on May 2.
President Maithripala Sirisena instructed to conduct the funeral proceedings with full state honors , earlier today.
Dr.Lester James Peries passed away at the age of 99, last night.
