## Title: Heavy price for stealing coconuts
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, March 3, 2018 - 01:00

Heavy price for stealing coconuts

Three youth were arrested by the Baddegama Police for allegedly robbing eight coconuts from a coconut estate at Pedruwatte, Halpathota in Baddegama. Baddegama Magistrate T.A.Dhammika Hemapala released them on a Rs 500,000 surety on each and ordered the suspects to report to the Baddegama police every last Sunday of each month and appear in courts on March 6.
The suspects are residents of Galhenkanda, Pedruwatte in Baddegama.
