## Title: Three-wheeler torched
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 23, 2017 - 01:00

Three-wheeler torched

A three-wheeler parked at a house at Division No: 8, Sheriff Hajiyar road in the Addalaichenai, was torched on August 20 at noon.
The incident had occurred while the owner had been at a relative's house.
The owner had complained to the Police soon after.
The three-wheeler was severely damaged by the fire. Akkaraipattu Police are investigating.
