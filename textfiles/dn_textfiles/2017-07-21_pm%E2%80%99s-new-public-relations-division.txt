## Title: PM’s new Public Relations Division
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, July 21, 2017 - 01:00

PM’s new Public Relations Division

The Prime Minister’s Office inaugurated a Public Relations Division besides Temple Trees, yesterday. The Public Relations Office was ceremonially opened by Law and Order and Southern Development Minister and Chief of Staff of the Prime Minister’s Office Sagala Ratnayaka.
Prime Minister’s Spokesperson and Deputy Chief of Staff Rosy Senanayake and other high officials were present at the occasion.
The new Public Relations Division of the Prime Minister’s Office is located at No. 101. R. A. de Mel Mawatha, Colombo 3.
