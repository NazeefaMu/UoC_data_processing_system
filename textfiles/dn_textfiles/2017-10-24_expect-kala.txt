## Title: Expect Kala
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Tuesday, October 24, 2017 - 01:00

Expect Kala

Sujeewa Gunaratne’s maiden cinematic direction ‘Kala’ focuses on a story from history. The film stars Mahendra Perera, Madhini Malwattege, W Jayasiri, Neeta Fernando, Jagath Manuwarna and others. The movie is due to be released on the CEL circuit cinemas from October 27.
