## Title: Death of Juliana Almeida Pigera
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, May 26, 2018 - 01:00

Death of Juliana Almeida Pigera

Social worker Juliana Almeida passed away, at the age of 97.She was the wife of late William Pigera (retired government officer) and mother of seven children including Nimal and A.S. Pigera of Lake House and Regina and Kanthie. Remains lie at No. 360/14 Samagi Mawatha Welisara, Ragama. Funeral at St. John’s Church Cemetery, Nagoda at 4.00 pm on Sunday (27).
