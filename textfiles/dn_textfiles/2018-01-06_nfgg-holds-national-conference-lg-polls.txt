## Title: NFGG holds national conference for LG polls
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Saturday, January 6, 2018 - 01:00

NFGG holds national conference for LG polls

The national conference for candidates of National Front for Good Governance (NFGG) was held at the Hotel Beachway, Kattankudy recently.
NFGG Chairman M.M. Abdul Rahman delivered the keynote speech and Deputy Chairman Siraj Mashoor, National Organiser M.P.M. Firthows Naleemi and others participated.
