## Title: Dr. Lester James Peries’s 99th birthday
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, April 6, 2018 - 01:00

Dr. Lester James Peries’s 99th birthday

Internationally acclaimed film producer and film director Dr. Lester James Peries’s 99th birthday fell yesterday (5) and President Maithripala Sirisena visited his residence in Thimbirigasyaya to extend his wishes to the maestro. The President looked into his well-being and also engaged in a cordial discussion with him. Picture by Kapila Ariyawansa
