## Title: Errant traders in South fetch over Rs. 1,138,000 to CAA
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 24, 2017 - 01:00

Errant traders in South fetch over Rs. 1,138,000 to CAA

Consumer Affairs Authority (CAA) Southern Region officials have collected over Rs. 1,138,000 from errant traders as court fines in sudden raids and mobile inspections conducted in the Southern region in January.
According to investigation officials, 155 raids have been conducted. Errant traders were prosecuted in the Magistrates’ courts of Galle, Matara and Hambantota districts. Charges against them were selling rice at exorbitant rates, hoarding of rice varieties, selling and displaying food items unsuitable for human consumption and selling and displaying expired food items.
