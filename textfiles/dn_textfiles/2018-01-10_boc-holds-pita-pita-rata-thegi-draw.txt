## Title: BOC holds Pita Pita Rata Thegi draw
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Wednesday, January 10, 2018 - 01:00

BOC holds Pita Pita Rata Thegi draw

The Bank of Ceylon ‘Pita Pita Rata Thegi’ raffle draw competition was conducted among the customers and awarded prizes at Bank of Ceylon Pottuvil branch recently under the patronage of S.P. Kiritharan Manager of BOC Pottuvil branch.
Customer A.M. Rafeek of Pottuvil was adjudged the winner of the raffle draw.
Here, the winner A.M. Rafeek is seen receiving his prize from Pottuvil branch Manager S.P. Kiritnaran, Assistant Manager G.H.A. Roshan and Bank Officer M.C.M. Hairath look on.
