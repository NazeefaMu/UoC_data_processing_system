## Title: Unity Sunday Service tomorrow
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, January 20, 2018 - 01:00

Unity Sunday Service tomorrow

A joint worship Service organised by the National Christian Council of Sri Lanka and the Catholic National Commission for Ecumenism and Interreligious Dialogue, on behalf of the Roman Catholic Church, will be held tomorrow, Sunday January 21 2018 at 4.30 p.m. at the St. Lawrence's Church, Wellawatte, Colombo 6.
This is in keeping with the tradition of observing the unity octave, an eight day period from January 18 to 25 each year.During this period Christians across the world will come together to promote Christian Unity.
