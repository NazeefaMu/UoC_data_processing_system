## Title: 26 wickets fall on day one at Kadirana
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, September 25, 2017 - 01:00

26 wickets fall on day one at Kadirana

Twenty-six wickets fell for 249 runs on day one of the match between Bandaranayake College Gampaha and St. Mary’s College Kegalle in the ‘Singer Trophy’ Under 19 inter schools cricket tournament played at Kadirana yesterday.
St. Mary’s College Kegalle led by 101 runs with four second innings wickets in hand.
St. Peter’s College Negombo took a first innings lead against Rahula College Matara at Matara.
Matches will be continued today
