## Date: Tuesday, February 20, 2018 - 01:00

Glorious moments

The Inter House Athletic Meet of Musaeus College, Colombo, was held on February 1 at the Nondescripts Cricket Club Ground (NCC Ground) Colombo 7.
General Sir John Kotelawala Defence University Vice Chancellor Rear Admiral Jagath Ranasinghe was the Chief Guest. De Abrew house won the championship at this year’s sports meet.
