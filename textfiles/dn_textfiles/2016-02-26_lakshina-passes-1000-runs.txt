## Title: Lakshina passes 1000 runs
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, February 26, 2016 - 01:00

Lakshina passes 1000 runs

 
Sri Lanka under 19 world cup discard, Sri Lanka Schools under 19 cap and wicket keeper batsman of St. Peter's College Bambalapitiya, Lakshina Rodrigo passed the 1000 runs when he scored a brilliant unbeaten 102 runs against Trinity College Kandy in their inter school first eleven cricket match played at Kandy and now his total stands at 106.
He became the sixth school boy to achieve this rare feat this season and his thousand runs consisted of five centuries and a half century with the top score of 132 versus Marist Stella College, Negombo.
