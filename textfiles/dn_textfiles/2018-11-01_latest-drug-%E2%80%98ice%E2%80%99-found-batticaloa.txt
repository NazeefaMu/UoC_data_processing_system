## Title: Latest drug ‘ice’ found in Batticaloa
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 1, 2018 - 01:00

Latest drug ‘ice’ found in Batticaloa

The latest narcotic drug ‘ice’ was found in the Batticaloa district for the first time when Kattankudy Police nabbed three youths transporting the drug from a tourist hotel in Pasikudah in a brand new car at Goodwin junction in Kattankudy on October 30 morning.
The car was also seized by the police.
Police said the suspects who are residents of Pasikudah were due to be produced in court yesterday.
