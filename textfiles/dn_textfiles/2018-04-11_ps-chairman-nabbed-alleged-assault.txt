## Title: PS Chairman nabbed for alleged assault
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, April 11, 2018 - 01:00

PS Chairman nabbed for alleged assault

TNA candidate N. Konalingam of the Vakarai division, who was recently elected Koralai Pattu North Pradeshiya Sabha Chairman, was arrested by police, yesterday, in connection with an alleged assault.The Grama Niladhari of the same area, had also been arrested recently. Konalingam was said to have been absconding for four days after carrying out the alleged assault along with several accomplices. The victim was admitted to the Batticaloa Teaching Hospital. Vakarai Police are investigating.
