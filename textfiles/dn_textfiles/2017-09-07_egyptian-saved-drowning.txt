## Title: Egyptian saved from drowning
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 01:00

Egyptian saved from drowning

An Egyptian national was saved from drowning by the lifesaving unit of Tangalle Police when he was sea bathing at Medilla sea stretch.
The victim is Mohamed Salem Abdulla, 21 years. After rendering first aid, the victim was admitted to the Tangalle base hospital for treatment.
