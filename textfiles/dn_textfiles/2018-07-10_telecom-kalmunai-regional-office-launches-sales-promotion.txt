## Title: Telecom Kalmunai regional office launches sales promotion
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Tuesday, July 10, 2018 - 01:00

Telecom Kalmunai regional office launches sales promotion

The Sri Lanka Telecom PLC Kalmunai regional office launched a sales promotion giving gifts to the customers to increase the sale of Broadband connections at their teleshops in Kalmunai area recently.
The Sri Lanka Telecom PLC Kalmunai Regional Manager F Newton organized the programme.
Hand bills were issued at the event. Broadband connections were also sold at the promotion
