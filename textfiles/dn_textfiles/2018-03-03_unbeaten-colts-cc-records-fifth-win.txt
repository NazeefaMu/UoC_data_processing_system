## Title: Unbeaten Colts CC records fifth win
## Supertitle: SLC Major T20 Inter-Club Cricket :
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, March 3, 2018 - 01:00

SLC Major T20 Inter-Club Cricket :
Unbeaten Colts CC records fifth win

Unbeaten Colts CC recorded their fifth win in five matches in the SLC Major T20 Inter- Club cricket tournament conduct by Sri Lanka Cricket yesterday.
Moors SC also unbeaten scored their fourth successive win.
