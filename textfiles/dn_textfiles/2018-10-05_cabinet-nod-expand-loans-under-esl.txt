## Title: Cabinet nod to expand loans under ESL
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, October 5, 2018 - 01:00

Cabinet nod to expand loans under ESL

Cabinet approved five proposals submitted by Finance and Mass Media Minister Mangala Samaraweera and Lands and Parliamentary Reforms Minister Gayantha Karunatileka.
Minister Karunatileka said that Cabinet approval had been granted to expand loan facilities under Enterprise Sri Lanka (ESL), with long-term and short-term conditions for Production Planning Engineering Companies.
He was addressing the media yesterday, at the Government Information Department.
Cabinet approval had also been granted to extend Sri Lanka’s support to raise institutional capital and primary capital of the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development, a member institution of the World Bank and the International Finance Corporation.
Minister Samaraweera’s proposal to allocate funds for the Nuwara Eliya and Matara development projects had also been approved by the Cabinet of ministers.
Approval had also been granted to prepare government financial statements on an accrual basis.
