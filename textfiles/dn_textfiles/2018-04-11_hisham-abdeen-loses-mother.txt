## Title: Hisham Abdeen loses mother
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, April 11, 2018 - 01:00

Hisham Abdeen loses mother

Shafra Abdeen, mother of former Sri Lanka rugby sevens captain Hisham Abdeen passed away at the age of 83 yesterday.
She was the wife of late Cader Abdeen. Janaza takes place from Arthur’s Place, Angulana, Moratuwa at 2.30 pm to the Dehiwela Muslim Burial grounds.
