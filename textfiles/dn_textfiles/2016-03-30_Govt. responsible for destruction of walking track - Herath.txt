## Title: Govt. responsible for destruction of walking track - Herath
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Wednesday, March 30, 2016 - 01:00

Govt. responsible for destruction of walking track - Herath

JVP Propaganda Secretary Vijitha Herath yesterday said the Government should take the responsibility for destroying the walking track at Mabola.
He was of the view that those responsible need to be dealt with seriously. He added that both Tourism Development Minister John Amaratunga and Megapolis and Western Development Minister Patali Champaka Ranawaka are criticizing each other for the destruction Herath said that maximum punishment should be given to those who were involved in the destruction of this walking path.
“The governemnt should reconstruct this walking track using those who were involved in destroying it,”he said.
