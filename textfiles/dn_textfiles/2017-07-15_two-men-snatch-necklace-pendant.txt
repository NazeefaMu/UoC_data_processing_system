## Title: Two men snatch necklace, pendant
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, July 15, 2017 - 01:00

Two men snatch necklace, pendant

Two men who arrived on a motorcycle snatched a necklace and a pendant from a woman at Denipitiya junction in Weligama, Weligama Police said.
The woman who arrived in a bus with her husband and a few others had alighted from the bus and was on the roadside when the necklace and the pendant were snatched.
Weligama Police are investigating.
