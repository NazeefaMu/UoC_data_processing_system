## Title: Glyphosate weedicide ban on tea, rubber lifted
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 2, 2018 - 15:55

Glyphosate weedicide ban on tea, rubber lifted

 
Plantation Industries Minister Navin Dissanayake said today that the ban imposed against using Glyphosate weedicide was lifted for tea and rubber industries.
