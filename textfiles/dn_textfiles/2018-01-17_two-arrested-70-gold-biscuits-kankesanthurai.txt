## Title: Two arrested with 70 gold biscuits at Kankesanthurai
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 17, 2018 - 10:31

Two arrested with 70 gold biscuits at Kankesanthurai

 
Two individuals with 70 golden biscuits were arrested by the Sri Lanka Navy in the Kankesanthurai seas earlier today.
The gold is estimated to be worth Rs. 50 million.
Further investigations are underway.
