## Title: Navy rescues elephant from the sea
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, July 12, 2017 - 11:00

Navy rescues elephant from the sea

 
An elephant caught by the Kokkuthuduwai, Kokilai, Mullaitivu eastern sea tides was rescued by the Sri Lanka Navy yesterday.
Navy troops during a routine patrol has seen the elephant and the naval authorities has rushed several craft including a Fast Attack Craft and Cedric boats with divers to the location to rescue the animal in distress.
Group of officials of the Wildlife Department also joined in the rescue mission.
With Wildlife officials’ instructions, Navy troops were able to direct the elephant towards the coast without causing any harm.
After safely guiding the elephant to the Yan Oya area in Pulmodai the animal was handed over to the Wildlife officials.
