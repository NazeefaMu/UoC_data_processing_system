## Title: Wigneswaran takes over NP Agriculture, Education Ministries
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 21, 2017 - 17:00

Wigneswaran takes over NP Agriculture, Education Ministries

 
North Province (NP) Chief Minister C.V Wigneswaran took over the NP Agriculture and Education Ministries today.
The Chief Minister took oaths before North Provincial Council Governor Reginald Cooray.
Previously Ponnuthurai Ayngaranesan served as the NP Agriculture Minister and Thambyrajah Gurukularajah served as the NP Education Minister.
They handed over their resignations to NPC Governor Reginald Cooray after being convicted of corruption and fraud.
