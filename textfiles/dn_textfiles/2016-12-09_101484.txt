## Title: First innings win for Sangabodhi
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, December 9, 2016 - 01:00

First innings win for Sangabodhi

A fine bowling spell of 10 for 59 by Kavindu Umayanga guided Sangabodhi College, Nittambuwa to record a first innings win over Ranpokunagama Maha Vidyalaya in their inter-school under 13, division III cricket match played at Ranpokunagama MV Grounds recently.
Homesters won the toss and decided to field. Hirusha Lakshan and Tharindu Dilshan contributed with 50 and 42 runs respectively help Sangabodhi to make 201 in the first innings.
In reply homesters were bundled out for a paltry 69 runs with Kavindu Umayanga doing the damage to claim 6 for 18.
In the second essay visitors scored 100 for 4 wickets with Thishaka Uddale 56. Homesters replied with 99 runs for 7 wickets and again Kavindu Umayanga bagged 4 wickets for 41.
Sangabodhi College – 201/6 in 46.1 overs (Hirusha Lakshan 50, Tharindu Dilshan 42, M. Pabasara 3 for 40) and 100 for 4 (Thishaka Uddale 56).
Ranpokunagama MV – 69 in 26.3 overs (Kavindu Umayanga 6 for 18) and 99/7 (R.C. Nissanka 27, Kavindu Umayanga 4 for 41).
No decision
Another match between Sangabodhi College and Lyceum International School, Gampaha (LISG) worked off at Kirindiwela Public Grounds last weekend ended in a no decision.
LISG invited to Sangabodhians to bat first after they won the toss. Sangabodhians declared at 157 runs for 8 wickets. Hirusha Lakshan top scored with 47 while Pabasara Dilhara made 30. Janidu Oshada claimed 3 wickets for 44.
At the end of the day LISG were 87 for 7 with R. Ahamed 18. Kavindu Umayanga and Kavindu Gayantha bagged 5 for 23 and 2 for 17 respectively.
