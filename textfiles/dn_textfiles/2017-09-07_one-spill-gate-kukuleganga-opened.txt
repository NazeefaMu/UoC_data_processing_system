## Title: One spill gate of Kukuleganga opened
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 11:30

One spill gate of Kukuleganga opened

 
One spill gate of the Kukuleganga reservoir has been  opened due to heavy rainfall.
Residents of Palindanuwara, Agalawatta, Baduraliya, Ingiriya, Walallawita and areas downstream are requested to be vigilant.
