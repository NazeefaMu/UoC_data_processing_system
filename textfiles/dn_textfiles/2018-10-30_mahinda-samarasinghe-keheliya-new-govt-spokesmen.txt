## Title: Mahinda Samarasinghe, Keheliya new Govt. Spokesmen
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 30, 2018 - 01:05

Mahinda Samarasinghe, Keheliya new Govt. Spokesmen

United People’s Freedom Alliance (UPFA) MPs Mahinda Samarasinghe and Keheliya Rambukwella have been appointed as new Government Spokesmen.
Mahinda Samarasinghe also remains as the Media Spokesman of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party (SLFP).
