## Title: Thisaru Dilshan takes 7 wickets for 48 runs
## Supertitle: SLC’s Under 23 Super Provincial Cricket tournament:
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, September 29, 2018 - 01:00

SLC’s Under 23 Super Provincial Cricket tournament:
Thisaru Dilshan takes 7 wickets for 48 runs

Thisaru Dilshan left arm paceman of Kandy Under 23 took 7 wickets for 48 runs against Dambulla Under 23 on the day one of 3rd weekend of the SLC’s Under 23 Super Provincial 3 day Cricket tournament at Thurstan grounds yesterday.
Colombo Under 23 is leading on first inning against Galle U23 and Kandy Under 23 in command of the game against Dambula Under 23 at Colts.
Match will be continued today
At Thurstan. Dambulla U23: 188 in 52.1 overs (Damitha Silva 58, Thisaru Dilshan 7/48, Nimesh Mendis 3/20) Kandy U23: 170/4 in 28 overs (Thanuka Dabare 79, U. Parakrama 48 n.o.)
