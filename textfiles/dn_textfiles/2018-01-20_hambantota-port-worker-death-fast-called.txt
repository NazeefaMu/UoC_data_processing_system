## Title: Hambantota port worker death fast called off
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, January 20, 2018 - 01:00

Hambantota port worker death fast called off

Workers attached to Hambantota Port yesterday morning called off their fast-unto-death protest following a discussion with Ports and Shipping Minister Mahinda Samarasinghe.
The minister had told them that 138 employees out the total of 438 employees will be given employment at the Ports Authority and the rest of employees will be paid compensation over Rs 1 million. Magampura Port Workers’ Union President I. K. Omesh said they called off the protest and hunger strike following the promise of the minister.
The meeting between the minister and union representatives was facilitated by Ven.Omalpe Sobitha Thera.The protest of the Hambantota workers had been continuing for 60 days and the fast-unto death campaign continued for 11 days.Several workers engaged in the hunger strike were hospitalised due to deteriorated health conditions.
