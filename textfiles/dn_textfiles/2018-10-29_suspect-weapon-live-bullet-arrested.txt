## Title: Suspect with weapon, live bullet arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 29, 2018 - 01:00

Suspect with weapon, live bullet arrested

Pannala Police arrested a suspect with a locally made weapon and one live bullet from Mediriwila on a tip-off on Saturday.
The 37-year-old suspect, Lokuge Lasantha Pradeep alias Tangalle Sudda, is a resident of Polwatte, Iliwa Junction, Horana.
Police said the suspect is a retired army soldier. He was produced before the Kuliyapitiya Magistrate’s Court yesterday. Pannala police are investigating.
