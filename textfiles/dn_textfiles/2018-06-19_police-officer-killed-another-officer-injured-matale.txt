## Title: Police officer killed, another officer injured in Matale
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, June 19, 2018 - 09:20

Police officer killed, another officer injured in Matale

 
A vehicle ran over two Policemen killing one and injuring the other at Palapthwala, Matale, last night.
The incident took place while the two Police officers were engaged in night duty.
Further investigations are underway.
