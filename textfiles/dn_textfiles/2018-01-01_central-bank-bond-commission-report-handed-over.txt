## Title: Central Bank Bond Commission report handed over
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, January 1, 2018 - 01:00

Central Bank Bond Commission report handed over

The final report of the Presidential Commission of Inquiry (PCoI) on the Bond Issuance of the Central Bank was handed over to President Maithripala Sirisena on Saturday (Dec. 30). Commission’s Chairman Justice K.T. Chitrasiri handed over the report and two other members of the Commission Justice P. S. Jayawardene and retired Deputy Auditor General V. Kandasamy were present together with Secretary to the Commission S. Udugamsuriya. Secretary to the President Austin Fernando was also present.
