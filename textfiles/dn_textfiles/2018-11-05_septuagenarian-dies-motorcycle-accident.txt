## Title: Septuagenarian dies in motorcycle accident
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, November 5, 2018 - 01:00

Septuagenarian dies in motorcycle accident

A 78-year-old died when a motorbike ran over him while he was crossing the road along the zebra line in Godawela on Saturday morning.
The accident occurred near the People’s Bank in Godawela on the Colombo- Kurunegala main road.
The victim was pronounced dead on admission to the Polgahawela Hospital. He was identified as Herath Mdiyanseaage Wijeratne, a resident of Welikadahena, Polgahawela, police said.
The motorcyclist was arrested. He was to be produced before the Polgahawela Magistrate’s Court.
Investigations are continuing.
