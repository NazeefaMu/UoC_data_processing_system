## Title: Lankaputra Development Bank celebrates 11th anniversary
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Wednesday, July 12, 2017 - 01:00

Lankaputra Development Bank celebrates 11th anniversary

The Lankaputra Development Bank celebrated its 11th anniversary in grand manner at its headquarters in Colombo recently.
This ceremony gave priority to all religious observances. The ceremony was headed by Chairman Ruwan Gallage and senior management including Acting General Manager/CEO Ranjith Dissanayake and Working Director Thuan Riza Hameed.
In line with the 11th anniversary celebration at the headquarters, ceremonies have been organized at other branches of the LDB as well. Expressing hisviews at the event which was heald at the Bank's headquarters, Chairman Gallage said LDB which was initiated in 2006 has planned a long journey in the development banking sector.
Established with the objective of encouraging the small and medium scale entrepreneurs, LDB has identified the current challenges in the banking sector and proper solutions will be given to all these issues. Gallage further said that especially the LDB as a development-oriented bank will be committed to provide more flexible service to the public.
LDB has opened branches in Colombo, Hambantota, Polonnaruwa, Kandy, Kegalle, Wennappuwa, Batticaloa and Trincomalee areas. These branches are rendering a unique service to the small and medium scale entrepreneurs (SMEs) in these areas.
LDB grants attractive interest rates for savings account holders. Particularly, higher interest rates are paid for fixed deposits of senior citizens.
