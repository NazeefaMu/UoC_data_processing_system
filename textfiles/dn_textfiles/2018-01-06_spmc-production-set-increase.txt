## Title: SPMC production set to increase
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, January 6, 2018 - 01:00

SPMC production set to increase

The production of the State Pharmaceuticals Manufacturing Corporation (SMPC) to be increased up to four billion tablets by March, <U+200B>this<U+200B> year, Health Minist<U+200B>ry<U+200B><U+200B> sources said.
According to <U+200B>these sources, <U+200B>the new pharmaceuticals production factory of the SPMC will commence production in March next year and the total production of the SPMC will increase to four billion tablets. <U+200B>
<U+200B>T<U+200B>he State Pharmaceuticals Corporation (SPC), the State Pharmaceuticals Manufacturing Corporation (SMPC), Ayurveda Corporation and Sri Jayawardenepura General Hospital which comes under the purview of the Health Ministry are now making profits, the sources said<U+200B>.
“The Sri Jayawardenepura General Hospital does not obtain funds from the Treasury at present. State corporations can make profits, and I have proved this fact. it is only a myth that state corporations cannot make profits,” <U+200B>the sources added<U+200B>.
