## Title: Kovil till robbers arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, July 21, 2017 - 01:00

Kovil till robbers arrested

Two persons who had robbed money from the Sri Kadireshan Hindu Kovil till were arrested by the Special Investigation Unit attached to the Kurunegala Police recently. The suspects were identified as Selwarasa Muththurasa and Pradeep Kumar of Kurunegala Kovil Road. The first suspect Selwerasa had been involved in a number of robberies, the Police said.
They had robbed Rs. 4,000,000 from the till, while the Police were able to recover Rs. 17,000 from under the table of the Kovil.
