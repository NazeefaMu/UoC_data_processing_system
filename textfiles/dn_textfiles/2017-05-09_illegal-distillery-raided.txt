## Title: Illegal distillery raided
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 01:00

Illegal distillery raided

An illegal distillery in the Atubediyawa area near Lenadora was raided by Dambulla police on May 6 and seized 25 bottles of illicit liquor and distilling equipment. One suspect was arrested.
The arrested is the main distributor of illegal arrack in the area and suburbs, police said.
He was released on police bail.
