## Title: New Year festivities planned
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 12, 2018 - 01:00

New Year festivities planned

The Central Cultural Fund in collaboration with Dambulla and Sigiriya tourism promotion association will hold New Year festivities on a grand scale at the foot of the Sigirya rock, on April 20.Over 35 traditional New Year events are to be held for foreign and local tourists. Pillow fight, tug-of-war and races are among the events planned.
