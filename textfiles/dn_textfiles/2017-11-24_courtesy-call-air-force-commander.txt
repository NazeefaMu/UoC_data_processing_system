## Title: Courtesy call on Air Force Commander
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, November 24, 2017 - 01:00

Courtesy call on Air Force Commander

Navy Commander Vice Admiral Sirimevan Ranasinghe paid a courtesy call on Air Force Commander Air Marshal Kapila Jayampathy at the Air Force Headquarters in Colombo yesterday (23). The Navy Commander was accorded a Guard of Honour on his arrival at the premises.
