## Title: Tree-planting campaign
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, August 27, 2018 - 01:00

Tree-planting campaign

The Nintavur Ayurvedic Research Hospital's Non-Communicable Disease Unit organised a medicinal tree-planting campaign at the Al-Jazeera Vidyalaya in Meelath Nagar, Addalaichenai, recently.
Nintavur Ayurvedic Research Hospital Director and Health, Nutrition and Indigenous Medicine Ministry Advisor Dr. K.L.M. Nagfer was the chief guest.
Dr. J. Yoosuf, School Principal M.I.M. Riyas, Deputy Principal M.I. Jawfer, teachers P.T. Iqbal, A.B. Athaur Rahman, A.L. Ismail and A.M.M. Lukshman, as well as students participated.
