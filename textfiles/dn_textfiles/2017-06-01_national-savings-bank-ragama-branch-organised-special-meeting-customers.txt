## Supertitle:

National Savings Bank Ragama Branch organised a special meeting for customers

 National Savings Bank Ragama Branch organised a special meeting for customers at the Saninro Hall, Ragama recently. NSB Head Office Deputy General Manager Athula Gamage, Regional Manager Sandya
Fernando, Ragama Medical Faculty Prof. Ranil Fernando, NSB Branch Manager Mahinda Kumara and other officials were present at the occasion. Pictures by Sudath Malaweera
