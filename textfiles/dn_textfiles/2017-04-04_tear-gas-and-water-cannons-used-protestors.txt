## Title: Tear gas and water cannons used on protestors
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, April 4, 2017 - 17:00

Tear gas and water cannons used on protestors

(File Photo)
Tear gas and water cannons were used against the Inter University Bikkhu Federation on Lotus Road, a short while ago.
The IUBF is protesting university admission shortages, and asking that more funds are allocated to education.
