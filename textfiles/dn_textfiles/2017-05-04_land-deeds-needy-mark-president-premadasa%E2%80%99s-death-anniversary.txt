## Title: Land deeds for needy to mark President Premadasa’s death anniversary
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 4, 2017 - 01:00

Land deeds for needy to mark President Premadasa’s death anniversary

The 24th memorial day of President R. Premadasa, was held at the Premadasa statue in Hultsdorf, Colombo on May 1.
The Housing Construction Ministry with the assistance of the National Housing Development Authority distributed more than 1,500 deeds and house ownership certificates at the event.
About 200 families received housing loans under the Samata Sevena NHDA Housing Loan programme.
