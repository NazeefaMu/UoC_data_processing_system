## Title: Muslim Congress to vote in favor of Premier
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, April 3, 2018 - 23:43

Muslim Congress to vote in favor of Premier

 
The Muslim Congress decided to vote against the No Confidence Motion signed against Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe a short while ago.
In the meantime the meeting held between the Sri Lanka Freedom Party (SLFP) members and President Maithripala Sirisena regarding the No Confidence Motion also concluded.
The No Confidence Motion is scheduled to be taken up for debate in Parliament tomorrow.
