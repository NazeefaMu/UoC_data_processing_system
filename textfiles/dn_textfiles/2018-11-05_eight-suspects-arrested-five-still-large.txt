## Title: Eight suspects arrested; five still at large
## Supertitle: Alleged assault on police officers
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, November 5, 2018 - 01:00

Alleged assault on police officers
Eight suspects arrested; five still at large

Galewela Police recently arrested eight suspects over the assault on police officers during a raid conducted at a drug house in Kiralagolla.
Police said that another five suspects involved in the assault, including a woman, were still absconding.
The establishment where the raid was carried out had also functioned as a brothel, they added.
Investigations are continuing.
