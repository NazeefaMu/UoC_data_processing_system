## Title: Student gets bail in assault case
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Wednesday, August 8, 2018 - 01:00

Student gets bail in assault case

Tangalle Chief Magistrate LilanTilantha released a student who was convicted of having beaten another student at the Tangalle bus stand, on bail of Rs. 2 lakhs.
The student released on bail is a resident of Tangalle and was to sit for the GCE Advanced Level examination.
The same student has a case in court for robbing items from a shop in a previous instance.
The magistrate declared that the suspect’s bail conditions will be cancelled if he commits any other offence.
Tangalle Acting HQI Senaka Dissanayaka and a team of police officers took the suspect into custody.
