## Title: Japanese Ambassador on Disarmament meets Defence Secretary
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 13, 2018 - 01:00

Japanese Ambassador on Disarmament meets Defence Secretary

The Permanent Representative of Japan to the Conference on Disarmament in Geneva, Ambassador Nobushige Takamizawa made a courtesy call on Defence Secretary Kapila Waidyaratne PC, last Monday.
The Secretary and Ambassador Takamizawa had cordial discussions during the meeting. The Defence Ministry’s Military Liaison Officer Major General D.A.R. Ranawaka, Head of the Arms Trade Treaty Secretariat, Dumisani Dladla, First Secretary, Permanent Representative of Japan to the Conference on Disarmament, Yoko Owatari and First Secretary, Head of the Political Section of the Japanese Embassy, Kiishiro IWASE were also present at the occasion.
