## Title: Man fined for illegally transporting sand
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 4, 2017 - 01:00

Man fined for illegally transporting sand

A man previously convicted for illegal sand transportation was taken into custody by Galewela Police when he was transporting sand without a permit in a tipper at the midnight.
The suspect was produced before Dambulla District Judge and Magistrate I.M.S.B. Illangasingha. He was fined Rs. 150,000 after pleading guilty.
