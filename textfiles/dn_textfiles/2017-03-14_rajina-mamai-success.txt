Rajina Mamai - a success

A concert series to celebrate 70 years of songs by veteran vocalists Lata Walpola and C T Fernando was held in Australia. C T Fernando’s son Priyantha Fernando sang at the three concerts which was held on February 25 in Melbourne, March 4 in Brisbane and March 11 in Oakland, New Zealand.
