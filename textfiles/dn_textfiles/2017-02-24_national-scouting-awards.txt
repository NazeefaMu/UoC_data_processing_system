## Title: National scouting awards
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 24, 2017 - 01:00

National scouting awards

The ‘national scouting awards’ ceremony organised by the Sri Lanka Scout Association once in four years, was held at the Nelum Pokuna Theatre, Colombo on February 22 under the patronage of Chief Scout President Maithripala Sirisena.
The ceremony honoured scout masters and lay members of the scout Association who voluntarily serve the scout movement.
A batch of 20 persons received the ‘Silver Lioness” award from the President for the service they have rendered towards the scout movement. Another 565 persons received other categories of awards.
Concurrently, the 160th birth anniversary of Sir Baden Powell, the founder of the scout member was also marked. Education Minister Akila Viraj Kariyawasam, Chief Scout Commissioner Prof. Nimal de Silva, Sri Lanka Scout Association President Srinath Gunaratne, Executive Committee Chairman Ransiri Perera, Deputy Commissioner Janaprith Fernando and Commissioner Sarath Godakandaarachchi were also present.
