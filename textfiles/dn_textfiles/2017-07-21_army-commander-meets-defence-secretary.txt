## Title: Army Commander meets Defence Secretary
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, July 21, 2017 - 01:00

Army Commander meets Defence Secretary

Army Commander Lieutenant General Mahesh Senanayake made a courtesy call on the newly appointed Defence Secretary Kapila Waidyaratne at the latter’s office in the Defence Ministry (MoD) yesterday (20). They exchanged mementos as symbols of goodwill and understanding.
