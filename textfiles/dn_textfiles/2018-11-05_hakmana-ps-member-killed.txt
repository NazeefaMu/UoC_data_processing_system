## Title: Hakmana PS member killed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, November 5, 2018 - 01:00

Hakmana PS member killed

Hakmana Pradeshiya Sabha member L.H.Shantha (SLPP) was shot dead in the Kebiliyapola area yesterday. He was shot by persons who arrived on a motor bicycle and a van. He succumbed to injuries after being immediately admitted to the Gangodagama hospital.
Hakmana police launched a special operation to arrest the culprits connected to this shooting incident.No suspects had been identified so far.
