## Title: Death of Nandasena Panagoda
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 28, 2018 - 01:00

Death of Nandasena Panagoda

Sirasa provincial correspondent Nandasena Panagoda passed away on December 26. A father of two Nandasena will be laid to rest on Saturday, December 29, at the Avissawella public cemetery, His body now lies at his residence at Vidyala Mawatha, Avissawella.
