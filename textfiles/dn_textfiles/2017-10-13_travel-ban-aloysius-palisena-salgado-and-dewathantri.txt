## Title: Travel ban on Aloysius, Palisena, Salgado and Dewathantri
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 2, 2017 - 20:30

Travel ban on Aloysius, Palisena, Salgado and Dewathantri

 
The Fort Magistrate this evening issued an order preventing former Director of Perpetual Treasuries Limited (PTL) Arjun Aloysius, CEO Kasun Palisena, Chief Dealer Nuwan Salgado and IT Executive Sachith Dewathantri from leaving the country.
