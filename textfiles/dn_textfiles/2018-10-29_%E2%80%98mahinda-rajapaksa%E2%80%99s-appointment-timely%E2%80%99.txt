## Title: ‘Mahinda Rajapaksa’s appointment timely’
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 29, 2018 - 01:00

‘Mahinda Rajapaksa’s appointment timely’

The Anunayaka Thera of the Asgiri Chapter, Venerable Wedaruwe Upali Thera said yesterday the decision by President Maithripala Sirisena to appoint Mahinda Rajapaksa as the Prime Minister was a timely move.
The Thera said the Maha Sangha hopes that the new Prime Minister will find viable answers to the numerous issues facing the country today.
The Thera ws speaking when the newly appointed Prime Minister visited the Asgiriya Maha Viharaya in Kandy yesterday. While recalling that Mahinda Rajapaksa during his tenure as the President had saved the country from the clutches of terrorism, the Thera said the people would ever remember his service to the nation. The Thera also said the people had always stood behind Mahinda Rajapaksa during good and bad times.
