## Title: Banking on youth talent
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Tuesday, April 3, 2018 - 01:00

Banking on youth talent

The final of ITN’s ‘Youth with Talent’ reality show was held recently at Maharagama Youth Centre auditorium. Janaka Kanchana Mudunnayake became the winner at the event with his strong man act. Nishfahan the mathematical genius from Nawalapitiya was the runner up. The jury was made up of Rookantha Gunathilake, Umara Sinhawansa, Nadeesha Hemamali, and Kishu Gomes. The guest Judge was Hemal Ranasinghe. Pictures by Sarath Peiries
