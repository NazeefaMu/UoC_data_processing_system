Massive win for Cambrian under 13 team

 
 A fine bowling spell of 5 for 22 boy Thinuka Fernando helped Prince of Wales College to beat formidable Wesley College by a massive 266 runs in their inter school under 13 division one tournament cricket match played Colombo.
Homesters batting first were dismissed for 111 runs and visitors replied with 377 for 6 with Induwara de Alwis 88, Tharindu Amerashinghe 59, Rehan Chamika 57 and Shehan Rodrigo and Vinuja Rampul unbeaten on 74 and 71 runs respectively.
