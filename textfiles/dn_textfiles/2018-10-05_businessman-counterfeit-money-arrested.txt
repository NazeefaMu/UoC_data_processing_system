## Title: Businessman with counterfeit money arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, October 5, 2018 - 01:00

Businessman with counterfeit money arrested

A businessman possessing counterfeit money was arrested by the Kurunegala Police Anti-Vice Division on Wednesday.
The suspect had 414 bundles of counterfeit Rs.5,000 notes with him at the time of the arrest, with each bundle containing 100 notes.
The suspect was to be handed over to the Criminal Investigation Department for further investigations, a police chief in Kurunegala told the Daily News.
