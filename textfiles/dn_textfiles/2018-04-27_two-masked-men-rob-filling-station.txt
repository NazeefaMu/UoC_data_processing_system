## Title: Two masked men rob filling  station
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, April 27, 2018 - 01:00

Two masked men rob filling  station

Two masked men arrived at the Bangadeniya Petrol Filling Station in a grey car with concealed number plates around 5 am on April 23 had stolen cash from the station locker threatening the supervisor at knife-point, a complaint made by the victim, Geekiyanage Wickremasinghe Fernando to Chilaw Police said.
He said the cash robbed was roughly around Rs. 98,140. Investigations are continuing.
