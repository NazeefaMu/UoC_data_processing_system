## Title: Chief Ministers Summit at Habarana
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Sunday, May 7, 2017 - 14:00

Chief Ministers Summit at Habarana

 
This years’ Chief Ministers Summit was held at Habarana.
President Maithripala Sirisena participated as the chief guest.

At the summit a number of resolutions of national and provincial level importance were submitted to the president and passed. 
Among them were the resolutions to make dhamma education compulsory and to provide employment to the graduates at the provincial level. 
It was decided at the conference that unemployed graduates should be recruited as teachers and also absorbed into the provincial public service as development officers.
