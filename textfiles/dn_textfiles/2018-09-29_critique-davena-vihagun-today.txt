## Title: Critique on Davena Vihagun today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, September 29, 2018 - 01:00

Critique on Davena Vihagun today

A discussion on Sanjeewa Pushpakumara’s award winning film Davena Vihagun (Burning Birds) will be held today at Centre for Society and Religion (CSR) auditorium, No. 281, Deans Road, Maradana from 3.00pm onwards.
Film critics Ajith Galappaththi, Boopathi Nalin Wickramage and Sumith Chaminda will deliver talks on the film while Nuwan Jude Liyanage will moderate the discussion.
