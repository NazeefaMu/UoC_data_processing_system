## Title: Korean cultural festival
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, June 17, 2017 - 01:00

Korean cultural festival

The Korean Embassy in Colombo, the National Unification Advisory Council South West Asia Chapter, the Korean Residents Association of Lanka and the Korean Businessmen’s Association in Sri Lanka will hold programmes to celebrate the 40th anniversary of diplomatic ties between Korea and Sri Lanka.
A Korean Cultural Festival 2017 will be held at the Bishop’s College Auditorium on 1 July. Internal Affairs, Wayamba Development and Culture Affairs Ministry and the Sri Lanka Rupavahini Corporation will support the event.
The 2017 Festival consists of two main events: the Quiz-on Korea and the K-Pop World Festival.
