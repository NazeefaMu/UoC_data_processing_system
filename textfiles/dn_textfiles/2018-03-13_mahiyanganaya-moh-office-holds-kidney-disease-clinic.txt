## Title: Mahiyanganaya MOH office holds kidney disease clinic
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, March 13, 2018 - 01:00

Mahiyanganaya MOH office holds kidney disease clinic

A clinic for kidney patients was recently organised by the Mahiyanganaya Medical Officer of Health (MOH) at Dambaana, Mahiyanganaya for those unable to visit medical care centres. The event was co-ordinated by the Haddaththeva Public Health Inspector's Office
Residents of Velpallevela, Vathuyaaya, Gurukumbura, Vevaththa, Kukulaapola, Bimmalamulla and Beligalla around the Dambaana Divisional Hospital, visited the clinic.
