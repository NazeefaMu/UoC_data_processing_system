## Title: Turkey Embassy celebrates Proclamation of the Republic Day
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 1, 2018 - 01:00

Turkey Embassy celebrates Proclamation of the Republic Day

The Embassy of Turkey celebrated its 95th Anniversary of the Proclamation of the Republic Day in Colombo under the patronage of Turkey Ambassador Tunca - Ozcuhadar. The Ambassador read out the message of the President of Turkey Tayyip Erdogan, and said Sri Lanka and Turkey have good relations.
Turkey had helped those affected by the tsunami in Sri Lanka, he said.
Turkey had also supported Sri Lankan journalists to obtain scholarships and carry out training in Turkey. The country has also donated media equipment to Sri Lankan journalists, he said.
The Ambassador added that Sri Lanka and Turkey bilateral trade relations are strong. Turkey buys Sri Lanka’s tea and supports water supply projects and projects in the construction field.
He conveyed Turkey’s best wishes for peace, unity, harmony and progress of the Sri Lankan government and people.
The ceremony was attended by a large number of participants from various walks of life including members of the Sri Lankan business community.
The Turkish National Day Raffle Draw was held and four round trip air tickets from Colombo to Istanbul with Turkish Airlines were offered to Sri Lankans.
Foreign Ministry’s former Secretary, Member of Parliament A. H. M. Fowzie, former Defence Secretary Gotabhaya Rajapaksa, former Minister Prof. G. L. Pieris, diplomatic community, Sri Lanka ‘s Army, Navy, Air Force chiefs participated.
