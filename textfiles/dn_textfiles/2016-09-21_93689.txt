## Title: Dialog Cricket Awards postponed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, September 21, 2016 - 01:00

Dialog Cricket Awards postponed

Sri Lanka Cricket wishes to announce that it is formulating a new selection criteria for the upcoming Cricket Awards, taking into consideration the best Junior and Senior cricketers who have performed exceptionally well during the last year.
Hence the Dialog Cricket Awards 2016 which was scheduled to be held on September 21 has been postponed to November 30.
