## Title: Last year runner up Ananda will meet St. Sebastian’s in semis today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, March 28, 2016 - 01:00

Last year runner up Ananda will meet St. Sebastian’s in semis today

Defending runner-up Ananda College, Colombo will meet St. Sebastian’s College, Moratuwa in the semi final of ‘Singer Trophy’ Under 19 inter schools limited overs cricket tournament at R. Premadasa International Cricket Stadium, Colombo today.
St. Sebastian’s College beat Royal College, Colombo and Ananda College beat St. Joseph’s College, Colombo in the pre-quarter finals.
St. Sebastian’s College beat Isipathana College, Colombo and Ananda College beat Prince of Wales College, Moratuwa in the quarter finals. Avishka Fernando will lead St. Sebastian’s College and Sandaruwan Fernando will assist him as deputy skipper. Ananda College Colombo will be led by Nisal Francisco and Kavishka Anjula is the deputy skipper.
St. Sebastian’s College squad: Avishka Fernando (Captain), Sandaruwan Fernando (vice captain), Nimesh Bandara, Mishen Silva, Shachintha Silva, Praveen Perera, Nuwanidu Fernando, Praveen Jayawickrama, Malintha Peiris, Gihan Senanayake, Nishitha Abilash, Damian De Buyzer, Asher Warnakulasuriya, Preshain Fernando, Thashik Perera, Tharusha Fernando, Mevan Muhandiramge, Imal Boteju (Head Coach), Manoj Chanaka (Assistant coach), Frank Cooray (Master in Charge)
Ananda College squad: Nisal Francisco (Captain), Kavishka Anjula (Vice Captain),Sammu Ashan, Dileepa Jayalath, Nipun Hakgalla, Dinuka Jayarathne, Rahal Avishka, Ashel Sigera, Supun Waragoda, Lahiru Udayanga, Sahan Suraweera, Dushan Hettige, Kamesh Nirmal, Kalana Perera, Aveesha Wilwalaarachchi, Lahiru Atthanayake, Achila Iranga, Rohan Soysa (Head Coach), Thushira Perera (Asst. Coach), Priyankara Nandasena (Master In Charge)
Trinity takes on Nalanda in second semi final
Trinity College, Kandy advanced to the semi final of ‘Singer Trophy’ Under 19 inter schools limited overs cricket tournament when they beat St. Peter’s College, Bambalapitiya by 173 runs in the quarter final played at FTZ ground, Katunayake yesterday.
Trinity won the toss and decided to bat scored 327runs in 50 overs. Dilan Jayalath scored 129 runs in 82 balls with 11 boundaries and 7 sixes. Hasitha Boyagoda scored 70 runs and opener Nushan Weerasinghe scored 62 runs. Dilan Jayalath and Hasitha Boyagoda made 163 runs stand for 4th wicket. Nushan Weerasinghe and Shavindu Amarasinghe made 64 runs stand for first wicket. Taneesha Weerakoon took 2 wickets.
St. Peter’s bowled out for 154 runs in 47.1 overs. Skipper Vinu Mohotti scored 42 runs and Ranmith Jayasena scored 35 runs. Raveen Sayer and Lahiru Kumara took 3 wickets each.
Trinity Kandy will meet Nalanda, Colombo in the semi final tomorrow (29) at RPICS.
Trinity: 327/6 in 50 overs (Dilan Jayalath 129, Hasitha Boyagoda 70, Nushan Weerasinghe 62, Shavindu Amarasinghe 20, Taneesha Weerakoon 2/62)
St. Peter’s: 154 in 47.1 overs (Vinu Mohotti 42, Ranmith Jayasena 35, Lakshina Rodrigo 20, Raveen Sayer 3/21, Lahiru Kumara 3/29)
