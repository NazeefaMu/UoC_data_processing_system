## Title: Water level of Moragahakanda reservoir on the rise
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, January 23, 2020 - 11:04

Water level of Moragahakanda reservoir on the rise

The water level of the Moragahakanda reservoir has reached a very high level following recent heavy rains and the sluice gates are scheduled to be opened.
