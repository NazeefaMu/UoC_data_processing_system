## Title: Jeep shot at near IDH
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, July 21, 2017 - 01:00

Jeep shot at near IDH

Two unidentified motorcyclists opened fire at a jeep near the IDH fuel station yesterday evening, Police sources said.
No person was injured and the bullets pierced the windscreen of the jeep, police said. The gunmen had fled the area following the shooting. Police suspects a clash between two underworld groups may have led to the shooting.
Gothatuwa police are investigating
