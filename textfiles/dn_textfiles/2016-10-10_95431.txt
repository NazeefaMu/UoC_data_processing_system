## Title: STC Matara, Seetha Devi emerge champs
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, October 10, 2016 - 01:00

STC Matara, Seetha Devi emerge champs

St Thomas’ College Matara and Seetha Devi Balika Kandy showed their prowess at sevens hockey by winning the boys and girls titles at the 14th Singer Blue and Gold Hockey Sevens concluded at Reid Avenue yesterday.
In the Boys Cup final St Thomas’ beat Joseph Vaz Negombo 3-0 with the goals coming off the sticks of NR Gunaratne, ACB Prahitiara and TCS Bandara. Nalanda College took third place.
The Girls Cup final saw Seetha Devi Balika pull off a 1-0 win over Sangamitta Balika Vidyalaya Galle with the winning goal being scored by Tharaki Paranagama. Yashodara BMV finished third.
M Kurera of Joseph Vaz won the Best Player’s award (boys) and CN Abeyratne of Seetha Devi (girls). The award for Best Goal Keeper was won by A Ranaweera of St Thomas’ Matale (boys) and N Kodituwakku of Sangammita (girls).
Results of Bowl and Plate finals:
Bowl (boy’s): Christchurch Matale beat St Aloysius 3-0; (girls): Janadipathi Balika beat Sirimavo 4-0.
Plate (boy’s): DS Senanayake beat Maris Stella 1-0; (girls): Musaeus College beat Visakha Vidyalaya 1-0.
