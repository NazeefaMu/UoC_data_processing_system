## Title: Rogue foreign employment agent arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 28, 2018 - 01:00

Rogue foreign employment agent arrested

A rogue foreign employment agent who had allegedly operated at an office without sanctions of authorities and swindled money from foreign job aspirants, was arrested recently.According to the Sri Lanka Bureau of Foreign Employment (SLBFE), the agent from Kuliyapitiya had duped people into paying for fake lucrative overseas jobs which never.
The agent had been arrested with 49 passports at his residence in Kuliyapitiya.
An SLBFE spokesman said the suspect had obtained large sums of money from a number of people in many parts of the country and an investigation had been commenced to identify them.
Meanwhile, the SLBFE yesterday urged people to verify their sources of overseas employment with authorities before committing to any financial obligation.
They urged employment seekers to inform the nearest SLBFE office immediately, if they were suspicious about their foreign employment sources.
