## Title: Jaffna university students shooting: Five policemen re-remanded
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Thursday, February 23, 2017 - 16:30

Jaffna university students shooting: Five policemen re-remanded

 
The five policemen arrested for allegedly being involved in Jaffna university students’ shooting incident were re-remanded today (23) till March 9.
The Jaffna Magistrate issued the order against the five policemen.
The two Jaffna university students were shot dead while returning home from a function on October 20, 2016.
