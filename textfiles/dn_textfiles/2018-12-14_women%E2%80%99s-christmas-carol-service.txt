## Title: Women’s Christmas carol service
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 14, 2018 - 01:00

Women’s Christmas carol service

The North East Methodist District Women's Unity Society of the Methodist Church of Sri Lanka held their women's Christmas carols service on ‘Life given by God’ at the Kalmunai Methodist Church recently.
It was held under the patronage of North East Secretary of Methodist Church of Sri Lanka Rev. Sujiver Sivanayagam, with the participation of more than 300 members of the women's unity societies from Jaffna, Mannar, Vavuniya, Mullaitivu, Trincomalee, Batticaloa, and Ampara.
Methodist Church of Sri Lanka Deputy President P. Dammika Fernando was the chief guest at the event. Deputy President of the Methodist Church of Sri Lanka, P. Dammika Fernando, was felicitated with the memento by former Deputy Methodist Church President V. Prabakaran and Methodist Church of Kalmunai Pastor S.D. Vinoth.
