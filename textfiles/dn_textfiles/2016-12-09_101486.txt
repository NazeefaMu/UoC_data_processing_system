## Title: The second SLT Silk Sports Awards 2016
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, December 9, 2016 - 01:00

The second SLT Silk Sports Awards 2016

The second SLT SILK SPORTS AWARDS 2016, Sri Lanka’s first annual sports awards ceremony organized by sportsinfo.lk and sponsored by Sri Lanka Telecom was held at the Grand Ballroom of Waters Edge Hotel recently.
Sports Minister Dayasiri Jayasekara was the chief guest while Minister Of Ports Arjuna Ranatunga participated as the guest of honour.
