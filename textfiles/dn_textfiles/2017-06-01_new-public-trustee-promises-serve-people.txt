## Title: New Public Trustee promises to serve people
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 1, 2017 - 01:00

New Public Trustee promises to serve people

Newly appointed Public Trustee Sanath Weeraratne said yesterday that he would work efficiently with transparency for the benefit of the people.
The Public Trustee Department will turn into one of the best administered organisations.
Plans will also be drawn up to earn public trust and confidence by working efficiently and transparently,” he said.
Addressing the media after being sworn in as the Public Trustee, he said he would give priority to Buddhism.
He said reports of the Public Trustee Department will be studied to make the department more efficient and people- friendly.
