## Title: Beruwala Capsizing: Twenty-four year-old skipper arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 24, 2017 - 14:45

Beruwala Capsizing: Twenty-four year-old skipper arrested

 
The twenty-four-year-old youth who operated the boat that capsized in Katukurunda on Sunday, February 19, was arrested last evening.
He was produced before the Kalutara Magistrate who ordered him remanded till March 6.
The youth is a resident of Beruwala.
In addition to overloading the boat, the youth had also allegedly failed to provide life jackets to the passengers, Police said.
Sixteen people died in the incident.
Kalutara Police is investigating.
