## Title: Woman, child injured in accident
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 28, 2018 - 01:00

Woman, child injured in accident

A woman and her five-year-old child were critically injured when they were knocked down by a speeding bus at Wellawaya, yesterday morning. The victims had then been rushed to the Wellawaya Base Hospital and were later transferred to the Moneragala District General Hospital.
The incident had occurred when a Sri Lanka Transport Board (SLTB) bus had attempted to overtake the motorcycle the victims had been travelling on.
It was revealed that the victims are residents of the Weerasekaragama area in Wellawaya.
The Wellawaya Police Traffic Unit had arrested the bus driver. Investigations are continuing.
