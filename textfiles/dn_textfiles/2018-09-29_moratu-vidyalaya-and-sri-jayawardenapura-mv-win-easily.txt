## Title: Moratu Vidyalaya and Sri Jayawardenapura MV win easily
## Supertitle: Singer Cup’ Under 19 Inter School Cricket Tournament:
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, September 29, 2018 - 01:00

Singer Cup’ Under 19 Inter School Cricket Tournament:
Moratu Vidyalaya and Sri Jayawardenapura MV win easily

Moratu Vidyalaya and Sri Jayawardenapura MV Kotte won the matches in their fixtures of the ‘Singer Trophy’ Under 19 inter Schools Cricket tournament played at Moratuwa and Kotte yesterday.
Moratu Vidyalaya beat Lumbini College Colombo by 7 wickets and Sri Jayawardenapura MV Kotte beat Tissa Central College Kalutara by innings and 26 runs. Other matches were drawn.
Santhush Gunathilake (St. Peter’s), Lakshitha Rasanjana (Nalanda), Piyumal Fernando (Loyola), Heshan Madusanka (Piliyandala Central), Visara Sasmitha (Kalutara Vidyalaya) and Pabod Wanigasinghe (Kalutara Vidyalaya) made centuries. Heshan Jayasekara of Kuliyapitiya Central took 8 wickets for 77 runs against Loyola Negombo.
