## Title: 14th United Nations Vesak Celebrations
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Friday, May 12, 2017 - 01:00

14th United Nations Vesak Celebrations


14th United Nations Vesak Celebrations will be held with the participation of H.E. the President of the Democratic, Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka Maithripala Sirisena, H.E. the President of Nepal Bidya Devi Bhandari, the Prime Minister of India H.E. Narendra Modi and the Hon. Prime Minister of Sri Lanka Ranil Wickremasinghe.
