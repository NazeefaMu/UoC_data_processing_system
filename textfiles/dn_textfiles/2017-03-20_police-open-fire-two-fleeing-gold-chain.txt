## Title: Police open fire at two fleeing with gold chain
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, March 20, 2017 - 11:30

Police open fire at two fleeing with gold chain

 
Police in Arachchikattuwa, Chilaw this morning opened fire at two men on a motorcycle who were attempting to flee after snatching a gold chain from a woman’s neck.
One man was injured in the incident.
Investigations are underway.
