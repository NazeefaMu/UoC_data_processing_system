## Title: Garbage dump slide blocks Sri Pada – Hatton road
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, October 19, 2017 - 01:00

Garbage dump slide blocks Sri Pada – Hatton road

A garbage mountain heaped at Mahagiridamba on the Nallathanniya – Udamaluwa road awaiting clearing, slipped due to the heavy rain blocking the Sri Pada – Hatton in the wee hours of yesterday.
The Army and Police were involved in clearing the road with the assistance of residents in the area. Sri Pada Sthanadhipathi Ven. Bengamuwe Dhammadinne Thera said waste dumped in and around Sri Pada by pilgrims were heaped at the spot for clearance but the work was held up due to incessant rains resulting in yesterday’s slide.
