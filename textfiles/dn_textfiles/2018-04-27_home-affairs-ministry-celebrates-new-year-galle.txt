## Title: Home Affairs Ministry  celebrates New Year in Galle
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, April 27, 2018 - 01:00

Home Affairs Ministry  celebrates New Year in Galle

The Sinhala – Hindu New Year celebration organized by the Home Affairs Ministry was held throughout the whole day on April 25 at Galle Samanala Grounds with the participation of Galle public. The inauguration of the festivity was held under the patronage of Home Affairs Ministry Secretary S. T. Kodikara.
The event was coordinated by Galle District Secretariat together with Galle Four Gravets Divisional Secretariat. The opening contests of the New Year festival were 100 kilometer grand bicycle race and marathon race which commenced from the entrance of Galle District Secretariat.
The prize distribution of the New Year festival was held with Home Affairs Minister Vajira Abeywardana as the Chief Guest.
