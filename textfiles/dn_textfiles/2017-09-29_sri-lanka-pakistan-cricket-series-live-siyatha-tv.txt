## Title: Sri Lanka-Pakistan cricket series live on Siyatha TV
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, September 29, 2017 - 01:00

Sri Lanka-Pakistan cricket series live on Siyatha TV

The ongoing cricket series between Sri Lanka and Pakistan will be telecast live by Siyatha TV who have secured the rights from LSD Media, a sports marketing and media rights syndication company based out of the UAE. Siyatha outbid Sri Lanka Rupavahini Corporation who was also a contender for the television rights.
The matches are shown live from 11 am Sri Lanka time.
