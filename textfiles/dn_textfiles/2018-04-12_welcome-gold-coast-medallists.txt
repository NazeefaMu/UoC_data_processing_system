## Title: Welcome for Gold Coast medallists
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 12, 2018 - 01:08

Welcome for Gold Coast medallists

The first of the Sri Lankan medallists at the 21st Commonwealth Games in Gold Coast Australia, weightlifters Indika Dissanayake, Dinusha Hansani Gomes and Chaturanga Lakmal Jayasuriya who won Silver and Bronze medals arrived in the country. They were received by Sports Minister Dayasiri Jayasekera and sports ministry officials at the Bandaranaike International Airport yesterday.
