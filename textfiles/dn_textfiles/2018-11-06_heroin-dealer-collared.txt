## Title: Heroin dealer collared
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, November 6, 2018 - 01:00

Heroin dealer collared

Police arrested a 25-year-old suspect possessing 6.8 grams of heroin, recently. He is a resident of the same area.
It was revealed that the suspect, as well as his mother and sister, had been dealing heroin to addicts in the district for a long time. They had been convicted for the same offence previously as well, police said.
The suspect was to be produced at the Galle Magistrate’s Court, yesterday.
