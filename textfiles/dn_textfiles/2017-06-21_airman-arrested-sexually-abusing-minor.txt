## Title: Airman arrested for sexually abusing minor
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 21, 2017 - 01:00

Airman arrested for sexually abusing minor

A sergeant attached to the Air Force band was arrested on suspicion for sexually abusing a minor who accompanied her mother, grandmother and a cousin on a pilgrimage to Anuradhapura.
The accused was remanded till June 30 when he was produced before Anuradhapura Chief Magistrate and Additional District Judge K.P. Shanaka Harshan on June 19.
Anuradhapura police told court that the suspect who sat in a bus next to the girl had persuaded her to commit a sexual act with him. When the girl complained to the mother seated behind about it, the suspect had jumped off the bus but onlookers had apprehended him and brought him to the police station in a vehicle.
