## Title: President opens Beira path
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, November 24, 2017 - 01:05

President opens Beira path

The first phase of the Colombo pedestrian web connecting D.R. Wijewardene Mawatha and Olcott Mawatha along the boundary of the Lake House and the Beira Lake Canal was opened by President Maithripala Sirisena last morning. Picture by Saman Sri Wedage
