## Title: KDU Convocation-2018 held at BMICH
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, November 5, 2018 - 01:00

KDU Convocation-2018 held at BMICH

The 28th General Convocation of the General Sir John Kotelawala Defence University (KDU) was held on November 1-2 at the BMICH under the patronage of Higher Education Minister Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe. The Defence Secretary, Defence Staff Chief, Tri-Service Commanders, KDU Chancellor and Vice Chancellor, Vice Chancellors of Sri Lankan Universities, Deputy Vice Chancellor (Defence & Admin), Deputy Vice Chancellor (Academic), Rector (Southern Campus, KDU), All Deans, KDU Academic and Non Academic Staff, Senior Officers of Tri Services, Military and Civilian Graduates among others participated. The KDU is the only university in the island which offers higher education opportunities for military and civilian personnel in a disciplined environment. One thousand two hundred and fifteen (1215) students in eight different faculties graduated at this year’s Convocation. Two PhD degrees, 213 Masters’ degrees, 1,000 postgraduate diplomas and Bachelor’s Degrees were conferred.
