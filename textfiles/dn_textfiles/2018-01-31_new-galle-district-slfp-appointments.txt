## Title: New Galle District SLFP appointments
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Wednesday, January 31, 2018 - 01:00

New Galle District SLFP appointments

Attorney Sampath Sujeewa Ruwanpura, who functioned as the Ratgama UNP organiser in 2015 and Ratgama UNP legal secretary since 2000, has been appointed the Galle District SLFP organiser. He received his appointment letter from President Maithripala Sirisena at the Presidential Secretariat, yesterday. Meanwhile, Hemachandra Withanachchi received a letter appointing him the Galle District SLFP co-ordinating officer. Pictures by the President's Media Unit.
