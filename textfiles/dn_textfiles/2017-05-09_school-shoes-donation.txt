## Title: School shoes donation
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 01:00

School shoes donation

Nugegoda Damsaviya Foundation donated 100 DSI school shoes to school children of Vetamulla village. The charity event was done as a part of Parakaduwa Vevakasenasana Viharare’s Methsith Foundation’s monthly charity work. The donation took place at Abhinavarama Vetamulla. The Methsith Foundation is headed by Venurika Jayasinghe, Madhurika Samarakoon and Lakmali Abeykoon and it is under the guidance of Parakaduwa Vivekarama chief incumbent Ven Thoranakada Upatissa.
