## Title: Held for stealing gold Buddha statue
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 14, 2018 - 01:00

Held for stealing gold Buddha statue

Colombo Crime Investigations Division officials arrested six suspects, including an ex-police constable, who had stolen a gold Buddha statue worth Rs.7 million from a house in Narahenpita on December 12.
A Defender jeep that the suspects had used to commit the robbery was also seized.
Investigations are continuing.
