## Title: Dengue prevention: Programme to monitor govt, private institutions
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, July 21, 2017 - 01:00

Dengue prevention: Programme to monitor govt, private institutions

The Provincial Councils and Local Government Ministry initiated a special programme to monitor government and private institutions for dengue breeding sites under the programme “A workplace free of garbage”.
The programme started from the ministry yesterday. The Chief Medical Officer of Health of the CMC, Doctor Ruwan Wijayamuni and a group of Public Health Inspectors inspected buildings and the ministry premises.
The Paddy Marketing Board, Food Commissioners Department and Cooperative Development Department, were also inspected. A dengue mosquito breeding site was found in the Paddy Marketing Board premises.
Minister Faiszer Musthapha also participated.
