## Title: Nabbed for possessing heroin
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 30, 2018 - 01:00

Nabbed for possessing heroin

Three drug addicts who were loitering in the Dambulla town were taken into custody by Dambulla Police. Police said 700 mg of heroin were discovered in their possession at the time of the arrest.
Later, they were produced before Dambulla District Magistrate I. M. S. B. Illangasingha.
It was revealed that one of the suspects had been previously convicted for a similar offence.
Subsequently, the magistrate convicted and fined the individuals Rs. 10,000 each, following a unanimous plea of guilty.
The accused are residents of the same area. Dambulla Police prosecuted.
