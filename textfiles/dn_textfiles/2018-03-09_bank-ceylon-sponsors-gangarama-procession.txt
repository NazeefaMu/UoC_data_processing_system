## Title: Bank of Ceylon sponsors Gangarama procession
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Friday, March 9, 2018 - 01:00

Bank of Ceylon sponsors Gangarama procession

In sponsoring the preservation of national heritage, under the sustainability policy of the Bank, and culture the Bank of Ceylon donated for the Gangarama Temple’s annual cultural and religious procession. Shown in the picture are the BOC’s Chairman, President’s Counsel, Ronald C. Perera and the General Manager, Senarath Bandara handing over the sponsorship payment to the Chief Incumbent of the Gangarama Temple, Venerable Galaboda Gnanissara Thero. Bank’s other officials also participated for this occasion.
