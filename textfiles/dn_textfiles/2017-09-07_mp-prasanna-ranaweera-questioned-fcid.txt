## Title: MP Prasanna Ranaweera questioned by FCID
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 01:00

MP Prasanna Ranaweera questioned by FCID

Gampaha District MP Prasanna Ranaweera was questioned by the Financial Crimes Investigation Division (FCID) yesterday regarding complaints made against him on incidents that took place when he was the Kelaniya Pradeshiya Sabha Chairman. Here, MP Prasanna Ranaweera arriving at the FCID. Picture by Shan Rupassara.
