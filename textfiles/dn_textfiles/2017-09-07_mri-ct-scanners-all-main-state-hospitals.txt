## Title: MRI, CT scanners for all main state hospitals
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 01:00

MRI, CT scanners for all main state hospitals

The government will provide MRI and CT scanners for all main state hospitals by 2018, according to a decision by Health Minister Dr. Rajitha Senaratne, Health Ministry sources said.
According to the sources, a survey will be conducted to find out the required number of MRI and CT Scanners for main state hospitals. Minister Dr. Senaratne had instructed health officials to supply automatic machines when supplying medical equipment to state hospitals.
Arrangements will be made to import Linear Accelerator machines to serve cancer patients free of charge. All drugs for cancer patients will be supplied without any delay or interruption under the direction of the minister, the sources said.
Minister Dr. Senaratne instructed doctors and officials to provide him a list of drugs required for cancer patients.
