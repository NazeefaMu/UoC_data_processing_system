## Title: Horana tragedy:  Case to be called today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Friday, April 27, 2018 - 01:00

Horana tragedy:  Case to be called today

The case regarding the incident at a latex factory in Horana where five people were killed recently, will be taken up today at the Horana Magistrate’s Court.
When the case was called on April 25, counsel for the company told court that the company has agreed to pay Rs. 250,000 to each of the dead persons as compensation on sympathetic grounds.
Horana Police also submitted a further report to court.
Horana Acting Magistrate Chandana Wijesuriya further remanded the company’s factory manager A.K.D.R. Wijesinghe and Chief Chemist Inoka Sangeewani Welikada till today and postponed the case for today, to be called before the permanent magistrate.
