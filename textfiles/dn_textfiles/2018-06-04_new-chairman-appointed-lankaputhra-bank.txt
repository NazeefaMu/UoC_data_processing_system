## Title: New Chairman appointed for Lankaputhra Bank
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, June 4, 2018 - 01:00

New Chairman appointed for Lankaputhra Bank

Senior lawyer J. T. S. P. Kariyawasam, who had held the chairmanship of several state institutions during the past, was appointed Lankaputhra Bank Chairman by the Finance Ministry.
He had obtained a Buisness Administration Degree from the Sri Jayawardenepura University and is currently a senior member of the Sri Lanka Institute of Chartered Accountants.
