## Title: Three Customs Officers remanded
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, October 16, 2015 - 10:45

Three Customs Officers remanded

The three Customs Officers who reportedly accepted a bribe of Rs.125 million has been remanded untill October 30 by the Colombo Chief Magistrate today. The three officers were arrested by the Bribery Commission yesterday for accepting a bribe to free goods worth Rs. 1500 million without customs duties.
