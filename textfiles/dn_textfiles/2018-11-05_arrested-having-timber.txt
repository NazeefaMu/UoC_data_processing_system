## Title: Arrested for having timber
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, November 5, 2018 - 01:00

Arrested for having timber

A suspect who had a large consignment of expensive timber was nabbed by Special Task Force officers attached to the Mullaitivu camp on Saturday.
According to the police, the suspect was arrested on a tip-off received by the STF, which handed over the timber called palu to the Wildlife Range Office in Killinochchi.
The suspect was identified as an owner of a sawing mill in the area. He was later handed over to the Killinochchi Police for legal proceedings.
