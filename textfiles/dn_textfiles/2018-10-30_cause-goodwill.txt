## Title: Cause of goodwill
## Supertitle: 
## Section: T&C
## Date: Tuesday, October 30, 2018 - 01:00

Cause of goodwill

The Ceylon Electricity Board’s Colombo West Customer Services Centre launched its annual social welfare activity by distributing text books, school uniforms, school bags and shoes to the students of Thanthirikanda Primary School in Dehiowita recently. The event was spearheaded by the Electrical Supervisor Ajantha Ranatunga. The zonal director of education also attended.
