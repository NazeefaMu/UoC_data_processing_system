## Title: President participates in religious activities on Adi-Poson Poya
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 30, 2018 - 01:00

President participates in religious activities on Adi-Poson Poya

The Dhamma sermon for Adi-Poson Poya Day in the series ‘Sadaham Yathra Dhamma Sermon’ was held at the Paramartha Dharmakara Piriven Viharasthanaya, Pethiyagoda, Kelaniya, yesterday (29) with the participation of President Maithripala Sirisena.
The Dhamma sermon was delivered by the Ven. Korathota Wessabu Thera.
The President first engaged in religious activities at the temple. He also looked into the well-being of Ven. Dompe Indrasiri Nayaka Thera.
Later, the President joined in listening to the Dhamma sermon. Ministers and MPs including Ministers Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe, Sarath Fonseka, Ranjith Siyambalapitiya, Deputy Minister Lasantha Alagiyawanna, Western Province Governor Hemakumara Nanayakkara, public servants and devotees in the area also participated in this occasion.
Meanwhile, President Sirisena opened the renovated historic Vihara Mandiraya of the Kandyan period and the Vishnu Devalaya at the Paramartha Dharmakara Piriven Viharasthanaya. The President also unveiled the newly built Buddha Mandiraya at the Viharasthanaya.
President Sirisena also opened a new micro solar power unit installed at the temple.
The Maha Sangha, including the Maha Nayaka of Kotte Sri Kalyani Samagri Dharma Maha Sangha Sabha Ven. Dr. Iththepane Dhammalankara Thera and Chancellor of the Kelaniya University, Most Ven. Welamitiyawe Kusala Dhamma Thera participated in this occasion.
