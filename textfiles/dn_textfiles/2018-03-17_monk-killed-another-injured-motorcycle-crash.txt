## Title: Monk killed, another injured in motorcycle crash
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, March 17, 2018 - 01:00

Monk killed, another injured in motorcycle crash

Two monks from the Tissapura, Alokarama Viharaya in Bisobandara, Medirigiriya had met with an accident when the motorcycle they were riding in had veered off the road and crashed into a lamp post in the Bisobandara area in Medirigiriya yesterday.
One of the monks had died in the accident while the other monk had sustained injuries.
The Police said the 16-year-old monk who was killed in the accident is said to have been riding the motorcycle. He has been identified as Medirigiriye Saddhananda. The other 14-year-old monk, identified as Medirigiriye Naradha has been admitted to the Medirigiriya hospital for treatment.
