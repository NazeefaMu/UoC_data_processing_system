## Title: Liyanage puts St. Sylvester’s on top
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, February 12, 2016 - 01:00

Liyanage puts St. Sylvester’s on top

Thenuka Liyanage of St. Sylvester’s College Kandy displayed his all-round prowess when he scored a century and took three wickets against Dharmaraja College Kandy on day one of their inter schools cricket fixture of the Singer Trophy Under 19 tournament at Lake View yesterday.
Scores:
At Lake View Kandy:
St. Sylvester’s: 286 in 81 overs (Thenuka Liyanage 115, Muditha Hashan 56, Rukmal Dissanayake 3/62)
Dharmaraja: 25/4 at close in 12 overs (Thenuka Liyanage 3/14)
At Kandana:
Gurukula: 209 in 59 overs (Jayamal Wanniarachchi 73, Malidu Viduranga 40, Deshan Fernando 5/31, Nikila Geeth 2/20)
De Mazenod: 159/3 at close in 36 overs (Lasith Croospulle 39 n.o., Deshan Fernando 38, Saliya Jude 36 n.o.) - YK
