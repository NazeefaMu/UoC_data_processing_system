## Title: National Dental Teaching Hospital open from 7.00 am to 7.00 pm
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 10, 2017 - 01:00

National Dental Teaching Hospital open from 7.00 am to 7.00 pm

The public can obtain dental services from the newly opened National Dental Teaching Hospital in Colombo from 7.00 am to 7.00 pm, Health Services acting Director General Dr. Jayasundara Bandara said.
Addressing the media at the Health Education Bureau in Colombo on Wednesday, he said treatment is available at the hospital from 7.00 am to 7.00 pm. Twelve doctors work for one shift. Over 30 dentists, community dental surgeons and Oral and Maxillo-Facial Surgeons serve at the hospital.
According to Dr. Bandara, the Colombo South Teaching Hospital (Kalubowila Teaching Hospital) will be developed under a master plan. A new building complex will be established by the year end.
Dr. Bandara said the public can make complaints to hospital directors if they undergo harassment when obtaining services from the health staff.
