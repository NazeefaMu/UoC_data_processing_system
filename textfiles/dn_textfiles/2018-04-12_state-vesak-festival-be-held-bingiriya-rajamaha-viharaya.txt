## Title: State Vesak festival to be held at Bingiriya Rajamaha Viharaya
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 12, 2018 - 01:00

State Vesak festival to be held at Bingiriya Rajamaha Viharaya

The 2562 BC State Vesak festival this year would be held at the Bingiriya Ancient Rajamaha Viharaya in Kurunegala.
The State Vesak week would commence on April 26 and end on May 2, held under the patronage of the Maha Sangha with the participation of President Maithripala Sirisena and Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe.
The festival is jointly organized by the Department of Buddhist Affairs and the Ministry of Buddha Sasana.
