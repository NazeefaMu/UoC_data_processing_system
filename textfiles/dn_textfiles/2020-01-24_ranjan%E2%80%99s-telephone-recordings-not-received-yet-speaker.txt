## Title: Ranjan’s telephone recordings not received yet - Speaker
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, January 24, 2020 - 13:21

Ranjan’s telephone recordings not received yet - Speaker

Speaker Karu Jayasuriya today announced that United National Party (UNP) Parliamentarian Ranjan Ramanayake has not tabled recordings of his phone conversations in Parliament as of yet.He made this observation responding to a question raised in Parliament today.
