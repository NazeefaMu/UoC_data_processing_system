## Title: LG election winning members meet SLMC leader
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Thursday, February 15, 2018 - 01:00

LG election winning members meet SLMC leader

Sri Lanka Muslim Congress party winning members of February 10 Local Government election met their Party Leader and Minister Rauff Hakeem on February 13 at the Slave Island SLMC Head Quarters "Darussalam".
They discussed on the Party progress over the 19 Districts contested where SLMC will be forming an alliance with UNP elephant symbol in some of the areas. Trincomalee district contested using their own party SLMC - Tree symbol.
"Altogether, SLMC obtained 185 winning seats from 19 Districts", said SLMC Leader Rauff Hakeem.
