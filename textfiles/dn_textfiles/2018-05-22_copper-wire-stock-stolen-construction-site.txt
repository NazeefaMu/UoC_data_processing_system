## Title: Copper wire stock stolen from construction site
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, May 22, 2018 - 01:00

Copper wire stock stolen from construction site

A stock of copper wires had been stolen from a storeroom at the Athudawa construction site of the Southern Expressway Extension Project recently.
A complaint in this connection was lodged with the Thihagoda Police by the Chinese manager in charge of the construction site. According to the complaint, the stolen stock of copper wire had been worth nearly Rs. 100,000. Upon conducting initial investigations, police had taken an employee attached to the site into custody. The 42-year-old suspect is a resident of the Deeyagaha area in Naimana, Matara. He was to be produced at the Matara Magistrate’s Court, police officials said.
