## Title: Professional MET Consultancy wins Silver at National Energy Efficiency Awards
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Wednesday, January 11, 2017 - 01:00

Professional MET Consultancy wins Silver at National Energy Efficiency Awards

Professional MET Consultancy Services (PROMET) won the Silver Award in the Technology Providers category of the Best Energy Services Company Award in the Sri Lanka National Energy Efficiency Awards 2016.
The company was awarded this in recognition of their contribution to reduce energy use through energy management efforts. PROMET Eco Care Chairman and Managing Director M. A. Justin received the award from Power and Renewable Energy Minister Ranjith Siyambalapitiya. President Maithripala Sirisena was the chief guest of the ceremony.
PROMET is a listed consultancy firm in the Central Environmental Authority (CEA) for technical guidance on pollution control activities and also a registered energy services company in the Sustainable Energy Authority (SEA). The company is engaged in turnkey projects of biomass/oil fired steam/hot water boilers, themic fluid heaters, waste water,raw water sewerage treatment plants and operation and maintenance of these plants.
PROMET's Chairman and Managing Director, M. A. Justin is a well qualified engineer who gives his enormous contribution in making all the projects company undertakes a great success. PROMET obtained world renowned ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System (QMS) certification. By obtaining ISO 9001:2015 certification for its QMS by the assessing body, Sri Lanka Standards Institution (SLSI), PROMET becomes the first energy services company to receive the ISO accreditation. As future activities, PROMET is planning to introduce new technologies in the relevant field.
Such as, higher efficiency hybrid boilers, wind-solar hybrid small power plants, micro co-generation power plants, tri-generation for green electricity anddevelopment of our own fired wood plantation.
