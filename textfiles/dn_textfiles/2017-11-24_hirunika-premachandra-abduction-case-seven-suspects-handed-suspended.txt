## Title: Hirunika Premachandra Abduction Case: Seven suspects handed suspended sentences
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Friday, November 24, 2017 - 13:53

Hirunika Premachandra Abduction Case: Seven suspects handed suspended sentences

 
Seven suspects in the Hirunika Premachandra abduction case were sentenced to two-years rigorous imprisonment suspended for 12 years by the Colombo High Court today.
At an earlier occasion the seven suspects plead guilty in the case; the eighth suspect, Hirunika Premachandra plead not guilty.
The seven are alleged to have masterminded and conducted the abduction of a 34-year-old youth in Dematogoda in December 2015 using a Land Rover belonging to Premachandra.
They are also charged by the Attorney General on 29 counts including threatening, assaulting and intimidating the victim Amila Priyankara.
