## Title: Mechanic dies in tyre explosion
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, July 23, 2018 - 01:00

Mechanic dies in tyre explosion

A 28-year-old mechanic died when a JCB machine tyre he was inflating exploded at a tyre service centre, Matugama. He was thrown about 10 feet in the explosion, sources said. He succumbed to his injuries while under treatment at hospital.
