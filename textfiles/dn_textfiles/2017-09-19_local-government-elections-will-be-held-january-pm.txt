## Title: Local Government Elections will be held in January: PM
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, September 19, 2017 - 14:15

Local Government Elections will be held in January: PM

 
Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe said that Local Government Elections will be held in January, next year.
The Premier said this in response to a question raised by former President Mahinda Rajapaksa at today’s Parliamentary session in Colombo.
