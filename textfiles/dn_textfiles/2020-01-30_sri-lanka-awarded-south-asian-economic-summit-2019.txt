## Title: Sri Lanka awarded at the South Asian Economic Summit 2019
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, January 30, 2020 - 14:54

Sri Lanka awarded at the South Asian Economic Summit 2019

 
The 16th South Asian Economics Summit (SAES) was held in Kathmandu, Nepal from January 21-26.
 
"Mahbub Ul Haq & Amarty Sen", the award for the South Asia Region's Best Research Paper won by Kanishka Werawella of University of Colombo.
 
This is the second time a Sri Lankan scholar has won this award.
 
Kanchana Geet Samaraweera, Janith Kaluarachchi, Pasan Wijewardene and Nilupuli Ratnayake  won the sub-themes of the conference.
