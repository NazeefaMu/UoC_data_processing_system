## Title: ‘Watadage’ replica soothes Eheliyagoda patients
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, September 29, 2017 - 01:00

‘Watadage’ replica soothes Eheliyagoda patients

The newly built real size replica of the famous 'Watadage' in Eheliyagoda Base Hospital premises, has become a heart-soothing symbol not only for the patients, but also for the other visitors who come daily to the hospital, Sabaragamuwa Chief Minister Mahipala Herath said.
Chief Minister Herath said so at a meeting at the Eheliyagoda Base Hospital new auditorium after opening a new emergency treatment branch two storeyed building, large-sized replica of 'Watadage' and the foundation laying for a new drug store building for the hospital.
The replica of this nature can only be found in this hospital, the Chief Minister said. Prior to the meeting, Puravidya Chakrawarthi Ven. Ellawala Medhananda Thera performed religious rites at the Watadage.
Chief Minister Herath said the secret behind the progress of the hospital is due to praiseworthy administration and the united effort as well as the generous contributions of the donors in the area.
He said: "We could render a yeoman service for the people of the province due to united decisions and mutual understanding between the ruling party and the opposition of the Sabaragamuwa Provincil Council. Our aim was to uplift the living standards of the people."
