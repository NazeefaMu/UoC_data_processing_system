## Title: SP Chief Minister Shan Wijayalal assumes new duties
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, September 19, 2017 - 01:00

SP Chief Minister Shan Wijayalal assumes new duties

Southern Province Chief Minister Shan Wijayalal De Silva, who was sworn in as Sports and Youth Affairs, Rural Development, Cultural and Art Affairs, Social Welfare, Probation and Child Care Services, Women’s Affairs, Home Economic Enhancement, Housing and Constructions, and Manpower and Employment Provincial Minister before Southern Province governor Dr. Hemakumara Nanayakkara, recently, assumed duties of respective ministerial portfolios at the Provincial Ministry yesterday.
The change of the ministerial portfolios was subsequent to the removal of former minister Weerasumana Weerasinghe following a directive of the governor.
The chief minister is also the Provincial Minister of Finance, Planning, Law and Order, Engineering Services, Health, Indigenous Medicine, Local Government, Transport and Tourism.
Soon after the assuming of duties, he arranged the hanging of a photograph of President Maitripala Sirisena on the wall behind his chair and table of the new ministry.
