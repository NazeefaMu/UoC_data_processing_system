## Title: Annual Literary Awards Presentation Ceremony - 2017
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 01:00

Annual Literary Awards Presentation Ceremony - 2017

Kurunegala District UNP Parliamentarian and Internal Affairs, Wayamba Development and Cultural Affairs Minister S. B. Nawinna told the Daily News in Kurunegala that his Ministry has made arrangements to hold the 'Annual Literary Awards Presentation Ceremony - 2017' on September 8 at Nelum Pokuna Theatre Hall, Ananda Kumaraswamy Mawatha, Colombo 07 at 4.30 p.m, yesterday.
It will be conducted under the patronage of President Maithripala Sirisena. All outstanding authors who have contributed their best in creative writing work in Sinhala, Tamil and English mediums are to be felicitated on this occasion. About 1,200 artistes are to be felicitated.
