## Title: Development Lotteries Board Chairman removed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 01:00

Development Lotteries Board Chairman removed

Development Lotteries Board Chairman Romesh Jayawardena has been removed from his post by Finance Minister Mangala Samaraweera.
Minister Samaraweera, through the Extraordinary Gazette Notice No. 2035/2 issued on August 31 and published on Monday, exercised his powers vested in Section 5 of the Development Lotteries Board Act No. 20 of 1997 to remove Jayawardena from his post.
