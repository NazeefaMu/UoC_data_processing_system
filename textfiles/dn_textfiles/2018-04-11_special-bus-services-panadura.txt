## Title: Special bus services from Panadura
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, April 11, 2018 - 01:00

Special bus services from Panadura

Special bus services had been deployed from the Panadura main bus depot with effect from April 9 to 19 during the Avurudu season for the convenience of passengers, on the instructions of Western Province Road Passenger Authority Chairman Thusitha Kularathne. According to the officer in charge of the Panadura Main Bus Depot O. K. Viraj Nanayakkara, buses will be deployed from Panadura to Tangalle, Matara, Bibile, Kataragama, Anuradhapura, Trincomalee, Jaffna, Kurunegala, Ratnapura and other destinations for people to visit their hometowns.
