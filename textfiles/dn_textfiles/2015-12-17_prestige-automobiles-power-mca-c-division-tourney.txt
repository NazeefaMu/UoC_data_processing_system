## Title: Prestige Automobiles power MCA 'C' Division Tourney
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, December 17, 2015 - 01:00

Prestige Automobiles power MCA 'C' Division Tourney

Prestige Automobile (Pvt) Ltd for the fifth Successive year, will sponsor the MCA "C" Division 50-Over League Tournament 2016 and once again will offer the "BMW-Hyundai" Trophy to the Winner.
The tournament will get underway on December 19(Saturday) with the participation of 09 teams, pooled in 02 groups, as follows.
Group "A" : HSBC "A" McLarens Group, Nations Trust Bank "A", JL Morison & Colombo Dockyard PLC (05 teams)
Group "B" : Peoples Leasing and Finance PLC, Airport and Aviation Services, Standard Chartered Bank and PABC Bank (04 teams)
The tournament will be conducted in two stages, the "League" and the "Knock-out", consisting 23 matches.
The League Stage with 16 matches will conclude on January 24,2016 , spread around MCA, D.S. Senanayake College, St. Benedict's College, Wesley College, Colombo Colts and FTZ/Katunayake Grounds. (06 grounds)
At the completion of the above stage, the first 04 teams of each group will advance to the Knock-out stage to play the criss-cross quarter -finals.
The quarter finals will take place on January 30 and 31, 2016 , the Semi-Finals on February 6, 2016.
The 04 special awards on offer are : Best Batsman, Best Bowler, Man-of-the tournament and Man of the Final.
All matches will be supervised by a MCA appointed Match Referee.
In the 2015 Season Tournament, LB Finance "B" lifted the BMW - Hyundai Trophy beating Standard Chartered Bank by 79 runs on the Duckworth-Lewis method.
