## Title: Showers expected in several provinces after 2pm
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, January 24, 2020 - 10:36

Showers expected in several provinces after 2pm

Several spells of showers will occur in Uva, Eastern and North-central provinces and in Matale and Hambanthota districts, said the Department of Meteorology.Showers or thundershowers may occur at a few places in Sabaragamuwa and Western provinces and in Galle, Matara, Kandy and Nuwara-Eliya districts after 2.00 p.m.Wind speed can be increased up to 50 kmph at times in North-western, North-Central, Northern, Central, Eastern and Southern provinces.General public is requested to take adequate precautions to minimize damages caused by temporary localized strong winds and lightning during thundershowers.
