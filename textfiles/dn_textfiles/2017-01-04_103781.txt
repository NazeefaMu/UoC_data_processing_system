## Title: Chinese Defence Attaché meets State Minister
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 4, 2017 - 10:45

Chinese Defence Attaché meets State Minister

 
The new Defence Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Sri Lanka, Senior Colonel Xu Jianwei met with State Minister of Defence Ruwan Wijewardene at the ministry yesterday (3).
Cordial discussions were held between State Minister Wijewardene and the Chinese Defence Attaché on matters of bilateral importance and mutual relevance during the meeting.
The State Minister also presented a memento to the visiting Defence Attaché to mark the occasion.
