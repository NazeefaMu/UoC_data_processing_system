## Title: One dead, one missing in Ganemulla swimming incident
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, March 16, 2017 - 16:15

One dead, one missing in Ganemulla swimming incident

 
A person drowned, and another is missing after swimming in the Ketawlala Canal in Ganemulla today.
 Search and rescue operations are underway.
