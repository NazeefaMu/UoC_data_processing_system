## Title: Singer Cup U17 Division 1 Schools Cricket: Royal qualifies for quarterfinals
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, August 3, 2017 - 01:00

Singer Cup U17 Division 1 Schools Cricket: Royal qualifies for quarterfinals

Royal College Colombo advanced to the quarterfinals of the ‘Singer Trophy’ Under 17 division 1 inter schools cricket tournament yesterday.
Royal beat Sri Sumangala College Panadura by 215 runs in the pre-quarterfinal played at Reid Avenue.
Ahan Wickramasinghe scored 105 runs in 110 balls with 15 fours and Kavindu Madarasinghe scored an unbeaten 104 off 86 balls with 15 fours and 3 sixes.
Royal will meet last year’s runner-up Thurstan College in the quarter final.
Scores:
Royal: 327/4 in 50 overs (Ahan Wickramasinghe 105, Kavindu Chathuranga 104 n.o., Thevindu Senaratna 39, Weijith Wimalasiri 29, Pulindu Mallikarachchi 20 n.o., Tharana Ghandara 2/65)
Sri Sumangala: 112 in 30.5 overs (Tharana Ghandara 35, Pasindu Sahandith 22, Bisandu Ranasinghe 3/23, Harindu Palihawadana 2/10, Manula Perera 2/13, Pulindu Mallikarachchi 2/27) - YK
