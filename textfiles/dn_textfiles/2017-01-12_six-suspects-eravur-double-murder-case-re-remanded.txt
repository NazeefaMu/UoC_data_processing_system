## Title: Six suspects in Eravur double murder case re-remanded
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, January 12, 2017 - 01:00

Six suspects in Eravur double murder case re-remanded

 Six suspects in the Eravur double murder case were re-remanded until January 25 by Batticaloa District Additional Magistrate M.I.M. Rizvi yesterday. The bodies of Noor Mohamed Husaira, 56, and her daughter Yusupe Jeshira Bhanu, 32, were found murded at their house at Mukanthiram Road, Eravur on September 11.
