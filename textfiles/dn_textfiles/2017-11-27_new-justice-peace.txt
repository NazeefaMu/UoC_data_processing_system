## Title: New Justice of Peace
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, November 27, 2017 - 01:00

New Justice of Peace

J. P. Ajith Nihal Jayasundera of No. 21, Janaraja Mawatha, Brandiyamulla, Gampaha, has been appointed as an All-Island Justice of Peace.
Jayasundera, who joined the Judicial Service in 1985, is currently serving as the additional registrar of the Chilaw High Court.
