## Title: Six a side cricket tourney
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, October 21, 2016 - 01:00

Six a side cricket tourney

 
The Lionel Sandanayake Memorial six a side cricket tournament, organised by the Thurunu Tharu Welfare Society, Labugama, Yalagala will be held for the fifth successive year on October 22 and 23 at Vijitha Vidyalaya’s sports ground, Horana Labugama.
