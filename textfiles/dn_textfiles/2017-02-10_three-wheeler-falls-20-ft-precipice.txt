## Title: Three-wheeler falls into 20 ft precipice
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 10, 2017 - 15:00

Three-wheeler falls into 20 ft precipice

 
A three-wheeler had skidded into a twenty feet precipice at Dickoya, Hatton while returning home from a ceremony held at an estate Kovil, earlier today (10).
An individual named S. Rajaratnam (40) had died and two children, a woman and three others were severely injured.
The Hatton Police says the driver cited that the three-wheeler’s breaks had jammed.
The Hatton Police is investigating.
