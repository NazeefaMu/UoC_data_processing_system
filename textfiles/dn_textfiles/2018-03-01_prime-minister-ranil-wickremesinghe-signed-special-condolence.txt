## Title: Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe signed the special condolence
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, March 1, 2018 - 01:01

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe signed the special condolence

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe yesterday signed the special condolence book to mark the death of UN Resident Coordinator and UNDP Resident Representative in Sri Lanka, Ms. Una McCauley at the United Nations Office in Sri Lanka. The Premier extended his condolences to Gita Sabharwal and Nina Brandstrup of the UN Colombo Office.Youth Affairs and Southern Development Minister Sagala Ratnayaka was also present.
