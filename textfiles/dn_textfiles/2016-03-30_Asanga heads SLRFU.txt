## Title: Asanga heads SLRFU
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, March 30, 2016 - 01:00

Asanga heads SLRFU

K. D. S Ruwanchandra, Director General, Department of Sports Development has appointed the following office bearers for the Sri Lanka Rugby Football Union (SLRFU) as there were no other nominations for the posts.
- President
Asanga Seneviratne
- Vice President
Lasitha Gunaratne
- Hony. Secretary
Nazeem Mohamed
- Hony. Treasurer
Abdul Amza
Mohamed Riyaz
-Council Members
Fazal Mohamed, Roshan Deen, Tuan Dole, Brg. Sunil Ranasinghe (Retd.), Iftikar Abdul Wahid, Shamrath Fernando, Jude Pillai.
