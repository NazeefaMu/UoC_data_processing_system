## Title: Rauff Hakeem on campaign trail
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Wednesday, January 17, 2018 - 01:00

Rauff Hakeem on campaign trail

City Planning, Water Supply and Drainage Minister and SLMC Leader Rauff Hakeem addressed a series of public meetings recently in the Akkaraipattu, Sainthamaruthu, Sammanthurai and Palamunai areas to ensure UNP’s victory at the forthcoming local government elections to be held on February 10.
