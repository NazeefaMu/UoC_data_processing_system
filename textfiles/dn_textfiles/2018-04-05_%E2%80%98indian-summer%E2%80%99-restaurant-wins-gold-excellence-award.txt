## Title: ‘Indian Summer’ Restaurant wins Gold Excellence Award
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, April 5, 2018 - 01:00

‘Indian Summer’ Restaurant wins Gold Excellence Award

Fine Dining and Fusion Cuisine Restaurant ‘Indian Summer’ clinched the Gold Award at the Tourism Excellence Awards 2017 awards ceremony organized by the Western Province Tourism Board held recently at the Galadari Hotel.
General Manager, Indian Summer Restaurant, Sharad Bajpal said, “We are constantly on a journey to deliver food that is carefully sourced and thoughtfully served ensuring our patrons experience a memorable dining experience every time. Winning the gold award is testimony of our efforts.”
The Indian Summer Restaurant, located at Horton Place, Colombo 7 offers an inimitable ambience and elegance providing an intimate and relaxed setting assuring its clientele of an evening of grace, refinement, congeniality and a treat for their palates. Indian Summer serves halal food and vegetarian cuisine, and strictly maintains a no-liquor policy.
Indian Summer Restaurants also have branches in Beruwela and Nuwara Eliya.
