## Title: Kandy General Hospital included in e-Health Programme
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, May 12, 2017 - 12:00

Kandy General Hospital included in e-Health Programme

Health Minister Dr. Rajitha Senaratne registers a patient at Kandy General Hospital under the new system.
The Health Ministry said today that Health Minister Rajitha Senaratne has added Kandy General Hospital to the e-Health Programme.
The Ministerial spokesman said a total of 200 more state hospitals will be added to the e-Health Programme this year and forty state hospitals were added to the programme last year.
We expect to add all state hospitals to the network under the programme, the spokesperson said.
He said that the e-Health Programme registers all patients admitted to state hospitals and their medical records in to a computerized system.  
The Health Ministry has set up a National e-Health Steering Committee supported by the WHO, he said. 
Under the guidance of this committee, National e-Health Guidelines formulated and standards have been drafted the spokesperson said.
A unique and specialized workforce has been established to manage health information management he said.
President Maithripala Sirisena inaugurated e-Health Asia 2015 Conference held in Colombo in October 2015, he added.
