## Title: Minister to be chief guest at workforce productivity session
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, June 4, 2018 - 01:00

Minister to be chief guest at workforce productivity session

Science, Technology, Research, Skills Development and Kandyan Heritage Minister Dr. Sarath Amunugama will be the chief guest at a session on strategies for improving workforce productivity in the public sector held at the auditorium of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Sri Lanka on June 7, at 9.00 a.m.
The event will be organised by the National Human Resources Development Council of Sri Lanka (NHRDC), together with the public sector wing of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Sri Lanka (CASL), Association of Human Resources Professional (AHRP) and the International Chamber of Commerce Sri Lanka (ICC SL).
