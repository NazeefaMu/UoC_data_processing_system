## Title: Graceful dining!
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Thursday, April 6, 2017 - 01:00

Graceful dining!

Grace Kitchen, Colombo Hilton's newest theatre of dining is surely the ideal place to experience a world of culinary delights prepared by Hilton's team of multinational chefs. The team is headed by Executive chef Kazi Hassan from Australia and includes Chamalie, Amila, Ranjith and Kusum from Sri Lanka,Yu Min from China,Treeapong from Thailand,Hikaru from Japan,Massmo from Italy and Chandan from India.
