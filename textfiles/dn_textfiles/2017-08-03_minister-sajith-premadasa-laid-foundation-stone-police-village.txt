## Title: Minister Sajith Premadasa laid the foundation stone for a police village
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, August 3, 2017 - 01:00

Minister Sajith Premadasa laid the foundation stone for a police village

 National Housing Development Authority is constructing over 350 Gam Udawa villages island wide under the ‘Samata Sevena’ housing programme. Housing and Construction Minister Sajith Premadasa laid the foundation stone for a police village at Tissapura area, recently. The village is for police officers living without houses in the Hambantota District. Senior police officers also participated. Picture by Ashraff. A. Samad
