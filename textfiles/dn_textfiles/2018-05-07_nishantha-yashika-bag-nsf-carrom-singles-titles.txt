## Title: Nishantha, Yashika bag NSF carrom singles titles
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, May 7, 2018 - 01:00

Nishantha, Yashika bag NSF carrom singles titles

Former world singles champion Nishantha Fernando of Western Province and seasoned slicer Yashika Rahubadda of Sabaragamuwa Province won the men’s and women’s singles carrom titles respectively when the 44th National Sports Festival’s (NSF) carrom events concluded at the Torrington Indoor Stadium yesterday.
Both men’s and women’s doubles titles and women’s team championship were won by Western Province while Uva Province clinched the men’s team title.
