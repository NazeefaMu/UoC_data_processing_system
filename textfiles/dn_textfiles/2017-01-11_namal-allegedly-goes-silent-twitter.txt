## Title: Namal allegedly goes silent on Twitter
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 11, 2017 - 01:00

Namal allegedly goes silent on Twitter

Parliamentarian Namal Rajapaksa who opened a question and answer session on twitter had suddenly gone silent after he was allegedly asked about how he obtained a law degree,it is learnt.
Rajapaksa tweeted that he would accept questions from the public on twitter yesterday morning. Many youths on twitter had reportedly asked him not to mislead the public in Hambantota by trying to grab power by selling innocent people and religion. However, Rajapaksa who was online for several minutes had suddenly disappeared,after he was questioned about his legal qualifications.
