## Title: 28th national Youth Sports Festival opens in Badulla
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, September 23, 2016 - 01:00

28th national Youth Sports Festival opens in Badulla

 
The 28th National Youth Sports Festival began yesterday with the participation of Telecommunication and Infrastructure Minister Harin Fernando at Vincent Dias Stadium Badulla in the presence of 2500 competitors from various parts of the country.
The event was organised by National Youth Services Council, the Sri Lanka Youth Clubs Association and the National Policy and Economic Development Ministry.
The Olympic flame was lit by Chanuka Chaturanga from Central Youth Club Badulla Welimada and Shashika Lakshani Vidya Guruge from Venus Youth Club Badulla.
