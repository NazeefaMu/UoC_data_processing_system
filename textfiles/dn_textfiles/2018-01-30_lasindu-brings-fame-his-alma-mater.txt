## Title: Lasindu brings fame to his Alma Mater
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Tuesday, January 30, 2018 - 01:00

Lasindu brings fame to his Alma Mater

Lasindu Sheran Kumar of Pitipana Maha Vidyalaya, Negombo has established a school record by winning the Silver Medal at the Sri Lanka National Karate Championship held at the Sugathadasa Indoor Stadium recently. He played for 14 - 15 years Male Kumite , Kyu - 52kg event. He has secured the First Runner- up position at the Western Province Karate Championship and the Second Runner- up position at the Gampaha District Karate Championship, winning the Silver Medal and Bronze Medal. He is coached by Sense Mahinda Suwaris.
