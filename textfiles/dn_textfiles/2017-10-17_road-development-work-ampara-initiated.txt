## Title: Road development work at Ampara initiated
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 17, 2017 - 01:00

Road development work at Ampara initiated

City Planning, Water Supply and Drainage Minister and SLMC Leader Rauff Hakeem inaugurated the carpet road development work at Irrakkamam in the Ampara district, recently.Minister Rauff Hakeem, who was the chief guest of the ceremony, laid the foundation stone for the 3km main road development. The project cost the Ministry Rs. 86 million.
Health, Nutrition and Indigenous Medicine Deputy Minister Faizal Cassim, Sports Deputy Minister H. M. M. Harees, Digamadulla District Parliamentarian and Ampara District Development Committee Co-Chairman M. I. M. Mansoor, Former Eastern Province Health Minister A. L. M. Nazeer, former EP provincial councilors, local politicians and many others participated.
