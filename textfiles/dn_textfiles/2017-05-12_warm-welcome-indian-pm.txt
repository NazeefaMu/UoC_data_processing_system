## Title: Warm welcome for indian pm
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, May 12, 2017 - 01:00

Warm welcome for indian pm

Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi arrived in the country yesterday on a two-day visit at the invitation of President Maithripala Sirisena to participate in the UN International Day of Vesak celebrations as the Chief Guest. The Indian Prime Minister was warmly received by his Sri Lankan counterpart Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe on arrival at the Bandaranaike International Airport in Katunayake.
