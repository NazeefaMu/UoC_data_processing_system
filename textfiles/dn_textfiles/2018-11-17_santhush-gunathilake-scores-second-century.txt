## Title: Santhush Gunathilake scores second century
## Supertitle: ‘Singer Cup’ Under 19 Inter Schools Cricket Tournament:
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, November 17, 2018 - 01:00

‘Singer Cup’ Under 19 Inter Schools Cricket Tournament:
Santhush Gunathilake scores second century

Santhush Gunathilake last year’s captain of St. Peter’s College Bamblapitiya scored his second century for this season of the ‘Singer Trophy’ Under 19 inter Schools Cricket tournament played at Bambalapitiya yesterday.
Laksitha Rasanjana of Nalanda Colombo took 7 wickets for 41 runs against De Mazenod College Kandana at Campbell place yesterday.
Pawantha Weerasinghe (101*) and Vinuka Rubasinghe (53*) openers of Mahanana Colombo put on an unbroken 170 runs partnership for first wicket against St. Mary’s Kegalle.
All matches were drawn yesterday.
St. Peter’s Bambalapitiya, Nalanda Colombo, Royal Colombo and S. Thomas’ Mt. Lavinia were leading on first innings against President’s Kotte, De MazenodKandana, Bandaranayake Gampaha and Prince of Wales Moratuwa.
