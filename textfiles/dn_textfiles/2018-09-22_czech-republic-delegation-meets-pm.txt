Czech Republic delegation meets PM

A delegation from the Czech Republic’s Senate headed by Milan Stech met Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe at his office in Parliament yesterday.The delegation included Tomas Grulich and Ludek Jenista of the Senate’s European Affairs Committee. Picture by PM’s Media Division
