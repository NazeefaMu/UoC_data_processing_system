## Title: Acing Chinese
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 01:00

Acing Chinese

A Sri Lanka China Cultural Show and certificate awarding ceremony was held at Gothami Balika Vidyalaya on April 27 to award certificates to Gothami Balika studentsd who have excelled in the Chinese language. Chinese Embassy Cultural Director Lee Thung was the chief guest. National Education Section chief executive Ven Theripaha Medhankara and many others graced the event. Gothami Balika principal Subashini Dematagoda and Chinese language teacher in charge Dharshani Jayatileke were also present.
