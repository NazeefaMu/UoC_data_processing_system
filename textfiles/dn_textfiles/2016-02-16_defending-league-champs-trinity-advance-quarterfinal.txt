## Title: Defending league champs Trinity advance to quarterfinal
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, February 16, 2016 - 01:00

Defending league champs Trinity advance to quarterfinal

 
Defending champions Trinity College Kandy advanced to the quarterfinal of the Singer Trophy Under 19 division 1 inter-schools cricket tournament when they won their pre quarterfinal match against Zahira College Maradana on the first innings at Asgiriya yesterday.
* At Asgiriya:
Trinity won on first innings
Trinity: 367/9 in 90 overs (Shanogeeth Shanmuganathan 71, Tasila Dodanwela 52 n.o., Hasitha Boyagoda 51, Shavindu Amerasinghe 53, Raveen Sayer 31, Isham Samsudeen 3/106, Mohammed Althaf 3/76) and 92/3 decl. in 15 overs (Dilan Jayalath 59 n.o.)
Zahira: 70 in 30.3 overs (Iroshan Lakruwan 16, Rashmika Dilshan 5/26, Duvindu Thilakaratne 3/23) and 99/9 in 36.1 overs (Sajith Sameera 47, Raveen Sayer 6/39)
Mishen Silva hits unbeaten 150 for Sebs
Mishen Silva of St. Sebastian’s Moratuwa scored an unbeaten 150 off 157 balls with 13 fours and 9 sixes against St. Benedict’s in their under 19 schools match played at Kotahena yesterday.
Most of the matches were either curtailed by rain or bad light and ended early.
