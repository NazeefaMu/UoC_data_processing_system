## Title: STEPS awards winners
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Wednesday, January 17, 2018 - 01:00

STEPS awards winners

STEPS Trade Centre Akkaraipattu December raffle draw winners were awarded prizes recently.
In view of this raffle-draw the STEPS Trade Centre gave away gift items to customers, for the purchase of over Rs. 10,000/= from from December 15 2017, to January 5, 2018.
Thirty customers were adjudged winners and received valuable prizes. STEPs Trade Centre Akkaraipattu Managing Director M.B. Thaha Hashim, Director A.L. Mafaith and the staff were present at the event.
