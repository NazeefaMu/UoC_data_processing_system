## Title: Heritage Cricket Group, South Africa play four matches here
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, May 31, 2017 - 01:00

Heritage Cricket Group, South Africa play four matches here

Heritage Cricket Group from South Africa visited Sri Lanka on an invitation of the tour handlers. Infinity Vacations, Mouth Lavinia and played four matches against St Anne’s College, St Anthony’s College, Kandy, Pyramid Lanka Ltd and St Sebastian’s College, Moratuwa.
Chief scores
St Anne’s College – 255 in 47 overs (C Piyatilleke 33, UN Ashok 73, P Sedara 59, K Amerasinghe 37, Armand Bester 3/29, Kelvin Oliver 2/29, Zander Coizer 3/34) beat
Heritage Cricket Group – 204 in 40.5 overs (Kelvin Oliver 21, Janrich Viljoen 50, Arthur Harrison 34, C Madusitha 2/22, C Warnakulasuriya 4/32, K Subasinghe 2/20) by 51 runs
Heritage Cricket Group – 213 in 45.1 overs (Arthru Harrison 38, Karl Roos 54, Dewalyt 21, Pramudith Panditharathne 2/36, Gayan 2/41, Thamashana Abeykoon 2/35)lost to St Anthony’s – 216/4 in 30.1 overs (Rukshan 51, Theekshana 50, Dayan 38, Kamil 33 n/o) by 4 wickets.
Pyramid Lanka Ltd – 245 in 33 overs (Nalin Shehan 113, D Jayakody 21, L Fernando 34, A Dharmasena 24, Josuha Tuck 6/23, Zander Coizer 2/23) beat Heritage Cricket Group – 173 in 30.4 overs (Bendon Swarts 20, Karl Roos 53, Dylan Smith 34, A Warnakulasuriya 2/35, S Lakmal 3/17, L Fernando 3/21, B Paul 2/8) by 72 runs.
St Sebastian’s College – 257 in 48.4 overs (Jasishka Fernando 122, Romesh Perera 44, Dwald Jacobs 2/28, Joel Crapton 2/24) beat Heritage Cricket Group – 174 in 37.1 overs (J Vilijoen 61, Josh Tuck 25, T Vanzyl 24, S Fernando 3/110, S Perera 2/15, B Salgado 2/39) by 83 runs.
