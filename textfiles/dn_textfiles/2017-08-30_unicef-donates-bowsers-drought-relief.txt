## Title: UNICEF donates bowsers for drought relief
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 30, 2017 - 01:00

UNICEF donates bowsers for drought relief

A fleet of four water bowsers were donated by UNICEF to assist the further extension of water supply to people affected by the drought. these bowsers were officially handed over to President Maithripala Sirisena by UNICEF Sri Lanka Representative Tim Sutton at the Presidential Secretariat yesterday.
The President in turn handed over the documents relating to the bowsers to City Planning and Water Supply Minister Rauf Hakeem, Deputy Minister Sudarshani Fernandopulle and Ministry Secretary Sarathchandra Vithana.
