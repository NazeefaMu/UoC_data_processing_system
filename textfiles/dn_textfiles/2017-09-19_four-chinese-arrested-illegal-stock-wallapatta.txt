## Title: Four Chinese arrested with illegal stock of wallapatta
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, September 19, 2017 - 01:00

Four Chinese arrested with illegal stock of wallapatta

Four Chinese men were arrested on Sunday for illegal possession of a stock of wallapatta agarwood, contraband cigarettes and Goda.
Kollupitiya Police arrested them at St. Anthony’s Mawatha, Kollupitiya. The suspects were arrested with 830 grams of wallapatta, 16,200 illegally imported cigarettes and 110 litres of Goda. They were between the ages of 21 and 44.
The arrests were made following a tip-off, police said.
