## Title: Blessings for PM at Kelaniya Rajamaha Viharaya
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, July 24, 2017 - 01:00

Blessings for PM at Kelaniya Rajamaha Viharaya

A Bodhi Pooja Pinkama to invoke blessings on Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe on his completion of 40 years in Parliamentary politics was held yesterday evening at the Kelaniya Rajamaha Viharaya. Prof Maithri Wickramasinghe,UNP’s Kelaniya Chief Organiser Regional Development Minister Field Marshal Sarath Fonseka, Minister Ravi Karunanayake, several Parliamentarians and the public were present. Prime Minister Wickremesinghe participates in a special Kapruka Pooja at the temple. Picture by Roshan Pitipana.
