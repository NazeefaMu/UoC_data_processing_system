## Title: Bangladesh shot out for 84 by India
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, May 31, 2017 - 01:00

Bangladesh shot out for 84 by India

In another warm-up match played at the Oval, India shot out Bangladesh for 84 in 23.5 overs to win by the huge margin of 240 runs.
India batting first notched up 324-7 with Dinesh Kartik (94), Hardik Pandya (80 n.o.) and Sikhar Dhawan (60) making half tons. Then India’s seam bowlers Buvneshwar Kumar and Umesh Yadav rattled the Bangladesh batting sharing six wickets between them.
