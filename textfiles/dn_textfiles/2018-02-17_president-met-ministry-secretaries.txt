## Title: President met Ministry Secretaries
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, February 17, 2018 - 01:00

President met Ministry Secretaries

President Maithripala Sirisena met Ministry Secretaries at the Presidential Secretariat yesterday. President's Secretary Austin Fernando and Cabinet Secretary Sumith Abeysinghe were among those present. Picture by President's Media Division.
