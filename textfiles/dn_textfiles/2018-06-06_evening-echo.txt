## Date: Wednesday, June 6, 2018 - 01:00

Evening  echo

Thalangama Wickramage’s second poetry collection titled ‘Jeevithaya Sundarai’ was launched recently at the Lanka Sabha Primary School auditorium in Thalangama. Wickramage and his son Saman Ravindra handed over the first copy of the book to the All Island Sinhala Poetry Forum secretary poetess G M Ajith Warakapola Irene Wilson and her relatives. All Island Sinhala Poetry Forum president Nugegoda Premadasa and many other poets and poetesses took part in this event.
