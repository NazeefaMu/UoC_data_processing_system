## Title: Eleven Star SC clinch Thavam Trophy
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, June 4, 2016 - 01:00

Eleven Star SC clinch Thavam Trophy

Eleven Star Sports Club, Oluvil beat New Star Sports Club, Addalaichenai in the finals to emerge champions of the ‘Thavam Challenge Trophy-2016’ limited overs cricket tournament conducted by ‘Ithu Unmai’ Media Network at the Sinna Palamunai Super Orchid Play Grounds recently.
This tournament which saw the participance of 28 teams was organized by the New Star Sports Club President and Ithu Unmai Media Network Chairman M.I.M.Riyas.
Eleven Star Sports Club batted first and scored 69 runs for loss of three wickets in their allocated five overs. In reply New Star SC, they scored 45 runs for loss of six wickets.
M.Sifan of Eleven Star SC was adjudged as the Man of the match while A.Mafa of the New Star SC was picked as the Best Bowler of the series .
Eastern Provincial Councilor A.L.Thavam was the chief guest and presented the Thavam Challenge Trophy and cash prize of Rs 5,000 to the winning team captain of Oluvil Eleven Star Sports Club. Runner up New Star SC also recived cash prizes and trophy.
Former Addalaichenai Pradeshiya Sabha Member Thameem Abdeen, Senior Attorney S.M.A.Gafoor, Addalaichenai Ayurvedic District Hospital Medical Superintendent Dr. K. L. M. Nackfer, Principal A.L. Yaseen, Vice Principals B. Muhajireen, A.L. Fathah and New Star SC Advisor M.C. Kuddoos were also present at the occasion.
