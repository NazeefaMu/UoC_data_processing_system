## Title: Kuliyapitiya UC Budget passed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Friday, December 14, 2018 - 01:00

Kuliyapitiya UC Budget passed

The Kuliyapitiya Urban Council (UC) Budget for 2019 was unanimously passed at a recent meeting held at its auditorium. UC Chairman Lakshman Adakari presided over the meeting.
Furthermore, the Kuliyapitiya UC had recently been selected as this year’s best UC in the Kurunegala district in a performance competition held by the North Western Province Local Government Department.
Meanwhile, the Narammala Pradeshiya Sabha (PS) Budget for 2019 was passed with a five-vote majority. Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna councillors voted for the budget, while People’s Liberation Front and United National Party councillors voted against it, Narammala PS Chairman D.M. Sumanasiri told the Daily News.
