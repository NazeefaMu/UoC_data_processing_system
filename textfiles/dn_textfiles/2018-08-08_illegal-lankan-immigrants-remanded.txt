## Title: Illegal Lankan immigrants remanded
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 8, 2018 - 01:00

Illegal Lankan immigrants remanded

Colombo Additional Magistrate Dulani Amarasekara yesterday ordered to remand 21 illegal Sri Lankan immigrants arrested from a trawler sailing to the Reunion Island in France.
The suspects included 19 men and two women. Two were army deserters.
Navy officials of the Fast Attack Craft at the Western Naval Command arrested the suspects, 117 nautical miles off Chilaw. They were handed over to Colombo Harbour police.
The suspects were remanded till August 20.
