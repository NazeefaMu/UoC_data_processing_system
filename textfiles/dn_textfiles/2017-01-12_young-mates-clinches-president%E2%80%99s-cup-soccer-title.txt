## Title: Young Mates clinches President’s Cup soccer title
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, January 12, 2017 - 01:00

Young Mates clinches President’s Cup soccer title

Young Mates Club, Hatton beat Sripadians Sports Club 2-0 in the Hatton Football League President’s Cup 2016 final which was worked off at the Dunbar grounds, Hatton recently.
Thileepan and Faizer scored in the 16th and 64th minutes respectively to grab victory for Young Mates Club in the final. Vijayakumar and Asiri from Young Mates Club were adjudged the Best Goal-keeper and Best Player of the final respectively.
