## Title: CSD opens bakery training school in Seruwila
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 17, 2018 - 01:00

CSD opens bakery training school in Seruwila

The Civil Security Department opened a bakery training school in Seruwila on the directions of the Director General Chandraratne Pallegama, under the supervision of the Director Welfare with the aim of enhancing the trade skills of CSD personnel.
The CSD personnel who successfully complete the bakery training programme will be deployed in various areas where local bakery shops will be established to provide clean and hygienic bakery products such as bread, pastries etc at a reasonable price.The CSD has been engaged in an endeavour to uplift the quality of life of people living in remote villages. The bakery school and outlet established at the Seruwila temple building was declared open by the Director General on Monday.
