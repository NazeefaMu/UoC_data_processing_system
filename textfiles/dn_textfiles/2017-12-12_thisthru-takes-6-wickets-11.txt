## Title: Thisthru takes 6 wickets for 11
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, December 12, 2017 - 01:00

Thisthru takes 6 wickets for 11

A very fine bowling spell of 6 for 11 by Thisthru Fernando helped St. Sebastians College to beat Dharmasoka College by 47 runs on first innings in their inter school under 13 division one second round first match played at Moratuwa.
Batting first Sokians were all out for 49 runs and Sebs replied with 96 runs and at stumps visitors were 144 for 6.
Dharmasoka College 49 (Thithru Fernando 6/11, Yenu Hemachandra 3/11 and 144/6 Senitha Halambage 47, Chanaka Tharanga 31, Ashad Mendis 26, Kaveesha Mendis 20, Sheran Fernando 2/25, Thithru Fernando 2/25.
St. Sebastians 96 Dilakashana Fernando 37, Hiranda Silva 4/14, Thavin Sithupa 3/26.
