## Title: Events to mark Indian Independence
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, August 17, 2017 - 01:00

Events to mark Indian Independence

The Consulate General of India in Hambantota will hold a series of events to mark the 70th anniversary of India’s Independence in Hambantota.
On August 18, a violin concert will be held by Mysore Brothers at St. Mathew’s MV at Deniyaya. On August 19, a lecture cum demonstration by India’s leading percussion maestro Bickram Ghosh, an Oscar award nominee artiste, will be held at the University of Ruhuna.
On August 21, a live in concert by percussion maestro Ghosh and his crew will be held at Hambantota beach park.
Entrance is free for all events.
