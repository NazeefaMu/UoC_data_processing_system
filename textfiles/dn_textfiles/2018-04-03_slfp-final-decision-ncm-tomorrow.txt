## Title: SLFP final decision  on NCM tomorrow
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Tuesday, April 3, 2018 - 01:00

SLFP final decision  on NCM tomorrow

The Sri Lanka Freedom Party (SLFP) is to announce its stance on the No Confidence Motion (NCM) on April 4 morning, Port and Shipping Affairs Deputy Minister Nishantha Muthuhettigama said.
He said SLFP Ministers discussed the issue with the President at length yesterday night and it was decided to have a another meeting on the NCM today evening (3) and tomorrow (4) before taking a final decision.
