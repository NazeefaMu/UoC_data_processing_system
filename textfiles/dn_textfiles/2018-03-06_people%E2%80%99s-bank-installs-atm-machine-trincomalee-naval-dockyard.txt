## Title: People’s Bank installs ATM machine at Trincomalee Naval Dockyard
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Tuesday, March 6, 2018 - 01:00

People’s Bank installs ATM machine at Trincomalee Naval Dockyard

A new Automatic Teller Machine (ATM) of the People's Bank was recently opened by Rear Admiral N.A.N Sarath Senatheera - Commander (Eastern Naval area), at the Trincomalee Naval Dockyard.
Chief Executive Officer and General Manager of People’s Bank N. Vasantha Kumar, Senior Deputy General Manager (Business Banking) Rasitha Gunawardena, Senior Deputy General Manager (Banking Operations) Bonniface Silva, Assistant General Manager (ATM Operations) Krishantha Gunaratne, Regional Manager (Trincomalee) R. Ravikaran, Trincomalee Branch Manager Mehala Subahaharan, People's Bank staff as well as Chief Navy Officers were present at the occasion.
