## Title: Randy Father-In-Law remanded
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 29, 2018 - 01:00

Randy Father-In-Law remanded

A Father-In-Law who had tried to sexually abuse his Daughter-In-Law was arrested by the Kotadeniyawa Police station on Monday.
According to Police, the 65-year-old-suspect who is a permanent resident of Delwagura, Damwalkatuwa in the Kotadeniya Police Division had reportedly tried to sexually abused his 29-year-old widowed Daughter-In-Law who is residing in the suspect’s house.
The suspect was arrested following a complaint lodged by the Daughter-In-Law to the Kotadeniyawa Police.
The suspect who was produced before the Minuwangoda Magistrate’s Courts was remanded.Kotadeniyawa Police are investigating.
