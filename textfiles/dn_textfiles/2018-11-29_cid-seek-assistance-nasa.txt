## Title: CID to seek the assistance of NASA
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 29, 2018 - 16:19

CID to seek the assistance of NASA

CID informed court that they are expecting to seek the assistance of National Aeronautics and Space Administration.
And has informed Additional Magistrate Isuru Neththikumara that they are expecting to obtain satellite videos and images from NASA in order to ascertain the vehicle and persons who followed Wasim Thajudeen’s vehicle prior to his death.
