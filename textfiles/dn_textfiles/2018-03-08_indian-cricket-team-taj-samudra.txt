INDIAN CRICKET TEAM AT THE TAJ SAMUDRA

Indian Cricket Team arrived at the Taj Samudra, Colombo on March 5.The 2018 Nidahas Trophy is a cricket tournament scheduled to be held in Colombo from March 6. The tournament is being played to celebrate Sri Lanka’s 70th year of Independence.
