## Title: Special Ramadan gifts to families at Nintavur
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Tuesday, July 10, 2018 - 01:00

Special Ramadan gifts to families at Nintavur

 People’s Bank, Nintavur branch distributed special Ramadan gifts to families at Nintavur recently. The event was held under the patronage of Peoples Bank, Nintavur branch Manager P.Nasrudeen at the branch premises. Ampara Regional Manager D.M.Kapila Dissanayakka and Ampara Assistant Regional Manager A. Samsudeen were present on this occasion. Picture by: I.L.M.RIZAN, Addalaichenai Central Correspondent
