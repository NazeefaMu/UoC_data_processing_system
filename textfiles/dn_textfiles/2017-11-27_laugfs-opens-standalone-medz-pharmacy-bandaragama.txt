## Title: LAUGFS opens standalone MEDZ Pharmacy in Bandaragama
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Monday, November 27, 2017 - 01:00

LAUGFS opens standalone MEDZ Pharmacy in Bandaragama

Taking a yet another strategic step in its vision to become a multi-faceted force in the country’s healthcare sector, LAUGFS Health and Wellness, a subsidiary of LAUGFS Holdings, recently opened its first standalone pharmacy in Bandaragama.
Well-equipped with high quality pharmaceutical and nutraceutical products together withqualified professionals registered with the Sri Lanka Medical Council, the new pharmacy outlet will add to the MEDZ chain of Pharmacies. In addition to dispensing prescription medication, the outlet will serve as a small scale grocery store for the benefit of residents in the area.
“We are taking numerous strategic steps to become an impactful player in the country’s healthcare sector. As a truly Sri Lankan company, we have strong ambitions to expand our services in the healthcare sector to better serve our local consumers. Our pharmacy chain which was primarily located within LAUGFS Supermarkets will be further expanded with a network of standalone pharmacy outlets,” commented LAUGFS Health and Wellness Chief Operating Officer, Sujeewa Goonetilleka.

The MEDZ Pharmacy and Wellness outletin Bandaragama.
Currently, LAUGFS Health and Wellness runs 21 pharmacy units at LAUGFS Supermarket premises throughout Sri Lanka. The chain will expand further with new MEDZ pharmacies located together with LAUGFS Supermarkets and two additional standalone outlets during the financial year.
LAUGFS has already set specific investments in motion to reinforce its presence in the healthcare sector and plans are well in place for aggressive expansion by the end of the current financial year.
LAUGFS is one of the largest diversified business conglomerates and a trusted name in Sri Lanka. Founded in 1995, LAUGFS today has expanded across 20 industries in Sri Lanka and overseas, establishing a strong presence as a leader and pioneer in the power and energy, retail, industrial, services, leisure, logistics and real estate sectors. With over 4,000 employees and an annual turnover exceeding Rs.28 billion, LAUGFS continues to expand and empower millions as a trusted Sri Lankan brand.
