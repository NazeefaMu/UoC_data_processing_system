## Title: Conference to decide first day of Jumada al-Oola
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 17, 2018 - 01:00

Conference to decide first day of Jumada al-Oola

A conference to decide the first day of the month of Jumada al-Oola 1439 H, chaired by Colombo Grand Mosque Hilal Committee Chairman Moulavi J. Abdul Hameed (Bahji), would be held on January 18 at the Colombo Grand Mosque premises, immediately after Salaathul Maghrib.
