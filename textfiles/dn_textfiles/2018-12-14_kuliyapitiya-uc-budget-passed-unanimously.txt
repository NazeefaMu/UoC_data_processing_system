## Title: Kuliyapitiya UC Budget passed unanimously
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Friday, December 14, 2018 - 01:00

Kuliyapitiya UC Budget passed unanimously

The Kuliyapitiya Urban Council(UC) Budget proposal for 2019 was passed unanimously at a meeting held at the UC auditorium recently. UC Chairman Lakshman Adhikari(UNP) presided over the meeting.
The Kuliyapitiya UC became the 'best UC of the year' in the Kurunegla district at a performance competition organised by the North Western Province Local Government Department recently.
