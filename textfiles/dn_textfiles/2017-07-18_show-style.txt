## Date: Tuesday, July 18, 2017 - 01:00

Show of style!

The International Graduate Fashion Spectacle organized by The French embassy, LIFT and Mod’Art International saw some stunning designs on the ramp at the Ballroom of Hilton Colombo. This event brought together fashion graduates from across the world to celebrate their talent and success by representing their nation on one platform.
