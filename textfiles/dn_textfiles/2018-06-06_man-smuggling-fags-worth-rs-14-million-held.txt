## Title: Man smuggling fags worth Rs. 1.4 million held
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 6, 2018 - 01:00

Man smuggling fags worth Rs. 1.4 million held

Customs officials attached to the Bandaranaike International Airport apprehended a Sri Lankan male who was trying to smuggle 120 cartons and 398 packets of foreign cigarettes into the country, yesterday.
The cartons and packets contained 29,960 cigarettes worth Rs. 1,498,000.
The suspect arrived at the green channel arrival terminal by flight FZ 551.
The detention was made by the Superintendent and Assistant Superintendent of Customs. Further investigations are carried out under the guidance of the Customs Director.
