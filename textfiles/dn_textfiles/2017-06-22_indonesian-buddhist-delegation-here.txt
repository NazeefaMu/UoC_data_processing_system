## Title: Indonesian Buddhist delegation here
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 22, 2017 - 01:00

Indonesian Buddhist delegation here

An Indonesian Buddhist delegation led by Most Ven. Phrakhru Vinetsasanakid Sophit Chatchawan
Srisuk Maha Thera, the Chief Monk of the Vihara Dhamma Meththa Arama, Pekanbaru arrived in Sri Lanka yesterday. The pictures show Ven. Yatamalagala Sumanasara Thera , the Executive Director of Dhamma Voice International Buddhist Centre, Pattivila Road, Gonawala, Kelaniya and Advisor to the Qing Liang in Singapore, welcoming the delegates at the Bandaranaike International Airport,
Katunayake and presenting a Karanduwa (casket) to the Chief Monk of Vihara Dhamma Meththa
Arama. The Indonesian Buddhist delegation will visit the Atamasthaana in Anuradhapura, the Sri Dalada Maligawa in Kandy and several other religious sites of histocal importance.
Picture by Katunayake Airport Correspondent Kumarasiri Prasad
