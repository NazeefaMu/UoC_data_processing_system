## Title: UN ready to support SL
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 1, 2017 - 01:00

UN ready to support SL

UN Secretary General Antonio Guterres is deeply concerned by the devastating impact caused by Cyclone Mora on Sri Lanka and Bangladesh, spokesman Stephane Dujarric said in a statement.
The United Nations stands ready to scale up its support to the government-led response efforts in both countries”, the statement added.
