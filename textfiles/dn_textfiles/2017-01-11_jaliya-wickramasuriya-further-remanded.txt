## Title: Jaliya Wickramasuriya further remanded
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Wednesday, January 11, 2017 - 15:00

Jaliya Wickramasuriya further remanded

 
Fort Magistrate this morning remanded former Ambassador to US Jaliya Wickramasuriya till January 13.
Wickramasuriya was arrested by the Financial Investigation Division (FCID) on November 17, 2016.
