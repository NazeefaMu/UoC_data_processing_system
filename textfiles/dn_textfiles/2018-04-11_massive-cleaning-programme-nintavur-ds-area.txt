## Title: Massive cleaning programme in Nintavur DS area
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, April 11, 2018 - 01:00

Massive cleaning programme in Nintavur DS area

The Nintavur Predeshiya Saba has launched a massive cleaning programme in the Nintavur Divisional Secretariat area recently as the environment in and around of Nintavur is being polluted by dumping garbage at some busy places in the village.
The Newly Elected Chairman M. A. M. Thahir took immediate action following the complaints received from the residents of those areas where garbage were carelessly dumped.
