## Title: Four die, 19 injured in bus plunge
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, November 30, 2018 - 01:03

Four die, 19 injured in bus plunge

Four passengers were killed and 19 injured when a luxury bus plying from Jaffna to Colombo plunged into the Hamilton Canal around 3.10 last evening.
Police media spokesman SP Ruwan Gunasekara said the accident had occurred in the Nattandiya, Walahapitiya area in Marawila.
The injured were admitted to the Marawila Hospital for treatment.
Marawila Police are investigating.
