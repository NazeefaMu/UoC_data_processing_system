## Title: Sampanthan meets President Sirisena
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, April 3, 2018 - 14:11

Sampanthan meets President Sirisena

 
Opposition Leader R. Sampanthan together with Tamil National Alliance (TNA) party members met President Maithripala Sirisea this morning.
At the meeting the Opposition Leader said that a final decision is yet to be made by the party regarding the No Confidence Motion filed against Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe.
Furthermore Sampanthan said that their party decision will be informed to President by this evening.
