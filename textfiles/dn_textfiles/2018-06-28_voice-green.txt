## Title: Voice  of Green
## Supertitle: 
## Section: T&C
## Date: Thursday, June 28, 2018 - 01:00

Voice  of Green

Isipathana College will present Voice of Green 2018 (Live in Concert) and Art of Green (Art Exhibition) on June 28 at the BMICH, main auditorium. School show of Voice of Green will be held at 2.30pm prior to the public show which will be held at 6.30pm.
The musical extravaganza will be backed by College Amateur Orchestra consisting of around 200 members between the ages of 8 to 18. The art exhibition will be held from 12pm to 8pm at the same venue. The Chief Guests of the event are former music teachers of the college Ananda Weerasiri, Lakshman Wijesekera and Keerthi Pasquel.
