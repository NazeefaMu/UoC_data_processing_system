## Title: Schools in Gampaha and Southern Province reopen
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 30, 2018 - 11:59

Schools in Gampaha and Southern Province reopen

 
The Southern Provincial Education Ministry said that schools which were temporarily closed due to the spread of influenza epidemic were re-opened today.
Secretary to the Southern Provincial Education Ministry Y. Wickremesinghe said accordingly, activities at primary and secondary sections will commence.
Schools in Gampaha district, which were closed down due to the adverse weather condition, will also reopen from today; the Western Province Chief Minister Isura Devapriya.Chairperson of the Government Midwifery Services Association said, adding that the public who were affected by the floods should pay more attention to sanitation.
The Provincial Ministry decided to close schools on May 22 due to the viral fever, following a request made by parents and health divisions.
Secretary Wickremesinghe further noted that all pre-schools in the province will be closed till this weekend.
