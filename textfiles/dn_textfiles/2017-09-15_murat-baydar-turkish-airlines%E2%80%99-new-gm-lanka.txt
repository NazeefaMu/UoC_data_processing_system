## Title: Murat Baydar is Turkish  Airlines’ new GM in Lanka
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Friday, September 15, 2017 - 01:00

Murat Baydar is Turkish  Airlines’ new GM in Lanka

Murat Baydar is the new General Manager for Turkish Airlines in Sri Lanka. He assumed duties from September 1.
After working in multiple departments in Turkish Airlines in many countries, Baydar has a wealth of experience. He was General Manager in Salzburg, Austria, from 2013 to 2017.
He joined Turkish Airlines in 2008. Baydar holds an MBA from Istanbul Kultur University.
Turkish Airlines has added more value to Sri Lanka Tourism due to its vast network of 300 destinations around the globe. Awarded ‘The Best Airline’ in four categories at the Skytrax Passengers Choice Awards 2017, Turkish Airlines has given the Sri Lankan market an opportunity to choose flying with an award winning European carrier and experience quality air travel.
