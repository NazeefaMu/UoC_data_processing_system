## Title: Suspect with pistol nabbed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 29, 2018 - 01:00

Suspect with pistol nabbed

A suspect who kept a locally made pistol in his possession was nabbed by the Pannala police last Saturday.
Police media spokesman’s office said the 37 year old suspect was arrested on a tip off received by police in the Madiriwila area in the Pannala Police Division on Saturday. The suspect Lokuge Lassantha Pradeep (37) alias Tangalle Suddha is from Polwatta, Horana.
The suspect a soldier for sometime had already left the forces few years back. The suspect was produced before the Kuliyapitiya Magistrate’s courts in yesterday.
