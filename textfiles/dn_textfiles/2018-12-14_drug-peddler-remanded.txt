## Title: Drug-peddler remanded
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Friday, December 14, 2018 - 01:00

Drug-peddler remanded

Panadura Additional Magistrate Kalhari Liyanage remanded a suspect arrested for transporting heroin on Tuesday.
The accused had been arrested by Panadura North Police while he was transporting 22 milligrams of heroin by three-wheeler.
