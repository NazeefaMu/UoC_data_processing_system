## Title: Gold jewelry worth Rs. 4.5 million nabbed at BIA
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 9, 2018 - 11:55

Gold jewelry worth Rs. 4.5 million nabbed at BIA

 
Gold jewelry worth Rs. 4.5 million was nabbed by the Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA) Narcotics Breuer from a Sri Lankan woman this morning.
The alleged woman was caught while attempting to smuggle in the gold from Dubai.
The nabbed gold jewelry consists of 12 bangles and 3 necklaces.
The suspected woman was directed to the BIA Customs for further investigations.
