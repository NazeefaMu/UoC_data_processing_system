## Title: Media persons subjected to harassment: Cabinet Sub Committee open for appeals and submissions
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 13, 2017 - 01:00

Media persons subjected to harassment: Cabinet Sub Committee open for appeals and submissions

The Cabinet Sub Committee on granting measures of relief to media persons who were subjected to harassment and oppression during the period 2005 - 2015 has decided to expand its recommendations. The committee will provide another opportunity for persons and organisations unable to make appeals and submissions to the committee.
The Sub Committee has decided to call for appeals and submissions on journalists and media workers those who have been murdered, attacked and physically harassed during the period.
The Sub Committee requests to forward the appeals and submissions to the Director General of Government Information (Secretary to the Cabinet Subcommittee), Department of Government Information, No. 163, Kirulapona Avenue, Colombo 5, fax - 011 - 2 514 753 or email to [email protected].
