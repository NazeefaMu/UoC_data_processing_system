## Title: Alex Lazarus makes his final journey today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, October 30, 2018 - 01:00

Alex Lazarus makes his final journey today

Today is the final journey for former rugby player and coach Alex S Lazarus who died on Friday at the age of 71. His burial ceremony will be held at his home town public cemetery Mahiyawa at 5.30 pm.
His remains lie at the funeral parlor for the public to pay their last respect and will be brought to the Trinity College chapel in the morning. Religious service will take place at 4 pm.
Lazarus who represented his school and Kandy SC at rugby was also one time curator of the Asgiriya International Cricket Stadium.
