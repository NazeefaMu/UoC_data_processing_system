## Title: Preparing for Vesak
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 01:00

Preparing for Vesak

A myriad of colourful decorations are coming up in the city with Sri Lanka preparing to celebrate the United Nations Day of Vesak for the first time in the country. Here, trainees at the National Police Academy are decorating the road leading to Gangaramaya Temple in Colombo while contributing to the elaborate arrangements. Picture by Saman Sri Wedage
