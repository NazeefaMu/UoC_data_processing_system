## Title: Little swimmers celebrate independence
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, April 5, 2018 - 01:00

Little swimmers celebrate independence

A swimming meet organized by the Thomas Gall International School to commemorate 70th independence anniversary was held in the college swimming pool complex recently. The swimming meet is a part of schools’ array of events to celebrate country’s Independence. School Principal Jessicca Sans, teachers and students were also present on the occasion.
