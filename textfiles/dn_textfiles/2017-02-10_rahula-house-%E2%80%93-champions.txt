## Title: Rahula House – champions
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, February 10, 2017 - 01:00

Rahula House – champions

Rahula House emerged champions at the inter-house sports meet of Daranagama Maha Vidyalaya recently. Here the Western Provincial Council member Gunasiri Jayanath presenting a Trophy to the skipper of Victorious House. Principal L.S. Jayathilake Tasma group chairman Nimal Subasinghe, Biyagama former PS members Nalin Senaka Dissanayake and U.V. Pathama Kumara are also seen in the picture.
