## Title: Appointments for graduates
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, June 17, 2017 - 01:00

Appointments for graduates

The Eastern Province Education Ministry appointed 259 graduate teachers from the Ampara, Batticaloa and Trincomalee districts. The new teachers were presented appointment letters at a ceremony at the Orr’s Hill Vivekananda College Auditorium, Trincomalee recently.
