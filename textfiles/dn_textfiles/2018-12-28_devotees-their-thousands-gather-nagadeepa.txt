## Title: Devotees in their thousands gather at Nagadeepa
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 28, 2018 - 01:00

Devotees in their thousands gather at Nagadeepa

A large number of devotees visited Nagadeepa in Jaffna on last weekend.
Buddhist and Hindu devotees engaged in religious observations amidst rains. During the long weekend, people from many parts of the country visited religious places in Jaffna despite heavy rains, police said.
