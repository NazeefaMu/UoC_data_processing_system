## Title: Kalutara prison bus shooting: Two more arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 10:45

Kalutara prison bus shooting: Two more arrested

 
Colombo Crimes Investigation Division arrested two more suspects last evening from Athurugiriya for being involved in the Kalutara prison bus shooting.
The suspects are residents in Athurugiriya.
On February 27, a prison bus carrying prisoners to the Kalutara Magistrate Court was ambushed.
Seven were killed in the attack, including two prison officials.
