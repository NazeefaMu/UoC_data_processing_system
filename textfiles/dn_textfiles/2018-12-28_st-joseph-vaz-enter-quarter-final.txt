## Title: St. Joseph Vaz enter quarter-final
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, December 28, 2018 - 01:00

St. Joseph Vaz enter quarter-final

St. Joseph Vaz College, Wennappuwa qualified for the Pre-Quarter finals of the All Island Inter Schools Under 13 Division One Cricket Tournament conducted by Sri Lanka Schools Cricket Association for the Singer Trophy.
The significant event is Vazians entered the pre-quarters last year too and in the second round they were unbeaten and they met St. Sylvesters, Isipathana P de S Kularathne Vidyalaya and Wesley. Out of four games they won two and two were no decisions.
They are captained by Navindu Rashmika Wijesuriya and Merusha Perera is his deputy. Kaveesh Ranatunge and Inesh Dulanjana are the top run getters with 218 and 217 runs respectively while Dasun Perera is the highest wicket taken with 28 scalps. Their last game against Wesley College ended in a no decision.
Chief Scores
St. Joseph Vaz College – 174/7 – Kaveesh Ranatunge 60, Vimukthi Fernando 39, Dinal Subasinghe 6/46.
Wesley College – 95/5 – Yazir Afdil 47 N/O, Dasun Perera 2/18.
