## Title: Chocolate Lavariya with flavored custard sauce

Chocolate Lavariya with flavored custard sauce

Ingredients for chocolate lavariya
White rice flour 400g
Coco powder 20g
Salt 1tbls (5G)
Warm water 175 ml (or as needed)
Sift the flour and coco powder into a wide bowl.
Add the salt to the warm water and then gradually add
the warm water to the flour and work into soft dough.
Ingredients for Coconut  and dates stuffing
Fresh grated Coconut 150g
Cardamom pods, bruised 02 No
Seedless dates (cut small pieces) 20g
Cinnamon Sticks
Grated jiggery 75g
To make the pani pol, place the Grated Jiggery and all the spices and dates in a saucepan and stir over medium heat for 5 minutes or until all the jiggery has dissolved. Don’t cook it too long as Jiggery will caramelize and it will set too hard. Add the coconut and stir till all the coconut is coated with the Melted Jiggery mix and the coconut is warm
Making Chocolate Lavariya
* Place the dough in a string hopper mold and
press the both handle to squeeze onto banana leaf.
* Place one teaspoon of the coconut and dates mixture
in the center of the string hopper and fold the
banana leaf.
* Press the edges to seal properly. And transfer
lavariya to string hopper mat
* Steamed folded lavariyafor 10 minutes
Ingredients For lemongrass flavored custard sauce
1 cup milk
1 egg yolk
2 tablespoons sugar
1/4 teaspoon cornstarch
1/2vanilla beans ,
1 crushed lemongrass bottom
Directions
1. Bring milk, vanilla Beans, sugar and lemongrass bottom to a simmer in a saucepan without stirringfor five minutes. Remove vanilla &lemongrass,whiskeggyolks in a bowl until blended, then gradually whisk in hot milk mixture. Return to saucepan and cook over moderate heat, stirring constantly, until sauce thickens enough to coat the back of a wooden spoon.
2. Immediately pour sauce through a fine sieve into a metal bowl and place the bowl in a large bowl of ice and cold water. Stir in until cool.
3. Chill in the refrigerator.
Note -Best served warm
