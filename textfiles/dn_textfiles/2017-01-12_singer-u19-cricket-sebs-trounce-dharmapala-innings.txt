## Date: Thursday, January 12, 2017 - 01:00

Singer U19 Cricket  : Sebs trounce Dharmapala by an innings

St. Sebastian’s College Moratuwa beat Dharmapala College Pannipitiya by an innings and six runs in the Singer Cup under 19 inter-schools cricket match concluded at Moratuwa yesterday.
There were also wins for St. Anthony’s College Kandy who beat Karandeniya Central College, Rajasinghe Central College Hanwella beat Sri Rahula National School Kandy and St. John’s College beat St. Thomas College Kotte. The rest of the matches were all drawn.
Nishan Madhushanka of Moratu Vidyalaya scored 178 off 272 balls with 15 fours and 6 sixes against Zahira College Colombo and left-arm leg-spinner Rashan Fernando took 11 wickets for 65 runs.
