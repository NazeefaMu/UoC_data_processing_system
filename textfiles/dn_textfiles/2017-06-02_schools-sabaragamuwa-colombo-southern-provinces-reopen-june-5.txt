## Title: Schools in Sabaragamuwa, Colombo, Southern provinces to reopen on June 5
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, June 2, 2017 - 16:30

Schools in Sabaragamuwa, Colombo, Southern provinces to reopen on June 5

 
The Sabaragamuwa Provincial Education Secretary said today that schools in Sabaragamuwa province will reopen on June 5.
Fourteen schools will be reopened on June 6 in Nivithigala.
Schools in Southern and Western province will also reopen on June 5, except for thirty –nine schools which were damaged and are utilized for flood relief work in the Southern province.
