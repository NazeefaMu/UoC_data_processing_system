## Title: Devapathiraja v Madampa MMV : First Battle of the Brothers today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, April 1, 2017 - 01:00

Devapathiraja v Madampa MMV : First Battle of the Brothers today

Devapathiraja College Rathgama will meet Madampa MMV both schools from Ambalangoda Education zonal in the first Battle of the Brothers annual cricket encounter for the Sir Earnest de Silva Memorial Challenge Trophy at Rathgama public ground today and tomorrow.
Raveen Yasas will lead Devapathiraja and Tharusha Dilshan Madampa MMV. – (YK)
