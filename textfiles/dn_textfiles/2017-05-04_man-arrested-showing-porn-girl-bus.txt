## Title: Man arrested for showing porn to girl in bus
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 4, 2017 - 01:00

Man arrested for showing porn to girl in bus

A 26-year-old married man resident at Devalahandiya was arrested by Ududumbara Police for allegedly showing a pornographic video on his mobile phone to an underaged girl travelling home by bus after school.
The suspect produced before the Theldeniya Acting Magistrate Shiromi Prabodhika Niyagama was severely warned and remanded till May 8.
Police investigations are continuing.
