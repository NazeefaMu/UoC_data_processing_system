## Title: INS Trikand leaves Colombo harbour
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, July 10, 2018 - 01:00

INS Trikand leaves Colombo harbour

The Indian Naval Ship INS Trikand which arrived in Sri Lanka on July 7 on a goodwill visit, left the Colombo harbour yesterday after a successful tour. The Sri Lanka Navy bade farewell to the ship in compliance with naval traditions, at the harbour premises.
