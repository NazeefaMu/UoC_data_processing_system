## Title: One killed in STF, criminal gang shootout
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 24, 2017 - 01:00

One killed in STF, criminal gang shootout

One person was killed and seven others were arrested following a shootout between a team of Special Task Force (STF) Police personnel and a suspected criminal gang at Henegedara,in the Divulapitiya Police Division yesterday.
The STF, after receiving a tip off about the presence of an armed gang in Henegedara, Doonagaha Ballapana road were conducting a search operation in the area when the search party was shot at by a member of the gang using a pistol, Police Media sources said.
When the STF team countered the attack, one of the criminals in the group was injured and taken to the Divulapitiya hospital. He later succumbed to his injuries. The STF team took seven persons into custody at the scene and seized two pistols, a plastic pistol and a hand grenade from their possession.
