## Title: Administrative Division overall champions
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, February 20, 2016 - 01:00

Administrative Division overall champions

Tha Administrative Division of the Akkaraipattu Zonal Education Office became overall champions of the annual sports festival held at the Beach Grounds, Akkaraipattu recently.
This sports festival was held under the guidance and supervision of Zonal Education Director A.L.M.Cassim. Deputy Education Directors A.M.Rahmathullah, A.H.M.Fous, the Accountant K.Rizvi Yahzer, Administrative Officer A.L.Nagoor Thamby and staff all participated.
The Akkaraipattu Educational Zone Physical Education Assistant Director S.M.Jameel and Physical Education In- Service Advisor M. H. Hammath were served as the referees for all games.
The positions are as follows
1st place Administrative division - 75 points, 2nd place Finance division - 70 points, 3rd place Works and Development divisions -68 points, 4th place Planning division - 66 points.
