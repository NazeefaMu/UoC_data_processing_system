## Title: Nabbed with  cannabis plants
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, April 27, 2018 - 01:00

Nabbed with  cannabis plants

An individual who had cannabis plants within his home premises in Kinniya, was arrested around 11.30 pm on Wednesday. According to police, the suspect, 23, had grown three cannabis trees. The suspect was handed over to Kinniya Police by the Trincomalee Regional Narcotics Division. Investigations are ongoing.
