## Title: Fertiliser for 3,784 farmers
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 14, 2018 - 01:00

Fertiliser for 3,784 farmers

The Ganewatta Agriculture and Agrarian Department in Hiriyala would soon distribute fertiliser for 3,784 farmers under a government project.
Under the project, 87 percent of paddy lands in the Hiriyala electorate would cultivated during the upcoming Maha Season.
In addition to this, the Agrarian Department has been provided with seeds and other necessities as well.
