## Title: Iraq celebrates victory over ISIS
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, July 15, 2017 - 01:00

Iraq celebrates victory over ISIS

The Iraq Embassy in Colombo opened a book from July 12 to 14, 2017 to mark the victory achieved by the Iraqi forces over the ISIS.
The federal police and the national mobilization liberated the City of Mosul from ISIS terrorists.
Guests from the diplomatic corps, Government bodies and well wishers expressed their views to mark this victory in a special book at the embassy in Colombo. Iraq embassy’s head of mission Ahmed Hameed Mohammed and embassy officials were present.
