## Title: Two arrested for allegedly providing vehicle for Prison bus attack
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, April 1, 2017 - 01:00

Two arrested for allegedly providing vehicle for Prison bus attack

Two arrested for allegedly providing vehicle for Prison bus attack Peliyagoda Crimes Division yesterday arrested two persons who have allegedly provided the vehicle for the underworld gangsters to carry out the attack on prison bus at Ethanamadala.
The police have revealed that these two suspects have fixed forged number plates to the van and handed over to the underworld gangsters.However, they have removed the forged number plates after carrying the attack on prison bus which claimed seven lives including two prisons officials.
