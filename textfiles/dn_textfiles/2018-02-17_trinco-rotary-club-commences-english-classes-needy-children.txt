## Title: Trinco Rotary Club commences English classes for needy children
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, February 17, 2018 - 01:00

Trinco Rotary Club commences English classes for needy children

The Rotary Club of Trincomalee started special primary English classes to schoolchildren in the Pallikudiiruppu Kalaimakal Hindu College in the Muthur East Area.
The programme was started with 107 schoolchildren for six months. Rotarians Jayaratnam, Thirumugam, President Elect Rev.Father Laxman Peris and Dr. Gnanakunalan attended and spoke about the programme. The Rotary Club of Arlesheim of Switzerland funded the programme.
