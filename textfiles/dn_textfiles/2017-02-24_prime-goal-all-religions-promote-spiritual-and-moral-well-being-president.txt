## Title: Prime goal of all religions to promote spiritual and moral well-being: President
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 24, 2017 - 17:15

Prime goal of all religions to promote spiritual and moral well-being: President

 
President Sirisena, in a statement today (24) said: ‘The prime goal of all religions is to promote spiritual and moral well-being of human kind’.
Promoting religious way of life, he said that ‘spiritual awakening can be considered as an essential ingredient for building a better society’.
President said that he wish that all Hindu devotees will achieve their aspirations by paying gratitude to Lord Shiva at this Maha Shivarathri celebration, at the end.
