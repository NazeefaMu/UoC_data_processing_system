## Title: Two injured in Periyakallar accident
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Sunday, March 5, 2017 - 10:15

Two injured in Periyakallar accident

Two motorcyclists were injured when a car spun out of control and crashed into a boundary wall in Periyakallar in the Batticaloa district.
The driver of the car sustained minor injuries.
The two injured motorcyclist were taken to the Kaluwanchikudy Base Hospital.
Kaluwanchikudy Police is investigating.
