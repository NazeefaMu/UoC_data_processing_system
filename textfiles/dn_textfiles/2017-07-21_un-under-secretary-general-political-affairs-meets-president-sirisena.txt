## Title: UN Under-Secretary- General for Political Affairs meets President Sirisena
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, July 21, 2017 - 16:45

UN Under-Secretary- General for Political Affairs meets President Sirisena

 
UN Under-Secretary- General for Political Affairs Jeffrey Feltman held discussions with President Maithripala Sirisena today.
Earlier today the UN Under-Secretary- General met with Opposition party Leader Sampanthan and party members.
Jeffrey Feltman is on a three day visit which commenced on July 19, to hold discussions with party leaders on the progress of peace building in Sri Lanka.
