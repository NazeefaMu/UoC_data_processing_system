## Title: Arundika Fernando crosses over to Joint Opposition
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, September 19, 2017 - 14:30

Arundika Fernando crosses over to Joint Opposition

 
UPFA Parliamentarian Arundika Fernando crossed over to the Joint Opposition (JO) today after making a statement at the Parliament.
He was removed from his Ministerial portfolios as Deputy Minister of Tourism Development and Christian Religious Affairs by President Maithripala Sirisena on September 12.
