## Title: New SLFP District  and Electorate Organizers appointed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 26, 2018 - 17:36

New SLFP District  and Electorate Organizers appointed

 
Ajith Basnayake was appointed as the organizer of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party (SLFP) for Gampaha Electorate and he received his appointment letter from President Maithripala Sirisena at the President’s Official Residence today.
Chaminda Kumara Sudusinghe was also appointed as the District organizer of the SLFP for Colombo District.
He too received his appointment letter from the President Maithripala Sirisena at the President’s Official Residence today.
