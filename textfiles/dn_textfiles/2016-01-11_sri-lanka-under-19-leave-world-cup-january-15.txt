## Title: Sri Lanka Under 19 to leave for World Cup on January 15
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, January 11, 2016 - 01:00

Sri Lanka Under 19 to leave for World Cup on January 15

Sri Lanka Under 19 Team captained by Charith Asalanka will leave the Island on January 15 for the month long Under 19 World Cup which will be held in Bangladesh.
The junior Lions boasts of talented players of the calibre of Avishka Fernando, Charith Asalanka, Jehan Daniel and Sammu Ashan and given the similar conditions in Bangladesh will be a threatening force if they perform to their full potential.
The young Lankans have been consistent performers during the last year coming into the finals before losing out to India in the recently concluded U 19 Triangular tournament.Charana Nanayakkara of Thurstan College has been the only addition to the squad, as he was not part of the recent Triangular series.
Sri Lanka are in Group B of the tournament's league stage, alongside Canada, Afghanistan and Pakistan, and will play their first match on January 28, against Canada.
Sri Lanka Under 19 squad: Kaveen Bandara, Salindu Ushan, Shammu Ashan (vc), Charith Asalanka (capt), Avishka Fernando, Wanidu Hasaranga, Kamindu Mendis, Charana Nanayakkara, Vishad Randika, Lahiru Samarakoon, Asitha Fernando, Lahiru Kumara, Jehan Daniel, Damitha Silva, Thilan Nimesh
