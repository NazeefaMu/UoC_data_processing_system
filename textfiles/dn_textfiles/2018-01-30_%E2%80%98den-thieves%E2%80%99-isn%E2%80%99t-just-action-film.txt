## Title: ‘Den Of Thieves’ isn’t just an action film
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Tuesday, January 30, 2018 - 01:00

‘Den Of Thieves’ isn’t just an action film

Gerard Butler says his latest film isn’t just an action movie.
Den Of Thieves - which is out here next month - follows the lives of the LA County Sheriff’s Department and a group of successful bank robbers, who are planning a heist.
The star says while there are action sequences, there’s much more to it than that. K Fm
