## Title: Akmeemana Dayarathna Thera, seven others further remanded
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 9, 2017 - 11:30

Akmeemana Dayarathna Thera, seven others further remanded

 
Mount Lavinia Magistrate court ordered today to further remand Akmeemana Dayarathna Thera and seven others till October 16.
They were arrested in connection to the attack on the Rohingya safe house in Mt Lavinia on September 26.
