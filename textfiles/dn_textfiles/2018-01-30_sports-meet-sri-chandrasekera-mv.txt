## Title: Sports meet of Sri Chandrasekera MV
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, January 30, 2018 - 01:00

Sports meet of Sri Chandrasekera MV

The annual sports meet of Sri Chandrasekera Maha Vidyalaya, Horethuduwa Moratuwa will be held on 30th January 2018 at 1.30 pm at the school ground .
The Lieutenant Colonel 9th Artillery Regiment S.N.Wijesinghe will be the chief Guest and will award the certificates and the champions trophies to the winners.
