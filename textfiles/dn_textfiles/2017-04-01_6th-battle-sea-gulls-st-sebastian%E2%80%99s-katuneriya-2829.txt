## Title: 6th Battle of the Sea Gulls: St. Sebastian’s Katuneriya 282/9
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, April 1, 2017 - 01:00

6th Battle of the Sea Gulls: St. Sebastian’s Katuneriya 282/9

 St. Peter’s College Negombo scored 47 runs for 3 wickets in reply to St Sebastian’s College Katuneriya’s first innings of 282 for 9 declared on the opening day of the 6th Battle of the Sea Gulls annual cricket encounter played at Kadirana ground yesterday.
St. Peter’s won the toss and elected to field and for St. Sebastian’s Shaun Peshala scored 82 and Samitha Dilshan 80. Dineth Thathsara took 6 wickets for 88 runs.
St. Peter’s lost three wickets for 47 in 20 overs at the close with Umesh Lakshan remaining unbeaten on 31. Ashen Charuka took 2 wickets for 3 runs.
The match will be continued today.
St. Sebastian’s Katuneriya: 282/9 decl. in 67.4 overs (Shaun Peshala 82, Samitha Dilshan 80, Sheshan Udara 36, Dineth Thathsara 6/88, Sanidu Madhushanka 3/68,)
St. Peter’s Negombo: 47/3 at close in 20 overs (Umesh Lakshan 31 n.o., Ashen Charuka 2/3) – (YK)
