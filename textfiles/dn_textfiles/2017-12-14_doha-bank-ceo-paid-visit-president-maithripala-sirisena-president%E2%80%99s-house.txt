## Title: Doha Bank  CEO  paid a visit to President Maithripala Sirisena at President’s House
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, December 14, 2017 - 01:00

Doha Bank  CEO  paid a visit to President Maithripala Sirisena at President’s House

Doha Bank Chief Executive Officer R. Sitharaman paid a visit to President Maithripala Sirisena at President’s House, last Tuesday. Qatari Ambassador for Sri Lanka, A. S. P. Liyanage was also present.
