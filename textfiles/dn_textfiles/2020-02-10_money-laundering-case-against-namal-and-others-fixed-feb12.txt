## Title: Money Laundering case against Namal and others fixed for Feb.12
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Monday, February 10, 2020 - 17:07

Money Laundering case against Namal and others fixed for Feb.12

The case against Parliamentarian Namal Rajapaksa and five others under Money Laundering Act was today fixed for further trial on February 12 by Colombo High Court.
The case was today taken up before Colombo High Court Judge Pradeep Hettiarachchi.
