## Title: Maurice Fernando dies
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, October 24, 2017 - 01:00

Maurice Fernando dies

Old Cambrian and former Treasurer of Moratuwa Sports Club Maurice Condred Fernando passed away after a brief illness.
He was actively involved as the Treasurer of the organizing committees in eleven One Day International Matches played at De Soysa Staduim, Moratuwa. His remains lie at 91/10, St. Peter's Road (Duwa Watte), Koralawella, Moratuwa and the burial will take place after the church service at St. Peter's Church, Koralawella and the cortege leaves residence at 4.30 p.m. on 24th October 2017.
