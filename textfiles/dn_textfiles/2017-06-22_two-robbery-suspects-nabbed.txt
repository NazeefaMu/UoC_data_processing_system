## Title: Two robbery suspects nabbed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 22, 2017 - 01:00

Two robbery suspects nabbed

Two suspects in connection with robbing of valuables from the office of the first entrance of the Yala National Park were arrested by Tangalle Police at Yodakandya in Tissamaharama.
The office was robbed on March 30, 2016.
Police said that one of the suspects had a camera in his posession when they were taken into custody. The teller machine that was robbed has not been found yet, Police added.
The suspects, who are residents of Yodakandiya, were handed over to the Tissamaharama Police for interrogation according Tangalle SSP Dhanapala Abeywickrama. A team of police officers led by Crime Eradication Bureau OIC K.K. Rangajeewa took the suspects into custody.
