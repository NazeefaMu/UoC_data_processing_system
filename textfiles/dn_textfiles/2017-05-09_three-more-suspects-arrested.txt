## Title: Three more suspects arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 01:00

Three more suspects arrested

Three more individuals who were allegedly involved in the abduction of child in Gampola, were arrested by Police yesterday.
The suspects identified as residents of Kattankudi, Batticaloa, were to be produced before the Gampola Magistrate.
Police have nabbed six persons, including two women over the incident.
