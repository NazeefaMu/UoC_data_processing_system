## Title: ‘The Man Who Knew  Too Much’ at ICES
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Tuesday, February 27, 2018 - 01:00

‘The Man Who Knew  Too Much’ at ICES

The International Centre for Ethnic Studies (ICES) has organized a festival of American movie classics at the ICES Auditorium, 2, Kynsey Terrace, Colombo 8.
The Alfred Hitchcock masterpiece ‘The Man Who Knew Too Much’ will be screening on March 2 at 4.30 pm.
James Stewart and Doris Day give a magnificient performances as Ben and JoMcKenna ,
an American couple vacationing in Morocco, whose son is kidnapped and taken to England. Caught up in international espionage, the McKennas’ lives hang in the balance as they race to save their son in the chilling, climatic showdown in London’s famous Royal Albert Hal.
