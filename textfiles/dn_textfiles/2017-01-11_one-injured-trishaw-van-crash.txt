## Title: One injured in trishaw- van crash
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 11, 2017 - 01:00

One injured in trishaw- van crash

One person was critically injured when a trishaw collided head on with a van at Kudagama in the Nuwaraeliya - Hatton road yesterday. The trishaw driver in a critical condition, was admitted to hospital. Police are conducting investigations. Picture by Asela Kuruluwansa
