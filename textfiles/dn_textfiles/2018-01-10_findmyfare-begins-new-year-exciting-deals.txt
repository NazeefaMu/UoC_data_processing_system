## Title: Findmyfare begins New Year with exciting deals
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Wednesday, January 10, 2018 - 01:00

Findmyfare begins New Year with exciting deals

 One of Sri Lanka’s largest online travel company Findmyfare commenced the New Year with a new promotion for Nations Trust Bank American Express Cards customers, wherein they will enjoy an exclusive saving of 12% on bookings to any destination available on www.findmyfare.com.
Additionally, Nations Trust Bank American Express Cards customers will have the ability to pay for their bookings with the bank's best-in-class 12-month interest free installment plan. 
The booking period for the Findmyfare promotion is valid until January 15, 2018, while there are no restrictions on the travel period itself so you can travel any time you like. Visit www.findmyfare.com for more exciting deals added virtually every day.
Findmyfare is also widely known for offering a hassle-free booking experience via its innovative, easy-to-use website. 
Recently, Findmyfare extended its offerings to provide consultancy on visa procedures, to ensure travelers have access to the correct information on particular visas and related regulations, advice on obtaining relevant documents required for the entire visa process, as well as the visa application process itself. 
Additionally, www.findmyfare.com makes the booking process even easier by providing a number of options for credit card installment plans that are immediately converted online, without the need or hassle of a call to the bank. This is applicable to a number of local and international banks in Sri Lanka.  
Since its launch in 2012, Findmyfare has become Sri Lanka’s ultimate one-stop travel shop, providing the best travel deals available in the market-place.
