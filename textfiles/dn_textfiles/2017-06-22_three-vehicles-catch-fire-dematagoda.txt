## Title: Three vehicles catch fire at Dematagoda
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 22, 2017 - 11:30

Three vehicles catch fire at Dematagoda

 
A car plying the Dematagoda Road near St.Anthonys’ Church caught fire this morning and was engulfed in flames, but the passengers were able to escape without any injuries.
Two other vehicles nearby had also caught fire due to this incident.
Police believed that the fire had occurred due to a technical fault in the car.
Dematagoda Police is investigating
