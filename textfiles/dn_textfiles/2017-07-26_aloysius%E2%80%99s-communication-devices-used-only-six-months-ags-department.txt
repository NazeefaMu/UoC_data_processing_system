## Title: Aloysius’s communication devices used only for six months: AG's Department
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, July 26, 2017 - 11:00

Aloysius’s communication devices used only for six months: AG's Department

 
The Attorney Generals’s Department informed the Presidential Commission of Inquiry into Central Bank Bond issue today that the communication devices Perpetual Treasuries Director Arjun Aloysius’s submitted were only used for six months.
Aloysius was ordered to submit all personal communication devices used by him in the years 2015 to 2016 to the CID team which is attached to the Presidential Commission of Inquiry investigating into the Treasury bond issue on July 24.
He is alleged to have telephoned a witness related to the CB Bond issue.
Furthermore the AG’s Department directed the Presidential Commission of inquiry to issue an order to former CB Governor Arjuna Mahendran to submit all his digital communication devices, today.
