## Title: Alleged cattle robber hacked to death
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 01:00

Alleged cattle robber hacked to death

A 34-year-old person has been hacked to death at Julegamuwa in Hambantota.
The victim is alleged to be a reputed cattle robber. Ratnayaka Manapperige Chamila alias Shokesudda, 34, is a resident of Badgiriya.
He succumbed to his fatal injuries while being brought to the Hambantota General Hospital. The suspects are absconding, Hambantota Police said.
