## Title: ADEs appointed in Eastern Province
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 17, 2018 - 01:00

ADEs appointed in Eastern Province

Officers in Grade III of the Sri Lanka Education Administrative Service (SLEAS) were appointed as Assistant Education Directors (ADE) in educational zones in the Eastern Province.
They were granted appointment letters at a ceremony held at the Eastern Province Education Ministry Auditorium, Trincomalee, recently.
A total of 41 selected officers in Grade III have been appointed as Assistant Education Directors in zonal education offices of Eastern Province.Assistant Education Directors A.M.Nowferdeen (English) and S.Amjathkhan (Primary Education) have been appointed to Akkaraipattu Zonal Education Office.
Akkaraipattu Zonal Education Director A.M. Rahmathullah, Deputy Education Directors A.H.Fows, A.G.Fasmil, Assistant Education Director M.M. Fathima and Administrative Officer M.M. Ahamed Mohideen were present at this event.
