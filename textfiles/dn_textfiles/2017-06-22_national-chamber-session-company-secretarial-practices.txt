## Title: National Chamber session on 'Company Secretarial Practices'
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, June 22, 2017 - 01:00

National Chamber session on 'Company Secretarial Practices'

The National Chamber of Commerce in association with the Registrar of Companies will conduct a session on 'Company Secretarial Practices' on Thursday, June 29, from 9.30 am to 1.00 pm, at 450 D.R. Wijewardene Mawatha, Colombo 10.
This session will give an insight into the practical issues encountered by a company particularly with regard to ensuring compliance with statutory and regulatory requirements and for ensuring that decisions of the board of directors are implemented and adhered to according to the Companies Act 2014.
The speakers from the Registrar of Companies are Sumith Ratnayake, Attorney-at-law/Assistant of Registrar of Companies and Heshan Matugamage, Attorney at Law, Registrar of Companies.
