## Title: Sandadiya Mankada in Ratnapura
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Tuesday, October 17, 2017 - 01:00

Sandadiya Mankada in Ratnapura

Sandadiya Mankada, the musical show featuring Keerthi Pasquel, Umariya Sinhawansha and Aathma Liyanage will be held at the New Town Library auditorium, Rathnapura, on October 21. The event is held to raise funds to buy scanning machines to check dengue patients for the hospitals in Rathnapura. Vajira D Rathnayake composed the music. The compere is Nalin Karunaratne. The event is organized by Southern Engineers.
