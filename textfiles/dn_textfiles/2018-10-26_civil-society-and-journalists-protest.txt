## Title: Civil society and journalists protest
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, October 26, 2018 - 01:00

Civil society and journalists protest

Civil society representatives and journalists held a protest yesterday in front of the Saudi Arabian Embassy in Colombo, to voice their dissent against the killing of Saudi Journalist Jamal Kashoggi at the Saudi Embassy in Istanbul. Pictures by Rukmal Gamage
