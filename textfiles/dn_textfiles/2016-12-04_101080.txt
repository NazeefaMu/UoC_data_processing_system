## Title: Dengue fine increased to Rs. 25, 000
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Sunday, December 4, 2016 - 21:00

Dengue fine increased to Rs. 25, 000

 
The fine imposed on failing to keep personal or business premises clean and thereby encouraging mosquito breeding has been increased to Rs. 25, 000 Health Minister Rajitha Senaratne said.
Over 46, 000 cases of dengue were reported this year
