Singer U19 Cricket Kavindu’s ton takes Royal to 300

Royal College Colombo scored 300 all out on the opening day of their Singer Cup under 19 inter-schools cricket match against St. Benedict’s College Kotahena at Reid Avenue yesterday.
Kavindu Madarasinghe scored 111 runs off 207 balls with 18 fours in Royal’s total.
