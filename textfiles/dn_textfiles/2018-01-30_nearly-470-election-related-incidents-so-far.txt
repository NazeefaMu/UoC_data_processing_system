## Title: Nearly 470 election-related incidents so far
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, January 30, 2018 - 01:00

Nearly 470 election-related incidents so far

The Police Media Spokesman SP Ruwan Gunasekara said so far from December 12, 2017 to date, a total of 468 election-related incidents had been reported.
Among them were 345 complaints and 123 incidents of elections law violations.
During the period, 79 arrests have been made while among those arrested were 14 candidates contesting the LG elections. During the 24-hour period from 6 am on January 28 to 6 am on January 29, seven suspects had been arrested due to 21 election-related incidents.
Among the complaints lodged are 41 incidents of assaults and causing injuries while 94 incidents of threatening and verbal abuse were also reported.
Meanwhile, CAFÉ organization reported 249 incidents related to election violence up to 4 am on Sunday.
