## Title: Dhammaloka Thera allowed to go abroad
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Tuesday, December 12, 2017 - 14:50

Dhammaloka Thera allowed to go abroad

 
Ven. Uduwe Dhammaloka Thera, whose passport was impounded by Court as part of indictment filed by Attorney General over an alleged offence of possessing an elephant without a valid license was permitted to travel abroad by the Colombo High Court, today.Ven. Uduwe Dhammaloka Thera through his lawyer requested Colombo High Court Judge Sampath Wijeratne to release his passport for a five-week- period since he intended to travel to several countries including Thaiwan in order to attend in Dhamma sermon programs. Accordingly, Dhammaloka Thera was permitted to go abroad and his passport was ordered to be released until January 18 by the High Court.
