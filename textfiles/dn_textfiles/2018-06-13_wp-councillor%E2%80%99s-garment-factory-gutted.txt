## Title: WP Councillor’s garment factory gutted
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 13, 2018 - 01:00

WP Councillor’s garment factory gutted

A garment factory in Dodangoda, Kalutara, was completely destroyed by fire, at 5.30 am on Monday, June 11.
This garment factory belongs to Western Provincial Councillor Jagath P. Vithana, Dodangoda Police said. The damage has not yet been estimated. The cause of the fire has not yet been identified. Dodangoda Police is conducting investigations. The officials of the Government Analyst’s Department have been summoned to conduct investigations, together with Labour Department officials.
