## Title: New Cuban envoy presents credentials to President
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 2, 2016 - 01:00

New Cuban envoy presents credentials to President

The newly-appointed Ambassador of Cuba to Sri Lanka, Juana Elena Ramos Rodriguez presented credentials to President Maithripala Sirisena at the Presidential Secretariat yesterday.
The President expressed his condolences over the demise of former President of the Republic of Cuba Fidel Castro Ruz, to the Ambassador and recalled the past memories of Fidel Castro, the iconic revolutionary of the 20th century. The President expressed the belief that the longstanding friendship between Sri Lanka and Cuba will reach to new heights.
