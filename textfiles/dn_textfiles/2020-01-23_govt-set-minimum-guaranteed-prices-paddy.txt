## Title: Govt. to set minimum guaranteed prices for paddy
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, January 23, 2020 - 11:26

Govt. to set minimum guaranteed prices for paddy

Cabinet approval has been granted to set a minimum guaranteed price of Rs. 50 per kilo of paddy with recommended moisture level from Maha harvest.This was announced during the weekly Cabinet media briefing held at the Government Information Department.
