## Section: Features
## Date: Thursday, June 22, 2017 - 01:00

Humble Titan

Full Name- Lasintha De CosthaHometown- ColomboStatus- Married with a DaughterBirthday- September 9Passions- RugbyFavourite Colour- White 
He played School rugby as a centre for his Alma Mater, Isipathana College, in the inter-schools rugby championship and played club rugby for CR & FC, which he captained and Police SC. He also went on to play for Sri Lanka’s national rugby team. Presently he is the head coach of his old school Isipathana College. Today we go ‘Off the Field’ with Lasintha de Costha a talented rugby player cum head coach.
What made you enter the world of Sports?
The influence of my father and mother.
Who has supported you the most?My parents and family.
What do you do for fun?Hanging out with my friends.
What are the priorities in your life?My family.
What’s your favourite holiday destination?Unawatuna.
What was your most unforgettable memory/incident?The day I represented my school, Isipathna College, and Sri Lanka national team for the first time.
What is the greatest accomplishment in your Life?Becoming the head coach of my beloved school Isipathana College.
Who do you sometimes compare yourself to?No one.
If you had one day left to live, what will you do?I will spend it with my whole family and friends.
What’s the best compliment you have received?When well wishers said well done after I scored two back to back tries for CR & FC against the formidable Kandy SC at Nittawela.
Are you happy with all your achievements?Yes, very much.
What’s your favourite movie?Remember the Titans.
Are you doing what you believe in, or are you settling for what you are doing?I am doing what I believe in.
How do you describe yourself in two words?Patient and Humble.
