## Title: Sri Lankan Consul to Albania appointed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, September 19, 2017 - 01:00

Sri Lankan Consul to Albania appointed

Sri Lankan Ambassador to Italy Daya Pelpola handed the Commission of Appointment to Mirton Lika as a Sri Lankan Consul to Albania on September 15.
Lika holds Bachelors and Masters Degrees in Business Administration from the University of Tirana and is a business entrepreneur based in Tirana, Albania. The appointment of the Consul to Albania would be greatly beneficial for both countries to enhance bilateral relations in all spheres.
