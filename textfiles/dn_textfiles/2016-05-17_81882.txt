## Title: Unbeaten 49 by Udayanga in rain hit match
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, May 17, 2016 - 01:00

Unbeaten 49 by Udayanga in rain hit match

Lankan Cricket Club drew with Paragon Cricket League in their inter club division three tournament cricket match conducted by Colombo District Cricket Association and played at Moratuwa.
When play came to a halt due to rain. Lankans were 169 for 6 with Udayanga Nirmal unbeaten on 49 runs.
Lankan CC – 169/6 in 37.3 overs (Udayanga Nirmal 49 n/o, S. Sirinuwan 22, Susantha Dissanayake 27, Amar Sherif 2/33).
