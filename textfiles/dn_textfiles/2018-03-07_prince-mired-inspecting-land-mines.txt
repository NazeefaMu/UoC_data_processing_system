## Title: Prince Mired inspecting land mines
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, March 7, 2018 - 01:05

Prince Mired inspecting land mines

Special Envoy on Anti Personnel Mine Ban Convention Prince Mired Ra’ad Zeid Al-Hussein inspecting land mines that were removed during his visit to Muhumalai, Jaffna, yesterday. He was accompanied by Minister D.M. Swaminathan and Commander, Security Forces Headquarters Jaffna Major General Dharshana Hettiarachchi and Director Anti-Personnel Mine Ban Convention Implementation Support Unit (ISU) Juan Carlos Ruan.
