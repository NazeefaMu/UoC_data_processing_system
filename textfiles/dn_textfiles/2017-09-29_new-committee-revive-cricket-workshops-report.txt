## Title: New committee to revive cricket workshops report
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, September 29, 2017 - 01:00

New committee to revive cricket workshops report

The Sports Minister has appointed a new committee headed by former Cricket Interim Committee chairman Hemaka Amarasooriya to study the report on several workshops conducted with the aim to take Sri Lankan national cricket team to the right track and make long, medium and short term initiatives to put those plans into action within two years.
The committee comprises Anura Tennakoon, Aravind de Silva, Kumar Sangakkara and Sports Medicine Institute Director General Dr. Lakshman Edirisinghe. Former national captain and veteran cricketer Mahela Jayawardena was also invited to the committee.
The committee will submit their proposals in three months and SLC is facilitating the process.
