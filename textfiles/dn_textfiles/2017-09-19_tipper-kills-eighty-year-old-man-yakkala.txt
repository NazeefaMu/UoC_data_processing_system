## Title: Tipper kills eighty-year-old man in Yakkala
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, September 19, 2017 - 12:15

Tipper kills eighty-year-old man in Yakkala

 
An eighty-year-old man was run over by a tipper on the Colombo – Kandy road in Yakkala earlier today.
He succumbed to severe head injuries while being rushed to the Gampaha Hospital.
The deceased has been identified as Weeram Seeraiah, a resident of Borella.
The tipper driver has fled the scene after the accident.  
Yakkala Police said preliminary investigations revealed the tipper belongs to a resident in Kantale.
Yakkala Police is investigating.
