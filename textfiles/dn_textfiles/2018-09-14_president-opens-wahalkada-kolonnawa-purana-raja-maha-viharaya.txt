## Title: President opens wahalkada at Kolonnawa Purana Raja Maha Viharaya
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, September 14, 2018 - 01:00

President opens wahalkada at Kolonnawa Purana Raja Maha Viharaya

President Maithripala Sirisena opens the newly built wahalkada at the Kolonnawa Purana Raja Maha Viharaya on Wednesday. Chief Incumbent of the Temple, Ven. Kolonnawe Dhammika Nayaka Thera and Colombo District MP S.M. Marikkar look on. Picture courtesy President’s Media Division
