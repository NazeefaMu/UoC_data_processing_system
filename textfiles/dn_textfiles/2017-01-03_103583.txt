## Title: Woman’s body  found below ‘Mohini Ella’
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, January 3, 2017 - 01:00

Woman’s body  found below ‘Mohini Ella’

An unidentified woman’s body was found in the Maussakelle Reservoir below ‘Mohini Ella’ by the Nallathanniya Police on January 1 afternoon. Police said they suspected the woman to be a member of the Sri Pada pilgrim group.
Hatton District Judge and Magistrate was due to conduct the magisterial inquiry yesterday.
