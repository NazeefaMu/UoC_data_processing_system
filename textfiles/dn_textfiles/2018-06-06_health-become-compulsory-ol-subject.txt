## Title: Health to become compulsory O/L subject
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Wednesday, June 6, 2018 - 15:45

Health to become compulsory O/L subject

 
At yesterday's cabinet meeting, the ministers approved a proposal made by Health Minister Rajitha Senevirathna to make the subject of health compulsory in GCE (O/L) examinations. The proposal was originally submited by the Committee of Experts on Educational Reforms. According to a release by the cabinet, the proposal will be implemented subject to the approval of the National Education Commission.
