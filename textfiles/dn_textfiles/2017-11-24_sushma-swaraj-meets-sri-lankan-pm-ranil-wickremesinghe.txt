## Title: Sushma Swaraj meets Sri Lankan PM Ranil Wickremesinghe
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, November 24, 2017 - 14:14

Sushma Swaraj meets Sri Lankan PM Ranil Wickremesinghe

 
Union Minister of External Affairs Sushma Swaraj met Sri Lankan Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe in the national capital today.
During the meeting, both the dignitaries discussed various bilateral issues.
Earlier on Thursday, Prime Minister Narendra Modi met his Sri Lankan counterpart Ranil Wickremesinghe and held wide-ranging talks.
The Sri Lankan Prime Minister is on a four-day visit to India.
