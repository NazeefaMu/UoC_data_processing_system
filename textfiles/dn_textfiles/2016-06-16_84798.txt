## Title: Dharmapala VS DSS – a draw
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, June 16, 2016 - 01:00

Dharmapala VS DSS – a draw

Dharmapala Vidyalaya drew with D S Senanayake Vidyalaya Colombo in their inter school under 15 division two tournament cricket match played at Pannipitiya.
Visitors batting first were all out for 143 runs with 69 runs and Mihan Sethpura claimed 5 for 62 and in reply at stumps palians were 112 for 8 with Dulaj Wathsara scoring 56 runs.
D S Senanayake Vidyalaya – 143 in 56.3 overs (Ishara Akalanka 69, Mihan Sethpura 5/62, Kishal Thiumin 2/14.)
Dharmapala Vidyalaya – 112/8 in 48 overs (Dulaj Wathsara 56, Sahan Deshapriya 3/13, Binura Lolupitiya 3/28.)
