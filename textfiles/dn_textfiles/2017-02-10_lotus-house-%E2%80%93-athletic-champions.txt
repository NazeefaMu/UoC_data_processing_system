## Title: Lotus House – athletic champions
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, February 10, 2017 - 01:00

Lotus House – athletic champions

The annual inter-house sports meet of Malwana Al-Mumbarak Muslim Primary School was held at the schools grounds on Saturday. Here the Western Provincial Council member Sabi Raheem presenting a trophy to the skipper of champion Lotus House.
Principal S.L.M. Jeseem and School’s Welfare Society chairman M. Farook were also presents. – Mahanama Vithanage Malwana Group and Malwana Special
