## Title: Defending champions Richmond and Mahinda enter semi-finals
## Supertitle: ‘Singer Cup’ Under 19 Division One inter Schools Cricket tournament
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, April 11, 2018 - 01:00

‘Singer Cup’ Under 19 Division One inter Schools Cricket tournament
Defending champions Richmond and Mahinda enter semi-finals

Defending champions Richmond College Galle and Mahinda College Galle advanced to the semi finals of the ‘Singer Cup’ Under 19 inter Schools Limited Overs Knock-out Cricket tournament yesterday.
Defending champion Richmond College Galle beat Trinity College Kandy by 8 wickets in the quarter final played at St. Anne’s College ground Kurunegala yesterday.
Mahinda College Galle beat Prince of Wales College Moratuwa by 6 wickets in the quarter final played at Mt. Lavinia yesterday.
Avindu Theekshana and Kamindu Mendis played a key role in Richmond’s success and Subhanu Rajapaksha and Hansika Welihinda steered Mahinda to victory.
Richmond College Galle will meet S. Thomas’ College Mt. Lavinia and Mahinda College Galle will meet St. Peter’s College Bambalapitiya in the semi finals.
At Kurunegala. Richmond beat Trinity by 8 wickets.
Trinity: 125 in 41.4 overs (Hasintha Jayasooriya 42, Hasitha Boyagoda 21, Avindu Theekshana 5/07, Dilum Sudeera 2/11)
Richmond: 129/2 in 39.4 overs (Kamindu Mendis 67 n.o., Dhananjaya Lakshan 22)
At Mt. Lavinia. Mahinda beat Prince of Wales by 6 wickets. Prince of
Wales: 145 in 43 overs (Sanoj Darshika 38 n.o., Sandun Fernando 22, Prince
Fernando 22, Subhanu Rajapaksha 4/26, Pasan Pethangoda 3/43
Mahinda: 146/4 in 33.1 overs (Hansika Welihinda 54 n.o., Vinura Dulsara 38, Nipun Malinga 23) (YK)
