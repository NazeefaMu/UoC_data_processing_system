## Title: Japanese Defence Minister Itsunori Onodera tour around the Trincomalee harbour
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, August 23, 2018 - 01:02

Japanese Defence Minister Itsunori Onodera tour around the Trincomalee harbour

Visiting Japanese Defence Minister Itsunori Onodera accompanied by Navy’s Eastern Area Naval Commander Rear Admiral Sumith Weerasinghe making a tour around the Trincomalee harbour yesterday.The Minister visited the Japanese Naval ship ‘Ikazuchi’ anchored at the Trincomalee harbour where he addressed the ship’s crew.
