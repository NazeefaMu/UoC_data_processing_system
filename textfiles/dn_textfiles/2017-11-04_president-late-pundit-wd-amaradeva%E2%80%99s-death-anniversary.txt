## Title: President at late Pundit W.D. Amaradeva’s death anniversary
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, November 4, 2017 - 01:00

President at late Pundit W.D. Amaradeva’s death anniversary

 President Maithripala Sirisena yesterday visited the residence of the late Pundit W.D. Amaradeva in Maharagama in view of his first death anniversary. The President is in conversation with his wife Wimala Amaradeva and son Ranjana Amaradeva. Picture by Udesh Gunaratne
