## Title: President meets Bangladesh Foreign Minister
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, July 15, 2017 - 01:00

President meets Bangladesh Foreign Minister

President Maithripala Sirisena who is on a three-day State visit to Bangladesh met Bangladesh Foreign Minister A. H. Mahmood Ali at the Hotel Sonargaon, on Thursday. Minister Ravi Karunanayake, State Ministers Wasantha Aluwihare and Deputy Ministers Mohan Lal Grero, Nishantha Muthuhettigama and others participated. Picture by Sandaruwan Amarasinghe
