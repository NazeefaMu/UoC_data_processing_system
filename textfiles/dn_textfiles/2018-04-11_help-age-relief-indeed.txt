## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Wednesday, April 11, 2018 - 01:00

Help in age,  a relief indeed

HelpAge Vesak and New Year greeting cards once again will be at the forefront. The name of HelpAge Sri Lanka (HASL) will never erase from the minds of young and old, especially during this Vesak and New Year period. Many of them are in the habit of sending `HelpAge Cards’ to their loved ones.
This year HelpAge produced over 200,000 Vesak and New Year Cards to coincide with two festivals that commemorate the Thrice Blessed Day of Lord Buddha and the traditional Sinhala and Tamil New Year.The proceeds will be utilized to carry out free cataract surgeries for underprivileged Senior Sri Lankan Citizens.
The Vesak greeting cards are adorned with 10 colourful graphic designs and depict the events of Lord Buddha’s Birth to the Parinibbana, while New Year cards display the true spirit of the New Year. HASL, Executive Director, Samantha Liyanawaduge said although many destitute and underprivileged elderly citizen in the country need cataract surgeries, they are not financially stable to get them done.
“HelpAge Eye Hospital at Sinsapa Road, Wellawatta is open all five days of the week to help elders free of charge. We conduct 20 cataract surgeries per day completely free of charge using latest Eye Lenses. Over 150 people who suffer from various eye ailments visit the hospital every day from all parts of the country.”
HASL, Manager Fundraising, Ruchilalani Batuwantudawa said in addition, pocket Vesak cards have also been released for school children and all cards are printed on high-quality ivory boards.
They are sold at very affordable prices.
She said those who purchase over 1000 cards will be offered attractive discounts and requested people to support for this worthy cause of restoring the vision of elders Cards are available at leading Super Centres and Book Shops in Colombo and suburban areas and at HASL Head office , Raththanapitiya , Boralesgamuwa.
