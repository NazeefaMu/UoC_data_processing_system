## Title: PHI remanded for accepting bribe
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, January 21, 2017 - 01:00

PHI remanded for accepting bribe

A Public Health Inspector accepting a Rs. 10,000 bribe was arrested by Bribery Commission officers yesterday.
The bribe was taken from a manager of a hotel in Hantana to provide recommendations for the trade license for 2017. The PHI attached to Tennekumbura Public Health Inspectors' Office is a resident of Bowala. The suspect produced before the Kandy magistrate was remanded until January 26.
