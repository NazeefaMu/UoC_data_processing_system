## Title: St. Anne’s 167; St. Thomas’ Matara 93/5
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, April 12, 2017 - 01:00

St. Anne’s 167; St. Thomas’ Matara 93/5

St. Thomas College Matara lost five wickets for 93 runs by the close of the first day of their Singer Trophy Under 19 division two inter-schools two-day knock-out cricket semi-final match against St. Anne’s College Kurunegala at Aarons Country Club ground Horana yesterday.
St. Anne’s won the toss and electing to bat first were bowled out for 167 with skipper Wanitha Wanninayake scoring 64 off 99 balls with 9 fours and a six. Geesanthika Jayaweera took 3 wickets for 41 runs.
In St. Thomas’ reply Raminda Pravinath scored 39 and Tharusha Kavindya was unbeaten on 26. Randeera Ranasinghe had taken 4 wickets for 20 runs.
The match continues today.
Scores:
St. Anne’s: 167 in 62.3 overs (Wanitha Wanninayake 64, Dineth Chandimal 21, Tharinda Wijesinghe 19, Geesanthika Jayaweera 3/41)
St. Thomas: 93/5 at close in 32 overs (Raminda Pravinath 39, Tharusha Kavindya 26 n.o., Randeera Ranasinghe 4/20) - (YK)
