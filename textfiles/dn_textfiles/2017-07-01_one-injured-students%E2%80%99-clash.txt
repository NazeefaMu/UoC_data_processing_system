## Title: One injured in students’ clash
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, July 1, 2017 - 01:00

One injured in students’ clash

One student was seriously injured in a clash between two groups of students of Panduwasnuwara S. W. R. D. Bandaranaike National School, Kurunegala on June 28.
The injured was admitted to Kurunegala General Hospital for urgent medical treatment. Hettipola Police told the Daily News that the students had used clubs to attack their opponents. About 15 students were arrested by Hettipola Police. Hettipola Police are investigating.
