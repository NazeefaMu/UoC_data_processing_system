## Title: Cabinet reshuffle on Feb. 20
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, February 17, 2018 - 01:00

Cabinet reshuffle on Feb. 20

The second Cabinet reshuffle of the unity government will take place on February 20, Higher Education and Highways Minister Lakshman Kirielle told the Daily News.
It is to take place under the blessings of President Maithripala Sirisena and Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe, added the Minister, when asked about the negotiations undertaken to stabilize the government.
