## Title: BUS, TIPPER COLLISION; 25 INJURED
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, April 1, 2017 - 01:00

BUS, TIPPER COLLISION; 25 INJURED

Twenty Five passengers were injured when a bus plying from Badulla to Mahiyanganaya collided head on with a tipper carrying sand at Paragasthenna, Thaldena yesterday morning. The injured were admitted to Meegahakivula Hospital. Six persons including the bus driver who were in critical condition were later transferred to the Badulla General Hospital. Picture by N. Nawaratn, Meegahakivula Group Corr.
