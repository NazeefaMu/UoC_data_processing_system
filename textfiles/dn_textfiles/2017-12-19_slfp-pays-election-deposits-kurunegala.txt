## Title: SLFP pays election deposits in Kurunegala
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Tuesday, December 19, 2017 - 01:00

SLFP pays election deposits in Kurunegala

Sri Lankan Freedom Party Kurunegala district leader Anura Priyadarshana Yapa yesterday paid the party’s deposits to 19 Pradeshiya Sabha in the Kurunegala district before the Kurunegala district election officer.
Sports Minister Dayasiri Jayasekara and State Minister T. B. Ekanayake also participated in this event.
