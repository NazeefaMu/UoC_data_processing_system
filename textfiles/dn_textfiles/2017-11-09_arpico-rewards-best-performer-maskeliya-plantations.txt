## Title: Arpico rewards best performer of Maskeliya Plantations
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, November 9, 2017 - 01:00

Arpico rewards best performer of Maskeliya Plantations

Maskeliya Plantations, which is owned by Richard Pieris Group of Companies awarded a new land cruiser prado to Iroshan Weerakoon, the Cluster Senior Manager of Talawakelle Estate, Talawakelle.
Iroshan was appreciated and honoured, by the Group Chairman Dr. Sena Yaddehige and by Manoj Pathiraja, the Chief Executive Officer of Maskeliya Plantations, and Namunukulla Plantations (Uva Range) awarded for the Shadow Behind Every Success of the Season 2016/ 2017.
The award was presented to Iroshan Weerakoon by Dr. Sena Yaddehige, the Group Chairman of Richard Pieris Group of Companies and by Manoj Pathiraja, the Chief Executive Officer of the Maskeliya Plantations and for Namunukulla Plantations, (Uva Range) at a ceremony held on November 2, at the Arpico Office premises, situated at Vauxhall Lane, Colombo 02 for his best performance for season 2016/2017.
Senior Executives of Richard Pieris Group of Companies and Plantation Executives were present to grace this occasion.
