## Title: Lightning damages electrical items
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, September 15, 2017 - 01:00

Lightning damages electrical items

A large number of electrical appliances such as fridges, cassette recorders, computers, hand phones and fans were burnt due to lightning and thundering followed by the heavy rain in Hapugaskumbura, Udadumbara on September 12.
The residents say that this happened due to cellular phones towers in the area. They seek an immediate solution to this problem as the rainy season will come in the near future.
