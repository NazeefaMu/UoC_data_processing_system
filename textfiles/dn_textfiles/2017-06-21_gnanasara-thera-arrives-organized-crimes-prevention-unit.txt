## Title: Gnanasara Thera arrives at Organized Crimes Prevention Unit
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 21, 2017 - 13:00

Gnanasara Thera arrives at Organized Crimes Prevention Unit

 
Bodu Bala Sena (BBS) General Secretary Galagodaatte Gnanasara Thera arrived at the Organized Crimes Prevention Unit a short while ago to make a statement.
He was released on bail by the Colombo Fort Magistrate Courts after surrendering to courts today.
