## Title: Lakmal Madhushanka wins Best Sales Executive – Silver at NASCO Awards
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Monday, August 27, 2018 - 01:00

Lakmal Madhushanka wins Best Sales Executive – Silver at NASCO Awards

A Sales Executive of the India – Sri Lanka joint venture tyre manufacturer CEAT Kelani Holdings has won the Silver Award for Best Sales Executive in the ‘Automotive’ category at the 2018 National Sales Congress (NASCO) Awards presented by the Sri Lanka Institute of Marketing (SLIM).
The executive, Lakmal Madhushanka commenced his employment with CEAT as a Sales Administration Assistant and later opted to join the sales team. The Company says he has performed commendably since taking over the North Western area, exceeding his targets every month and positively impacting sales growth in the region.
