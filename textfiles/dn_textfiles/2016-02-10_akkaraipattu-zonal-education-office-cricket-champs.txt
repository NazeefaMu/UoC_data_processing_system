## Title: Akkaraipattu Zonal Education Office cricket champs
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, February 10, 2016 - 01:00

Akkaraipattu Zonal Education Office cricket champs

Zonal Education Office cricket team of Akkaraipattu beat Base Hospital cricket team, Akkaraipattu by nine wickets in the final to emerge champions of the limited over cricket tournament worked off at Akkaraipattu Public Play Ground recently.
The Akkaraipattu Divisional Secretariat conducted a program to promote goodwill among staffers and to popularize traditional sports in the region with the support of Public Administration and Home Affairs Ministry.
This tournament was held under the patronage of Akkaraipattu Divisional Secretary M.I.A.Latheef. Akkaraipattu Divisional Secretariat, Zonal Education Office, Base Hospital, Police Station, National Water Supply and Drainage Board, Municipal Council, Pradeshiya Sabha, Road Development Authority and Technical College teams were took part in the tournament.
Base Hospital cricket team batted first and they were all out for 28 runs. In reply Zonal Education Office cricket team reached the target with nine wickets intact.
The Deputy Sports Minister H.M.M.Harees, Health Deputy Minister Faizal Cassim, Eastern Provincial Councilor A.L.Thavam and Department Heads were participated as guests.
