## Title: Plan to assist Samurdhi beneficiaries
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, August 19, 2017 - 01:00

Plan to assist Samurdhi beneficiaries

The government has taken measures to enhance livelihood activities of Samurdhi beneficiaries in the Mulatiyana Divisional Secretariat Division.
The project includes the construction of trade stalls and houses, imparting technical know-how on goods production to people, setting up of green parks.
Rs. 2,570,000 has been set apart for the project.
