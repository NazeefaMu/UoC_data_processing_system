## Title: Time is ripe for SLFP to leave govt - MR
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Friday, October 5, 2018 - 01:00

Time is ripe for SLFP to leave govt - MR

The time is now ripe for the SLFP to leave the present government, former President Mahinda Rajapaksa said.
He was speaking to the gathering at a religious ceremony at a temple in Ganemulla.
The former President said he hoped President Maithripala Sirisena would take urgent action in this direction.
