## Title: Sinhabahu at Bandarawela Town Hall
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Friday, September 15, 2017 - 01:00

Sinhabahu at Bandarawela Town Hall

Sinhabahu, one of the major creations of Prof. Ediriweera Sarachchandra, which is included in the Sinhala and Aesthetic syllabi in school, will be staged at the Town Hall in Bandarawela on September22 at 9.30 am and 12.30 pm.
JanakaMunasinghe, RajithaHewathanthrige, Nihari Wickramarachchi, Tharanga Kumari, Lakshani Amarathunga, Ganga Paranavithana along with many other prominent artistes will be included in performing and singing.
Make up will be by Upul Mahanama, stage lighting by Upali Weerasinghe and stage management by Dhanapala Bogoda.
Sinhabahu will be directed by LalithaSarachchandra while it will be organised by Priyantha Ranjan.
