## Title: RDB Peradeniya branch moves to modernised location
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, April 6, 2017 - 01:00

RDB Peradeniya branch moves to modernised location

Peradeniya Branch of Regional Development Bank (RDB) moved into new and modernised location on April 3, at Colombo Road, Peradeniya to provide better facilities to its customers.
Chairman of RDB Prasanna Premaratne, Executive Director Priyantha Abeysinghe, General Manager and CEO TA. Ariyapala, Kandy District secretariat H.M.Hitisekera, Regional General Manager DM. Senaratna Bandara, Assistant General Manager D.P. Dharmadasa, District Manager Mangalika Weerasinghe, Asst. Districct Manager Asela Navaratne, Branch Manager Rasika Yalagala, Deputy Manager Sagarika Premawansha and staff members and customers were presented at the ceremony.
Vice Chancellor of University of Peradeniya Prof Upul B. Dissanayake delivered his special wishes at the occasion.
Distribution of credit facilities and gift items to the highest achievers of children’s accounts was also witnessed at the event.
