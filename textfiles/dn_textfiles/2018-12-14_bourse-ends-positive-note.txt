## Title: Bourse ends on positive note
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Friday, December 14, 2018 - 01:00

Bourse ends on positive note

The bourse ended on a positive note yesterday as the ASPI increased by 0.41% or 24.93 points to close at 6,060.20 points, while the S&P SL20 Index also increased by 0.11% or 3.42 points to close at 3,154.42.
Market Turnover decreased by 25.32% relative to yesterday to amount to Rs. 0.68 billion while the crossings for the day amounted to 51.7% of the day’s total turnover. Foreign investors meanwhile, recorded a net outflow of Rs. 351.2 million over the day compared to a net outflow of Rs. 272.2 million recorded yesterday.
