## Title: CWC to support Premier Mahinda
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 29, 2018 - 01:00

CWC to support Premier Mahinda

The Ceylon Workers Congress (CWC) leader Arumugam Thondaman yesterday said he will support new Prime Minister Mahinda Rajapaksa.
Thondaman said Rajapaksa had promised to increase the daily wage of plantation workers to Rs.1,000.
The CWC leader said his party has been agitating for a raise in plantation sector workers over a long period and added that the Premier’s responses gives hope that this can be achieved shortly.
Speaking said to the media in Colombo, he said he had no doubt that the UPFA will show a majority in Parliament shortly.
