## Title: Trinity draw with Wesley
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, October 29, 2016 - 01:00

Trinity draw with Wesley

The Singer under 19 cricket match between Trinity College, Kandy and Wesley College ended in a draw at Asgiriya Stadium yesterday. In reply Wesley’s first innings of 208, Trinity replied with 210. Hasintha Jayasuriya and Poorna Nethumal made half centuries apiece for Trinity. In their second innings Wesley were 122-6.
Scores: Wesley 208 and 122 for 6 in 41.1 overs (Thisuraka Akmeemana 76, Kevin de Silva 20, Sanogeeth Shanmuganathan 3 for 18, Ashok Kumar 2 for 30) Trinity 210 in 59.4 overs (Hasintha Jayasuriya 59, Hasitha Boyagoda 24, Poorna Nethumal 51, Sakuntha Liyanage 4 for 45, Samodh Athulathmudali 2 for 27, Kavinda Dissanayake 2 for 64).
