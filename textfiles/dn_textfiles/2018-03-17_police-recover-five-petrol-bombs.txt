## Title: Police recover five petrol bombs
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, March 17, 2018 - 01:00

Police recover five petrol bombs

During a search programme for mosquito breeding grounds, Kurunegala Police discovered five petrol bombs wrapped in a bag at a residence in the Ibbagamuwa area on Thursday.
Accordingly, a 26-year-old resident of the house was arrested. Police are investigating.
