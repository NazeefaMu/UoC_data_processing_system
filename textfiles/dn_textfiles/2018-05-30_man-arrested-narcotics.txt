## Title: Man arrested with narcotics
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 30, 2018 - 01:00

Man arrested with narcotics

The Colombo Central Law Enforcement Unit arrested a man at Maradana with 89 drug tablets, 9 g of cocaine and 6 g and 100 mg of the drug ‘Ice’ on Monday.
The suspect was transferred to the Maradana police station.
He was identified as 38-year-old resident of Maligawatta. The suspect was produced before the Maligakanda Magistrate’s Court yesterday. The Maradana police are investigating. (FJ)
