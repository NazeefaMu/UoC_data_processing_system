## Title: LCB Finance head office opens in Kohuwala
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, June 28, 2018 - 01:00

LCB Finance head office opens in Kohuwala

LCB Finance, the finance company in the Southern region after taking over the troubled City Finance and offering relief to its depositors opened their head office and corporate branch in Kohuwala last week.
CEO of LCB Finance Gamage Leelananda said they would offer a host of benefits which include business, personal, micro and property loans and leasing facilities at very competitive rates.
LCB Finance also has branches at Karandeniya, Mirissa and Pelawatta. “We are planning to open more branche in Negombo, Kurunegala, Anuradhapura, Kohuwala and Rathgama soon.”
Deputy Minister Telecommunication, Digital Infrastructure and Foreign Employment. Manusha Nanayakkara and MP,Bandula Gunawardana opened the new office while representatives of Finance Companies, Board of Directors, depositors, and invitees participated in the event.
