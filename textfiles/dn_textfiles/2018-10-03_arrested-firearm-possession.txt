## Title: Arrested for  firearm possession
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Wednesday, October 3, 2018 - 01:00

Arrested for  firearm possession

Elpitiya Police arrested two suspects possessing a 12-calibre shotgun on Monday, at Mandakanda, Elpitiya.
The suspects, identified as residents of Elpitiya and Karandeniya, are of the ages 34 and 41. They were to be produced at the Karandeniya Magistrate’S Court.
Investigations are continuing.
