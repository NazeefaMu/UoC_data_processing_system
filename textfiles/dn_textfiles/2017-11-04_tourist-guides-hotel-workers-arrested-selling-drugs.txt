## Title: Tourist guides, hotel workers arrested for selling drugs
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Saturday, November 4, 2017 - 01:00

Tourist guides, hotel workers arrested for selling drugs

Nine tourist guides and hotel workers who attempted to sell harmful drugs to foreigners have been arrested by Police personnel from different areas of the North Western Province during the past 10 months.
Five tourist guides had been arrested from the Kurunegala district.
Meanwhile, a police team led by Wennapuwa Police OIC IP L. B. Abeyratne, succeeded in arresting a tourist guide with a parcel of 50g of ganja, yesterday. The suspect is a 38-year-old.
Investigations are continuing.
