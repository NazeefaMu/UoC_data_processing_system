## Title: IUBF protest hampers traffic in Fort
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, April 4, 2017 - 16:30

IUBF protest hampers traffic in Fort

(File Photo)
A protest organised by the Inter University Bikkhu Federation has hampered traffic in areas around Fort today.
The protest is in demand of action on university admission shortages and to allocate 6 percent of funds to education.
