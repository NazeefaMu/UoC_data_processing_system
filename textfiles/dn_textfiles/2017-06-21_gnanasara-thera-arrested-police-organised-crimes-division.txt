## Title: Gnanasara Thera arrested by Police Organised Crimes Division
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 21, 2017 - 15:30

Gnanasara Thera arrested by Police Organised Crimes Division

(File Photo)
Bodu Bala Sena (BBS) General Secretary Galagodaatte Gnanasara Thera was arrested by the Police Organised Crimes Division a short while ago.
He will be presented before the Colombo Additional Magistrate Courts.
