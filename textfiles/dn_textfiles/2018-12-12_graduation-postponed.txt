## Title: Graduation postponed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, December 12, 2018 - 01:00

Graduation postponed

The Pracheena Bashopakara Samagama’s Pracheena Pandita graduation ceremony which was to be held tomorrow (December 13) under the patronage of President Maithripala Sirisena has been postponed till January 2019 due to the unavoidable circumstances.
The Education Ministry’s PracheenaBashopakara Samagama will inform the relevant graduates by post in this regard.
