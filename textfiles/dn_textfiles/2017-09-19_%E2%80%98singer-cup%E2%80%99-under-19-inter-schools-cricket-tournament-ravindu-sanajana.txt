‘Singer Cup’ Under 19  Inter Schools Cricket Tournament: Ravindu Sanajana makes second ton of the season

Ravindu Sanjana right hand batsman of St. Aloysius College, Galle made second ton in this season in the ‘Singer Trophy’ Under 19 inter Schools Cricket tournament yesterday.
Ravindu Sanjana scored unbeaten 141 runs against Bandaranayake College, Gampaha at Kadirana.
Meanwhile Ayana Siriwardene (Isipathana) and Pubudu Reshan (Mahanama Panadura) scored centuries in the same tournament matches at Colts grounds and Bandaragama.
Chamidu Lakshitha (Dharmapala), Kavika Dilshan (St. Aloysius), Sampranath Vishwameth (Ananda Maithreeya) and Ishara Abeysinghe (Sri Buddha Jayanthi) shown good baowling performances in yesterday’s matches. Matches will be continued today.
