## Title: Sabaragamuwa Province facilitated artistes
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, December 8, 2016 - 01:00

Sabaragamuwa Province facilitated artistes

The Sabaragamuwa Province Arts and Cultural Festival was held at the provincial auditorium on Tuesday with the participation of artistes, schoolchildren and provincial political leaderships under the guidance of Provincial Education and Cultural Minister Bhanu Munipriya.
The winners who represented schools of the Ratnapura and Kegalle districts were evaluated and awarded. The winners performed well at various arts and cultural segments were handed over certificates.
Moreover, a few artistes of the Sabaragamuwa Province were felicitated for their unique service rendered to the arts and culture sector for decades. Ramya Wanigasekara, Nissanka Diddeniya, Dulika Marapana, Jayantha Wijerathne Herath, Raja Wickremasinghe were feted with “Prathiba Pranama” awards. Twenty eight traditional provincial artistes were also felicitated.
