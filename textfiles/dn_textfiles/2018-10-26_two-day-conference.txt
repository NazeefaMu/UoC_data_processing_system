## Title: Two day conference
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, October 26, 2018 - 01:00

Two day conference

The National Dangerous Drugs Control Board will hold a two day conference on illicit drugs control and prevention at the Waters Edge in Battaramulla on October 29,30. Both local and foreign experts will take part.
Law and Order Minister Ranjith Maddumabandara, Justice Minister Thalatha Athukorale and Deputy Law and Order Minister Nalin Bandara will also attend as invitees.
