## Title: Nalanda and St. Sebastian’s into semis
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, March 25, 2016 - 01:00

Nalanda and St. Sebastian’s into semis

Nalanda College, Colombo and St. Sebastian’s College, Moratuwa advanced to the semi finals of the ‘Singer Trophy’ Under 19 inter schools limited overs cricket tournament yesterday.
Nalanda College beat St. Aloysius College, Galle by 111 runs in the quarter final played at St. Sebastian’s College ground Moratuwa and St. Sebastian’s College beat Isipathana College, Colombo by 44 runs in the quarter final played at Colts grounds.
*At St. Sebastian’s ground
Nalanda beat St. Aloysius by 111 runs
Nalanda: 199/9 in 50 overs (Kasun Sandaruwan 67, Kavindu Bandara 50, Asel Kulathunge 27, Madhushan Hasaranga 26 n.o., Hareen Buddila 3/32, Probodha Guruge 2/22, Gihan Niroshan 2/48, Sajith Sankalpa 2/54)
St. Aloysius: 88 in 33.4 overs (Navindu Nirmal 20, Umeksha Dilshan 3/16, Suhanga Wijewardane 3/17, Kavindu Bandara 2/07)
*At Colts ground
St. Sebastian’s beat Isipathana by 44 runs
St. Sebastian’s: 215 in 50 overs (Tharusha Fernando 41, Mishane Silva 38, Avishka Fernando 30, Sandaruwan Fernando 30, Ayana Siriwardhane 3/45, Lahiru Dilshan 3/49, Iwanka Sanjula 2/20)
Isipathana: 171 in 47 overs (Salinda Ushan 45, Praveen Jayawickrame 4/22, Nimesh Bandara 3/35) - YK
