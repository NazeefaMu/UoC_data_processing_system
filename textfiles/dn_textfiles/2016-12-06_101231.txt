## Title: Adventurer Kev Brady gears up for Sri Lanka challenge
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, December 6, 2016 - 12:45

Adventurer Kev Brady gears up for Sri Lanka challenge

 
Adventurer Kev Brady is getting ready for a new challenge in Sri Lanka.
The 33-year-old from Gloucester will attempt to paddle board the length of the country's longest river - the Mahaweli - which is 208 miles (335 km) long
Then he'll attempt to paddle all the way around the island.
Kev has already canoed along the Mississippi and swum the length of the River Severn.
