## Title: Man with hashish arrested
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 30, 2018 - 01:00

Man with hashish arrested

The Organised Crimes Prevention Division arrested a suspect with 9.125g of hashish in Aluthkade, Keselwatte, on Saturday on a tip-off. The 23-year-old suspect is a resident of Kittampahuwa, Wellampitiya. He was produced before the Maligakanda Magistrate.
