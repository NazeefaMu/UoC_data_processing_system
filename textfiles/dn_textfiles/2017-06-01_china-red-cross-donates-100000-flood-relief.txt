## Title: China Red Cross donates $ 100,000 for flood relief
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 1, 2017 - 01:00

China Red Cross donates $ 100,000 for flood relief

The China Red Cross Society donated US$ 100,000 to the Sri Lanka Red Cross Society (SLRCS) supporting Sri Lanka's relief and rebuilding efforts on the present disaster of flooding and landslides.
On May 30, Chinese Ambassador Yi Xianliang, on behalf of the Red Cross Society of China, handed over the donation to National Secretary of SLRCS, Nimal Kumar.
Ambassador Yi extended the deepest sympathy and condolences to the Sri Lankan people, especially the families affected by the disaster. He hoped the donation could assist the current relief operation of SLRCS and provide help to the people in the disaster-affected areas.
Kumar introduced the disaster situation and expressed appreciation and gratitude to the generous donation from the Red Cross Society of China. He said that the Chinese Government and people always helped Sri Lanka when faced with natural disasters. Sri Lankan people would never forget the sincere friendship and generosity of the Chinese Government and people, he said.
