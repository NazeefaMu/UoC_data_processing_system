## Title: 13 accused in 2008 youth abduction case released on bail
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Friday, January 24, 2020 - 13:52

13 accused in 2008 youth abduction case released on bail

The Permanent High Court at Bar ordered to release on bail the 13 accused including the Former Naval Media Spokesperson Commodore DKP Dassanayake, in connection with the abduction and disappearance of 11 youth between 2008 and 2009.Accordingly, each accused has been released on a cash bail of Rs. 500,000 and two sureties of Rs. 2.5 million each.
