## Title: Air Force rescues missing fishermen
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 13, 2018 - 01:00

Air Force rescues missing fishermen

The Air Force had rendered their assistance in locating two fishermen on June 6, who were lost at sea. They had gone out fishing from the Pallikudawa fishing harbour in Tangalle and were lost at sea for four days.
The Air Force search team had utilised their surveillance capabilities to locate the missing fishermen. The Air Force team had deployed a Y12 aircraft for the search operation and located the missing fishing boat off the Southern coast.
Based on the information provided by the Air Force, the fishermen had been rescued with the assistance of the Navy.
