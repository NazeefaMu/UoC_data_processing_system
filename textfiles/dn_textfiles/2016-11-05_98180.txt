## Title: St Sylvester’s dinner dance today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, November 5, 2016 - 01:00

St Sylvester’s dinner dance today

St. Sylvester’s College Kandy who are celebrating their 76th year will hold their dinner dance “Rumble of the Vesters” at the Kings Court, Cinnamon Lake Side today.
The event is organized by the Old Boys committee in view of the school’s sports development fund.
