## Title: Trap gun injures youth
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, December 24, 2018 - 01:00

Trap gun injures youth

A 23-year-old person was admitted to the Badulla Hospital yesterday with severe injuries when a trap gun set for animals when off.
He has entangled with the trap gun while he was on his way to chase porcupines in his garden in the morning, Kandaketiya police said. Sarath Wijesinghe of Wasanagama, Beramada was hospitalized in this way. No one was arrested for the incident and Kandaketiya police are investigating.
