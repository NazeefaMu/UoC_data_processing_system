## Title: SAARC Secretary General meets Ravi
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 1, 2017 - 01:00

SAARC Secretary General meets Ravi

The new Secretary General of the South Asian Association for Regional Cooperation (SAARC) Amjad Hussain B.Sial in his customary introductory calls on Member States visited Sri Lanka from May 29-31.
During his stay in Sri Lanka, the Secretary General paid a courtesy call on President Maithripala Sirisena, at the Official Residence on May 29.
Sial also called on Foreign Affairs Minister Ravi Karunanayake and Foreign Affairs Ministry Secretary Esala Weerakoon on the same day at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
The Foreign Minister congratulated Sial on his appointment as the new Secretary General of SAARC and conveyed Sri Lanka’s fullest support to SAARC. Minister Karunanayake highlighted the importance of strengthening the SAARC mechanism and ensured Sri Lanka’s support to Secretary General’s endeavours to strive for a regional organisation that is productive, result oriented and an efficient entity. The importance of a higher input on Intra-trade in the region was also emphasized by the Minister.
M.A.K.Girihagama, DG/SA & SAARC and Mrs.T.P.Dharmasena, D/SA & SAARC were present at the meeting.
The Secretary General visited the SAARC Cultural Centre at Colombo 2 during the visit.
