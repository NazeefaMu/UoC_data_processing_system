## Title: Weber grills expands footprint to SL at World of Outdoor
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, April 6, 2017 - 01:00

Weber grills expands footprint to SL at World of Outdoor

Weber Barbecue Grills, a product of Weber Stephen Products LLC, will expand its global footprint when they open their first flagship store in Sri Lanka in collaboration with World of Outdoor.
Weber’s partners in Sri Lanka are non-other than World of Outdoor who since they first opened their galleries in January 2011, have enhanced the business into life style sector, carrying the brand name of “World Of Outdoor” to the domestic as well as hospitality and industrial sectors, providing solutions which will protect your furniture and effects from the hazardous rays of the sun as well as rain. The premier grilling products manufacturer has introduced its extensive range of grilling products for campers, barbecue party enthusiasts, families, restaurants, cafes, hotels, holiday bungalows to villas; B2B and B2C customers, including the signature charcoal grills and gas grills along with grilling accessories.
Going forward with its essence, “Don't just grill, let's Weber”- Weber is here to add a new dimension to your culinary experience. The advantages of grilling with Weber are manifold.
Weber Grills retain the nutrients of the meat while greatly reducing the oil content one would usually use when grilling as the excess fat drips off the meat, leaving less fat in the cooked food.
Weber’s vision is to change the way of grilling with the ultimate barbecue experience — done the Weber way.
It brings together communities and allows users to spend quality time with friends and family. With the invention of the covered lid grill, Weber has made possible a healthy way of cooking that prepares mouthwatering food that is both healthy and tasty, minimizing the use of oil.
Weber’s Charcoal and Gas grills are both compact yet versatile and can easily be connected to domestic or portable LPG cylinders.
Weber has a grill for every type of space, be it a compact balcony, park, yard, terrace, five star kitchen or even for camping purposes.
In addition, Weber also provides different functional accessories for the basic home kitchen chef to the professional Michelin Star chef.
