## Title: Increments for teacher assistants soon
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 4, 2017 - 01:00

Increments for teacher assistants soon

Steps have been taken to increase the allowances of teacher assistants employed in government schools countrywide with effect from February this year, Education Minister Akila Viraj Kariyawasam said. He was addressing a press conference in Kuliyapitiya on Tuesday.
At present, they are paid a monthly allowance of Rs. 6,000 and that amount will be increased to Rs. 10,000. Due arrears will also be paid to them soon, the minster said.
The teacher Assistants are now at service in estate schools and remote government schools countrywide, the minister added.
