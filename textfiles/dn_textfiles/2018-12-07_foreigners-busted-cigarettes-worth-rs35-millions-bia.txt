## Title: Foreigners busted with cigarettes worth Rs.3.5 millions at BIA
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 7, 2018 - 10:51

Foreigners busted with cigarettes worth Rs.3.5 millions at BIA

The three passengers arrived from Hong Kong, have been detained at the Bandaranaike International Airport last night. 
 Accordingly, the Customs officers have seized 63,000 sticks of foreign cigarettes contained in 318 cartons.
The contraband is estimated to be worth over Rs.3.5 million.
Further investigations are carried out by the Customs officers attached to the BIA.
