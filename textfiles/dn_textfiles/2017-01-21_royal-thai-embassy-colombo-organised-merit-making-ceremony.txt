## Title: The Royal Thai Embassy in Colombo organised a merit making ceremony
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, January 21, 2017 - 01:00

The Royal Thai Embassy in Colombo organised a merit making ceremony

The Royal Thai Embassy in Colombo yesterday organised a merit making ceremony to mark the hundredth day of the passing away of King of Thailand Bhumibol Adulyadej at Kelaniya Rajamahaviharaya. Here, Thailand envoy in Sri Lanka Nopporn Adchariyavanich in conversation with Kelaniya Rajamaha Viharaya Chief Incumbent Ven. Prof. Kollupitiye Mahinda Sangharakkhitha Thera.
