## Title: Girl falls to her death during school activity
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, March 1, 2018 - 11:09

Girl falls to her death during school activity

 
An 8-year-old who accidently fell on her face while engaging in a school activity in Avissawella, died last morning.
The activity had required the girl to walk with the aid of two coconut shells, tied to a rope.
She succumbed to injuries after being rushed to the Avissawella Hospital.
The deceased girl identified as Saheli Nawarthana Mandakini was a resident of Piriwena Road, Dehiowita.
Postmortem examinations will be carried out today.
Avisawella Police is conducting further investigations.
