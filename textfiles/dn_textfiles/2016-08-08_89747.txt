## Title: Lisula and Neranjan help BRC collect vital points
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, August 8, 2016 - 01:00

Lisula and Neranjan help BRC collect vital points

BRC beating Air Force, their closest rivals in the points table today at P Sara Oval consolidated their position in group B ahead of Air Force. After Neranjan Wanniarachchi’s four-wicket burst restricted Air Force to a below-par 115, Lisula Lakshan hammering an unbeaten 75 took BRC home in just under 16 overs to win the match by 7 wickets. 
Air Force SC U23: 115/8 in 20 overs (Dilip Tharaka 23, Neranjan Wanniarachchi 4/17)
BRC U23: 117/3 in 15.5 overs (Lisula Lakshan 75*, Hashan Fernando 2/28)
