## Title: Malinga appears before expert pannel of doctors
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, April 20, 2016 - 18:00

Malinga appears before expert pannel of doctors

Sri Lankan cricketer Lasith Malinga today appeared for the fitness test before an expert panel of doctors appointed by the Sri Lanka Cricket Board.  Earlier, IPL’s Mumbai Indians' medical team deemed him unfit for at least another four months.
