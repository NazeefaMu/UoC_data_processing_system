## Title: Amal International Annual Inter House Sports Meet
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Tuesday, February 27, 2018 - 01:00

Amal International Annual Inter House Sports Meet

Amal International School’s Annual Inter House Sports Meet was held at the Havelock Grounds recently. It was held under the patronage of the school principal M M Shazuli.
Arqam House became the champion and Umar Zahaar who is the Captain of the House received the Challenge Cup from the Chief Guest former Sri Lankan Cricketer and Captain Kumar Sangakkara.
The school presented a hand drawn portrait of the chief guest as a token of appreciation to Sangakkara. Chairman of the School M Ashroff Rumi, board of directors, teachers, parents and students were present.
