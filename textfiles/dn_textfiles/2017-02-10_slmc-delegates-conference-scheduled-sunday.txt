## Title: SLMC Delegates Conference scheduled for Sunday
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 10, 2017 - 01:00

SLMC Delegates Conference scheduled for Sunday

The 27th Delegates Conference of the Sri Lanka Muslim Congress (SLMC) will be held on February 12 at 9.30 a.m. at the Bandaranaike Memorial International Conference Hall (BMICH).
The High Command Secretary Mansoor A. Cader told the Daily News that it is the 27th Delegates Conference of the party.
A mandatory High Command Meeting will be held prior to the Delegates Conference to select new office- bearers for the party.
The party expects around 1,000 delegates from all over the island to the Delegates Conference.
According to the Department of Elections, a party must have their Delegates Conference every 14 months.
According to party supporters, the Mandatory High Command Meeting will be a significant as the SLMC stands to seek a solution to problems within the party.
