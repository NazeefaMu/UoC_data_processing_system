## Title: Remanded for abusing boy
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, June 19, 2018 - 01:00

Remanded for abusing boy

A man who abused an 11 year-old boy was remanded till June 20, by Kurunegala Chief Magistrate Sampath Hewawasam.
When the boy was returning home after attending a funeral, the suspect had taken him into a thicket close by and abused him. Kurunegala OIC IP Mangala Wickramanayake is investigating the incident further.
