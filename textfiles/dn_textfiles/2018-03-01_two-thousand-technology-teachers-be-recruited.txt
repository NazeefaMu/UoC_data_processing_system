## Title: Two thousand Technology teachers to be recruited
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, March 1, 2018 - 01:00

Two thousand Technology teachers to be recruited

Kurunegala District Parliamentarian and Education Minister Akila Viraj Kariywasam told the Daily News yesterday in Kuliyapitiya, Kurunegala, that his Ministry has decided to recruit about 2,000 Technology teachers to teach G.C.E. (A.L.) students at national schools.
The Competitive Examination for recruiting these teachers would be held by the Examinations Department on March 10. About 11,000 qualified graduates have applied for these vacancies.
About 42 schools in the country are currently teaching this subject to A.L. students and about 152 more schools are to start teaching this subject from this year onward , Minister Akila Viraj Kariyawasam further said.
