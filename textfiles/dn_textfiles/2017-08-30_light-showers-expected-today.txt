## Title: Light showers expected today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 30, 2017 - 11:15

Light showers expected today

 
Several spells of light showers will occur in the Western, Sabaragamuwa, North-western and Central provinces and in the Galle and Matara districts today, the Meteorology Department said.
Rains or thundershowers will occur at several places in the Uva, Eastern and Northern provinces today after 2.00 p.m.
Thunder showers are expected to increase in the Northern, North-Central, Eastern, Uva and Central provinces from August 31 till September 2.
The Met. Department said that fairly strong winds about 40-50 kmh can be expected over the Hambanthota and Maduru-oya areas.
The sun will be placed directly over districts such as Kondaichchi, Varikutturuwa, Maniyarakulam, Tammannewa, Pettewa and Irakkandi today at noon.
 Temporary localized strong winds are expected during thundershowers.
The Meteorology Department urged the public to take adequate precautions to minimize damages caused by lightning.
