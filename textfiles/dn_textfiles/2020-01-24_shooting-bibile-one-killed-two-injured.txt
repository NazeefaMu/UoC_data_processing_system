## Title: Shooting in Bibile : One killed, Two injured
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, January 24, 2020 - 12:02

Shooting in Bibile : One killed, Two injured

A woman was killed and two were injured  following a shooting incident at Pitakumbura Area in Bibile last night.According to the Police, the incident occurred at around 8:30 pm yesterday and the cause of the shooting was over a family dispute.Injured persons were rushed to the Bibila Hospital.Karandugala Police is conducting further investigations.
