## Title: Construct Exhibition 2018
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Monday, August 27, 2018 - 01:00

Construct Exhibition 2018

The Construct Exhibition 2018 was held at the BMICH from August 25-26. Sri Lanka’s premier construction expo comprised stalls on building material, engineering consultation, architecture and other services.
