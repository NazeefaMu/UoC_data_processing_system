## Title: Water cut in Tangalle
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 28, 2018 - 01:00

Water cut in Tangalle

A 48 - hour water cut would be imposed between 8 am on Friday, December 28, today, to 8 am on Sunday, December 30, due to urgent maintenance work of the Tangalle supply system, the NWSDB announced.
The water supply to Palapotha, Kandurupokuna, Labeema, Seenimodara, Unakuruwa, Goyambokka, Koskoratuwa, Koswatta, Pallikudawa and surrounding areas would be interrupted.
