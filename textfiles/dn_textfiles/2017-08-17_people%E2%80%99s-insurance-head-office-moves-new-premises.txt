## Title: People’s Insurance head office moves to new premises
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, August 17, 2017 - 01:00

People’s Insurance head office moves to new premises

People’s Insurance Limited, the Insurance arm of People’s Bank and People’s Leasing and Finance Group re-located its headquarters at its modern office complex at Havelock Road, Colombo – 05.
The Chairman People’s Bank, Hemasiri Fernando was the Chief Guest at this occasion with the participation of Chairman People’s Insurance, Jehan Amaratunga, Founder Managing Director D.P. Kumarage, Chief Executive Officer Peoples’s Bank, N. Vasantha Kumar, Chief Executive Officer People’s Leasing and Finance, Sabri Ibrahim, Chief Executive Officer People’s Insurance, Deepal Abeysekera, members of the Board of Directors, staff and policyholders.
People’s Insurance Ltd offers its General Insurance services in Sri Lanka through over hundred island wide locations.
