## Title: Heavy rains in Ampara
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, February 17, 2018 - 01:00

Heavy rains in Ampara

Heavy rains were reported in the coastal areas of the Ampara District on Thursday evening. Farmers are in critical situation as many acres of paddy fields were under water. Harvesting of the Maha season is in full swing in the Ampara district and farmers have reaped a bountiful harvest this season.
