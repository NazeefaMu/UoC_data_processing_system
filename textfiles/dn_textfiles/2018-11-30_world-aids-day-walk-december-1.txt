## Title: World AIDS day walk on December 1
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, November 30, 2018 - 01:00

World AIDS day walk on December 1

World AIDS Day is celebrated all over the world on December 1 to raise awareness among people. This year’s theme is ‘Know your status to end AIDS by 2025'. The National STD/AIDS Control Programme (NSACP) in collaboration with its stakeholders will conduct ‘World AIDS Day walk’ this Saturday, December 1. The walk will commence at 9 am from the front lawn of the Colombo Municipal Council (CMC) and end at the BMICH. There, the National programme on the 30th annual World AIDS Day will be held from 10am onwards.
Over 3000 people representing various institutions will take part in the walk. Health, Nutrition and Indigenous Medicine Minister Chamal Rajapaksa will be the chief guest.
