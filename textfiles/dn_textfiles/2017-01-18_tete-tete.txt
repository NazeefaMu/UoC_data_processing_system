## Title: Tete-a-tete
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 18, 2017 - 01:00

Tete-a-tete

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe who is in Davos to participate in the World Economic Summit met Professor of Columbia University School of International and Public Affairs Joseph Stiglitz yesterday and invited him to visit Sri Lanka to provide guidance to the government after studying the country’s future economic development plan.
