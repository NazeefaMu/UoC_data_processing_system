## Title: Cambrians win by 108 runs
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, October 31, 2016 - 01:00

Cambrians win by 108 runs

A match bag of 11 for 44 runs (7/18 and 4/26) by last years under 13 division one skipper and all-rounder Induwara de Alwis helped Prince of Wales College to beat formidable St. Joseph Vaz College by 108 runs in their inter school under 13 division one second round second match played at Wennapuwa.
So far Induwara has claimed 26 wickets with his leg spin and collected 383 runs with four half centuries.
Vazians batting first were bowled out for a paltry 92 runs and in reply Cambrians declared at 200 for 8 with the leading run getter of this tournament Vinuja Ranpul scoring 59 runs and in their second essay at stumps homesters wee reeling at 75 for 6 wickets.
St. Joseph Vaz – 92 in 40.4 overs (Induwara de Alwis 7/18 and 75/6 Chamod Heshan 24 n/o, Induwara de Alwis 4/26).
Prince of Wales 200/8 dec in 40.5 overs (Vinuja Ranpul 59, Tharindu Amerasinghe 47, Geeshan Fernando 48, Induwara de Alwis 26, Kavindu Ishan 3/46, Banuka Jayamanne 2/57).
