## Title: International Coconut Rubber and Tea (CRT) Trade Fair
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, August 12, 2017 - 01:00

International Coconut Rubber and Tea (CRT) Trade Fair

President Maithripala Sirisena inaugurated the International Coconut Rubber and Tea (CRT) Trade Fair at the Sirimvavo Bandaranaike exhibition Centre in the BMCIH premises yesterday. 
The CRT is an event with facilities for global players in coconut, rubber and tea industries to meet, network, interact, share and learn latest knowledge of respective industry operations. Here, the President at a stall. Plantation Industries Minister Navin Dissanayake was also present.
