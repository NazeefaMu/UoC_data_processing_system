## Title: Murder suspect held
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 31, 2018 - 01:00

Murder suspect held

A person who had allegedly committed the murder of his brother-in-law by chopping him and severing a leg and a hand at Siyambalakotuwa, Hamangoda, has been arrested by Katugastota Police on January 29 after he surrendered to the police.
The victim was identified as B. G. Nisanka, 30, a father of two.
Investigation are continuing.
