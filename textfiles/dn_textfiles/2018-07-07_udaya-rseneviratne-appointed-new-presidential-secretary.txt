## Title: Udaya R.Seneviratne appointed as new Presidential Secretary
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, July 7, 2018 - 01:00

Udaya R.Seneviratne appointed as new Presidential Secretary

Udaya R.Seneviratne received his appointment letter as the new Secretary to the President yesterday. He was a senior officer of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service and a former Ministry Secretary with over 37 years experience. (Picture by President’s Media Division)
