## Title: Boy reported missing found dead in jungle
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, February 27, 2018 - 10:31

Boy reported missing found dead in jungle

Picture by Jude Samantha
A 10-year-old boy who was reported missing for two days was found dead in a jungle in Iranawala, Chilaw this morning.
The deceased boy identified as Susin Nirmal was reported missing from February 25.
He is a grade 4 student in the Iranawala Government School and the third in the family of five siblings.
His father is a fisherman by profession.
A resident of the village had last seen the boy walking towards a nearby mangrove accompanied by an unidentified man, the Police said.
Further investigations are underway.
