## Title: National Chamber’s workshop on “Import Procedures & Documentation” on Feb 28
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, February 22, 2018 - 01:00

National Chamber’s workshop on “Import Procedures & Documentation” on Feb 28

The National Chamber of Commerce will conduct a one day workshop on “Import Procedures & Documentation “with special attention on WTO Goods Valuation Agreement, WTO Trade Facilitation Agreement (TFA) and Goods Classification and Harmonized System on February 28, 2018 from 8.30 am to 4.30 p.m at the National Chamber Auditorium, No 450 D. R Wijewardene Mw, Colombo – 10. The workshop is designed for those who are involved in importing, buying and sourcing from overseas including sales, purchasing, production and accounts staff. It provides an overview of the principles involved in successful importing, tips on how to avoid problems and ensure smooth running of contracts, profitably and gives practical information that will help protect your company’s interests and maximize your opportunities in the international market.Subjects that would be touched on are : Introduction to Imports, planning, International Trading, Sales Contract, Imports Restrictions , Terms of Sale, Incoterms, Payment Terms (including Letter of credit and Document Collections), Clearance of Commercial Cargo through Customs - Sea Cargo and Air Cargo, Clearance of Gift Parcels, Unaccompanied Passengers Baggage, Samples through Customs, Free Trade Agreements, WTO Goods Valuation Agreement , WTO Trade Facilitation Agreement (TFA) Goods Classification.
Participants will have an understanding of the necessary skills to ensure due diligence, seek quotations and place orders efficiently, ensure correct documentation for customs clearance into Sri Lanka including VAT compliance and complete costing to ensure profitable trading.
The resource person will be T Raviendrarajah, Director of Customs with over 34 years’ experience in this trade.More information could be obtained from
