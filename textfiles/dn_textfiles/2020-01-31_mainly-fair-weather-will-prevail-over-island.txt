## Title: Mainly fair weather will prevail over the island
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, January 31, 2020 - 09:48

Mainly fair weather will prevail over the island

Mainly fair weather will prevail over most parts of the island, said the Department of Meteorology.Misty conditions can be expected at some places in Central, Sabaragamuwa and Uva provinces during the morning.
