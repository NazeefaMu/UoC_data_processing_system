## Title: Dengue eradication programme in all seven ports
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 22, 2017 - 01:00

Dengue eradication programme in all seven ports

In accordance with the recommendations of the President, a dengue eradication programme has been implemented in all government institutions.
A dengue eradication programme will be launched at all seven ports, centered on the Colombo Port within the next few days under the leadership of Ports and Shipping Minister Mahinda Samarasinghe. This programme will be implemented regularly for the control of dengue breeding sites.
Ports and Shipping Deputy Minister Nishantha Mutuhettigama, Ministry Secretary L.P. Jayampathy, SLPA Chairman Dr. Parackrama Dassanayake, Vice Chairman P.G. Dassanayake and SLPA employees attended this programme.
