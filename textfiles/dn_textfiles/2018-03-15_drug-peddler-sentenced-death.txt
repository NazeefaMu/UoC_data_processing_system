## Title: Drug peddler sentenced to death
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Thursday, March 15, 2018 - 01:00

Drug peddler sentenced to death

A-56-year old man who was found guilty for possessing and peddling 14.6 grams of heroin in 2008 was yesterday sentenced to death by Colombo High Court.
While handing out a judgment against the accused, High Court judge Gihan Kulatunga observed that the prosecutors have proved the accused’s guilty beyond a reasonable doubt. The accused M.A. Sumanasiri, a resident of Kalubowila had been arrested by police Narcotics Bureau on October 24, 2008. The Attorney General (AG) had indicted the accused under the Poisons, Opium and Dangerous Drugs (Amendment) Act No. 13 of 1984.
