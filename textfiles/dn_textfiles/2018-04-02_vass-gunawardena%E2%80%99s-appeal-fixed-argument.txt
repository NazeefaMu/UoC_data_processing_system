## Title: Vass Gunawardena’s appeal fixed for argument
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Monday, April 2, 2018 - 14:44

Vass Gunawardena’s appeal fixed for argument

 
The appeal petition filed by former DIG Vaas Gunawardena, his son Ravindu Vaas Gunawardena and four police officers challenging Colombo High Court's judgment to convict them for committing the murder of millionaire businessman Mohamed Shyam was fixed for argument by the Supreme Court, today.They were sentenced to death by the Colombo High Court Trial–at-Bar Bench for committing the murder of millionaire businessman Mohamed Shyam in 2013 at Dompe.
