## Title: No decision
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, July 21, 2017 - 01:00

No decision

Royal College, Colombo held St Thomas College, Matara to a no decision in their inter school under 15 division one tournament cricket match played at Colombo.
Batting first Royalists declared at 200 for 9 with Sandun Appuhamy scoring 65 runs and at the close Matara Thomians were 146 for 8 with Hirantha Jayasinghe scoring 58 runs and Kanika Dayarathne captured 5 for 61.
Chief scores
Royal College – 200/9 in 60 overs (Sandun Appuhamy 65, Sunal Amarasekera 23, Kanika Dayarathne 27 n/o, Nipuna Ruchira 3/84, Hirantha Jayasinghe 3/43)
St Thomas' College – 146/8 in 41 overs (Hirantha Jayasinghe 58, Sameera Rashmika 27, Kanika Dayarathne 5/61, Ushan Boruppa 2/21)
