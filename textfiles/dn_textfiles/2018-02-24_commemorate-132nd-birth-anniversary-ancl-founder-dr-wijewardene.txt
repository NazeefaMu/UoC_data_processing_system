## Title: Commemorate the 132nd birth anniversary of ANCL founder D.R. Wijewardene
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, February 24, 2018 - 01:00

Commemorate the 132nd birth anniversary of ANCL founder D.R. Wijewardene

An all night pirith chanting followed by an alms giving was held yesterday to commemorate the 132nd birth anniversary of ANCL founder D.R. Wijewardene at the Lake House premises. Picture shows Ranjith Wijewardene, son of D.R. Wijewardene offering Pirikara to the Mahanayake of the Amarapura Chapter Most Ven.Kotugoda Dhammawasa Thera. Anunayake of the Rammanya Maha Nikaya Ven.Matale Dhammakusala Anunayake Thera, Chief Incumbent of the Mihintale Raja Maha Viharaya Ven. Walawahengunaweve Dhammarathana Thera,Ven.Dr.Vitharandeniye Kassapa Thera of the Hunupitiya Gangaramaya and Senior officials of the ANCL management were present. Picture by Wimal Karunathilake
