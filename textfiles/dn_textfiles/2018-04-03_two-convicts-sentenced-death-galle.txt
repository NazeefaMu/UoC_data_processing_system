## Title: Two convicts sentenced to death in Galle
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, April 3, 2018 - 11:13

Two convicts sentenced to death in Galle

 
Two individuals who were convicted of murdering a woman and injuring two other individuals by striking a bomb were sentenced to death in Galle, yesterday.
The bomb strike took place on May 31, 1999 in Wadugegoda, Ahangama.
The two convicts were sentenced to death by the Galle High Court and an open warrant was issued against one convict, for evading courts.
