## Title: Sri Lanka Engineers salute new Army Commander
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, July 15, 2017 - 01:00

Sri Lanka Engineers salute new Army Commander

Sri Lanka Engineers (SLE) of the Army in conformity with military traditions presented a Guard Turnout plus a Parade to the newly-appointed Commander of the Army, Lieutenant General Mahesh Senanayake, on Thursday (13) at the SLE Regimental Headquarters, Panagoda. Lieutenant General Mahesh Senanayake before he took an inter-unit transfer to the Special Forces (SF) in 1989 served in the SLE after receiving his commissions from the Sri Lanka Military Academy (SLMA).
On his arrival at the premises, a Guard Turnout greeted him after he was welcomed by SLE Colonel Commandant Major General Sudantha Ranasinghe.Tthe Chief Guest laid a floral tribute to the SLE war memorial and paid his respect to the memory of fallen SLE War Heroes.
He was ushered to the main area for receipt of military honours in a parade accompanied by the SLE Colonel Commandant.The Commander of the Army, the Chief Guest for the occasion reviewed the parade upon the formal request of the parade commander.
Thereafter, the Commander of the Army delivered a customary address to the troops at the venue. Afterwards, the Commander shared refreshments with All Ranks and sat for a group photo with the attendees at the location.
The visiting Commander of the Army took time off to have a look at the ongoing SLE auditorium construction project in the company of Colonel Commandant, Centre Commandant, and a few Senior Officers.
Before his departure, he signed in the Visitors’ Book at the SLE Regimental Headquarters.
The SLE Senior Officers and officers participated.
