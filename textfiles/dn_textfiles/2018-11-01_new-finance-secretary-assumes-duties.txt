## Title: New Finance Secretary assumes duties
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 1, 2018 - 01:00

New Finance Secretary assumes duties

Deputy Treasury Secretary S.R. Attygalle who has been appointed as Secretary to the Finance and Economic Affairs Ministry and Secretary to the Treasury by President Maithripala Sirisena, assumed duties in his new post at the Finance Ministry yesterday.
Attygalle, who is from the Central Bank was thus far serving on release, as a Deputy Secretary to the Treasury for more than 10 years. He holds a Master’s (MSc) degree in Quantitative Development Economics from the University of Warwick, UK, and a Bachelor of Science (BSc) degree in Physical Science from the University of Colombo.
Attygalle has also served as the Director General of the Fiscal Policy Department and Public Enterprises Department of the Finance and Planning Ministry and has contributed substantially to the government’s endeavours in the fiscal consolidation process.
He has also served as a Director and Acting Chairman of National Savings Bank and a Director to the Board of the Regional Development Bank and also represented the Treasury as a Board Member in several Boards in key government institutions such as the Sri Lanka Ports Authority.
At present, he represents the Treasury at the Securities and Exchange Commission of Sri Lanka.
