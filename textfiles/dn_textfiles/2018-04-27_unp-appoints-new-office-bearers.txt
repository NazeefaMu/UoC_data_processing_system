## Title: UNP appoints  new office-bearers
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, April 27, 2018 - 01:00

UNP appoints  new office-bearers

Kurunegala District UNP Parliamentarian and United National Party (UNP) Re-organisation Central Working Committee Committee Member J. C. Alawathuwala told the Daily News yesterday in Kurunegala, that top office positions of the UNP were nominated yesterday at the re-organization committee meeting which was held under the patronage of Prime Minister and UNP Leader Ranil Wickremesinghe.
Accordingly, Education Minister Akila Viraj Kariyawasam was nominated for the post of UNP General Secretary, while former UNP General Secretary Minister Kabir Hashim was nominated as UNP Chairman.
Minister Navin Dissanayake was nominated for the post of UNP National Organiser, while Minister Sajith Premadasa and Ravi Karunanayake were nominated as UNP Deputy Leader and Vice Chairman, respectively.
