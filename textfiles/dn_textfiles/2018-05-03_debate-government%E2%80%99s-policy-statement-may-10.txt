## Title: Debate on Government’s Policy Statement on May 10
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 3, 2018 - 12:21

Debate on Government’s Policy Statement on May 10

 
Party Leaders decided to hold a one day debate on Government’s Policy Statement on May 10 in Parliament this morning.
Furthermore it was decided in the meeting to deliver Government’s Policy Statement on May 8 at the inaugural session of the new Parliament.
