## Title: Yoshitha’s travel ban lifted for 20 days
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Friday, November 24, 2017 - 15:15

Yoshitha’s travel ban lifted for 20 days

 
Yoshitha Rajapaksa, the second son of former President Mahinda Rajapaksa whose passport was impounded by Court as part of Magisterial inquiry over alleged money laundering at Carlton Sports Network (CSN) was today permitted to travel abroad for a period of 20 days by the Colombo High Court.
