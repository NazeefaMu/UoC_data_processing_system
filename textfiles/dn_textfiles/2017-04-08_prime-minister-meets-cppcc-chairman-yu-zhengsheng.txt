## Title: Prime Minister meets CPPCC Chairman Yu Zhengsheng
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, April 8, 2017 - 01:00

Prime Minister meets CPPCC Chairman Yu Zhengsheng

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe yesterday met Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People’s Political consultative Conference (CPPCC), Yu Zhengsheng at Temple Trees yesterday. Ministers Ravi Karunanayake, Malik Samarawickrema, Sagala Ratnayake and DM Swaminathan were present.
