## Title: ‘Seylan Pahasara’ opens libraries  in Gampaha,  Trincomalee
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Monday, September 25, 2017 - 01:00

‘Seylan Pahasara’ opens libraries  in Gampaha,  Trincomalee

Envisioning to offer quality education to the children of Sri Lanka, “Seylan Pahasara” opened its 132nd and 133rd Libraries for school in Gampaha and Trincomalee respectively.
The Bank handed over the 132nd library to the students of St. Peter’s College in Gampaha district, enabling over 3,000 students and teachers at the school to benefit from this facility.
St. Joseph’s College, Trincomalee received the 133rd library, with special equipment including a multimedia projector allowing students to benefit with other infrastructure upgraded facilities.
