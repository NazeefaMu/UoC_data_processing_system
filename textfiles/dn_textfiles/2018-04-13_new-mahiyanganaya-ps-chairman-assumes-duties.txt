## Title: New Mahiyanganaya PS  Chairman assumes duties
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, April 13, 2018 - 01:00

New Mahiyanganaya PS  Chairman assumes duties

R. M. Karu Weeraratna, who was recently elected Mahiyanganaya Pradeshiya Sabha (PS) Chairman, assumed duties on Wednesday (11).
The Mahiyanganaya District Secretariat had organised a ceremony to welcome new Chairman Weeraratna, Vice Chairman E. M. Sampath Ekanayaka and all other newly-elected officials.
Mahiyanganaya United National Party (UNP) Chief Organiser and Uva Provincial Council member Upali Senaratna, Mahiyanganaya Sri Lanka Freedom Party (SLFP) Chief Organiser K. P. Gunawardhana, Mahiyanganaya District Secretary D. M. Dissanayaka and a group of government officials were present at the function.
