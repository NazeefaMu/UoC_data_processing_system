## Title: ITAK to contest four LG bodies in Batticaloa
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Saturday, December 9, 2017 - 01:00

ITAK to contest four LG bodies in Batticaloa

The Ilangai Tamil Arasu Kachchi (ITAK) party has paid deposits to contest four Local Government bodies in the Batticaloa district. Ilangai Tamil Arasu Kachchi (ITAK) General Secretary K. Thurairajasingam speaking after paying the deposit for the Koralai Pattu, Eravur Town, Eravur Pattu and Manmunai Pattu for the forthcoming local government election said officially no intimation has been given of any of the constituent parties leaving the ITAK.
There are twelve local government bodies in the Batticaloa district. The Ilangai Tamil Arasu Kachchi (ITAK) has paid the deposit in respect of four local bodies at the Batticaloa district Assistant Election Commissioner’s office yesterday (8).
ITAK General Secretary K. Thurairajasingam, former TNA Parliamentarians Pon Selvarasa and P.Ariyanenthiran led supporters to the election office and paid the deposits with the Batticaloa District Assistant Election Commissioner S. Saseelan.
The General Secretary said that they are confident of winning every seat they are contesting under the House symbol.
