## Title: Passenger on top of 'Samudra' severely injured
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 01:00

Passenger on top of 'Samudra' severely injured

A man travelling on top of the ‘Samudra Devi’ express train plying from Maradana to Galle was critically injured on May 4 after being entangled with a wire fixed over the railway track.
The victim was admitted to the Balapitiya Base Hospital and later transferred to the Karapitiya Teaching Hospital.
Kosgoda Police are investigating.
