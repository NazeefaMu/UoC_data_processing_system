## Title: Father sentenced for assaulting his young son
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Thursday, May 3, 2018 - 01:00

Father sentenced for assaulting his young son

A 32-year-old father was found guilty of assaulting his 10-year-old son, a school boy and causing grievous hurt to him as a result of a heated argument.
Pronouncing the judgement in the case concluding the trial, the Panadura Provincial High Court Judge Dhamith Thotawatta on May 2 said, the accused is found guilty of the indictment and sentenced him to four years imprisonment and fined Rs. 5,000 in default of two months simple imprisonment. The Judge also ordered the accused to pay Rs. one lakh as compensation to the injured child.
In this case, Punchipatabedige Dimuthi Kumara of Bellapitiya, Hoarana was indicted by the Attorney General with assaulting his 10-year-old son, a school boy and causing grievous hurt to him on November 17, 2011 at his house by assaulting him.
State Counsel Jayani Wegodapola appeared for the prosecution.
