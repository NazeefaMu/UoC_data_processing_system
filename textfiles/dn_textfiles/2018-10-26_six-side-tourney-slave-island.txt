## Title: Six-a-side tourney at Slave Island
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, October 26, 2018 - 01:00

Six-a-side tourney at Slave Island

A six-a-side Cricket tournament organised by the Past Pupils Association of Maha Bodhi Vidyalaya, Colombo on a concept of its President Provincial Council member Mohamed Akram will be held at the Malay playground Slave Island, Colombo on October 28 from 8 am. The event has been organised to mark the schools 117th anniversary.
All past pupils have been invited to attend the occasion. Further details could be obtained from Chamika on mobile 077 2269005.
