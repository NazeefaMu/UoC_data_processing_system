## Title: President opens National Art Centre in Kundasale
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, June 4, 2018 - 01:00

President opens National Art Centre in Kundasale

The Jathika Kala Nikethanaya (National Art Centre) in Kundasale was vested in the public on Saturday (2) by President Maithripala Sirisena.
The Centre, constructed at a cost of Rs. 465 million, has the capacity to accommodate 100 artistes at a time. The Jathika Kala Nikethanaya comprises an open air theatre and two rehearsal halls on five acres of land. It is the only national-level theatre in Sri Lanka.
The Cultural Affairs Department plans to use this centre to conduct training courses for young people and artistes. President Sirisena, after unveiling the plaque, made a note in the souvenir book for special guests. Minister Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe, Central Province Governor P.B. Dissanayake, Chief Minister Sarath Ekanayake, Cultural Affairs Department Director General Anusha Gokula and senior artistes were also present on this occasion.
