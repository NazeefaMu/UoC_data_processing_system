## Title: Last rites for Most  Ven. Ahangama Ananda  Thera
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, April 11, 2018 - 01:00

Last rites for Most  Ven. Ahangama Ananda  Thera

Vishwa Keerthi Shri, Sahithya Visharada, Adikarana Sanganayaka Most Ven. Ahangama Ananda Thera, the Chief Incumbent of the Shri Wijayaramaya, Wellawatta and Chief Advisor of the Methsaviya Foundation passed away on April 7.
The funeral was held on April 9 at 4.00 pm at the Henry Pedris Park, Colombo 5.
