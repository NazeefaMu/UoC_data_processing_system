## Title: Netherlands crowned Miss Tourism 2016
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Friday, February 26, 2016 - 01:00

Netherlands crowned Miss Tourism 2016

Competing with nineteen other countries, Miss Netherlands Dome Akkaniphat was crowned Miss Tourism on February 21 at the grand finale of the Miss Tourism Sri Lanka International Pageant held at the Hilton Colombo. Miss Malaysia Rubini Sambanthan came in as First Runner-Up while Miss Lithunia Enrika Trepkute-Zilinskiene came Second Runner-Up. These are the highlights of the grand finale caught on lens by Sulochana Gamage.
