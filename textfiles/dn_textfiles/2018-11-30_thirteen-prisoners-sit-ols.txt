## Title: Thirteen prisoners to sit for O/Ls
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, November 30, 2018 - 01:00

Thirteen prisoners to sit for O/Ls

Thirteen prisoners will sit for the GCE O/L examination this year, according to Prison Spokesman Thushara Upuldeniya.
They are from the Welikada Prison, Colombo Magazine Prison and Watareka Prison, he said.
Eleven are from the Welikada Prison, while one is from the Colombo Magazine Prison and one from the Watareka Prison.
One of the candidates will sit the examination in the Tamil medium.
The Prisons Department said the Examinations Department will set up an examination centre at the Colombo Magazine Prison premises for these inmates to sit the GCE O/L examination.
