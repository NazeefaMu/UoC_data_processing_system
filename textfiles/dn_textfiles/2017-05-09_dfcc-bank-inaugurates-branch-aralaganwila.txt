## Title: DFCC Bank inaugurates branch in Aralaganwila
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 01:00

DFCC Bank inaugurates branch in Aralaganwila

In keeping with its commitment to ensure financial inclusion and facilitate easy access to its wide array of commercial and developmental banking services, DFCC Bank opened a new branch in Aralaganwila on April 27.
The DCEO of the Bank, Lakshman Silva; senior officials and staff from the Bank, and representatives from the local community were present at the branch opening.
The Bank offers a variety of retail banking products and services such as current and savings accounts, fixed deposits, loans, pawning services, leasing facilities, NRFC/RFC accounts, credit cards and remittances.
The business communities in the area can also avail of DFCC’s expertise in Corporate and SME Banking, which are further augmented by the Bank’s innovative service delivery channels. The new branch is located at New Town, Aralaganwila.
