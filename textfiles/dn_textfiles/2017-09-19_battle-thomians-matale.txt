## Title: Battle of the Thomians at Matale
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, September 19, 2017 - 01:00

Battle of the Thomians at Matale


The third encounter of the ‘Battle of the Thomians’ Hockey Big match between S.Thomas’ College,Mount Lavinia and St.Thomas’ College,Matale two of the leading Hockey playing Schools in the Island will be played at Edward Park Matale on 1 st October,2017(Sunday) at 4.00 p.m. Prior to this big event the Junior and Old Boys hockey matches will be played at 2.00 p.m.and 3.00 p.m. respectively. The match sponsors are Nations Trust Bank and Ceylinco Life Insurance Company which handed over the Sponsorship package to St.Thomas’ Mr.Lavinia.
