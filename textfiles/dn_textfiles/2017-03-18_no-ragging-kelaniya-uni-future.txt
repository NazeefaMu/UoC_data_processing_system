## Title: 'No ragging at Kelaniya Uni in future'
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, March 18, 2017 - 01:00

'No ragging at Kelaniya Uni in future'

The University of Kelaniya would put an end to ragging and legal actions would be taken against the students who engage in ragging, Kelaniya University Vice Chancellor Sunanda Madduma Bandara said.
He was speaking at the ceremony to welcome 2550 new students for the academic year of 2015/2016.
The Vice Chancellor said this academic year had reported the highest number of student enrollments to the university.
The ceremony was themed ‘stop ragging and build bonds’. Senior students welcomed the newcomers by presenting souvenirs. Induction programme for the new students will be held from March 8 to 20 under the theme ‘To establish a centre to create and provide knowledge in order to achieve sustainable development’.
Senior Prof. Semasinghe participated.
