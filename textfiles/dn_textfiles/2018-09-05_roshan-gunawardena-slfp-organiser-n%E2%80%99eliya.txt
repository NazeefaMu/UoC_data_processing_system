## Title: Roshan Gunawardena, SLFP organiser for N’Eliya
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Wednesday, September 5, 2018 - 01:00

Roshan Gunawardena, SLFP organiser for N’Eliya

Roshan Gunawardena was appointed as the new SLFP organiser for the Nuwara Eliya electorate yesterday.
The President has also appointed him as the Chairman of the Nuwara Eliya Regional Coordinating Committee.
Gunawardena obtained his letter of appointment from President Maithripala Sirisena at the President’s office yesterday morning, the President’s Media Division said.
