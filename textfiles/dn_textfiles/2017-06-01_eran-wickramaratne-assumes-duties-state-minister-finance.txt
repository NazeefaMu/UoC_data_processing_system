## Title: Eran Wickramaratne assumes duties as State Minister of Finance
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 1, 2017 - 13:30

Eran Wickramaratne assumes duties as State Minister of Finance

 
New State Minister of Finance Eran Wickramaratne assumed duties at the Ministry of Finance this morning.
Secretary to the Ministry Of Finance Dr.R.H.S Samarathunga, Deputy Secretary to Treasury G.D.C Ekanayake and the Additional Secretary to the Ministry R. Semasinghe were present.
