## Title: JBIC pledge to support Sri Lanka’s economic endeavours
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 17, 2017 - 14:00

JBIC pledge to support Sri Lanka’s economic endeavours

 
The Japan Bank of International Cooperation (JBIC) Chief Executive Officer (CEO) and Executive Managing Director Tadashi Maeda pledged to render their full support to Sri Lanka in future economic development endeavours.
He said this at a meeting held with Finance and Mass Media Minister Mangala Samaraweera on the sidelines of the World Bank Annual Meeting held in Washington, D.C.
The JBIC CEO and Executive Managing Director and a delegation will visit Sri Lanka on an official tour to further discuss this matter, in December.
