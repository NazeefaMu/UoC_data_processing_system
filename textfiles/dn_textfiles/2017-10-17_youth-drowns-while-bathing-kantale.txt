## Title: Youth drowns while bathing in Kantale
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 17, 2017 - 01:00

Youth drowns while bathing in Kantale

A 20- year-old youth drowned when he went for a bath at Kantale Tank in Trincomalee district on October12. He was identified as M. J. Mohamed Fahim, of Al-Minan Road, Maruthamunai in the Ampara district. The investigations are continuing.
