## Title: Drug peddlers arrested in Vavuniya
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, March 18, 2017 - 01:00

Drug peddlers arrested in Vavuniya

Three suspects including a husband and wife involved in the illegal sale of heroin targeting employees of state institutions and schoolchildren in Vavuniya were arrested by Police in the Vavuniya town recently.
Vavuniya Police said investigations revealed that the suspects had engaged in the illegal business taking cover behind a tyre repair shop maintained in Vavuniya.
They had used their residence to pack the drugs bought from Colombo, into small packets and sell each packet for Rs. 2,000.
Further investigations are continuing.
