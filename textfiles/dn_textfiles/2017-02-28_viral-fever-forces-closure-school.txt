## Title: Viral fever forces closure of school
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, February 28, 2017 - 01:00

Viral fever forces closure of school

The Pattiyagama Sinhala Primary School in Patha Hewaheta is closed indefinitely due to the high incidence of a contagious viral fever among school children. The Deltota Medical Officer of Health ordered the closure of the school without approval from the Kandy Zonal Education Director’s office.
