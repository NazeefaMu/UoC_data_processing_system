## Title: Mahanama’s Pawan and Pawantha figure in double century
## Supertitle: Singer Cup U-19 Schools’ Cricket
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, February 2, 2018 - 01:00

Singer Cup U-19 Schools’ Cricket
Mahanama’s Pawan and Pawantha figure in double century

Pawan Ratnayake (175) and Pawantha Weerasinghe (132) figured in a 244-run stand for the third wicket for Mahanama College against President’s College Kotte in their ‘Singer Cup’ Under 19 inter-schools cricket match played at Welagedara Stadium Kurunegala yesterday.
Ratnayake scored 175 off 237 balls with 23 fours and Weerasinghe 132 off 205 balls with 17 fours and 2 sixes.
St Sebastian’s College Katuneriya’s left-arm spinner Sithum Akilanka took a match bag of 12 wickets against Sri Jayawardhanapura MV Kotte at Katuneriya to help them win by innings and 287 runs.
