## Title: President Sirisena leaves for Bangladesh tomorrow
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, July 12, 2017 - 12:00

President Sirisena leaves for Bangladesh tomorrow

 
The President’s Media Division said today that President Maithripala Sirisena will leave for Bangladesh on a state visit tomorrow morning till July 15.
The President’s Media Division spokesperson told Daily News Online that the President will hold discussions with Bangladesh President Abdul Hamid and Premier Sheikh Hasina during the visit.
At these meetings President Sirisena will hold bilateral discussions and come into bilateral agreements to strengthen economic and agricultural ties between the two countries.
He is also due to attend a business forum on the final day of the visit where several agreements will be signed between Sri Lankan and Bangladesh businessmen.
