## Title: A copy of 'Nivana' presented to PM
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, April 1, 2017 - 01:00

A copy of 'Nivana' presented to PM

A copy of 'Nivana' national Buddhist newspaper was presented to Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe by its Consultant Editor Ven. Yatagalamala Sumanasara Thera at Temple Trees, Colombo recently. 'Nivana' is published monthly by the Damma Voice International Buddhist Centre on the Pattivila Road, Gonawala, Kelaniya.
