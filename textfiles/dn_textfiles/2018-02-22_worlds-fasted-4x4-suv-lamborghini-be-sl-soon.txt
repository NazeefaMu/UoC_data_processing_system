## Title: Worlds fasted 4x4 SUV Lamborghini to be in SL soon
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, February 22, 2018 - 01:00

Worlds fasted 4x4 SUV Lamborghini to be in SL soon

World’s fastest 4x4 SUV Lamborghini Urus is to be launched in Colombo by Micro Cars soon.
The principals of the company will be in Colombo to plan this launch.
An official from Micro Cars said that in the recent past many top end cars have are entering, Sri Lanka which included, Rolls-Royce, Bentley, Maybach, Maserati, Porsche McLaran, Ferrari, Tesla, MB, BMW, Lexus, Audi etc.
“With the entry of these exotic brands to Sri Lanka which have high taxes have helped Sri Lanka Custom Department to earn higher revenue as well with smaller number of vehicles to handle.”
Franchisees and most importantly Vehicle lovers too benefit from these new arrivals. This will certainly lead to change the future lifestyles of vehicles and it seems that Sri Lankans are now moving from the age old top brands to exotic brands.”
