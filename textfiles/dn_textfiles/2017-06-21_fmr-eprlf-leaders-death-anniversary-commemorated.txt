## Title: Fmr EPRLF leader's death anniversary commemorated
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 21, 2017 - 01:00

Fmr EPRLF leader's death anniversary commemorated

A commemorative ceremony to mark the 27th death anniversary of former EPRLF leader K. Pathmanabha, who was assassinated by the LTTE, was held at the Kurunegala District Secretariat.
It was organized by the Tamil Social Democratic party-a splinter group of the EPRLF-on June 19.
Twenty other EPRLF members killed by the LTTE were also commemorated, after which a free distribution of spectacles and wheelchairs followed.
Tamil Social Democratic Party Secretary S. Sivakumar was also present.
