## Title: Thirimanne to lead SLC President’s XI against India
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, July 21, 2017 - 01:00

Thirimanne to lead SLC President’s XI against India

Sri Lanka cricketer Lahiru Thirimanne will lead the Sri Lanka Cricket President’s XI in a two-day warm-up match against India at the CCC grounds today and tomorrow.
Sri lanka cricket president’s team: Danushka Gunathilaka, Kaushal Silva, Lahiru Thirimanne (captain), Dhananjaya de Silva, Roshen Silva, Ashan Priyanjan, Sandun Weerakkody (wk), Kithuruwan Vithanage, Kasun Rajitha, Lahiru Gamage, Vikum Sanjaya, Vishwa Fernando, Shehan Jayasuriya, Tharindu Kaushal.
