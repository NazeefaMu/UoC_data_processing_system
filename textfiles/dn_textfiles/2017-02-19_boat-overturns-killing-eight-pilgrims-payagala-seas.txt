## Title: Boat overturns killing eight pilgrims in Payagala seas
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Sunday, February 19, 2017 - 13:15

Boat overturns killing eight pilgrims in Payagala seas

(File Photo)
A boat carrying pilgrims overturned killing at least eight people in Payagala, Beruwela seas, today (19).
More pilgrims are reportedly missing.
The deceased were taken to the Nagoda Hospital, Police said.
