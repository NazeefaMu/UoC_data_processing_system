## Title: Possible disqualification for five exam cheats
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 14, 2018 - 10:44

Possible disqualification for five exam cheats

The results of five students, who sat the G.C.E Ordinary Level Examination 2018, have decided to disqualify due to various irregularities such as using mobile phones to answer questions and impersonating,Examinations Commissioner General Sanath Pujitha said.
