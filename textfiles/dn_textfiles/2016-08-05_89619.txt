## Title: Speaker ratifies RTI Bill
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Friday, August 5, 2016 - 01:00

Speaker ratifies RTI Bill

Speaker Karu Jayasuriya ratified the long awaited Right to Information (RTI) Bill yesterday.
The Bill was unanimously passed in
Parliament on June 24 with amendments. Parliamentary sources revealed that Speaker Jayasuriya signed the Bill into law.
