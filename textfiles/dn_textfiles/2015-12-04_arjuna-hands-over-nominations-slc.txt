## Title: Arjuna hands over nominations to SLC
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, December 4, 2015 - 09:30

Arjuna hands over nominations to SLC

 
Ports and Shipping Minister Arjuna Ranatungahad reportedly handed over nominations to the Sri Lanka Cricket (SLC) this morning (04) for the post of Vice President.
He will be representing Nishantha Ranatunga’s party at this year’s contest.
Nishantha Ranatunga submitted nominations for the post of President of SLC on 26 November.He is a former SLC Secretary who was removed along with other board members including President Jayantha Dharmadasa before the government appointed an interim committee.
