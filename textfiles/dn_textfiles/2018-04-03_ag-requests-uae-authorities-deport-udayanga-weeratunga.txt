## Title: AG requests UAE authorities to deport Udayanga Weeratunga
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Tuesday, April 3, 2018 - 16:06

AG requests UAE authorities to deport Udayanga Weeratunga

 
The Attorney General informed Colombo Fort Magistrate that the Ministry of Foreign Affairs has requested the United Arab Emirates (UAE) authorities to deport former Ambassador to Russia Udayanga Weeratunga to Sri Lanka in connection with the controversial MiG Aircrafts deal.Udayanga was arrested by UAE authorities and is detained in Abu Dhabi. At a previous occasion, the Magistrate issued an open warrant written in English through the Interpol to arrest Udayanga Weeratunga, a first cousin of former President Mahinda Rajapaksa.
He is alleged to have been directly involved in a financial fraud which took place when procuring seven MiG-27 ground attack crafts for the Sri Lanka Air Force (SLFA).
