## Title: Student hit on the head with tile
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, June 21, 2017 - 01:00

Student hit on the head with tile

A Grade 11 science stream student of a leading school in Nittambuwa was admitted to the Wathupitiwala Hospital yesterday after being hit on the head with a tile by another student in the commerce stream.
Police investigations revealed the same student had started a quarrel with the science stream student in the morning and assaulted him.
The principal had summoned the two boys and asked them to settle matters amicably. Subsequently, the commerce stream student had attacked the science stream student on the back of his head with a tile.
Investigations revealed that the suspect student had been harassing other colleagues constantly claiming that his father is a member of the security forces.
Nittambuwa police are investigating.
