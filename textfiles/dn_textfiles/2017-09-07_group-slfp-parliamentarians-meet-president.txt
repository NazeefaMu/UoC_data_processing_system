## Title: Group of SLFP Parliamentarians to meet President
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 01:00

Group of SLFP Parliamentarians to meet President

Deputy Minister Arundika Fernando yesterday said that that a group of SLFP Parliamentarians including himself will make a request to President Maithripala Sirisena to leave the government.
He said that they will discuss the issues with the President after the SLFP Parliamentary Group Meeting which was scheduled to be held at the Presidential Secretariat yesterday. However, the meeting was in progress at the time this edition went to press.
