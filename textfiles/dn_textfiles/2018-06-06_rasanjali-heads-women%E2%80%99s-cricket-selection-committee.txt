## Title: Rasanjali heads women’s cricket selection committee
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Wednesday, June 6, 2018 - 01:00

Rasanjali heads women’s cricket selection committee

Rasanjali de Alwis was appointed head selector of the four-member women’s cricket selection committee for a period of one year commencing May 16, 2018.
The four-member selection committee comprises Ms. Rasanjali de Alwis (chairman), Ms. K.K.J. Indika (member), Ms. B.A.T.N. Gunaratne (member), Waruna Waragoda (member).
