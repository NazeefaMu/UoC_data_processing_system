## Title: Science Minister visits NSBM
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, June 19, 2018 - 01:00

Science Minister visits NSBM

Science, Technology, Research, Skills Development and Vocational Training and Upcountry Heritage Minister Dr. Sarath Amunugama visited the National School of Business Management (NSBM) Green University Town on June 8. The minister was welcomed by newly-appointed NSBM Chairman P. Ranepura and NSBM Vice Chancellor Dr. E. A. Weerasinghe. A small gathering was held at the NSBM auditorium, during which the progress of the NSBM Project was presented to Minister Dr. Amunugama by Dr. Weerasinghe. The university also presented the Chathur Widha Man Peth, an all-island programme for the development of schoolchildren to suit the 21st Century.
Further, the NSBM’s Information Technology Research and Development (IT R and D) and Business Incubation Centre was opened by the minister during the tour and three companies, namely 99X Technology Limited, Omobio Private Limited and Creative Technology Solutions Private Limited commenced their operations at the Incubation Centre.
The NSMB also signed Memorandums of Understanding (MoUs) with the Charted Institute of Management (CIMA) and the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA), to provide concessionary benefits and accelerated pathways to NSBM undergraduates for the advancement of their professional qualifications.
Dr. Sarath Amunugama expressed his satisfaction and gratitude to the services provided by the NSBM, and also took part in a tree-planting ceremony to mark the occasion. A special gift was also presented to the minister by Dr. E. A. Weerasinghe during the visit.
Science, Technology, Research, Skills Development and Vocational Training and Upcountry Heritage Ministry Secretary Sandya Wijebandara, NSBM Deputy Vice Chancellor Chaminda Rathnayake, academic and non-academic staff of the NSBM and others participated.
