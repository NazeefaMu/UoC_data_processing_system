## Title: Stamp exhibition
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, May 15, 2018 - 01:00

Stamp exhibition

The Postal Department will hold an exhibition of stamps from May 25–27 at the postal headquarters on D.R. Wijewardene Road, Colombo 10.
Old stamps, coins, notes, both local and foreign will be exhibited.
