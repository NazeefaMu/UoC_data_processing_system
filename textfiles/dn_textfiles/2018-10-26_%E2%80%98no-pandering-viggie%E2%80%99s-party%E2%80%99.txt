## Title: ‘No pandering for Viggie’s party’
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, October 26, 2018 - 01:00

‘No pandering for Viggie’s party’

National Integration, Reconciliation and Official Languages Minister Mano Ganesan said that his party would not pander the new political party formed by Northern Chief Minister C.V. Vigneswaran.
The minister addressed the media at a welcome ceremony in Vavuniya during his visit to Jaffna.
