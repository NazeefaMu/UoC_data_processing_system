## Title: Former President Mahinda Rajapaksa’s sister passes away
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, May 8, 2017 - 11:15

Former President Mahinda Rajapaksa’s sister passes away

 
Former President Mahinda Rajapaksa’s younger sister Gandini Rajapaksa Ranawaka passed earlier today while being treated at a private hospital.
She was fifty-eight at the time.
Her remains will be at her residence in Ebuldeniya, Nugegoda.
Final rites will be performed at the Udahamulla burial ground, tomorrow evening.
Gandini Rajapaksa is the youngest of the Rajapaksa family of nine.
