## Title: Police fire at speeding motorcycle; two injured
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, February 23, 2017 - 01:00

Police fire at speeding motorcycle; two injured

Two persons riding a motorcycle along CEB Mawatha Digana were injured when two constables attached to the Teldeniya Police on motor patrol fired at the vehicle which sped away ignoring a police halt order. The duo identified as Indika Sanjeewa (35) and Ruchira Gayan (28) of Rajawella Digana were admitted to the Teldeniya hospital. Investigations are continuing.
