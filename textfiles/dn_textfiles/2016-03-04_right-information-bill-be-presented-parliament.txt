## Title: Right to Information Bill to be presented in Parliament
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Friday, March 4, 2016 - 01:00

Right to Information Bill to be presented in Parliament

 
The long awaited Right to Information Bill will be presented to Parliament on the next sitting day (March 8), Mass Media and Parliamentary Reforms Minister Gayantha Karunathilake said.
He made these observations at yesterday's Cabinet briefing at the Information Department Auditorium.
He also stated this Bill will be presented in Parliament on March 8.
He added that this was a historic Bill that is to be presented in Parliament. The Bill will be included in the agenda for its first reading.
Agriculture Minister Duminda Dissanayake, Mass Media Deputy Minister Karunaratne Paanavithana, Foreign Affairs Deputy Minister Dr Harsha de Silva and Acting Development Strategies and International Trade Minister Sujeewa Senasinghe also present on the occasion.
