## Title: Thewarapperuma resigns as UNP Bulathsinhala organiser
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Thursday, March 22, 2018 - 01:00

Thewarapperuma resigns as UNP Bulathsinhala organiser

Deputy Minister-Internal Affairs Cultural Affairs and Wayamba Development Palitha Thewarapperuma has resigned from the post of UNP organiser for Bulathsinhala electorate
Thewarapperuma has sent a letter citing the reasons as to why he decided to vacate the post to Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe.
Thewarapperuma in a lengthy letter to the Prime Minister has said that he remained with the party when many UNP strongmen left the party seeking perks and privileges and he protected and promoted the party in the district at the risk of his life.
