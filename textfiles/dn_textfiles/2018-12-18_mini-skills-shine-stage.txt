## Title: Mini skills shine on stage
## Supertitle: 
## Section: T&C
## Date: Tuesday, December 18, 2018 - 01:00

Mini skills shine on stage

Asiri Early Childhood Development Centre, Nusawella, Kandy held its annual end of the year concert titled 'Asiri Variety Show' at the St Anthony’s College auditorium in Katugastota recently. The tiny tots of the school showcased their skills in a variety of colourful items. The Principal of the school K M P K Wickramasinghe graced the event.
