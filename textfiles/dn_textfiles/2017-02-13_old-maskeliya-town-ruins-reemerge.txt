## Title: Old Maskeliya Town ruins reemerge
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, February 13, 2017 - 01:00

Old Maskeliya Town ruins reemerge

The ruins of old Maskeliya Town has reemerged due to the reduction of the Moussakelle reservoir’s water level. The water level of the reservoir had gone down alarmingly due to the prolonged drought.
Meanwhile the water level at Carstlereigh and Canyon which feed the hydro electric plants at Laxapana, new Laxapana and Canyon had been reduced to nearly 40 percent of its capacity. A number of people visit Moussakelle these days to witness the ruins of the old Maskeliya town.
