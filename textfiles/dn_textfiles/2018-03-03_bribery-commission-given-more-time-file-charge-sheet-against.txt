## Title: Bribery Commission given more time to file charge sheet against Mahindananda Aluthgamage
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Friday, March 2, 2018 - 14:14

Bribery Commission given more time to file charge sheet against Mahindananda Aluthgamage

 
The Colombo Chief Magistrate’s Court granted further time to the Bribery Commission to file a charge sheet against former Sports Minister Mahindananda Aluthgamage and two others for allegedly committing a Rs. 53.1 million fraud while importing sports equipment during the last Presidential Election.
Accordingly, the Bribery Commission was directed to file a charge sheet against the accused on May 15 by Chief Magistrate Lal Ranasinghe Bandara.
The Commission to Investigate Allegations of Bribery and Corruption filed a corruption case at the Colombo Chief Magistrate's Court against former Sports Minister, Joint Opposition MP Mahindananda Aluthgamage, and another official at the Sports Ministry, for over a Rs. 53.1 million fraud said to have taken place, while importing sports equipment to the sports clubs.
