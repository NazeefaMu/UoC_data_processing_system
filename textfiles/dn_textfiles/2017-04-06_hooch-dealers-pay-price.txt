## Title: Hooch dealers pay price
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, April 6, 2017 - 01:00

Hooch dealers pay price

Eleven men who pleaded guilty to manufacturing illicit liquor, possessing equipment for brewing illegal liquor, possessing and selling arrack and beer without permits were fined Rs. 90,000 by Matara Chief Magistrate and Additional District Judge Uresha de Silva.
The men were arrested in raids in Weligama, Akuressa, Kananke, Palatuwa and Telijjawila in Matara.
