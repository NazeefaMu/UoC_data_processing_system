## Title: Woman arrested for possession of heroin
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 31, 2017 - 16:45

Woman arrested for possession of heroin

 
Mirihana Police arrested a woman for possession of heroin in Madiwela yesterday.
Police said she had more than 10g of heroin during the arrest.
The suspect is identified as a 33-year-old resident of Gajabapura.
 She will be produced to the Nugegoda Magistrate Court today.
Mirihana Police is investigating.
