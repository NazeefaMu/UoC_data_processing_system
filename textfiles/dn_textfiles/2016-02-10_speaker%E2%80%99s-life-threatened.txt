## Title: Speaker’s life threatened
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Wednesday, February 10, 2016 - 16:15

Speaker’s life threatened

 
Speaker of the House Karu Jayasuriya today stated in Parliament that an unidentified group had threatened his life if the demand of the “Joint Opposition” to be recognised as a separate group was  not met. The Speaker further added that he was not frightened by such threats or dying.
