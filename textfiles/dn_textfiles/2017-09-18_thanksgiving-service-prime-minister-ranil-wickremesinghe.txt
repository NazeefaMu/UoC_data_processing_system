## Title: Thanksgiving Service for Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, September 18, 2017 - 01:00

Thanksgiving Service for Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe

A special religious service commemorating the 40 years of service of Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe as a Parliamentarian, Cabinet Minister and Prime Minister will be held at St. Mary’s Church, Lauries Road, Bambalapitiya, Colombo 4 at 5.15 pm on September 19. The special service will be presided over by Archbishop of Colombo Malcolm Cardinal Ranjith.
