## Title: Three arrested with a gun
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, December 25, 2018 - 01:00

Three arrested with a gun

Kuruwita Police, on a tip off, took into custody three suspects with a repeater gun and three live bullets in their possession on the Lelgoda - Pohorabawa road at a road block, yesterday. The suspects were identified as residents of Parakaduwa. They are in the age groups of 30, 31, and 32. They were to be produced before the Ratnapura Magistrate’s Court yesterday.
Further investigations are continuing.
