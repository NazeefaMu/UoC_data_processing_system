## Title: Guitar performance
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Wednesday, January 10, 2018 - 01:00

Guitar performance

Solidarity with Cuba and Venezuela, a classical Guitar Concert powered by Yamaha Music Centre, will be held at the Russian Centre on January 20 at 6 pm, in aid of Guitar Fest 2018.
The event is organized by the Guitar Association of Sri Lanka. Compositions by Leo Brouwer (Cuba) and Antonio Lauro (Venezuela) the concert will be performed by Aditya Gandhi from India.
