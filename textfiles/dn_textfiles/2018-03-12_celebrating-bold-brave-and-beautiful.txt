Celebrating bold, brave and beautiful

Ladies VIP night partnered with Shangri-la hotel Colombo to celebrate International Women’s Week was held recently. It was an evening of empowerment and eco-vision in keeping with the global theme of #press for progress. Daily News staff photographer, Shan Rambukwella captured special moments and people on his camera.
