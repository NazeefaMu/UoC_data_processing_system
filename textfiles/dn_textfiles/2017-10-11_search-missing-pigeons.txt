## Title: Search for missing pigeons
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, October 11, 2017 - 01:00

Search for missing pigeons

A resident in Pahala Olidaluwa, Kakkapalliya named Uduwarage Vasantha de Silva in a complaint to the Chilaw Police said that someone had robbed all his pigeons numbering about 17 to 18 from the pigeon cage in his home garden when he and his wife were away at work recently.
His wife had observed that the pigeons were missing when she came back to their house during the lunch interval.
Chilaw Police is conducting investigations.
