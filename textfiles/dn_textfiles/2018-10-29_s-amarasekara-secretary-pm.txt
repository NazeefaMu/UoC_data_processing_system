## Title: S. Amarasekara is Secretary to PM
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 29, 2018 - 01:00

S. Amarasekara is Secretary to PM

S. Amarasekara has been appointed as the new Secretary to the Prime Minister. President Maithripala Sirisena has made the appointment under powers vested in him by Section 51(1) of the Constitution of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.
