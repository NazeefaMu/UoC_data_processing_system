## Title: Newly Korean Ambassador to Sri Lanka
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 31, 2018 - 01:00

Newly Korean Ambassador to Sri Lanka

Newly-appointed Korean Ambassador to Sri Lanka Lee Heon presented his credentials to President Maithripala Sirisena at the President’s House in Colombo, recently. Foreign Affairs Minister Tilak Marapana and President’s Secretary Austin Fernando attended the occasion.
