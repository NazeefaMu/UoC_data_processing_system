## Title: Clamp down on errant traders in Anuradhapura
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, August 19, 2017 - 01:00

Clamp down on errant traders in Anuradhapura

The Anuradhapura District Consumer Affairs Authority has taken steps to raid errant traders in the district.
District Consumer Affairs Authority OIC R.M.P.K.Rathnayake said hiding goods, selling electrical appliances without a guarantee, non display of price lists and sale of expired goods are the main offences detected.
He said a pharmacy owner in Galenbindunuwewa was caught while selling expired pressure tablets.
Rathnayake said about 676 raids were conducted this year.
