## Title: Mahiyangana – Kandy main road obstructed due to protest
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, April 13, 2018 - 11:57

Mahiyangana – Kandy main road obstructed due to protest

 
The Mahiyangana –Kandy main road is obstructed due to a protest at the Teldeniya – Moragahamulla road.
Commuters are advised to use alternate routes.
