## Title: St. Anne’s 166; Kingswood 19/1
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, February 27, 2017 - 01:00

St. Anne’s 166; Kingswood 19/1

Kingswood College Kandy scored 19 runs for one wicket in reply to St. Anne’s College Kurunegala first innings of 166 on the first day of the Singer Cup Under 19 inter-schools cricket match played at Kurunegala yesterday.
Scores:
St. Anne’s: 166 in 64.3 overs (Wanitha Wanninayake 60, Vimukthi Bandara 39, Ganindu Kaushalya 3/17)
Kingswood: 19/1 at close in 7 overs - YK
