## Title: China Geo Engineering donates for flood relief
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, June 10, 2017 - 01:00

China Geo Engineering donates for flood relief

China Geo Engineering Corporation (CGC), a Chinese infrastructure company handling water supply, flood control, and wastewater treatment system projects since 1995, donated Rs 500,000 to assist the recent flood and landslide victims.China Geo Engineering Corporation’s Deputy General Manager Shu Dehua handed over the cheque to Disaster Management Ministry Secretary S. S Miyanawala before top officials recently. This is the second contribution in a year to assist the government in helping disaster-stricken people after the company donated Rs 200,000 last year for the Aranayaka landslide victims.
