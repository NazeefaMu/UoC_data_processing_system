## Title: Minister assists needy patients
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, September 29, 2018 - 01:00

Minister assists needy patients

Health Minister Dr. Rajitha Senaratne has helped three children and eight patients in need of medical assistance, Health Ministry Spokesman said. Minister Senaratne had assisted three children to obtain funds from the Health Development Fund. The six-year-old three kids will undergo surgeries locally and abroad. They are from Matara, Walasmulla and Waskaduwa.
Two of them will undergo bone marrow transplants for thalassaemia and one will undergo a liver surgery in India. The three kids were granted around 9.1 million rupees for their medical treatment, he said. Minister Senaratne also assisted eight patients to undergo various surgeries. The Minister came to know about them from various newspaper ads requesting public assistance.
Around Rs. 8.1 million was granted to them.
