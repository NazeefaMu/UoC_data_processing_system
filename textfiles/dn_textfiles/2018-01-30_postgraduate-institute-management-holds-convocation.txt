## Title: Postgraduate Institute of Management holds convocation
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Tuesday, January 30, 2018 - 01:00

Postgraduate Institute of Management holds convocation

The Postgraduate Institute of Management (PIM), the Nation’s Management Mentor, presented another batch of 366 managers at the convocation themed ‘Celebrating the richness of human imagination’, held recently at the BMICH. Prof. Michael O’Donnell, Head of School of Business, University of New South Wales, Canberra, Australia, was the chief guest and addressed the convocation themed ‘Human Imagination for Innovation and Creativity’.
The convocation was opened by the Chancellor, Ven. Bellanvila Wimalratne Thera, Prof. Ajantha Dharmasiri, Director of PIM, Prof. Sampath Amarthunga, Vice Chancellor of University of Sri Jayewardenepura and several deans as well as senior academics participated in the proceedings.
Three candidates received the Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) degree in Business Administration. Candidates who had excelled in their performances received gold medals. The Prof. Uditha Liyanage Gold Mmedal, Dr. G. C. B. Wijeyasinghe Gold Medal and the Dr. Saman Kelegama gold medal were presented at the convocation.
