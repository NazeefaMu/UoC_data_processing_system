## Title: Dulaj Ranatunge capture 7/96 against Joes
## Supertitle: Singer Cup U-19 Inter Schools Cricket
## Section: Sports
## Date: Tuesday, October 31, 2017 - 01:00

Singer Cup U-19 Inter Schools Cricket
Dulaj Ranatunge capture 7/96 against Joes

Dulaj Ranatunge of Maliyadeva College, Kurunegala took 7/96 against St. Joseph’s College Colombo in their ‘Singer Cup’ Under 19 inter Schools Cricket tournament match at ST. Joseph’s grounds, Colombo yesterday.
Ramesh Madhushanka of Tissa Central College Kalutara scored 103 runs against St. John’s College Panadura.
Lumbini College Colombo leading first inning against St. Sylvester’s College Kandy at BRC. Shashika Chandeera of Lumbini took 6 wickets for 25 runs agains St. Sylvester’s and Pasan Hettiarchchi of St. Sylvester’s took 6 wickets for 14 runs against Lumbini.
