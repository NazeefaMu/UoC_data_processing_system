## Title: Pakistan Foreign Secretary here for talks
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 17, 2017 - 01:00

Pakistan Foreign Secretary here for talks

Pakistan Foreign Secretary Tehmina Janjua is visiting Sri Lanka to take part in the 5th Round of Bilateral Political Consultations commencing today between Pakistan and Sri Lanka, the Pakistan High Commission in Colombo said.
The 4th round of talks was held in August 2014 in Islamabad.
During her stay, the Foreign Secretary will call on President Maithripala Sirisena, Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe and Foreign Minister Tilak Marapana. She will hold discussions on bilateral relations and matters of mutual interest pertaining to the regional and international arena, the High Commission in a statement said.
During her stay in Sri Lanka, the Foreign Secretary will also launch the “Pakistan Alumni Society of Sri Lanka”.
Pakistan and Sri Lanka enjoy cordial and friendly relations in all walks of life that are firmly based on mutual trust, sovereign equality and mutual interests.
The visit of the Foreign Secretary would further contribute in bolstering the existing ties of friendship and cooperation between Pakistan and Sri Lanka, the Pakistan High Commission statement added.
