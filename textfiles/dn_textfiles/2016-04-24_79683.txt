## Title: Kaushal Silva airlifted to Colombo
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Sunday, April 24, 2016 - 19:15

Kaushal Silva airlifted to Colombo

 
Sri Lankan cricketer Kaushal Silva has been airlifted to Colombo from the Pallekele International Cricket Stadium after being his head was hit by a ball, during a training session. 
 
Kaushal Silva plays for the Sinhalese Sports Club and the Sri Lankan Test team. He made his first class debut in the 2001/02 season. He is a right-handed wicket-keeper batsman.
