## Title: Bribery Commission public awareness programme
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, December 5, 2015 - 01:00

Bribery Commission public awareness programme

The Commission to Investigate Allegations of Bribery or Corruption on Thursday launched a special public awareness programme along with a Collective of Civil Society Organizations in educating the people on the need to eradicate corruption.
The Bribery Commission has launched this programme to coincide with Anti Corruption Day which falls on December 9. Commission to Investigate Allegations of Bribery or Corruption Director General Dirukshi Dias Wickremasinghe, Deputy Transport Minister Ashoka Abeysinghe, Campaign for Free and Fair Election (CaFFE) Executive Director Keerthi Thennakoon, Transparency International Senior Manager Shan Wijethunga and Ceylon Private Bus Owners Association Chairman Gamunu Wijeratne also participated in this programme.
A special walk to mark the occasion will commence from the Vihara Maha Devi Park at 3.00 pm on December 9 to Independence Square.
The "I Paid The Bribe" website will also be launched at this ceremony.
