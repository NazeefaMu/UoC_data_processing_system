## Title: Husband stabs wife to death, paramour critical
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, February 22, 2018 - 01:00

Husband stabs wife to death, paramour critical

The Marawila police is looking for a suspect who had stabbed his wife to death and caused stab injuries to her alleged paramour on his return home.
The deceased was identified as Manjula Priyadarshanee Senanayake of Ambagahawatta, Marawila.She has succumbed to her stab injuries on admission to the Marawila hospital by neighbours who rushed to the scene.
Her alleged paramour who was also admitted to the same hospital was later transferred in a serious condition to the Colombo National Hospital.
