## Title: Heavy fine for electricity pirate
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, July 15, 2017 - 01:00

Heavy fine for electricity pirate

A man who was found guilty of obtaining electricity by manipulating the power meter of his house, was fined Rs. 146,056 by Morawaka Magistrate Janaka Samarasinhe.
The accused is a resident of Puwakbadowita, Bopagoda in Akuressa.
Officials led by K.G.Saranadasa attached to Colombo Special Investigation Unit of the CEB and Pitabeddara Police jointly raided.
