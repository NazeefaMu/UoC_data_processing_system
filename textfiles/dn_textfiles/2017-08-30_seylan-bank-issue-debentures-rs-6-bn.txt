## Title: Seylan Bank to issue debentures for Rs 6 bn
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Wednesday, August 30, 2017 - 01:00

Seylan Bank to issue debentures for Rs 6 bn

Seylan Bank Plc will issue subordinated redeemable public debentures to the tune of Rs. 6 billion. The bank also has the option to sell a further 4 billion if there was demand.
The debentures would have a tenor of 5 to 10 years.
