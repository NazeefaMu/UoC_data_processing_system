## Title: Showers to ease after today
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, September 7, 2017 - 01:00

Showers to ease after today

The Meteorology Department said the prevailing showery condition is expected to ease after today.
However, showers will continue in the South-Western part of the country.
Showers or thunder showers are expected in the Western, Southern, Sabaragamuwa, Central and North-Western Provinces after 2 pm.
The Met Department added that heavy falls are expected in the Ratnapura, Kegalle, Kalutara, Galle and Matara districts.
