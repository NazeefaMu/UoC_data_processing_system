## Title: A/L student needs help
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, September 15, 2017 - 01:00

A/L student needs help

G. G. Deshaja Devmith, a GCE Advanced Level student attending a national school in Colombo, is suffering from an acute problem in his brain due to the unnatural placement of some veins carrying blood and needs urgent surgery to save his life according to Consultant Dr. (Ms) Lakmali Paranahewa and Dr. Nihal Wijewardene.
The surgery is estimated to cost Rs. 1 million. His father a former Principal says being a heart patient himself, his family is not in a position to foot this bill due to their financial plight having to care for another sick child.
He requests philanthropists and the public to assist them in their plight by sending whatever contributions possible to helps expedite the surgery and save his life.
All contributions should be remitted to A/c No. 70427162 at the Bank of Ceylon Borella branch or A/c 100610210625 at the National Savings Bank.
Their contact number is 0716473833.
