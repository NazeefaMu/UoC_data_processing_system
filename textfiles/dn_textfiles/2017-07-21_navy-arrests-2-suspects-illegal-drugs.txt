## Title: Navy arrests 2 suspects with illegal drugs
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, July 21, 2017 - 01:00

Navy arrests 2 suspects with illegal drugs

On receipt of intelligence information to the Navy, Naval personnel attached to the Western Naval Command combined with the Peliyagoda Police apprehended 2 suspects with illegal drugs during a raid carried out in the Batuwatta locality in Negombo yesterday.
Accordingly, thousand tablets of illegal drugs (Tramadol) which are believed to be used for intoxication, were seized while being transferred in a three-wheeler.
The arrested persons and the consignment of illegal drugs were handed over to the Ragama Police for further investigations.
