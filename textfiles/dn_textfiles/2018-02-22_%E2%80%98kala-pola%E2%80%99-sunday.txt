## Supertitle:

## Date: Thursday, February 22, 2018 - 01:00

‘Kala Pola’ this Sunday

The Silver Anniversary edition of Sri Lanka’s acclaimed open-air art fair, Kala Pola, will take place at Ananda Coomaraswamy Mawatha, Colombo 07, on Sunday February 25.
The official ceremony will be graced by High Commissioner of India to Sri Lanka, Taranjit Singh Sandhu. Presented by The George Keyt Foundation in association with John Keells Holdings PLC, Kala Pola is free of charge and open to the public from 8 am - 9 pm. (www.kalapola.lk)
