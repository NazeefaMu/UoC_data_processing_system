## Title: Student arrested for distributing A/L Chemistry questions
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 23, 2017 - 16:30

Student arrested for distributing A/L Chemistry questions

 
The Criminal Investigation Department (CID) arrested a student who had allegedly leaked questions in the A/L Chemistry paper.
The alleged student has distributed three questions of the Chemistry paper during the exam through a smart phone.
