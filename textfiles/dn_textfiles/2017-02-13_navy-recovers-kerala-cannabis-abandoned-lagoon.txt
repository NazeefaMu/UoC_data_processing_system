## Title: Navy recovers Kerala cannabis abandoned in lagoon
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, February 13, 2017 - 01:00

Navy recovers Kerala cannabis abandoned in lagoon

Naval personnel attached to the Naval Deployment Vettilerkerni in the Northern Naval Command during a search, recovered 31 kgs of Kerala cannabis in two baggages abandoned in Aliyawalai Lagoon.
The stock of cannabis was handed over to the Maradankeni Police for further action.
