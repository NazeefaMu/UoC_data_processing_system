## Title: Ravi to make special statement in Parliament
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, August 10, 2017 - 11:30

Ravi to make special statement in Parliament

 
Former Finance Minister, now Foreign Affairs Minister Ravi Karunanayake is due to make a special statement in Parliament today.
The Minister is alleged to have obtained a lease close to Rs. 8.7 million from Arjun Aloysius, the former CEO of Perpetual Treasuries for a fifth-floor super luxury condominium apartment at the Monarch residencies.
However the Minister denied to have known of such a transaction.
