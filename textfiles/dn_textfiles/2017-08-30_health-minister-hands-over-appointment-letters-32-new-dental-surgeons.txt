## Title: Health Minister hands over appointment letters to 32 new dental surgeons
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 30, 2017 - 01:00

Health Minister hands over appointment letters to 32 new dental surgeons

Health Minister Dr. Rajitha Senaratne handed over appointment letters to 32 new dental surgeons, recently. At the moment, there are 2,000 dental surgeons serving in Sri Lanka, a Health Ministry spokesman said.
According to the spokesman, after handing over appointment letters, Minister Dr. Senaratne had stated steps will be taken to further increase the number of dental surgeons serving in the country.
Minister Dr. Senaratne had also stated that trade union action were taken to obtain facilities for the dental surgeons' services and the everything was obtained through cordial discussions with the higher authorities.
Deputy Health Minister Faizal Kassim and many other officials had attended the event.
