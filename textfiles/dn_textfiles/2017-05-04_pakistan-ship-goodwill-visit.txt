## Title: PAKISTAN SHIP ON GOODWILL VISIT
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 4, 2017 - 01:00

PAKISTAN SHIP ON GOODWILL VISIT

Pakistan Maritime Security Ship ‘Dasht’ arrived at the Port of Colombo on a goodwill visit yesterday. The visiting ship was welcomed by the Sri Lanka Navy in accordance with naval traditions on her arrival (Navy media)
