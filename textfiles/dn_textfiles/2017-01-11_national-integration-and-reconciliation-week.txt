## Title: National Integration and Reconciliation week
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, January 11, 2017 - 01:00

National Integration and Reconciliation week

The inaugural gathering of the National Integration and Reconciliation week from January 8 to 14 commenced at the Royal College Navaranga Hall on Monday with the participation of former President Chandrika Bandaranaike Kumaratunge, who addressed the meeting followed by National Integration and Reconciliation Ministry Secretary V. Sivagnanasothy, State Secretary MM Zuhair and Royal College Principal BS Abeyrathne.
