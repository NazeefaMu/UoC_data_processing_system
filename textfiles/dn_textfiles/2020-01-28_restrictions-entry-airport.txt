## Title: Restrictions on entry to the airport
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, January 28, 2020 - 08:30

Restrictions on entry to the airport

All persons except passengers have been temporarily suspended from entering the Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA) Terminal from 6 am today (28).
 
This measure has been taken to prevent the spread of the corona virus.
