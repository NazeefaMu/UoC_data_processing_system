## Title: College of Technology Maradana celebrates 125th anniversary
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Friday, December 14, 2018 - 01:00

College of Technology Maradana celebrates 125th anniversary

The Sri Lanka College of Technology (Ceylon Technical College) Maradana held its 125th anniversary celebrations at its auditorium recently.
An exhibition was also held at the college premises to mark the event. Prof. Y.A.D.S. Samarathunga and K.M.D. Perera were honoured with the opening of two halls.
