## Title: Banku Weeraya on July 15, 16
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Wednesday, July 12, 2017 - 01:00

Banku Weeraya on July 15, 16

Veteran play writer, director and journalist Kapila Kumara Kalinga’s newest stage play Banku Weeraya will be on stage at Hall de Galle and British College Auditorium on July 15 and 16 at 3.30 and 6.45 pm respectively.
The cast consists Dharmapriya Dias, Jayalath Manorathna, Janaka Kumbukage, Ajith Lokuge, Prasad Suriyarachchi, Wasantha Wittachchi, Ganga Jeewani, Nimal Yatiwella, Sanjeewa Dissanayake and Kelum Gamlath.
