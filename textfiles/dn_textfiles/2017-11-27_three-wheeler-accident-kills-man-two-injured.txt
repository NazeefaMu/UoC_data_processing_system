## Title: Three wheeler accident kills man, two injured
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, November 27, 2017 - 01:00

Three wheeler accident kills man, two injured

One person died and two others were injured and admitted to the Badulla General Hospital when the three wheeler they were travelling in toppled after hitting a dog at the Katugahatenna Junction in Passara on November 25.
The deceased was identified as Marimuttu Sundararaj and the injured are Sahana Prasandance (28) and Jayanthi Muller (34) of Gonapala, Passara.
They had boarded the three wheeler driven by an officer of the Ella Police from Parapawa. The police officer had escaped with minor injuries. Investigations are continuing.
