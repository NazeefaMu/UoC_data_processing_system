## Title: SDB Kekirawa Branch shifts to new location
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, April 6, 2017 - 01:00

SDB Kekirawa Branch shifts to new location

Kekirawa branch of the SD B bank relocated to its new premises on April 3. SDB Kekirawa branch was shifted to Thalawa Road, Kekirawa with the objective of providing more convenient and efficient service to its customers with more sophisticated facilities.
SANASA Development Bank PLC is a public quoted company with limited liability incorporated under the Companies act 17 of 1982 and re-registered under Companies at No.07 of 2007 and registered as a licensed specialized bank by Central Bank of Sri Lanka under the Banking Act no 30 of 1988, operates with a branch network of 90 branches all over the country.
SDB bank has been able to introduce a variety of banking products and services with a vast coverage of customers, from new born babies to all categories of senior citizens.
Those products and services comes under different brand names of SDB Dayada, Lakdaru Children's Savings, Jawaya, Uththamavee, Upahara, Dashaka, SDB Ayojana, Swarnakirana, SDB Leasing, SM E Plus, etc. introduced for the convenience of Bank's customers.
K.H. Swarnalatha is the Manager of Kekirawa branch and it has been serving the area for nearly last 6 years.
Relocated Kekirawa branch was opened with the participation of some prestigious invitees including Samadanie Kiriwandeniya Chairperson of SDB bank, Nimal C. Hapuarachchi General Manager and Chief Executive Officer of SDB bank, Sirimewan Dharmasena Cooperative Commissioner of North Central Province, W.D.K.R. Ranjith - Head of Business of SDB bank, Ranmalie Fernando Head of HR of SDB bank, SurandikaWimalarathne, Regional Manager North Central of SDB bank, K.H. Swarnalatha Manager of Kekirawa branch and all the managers of North Central region.
