## Title: Kingswood beat Rajans
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, March 28, 2016 - 01:00

Kingswood beat Rajans

Kingswood College, Kandy beat Dharmaraja College, Kandy by 2 wickets in their annual 50 overs cricket match conducted at the Pallekelle Indoor Stadium yesterday.
Dharmaraja - 212 for 9 wickets in 50 overs (Bawantha Udangamuwa 37, Deshan Gunasinghe 70, Lahiru Samarakoon 20, Vimukthi Wijesiriwardena 3 for 33, Janith Tennakoon 2 for 24, Dilshan Walisundera 1 for 26, Tharindu Liyanage 1 for 34, Thuthira Herath 1 for 47).
Kingswood - 215 for 8 wickets in 50 overs (Meedum Weerasinghe 43, Upamal Heenkenda 42, Tharindu Liyanage 42, Dilshan Walisundera 40, Ruwantha Haturusinghe 22, Gihan Witharanage 3 for 35, Venuka Galahitiyawa 4 for 38).
