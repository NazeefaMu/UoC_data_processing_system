## Title: Professor Dissanayake assumes office as Ports Authority Chairman
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 1, 2017 - 12:30

Professor Dissanayake assumes office as Ports Authority Chairman

 
Professor Parakrama Dissanayake assumed office as Ports Authority Chairman of the Ports and Shipping Ministry today.
The appointment was made by Ports and Shipping Minister Mahinda Samaraweera.
Ports Ministry Secretary L.G. Jayampathi also participated.
