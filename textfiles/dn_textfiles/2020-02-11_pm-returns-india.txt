## Title: PM returns from India
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, February 11, 2020 - 13:30

PM returns from India

Prime Minister Mahinda Rajapaksa has returned to the island after concluding his four-day state visit to India.During the visit, PM met with Indian External Affairs Minister S. Jaishankar, Indian President Ram Nath Kovind, Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi, the Indian Minister of State for Human Resources Development Sanjay Shamrao Dhotre held bilateral talks.PM Rajapaksa also visited historic religious sites in India, including Varanasi, Sarnath, Bodh Gaya, and Tirupati.
