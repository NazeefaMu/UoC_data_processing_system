## Title: Youth struck by lightening dies
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, April 11, 2018 - 01:00

Youth struck by lightening dies

An 18-year-old youth who was staying in a hut of a paddy field died after being struck by lighting ( by a thunderbolt) during the raining time on April 6 during night time.
The dead person has been identified as K. M. Samith Prasantha Kumara of Meegalewa, an unmarried youth. The youth had died from the shock of the impact made by the thunderbolt.
Meegalewa Police IP Bandara Wijesuriya and the Meegalewa Police are investigating the sudden death. A senior Police Chief in Kurunegala told
Daily News yesterday (10) that 8 persons have died due to being hit by thunderbolts and lightning countrywide during the past six months.
