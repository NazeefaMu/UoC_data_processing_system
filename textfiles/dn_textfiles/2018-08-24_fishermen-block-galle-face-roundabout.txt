## Title: Fishermen block Galle Face roundabout
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, August 24, 2018 - 01:00

Fishermen block Galle Face roundabout

Galle Road towards Pettah was blocked for traffic near the Galle Face Roundabout for a while due to a protest by a group of fishermen.
The protest was organised demanding to reduce the price of diesel which was recently increased. The protesters demanded to decrease the price of diesel to its previous price.
The protesters attempted to march towards the Presidential Secretariat building in Colombo but were thwarted by the Police.
