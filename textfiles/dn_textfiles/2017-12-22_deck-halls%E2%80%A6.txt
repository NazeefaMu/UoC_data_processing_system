## Title: Deck the halls…
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Friday, December 22, 2017 - 01:00

Deck the halls…

Christmas is back, bringing with it a rainbow of festive colours. This occasion to light up the streets with multi-hued stars, colourful festoons and lights is eagerly anticipated by people of all age groups. Gearing up for the festive season are shops in the city that are stocking all kinds of products associated with Christmas. Hotels, malls, stores and the streets are already donning the colours of celebrations. Daily News photographers Sarath Peiris and Shan Rambukwella have clicked these pictures which captures the spirit of the season that had already set in our hearts.
