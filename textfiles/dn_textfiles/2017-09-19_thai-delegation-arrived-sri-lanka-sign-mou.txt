## Title: Thai delegation arrived in Sri Lanka to sign a MoU
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, September 19, 2017 - 01:00

Thai delegation arrived in Sri Lanka to sign a MoU

Thai delegation that arrived in Sri Lanka to sign a Memorandum of Understanding with the Primary Industries Ministry visited to the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic, accompanied by Primary Industries Minister Daya Gamage.
