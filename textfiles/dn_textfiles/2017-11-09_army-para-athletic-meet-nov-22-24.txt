## Title: Army Para Athletic Meet from Nov. 22 to 24
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, November 9, 2017 - 01:00

Army Para Athletic Meet from Nov. 22 to 24

The Sri Lanka Army Para Athletic Meet will be worked off from November 22 to 24 at the Diayagama Mahinda Rajapaksa Sports Complex.
The event has paved a strong platform for many Army para athletes to showcase their talent who have payed an immeasurable price from their blood and flesh in saving the motherland from the separatists Elam Tigers. The Army Para Athletic Meet also serves as a talent search and a selection trial in picking brave hearts
to represent Sri Lanka at national and international para events including Paralympics, Para World Championship and Asian Para Athletic Championship.
The meet is considered as the most looked forward to annual event among Army para athletes as the sporting spirit of this event has become a great inspiration for them to mitigate their physical inabilities while providing an enormous moral boost to live their lives more independent and undependable mentality in order to become stakeholders of the country's development in their rehabilitation process.
