## Title: New Army Commander calls on State Defence Minister
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, July 15, 2017 - 01:00

New Army Commander calls on State Defence Minister

New Commander of the Army Lieutenant General Mahesh Senanayake paid a courtesy call on State Defence Minister Ruwan Wijewardene on July 13 at his office in the Ministry.
The State Minister and the Commander of the Army covered discussions on matters of relevance to the Army.
During the meeting the Commander assured that he would cooperate with the State Minister all the time on matters pertaining to the Army. Lieutenant General Senanayake briefed him on a couple of future plans that he intends to launch within the organization.
State Minister Wijewardene wished good luck to the new commander in his future endeavours.Mementoes were exchanged between State Minister Wijewardene and Lieutenant General Mahesh Senanayake to mark the occasion.
