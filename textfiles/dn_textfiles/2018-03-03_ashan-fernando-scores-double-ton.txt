## Title: Ashan Fernando scores a double ton
## Supertitle: ‘Singer Cup’ U19 Schools Cricket :
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, March 3, 2018 - 01:00

‘Singer Cup’ U19 Schools Cricket :
Ashan Fernando scores a double ton

Sri Lanka youth cap Lasith Croospulle scored his fourth century of the season and Ashan Fernando hit a superb double century – 203 off 201 balls for Maris Stella Negombo in their drawn ‘Singer Cup’ Under 19 inter-schools cricket match against De Mazenod Kandana at Kandana yesterday.
Fernando struck 21 fours and 2 sixes and shared an opening stand of 210 with Croospulle after Maris Stella had been forced to follow-on.
