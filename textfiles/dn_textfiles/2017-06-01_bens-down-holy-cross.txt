## Title: Bens down Holy Cross
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, June 1, 2017 - 01:00

Bens down Holy Cross

St. Benedict’s College, Kotahena pulled off a fine two wicket win over Holy Cross College, Kalutara in their Inter School under 17 Division One Tournament Cricket Match conducted by Sri Lanka Schools Cricket Association and played at Kalutara.
Batting initially Holy Cross College piled up 259 for 8 in their allotted overs with Dineth de Silva scoring 77 runs and Umayanga Suwaris contribution 42 runs. In reply Bens were 263 for 8 with Brian Johnpulle missing a well deserved century by ten runs and Shehan Fernando too scored 61 runs.
Holy Cross College - 259/8 in 50 overs - Dineth de Silva 77” Umayanga Suwaris 42, Kaushan Kandegama 38, Dilhara Nanayakkara 28 n/o, Imesh Fernando 2/49, Mahesh Theekshana 2/35
St. Benedict’s - 263/8 in 49.2 overs - Visitha Saranga 32, Brian Johnpulle 90, Shehan Fernando 61, Imesh Fernando 22, Nirmal Perera 2/51, Dineth de Silva 3/42, Dilhara Nanayakkara 3/59
