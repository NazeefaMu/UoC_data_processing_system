## Title: Government schools in Kandy to remain closed
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, March 7, 2018 - 13:24

Government schools in Kandy to remain closed

 
All government schools in Kandy administrative district will continue to be closed until further notice, the Education Ministry said a short while ago.
The decision was made following the recent conflicts which erupted between two groups on Monday.
