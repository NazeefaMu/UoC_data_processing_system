## Title: Accident claims one, injures another
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, August 27, 2018 - 01:00

Accident claims one, injures another

One died and another was injured when a lorry plying on the A6 road collided with a pickup truck running in the same direction at Ibbankatuwa, last Friday night. The deceased was identified as 41-year-old Dissanayaka Mudiyanselage Jayantha Ajith, a resident of Yapagama (the truck driver); while the injured was identified as 35-year-old A.M. Amaranath Dissanayaka of Rathmalagahaela (his assistant).
They had been admitted to the Dambulla Weera Denzil Kobbekaduwa Base Hospital.
Investigations revealed that upon collision, the pickup truck had crashed into a shop. Police arrested the lorry driver for suspected reckless driving. Investigations are continuing.
