## Title: Seylan Bank  welcomes 2018
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Wednesday, January 10, 2018 - 01:00

Seylan Bank  welcomes 2018

Senior management and staff of Seylan Bank welcomed the New Year at the Seylan Bank Head Office in Kolpetty, whilst commencing the first working day for 2018.
Seylan Bank’s islandwide branches also celebrated the New Year with multi-religious ceremonies that enhanced the cultural values of the respective branches.
