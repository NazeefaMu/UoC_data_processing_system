## Title: Two injured in collision
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 14, 2018 - 01:00

Two injured in collision

Two people were admitted to Tangalle Base Hospital after their three wheeler collided with a lorry in Godawanagoda on the Tangalle - Weeraketiya road on Tuesday.
The Three wheeler was badly damaged.
The traffic branch of Tangalle Police are investigating.
