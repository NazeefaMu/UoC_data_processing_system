## Title: 50 days for Aadaraneeya Kathawak
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Tuesday, August 9, 2016 - 01:00

50 days for Aadaraneeya Kathawak

Priyantha Colombage’s movie 'Adaraneeya Kathawak’ celebrated 50 days in cinemas recently. The team gathered to Lido 3D Cinema, Borella for the event. Lido has now been converted to a 2k digital cinema with surrounding sound systems and a large screen. Sinhala movies will be screened at the venue.
