## Title: Andre Fletcher powers Windies to seven-wicket win over Sri Lanka
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Sunday, March 20, 2016 - 22:45

Andre Fletcher powers Windies to seven-wicket win over Sri Lanka

Samuel Badree starred for the West Indies with the ball, taking three wickets for 12 runs to restrict Sri Lanka to 122 for nine. Andre Fletcher smashed an unbeaten 84 off 64 balls as the West Indies chased down the paltry target in 18.2 overs to register their second successive win in the 2016 World T20.  (NDTVSports)
Sri Lanka 122/9 (20ov)
West Indies 127/3 (18.2ov)
