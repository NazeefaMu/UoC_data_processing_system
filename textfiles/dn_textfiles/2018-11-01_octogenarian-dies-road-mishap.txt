## Title: Octogenarian dies in road mishap
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 1, 2018 - 01:00

Octogenarian dies in road mishap

An 85-year-old man succumbed to his injuries after he was run over by a lorry while crossing the road in Pilimathalawa yesterday.
The victim died on admission to Kaddugannawa Government Hospital. He was identified as Godawela Widanelage Dingiri Banda, a resident of Padaruldeniya in Pilimathalawa.
The postmortem was to be performed at the Kandy Hospital yesterday. The suspect driver was taken into police custody.
Kadugannawa Police are investigating.
