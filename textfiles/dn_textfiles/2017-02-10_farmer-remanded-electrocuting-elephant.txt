## Title: Farmer remanded for electrocuting elephant
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 10, 2017 - 01:00

Farmer remanded for electrocuting elephant

A farmer in Dahanakwewa, Mookiriyawa, Kahatagasdigiliya arrested by Kahatagasdigiliya Police for allegedly causing the death of a 20-year-old tusker by electrocuting it by erecting an electrical fence in his farm was remanded by till February 16. He was remanded by Anuradhapura Chief Magistrate and Additional District Judge K. P. Shanaka Harshan when produced by Kahatagasdigiliya Police on February 8.
The Judge also ordered the Superintendent of the Anuradhapura Prison to produce the suspect before the Kahatagasdigiliya Circuit Magistrate on February 16.
