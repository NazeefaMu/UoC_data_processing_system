## Title: AIA’s Wealth Planners on tour to Thailand
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, July 26, 2018 - 01:00

AIA’s Wealth Planners on tour to Thailand

AIA’s Gold and Platinum Club Wealth Planners and Wealth Planners’ Managers were rewarded recently with a fully paid tour to Pattaya and Bangkok.
Three groups consisting 221 were on a five-day tour, is a part of an ongoing program which offers AIA’s sales-force the opportunity to travel the world as part of their performance rewards.
