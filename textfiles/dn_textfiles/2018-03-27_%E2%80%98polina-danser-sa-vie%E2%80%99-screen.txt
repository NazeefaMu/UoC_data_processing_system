## Title: ‘Polina, danser sa vie’ on screen
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Entertainment
## Date: Tuesday, March 27, 2018 - 01:00

‘Polina, danser sa vie’ on screen

Bonjour cinema 2018 is now happening in Colombo. The next movie will be screening on March 28 at 7 pm. ‘Polina, danser sa vie’ directed by Angelin Preljocaj, Valérie Müller will be screening on that day. The French version with English subtitles will be screened at Empire Arcade, Independence Square. Entrance free.
The film is based on a young girl who studies classical ballet. As a young woman she turns to modern dance and choreography.
