## Title: Workshop cancelled
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, November 29, 2017 - 01:00

Workshop cancelled

The workshop organised for Western Province Information Officers on the Right to Information Act, under the patronage of Finance and Mass Media Minister Mangala Samaraweera, scheduled to be held on November 30 at Temple Trees has been cancelled due to unavoidable circumstances.
This workshop under the patronage of Finance and Mass Media Minister Mangala Samaraweera will be held on December 11 from 9.00 am-2.30pm instead at Temple Trees.
