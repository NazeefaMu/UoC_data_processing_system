## Title: Father of three arrested for sexual abuse of minor
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, May 9, 2017 - 11:00

Father of three arrested for sexual abuse of minor

 
Rasnaykapura Police has arrested a thirty-two year old father of three for sexually abusing a fourteen year-old schoolgirl continuously for one year.
The arrested was made on a complaint received by the National Child Protection Authority (NCPA).
The fourteen year-old victim has been admitted to hospital for testing.
