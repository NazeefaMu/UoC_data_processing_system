## Title: Hemas Vesak Lantern  inaugurated
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Friday, April 27, 2018 - 01:00

Hemas Vesak Lantern  inaugurated

The Hemas Vesak lantern was inaugurated by Ven. Assaji Thera of the Hunupitiya Gangarama temple at the Hemas House recently.  The Vesak Lantern which has been a landmark at Braybrooke Place since 1998 will be on display at Hemas House till May 4.
