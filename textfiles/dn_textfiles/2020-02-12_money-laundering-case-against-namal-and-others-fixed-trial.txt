## Title: Money Laundering case against Namal and others fixed for trial
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Wednesday, February 12, 2020 - 16:14

Money Laundering case against Namal and others fixed for trial

The case against Parliamentarian Namal Rajapaksa and five others under Money Laundering Act was today fixed for further trial on July 09 by the Colombo High Court.The case was today taken up before Colombo High Court Judge Pradeep Hettiarachchi.
