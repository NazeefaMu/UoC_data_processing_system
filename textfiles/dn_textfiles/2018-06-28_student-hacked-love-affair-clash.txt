## Title: Student hacked in love affair clash
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 28, 2018 - 01:00

Student hacked in love affair clash

Welipenna police are investigating into an incident where two student factions had clashed over a love affair in Paregama, Pahalawela using a sword.
The victim came under attack when he went to the Aluthgama town with his friends on push bicycles to patronise a Dansela.
The injured student was admitted to the Ittepana District hospital from severe cut injuries and he was later transferred to the Kalutara General Hospital.
Police have already identified the two schoolchildren who had brought a sword hidden in an umbrella to carry out the attack on their colleague.
The attackers had an argument with the victim before being attacked.
