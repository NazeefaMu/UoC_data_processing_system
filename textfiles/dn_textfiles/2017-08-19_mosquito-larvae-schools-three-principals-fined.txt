## Title: Mosquito larvae in schools; three principals fined
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, August 19, 2017 - 01:00

Mosquito larvae in schools; three principals fined

Three principals attached to government schools in the Kirinda-Puhulwella Divisional Secretariat Division who were found guilty of keeping plastic containers, yoghurt cups and water containers with mosquito larvae at their school premises were fined Rs. 1,500 each by Matara Additional Magistrate and Additional District Judge Pavithra Sajeewani Pathirana. Public Health Inspectors attached to the Office of the Divisional Medical Officer of Health Kirinda-Puhulwella made the detections.
