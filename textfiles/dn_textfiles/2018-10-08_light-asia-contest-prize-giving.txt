## Title: Light of Asia contest prize-giving
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 8, 2018 - 01:00

Light of Asia contest prize-giving

The prize-giving of the ‘Light of Asia’ annual oratory contest conducted by the Colombo Young Men’s Buddhist Association (YMBA), was held on September 22 at the YMBA Main Hall in Borella.
Thevindu Kennula Kandanaarachchi, a student of Buddhayatana Dhamma School in Mt. Lavinia was the overall winner and was awarded by chief guest Sri Lanka International Buddhist Academy Vice Chancellor Dr. Upali M. Sedere.
Colombo YMBA English Literary Activities Branch Secretary Lakshman Perera was also present.
