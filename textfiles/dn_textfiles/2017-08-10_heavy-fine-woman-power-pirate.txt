## Title: Heavy fine for woman power pirate
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, August 10, 2017 - 01:00

Heavy fine for woman power pirate

A woman who was found guilty of tapping electricity by manipulating the power meter at her house, was fined Rs. 11,500 by Angunakolapelesse Magistrate Kushika Kumarasiri.
The Magistrate also ordered the accused to pay Rs. 64,609 as compensation to the CEB.
The woman is a resident of Pahalagama in Uswewa.
Officials attached to Special Investigation unit of the Colombo CEB and Angunakolapelesse Police jointly conducted the raid.
