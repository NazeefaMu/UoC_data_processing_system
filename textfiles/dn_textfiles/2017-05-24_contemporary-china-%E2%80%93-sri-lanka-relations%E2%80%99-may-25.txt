## Title: Contemporary China – Sri Lanka Relations’ on May 25
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 24, 2017 - 01:00

Contemporary China – Sri Lanka Relations’ on May 25

The Bandaranaike Centre for International Studies (BCIS) is to hold a seminar together with the Embassy of the People’s Republic of China in Sri Lanka and the China Institute of International Studies (CIIS) on 'Contemporary China – Sri Lanka Relations' on May 25 at the BCIS Auditorium from 8.30 am – 1.00 pm to mark the 60th anniversary of the Diplomatic Relations between China and Sri Lanka.
The seminar will commence with remarks made by Ambassador Su Ge, President, CIIS, and the keynote speech will be delivered by Dr. Wang Youming, Director, Department for Developing Countries Studies, CIIS on “China in Global Governance.”
Prominent experts on China–Sri Lankan relations Prof. Amal Jayawardena, Senior Professor, Sir John Kotelawala University, Admiral Dr. Jayanath Colombage, Director of the Centre for Indo-Lanka Initiatives, Pathfinder, George Cooke, Research Associate, BCIS, Ning Shengnan, Assistant Research Fellow, CIIS will present during the two sessions.
Former Ambassador to the People’s Republic of China Nihal Rodrigo and the Executive Director, United States-Sri Lanka Fulbright Commission, Tissa Jayathilaka, will chair the two sessions.
