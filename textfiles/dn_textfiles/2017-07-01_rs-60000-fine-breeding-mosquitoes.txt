## Title: Rs. 60,000 fine for breeding mosquitoes
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, July 1, 2017 - 01:00

Rs. 60,000 fine for breeding mosquitoes

Six householders who pleaded guilty for having mosquito breeding grounds with larvae in their home gardens were fined Rs. 60,000 by Rambadagalla Magistrate G. N. Priyadarshani.
The suspects are from Rideegama area in Kurunegala. Dr. W. A. Lionel, MOH of Rideegama, Kurunegala prosecuted.
