## Title: Hashan Tillakaratne, Upul Chandana to coach national team
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, December 3, 2016 - 14:45

Hashan Tillakaratne, Upul Chandana to coach national team

 
Former cricket captain Hashan Tillakaratne and player Upul Chandana were appointed as coaches to the national team.
Hashan Tillakaratne will be batting coach and Upul Tharanga fielding coach.
