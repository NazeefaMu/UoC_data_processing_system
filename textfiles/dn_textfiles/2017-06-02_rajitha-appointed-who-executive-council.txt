## Title: Rajitha appointed to WHO Executive Council
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, June 2, 2017 - 01:00

Rajitha appointed to WHO Executive Council

Health Minister Dr. Rajitha Senaratne has been appointed to the Executive Council of the WHO, a Health Ministry spokesman said.
According to the spokesman, a total of 34 members are appointed to the Executive Council for a period of two years. Professionals with technical knowledge are appointed to the council.
The council meeting was to be held in Geneva, Switzerland yesterday and today. Minister Dr. Senaratne is to take part in the proceedings. Members from four Asian countries received appointments for the Council this year and Minister Dr. Senaratne is one of them, the spokesman said.
A special discussion was held between WHO Director General Dr. Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus in Geneva, Switzerland and Minister Dr. Senaratne. During the discussion, the WHO Director General extended his fullest support towards Sri Lanka's health sector development, the spokesman added.
