## Title: Three-month remembrance on May 5 and 6
## Supertitle: Late Prof. Bellanwila Wimalarathana Anunayake Thera:
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, May 3, 2018 - 01:00

Late Prof. Bellanwila Wimalarathana Anunayake Thera:
Three-month remembrance on May 5 and 6

Former Chancellor of the Sri Jayewardenepura University and the Viharadhikari at Bellanwila Raja Maha Viharaya, late Ven. Prof. Bellanwila Wimalarathana Anunayake Thera’s three-month remembrance Dhamma sermon and almsgiving to more than 150 members of the Maha Sangha will be held at Bellanwila Raja Maha Vihara premises on May 5 and 6.
Ven. Prof. Kollupitiye Sri Mahinda Sangharakkitha Nayake Thera of Kelaniya Raja Maha Viharaya, who is also a senior lecturer at Kelaniya University, has been invited to deliver the commemorative Dhamma sermon at 7.00 pm on Saturday (May 5) at Bellanwila Raja Maha Viharaya.
The series of meritorious deeds, organized under the aegis of Ven. Dr. Bellanwila Dhammarathana Nayake Thera, Incumbent, Bellanwila Raja Maha Viharaya, Chief Sanghanayake of Singapore and the Advisor at Singapore Buddhist Library, culminates on the following day (May 6) with an almsgiving (Sangeeka Daana) to more than 150 Buddhist monks, headed by the Malwatte and Asgiriya Maha Nayake Theras, Anunayake Theras, Maha Nayake Thera of Kotte Sri Kalyani Samagri Dharma Maha Sangha Sabhawa, Chief Prelates and other members of the Maha Sangha.
To coincide with the commemorative event, more than 300 selected school students of underprivileged families will receive gift parcels of school and educational accessories, donated by Singaporeans, attached to the Buddhist Library of Singapore. In addition, a parallel blood donation campaign, arranged by old students of the Sri Lanka Community Development Foundation at Bellanwila Raja Maha Viharaya will get underway on Sunday (6) at Sri Somarathana pilgrims’ rest, adjacent to the temple premises.
Similarly, Bellanwila Tharuna Bauddha Sanvidanaya has planned to donate dry rations and food items to needy families in the area as another meritorious programme meant to transfer merits on late Ven. Bellanwila Wimalarathana Anunayake Thera.
All organizations including the Dayaka Sabha and the Sri Bodhiraja Kulagana Samithiya in the temple have coordinated these commemorative programmes.
