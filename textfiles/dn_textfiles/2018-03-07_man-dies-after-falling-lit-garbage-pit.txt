## Title: Man dies after falling into lit garbage pit
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, March 7, 2018 - 10:47

Man dies after falling into lit garbage pit

 
A 67-year-old man who fell into a garbage pit which was on fire, died at his residence in Thibirigolla, Pallama last morning.
The Police said that the garbage pit was set fire by the deceased man himself.
The deceased individual was identified as Karuna Pedighe Piyadasa.
Postmortem examinations will be conducted today.
Pallama Police is conducting further investigations.
