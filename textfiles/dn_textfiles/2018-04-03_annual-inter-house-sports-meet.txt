## Title: Annual Inter-house Sports Meet
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Tuesday, April 3, 2018 - 01:00

Annual Inter-house Sports Meet

Negombo South International School, Piliyandala branch’s Annual Inter-house Sports Meet 2018 was held recently at the Municipal grounds in Piliyandala. Negombo South International School Chairman Dr Paul Perera graced the event.
The winners received their medals and trophies from the Negombo South International School Group director Anushka Perera, registrar Sulochana Perera, the directress of the primary section Goonewardena, the disciplinarian B H Wijesinghe and the Recovery Officer Anura Ranasinghe.
