## Title: Navy Commander called on Prime Minister Mahinda Rajapaksa
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 1, 2018 - 01:00

Navy Commander called on Prime Minister Mahinda Rajapaksa

Navy Commander Vice Admiral Sirimevan Ranasinghe called on Prime Minister Mahinda Rajapaksa at the Prime Minister’s office, on Monday. The Commander congratulated the Premier on his new appointment and also presented him with a memento.
