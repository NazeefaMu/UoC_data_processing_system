## Title: Milk powder price hikes
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, May 4, 2018 - 11:15

Milk powder price hikes

 
The price of milk powder will increased by midnight today, the Consumer Affairs Authority (CAA) said.
Accordingly the price of 400 grams will be increased by Rs. 20 and 1 kilogram will increase by Rs. 50.
Formula milk powder will be exempted from the price hike, the CAA added.
