## Title: Premier to India tomorrow on official tour
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, November 20, 2017 - 16:48

Premier to India tomorrow on official tour

 
Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe will leave to India on an official tour tomorrow.
He will hold bilateral meetings with Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi and call on Indian President Ram Nath Kovind during his stay in New Delhi.
Aside from these, the Premier is due to attend the 5th Global Conference on Cyberspace at the Aero City, New Delhi.
