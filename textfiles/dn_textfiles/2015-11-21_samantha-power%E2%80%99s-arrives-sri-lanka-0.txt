## Title: Samantha Power’s arrives in Sri Lanka
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Saturday, November 21, 2015 - 17:00

Samantha Power’s arrives in Sri Lanka

United States Ambassador to the United Nations Samantha Power arrived in Sri Lanka a short while ago. 
 
During her visit Ambassador Power will travel to the northern city of Jaffna to meet with local government officials and communities affected by the recently concluded conflict. She is also schedule to meet senior government officials in Colombo.
