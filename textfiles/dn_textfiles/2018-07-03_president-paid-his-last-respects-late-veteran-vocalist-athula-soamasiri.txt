## Title: President paid his last respects to the late veteran vocalist Athula Soamasiri
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, July 3, 2018 - 01:00

President paid his last respects to the late veteran vocalist Athula Soamasiri

President Maithripala Sirisena paid his last respects to the late veteran vocalist Athula Soamasiri at a private funeral parlour yesterday. The President also condoled with Somasiri’s family members, relatives, artistes and fans who were present.
