## Title: Kakkamunai National SC wins soccer title
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Thursday, November 9, 2017 - 01:00

Kakkamunai National SC wins soccer title

The Kakkamunai National Sports Club football team, Kinniya became champion of the 52nd soccer tournament organized by the Kinniya Ailiyadi Madina Sports Club when they beat the defending champions Maharu Killoor Sports Club Football team (5/0) in the final played at Ailiyadi play grounds recently. Trincomalee District Parliamentarian Imran Mahroof was the chief guest at the award ceremony.
Kinniya, Vaan-Ele Police Station OIC Arjuna Kodikara, Kinniya Buhari Vidyalaya Principal Jabir Salam, Former Pradeshiya Sabha Member Sharif Lebbe and the Madina SC President M. Ansar were participated as guests.
