## Title: 'Thai Poosam' water cutting ceremony
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 10, 2017 - 01:00

'Thai Poosam' water cutting ceremony

The annual 'Thai Poosam' water cutting ceremony of Koddaimunai Arasady Pillaiyar Temple, was held at Kallady sea beach yesterday. The chariot festival went along the Kallady bridge before the water cutting ceremony. Picture by Sivam Packiyanathan, Batticaloa Special Correspondent
