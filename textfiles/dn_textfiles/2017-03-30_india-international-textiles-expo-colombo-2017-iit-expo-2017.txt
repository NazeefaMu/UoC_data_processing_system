## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, March 30, 2017 - 01:00

The India International Textiles Expo-Colombo 2017 (IIT Expo 2017)

The India International Textiles Expo-Colombo 2017 (IIT Expo 2017) organised by the powerloom Development and Export Promotion Council (PDEXCIL) (under Ministry of extiles, Government of India) was held at the Galadari Hotel, Colombo yesterday. Sixteen ndian exhibitors displayed their products in this two day event which was an unique opportunity for manufacturers and exporters to showcase their products before Sri Lankan buyers. Indian High Commission’s Economic and Commercial second Secretary Rajeev Arora, Economic and Commercial First Secretary Suja K. Menon and Development Strategies and International Trade Ministry Secretary Chandani Bandara at the IIT Expo 2017. Picture by Thushara Fernando.
