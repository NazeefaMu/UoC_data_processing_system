## Title: Archbishop attends funeral mass for Katukurunda boat accident victims
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, February 23, 2017 - 01:00

Archbishop attends funeral mass for Katukurunda boat accident victims

The funeral mass for seven persons killed in the Katukurunda boat tragedy on Sunday was held at St.Annes Church under the patronage of Colombo Archbishop Malcolm Cardinal Ranjith yesterday. Twelve persons were killed and 27 others injured when their boat overturned during the annual St. Lazarus boat procession on Sunday. Picture by Rukmal Gamage
