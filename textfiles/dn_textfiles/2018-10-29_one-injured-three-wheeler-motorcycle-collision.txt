## Title: One injured in three wheeler- motorcycle collision
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, October 29, 2018 - 01:00

One injured in three wheeler- motorcycle collision

A three wheeler plying on A 9 road towards Dambulla collided with a motorcycle at the Pannampitiya Junction injuring the motorcyclist on Saturday morning. The victim was admitted to Dambulla Base Hospital.
He was identified as Indrajeeth Jayampathi Premasiri (28) of Wahacotte. He was on the way to his chena when he met the accident.
The three wheeler driver was arrested by Dambulla police. Investigations are continuing.
