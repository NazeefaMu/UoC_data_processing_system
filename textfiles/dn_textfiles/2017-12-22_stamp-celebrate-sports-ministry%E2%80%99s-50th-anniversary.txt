## Title: Stamp to celebrate Sports Ministry’s 50th anniversary
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, December 22, 2017 - 01:00

Stamp to celebrate Sports Ministry’s 50th anniversary

In celebration of the Sports Ministry's 50th anniversary, issuing of a stamp and a First Day Cover took place at the President's official residence on December 21. The First Day cover and the stamp were presented to the President by the Sports Ministry secretary Jayantha Weerasekara.
