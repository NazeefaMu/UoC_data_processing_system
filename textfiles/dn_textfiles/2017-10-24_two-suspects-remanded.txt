## Title: Two suspects remanded
## Supertitle: Assault on OIC
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 24, 2017 - 01:00

Assault on OIC
Two suspects remanded

Colombo Chief Magistrate Lal Ranasinghe yesterday ordered to remand until November 6 the two suspects who allegedly assaulted and bit the ear of the Officer-In-Charge of the President’s Security Division.
The suspects, constable Asiri Nuwan Seneviratne and navy sailor Sameer Dilhara Muthunayake were attached to the President’s Security Division. Cinnamon Garden Police informed the Chief Magistrate that extensive investigation was carried out to arrest the suspects and more suspects were to be arrested.
