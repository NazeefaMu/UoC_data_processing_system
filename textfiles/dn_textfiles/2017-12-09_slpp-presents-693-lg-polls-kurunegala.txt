## Title: SLPP presents 693 for LG Polls in Kurunegala
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Saturday, December 9, 2017 - 01:00

SLPP presents 693 for LG Polls in Kurunegala

Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna (SLPP) deposited Rs. 1,039,500 for 693 candidates for 19 Pradesheeya Sabhas and two Municipal Councils in the Kurunegala districts, before the Assistant Election Commissioner M.J.S. Madhawa for the forthcoming LG polls yesterday.
Former Botanical Gardens Minister Jayaratne Herath and several NWP PC members were present.
