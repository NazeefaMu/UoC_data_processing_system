## Title: EPIC Group exhibits at Mobile Money and Agent Banking Summit in Myanmar
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, February 23, 2017 - 01:00

EPIC Group exhibits at Mobile Money and Agent Banking Summit in Myanmar

 
Epic Technology group synonymous for innovative world class technology solutions,was on show at the eighth Annual Mobile Money and Agent Banking Summit 2017,held in Myanmar.
The Annual Mobile Money and Agent Banking Summit in Myanmar is the only authoritative summit bringing together regulators (telecom officials and central bankers), financial institutions, payment solution providers, technology infrastructure developers and commercial end-users all in one forum.
Over 200 participants attended the summit from the likes of Directors, CEOs, CTOs, COOs, and Senior Managers of organizations operating in the banking and financial services domain, and views were exchanged on the hot topic of “Mobile Moneyand Agent Banking”.
Epic Technology Group CEO and Group Director Viraj Mudalige who was one of the speakers at the Banking Summit 2017 said they want the region to get a first-hand experience of Epic's high class innovative and disruptive “Fintech” Solutions.
"Our out-of-the box approach in developing advanced yet affordable technology solutions has changed the traditional landscape of document management, secure mobile communication, mobile enterprise automation, and information security domains”.
“Epic's innovative technology solutions are designed and created by an in-house talent pool of over 200 in-house engineers and experts.We also have our very own Research and Innovation Centre with dedicated researchers who continue to push the limits of innovative world-class technology solutions”. The Epic Mobile Wallet is one such innovation which is the World's first ever application that integrates payer and payee into a single wallet, whilst offering multiple payment modes as preferred by the users. This revolutionary product together with our Branch less Banker solution, Card Management solution, Dispute Management solution and 3D Secure solution which are part of our versatile payment portfolio,have been experienced by the delegates and visitors from the summit, and the enthusiasm shown towards our FinTech solutions is truly encouraging,” Mudalige said.
