## Title: Toddler killed in accident
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, February 17, 2018 - 01:00

Toddler killed in accident

A child was killed when the motorcycle he was travelling on with his parent collided with a tipper.
The deceased was identified as Yahiyaa Yoosuff, one and a half years old child of Haroon, a multi-store owner in Thambalagama.
The parents of the child were injured and admitted to the Kinniya Base Hospital.
Investigations are continuing.
