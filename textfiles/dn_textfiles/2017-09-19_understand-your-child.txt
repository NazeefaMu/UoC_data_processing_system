## Title: Understand your child
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Tuesday, September 19, 2017 - 01:00

Understand your child

Veteran journalist Champika Deepani Ranasinghe has launched her latest research work on child’s mind. The book is based on a series of interviews conducted with Dr Sudarshi Seneviratne a specialist on juvenile mental health. Ranasinghe has won several awards in recognition of her contribution to health journalism.
