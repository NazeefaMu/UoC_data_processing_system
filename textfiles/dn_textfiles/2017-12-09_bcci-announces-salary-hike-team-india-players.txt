## Title: BCCI announces salary hike for Team India players
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, December 9, 2017 - 01:00

BCCI announces salary hike for Team India players

Indian cricketers are one of the highest paid when it comes to their annual salary. In fact, other than the captain of the Indian cricket team, coach Ravi Shastri is also paid a handsome amount.
Recently, BCCI announced that it is going to declare a major hike in the salaries of the Team India cricketers. And, finally, it is announced.
These cricketers were paid an amount of INR 1 crore per annum.
Other than this, the captain of the Indian cricket team will be paid more than the mentioned amount. As per a report, these changes are made in order to match the salaries of players from other countries.
