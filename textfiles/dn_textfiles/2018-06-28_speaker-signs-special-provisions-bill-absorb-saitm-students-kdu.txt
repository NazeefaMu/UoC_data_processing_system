## Title: Speaker signs Special Provisions Bill to absorb SAITM students to KDU
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, June 28, 2018 - 13:34

Speaker signs Special Provisions Bill to absorb SAITM students to KDU

 
Speaker Karu Jayasuriya signed the Special Provisions Bill to absorb the South Asian Institute of Medicine and Technology (SAITM) to Kothalawala Defense University (KDU) today.
The bill will come in effect from today.
According to the Bill, students registered with the SAITM between September 15, 2009, and May 15, 2017, will be absorbed to the Kotelawala University to follow the MBBS Degree programme if they have obtained basic qualifications.
