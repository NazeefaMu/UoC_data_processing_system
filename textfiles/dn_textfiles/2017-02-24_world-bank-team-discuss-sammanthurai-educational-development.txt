## Title: World Bank team discuss Sammanthurai educational development
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 24, 2017 - 01:00

World Bank team discuss Sammanthurai educational development

A team of officials of the World Bank visited the Samanthurai Zonal Education Office recently and discussed issues relating to educational development activities in the region.
Eastern Provincial Education Director M.T.A. Nizam, Team Leader M.T.M. Saheed, Deputy Education Director (Planning) M.A.M. Unais, Samanthurai Zonal Education Director M.S.S. Najeem and Education Department officials participated.
The Samanthurai Zonal Education Director said the activities in the past and the proposed plans in the future were discussed in detail. The team of World Bank officials also visited the Sammanthuirai, Irakkamam and Navithanveli Divisional Schools and Irakkamam Divisional Education Office in the Sammanthurai Educational Zone. The educational activities of the Zonal Education Office and the achievements of students were commended by the World Bank team.
