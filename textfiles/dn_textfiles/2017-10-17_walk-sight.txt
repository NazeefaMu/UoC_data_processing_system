## Title: Walk for sight
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Tuesday, October 17, 2017 - 01:00

Walk for sight

A walk was held on October 12 to mark World Sight Day 2017. The event was organized by the National Eye Hospital under the theme ‘Make Vision Count’. The walk commenced from the Viharamaha Devi Park to the National Eye Hospital premises. National Eye Hospital director Dr L J Muhamdiramge, Director Dr I M Rifaz, Eye Surgeon Dr Mudhitha Kulathunga, Non Communicable Disease Unit Director Dr F L Siriwardena, Senior Associate Secretary Dr U S B Ranasinghe, Consultant Community Physician Kapila Piyasena, Quality Assurance Nursing Officer at the National Eye Hospital Nadee Rajakaruna headed the event.
