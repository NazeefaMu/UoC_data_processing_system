## Title: Reginald Cooray reappointed as Northern Province Governor
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, April 13, 2018 - 11:05

Reginald Cooray reappointed as Northern Province Governor

 
Central Province Governor Reginald Cooray was reappointed as the Northern Province Governor by President Maithripala Sirisena, this morning.
He was appointed as the Central province Governor yesterday.
