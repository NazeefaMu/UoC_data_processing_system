## Title: Matara Police plan special security measures for Avurudu
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, April 11, 2018 - 01:00

Matara Police plan special security measures for Avurudu

Matara Police had planned special security measures for the upcoming Sinhala and Tamil New Year (Avurudu) festive season, in order to protect the public from pickpockets and other criminal gangs. According to police intelligence, there had been a reported rise in the numbers of such criminals entering the Matara district, recently. Accordingly, police had currently been deployed in the Matara, Weligama, Kamburupitiya, Hakmana, Akuressa, Morawaka, Deniyaya, Kirinda, Dickwelle and Kekanadura towns. The aforementioned security measures had been initiated on the instructions of Matara Division Superintendent of Police G. H. Marapana.
