## Title: Newcomers Matara Thomians shock Bens
## Supertitle: ‘Singer Cup’ U19 Schools Cricket :
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, January 13, 2018 - 01:00

‘Singer Cup’ U19 Schools Cricket :
Newcomers Matara Thomians shock Bens

Newcomers to division one St. Thomas’ College Matara pulled off a stunning six wickets win over St. Benedict’s College Kotahena in their ‘Singer Cup’ Under 19 inter-schools cricket match played at Matara yesterday.
The Bens were shot out for totals of 101 and 97 in a low scoring game where the Thomians also didn’t make much headway with the bat scoring 131 in their first innings and chasing down a target of 69 in the fourth innings.
Mahinda College Galle and St Joseph Vaz College Wennappuwa were the other schools to win yesterday over St. Sylvester’s College Kandy and St. Mary’s College Kegalle respectively.
Twenty-five wickets fell on the first day of the match between St. Peter’s College Bambalapitiya and St. Sebastian’s College at Moratuwa yesterday.
