## Title: Lasantha Alagiyawanna appointed Finance, Mass Media Deputy Minister
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 31, 2017 - 16:30

Lasantha Alagiyawanna appointed Finance, Mass Media Deputy Minister

 
President Maithripala Sirisena appointed Lasantha Alagiyawanna as the Deputy Minister of Finance and Mass Media today.
Apart from this appointment, four new State Ministers and three new Deputy Ministers were appointed by President Sirisena earlier today.
