## Title: Death former Silumina Journalist Karunadasa Vidanegamage
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, August 30, 2017 - 01:00

Death former Silumina Journalist Karunadasa Vidanegamage

The death occurred of former Silumina Journalist Karunadasa Vidanegamage. He was 70. His remains are lying at his residence at number 322/2, Rattandaluwatta Road, Dalugangoda near the Kelaniya University. Funeral takes place at the Hunupitiya Public Cemetery on Thursday, August 31 at 5 p.m.
