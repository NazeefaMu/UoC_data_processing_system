## Title: Arrested for alleged sexual assault
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 30, 2018 - 01:00

Arrested for alleged sexual assault

Nikaweratiya Police arrested a karate master in Kurunegala in connection with the sexual assault on a 14-year-old girl. The victim is one of his students.
The suspect, a 32-year-old, had allegedly assaulted the girl at an abandoned house. The victim said that he had abused her on several other occasions as well.
