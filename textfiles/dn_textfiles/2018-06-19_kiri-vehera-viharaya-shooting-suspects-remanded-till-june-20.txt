## Title: Kiri Vehera Viharaya shooting: Suspects remanded till June 20
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Law & Order
## Date: Tuesday, June 19, 2018 - 01:00

Kiri Vehera Viharaya shooting: Suspects remanded till June 20

The two suspects fired at Kiri Vehera Viharaya Chief Incumbent Ven.Kobawaka Dhamminda Thera were remanded till June 20 by the Tissamaharama Magistrate yesterday.
The two suspects in the 17–19 age group were arrested on Sunday evening.The main suspect Asela Bandara had been working as the Chief Kapurala of the Mahasen Devalaya and three other suspects were also remanded till June 20.
Ven.Kobawaka Dhamminda Thera and Ven. Deedapola sobitha Thera were injured when a group of gunmen fired at them near the Kirivehera cetiya.
The vehcile that was left behinfd by the suspects after the shooting was recovered by Police and they searched for the main suspect following a tip off.
The main suspect was arrested on Saturday at Pepiliyana with two other suspects by a special Police team attached to the Tangalle Police Division.
