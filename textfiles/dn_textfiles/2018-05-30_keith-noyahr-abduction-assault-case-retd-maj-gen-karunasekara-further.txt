## Title: Keith Noyahr abduction, assault case: (Retd.) Maj. Gen. Karunasekara further remanded
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, May 30, 2018 - 13:33

Keith Noyahr abduction, assault case: (Retd.) Maj. Gen. Karunasekara further remanded

 
The Former Military Intelligence Director and Chief of Staff of the Army, Major General (Retired) Amal Karunasekara who is alleged to have  involved in abducting and assaulting Journalist Keith Noyahr was further remanded until June 13.
Journalist Keith Noyahr was abducted and assaulted in 2008.
