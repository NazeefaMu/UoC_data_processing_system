## Title: Best Fashion Moments From The Red Carpet Cannes Film Festival 2017
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Features
## Date: Friday, June 2, 2017 - 01:00

Best Fashion Moments From The Red Carpet Cannes Film Festival 2017

After 12 magical days in the oh-so-glamorous French Riviera, we bid the 2017 Cannes Film Festival adieu.
With 17 films competing for the iconic Palme d’Or prize and over 12,000 film professionals in attendance, you bet everyone brought their fashion A game when it came to the red carpet. Although numerous worldly celebs looked incredible throughout the festival, we could only pick a few to land on our best dressed list ... and here they are.
