## Title: GMOA calls off strike
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, January 30, 2018 - 13:45

GMOA calls off strike

 
The Government Medical Officers Association (GMOA) called off the token strike having held talks with Health Minister Rajitha Senaratne, today.
The token strike was launched over demands of a viable solution to several matters including the SAITM. 
The strike was previously intended to be carried out from 8 a.m. today until 8 a.m. tomorrow.
