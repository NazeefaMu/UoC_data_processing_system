## Title: Over 2,000 hectares of paddy lands in Matara destroyed by floods
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, July 21, 2017 - 01:00

Over 2,000 hectares of paddy lands in Matara destroyed by floods

Over 2,000 hectares of paddy lands have been destroyed in the Matara district by the recent floods.
According to Matara Assistant Director of Agriculture N.M.A.Dharmapriya, about 8,000 farmer families in the Matara district were adversely affected.
According to surveys carried out at Divisional Secretariat Division level in the district, the largest number of paddy lands of 337 hectares had been destroyed in Divisional Secretariat Division of Welipitiya.
A total of 330 hectares of paddy cultivated lands in the Thihagoda Divisional Secretariat Division, 310 hectares of paddy cultivated lands in the Mulatiyana Divisional Secretariat Division, 180 hectares in the Pitabeddara DS Division, 174 hectares in the Pasgoda DS Division, 165 hectares in the Weligama DS Division and 100 hectares in the Athuraliya Divisional Secretariat Division had been completely destroyed.
Tender paddy plants and recently sown paddy lands in the Matara district have thus been badly affected by flood waters, Matara District Assistant Director of Agriculture Dharmapriya said.
As relief measures, the Agriculture Ministry has provided seed paddy to affected farmer families in the Matara district, he added.
