## Title: Compensation for Bogala mine victim’s family
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 24, 2017 - 01:00

Compensation for Bogala mine victim’s family

The Labour and Trade Union Ministry provided compensation of Rs. 2.4 million to the family of the person who died in the Bogala mine. Scholarships and school instruments were also provided to his children, Labour and Trade Union Relations Ministry sources said. Scholarships and school instruments were provided with the assistance of the Shrama Wasana Fund. Two scholarships worth Rs. 100,000 were provided to two children.
The company has made arrangements to pay the salary of the worker to his wife until his retirement age. The family of the worker will be allowed to stay in the quarters given to him until his retirement age. The worker had been working in the Bogala mine since 1996 and killed in a tunnel in 350 feet down.
