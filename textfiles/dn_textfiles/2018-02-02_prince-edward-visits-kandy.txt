## Title: Prince Edward Visits  Kandy
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, February 2, 2018 - 01:09

Prince Edward Visits  Kandy

The Earl of Wessex, Prince Edward and Countess Sophie who visited Kandy yesterday were welcomed to the city by Central Province Governor Niluka Ekanayake and Central Province Chief Minister Sarath Ekanayake who presented them with a sheaf of betel - the traditional method of greeting, near the Queen’s Hotel in Kandy.Picture by Asela Kuruluwansha
