## Title: Motorcycle stolen
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Wednesday, September 26, 2018 - 01:00

Motorcycle stolen

A 34-year-old resident in Bopathgama, Arachchikattuwa has complained to Arachchikattuwa police that his motorcycle valued at Rs. 95,000 was robbed while it was parked in a garage at the adjoining house to his mother’s residence.
He had parked the motorcycle before he was on a trip to Kataragama.
Investigations are continuing.
