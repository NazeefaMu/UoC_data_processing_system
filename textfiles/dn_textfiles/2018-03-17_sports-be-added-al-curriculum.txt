## Title: Sports to be added to AL curriculum
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, March 17, 2018 - 01:00

Sports to be added to AL curriculum

Kurunegala District UNP Parliamentarian and Education Minister Akila Viraj Kariyawasam has made arrangements to introduce Sports as a subject to the G. C. E. (A. L.) school curriculum in the near future.
Every student should engage in some type of sport at school and it should be compulsory for all students. In this regard, a circular had been issued to all schools countrywide. For developing sports activities among students, a sum of Rs. 2.5 million had been currently allocated, Minister Akila Viraj Kariyawasam told the Daily News in Kurunegala, yesterday.
