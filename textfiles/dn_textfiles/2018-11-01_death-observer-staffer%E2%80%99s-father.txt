## Title: Death of Observer staffer’s father
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, November 1, 2018 - 01:00

Death of Observer staffer’s father

Nandadasa Deegodage (ex-Govt. Press), father of Gaya Mihirini of the Sunday Observer Editorial passed away on Tuesday. His remains lie at the Chandra Panagoda Parlour, Athurugiriya Road, Malabe. Funeral will take place today (Thursday) at the Thalahena cemetery at 5.00 p.m.
