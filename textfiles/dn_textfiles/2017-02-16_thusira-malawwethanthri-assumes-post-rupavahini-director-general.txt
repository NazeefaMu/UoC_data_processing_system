## Title: Thusira Malawwethanthri assumes post of Rupavahini Director General
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, February 16, 2017 - 17:30

Thusira Malawwethanthri assumes post of Rupavahini Director General

 
Attorney-At-Law Thusira Malawwethanthri assumed post of the Director General of the Sri Lanka Rupavahini Corporation earlier today (16).
He had received his letter of appointment from Parliamentary Reforms and Mass Media Minister Gayantha Karunathilake, at the ministry premises yesterday.
Pakistan High Commissioner Shakeel Husain, Media Ministry Secretary Nimal Bopage, Provincial Council Member Nishantha Sri Warnasinghe, former minister Achala Jagoda, Sri Lanka Rupavahini Radio Corporation Director Nanda Muruththettuwegama, Inspector General of Police Pujith Jayasundara, Special Task Force Chief M.R. Latheef, Senior Superintendent of Police Ravi Vaidyalankara, W. Jayasiri, Buddhadasa Galappathi, Dr. Samantha Herath, Dharmasiri Bandaranayake, Dharmasiri Pathiraja, Dayan Witharana, Amitha Wadisinghe, Noeline Honter, Paul Marie Mendis and many other distinguished guests participated.
