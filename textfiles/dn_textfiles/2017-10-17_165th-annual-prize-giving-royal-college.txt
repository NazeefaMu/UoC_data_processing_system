## Title: The 165th Annual Prize Giving of Royal College
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Tuesday, October 17, 2017 - 01:15

The 165th Annual Prize Giving of Royal College

The 165th Annual Prize Giving of Royal College, Colombo was held under the patronage of President Maithripala Sirisena at the college premises yesterday. Picture shows the President in conversation with Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe. Ministers Akila Viraj Kariyawasam, Rauff Hakeem, Gayantha Karunathilake , Ranjith Siyambalapitiya and Royal College Principal B.A. Abeyratne were also present.
