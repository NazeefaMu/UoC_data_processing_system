## Title: Applications called for Cultural competitions
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, December 14, 2018 - 01:00

Applications called for Cultural competitions

The Cultural Affairs Department has called for applications from suitable candidates who study music and dancing at institutions which are registered with the Cultural Affairs Department for their annual competitions.
The closing date for applications will be January 10, 2019 at 4 pm. Eligible candidates must indicate the category to which they apply on the envelope. Application forms could be obtained at www.culturaldept.gov.lk. Registrations will be continued till January 5, 2019, Department sources stated.
