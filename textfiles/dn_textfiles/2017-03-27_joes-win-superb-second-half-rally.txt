## Title: Joes win with superb second half rally
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Monday, March 27, 2017 - 01:00

Joes win with superb second half rally

St Joseph’s College gave a superb second half performance to beat St Anthony’s College Katugastota by 24 points (2 goals, 2 tries) to 11 (1 try, 2 penatlies) in their Singer schools rugby match played at Bogambara Stadium yesterday.
At half time St Anthony’s led 8-5.
Referee: Irshad Cader.
