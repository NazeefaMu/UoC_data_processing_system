## Title: SriLankan Airlines Board meets Minister Kiriella
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Finance
## Date: Thursday, April 5, 2018 - 01:00

SriLankan Airlines Board meets Minister Kiriella

The SriLankan Airlines Board headed by Ranjith Fernando met the Minister of Public Enterprise and Kandy Development Lakshman Kiriella yesterday to discuss the future strategies of the airline and how it could be turned around. Here the new Board with the Minister in Colombo yesterday.
