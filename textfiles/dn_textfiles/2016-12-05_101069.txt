## Title: Three arrested  for stoning SLTB  buses, trains
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Monday, December 5, 2016 - 01:00

Three arrested  for stoning SLTB  buses, trains

 
Ahangama Police arrested three suspects for pelting trains and SLTB buses with stones on the day private buses went on strike (Dec. 2). A railway security officer was injured when the Colombo-Matara express rain was pelted with stones,
A lorry was also damaged due to stone pelting in the Ahangama Kanda area. Police said a single group was responsible for all these attacks. Investigations are contusing.
