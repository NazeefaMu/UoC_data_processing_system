## Title: Tony Blair in SL for Economic Forum
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 January 2016 03:48 pm

Tony Blair in SL for Economic Forum


	

Former Prime Minister of the United Kingdom, Tony Blair had arrived in Sri Lanka this evening from a special flight to take part in the Economic Forum organised by the government and to be held on January 7and 8 in Colombo. (T.K.Kapila)
