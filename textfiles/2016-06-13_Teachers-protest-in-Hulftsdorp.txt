## Title: Teachers protest in Hulftsdorp
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 13 June 2016 05:05 pm

Teachers protest in Hulftsdorp


	



The JVP affiliated Ceylon Teacher Services Union (CTSU) held a protest in Hulftsdorp, Colombo today, against what the union called ‘irregular’ manner of recruitment of teachers in the Southern Province and injustices meted on those who passed the Sri Lanka Education Administrative Examinations.
