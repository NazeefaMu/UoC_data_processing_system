## Title: Video: Protest against SAITM
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 5 July 2016 01:36 pm

Video: Protest against SAITM


	




The Centre Against Private Medical Colleges and the Inter Medical Faculty Students Action Committee staged a protest against the South Asian Institute of Technology and Medicine (SAITM) outside the Hulftsdorp Court Complex today.
