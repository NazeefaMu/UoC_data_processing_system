## Title: Indian Republic Day marked in Colombo
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 26 January 2015 07:44 am

Indian Republic Day marked in Colombo


	


A ceremony to mark the 66th Republic Day of India was held today at the India House in Colombo under the aegis of High Commissioner Y. K. Sinha. Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
