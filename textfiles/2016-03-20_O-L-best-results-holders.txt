## Title: 2015 O/L best results holders
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 20 March 2016 02:26 pm

2015 O/L best results holders


	

The best result holders of the G.C.E. Ordinary level Examination 2015 are seen here in photographs.

Sathsarani Hettiarrachi (up-left), from Visakha Vidyalaya, Colombo got the first place at the G.C.E. Ordinary level Examination 2015.

Supun Punsara (up-right) of Nalanda College Colombo was placed second while Maleena Rathnayake (down-left) of Devi Balika Vidyalaya and Indeewaree Umayanga (down-right) of Mahamaya Balika Vidyalaya Kandy were placed third. Pix by Lahiru Harshana and J.A.L.Jayasinghe
