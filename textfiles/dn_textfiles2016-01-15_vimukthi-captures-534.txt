## Title: Vimukthi captures 5/34
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Friday, January 15, 2016 - 01:00

Vimukthi captures 5/34

Exchanges were even at the end of the first day's play in the inter school cricket match between Kingswood College and St. Mary's College, Kegalle which began at the Pallekele International Cricket Stadium yesterday.
The match will be continued today at 9.25 am.
The Marians winning the toss took first lease of the wicket and were dismissed for 194. Only Mas Raheem batted confidence to remain unbeaten on 57 with 8 fours. Left arm spinner Vimukthi Wijesiriwardena well to bowled capture 5 for 34.
At close the Kingswoodian had replied with 95 for 5 in 31 overs. There was no big score by any Kingswoodian who were tied by the left arm spin of Lasith Udage and Timira Kumara. But Ashan Senadeera was most successful of the bowlers capturing 3 for 19.
St. Mary's College - 194 all out in 47.5 overs (Sandaru Sriyashantha 21, G. Kotugoda 14, Sujeewa Ranjith 24, Thimira Supun Kumara 32, Mas Raheem 57 not out, I. Herath 2 for 28, Vimukthi Wijesiriwardena 5 for 35, Rangana Ratnayaka 2 for 39).
Kingswood College - 95 for 5 wkts in 31 overs (Meedum Weerasinghe 22, Dilshan Walisundera 20, Avishka Chanrasiri 21 not out, Nuvantha Hathurusinghe 19 not out, Ashen Senadeera 3 for 19).
