## Title: NSS BIA officers re-remanded till August 4
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 August 2016 12:28 pm

NSS BIA officers re-remanded till August 4


	

The Chairman of Nidahas Sewaka Sangamaya (NSS) of the Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA) Airport & Aviation Branch Warnasiri Bandara and two other drivers were further remanded till August 4 by the Warakapola Magistrate today.

They were arrested on Saturday for using two State owned vehicles for paying a floral tribute to the statue of former minister Dharmasiri Senanayake during the Joint Opposition’s Paada Yathra march. (Chaminda Jayalath)
