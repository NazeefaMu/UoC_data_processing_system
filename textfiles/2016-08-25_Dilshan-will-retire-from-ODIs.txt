## Title: Dilshan will retire from ODIs
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 August 2016 02:27 pm

Dilshan will retire from ODIs


	

TM Dilshan will retire from one day international (ODI) cricket after the third ODI between Sri Lanka and Australia on August 28 in Dambulla.

However, he will be available  for selection of T20S, Sri Lanka Cricket said.
