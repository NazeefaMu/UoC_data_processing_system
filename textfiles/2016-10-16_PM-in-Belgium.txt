## Title: PM in Belgium
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 October 2016 08:29 am

PM in Belgium


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe left for Brussels, Belgium this morning for high level discussions with EU to obtain the GSP Plus relief back to Sri Lanka.

The Prime Minister is expected to meet his Belgian counterpart Charles Michel and Prince Laurent as well as top officials of European Commission and European Union during his tour.(T.K.G. Kapila)
