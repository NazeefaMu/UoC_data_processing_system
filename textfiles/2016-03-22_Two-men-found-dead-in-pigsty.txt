## Title: Two men found dead in pigsty
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 March 2016 11:49 am

Two men found dead in pigsty


	

Two men were found dead in a pigsty at Eppawala this morning, Police said.One of them had injuries similar to those inflicted by a sharp weapon while the other had been strangled with a rope.

Police said they believe that the men might be in their sixty’s.

Investigations are continuing.
