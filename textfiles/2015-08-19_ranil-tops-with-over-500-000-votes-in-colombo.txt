## Title: Ranil tops with over 500,000 votes in Colombo
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 August 2015 03:15 am

Ranil tops with over 500,000 votes in Colombo


	

Incumbent Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe has topped the UNP Colombo district list with 500,566votes. UPFA former parliamentarian Wimal Weerawansa, who obtained 313,801 votes, has topped the UPFA list.Colombo District list
