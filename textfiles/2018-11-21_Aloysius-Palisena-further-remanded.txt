## Title: Aloysius, Palisena further remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 21 November 2018 03:49 pm

Aloysius, Palisena further remanded


	

Perpetual Treasuries Limited (PTL) Owner Arjun Aloysius and its CEO Kasun Palisena, who were arrested by the CID over the Central Bank bond scam, were further remanded till December 29 by the Fort Magistrate’s Court today. (TFT)
