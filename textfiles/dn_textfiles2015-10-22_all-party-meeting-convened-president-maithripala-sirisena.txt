## Title: An All Party Meeting convened by President Maithripala Sirisena
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Thursday, October 22, 2015 - 19:30

An All Party Meeting convened by President Maithripala Sirisena

An All Party Meeting convened by President Maithripala Sirisena to ascertain opinions of leaders of political parties about how the government should act regarding the Geneva proposals was held at the Presidential Secretariat yesterday. The picture shows the President discussing relevant matters with political party leaders.
