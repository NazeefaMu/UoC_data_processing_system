## Title: HC permits Dhammaloka Thera to travel to UK
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 August 2016 11:01 am

HC permits Dhammaloka Thera to travel to UK


	

The Colombo High Court today permitted Ven. Uduwe Dhammaloka Thera to travel abroad.

Yesterday the Thera sought to travel to UK from September 3 to 7, to attend a religious event.

High Court Judge Nissanka Bandula Karunaratne permitting the suspect to travel abroad ordered him to return his passport on September 13.

The Thera was indicted by the Attorney General on two counts under the Flora and Fauna Act and the Public Properties Act for the alleged possession of a baby elephant without a license at Alan Mathiniyarama Temple. (Shehan Chamika Silva and T. Farook Thajudeen)
