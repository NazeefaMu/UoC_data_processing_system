## Title: Fire wreaks havoc
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 26 August 2016 07:27 pm

Fire wreaks havoc


	



The Naranminiya, Peliyagoda Chemical factory which was completely destroyed by a major fire this evening, is seen going up on flames. The fire destroyed the entire factory and some of the vehicles in the vicinity. Pix by Samantha Perera and Lal S Kumara
