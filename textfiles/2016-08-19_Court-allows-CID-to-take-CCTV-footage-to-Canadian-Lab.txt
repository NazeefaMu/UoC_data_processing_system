## Title: Court allows CID to take CCTV footage to Canadian Lab
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 August 2016 05:42 pm

Court allows CID to take CCTV footage to Canadian Lab


	

Colombo Additional Magistrate Nishantha Peiris today permitted CID officials to handover the CCTV footage obtained from the site of Wasim Thajudeen's death to be checked at the laboratory of the British Columbia Institute in Canada.

More than CDs are to be sent for further clarification.

Some CID officers will leave for Canada on August 22 with the CCTV footage which were obtained from cameras installed at the Narahenpita-Baseline Road Junction. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
