## Title: Chennai floods: no report of Sri Lankan affected so far
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 2 December 2015 03:43 pm

Chennai floods: no report of Sri Lankan affected so far


	

The Foreign Affairs Ministry said today that they had not received any intimation of any Sri Lankan stranded in Chennai due to the floods so far, and that if there were any persons stranded there they would be taken care of.
The Ministry said anyone affected by the floods in Chennai could contact in Sri Lankan mission in Chennai on the following numbers if in need of any assistance.
Dharmapala : 00919962627956
Indraka: 00919444010999
