## Title: NTB concludes annual Investor Forum on high note
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 25 August 2016 12:00 pm

NTB concludes annual Investor Forum on high note


	







 

 

Nations Trust Bank recently concluded their sixth annual Investor Forum, showcasing the banks financial performance and noteworthy achievements in the first half, 2016. 
Held at the Hilton Colombo under the theme of “Stepping up to the Challenge” the forum was largely attended by investment advisors, stock brokers and fund managers. 
The bank addressed the importance of improving efficiencies and emphasized on the key reasons for their growth and progression this year. The forum also discussed the opportunities that lay ahead for the bank in addition to their future plans and aspirations for the year 2016.
