## Title: 382 drunk drivers nabbed during past 24 hours
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 April 2018 08:14 pm

382 drunk drivers nabbed during past 24 hours


	

A total of 382 drivers were nabbed for drunk driving island-wide during the 24 hours ended 6 a.m. Today, Police said.

Accordingly another 66 had been fined for reckless driving while a total of 347 were fined for speeding.

A total of 10,147 drivers had been fined island-wide for traffic violations during the 24 hours from April 12 to 13. (Thilanka Kanakarathna)
