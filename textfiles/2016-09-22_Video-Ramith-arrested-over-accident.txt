## Title: Video: Ramith arrested over accident
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 September 2016 08:24 am

Video: Ramith arrested over accident


	

Sri Lankan cricketer Ramith Rambukkwella had been arrested over a motor accident on the Independence Avenue in Colombo early this morning, the Police said.


The Police said that they suspect the cricketer to be drunk at the time of the incident and he had been taken in for a medical test. No one had been injured in the accident, according to Police.
