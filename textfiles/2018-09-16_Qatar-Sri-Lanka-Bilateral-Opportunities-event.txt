## Title: Qatar-Sri Lanka Bilateral Opportunities’ event
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 16 September 2018 10:20 am

Qatar-Sri Lanka Bilateral Opportunities’ event


	



Sri Lanka’s Ambassador to Qatar, A S P Liyanage, attended and addressed a knowledge sharing session hosted by the Doha Bank on ‘Qatar-Sri Lanka Bilateral Opportunities in Doha recently.

The Ambassador said bilateral relations between Qatar and Sri Lanka in terms of trade, investment, tourism and other activities will be further bolstered with the launch of ‘Sri Lanka Qatar Business Council’. Doha Bank CEO Dr R Seetharaman, Sri Lanka’s Central Bank Governor Dr Indrajit Coomaraswamy also attended the event.
