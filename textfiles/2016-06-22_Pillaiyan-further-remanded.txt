## Title: Pillaiyan further remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 June 2016 12:30 pm

Pillaiyan further remanded


	

Former Eastern Province Chief Minister Sivanesathuai Chandrakanthan alias Pillaiyan who had been held in remand in connection with the alleged assassination of Parliamentarian Joseph Pararajasingham, was further remanded for 14 days till July 07.
