## Title: SL Cricket Chief should resign: Arjuna
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 August 2017 03:45 pm

SL Cricket Chief should resign: Arjuna


	

Sri Lanka Cricket’s Chairman should step down following the washout by the Indian cricket team, former Sri Lanka cricket Captain Arjuna Ranatunga called.

“The Chairman of the Sri Lanka Cricket should take the responsibility of loss of matches and resign," Minister Ranatunga tweeted.
