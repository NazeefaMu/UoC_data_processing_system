## Title: Student protest causes traffic snarl on High Level Rd
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 31 August 2016 02:02 pm

Student protest causes traffic snarl on High Level Rd


	

Heavy traffic is reported along the High Level Road in Colombo due to a protest march carried out by the members of the Inter University Students' Federation (IUSF) against the South Asian Institute of Technology and Medicine (SAITM).
