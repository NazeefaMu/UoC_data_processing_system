## Title: Arrest the culprits
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 26 October 2015 08:04 am

Arrest the culprits


	


Drivers of long-distance express buses held a demonstration at the Matara bus terminal today urging the authorities to arrest the culprits who are alleged to have assaulted a Tangalle bus driver and a bus conductor at Ambalangoda recently. Pix by Krishan Jeewaka Jayaruk
