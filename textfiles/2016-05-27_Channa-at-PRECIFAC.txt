## Title: Channa at PRECIFAC
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 May 2016 10:12 am

Channa at PRECIFAC


	

Well-known dancer Channa Wijewardane arrived at the PRECIFAC a short while ago to give a statement on the monies paid to him for his performances at the opening ceremony of the Hambantota Harbour.

A spokesman for the commission said popular singer Nalin Perera was also expected to report to the commission at 12.00 noon today over the same matter.
