## Title: Namal is still dancing, says Ranjan
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 1 August 2016 04:50 pm

Namal is still dancing, says Ranjan


	



Deputy Minister Ranjan Ramanayake at a news conference said today that the Paada Yathrawa of the Joint Opposition was organised by group of people, whose past has been tainted with fraud and corruption. Displaying photos of MP Namal Rajapaksa dancing in the Caribbean Islands couple of years ago, he said the MP had danced then and continues to do so, and displayed a photo of the MP Rajapaksa dancing during the Paada Yathrawa.
