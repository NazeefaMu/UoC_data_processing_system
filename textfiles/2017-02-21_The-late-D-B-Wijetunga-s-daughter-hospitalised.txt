## Title: The late D.B. Wijetunga’s daughter hospitalised
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 21 February 2017 05:26 pm

The late D.B. Wijetunga’s daughter hospitalised


	

The late President D.B. Wijetunga’s daughter Chithrangani Wijetunga was today admitted to a private hospital in Kandy due to a sudden illness, Hospital sources said. She is to be transferred to a hospital in Colombo for further treatment. (J.A.L. Jayasinghe)
