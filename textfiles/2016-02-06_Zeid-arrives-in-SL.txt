## Title: Zeid arrives in SL
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 February 2016 09:19 am

Zeid arrives in SL


	

The UN High Commissioner for Human Rights Prince Zeid Ra’ad Al Hussein arrived in Sri Lanka this morning on a four-day official visit.

Following an invitation extended by the Sri Lankan Government, the UNHR Chief arrived at the Bandaranaike International Airport, Katunayake with a six-member delegation whom were welcomed by officials of the Foreign Affairs Ministry.

During his four-day visit, Prince Zeid is scheduled to meet President Maithripala Sirisena, Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe, Foreign Affairs Minister Mangala Samaraweera, other ministers and high-level Government officials. (T.K.G.Kaplia)
