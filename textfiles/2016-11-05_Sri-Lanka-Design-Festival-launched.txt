## Title: Sri Lanka Design Festival launched
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 5 November 2016 12:36 pm

Sri Lanka Design Festival launched


	




Sri Lanka Design Festival-2016, the lifestyle and fashion exhibition organised by the AOD was launched at the SLECC yesterday.  The SLDF, which is considered as one of South Asia’s most business-friendly and commercially successful global platforms for design and related industries would be concluded on Sunday.
