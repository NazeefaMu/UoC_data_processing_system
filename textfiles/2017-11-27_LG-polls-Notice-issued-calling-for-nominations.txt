## Title: LG polls: Notice issued calling for nominations
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 November 2017 01:05 pm

LG polls: Notice issued calling for nominations


	

The notice calling for nominations for 93 Local Government (LG) bodies was issued by respective returning officers today, the Elections Commission said.

Accordingly, the accepting of candidate’s deposits will begin today and continue till December 13. Nominations will be accepted from December 11 to 14.
