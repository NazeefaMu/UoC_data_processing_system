## Title: Tense situ at Meethotamulla protest
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 December 2015 04:55 pm

Tense situ at Meethotamulla protest


	

A police complaint had been lodged by the people who were protesting against a move by the Colombo Municipality to dump more garbage into Meethotamulla area claiming that the supporters of Kolonnawa Mayor had attacked them during the protest held today.
Meanwhile, the supporters of the Mayor had also lodged a complaint claiming that they were attacked by the protestors.
