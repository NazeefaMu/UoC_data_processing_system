## Title: Compassion!
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 8 August 2017 09:08 pm

Compassion!


	



Ven. Buddangala Ananda Thera (Retired Major General Ananda Weerasekera) today paid a visit to Jaffna and met High Court Judge M. Ilanchelian. The Thera expressed his gratitude to the Judge over his concern about the family of late Sub Inspector Sarath Hemachandra who was shot at while protecting the Judge. The Thera also handed over a letter to the judge.
