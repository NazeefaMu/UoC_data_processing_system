## Title: CB bond issue: Harsha at PCol
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 June 2017 11:50 am

CB bond issue: Harsha at PCol


	

Deputy Minister Harsha de Silva arrived at the Presidential Commission of Inquiry to Investigate and Inquire into the Issuance of Treasury Bonds this morning to give a statement in connection with the issuance of Central Bank bonds, a spokesman for the commission said.
