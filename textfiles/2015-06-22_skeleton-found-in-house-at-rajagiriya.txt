## Title: Video: Skeleton found in house at Rajagiriya
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 June 2015 10:00 am

Video: Skeleton found in house at Rajagiriya


	


	A skeleton was recovered from a house at Silva Mawatha in Welikada, Rajagiriya, police said.
	They said skeleton of the 55-year-old householder who lived alone was found by his sister.
	She had visited the house because there was no communication from her brother in a long time. “Investigations are being carried out to determine if the skeleton is that of the householder” police said.
