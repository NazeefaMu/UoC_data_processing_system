## Title: <U+200B>No Sri Lankans affected by Paris attack -SL
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 14 November 2015 03:34 am

<U+200B>No Sri Lankans affected by Paris attack -SL


	


		 		 
		 
			
Sri Lanka said that so far there have been no reports of any Sri Lankan nationals in Paris, France being affected by a series of terror attacks that rocked Paris on Friday evening.
“No reports of Sri Lankans affected so far. The Embassy in Paris is in close and constant contact with the relevant agencies,” the Foreign Ministry tweeted.
The ministry also said that Sri Lankans in Paris who need assistance could contact Col. Nazeer Majeed via +33620505232 or Deputy Chief of Mission Ms. Gunasekera via +33677048117
