## Title: Drunken school-van driver nabbed
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 May 2016 02:38 pm

Drunken school-van driver nabbed


	

A school van driver had been arrested in Piliyandala for consuming liquor, while transporting fifteen school children, the Police said.


The Police said the 40-year-old driver had been after noticing an alcoholic smell coming from the van by Traffic Policeman in front of a certain school.


The driver, who is under Police custody, would be produced in court tomorrow, the Police said.  (Bimal Shaman Jayasinghe)
