## Title: Relief period to pay water and electricity bills in affected areas
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 May 2016 07:43 pm

Relief period to pay water and electricity bills in affected areas


	

Finance Ministry said today a 30-day relief period has been declared for the households in disaster stricken areas to settle their water and electricity bills.

Finance Minister Ravi Karunanayake said the decision was taken at the Cabinet meeting today.
