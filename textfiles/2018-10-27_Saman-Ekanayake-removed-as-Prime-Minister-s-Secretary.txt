## Title: Saman Ekanayake removed as Prime Minister’s Secretary
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 October 2018 01:42 pm

Saman Ekanayake removed as Prime Minister’s Secretary


	

President Maithripala Sirisena has removed Saman Ekanayeka as the Secretary to former Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe.
