## Title: Shashi Welgama released on bail
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 March 2017 11:59 am

Shashi Welgama released on bail


	

Former SLTB Chairman Shashi Welgama, who was remanded on charges of a financial misappropriation amounting to Rs. 125 million at the SLTB, was released on bail by the Colombo High Court today.

He was released on a cash bail of Rs.200, 000 and two sureties of Rs. 5 million each.

Mr. Welgama was also banned from leaving the country.  (T. Farook Thajudeen)
