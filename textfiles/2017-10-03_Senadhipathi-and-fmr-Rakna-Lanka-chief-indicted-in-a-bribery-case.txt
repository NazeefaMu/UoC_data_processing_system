## Title: Senadhipathi and fmr Rakna Lanka chief indicted in a bribery case
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 October 2017 01:34 pm

Senadhipathi and fmr Rakna Lanka chief indicted in a bribery case


	

Avant – Garde Chairman Nissanka Senadhipathi and former Rakna Lanka Chairman,(Rtd) Major General Palitha Fernando have been indicted for Rs.35.5 million bribery case by the Colombo High Court Judge R. Gurusinghe today.
The trials will be held from March 19 onwards.
