## Title: Protest by fishermen disrupts coastal railway service
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 July 2016 09:30 am

Protest by fishermen disrupts coastal railway service


	

Train services on the coastal railway line between Dehiwala and Mount Lavinia is currently disrupted because of a protest by fishermen, along the railway line.

The railway line was disrupted when the fishermen had torched a fishing trawler on the railway line.
