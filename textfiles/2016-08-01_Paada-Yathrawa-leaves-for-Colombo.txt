## Title: Paada Yathrawa leaves for Colombo
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 August 2016 09:53 am

Paada Yathrawa leaves for Colombo


	

The Joint Opposition’s Jana Satana Paada Yathrawa left Kiribathgoda this morning and is heading to its destination in Colombo.

The march which began on Thursday from Peradeniya reached Kiribathgoda last night and is expected to reach the Peliyagoda area around 1pm.

However, the location of the final rally which is to be held when the march reaches Colombo has not been revealed yet.  

MPs Wimal Weerawansa, Namal Rajapaksa, Udaya Gammanpila, Mahindananda Aluthgamage, Rohitha Abeygunawardane, C.B. Ratnayake, Johnston Fernando and other MPs of the joint opposition have joined the last day of the march.
