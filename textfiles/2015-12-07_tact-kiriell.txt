## Title: MR’s security intact: Kiriella
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 7 December 2015 09:32 am

MR’s security intact: Kiriella


	

House Leader and Minister Lakshman Kiriella today informed parliament that the security provided to former president Mahinda Rajapaksa had not been reduced.
Responding to a question asked by UPFA MP Dinesh Gunawardene on Mr. Rajapaksa’s security, the minister said it had not been reduced nor would be reduced in the future. (Yohan Perera)
