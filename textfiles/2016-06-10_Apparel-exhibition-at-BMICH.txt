## Title: Apparel exhibition at BMICH
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 10 June 2016 06:33 pm

Apparel exhibition at BMICH


	

The Apparel Industry Suppliers Exhibition (AISEX) was started yesterday at the Sirimavo Bandaranaike Memorial Exhibition Centre which aims to bring all suppliers and service organizations under one roof. The exhibition will focus on a wide range of textile machinery, accessories and services from many parts of the world.  Pics by Kithsiri De Mel
