## Title: Video: STF seize 85 kilos of Kerala Ganja
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 7 July 2016 03:21 pm

Video: STF seize 85 kilos of Kerala Ganja


	



More than 85 kilos of Kerala Ganja smuggled in from India were seized and two suspects were arrested during a raid in Kalpitiya yesterday morning by the Special Task Force (STF). Pix by Hiran Priyankara
