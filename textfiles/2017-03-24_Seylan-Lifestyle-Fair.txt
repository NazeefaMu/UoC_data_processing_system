## Title: 'Seylan Lifestyle Fair 2017'
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 24 March 2017 03:30 pm

'Seylan Lifestyle Fair 2017'


	


The Seylan Lifestyle Fair 2017 began today at the Sirimavo Bandaranaike Memorial Exhibition Center (BMIC) and it will be held till 26. The exhibition will showcase over 200 stalls with electronics, clothing and many more consumer goods. Pix by Kithsiri de Mel
