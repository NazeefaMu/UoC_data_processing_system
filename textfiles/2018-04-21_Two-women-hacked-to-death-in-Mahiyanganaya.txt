## Title: Two women hacked to death in Mahiyanganaya
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 21 April 2018 10:02 am

Two women hacked to death in Mahiyanganaya


	

A 59-year-old woman and her 40-year-old daughter were found hacked to death at Mapakadawewa in Mahiyanganaya last night, Police said.

They said the post-mortem examination would be held today and investigations are ongoing to arrest the suspects. (Palitha Ariyawansha)
