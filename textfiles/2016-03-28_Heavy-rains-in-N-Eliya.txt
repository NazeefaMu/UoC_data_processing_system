## Title: Heavy rains in N’ Eliya
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 28 March 2016 01:18 pm

Heavy rains in N’ Eliya


	



Nuwara Eliya experienced heavy rains today after almost two months of drought. Rains were also reported in Shanthipura and Hawa Eliya for about one and half hours. Pix by Shelton Hettiarachchi
