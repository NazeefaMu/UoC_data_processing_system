## Title: Pera students clash during Shramadana
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 7 July 2016 04:13 pm

Pera students clash during Shramadana


	

Eleven students of the Peradeniya University have been hospitalized following a clash among two groups of students today.

The injured students were admitted to the Peradeniya Teaching Hospital with minor injuries.

Police said a heated argument during a shramadana campaign held at the University had turned in to a clash.
