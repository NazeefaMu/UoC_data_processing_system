## Title: Celebration on fourth day
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 16 August 2016 09:47 pm

Celebration on fourth day


	



Sri Lanka’s Kausal Silva raises his bat and helmet in celebration after scoring a century (100 runs) during the fourth day of the third and the final Test cricket match between Sri Lanka and Australia at the Sinhalese Sports Club (SSC) ground in Colombo on August 16.
