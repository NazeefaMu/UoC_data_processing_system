## Title: Aluthgamage re-remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 September 2016 12:01 pm

Aluthgamage re-remanded


	

Mahindananda Aluthgamage, who was arrested by the CID over money laundering charges for allegedly purchasing a property worth Rs. 27 million in Colombo 7, was today ordered to be re-remanded till September 27 by Colombo Chief Magistrate Gihan Pilapitiya. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
