## Title: Heroin haul seized in Grandpass
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 6 March 2017 10:12 pm

Heroin haul seized in Grandpass


	



A suspect was arrested with a large heroin haul valued at Rs. 800 million during a raid carried out in Grandpass area by the Police today. The suspect was arrested at his residence. Police spokesman said that the suspect would be produced in the Colombo Magistrate’s Court on tomorrow. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
