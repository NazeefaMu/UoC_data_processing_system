## Title: One drowns, two missing while sea bathing
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 September 2016 02:45 pm

One drowns, two missing while sea bathing


	

One man drowned while two others had gone missing today when bathing in the sea off Panadura, Police said.

The victims were identified as residents of Kahathuduwa. Police have launched search operations with the help of the lifeguards. (Bimal Shyaman Jayasinghe)
