## Title: Two die of electrocution
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 May 2016 11:07 am

Two die of electrocution


	

Two youths have died last night after being electrocuted, the Embilipitiya Police said.

They said the victims were in their twenties and from Embilipitiya area. The two youths were electrocuted after getting entangled in an electric wire while they were in a banana plantation.
