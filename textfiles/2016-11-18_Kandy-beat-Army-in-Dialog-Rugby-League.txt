## Title: Kandy beat Army in Dialog Rugby League
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 18 November 2016 11:00 pm

Kandy beat Army in Dialog Rugby League


	




Kandy SC beat Army SC 34-10 in the Dialog Rugby League match played at Racecourse International Rugby Stadium today. This was Kandy’s second win of the season. Pix by Pradeep Dilruckshana
