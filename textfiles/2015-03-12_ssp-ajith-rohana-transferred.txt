## Title: SSP Ajith Rohana transferred
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 12 March 2015 03:37 am

SSP Ajith Rohana transferred


	

SSP Ajith Rohana has been appointed as Colombo - North SSP and ASP Ruwan Gunasekara has been appointed as new police media spokesperson with immediate effect, Police Headquarters said today.
The officers have been transferred by approval of Secretary to the Public Security, Christian Religious Affairs and Disaster Management Ministry, Police said.
