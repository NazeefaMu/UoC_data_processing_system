## Title: Two boys drown in Teldeniya
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 12 November 2016 04:19 pm

Two boys drown in Teldeniya


	

Two boys aged 17 drowned at Hulu Ganga at Oruthota, Teldeniya when they went to bathe with a group of friends this afternoon, police said.

They said a group of six school children had gone to bathe in the Hulu Ganga and two of them had drowned while the other four were safe.
