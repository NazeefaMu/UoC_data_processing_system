## Title: Ayurveda protest march
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 26 July 2016 06:07 pm

Ayurveda protest march


	



Ayurvedic doctors and students today staged a protest march from the Ayurveda College, Rajagiriya to presidential Secretariat urging the government to formulate a national policy on annual recruitments. Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
