## Title: 2017 Budget- 2nd reading passed
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 November 2016 06:09 pm

2017 Budget- 2nd reading passed


	

The second reading of the 2017 Budget was passed in Parliament with a majority of 107 votes.

One hundred and sixty two parliamentarians voted in favour of the Budget while 55 voted against.

Seven MPs were absent during the voting.
