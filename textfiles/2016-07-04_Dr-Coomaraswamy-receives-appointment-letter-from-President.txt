## Title: Dr. Coomaraswamy receives appointment letter from President
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 July 2016 08:54 am

Dr. Coomaraswamy receives appointment letter from President


	

Dr. Indrajit Coomaraswamy received his appointment letter as the new Governor of the Central Bank from President Maithripala Sirisena a short while ago.

The President decided to appoint Dr. Coomaraswamy as the new Governor last Saturday.
