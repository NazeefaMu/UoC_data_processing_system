## Title: PM visits Gintota
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 19 November 2017 02:05 pm

PM visits Gintota


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe visited the Gintota in Galle today after the tense situation. Ministers Sagala Ratnayake, Vajira Abeywardane and IGP Pujith Jayasundara were also present.
