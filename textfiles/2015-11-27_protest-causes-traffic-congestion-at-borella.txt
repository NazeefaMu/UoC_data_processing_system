## Title: Protest causes traffic congestion at Borella
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 November 2015 09:29 am

Protest causes traffic congestion at Borella


	

Traffic congestion was reported in Borella because of a protest march by the Inter University Bikkhu Federation (IUBF) and Inter University Students Federation (IUSF).
The protest march had reached Borella Junction.
