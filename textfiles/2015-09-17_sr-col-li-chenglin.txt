## Title: Sr. Col. Li Chenglin calls on State Minister
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 17 September 2015 09:44 am

Sr. Col. Li Chenglin calls on State Minister


	


Sr. Col. Li Chenglin, the Military, Naval and Air Attaché of Chinese Embassy in Sri Lanka yesterday met State Minister of Defence Ruwan Wijewardene at the Ministry of Defence in Colombo. Minister and Sr. Col. Li Chenglin held bilateral discussions and pledged to support the two countries. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
