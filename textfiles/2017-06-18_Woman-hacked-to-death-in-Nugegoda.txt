## Title: Woman hacked to death in Nugegoda
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 June 2017 09:16 am

Woman hacked to death in Nugegoda


	

A 25-year-old woman has been hacked to death at Sooriyamal Mawatha in Nugegoda last night, Police said.

The victim was identified as a resident of Mathugama.

Kohuwala police arrested a suspect believed be the fiancee of the victim, in connection with the murder.
