## Title: Video: Family members visit Yoshitha at Welikada
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 February 2016 01:53 pm

Video: Family members visit Yoshitha at Welikada


	

Former president Mahinda Rajapaksa’s second son Yoshitha, currently in remand custody at Welikada Prison was this morning visited by his mother Shiranthi, brother Rohitha and several politicians this morning.
