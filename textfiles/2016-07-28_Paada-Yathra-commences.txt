## Title: ‘Paada Yathra’ commences
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 28 July 2016 10:40 am

‘Paada Yathra’ commences


	

The 'Paada Yathra’, organized by the Joint Opposition, commenced at the Peradeniya junction-just outside the Kandy city limit-a short while ago after performing religious activities by the Joint Opposition MPs led by former President Mahinda Rajapaksa at the Getambe Raja Maha Viharaya.
