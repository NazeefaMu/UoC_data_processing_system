## Title: 13-year-old girl found dead
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 28 January 2016 06:08 pm

13-year-old girl found dead


	

The body of a 13-year-old girl was found from a home in Mawathagama this evening, Police said.
They said the body was found in the kitchen but the cause of death was not known as yet.
The Mawathagama police were investigating the matter.
