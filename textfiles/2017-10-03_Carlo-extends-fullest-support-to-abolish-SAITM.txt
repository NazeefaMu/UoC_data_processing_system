## Title: Carlo extends fullest support to abolish SAITM
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 3 October 2017 05:25 pm

Carlo extends fullest support to abolish SAITM


	



Former Sri Lanka Medical Council (SLMC) Chairman Professor Carlo Fonseka said today that he would extend his utmost support to abolish the South Asian Institute of Technology and Medicine (SAITM), when he visited the protest campaign conducted by the Inter University Students' Federation (IUSF) and other anti-SAITM students outside the Fort Railway Station in Colombo.
