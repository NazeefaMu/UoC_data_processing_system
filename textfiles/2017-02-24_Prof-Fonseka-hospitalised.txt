## Title: Prof. Fonseka hospitalised
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 February 2017 08:01 am

Prof. Fonseka hospitalised


	

President of Sri Lanka Medical Council (SLMC), Professor Carlo Fonseka was admitted to the National Hospital due to a brief illness. However, Hospital sources said Prof. Fonseka is to be discharged soon.
