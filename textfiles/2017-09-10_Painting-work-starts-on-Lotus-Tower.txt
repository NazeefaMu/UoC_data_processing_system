## Title: Painting work starts on Lotus Tower
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 10 September 2017 06:01 pm

Painting work starts on Lotus Tower


	

The Lotus Tower, which would soon bloom to become the tallest man-made free-standing structure in Sri Lanka, was seen being given a white wash for the first time by a group of workers on a gondola along its massive trunk. Pix by Nisal Baduge
