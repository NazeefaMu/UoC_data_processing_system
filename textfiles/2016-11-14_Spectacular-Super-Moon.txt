## Title: Spectacular Super Moon
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 14 November 2016 11:08 pm

Spectacular Super Moon


	



Sri Lankans were able to view the rare sight of the ‘super moon’ this evening which appeared after January 26, 1948, while the next super moon will appear on November 25, 2034. The super moon appeared 14% bigger and 30% brighter. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
