## Title: Google live traffic in SL
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 November 2015 01:02 pm

Google live traffic in SL


	


	Google has made its live traffic updates available in Sri Lanka providing great ease for commuters to find roads with less traffic.
	This can be viewed through the Google Maps mobile application that can be downloaded on smart phones. Roads with heavy traffic would be indicated in red and roads with less or no traffic would be coloured in green on the map.
