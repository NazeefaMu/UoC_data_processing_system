## Title: Unhygienic food raids
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 7 April 2017 04:11 pm

Unhygienic food raids


	



The Colombo Municipality launched several special raids in Colombo City to halt the sale of unhygienic and expired food during the festival season. Large stocks of expired and unhygienic food were destroyed by officials. Pix by Damith Wickramasinghe
