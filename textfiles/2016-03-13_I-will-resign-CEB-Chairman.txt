## Title: I will resign – CEB Chairman
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 13 March 2016 05:55 pm

I will resign – CEB Chairman


	

Ceylon Electricity Board Chairman Anura Wijepala has decided to resign from his post, claiming that he will take responsibility for the three blackouts that occurred in the country during the last six months.


“There was nobody to take responsibility for the three blackouts during the last six months.

So I have decided to take responsibility and will send in my resignation letter soon after power is restored today,” Mr. Wijepala told the Daily Mirror.
