## Title: Man shot dead in Minuwangoda
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 August 2018 12:14 pm

Man shot dead in Minuwangoda


	

A 62-year-old man was shot dead by unidentified gunman at Yatiyana in Minuwangoda this morning, Police said.

They said the victim was identified as a resident of Minuwangoda.
