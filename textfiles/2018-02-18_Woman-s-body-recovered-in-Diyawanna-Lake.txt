## Title: Woman’s body recovered in Diyawanna Lake
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 February 2018 02:37 pm

Woman’s body recovered in Diyawanna Lake


	

A body of a 45 to 50-year-old woman had been recovered in Diyawanna Lake a short while ago, the Police said.

The identity of the woman has not yet established.

The Police said that the body would be sent to the Colombo Police mortuary for the post-mortem. (TK)
