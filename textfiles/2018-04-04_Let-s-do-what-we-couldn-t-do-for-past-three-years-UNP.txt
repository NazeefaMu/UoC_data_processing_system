## Title: Let’s do what we couldn’t do for past three years: UNP
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 April 2018 03:15 pm

Let’s do what we couldn’t do for past three years: UNP


	

UNP MP Dr. Jayampathy Wickramaratne today said the Government should engage in a course correction act and it should do what it could not do for the past three years.

Dr. Wickramaratne expressed this sentiment during the debate on the no-confidence motion against Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe in the House this afternoon. (Yohan Perera and Ajith Siriwadana)
