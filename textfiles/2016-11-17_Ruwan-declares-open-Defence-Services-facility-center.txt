## Title: Ruwan declares open Defence Services facility center
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 17 November 2016 08:10 pm

Ruwan declares open Defence Services facility center


	


State Minister of Defence Ruwan Wijewardene declared open facility center of Defence Services Command and Staff College at Batalanda today. State Ministry Secretary A.P.G.Kithsiri and Commandant of the Defence Services Command and Staff College, Major General Udaya Perera was also present. The distinguished guests inspected the facility after declaring it open. Pix by pradeep pathirana.
