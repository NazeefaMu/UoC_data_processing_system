## Title: SL fans kicked out by NZ police
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 10 January 2016 06:17 am

SL fans kicked out by NZ police


	


About six Sri Lankan fans were removed by New Zealand police and security guards from the New Zealand Vs Sri Lanka Twenty20 cricket match. Officers confiscated a number of small drums from the Sri Lankan fans. (Getty Images)
