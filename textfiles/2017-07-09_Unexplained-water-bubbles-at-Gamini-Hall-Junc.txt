## Title: Unexplained water bubbles at Gamini Hall Junc.
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 9 July 2017 05:29 pm

Unexplained water bubbles at Gamini Hall Junc.


	

A strange water phenomenon was captured this morning near the Maradana Gamini Hall Junction. Residents said the area was flooding even to a light shower and unexplained water bubbles could be seen during rains. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
