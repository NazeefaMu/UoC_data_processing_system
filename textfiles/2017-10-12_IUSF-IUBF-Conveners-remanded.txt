## Title: IUSF, IUBF Conveners remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 12 October 2017 01:33 pm

IUSF, IUBF Conveners remanded


	

Inter-University Student’s Federation (IUSF) Convener  Lahiru Weerasekara and Inter University Bhikkhu’s Federation (IUBF) Convener Tempitiye Sugathananda Thera were today remanded till October 26 by  Colombo Fort Magistrate Lanka Jayaratne in connection with the protest  carried out by them in Kollupitiya on Tuesday by violating court orders.
