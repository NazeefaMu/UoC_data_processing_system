## Title: Minister rejects CEB Chairman’s resignation
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 14 March 2016 10:53 am

Minister rejects CEB Chairman’s resignation


	

Minister of Power and Energy Ranjith Siyambalapitiya today rejected the resignation letter of Ceylon Electricity Board Chairman Anura Wijepala.

Mr. Wijepala yesterday decided to resign from his post, claiming that he would take responsibility for the three blackouts that occurred in the country during the last six months.
