## Title: Police curfew lifted
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 November 2017 09:35 am

Police curfew lifted


	

The12-hour police curfew which was imposed yesterday at 6.00pm in some Grama Seva Division within the Galle District has been lifted  at 6.00 am today, Police said.

The Police curfew was imposed in Kurunduwatha, Mahahapugala, Welipitimodara, Ukwaththa, Gintota East, Gintota West and Piyandigama following the tense situation in the area.

Police said the situation in those areas was now completely under control.
