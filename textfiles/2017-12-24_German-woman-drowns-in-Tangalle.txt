## Title: German woman drowns in Tangalle
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 December 2017 10:19 am

German woman drowns in Tangalle


	

A 59-year-old German woman drowned last evening while she was having a sea bath in Marakolliya, Tangalle. Police said.

They said the woman had died after admitted to the Tangalle Hospital.
