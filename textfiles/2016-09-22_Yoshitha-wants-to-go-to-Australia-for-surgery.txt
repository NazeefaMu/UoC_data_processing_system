## Title: Yoshitha wants to go to Australia for surgery
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 September 2016 10:57 am

Yoshitha wants to go to Australia for surgery


	

The request made by Yoshitha Rajapaksa in High Court seeking court permission to travel abroad for a medical treatment was fixed for consideration on September 28 by the Colombo High Court Judge A.A.R. Heiyanthuduwa today.

Former President Mahinda Rajapaksa’s son has sought court permission to travel to Australia for a month to undergo a surgery.

Earlier, Kaduwela Magistrate had imposed a travel ban on the Yoshitha over an inquiry conducted against him on money laundering charges. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
