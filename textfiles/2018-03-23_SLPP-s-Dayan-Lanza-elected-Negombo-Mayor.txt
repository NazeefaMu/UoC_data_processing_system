## Title: SLPP’s Dayan Lanza elected Negombo Mayor
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 March 2018 01:28 pm

SLPP’s Dayan Lanza elected Negombo Mayor


	

Warnakulasuriya Moses Dayan Lanza of the Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna (SLPP) has been elected as the Mayor of the Negombo Municipal Council.

Mr. Lanza received 25 votes while Royce Fernando of the UNP received 19 votes. Text and Pics by Hubert Feranndo
