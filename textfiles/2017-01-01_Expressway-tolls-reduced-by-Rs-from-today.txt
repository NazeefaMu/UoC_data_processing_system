## Title: Expressway tolls reduced by Rs. 50 from today
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 January 2017 09:43 am

Expressway tolls reduced by Rs. 50 from today


	

Tolls levied from vehicles on expressways from 9pm to 5am, have been reduced by Rs. 50 with effect from today, the Road Development Authority (RDA) said.

Director, Expressway Operations, Maintenance and Management of the RDA, S. Opanayaka said this reduction has been implemented under the 2017 budget proposal.
