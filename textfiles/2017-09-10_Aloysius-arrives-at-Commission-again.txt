## Title: Aloysius arrives at Commission again
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 10 September 2017 10:38 am

Aloysius arrives at Commission again


	

The owner of the Perpetual Treasuries Ltd, Arjun Aloysius arrived at the Presidential Commission of Inquiry (PCoI) for the fourth day, this morning.

President’s Counsel Anuja Premaratne appearing for PTL owner Arjun Aloysius on Friday informed the PCoI that he would file a legal submission tomorrow challenging the PCoI’s order summoning Mr. Aloysius to give evidence at the PCoI
