## Title: Pakistan President Mamnoon arrives
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 8 March 2016 02:17 pm

Pakistan President Mamnoon arrives


	

Pakistan President Mamnoon Hussain arrived at the Bandaranaike International Airport, Katunayake today on an official visit to Sri Lanka.

Mr. Hussain is scheduled to hold talks with President Maithripala Sirisena and Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe during his short visit. (T.K.G. Kapila)
