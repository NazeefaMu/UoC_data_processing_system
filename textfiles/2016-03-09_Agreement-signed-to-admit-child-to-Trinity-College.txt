## Title: Agreement signed to admit child to Trinity College
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 March 2016 10:44 am

Agreement signed to admit child to Trinity College


	

A Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) was signed by Education Minister Akila Viraj Kariyawasam and Principal of the Trinity College, Kandy Andrew Fowler-Watt to admit the six-year-old boy, who was deprived of a school over rumours that he was HIV infected.

Education Minister’s Media Secretary Kalpa Gunarathne said the principal has also expressed his willingness to support the child’s expenses throughout his schooling. (Piyumi Fonseka)
