## Title: Marine Drive Wellawatte exit lane closed for repairs
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 21 July 2015 07:11 am

Marine Drive Wellawatte exit lane closed for repairs


	

The Marine Drive exit lane at Wellawatte leading from E.A. Cooray Lane to Vivekananda Mawatha will be closed today and tomorrow for reconstruction work, police said. (LSP)
