## Title: Guard-of-Honour by SL Army for outgoing IGP
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 8 April 2016 04:31 pm

Guard-of-Honour by SL Army for outgoing IGP


	



Outgoing IGP N.K. Illangakoon today received a Guard-of-Honour by the Sri Lanka Army troops when he paid a courtesy visit to the Defence Ministry grounds. The IGP is seen saluting, during a parade. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
