## Title: Navy passing out parade
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 30 October 2016 10:42 pm

Navy passing out parade


	




State Minister of Defence Ruwan Wijewardene , who was the chief guest  on the occasion, inspects a guard of honour during  the passing  out parade  for navy  officer at the Eastern navy headquarters today. 30 navy officers who had graduated passed out on the occasion.  Navy Chief Ravindra Wijegunawardane  was also present.
