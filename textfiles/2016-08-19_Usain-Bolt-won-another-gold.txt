## Title: Usain Bolt won another gold
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 19 August 2016 08:56 am

Usain Bolt won another gold


	



Usain Bolt completed the Olympic sprint double for a third straight Games and then declared himself to be the best there’s ever been.

The Jamaican led from trigger to tape to win in 19.78 seconds, with Andre de Grasse (20.02) second and Christophe Lemaitre (20.12) third.
