## Title: Prez to swear in new Cabinet tomorrow: Gamini Lokuge
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 December 2018 08:32 pm

Prez to swear in new Cabinet tomorrow: Gamini Lokuge


	

President Maithripala Sirisena has informed UPFA MP’s that the new Cabinet would be sworn in tomorrow morning, UPFA MP Gamini Lokuge said today.
