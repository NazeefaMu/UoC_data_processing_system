## Title: Video: Heavy rains wreak havoc
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 16 May 2016 02:32 pm

Video: Heavy rains wreak havoc


	




Two temporary shops were destroyed in a landslide at Gagulathenna on the Nallathanni-Adam’s Peak Road today. No casualties were reported. Meanwhile, the water level of the Puttalam Lagoon had increased due to torrential rains. Pix by Gamini Jayasinghe, M.C. Jayaweera and Hiran Priyankara
