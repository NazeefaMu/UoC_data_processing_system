## Title: Geetha to chair Galle DDC
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 December 2015 06:25 pm

Geetha to chair Galle DDC


	

Actress turned politician Geetha Kumarasinghe was likely to be appointed as the Chairperson of the Galle District Development Committee, well informed sources said today.
She has been active in the Galle District development Committee in the recent past and was seen involved in arguments with the fellow members of the Committee.
Ms. Kumarasinghe was a notable absentee at the second and third reading debate on the budget.
Sources said she was likely to back President Maithripala Sirisena and the government in the future. (Yohan Perera)
