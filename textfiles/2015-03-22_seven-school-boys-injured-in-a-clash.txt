## Title: Seven school boys injured in clash
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 March 2015 03:54 pm

Seven school boys injured in clash


	

Seven students were injured in a clash between students of D.S. Senanayake College and Mahanama College at the end of the cricket encounter between them held at the SSC grounds this evening.
Police said six of the injured were discharged from the National Hospital, Colombo and one was undergoing treatment.
The students had run onto the grounds when the match ended and begun to fight.
