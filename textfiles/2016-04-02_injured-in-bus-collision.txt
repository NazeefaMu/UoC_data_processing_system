## Title: 15 injured in bus collision
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 2 April 2016 11:52 am

15 injured in bus collision


	

At least 15 passengers were injured after two buses collided head on at Nevilvila on the Badulla-Bibila main road toady, Police said.

The injured, including the two drivers were rushed to the Bibila Hospital.

Police said a driver and two other passengers were in critical condition.
