## Title: Regulatory commission for three-wheelers
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 20 August 2018 08:06 pm

Regulatory commission for three-wheelers


	



Finance Minister Mangala Samaraweera said a regulatory commission will be set up for three-wheelers. A draft proposal has been sent to the Attorney General’s Department in this regard to prepare the necessary legislation. Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
