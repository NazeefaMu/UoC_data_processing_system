## Title: Bangladesh ship caring floods relief arrives
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 5 June 2016 04:27 pm

Bangladesh ship caring floods relief arrives


	


Bangladesh Navy Ship “BNS Bangabandhu” arrived at the Port of Colombo with a stock of relief materials for Sri Lankans who were affected by floods and landslides. The ship was ceremonially welcomed by the Sri Lanka Navy in accordance with naval traditions. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
