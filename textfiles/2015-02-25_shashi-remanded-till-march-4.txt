## Title: Video: Shashi further remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 February 2015 08:29 am

Video: Shashi further remanded


	

Former Minister Wimal Weerawansa’s wife Shashi Weerawansa was remanded till March 4 by the Colombo Chief Magistrate today.
The Magistrate had yesterday ordered the prison authorities to transfer Ms. Weerawansa from a private hospital in Malabe to the Prison Hospital.Video by Sanath Desmond
