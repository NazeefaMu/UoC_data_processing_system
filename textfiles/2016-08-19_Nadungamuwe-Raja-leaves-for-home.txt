## Title: Nadungamuwe Raja leaves for home
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 19 August 2016 04:04 pm

Nadungamuwe Raja leaves for home


	



Nadungamuwe Raja which carried the sacred relics casket in the Kandy Perahera is seen paying homage to the Dalada Maligawa before leaving for Nadungamuwa at an auspicious time after completing its services at the Perahera. Pix by J.A.L. Jayasinghe
