## Title: High-tech training centre in Nawalapitiya
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 27 November 2017 11:29 am

High-tech training centre in Nawalapitiya


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe yesterday laid the foundation stone for a new high-tech training centre in Nawalapitiya. Minister of Higher Education and Highways Lakshman Kiriella and MP Ananda Aluthgamage were also present. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
