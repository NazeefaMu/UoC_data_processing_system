## Title: Video: Protest against VAT in Piliyandala
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 20 July 2016 02:17 pm

Video: Protest against VAT in Piliyandala


	



Most of the shops in Piliyandala were shut today in support of a protest against the government’s move to increase the Value Added Tax (VAT) to 15%. The protest has been organized by the Piliyandala-based merchants and consumers. Pix by Bimal Jayasinghe
