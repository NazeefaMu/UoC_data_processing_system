## Title: Sanath Nandasiri new Chancellor of Visual and Performing Arts Uni.
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 November 2016 03:07 pm

Sanath Nandasiri new Chancellor of Visual and Performing Arts Uni.


	

Veteran Musician Professor at the University Sanath Nandasiri has been appointed as the Visual and Performing Arts University’s Chancellor, which was earlier held by the late Maestro Pandith Amaradeva.

He received his appointment letter from President Maithripala Sirisena today.
