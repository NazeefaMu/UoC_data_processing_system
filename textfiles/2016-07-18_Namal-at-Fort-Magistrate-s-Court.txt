## Title: Namal at Fort Magistrate’s Court
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 July 2016 09:30 am

Namal at Fort Magistrate’s Court


	

UPFA MP Namal Rajapaksa, who was arrested and remanded on charges of financial misappropriation, arrived at the Fort Magistrate’s Court a short while ago.

He was re-remanded till today over the inquiry conducted against him on allegations of misappropriating Rs. 70 million worth of funds belonging to 'Krrish Company.’
