## Title: Stay order on full face helmet ban extended
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 May 2015 05:08 am

Stay order on full face helmet ban extended


	

The Court of Appeal today extended the interim order staying the police circular to ban the use of full face helmets until July 2.    
Two petitioners, a chartered accountant and a retired army major earlier filed a writ application challenging the full face helmet ban circular of the police.  (Lakmal Sooriyagoda)
