## Title: Minor oil leak in supply line at Colombo port
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 28 January 2017 02:31 pm

Minor oil leak in supply line at Colombo port


	

A minor oil leak was reported in a supply line at the Colombo port premises carrying oil to Kolonnawa today.

Petroleum Industries Minister Chandima Weerakkody said the leak was reported during a test run on an abandoned pipe line which was to be repaired.

He said the supply line would be repaired after cleaning the damaged area.
