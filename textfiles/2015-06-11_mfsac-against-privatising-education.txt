## Title: MFSAC against privatising education
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 11 June 2015 03:19 pm

MFSAC against privatising education


	


Members of the ‘Medical Faculty Students Action Committee’ (MFSAC) today handed over a ‘Sandeshaya’ to the Sri Lanka Medical Council (SLMC) in Colombo against the establishment of private medical colleges. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
