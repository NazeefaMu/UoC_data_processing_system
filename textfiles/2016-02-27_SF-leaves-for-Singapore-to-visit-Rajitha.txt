## Title: SF leaves for Singapore to visit Rajitha
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 February 2016 03:01 pm

SF leaves for Singapore to visit Rajitha


	

Regional Development Minister and Field Marshal Sarath Fonseka left for Singapore this afternoon to visit Health Minister Rajitha Senarathne, who is undergoing treatment at the Mount Elizabeth Hospital in Singapore.

President Maithripala Sirisena also left for Singapore this morning to visit Minister Senarathne.
