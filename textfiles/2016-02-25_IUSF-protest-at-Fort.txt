## Title: IUSF protest at Fort
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 25 February 2016 10:10 pm

IUSF protest at Fort


	



Members of the Inter University Students Federation (IUSF) today staged a protest in front of the Fort Railway Station urging authorities to resolve several demands, including the immediate filling up of university vacancies and increase of university admissions.
