## Title: Four women, one man drowned while bathing
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 July 2015 11:22 am

Four women, one man drowned while bathing


	

The bodies of four people including that of a man and three women were recovered from the Warapitiya Lake in Walasmulla in a search operation to the find the five who had gone missing while bathing, police said.
They said the woman who was rescued and rushed to the Kirama Hospital had died during treatment.
