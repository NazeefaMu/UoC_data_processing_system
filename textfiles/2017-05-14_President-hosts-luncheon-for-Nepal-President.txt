## Title: President hosts luncheon for Nepal President
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 14 May 2017 01:17 pm

President hosts luncheon for Nepal President


	



President Maithripala Sirisena hosted a luncheon today for Neal President Nepal Bidhya Devi Bhandari who was in Sri Lanka to participate in the concluding ceremony of the International Vesay Day celebration, at Hilton Hotel Colombo.Ministers Wijayadasa Rajapaksha and Thalatha Athukorala also were present at this occasion.Pix by President’s Media
