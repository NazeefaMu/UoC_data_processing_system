## Title: Three female students consume poison
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, December 5, 2015 - 01:00

Three female students consume poison

 
Three female students of Ramboda-Hindu High School consumed poison on Thursday after their Vice Principal refused to give them admission for the G C E O/L examination saying that they had not obtained enough marks at the term test.
The three students, residents of Kotmale-Ramboda were admitted to Gampola hospital.They were expected to sit the Ordinary Level examination which is to commence on December 8.
The students consumed poison on their way to the school.
