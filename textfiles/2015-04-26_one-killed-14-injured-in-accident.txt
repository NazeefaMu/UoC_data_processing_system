## Title: One killed, 14 injured in accident
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 April 2015 06:29 am

One killed, 14 injured in accident


	

One person was killed and 14 were injured after a van collided with a parked lorry at Palwehera, Dambulla on the Dambulla-Polonnaruwa Main Road early this morning, the Police said.
They said the victim was residents of Kekirawa. The injured were rushed to the Dambulla Hospital.
