## Title: Protestors demand approval to appointments
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 27 June 2016 03:12 pm

Protestors demand approval to appointments


	



The Ceylon Teachers Service Union and the Collective of the Western Province Undergraduates today staged a protest in Fort demanding approval to the appointments of the undergraduates. Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
