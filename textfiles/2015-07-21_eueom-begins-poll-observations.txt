## Title: EUEOM begins poll observations
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 21 July 2015 09:11 am

EUEOM begins poll observations


	


The European Union Election Observation Mission (EUEOM) today held a news conference in Colombo to clarify their election observance process in Sri Lanka. The EUEOM had deployed observers following a request made by the Elections Commissioner. Pix by  Pradeep Dilrukshana
