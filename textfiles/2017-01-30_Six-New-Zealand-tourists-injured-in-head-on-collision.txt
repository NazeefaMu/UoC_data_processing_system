## Title: Six New Zealand tourists injured in head-on collision
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 January 2017 09:39 am

Six New Zealand tourists injured in head-on collision


	

Eight persons including six New Zealand nationals were injured when a van and a car collided head-on at Kalalpitiya in Nittambuwa on the Colombo-Kandy Main Road this morning.

Police said the injured were admitted to the Wathupitiwala Hospital.

They said the van which was traveling towards Kandy with the foreigners had collided with the car.
