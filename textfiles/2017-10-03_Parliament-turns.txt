## Title: Parliament turns 70
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 3 October 2017 04:20 pm

Parliament turns 70


	



A special session to commemorate the 70th Anniversary of Parliament was held today. President Maithripala Sirisena, Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe and Speakers of the SAARC countries attended the session.
