## Title: President Maithripala Sirisena deposited Sacred objects in the Moragahakanda Reservoir
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, November 7, 2015 - 01:00

President Maithripala Sirisena deposited Sacred objects in the Moragahakanda Reservoir

President Maithripala Sirisena yesterday deposited Sacred objects in the Moragahakanda Reservoir which is under construction in the Matale district. The people in the Anuradhapura, Polonnaruwa, Matale and Trincomalee districts will be ensured water for drinking and agricultural purposes through this project.
