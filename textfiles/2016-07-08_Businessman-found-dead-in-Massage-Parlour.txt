## Title: Businessman found dead in Massage Parlour
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 8 July 2016 05:26 pm

Businessman found dead in Massage Parlour


	

A 64-year-old businessman was found dead inside a washroom of an Ayurvedic Massage Parlour in Katugastota, Kandy last evening, police said.


They said the man who had come to the Massage Parlour for a massage had gone to the washroom but had not returned for a long time and his body was found inside the washroom.

Katugastota police are investigating.
