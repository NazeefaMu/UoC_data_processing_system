## Title: SLTB bus topples killing 1, injuring 13
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 8 August 2017 01:24 pm

SLTB bus topples killing 1, injuring 13


	

One woman passenger had been killed and 13 other injured when an SLTB bus toppled at Aranayake early today, the Police said.

The bus was travelling from Mawanella to Aranayaka and had veered off the road and toppled when it tried to negotiate a sharp turn to the Ussapitiya Road on the Mawanella-Aranayake Road.

The Police said that the injured were admitted to the Mawanella Hospital.
