## Title: Several office trains cancelled due to strike
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 November 2015 03:34 am

Several office trains cancelled due to strike


	

More than 20 office trains had been cancelled this morning after locomotive engine drivers launched a strike against the assault on an engine driver yesterday at the Ragama Railway station, Railway Deputy Transport Director Vajira Saman Polwattage said. (Chaturanaga Pradeep)
