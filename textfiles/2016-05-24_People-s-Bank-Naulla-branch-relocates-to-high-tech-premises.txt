## Title: People’s Bank Naulla branch relocates to high-tech premises
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 24 May 2016 12:00 am

People’s Bank Naulla branch relocates to high-tech premises


	



 

People’s Bank recently relocated its branch in Naulla to a new location. The new premises houses state-of-the-art banking facilities whilst offering comprehensive financial solutions, including loans and pawning. 
The branch also has a 24-hour PET machine that will provide greater levels of access to those in and around the area.
The new two-storied branch was ceremonially opened by N. Vasantha Kumar - CEO/General Manager in the presence of senior officials from People’s Bank including Deepal Abeysekara -Head of Marketing and Research, K.B.Rajapaksa - Deputy General Manager(Enterprise Banking),Boniface Silva - Deputy General Manager (Channel Management), Mahinda Premanath - Deputy General Manager (Banking Support Services), A. G. Ranbanda - Matale Regional Manager, S. Shivanandaval, Chithra Wijethunga - Assistant Regional Managers and A.A.S Senivirathne – Branch Manager. Staff, well-wishers and other dignitaries were also present at the event.
