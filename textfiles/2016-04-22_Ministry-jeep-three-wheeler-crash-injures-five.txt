## Title: Ministry jeep- three wheeler crash, injures five
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 April 2016 11:45 am

Ministry jeep- three wheeler crash, injures five


	

Four children and another individual were hospitalized last night when a jeep belonging to the Additional Secretary of the Disaster Management Ministry K. Amalanadan crashed into a three- wheeler in Chilaw.

The injured, travelling in the three- wheeler, were admitted to the Chilaw and Kathankudi hospitals.  

The Chilaw police are conducting further investigations. (T.I.Jawfer Khan)
