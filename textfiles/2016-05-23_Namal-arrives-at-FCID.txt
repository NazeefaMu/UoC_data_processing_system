## Title: Namal arrives at FCID
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 May 2016 10:36 am

Namal arrives at FCID


	

MP Namal Rajapaksa arrived at the Financial Crime Investigation Division (FCID) this morning to give a statement.

Sources said the parliamentarian was summoned to record a statement regarding the investigation into the construction of a house at Mihindu Mawatha, Mount Lavinia.

Earlier, the FCID examined the house on a court order in connection with the CSN investigation.
