## Title: Ceremony to hand over scholarships
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 9 February 2016 09:57 pm

Ceremony to hand over scholarships


	



Scholarships were handed over to the children of the army volunteer forces in a ceremony held at the BMICH. Defense Secretary Karunasena Hettiarachchi and the Commandant of the Sri Lanka Army Volunteer Force (SLAVF) HCP Goonetilleke also participated in the event.
