## Title: FIFA World Cup 2018: Russia beat Spain on 4-3 penalties
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 July 2018 10:35 pm

FIFA World Cup 2018: Russia beat Spain on 4-3 penalties


	

Russia beat Spain 4-3 in penalty shootout in a shock result to enter the quarter-finals in their World Cup last-16 clash in Moscow a short while ago.

The two were locked at one goal apiece at the full-time whistle leading to an extra half-an-hour of play. When neither side was able to break the deadlock the game went into penalties.
