## Title: Contact Operation Centers for emergency situations: MOD
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 May 2016 02:12 pm

Contact Operation Centers for emergency situations: MOD


	

The Ministry of Defence (MOD) advised public to inform the operational centers in case of emergency situations caused by the severe weather condition prevailing in the country.

Citizens can contact the nearest Police stations, Divisional Secretariat office or Disaster Management Centre if they could not contact the operational centers operated by the tri forces.

Army operation center - 011 2 434 251 / 011 3 818 578
Navy operation center - 011 2 445 368 / 011 2 212 230 / 011 2 212 231
Air Force operation center - 011 2 343 970 / 011 2 343 971
