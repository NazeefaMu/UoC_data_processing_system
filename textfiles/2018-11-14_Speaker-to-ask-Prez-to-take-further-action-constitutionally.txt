## Title: Speaker to ask Prez to take further action constitutionally
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 14 November 2018 01:20 pm

Speaker to ask Prez to take further action constitutionally


	

 The Speaker would take steps to send a copy of the No-Faith Motion and the resolution submitted by 122 MPs today that the recently appointed Prime Minister and the Cabinet was unconstitutional to President Maithripala Sirisena to take further action according to the Constitution, the Speaker’s Office said in a statement.
