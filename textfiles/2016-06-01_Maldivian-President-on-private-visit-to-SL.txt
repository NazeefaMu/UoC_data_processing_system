## Title: Maldivian President on private visit to SL
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 June 2016 07:04 pm

Maldivian President on private visit to SL


	

Maldivian President Abdulla Yameen arrived in Sri Lanka this afternoon with a seven-member delegation and expected to stay in the country till noon, tomorrow.

When asked, a spokesman for the Foreign Affairs Ministry said President Yameen was on transit and that it was a private visit. (T.K.G. Kapila)
