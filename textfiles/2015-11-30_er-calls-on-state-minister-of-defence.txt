## Title: US counterterrorism officer calls on state minister of defence
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 30 November 2015 11:14 am

US counterterrorism officer calls on state minister of defence


	


Stephan Goldrup, the US Counterterrorism Regional Coordinator called on State Minister of Defence Ruwan Wijewardene today at the Defence Ministry. US Embassy’s Political Officer Joseph Schaller and Ministry Secretary Karunasena Hettiarachchi were also present. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
