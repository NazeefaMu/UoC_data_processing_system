## Title: Term of LG bodies extended by 6 months
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 December 2015 08:01 am

Term of LG bodies extended by 6 months


	

The term of office of the local government bodies which was scheduled to expire on December 31 would be extended by six more months, President Maithripala Sirisena said today.   
He announced this decision while addressing a function held at the President’s College in Rajagiriya.
