## Title: Navy’s ambulance water craft completes its 100th journey
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 28 June 2016 11:33 am

Navy’s ambulance water craft completes its 100th journey


	



The ambulance water craft, built by the Sri Lanka Navy completed its 100th journey by last week. The craft was produced by the Navy for the benefit of civilians in the Northern Islands. The craft is equipped with three medical beds, two oxygen cylinders, IV stands, Blood Pressure monitoring equipment, patient monitoring facilities and all other necessities.
