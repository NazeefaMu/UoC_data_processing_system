## Title: SL-Finland sign MoUs
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 13 June 2018 01:07 am

SL-Finland sign MoUs


	



Sri Lanka and Finland signed three MOUs in the education, IT and Health sectors on June 12 as per negotiations which Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe had during his visit to Finland last year. Finland’s Economic Affairs and Employment Deputy Minister Petri Peltonen and several senior government officials participated in the event. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
