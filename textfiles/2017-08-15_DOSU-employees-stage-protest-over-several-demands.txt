## Title: DOSU employees stage protest over several demands
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 15 August 2017 11:18 pm

DOSU employees stage protest over several demands


	


Hundreds of employees of the Development Officers Service Union (DOSU) staged a protest in front of the Public Administration and Home Affairs Ministry today over several demands including salary anomalies. Pix by Nisal Baduge
