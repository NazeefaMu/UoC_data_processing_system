## Title: Sampath Bank opens 227th branch in Kelaniya
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 18 November 2016 12:00 am

Sampath Bank opens 227th branch in Kelaniya


	



 

Sampath Bank opened its newest branch in Kelaniya, at No: 1011, Biyagama Road, Singharamulla, Kelaniya, to provide its residents the world of state-of-the-art banking services to fulfil their banking needs with ease and convenience.
Sampath Bank invites all its valued customers to visit this newly opened branch to indulge superior banking experiences.
