## Title: UNP remains undefeated in Colombo MC
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 February 2018 02:16 pm

UNP remains undefeated in Colombo MC


	

The United National Party (UNP) continued to hold power of the Colombo Municipal Council obtaining a majority of 131,353 votes with 60 council seats.

The Sri Lanka Podujana Peramuna (SLPP) garnered 60,087 votes along with 23 seats.

Meanwhile, the Sri Lanka Freedom Party (SLFP) obtained 31,421 votes and 12 seats in the Council.
