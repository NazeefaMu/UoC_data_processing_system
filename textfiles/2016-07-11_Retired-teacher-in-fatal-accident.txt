## Title: Retired teacher in fatal accident
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 July 2016 05:31 pm

Retired teacher in fatal accident


	

A 76-year-old woman retired teacher has been killed last night, while crossing the road in Gonaduwa, Horana, police said.

She was hit by a van coming from Katunayake carrying German tourists to Kalutara.

The woman has died after being admitted to the hospital. (Bimal Shaman Jayasinghe)
