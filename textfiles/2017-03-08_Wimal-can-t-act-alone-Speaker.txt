## Title: Wimal can’t act alone: Speaker
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 8 March 2017 01:47 pm

Wimal can’t act alone: Speaker


	

Wimal Weerawansa’s Jathika Nidahas Peramuna (JNP) political party could not act as an independent party in Parliament, Speaker Karu Jayasuriya ruled in Parliament a short while ago.

The Parliament sessions had been suspended for ten minutes due to a tense situation ensuing the Speaker’s decision.
