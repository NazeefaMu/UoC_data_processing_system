## Title: Collective effort to eradicate dengue
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 30 March 2017 09:16 pm

Collective effort to eradicate dengue


	

The Civil Security Department (CSD) deployed 175 of its personnel in a campaign launched today to eradicate dengue breeding grounds in Colombo. The pilot project was conducted in Kotikawatta under the patronage of Director General of CSD Chandraratne Pallegama. Public Health Inspectors also joined in the today’s programme.
