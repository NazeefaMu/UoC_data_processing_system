## Title: President to guide SLFP at LG election
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 29 October 2015 01:41 pm

President to guide SLFP at LG election


	

President Maithripala Sirisena said today that he would provide leadership and guidance to the Sri Lanka Freedom Party (SLFP) to win the forthcoming Local Government election, the President’s Media Unit said.
