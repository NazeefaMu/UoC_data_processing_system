## Title: Indonesia military transport plane crashes in Medan
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 30 June 2015 08:29 am

Indonesia military transport plane crashes in Medan


	


Indonesian rescue personnel work at the scene of an Indonesian military C-130 Hercules aircraft crash in Medan today. An Indonesian military transport plane crashed on June 30 shortly after take-off in a city on Sumatra island, exploding in a ball of flames in a residential area. AFP
