## Title: President Sirisena calls on German President
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 18 February 2016 02:10 pm

President Sirisena calls on German President


	

President Maithripala Sirisena met German President Joachim Gauck at the Palace Bellevue in Berlin yesterday (February 17) evening. The two leaders held discussions on bilateral relations between the two countries. Pix by President's Media
