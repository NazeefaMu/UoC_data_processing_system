## Title: Basil, three others indicted
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 July 2016 01:53 pm

Basil, three others indicted


	

The Attorney General today served indictments on former minister Basil Rajapaksa and three others on charges of misappropriating Rs.33 million worth of funds belonging to the Divi Neguma Development Department during last year’spresidential election.

The Colombo High Court fixed the next hearing on August 30. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
