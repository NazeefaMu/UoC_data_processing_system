## Title: GL suspended from SLFP
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 November 2016 01:05 pm

GL suspended from SLFP


	

In response to online reports that he has been suspended from    his membership of Sri Lanka Freedom Party (SLFP), former Minister Prof.  G.L. Peiris said President Maitripala Sirisena was also suspended from party, and later it was revoked.

He said President Sirisena was suspended after he joined hands with a rival party to contest against the Presidential Candidate of his own party. Therefore, he said the two situations should not be dealt with differently.

Prof. Peiris recently became the chairman of new political party called ‘Sri Lanka Podu Jana   Peramuna’.
