## Title: Suspect commits suicide in prison cell in Kuruwita
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 December 2018 10:30 am

Suspect commits suicide in prison cell in Kuruwita


	

A 22-year-old suspect who was in remand custody at the Kuruwita Prisons is alleged to have committed suicide in his cell yesterday evening, Prisons Spokesman T.N. Upuldeniya said.

He said the suspect, a resident of Ruwanwella was remanded on December 21, over a drug-related offence.
