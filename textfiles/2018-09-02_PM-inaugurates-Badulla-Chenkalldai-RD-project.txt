## Title: PM inaugurates Badulla-Chenkalldai RD project
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 2 September 2018 02:20 pm

PM inaugurates Badulla-Chenkalldai RD project


	



The work on the Badulla-Chenkalldai Road Development project was inaugurated by Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe in Padiyatalwa today. Ministers Daya Gamage, Kabir Hashim and State Ministers M. L. A. M Hisbullah, Priyani Wijewickrama, Deputy Minister Anoma Gamage also participated. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
