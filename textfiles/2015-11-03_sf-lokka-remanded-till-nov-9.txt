## Title: Video: SF Lokka remanded till Nov.9
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 November 2015 12:20 pm

Video: SF Lokka remanded till Nov.9


	


	Eron Ranasinghe alias SF Lokka, who was arrested over the killing of Anuradhapura nightclub owner Wasantha Soyza was remanded today by the Anuradhapura Magistrate.
	The Police media division said the SF Lokka was remanded till November 9.
	The Magistrate on Monday permitted the Police to detain and question SF Lokka for 24 hours, it said. (Chaturanga Pradeep)
