## Title: JVP urges the enactment of 19A
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 24 April 2015 04:55 pm

JVP urges the enactment of 19A


	


The Janatha Vimukthi Peramuna (JVP) today held a demonstration outside the Fort Railway Station urging the enactment of the 19th Amendment to the Constitution. Pix by Shantha Rathnayake
