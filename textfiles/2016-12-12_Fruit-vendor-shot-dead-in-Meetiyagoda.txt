## Title: Fruit vendor shot dead in Meetiyagoda
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 12 December 2016 11:25 am

Fruit vendor shot dead in Meetiyagoda


	

A 45-year-old fruit vendor was shot dead inside his fruit stall at Meetiyagoda in Ambalangoda by an unidentified gunman arrived on a motorbike this morning.  


Police said the victim, a resident of Meetiyagoda, was pronounced dead on admission to the Batapola Hospital.
