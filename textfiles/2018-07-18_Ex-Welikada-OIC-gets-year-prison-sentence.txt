## Title: Ex-Welikada OIC gets 1 year prison sentence
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 July 2018 09:06 pm

Ex-Welikada OIC gets 1 year prison sentence


	

The Supreme Court today ordered to implement the one-year prison sentenced previously imposed on former Welikada Police OIC Chief Inspector Kamal Amarasinghe over an assault case.
