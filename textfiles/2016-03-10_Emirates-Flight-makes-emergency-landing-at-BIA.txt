## Title: Emirates Flight makes emergency landing at BIA
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 10 March 2016 07:13 pm

Emirates Flight makes emergency landing at BIA


	

A Sydney bound flight was diverted to the Katunayake Bandaranaike International Airport this evening after a passenger suffered a stroke in the flight.


Airport official said the Emirates flight had landed at the BIA at around 6.30pm and the passenger was admitted to a hospital.


They said the flight would depart after few hours to its destination.
