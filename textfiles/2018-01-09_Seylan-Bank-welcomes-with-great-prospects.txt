## Title: Seylan Bank welcomes 2018 with great prospects
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 9 January 2018 12:00 am

Seylan Bank welcomes 2018 with great prospects


	





Senior management and staff of Seylan Bank ceremoniously welcomed the dawn of the New Year at the Seylan Bank Head Office in Kolpetty, whilst commencing the first working day for 2018.Seylan Bank’s island-wide branches also celebrated the New Year with various multi-religious ceremonies that enhanced the cultural values of the respective branches.
