## Title: World VW Day 2018
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 1 July 2018 09:56 pm

World VW Day 2018


	


Volkswagen Beetle parade organized by the Volkswagen Beetle Owners' Club Sri Lanka was held at Colombo today for the seventh consecutive year marking the Annual World Volkswagen Day. A grand parade started from Galle Face Hotel with hundreds of ‘Mighty Bugs’ move towards Mount Lavinia Hotel.
Volkswagen clubs across the world, usually commemorate this annual celebration on June 22. - Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
