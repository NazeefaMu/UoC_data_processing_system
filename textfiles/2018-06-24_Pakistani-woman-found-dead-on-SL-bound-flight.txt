## Title: Pakistani woman found dead on SL-bound flight
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 June 2018 09:23 am

Pakistani woman found dead on SL-bound flight


	

A Pakistani woman, who was on board the UL 306 flight from Changi Airport in Singapore to Sri Lanka, was found dead yesterday, Police said

The deceased was identified as 86-year old Nisa Alimun.

The body is lying at the Negombo Hospital for post-mortem examination.

The Airport police are investigating.
