## Title: Protest rally against ETCA
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 11 February 2016 07:07 pm

Protest rally against ETCA


	



United Professionals’ Movement and organizations consisting of Engineers, Doctors and Professionals, today held a protest rally at the Viharamahadevi Park against the proposed Indo-Sri Lanka Economic and Technology Co-operative Agreement (ETCA). Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
