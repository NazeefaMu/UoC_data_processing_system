## Title: Special commodity levy on potatoes increased
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 August 2016 12:16 pm

Special commodity levy on potatoes increased


	

The Special commodity levy on a kilogram of imported potatoes has been increased by Rs. 5 with effect from today, the Finance Ministry said.

The commodity levy on a kilogram of imported potatoes was Rs. 35 earlier.

The ministry said the decision was taken to protect the local producers.
