## Title: No double standards: Ki-moon
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 September 2016 10:26 pm

No double standards: Ki-moon


	




United Nations Secretary General Ban Ki-Moon said he regretted the inaction of the United Nations in the last stages of the war in Sri Lanka and that the world body does not have double standards. He said the UN sticks to a single standard with regard to all member nations. He made these remarks at the concluding press conference held at the Galle Face Hotel in Colombo today.
