## Title: Thewarapperuma, three others remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 July 2016 08:05 pm

Thewarapperuma, three others remanded


	

Deputy Minister Palitha Thewarapperuma and three others, who were arrested today, were remanded till July 7 by the Matugama Magistrate’s Court a short while ago.
