## Title: Jim Beanz in Sri Lanka
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 12 October 2016 01:31 pm

Jim Beanz in Sri Lanka


	



Jim Beanz, the American vocal producer arrived at the Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA) this morning. The American producer is expected to conduct workshops and training to the local producers. Pix by Deepa Adikari
