## Title: Ruwan at Kelaniya
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 25 April 2016 09:52 pm

Ruwan at Kelaniya


	



State Minister of Defence Ruwan Wijewardene visited the Kelaniya Raja Maha Viharaya this evening to seek blessings after he was appointed as the new Assistant Deputy General Secretary Buddhist Affairs of the UNP. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
