## Title: Video: Pichcha Mal Pujawa at Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 24 July 2016 05:59 pm

Video: Pichcha Mal Pujawa at Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi


	



The annual Pichcha Mal Pujawa (offering of Jasmine flowers) was held at the Sri Maha Bo Tree premises in Anuradhapura yesterday. Thousands of devotes and monks participated in the annual offering which was held successfully. Pix by Dayarathna Abogama
