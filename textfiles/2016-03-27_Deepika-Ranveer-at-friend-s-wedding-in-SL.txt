## Title: Deepika, Ranveer at friend's wedding in SL
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 27 March 2016 10:51 am

Deepika, Ranveer at friend's wedding in SL


	



Bollywood actress Deepika Padukone has recently taken a break from the shooting of her Hollywood debut flick "xXx: The Return of Xander Cage." Deepika, with rumoured boyfriend Ranveer Singh, attended a friend's wedding in Sri Lanka. It has been reported that Deepika reached her best friend's wedding alone, where Ranveer surprised her with his presence. The duo is reportedly spending some quality time, far away from the paparazzi.
