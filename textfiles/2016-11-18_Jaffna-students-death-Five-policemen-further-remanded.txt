## Title: Jaffna students’ death: Five policemen further remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 November 2016 10:57 am

Jaffna students’ death: Five policemen further remanded


	

The five policemen who were arrested over the death of two Jaffna University students were further remanded till December 2 by the Jaffna Magistrate today.
