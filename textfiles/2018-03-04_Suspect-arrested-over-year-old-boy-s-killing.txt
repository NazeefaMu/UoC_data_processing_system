## Title: Suspect arrested over 10-year-old boy’s killing
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 March 2018 11:27 am

Suspect arrested over 10-year-old boy’s killing


	

The suspect in the abduction and killing of a 10-year-old boy in Chilaw, had been arrested from a fisheries village in Nayaru, Mullaitivu yesterday, Police said.

The body of the 10-year-old boy who was missing since Sunday (25) was found two days later at a forest area in Iranawila, Chilaw. (Jude Samantha)
