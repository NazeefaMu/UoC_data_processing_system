## Title: SLASA Bakmaha Ulela
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 29 April 2018 07:37 pm

SLASA Bakmaha Ulela


	



The Bakmaha Ulela of the Sri Lanka Singers Association (SLASA)  was held at the Kotte Ananda Shastralaya Grounds yesterday.

The event included all traditional Avurudu games and a cricket tournament that attarcted a large number of singers and fans. Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
