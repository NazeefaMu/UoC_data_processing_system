## Title: Uduwe Dhammaloka Thera remanded
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 9 March 2016 09:43 pm

Uduwe Dhammaloka Thera remanded


	



The Ven. Uduwe Dhammaloka Thera was today remanded till March 17 by Colombo Additional Magistrate Nishantha Peiris in the case involving a two and a half year old unregistered elephant calf that was found within the premises of his temple called 'Alan Mathiniyaramaya' in Polhengoda, Colombo.
