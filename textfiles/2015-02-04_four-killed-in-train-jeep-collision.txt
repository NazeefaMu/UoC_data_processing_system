## Title: Four killed in train-jeep collision
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 February 2015 09:40 am

Four killed in train-jeep collision


	

At least four people were killed when a jeep collided with a train travelling from Matara to Vavuniya at the Batuwatte Railway Crossing in Ragama, a short while ago.
Two injured persons were admitted to the Ragama Hospital, sources said. (LSP)
