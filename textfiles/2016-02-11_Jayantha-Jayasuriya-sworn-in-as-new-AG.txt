## Title: Jayantha Jayasuriya sworn in as new AG
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 February 2016 10:18 am

Jayantha Jayasuriya sworn in as new AG


	

Senior Solicitor General Jayantha Jayasuriya was sworn in as the 29th Attorney General of Sri Lanka before President Maithripala Sirisena a short while ago.


The Constitutional Council yesterday recommended Mr. Jayasuriya for the post of Attorney General (AG).
