## Title: No Lankan casualties in Brussels terror attack
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 March 2016 04:08 pm

No Lankan casualties in Brussels terror attack


	

There have been no reports so far of any Sri Lankans being injured in the terror attack at the Brussels Airport in Belgium, the Foreign Affairs Ministry said a short while ago.

“No reports of any Sri Lankan casualties so far. The Sri Lankan Mission is in touch with Belgium authorities on the crisis there,” the Ministry said.
