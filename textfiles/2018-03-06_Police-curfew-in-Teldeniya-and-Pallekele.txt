## Title: Police curfew in Teldeniya and Pallekele
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 March 2018 10:53 am

Police curfew in Teldeniya and Pallekele


	

A Police curfew has been imposed in the Teldeniya and Pallekela police divisions with immediate effect until 6 pm today, police said.

The Police curfew, which was imposed yesterday in the Kandy Administrative district, was lifted 6 am this morning.
