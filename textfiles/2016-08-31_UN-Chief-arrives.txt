## Title: UN Chief arrives
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 31 August 2016 07:44 pm

UN Chief arrives


	

The Secretary-General of the United Nations Ban Ki-moon arrived in Sri Lanka a short while ago.

During his three-day visit, he will meet President Maithripala Sirisena, Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe, and other members of the Government and of the Parliament.

Ban Ki-moon was welcomed by Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Harsha De Silva at the Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA). (T.K.G. Kapila)
