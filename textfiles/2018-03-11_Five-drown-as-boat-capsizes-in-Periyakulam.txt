## Title: Five drown as boat capsizes in Periyakulam
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 March 2018 01:52 pm

Five drown as boat capsizes in Periyakulam


	

Three men and two women drowned in the Periyakulam Tank in Nilaveli this morning when their boat capsized, the Police said.

Police Spokesman SP Ruwan Gunasekara said the boat capsized while they were plucking lotus flowers at the tank.

The Nilaveli Police are investigating.
