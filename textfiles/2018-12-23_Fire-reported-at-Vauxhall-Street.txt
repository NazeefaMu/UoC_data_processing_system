## Title: Fire reported at Vauxhall Street
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 December 2018 11:55 am

Fire reported at Vauxhall Street


	



A fire has been reported at a factory at Vauxhall Street in Colombo a short while ago, the fire brigade said.

They advised fire trucks from the Colombo Fire Brigade, Army and the Air Force had been deployed to douse the fire.
