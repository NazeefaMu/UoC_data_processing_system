## Title: Seminars, tuition targeting O/L banned from Dec. 02
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 November 2015 11:25 am

Seminars, tuition targeting O/L banned from Dec. 02


	

Conducting of tuition classes, seminars and workshops targeting the coming G.C.E Ordinary Level has been prohibited with effect from December 02 midnight till December 17, Examination Department announced.
Accordingly, the conducting of seminars related to a G.C.E. O/L subjects, the distribution of model question papers, the erection of banners and posters stating that model question papers will be distributed, the distribution of leaflets and handbills and publishing such activities through the use of print or electronic media have been prohibited.
