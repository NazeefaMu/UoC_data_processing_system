## Title: President inspects cocaine haul
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 14 June 2016 03:03 pm

President inspects cocaine haul


	

President Maithripala Sirisena today visited the customs Orugodawatte Rank Container Terminal (RCT) and inspected the Cocaine haul seized by Custom officials.

He thanked Customs and the Special Task Force officials for the detection and assured stern action would be taken against those engaged in drug smuggling. (Darshana Sanjeewa)
