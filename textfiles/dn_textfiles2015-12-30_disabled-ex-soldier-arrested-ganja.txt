## Title: Disabled ex-soldier arrested with ganja
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Wednesday, December 30, 2015 - 01:00

Disabled ex-soldier arrested with ganja

Police arrested a disabled ex-soldier for possession of 4 kg of cannabis, on Monday. He was traveling in a bus from Thanamalwila to Siyabalanduwa. Police said the arrest was made following a tip-off.
The accused, aged 50, is a resident of of Medahapara , Ekirikanda in Medawachchiya. He is to be produced before the Moneragala Magistrate Court. Siyabalanduwa police are investigating.
