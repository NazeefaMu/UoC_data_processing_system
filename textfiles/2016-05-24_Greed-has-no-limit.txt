## Title: Greed has no limit!
## Section: Political Gossip
## Date: 24 May 2016 12:00 am

Greed has no limit!


	



A purohita who is now putting the finishing touches to a two-storied building he recently put up to house his liquor outlet has been caught pirating electricity to the building. 

 


This purohita facing a financial crime charge is said to be raking in a huge profit from his roaring liquor business. Not contented, he had tried to save the money involved in getting a power connection and paying a monthly bill for supply of electricity!
