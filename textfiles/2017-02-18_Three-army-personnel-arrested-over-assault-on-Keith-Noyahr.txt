## Title: Three army personnel arrested over assault on Keith Noyahr
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 February 2017 09:10 am

Three army personnel arrested over assault on Keith Noyahr


	

Three army personnel including a major were arrested by the CID in connection with the abduction and assault on journalist Keith Noyahr in 2008, Police said.

They are to be produced in Mount Lavinia Magistrate’s Courts today.
