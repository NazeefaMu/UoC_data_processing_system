## Title: Traffic on Galle road restricted
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 8 January 2017 09:04 am

Traffic on Galle road restricted


	

Traffic on Galle road from NSA roundabout to the Galle Face Roundabout has been restricted from 8.00 to 10.00am today in view of the Late Prime Minister S.W.R.D Bandaranaike’s 118th birth anniversary commemoration in front of the Bandaranaike statue in the Galle Face.

Galle road from NSA Roundabout to the Janadhipathi Mawatha in the Fort will also be closed for traffic from 10.00 am to 1.00pm.
