## Title: Isipathana-Thurstan cycle parade
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 26 February 2015 03:45 pm

Isipathana-Thurstan cycle parade


	


A cycle parade in the lead up to the 52nd Isipathana-Thurstan cricket encounter was organized today. The parade began at Isipathana College, leading up to Thurstan College, before returning to Havelock. The encounter will be played tomorrow and day after, and will be the first big match of the year.
