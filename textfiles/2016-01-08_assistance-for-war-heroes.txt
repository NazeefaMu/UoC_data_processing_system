## Title: Assistance for war heroes
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 8 January 2016 10:02 am

Assistance for war heroes


	


State Minister of Defense Ruwan Wijewardene is seen in conversation with Mona Dayalathi wife of Major Marsood who died in the war seven years ago. He presented a certificate pledging assistance to build a house under Sathviru Sanhinda housing assistance scheme. The ceremony was held at Defense services College Slave Island in the presence of Army Commander Krishantha De Silva. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
