## Title: Gota arrives at PRECIFAC again
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 8 January 2016 04:19 am

Gota arrives at PRECIFAC again


	

Former defense secretary Gotabhaya Rajapaksa and several navy officials arrived at the PRECIFAC a short while ago to give a statement over the Rakna Arakshaka Lanka controversy.
An official of the commission said a navy official was giving evidence before the commission at the moment.
They appeared before the commission over the matter yesterday as well.(Ajith Siriwardana)
