## Title: Ruwan visits Kandy
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 23 April 2016 02:14 pm

Ruwan visits Kandy


	



State Minister of Defence Ruwan Wijewardene today visited the Sri Dalada Maligawa and offered flowers to the Temple of the Tooth Relic. He also met the Mahanayaka Theras of Malwatte and Asgiriya Chapters. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
