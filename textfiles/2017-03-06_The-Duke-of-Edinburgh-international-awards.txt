## Title: “The Duke of Edinburgh international awards 2016”
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 6 March 2017 09:27 pm

“The Duke of Edinburgh international awards 2016”


	



The Duke of Edinburgh International Awards 2016 was held at Temple Trees today with the participation of Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe. Prof. Maithri Wickremesinghe and several other Ministers including State Minister of National policies and Economic Affairs Niroshan Perera and Minister Mano Ganesan took part in the event.
