## Title: Navy vessels in India on goodwill visit
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 23 October 2016 06:02 pm

Navy vessels in India on goodwill visit


	




Sri Lanka Navy's Offshore Patrol Vessel SLNS ‘Sayura’ and Fast Missile Vessel SLNS ‘Suranimala’ left for India yesterday on a training and goodwill mission.


The Navy said the two vessels would remain in India till October 28. They are scheduled to take part in a series of training programmes.
