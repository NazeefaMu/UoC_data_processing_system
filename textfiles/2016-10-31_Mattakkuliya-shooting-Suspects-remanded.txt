## Title: Mattakkuliya shooting: Suspects remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 31 October 2016 07:53 pm

Mattakkuliya shooting: Suspects remanded


	

Eleven suspects, who were arrested in connection with the Mattakkuliya shooting incident in which four men were killed, were ordered to be remanded till November 11 by Colombo Additional Magistrate Chandana Kalansuriya today.

A team led by Kudu Roshan, is alleged to have been involved in the shooting. The shooting occurred at Kudu Roshan’s house situated in the Mattakkuliya housing scheme. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
