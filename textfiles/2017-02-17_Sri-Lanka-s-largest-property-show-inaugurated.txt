## Title: Sri Lanka’s largest property show inaugurated
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 17 February 2017 10:10 am

Sri Lanka’s largest property show inaugurated


	



Sri Lanka’s largest property focused show “Lanka Property Show 2017” was inaugurated today by Minister of Megapolis and Western province Development Patali Champika Ranawaka and Finance Minister Ravi Karunanayake at JAIC Hilton. Minister Susil Premajayantha was the guest of honour of the event. The two day exhibition is organized by Lanka Property Web.com. Pix by Nisal Baduge
