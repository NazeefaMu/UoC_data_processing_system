## Title: SriLankan to resume flights to Dubai tomorrow
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 August 2016 09:38 pm

SriLankan to resume flights to Dubai tomorrow


	

SriLankan Airlines will resume its flights to Dubai tomorrow after the reopening of the runway which was closed due to an Emirates passenger jet crash landed at Dubai Airport this morning, a spokesman for the SriLankan Airlines said.

Accordingly, UL225 is expected to depart from Colombo at 07.20 a.m. tomorrow. For further information please contact 1979.

An Emirates passenger jet crash landed at Dubai Airport this morning before exploding in a massive fireball on the runway.
