## Title: Road towards Galle Face closed from Lotus roundabout
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 31 October 2016 10:53 am

Road towards Galle Face closed from Lotus roundabout


	

The road towards Galle Face Green has been closed from the Lotus Roundabout due to a protest march carried out by disabled military and police personnel, police said.
