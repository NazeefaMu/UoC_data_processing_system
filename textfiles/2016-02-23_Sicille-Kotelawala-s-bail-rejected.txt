## Title: Sicille Kotelawala’s bail rejected
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 February 2016 12:07 pm

Sicille Kotelawala’s bail rejected


	

Sicille Kotelawala’s bail application was rejected by the Colombo High Court today.  

Colombo High Court Judge Manilal Waidyatilleka further remanded the sixth accused of the Golden Key case till April 27, when the case would be taken up again.  

Bail was rejected on the grounds of the accused, a major shareholder of the company and the wife of Mr. Kotelawala, absconding for several years. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
