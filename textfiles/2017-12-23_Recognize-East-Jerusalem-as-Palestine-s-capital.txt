## Title: Recognize East Jerusalem as Palestine’s capital
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 23 December 2017 01:42 pm

Recognize East Jerusalem as Palestine’s capital


	



The Sri Lanka-Palestine Friendship Association yesterday organised a campaign at the New Town Hall in Colombo to lobby for the recognition of East Jerusalem as the capital of Palestine. At this campaign, the organisers invited representatives of all political parties gathered there to sign a petition for this cause. Ministers Rajitha Senaratne and Rauf Hakeen are seen signing the petition. Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
