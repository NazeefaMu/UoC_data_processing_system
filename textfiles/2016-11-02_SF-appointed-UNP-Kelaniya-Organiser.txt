## Title: SF appointed UNP Kelaniya Organiser
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 2 November 2016 05:32 pm

SF appointed UNP Kelaniya Organiser


	



Field Marshal Minister Sarath Fonseka was appointed as the United National Party (UNP) Organiser of Kelaniya by UNP Leader Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe at Sirikotha today. Pix by Damith Wickramasingha
