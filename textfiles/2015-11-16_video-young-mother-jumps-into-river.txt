## Title: Video: Young mother jumps into river
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 November 2015 12:39 pm

Video: Young mother jumps into river


	

A-32-year old mother of one had jumped into Nilwala River from the Mahanama Bridge in Matara in an attempt to commit suicide, the Police said.
The woman, a resident of Morawaka, Neluwa, had visited Matara with her husband, and her 4-year-old son.
The Police said she had allegedly jumped into the river when the husband had gone to a mobile service provider along with the son, to get a detailed bill of the wife’s phone.
However, the woman who was struggling in the water for several minutes was rescued by residents. (Tex and Pix by Krishan Jeewaka Jayaruk)
