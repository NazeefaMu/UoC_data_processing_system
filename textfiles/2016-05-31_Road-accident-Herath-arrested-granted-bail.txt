## Title: Road accident: Herath arrested; granted bail
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 31 May 2016 01:05 pm

Road accident: Herath arrested; granted bail


	

Janatha Vimukthi Peramuna (JVP) Propaganda Secretary Vijitha Herath was arrested by the Welikada Police after his vehicle crashed into a telephone post at Rajagiriya last night, Police said.

They said he was released on police bail today.

According to reports the traffic police is expected to file a case against him on charges of damaging public property.  (Irangi Edirisinghe)
