## Title: Building collapses in Kaththankudi injuring 8
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 21 May 2017 07:45 am

Building collapses in Kaththankudi injuring 8


	

At least eight persons were hospitalised when a concrete slab of a building which was being constructed at Mary Amman Kovil in Kaththankudi collapsed yesterday.
 
Police said the injured were admitted to the Batticaloa and Ariyampathi Hospitals.
