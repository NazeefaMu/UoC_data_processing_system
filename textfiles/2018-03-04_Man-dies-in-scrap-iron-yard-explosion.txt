## Title: Man dies in scrap iron yard explosion
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 March 2018 12:31 pm

Man dies in scrap iron yard explosion


	

A 29-year-old man died in an explosion in a scrap iron yard at Palaviya in Puttlam today, Police said.

The victim was identified as an employee of the yard. (Jude Samantha)
