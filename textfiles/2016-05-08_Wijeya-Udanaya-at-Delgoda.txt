## Title: "Wijeya Udanaya 2016 at Delgoda"
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 8 May 2016 08:34 pm

"Wijeya Udanaya 2016 at Delgoda"


	



An event was organised for the Sinhala and Hindu New Year named “Wijeya Udanaya -2016” and featured many events including old ones played during this time of the year. It was held at the Delgoda Public Grounds today under the patronage of State Defence Minister Ruwan Wijewardene. Pic by Pradeep Pathirana
