## Title: Weather: Schools in Sabaragamuwa pro. closed today
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 26 May 2017 08:08 am

Weather: Schools in Sabaragamuwa pro. closed today


	

All schools in the Sabaragamuwa province will be closed today due to the prevailing adverse weather and floods situation, Sabaragamuwa Provincial Education Secretary Mahinda S. Weerasuriya said. (Ajithlal Shanthaudaya)
