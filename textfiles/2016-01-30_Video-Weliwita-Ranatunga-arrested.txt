## Title: Video: Weliwita, Ranatunga arrested
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 January 2016 11:34 am

Video: Weliwita, Ranatunga arrested


	

Former Presidential coordinating secretary and CSN Channel founding director Rohan Weliwita and its Director Nishantha Ranatunga were arrested by the FCID today over the alleged financial irregularities taken place at the CSN Channel.
The arrested are to be produced in the courts soon, it was reported.
