## Title: Woman hacked to death in Dehiwela
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 April 2017 11:34 am

Woman hacked to death in Dehiwela


	

A woman had been hacked to death, allegedly by her husband, at Attidiya, Dehiwela this morning, the Police said.

The 35-year-old was deceased is identified as resident of Dehiwela.

The Police said the husband alleged to have assaulted his wife due to a family dispute.

The suspect had been arrested and is to produced in the Mount Lavinia Magistrate’s Curt today.
