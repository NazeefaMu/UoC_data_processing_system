## Title: Pro Food/Pro Pack and Ag-Biz 2016
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 13 August 2016 02:09 pm

Pro Food/Pro Pack and Ag-Biz 2016


	



Pro Food/Pro Pack and Ag-Biz 2016, the processed food, beverage, packaging and agriculture exhibition organized by the Sri Lanka Food Processors Association with Lanka Exhibition and Conference Services was launched at the  BMICH on Friday by Ministers Rishard Bathiudeen and Minister Daya Gamage. It will end tomorrow. Pix by Kithsiri De Mel
