## Title: A.L.M Nazeer sworn in as UNP MP
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 February 2018 11:07 am

A.L.M Nazeer sworn in as UNP MP


	

A.L.M Nazeer sworn in as United National Party (UNP) MP today filling the National List vacancy created by the resignation of M.H.M Salman.
