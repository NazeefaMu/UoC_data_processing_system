## Title: Swapping positions
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 25 May 2017 01:48 pm

Swapping positions


	



Former Finance Minister Ravi Karunanayake today assumed duties as the Foreign Affairs Minister at a function held at the Foreign Ministry. He was greeted by his predecessor who is also his successor as the Minister of Finance, Mangala Samaraweera. Pix by AFP - Ishara S. Kodikara and Pradeep Pathirana
