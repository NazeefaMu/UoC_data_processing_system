## Title: A total of 230 arrested in recent violent acts
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 March 2018 09:41 pm

A total of 230 arrested in recent violent acts


	

A total of 230 suspects have been taken into custody as of today in connection with the recent violence incidents islandwide, Police Media Spokesman SP Ruwan Gunasekara said.

He said 161 suspects out of the total were taken into custody within the Kandy Administrative District.
The other suspects were taken into custody from several other districts.

SP Ruwan Gunasekara said Police is in the process of filing charges against these suspects.(Thilanka Kanakarathna)
