## Title: Video: Tissa Attanayake arrested
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 2 February 2015 08:28 am

Video: Tissa Attanayake arrested


	

Former Health Minister Tissa Attanayake had been arrested by the Colombo Crimes Division (CCD) a short while ago in Colombo on charges of creating forged documents during the January Presidential Election, Police Spokesman Ajith Rohana said.
Addressing a news conference held at the Police Headquarters a while ago, he said the MP was being interrogated by the CCD at the moment. (Lahiru Pothmulla & Sanath Desmond)
