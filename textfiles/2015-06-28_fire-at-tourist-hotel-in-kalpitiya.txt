## Title: Fire at tourist hotel in Kalpitiya
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 28 June 2015 03:40 am

Fire at tourist hotel in Kalpitiya


	

Forty foreign tourists had been evacuated following a fire that broke out at a tourist hotel in Kalpitiya last night, the Police said
The cause of the fire is not yet known.
Kalpitiya Police and villagers doused the fire and the tourists were transferred to other hotels in the area.  No casualties were reported.
The main building and seventeen Cabanas of the hotel had been gutted by the fire, the Police said.
Kalpitiya Police are investigating. Police are deployed at the scene to protect the property.(DS)
