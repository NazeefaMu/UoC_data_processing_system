## Title: People’s Bank foreign remittance promotion rewards 15 to-date
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 17 October 2016 12:00 am

People’s Bank foreign remittance promotion rewards 15 to-date


	



People’s Bank ‘Foreign Remittance Gold Fortune 2016’ promotion recently saw the completion of its 15thweek draw, announcing the winner of the week. 


The draw was launched with the aim of promoting the use of People’s InstantRemit, People’s e-Remittance, Swift and Telex amongst those living and working abroad when sending money to their loved ones in Sri Lanka.


Present at the draw was Kishani Narangoda - Assistant General Manager (Corporate Banking), People’s Bank and Nipunika Wijayarathne - Assistant General Manager (OCS & Financial Institutions), People’s Bank. The winner of the 15th Week draw is K. Renuka Devi from Kinniya branch.
The promotion encourages people to make remittances of any amount to be eligible to receive a gold sovereign coin on a weekly basis for a period of 28 weeks up to December 2016.
