## Title: Postal TUs protest
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 12 June 2017 02:47 pm

Postal TUs protest


	

The United Postal Trade Union’s Front staged a protest outside the Postal Headquarters in Colombo today over the government’s allege attempt to handover several buildings which house postal offices in Nuwara Eliya, Galle and Kandy to an Indian company to build hotels. -Pix by Waruna Wanniarachchi
