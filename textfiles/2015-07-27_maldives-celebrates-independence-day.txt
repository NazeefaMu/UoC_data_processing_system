## Title: Maldives celebrates Independence Day
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 27 July 2015 01:53 pm

Maldives celebrates Independence Day


	


The 59th Independent Day celebrations of Maldives were held yesterday at the country’s capital Male. Sri Lankan President Maithripala Sirisena graced the celebration as the Chief Guest. He was warmly welcomed by Maldivian President Abdulla Yameen Abdulla Gayoom. Pix by President’s Media
