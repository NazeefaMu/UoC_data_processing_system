## Title: South Africa win by four wickets
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 August 2018 09:57 pm

South Africa win by four wickets


	

South Africa won by four wickets in the 2nd ODI held with Sri Lanka in Dambulla. Quinton de Kock’s 87 paved the way for South Africa to pass the target of 244 set by Sri Lankans. The catches dropped by Sri Lankans also released the pressure that South Africans had as the match progressed.
