## Title: Avurudu Neketh Seettuwa handed over to President
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 11 April 2017 01:06 pm

Avurudu Neketh Seettuwa handed over to President


	



Avurudu Neketh Seettuwa (The document containing the auspicious times for the Sinhala and Tamil New Year) was handed over to President Maithripala Sirisena this morning at the President’s Official Residence in Colombo. In keeping with the tradition, the document was handed over to the President by the Minister of Internal Affairs, Wayamba Development and Cultural Affairs, S. B. Nawinna and the Secretary to the Ministry D. Swarnapala.Pix by President's Media
