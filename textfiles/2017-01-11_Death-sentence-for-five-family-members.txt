## Title: Death sentence for five family members
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 January 2017 03:44 pm

Death sentence for five family members


	

Matara Magistrate DamithThotawatte today sentenced to death five family members including two women after they were found guilty of clubbing to death a 44-year-old resident of Gandara Kottegeda in 2009. (Krishan Jeewak Jayaruk)
