## Title: Namal summoned by CID
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 8 June 2015 10:48 am

Namal summoned by CID


	

Former President Mahinda Rajapaksa’s eldest son, MP Namal Rajapaksa had been summoned to appear at the CID on June 12 to record a statement, MP Rajapaksa tweeted.
“Just received notice to be present at the CID on June 12 to record a statement, but no information has been given as to the reason,” the tweet said.
