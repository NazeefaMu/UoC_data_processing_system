## Title: 'Real SLFPers will form a govt. under Maithri's leadership'
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 September 2016 09:45 pm

'Real SLFPers will form a govt. under Maithri's leadership'


	

Despite obstacles, the real SLFP members would go ahead with the plans to form a government under the leadership of President Maithripala Sirsena, UPFA Secretary and Minister Mahinda Amaraweera said today.

He said him and President Sirisena, who is also the Chairman of the SLFP, are still making their utmost effort to unite all the parties in the future plans to form a SLFP government.
