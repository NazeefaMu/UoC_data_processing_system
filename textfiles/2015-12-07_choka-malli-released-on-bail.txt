## Title: Premalal Jayasekara released on bail
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 7 December 2015 09:57 am

Premalal Jayasekara released on bail


	

UPFA MP Premalal Jayasekara alias ‘Choka Malli’ who was in remand custody over a shooting incident in Kahawatta during the presidential election campaign, was granted bail by the Appeal Court today.
