## Title: Vocational Training Center in Mattala  inaugurated
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 7 January 2017 07:36 pm

Vocational Training Center in Mattala  inaugurated


	


Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe participated as a chief guest to inaugurated a vocational training center in Mattala as part of a massive southern development project. Ministers Sagala Ratnayake, Sajith Premadasa and Chinese Ambassador Yi Xianliang were also present .Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
