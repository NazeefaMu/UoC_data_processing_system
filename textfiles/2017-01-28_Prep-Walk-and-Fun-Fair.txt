## Title: Prep Walk and Fun Fair 2017
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 28 January 2017 10:51 pm

Prep Walk and Fun Fair 2017


	



Prep Walk and Fun Fair 2017, organised by S. Thomas' Preparatory School Kollupitiya as a fund raiser for the refurbishment of the school's Fort Rose building, was held today. Present and past students of the school participated at the event, which began with a walk from Taj Samudra to school, followed by a fun fair. Pix by Damith Wickramasinghe
