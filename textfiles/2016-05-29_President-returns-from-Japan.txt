## Title: President returns from Japan
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 29 May 2016 05:15 pm

President returns from Japan


	

President Maithripala Sirisena arrived in the country today after participating in the G7 Summit which was held in Japan on May 26 and 27.

The 42nd G7 summit was held with Leaders of G7 member states – Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, UK, USA, and representatives of the European Union. (T.K.G.Kapila)
