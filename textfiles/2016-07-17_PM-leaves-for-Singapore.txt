## Title: PM leaves for Singapore
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 July 2016 09:33 am

PM leaves for Singapore


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe left for a three-day visit to Singapore early this morning.

During the visit, Wickremasinghe and Singapore Prime Minister, Lee Hsien Loong, will sign several bilateral agreements.(T.K.G.Kapila)
