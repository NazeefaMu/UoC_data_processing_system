## Title: Bell 206 makes “hard landing” in Hinguraggoda
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 28 April 2016 12:13 pm

Bell 206 makes “hard landing” in Hinguraggoda


	

Bell 206 training helicopter has made a ‘hard landing’ during a training session in Hinguraggoda this morning, Air Force said.


Air Force Media Spokesman Chandima Alwis said trainee cadet officer and an instructor pilot had been in the helicopter at the time of the incident but no injuries were reported.
