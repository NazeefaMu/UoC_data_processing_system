## Title: Celebrating 75 years!
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 25 February 2017 07:10 am

Celebrating 75 years!


	



St. Joseph's Girls School, Nugegoda celebrates their 75th anniversary this year. Students, teachers and past pupils took part in a walk organised to mark the anniversary yesterday. The walk which commenced from Navinna was concluded at the school premises. Pix by Damith Wickramasinghe
