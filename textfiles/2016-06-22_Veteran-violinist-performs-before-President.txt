## Title: Veteran violinist performs before President
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 22 June 2016 06:28 pm

Veteran violinist performs before President


	



An eminent Indian violinist Professor V. Balaji of the Varanasi University, India called on President Maithripala Sirisena at the President’s Official Residence, today and performed before the President. Pic by President’s Media
