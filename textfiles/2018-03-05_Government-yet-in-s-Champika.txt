## Title: Government yet in 1980's: Champika
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 March 2018 09:23 am

Government yet in 1980's: Champika


	

Although the world is leading in social and economics with technological development, the Government and the country’s entire politics is yet in the 1980 decade, Megapolis and Western Development Minister Champika Ranawaka said.

He said the human civilization had moved in to fifth generation however it is an unfortunate that country’s politics yet remaining in minus fifth generation.

Therefore he said fifth generation politics is now needed to Sri Lanka. (Thilanka Kanakarathna)
