## Title: Killing of two Jaffna students: 5 policemen re-remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 December 2016 02:31 pm

Killing of two Jaffna students: 5 policemen re-remanded


	

The five policemen remanded in connection with the killing of two Jaffna University students were further remanded today till January 13 by Jaffna Magistrate S. Satheeshkaran.

The five policemen were arrested by the CID on October 21, in the aftermath of the two student’s deaths. (Romesh Madusanka)
