## Title: IUSF protests against SAITM issue
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 25 January 2018 05:39 pm

IUSF protests against SAITM issue


	



The Inter University Students' Federation (IUSF) staged protest march in Colombo today against the government’s move to convert the South Asian Institute of Technology and Medicine (SAITM) into the Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (SLIIT).
