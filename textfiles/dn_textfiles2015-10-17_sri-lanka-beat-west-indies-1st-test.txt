## Title: Sri Lanka beat West Indies in the 1st Test
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Sports
## Date: Saturday, October 17, 2015 - 08:30

Sri Lanka beat West Indies in the 1st Test

Sri Lanka beat the West Indies by an innings and 6 runs on the 4th day of the 1st Test being played at the Galle International Stadium a short while ago. Sri Lanka scored 484 in the 1st innings while West Indies  scored  251 & 227. Sri Lanka lead the series 1-0.
