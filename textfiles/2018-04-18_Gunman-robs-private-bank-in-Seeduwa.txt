## Title: Gunman robs private bank in Seeduwa
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 April 2018 11:06 am

Gunman robs private bank in Seeduwa


	

An unidentified gunman wearing a full face helmet robbed a private bank in Seeduwa town this morning.

Police said the suspect had fled the scene in his motorbike. Officials have taken steps to calculate how much was stolen from the bank.
