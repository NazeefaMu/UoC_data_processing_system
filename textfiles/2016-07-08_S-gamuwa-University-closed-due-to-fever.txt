## Title: S’gamuwa University closed due to fever
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 8 July 2016 12:26 pm

S’gamuwa University closed due to fever


	

The Sabaragamuwa university has been closed till July 20 due to a fever that is spreading among the students.

Acting Vice Chancellor H.S.R. Rosairo said the university was closed as a precautionary measure.

The students were asked to leave the university premises before 6.00 p.m. tomorrow. (Prince Ratnayake)
