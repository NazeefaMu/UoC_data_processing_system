## Title: Two youths killed in accident
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 October 2016 09:46 am

Two youths killed in accident


	

Two youths, both 17, were killed when their motorcycle skidded off the road in Sathuru Kondan, Batticaloa on the Eravur-Batticaloa main road last night.

Police said the victims were identified as Vajidaran Radikan and Kandasami Vidushan residents of Kudirippu. They had succumbed to their injuries after being admitted to the Batticaloa Hospital.
