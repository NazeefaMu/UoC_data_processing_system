## Title: Energy-from-waste
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 11 August 2017 06:34 am

Energy-from-waste


	



Construction work for two waste-to-power plants commenced at Muthurajawela under the patronage of President Maithripala Sirisena yesterday. Megapolis and Western Development Minister Champika Ranawaka and several ministers participated at the event. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
