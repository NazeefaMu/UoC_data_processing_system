## Title: Chulananda Perera is new Customs DG
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 December 2015 04:42 pm

Chulananda Perera is new Customs DG


	


	Former Immigration and Emigration Controller Chulananda Perera has been appointed as the New Director General of Customs, Customs Spokesman Leslie Gamini said. (Chaturanga Pradeep)
