## Title: A touch of India
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 24 October 2016 12:55 pm

A touch of India


	


Hi Life Exhibition, considered as India’s most fashionable showcase, was launched at the Cinnamon Lakeside in Colombo 2 yesterday. Designer-wear, home decor, accessories and luxury products created by leading Indian fashion designers will be available at the exhibition which would be concluded on Wednesday.
