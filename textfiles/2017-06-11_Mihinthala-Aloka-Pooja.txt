## Title: Mihinthala Aloka Pooja
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 11 June 2017 04:08 am

Mihinthala Aloka Pooja


	



Annual Mihinthala Aloka Pooja organised by the Associated Newspapers of Ceylon Ltd (Lake House) was illuminated by State Defence Minister Ruwan Wijewardene in Mihinthale today. Mihinthala Raja Maha Vihara Chief Incumbent Walawa Hangunawawe Dharmarathana Thera, Member of Parliament Chandima Gamage, Mihinthale UNP Organizer Sanjeewa Senevirathna North Central Provincial Council Opposition Leader Chanka Illaperuma were presented at the event. - Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
