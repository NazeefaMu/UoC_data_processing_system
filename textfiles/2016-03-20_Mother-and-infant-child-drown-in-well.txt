## Title: Mother and infant child drown in well
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 20 March 2016 09:05 am

Mother and infant child drown in well


	

Bodies of a 32-year-old woman and her child had been found in a well at Periyapei, Palai last night.

The Police said the postmortem investigations into the death had revealed that the woman and the child- aged one-and-half-year-old were died due to drowning.
