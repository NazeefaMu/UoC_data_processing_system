## Title: Train-car collision
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 16 September 2018 01:21 pm

Train-car collision


	



Four women were killed and another three hospitalised when the KKS –Matara express train crashed with a car at an unprotected railway crossing at Pandrikeida Kulam in Omanthai yesterday morning. Pics By Romesh Madhushanka
