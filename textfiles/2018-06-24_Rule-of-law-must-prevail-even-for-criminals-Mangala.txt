## Title: Rule of law must prevail even for criminals: Mangala
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 June 2018 08:56 am

Rule of law must prevail even for criminals: Mangala


	

In the wake of the death of Matara robbery’s main suspect, Finance and Media minister Mangala samaraweera said in a twitter message that the rule of law must prevail even when dealing with criminals.

He tweeted that the brutal murder of a Police Constable on duty in Matara cannot be justified by an equally brutal murder of the suspect while in police custody.
