## Title: Police Commission to investigate attack on students
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 31 October 2015 06:10 am

Police Commission to investigate attack on students


	

The National Police Commission (NPC) has decided to launch an investigation into the police attack on the students of the Higher Diploma in Accountancy while they were on a protest march on Thursday.
Commission Secretary Ariyadasa Cooray said an independent investigator would be appointed after having discussions with senior police officers with regard to the investigations.
