## Title: Imtiaz Bakeer Marker's son Adhil passes away
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 13 October 2016 02:07 pm

Imtiaz Bakeer Marker's son Adhil passes away


	

National Media Centre Chairman and former Media, Postal and Telecommunications Minister Imtiaz Bakeer Marker's son Adhil had passed away in London, UK yesterday.

Adhil was an Attorney-at-Law and was studying for his Masters in Comparative Politics at The London School of Economics and Political Science.
