## Title: Mahindananda grilled by CID
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 September 2016 10:29 am

Mahindananda grilled by CID


	

Parliamentarian Mahindananda Aluthgamage arrived at the CID to give a statement in connection with the construction of a house belongs to him at Kinsey Road in Colombo, Police said.
