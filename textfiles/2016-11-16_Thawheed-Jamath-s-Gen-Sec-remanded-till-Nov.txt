## Title: Thawheed Jamath's Gen. Sec. remanded till Nov 29
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 November 2016 03:44 pm

Thawheed Jamath's Gen. Sec. remanded till Nov 29


	

Thawheed Jamath's General Secretary Abdul Razik, arrested on charges of insulting Buddhism and reprimanding Bodu Bala Sena (BBS) General Secretary Galagodaaththe Gnanasara Thera in filthy language on social media, was today remanded till November 29 by Colombo Additional Magistrate Chandana Kalansuriya.(Shehan Chamika)
