## Title: State Minister Wijewardene facilitates assist to Salawa residents
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 15 September 2016 10:37 am

State Minister Wijewardene facilitates assist to Salawa residents


	


State Minister of Defence Ruwan Wijewardene attended a function at Rajasinghe National School in Hanwella on Wednesday where residents of Salawa those who were affected by the army ammunition dump fire were gifted with valuable kitchen utensils. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
