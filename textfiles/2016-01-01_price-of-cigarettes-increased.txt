## Title: Prices of cigarettes increased
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 January 2016 12:39 pm

Prices of cigarettes increased


	

The prices of all brands of cigarettes have been increased by rupees one or two from today, the Ceylon Tobacco Company said.
It said the prices of Gold Leaf, Dunhill Light and Benson & Hedges cigarettes has been increased by Rs.2 while that of Bristol and Capstan has been increased by Rs.1.
