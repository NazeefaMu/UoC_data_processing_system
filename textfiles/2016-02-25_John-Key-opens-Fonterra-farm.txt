## Title: John Key opens Fonterra farm
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 25 February 2016 08:38 am

John Key opens Fonterra farm


	



New Zealand Prime Minister John Key opened Fonterra demonstration and training farm in Pannala yesterday. Sustainable Development and Wildlife Minister Gamini Jayawickrama Perera and Fonterra Chairman John Wilson were also present. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
