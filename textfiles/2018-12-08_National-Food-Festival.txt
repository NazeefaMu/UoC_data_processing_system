## Title: National Food Festival
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 8 December 2018 06:21 am

National Food Festival


	

National Food Festival organized by the National Food Promotion Board was launched yesterday (7) at Green Path, Colombo. The exhibition which would be held until tomorrow(9) includes traditional, foreign and latest food items.  Pix by Kithsiri De Mel
