## Title: Korean job seekers
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 2 October 2016 01:00 pm

Korean job seekers


	

The Korean Language Proficiency Examination was started today at several centers in Colombo including Thurstan College, Isipathana College.

According to the Sri Lanka Foreign Employment Bureau, more than 13, 000 candidates sat the examination last year. Pix by Nisal Baduge
