## Title: Explosives found in Chavakachcheri house
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 30 March 2016 12:20 pm

Explosives found in Chavakachcheri house


	



A suicide kit, four claymore mines, four parcels of C4 explosives and ammunition were found in a house in Vallakulam, Chavakachcheri this morning.
