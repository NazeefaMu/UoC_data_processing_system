## Title: First Int’l Yoga Day in Colombo
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 21 June 2015 07:50 am

First Int’l Yoga Day in Colombo


	


Sri Lanka joined the first “International Day of Yoga’’ (IDY) celebrations with an official event at Galle Face, Colombo this morning. The event has been planned by the Indian High Commission in Sri Lanka. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
