## Title: Avant Garde operations handed over to Navy: Rajitha
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 November 2015 02:03 pm

Avant Garde operations handed over to Navy: Rajitha


	

Minister Rajitha Senaratne today said the President has ordered to hand over the operations of Avant Garde to the Sri Lanka Navy with immediate effect.
The decision was taken at a meeting held with the participation of President Maithripala Sirisena this afternoon.
Police Chief N.K. Ilangakoon, Attorney General, Navy Commander, representatives from the Rakna Lanka, Ministers Rajitha Senaratne, Patali Champika Ranawaka and Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe participated in the discussion.
