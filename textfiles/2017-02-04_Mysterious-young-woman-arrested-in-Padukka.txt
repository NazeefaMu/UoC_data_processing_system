## Title: Mysterious young woman arrested in Padukka
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 February 2017 11:23 am

Mysterious young woman arrested in Padukka


	

A 17-year-old female who had been loitering in the Padukka area for the past month and a half in a suspicious manner was taken into custody following information received by the Special Operation Investigating Unit, Padukka police said.

They said the unmarried young woman who had studied up to GCE O/L and did not have an identity card or any document in her possession to establish her identity had behaved like a dumb character when questioned, police said.

She is to be produced before the Awissawella Magistrate. (Somasiri Hinguralaarachchi)
