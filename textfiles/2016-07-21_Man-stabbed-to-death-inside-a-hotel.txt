## Title: Man stabbed to death inside a hotel
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 21 July 2016 11:42 am

Man stabbed to death inside a hotel


	

A 32-year-old man was stabbed to death inside a hotel at Minuwangoda early this morning, Police said.

They said a brawl between two employees of the hotel had led to the killing.

The suspect was arrested by the police.
