## Title: Rains to continue
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 October 2016 07:16 pm

Rains to continue


	

Rainy condition is expected to enhance to a certain extent in Northern and South-Western parts of the island in the next few days, Met. Department said.

According to them, showers or thundershowers will occur at times in the Northern, Western, Central, Sabaragamuwa and North-Western provinces and in the Galle and Matara districts.

Showers or thundershowers will occur elsewhere particularly, after 2.00 p.m.

Fairly heavy falls (about 75-100mm) are also likely at some places.

Temporary localised strong winds during thundershowers are expected and public is requested to take adequate precautions to minimise damages caused by lightning.
