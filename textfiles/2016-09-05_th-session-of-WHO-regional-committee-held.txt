## Title: 69th session of WHO regional committee held
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 5 September 2016 05:35 pm

69th session of WHO regional committee held


	



The 69th session of the World Health Organisation’s (WHO) South East Asian Regional Committee was held today at the Nelum Pokuna Theatre in Colombo. Premier Ranil Wickremesinghe, Health, Nutrition and Indigenous Medicine Minister Rajitha Senaratne, WHO General Director Margaret Chan and WHO South East Asia Director General Poonam Singh were present at the event.
