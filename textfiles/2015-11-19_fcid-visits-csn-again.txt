## Title: <U+200B>FCID visits CSN again
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 November 2015 05:15 pm

<U+200B>FCID visits CSN again


	


		 		 
		 
			
Police Financial Crimes Investigation Division officers visited the Carlton Sports Network (CSN) television office today for the second time to collect evidence relevant to the ongoing probe.
Police Spokesman ASP Ruwan Gunasekara said the officers went to the CSN office last evening to collect certain documents pertaining to the inquiry and relating to its assets.
The FCID officers had been escorted by a senior staff officer of the TV channel when they went to collect the documents. (Kurulu Koojana Kariyakarawana)
