## Title: Aluthgamage remanded
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 16 April 2018 06:26 pm

Aluthgamage remanded


	



MP Mahindananda Aluthgamage, who was arrested, released on bail and then remanded, is seen being taken to remand custody from the Fort Magistrate’s Court.
