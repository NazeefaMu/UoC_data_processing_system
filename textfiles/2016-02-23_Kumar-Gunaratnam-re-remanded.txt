## Title: Kumar Gunaratnam re-remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 February 2016 01:28 pm

Kumar Gunaratnam re-remanded


	

Frontline Socialist Party (FSP) senior member Kumar Gunaratnam was further remanded till March 1 by Kegalle Magistrate Prasanna Alwis today.

He was arrested by the Police in the Anguruwella area in Kegalle on November 04, last year on charges of violating immigration laws. (Rohan Kumara)
