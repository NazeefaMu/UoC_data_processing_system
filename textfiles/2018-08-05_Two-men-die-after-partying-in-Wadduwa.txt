## Title: Two men die after partying in Wadduwa
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 August 2018 10:01 am

Two men die after partying in Wadduwa


	

Two men aged 20 and 38, who were admitted to the Panadura Hospital last night due to an illness following a party at a hotel in Wadduwa, were pronounced dead while undergoing treatment.

Police said four men were admitted to the hospital due to the sudden illness.

The deceased were identified as residents of Divulapitiya and Kesbewa.

Police said the cause of the death had not been ascertained yet and that the post-mortem examination would be held today.
