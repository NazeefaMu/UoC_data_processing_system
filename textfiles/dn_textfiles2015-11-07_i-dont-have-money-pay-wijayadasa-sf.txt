## Title: I don't have money to pay Wijayadasa - SF
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, November 7, 2015 - 01:00

I don't have money to pay Wijayadasa - SF

 
Democratic Party Leader Field Marshal Sarath Fonseka yesterday hit back at Justice Minister Wijayadasa Rajapaskshe saying that he does not have money to pay what Rajapakshe demanded. "I am getting a megre pension of Rs. 50,000 and I do not have money to pay him as damages demanded by him," he said.
Fonseka added that Rajapakshe will have to provide accommodation at his house.
"He will have to feed me too," he said.
