## Title: 1,000 bikers killed in 2015
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 31 December 2015 08:13 am

1,000 bikers killed in 2015


	


	A total of 1,000 motorbike riders and passengers had been killed in fatal accidents this year, the Police said today.
	They said a majority of deaths had been reported in accidents which had occurred when motorbikes had collided with other vehicles.
