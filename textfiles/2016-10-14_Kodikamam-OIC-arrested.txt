## Title: Kodikamam OIC arrested
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 14 October 2016 01:22 pm

Kodikamam OIC arrested


	

Kodikamam Police OIC, who served as the Chunnakam Police OIC in 2011, was arrested by the CID last evening in connection with an incident where a suspect held in Chunnakam police custody was found dead.

Police said the suspect was found dead in a cell of the Chunnakam Police in November 2011.
