## Title: Employees protest against Archaeology DG’s removal
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 25 May 2017 09:20 pm

Employees protest against Archaeology DG’s removal


	



A group of employees of the Archaeological Department today organised a protest outside the department against the alleged move to remove its Director General. Protestors urged the Prime Minister to look into the matter and take necessary steps. Pics by Kushan Pathiraja
