## Title: Two including Pakistani arrested with heroin
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 February 2017 04:29 pm

Two including Pakistani arrested with heroin


	

Two men including a Pakistani national were arrested with 1.5 Kgs of heroin by the officials of the Police Narcotics Bureau (PNB) last night, Police said.

They said the Pakistani national was arrested at a hotel in Bambalapitiya with 1 kg of heroin and the Sri Lankan was arrested later in Wellawatte on information received by the Pakistani national.

They are being questioned under police custody.
