## Title: Arundika sits in Opposition
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 September 2017 02:38 pm

Arundika sits in Opposition


	

Former UPFA deputy minister Arundika Fernando sat in the Opposition in Parliament a short while ago after making a special statement.

The MP’s ministerial portfolio was stripped off by President Maithripala Sirisena on September 12.
