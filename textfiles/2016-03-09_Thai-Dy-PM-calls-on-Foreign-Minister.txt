## Title: Thai Dy PM calls on Foreign Minister
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 9 March 2016 05:16 pm

Thai Dy PM calls on Foreign Minister


	

 

Thailand Deputy Prime Minister Somkid Jatusripitak called on Foreign Affairs Minister Mangala Samaraweera at the Ministry in Colombo on Wednesday. Prime Minister Somkid arrived in Sri Lanka on Tuesday on a five-day official visit. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
