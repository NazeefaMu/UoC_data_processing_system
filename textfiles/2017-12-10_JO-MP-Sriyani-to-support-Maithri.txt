## Title: JO MP Sriyani to support Maithri
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 10 December 2017 08:53 pm

JO MP Sriyani to support Maithri


	

The UPFA Digamadulla MP Sriyani Wijewickrama who represented the joint opposition pledged her support to President Maithripala Sirisena today.

President Media reported that the MP met the President this evening at the President’s House and vowed to support his ongoing progrmme.
