## Title: ComBank opens branch at BIA’s Departures Terminal
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 3 February 2016 12:00 am

ComBank opens branch at BIA’s Departures Terminal


	

Opening of Commercial Bank branch at BIA Departures Terminal by the bank’s Assistant General Manager Operations Palitha Perera accompanied by Branch Manager Ushan Perera, Senior Regional Manager Colombo North Dharshanie Perera and Managing Director/CEO Jegan Durairatnam

 

 

Commercial Bank of Ceylon has opened a second branch at Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA) Katunayake, to serve the foreign currency needs of passengers using its Departures Terminal.


The new branch will be open around the clock, 365 days of the year and will serve Sri Lankans and foreigners leaving the country, the bank said. The new branch is the bank’s 248th in Sri Lanka. An ATM machine was also unveiled at the Departures Terminal on the same day by the bank. In October last year, the bank opened a similar branch at the airport’s Arrivals Terminal.


Commercial Bank also has a branch in Seeduwa, which operates from 9:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m., 365 days of the year, for the benefit of area residents and for the convenience of travellers flying  through BIA.
