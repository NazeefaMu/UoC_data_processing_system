## Title: Solangaarachchi appointed as Awissawella SLFP organiser
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 21 July 2015 08:17 am

Solangaarachchi appointed as Awissawella SLFP organiser


	


	Former Chairman of the Kotikawatte-Mulleriyawa Pradeshiya Sabha Prasanna Solangaarachchi has been appointed as the Awissawella SLFP organiser by President Maithripala Sirisena.
	President’s Media said the appointment letter of Solangaarachchi had been handed over by President at the Presidential Secretariat today. (LSP)
