## Title: Bus topples; 29 injured
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 21 July 2015 04:22 am

Bus topples; 29 injured


	


	At least 29 passengers including 20 students were injured after the bus they were travelling toppled into a paddy field at Dulpitiya on the Dikpitiya Road in Aranayaka this morning.
	Police said the injured passengers and the bus driver were admitted to the Aranayaka Hospital.Pix by Karunarathne Athukorala-Kegalle
