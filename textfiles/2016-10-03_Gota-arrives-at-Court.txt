## Title: Gota arrives at Court
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 October 2016 10:29 am

Gota arrives at Court


	

Former Defence Secretary Gotabaya Rajapaksa today arrived at the Colombo Chief Magistrate’s Court.

Rajapaksa is to request for permission to travel abroad amidst the inquiry conducted against him and seven others related to Avant Garde.

The former Defence Secretary, Avant Garde Maritime Services Chairman Nissanka Senadipathi and five others were granted bail on September 30 by Colombo Chief Magistrate Gihan Pilapitiya against charges filed by the Bribery Commission last week. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
