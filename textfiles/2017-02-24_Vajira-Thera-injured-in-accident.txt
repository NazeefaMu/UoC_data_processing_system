## Title: Vajira Thera injured in accident
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 February 2017 05:04 pm

Vajira Thera injured in accident


	

Sabaragamuwa University Chancellor the Ven. Professor Kamburugamuwe Vajira Thera has suffered minor injuries when the vehicle he was in had plunged down a precipice at Kadugannawa today, police said.

Four passengers including the Thera were injured in the accident and admitted to the nearest hospitals.

Hospital sources said the prelate was out of danger.
