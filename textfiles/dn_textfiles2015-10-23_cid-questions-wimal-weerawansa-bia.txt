## Title: CID questions Wimal Weerawansa at BIA
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Friday, October 23, 2015 - 05:00

CID questions Wimal Weerawansa at BIA

UPFA MP Wimal Weerawansa was questioned by the Criminal Investigations Department this morning, while trying to leave the country. MP Weerawansa was reportedly scheduled to leave for an European country when he was questioned by the CID at the Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA) regarding an issue with his passport.
