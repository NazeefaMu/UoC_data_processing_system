## Title: Hand grenades found in Kuliyapitiya
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 April 2016 11:15 am

Hand grenades found in Kuliyapitiya


	

Three hand grenades, hidden inside an ice cream container, were recovered from a land in Hettipola, Kuliyapitiya.

Police said they had requested the STF to dispose the hand grenades.
