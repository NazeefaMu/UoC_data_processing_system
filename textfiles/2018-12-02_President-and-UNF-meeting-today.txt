## Title: President and UNF meeting today
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 2 December 2018 02:20 pm

President and UNF meeting today


	

President Maithripala Sirisena and members of the United National Front (UNF) would have a special discussion this evening, sources said.

The President and the UNF representatives had a discussion last Friday.

UNP General Secretary Akila Viraj Kariyawasam said that the meeting on Friday was positive.
