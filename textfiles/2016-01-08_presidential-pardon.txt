## Title: Presidential Pardon
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 8 January 2016 12:44 pm

Presidential Pardon


	


President Maithripala Sirisena met the former LTTE cadre, Sivaraja Jenivan alias Senan, convicted on charges of attempting to assassinate him in 2006 and granted the convict a presidential pardon during the national celebration at the BMICH to mark the President’s first year in office.
