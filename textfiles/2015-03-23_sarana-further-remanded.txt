## Title: Sarana further remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 March 2015 09:53 am

Sarana further remanded


	

UPFA MP and Former Minister Sarana Gunawardane was further remanded till March 30 by the Attanagalle Magistrate Kavindya Nanayakkara.
Mr. Gunawardane was produced in the Courts today in connection with the case where he had allegedly swindled Rs. 9.6 million from a businessman. (Ananda Jayakodi)
