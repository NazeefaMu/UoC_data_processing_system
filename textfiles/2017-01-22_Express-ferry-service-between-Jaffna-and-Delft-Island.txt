## Title: Express ferry service between Jaffna and  Delft Island
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 22 January 2017 01:09 pm

Express ferry service between Jaffna and  Delft Island


	



‘Neduntharakei’, an express ferry service between Jaffna and Delft Island was inaugurated on Saturday (21). The project was funded by the  World Bank and the Australian government. Minister of Provincial Councils and Local Government Faiszer Musthapha, Chief Minister of the Northern Province C.V. Vigneswaran and several politicians were present at the occasion.
