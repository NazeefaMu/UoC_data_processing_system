## Title: A sister’s sacrifice for her younger brother
## Section: Political Gossip
## Date: 10 May 2016 12:00 am

A sister’s sacrifice for her younger brother


	



 

There is a Diyawanna member who secured his seat in the august assembly thanks to the feminine charms used by his elder sister on a former leading politician.

 


This bit of information came out during the recent fisticuffs that turned the House into a typical scene at Mariakade.


The Diyawanna member’s sister had to pander to every whim of former political heavyweight in quest of his political patronage for her younger brother and finally her efforts were crowned with success.
