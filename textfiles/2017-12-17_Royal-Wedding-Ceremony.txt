## Title: "Royal Wedding Ceremony”
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 17 December 2017 10:54 pm

"Royal Wedding Ceremony”


	



50 young Chinese couples entered into wedlock in a glittering wedding ceremony held at the Town-hall Grounds, Colombo this evening. The event was hosted by the Ministry of Tourism Development and Christian Religious Affairs incorporation with the Megapolis and Western Development Ministry. Ministers Ranawaka was joined by Ministers Rajitha Senaratne and John Amaratunga gave away marriage certificates to the 50 couples.
