## Title: NPC Dy. Chairman dies of heart attack
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 October 2016 11:49 am

NPC Dy. Chairman dies of heart attack


	

Deputy Chairman of the Northern Provincial Council Antony Jeganathan died of a heart attack this morning, Hospital sources said.

He is reported to have suffered a heart attack while he was riding his motorbike to Mulaitive this morning.

He was pronounced dead on admission to the Mancholai Base Hospital. (Romesh Madushanka)
