## Title: Devi Neguma to revert to Samurdhi Dept.
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 December 2015 06:02 am

Devi Neguma to revert to Samurdhi Dept.


	

Ministers had yesterday approved the reverting of the Divineguma Department to the Samurdhi Department, Cabinet Spokesman Rajitha Senaratne said today.
