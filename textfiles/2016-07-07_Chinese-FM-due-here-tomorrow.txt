## Title: Chinese FM due here tomorrow
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 7 July 2016 06:28 pm

Chinese FM due here tomorrow


	

Chinese Foreign Minister Wang Yi will arrive in Sri Lanka tomorrow at the invitation of Foreign Minister Mangala Samaraweera, the Ministry said today.

The visiting minister is scheduled to meet President Maithripala Sirisena, Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe and the Foreign Affairs Minister. He is scheduled to leave on Sunday.
