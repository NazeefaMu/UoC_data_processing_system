## Title: Protest March by pensioners in Colombo
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 9 October 2017 10:02 pm

Protest March by pensioners in Colombo


	



A Protest March was carried out today by several Pensioners’ Associations from many districts urging the officials to look into the inefficiencies in the Department to disburse pension payments to the Pensioners. Pix by Nisal Baduge
