## Title: Three injured in student group clash
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Local
## Date: Saturday, December 5, 2015 - 01:00

Three injured in student group clash

 
Three students were admitted to the Kandy Hospital with cut injuries following a clash between two student groups of two leading schools in Kandy at Ampitiya around 3.00 p.m. on December 3.
The injured students were attending GCE Advanced Level classes. Kandy Police investigating the incident said a longstanding rivalry among the students had led to the clash.
