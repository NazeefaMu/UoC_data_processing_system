## Title: Museum Director’s vehicle plunges into pond
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 November 2016 06:45 pm

Museum Director’s vehicle plunges into pond


	

The car belonging to the Director of the National Museum is reported to have plunged into the pond in front of Parliament this evening, Police said.

The driver, the sole occupant in the vehicle, escaped unhurt.
