## Title: Wanted murder suspect shoots himself
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 21 April 2016 11:23 am

Wanted murder suspect shoots himself


	

Sumith Dayaratne alias Podi Sagara, a suspect wanted for several crimes including four murders in Katunyake and Seeduwa areas had shot himself while being confronted by the Police at Gokarella last night.

The suspect who is a resident of Seeduwa had fled the area and had been hiding in the house of his ex-wife when the Police had confronted him. (Jude Samantha)
