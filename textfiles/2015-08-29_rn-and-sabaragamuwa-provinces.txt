## Title: Showers expected in Western and Sabaragamuwa Provinces
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 29 August 2015 09:57 am

Showers expected in Western and Sabaragamuwa Provinces


	

The Meteorology Department said today showers could be expected in the Western, Sabaragamuwa, southern and central provinces.
It said thundershowers may develop at several places in the Uva and Eastern provinces in the afternoon.
The department said southwest monsoonal winds could be strengthened up to 50 kmph at times over the country particularly over central hills.
