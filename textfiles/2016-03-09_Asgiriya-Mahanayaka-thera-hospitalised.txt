## Title: Asgiriya Mahanayaka thera hospitalised
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 March 2016 05:35 pm

Asgiriya Mahanayaka thera hospitalised


	

Mahanayake of the Asgiriya Chapter Ven. Galagama Attadassi Thera has been hospitalised after he fell in the bathroom of the temple this evening, hospital sources said.


The thera was admitted to the accident ward of the Kandy General Hospital.
