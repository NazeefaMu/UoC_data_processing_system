## Title: Old is gold: Volkswagen Day Celebrations
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 26 June 2016 12:31 pm

Old is gold: Volkswagen Day Celebrations


	

Annual World Volkswagen Day Celebration was held today with the participation of seventy three Volkswagen owners at the Galle Face Hotel.

The parade organized by the Volkswagen Beetle Owners’ Club was ended at the Mount Lavinia Hotel. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
