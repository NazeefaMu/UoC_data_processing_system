## Title: 11 injured in road accident
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 24 February 2017 03:28 pm

11 injured in road accident


	

Eleven people were injured when a van travelling towards Wellawaya skidded off the road and toppled over at Hulandawa on the Moneragala-Bibila main road this evening, Police said.

They said five women, three children and three men were among the injured who were admitted to the Moneragala Hospital.

The victims are residents of Dehiattakandiya.
