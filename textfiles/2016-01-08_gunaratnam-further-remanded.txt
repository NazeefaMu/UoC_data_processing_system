## Title: <U+200B>Gunaratnam further remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 8 January 2016 05:05 am

<U+200B>Gunaratnam further remanded


	


		 		 
		 
			
Senior member of Frontline Socialist Party (FSP) Kumar Gunaratnam was further remanded till January 22, by the Kegalle Magistrate’s Court today.
He was arrested by the Police in the Anguruwella area in Kegalle on November 04, last year on charges of violating immigration laws. (Rohan Kumara-Kegalle)
