## Title: Seylan Bank celebrates Thai Pongal
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 27 January 2016 02:58 am

Seylan Bank celebrates Thai Pongal


	


The staff of Seylan Bank celebrated the annual Thai Pongal festival on January 18, 2016, at the Seylan Bank head office, Kollupitiya.
Director/CEO Kapila Ariyaratne, the senior management and staff members of Seylan Bank graced the occasion.
