## Title: Video: Army rescue flood victims
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 15 November 2015 07:45 am

Video: Army rescue flood victims


	


Thousands of people affected and around two thousand people have been displaced due to the severe weather and floods. In these photographs, Sri Lanka Army is seen while rescuing hundreds of people trapped in houses after their places are flooded with rain water.Pix by Romesh Padushanka, Hiran Piyankara and Kanchana Ariyadasa
