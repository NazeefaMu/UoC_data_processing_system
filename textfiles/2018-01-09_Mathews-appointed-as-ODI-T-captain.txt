## Title: Mathews appointed as ODI, T20 captain
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 January 2018 11:40 am

Mathews appointed as ODI, T20 captain


	

Angelo Mathews has been appointed as the ODI and T20 captain of the Sri Lanka national team, the Sri Lanka Cricket (SLC) announced short while ago.

SLC President Thilanga Sumathipala officially announced the decision at a press briefing being held in Colombo.

The Sri Lanka Cricket Selection committee has decided to appoint Mathews as the captain following a discussion held with newly appointed head coach Chandika Hathurusinghe.Related
