## Title: Horse runs amok, trainer injured in Dambulla
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 October 2016 03:23 pm

Horse runs amok, trainer injured in Dambulla


	

A 54-year-old horse trainer is reported to have been injured after a horse went on rampage while it was being trained on the Dambulla-Kandalama Road.

The man is said to have been dragged along about 900 meters by the horse that panicked due to the sound of the vehicles.

He was admitted to the Dambulla Hospital and later transferred to the Kandy Hospital. (Kanchana Ariyadasa)
