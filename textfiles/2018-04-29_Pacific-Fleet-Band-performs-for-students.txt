## Title: Pacific Fleet Band performs for students
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 29 April 2018 09:23 pm

Pacific Fleet Band performs for students


	



The Pacific Fleet Band which is in Sri Lanka as a part of the Pacific Partnership 2018 performed at a number of schools in the Trincomalee area. Pictures show the band is performing at the Royal College Trincomalee.  Pix courtesy US Embassy Sri Lanka
