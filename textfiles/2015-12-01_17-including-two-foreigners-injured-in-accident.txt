## Title: 17 including two foreigners injured in accident
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 December 2015 04:39 am

17 including two foreigners injured in accident


	


	At least 17 passengers including two foreign women were injured when a private bus skidded off the road and plunged 80 feet down a precipice at Aruppala in Kandy last night.
	Police said the bus was travelling from Watapaluwa to Kandy when the accident occurred.
	Thirteen women including a German and a Canadian woman have been admitted to the Kandy Hospital.
	The bus driver and three others men were among the injured.
