## Title: ICC SL and CA SL signs MOU to promote Professional Development
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 11 December 2017 10:36 am

ICC SL and CA SL signs MOU to promote Professional Development


	



CA Sri Lanka and the International Chamber of Commerce Sri Lanka signed a MOU to jointly develop/enhance curricula relevant to the industry and Jointly promote Sri Lanka as a  business hub. Seen here are ICC Chairman Dinesh Weerakkody and CA Sri Lanka President Lasantha Wickremasinghe exchanging the agreement. Also in the picture are ICC Secretary Shanil Fernando, Treasure Sheanath de Soysa, ICC Senior Manager Nihara Nanayakkara, CA Vice President Jagath  Perera and CEO Aruna Alwis
