## Title: PM declares open Royal MAS Arena
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 28 March 2016 08:39 pm

PM declares open Royal MAS Arena


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremasinghe declared open the Royal MAS Arena today. Principal of the Royal College B. A. Abeyratne and MAS Holding Chairman Mahes Amalen were also present at the event. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
