## Title: Young purohita hooked on drugs?
## Section: Political Gossip
## Date: 3 May 2016 12:00 am

Young purohita hooked on drugs?


	

There is hush-hush talk in political circles that a certain young promising purohita with the gift of the gab has got hooked on some barbiturate.




His colleagues have observed an unusual tell–tale red in his eyes, which they suspect, has resulted from the frequent taking of a drug extracted from an exotic flower found in abundance in the country of the Rising Sun.
