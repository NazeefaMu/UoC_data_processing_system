## Title: Four killed in two road accidents
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 September 2016 10:54 am

Four killed in two road accidents


	

Four people were killed and three others were injured in two separate road accidents in Kuruvita and Naula yesterday, Police said.

They said two passengers who were travelling in a three-wheeler were killed and the driver was injured when their vehicle collided with a lorry and another three-wheeler at Willapitiya in Kuruvita last evening.

Police said two passengers in the other three-wheeler suffered injuries in the accident.

The deceased were identified as residents of Delgamuwa and Kuruvita. The driver of the three-wheeler was transferred to the Colombo National Hospital while the two injured passengers were admitted to the Ratnapura Hospital.

Meanwhile, a 16-year-old youth and a 27-year-old man were killed when their motorcycle collided head-on with a tipper truck at Naula on the Dambulla-Matale Road yesterday.

Police said the victims were identified as residents of Ududeniya. Driver of the tipper truck had been arrested.
