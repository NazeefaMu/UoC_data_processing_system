## Title: IUSF protest: Five hospitalized
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 31 August 2016 08:00 pm

IUSF protest: Five hospitalized


	

Five university students were admitted to General Hospital, Colombo during a protest at Kollupitiya Junction staged by the Inter University Students’ Federation (IUSF) against the controversial private medical campus South Asian Institute of Technology and Medicine (SAITM) in Malabe.

Police fired tear gas and used water cannons, on multiple occasions, to disperse the protest. (Piyumi Fonseka)
