## Title: Five more bodies recovered in Aranayake
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 22 May 2016 02:44 pm

Five more bodies recovered in Aranayake


	

Five more bodies had been recovered from the Aranayake landslide site making the total number of death 35, the Aranayake Divisional Secretary said today.

More than 80 houses have been completely destroyed while 109 people were reported missing due to the landslide. A contingent of 200 Army personnel is continuing relief operations in Aranayake area.
