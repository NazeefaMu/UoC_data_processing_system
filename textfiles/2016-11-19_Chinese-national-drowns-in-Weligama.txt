## Title: Chinese national drowns in Weligama
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 November 2016 09:09 am

Chinese national drowns in Weligama


	

A 29-year-old Chinese national drowned in the sea off Udupila, Weligama yesterday while he was sea bathing with a group of foreigners, Police said.

They said the victim was identified as Zhang Jing from China.

His body is lying at the Matara Hospital.
