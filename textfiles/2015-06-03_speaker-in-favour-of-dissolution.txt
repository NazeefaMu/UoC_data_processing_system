## Title: Speaker in favour of dissolution
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 June 2015 05:40 am

Speaker in favour of dissolution


	

Speaker Chamal Rajapaksa today told the parliament he was in favour of dissolving it when a large number of parliamentarians expressed sentiments to dissolve the parliament.   
 
The parliament session was later suspended for some time to hold a party leaders meeting regarding today’s’ business on appointing the members of the Constitutional Council.
