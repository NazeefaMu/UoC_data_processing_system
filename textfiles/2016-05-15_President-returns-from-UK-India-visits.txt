## Title: President returns from UK, India visits
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 15 May 2016 11:51 am

President returns from UK, India visits


	

President Maithripala Sirisena returned to the country last night from his official visit to the United Kingdom and India.

During his official visits, President Sirisena held talks with British Prime Minister David Cameron and Indian Premier Narendra Modi.

He also addressed the valedictory session at the Vaicharick Mahakumbh, which is being held as part of the Simhastha Mahakumbh in Ujjain, India. (T.K.G.Kapila)
