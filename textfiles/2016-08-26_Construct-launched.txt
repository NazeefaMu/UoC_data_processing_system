## Title: 'Construct 2016' launched
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 26 August 2016 12:30 pm

'Construct 2016' launched


	

Construct-2016 exhibition organized by the National Construction Association of Sri Lanka (NCASL) was launched by Minister Sajith Premadasa at the Sirimavo Bandaranaike Memorial Exhibition Center (SBMEC) in Colombo today. Ministers Wajira Abeywardane, Susil Premajayantha and Akila Wiraj Kariyawasam and Field Marshal Minister Sarath Fonseka were also present at the launch. The exhibition would be concluded on Sunday.
