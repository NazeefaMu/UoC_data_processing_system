## Title: EPIC Technology on show at Mobile Money & Agent Banking Summit in Myanmar
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 23 February 2017 12:00 am

EPIC Technology on show at Mobile Money & Agent Banking Summit in Myanmar


	

Sri Lanka’s premier and award-winning technology conglomerate, Epic Technology Group, synonymous for innovative world-class technology solutions, was on show at the eighth Annual Mobile Money & Agent Banking Summit 2017, held in Myanmar on February 21 and 22.
The eighth Annual Mobile Money & Agent Banking Summit in Myanmar is the only authoritative summit bringing together regulators (telecom officials and central bankers), financial institutions, payment solution providers, technology infrastructure developers and commercial end-users all in one forum. 
Over 200 participants attended the summit from the likes of directors, CEOs, CTOs, COOs and senior managers of organisations operating in the banking and financial services domain and views were exchanged on the hot topic of ‘Mobile Money & Agent Banking’.
Epic Technology Group CEO and Group Director Viraj Mudalige, who was one of the distinguished speakers at the Mobile Money & Agent Banking Summit 2017, stated, “I want the region to get a first-hand experience of Epic’s high-class innovative and disruptive ‘Fintech’ Solutions. Our out-of-the box approach in developing advanced yet affordable technology solutions has changed the traditional landscape of document management, secure mobile communication, mobile enterprise automation and information security domains.”
“Epic’s innovative technology solutions are designed and created by an in-house talent pool of over 200 in-house engineers and experts. We also have our very own Research and Innovation Centre with dedicated researchers who continue to push the limits of innovative world-class technology solutions.”
The Epic Mobile Wallet is one such innovation which is the world’s first-ever application that integrates payer and payee into a single wallet, whilst offering multiple payment modes as preferred by the users. 
This revolutionary product together with our Branchless Banker solution, Card Management solution, Dispute Management solution and 3D Secure solution, which are part of our versatile payment portfolio, have been experienced by the delegates and visitors from the summit and the enthusiasm  shown towards our Fintech solutions is truly  encouraging,” concluded Mudalige.
Epic Technology Group is an award-winning technology leader of international repute that ‘bridges humans and technology’ through innovative solutions. 
With an unprecedented number of national and international accolades won over the past years that includes the highest number of awards won by a single company at the National Best Quality Software Awards for five years, National Business Excellence Gold Award in ICT for three consecutive years, the first Gold Award at APICTA for Sri Lanka in the financial services sector shows technology dominance, product supremacy and entrepreneurial excellence of Epic.
