## Title: Shops in Olcott Mawatha catch fire
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 21 July 2016 05:30 pm

Shops in Olcott Mawatha catch fire


	

Several shops along the Olcott Mawatha in Pettah, near the Fort Railway Station, had caught fire a short while ago, police said.

They said the fire brigade officials had reached the location.
