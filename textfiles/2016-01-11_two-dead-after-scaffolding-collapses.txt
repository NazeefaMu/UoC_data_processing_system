## Title: Two dead after scaffolding collapses
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 11 January 2016 06:32 am

Two dead after scaffolding collapses


	

Two construction workers died after a scaffolding collapsed on them at the new Anuradhapura Bus Terminal, police said. The victims were middle-aged construction workers. The bodies were taken to the Anuradhapura Hospital, police said.
