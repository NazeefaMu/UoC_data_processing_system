## Title: Bishop Rayappu Joseph at ICU
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 May 2015 10:36 am

Bishop Rayappu Joseph at ICU


	

Bishop of Mannar Rev. Rayappu Joseph, who was on his way to Colombo to meet the visiting US Secretary of State John Kerry had fallen ill and was hospitalised on Saturday evening. 
He was to meet the US Secretary of State this morning. 
Source said the Bishop had suffered a stroke. According to sources from Bishop’s House Mannar he is undergoing treatment at the Intensive Care Unit of the National Hospital Colombo. (YP)
