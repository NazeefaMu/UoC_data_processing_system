## Title: Housemaid found dead at a house in Mt. Lavinia
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 28 March 2017 09:40 am

Housemaid found dead at a house in Mt. Lavinia


	

The body of 37-year-old woman, who was working as a housemaid at a house in Kaldemulla, Mount Lavinia, was found dead inside the bathroom of the house with burn injuries, Police said.

They said the victim, Thirumoorthi Thiruchelmi identified as a resident of Madolsima.
