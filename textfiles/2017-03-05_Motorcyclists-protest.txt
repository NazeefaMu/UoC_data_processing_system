## Title: Motorcyclists’ protest
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 5 March 2017 01:49 pm

Motorcyclists’ protest


	



A protest rally organised by the Ceylon Motorcyclists Association (CMA) was held today at the Galle Face Roundabout in Colombo. Hundreds of motorcyclists were seen walking towards the Presidential Secretariat before they were obstructed by the Police. Pix by Nisal Baduge
