## Title: Speaker informs President he will not attend all-party conference
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 18 November 2018 12:22 pm

Speaker informs President he will not attend all-party conference


	

Speaker Karu Jayasuriya today informed President Maithripala Sirisena that he would not attend the all-party conference scheduled for this evening at the Presidential Secretariat in Colombo.

A spokesman at the Speaker's Office said the Speaker would not attend the discussions on the basis that it would be among political party representatives.
