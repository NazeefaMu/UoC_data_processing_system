## Title: All-party meeting commenced
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 22 October 2015 01:05 pm

All-party meeting commenced


	


 

	The all-party meeting to consult the opinions of political party leaders to discuss a domestic mechanism for inquiring into the alleged war crimes, leveled through the UNHRC resolution, is currently underway at the Presidential Secretariat. President Maithripala Sirisena had invited 21 political parties to take part in the meeting.  Pix by Nisal Baduge
