## Title: Yoshitha gives statement to FCID
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 10 May 2016 09:24 am

Yoshitha gives statement to FCID


	

Former president Mahinda Rajapaksa’s son Yoshitha arrived at the Financial Crime Investigation Division (FCID) a short while ago.

He was summoned to give a statement regarding the alleged financial irregularities at the Carlton Sports Network (CSN).
