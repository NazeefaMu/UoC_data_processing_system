## Title: Police teargas protesting students
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 31 August 2016 05:08 pm

Police teargas protesting students


	

Police fired tear gas and used water cannon to disperse protesters of the Inter University Students' Federation (IUSF) who were marching towards the Temple Trees a short while ago.

The protest begun from Sri Jayewardenepura University and the protesters were headed towards the Temple Trees violating a court’s order issued not to proceed towards High Security Zones.

The IUSF launched the protest against the South Asian Institute of Technology and Medicine (SAITM).
