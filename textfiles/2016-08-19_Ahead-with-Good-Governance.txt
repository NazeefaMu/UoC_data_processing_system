## Title: Ahead with Good Governance
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 19 August 2016 06:14 pm

Ahead with Good Governance


	



The First Anniversary of the Good Governance Government (Yahapalanaya) was marked at the Sanath Jayasuriya Stadium in Matara today. President Maithripala Sirisena, Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe, Foreign Minister Mangala Samaraweera, SLFP General Secretary Duminda Dissanayake, Ministers Daya Gamage, Sagala Ratnayake, Gayantha Karunathilake and MP Buddhika Pathirana were on the stage waving to the large crowd of supporters who had gathered there.
