## Title: Maradana Community Police OIC found dead
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 5 March 2017 08:58 pm

Maradana Community Police OIC found dead


	

The OIC (IP) attached to the Community Police Unit of the Maradana Police Station was found dead in his official quarters this evening, police said.

Nihal Jayaweera ,58, was found dead in his room this evening.

Maradana Police is conducting further investigations into the incident.
