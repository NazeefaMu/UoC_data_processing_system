## Title: SL nominates Amari Wijewardena to UK
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 7 February 2016 11:02 pm

SL nominates Amari Wijewardena to UK


	



The government has nominated the Swadeshi Industrial Works PLC Chairperson Amari Wijewardena as the new Sri Lankan High Commissioner in London, UK, sources said.

The name of Ms Wijewardene has reportedly been submitted to the Parliament’s Committee on High Posts for approval.
The post fell vacant following the resignation of Dr Chris Nonis in 2014.(Thilini de SIlva)
