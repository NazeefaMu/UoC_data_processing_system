## Title: Rear Admiral Neil Rosayro transferred to Colombo
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 27 May 2016 10:43 pm

Rear Admiral Neil Rosayro transferred to Colombo


	

Navy Commanding Officer of the Eastern province, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Navy Rear Admiral Neil Rosayro has been transferred to Colombo.


He has been transferred Colombo as the Deputy Chief of Staff of Sri Lanka Navy and Commandant Volunteer Naval Force. Meanwhile, Rear Admiral Travis Sinniah has been appointed as Commanding Officer of the Eastern province.
