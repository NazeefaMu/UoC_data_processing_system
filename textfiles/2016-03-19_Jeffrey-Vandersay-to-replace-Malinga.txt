## Title: Jeffrey Vandersay to replace Malinga
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 March 2016 02:23 pm

Jeffrey Vandersay to replace Malinga


	

Leg-spinner Jeffrey Vandersay has been named for the Sri Lanka squad in the T-20 World Cup as the replacement for Lasith Malinga, who returned to Sri Lanka due to his knee injury, sources said.
