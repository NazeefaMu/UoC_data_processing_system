## Title: Freedom!
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 7 July 2016 05:17 pm

Freedom!


	



Deputy Minister Palitha Thewarapperuma, who was arrested and remanded along with nine mothers on charges of unlawful assembly and forcing the admission of 10 students to the Meegahatenna Primary School, was released yesterday. He is seen surrounded by the students who are overjoyed that their mothers were released today. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
