## Title: Sea rescue training at Panadura beach
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 21 April 2016 02:12 pm

Sea rescue training at Panadura beach


	

 



The Sri Lanka Air Force conducted an Air Sea Rescue (ASR) refresher exercise along the shoreline of Panadura yesterday morning. Pilots, Fire fighters, Gunners and Air Gunners along with instructors took part in this training exercise conducted by the No. 4 Helicopter Squadron at SLAF Base Ratmalana.Pix by SLAF media
