## Title: Deshapriya appointed Elections Commission Chairman
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 November 2015 12:17 pm

Deshapriya appointed Elections Commission Chairman


	

Commissioner of Elections Mahinda Deshapriya has been appointed as the Chairman of the Elections Commission, Mr. Deshapriya said today.
Meanwhile, N.J. Abeysekara and former Vice Chancellor of Jaffna University, S. Ratnajeevan H. Hoole had also been appointed as members of the Commission.
