## Title: Paada Yathra now in Weweldeniya town
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 July 2016 04:12 pm

Paada Yathra now in Weweldeniya town


	

The Jana Satana Paada Yathra organised by the Joint Opposition was passing Weweldeniya town a short while ago on its third day.

The procession commenced its third day in Galigamuwa Town this morning and would march to Nittambuwa Town today.

Former president and MP Mahinda Rajapaksa had not participated in the protest march today.
The procession would commence its fourth day from Nittambuwa Town tomorrow.
