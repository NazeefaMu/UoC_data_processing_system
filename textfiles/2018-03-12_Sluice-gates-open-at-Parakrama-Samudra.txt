## Title: Sluice gates open at Parakrama Samudra
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 12 March 2018 09:49 pm

Sluice gates open at Parakrama Samudra


	



Twelve spill gates of the Parakrama Samudra were opened this evening because of the rising water levels after the heavy rains in the catchment areas. Pix by W.A Piyathilaka
