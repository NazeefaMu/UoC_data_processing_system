## Title: PM returns from Singapore
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 September 2016 09:36 am

PM returns from Singapore


	

Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe returned to Sri Lanka last night from Singapore where he attended the Indian Ocean Conference 2016 organised by the Indian Foundation.

Mr. Wickremesinghe met US Assistant Secretary of State for Central and South Asian Affairs Nisha Biswal and Former President of Maldives Mahamoon Abdul Gayoom in Singapore during his visit. (T.K.G.Kapila)
