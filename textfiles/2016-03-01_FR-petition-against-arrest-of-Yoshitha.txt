## Title: FR petition against arrest of Yoshitha
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 1 March 2016 11:25 am

FR petition against arrest of Yoshitha


	

A Fundamental Rights petition has been filed at the Supreme Court today on behalf of Yoshitha Rajapaksa and three others against their arrest and being kept in remand custody.

The petition cites the IGP, FCID officers including twelve others as respondents. (Shehan Chamika Silva)
