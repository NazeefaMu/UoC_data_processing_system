## Title: 'Buddhist Ideals of Good Governance' launched
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 17 January 2017 01:19 pm

'Buddhist Ideals of Good Governance' launched


	



'Buddhist Ideals of Good Governance', a book authored by Ven. Tampalawela Dhammaratana Thera and Prof. François Chenet on the Buddhist teachings of good governance was launched yesterday by President Maithripala Sirisena at Water's Edge in Battaramulla.
