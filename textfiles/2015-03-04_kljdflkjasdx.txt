## Title: Customs donate ancient Ola leaves to National Archives
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 4 March 2015 04:20 pm

Customs donate ancient Ola leaves to National Archives


	


A large quantity of ancient Ola leaf manuscripts that had been confiscated by Sri Lanka Customs on various occasions was donated to the National Archives Department today. Director General Sri Lanka Customs Jagath P Wijeweera donated these manuscripts to Director National Archives Dr. Saroja Wettasinghe at a special ceremony held at the Customs Headquarters in Colombo Fort.
