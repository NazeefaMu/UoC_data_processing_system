## Title: Wellamadama premises of Ruhunu Uni.  temporarily closed
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 February 2017 04:30 pm

Wellamadama premises of Ruhunu Uni.  temporarily closed


	

The Wellamadama premises of the Ruhunu University were temporarily closed due to a tense situation prevailed between the students and the villagers, sources said.
