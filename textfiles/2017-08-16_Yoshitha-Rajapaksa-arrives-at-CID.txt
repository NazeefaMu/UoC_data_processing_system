## Title: Yoshitha Rajapaksa arrives at CID
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 16 August 2017 10:34 am

Yoshitha Rajapaksa arrives at CID


	



Former president Mahinda Rajapaksa’s son Yoshitha Rajapaksa arrived at the CID this morning to give statements in connection with the defender jeep donated to the Siriliya Saviya Foundation by the Red Cross in 2011 and thereafter allegedly been used to provide security to a VIP.

Former first lady Shiranthi Rajapaksa also gave a statement to the CID yesterday over the same matter.
