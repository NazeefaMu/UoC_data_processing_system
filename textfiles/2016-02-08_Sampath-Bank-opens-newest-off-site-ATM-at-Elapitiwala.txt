## Title: Sampath Bank opens newest off-site ATM at Elapitiwala
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 8 February 2016 08:47 am

Sampath Bank opens newest off-site ATM at Elapitiwala


	



Sampath Bank opened the newest off-site ATM at Nawaloka Junction, Elapitiwela, Ragama. 
This new ATM is equipped with general ATM features such as cash withdrawals, fund transfers, utility bill payments, Cardless Cash facility, Mobile Cash facility etc. Sampath Bank believes that this would add more convenience to their valued customers.
