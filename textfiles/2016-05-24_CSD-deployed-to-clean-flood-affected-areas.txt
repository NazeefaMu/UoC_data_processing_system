## Title: CSD deployed to clean flood affected areas
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 24 May 2016 09:03 pm

CSD deployed to clean flood affected areas


	



The Civil Security Department deployed more than thousand soldiers to clean the areas which were affected by heavy floods. They were deployed under the instructions of CSD Director General Chandraratne Pallegama. The initiative was launched ceremoniously at the Kolonnawa Divisional Secretariat.
