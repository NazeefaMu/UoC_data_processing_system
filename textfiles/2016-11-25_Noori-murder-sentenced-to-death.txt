## Title: Noori murder: 18 sentenced to death
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 November 2016 01:20 pm

Noori murder: 18 sentenced to death


	

Eighteen suspects including former Deraniyagala Pradeshiya Sabha Chairman Anil Chinthaka alias Athakota were sentenced to death by the Awissawella High Court today after they were found guilty of murdering Noori Estate Superintendent Nihal Perera and assaulting two of his security guards.

Three suspects were acquitted from the charges. (T. Farook Thajudeen)
