## Title: E01 segment cleared
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 9 February 2016 10:48 am

E01 segment cleared


	



The segment from Welipenna to Kurundugahahetekma on the Southern Expressway (E01) was reopened to motorists today, police said.

The segment was closed from 1.00 a.m. to 3.00 a.m. this morning due to a removal of a toppled container truck. Pix by Ethkandure Janath de Silva
