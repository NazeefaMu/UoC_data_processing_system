## Title: Allianz and SIB sponsor ‘Peter Pan Musical’
## Section: Financial News
## Date: 9 December 2016 12:00 am

Allianz and SIB sponsor ‘Peter Pan Musical’


	

Senaratne Insurance Brokers facilitated and Allianz Insurance sponsored an insurance cover to protect the cast and the crew of the ‘Peter Pan Musical’ performed by the students of St. Joseph’s College, Colombo and St. Bridget’s Convert, Colombo.The cover provides a personal accident cover, hospitalization and a liability cover during rehearsals and performance at Lionel Wendt Theatre from 12th to 18th December.
