## Title: Double homicide in Sella Kataragama
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 6 April 2016 09:53 am

Double homicide in Sella Kataragama


	

Two male bodies were recovered from a canal in Sella Kataragama on the Buttala Road this morning, Police said.

The victims, 45 and 50, who were residents of Tissamaharama had been hacked to death.

 Police also recovered a motorcycle near the scene which they suspect may have been used by the suspects for the crime.

Police are investigating the murders.
