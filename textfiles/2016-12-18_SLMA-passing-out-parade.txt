## Title: SLMA passing out parade
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 18 December 2016 11:55 am

SLMA passing out parade


	



A group of 124 Sri Lanka Military Academy (SLMA) Officer Cadets, including women officers were passed out during a passing out parade ceremony at Diyatalawa yesterday morning. Field Marshal Sarath Fonseka graced the ceremony as the Chief Guest at the invitation of the Commander of the Army, Lieutenant General Crishanthe De Silva and SLMA Commandant.
