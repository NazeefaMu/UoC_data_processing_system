## Title: TU Collective of the Auditor General protest
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 13 June 2016 07:08 pm

TU Collective of the Auditor General protest


	



The Trade Union Collective of the Auditor General’s Department yesterday staged a protest outside the Fort Railway Station demanding immediate approval of Parliament to the Audit Bill and to change the decision taken to amend the Bill. Pix by Samantha Perera
