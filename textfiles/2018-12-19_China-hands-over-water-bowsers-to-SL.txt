## Title: China hands over water bowsers to SL
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 19 December 2018 06:33 pm

China hands over water bowsers to SL


	



The Chinese Government handed over 90 water bowsers to Sri Lanka at a ceremony under the China Aid Project today. Chinese Ambassador in Sri Lanka, Cheng Xueyuan ceremoniously handed over the vehicles to President Maithripala Sirisena at the Presidential Secretariat. Pix by President's Media<U+200B>
