## Title: Son of MP Mahindananda arrested for drunk driving
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 25 September 2016 10:54 am

Son of MP Mahindananda arrested for drunk driving


	

MP Mahindananda Aluthgamage’s son has been arrested by the Kirulapone Police last night on charges of driving under influence of liquor.

Police said, Kanishka Aluthgamage was arrested in Kirulapone and has been released on police bail.
