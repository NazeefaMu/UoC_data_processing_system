## Title: Royal Omani Naval Ship arrives
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 12 October 2016 03:05 pm

Royal Omani Naval Ship arrives


	



The Royal Omani Naval Ship,‘Al Naasir’ arrived at the Port of Colombo this morning on a goodwill and replenishment visit. The ship was ceremonially welcomed by the Sri Lanka Navy in accordance with naval traditions on her arrival. The Omani vessel is scheduled to depart from Colombo Harbour on October 13th. Pictures courtesy Navy.lk
