## Title: Nalaka Godahewa and two others remanded
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 7 December 2015 10:31 am

Nalaka Godahewa and two others remanded


	


	Dr. Nalaka Godahewa, former Chairman of Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) and two others who were arrested in connection with an alleged fraud at the SEC in 2013, were remanded till December 14 by the Colombo Fort Magistrate.
	The other two who were remanded are SEC former Deputy Director Dhammika Manjira Perera and Roni Ibrahim.
