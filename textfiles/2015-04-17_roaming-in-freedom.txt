## Title: Roaming in freedom !
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 17 April 2015 11:51 am

Roaming in freedom !


	


Two leopards are seen at the Pinnawala open-air zoo declared open today by Sports and Tourism Minister Naveen Dissanayake. A large crowd visited the zoo after the opening. Local and foreign tourists will be admitted free-of-charge till April 30, 2015. Pic by Chamil Rupasinghe
