## Title: SriLankan welcomes first A320neo aircraft
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 28 February 2017 10:06 am

SriLankan welcomes first A320neo aircraft


	



SriLankan’s first A320neo aircraft was welcomed yesterday at the Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA) with a traditional water canon salute. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
