## Title: Speaker to probe MPs' bash?
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 December 2017 10:45 am

Speaker to probe MPs' bash?


	

A top official in Parliament is to lodge a complaint with Speaker Karu Jayasuriya against a group of MPs and some employees in the House who had allegedly held a party in the garage of the Parliamentary complex, inside sources said yesterday. It was reported that they had been consuming liquor.(YP & AS)
