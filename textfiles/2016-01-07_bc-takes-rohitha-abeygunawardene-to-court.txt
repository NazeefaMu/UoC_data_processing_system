## Title: BC takes Rohitha Abeygunawardene to Court
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 7 January 2016 10:45 am

BC takes Rohitha Abeygunawardene to Court


	

The Bribery Commission today filed legal action against UPFA parliamentarian Rohitha Abeygunawardene in the Colombo High Court on charges of unlawfully acquiring assets worth Rs.41.2 million between 2004 and 2006, sources said. (Shehan Chamika)
