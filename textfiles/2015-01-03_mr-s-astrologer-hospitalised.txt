## Title: MR’s astrologer hospitalised
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 3 January 2015 09:42 am

MR’s astrologer hospitalised


	

President Mahinda Rajapaksa’s Personal astrologer Sumanadasa Abeygunawardena was reportedly admitted to the Sri Jayewardenepura Hospital following a heart attack yesterday.
Hospital sources said that he was out of danger.
