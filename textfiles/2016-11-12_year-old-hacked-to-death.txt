## Title: 55–year-old hacked to death
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 12 November 2016 08:48 pm

55–year-old hacked to death


	

A 55-year-old father of three was allegedly hacked to death by his brother in law at Viharagala in Sooriyaweva today.

Police said the suspect was arrested with the sharp object which was used to kill the man. They said a family dispute may have led to the murder.
