## Title: Dullas resigns
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 19 August 2016 11:41 am

Dullas resigns


	

UPFA MP Dullas Allahapperuma today said he would resign from his post as SLFP Matara district leader.

He told a media briefing that he would inform President Maithripala Sirisena, who is the Chairman of the party, of his resignation on Monday.

“I decided to resign because MPs Mahinda Yapa Abeywardane and Kanchana Wijesekara were removed from their post as orgarnisers previously,” he said. (Kalathma Jayawardhane)
