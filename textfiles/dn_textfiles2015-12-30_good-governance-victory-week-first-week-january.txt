## Title: 'Good Governance Victory Week' - first week of January
## Supertitle: 
## Section: Political
## Date: Wednesday, December 30, 2015 - 01:00

'Good Governance Victory Week' - first week of January

The Government has declared the first week of January 2016 as 'Good Governance Victory Week' to commemorate President Maithripala Sirisena's victory at the last Presidential polls held on January 8 2015. Cabinet Spokesman cum Health Minister Rajitha Senaratne said the aim will be to celebrate the implementation of good governance.
