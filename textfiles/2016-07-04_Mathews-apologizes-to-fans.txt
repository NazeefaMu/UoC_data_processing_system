## Title: Mathews apologizes to fans
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 4 July 2016 12:06 pm

Mathews apologizes to fans


	

Sri Lanka Cricket Captain Angelo Mathew yesterday told fans that he was sorry he had let them down following the defeat of our team by England.

In a twitter message, Mathews said, “There will be some who will hate us, some will still love us, sorry for letting you down. It is tough times but will come back hard,”
