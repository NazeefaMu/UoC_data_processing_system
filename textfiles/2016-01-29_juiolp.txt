## Title: MR arrives at PRECIFAC
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 29 January 2016 05:49 am

MR arrives at PRECIFAC


	

Former president Mahinda Rajapaksa arrived at the PRECIFAC this morning to give a statement over the non-payment of bills owed to the ITN.
Mr. Rajapaksa was questioned regarding this matter on several previous occasions and he had averred that the Sri Lanka Freedom Party should be held accountable.Video by Richan
