## Title: Latvian President arrives in SL
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 7 January 2018 10:10 am

Latvian President arrives in SL


	

Latvian President Raimonds Vejonis arrived in Sri Lanka for a ten-day state visit yesterday.

The Latvian delegation is scheduled to meet President Maithripala Sirisena, Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe during their visit in Sri Lanka.(T.K.G. Kapila)
