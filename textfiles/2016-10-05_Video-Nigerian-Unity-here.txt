## Title: Video: Nigerian 'Unity' here
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 5 October 2016 05:02 pm

Video: Nigerian 'Unity' here


	



The Nigerian naval ship 'Unity' arrived at the Port of Colombo this morning on a goodwill visit. The ship was ceremonially welcomed by the Sri Lanka Navy in accordance with naval traditions on her arrival. The ship which is due to stay in Sri Lanka until 7th of this month is scheduled to engage in a number of programmes organized by the Sri Lanka Navy.
