## Title: 101st death anniversary of Henry Pedris commemorated
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 7 July 2016 10:48 pm

101st death anniversary of Henry Pedris commemorated


	



Minister of Mass Media and Chief Government Whip Gayantha Karunatilake garlands the stature of the late Henry Pedris, to mark the 101st year of his execution by the British rulers. The commemorative ceremony was held at Havelock Town. Pix by Kushan Pathiraja
