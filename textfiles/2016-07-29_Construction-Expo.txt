## Title: Construction Expo 2016
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 29 July 2016 02:05 pm

Construction Expo 2016


	



CONSTRUCTION EXPO 2016, the Building, Interior and Lifestyle Show, organised by the Ceylon Institute of Builders (CIOB) together with Lanka Exhibitions & Conference Services was launched by Megapolis and Western Development Minister at BMICH on Friday. Over 200 stalls in construction and building equipment, furniture, ceramic and tiles, interior and exterior household products and many more are available at the exhibition which would be concluded on Sunday.
