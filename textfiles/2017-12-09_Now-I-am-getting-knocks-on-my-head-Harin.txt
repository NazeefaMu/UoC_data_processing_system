## Title: Now I am getting knocks on my head ; Harin
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 9 December 2017 08:42 am

Now I am getting knocks on my head ; Harin


	

Telecommunication and Digital Infrastructure Minister Harin Fernando said that he could not give a single job to anyone from Badulla though he received more than 200,000 votes from that area and a a result now he was getting knocks on his head.

“I am helpless. I might be defeated next time, but can't help,” he lamented.(YP & AS)
