## Title: Rajitha case sent to Magistrate’s Court
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 2 April 2015 06:34 am

Rajitha case sent to Magistrate’s Court


	

Colombo High Court Judge Lalith Jayasuriya today ordered the Magistrate’s Court to submit a report after hearing the case filed through a Habeas Corpus petition against Minister Rajitha Senaratne, his wife and the younger son on charges of abducting an underage girl.
All the parties of the case agreed to the Magistrate’s Court hearing. The Judge ordered the complainant party to report the progress of the hearing on June 3. (TFT & MG)
