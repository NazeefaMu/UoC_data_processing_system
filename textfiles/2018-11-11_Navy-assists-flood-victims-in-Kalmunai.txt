## Title: Navy assists flood victims in Kalmunai
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 11 November 2018 12:56 pm

Navy assists flood victims in Kalmunai


	

Naval personnel belonging the Southeastern Naval Command reached out to assist the flood victims of Thirawanthiyanmadu in Kalmunai, trapped in the area without a mode of transportation, following the heavy downpour prevailed during the last couple of days.
