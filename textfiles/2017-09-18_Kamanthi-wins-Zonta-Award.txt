## Title: Kamanthi wins Zonta Award
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 18 September 2017 10:56 am

Kamanthi wins Zonta Award


	



Kamanthi Wickramasinghe of the Daily Mirror won the Zonta Woman of Achievement Award for Media at the Awards Ceremony held at the Cinnamon Grand Hotel last night. Pics by Kushan Pathiraja
