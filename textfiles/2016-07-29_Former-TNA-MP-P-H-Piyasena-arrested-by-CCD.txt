## Title: Former TNA MP P.H. Piyasena arrested by CCD
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 29 July 2016 01:20 pm

Former TNA MP P.H. Piyasena arrested by CCD


	

Former TNA Ampara District MP P.H. Piyasena was arrested by the Colombo Crime Division (CCD) in Colombo this morning over an alleged misuse of a government vehicle, Police said.
