## Title: Sumanthiran's security beefed up
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 10 February 2017 10:05 am

Sumanthiran's security beefed up


	

Speaker Karu Jayasuriya yesterday informed Parliament that he has ordered the defense authorities to provide adequate security to TNA MP M A Sumanthiran who is said to be facing death threats.

The Speaker said he made this decision following a request made by MP Sumanthiran and on reports of a plot to assassinate him. (Yohan Perera)
