## Title: CSD opens Hindu Kovil in Kilinochchi
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 6 March 2016 12:43 pm

CSD opens Hindu Kovil in Kilinochchi


	



The Civil Security Department (CSD) Director General Chandraratne Pallegama declared opened a Hindu Kovil constructed in Kilinochi yesterday. The CSD has also laid foundation stone for another Kovil in Vishwamadu. Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
