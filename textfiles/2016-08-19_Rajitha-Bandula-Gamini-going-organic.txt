## Title: Rajitha, Bandula & Gamini going organic
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 19 August 2016 07:51 pm

Rajitha, Bandula & Gamini going organic


	


Health Minister Rajitha Senaratne is seen sharing kiribath with former ministers Bandula Gunawardena and Gamini Lokuge, who represent the Joint Opposition, at the opening ceremony of Mr. Gunawardena’s son Chathura’s organic food centre in Athurugiriya today.
