## Title: Speaker visits Blue Ridge
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 29 March 2016 03:06 pm

Speaker visits Blue Ridge


	



Speaker Karu Jayasuriya, accompanied by Ministers Mangala Samaraweera and Wijeyadasa Rajapakshe, went on-board the US Navy’s flag ship ‘Blue Ridge’ that was part its 7th fleet, which arrived at the Colombo Port on a goodwill visit.

Speaker Jayasuriya is seen cutting a cake to mark the occasion in the presence of the two ministers and the US Ambassador in Sri Lanka, Atul Keshap. Pix by Pradeep Dilrukshana
