## Title: Shilpa Kala-2017
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 25 May 2017 01:49 pm

Shilpa Kala-2017


	


The handicrafts sales exhibition, Shilpa Kala-2017, organised by the Indian High Commission was launched by Speaker Karu Jayasuriya at the Mihilaka Medura in BMICH, Colombo on Thursday. Indian High Commissioner Taranjit Singh Sandhu also attended the event. The exhibition will be held till Sunday. Pix by Kithsiri De Mel
