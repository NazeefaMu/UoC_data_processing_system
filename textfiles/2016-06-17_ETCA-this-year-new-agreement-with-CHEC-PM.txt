## Title: ETCA this year; new agreement with CHEC: PM
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 17 June 2016 01:27 pm

ETCA this year; new agreement with CHEC: PM


	

The Indo-Sri Lanka Economic and Technology Cooperative Agreement (ETCA) will be signed this year while a new agreement will be signed with the China Harbour Engineering Company Ltd. (CHEC) on the Colombo Port City, Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe said today.

The Premier said this while making a special announcement at Temple Trees on the European Union (EU) revoking its ban on Sri Lankan fish exports.

He said Sri Lanka would also be granted the GSP+ facility by the end of this year. (Yohan Perera)
