## Title: Convocation of Army Command and Staff College
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 18 December 2015 06:49 am

Convocation of Army Command and Staff College


	


Graduation Ceremony of the Defence Services Command and Staff College for the Course No-9 was held on Thursday night under the patronage of Defence Minister of State, Ruwan Wijewardene. Dean of the Academy Major General Ubaya Perera was also present. Picture shows the Minister presenting a trophy to a graduate student, SQN leader M.Q.
