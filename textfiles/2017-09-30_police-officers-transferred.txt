## Title: 65 police officers transferred
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 30 September 2017 12:03 pm

65 police officers transferred


	

A group of 65 police officers attached to the Katugastota Police station have been transferred to the Kandy Police station with immediate effect, police said.

Police Spokesman SP Ruwan Gunasekera said that these transfers were made based on service requirements.
