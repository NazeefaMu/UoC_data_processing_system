## Title: Blood donation campaign at Gangaramaya
## Section: Caption Story
## Date: 22 October 2015 11:47 am

Blood donation campaign at Gangaramaya


	


A blood donation campaign organised by the Civil Security Department (CSD) and the National Blood Transfusion Service was held today at the Gangaramaya Temple under the instructions of Ven. Galaboda Gnanissara Thera and CSD Director General Chandraratne Pallegama. Defence Secretary Karunasena Hettiarachchi and Colombo Chief Magistrate Gihan Pilapitiya were also present at the event.  Pix by Pradeep Pathirana
