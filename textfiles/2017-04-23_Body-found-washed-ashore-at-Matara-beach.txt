## Title: Body found washed ashore at Matara beach
## Section: Breaking News
## Date: 23 April 2017 09:04 am

Body found washed ashore at Matara beach


	

The body of 59-year old male was found washed ashore at Matara beach last evening, Police said.

They said the victim was identified as resident of Kottegoda. Magistrate's inquiry and post-mortem examination are to be held today.
